
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

##### ยง 81.1 Definitions.
###### Recognized slaughtering establishment.

An establishment where slaughtering operations are regularly carried out under Federal or State inspection and which has been approved by the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service to receive animals for slaughter.
