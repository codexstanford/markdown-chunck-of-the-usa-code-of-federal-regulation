
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products
## Chapter I - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter F - User Fees
#### PART 130 - USER FEES
##### ยง 130.1 Definitions.
###### Miniature horse.

Any horse which at maturity measures 34 inches high or less from the ground to the base of the last hair of the mane at the withers.
