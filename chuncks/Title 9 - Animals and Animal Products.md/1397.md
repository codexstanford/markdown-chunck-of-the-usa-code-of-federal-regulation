
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

####### Certified brucellosis-free herd.

A herd of cattle or bison which has qualified for and whose owner has been issued a certified brucellosis-free herd certificate signed by the appropriate State animal health official and the Veterinarian in Charge.
