##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 317.16 Labeling and containers of custom prepared products.

Products that are custom prepared under ยง 303.1(a)(2) of this subchapter must be packaged immediately after preparation and must be labeled (in lieu of information otherwise required by this part 317) with the words "Not For Sale" in lettering not less than three-eighth inch in height.
