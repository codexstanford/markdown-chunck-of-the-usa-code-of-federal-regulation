##### Subpart R - Meat Salads and Meat Spreads
###### ยง 319.760 Deviled ham, deviled tongue, and similar products.
####### Provided,

(a) "Deviled Ham" is a semiplastic cured meat food product made from finely comminuted ham and containing condiments. Mechanically Separated (Species) may be used in accordance with ยง 319.6.
