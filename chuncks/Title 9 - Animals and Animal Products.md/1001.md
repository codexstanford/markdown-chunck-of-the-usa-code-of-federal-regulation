
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

##### ยง 56.1 Definitions.
###### Official appraiser (APHIS official appraiser, State official appraiser).

A person authorized by APHIS to appraise poultry for the purposes of this part. A State official appraiser is selected by a State and authorized by APHIS.
