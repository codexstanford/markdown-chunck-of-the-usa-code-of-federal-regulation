
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

##### ยง 101.3 Biological products and related terms.
###### Bacterial extract.

(p)An inactivated bacterial product which consists of the sterile, nontoxic, antigenic derivatives extracted from bacterial organisms or from culture medium in which bacterial organisms have grown.
