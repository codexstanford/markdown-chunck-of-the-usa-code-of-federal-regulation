###### ยง 113.47 Detection of extraneous viruses by the fluorescent antibody technique.
####### Provided,

(1) If the material under test shows any evidence of specific viral fluorescence, it is unsatisfactory and may not be used;That, if specific fluorescence attributable to the virus being tested for is absent in the positive control monolayers, the test is a No Test and may be repeated.
