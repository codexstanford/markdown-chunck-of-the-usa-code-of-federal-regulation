### Subchapter D - Exportation and Importation of Animals (Including Poultry) and Animal Products

##### § 94.32 Restrictions on the importation of live swine, pork, or pork products from certain regions free of classical swine fever.
###### Pork or pork products.

(c)The pork or pork products must be accompanied by a certification issued by a full-time salaried veterinary officer of the national government of the region of export. Upon arrival of the pork or pork products in the United States, the certification must be presented to an authorized inspector at the port of arrival.
