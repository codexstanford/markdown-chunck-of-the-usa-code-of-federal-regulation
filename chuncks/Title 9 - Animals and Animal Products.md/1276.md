
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

##### Subpart B - Cattle and Bison
###### ยง 77.14 Interstate movement from accreditation preparatory States and zones.
####### Except that:

(c) The cattle or bison are sexually intact animals; are not from an accredited herd; are officially identified; and are accompanied by an ICVI stating that the herd from which they originated was negative to a whole herd test conducted within 1 year prior to the date of movement and that the individual animals to be moved were negative to two additional official tuberculin tests conducted at least 60 days apart and no more than 6 months apart, with the second test conducted within 60 days prior to the date of movement;The second additional test is not required if the animals are moved interstate within 60 days following the whole herd test.
