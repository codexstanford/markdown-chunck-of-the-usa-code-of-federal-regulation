
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products
## Chapter I - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter B - Cooperative Control and Eradication of Livestock or Poultry Diseases
#### PART 56 - CONTROL OF H5/H7 LOW PATHOGENIC AVIAN INFLUENZA
##### ยง 56.1 Definitions.
###### Breeding flock.

A flock that is composed of stock that has been developed for commercial egg or meat production and is maintained for the principal purpose of producing progeny for the ultimate production of eggs or meat for human consumption.
