##### ยง 53.5 Disinfection or destruction of materials.
###### Provided, however,

(a) In order to prevent the spread of disease, materials contaminated by or exposed to disease shall be disinfected:That in all cases in which the cost of disinfection would exceed the value of the materials or disinfection would be impracticable for any reason, the materials shall be destroyed, after appraisal as provided in ยง 53.3.
