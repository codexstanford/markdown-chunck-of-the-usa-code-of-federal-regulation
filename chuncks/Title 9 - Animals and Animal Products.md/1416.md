
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

####### Surveillance,Brucellosis ring test.

(a)-(1)The brucellosis ring test shall be conducted in the State or area at least four times per year at approximately 90-day intervals. All herds producing milk for sale shall be included in at least three of the four brucellosis ring tests per year.
