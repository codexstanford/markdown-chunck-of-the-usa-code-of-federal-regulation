
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products

### Subchapter A - Animal Welfare
#### PART 1 - DEFINITION OF TERMS
##### ยง 1.1 Definitions.
###### Dwarf hamster

means any species of hamster such as the Chinese and Armenian species whose adult body size is substantially less than that attained by the Syrian or Golden species of hamsters.
