
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products
## Chapter I - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter C - Interstate Transportation of Animals (Including Poultry) and Animal Products
#### PART 77 - TUBERCULOSIS
##### Subpart B - Cattle and Bison
###### ยง 77.5 Definitions.
####### Nonaccredited State or zone.

A State or zone that is or is part of a State that does not meet the standards of the "Uniform Methods and Rules-Bovine Tuberculosis Eradication" or in which tuberculosis is prevalent in 0.5 percent or more of the total number of herds of cattle and bison in the State or zone.
