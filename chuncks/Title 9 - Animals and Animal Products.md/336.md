####### Facilities and Operating Standards
######## ยง 3.75 Housing facilities, general.
######### Structure:

(a)construction. Housing facilities for nonhuman primates must be designed and constructed so that they are structurally sound for the species of nonhuman primates housed in them. They must be kept in good repair, and they must protect the animals from injury, contain the animals securely, and restrict other animals from entering.
