### Subchapter A - Animal Welfare
#### PART 1 - DEFINITION OF TERMS
##### ยง 1.1 Definitions.
###### Major operative procedure

means any surgical intervention that penetrates and exposes a body cavity or any procedure which produces permanent impairment of physical or physiological functions.
