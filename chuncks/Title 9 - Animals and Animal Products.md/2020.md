
# Title 9 - Animals and Animal Products
## Chapter I - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter C - Interstate Transportation of Animals (Including Poultry) and Animal Products
#### PART 88 - COMMERCIAL TRANSPORTATION OF EQUINES FOR SLAUGHTER
##### ยง 88.1 Definitions.
###### Euthanasia.

The humane destruction of an animal by the use of an anesthetic agent or other means that causes painless loss of consciousness and subsequent death.
