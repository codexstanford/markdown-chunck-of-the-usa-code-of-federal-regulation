###### ยง 51.20 Definitions.
####### Designated brucellosis epidemiologist.

An epidemiologist selected by the State animal health official and the Veterinarian in Charge to perform the functions required. The regional epidemiologist and the APHIS brucellosis staff must concur in the selection and appointment of the designated epidemiologist.
