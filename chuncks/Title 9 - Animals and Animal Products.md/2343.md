##### Subpart C - Horses
###### ยง 93.301 General prohibitions; exceptions.
####### Specific prohibitions regarding contagious equine metritis; exceptions,Importation prohibited.

(c)-(1)Except as provided in paragraph (c)(2) of this section, notwithstanding the other provisions of this part concerning the importation of horses into the United States, the importation of all horses from any region that APHIS considers to be affected with contagious equine metritis (CEM) and the importation of all horses that have been in any such region within the 12 months immediately preceding their being offered for entry into the United States is prohibited.
