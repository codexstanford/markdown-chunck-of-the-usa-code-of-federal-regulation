
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter B - Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations
##### PART 713 - ACTIVITIES INVOLVING SCHEDULE 2 CHEMICALS
###### ยง 713.3 Annual declaration and reporting requirements for exports and imports of Schedule 2 chemicals.
####### Mixtures containing a Schedule 2 chemical.

(2)The quantity of each Schedule 2 chemical contained in a mixture must be counted for the declaration or reporting of an export or import only if the concentration of each Schedule 2 chemical in the mixture is 30% or more by volume or by weight, whichever yields the lesser percent. You must declare separately each Schedule 2 chemical whose concentration in the mixture is 30% or more.
