
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 772 - DEFINITIONS OF TERMS
###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Civil aircraft.

(Cat 1, 3, 4, 7 and 9) Those "aircraft" listed by designation in published airworthiness certification lists by civil aviation authorities of one or more Wassenaar Arrangement Participating States to fly commercial civil internal and external routes or for legitimate civil, private or business use. (see also "aircraft")
