
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce
### PART 8b - PROHIBITION OF DISCRIMINATION AGAINST THE HANDICAPPED IN FEDERALLY ASSISTED PROGRAMS OPERATED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE
#### Subpart B - Employment Practices
##### ยง 8b.15 Employment on ships.
###### et seq.,et seq.

No qualified handicapped individual possessing an appropriate license or certificate obtained from the United States Coast Guard pursuant to the requirements of 46 CFR 10.01-1and 12.01-1shall, on the basis of handicap, be subjected to discrimination in employment on ships under any program or activity to which this part applies.
