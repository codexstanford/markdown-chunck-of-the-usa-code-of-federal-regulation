###### ยง 781.1 Definitions of terms used in the Additional Protocol Regulations (APR).
####### International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA).

The United Nations organization, headquartered in Vienna, Austria, that serves as the official international verification authority for the implementation of safeguards agreements concluded pursuant to the Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of Nuclear Weapons (NPT).
