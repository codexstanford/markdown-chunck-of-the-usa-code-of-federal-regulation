###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Communications Channel Controller.

(Cat 4)-The physical interface which controls the flow of synchronous or asynchronous digital information. It is an assembly that can be integrated into computer or telecommunications equipment to provide communications access.
