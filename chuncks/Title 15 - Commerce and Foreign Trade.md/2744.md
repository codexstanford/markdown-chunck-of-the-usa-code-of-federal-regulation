
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter B - Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations
##### PART 710 - GENERAL INFORMATION AND OVERVIEW OF THE CHEMICAL WEAPONS CONVENTION REGULATIONS (CWCR)
###### ยง 710.1 Definitions of terms used in the Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations (CWCR).
####### You.

The term "you" or "your" means any person (see also definition of "person"). With regard to the declaration and reporting requirements of the CWCR, "you" refers to persons that have an obligation to report certain activities under the provisions of the CWCR.
