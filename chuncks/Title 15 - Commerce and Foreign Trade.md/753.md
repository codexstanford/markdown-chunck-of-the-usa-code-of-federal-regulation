
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce
### PART 19 - COMMERCE DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 19.1 What definitions apply to the regulations in this part?
###### Debt,et seq.

means any amount of money, funds or property that has been determined by an appropriate official of the Federal Government to be owed to the United States by a person. As used in this part, the term "debt" can include a Commerce debt but does not include debts arising under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (26 U.S.C. 1).
