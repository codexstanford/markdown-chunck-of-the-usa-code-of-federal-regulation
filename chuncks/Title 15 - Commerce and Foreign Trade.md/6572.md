
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### Subpart C - Hearing and Appeal Procedures
####### Discovery
######## ยง 904.241 Depositions.
######### Alternative deposition methods.

(c)By order of the Judge, the parties may use other methods of deposing parties or witnesses, such as telephonic depositions, depositions through videoconference, or depositions upon written questions. Objections to the form of written questions are waived unless made within 5 days of service of the questions.
