
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter D - Additional Protocol Regulations
##### PART 781 - GENERAL INFORMATION AND OVERVIEW OF THE ADDITIONAL PROTOCOL REGULATIONS (APR)
###### ยง 781.1 Definitions of terms used in the Additional Protocol Regulations (APR).
####### Location specified by the IAEA.

A location that is selected by the IAEA to:

(1) Verify the absence of undeclared nuclear material or nuclear activities; or

(2) Obtain information that the IAEA needs to amplify or clarify information contained in the U.S. declaration.
