
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

#### Subchapter D - Additional Protocol Regulations
##### PART 784 - COMPLEMENTARY ACCESS
###### ยง 784.3 Scope and conduct of complementary access.
####### General.

(a)IAEA complementary access shall be limited to accomplishing only those purposes that are appropriate to the type of location, as indicated in ยง 784.1(b) of the APR and shall be conducted in the least intrusive manner, consistent with the effective and timely accomplishment of such purposes.
