
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

####### TAC certification.

A statement that a TAC submits to BIS, supported by reasonable evidence, documented as in a FAS, that foreign availability to a controlled country exists for an item that falls within the TAC's area of technical expertise.
