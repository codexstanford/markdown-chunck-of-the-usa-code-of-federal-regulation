
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

####### Introduction.

(a)This section authorizes exports, reexports, and transfers (in-country), including releases within a single country of software source code and technology to foreign nationals, in lieu of a license that would otherwise be required pursuant to part 742 of the EAR.
