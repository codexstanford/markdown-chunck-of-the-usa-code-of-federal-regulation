
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 758 - EXPORT CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS AND AUTHORITIES
###### ยง 758.7 Authorities of the Bureau of Industry and Security, Office of Export Enforcement (OEE).
####### Obtaining samples.

(A)The sample will be obtained by the official making the inspection in accordance with the provisions for sampling imported merchandise. The size of the sample will be the minimum representative amount necessary for identification or analysis. This will depend on such factors as the physical condition of the material (whether solid, liquid, or gas) and the size and shape of the container.
