
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter B - Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations
##### PART 712 - ACTIVITIES INVOLVING SCHEDULE 1 CHEMICALS
###### ยง 712.6 Advance notification and annual report of all exports and imports of Schedule 1 chemicals to, or from, other States Parties.
####### Annual report on exports and imports.

(1)Declared and undeclared facilities, trading companies, and any other person subject to the CWCR that exported or imported any quantity of a Schedule 1 chemical to or from another State Party in a previous calendar year must submit an annual report on exports and imports.
