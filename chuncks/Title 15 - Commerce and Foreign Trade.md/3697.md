
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 740 - LICENSE EXCEPTIONS
###### ยง 740.17 Encryption commodities, software, and technology (ENC).
####### Non-"mass market" "components," toolsets, and toolkits.

(i)Specified components classified under ECCN 5A002.a and equivalent or related software classified under ECCN 5D002 that do not meet the criteria set forth in Note 3 to Category 5-Part 2 of the CCL (the "mass market" note) and are not described by paragraph (b)(2) or (b)(3)(ii) of this section, as follows:
