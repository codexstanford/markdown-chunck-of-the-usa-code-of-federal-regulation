###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Intent to Deny (ITD) letter.

A letter informing the applicant:

(a) Of the reason for BIS's decision to deny a license application; and

(b) That the application will be denied 45 days from the date of the ITD letter, unless the applicant provides, and BIS accepts, a reason why the application should not be denied for the stated reason. See ยง 750.6 of the EAR.
