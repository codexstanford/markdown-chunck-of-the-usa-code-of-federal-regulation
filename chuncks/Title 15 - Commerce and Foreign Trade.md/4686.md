
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 758 - EXPORT CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS AND AUTHORITIES
###### ยง 758.10 Entry clearance requirements for temporary imports.
####### not

(1) An authorization under the EAR isrequired for the temporary import of "items" that are "subject to the EAR," including for "items" "subject to the EAR" that are on the USMIL. Temporary imports of firearms described in this section must meet the entry clearance requirements specified in paragraph (b) of this section.

(2) Permanent imports are regulated by the Attorney General under the direction of the Department of Justice's Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (see 27 CFR parts 447, 478, 479, and 555).
