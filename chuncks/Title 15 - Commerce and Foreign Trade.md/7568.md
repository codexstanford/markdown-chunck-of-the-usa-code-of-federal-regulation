###### ยง 946.2 Definitions.
####### Unplanned decrease in staff

means a temporary reduction in the number of employees available for duty at a field office resulting from employee retirement, resignation, extended sick leave or emergency leave, or voluntary acceptance of training or of a position outside that field office.
