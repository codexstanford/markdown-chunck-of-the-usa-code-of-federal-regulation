####### Eligibility,Items for personal use by personnel and agencies of the U.S. Government.

(2)-(i)This provision is available for items in quantities sufficient only for the personal use of members of the U.S. Armed Forces or civilian personnel of the U.S. Government (including U.S. representatives to public international organizations), and their immediate families and household employees.
