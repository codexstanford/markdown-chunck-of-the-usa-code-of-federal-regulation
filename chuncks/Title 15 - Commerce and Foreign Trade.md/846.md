
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce
### PART 19 - COMMERCE DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart C - Procedures for Offset of Commerce Department Payments To Collect Debts Owed to Other Federal Agencies
##### ยง 19.20 How do other Federal agencies use the offset process to collect debts from payments issued by a Commerce entity?
###### Administrative offset (including salary offset); certification.

(b)A Commerce entity will initiate a requested offset only upon receipt of written certification from the creditor agency that the debtor owes the past-due, legally enforceable debt in the amount stated, and that the creditor agency has fully complied with all applicable due process and other requirements contained in 31 U.S.C. 3716, 5 U.S.C. 5514, and the creditor agency's regulations, as applicable. Offsets will continue until the debt is paid in full or otherwise resolved to the satisfaction of the creditor agency.
