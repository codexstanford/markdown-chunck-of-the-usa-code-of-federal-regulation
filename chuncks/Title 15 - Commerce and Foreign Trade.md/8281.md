(b)The Strategic Operational Plan shall contain a Background and Context section that describes:

(1) The role of the RCOS in furthering the development of the regional component of the System;

(2) The process by which the RCOS updates the Strategic Operational Plan at least once every five years and how the RICE seeks inputs from the broader user community; and

(3) The RCOS's primary partners and any contributing observing systems. For the purposes of ยง 997.23, NOAA defines a primary partner as any organization or individual that contributes significant staff time, funding or other resources to project activities. This is not an exhaustive list of all RCOS partners but the primary partners the RICE is working with on a given project.
