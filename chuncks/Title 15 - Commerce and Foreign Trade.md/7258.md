###### Subpart S - Mallows Bay - Potomac River National Marine Sanctuary
####### ยง 922.201 Definitions.
######## Sanctuary resource

(1)means any historical resource with the Sanctuary boundaries, as defined in ยง 922.3. This includes, but is not limited to, any sunken watercraft and any associated rigging, gear, fittings, trappings, and equipment; the personal property of the officers, crew, and passengers, and any cargo; and any submerged or partially submerged prehistoric, historic, cultural remains, such as docks, piers, fishing-related remains (e.g., weirs, fish-traps) or other cultural heritage materials. Sanctuary resource also means any archaeological, historical, and cultural remains associated with or representative of historic or prehistoric American Indians and historic groups or peoples and their activities.
