#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 744 - CONTROL POLICY: END-USER AND END-USE BASED
###### Supplement No. 4 to Part 744 - Entity List

This Supplement lists certain entities subject to license requirements for specified items under this parts 744 and 746 of the EAR. License requirements for these entities include exports, reexports, and transfers (in-country) unless otherwise stated.
