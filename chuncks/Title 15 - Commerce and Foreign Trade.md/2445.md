
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

##### PART 700 - DEFENSE PRIORITIES AND ALLOCATIONS SYSTEM

####### ยง 700.8 Definitions.
######## Approved program.

A program determined as necessary or appropriate for priorities and allocations support to promote the national defense by the Secretary of Defense, the Secretary of Energy, or the Secretary of Homeland Security, under the authority of the Defense Production Act and Executive Order 13603, or the Selective Service Act and Executive Order 12742.
