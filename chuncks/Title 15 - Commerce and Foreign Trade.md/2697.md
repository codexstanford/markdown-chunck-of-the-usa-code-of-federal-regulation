###### ยง 710.1 Definitions of terms used in the Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations (CWCR).
####### By-product.

Means any chemical substance or mixture produced without a separate commercial intent during the manufacture, processing, use or disposal of another chemical substance or mixture.
