
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce

###### Department-wide.

(1)(i) Title VI Civil Rights hearings conducted by the Department under 42 U.S.C. 2000d-1 and 15 CFR 8.12(d).
