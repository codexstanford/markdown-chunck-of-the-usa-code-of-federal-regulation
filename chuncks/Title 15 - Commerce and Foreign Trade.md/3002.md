
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter B - Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations
##### PART 718 - CONFIDENTIAL BUSINESS INFORMATION
###### ยง 718.3 Disclosure of confidential business information.
####### U.S. Government designation of information to the Technical Secretariat.

(iii)It is the policy of the U.S. Government to designate all facility information it provides to the Technical Secretariat in declarations, reports and Schedule 1 advance notifications as "protected." It is the policy of the U.S. Government to designate confidential business information that it discloses to Inspection Teams during inspections as "protected" or "highly protected," depending on the sensitivity of the information. The Technical Secretariat is responsible for storing and limiting access to any confidential business information contained in a document according to its established procedures.
