######## ยง 971.205 Vessel safety and documentation.
######### U.S. flag vessel.

(a)All mining ships and at least one of the transport ships used by each permittee must be documented under the laws of the United States. To the extent that the applicant knows which United States flag vessels it will use, it must include with its application copies of the vessels' current valid Coast Guard Certificates of Inspection.
