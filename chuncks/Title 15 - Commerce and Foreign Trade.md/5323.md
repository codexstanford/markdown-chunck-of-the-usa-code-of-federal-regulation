###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Missile Technology Export Control Group (MTEC).

Chaired by the Department of State, the MTEC primarily reviews applications involving items controlled for Missile Technology (MT) reasons. The MTEC also reviews applications involving items not controlled for MT reasons, but destined for a country and/or end-use/end-user of concern.
