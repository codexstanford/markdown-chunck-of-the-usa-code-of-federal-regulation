
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Repeatability.

(Cat 7)-The closeness of agreement among repeated measurements of the same variable under the same operating conditions when changes in conditions or non-operating periods occur between measurements. (Reference: IEEE STD 528-2001 (one sigma standard deviation))
