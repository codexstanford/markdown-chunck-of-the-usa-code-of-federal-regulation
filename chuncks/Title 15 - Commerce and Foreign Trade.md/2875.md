
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter B - Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations
##### PART 714 - ACTIVITIES INVOLVING SCHEDULE 3 CHEMICALS
###### ยง 714.2 Annual reporting requirements for exports and imports in excess of 30 metric tons of Schedule 3 chemicals.
####### Mixtures containing a Schedule 3 chemical.

(2)The quantity of a Schedule 3 chemical contained in a mixture must be counted for reporting an export or import only if the concentration of the Schedule 3 chemical in the mixture is 80% or more by volume or by weight, whichever yields the lesser percent. For reporting purposes, only count the weight of the Schedule 3 chemical in the mixture, not the entire weight of the mixture.
