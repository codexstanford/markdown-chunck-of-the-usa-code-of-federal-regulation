
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce
### PART 19 - COMMERCE DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart B - Procedures To Collect Commerce Debts
##### § 19.12 How will Commerce entities offset a Federal employee's salary to collect a Commerce debt?
###### Federal salary offset.,see

(a)(1) Salary offset is used to collect debts owed to the United States by Commerce Department and other Federal employees. If a Federal employee owes a Commerce debt, Commerce entities may offset the employee's Federal salary to collect the Commerce debt in the manner described in this section. For information on how a Federal agency other than a Commerce entity may collect debt from the salary of a Commerce Department employee,§§ 19.20 and 19.21, subpart C, of this part.
