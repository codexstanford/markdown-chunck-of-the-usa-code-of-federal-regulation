### Chapter IX - National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Department of Commerce

##### PART 960 - LICENSING OF PRIVATE REMOTE SENSING SPACE SYSTEMS
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 960.4 Definitions.
######## Ground sample distance,GSD

orrefers to the common measurement for describing the spatial resolution of unenhanced data created from most remote sensing instruments, typically measured in meters. A resolution "finer than" X meters GSD means the resolution is a number lower than X. For example, 5 meters GSD is finer than 10 meters GSD.
