
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Commerce
### PART 27 - PROTECTION OF HUMAN SUBJECTS
#### ยง 27.102 Definitions for purposes of this policy.
##### Private information

(4)includes information about behavior that occurs in a context in which an individual can reasonably expect that no observation or recording is taking place, and information that has been provided for specific purposes by an individual and that the individual can reasonably expect will not be made public (e.g., a medical record).
