###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Fly-by-light system.

(Cat 7) A primary digital flight control system employing feedback to control the "aircraft" during flight, where the commands to the effectors/actuators are optical signals.
