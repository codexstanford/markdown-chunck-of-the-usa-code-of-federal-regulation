
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Routed export transaction.

A transaction where the foreign principal party in interest authorizes a U.S. forwarding or other agent to facilitate export of items from the United States.
