
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 772 - DEFINITIONS OF TERMS
###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Bias.

(accelerometer) (Cat 7)-The average over a specified time of accelerometer output measured at specified operating conditions, that has no correlation with input acceleration or rotation. "Bias" is expressed in g or in meters per second(g or m/s) (IEEE Std 528-2001) (Micro g equals 1x10g).

"Bias". (gyro) (Cat 7) The average over a specified time of gyro output measured at specified operating conditions that has no correlation with input rotation or acceleration. "Bias" is typically expressed in degrees per hour (deg/hr). (IEEE Std 528-2001).
