
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Optical integrated circuit.

(Cat 3)-A "monolithic integrated circuit" or a "hybrid integrated circuit", containing one or more parts designed to function as photosensor or photoemitter or to perform (an) optical or (an) electro-optical function(s).
