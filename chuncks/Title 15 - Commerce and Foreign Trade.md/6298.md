
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter D - Additional Protocol Regulations
##### PART 783 - CIVIL NUCLEAR FUEL CYCLE-RELATED ACTIVITIES NOT INVOLVING NUCLEAR MATERIALS
###### Supplement No. 3 to Part 783 - List of Specified Equipment and Non-Nuclear Material for the Reporting of Imports
####### Explanatory Note

Specially designed or prepared process systems including: Feed autoclaves (or stations), used for passing UFto the centrifuge cascades at up to 100 kPa (15 psi) and at a rate of 1 kg/h or more; desublimers (or cold traps) used to remove UFfrom the cascades at up to 3 kPa (0.5 psi) pressure. The desublimers are capable of being chilled to 203 K (-70 &#xB0;C) and heated to 343 K (70 &#xB0;C); "Product" and "Tails" stations used for trapping UF6 into containers. This plant, equipment and pipework is wholly made of or lined with UF-resistant materials (seeto section 5.2 of this Supplement) and is fabricated to very high vacuum and cleanliness standards.
