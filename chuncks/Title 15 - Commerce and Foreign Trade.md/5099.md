
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 768.4 Initiation of an assessment.
####### Assessment request.

(a)To initiate an assessment, each claimant or TAC must submit a FAS or a TAC Certification to BIS. TACs are authorized to certify foreign availability only to controlled countries. Claimants can allege foreign availability for either controlled or non-controlled countries.
