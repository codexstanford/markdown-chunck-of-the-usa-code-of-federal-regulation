
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Country Groups.

For export control purposes, foreign countries are separated into five country groups designated by the symbols A, B, C, D, and E. (See supplement no. 1 to part 740 of the EAR for a list of countries in each Country Group.)
