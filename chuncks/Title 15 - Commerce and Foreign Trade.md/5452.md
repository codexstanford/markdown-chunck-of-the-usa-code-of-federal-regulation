
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Three dimensional integrated circuit".

"(Cat 3) A collection of semiconductor dies or active device layers, integrated together, and having through semiconductor via connections passing completely through an interposer, substrate, die or layer to establish interconnections between the device layers. An interposer is an interface that enables electrical connections.
