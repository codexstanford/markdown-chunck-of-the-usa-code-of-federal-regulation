
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 7.2 Definitions.
###### Foreign adversary

means any foreign government or foreign non-government person determined by the Secretary to have engaged in a long-term pattern or serious instances of conduct significantly adverse to the national security of the United States or security and safety of United States persons.
