
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations

###### ยง 772.1 Definitions of terms as used in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
####### Specially Designated Terrorist (SDT).

Any person who is determined by the Secretary of the Treasury to be a specially designated terrorist under notices or regulations issued by the Office of Foreign Assets Control (see 31 CFR chapter V).
