###### ยง 710.1 Definitions of terms used in the Chemical Weapons Convention Regulations (CWCR).
####### Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC or Convention).

Means the Convention on the Prohibition of the Development, Production, Stockpiling and Use of Chemical Weapons and on Their Destruction, and its annexes opened for signature on January 13, 1993.
