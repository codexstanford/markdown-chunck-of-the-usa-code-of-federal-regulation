(2)For all exports of firearms controlled under ECCNs 0A501.a or .b, or shotguns with a barrel length less than 18 inches controlled under ECCN 0A502, the exporter must provide to CBP the serial number, make, model, and caliber for each firearm being exported by entering this information under the "Description of Articles" field of the CBP Form 4457, Certificate of Registration for Personal Effects Taken Abroad.
