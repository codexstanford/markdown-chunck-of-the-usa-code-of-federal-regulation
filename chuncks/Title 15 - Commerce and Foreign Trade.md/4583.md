
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Commerce and Foreign Trade
### Chapter VII - Bureau of Industry and Security, Department of Commerce
#### Subchapter C - Export Administration Regulations
##### PART 754 - SHORT SUPPLY CONTROLS
###### ยง 754.1 Introduction.
####### Scope.

(a)In this part, references to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) are references to 15 CFR chapter VII, subchapter C. This part implements the provisions of section 7, "Short Supply Controls," of the Export Administration Act (EAA) and similar provisions in other laws that are not based on national security and foreign policy grounds.
