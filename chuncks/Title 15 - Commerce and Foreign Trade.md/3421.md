
# Title 15 - Commerce and Foreign Trade

##### PART 738 - COMMERCE CONTROL LIST OVERVIEW AND THE COUNTRY CHART
###### ยง 738.3 Commerce Country Chart structure.
####### Cells.

(d)The symbol "X" is used to denote licensing requirements on the Country Chart. If an "X" appears in a particular cell, transactions subject to that particular Reason for Control/Destination combination require a license. There is a direct correlation between the number of "X"s applicable to your transaction and the number of licensing reviews your application will undergo.
