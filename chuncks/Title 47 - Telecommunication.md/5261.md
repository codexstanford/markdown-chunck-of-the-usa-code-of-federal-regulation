
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 27 - MISCELLANEOUS WIRELESS COMMUNICATIONS SERVICES
##### Subpart Q - 3.45 GHz Service (3450-3550 MHz)
###### ยง 27.1604 Reimbursement of relocation expenses of non-Federal radiolocation incumbents.
####### Relocation reimbursement contribution.,pro rata

(a)Each entity granted an initial license (not a renewal) in the 3.45 GHz Service (Licensee) must pay aportion to reimburse the costs incurred by authorized non-Federal, secondary radiolocation licensees for relocating from the 3.3-3.55 GHz band. These costs include the cost of a clearinghouse's administration of the reimbursement, which the radiolocation licensees will pay initially and include in their reimbursable costs.
