
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 20 - COMMERCIAL MOBILE SERVICES
##### ยง 20.20 Conditions applicable to provision of CMRS service by incumbent Local Exchange Carriers.
###### Broadband Commercial Mobile Radio Service (Broadband CMRS).

For the purposes of this section, "broadband CMRS" means Cellular Radiotelephone Service (part 22, subpart H of this chapter), Specialized Mobile Radio (part 90, subpart S of this chapter), and broadband Personal Communications Services (part 24, subpart E of this chapter).
