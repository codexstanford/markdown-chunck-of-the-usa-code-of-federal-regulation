
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart Z - Communications Assistance for Law Enforcement Act
###### ยง 1.20007 Additional assistance capability requirements for wireline, cellular, and PCS telecommunications carriers.
####### Definition,Call-identifying information.

(a)-(1)Call identifying information means dialing or signaling information that identifies the origin, direction, destination, or termination of each communication generated or received by a subscriber by means of any equipment, facility, or service of a telecommunications carrier. Call-identifying information is "reasonably available" to a carrier if it is present at an intercept access point and can be made available without the carrier being unduly burdened with network modifications.
