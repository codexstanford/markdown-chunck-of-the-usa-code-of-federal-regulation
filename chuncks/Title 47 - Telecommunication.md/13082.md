
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 95 - PERSONAL RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart K - Personal Locator Beacons and Maritime Survivor Locating Devices
###### ยง 95.2903 Definitions, PLBs and MSLDs.
####### Personal Locator Beacon (PLB).

A small portable transmitter, compliant with all of the rules in this subpart, that is intended to provide individuals in remote areas a means to alert others of an emergency situation and to aid search and rescue personnel to locate those in distress.
