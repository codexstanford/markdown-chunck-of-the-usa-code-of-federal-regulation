####### Broadcast services.,i.e.,

This term shall have the same meaning as that for "broadcasting" in section 3(6) of the Communications Act of 1934,"the dissemination of radio communications intended to be received by the public, directly or by the intermediary of relay stations." 47 U.S.C. 153(6).
