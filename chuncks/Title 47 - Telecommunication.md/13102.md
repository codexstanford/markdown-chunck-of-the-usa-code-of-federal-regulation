
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 95 - PERSONAL RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart L - DSRCS On-Board Units
###### § 95.3103 Definitions, OBUs.
####### On-Board Unit (OBU).,see,See

OBUs are low-power devices on vehicles that transfer data to roadside units in the Dedicated Short-Range Communications Service (§§ 90.371-90.383 of this chapter), to improve traffic flow and safety, and for other intelligent transportation system purposes.§ 90.7 of this chapter.
