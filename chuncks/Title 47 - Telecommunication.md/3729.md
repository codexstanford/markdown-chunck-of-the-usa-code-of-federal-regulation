
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 20 - COMMERCIAL MOBILE SERVICES
##### ยง 20.23 Contraband wireless devices in correctional facilities.
###### DCFO list.

(1)The Commission will maintain a publicly available list of DCFOs that are authorized to transmit qualifying disabling requests. Authorized DCFOs that seek to be recognized on the Commission's DCFO list must send a letter to the Commission's Contraband Ombudsperson, signed by the relevant state attorney general or the relevant Bureau of Prisons Regional Director and providing:

(i) The individual's name;

(ii) The individual's official government position; and

(iii) A list of correctional facilities over which the individual has oversight and management authority.
