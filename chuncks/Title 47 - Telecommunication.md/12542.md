
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 90 - PRIVATE LAND MOBILE RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart V - Competitive Bidding Procedures for 800 MHz Specialized Mobile Radio Service
###### § 90.911 Partitioned licenses and disaggregated spectrum.
####### License term.

(c)The license term for a partitioned license area and for disaggregated spectrum shall be the remainder of the original licensee's license term as provided for in §§ 90.629(a), 90.665(a) or 90.685(a).
