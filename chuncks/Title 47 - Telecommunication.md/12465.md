####### ยง 90.699 Transition of the upper 200 channels in the 800 MHz band to EA licensing.
######## System.,i.e.,

(1)System is defined functionally from the end user's point of view (a system is comprised of base station facilities that operate on an integrated basis to provide service to a common end user, and all mobile units associated with those base stations). A system may include multiple-licensed facilities that share a common switch or are otherwise operated as a unitary system, provided that the end user has the ability to access all such facilities. A system may cover more than one EA if its existing geographic coverage extends beyond the EA borders.
