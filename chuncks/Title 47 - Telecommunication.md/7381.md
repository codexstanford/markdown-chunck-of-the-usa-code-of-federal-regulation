###### ยง 63.24 Assignments and transfers of control.
####### Assignments.

(b)For purposes of this section, an assignment of an authorization is a transaction in which the authorization is assigned from one entity to another entity. Following an assignment, the authorization is held by an entity other than the one to which it was originally granted.
