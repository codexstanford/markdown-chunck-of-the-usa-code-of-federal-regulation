
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services

###### ยง 63.09 Definitions applicable to international Section 214 authorizations.
####### Facilities-based carrier

(a)means a carrier that holds an ownership, indefeasible-right-of-user, or leasehold interest in bare capacity in the U.S. end of an international facility, regardless of whether the underlying facility is a common carrier or non-common carrier submarine cable or a satellite system.
