
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart C - General Information

######## Part 0, Commission organization.

(1)Part 0 describes the structure and functions of the Commission, lists delegations of authority to the staff, and sets forth information designed to assist those desiring to obtain information from, or to do business with, the Commission. This part is designed, among other things, to meet certain of the requirements of the Administrative Procedure Act, as amended.
