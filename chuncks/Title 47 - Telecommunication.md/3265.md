
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart E - Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure Devices
###### ยง 15.403 Definitions.
####### Digital modulation.

The process by which the characteristics of a carrier wave are varied among a set of predetermined discrete values in accordance with a digital modulating function as specified in document ANSI C63.17-1998.
