
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

### Subchapter C - Broadcast Radio Services

##### Subpart W - Encoding Rules
###### ยง 76.1902 Definitions.
####### Encode

(j)shall mean, in the transmission of commercial audiovisual content, to pass, attach, embed, or otherwise apply to, associate with, or allow to persist in or remain associated with such content, data or information which when read or responded to in a covered device has the effect of preventing, pausing, or limiting copying, or constraining the resolution of a program when output from the covered device.
