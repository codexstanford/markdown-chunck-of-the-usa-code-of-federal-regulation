
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission

#### PART 1 - PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart F - Wireless Radio Services Applications and Proceedings
###### Application Requirements and Procedures
####### ยง 1.948 Assignment of authorization or transfer of control, notification of consummation.
######## Partitioning and disaggregation.

(f)Where a licensee proposes to partition or disaggregate a portion of its authorization to another party, the application will be treated as a request for partial assignment of authorization. The assignor must notify the Commission on FCC Form 603 of the geographic area or spectrum that will be deleted from its authorization upon consummation of the assignment.
