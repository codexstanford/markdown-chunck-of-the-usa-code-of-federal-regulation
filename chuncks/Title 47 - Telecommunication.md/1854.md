####### Reimbursement Program recipient (or recipient).

(h)The term "Reimbursement Program recipient" or "recipient" means an eligible advanced communications service provider that has requested via application and been approved for funding in the Reimbursement Program, regardless of whether the provider has received reimbursement funds.
