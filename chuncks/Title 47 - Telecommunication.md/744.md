
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart F - Wireless Radio Services Applications and Proceedings

####### ยง 1.949 Application for renewal of authorization.
######## Filing requirements.,i.e.,

(a)Applications for renewal of authorizations in the Wireless Radio Services must be filed no later than the expiration date of the authorization, and no sooner than 90 days prior to the expiration date. Renewal applications must be filed on the same form as applications for initial authorization in the same service,FCC Form 601 or 605.
