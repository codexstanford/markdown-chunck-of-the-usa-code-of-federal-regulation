####### Inclination of an Orbit (of an earth satellite).

The angle determined by the plane containing the orbit and the plane of the Earth's equator measured in degrees between 0&#xB0; and 180&#xB0; and in counter-clockwise direction from the Earth's equatorial plane at the ascending node of the orbit. (RR)
