
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission

##### Subpart O - Competitive Access to Cable Programming
###### ยง 76.1000 Definitions.
####### Subdistribution agreement.

(k)The term "subdistribution agreement" means an arrangement by which a local cable operator is given the right by a satellite cable programming vendor or satellite broadcast programming vendor to distribute the vendor's programming to competing multichannel video programming distributors.
