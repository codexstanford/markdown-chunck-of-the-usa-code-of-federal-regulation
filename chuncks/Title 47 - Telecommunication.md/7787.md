
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 64 - MISCELLANEOUS RULES RELATING TO COMMON CARRIERS
##### Subpart K - Changes in Preferred Telecommunications Service Providers
###### ยง 64.1150 Procedures for resolution of unauthorized changes in preferred carrier.
####### Notification of alleged unauthorized carrier change.

(a)Executing carriers who are informed of an unauthorized carrier change by a subscriber must immediately notify both the authorized and allegedly unauthorized carrier of the incident. This notification must include the identity of both carriers.
