
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 52 - NUMBERING
##### Subpart A - Scope and Authority
###### ยง 52.5 Definitions.
####### Telecommunications carrier or carrier.

(i)A "telecommunications carrier" or "carrier" is any provider of telecommunications services, except that such term does not include aggregators of telecommunications services (as defined in 47 U.S.C. 226(a)(2)). For the purposes of this part, the term "telecommunications carrier" or "carrier" includes an interconnected VoIP service provider.
