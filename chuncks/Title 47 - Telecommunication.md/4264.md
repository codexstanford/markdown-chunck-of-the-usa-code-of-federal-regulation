
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services

##### Subpart C - Technical Standards
###### ยง 24.51 Equipment authorization.

(a) Each transmitter utilized for operation under this part and each transmitter marketed, as set forth in ยง 2.803 of this chapter, must be of a type that has been authorized by the Commission under its certification procedure for use under this part.
