
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 54 - UNIVERSAL SERVICE
##### Subpart O - Uniendo a Puerto Rico Fund and Connect USVI Fund
###### ยง 54.1509 Uniendo a Puerto Rico Fund and the Connect USVI Fund - Stage 2 for mobile service.
####### Phase-down of legacy support.

(e)An eligible mobile carrier may elect or decline to participate in Stage 2 of the mobile Uniendo a Puerto Rico and/or the mobile Connect USVI Fund. Beginning on a date to be determined by the Bureau and announced by public notice, an eligible mobile carrier that declines to participate in Stage 2 will receive one-half of its prior frozen fixed support amortized for a 12-month period and zero fixed support thereafter.
