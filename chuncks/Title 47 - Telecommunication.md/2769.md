
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 9 - 911 REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart E - Telecommunications Relay Services for Persons with Disabilities
###### ยง 9.14 Emergency calling requirements.
####### E911 Service for IP CTS on or after January 6, 2021, for fixed services, and on or after January 6, 2022,,for non-fixed services,Scope.

(e)-(1)The following requirements of paragraphs (e)(1) through (4) of this section are only applicable to "covered IP CTS providers," who are providers of IP CTS to the extent that the IP CTS provider, itself or through an entity with whom the IP CTS provider contracts, places or routes voice calls to the public switched telephone network. Further, these requirements apply only to 911 calls placed by a registered user whose dispatchable location is in a geographic area served by a Wireline E911 Network and is available to the provider handling the call.
