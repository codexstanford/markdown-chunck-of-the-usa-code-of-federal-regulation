####### ยง 1.718 Unsatisfied informal complaints; formal complaints relating back to the filing dates of informal complaints.
######## Provided,

When an informal complaint has not been satisfied pursuant to ยง 1.717, the complainant may file a formal complaint with this Commission in the form specified in ยง 1.721. Such filing will be deemed to relate back to the filing date of the informal complaint:That the formal complaint: Is filed within 6 months from the date of the carrier's response, or if no response has been filed, within 6 months of the due date for the response; makes reference to the date of the informal complaint, and is based on the same cause of action as the informal complaint. If no formal complaint is filed within the 6-month period, the informal complaint proceeding will be closed.
