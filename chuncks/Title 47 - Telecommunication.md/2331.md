
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### General
###### ยง 3.2 Terms and definitions.
####### International Telecommunication Union (ITU).

(l)One of the United Nations family organizations headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland along with several other United Nations (UN) family organizations. The ITU is the UN agency responsible for all matters related to international telecommunications. The ITU has over 180 Member Countries, including the United States, and provides an international forum for dealing with all aspects of international telecommunications, including radio, telecom services and telecom facilities.
