
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services

##### Subpart P - Calling Party Telephone Number; Privacy
###### ยง 64.1600 Definitions.
####### Short message service (SMS).

(m)The term "short message service" or SMS refers to a wireless messaging service that enables users to send and receive short text messages, typically 160 characters or fewer, to or from mobile phones and can support a host of applications.
