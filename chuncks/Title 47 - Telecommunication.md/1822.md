
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart AA - Competitive Bidding for Universal Service Support
###### ยง 1.21004 Winning bidder's obligation to apply for support
####### Timely and Sufficient Application.

(a)A winning bidder has a binding obligation to apply for support by the applicable deadline. A winning bidder that fails to file an application by the applicable deadline or that for any reason is not subsequently authorized to receive support has defaulted on its bid.
