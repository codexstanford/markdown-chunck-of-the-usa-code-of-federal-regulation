
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 53 - SPECIAL PROVISIONS CONCERNING BELL OPERATING COMPANIES
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 53.3 Terms and definitions.
####### Affiliate.

An affiliate is a person that (directly or indirectly) owns or controls, is owned or controlled by, or is under common ownership or control with, another person. For purposes of this part, the term "own" means to own an equity interest (or the equivalent thereof) of more than 10 percent.
