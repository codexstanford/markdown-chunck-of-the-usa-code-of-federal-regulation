
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 52 - NUMBERING
##### Subpart A - Scope and Authority
###### ยง 52.5 Definitions.
####### Telecommunications service.

(j)The term "telecommunications service" refers to the offering of telecommunications for a fee directly to the public, or to such classes of users as to be effectively available directly to the public, regardless of the facilities used. For purposes of this part, the term "telecommunications service" includes interconnected VoIP service as that term is defined in 47 U.S.C. 153(25).
