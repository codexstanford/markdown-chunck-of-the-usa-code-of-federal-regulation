### Subchapter C - Broadcast Radio Services
#### PART 78 - CABLE TELEVISION RELAY SERVICE
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 78.5 Definitions.
####### Authorized bandwidth.

(h)The maximum bandwidth authorized to be used by a station as specified in the station license. (See §§ 2.202 and 78.104.)
