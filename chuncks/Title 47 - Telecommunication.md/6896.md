
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 54 - UNIVERSAL SERVICE
##### Subpart M - High Cost Loop Support for Rate-of-Return Carriers
###### ยง 54.1304 Calculation of safety net additive.
####### Calculation of safety net additive support for companies that qualified based on 2011 or prior year costs.

(b)Safety net additive support is equal to the amount of capped support calculated pursuant to this subpart M in the qualifying year minus the amount of support in the year prior to qualifying for support subtracted from the difference between the uncapped expense adjustment for the study area in the qualifying year minus the uncapped expense adjustment in the year prior to qualifying for support as shown in the following equation: Safety net additive support = (Uncapped support in the qualifying year-Uncapped support in the base year)-(Capped support in the qualifying year-Amount of support received in the base year).
