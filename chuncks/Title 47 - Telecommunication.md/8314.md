
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 68.3 Definitions.
####### Demarcation point (also point of interconnection).

As used in this part, the point of demarcation and/or interconnection between the communications facilities of a provider of wireline telecommunications, and terminal equipment, protective apparatus or wiring at a subscriber's premises.
