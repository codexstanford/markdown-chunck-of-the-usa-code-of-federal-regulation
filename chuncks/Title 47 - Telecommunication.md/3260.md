
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart E - Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure Devices
###### ยง 15.403 Definitions.
####### Average Symbol Envelope Power.

The average symbol envelope power is the average, taken over all symbols in the signaling alphabet, of the envelope power for each symbol.
