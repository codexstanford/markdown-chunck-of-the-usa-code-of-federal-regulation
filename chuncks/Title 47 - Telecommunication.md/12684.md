
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 95 - PERSONAL RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart A - General Rules for the Personal Radio Services
###### ยง 95.335 Operation of non-certified transmitters prohibited.
####### See

Except as provided in paragraph (a) of this section, no person shall operate a transmitter in any Personal Radio Service unless it is a certified transmitter; that is, a transmitter of a type which has obtained a grant of equipment certification for that service, pursuant to part 2, subpart J of this chapter. Use of a transmitter that is not FCC-certified voids the user's authority to operate that station.sections 302(a), (b), and (e) of the Communications Act (47 U.S.C. 302(a), (b), and (e)).
