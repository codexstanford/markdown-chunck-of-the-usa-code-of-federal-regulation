
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

### Subchapter C - Broadcast Radio Services

##### Subpart E - Television Broadcast Stations
###### ยง 73.681 Definitions.
####### Antenna mechanical beam tilt.

The intentional installation of a transmitting antenna so that its axis is not vertical, in order to change the normal angle of maximum radiation in the vertical plane.
