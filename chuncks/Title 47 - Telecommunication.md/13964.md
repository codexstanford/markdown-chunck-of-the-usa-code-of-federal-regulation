
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter III - National Telecommunications and Information Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 300 - MANUAL OF REGULATIONS AND PROCEDURES FOR FEDERAL RADIO FREQUENCY MANAGEMENT
#### ยง 300.1 Incorporation by reference of the Manual of Regulations and Procedures for Federal Radio Frequency Management.
##### et seq.

(a) The Manual of Regulations and Procedures for Federal Radio Frequency Management (the NTIA Manual) is issued by the Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Communications and Information, and is specifically designed to cover the Assistant Secretary's frequency management responsibilities pursuant to delegated authority under 47 U.S.C. 901and Executive Order 12046 (March 27, 1978). Federal agencies must comply with the requirements in the NTIA Manual specified in paragraph (b) of this section.
