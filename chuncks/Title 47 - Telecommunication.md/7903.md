
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services

##### Subpart P - Calling Party Telephone Number; Privacy
###### ยง 64.1600 Definitions.
####### Threatening call.

(q)The term "threatening call" is any call that conveys an emergency involving danger of death or serious physical injury to any person requiring disclosure without delay of information relating to the emergency.
