
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 54 - UNIVERSAL SERVICE
##### Subpart Q - Emergency Connectivity Fund
###### ยง 54.1702 Emergency Connectivity Fund eligible equipment and services.
####### Eligible equipment.

(a)For the purposes of this subpart, the following shall be considered equipment eligible for Emergency Connectivity Fund support:

(1) Wi-Fi hotspots;

(2) Modems;

(3) Routers;

(4) Devices that combine a modem and a router; and

(5) Connected devices.
