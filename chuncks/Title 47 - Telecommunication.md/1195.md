
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart O - Collection of Claims Owed the United States

######## Refunds.

(n)(1) If a hearing official, at a hearing held pursuant to paragraph (f)(3) of this section, determines that a debt is not legally due and owing to the United States, the Commission shall promptly refund any amount collected by means of administrative wage garnishment.

(2) Unless required by Federal law or contract, refunds under this section shall not bear interest.
