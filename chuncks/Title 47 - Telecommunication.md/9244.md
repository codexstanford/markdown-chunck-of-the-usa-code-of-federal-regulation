
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter C - Broadcast Radio Services
#### PART 73 - RADIO BROADCAST SERVICES
##### Subpart H - Rules Applicable to All Broadcast Stations
###### ยง 73.1545 Carrier frequency departure tolerances.
####### FM stations.

(b)(1) The departure of the carrier or center frequency of an FM station with an authorized transmitter output power more than 10 watts may not exceed +/-2000 Hz from the assigned frequency.

(2) The departure of the carrier or center frequency of an FM station with an authorized transmitter output power of 10 watts or less may not exceed +/-3000 Hz from the assigned frequency.
