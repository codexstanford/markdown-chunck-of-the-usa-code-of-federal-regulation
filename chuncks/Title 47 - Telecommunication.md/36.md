(c) Procedures pertaining to the filing and disposition of interlocutory pleadings in hearing proceedings are set forth in §§ 1.291 through 1.298 of this chapter. (d) The Commission, by vote of a majority of the members then holding office, may delegate its functions either by rule or by order, and may at any time amend, modify, or rescind any such rule or order.

(1) Functions of a continuing or recurring nature are delegated by rule. The rule is published in theand is included in this subpart.

(2) Functions pertaining to a particular matter or proceeding are delegated by order. The order is published in theand associated with the record of that matter or proceeding, but neither the order nor any reference to the delegation made thereby is included in this subpart.
