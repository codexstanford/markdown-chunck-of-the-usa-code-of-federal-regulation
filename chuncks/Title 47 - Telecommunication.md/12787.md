
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 95 - PERSONAL RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart D - CB Radio Service
###### ยง 95.927 CBRS quiet hours.
####### i.e.,

If a CBRS station causes harmful interference to broadcast or communications services received by the public, and such harmful interference can not be eliminated by technical means (filters), the FCC may, by written notice to the CBRS station operator, impose limits on the hours of operation of the CBRS station.
