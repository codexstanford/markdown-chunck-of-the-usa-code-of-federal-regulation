
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

#### PART 15 - RADIO FREQUENCY DEVICES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 15.3 Definitions.
####### Unintentional radiator.

(z)A device that intentionally generates radio frequency energy for use within the device, or that sends radio frequency signals by conduction to associated equipment via connecting wiring, but which is not intended to emit RF energy by radiation or induction.
