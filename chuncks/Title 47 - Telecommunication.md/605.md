
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter A - General

The rules in this subpart are issued pursuant to the Communications Act of 1934, as amended, 47 U.S.C. 151The purpose of the rules in this subpart is to establish the requirements and conditions under which entities may be licensed in the Wireless Radio Services as described in this part and in parts 13, 20, 22, 24, 27, 30, 74, 80, 87, 90, 95, 96, 97, and 101 of this chapter.
