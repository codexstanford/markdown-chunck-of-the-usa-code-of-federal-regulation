
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 53 - SPECIAL PROVISIONS CONCERNING BELL OPERATING COMPANIES
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 53.3 Terms and definitions.
####### Local Exchange Carrier (LEC).,LEC

Ais any person that is engaged in the provision of telephone exchange service or exchange access. Such term does not include a person insofar as such person is engaged in the provision of commercial mobile service under section 332(c) of the Act, except to the extent that the Commission finds that such service should be included in the definition of such term.
