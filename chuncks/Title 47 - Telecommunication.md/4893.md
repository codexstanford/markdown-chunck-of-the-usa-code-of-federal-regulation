
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart B - Applications and Licenses
###### ยง 27.16 Network access requirements for Block C in the 746-757 and 776-787 MHz bands.
####### Burden of proof.

(f)Once a complainant sets forth a prima facie case that the C Block licensee has refused to attach a device or application in violation of the requirements adopted in this section, the licensee shall have the burden of proof to demonstrate that it has adopted reasonable network standards and reasonably applied those standards in the complainant's case. Where the licensee bases its network restrictions on industry-wide consensus standards, such restrictions would be presumed reasonable.
