
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

##### Subpart D - Alert Message Requirements

####### National Alert.

(a)A National Alert is an alert issued by the President of the United States or the President's authorized designee, or by the Administrator of FEMA. National Alerts may be either nationwide or regional in distribution.
