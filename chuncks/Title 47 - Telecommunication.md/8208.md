
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

###### ยง 64.6300 Definitions.
####### Authenticate caller identification information.

(a)The term "authenticate caller identification information" refers to the process by which a voice service provider attests to the accuracy of caller identification information transmitted with a call it originates.
