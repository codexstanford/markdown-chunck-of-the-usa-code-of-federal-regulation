
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 18 - INDUSTRIAL, SCIENTIFIC, AND MEDICAL EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 18.107 Definitions.
####### Industrial, scientific, and medical (ISM) equipment.

(c)Equipment or appliances designed to generate and use locally RF energy for industrial, scientific, medical, domestic or similar purposes, excluding applications in the field of telecommunication. Typical ISM applications are the production of physical, biological, or chemical effects such as heating, ionization of gases, mechanical vibrations, hair removal and acceleration of charged particles.
