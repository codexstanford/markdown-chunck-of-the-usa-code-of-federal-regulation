
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 90 - PRIVATE LAND MOBILE RADIO SERVICES
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 90.7 Definitions.
####### Line C.

An imaginary line in Alaska approximately paralleling the border with Canada, East of which Commission coordination with Canadian authorities in the assignment of frequencies is generally required. It begins at the intersection of 70&#xB0; N., 144&#xB0; W., thence by great circle arc to the intersection of 60&#xB0; N., 143&#xB0; W., thence by great circle arc so as to include all the Alaskan Panhandle.
