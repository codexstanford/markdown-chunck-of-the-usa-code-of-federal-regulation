
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services
#### PART 54 - UNIVERSAL SERVICE
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 54.5 Terms and definitions.
####### LAN.

"LAN" is a local area network, which is a set of high-speed links connecting devices, generally computers, on a single shared medium, usually on the user's premises.
