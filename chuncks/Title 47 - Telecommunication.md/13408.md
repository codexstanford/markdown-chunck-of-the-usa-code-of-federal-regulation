
# Title 47 - Telecommunication
## Chapter I - Federal Communications Commission
### Subchapter D - Safety and Special Radio Services
#### PART 101 - FIXED MICROWAVE SERVICES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 101.3 Definitions.
####### Coordination distance.

The distance on a given azimuth from a station beyond which another station neither causes nor is subject to interfering emissions greater than a permissible level.
