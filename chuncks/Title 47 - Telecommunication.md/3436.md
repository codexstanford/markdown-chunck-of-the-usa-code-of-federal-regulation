####### Mode II personal/portable and mobile device information to database.

(h)(1) A mobile device and a personal/portable device operating in Mode II shall provide the database its FCC Identifier (as required by ยง 2.926 of this chapter) and serial number as assigned by the manufacturer.

(2) A personal/portable device operating in Mode II shall provide the database the device's geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude (NAD 83)).
