
# Title 47 - Telecommunication

###### ยง 90.7 Definitions.
####### Fire licensee.

Any territory, possession, state, city, county, town, or similar governmental entity, and persons or organizations charged with specific fire protection activities that operate radio stations for transmission of communications essential to official fire activities.
