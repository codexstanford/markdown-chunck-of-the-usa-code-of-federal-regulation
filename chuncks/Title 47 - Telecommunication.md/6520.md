### Subchapter B - Common Carrier Services

###### ยง 54.501 Eligible recipients.
####### Schools.

(a)(1) Only schools meeting the statutory definition of "elementary school" or "secondary school" as defined in ยง 54.500 of this subpart, and not excluded under paragraphs (a)(2) or (3) of this section shall be eligible for discounts on telecommunications and other supported services under this subpart.
