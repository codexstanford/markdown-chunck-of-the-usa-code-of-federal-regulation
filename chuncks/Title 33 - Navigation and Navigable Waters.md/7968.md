
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 165 - REGULATED NAVIGATION AREAS AND LIMITED ACCESS AREAS
##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas
###### Seventeenth Coast Guard District
####### ยง 165.1702 Gastineau Channel, Juneau, Alaska - safety zone.

(a) The waters within the following boundaries are a safety zone: A line beginning at position 58&#xB0;17.8&#x2032; N., 134&#xB0;24.9&#x2032; W., in the direction of 140&#xB0; True to Rock Dump Lighted Buoy 2A (LLNR 23685) at position 58&#xB0;17.1&#x2032; N., 134&#xB0;23.8&#x2032; W.; thence in the direction of 003&#xB0; true to a point at position 58&#xB0;17.4&#x2032; N., 134&#xB0;23. 8&#x2032; W., on the north shore of Gastineau Channel; thence northwesterly along the north shore of Gastineau Channel to the point of origin.

(b) Special Regulations:

(1) All vessels may transit or navigate within the safety zone.

(2) No vessels, other than a large passenger vessel (including cruise ships and ferries) may anchor within the Safety zone without the express consent from the Captain of the Port, Southeast Alaska.
