
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 165 - REGULATED NAVIGATION AREAS AND LIMITED ACCESS AREAS
##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas
###### Seventh Coast Guard District
####### ยง 165.711 Safety Zone: Port Everglades, Fort Lauderdale, FL.
######## Regulated area.

(a)A moving safety zone is established in the following area:

(1) The waters around naval aircraft carriers entering Port Everglades in an area 700 yards forward, 500 yards astern and 350 yards on either side of each vessel, beginning at the Port Everglades Sea Buoy in approximate position 26&#xB0;05.5&#x2032; N, 80&#xB0;04.8&#x2032; W and continuing until the vessel is safely moored in approximate position 26&#xB0;04.9&#x2032; N, 80&#xB0;06.9&#x2032; W. All coordinates referenced use datum: NAD 83.

(2) The waters around naval aircraft carriers departing Port Everglades in an area 700 yards forward, 500 yards astern and 350 yards on either side of each vessel beginning at the Pier in approximate position 26&#xB0;04.9&#x2032; N, 80&#xB0;06.9&#x2032;W, and continuing until the stern passes the Port Everglades Sea Buoy, in approximate position 26&#xB0;05.5&#x2032; N, 80&#xB0;04.8&#x2032; W. All coordinates referenced use datum: NAD 83.
