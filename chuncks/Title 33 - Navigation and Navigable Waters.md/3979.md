
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter NN - Deepwater Ports
#### PART 148 - DEEPWATER PORTS: GENERAL
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 148.5 How are terms used in this subchapter defined?
####### Pumping platform complex

means a platform or a series of interconnected platforms, exclusive of a deepwater port, consisting of one or more single point moorings or submerged turret loading buoys that can pump oil or natural gas and that has one or more of the following features or capabilities:

(1) Can handle the mooring and loading of small vessels;

(2) Has berthing and messing facilities; and

(3) Has a landing area for helicopters.
