### Subchapter H - Maritime Security

###### ยง 101.105 Definitions.
####### Maritime Security (MARSEC) Directive

means an instruction issued by the Commandant, or his/her delegee, mandating specific security measures for vessels and facilities that may be involved in a transportation security incident.
