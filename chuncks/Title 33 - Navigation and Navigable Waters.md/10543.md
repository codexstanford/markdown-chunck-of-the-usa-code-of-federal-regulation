##### The danger zone.

(a)(1) A section extending seaward for a distance of 12,000 yards between two radial lines bearing 030&#xB0; True and 083&#xB0; True, respectively, from a point on shore at latitude 36&#xB0;46&#x2032;48&#x2033; N, longitude 75&#xB0;57&#x2032;24&#x2033; W; and an adjacent sector extending seaward for a distance of 15 nautical miles between two radial lines bearing 083&#xB0; True and 150&#xB0; True, respectively, from the same shore position. The datum for these coordinates is WGS-1984.
