####### ยง 165.1403 Security Zones; Tinian, Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.

(a)The following areas are security zones:

(1) The waters of the Pacific Ocean off Tinian between 14&#xB0;59&#x2032;04.9&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;34&#x2032;58.6&#x2033; E to 14&#xB0;59&#x2032;20.1&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;35&#x2032;41.5&#x2033; E to 14&#xB0;59&#x2032;09.8&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;36&#x2032;02.1&#x2033; E to 14&#xB0;57&#x2032;49.3&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;36&#x2032;28.7&#x2033; E to 14&#xB0;57&#x2032;29.1&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;35&#x2032;31.1&#x2033; E and back to 14&#xB0;59&#x2032;04.9&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;34&#x2032;58.6&#x2033; E. This zone will be enforced when one, or more, of the Maritime Preposition Ships is in the zone or moored at Mooring A located at 14&#xB0;58&#x2032;57.0&#x2033; N and 145&#xB0;35&#x2032;40.8&#x2033; E or Mooring B located at 14&#xB0;58&#x2032;15.9&#x2033; N, 145&#xB0;35&#x2032;54.8&#x2033; E.

(2) Additionally, a 50-yard security zone in all directions around Moorings A and B will be enforced when no vessels are moored thereto but mooring balls are anchored and on station.
