
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 162 - INLAND WATERWAYS NAVIGATION REGULATIONS

###### Mooring on Mississippi River below Baton Rouge, La., including South and Southwest Passes.

(b)(1) When tied up individually or in fleets, vessels shall be moored with sufficient lines and shore fastenings to insure their remaining in place and withstanding the action of winds, currents and the suction of passing vessels.
