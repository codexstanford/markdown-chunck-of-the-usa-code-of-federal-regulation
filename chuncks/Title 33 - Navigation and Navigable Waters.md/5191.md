### Subchapter O - Pollution

##### Subpart I - Salvage and Marine Firefighting
###### ยง 155.4025 Definitions.
####### Primary resource provider

means a resource provider listed in the vessel response plan as the principal entity contracted for providing specific salvage and/or marine firefighting services and resources, when multiple resource providers are listed for that service, for each of the COTP zones in which a vessel operates. The primary resource provider will be the point of contact for the planholder, the Federal On Scene Coordinator (FOSC) and the Unified Command, in matters related to specific resources and services, as required in ยง 155.4030(a).
