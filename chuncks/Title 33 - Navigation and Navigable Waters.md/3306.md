
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 127.005 Definitions.
####### Marine transfer area for LNG

means that part of a waterfront facility handling LNG between the vessel, or where the vessel moors, and the last manifold or valve immediately before the receiving tanks.
