### Subchapter J - Bridges

##### Subpart B - Specific Requirements
###### Oregon
####### ยง 117.889 Siuslaw River.

(a) The draw of the US101 bridge, mile 5.0 at Florence, shall open on signal if at least two hours notice is given.
