
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Pollution
#### PART 159 - MARINE SANITATION DEVICES
##### Subpart C - Design, Construction, and Testing
###### ยง 159.115 Temperature range test.

(a) The device must be held at a temperature of 60 &#xB0;C or higher for a period of 16 hours.

(b) The device must be held at a temperature of -40 &#xB0;C or less for a period of 16 hours following winterization in accordance with manufacturers' instructions.
