
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

### Subchapter O - Pollution

##### Subpart B - Vessel Equipment
###### ยง 155.230 Emergency control systems for tank barges.
####### Design.,at least

(i)The system must use an emergency towline withthe same pulling strength as required of the primary towline. The emergency towline must be readily available on either the barge or the vessel towing it. The towing vessel must have on board equipment to regain control of the barge and continue towing (using the emergency towline), without having to place personnel on board the barge.
