####### Northeast Gateway Deepwater Port (NEGDWP),Location.

(b)-(1)The safety zones for the NEGDWP consist of circular zones, each with a 500-meter radius and centered on each of the deepwater port's two submerged turret loading (STL) buoys. STL Buoy "A" is centered at the following coordinates: 42&#xB0;23&#x2032;38&#x2033; N, 070&#xB0;35&#x2032;31&#x2033; W. STL Buoy "B" is centered at the following coordinates: 42&#xB0;23&#x2032;56&#x2033; N, 070&#xB0;37&#x2032;00&#x2033; W. Each safety zone is located approximately 13 miles south-southeast of the City of Gloucester, Massachusetts, in Federal waters.
