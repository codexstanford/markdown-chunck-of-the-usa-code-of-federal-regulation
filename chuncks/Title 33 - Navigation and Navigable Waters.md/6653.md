####### ยง 165.535 Safety Zone: Atlantic Ocean, Vicinity of Cape Henlopen State Park, Delaware.

(a)The following area is a safety zone: All waters of the Atlantic Ocean within the area bounded by a line drawn north from the tip of Cape Henlopen located at latitude 38&#xB0;48.2&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;05.5&#x2032; W, to a point located at latitude 38&#xB0;49.4&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;05.5&#x2032; W; thence east to a point located at latitude 38&#xB0;49.4&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;01.4&#x2032; W; thence south to a point located at latitude 38&#xB0;43.0&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;01.4&#x2032; W; thence west to a point on the shoreline located at latitude 38&#xB0;43.0&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;04.5&#x2032; W; thence north following the shoreline, to a point located at latitude 38&#xB0;48.2&#x2032; N, longitude 75&#xB0;05.5&#x2032; W. All coordinate refer to Datum: NAD 1983.
