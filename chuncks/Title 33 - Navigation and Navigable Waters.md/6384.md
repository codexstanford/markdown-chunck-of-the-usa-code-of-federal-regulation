
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas

######## Designated representative.

(1)A "designated representative" is any Coast Guard commissioned, warrant or petty officer of the U.S. Coast Guard who has been designated by the Captain of the Port (COTP) to act on his or her behalf. A designated representative may be on an official patrol vessel or may be on shore and will communicate with vessels via VHF-FM radio or loudhailer. In addition, members of the Coast Guard Auxiliary may be present to inform vessel operators of this regulation.
