
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.784 East Pearl River, within the acoustic buffer area of the John C. Stennis Space Center, and adjacent to lands, in Hancock County, Mississippi; danger zone.
##### The area.

(a)A danger zone is established in and to the extent of waters of the United States, as defined in 33 CFR part 329, in the following reaches of the East Pearl River south of a point located at latitude 30.4030&#xB0; N., longitude -89.6815&#xB0; W., to a point below the confluence of Mikes River, located at latitude 30.3561&#xB0; N., longitude -89.6514&#xB0; W. The datum for these coordinates is NAD 1983.
