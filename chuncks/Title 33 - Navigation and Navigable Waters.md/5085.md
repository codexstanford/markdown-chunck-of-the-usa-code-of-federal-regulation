##### Subpart D - Tank Vessel Response Plans for Oil
###### ยง 155.1020 Definitions.
####### Response activity

means the containment and removal of oil from the water and shorelines, the temporary storage and disposal of recovered oil, or the taking of other actions as necessary to minimize or mitigate damage to public health or welfare or the environment.
