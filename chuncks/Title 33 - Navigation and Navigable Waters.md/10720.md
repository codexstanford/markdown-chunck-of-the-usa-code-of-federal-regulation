
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

#### ยง 334.740 North Shore Choctawhatchee Bay, Eglin Air Force Base, Fla.
##### The area.

(a)The restricted area shall encompass all navigable waters of the United States as defined at 33 CFR part 329 within the area bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at latitude 30&#xB0;28&#x2032;59.90&#x2033; N, longitude 086&#xB0;29&#x2032;08.88&#x2033; W; thence to latitude 30&#xB0;28&#x2032;59.61&#x2033; N, longitude 086&#xB0;29&#x2032;01.81&#x2033; W; thence to latitude 30&#xB0;29&#x2032;08.01&#x2033; N, longitude 086&#xB0;28&#x2032;47.78&#x2033; W; then following the mean high water line at a distance of 1,000 feet to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;26&#x2032;48.60&#x2033; N, longitude 086&#xB0;32&#x2032;31.95&#x2033; W, thence proceeding directly to a point on the shoreline at latitude 30&#xB0;26&#x2032;53.58&#x2033; N, longitude 086&#xB0;32&#x2032;41.81&#x2033; W. The area also includes all contiguous inland navigable waters that lie within the land boundaries of Eglin AFB.
