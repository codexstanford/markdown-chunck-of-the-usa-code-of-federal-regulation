
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S - Boating Safety
#### PART 175 - EQUIPMENT REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart B - Personal Flotation Devices
###### ยง 175.13 Definitions.
####### Wearable PFD

means a PFD that is intended to be worn or otherwise attached to the body. A PFD marked as Type I, Type II, Type III, or Type V with Type (I, II or III) performance is considered a wearable PFD.
