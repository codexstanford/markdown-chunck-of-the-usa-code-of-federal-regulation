### Subchapter J - Bridges

##### Subpart B - Specific Requirements
###### Virginia
####### ยง 117.1003 Chickahominy River.

The draw of the highway bridge, mile 1.5 at Barrets Ferry, shall open on signal; except that, from 11 p.m. to 7 a.m., the draw shall open on signal if at least 12 hours notice is given.
