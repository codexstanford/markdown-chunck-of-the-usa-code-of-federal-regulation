###### Garbage Pollution and Sewage
####### ยง 151.66 Operating requirements: Discharge of garbage in the Great Lakes and other navigable waters.
######## Bulk dry cargo residues

means non-hazardous and non-toxic residues, regardless of particle size, of dry cargo carried in bulk, including limestone and other clean stone, iron ore, coal, salt, and cement. It does not include residues of any substance known to be toxic or hazardous, such as nickel, copper, zinc, lead, or materials classified as hazardous in provisions of law or treaty.
