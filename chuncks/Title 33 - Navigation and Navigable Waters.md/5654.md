
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Pollution
#### PART 158 - RECEPTION FACILITIES FOR OIL, NOXIOUS LIQUID SUBSTANCES, AND GARBAGE
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 158.120 Definitions.
####### Solidifying NLS

means a Category A, B, or C NLS that has a melting point-

(1) Greater than 0 &#xB0;C but less than 15 &#xB0;C and a temperature, measured under the procedure in 46 CFR 153.908(d), that is less than 5 &#xB0;C above its melting point at the time it is unloaded; or

(2) 15 &#xB0;C or greater and has a temperature, measured under the procedure in 46 CFR 153.908(d), that is less than 10 &#xB0;C above its melting point at the time it is unloaded.
