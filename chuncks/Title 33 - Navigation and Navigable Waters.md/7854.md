(a)The following area is a regulated navigation area (RNA): All navigable waters of the Willamette River adjacent to the NW Natural Portland Gas Manufacturing (PGM) site, encompassed by a line connecting the following points beginning at 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;33.8&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;11.6&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;33.9&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;11.2&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;32.7&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;10.7&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;32.9&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;09.4&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;32.2&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;08.8&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;32.2&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;07.9&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;31.4&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;07.6&#x2033; W; thence to 45&#xB0;31&#x2032;30.9&#x2033; N, 122&#xB0;40&#x2032;10.7&#x2033; W; and along the shoreline back to the beginning point. Geographically this location starts on the west bank of the Willamette River at approximately river mile 12.2, 100 yards south of the Steel Bridge.
