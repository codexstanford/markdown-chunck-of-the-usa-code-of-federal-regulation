
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas

######## Designated representative

means any Coast Guard commissioned, warrant or petty officer who has been designated by the COTP to act on his or her behalf. The designated representative may be on an official patrol vessel or may be on shore and will communicate with vessels via VHF-FM radio or loudhailer. In addition, members of the Coast Guard Auxiliary may be present to inform vessel operators of this regulation.
