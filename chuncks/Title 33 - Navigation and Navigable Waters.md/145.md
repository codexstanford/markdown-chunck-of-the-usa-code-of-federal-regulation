###### ยง 3.65-1 Thirteenth district.

(a) The District Office is in Seattle, Wash.

(b) The Thirteenth Coast Guard District shall comprise Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and Montana; and the ocean area bounded by a line from California-Oregon state line westerly to latitude 40&#xB0; N. longitude, 150&#xB0; W., thence northeasterly to latitude 54&#xB0;40&#x2032; N., longitude 140&#xB0; W., thence due east to the Canadian coast.
