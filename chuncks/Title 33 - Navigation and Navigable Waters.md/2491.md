
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

###### ยง 110.230 Anchorages, Captain of the Port Puget Sound Zone, WA.
####### Non-anchorage area Port Angeles Harbor.

(14)Beginning at a point on the shore at latitude 48&#xB0;07&#x2032;03.83&#x2033; N, longitude 123&#xB0;24&#x2032;20.67&#x2033; W; thence to latitude 48&#xB0;07&#x2032;38.43&#x2033; N, longitude 123&#xB0;24&#x2032;04.67&#x2033; W; thence to latitude 48&#xB0;07&#x2032;36.03&#x2033; N, longitude 123&#xB0;23&#x2032;50.67&#x2033; W; thence to a point on the shoreline at latitude 48&#xB0;06&#x2032;56.73&#x2033; N, longitude 123&#xB0;24&#x2032;08.67&#x2033; W.

(i) No vessel may anchor in this non-anchorage area at any time.

(ii) Dragging, seining, fishing, or other activities which may foul underwater installations within this non-anchorage area are prohibited.

(iii) Vessels may transit this non-anchorage area, but must proceed by the most direct route and without unnecessary delay.
