
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - COAST GUARD AREAS, DISTRICTS, SECTORS, MARINE INSPECTION ZONES, AND CAPTAIN OF THE PORT ZONES
##### Subpart 3.05 - First Coast Guard District
###### ยง 3.05-1 First district.

(a) The District Office is in Boston, Massachusetts.

(b) The First Coast Guard District is comprised of: Maine; New Hampshire; Vermont; Massachusetts; Rhode Island; Connecticut; New York except that part north of latitude 42&#xB0; N. and west of longitude 74&#xB0;39&#x2032; W; that part of New Jersey north of 40&#xB0;18&#x2032; N. latitude, east of 74&#xB0;30.5&#x2032; W. longitude, and northeast of a line from 40&#xB0;18&#x2032; N. 74&#xB0;30.5&#x2032; W. north-northwesterly to the New York, New Jersey & Pennsylvania boundaries at Tristate; all U.S. Naval reservations on shore at Newfoundland; the ocean area encompassed by the Search and Rescue boundary between Canada and the United States easterly to longitude 63&#xB0; W.; thence due south to latitude 41&#xB0; N.; thence southwesterly along a line bearing 219&#xB0;T to the point of intersection at 37&#xB0; N. latitude, 67&#xB0;13&#x2032; W. longitude with a line bearing 122&#xB0;T from the New Jersey shoreline at 40&#xB0;18&#x2032; N. latitude (just south of the Shrewsbury River); thence northwesterly along this line to the coast.
