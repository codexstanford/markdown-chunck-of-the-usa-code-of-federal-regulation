
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 160 - PORTS AND WATERWAYS SAFETY - GENERAL
##### Subpart C - Notification of Arrival, Hazardous Conditions, and Certain Dangerous Cargoes
###### ยง 160.202 Definitions.
####### Charterer

means the person or organization that contracts for the majority of the carrying capacity of a ship for the transportation of cargo to a stated port for a specified period. This includes "time charterers" and "voyage charterers."
