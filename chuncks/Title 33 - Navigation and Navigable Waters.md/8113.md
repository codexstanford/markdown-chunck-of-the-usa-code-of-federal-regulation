##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 167.5 Definitions.
####### Separation zone or line

(d)means a zone or line separating the traffic lanes in which ships are proceeding in opposite or nearly opposite directions; or separating a traffic lane from the adjacent sea area; or separating traffic lanes designated for particular classes of ships proceeding in the same direction.
