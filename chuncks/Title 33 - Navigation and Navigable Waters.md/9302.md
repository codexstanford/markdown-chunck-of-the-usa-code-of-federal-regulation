
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 207 - NAVIGATION REGULATIONS
#### ยง 207.718 Navigation locks and approach channels, Columbia and Snake Rivers, Oreg. and Wash.
##### At Lower Monumental Lock and Dam.

(6)The waters restricted to all vessels, except Government vessels, are described as all waters commencing at the upstream of the navigation lock guidewall and running in a direction of 46&#xB0;25&#x2032; true for a distance of 344 yards; thence 326&#xB0;19&#x2032; true for a distance of 362 yards; thence 243&#xB0;19&#x2032; true for a distance of 218 yards; thence 275&#xB0;59&#x2032; true to the north shore a distance of about 290 yards. The downstream limits commence at the downstream end of the navigation lock guidewall; thence to the north shore, at right angles and parallel to the axis of the dam. Signs designate the restricted areas.
