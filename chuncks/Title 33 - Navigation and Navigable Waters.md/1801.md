(c)Bounded by a line commencing at the northern most point of Peach's Point at position latitude 42&#xB0;31&#x2032;08.6&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;50&#x2032;32.8&#x2033; W.; thence westerly to a point, at position latitude 42&#xB0;31&#x2032;21.9&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;51&#x2032;15.1&#x2033; W. off Fluen Point; thence westerly to a point at latitude 42&#xB0;31&#x2032;19.3&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;51&#x2032;47.4&#x2033; W. off Naugus Head; thence southwesterly to a point at latitude 42&#xB0;31&#x2032;00.3&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;51&#x2032;16.6&#x2033; W. east of Folger Point; thence to a point at latitude 42&#xB0;30&#x2032;38.3&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;52&#x2032;34.6&#x2033; W.; thence easterly to a point on Long Point at latitude 42&#xB0;30&#x2032;52.6&#x2033; N., longitude 70&#xB0;53&#x2032;05&#x2033; W. The areas will be principally for use by yachts and other recreational craft. Temporary floats or buoys for marking anchors will be allowed in the areas but fixed piles or stakes may not be placed. The anchoring of vessels, the placing of moorings, and the maintenance of fairways will be under the jurisdiction of the local Harbor Master.
