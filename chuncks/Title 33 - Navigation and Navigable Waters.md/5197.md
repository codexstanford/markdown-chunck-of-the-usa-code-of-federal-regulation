
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

###### ยง 155.4025 Definitions.
####### Subsurface product removal

means the safe removal of oil from a vessel that has sunk or is partially submerged underwater. These actions can include pumping or other means to transfer the oil to a storage device.
