
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.525 Atlantic Ocean off John F. Kennedy Space Center, FL; restricted area.
##### The area.

(a)The restricted area shall encompass all navigable waters of the United States, as defined at 33 CFR part 329, contiguous to the area offshore of the John F. Kennedy Space Center (KSC), Florida. The area is bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at the southwest portion of the area, at latitude 28&#xB0;35.008&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;34.448&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;35.716&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;32.938&#x2032; W, thence follow the mean high water line northerly at a distance of 1.5 nautical miles to a point at latitude 28&#xB0;43.566&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;39.094&#x2032; W, thence proceed westerly to terminate at a point on the shoreline at latitude 28&#xB0;43.566&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;41.189&#x2032; W.
