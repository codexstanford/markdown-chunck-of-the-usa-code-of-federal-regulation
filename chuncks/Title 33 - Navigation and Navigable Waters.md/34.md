
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 1.07 - Enforcement; Civil and Criminal Penalty Proceedings
###### ยง 1.07-10 Reporting and investigation.

(a) Any person may report an apparent violation of any law, regulation, or order that is enforced by the Coast Guard to any Coast Guard facility. When a report of an apparent violation has been received, or when an apparent violation has been detected by any Coast Guard personnel, the matter is investigated or evaluated by Coast Guard personnel. Once an apparent violation has been investigated or evaluated, a report of the investigation may be sent to the District Commander or other designated official in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section or a Notice of Violation under ยง 1.07-11 may be given to the party by an issuing officer.
