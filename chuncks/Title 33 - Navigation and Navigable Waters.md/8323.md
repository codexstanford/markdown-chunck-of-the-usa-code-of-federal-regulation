
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S - Boating Safety
#### PART 174 - STATE NUMBERING AND CASUALTY REPORTING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 174.3 Definitions.
####### Personal watercraft

means a vessel propelled by a water-jet pump or other machinery as its primary source of motive power and designed to be operated by a person sitting, standing, or kneeling on the vessel, rather than sitting or standing within the vessel's hull.
