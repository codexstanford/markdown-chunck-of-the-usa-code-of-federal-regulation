
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 321 - PERMITS FOR DAMS AND DIKES IN NAVIGABLE WATERS OF THE UNITED STATES
#### ยง 321.2 Definitions.
##### navigable waters of the United States

(a) The termmeans those waters of the United States that are subject to the ebb and flow of the tide shoreward to the mean high water mark and/or are presently used, or have been used in the past, or may be susceptible to use to transport interstate or foreign commerce. See 33 CFR part 329 for a more complete definition of this term.
