(a) Security systems and equipment must be in good working order and inspected, tested, calibrated and maintained according to the manufacturer's recommendation.

(b) The results of testing completed under paragraph (a) of this section must be recorded in accordance with ยง 104.235. Any deficiencies must be promptly corrected.
