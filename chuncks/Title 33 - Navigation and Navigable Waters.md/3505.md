##### Subpart A - Evidence of Financial Responsibility for Water Pollution (Vessels)

####### Scope.

(a)This subpart sets forth-

(1) The requirements and procedures each COFR Operator (as defined in ยง 138.30(b)) must use to establish and maintain the evidence of financial responsibility required by the OPA 90 and CERCLA (both defined in ยง 138.30), and to obtain Certificates of Financial Responsibility (COFR);
