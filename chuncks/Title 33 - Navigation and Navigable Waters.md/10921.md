
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.1150 Monterey Bay, Calif.
##### Firing range, Fort Ord, Calif.,The danger zone.

(a)-(1)(i) A rectangular area in Monterey Bay, the southerly limit of which is an extension seaward of the southerly line of the Fort Ord Military Reservation boundary and bears 307&#xB0; true, 8,000 yards from a point on the shore at latitude 36&#xB0;37&#x2032;47&#x2033;, longitude 121&#xB0;50&#x2032;28&#x2033;, and the northerly limit of which is a line bearing 307&#xB0; true, 8,000 yards, from a point on the shore at latitude 36&#xB0;41&#x2032;57&#x2033;, longitude 121&#xB0;48&#x2032;30&#x2033;, opposite Marina, Monterey County, California. The seaward boundary is a straight line joining the outer ends of the southerly and the northerly boundaries at the 8,000 yard range and is approximately parallel to the shore.

(ii) The danger zone is divided into a short range area, extending seaward from the shore a distance of 5,000 yards measured along the southerly and northerly boundaries, and a long range area embracing the entire danger zone.
