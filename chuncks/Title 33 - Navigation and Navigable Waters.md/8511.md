
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S - Boating Safety
#### PART 183 - BOATS AND ASSOCIATED EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart F - Flotation Requirements for Inboard Boats, Inboard/Outdrive Boats, and Airboats
###### ยง 183.110 Definitions.
####### Engine room bilge

means the area in the engine room or a connected compartment below a height of 12 inches measured from the lowest point where liquid can collect in these compartments when the boat is in its static floating position.
