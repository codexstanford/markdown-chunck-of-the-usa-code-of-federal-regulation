### Subchapter NN - Deepwater Ports
#### PART 148 - DEEPWATER PORTS: GENERAL
##### Subpart C - Processing Applications
###### Approval or Denial of the Application
####### ยง 148.283 When is the application process stopped before the application is approved or denied?

The Commandant (CG-5P) recommends to MARAD that the application process be suspended before the application is approved or denied if:

(a) All applications are withdrawn before MARAD approves one of them; or

(b) There is only one application; it is incomplete, and the applicant does not respond to a request by the Commandant (CG-5P) for further information, as per ยง 148.107 of this part.
