
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S - Boating Safety
#### PART 173 - VESSEL NUMBERING AND CASUALTY AND ACCIDENT REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 173.3 Definitions.
####### Sterndrive

means an engine, powering a propeller through a series of shafts and gears, mounted in front of the transom of a vessel and attached through the transom to a drive unit that is similar to the lower unit of an outboard; and may also be known as an inboard-outdrive or an inboard-outboard.
