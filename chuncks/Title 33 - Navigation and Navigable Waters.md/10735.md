#### ยง 334.760 Naval Support Activity Panama City and Alligator Bayou, a tributary of St. Andrew Bay, Fla.; naval restricted area.
##### The area.

(a)The waters within an area beginning at a point located along the shore at the southern end of the facility designated by latitude 30&#xB0;09&#x2032;45.6&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;44&#x2032;20.6&#x2033; W.; thence proceed 100 feet waterward of the mean high water line directly to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;09&#x2032;46.8&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;44&#x2032;20.6&#x2033; W. From this position the line meanders irregularly, following the shoreline at a minimum distance of 100 feet from the mean high water line to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;10&#x2032;16.7&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;45&#x2032;01.2&#x2033; W. located east of the south side of the entrance to Alligator Bayou; thence directly across the entrance to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;10&#x2032;23.4&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;45&#x2032;05.7&#x2033; W. located east of the north side of the entrance to Alligator Bayou; thence continuing the northerly meandering, following the shoreline at a minimum distance of 100 feet from the mean high water line to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;11&#x2032;11.3&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;45&#x2032;02.8&#x2033; W.; thence directly to the shoreline to a point at latitude 30&#xB0;11&#x2032;12.3&#x2033; N., longitude 85&#xB0;45&#x2032;03.2&#x2033; W. This encompasses an area reaching from the southern extent described to the northern extent described and extending from the mean high water line waterward a minimum distance of approximately 100 feet.
