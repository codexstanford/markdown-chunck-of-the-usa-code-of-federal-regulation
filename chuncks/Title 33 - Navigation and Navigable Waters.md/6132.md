
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 164 - NAVIGATION SAFETY REGULATIONS
##### ยง 164.46 Automatic Identification System.
###### International voyage

means a voyage from a country to which the present International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea applies to a port outside such country, or conversely.
