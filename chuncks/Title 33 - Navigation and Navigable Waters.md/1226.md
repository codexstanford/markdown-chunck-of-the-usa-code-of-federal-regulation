
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

###### ยง 100.724 Annual Augusta Invitational Rowing Regatta; Savannah River, Augusta, GA.
####### Definitions,Regulated area.

(a)-(1)The regulated area is formed by a line drawn directly across the Savannah River at U.S. Highway 1 (Fifth Street) Bridge at mile marker 199.45 and directly across the Savannah River at Eliot's Fish Camp at mile marker 197. The regulated area includes the width of the Savannah River between these two lines.
