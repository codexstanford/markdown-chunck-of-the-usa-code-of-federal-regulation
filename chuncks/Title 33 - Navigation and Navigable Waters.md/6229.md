
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas
###### First Coast Guard District

######## Designated representative

(1)means any Coast Guard commissioned, warrant, petty officer, or any federal, state, or local law enforcement officer who has been designated by the Captain of the Port (COTP) Northern New England, to act on his or her behalf. The designated representative may be on an official patrol vessel or may be on shore and will communicate with vessels via VHF-FM radio or loudhailer. In addition, members of the Coast Guard Auxiliary may be present to inform vessel operators of this regulation.
