
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter NN - Deepwater Ports
#### PART 148 - DEEPWATER PORTS: GENERAL
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 148.5 How are terms used in this subchapter defined?
####### Reconnaissance hydrographic survey

means a scientific study of fresh and saltwater bodies, currents and water content, cultural resources, seabed soils and subsea conditions, for example existing pipelines or subsea wells. A visual representation of the survey findings is normally depicted on a chart of the examined area.
