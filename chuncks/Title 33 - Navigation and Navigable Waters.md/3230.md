
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter L - Waterfront Facilities
#### PART 126 - HANDLING OF DANGEROUS CARGO AT WATERFRONT FACILITIES
##### ยง 126.3 Definitions.
###### Net weight,

in reference to material in a package, tank, or container, means the weight of the contents of a package, tank, or container and does not include the weight of any packaging material or containing devices.
