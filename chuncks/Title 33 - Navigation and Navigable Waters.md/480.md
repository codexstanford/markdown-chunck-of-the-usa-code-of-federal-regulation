
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 51 - COAST GUARD DISCHARGE REVIEW BOARD
##### ยง 51.4 Definitions.
###### Discharge.

Any formal separation of a member from the Coast Guard which is not termed "honorable", including dismissals and "dropping from the rolls". This term also includes the assignment of a separation program designator, separation authority, the stated reason for the discharge, and the characterization of service.
