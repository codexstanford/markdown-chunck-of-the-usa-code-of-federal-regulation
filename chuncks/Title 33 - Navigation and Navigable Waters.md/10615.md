
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense

#### ยง 334.505 St. Johns River, U.S. Coast Guard Station Mayport, Sector Jacksonville, Florida; restricted area.
##### The area.

(a)The restricted area encompasses all navigable waters of the United States as defined at 33 CFR part 329, within the area bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at latitude 30&#xB0;23.315366&#x2032; N, longitude 081&#xB0;26.056735&#x2032; W; thence directly to latitude 30&#xB0;23.325775&#x2032; N, longitude 081&#xB0;26.071548&#x2032; W; thence directly to latitude 30&#xB0;23.266063&#x2032; N, longitude 081&#xB0;26.132775&#x2032; W; thence to latitude 30&#xB0;23.215082&#x2032; N, longitude 081&#xB0;26.1287404&#x2032; W; thence proceed directly to a point on the shoreline at latitude 30&#xB0;23.204522&#x2032; N, longitude 081&#xB0;26.111753&#x2032; W thence following the mean high water line to the point of beginning. The datum for these coordinates is WGS84.
