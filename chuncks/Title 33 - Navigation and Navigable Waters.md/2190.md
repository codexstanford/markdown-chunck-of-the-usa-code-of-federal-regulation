####### The anchorage grounds,Anchorage A off the entrance to the Mispillion River.

(a)-(1)In Delaware Bay southwest of Brandywine Channel beginning at latitude 38&#xB0;53&#x2032;57&#x2033; N., longitude 75&#xB0;08&#x2032;00&#x2033; W., thence northwesterly to latitude 39&#xB0;01&#x2032;22&#x2033; N., longitude 75&#xB0;13&#x2032;25&#x2033; W., thence southwesterly to latitude 39&#xB0;00&#x2032;49&#x2033; N., longitude 75&#xB0;14&#x2032;57&#x2033; W., thence southeasterly to latitude 38&#xB0;53&#x2032;22&#x2033; N., longitude 75&#xB0;09&#x2032;26&#x2033; W., thence northeasterly to the point of beginning. Supervision over the anchoring of vessels and over all cargo transfer operations in Anchorage A is exercised by the Captain of the Port, Philadelphia. The regulations of paragraphs (b)(1) and (b)(2) of this section do not apply to this anchorage.
