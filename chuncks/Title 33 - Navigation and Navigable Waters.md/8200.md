
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety
#### PART 168 - ESCORT REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN TANKERS
##### ยง 168.40 Applicable waters and number of escort vessels.
###### Prince William Sound:

(a)Each tanker to which this part applies must be escorted by at least two escort vessels in those navigable waters of the United States within Prince William Sound, Alaska, and the adjoining tributaries, bays, harbors, and ports, including the navigable waters of the United States within a line drawn from Cape Hinchinbrook Light, to Seal Rocks Light, to a point on Montague Island at 60&#xB0;14.6&#x2032; North, 146&#xB0;59&#x2032; West, and the waters of Montague Strait east of a line between Cape Puget and Cape Cleare.
