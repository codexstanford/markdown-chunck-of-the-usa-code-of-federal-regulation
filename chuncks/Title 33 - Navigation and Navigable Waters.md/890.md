
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - International Navigation Rules
#### PART 82 - 72 COLREGS: INTERPRETATIVE RULES
##### ยง 82.5 Lights for moored vessels.
###### vessel at anchor

For the purposes of Rule 30 of the 72 COLREGS, aincludes a barge made fast to one or more mooring buoys or other similar device attached to the sea or river floor. Such a barge may be lighted as a vessel at anchor in accordance with Rule 30, or may be lighted on the corners in accordance with 33 CFR 83.30(h) through (l).
