
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter N - Outer Continental Shelf Activities
#### PART 147 - SAFETY ZONES
##### ยง 147.847 Safety Zone; BW PIONEER Floating Production, Storage, and Offloading System Safety Zone.
###### Description.

(a)The BW PIONEER, a Floating Production, Storage and Offloading (FPSO) system, is in the deepwater area of the Gulf of Mexico at Walker Ridge 249. The FPSO can swing in a 360 degree arc around the center point of the turret buoy's swing circle at 26&#xB0;41&#x2032;46.25&#x2033; N and 090&#xB0;30&#x2032;30.16&#x2033; W. The area within 500 meters (1640.4 feet) around the stern of the FPSO when it is moored to the turret buoy is a safety zone. If the FPSO detaches from the turret buoy, the area within 500 meters around the center point at 26&#xB0;41&#x2032;46.25&#x2033; N and 090&#xB0;30&#x2032;30.16&#x2033; W is a safety zone.
