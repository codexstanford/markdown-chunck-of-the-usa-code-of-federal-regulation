
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.480 Archers Creek, Ribbon Creek, and Broad River; U.S. Marine Corps Recruit Depot, Parris Island, South Carolina; danger zones.
##### The areas.

(a)(1) The danger zone on Archers Creek (between the Broad River and Beaufort River), Ribbon Creek, and the Broad River shall encompass all navigable waters of the United States, as defined at 33 CFR part 329, adjacent to the existing rifle range. This area is bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at the southernmost portion of the area, at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;59&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;42&#x2032;54&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;20&#x2032;05&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;16&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;21&#x2032;40&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;44&#x2032;54&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;22&#x2032;20&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;52&#x2033; W, thence to a point on the shoreline at latitude 32&#xB0;21&#x2032;34&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;42&#x2032;48&#x2033; W, thence follow the mean high water line southwesterly around Horse Island approximately 2.3 nautical miles to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;21&#x2032;22&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;42&#x2032;30&#x2033; W, thence to a point on the shoreline at latitude 32&#xB0;20&#x2032;56&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;41&#x2032;50&#x2033; W, thence follow the mean high water line southwesterly approximately 2.2 nautical miles to terminate at the southernmost portion of the area (the starting point).

(2) The danger zone on the Broad River shall encompass all navigable waters of the United States, as defined at 33 CFR part 329, adjacent to the existing pistol range. This area is bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at the easternmost portion of the area, at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;36&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;42&#x2032;34&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;23&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;42&#x2032;50&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;06&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;31&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;28&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;54&#x2033; W, thence to a point at latitude 32&#xB0;19&#x2032;59&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;28&#x2033; W, thence to a point on the shoreline at latitude 32&#xB0;20&#x2032;10&#x2033; N, longitude 80&#xB0;43&#x2032;10&#x2033; W, and thence follow the mean high water line southeasterly approximately 0.75 nautical miles to terminate at the easternmost portion of the area (the starting point).
