
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

### Subchapter H - Maritime Security
#### PART 101 - MARITIME SECURITY: GENERAL
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 101.105 Definitions.
####### Gross tonnage, ITC (GT ITC)

means the gross tonnage measurement of the vessel under 46 U.S.C. chapter 143, Convention Measurement. Under international conventions, this parameter may be referred to as "gross tonnage (GT)."
