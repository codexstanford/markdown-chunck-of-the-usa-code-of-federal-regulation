##### At McNary Lock and Dam.

(4)The waters restricted to all vessels, except to Government vessels, are described as all waters commencing at the upstream end of the Oregon fish ladder thence running in the direction of 39&#xB0;28&#x2032; true for a distance of 540 yards; thence 7&#xB0;49&#x2032; true for a distance of 1,078 yards; thence 277&#xB0;10&#x2032; for a distance of 468 yards to the upstream end of the navigation lock guidewall. Signs designate the restricted areas.
