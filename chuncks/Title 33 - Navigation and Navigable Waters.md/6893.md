
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter P - Ports and Waterways Safety

##### Subpart F - Specific Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas
###### Seventh Coast Guard District
####### ยง 165.760 Security Zones; Port of Palm Beach, Port Everglades, and Port of Miami, Florida.
######## Fixed security zone in Port of Miami, Florida.

(2)A fixed security zone encompasses all waters between Watson Park and Star Island from the MacArthur Causeway south to Port of Miami. The western boundary is formed by an imaginary line from points 25&#xB0;46&#x2032;45&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;10&#x2032;52&#x2033; W, northwest to 25&#xB0;46&#x2032;46&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;10&#x2032;54&#x2033; W, northeast to 25&#xB0;46&#x2032;53&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;10&#x2032;50&#x2033; W, and extending northeast ending at Watson Island at 25&#xB0;47&#x2032;00&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;10&#x2032;40&#x2033; W. The eastern boundary is formed by an imaginary line approximately 100 yards west of the Fisher Island Ferry terminal, in approximate position 25&#xB0;46&#x2032;20&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;09&#x2032;07&#x2033; W, extending southwest across the Main Channel to Port of Miami, at 25&#xB0;46&#x2032;16&#x2033; N, 080&#xB0;09&#x2032;11&#x2033; W. The fixed security zone is in effect when two or more passenger vessels, vessels carrying cargoes of particular hazard, or vessels carrying LHG, enter or moor within this zone.

(i) When the security zone is in effect, persons and vessels shall not enter or transit the security zone along the Miami Main Channel unless authorized by Captain of the Port of Miami or a designated representative.

(ii) Persons and vessels may transit the Miami Main Channel when only one passenger vessel, one vessel carrying cargoes of particular hazard or one vessel carrying LHG is berthed.

(iii) Law enforcement vessels can be contacted on VHF Marine Band Radio, Channel 16 (156.8 MHz).
