
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters

### Subchapter C - Aids to Navigation

##### Subpart C - Structures
###### ยง 64.23 Duration of marking on structures.

Markings determined to be required shall be established and maintained until:

(a) The structure is removed; or

(b) Otherwise directed by the District Commander.
