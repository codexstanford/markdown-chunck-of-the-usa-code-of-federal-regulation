
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Pollution
#### PART 157 - RULES FOR THE PROTECTION OF THE MARINE ENVIRONMENT RELATING TO TANK VESSELS CARRYING OIL IN BULK
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 157.03 Definitions.
####### Instantaneous rate of discharge of oil content

means the rate of discharge of oil in liters per hour at any instant, divided by the speed of the vessel in knots at the same instant.
