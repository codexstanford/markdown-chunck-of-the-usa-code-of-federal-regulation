
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 5 - COAST GUARD AUXILIARY
##### Subpart E - Auxiliary Markings
###### ยง 5.41 Auxiliary emblem.
####### Description.

(a)The Auxiliary emblem consists of a disk with the shield of the Coat of Arms of the United States circumscribed by an annulet edged and inscribed "U.S. COAST GUARD AUXILIARY", all in front of two crossed anchors.
