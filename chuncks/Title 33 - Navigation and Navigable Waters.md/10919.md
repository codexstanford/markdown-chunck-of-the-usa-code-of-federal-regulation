
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.1140 Pacific Ocean at San Miguel Island, Calif.; naval danger zone.
##### Markers.

(b)Range markers, as delineated below, are installed at points A and H for navigational purposes for both surface vessels and aircraft:

(1) At point A two triangular markers are installed facing southward, 10 feet in length on each side, with red and white diagonal stripes, each marker mounted atop 80-foot poles spaced 100 yards apart, each pole being placed on the line of longitude 120&#xB0;23&#x2032;17&#x2033; W. and near the southerly shoreline at latitude 34&#xB0;01&#x2032;32&#x2033; N. The southernmost marker is 20 feet below the other.

(2) At point H two triangular markers are installed facing true north 10 feet in length on each side, with red and white diagonal stripes, each marker mounted atop 80-foot poles spaced 100 yards apart, each pole being placed on the line of longitude 120&#xB0;23&#x2032;17&#x2033; W. and near the northwesterly shoreline at latitude 34&#xB0;03&#x2032;09&#x2033; N. The northernmost marker is 20 feet below the other.
