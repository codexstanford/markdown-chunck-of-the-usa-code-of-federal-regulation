
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter II - Corps of Engineers, Department of the Army, Department of Defense
### PART 334 - DANGER ZONE AND RESTRICTED AREA REGULATIONS
#### ยง 334.540 Banana River at the Eastern Range, 45th Space Wing, Cape Canaveral Air Force Station, FL; restricted area.
##### The area.

(a)The restricted area shall encompass all navigable waters of the United States, as defined at 33 CFR part 329, within the Banana River contiguous to the area offshore of Cape Canaveral Air Force Station, Florida. The area is bounded by a line connecting the following coordinates: Commencing from the shoreline at the southeast portion of the area, at approximately latitude 28&#xB0;25.17&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;36.24&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;25.18&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;36.65&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;25.25&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;36.66&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;25.22&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;38.36&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;26.23&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;38.25&#x2032; W, thence directly to latitude 28&#xB0;26.23&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;38.47&#x2032; W, thence to reach the south side of the Kennedy Space Center NASA Causeway East Roadway at approximately latitude 28&#xB0;30.74&#x2032; N, longitude 80&#xB0;36.63&#x2032; W.
