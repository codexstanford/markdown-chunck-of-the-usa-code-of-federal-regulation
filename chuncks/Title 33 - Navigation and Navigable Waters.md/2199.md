
# Title 33 - Navigation and Navigable Waters
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter I - Anchorages

##### Subpart B - Anchorage Grounds
###### ยง 110.157 Delaware Bay and River.
####### Anchorage 9 near entrance to Mantua Creek.

(10)On the southeast side of the channel along Mifflin Range, bounded as follows: Beginning at a point on the southeast edge of the channel at longitude 75&#xB0;14&#x2032;26&#x2033;; thence northeasterly along the edge of the channel to longitude 75&#xB0;12&#x2032;01.5&#x2033;; thence 203&#xB0;30&#x2032;, 933 yards; thence 233&#xB0;30&#x2032;, 3,058 yards; and thence 263&#xB0;30&#x2032;, 933 yards, to the point of beginning. Vessels must not cast anchor in this anchorage in such manner as to interfere unreasonably with the passage of other vessels to and from Mantua Creek.
