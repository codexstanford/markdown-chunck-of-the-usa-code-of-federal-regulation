
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 9 - SECURITY INFORMATION REGULATIONS
##### ยง 9.10 Mandatory declassification review
###### Processing.

(e)In responding to mandatory declassification review requests, the Department shall make a review determination as promptly as possible, but in no case more than one year from the date of receipt of the request, and notify the requester accordingly. When the requested information cannot be declassified in its entirety, the Department shall release all meaningful portions that can be declassified and that are not exempt from disclosure on other grounds.
