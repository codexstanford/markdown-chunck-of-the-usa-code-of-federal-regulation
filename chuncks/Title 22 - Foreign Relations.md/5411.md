
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter III - Peace Corps
### PART 306 - VOLUNTEER DISCRIMINATION COMPLAINT PROCEDURE
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 306.3 Definitions.
###### Applicant

means a person who has submitted a completed application required for consideration of eligibility for Peace Corps Volunteer service. "Applicant" may also mean a person who alleges that the actions of agency personnel precluded them from submitting such an application or any other information reasonably required by the appropriate personnel as necessary for a determination of the individual's eligibility for Volunteer service.
