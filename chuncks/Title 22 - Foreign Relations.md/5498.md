###### Review of Peace Corps records related to the debt.

(d)(1) An employee who intends to inspect or copy creditor agency records related to the debt in accordance with ยง 309.15(a)(5), must send a letter to the official designated in the notice of intent to offset stating his or her intention. (2) In response to a timely request submitted by the debtor, the designated official will notify the employee of the location and time when the employee may inspect and copy records related to the debt.
