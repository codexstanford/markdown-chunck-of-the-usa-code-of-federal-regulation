
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 240 - SOVEREIGN LOAN GUARANTEE - STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS
#### ยง 240.2 Definitions.
##### Fiscal Agent

means the bank or trust company or its duly appointed successor under the Fiscal Agency Agreement which has been appointed by the Borrower with the consent of USAID to perform certain fiscal agency and trust services for specified Eligible Note[s] pursuant to the terms of the Fiscal Agency Agreement.
