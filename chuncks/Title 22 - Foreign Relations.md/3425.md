
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter T - Hostage Relief
#### PART 191 - HOSTAGE RELIEF ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart B - Application of Soldiers' and Sailors' Civil Relief Act
###### ยง 191.12 Description of benefits.
####### Protection in court.

(c)Section 200 (50 U.S.C. App. 520) provides that if a hostage is made defendant in a court action and is unable to appear in court, the court shall appoint an attorney to represent the hostage and protect the hostage's interests. Further, if a judgment is rendered against the hostage, an opportunity to reopen the case and present a defense, if meritorious, may be permitted within 90 days after release.
