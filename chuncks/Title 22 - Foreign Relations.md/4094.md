
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations

### PART 212 - PUBLIC INFORMATION
#### Subpart K - FOIA Definitions
##### ยง 212.16 Glossary.
###### Exemptions

are nine categories of information that are not required to be released in response to a FOIA request because release would be harmful to a government or private interest. These categories are called "exemptions" from disclosures.
