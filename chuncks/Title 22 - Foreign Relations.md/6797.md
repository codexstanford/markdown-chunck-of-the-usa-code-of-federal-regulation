
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter X - Inter-American Foundation
### PART 1007 - SALARY OFFSET
#### ยง 1007.2 Definitions.
##### Hearing official

means an individual responsible for conducting any hearing with respect to the existence or amount of a debt claimed and who renders a decision on the basis of such hearing. A hearing official may not be under the supervision or control of the President of the Inter-American Foundation.
