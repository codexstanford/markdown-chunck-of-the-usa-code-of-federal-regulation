
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 20 - BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN FORMER SPOUSES
##### ยง 20.5 Survivor benefits.
###### Reduction because of receipt of other survivor benefits.

(c)If a former spouse is in receipt of a survivor annuity based on an election by the principal under section 806(f) or 2109 of the Act, the survivor benefits for the former spouse under this section shall be reduced on the effective date by the amount of such elected survivor annuity.
