### PART 503 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT REGULATION
#### ยง 503.8 Exemptions.
##### Exemption five-Internal memoranda.

(e)This exemption covers internal Government communications and notes that fall within a generally recognized evidentiary privilege. Internal Government communications include an agency's communications with an outside consultant or other outside person, with a court, or with Congress, when those communications are for a purpose similar to the purpose of privileged intra-agency communications.
