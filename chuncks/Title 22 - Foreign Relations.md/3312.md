
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter R - Access to Information

####### Notice to requester.

(i)Whenever the Department provides a submitter with notice and an opportunity to object to disclosure under paragraph (f) of this section, the Department shall also notify the requester.
