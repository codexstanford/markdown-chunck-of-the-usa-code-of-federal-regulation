
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter K - Economic and Other Functions
#### PART 103 - REGULATIONS FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF THE CHEMICAL WEAPONS CONVENTION AND THE CHEMICAL WEAPONS CONVENTION IMPLEMENTATION ACT OF 1998 ON THE TAKING OF SAMPLES AND ON ENFORCEMENT OF REQUIREMENTS CONCERNING RECORDKEEPING AND INSPECTIONS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 103.2 Definitions.
####### Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW).

The entity established by the Convention to achieve the object and purpose of the Convention, to ensure the implementation of its provisions, including those for international verification of compliance with it, and to provide a forum for consultation and cooperation among States Parties.
