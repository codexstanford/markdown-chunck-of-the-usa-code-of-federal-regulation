
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media

##### Reports and Records
###### ยง 518.50 Purpose of reports and records.

Sections 518.51 through 518.53 set forth the procedures for monitoring and reporting on the recipient's financial and program performance and the necessary standard reporting forms. They also set forth record retention requirements.
