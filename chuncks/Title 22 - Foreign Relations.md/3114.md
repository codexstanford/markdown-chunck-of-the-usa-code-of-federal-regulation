
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter O - Civil Rights
#### PART 146 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart B - Coverage
###### § 146.220 Admissions.
####### Application of §§ 146.300 through .310.

(c)Except as provided in paragraphs (d) and (e) of this section, §§ 146.300 through 146.310 apply to each recipient. A recipient to which §§ 146.300 through 146.310 apply shall not discriminate on the basis of sex in admission or recruitment in violation of §§ 146.300 through 146.310.
