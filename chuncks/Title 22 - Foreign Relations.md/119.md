
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 11 - APPOINTMENT OF FOREIGN SERVICE OFFICERS
##### ยง 11.20 Entry-level Foreign Service Officer career candidate appointments.
###### Eligibility.

(2)Before each FSOT, the Board of Examiners will establish a closing date for the receipt of applications for designation to take the test. No person will be designated to take the test who has not, as of that closing date, filed a complete application with the Board. To be designated to take the FSOT, an applicant, as of the date of the test, must be a citizen of the United States and at least 20 years of age.
