
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 18 - REGULATIONS CONCERNING POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
##### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
###### ยง 18.11 Answer.
####### Failure to deny or answer allegations in the complaint.

(c)Every allegation in the complaint which is not denied in the answer shall be deemed to be admitted and may be considered as proved. Failure to file an answer within the time prescribed in the notice to the respondent, except as the time for answer is extended by the Director General shall constitute a waiver of hearing, and the Director General may make his/her decision by default without a hearing or further procedure.
