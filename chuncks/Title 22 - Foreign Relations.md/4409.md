
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 217 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF HANDICAP IN PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart D - Postsecondary Education
##### ยง 217.42 Admissions and recruitment.
###### General.

(a)Qualified handicapped persons may not, on the basis of handicap, be denied admission or be subjected to discrimination in admission or recruitment by a recipient to which this subpart applies.
