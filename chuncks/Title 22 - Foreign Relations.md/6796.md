
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter X - Inter-American Foundation
### PART 1007 - SALARY OFFSET
#### ยง 1007.2 Definitions.
##### Disposable pay

means the amount that remains from an employee's federal pay after the required deductions for social security, federal, state or local income tax, health insurance premiums, retirement contributions, life insurance premiums, federal employment taxes, and any other deductions that are required to be withheld by law.
