
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 20 - BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN FORMER SPOUSES
##### ยง 20.9 Application procedure.
###### Payment of benefits delayed.

(c)Payment of benefits cannot be made to a former spouse until the application for benefits is approved by the Retirement Division of the Department. Upon such approval, benefits will be paid to an eligible former spouse retroactively, if necessary, back to the commencing date determined under this part.
