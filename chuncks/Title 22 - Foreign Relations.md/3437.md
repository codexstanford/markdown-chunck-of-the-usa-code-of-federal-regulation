
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter T - Hostage Relief

####### Assignment of life insurance policy.

(o)Section 305 (50 U.S.C. App. 535) provides that the assignee of a life insurance policy assigned as security, other than the insurer in connection with a policy loan, except upon certain conditions, shall not exercise any right with respect to the assignment during the period of captivity of the insured and one year thereafter, unless upon order of a court.
