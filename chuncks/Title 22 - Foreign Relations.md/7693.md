
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter XV - African Development Foundation
### PART 1509 - GOVERNMENTWIDE REQUIREMENTS FOR DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE)
#### Subpart D - Responsibilities of ADF Awarding Officials
##### ยง 1509.400 What are my responsibilities as a(n) ADF awarding official?

As a(n) ADF awarding official, you must obtain each recipient's agreement, as a condition of the award, to comply with the requirements in-

(a) Subpart B of this part, if the recipient is not an individual; or

(b) Subpart C of this part, if the recipient is an individual.
