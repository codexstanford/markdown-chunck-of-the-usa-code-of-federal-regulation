
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 521 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
#### ยง 521.2 Definitions.
##### Investigating Official

means the Inspector General for the Broadcasting Board of Governors or an officer or employee of the Office of Inspector General designated by the Inspector General and serving in a position for which the rate of basic pay is not less than the minimum rate of basic pay for grade GS-16 under the General Schedule.
