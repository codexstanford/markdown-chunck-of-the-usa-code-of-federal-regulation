
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter E - Visas

##### Subpart F - Business and Media Visas
###### ยง 41.51 Treaty trader, treaty investor, or treaty alien in a specialty occupation.
####### Trade.

(7)The term "trade" as used in this section means the existing international exchange of items of trade for consideration between the United States and the treaty country. Existing trade includes successfully negotiated contracts binding upon the parties that call for the immediate exchange of items of trade. This exchange must be traceable and identifiable. Title to the trade item must pass from one treaty party to the other.
