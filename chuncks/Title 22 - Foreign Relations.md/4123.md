##### ยง 212.22 Request for accounting of record disclosures.
###### Where accountings are not required.

(b)The component's FOIA office is not required to keep an accounting of disclosures in the case of:

(1) Disclosures made to employees within the Agency who have a need for the record in the performance of their duties; and

(2) Disclosures required under the FOIA.
