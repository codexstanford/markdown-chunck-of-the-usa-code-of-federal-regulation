
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter G - Public Diplomacy and Exchanges
#### PART 61 - WORLD-WIDE FREE FLOW OF AUDIO-VISUAL MATERIALS
##### ยง 61.2 Definitions.
###### Instruct or inform

-means to teach, train or impart knowledge through the development of a subject or aspect of a subject to aid the viewer or listener in a learning process. The instructional or informational character of audio-visual material may be evidenced by the presence of collateral instructional material.
