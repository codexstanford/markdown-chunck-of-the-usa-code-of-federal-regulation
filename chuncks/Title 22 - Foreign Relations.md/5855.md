
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 506 - PART-TIME CAREER EMPLOYMENT PROGRAM
#### ยง 506.6 Publicizing vacancies.

When applicants from outside the Federal service are desired, part-time vacancies may be publicized through various recruiting means, such as:

(a) Federal Job Information Centers.

(b) State Employment Offices.

(c) Broadcasting Board of Governors Vacancy Announcements.

(d) College and University Placement Offices.
