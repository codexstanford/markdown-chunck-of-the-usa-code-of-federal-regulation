
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter E - Visas

###### ยง 42.22 Returning resident aliens.
####### Returning resident alien originally admitted under the Act of December 28, 1945.

(c)An alien admitted into the United States under Section 1 of the Act of December 28, 1945 ("GI Brides Act") shall not be refused an immigrant visa after a temporary absence abroad solely because of a mental or physical defect or defects that existed at the time of the original admission.
