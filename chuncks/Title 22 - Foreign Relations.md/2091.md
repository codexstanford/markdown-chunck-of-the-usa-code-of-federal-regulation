
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 96.2 Definitions.
####### Post-placement

means after a grant of legal custody or guardianship of the child to the prospective adoptive parent(s), or to a custodian for the purpose of escorting the child to the identified prospective adoptive parent(s), and before an adoption.
