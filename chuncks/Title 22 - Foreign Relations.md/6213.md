
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 521 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
#### ยง 521.13 Parties to the hearing.

(a) The parties to the hearing shall be the defendant and the Broadcasting Board of Governors.

(b) Pursuant to 31 U.S.C. 3730(c)(5), a private plaintiff under the False Claims Act may participate in these proceedings to the extent authorized by the provisions of that Act.
