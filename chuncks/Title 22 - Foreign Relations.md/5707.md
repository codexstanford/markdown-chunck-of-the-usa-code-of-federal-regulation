(b)Your request for documents should provide as many details as possible that will help us find the records you are requesting. If there is insufficient information, we will ask you to provide greater details. Include your telephone number(s) to help us reach you if we have questions. If you are not sure how to write your request or what details to include, you may call the FOIA Office to request a copy of the Agency's booklet "Guide and Index of Records," or access the same information via the Internet on BBG's World Wide Web site (). The more specific the request for documents, the sooner the Agency will be able to respond to your request(s).
