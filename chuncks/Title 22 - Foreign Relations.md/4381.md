
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 217 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF HANDICAP IN PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 217.5 Assurances required.
###### Assurances.

(a)An applicant for Federal financial assistance to which this part applies shall submit an assurance, on a form specified by the Administrator, that the program or activity will be operated in compliance with this part. An applicant may incorporate these assurances by reference in subsequent applications to the Agency.
