
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 232 - REPUBLIC OF TUNISIA LOAN GUARANTEES ISSUED UNDER THE DEPARTMENT OF STATE, FOREIGN OPERATIONS, AND RELATED PROGRAMS APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2012, DIV. I, PUB. L. 112-74 - STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS
#### ยง 232.02 Definitions.
##### Principal amount

means the principal amount of any Eligible Notes issued by the Borrower. For purposes of determining the principal amount of any Eligible Notes issued by the Borrower, the principal amount of each Eligible Note shall be the stated principal amount thereof.
