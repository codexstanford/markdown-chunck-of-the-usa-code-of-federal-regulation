
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter M - International Traffic in Arms Regulations
#### PART 120 - PURPOSE AND DEFINITIONS
##### Subpart C - Definitions
###### ยง 120.43 Development, production, and related terms; Basic and applied research.
####### Development

(a)is related to all stages prior to serial production, such as design, design research, design analyses, design concepts, assembly and testing of prototypes, pilot production schemes, design data, process of transforming design data into a product, configuration design, integration design, and layouts. Development includes modification of an existing design.
