
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 505 - PRIVACY ACT REGULATION
#### ยง 505.12 Civil remedies and criminal penalties.
##### Other failures to comply with the Act.

(4)You may bring an action for any alleged failure by the Agency to comply with the requirements of the Act or failure to comply with any rule published by the Agency to implement the Act provided it can be shown that:

(i) The action was intentional or willful;

(ii) The Agency's action adversely affected you; and

(iii) The adverse action was caused by the Agency's actions.
