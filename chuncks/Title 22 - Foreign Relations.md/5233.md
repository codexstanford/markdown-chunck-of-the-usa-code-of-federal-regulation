
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 240 - SOVEREIGN LOAN GUARANTEE - STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS
#### ยง 240.2 Definitions.
##### Fiscal Agency Agreement

means the agreement among USAID, the Borrower and the Fiscal Agent pursuant to which the Fiscal Agent agrees to provide fiscal agency and trust services in respect of the Note[s], a copy of which Fiscal Agency Agreement shall be made available to Noteholders upon request to the Fiscal Agent.
