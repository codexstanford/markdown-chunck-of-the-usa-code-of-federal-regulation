
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 204 - HOUSING GUARANTY STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS
#### Subpart A - Definitions
##### ยง 204.1 Definitions.
###### Loss of Investment

(i)respecting any Eligible Note means an amount in Dollars equal to the total of the (1) Outstanding Investment determined as of the Date of Application, (2) Further Guaranteed Payments unpaid as of the Date of Application, and (3) interest accrued at the rate(s) specified in the Note(s) and unpaid on the Outstanding Investment and Further Guaranteed Payments to and including the date on which full payment thereof is made to the Lender or any Assignee.
