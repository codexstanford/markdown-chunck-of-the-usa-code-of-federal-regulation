
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter M - International Traffic in Arms Regulations
#### PART 120 - PURPOSE AND DEFINITIONS
##### Subpart B - General Policies and Processes
###### ยง 120.14 Licenses and related authorizations.
####### Export, reexport, retransfer, or temporary import, of defense articles.

(a)The approval of the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) must be requested and obtained before the export, reexport, retransfer, or temporary import of a defense article, unless an exemption under the provisions of this subchapter is applicable.
