
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter O - Civil Rights
#### PART 147 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 147.6 Information and communication technology requirements.
####### Alternative means of access when undue burden is imposed.

(c)When development, procurement, maintenance, or use of ICT that meets the standards as provided in 36 CFR part 1194.1 would impose an undue burden, the Department shall provide individuals with disabilities covered by this section with the relevant information and data by an alternative means of access that allows the individual to use the information and data.
