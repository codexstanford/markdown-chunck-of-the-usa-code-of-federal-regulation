
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 11 - APPOINTMENT OF FOREIGN SERVICE OFFICERS
##### ยง 11.20 Entry-level Foreign Service Officer career candidate appointments.
###### Background investigation.

(e)Candidates who pass the FSOA and elect to continue the hiring process will be subject to a background investigation. The background investigation must be conducted to determine the candidate's eligibility for a security clearance and serves as the basis for determining suitability for appointment to the Foreign Service (see 3 FAM 2212.1 (Security Investigation)).
