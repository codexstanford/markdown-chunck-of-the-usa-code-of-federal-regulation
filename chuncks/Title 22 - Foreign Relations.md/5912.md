
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 512 - COLLECTION OF DEBTS UNDER THE DEBT COLLECTION ACT OF 1982
#### Subpart C - Salary Offset
##### ยง 512.19 Definitions.
###### Disposable Pay

means that part of current basic pay, special pay, incentive pay, retired pay, retainer pay or authorized pay remaining after the deduction of any amount required to be withheld by law. The Board will exclude deductions described in 5 CFR 581.105 (b) through (f) to determine disposable pay subject to salary offset.
