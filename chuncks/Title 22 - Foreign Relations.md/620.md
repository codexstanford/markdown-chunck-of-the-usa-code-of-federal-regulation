## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter E - Visas

##### Subpart D - Security and Related Grounds

####### Waiver of ineligibility-212(a)(3)(D)(iv).

(g)lf an immigrant visa applicant is ineligible under INA 212(a)(3)(D) but is qualified to seek the benefits of INA 212(a)(3)(D)(iv), the consular officer shall inform the alien of the procedure for applying to DHS for relief under that provision of law.
