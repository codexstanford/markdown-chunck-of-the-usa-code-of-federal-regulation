
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter T - Hostage Relief
#### PART 191 - HOSTAGE RELIEF ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart B - Application of Soldiers' and Sailors' Civil Relief Act
###### ยง 191.12 Description of benefits.
####### Relief against penalties.

(e)Section 202 (50 U.S.C. App. 522) provides for relief against fines or penalties when a court proceeding involving a hostage is postponed, or when the fine or penalties are incurred for failure to perform any obligation. In the latter case, relief depends upon whether the hostage's ability to pay or perform is materially affected by being held captive.
