
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 512 - COLLECTION OF DEBTS UNDER THE DEBT COLLECTION ACT OF 1982
#### Subpart C - Salary Offset
##### ยง 512.19 Definitions.
###### Salary offset

means an administrative offset to collect a debt under 5 U.S.C. 5514 by deductions at one or more officially established pay intervals from the current pay account of an employee without his or her consent.
