### PART 215 - REGULATIONS FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF PRIVACY ACT OF 1974

##### Employee Equal Employment Opportunity Complaint Investigatory Records.

(4)This system is exempt under 5 U.S.C. 552a (k)(1) and (k)(2) from the provisions of 5 U.S.C. 552a (c)(3); (d); (e)(1); (e)(4) (G), (H), (I); and (f).
