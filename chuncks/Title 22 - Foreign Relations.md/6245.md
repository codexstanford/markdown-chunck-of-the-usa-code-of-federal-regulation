
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media
### PART 521 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
#### ยง 521.41 Stay pending appeal.

(a) An initial decision is stayed automatically pending disposition of a motion for reconsideration or of an appeal to the Director.

(b) No administrative stay is available following a final decision of the Director.
