
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter V - United States Agency for Global Media

#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 513.105 Definitions.
###### Debarment.

An action taken by a debarring official in accordance with these regulations to exclude a person from participating in covered transactions. A person so excluded is "debarred."
