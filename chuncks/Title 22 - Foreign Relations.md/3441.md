
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter T - Hostage Relief
#### PART 191 - HOSTAGE RELIEF ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart B - Application of Soldiers' and Sailors' Civil Relief Act
###### ยง 191.12 Description of benefits.
####### Income taxes.

(s)Section 513 provides for deferment of payment of income taxes. However, section 204 of the Hostage Relief Act of 1980 provides for deferment and certain other relief, and should be referred to in order to determine statutory tax benefits in addition to those in section 513 of the Civil Relief Act.
