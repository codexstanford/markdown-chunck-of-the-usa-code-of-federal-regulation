
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter G - Public Diplomacy and Exchanges
#### PART 61 - WORLD-WIDE FREE FLOW OF AUDIO-VISUAL MATERIALS
##### ยง 61.2 Definitions.
###### Serial certification

-means certification by the Department of materials produced in series form and which, for time-sensitive reasons, cannot be reviewed prior to production; but samples are provided on application, and the materials are subject to post-certification review.
