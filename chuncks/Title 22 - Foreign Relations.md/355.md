
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter B - Personnel
#### PART 20 - BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN FORMER SPOUSES
##### ยง 20.5 Survivor benefits.
###### Effect of election of alternate form annuity.

(b)If a principal elects an alternate form annuity under section 829 of the Act or 5 U.S.C. 8420a, survivor benefits for a former spouse under this section shall, nevertheless, be based on what the principal's annuity would have been had the principal not withdrawn retirement contributions in a lump sum.
