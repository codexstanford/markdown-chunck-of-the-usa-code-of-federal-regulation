
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter II - Agency for International Development
### PART 235 - HASHEMITE KINGDOM OF JORDAN LOAN GUARANTEES ISSUED UNDER THE FURTHER CONTINUING APPROPRIATIONS ACT, 2014, DIV. F, PUB. L. 113-6 - STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS
#### ยง 235.10 Payment to USAID of excess amounts received by a Noteholder.

If a Noteholder shall, as a result of USAID paying compensation under the Guarantee, receive an excess payment, it shall refund the excess to USAID.
