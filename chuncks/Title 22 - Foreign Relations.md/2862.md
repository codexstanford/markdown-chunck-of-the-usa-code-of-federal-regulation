
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State
### Subchapter N - Miscellaneous
#### PART 140 - PROHIBITION ON ASSISTANCE TO DRUG TRAFFICKERS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 140.3 Definitions.
####### Money laundering.

(f)The process whereby proceeds of criminal activity are transported, transferred, transformed, converted, or intermingled with legally acquired funds, for the purpose of concealing or disguising the true nature, source, disposition, movement, or ownership of those proceeds. The goal of money laundering is to make funds derived from or associated with illicit activity appear to have been acquired legally.
