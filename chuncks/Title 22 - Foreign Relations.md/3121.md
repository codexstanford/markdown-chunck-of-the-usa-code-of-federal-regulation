### Subchapter O - Civil Rights

##### Subpart B - Coverage
###### § 146.230 Transition plans.
####### Nondiscrimination.

(c)No policy or practice of a recipient to which § 146.225 applies shall result in treatment of applicants to or students of such recipient in violation of §§ 146.300 through 146.310 unless such treatment is necessitated by an obstacle identified in paragraph (b)(3) of this section and a schedule for eliminating that obstacle has been provided as required by paragraph (b)(4) of this section.
