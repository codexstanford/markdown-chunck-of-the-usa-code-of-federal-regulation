
# Title 22 - Foreign Relations
## Chapter I - Department of State

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 143.3 Definitions.
####### Subrecipient

(4)means any of the entities in the definition of "recipient" to which a recipient extends or passes on Federal financial assistance. A subrecipient is generally regarded as a recipient of Federal financial assistance and has all the duties of a recipient in these regulations.
