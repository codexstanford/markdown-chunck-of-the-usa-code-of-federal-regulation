
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 90 - JUDICIAL REVIEW OF PATENT TRIAL AND APPEAL BOARD DECISIONS
##### ยง 90.3 Time for appeal or civil action.
###### Time computation.,Rehearing.

(b)(1)A timely request for rehearing will reset the time for appeal or civil action to no later than sixty-three (63) days after action on the request. Any subsequent request for rehearing from the same party in the same proceeding will not reset the time for seeking judicial review, unless the additional request is permitted by order of the Board.
