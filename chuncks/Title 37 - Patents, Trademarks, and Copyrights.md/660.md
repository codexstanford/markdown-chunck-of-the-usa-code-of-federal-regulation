
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - RULES OF PRACTICE IN PATENT CASES
##### Subpart G - Biotechnology Invention Disclosures
###### Application Disclosures Containing Nucleotide and/or Amino Acid Sequences
####### § 1.834 Form and format for nucleotide and/or amino acid sequence submissions as the "Sequence Listing XML" in patent applications filed on or after July 1, 2022.
######## e.g.,

(a) A "Sequence Listing XML" encoded using Unicode UTF-8, created by any means (text editors, nucleotide/amino acid sequence editors, or other custom computer programs) in accordance with §§ 1.831 through 1.833, must:

(1) Have the following compatibilities:

(i) Computer compatibility: PC or Mac&#xAE;; and

(ii) Operating system compatibility: MS-DOS&#xAE;, MS-Windows&#xAE;, Mac OS&#xAE;, or Unix&#xAE;/Linux&#xAE;.
