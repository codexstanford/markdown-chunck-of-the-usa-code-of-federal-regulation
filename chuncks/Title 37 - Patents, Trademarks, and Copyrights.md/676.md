
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - RULES OF PRACTICE IN PATENT CASES
##### Subpart G - Biotechnology Invention Disclosures
###### Appendix G to Subpart G of Part 1 - Numeric Identifiers
####### Prior Art Citations
########## inter partes,inter partes,inter partes,ex parte

Citations by the patent owner in accordance with § 1.933 and by anreexamination third party requester under § 1.915 or § 1.948 will be entered in thereexamination file. The entry in the patent file of other citations submitted after the date of an order for reexamination pursuant to § 1.931 by persons other than the patent owner, or the third party requester under either § 1.913 or § 1.948, will be delayed until thereexamination proceeding has been concluded by the issuance and publication of a reexamination certificate. See § 1.502 for processing of prior art citations in patent and reexamination files during anreexamination proceeding filed under § 1.510.
