####### ยง 2.122 Matters in evidence.
######## Notices of reliance.
######### Depositions must be filed.

(h)All depositions which are taken must be duly filed in the Office. On refusal to file, the Office at its discretion will not further hear or consider the contestant with whom the refusal lies; and the Office may, at its discretion, receive and consider a copy of the withheld deposition, attested by such evidence as is procurable.
