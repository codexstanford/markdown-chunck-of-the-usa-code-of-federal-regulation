######## When claim text in clean version is required.,i.e.,i.e.

(3)The text of all pending claims not being currently amended shall be presented in the claim listing in clean version,, without any markings in the presentation of text. The presentation of a clean version of any claim having the status of "original," "withdrawn" or "previously presented" will constitute an assertion that it has not been changed relative to the immediate prior version, except to omit markings that may have been present in the immediate prior version of the claims of the status of "withdrawn" or "previously presented." Any claim added by amendment must be indicated with the status of "new" and presented in clean version,, without any underlining.
