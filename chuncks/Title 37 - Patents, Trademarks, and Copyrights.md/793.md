
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - ASSIGNMENT, RECORDING AND RIGHTS OF ASSIGNEE
##### Requirements for Recording
###### ยง 3.24 Requirements for documents and cover sheets relating to patents and patent applications.
####### For electronic submissions:

(a)Either a copy of the original document or an extract of the original document may be submitted for recording. All documents must be submitted as digitized images in Tagged Image File Format (TIFF) or another form as prescribed by the Director. When printed to a paper size of either 21.6 by 27.9 cm (8by 11 inches) or 21.0 by 29.7 cm (DIN size A4), the document must be legible and a 2.5 cm (one-inch) margin must be present on all sides.
