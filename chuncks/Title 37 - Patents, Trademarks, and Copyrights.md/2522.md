##### ยง 201.26 Recordation of documents pertaining to computer shareware and donation of public domain computer software.
###### Definitions.,computer shareware

(b)(1) The termis accorded its customary meaning within the software industry. In general, shareware is copyrighted software which is distributed for the purposes of testing and review, subject to the condition that payment to the copyright owner is required after a person who has secured a copy decides to use the software.
