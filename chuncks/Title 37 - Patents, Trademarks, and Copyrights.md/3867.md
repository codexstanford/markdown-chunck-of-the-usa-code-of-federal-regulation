
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter III - Copyright Royalty Board, Library of Congress
### Subchapter B - Copyright Royalty Judges Rules and Procedures
#### PART 351 - PROCEEDINGS
##### ยง 351.1 Initiation of proceedings.
###### Notice of commencement; solicitation of petitions to participate.

(a)All proceedings before the Copyright Royalty Judges to make determinations and adjustments of reasonable terms and rates of royalty payments, and to authorize the distribution of royalty fees, shall be initiated by publication in theof a notice of the initiation of proceedings calling for the filing of petitions to participate in the proceeding.
