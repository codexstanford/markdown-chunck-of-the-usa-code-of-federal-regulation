
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights

### Subchapter A - Copyright Office and Procedures
#### PART 201 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### ยง 201.10 Notices of termination of transfers and licenses.
###### Reliance on remitter-provided information.

(5)The Copyright Office will rely on the certifications submitted with a notice and the information provided by the remitter on Form TCS and, if provided, in an accompanying statement of service. The Office will not necessarily confirm the accuracy of such certifications or information against the submitted notice.
