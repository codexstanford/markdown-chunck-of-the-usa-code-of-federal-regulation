
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter II - U.S. Copyright Office, Library of Congress
### Subchapter A - Copyright Office and Procedures
#### PART 212 - PROTECTION OF VESSEL DESIGNS
##### ยง 212.3 Registration of claims for protection of eligible designs.
###### Photographs.

(4)High quality black and white or color photographs will be accepted provided that they are mounted on plain white 8&#x2033; x 11&#x2033; unlined paper and do not exceed two photographs per sheet. Photographs must be developed on double weight photographic paper and must be of sufficient quality so that all the details of the design are plainly visible and are capable of reproduction on the registration certificate, if issued.
