
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - ASSIGNMENT, RECORDING AND RIGHTS OF ASSIGNEE
##### ยง 3.1 Definitions.
###### Assignment

means a transfer by a party of all or part of its right, title and interest in a patent, patent application, registered mark or a mark for which an application to register has been filed.
