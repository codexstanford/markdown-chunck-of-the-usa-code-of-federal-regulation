
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter II - U.S. Copyright Office, Library of Congress
### Subchapter B - Copyright Claims Board and Procedures
#### PART 223 - OPT-OUT PROVISIONS
##### ยง 223.1 Respondent's opt-out.
###### Effect of opt-out on particular proceeding.

(a)A respondent may opt out of a proceeding before the Copyright Claims Board (Board) pursuant to 17 U.S.C. 1506(i) following the procedures set forth in this section. A respondent's opt-out shall result in the dismissal of the claim without prejudice.
