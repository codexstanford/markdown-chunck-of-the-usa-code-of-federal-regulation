
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter B - Administration
#### PART 104 - LEGAL PROCESSES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 104.3 Waiver of rules.
####### sua sponte

In extraordinary situations, when the interest of justice requires, the General Counsel may waive or suspend the rules of this part,or on petition of an interested party to the Director, subject to such requirements as the General Counsel may impose. Any such petition must be accompanied by a petition fee of $130.00.
