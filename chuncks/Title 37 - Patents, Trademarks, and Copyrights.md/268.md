
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights

##### Subpart B - National Processing Provisions
###### The Drawings
####### ยง 1.84 Standards for drawings.
######## Front page view.

(j)The drawing must contain as many views as necessary to show the invention. One of the views should be suitable for inclusion on the front page of the patent application publication and patent as the illustration of the invention. Views must not be connected by projection lines and must not contain center lines. Applicant may suggest a single view (by figure number) for inclusion on the front page of the patent application publication and patent.
