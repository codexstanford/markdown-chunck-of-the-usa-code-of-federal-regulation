
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights

### Subchapter B - Copyright Claims Board and Procedures

###### Submitting confidential information.

(4)Confidential discovery materials, or references to or discussions of confidential discovery materials in other documents, may be submitted to the Board by either filing them under seal or redacting the confidential document. If filed under seal, the confidential document must be accompanied by a redacted copy that may be included in the public record.
