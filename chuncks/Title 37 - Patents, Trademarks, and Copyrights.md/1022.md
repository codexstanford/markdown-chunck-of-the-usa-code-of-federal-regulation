####### In a single application for a trademark, service mark, and/or collective mark,

(a)an applicant may apply to register the same mark for goods, services, and/or a collective membership organization in multiple classes. In a multiple-class application, the applicant must satisfy the following, in addition to the application requirements of ยง 2.32 for a trademark or service mark, and ยง 2.44 for collective marks:

(2) Submit the application filing fee required by ยง 2.6 for each class; and
