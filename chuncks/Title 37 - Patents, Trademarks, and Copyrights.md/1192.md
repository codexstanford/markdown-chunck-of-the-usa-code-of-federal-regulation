
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - RULES OF PRACTICE IN TRADEMARK CASES
##### Correction, Disclaimer, Surrender, Etc.
###### ยง 2.173 Amendment of registration.
####### Form of amendment.,inter partes

(a)The owner of a registration may apply to amend a registration or to disclaim part of the mark in the registration. The owner must submit a written request specifying the amendment or disclaimer. If the registration is involved in anproceeding before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board, the request must be filed by appropriate motion to the Board.
