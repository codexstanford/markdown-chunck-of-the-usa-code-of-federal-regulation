
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter II - U.S. Copyright Office, Library of Congress
### Subchapter A - Copyright Office and Procedures
#### PART 201 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### ยง 201.30 Verification of Statements of Account.
###### Coordination and selection of verifying auditor.

(2)The Copyright Office will publish in thenotice of having received a notification of interest to institute a verification procedure. Interested copyright parties have one month from the date of publication of thenotice to notify the party interested in instituting the verification procedure of their intent to join with it and to participate in the selection of the verifying auditor. Any dispute about the selection of the verifying auditor shall be resolved by the parties themselves.
