### Subchapter B - Copyright Claims Board and Procedures
#### PART 222 - PROCEEDINGS
##### ยง 222.7 Order regarding second filing fee and electronic filing registration.
###### Issuance of order.

(a)Once a proceeding has become active with respect to all respondents who have been served and have not opted out within the 60-day period set forth in 17 U.S.C. 1506(i), the Board shall issue an order to all parties in the proceeding providing that within 14 days of the order-

(1) The claimant must submit the second payment of the filing fee set forth in 37 CFR 201.3(g) through eCCB; and

(2) All claimant(s) and respondent(s) must register for eCCB unless they have been granted a waiver pursuant to ยง 222.5(f).
