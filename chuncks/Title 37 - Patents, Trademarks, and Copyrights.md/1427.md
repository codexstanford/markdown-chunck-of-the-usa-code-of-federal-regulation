###### Patents, Trademarks, and Other Non-Patent Law
####### ยง 11.14 Individuals who may practice before the Office in trademark and other non-patent matters.
######## Attorneys.

(a)Any individual who is an attorney as defined in ยง 11.1 may represent others before the Office in trademark and other non-patent matters. An attorney is not required to apply for registration or recognition to practice before the Office in trademark and other non-patent matters.
