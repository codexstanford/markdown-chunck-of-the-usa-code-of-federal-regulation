
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights
## Chapter I - United States Patent and Trademark Office, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 90 - JUDICIAL REVIEW OF PATENT TRIAL AND APPEAL BOARD DECISIONS
##### ยง 90.3 Time for appeal or civil action.
###### Filing deadline.,For an appeal under 35 U.S.C. 141.

(a)(1)The notice of appeal filed pursuant to 35 U.S.C. 142 must be filed with the Director of the United States Patent and Trademark Office no later than sixty-three (63) days after the date of the final Board decision. Any notice of cross-appeal is controlled by Rule 4(a)(3) of the Federal Rules of Appellate Procedure, and any other requirement imposed by the Rules of the United States Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit.
