
# Title 37 - Patents, Trademarks, and Copyrights

### Subchapter B - Copyright Claims Board and Procedures
#### PART 234 - LAW STUDENT REPRESENTATIVES

###### Filing documents.

(g)All filings by a law student representative shall be made with the knowledge of the supervising attorney, who shall maintain an association with the law student representative in the Board's electronic filing system (eCCB). Supervising attorneys and law students shall maintain their own accounts in eCCB. A notice of withdrawal, and a notice of appearance if applicable, shall be filed whenever the identity of a law student representative or a supervising attorney has changed.
