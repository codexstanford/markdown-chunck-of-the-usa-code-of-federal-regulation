
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 18 - TRANSPORTATION IN BOND AND MERCHANDISE IN TRANSIT
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 18.0 Scope; definitions.
###### Port of diversion.

"Port of diversion" is the U.S. port to which merchandise is diverted while in transit from the origination port to the port of destination or the port of exportation.
