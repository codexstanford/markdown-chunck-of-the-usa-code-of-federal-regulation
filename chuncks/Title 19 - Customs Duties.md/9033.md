
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 192 - EXPORT CONTROL
#### Subpart A - Exportation of Used Self-Propelled Vehicles, Vessels, and Aircraft
##### ยง 192.2 Requirements for exportation.
###### For foreign-titled vehicles.

(2)For used, self-propelled vehicles that are registered or titled abroad, the owner must provide to Customs, at the time and place specified in this section, the original document that provides satisfactory proof of ownership (with an English translation of the text if the original language is not in English), and two complete copies of that document (and translation, if necessary).
