### PART 146 - FOREIGN TRADE ZONES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 146.1 Definitions.
###### Foreign merchandise.

"Foreign merchandise" is imported merchandise which has not been properly released from Customs custody in Customs territory.
