
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter II - United States International Trade Commission
### Subchapter B - Nonadjudicative Investigations

##### Subpart B - Investigations Relating to Global Safeguard Actions
###### ยง 206.12 Definitions applicable to subpart B of this part.
####### Commitment

(b)means commitments that a firm in the domestic industry, a certified or recognized union or group of workers in the domestic industry, a local community, a trade association representing the domestic industry, or any other person or group of persons submits to the Commission regarding actions such persons and entities intend to take to facilitate positive adjustment to import competition.
