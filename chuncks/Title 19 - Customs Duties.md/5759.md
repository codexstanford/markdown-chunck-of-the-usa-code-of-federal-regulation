
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 147 - TRADE FAIRS
#### Subpart E - Disposition of Articles Entered for Fairs
##### ยง 147.43 Entry under the Customs laws.
###### Payment of duties and taxes.

(a)Any applicable duties and internal revenue taxes on any article entered under any provision of the Customs laws must be paid on such article in its condition and quantity, and at the rate in effect, at the time of such entry.
