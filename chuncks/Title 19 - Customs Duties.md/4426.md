
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 123 - CBP RELATIONS WITH CANADA AND MEXICO
#### Subpart E - United States and Canada In-Transit Truck Procedures
##### ยง 123.42 Truck shipments transiting the United States.
###### Manifest required.

(a)Trucks with merchandise transiting the United States from point to point in Canada will be manifested on United States-Canada Transit Manifest, Customs Form 7512-B Canada 8. The driver, in accordance with Canadian Customs regulations, shall present the manifest in four copies to Canadian Customs at the Canadian port of departure for review and validation.
