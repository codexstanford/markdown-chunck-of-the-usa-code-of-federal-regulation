
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### ยง 122.48b Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS).
###### General requirement.

(a)Pursuant to section 343(a), Trade Act of 2002, as amended (19 U.S.C. 2071 note), in addition to the advance filing requirements pursuant to ยง 122.48a, for any inbound aircraft required to make entry under ยง 122.41, that will have commercial cargo aboard, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) must electronically receive from the inbound air carrier and/or another eligible ACAS filer, as specified in paragraph (c) of this section, certain information concerning the inbound cargo, as enumerated in paragraph (d) of this section.
