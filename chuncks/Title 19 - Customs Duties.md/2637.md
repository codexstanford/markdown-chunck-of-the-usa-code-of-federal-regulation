
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 18 - TRANSPORTATION IN BOND AND MERCHANDISE IN TRANSIT

##### ยง 18.11 General rules.
###### Divided shipments.

(b)One or more entire packages of merchandise covered by an invoice from one consignor to one consignee may be entered for consumption or warehouse at the port of first arrival, and the remainder entered for immediate transportation, provided that all of the merchandise covered by the invoice is entered and a TIR carnet which may cover such merchandise is discharged as to that merchandise.
