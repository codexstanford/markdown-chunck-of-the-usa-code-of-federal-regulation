##### ยง 142.21 Merchandise eligible for special permit for immediate delivery.
###### Split shipments.

(g)Merchandise subject to ยง 141.57(d)(2) of this chapter, which is invoiced and delivered to the carrier as a single shipment, but which, due to the carrier's inability to accommodate the merchandise on a single conveyance, is shipped by the carrier in separate portions to the same port of entry in the United States as listed on the original bill of lading, may be released incrementally under a special permit.
