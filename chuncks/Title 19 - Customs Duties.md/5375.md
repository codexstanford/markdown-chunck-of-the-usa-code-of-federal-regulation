
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 144 - WAREHOUSE AND REWAREHOUSE ENTRIES AND WITHDRAWALS
#### Subpart E - Rewarehouse Entries
##### § 144.41 Entry for rewarehouse.
###### Value and classification.

(e)The duties determined at the port where the original warehouse entry was filed shall be the duties chargeable under the rewarehouse entry, except in the cases provided for in §§ 159.7 (a) and (b) of this chapter, which pertain to certain classes of merchandise excluded from the liquidation of the original warehouse entry and merchandise on which rates of duty or tax are changed by an act of Congress or by a proclamation by the President.
