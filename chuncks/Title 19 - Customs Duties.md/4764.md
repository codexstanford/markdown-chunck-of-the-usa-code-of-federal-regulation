
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 133 - TRADEMARKS, TRADE NAMES, AND COPYRIGHTS
#### Subpart F - Procedure Following Forfeiture or Assessment of Liquidated Damages
##### ยง 133.52 Disposition of forfeited merchandise.
###### Trademark (other than counterfeit) or trade name violations.

(a)Articles forfeited for violation of the trademark laws, other than articles bearing a counterfeit trademark, shall be disposed of in accordance with the procedures applicable to forfeitures for violation of the Customs laws, after the removal or obliteration of the name, mark, or trademark by reason of which the articles were seized.
