
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 182 - UNITED STATES-MEXICO-CANADA AGREEMENT
#### Subpart K - Penalties
##### ยง 182.124 Framework for correcting claims or certifications of origin.
###### Exception in cases involving fraud or subsequent incorrect claims,Fraud.

(b)-(1)Notwithstanding paragraph (a) of this section, a person who acted fraudulently in making an incorrect claim or certification of origin may not make a voluntary correction of that claim or certification of origin. For purposes of this paragraph, the term "fraud" will have the meaning set forth in paragraph (C)(3) of Appendix B to part 171 of this chapter.
