###### Form and filing of petition.

(b)A petition under this section shall be typewritten, in English, and shall be filed, in triplicate, with the port of entry or filed electronically with CBP. The petition under this subpart should be on letterhead paper in the form of a letter, clearly designated as a "Petition for NAFTA Review of Adverse Marking Decision" and shall be signed by the exporter, producer or his authorized agent.
