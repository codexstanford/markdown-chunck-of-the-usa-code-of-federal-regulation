
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### ยง 122.86 Substitution of aircraft.
###### Transporting cargo,Forwarding.

(d)-(1)The carrier may forward all cargo which arrived on one aircraft by transferring it to another aircraft of the same airline to complete the inbound flight. The transfer shall be done under Customs supervision.
