
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 112 - CARRIERS, CARTMEN, AND LIGHTERMEN
#### Subpart B - Authorization of Carriers To Carry Bonded Merchandise
##### ยง 112.12 Application for authorization.
###### Common carriers other than railroad, steamship, or airline companies.

(1)Common carriers other than railroad, steamship, or airline companies generally known to be engaged in common carriage, shall file a certified extract of its articles of incorporation or charter showing that it is authorized to engage in common carriage, and a statement that it is operating or intends to operate as a common carrier.
