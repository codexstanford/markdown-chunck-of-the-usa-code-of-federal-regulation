
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 127 - GENERAL ORDER, UNCLAIMED, AND ABANDONED MERCHANDISE
#### Subpart C - Sale of Unclaimed and Abandoned Merchandise
##### ยง 127.29 Unsold merchandise.

Merchandise offered for sale but not sold shall be included in the next regular sale of unclaimed and abandoned merchandise. If the port director is satisfied that such merchandise is unsalable or of no commercial value, it shall be destroyed.
