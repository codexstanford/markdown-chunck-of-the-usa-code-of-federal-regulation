
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 182 - UNITED STATES-MEXICO-CANADA AGREEMENT
#### Subpart E - Restrictions on Drawback and Duty-Deferral Programs
##### § 182.44 Calculation of drawback.
###### General.,see

(1)For purposes of drawback under this subpart, the term "same kind and quality" has the same meaning as the 8-digit HTSUS substitution standard established in 19 U.S.C. 1313(b)(1) (§§ 190.2 and 190.22(a)(1)(i) of this chapter).
