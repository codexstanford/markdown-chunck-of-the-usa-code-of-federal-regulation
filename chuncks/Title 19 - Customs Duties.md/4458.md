
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 123 - CBP RELATIONS WITH CANADA AND MEXICO
#### Subpart G - Baggage
##### ยง 123.65 Domestic baggage transiting Canada or Mexico between ports in the United States.
###### Removal of special in-transit tag manifest.

(c)The special in-transit tag manifest shall be removed only by the Customs officers at the final port of reentry into the United States. If the officer finds the special in-transit tag manifest missing or not intact, or for any other reason believes that the baggage has been tampered with while outside the United States, he shall detain it for examination. Otherwise, baggage transported under the procedure in this section may be passed without examination.
