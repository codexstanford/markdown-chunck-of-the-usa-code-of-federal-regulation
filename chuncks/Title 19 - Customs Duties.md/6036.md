### PART 151 - EXAMINATION, SAMPLING, AND TESTING OF MERCHANDISE
#### Subpart A - General
##### § 151.12 Accreditation of commercial laboratories.
###### Assistant Commissioner.

In §§ 151.12 and 151.13, references to the "Assistant Commissioner" mean the Assistant Commissioner, Office of Information and Technology, or his designee, located in Washington, D.C.
