
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 122 - AIR COMMERCE REGULATIONS
#### Subpart L - Transit Air Cargo Manifest (TACM) Procedures
##### ยง 122.119 Transportation to another U.S. port.
###### Application.

(a)Air cargo shipments may be transferred for transportation as transit air cargo from the port of arrival to another port in the U.S. under this section. The director of the port of arrival may require Customs supervision of the transfer.
