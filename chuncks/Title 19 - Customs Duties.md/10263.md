
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 351 - ANTIDUMPING AND COUNTERVAILING DUTIES
#### Subpart B - Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Procedures
##### ยง 351.201 Self-initiation.
###### In general.

(b)When the Secretary self-initiates an investigation under section 702(a) or section 732(a) of the Act, the Secretary will publish in thenotice of "Initiation of Antidumping (Countervailing Duty) Investigation." In addition, the Secretary will notify the Commission at the time of initiation of the investigation, and will make available to employees of the Commission directly involved in the proceeding the information upon which the Secretary based the initiation and which the Commission may consider relevant to its injury determination.
