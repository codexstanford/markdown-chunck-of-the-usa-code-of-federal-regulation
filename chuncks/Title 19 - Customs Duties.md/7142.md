
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 174 - PROTESTS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 174.1 Definitions.
###### Further review.

"Further review" means review of the decision which is the subject of the protest by Customs officers on a level higher than the district, and in Region II by Customs officers who did not participate directly in the decision which is the subject of the protest.
