
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 146 - FOREIGN TRADE ZONES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 146.1 Definitions.
###### Port Director.

For those foreign trade zones located within the geographical limits of a port of entry, the term "port director" means the director of that port of entry. For those foreign trade zones located outside the geographical limits of a port of entry, the term "port director" means the director of the port of entry geographically nearest to where the foreign trade zone is located.
