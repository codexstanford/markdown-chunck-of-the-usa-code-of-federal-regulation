###### American manufacturer's petition upheld.

The liquidation of entries covering merchandise of that character made after publication of the court's decision shall be suspended in accordance with ยง 159.57 of this chapter pending any rehearing or review, then liquidated, or, if necessary, reliquidated in accordance with the final judicial decision.
