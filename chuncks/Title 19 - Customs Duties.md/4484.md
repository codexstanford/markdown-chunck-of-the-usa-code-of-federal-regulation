
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### Where there is no contract for Government cartage.

(b)At ports where there is no contract for Government cartage in effect, the cartage of packages designated for examination at the public stores shall be done by a licensed customhouse cartman or a bonded carrier designated by the port director for this purpose.
