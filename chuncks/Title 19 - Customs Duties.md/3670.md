
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 114 - CARNETS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 114.1 Definitions.
###### Guaranteeing association.

(c)"Guaranteeing association" means an association approved by the Commissioner to guarantee the payment of obligations under carnets covering merchandise entering the Customs territory of the United States under a Customs Convention or bilateral Agreement to which the United States has acceded.
