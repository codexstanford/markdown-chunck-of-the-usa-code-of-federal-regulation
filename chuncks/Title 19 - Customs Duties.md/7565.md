
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 181 - NORTH AMERICAN FREE TRADE AGREEMENT
#### Subpart J - Review and Appeal of Adverse Marking Decisions
##### ยง 181.115 Intervention in importer's protest.
###### Form and filing of intervention.

(b)In order to intervene in an importer's protest, as provided for in paragraph (a) of this section, the exporter or producer of the merchandise shall file, in triplicate, a typewritten statement of intervention, in English, with the Center director. The statement should be on letterhead paper in the form of a letter and should be clearly designated "NAFTA Exporter or Producer Intervention in Protest". The statement shall be signed by the exporter, producer or his authorized agent.
