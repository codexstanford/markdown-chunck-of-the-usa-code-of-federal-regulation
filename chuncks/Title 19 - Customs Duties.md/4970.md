##### ยง 141.58 Single entry for separately arriving portions of unassembled or disassembled entities.
###### Entry or special permit after arrival of all portions (Hold All).

(1)An importer may file an entry at such time as all portions of the entity have arrived at the same port of entry in the United States. Any portion that arrives at a different port must be transported in-bond to the destination port where entry will be made. In the alternative, the importer may file a special permit for immediate delivery after arrival of all portions of the entity provided that it is eligible for such a permit under ยง 142.21(a)-(d), (f) and (i) of this chapter.
