(a)An importer who makes a claim for preferential tariff treatment under ยง 10.583(b) of this subpart:

(1) Will be deemed to have certified that the good is eligible for preferential tariff treatment under the CAFTA-DR;

(2) Is responsible for the truthfulness of the claim and of all the information and data contained in the certification provided for in ยง 10.584 of this subpart;
