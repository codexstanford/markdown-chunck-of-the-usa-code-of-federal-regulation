
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 123 - CBP RELATIONS WITH CANADA AND MEXICO
#### Subpart B - International Traffic
##### ยง 123.12 Entry of foreign locomotives and equipment in international traffic.
###### Domestic and foreign locomotives and other railroad equipment defined.

(d)For the purpose of this section and ยง 123.13, locomotives or other railroad equipment manufactured in, or regularly imported into, the United States, shall be considered "domestic" if not subsequently formally entered and cleared through foreign customs into another country, nor used in foreign local traffic otherwise than as an incident of the return of the equipment to the United States.
