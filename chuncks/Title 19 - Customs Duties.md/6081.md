
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

#### Subpart A - General

###### Representation of Customs-accredited status.

(4)Commercial laboratories accredited by Customs must limit statements or wording regarding their accreditation to an accurate description of the tests for the commodity group(s) for which accreditation has been obtained. Use of terms other than those appearing in the notice of accreditation (see paragraph (g) of this section) is prohibited.
