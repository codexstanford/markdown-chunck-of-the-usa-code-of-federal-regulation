##### ยง 191.2 Definitions.
###### Certificate of manufacture and delivery. Certificate of manufacture and delivery

(d)(see ยง 191.24 of this part) means Customs Form 7552, or its electronic equivalent, Delivery Certificate for Purposes of Drawback, summarizing information contained in original documents, establishing:

(1) The transfer of an article manufactured or processed under 19 U.S.C. 1313(a) or 1313(b) from one party (transferor) to another (transferee);

(3) The assignment of drawback rights for the article transferred from the transferor to the transferee.
