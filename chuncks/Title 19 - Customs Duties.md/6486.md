
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 159 - LIQUIDATION OF DUTIES
#### Subpart F - Continued Dumping and Subsidy Offset
##### ยง 159.63 Certifications.
###### Content of certification.

(b)While there is no established format for a certification, the certification must identify the date of thenotice under which it is submitted, and the case name and the number of the particular order or finding cited in thenotice. The certification must be executed and dated by a party legally authorized to bind the domestic producer. The certification must also state that the information contained in the certification is true and accurate to the best of the certifier's knowledge and belief under penalty of law, and that the domestic producer has records to support the qualifying expenditures being claimed.
