
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 12 - SPECIAL CLASSES OF MERCHANDISE
#### Merchandise Subject to Economic Sanctions
##### ยง 12.152 Prohibitions and conditions on the importation and exportation of rough diamonds.
###### Maintenance of Kimberley Process Certificate,Ultimate consignee.

(f)-(1)The ultimate consignee identified on the CBP Form 7501, Entry Summary, or its electronic equivalent filed with CBP in connection with an importation of rough diamonds must retain the original Kimberley Process Certificate for a period of at least five years from the date of importation and must make the certificate available for examination at the request of CBP.
