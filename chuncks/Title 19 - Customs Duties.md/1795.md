
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 10 - ARTICLES CONDITIONALLY FREE, SUBJECT TO A REDUCED RATE, ETC.
#### Subpart Q - United States-Peru Trade Promotion Agreement
##### Origin Verifications and Determinations
###### ยง 10.927 Special rule for verifications in Peru of U.S. imports of textile and apparel goods.
####### Assistance by U.S. officials in conducting a verification abroad.

(d)U.S. officials may undertake or assist in a verification under this section by conducting visits in the territory of Peru, along with the competent authorities of Peru, to the premises of an exporter, producer, or any other enterprise involved in the movement of textile or apparel goods from Peru to the United States.
