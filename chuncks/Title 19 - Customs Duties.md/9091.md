
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter II - United States International Trade Commission
### PART 200 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Subpart C - Provisions Governing Statements of Employment and Financial Interests
##### ยง 200.735-118 Interests of employees' relatives.
###### member of an employee's immediate household

The interest of a spouse, minor child or other member of an employee's immediate household is considered to be an interest of the employee. For the purpose of this section,means those blood relations who are residents of the employee's household.
