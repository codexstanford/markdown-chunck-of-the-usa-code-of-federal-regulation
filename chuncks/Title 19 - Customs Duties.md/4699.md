#### Subpart C - Importations Bearing Recorded Marks or Trade Names
##### ยง 133.23 Restrictions on importation of gray market articles.
###### Restricted gray market articles defined.

(a)"Restricted gray market articles" are foreign-made articles bearing a genuine trademark or trade name identical with or substantially indistinguishable from one owned and recorded by a citizen of the United States or a corporation or association created or organized within the United States and imported without the authorization of the U.S. owner.
