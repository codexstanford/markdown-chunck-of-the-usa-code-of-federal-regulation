
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 149 - IMPORTER SECURITY FILING
#### ยง 149.5 Eligibility to file an Importer Security Filing, authorized agents.
##### Powers of attorney.

(c)Authorized agents must retain powers of attorney in English until revoked. Revoked powers of attorney and letters of revocation must be retained for five years after the date of revocation. Authorized agents must make powers of attorney and letters of revocation available to representatives of Customs and Border Protection upon request.
