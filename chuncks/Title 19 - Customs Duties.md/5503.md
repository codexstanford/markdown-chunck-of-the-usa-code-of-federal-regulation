
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 146 - FOREIGN TRADE ZONES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 146.1 Definitions.
###### Operator.

"Operator" is a corporation, partnership, or person that operates a zone or subzone under the terms of an agreement with the zone grantee. Where used in this part, the term "operator" also applies to a "grantee" that operates its own zone.
