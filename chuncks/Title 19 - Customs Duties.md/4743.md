
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### ยง 133.42 Infringing copies or phonorecords.
###### Importation prohibited.

(b)The importation of infringing copies or phonorecords of works copyrighted in the U.S. is prohibited by Customs. The importation of lawfully made copies is not a Customs violation.
