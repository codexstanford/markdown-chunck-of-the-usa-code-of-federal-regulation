
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

### PART 142 - ENTRY PROCESS
#### Subpart D - Line Release

###### Verification of data.

(b)If after scanning the bar code at the Line Release site, the Customs officer verifies the data on the bar code with the information on the invoice, he will key the quantity on the invoice and an entry number will be automatically assigned to the transaction. If there are any differences between the system data and the invoice and bar code, including any differences in entry filer, the Customs officer shall order an examination.
