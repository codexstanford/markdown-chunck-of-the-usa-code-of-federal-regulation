##### ยง 144.14 Removal to warehouse.

When the entry summary, Customs Form 7501, or its electronic equivalent and the bond on Customs Form 301, containing the bond conditions set forth in ยง 113.62 of this chapter have been filed, the merchandise shall be sent to the bonded warehouse, except for:
