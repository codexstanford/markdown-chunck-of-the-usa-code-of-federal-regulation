
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### Identical merchandise under decision favorable to Government.

(a)The principles of any court decision favorable to the Government shall be applied to all merchandise identical with that passed on by the court which is covered by unliquidated entries, whether for consumption or warehouse.
