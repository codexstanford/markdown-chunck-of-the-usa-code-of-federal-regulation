
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 351 - ANTIDUMPING AND COUNTERVAILING DUTIES
#### Subpart D - Calculation of Export Price, Constructed Export Price, Fair Value, and Normal Value
##### ยง 351.411 Differences in physical characteristics.
###### Introduction.,See

(a)In comparing United States sales with foreign market sales, the Secretary may determine that the merchandise sold in the United States does not have the same physical characteristics as the merchandise sold in the foreign market, and that the difference has an effect on prices. In calculating normal value, the Secretary will make a reasonable allowance for such differences. (section 773(a)(6)(C)(ii) of the Act.)
