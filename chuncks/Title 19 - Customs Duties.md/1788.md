
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### ยง 10.927 Special rule for verifications in Peru of U.S. imports of textile and apparel goods.
####### Procedures to determine whether a claim of origin is accurate,General.

(a)-(1)For the purpose of determining that a claim of origin for a textile or apparel good is accurate, CBP may request that the Government of Peru conduct a verification, regardless of whether a claim is made for preferential tariff treatment.
