### PART 148 - PERSONAL DECLARATIONS AND EXEMPTIONS

###### Failure to advise of incidental character of arrival.

(c)If the traveler fails to advise the CBP officer of the incidental character of his arrival, or for other reason declares any articles for allowance of the $800 or $1,600 exemption, such declaration will mark the beginning of the respective period or periods during which a further exemption cannot be granted.
