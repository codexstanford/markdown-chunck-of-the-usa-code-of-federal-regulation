
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter II - United States International Trade Commission
### PART 200 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Subpart B - Provisions Governing Ethical and Other Conduct and Responsibilities of Employees
##### ยง 200.735-112 General conduct prejudicial to the Government.

An employee shall not engage in criminal, infamous, dishonest, immoral, or notoriously disgraceful conduct, or other conduct prejudicial to the Government.
