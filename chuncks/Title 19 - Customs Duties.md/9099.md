
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter II - United States International Trade Commission
### PART 200 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Subpart D - Provisions for Administrative Enforcement of Postemployment Conflict of Interest Restrictions
##### ยง 200.735-125 Exemption from restrictions.
###### Scientific and technological information solicited by the Commission.

(a)Communications of a former Commission employee solely for the purpose of furnishing scientific or technological information solicited by the Commission in the course of its statutory investigations are exempted from the restrictions on postemployment practices.
