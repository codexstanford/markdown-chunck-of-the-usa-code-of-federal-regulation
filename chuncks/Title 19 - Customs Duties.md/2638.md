
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### Consolidated loads and combined shipments.

(c)Several importations may be consolidated into one immediate transportation entry when bills of lading or carrier's certificates name only one consignee at the port of first arrival. However, merchandise moving under cover of a TIR carnet may not be consolidated with other merchandise.
