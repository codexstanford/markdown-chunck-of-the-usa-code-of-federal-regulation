
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 12 - SPECIAL CLASSES OF MERCHANDISE
#### Merchandise Subject to Economic Sanctions
##### ยง 12.150 Merchandise prohibited by economic sanctions; detention; seizure or other disposition; blocked property.
###### Blocked property.

(d)Merchandise which constitutes property in which the government or any national of certain designated countries has an interest may be blocked (frozen) pursuant to OFAC's regulations and may not be transferred, sold, or otherwise disposed of without an OFAC license.
