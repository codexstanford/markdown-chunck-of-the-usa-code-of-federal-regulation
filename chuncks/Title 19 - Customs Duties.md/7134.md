
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 173 - ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW IN GENERAL
#### ยง 173.4 Correction of clerical error, mistake of fact, or inadvertence.
##### Authority to review and correct entries of merchandise made, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, before December 18, 2004.

(a)Even though a valid protest was not filed, the Center director, upon timely application and for entries of merchandise made, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, before December 18, 2004, may correct pursuant to section 520(c)(1), Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, a clerical error, mistake of fact, or other inadvertence meeting the requirements of paragraph (a)(1) of this section, by reliquidation or other appropriate action.
