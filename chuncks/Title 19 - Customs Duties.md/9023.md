
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 192 - EXPORT CONTROL
#### Subpart A - Exportation of Used Self-Propelled Vehicles, Vessels, and Aircraft
##### ยง 192.1 Definitions.
###### Certified.

"Certified" when used with reference to a copy means a document issued by a government authority that includes on it a signed statement by the authority that the copy is an authentic copy of the original.
