
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 351 - ANTIDUMPING AND COUNTERVAILING DUTIES
#### Subpart D - Calculation of Export Price, Constructed Export Price, Fair Value, and Normal Value
##### ยง 351.410 Differences in circumstances of sale
###### Reasonable allowance.

(f)In deciding what is a reasonable allowance for any difference in circumstances of sale, the Secretary normally will consider the cost of such difference to the exporter or producer but, if appropriate, may also consider the effect of such difference on the market value of the merchandise.
