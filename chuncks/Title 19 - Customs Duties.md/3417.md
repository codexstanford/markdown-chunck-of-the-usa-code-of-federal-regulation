
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 112 - CARRIERS, CARTMEN, AND LIGHTERMEN
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 112.1 Definitions.
###### Contract carrier.

A "contract carrier" is a carrier which undertakes to transport specific goods or merchandise for a specific person or group of persons, and is authorized to operate as such by any agency of the United States.
