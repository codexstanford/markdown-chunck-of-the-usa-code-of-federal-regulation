
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 103 - AVAILABILITY OF INFORMATION
#### Subpart B - Production or Disclosure in Federal, State, Local, and Foreign Proceedings
##### ยง 103.27 Procedure in the event of a demand for CBP information in a foreign proceeding.
###### Employee notification to Counsel.

(b)Whenever a demand in a foreign proceeding is made upon a CBP employee concerning pre-clearance activities within the territory of the foreign country, that employee shall immediately notify the appropriate Associate Chief Counsel responsible for the pre-clearance location. All other demands in a foreign proceeding shall be reported by CBP employees to the Chief Counsel. The CBP employee shall then await instructions from the Chief Counsel concerning the response to the demand.
