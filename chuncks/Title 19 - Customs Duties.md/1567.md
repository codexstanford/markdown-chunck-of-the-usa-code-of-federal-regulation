
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

#### Subpart O - Haitian Hemispheric Opportunity through Partnership Encouragement Act of 2006
##### ยง 10.846 Imported directly.
###### Documentary evidence.

(c)An importer making a claim for duty-free treatment under ยง 10.847 of this subpart may be required to demonstrate, to CBP's satisfaction, that the articles were "imported directly" as that term is defined in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section. An importer may demonstrate compliance with this section by submitting documentary evidence.
