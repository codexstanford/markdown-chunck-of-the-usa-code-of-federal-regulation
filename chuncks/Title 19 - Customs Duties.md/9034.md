
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 192 - EXPORT CONTROL
#### Subpart A - Exportation of Used Self-Propelled Vehicles, Vessels, and Aircraft
##### ยง 192.2 Requirements for exportation.
###### For untitled vehicles,Newly-manufactured vehicles issued an MSO.

(3)-(i)For newly-manufactured, self-propelled vehicles that are purchased from a U.S. manufacturer, distributor, or dealer that become used, as defined in this subpart, and are issued a Manufacturer's Statement of Origin (MSO), but not issued a Certificate of Title by any jurisdiction of the United States, the owner must provide to Customs, at the time and place specified in this section, the original MSO and two complete copies of the original MSO.
