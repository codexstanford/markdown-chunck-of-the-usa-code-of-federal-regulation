##### ยง 122.25 Exemption from special landing requirements.
###### Request.

(a)Any company or individual that has operational control over an aircraft required to give advance notice of arrival under ยง 122.23 may request an exemption from the landing requirements in ยง 122.24. Single overflight exemptions may be granted to entities involved in air ambulance type operations when emergency situations arise and in cases involving the non-emergency transport of persons seeking medical treatment in the U.S. All approvals of requests for overflight exemptions and the granting of authority to be exempted from the landing requirements are at the discretion of the port director. Exemptions may allow aircraft to land at any airport in the U.S. staffed by Customs. Aircraft traveling under an exemption shall continue to follow advance notice and general landing rights requirements.
