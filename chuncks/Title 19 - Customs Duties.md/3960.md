###### Mandatory data elements.,i.e.,

(1)The following data elements are required to be submitted at the lowest air waybill level (at the house air waybill level if applicable) by all ACAS filers:

(ii) Consignee name and address;

(iii) Cargo description;

(iv) Total quantity based on the smallest external packing unit;

(v) Total weight of cargo; and
