
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 123 - CBP RELATIONS WITH CANADA AND MEXICO
#### Subpart C - Shipments in Transit Through Canada or Mexico
##### ยง 123.29 Procedure on arrival at port of reentry.
###### Checking and breaking of seals,Checking seals.

(c)-(1)The Customs officer at the port of arrival shall check customs seals applied to the conveyance or compartment for unlocked or missing seals. Where the seals are unlocked or missing, the merchandise shall be treated as having been imported from the transited country.
