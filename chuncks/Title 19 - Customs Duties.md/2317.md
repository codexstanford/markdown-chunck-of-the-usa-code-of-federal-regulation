
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 12 - SPECIAL CLASSES OF MERCHANDISE
#### Consumer Products and Industrial Equipment Subject to Energy Conservation or Labeling Standards
##### ยง 12.50 Consumer products and industrial equipment subject to energy conservation or labeling standards.
###### Issuance of redelivery notice and demand for redelivery.

(3)If DOE or FTC notifies CBP in writing or electronically that noncompliant covered imports have not timely been brought into compliance, CBP will issue a refusal of admission notice to the importer and, in addition, CBP will demand the redelivery of the specified covered import to CBP custody. The demand for redelivery may be made concurrently with the notice of refusal of admission.
