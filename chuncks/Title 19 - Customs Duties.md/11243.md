
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 361 - ALUMINUM IMPORT MONITORING AND ANALYSIS SYSTEM
#### ยง 361.102 Online registration.
##### Information required to obtain a username.

(b)In order to obtain a username, the importer, importing company, customs broker or importer's agent will be required to provide general information. This information will include: The filer company name, employer identification number (EIN) or Customs ID number (the Customs-issued importer number) (where no EIN is available), U.S. street address, phone number, contact information and email address for both the company headquarters and any branch offices that will be applying for aluminum licenses. It is the responsibility of the applicant to keep the information up to date. This information will not be released by Commerce, except as required by U.S. law.
