
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 133 - TRADEMARKS, TRADE NAMES, AND COPYRIGHTS
#### Subpart C - Importations Bearing Recorded Marks or Trade Names
##### § 133.25 Procedure on detention of articles subject to restriction.
###### In general.

(a)Articles subject to the restrictions of §§ 133.22 and 133.23 shall be detained for 30 days from the date on which the merchandise is presented for Customs examination. The importer shall be notified of the decision to detain within 5 days of the decision that such restrictions apply. The importer may, during the 30-day period, establish that any of the circumstances described in § 133.22(c) or § 133.23(d) are applicable. Extensions of the 30-day time period may be freely granted for good cause shown.
