###### Maximum penalty with prior disclosure.

(c)If the person concerned has made a prior disclosure as provided in ยง 162.74, whether or not such person has been certified as a participant in the drawback compliance program under part 191 of this chapter, the monetary penalty under section 593A, Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (19 U.S.C. 1593a), cannot exceed:
