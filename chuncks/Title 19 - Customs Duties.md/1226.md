
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### ยง 10.722 General definitions.
####### Goods of a Party.

(i)"Goods of a Party" means domestic products as these are understood in the GATT 1994 or such goods as the Parties determine under the rules of origin as applied in the normal course of trade, and includes originating goods of a Party.
