
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 360 - STEEL IMPORT MONITORING AND ANALYSIS SYSTEM
#### ยง 360.101 Steel import licensing.
##### Informal entries.

(d)No import license shall be required on informal entries of covered steel products, such as merchandise valued at less than $2,000. This exemption applies to informal entries only, imports of steel valued at less than $2,000 that are part of a formal entry will require a license. For additional information, refer to 19 CFR 143.21 through 143.28.
