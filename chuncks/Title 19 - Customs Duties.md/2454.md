
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 12 - SPECIAL CLASSES OF MERCHANDISE
#### Pesticides and Devices
##### § 12.110 Definitions.
###### et seq.,Administrator

Except as otherwise provided below, the terms used in §§ 12.111 through 12.117 have the meanings set forth for those terms in the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act, as amended (7 U.S.C. 136), hereinafter referred to as "the Act." The termmeans the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.
