
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 133 - TRADEMARKS, TRADE NAMES, AND COPYRIGHTS
#### Subpart C - Importations Bearing Recorded Marks or Trade Names
##### § 133.24 Restrictions on articles accompanying importer and mail importations.
###### Detention.

(a)Articles accompanying an importer and mail importations subject to the restrictions of §§ 133.22 and 133.23 shall be detained for 30 days from the date of notice that such restrictions apply, to permit the establishment of whether any of the circumstances described in § 133.22(c) or § 133.23(d) are applicable.
