
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

###### Aircraft arriving in the U.S. Virgin Islands.

(a)Aircraft arriving in the U.S. Virgin Islands from a place other than the U.S. are governed by the provisions of this part which apply to aircraft arriving in the U.S. from a foreign area.
