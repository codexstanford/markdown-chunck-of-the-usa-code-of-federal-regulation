
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 133 - TRADEMARKS, TRADE NAMES, AND COPYRIGHTS
#### Subpart C - Importations Bearing Recorded Marks or Trade Names
##### ยง 133.21 Articles suspected of bearing counterfeit marks.
###### Counterfeit mark defined.

(a)A "counterfeit mark" is a spurious mark that is identical with, or substantially indistinguishable from, a mark registered on the Principal Register of the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.
