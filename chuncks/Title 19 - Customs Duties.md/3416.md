
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 112 - CARRIERS, CARTMEN, AND LIGHTERMEN
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 112.1 Definitions.
###### Common carrier.

A "common carrier" is a carrier owning or operating a railroad, steamship, or other transportation line or route which undertakes to transport goods or merchandise for all of the general public who choose to employ him.
