
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

#### Subpart Q - United States-Peru Trade Promotion Agreement
##### Rules of Origin
###### ยง 10.923 Packing materials and containers for shipment.
####### Effect on tariff shift rule.

(a)Packing materials and containers for shipment, as defined in ยง 10.913(m) of this subpart, are to be disregarded in determining whether the non-originating materials used in the production of the good undergo an applicable change in tariff classification set out in General Note 32(n), HTSUS.
