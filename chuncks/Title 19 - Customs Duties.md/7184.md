
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 174 - PROTESTS
#### Subpart C - Review and Disposition of Protests
##### ยง 174.22 Accelerated disposition of protest.
###### Failure to allow or deny protest within 30-day period.

(d)If the Center director fails to allow or deny a protest which is the subject of a request for accelerated disposition within 30 days from the date of mailing of such request, the protest shall be deemed to have been denied at the close of the 30th day following such date of mailing.
