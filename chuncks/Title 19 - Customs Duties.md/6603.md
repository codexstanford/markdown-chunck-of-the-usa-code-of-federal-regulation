
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 162 - INSPECTION, SEARCH, AND SEIZURE
#### Subpart G - Special Procedures for Certain Violations
##### § 162.71 Definitions.
###### Actual loss of revenue.

(1)When used in §§ 162.73a, 162.74, 162.77a and 162.79b, the term "actual loss of revenue" means the amount of drawback (see § 191.2(i) of this chapter) that is claimed and has been paid to the claimant and to which the claimant is not entitled.
