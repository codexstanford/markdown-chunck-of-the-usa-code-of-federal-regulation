
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 127 - GENERAL ORDER, UNCLAIMED, AND ABANDONED MERCHANDISE
#### Subpart D - Proceeds of Sale
##### ยง 127.36 Claim for surplus proceeds of sale.
###### Involuntarily abandoned merchandise,Warehouse goods deemed abandoned.

(2)-(i)Claims for surplus proceeds of sale of warehouse goods deemed involuntarily abandoned sold under section 559 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (19 U.S.C. 1559), shall be established by reference to the warehouse entry, or, if the right to withdraw the merchandise from warehouse has been transferred, by reference to the documents by which the transfer was made.
