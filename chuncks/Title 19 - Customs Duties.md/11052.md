
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce

#### ยง 354.12 Hearing.
##### Joinder or consolidation.

(b)The presiding official may order joinder or consolidation if sanctions are proposed against more than one party or if violations of more than one administrative protective order are alleged if to do so would expedite processing of the cases and not adversely affect the interests of the parties.
