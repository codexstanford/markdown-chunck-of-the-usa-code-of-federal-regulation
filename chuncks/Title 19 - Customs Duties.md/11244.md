
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 361 - ALUMINUM IMPORT MONITORING AND ANALYSIS SYSTEM
#### ยง 361.103 Automatic issuance of import licenses.
##### In general.

(a)Aluminum import licenses will be issued to registered importers, customs brokers or their agents through an automatic aluminum import licensing system. The licenses will be issued automatically after the completion of the form.
