
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 192 - EXPORT CONTROL
#### Subpart A - Exportation of Used Self-Propelled Vehicles, Vessels, and Aircraft
##### ยง 192.2 Requirements for exportation.
###### Documentation required,For U.S.-titled vehicles,Vehicles issued an original certificate of title.

(b)-(1)-(i)For used, self-propelled vehicles issued, by any jurisdiction in the United States, a Certificate of Title or a Salvage Title that remains in force, the owner must provide to Customs, at the time and place specified in this section, the original Certificate of Title or a certified copy of the Certificate of Title and two complete copies of the original Certificate of Title or certified copy of the original.
