
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 191 - DRAWBACK
#### Subpart E - Completion of Drawback Claims
##### ยง 191.51 Completion of drawback claims.
###### HTSUS number(s) or Schedule B commodity number(s) of imports and exports,General.

(c)-(1)Drawback claimants are required to provide, on all drawback claims they submit, the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (HTSUS) number(s) for the designated imported merchandise and the HTSUS number(s) or the Schedule B commodity number(s) for the exported article or articles.
