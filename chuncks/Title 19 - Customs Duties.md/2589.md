
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### ยง 18.1 In-bond application and entry; general rules.
###### Divided shipments.

(m)After reaching the destination port, the port to which the merchandise has been diverted under ยง 18.5(a), in-bond merchandise may be divided into multiple shipments with a portion of the initial in-bond shipment being entered for consumption or warehouse, and the remainder shipped under a new in-bond application. The carrier or any of the parties named in paragraph (c) of this section must, in accordance with the filing requirements of this section, submit a new in-bond application for each portion of the original shipment to be transported in-bond.
