
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 361 - ALUMINUM IMPORT MONITORING AND ANALYSIS SYSTEM
#### ยง 361.103 Automatic issuance of import licenses.
##### Low-value licenses.

(f)There is one exception to the requirement for obtaining a unique license for each Customs entry. If the total value of the covered aluminum portion of an entry is less than $5,000, applicants may apply to Commerce for a low-value license that can be used in lieu of a single-entry license for low-value entries.
