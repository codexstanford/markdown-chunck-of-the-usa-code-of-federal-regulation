
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter I - U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Department of Homeland Security; Department of the Treasury
### PART 12 - SPECIAL CLASSES OF MERCHANDISE
#### Electronic Products
##### § 12.90 Definitions.
###### et seq.,et seq.

As used in §§ 12.90 and 12.91, the term "the Act" shall mean the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 201), as amended by the Radiation Control for Health and Safety Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 263b), and as further amended from time to time.
