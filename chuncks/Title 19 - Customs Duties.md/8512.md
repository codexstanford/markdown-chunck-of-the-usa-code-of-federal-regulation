(c)A customs broker may be certified as a participant in the drawback compliance program only on behalf of a given claimant (ยง 190.194(b)). To do so, a customs broker who assists a claimant in filing for drawback must be able to demonstrate, for and on behalf of such claimant, conformity with the core requirements of the drawback compliance program as set forth in paragraph (b) of this section.
