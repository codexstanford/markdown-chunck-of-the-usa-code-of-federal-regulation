
# Title 19 - Customs Duties
## Chapter III - International Trade Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 360 - STEEL IMPORT MONITORING AND ANALYSIS SYSTEM
#### ยง 360.101 Steel import licensing.
##### Entries for consumption.

(b)All entries for consumption of covered steel products, other than the exception for "informal entries" listed in paragraph (d) of this section, will require an import license prior to the filing of Customs entry summary documents. The license number(s) must be reported on the entry summary (Customs Form 7501) at the time of filing. There is no requirement to present physical copies of the license forms at the time of entry summary. However, copies must be maintained in accordance with Customs' normal requirements. Entry summaries submitted without the required license number(s) will be considered incomplete and will be subject to liquidated damages for violation of the bond condition requiring timely completion of entry.
