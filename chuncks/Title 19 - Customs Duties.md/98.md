###### Penalties,Failure to report, enter, or pay duty.

If a violation occurs, the vessel, its tackle, apparel, and furniture, or a monetary amount up to their value as determined by CBP, is subject to seizure and forfeiture and is recoverable from the owner (see ยง 162.72 of this chapter).
