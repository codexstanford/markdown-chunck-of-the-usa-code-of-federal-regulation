
# Title 19 - Customs Duties

##### ยง 133.42 Infringing copies or phonorecords.
###### Definition.

(a)Infringing copies or phonorecords are "piratical" articles, i.e., copies or phonorecords which are unlawfully made (without the authorization of the copyright owner).
