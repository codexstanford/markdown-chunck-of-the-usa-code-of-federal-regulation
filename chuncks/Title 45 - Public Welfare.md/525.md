
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 32 - ADMINISTRATIVE WAGE GARNISHMENT
##### ยง 32.2 Definitions.
###### Business day

means Monday through Friday. For purposes of computation, the last day of the period will be included unless it is a Federal legal holiday, in which case the next business day following the holiday will be considered the last day of the period.
