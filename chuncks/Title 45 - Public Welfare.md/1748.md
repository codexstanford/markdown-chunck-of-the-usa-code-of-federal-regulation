
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 79 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES
##### § 79.21 Discovery.
###### documents

(b) For the purpose of this section and §§ 79.22 and 79.23, the termincludes information, documents, reports, answers, records, accounts, papers, and other data and documentary evidence. Nothing contained herein shall be interpreted to require the creation of a document.
