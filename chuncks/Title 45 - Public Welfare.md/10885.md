
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter VI - National Science Foundation
#### PART 670 - CONSERVATION OF ANTARCTIC ANIMALS AND PLANTS
##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 670.3 Definitions.
####### Native mammal

means any member, at any stage of its life cycle, of any species of the class Mammalia, which is indigenous to Antarctica or occurs there naturally through migrations, that is designated in subpart D of this part. It includes any part, product, offspring of or the dead body or parts thereof but excludes fossils.
