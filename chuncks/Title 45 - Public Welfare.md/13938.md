
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XIII - Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### Subchapter D - The Administration for Native Americans, Native American Programs
##### PART 1336 - NATIVE AMERICAN PROGRAMS
###### Subpart F - Native Hawaiian Revolving Loan Fund Demonstration Project
####### ยง 1336.62 Definitions.
######## Applicant

means an applicant for a loan from the Native Hawaiian Revolving Loan Fund. An applicant must be an individual Native Hawaiian or a Native Hawaiian organization. If the applicant is a group of people organized for economic development purposes, the applicant ownership must be 100% Native Hawaiian.
