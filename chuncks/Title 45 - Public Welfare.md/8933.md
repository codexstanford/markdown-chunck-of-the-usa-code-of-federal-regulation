
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter II - Office of Family Assistance (Assistance Programs), Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 265 - DATA COLLECTION AND REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
##### ยง 265.3 What reports must the State file on a quarterly basis?
###### Disaggregated Data on Families Receiving TANF Assistance-Section one.

(1)Each State must file disaggregated information on families receiving TANF assistance. This section specifies identifying and demographic data such as the individual's Social Security Number and information such as the amount of assistance received, educational level, employment status, work participation activities, citizenship status, and earned and unearned income. The data must be provided for both adults and children.
