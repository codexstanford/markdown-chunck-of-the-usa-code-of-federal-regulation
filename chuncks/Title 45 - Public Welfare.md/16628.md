
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### ยง 2550.80 What are the duties of the State entities?
###### Coordination,Coordination with other State agencies.,et seq.

(l)-(1)A State entity must coordinate its activities with the activities of other State agencies that administer Federal financial assistance programs under the Community Services Block Grant Act (42 U.S.C. 9901) or other appropriate Federal financial assistance programs.
