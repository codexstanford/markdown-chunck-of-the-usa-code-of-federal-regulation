
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

###### ยง 508.7 Action by the Commission.

After the conclusion of the hearing and a review of the r&#xE9;sum&#xE9;, the Commission may affirm, modify, or reverse its former action with respect to the claim, including a denial or reduction in the amount of the award theretofore approved.
