
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2553 - THE RETIRED AND SENIOR VOLUNTEER PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 2553.12 Definitions.
####### National Senior Service Corps (NSSC).

The collective name for the Senior Companion Program (SCP), Foster Grandparent Program (FGP), and the Retired and Senior Volunteer Program (RSVP), and Demonstration Programs, all of which are established under Parts A, B, C, and E, Title II of the Act. NSSC is also referred to as the "Senior Corps."
