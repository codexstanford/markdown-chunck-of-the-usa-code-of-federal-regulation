
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service

##### Subpart C - Other Requirements for Recipients of Corporation Assistance
###### ยง 2540.300 What must be included in annual State reports to the Corporation?
####### Local grantees.

(b)Each State may require local grantees that receive assistance under this chapter to supply such information to the State as is necessary to enable the State to complete the report required under paragraph (a) of this section, including a comparison of actual accomplishments with the goals established for the program, the number of participants in the program, the number of service hours generated, and the existence of any problems, delays or adverse conditions that have affected or will affect the attainment of program goals.
