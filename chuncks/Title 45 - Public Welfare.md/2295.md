
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 88 - PROTECTING STATUTORY CONSCIENCE RIGHTS IN HEALTH CARE; DELEGATIONS OF AUTHORITY
##### § 88.3 Applicable requirements and prohibitions.
###### Counseling and referral provisions of 42 U.S.C. 1395w-22(j)(3)(B) and 1396u-2(b)(3)(B)),Applicability.

(h)-(1)(i) The Department is required to comply with paragraphs (h)(2)(i) and (ii) of this section and § 88.6 of this part.
