
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter VI - National Science Foundation
#### PART 680 - NATIONAL SCIENCE FOUNDATION RULES OF PRACTICE
##### Subpart A - Rules of Practice for the National Science Foundation
###### ยง 680.10 Definitions; cross-references to employee ethical conduct standards and financial disclosure regulations.
####### Institution

(3)means any university, college, business firm, research institute, professional society, or other organization. It includes all parts of a university or college, including all institutions in a multi-institution State or city system. It includes any university consortium or joint corporation; but not the universities that belong to such a consortium. Those universities shall be considered separate institutions for purposes of this part.
