
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

#### Subchapter B - The Administration for Children and Families, Head Start Program

####### Head Start agency

means a local public or private non-profit or for-profit entity designated by ACF to operate a Head Start program to serve children age three to compulsory school age, pursuant to section 641(b) and (d) of the Head Start Act.
