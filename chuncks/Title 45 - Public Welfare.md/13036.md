##### Subpart C - Volunteer Activities
###### ยง 1226.7 Scope.

The provisions in this subpart are applicable to full time volunteers as described in ยง 1226.3(c), and to such part-time volunteers as may be otherwise specified herein. Full time volunteers are deemed to be acting in their capacity as volunteers:
