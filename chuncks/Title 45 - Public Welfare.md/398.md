
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 30 - CLAIMS COLLECTION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 30.2 Definitions.
####### Business day

means Monday through Friday. For purposes of computation, the last day of the period will be included unless it is a Federal holiday, in which case the next business day following the holiday will be considered the last day of the period.
