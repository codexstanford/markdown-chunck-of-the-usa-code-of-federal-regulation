
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter III - Office of Child Support Enforcement (Child Support Enforcement Program), Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 310 - COMPUTERIZED TRIBAL IV-D SYSTEMS AND OFFICE AUTOMATION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 310.1 What definitions apply to this part?
####### Office Automation

(7)means a generic adjunct component of a computer system that supports the routine administrative functions in an organization (e.g., electronic mail, word processing, internet access), as well as similar functions performed as part of an automated data processing system. Office Automation is not specifically designed to meet the programmatic and business-centric needs of an organization.
