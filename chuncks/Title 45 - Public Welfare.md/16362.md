
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2524 - AMERICORPS TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE AND OTHER SPECIAL GRANTS
##### ยง 2524.40 What are the guidelines for grants to involve persons with disabilities?
###### Outreach.

(2)Corporation grantees and any public or private nonprofit organization may apply for funds to conduct outreach to individuals with disabilities to recruit them for participation in AmeriCorps programs. Outreach funds can also be used by any organization to assist AmeriCorps programs in adapting their programs to encourage greater participation by individuals with disabilities.
