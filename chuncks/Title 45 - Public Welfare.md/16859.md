
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2553 - THE RETIRED AND SENIOR VOLUNTEER PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Application and Fiscal Requirements
###### ยง 2553.72 What are project funding requirements?
####### Is non-CNCS support required?

(a)(1) A CNCS grant may be awarded to fund up to 90 percent of the total project cost in the first year, 80 percent in the second year, and 70 percent in the third and succeeding years.

(2) A sponsor is responsible for identifying non-CNCS funds which may include in-kind contributions.
