
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XI - National Foundation on the Arts and the Humanities
#### Subchapter C - Federal Council on the Arts and the Humanities
##### PART 1160 - INDEMNITIES UNDER THE ARTS AND ARTIFACTS INDEMNITY ACT
###### ยง 1160.4 Eligibility for international exhibitions.
####### Example.

(d)(1)An American art museum is organizing a retrospective exhibition which will include more than 150 works of art by Impressionist painter Auguste Renoir. Museums in Paris and London have agreed to lend 125 works of art, covering every aspect of his career, many of which have not been seen together since the artist's death in 1919. The organizer is planning to include 25 masterpieces by Renoir from American public and private collections. The show will open in Chicago and travel to San Francisco and Washington.
