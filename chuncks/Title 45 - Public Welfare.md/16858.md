
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2553 - THE RETIRED AND SENIOR VOLUNTEER PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Application and Fiscal Requirements
###### ยง 2553.71 What is the process for application and award of a grant?
####### For what period of time does CNCS award a grant?

(e)CNCS awards an RSVP grant for a specified period that is usually three years in duration with an option for a grant renewal of three years, if the grantee's performance and compliance with grant terms and conditions are satisfactory. CNCS will terminate funding to a grantee when CNCS determines that the grant should not be renewed for an additional three year period.
