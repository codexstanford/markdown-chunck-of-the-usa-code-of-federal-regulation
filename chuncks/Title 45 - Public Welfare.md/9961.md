
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter IV - Office of Refugee Resettlement, Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 410 - CARE AND PLACEMENT OF UNACCOMPANIED ALIEN CHILDREN
##### Subpart A - Care and Placement of Unaccompanied Alien Children
###### ยง 410.101 Definitions.
####### Emergency

means an act or event (including, but not limited to, a natural disaster, facility fire, civil disturbance, or medical or public health concerns at one or more facilities) that prevents timely transport or placement of UACs, or impacts other conditions provided by this part.
