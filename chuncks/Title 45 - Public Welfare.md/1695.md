
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 77 - REMEDIAL ACTIONS APPLICABLE TO LETTER OF CREDIT ADMINISTRATION
##### ยง 77.5 Remedial action procedures.
###### Notice.

(a)Prior to taking remedial action, the Department will provide the recipient organization written notice of its intended action setting forth both the legal and factual reasons therefor. Notice may be provided by certified or express mail, TWX, telegram, delivery, or similar means.
