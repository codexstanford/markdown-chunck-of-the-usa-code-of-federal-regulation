
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2532 - INNOVATIVE AND SPECIAL DEMONSTRATION PROGRAMS
##### ยง 2532.10 Military Installation Conversion Demonstration programs.
###### Demonstration programs,Grants

(c)-(1)-The Corporation may make grants to communities and community-based agencies to pay for the Federal share of establishing and carrying out military installation conversion demonstration programs, to assist in converting to community use affected military installations located-

(i) Within the community; or

(ii) Within 50 miles of the community.
