
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### ยง 641.14 Definitions.
###### Environmental Action Memorandum

(f)means a document briefly describing a proposed action and its potential impacts, if any, on the antarctic environment prepared by the responsible official when he or she determines that a proposed action will have less than a minor or transitory impact on the Antarctic environment.
