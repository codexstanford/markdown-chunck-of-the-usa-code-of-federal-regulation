
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 96 - BLOCK GRANTS
##### Subpart C - Financial Management
###### ยง 96.30 Fiscal and administrative requirements.
####### Financial summary of obligation and expenditure of block grant funds,Block grants containing time limits on both the obligation and the expenditure of funds.

(b)-(1)After the close of each statutory period for the obligation of block grant funds and after the close of each statutory period for the expenditure of block grant funds, each grantee shall report to the Department:

(i) Total funds obligated and total funds expended by the grantee during the applicable statutory periods; and

(ii) The date of the last obligation and the date of the last expenditure.
