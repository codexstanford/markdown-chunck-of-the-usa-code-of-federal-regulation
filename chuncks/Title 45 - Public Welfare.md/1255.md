
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### Subpart D - Post Federal Award Requirements
###### Procurement Standards
####### ยง 75.329 Procurement procedures.
######## Procurement by small purchase procedures.

(b)Small purchase procedures are those relatively simple and informal procurement methods for securing services, supplies, or other property that do not cost more than the Simplified Acquisition Threshold. If small purchase procedures are used, price or rate quotations must be obtained from an adequate number of qualified sources.
