
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Requirements Relating to Health Care Access
#### PART 150 - CMS ENFORCEMENT IN GROUP AND INDIVIDUAL INSURANCE MARKETS
##### Subpart C - CMS Enforcement With Respect to Issuers and Non-Federal Governmental Plans - Civil Money Penalties
###### ยง 150.315 Amount of penalty - General.
####### et seq.

A civil money penalty for each violation of 42 U.S.C. 300ggmay not exceed $100 as adjusted annually under 45 CFR part 102 for each day, for each responsible entity, for each individual affected by the violation. Penalties imposed under this part are in addition to any other penalties prescribed or allowed by law.
