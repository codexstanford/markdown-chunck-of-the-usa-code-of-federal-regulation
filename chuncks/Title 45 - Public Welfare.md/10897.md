
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter VI - National Science Foundation
#### PART 670 - CONSERVATION OF ANTARCTIC ANIMALS AND PLANTS
##### Subpart B - Prohibited Acts, Exceptions
###### ยง 670.4 Prohibited acts.
####### Possession, sale, export, and import of native mammals, birds, plants and invertebrates.

(d)It is unlawful for any person to receive, acquire, transport, offer for sale, sell, purchase, export, import, or have custody, control, or possession of, any native bird, native mammal, native plant or native invertebrate which the person knows, or in the exercise of due care should have known, was taken in violation of the Act.
