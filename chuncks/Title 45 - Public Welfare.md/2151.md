
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 86.2 Definitions.
####### Institution of vocational education

(o)means a school or institution (except an institution of professional or graduate or undergraduate higher education) which has as its primary purpose preparation of students to pursue a technical, skilled, or semiskilled occupation or trade, or to pursue study in a technical field, whether or not the school or institution offers certificates, diplomas, or degrees and whether or not it offers fulltime study.
