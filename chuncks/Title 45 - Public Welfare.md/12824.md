
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

###### ยง 1184.8 How can I address concerns regarding my request?
####### FOIA Public Liaison.

(a)If you have questions or concerns regarding your request, your first point of contact should be the FOIA Public Liaison, who is responsible for reducing delays, increasing transparency and understanding of the status of requests, and assisting in the resolution of disputes.
