
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter II - Office of Family Assistance (Assistance Programs), Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 263 - EXPENDITURES OF STATE AND FEDERAL TANF FUNDS
##### Subpart C - What Rules Apply to Individual Development Accounts?
###### ยง 263.20 What definitions apply to Individual Development Accounts (IDAs)?
####### Qualified plan

means a business plan that is approved by a financial institution, or by a nonprofit loan fund having demonstrated fiduciary integrity. It includes a description of services or goods to be sold, a marketing plan, and projected financial statements, and it may require the eligible recipient to obtain the assistance of an experienced entrepreneurial advisor.
