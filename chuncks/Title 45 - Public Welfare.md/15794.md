
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2500 - AGENCY OPERATING NAME AND LOGOS
##### ยง 2500.1 Agency Operating Name.

(a) The Corporation for National and Community Service adopts AmeriCorps as its official agency operating name.
