
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 5 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION REGULATIONS
##### Subpart A - General Information About Freedom of Information Act Requests
###### ยง 5.3 Definitions.
####### Review,i.e.,

means examining records responsive to a request to determine whether any portions are exempt from disclosure. Review time includes processing a record for disclosure (doing all that is necessary to prepare the record for disclosure), including redacting the record and marking the appropriate FOIA exemptions.
