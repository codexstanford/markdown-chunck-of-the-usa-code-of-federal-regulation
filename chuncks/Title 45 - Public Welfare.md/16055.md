
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2510 - OVERALL PURPOSES AND DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 2510.20 Definitions.
###### Community-based entity.

The term community-based entity means a public or private nonprofit organization that-

(1) Has experience with meeting unmet human, educational, environmental, or public safety needs; and

(2) Meets other such criteria as the Chief Executive Officer may establish.
