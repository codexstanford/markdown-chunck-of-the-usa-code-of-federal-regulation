
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2530 - TRANSFER OF EDUCATION AWARDS
##### ยง 2530.40 Is there a limit on the number of individuals one may designate to receive a transferred award?
###### General Limitation.

(a)For each award an individual earns as a result of successfully completing a single term of service, an individual may transfer all or part of the award to a single designated individual. An individual may not transfer a single award attributable to successful completion of a single term of service to more than one designated individual.
