
# Title 45 - Public Welfare

##### ยง 2510.20 Definitions.
###### Community-based agency.,community-based agency

The termmeans a private nonprofit organization (including a church or other religious entity) that-

(1) Is representative of a community or a significant segment of a community; and

(2) Is engaged in meeting educational, public safety, human, or environmental community needs.
