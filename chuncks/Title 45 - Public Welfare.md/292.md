
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle A - Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Administration
#### PART 12a - USE OF FEDERAL REAL PROPERTY TO ASSIST THE HOMELESS
##### ยง 12a.6 Suitability criteria.
###### Inaccessible.

(6)A property that is inaccessible will be determined unsuitable. An inaccessible property is one that is not accessible by road (including property on small off-shore islands) or is land locked (e.g., can be reached only by crossing private property and there is no established right or means of entry).
