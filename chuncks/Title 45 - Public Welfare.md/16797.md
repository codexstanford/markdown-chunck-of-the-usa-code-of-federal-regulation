
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2552 - FOSTER GRANDPARENT PROGRAM
##### Subpart K - Non-CNCS Funded Foster Grandparent Projects
###### ยง 2552.112 What are the resources and benefits to which a non-CNCS funded project is entitled?

The Notice of Grant Award entitles the sponsor of a Non-CNCS funded project to:

(a) All technical assistance and materials provided to CNCS funded Foster Grandparent projects; and

(b) The application of the provisions of 42 U.S.C. 5044 and 5058.
