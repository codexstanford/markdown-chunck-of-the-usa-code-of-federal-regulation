
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2553 - THE RETIRED AND SENIOR VOLUNTEER PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Application and Fiscal Requirements
###### ยง 2553.72 What are project funding requirements?
####### May CNCS restrict how a sponsor uses locally generated contributions in excess of the non-CNCS support required?

(c)Whenever locally generated contributions to RSVP projects are in excess of the non-CNCS funds required (10 percent of the total cost in the first year, 20 percent in the second year and 30 percent in the third and succeeding years), CNCS may not restrict the manner in which such contributions are expended provided such expenditures are consistent with the provisions of the Act.
