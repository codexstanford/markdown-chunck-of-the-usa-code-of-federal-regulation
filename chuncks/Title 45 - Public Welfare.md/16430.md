
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2529 - PAYMENT OF ACCRUED INTEREST
##### ยง 2529.10 Under what circumstances will the Corporation pay interest that accrues on qualified student loans during an individual's term of service in an approved AmeriCorps position or approved Silver Scholar position?
###### Eligibility.

(a)The Corporation will pay interest that accrues on an individual's qualified student loan, subject to the limitation on amount in paragraph (b) of this section, if-

(1) The individual successfully completes a term of service in an approved AmeriCorps position or approved Silver Scholar position; and

(2) The holder of the loan approves the individual's request for forbearance during the term of service.
