
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2522 - AMERICORPS PARTICIPANTS, PROGRAMS, AND APPLICANTS
##### Subpart B - Participant Eligibility, Requirements, and Benefits
###### § 2522.235 Is there a limit on the number of terms an individual may serve in an AmeriCorps State and National program?
####### Release prior to serving fifteen percent of a term.

(c)If a person is released for reasons other than misconduct prior to completing fifteen percent of a term of service, the term will not be considered one of the terms of service for which an individual may receive the benefits described in §§ 2522.240 through 2522.250.
