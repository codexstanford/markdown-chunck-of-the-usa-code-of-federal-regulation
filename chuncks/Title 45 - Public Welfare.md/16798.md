
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2552 - FOSTER GRANDPARENT PROGRAM
##### Subpart K - Non-CNCS Funded Foster Grandparent Projects
###### ยง 2552.113 What financial obligation does CNCS incur for non-CNCS funded projects?

Issuance of an NGA to a sponsor of a non-CNCS funded project does not create a financial obligation on the part of CNCS for any costs associated with the project.
