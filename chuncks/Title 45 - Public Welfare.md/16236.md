
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XXV - Corporation for National and Community Service
#### PART 2522 - AMERICORPS PARTICIPANTS, PROGRAMS, AND APPLICANTS
##### Subpart B - Participant Eligibility, Requirements, and Benefits
###### ยง 2522.210 How are AmeriCorps participants recruited and selected?
####### National and State recruitment and selection.,et seq.

(b)(1)The Corporation and each State Commission will establish a system to recruit individuals who desire to perform national service and to assist the placement of these individuals in approved AmeriCorps positions, which may include positions available under titles I and II of the Domestic Volunteer Service Act of 1973 (42 U.S.C. 4951). The national and state recruitment and placement system will be designed and operated according to Corporation guidelines.
