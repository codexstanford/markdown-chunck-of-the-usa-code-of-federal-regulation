
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter II - Office of Family Assistance (Assistance Programs), Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 287 - THE NATIVE EMPLOYMENT WORKS (NEW) PROGRAM
##### Subpart C - NEW Program Funding
###### ยง 287.55 What time frames and guidelines apply regarding the obligation and liquidation periods for NEW Program funds?
####### i.e.,

(a) NEW Program funds provided for a FY are for use during the period July 1 through June 30 and must be obligated no later than June 30. Carry forward of an unobligated balance of NEW funds is not permitted. A NEW fund balance that is unobligated as of June 30 will be returned to the Federal government through the issuance of a negative grant award. Unobligated funds are to be reported on the SF-269A that Tribes must submit within 30 days after the funding period,no later than July 30. This report is called the interim financial report.
