
# Title 45 - Public Welfare
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Welfare
### Chapter XIII - Administration for Children and Families, Department of Health and Human Services
#### Subchapter C - The Administration for Community Living
##### PART 1325 - REQUIREMENTS APPLICABLE TO THE DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES PROGRAM
###### ยง 1325.3 Definitions.
####### Productivity.

The term "productivity" means engagement in income-producing work that is measured by increased income, improved employment status, or job advancement, or engagement in work that contributes to a household or community.
