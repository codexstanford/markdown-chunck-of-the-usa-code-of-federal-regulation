###### ยง 5.25 How does HHS handle requests that involve more than one OpDiv, StaffDiv, or Federal agency?
####### Re-routing of misdirected requests.

(a)When a FOIA Requester Service Center determines that a request was misdirected within HHS, the receiving FOIA Requester Service Center must route the request to the FOIA Requester Service Center of the proper OpDiv or StaffDiv within HHS.
