
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1090-DEFINING LARGER PARTICIPANTS OF CERTAIN CONSUMER FINANCIAL PRODUCT AND SERVICE MARKETS
#### Subpart B-Markets
##### § 1090.107 International Money Transfer Market.
###### Designated recipient

means any person specified by the sender as the authorized recipient of an international money transfer to be received at a location in a foreign country.
