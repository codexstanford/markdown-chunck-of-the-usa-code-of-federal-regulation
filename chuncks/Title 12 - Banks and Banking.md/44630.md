
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1213 - OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN
##### § 1213.5 Complaints from a person.
###### General.

(a)Any person that has a business relationship with a regulated entity or the Office of Finance may submit a complaint in accordance with procedures established by the Ombudsman.
