
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1081-RULES OF PRACTICE FOR ADJUDICATION PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart B-Initiation of Proceedings and Prehearing Rules
##### § 1081.209 Depositions.
###### Waiver of objections,To the notice.

(i)-(1)An objection to an error or irregularity in a deposition notice is waived unless promptly served in writing on the party giving the notice.
