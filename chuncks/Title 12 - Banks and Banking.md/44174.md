
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1207 - MINORITY AND WOMEN OUTREACH PROGRAM
##### § 1207.2 FHFA workforce diversity; Equal Employment Opportunity Program.
###### Responsibility.

(a)FHFA's Office of Minority and Women Inclusion (OMWI) shall have overall responsibility for diversity and inclusion in FHFA's employment practices.
