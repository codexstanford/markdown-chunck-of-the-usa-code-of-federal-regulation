
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 217.208 Incremental risk.
####### Calculation of incremental risk capital requirement.

(c)The incremental risk capital requirement is the greater of:

(1) The average of the incremental risk measures over the previous 12 weeks; or

(2) The most recent incremental risk measure.
