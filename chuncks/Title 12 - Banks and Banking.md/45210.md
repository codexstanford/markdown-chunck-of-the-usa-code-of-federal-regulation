
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1234 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart B - Credit Risk Retention
###### § 1234.9 Open market CLOs.
####### CLO manager,et seq.

means an entity that manages a CLO, which entity is registered as an investment adviser under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, as amended (15 U.S.C. 80b-1), or is an affiliate of such a registered investment adviser and itself is managed by such registered investment adviser.
