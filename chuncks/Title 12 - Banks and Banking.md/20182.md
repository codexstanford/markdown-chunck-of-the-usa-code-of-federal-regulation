
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 264a - POST-EMPLOYMENT RESTRICTIONS FOR SENIOR EXAMINERS
##### ยง 264a.3 What special post-employment restrictions apply to senior examiners?
###### Senior Examiners of Bank Holding Companies.

(b)An officer or employee of the Federal Reserve who serves as the senior examiner of a bank holding company for two or more months during the last twelve months of such individual's employment with the Federal Reserve may not, within one year of leaving the employment of the Federal Reserve, knowingly accept compensation as an employee, officer, director or consultant from-

(1) The bank holding company; or

(2) Any depository institution that is controlled by the bank holding company.
