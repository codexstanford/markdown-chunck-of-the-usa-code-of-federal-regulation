
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Subpart F-Special Rules for Private Education Loans
##### § 1026.47 Content of disclosures.
###### Application or solicitation disclosures.

(a)A creditor shall provide the disclosures required under paragraph (a) of this section on or with a solicitation or an application for a private education loan.
