
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 242 - DEFINITIONS RELATING TO TITLE I OF THE DODD-FRANK ACT (REGULATION PP)
##### ยง 242.2 Definitions.
###### State.

The term "State" includes any State, commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, American Samoa, Guam, and the United States Virgin Islands.
