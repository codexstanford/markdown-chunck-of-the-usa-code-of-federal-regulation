
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1202-FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT
##### § 1202.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### FOIA Officer, FOIA Official and Chief FOIA Officer

are persons designated by FHFA to process and respond to requests for FHFA records under the FOIA.
