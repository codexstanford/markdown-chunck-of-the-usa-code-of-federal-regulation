
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 22 - LOANS IN AREAS HAVING SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARDS
#### ยง 22.9 Notice of special flood hazards and availability of Federal disaster relief assistance.
##### Alternate method of notice.

(e)Instead of providing the notice to the borrower required by paragraph (a) of this section, a national bank or Federal savings association may obtain satisfactory written assurance from a seller or lessor that, within a reasonable time before the completion of the sale or lease transaction, the seller or lessor has provided such notice to the purchaser or lessee. The national bank or Federal savings association shall retain a record of the written assurance from the seller or lessor for the period of time it owns the loan.
