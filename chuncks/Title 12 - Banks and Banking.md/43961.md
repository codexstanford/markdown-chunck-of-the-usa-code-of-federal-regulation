
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1202-FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT
##### § 1202.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### Chief FOIA Officer

means the designated high-level official within FHFA (FHFA-OIG does not have a separate Chief FOIA Officer) who has overall responsibility for the agency's FOIA program and compliance with the FOIA.
