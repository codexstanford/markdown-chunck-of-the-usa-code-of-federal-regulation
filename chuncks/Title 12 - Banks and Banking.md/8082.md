
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 209-FEDERAL RESERVE BANK CAPITAL STOCK (REGULATION I)
##### ยง 209.2 Banks desiring to become member banks.
###### Application for stock or deposit.

(a)Each national bank in process of organization,each nonmember state bank converting into a national bank, and each nonmember state bank applying for membership in the Federal Reserve System under Regulation H, 12 CFR part 208, shall file with the Federal Reserve Bank (Reserve Bank) in whose district it is located an application for stock (or deposit in the case of mutual savings banks not authorized to purchase Reserve Bank stock) in the Reserve Bank. This application for stock must state whether the applicant's total consolidated assets exceed $12,124,000,000. The bank shall pay for the stock (or deposit) in accordance with ยง 209.4 of this part.
