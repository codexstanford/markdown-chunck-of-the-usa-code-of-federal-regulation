
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1081-RULES OF PRACTICE FOR ADJUDICATION PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart A-General Rules
##### § 1081.120 Settlement.
###### Availability.

(a)Any respondent in an adjudication proceeding instituted under this part, may, at any time, propose in writing an offer of settlement.
