
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1016-PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION (REGULATION P)
#### Subpart A-Privacy and Opt Out Notices
##### § 1016.5 Annual privacy notice to customers required.
###### Delivery.

(d)When you are required to deliver an annual privacy notice by this section, you must deliver it according to § 1016.9 of this part.
