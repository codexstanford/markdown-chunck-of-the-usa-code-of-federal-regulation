
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 715 - SUPERVISORY COMMITTEE AUDITS AND VERIFICATIONS
##### ยง 715.4 Audit responsibility of the Supervisory Committee.
###### Annual audit requirement.

(a)A federally-insured credit union is required to obtain an annual supervisory committee audit which occurs at least once every calendar year (period of performance) and must cover the period elapsed since the last audit period (period effectively covered).
