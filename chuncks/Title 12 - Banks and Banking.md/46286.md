
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1254 - VALIDATION AND APPROVAL OF CREDIT SCORE MODELS
##### § 1254.7 Credit Score Assessment.
###### Requirement for Credit Score Assessment.

(a)An Enterprise will undertake a Credit Score Assessment of each application that the Enterprise determines to be complete. An Enterprise must determine whether an application passes the Credit Score Assessment.
