
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1202-FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT
##### § 1202.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### Requester Service Centers

serve as the primary contacts for a requester when the requester has questions, is seeking information about how the FOIA works, or to check the status of their request.
