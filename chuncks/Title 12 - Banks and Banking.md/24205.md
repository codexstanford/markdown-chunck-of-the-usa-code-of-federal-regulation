
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 335-SECURITIES OF STATE NONMEMBER BANKS AND STATE SAVINGS ASSOCIATIONS
##### ยง 335.801 Inapplicable SEC regulations; FDIC substituted regulations; additional information.
###### Adjustment of the filing date.

(4)If an electronic filer in good faith attempts to file a beneficial ownership report with the FDIC in a timely manner but the filing is delayed due to technical difficulties beyond the electronic filer's control, the electronic filer may request an adjustment of the filing date of such submission. The FDIC may grant the request if it appears that such adjustment is appropriate and consistent with the public interest and the protection of investors.
