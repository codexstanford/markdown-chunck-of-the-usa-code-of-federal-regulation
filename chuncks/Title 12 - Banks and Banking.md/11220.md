
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 225-BANK HOLDING COMPANIES AND CHANGE IN BANK CONTROL (REGULATION Y)
##### Subpart C-Nonbanking Activities and Acquisitions by Bank Holding Companies
###### ยง 225.21 Prohibited nonbanking activities and acquisitions; exempt bank holding companies.
####### Family-owned companies.

(1)Any company that is a "company covered in 1970" (as defined in section 2(b) of the BHC Act), more than 85 percent of the voting securities of which was collectively owned on June 30, 1968, and continuously thereafter, by members of the same family (or their spouses) who are lineal descendants of common ancestors.
