
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 50.3 Definitions.
###### Foreign withdrawable reserves

means a national bank's or Federal savings association's balances held by or on behalf of the national bank or Federal savings association at a foreign central bank that are not subject to restrictions on the national bank's or Federal savings association's ability to use the reserves.
