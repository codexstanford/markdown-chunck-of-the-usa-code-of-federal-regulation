
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 151 - RECORDKEEPING AND CONFIRMATION REQUIREMENTS FOR SECURITIES TRANSACTIONS
#### ยง 151.40 What definitions apply to this part?
##### Asset-backed security,Asset-backed security

means a security that is primarily serviced by the cash flows of a discrete pool of receivables or other financial assets, either fixed or revolving, that by their terms convert into cash within a finite time period.includes any rights or other assets designed to ensure the servicing or timely distribution of proceeds to the security holders.
