###### Relationship to the Freedom of Information Act.

(b)The Privacy Act applies to records contained in a systems of records, as defined in ยง 404.15. If an individual submits a request for access to records and cites the Privacy Act, but the records sought are not contained in a Privacy Act system of records, then the request shall be processed only under subpart A of this part, Procedures for Disclosure of Records Under the Freedom of Information Act.
