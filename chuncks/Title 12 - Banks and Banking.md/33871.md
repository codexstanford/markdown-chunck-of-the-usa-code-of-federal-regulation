
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.4 General disclosure requirements; jointly offered services.
###### Additional information; disclosures required by other laws.,et seq.,et seq.

(b)A financial institution may include additional information and may combine disclosures required by other laws (such as the Truth in Lending Act (15 U.S.C. 1601) or the Truth in Savings Act (12 U.S.C. 4301) with the disclosures required by this part.
