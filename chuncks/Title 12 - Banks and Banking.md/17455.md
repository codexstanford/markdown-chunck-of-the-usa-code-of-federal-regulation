
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 242 - DEFINITIONS RELATING TO TITLE I OF THE DODD-FRANK ACT (REGULATION PP)
##### Appendix A to Part 242 - Financial Activities for Purposes of Title I of the Dodd-Frank Act
###### Credit insurance.

(i)Acting as principal, agent, or broker for insurance (including home mortgage redemption insurance) that is:

(A) Directly related to an extension of credit by the company or any of its subsidiaries; and

(B) Limited to ensuring the repayment of the outstanding balance due on the extension of creditin the event of the death, disability, or involuntary unemployment of the debtor.
