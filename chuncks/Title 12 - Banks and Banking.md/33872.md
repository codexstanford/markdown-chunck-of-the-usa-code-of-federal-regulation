
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.4 General disclosure requirements; jointly offered services.
###### Multiple accounts and account holders,Multiple accounts.

(c)-(1)A financial institution may combine the required disclosures into a single statement for a consumer who holds more than one account at the institution.
