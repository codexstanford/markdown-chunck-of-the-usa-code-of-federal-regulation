
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 267 - PROCEDURES FOR DEBT COLLECTION
##### ยง 267.5 Salary offset.
###### Applicability.

(a)(1) This section covers government-wide collection of a delinquent debt by administrative offset under 5 U.S.C. 5514 from salary payments of federal government employees other than current Board employees.

(2) This section does not apply where an employee consents to the recovery of a debt from his or her federal government salary.
