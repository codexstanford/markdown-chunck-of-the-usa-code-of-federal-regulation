### PART 1030-TRUTH IN SAVINGS (REGULATION DD)
#### Supplement I to Part 1030-Official Interpretations
##### Length of the period.

2.Institutions must disclose the length of both the interest calculation period and the statement period. For example, a statement could disclose a statement period of April 16 through May 15 and further state that "the interest earned and the annual percentage yield earned are based on your average daily balance for the period April 1 through April 30."
