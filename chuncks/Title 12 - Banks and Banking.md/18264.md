####### Controlled subsidiary

means, with respect to a company or a Board-regulated institution, a consolidated subsidiary or a company that otherwise meets the definition of "subsidiary" in section 2(d) of the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956 (12 U.S.C. 1841(d)).
