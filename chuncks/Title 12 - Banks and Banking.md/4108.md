
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 34 - REAL ESTATE LENDING AND APPRAISALS
#### Subpart H - Appraisal Management Company Minimum Requirements
##### ยง 34.214 Ownership limitations for State-registered appraisal management companies.
###### Good moral character of owners.

(b)An AMC shall not be registered by a State if any person that owns more than 10 percent of the AMC-

(1) Is determined by the State appraiser certifying and licensing agency not to have good moral character; or

(2) Fails to submit to a background investigation carried out by the State appraiser certifying and licensing agency.
