
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1211 - PROCEDURES
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1211.1 Definitions.
####### Non-Objection Letter

means a written statement issued to a regulated entity or the Office of Finance providing that FHFA does not object to a proposed transaction or activity.
