
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1208 - DEBT COLLECTION
##### Subpart B - Salary Offset
###### § 1208.20 Authority and scope.
####### Authority.

(a)FHFA may collect debts owed by employees to the Federal Government by means of salary offset under the authority of 5 U.S.C. 5514; 5 CFR part 550, subpart K; and this subpart B.
