
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 239 - MUTUAL HOLDING COMPANIES (REGULATION MM)
##### Appendix A to Part 239 - Mutual Holding Company Model Charter
###### pro rata

All holders of accounts of the Association shall be entitled to equal distribution of the assets of the Mutual Holding Company,to the value of their accounts in the Association, in the event of voluntary or involuntary liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Mutual Holding Company.
