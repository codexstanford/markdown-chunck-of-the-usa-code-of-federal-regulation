
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1223 - MINORITY AND WOMEN INCLUSION
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1223.2 Policy, purpose, and scope.
####### Scope.

(c)This part applies to each regulated entity's development, implementation, and adherence to diversity, inclusion, and non-discrimination policies, practices, and principles, including opportunities to award contracts for goods and/or services.
