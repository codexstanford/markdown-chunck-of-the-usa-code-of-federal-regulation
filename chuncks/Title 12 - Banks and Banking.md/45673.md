
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart B - Capital Requirements and Buffers
###### § 1240.10 Capital requirements.
####### Core capital.

(e)An Enterprise must maintain core capital not less than the amount equal to 2.5 percent of adjusted total assets.
