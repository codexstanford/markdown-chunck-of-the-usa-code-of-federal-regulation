
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1013 - CONSUMER LEASING (REGULATION M)
#### § 1013.4 Content of disclosures.
##### Penalties and other charges for delinquency.

(q)The amount or the method of determining the amount of any penalty or other charge for delinquency, default, or late payments, which must be reasonable.
