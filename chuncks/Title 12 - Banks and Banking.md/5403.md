
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 51 - RECEIVERSHIPS FOR UNINSURED NATIONAL BANKS
#### ยง 51.7 Powers and duties of receiver; disposition of fiduciary and custodial accounts.
##### Disposition of fiduciary and custodial accounts.

(b)The receiver for an uninsured bank closes the bank's fiduciary and custodial appointments and accounts or transfers some or all of such accounts to successor fiduciaries and custodians, in accordance with 12 CFR 9.16, and other applicable Federal law.
