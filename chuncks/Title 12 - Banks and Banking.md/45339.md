
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1235 - RECORD RETENTION FOR REGULATED ENTITIES AND OFFICE OF FINANCE
##### § 1235.5 Record hold.
###### Notification by FHFA.

(a)In the event that FHFA is requiring a record hold, FHFA shall notify the chief executive officer of the regulated entity or the Office of Finance. Regulated entities and the Office of Finance must have a written policy for handling notice of a record hold.
