
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 22 - LOANS IN AREAS HAVING SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARDS
#### ยง 22.9 Notice of special flood hazards and availability of Federal disaster relief assistance.
##### Notice requirement.

(a)When a national bank or Federal savings association makes, increases, extends, or renews a loan secured by a building or a mobile home located or to be located in a special flood hazard area, the bank or savings association shall mail or deliver a written notice to the borrower and to the servicer in all cases whether or not flood insurance is available under the Act for the collateral securing the loan.
