
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1229 - CAPITAL CLASSIFICATIONS AND PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
##### Subpart A - Federal Home Loan Banks
###### § 1229.2 Determination of a Bank's capital classification.
####### Notification to a Bank.

(b)Before finalizing any action to classify a Bank under this section, the Director shall provide a Bank written notice describing the proposed action and an opportunity to submit information that the Bank considers relevant to the proposed action in accordance with § 1229.12 of this subpart.
