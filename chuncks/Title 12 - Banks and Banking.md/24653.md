
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 345.43 Content and availability of public file.
####### Banks with less than satisfactory ratings.

(5)A bank that received a less than satisfactory rating during its most recent examination shall include in its public file a description of its current efforts to improve its performance in helping to meet the credit needs of its entire community. The bank shall update the description quarterly.
