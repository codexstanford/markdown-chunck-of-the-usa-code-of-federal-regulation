
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury

#### Subpart A - Capital Categories
##### ยง 6.2 Definitions.
###### Tangible equity

means the amount of tier 1 capital, as calculated in accordance with subpart B of part 3 of this chapter, plus the amount of outstanding perpetual preferred stock (including related surplus) not included in tier 1 capital.
