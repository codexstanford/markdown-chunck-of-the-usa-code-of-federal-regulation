
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1240.2 Definitions.
####### Clean-up call

means a contractual provision that permits an originating Enterprise or servicer to call securitization exposures before their stated maturity or call date.
