
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1270 - LIABILITIES
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1270.1 Definitions.
####### Participant's Securities Account

means an account in the name of a Participant at a Federal Reserve Bank to which Book-entry consolidated obligations held for a Participant are or may be credited.
