
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1217 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
##### § 1217.3 Basis for civil penalties and assessments.
###### Joint and several liability.

(c)A civil penalty or assessment may be imposed jointly and severally if more than one person is determined to be liable.
