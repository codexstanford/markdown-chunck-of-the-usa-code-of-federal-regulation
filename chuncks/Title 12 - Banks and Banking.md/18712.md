
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 252.12 Definitions.
####### Severely adverse scenario

means a set of conditions that affect the U.S. economy or the financial condition of a state member bank and that overall are significantly more severe than those associated with the baseline scenario and may include trading or other additional components.
