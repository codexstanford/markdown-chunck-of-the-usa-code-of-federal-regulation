
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1222 - APPRAISALS
##### Subpart B - Appraisal Management Company Minimum Requirements
###### § 1222.20 Authority, purpose, and scope.
####### Purpose.

(b)The purpose of this subpart is to implement sections 1109, 1117, 1121, and 1124 of FIRREA Title XI, 12 U.S.C. 3338, 3346, 3350, and 3353.
