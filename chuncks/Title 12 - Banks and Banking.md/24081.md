### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 332 - PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION
##### Appendix A to Part 332 - Model Privacy Form
###### Logo.

(b)A financial institution may include a corporate logo on any page of the notice, so long as it does not interfere with the readability of the model form or the space constraints of each page.
