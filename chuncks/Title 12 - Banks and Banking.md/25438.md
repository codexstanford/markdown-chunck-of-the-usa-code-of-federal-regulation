
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation

##### Subpart D - Compliance Program Requirement; Violations

####### Rebuttal of presumption.

(2)If upon examination or audit, the FDIC determines that the banking entity has engaged in proprietary trading or covered fund activities that are otherwise prohibited under subpart B or subpart C of this part, the FDIC may require the banking entity to be treated under this part as if it did not have limited trading assets and liabilities.
