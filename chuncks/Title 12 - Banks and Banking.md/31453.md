
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 707 - TRUTH IN SAVINGS
##### Appendix C to Part 707 - Official Staff Interpretations
###### Free for limited time.

7.If an account (or a specific account service) is free only for a limited period of time-for example, for one year following the account opening-the account (or service) may be advertised as free as long as the time period is stated.
