
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### PART 226-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
##### Appendix K to Part 226 - Total Annual Loan Cost Rate Computations for Reverse Mortgage Transactions
###### Assumption for closing costs.

(11)In calculating the total annual loan cost rate, creditors shall assume all closing and other consumer costs are financed by the creditor.
