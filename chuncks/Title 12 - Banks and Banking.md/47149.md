
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1281 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK HOUSING GOALS
##### Subpart B - Housing Goals
###### § 1281.13 Special counting requirements.
####### Non-conventional mortgages.

(4)The purchase of a non-conventional single-family mortgage shall be treated as a mortgage purchase for purposes of the housing goals only if the mortgage was acquired from a community-based AMA user.
