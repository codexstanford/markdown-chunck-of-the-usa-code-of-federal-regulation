
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1010 - LAND REGISTRATION (REGULATION J)
#### Subpart B - Reporting Requirements
##### § 1010.114 Recreational facilities.
###### Financial assurance of completion.,i.e.,

(5)If the construction of the facility is not complete, state whether there is any financial assurance of completion. If none, state "none." If such exists, state the type of assurance (bond, escrow, or trust). If no documentation for such assurance has been provided in § 1010.214 of the Statement of Record, then do not indicate such assurance on the chart, but in place of such assurance on the chart state "none."
