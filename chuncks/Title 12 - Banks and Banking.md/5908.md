
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 161 - DEFINITIONS FOR REGULATIONS AFFECTING ALL SAVINGS ASSOCIATIONS
#### ยง 161.33 Note account.
##### note account

The termmeans a note, subject to the right of immediate call, evidencing funds held by depositories electing the note option under applicable United States Treasury Department regulations. Note accounts are not savings accounts or savings deposits.
