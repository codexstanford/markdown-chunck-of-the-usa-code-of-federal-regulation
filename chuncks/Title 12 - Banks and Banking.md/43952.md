
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1201-GENERAL DEFINITIONS APPLYING TO ALL FEDERAL HOUSING FINANCE AGENCY REGULATIONS
##### § 1201.1 Definitions.
###### Resolution Funding Corporation or REFCORP

means the Resolution Funding Corporation established by section 21B of the Bank Act (12 U.S.C. 1441b).
