
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1237 - CONSERVATORSHIP AND RECEIVERSHIP
##### Subpart B - Claims
###### § 1237.9 Priority of expenses and unsecured claims.
####### Priority determined at default.

(c)The receiver will determine priority based on a claim's status at the time of default, such default having occurred at the time of entry into the receivership, or if a conservatorship immediately preceded the receivership, at the time of entry into the conservatorship provided the claim then existed.
