
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart F - Risk-weighted Assets - Market Risk
###### § 1240.202 Definitions.
####### Market risk

means the risk of loss on a position that could result from movements in market prices, including spread risk.
