### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)

##### Nonproprietary terminal in network.

1.An institution need not reflect on the periodic statement the street addresses, identification codes, or terminal numbers for transfers initiated in a shared or interchange system at a terminal operated by an institution other than the account-holding institution.
