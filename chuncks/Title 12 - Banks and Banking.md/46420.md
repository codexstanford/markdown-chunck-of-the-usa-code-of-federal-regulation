
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1263 - MEMBERS OF THE BANKS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1263.1 Definitions.
####### CDFI credit union

means a State-chartered credit union that does not have Federal share insurance and that has been certified as a CDFI by the CDFI Fund.
