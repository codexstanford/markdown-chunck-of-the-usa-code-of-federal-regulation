
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1254 - VALIDATION AND APPROVAL OF CREDIT SCORE MODELS
##### § 1254.2 Definitions.
###### Person

means an individual, sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, unincorporated association, trust, joint venture, pool, syndicate, organization, or other legal entity.
