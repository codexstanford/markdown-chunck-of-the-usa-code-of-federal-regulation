
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Payments that do not pay principal.,i.e.,

5.Although the label of the disclosure required by § 1026.37(i)(5) is "Principal and Interest Payments," and the section refers to periodic principal and interest payments, it includes a scheduled periodic payment that only covers some or all of the interest that is due and not any principal (an interest only or negatively amortizing payment).
