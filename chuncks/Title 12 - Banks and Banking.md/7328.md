
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 205-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
##### Supplement I to Part 205-Official Staff Interpretations
###### Alternative procedure for processing a stop-payment request.

3.If an institution does not have the capability to block a preauthorized debit from being posted to the consumer's account-as in the case of a preauthorized debit made through a debit card network or other system, for example-the institution may instead comply with the stop-payment requirements by using a third party to block the transfer(s), as long as the consumer's account is not debited for the payment.
