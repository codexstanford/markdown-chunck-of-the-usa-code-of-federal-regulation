
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1203 - EQUAL ACCESS TO JUSTICE ACT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1203.2 Definitions.
####### Final disposition date

means the date on which a decision or order disposing of the merits of the adversary adjudication or any other complete resolution of the adversary adjudication, such as a settlement or voluntary dismissal, becomes final and unappealable, both within the agency and to the courts.
