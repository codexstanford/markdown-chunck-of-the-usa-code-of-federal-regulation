
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1268 - ACQUIRED MEMBER ASSETS
##### § 1268.8 Administrative transactions and agreements between Banks.
###### Termination of agreements.

(b)Any agreement made between two or more Banks in connection with the administration of any AMA program may be terminated by any party after a reasonable notice period.
