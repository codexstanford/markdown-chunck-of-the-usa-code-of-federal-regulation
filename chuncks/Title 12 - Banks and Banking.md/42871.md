
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1070-DISCLOSURE OF RECORDS AND INFORMATION
#### Subpart A-General Provisions and Definitions
##### § 1070.2 General definitions.
###### General Counsel

(m)means the General Counsel of the CFPB or any CFPB employee to whom the General Counsel has delegated authority to act under this part.
