
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1271 - MISCELLANEOUS FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK OPERATIONS AND AUTHORITIES
##### Subpart A - Collection, Settlement, and Processing of Payment Instruments
###### § 1271.1 Definitions.
####### Account processing

includes charging, crediting, and settling of member or eligible institution accounts, excluding individual customer accounts.
