
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1282 - ENTERPRISE HOUSING GOALS AND MISSION
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1282.1 Definitions.
####### Minority census tract

means a census tract that has a minority population of at least 30 percent and a median income of less than 100 percent of the area median income.
