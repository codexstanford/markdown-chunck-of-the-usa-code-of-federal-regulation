
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Amount of credit extended under a reverse mortgage.

10.For closed-end reverse mortgage loans, the "amount of credit extended" for purposes of § 1026.36(d)(1) means either:

i. The maximum proceeds available to the consumer under the loan; or

ii. The maximum claim amount as defined in 24 CFR 206.3 if the mortgage is subject to 24 CFR part 206, or the appraised value of the property, as determined by the appraisal used in underwriting the loan, if the mortgage is not subject to 24 CFR part 206.
