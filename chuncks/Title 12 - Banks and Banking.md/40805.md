
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### No funds for borrower.,1

1.When the down payment and other funds from the borrower is determined in accordance with § 1026.37(h)(1)(iii)(A)(), the amount disclosed under § 1026.37(h)(1)(v) as funds for the borrower is $0.
