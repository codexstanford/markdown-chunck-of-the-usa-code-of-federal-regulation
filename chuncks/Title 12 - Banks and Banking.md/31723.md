
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 711 - MANAGEMENT OFFICIAL INTERLOCKS
##### ยง 711.1 Authority, purpose, and scope.
###### Purpose.

(b)The purpose of the Interlocks Act and this part is to foster competition by generally prohibiting a management official from serving two nonaffiliated depository organizations in situations where the management interlock likely would have an anticompetitive effect.
