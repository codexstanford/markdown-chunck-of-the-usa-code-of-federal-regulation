
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Estimate of closing costs paid before closing.

1.Under § 1026.38(e)(3)(i), the "Loan Estimate" amount for "Closing Costs Subtotal Paid Before Closing" is always shown as "$0," because an estimate of such amount is not disclosed on the Loan Estimate.
