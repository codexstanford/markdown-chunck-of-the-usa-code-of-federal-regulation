
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1281 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK HOUSING GOALS
##### Subpart B - Housing Goals
###### § 1281.15 Housing plans.
####### Notice of approval and disapproval.

(2)The Director shall provide written notice to a Bank submitting a housing plan of the approval or disapproval of the plan, which shall include the reasons for any disapproval of the plan, and of any extension of the period for approval or disapproval.
