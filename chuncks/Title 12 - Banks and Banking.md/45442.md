
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1239 - RESPONSIBILITIES OF BOARDS OF DIRECTORS, CORPORATE PRACTICES, AND CORPORATE GOVERNANCE
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1239.2 Definitions.
####### Business risk

means the risk of an adverse impact on a regulated entity's profitability resulting from external factors as may occur in both the short and long run.
