
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1213 - OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN
##### § 1213.4 Complaints and appeals from a regulated entity or the Office of Finance.
###### Matters subject to complaint.

(2)A regulated entity or the Office of Finance may submit a complaint regarding any matter relating to the regulation and supervision of a regulated entity or the Office of Finance by FHFA that is not subject to appeal or in litigation, arbitration, or mediation. The Ombudsman may further define what matters are subject to complaint.
