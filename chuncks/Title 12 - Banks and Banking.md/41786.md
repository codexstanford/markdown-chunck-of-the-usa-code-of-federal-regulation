
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Closed account fees.,3,3

6.Section 1026.52(b)(2)(i)(B)() prohibits a card issuer from imposing a fee based on the closure or termination of an account. For example, § 1026.52(b)(2)(i)(B)() prohibits a card issuer from:

i. Imposing a one-time fee to consumers who close their accounts.
