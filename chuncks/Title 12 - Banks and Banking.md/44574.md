
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1209 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart F - Suspension or Removal of an Entity-Affiliated Party Charged With Felony
###### § 1209.103 Recommended and final decisions.
####### Effect of notice or order.

(c)A removal or prohibition by order shall remain in effect until terminated by the Director. A suspension or prohibition by notice remains in effect until the criminal charge is disposed of or until terminated by the Director.
