
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart E - Risk-Weighted Assets - Internal Ratings-Based and Advanced Measurement Approaches
###### § 1240.121 Minimum requirements.
####### Internal operational loss event data.

(A)The Enterprise must have a systematic process for capturing and using internal operational loss event data in its operational risk data and assessment systems.
