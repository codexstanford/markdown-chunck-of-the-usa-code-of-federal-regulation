
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1292 - COMMUNITY INVESTMENT CASH ADVANCE PROGRAMS
##### § 1292.5 Community Investment Cash Advance Programs.
###### Pricing and Availability of advances,Advances to members.

(d)-(1)For CICA programs other than AHP and CIP, a Bank shall price advances to members as provided in § 1266.5 of this chapter, and may price such advances at rates below the price of advances of similar amounts, maturities and terms made pursuant to section 10(a) of the Bank Act. (12 U.S.C. 1430(a)).
