### Subchapter C - Enterprises

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1240.2 Definitions.
####### Qualifying central counterparty (QCCP)

(1)(i) Is a designated financial market utility (FMU) under Title VIII of the Dodd-Frank Act;
