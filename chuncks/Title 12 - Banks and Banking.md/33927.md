
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.10 Preauthorized transfers.
###### Notice by payor.

(2)A financial institution need not provide notice of a transfer if the payor gives the consumer positive notice that the transfer has been initiated.
