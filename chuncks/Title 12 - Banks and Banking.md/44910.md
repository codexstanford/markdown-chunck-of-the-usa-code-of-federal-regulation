
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.2 Definitions.
####### Person

means an individual, sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, unincorporated association, trust, joint venture, pool, syndicate, organization, or other entity.
