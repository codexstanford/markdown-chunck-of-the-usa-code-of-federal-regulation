
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 239.59 Offers and sales of stock.
####### Offering conversion shares.

(b)(1) The mutual holding company may offer to sell the conversion shares if the Board approves the conversion, subject to compliance with requirements of the Securities and Exchange Commission.

(2) The offer may commence at the same time as the proxy solicitation of the members begins.
