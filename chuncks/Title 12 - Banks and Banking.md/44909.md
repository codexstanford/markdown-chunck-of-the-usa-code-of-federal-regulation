
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.2 Definitions.
####### Covered transaction

means a contract, agreement, or financial or business relationship between a regulated entity and a person and any affiliates thereof.
