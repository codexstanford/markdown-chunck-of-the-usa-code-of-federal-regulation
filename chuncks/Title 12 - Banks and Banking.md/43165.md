
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1075-CONSUMER FINANCIAL CIVIL PENALTY FUND RULE
#### § 1075.101 Definitions.
##### Final order

means a consent order or settlement issued by a court or by the Bureau, or an appealable order issued by a court or by the Bureau as to which the time for filing an appeal has expired and no appeals are pending. For purposes of this definition, "appeals" include petitions for reconsideration, review, rehearing, and certiorari.
