
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1204 - PRIVACY ACT IMPLEMENTATION
##### § 1204.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### System of Records Notice

means a notice published in thewhich announces the creation, deletion, or amendment of one or more system of records. System of records notices are also used to identify a system of records' routine uses.
