
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1266 - ADVANCES
##### Subpart A - Advances to Members
###### § 1266.10 Collateral valuation; appraisals.
####### Collateral valuation.

(a)Each Bank shall determine the value of collateral securing the Bank's advances in accordance with the collateral valuation procedures set forth in the Bank's member products policy established pursuant to § 1239.30 of this chapter.
