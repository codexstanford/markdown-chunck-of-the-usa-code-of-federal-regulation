
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Calculation of allowable points and fees.

2.A creditor must determine which category the loan falls into based on the face amount of the note (the "loan amount" as defined in § 1026.43(b)(5)). For categories with a percentage limit, the creditor must apply the allowable points and fees percentage to the "total loan amount," which may be different than the loan amount.
