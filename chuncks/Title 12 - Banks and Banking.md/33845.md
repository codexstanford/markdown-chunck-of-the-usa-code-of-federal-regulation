
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.1 Authority and purpose.
###### Authority.,et seq.,et seq.

(a)The regulation in this part, known as Regulation E, is issued by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) pursuant to the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (15 U.S.C. 1693). The information-collection requirements have been approved by the Office of Management and Budget under 44 U.S.C. 3501and have been assigned OMB No. 3170-0014.
