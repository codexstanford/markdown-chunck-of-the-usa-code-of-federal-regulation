
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 165 - PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
#### ยง 165.8 Procedures for reclassifying a Federal savings association based on criteria other than capital.
##### Response to notice of proposed reclassification.

(3)A Federal savings association may file a written response to a notice of proposed reclassification within the time period set by the OCC. The response should include:

(i) An explanation of why the savings association is not in unsafe or unsound condition or otherwise should not be reclassified; and

(ii) Any other relevant information, mitigating circumstances, documentation, or other evidence in support of the position of the savings association or company regarding the reclassification.
