
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 28 - INTERNATIONAL BANKING ACTIVITIES
#### Subpart B - Federal Branches and Agencies of Foreign Banks
##### ยง 28.12 Approval of a Federal branch or agency.
###### Written notice for an additional intrastate Federal branch or agency.

(2)(i) In a case where a foreign bank seeks to establish intrastate an additional Federal branch or agency, the foreign bank shall provide written notice 30 days in advance of the establishment of the intrastate Federal branch or agency.

(ii) The OCC may waive the 30-day period required under paragraph (e)(2)(i) of this section if immediate action is required. The OCC also may suspend the notice period or require an application if the notification raises significant policy or supervisory concerns.
