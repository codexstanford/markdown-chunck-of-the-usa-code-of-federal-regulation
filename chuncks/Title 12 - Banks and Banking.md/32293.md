
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions

##### Subpart A-Procedures for Appealing Material Supervisory Determinations
###### ยง 746.108 Composition of Supervisory Review Committee.
####### ) Formation and composition of committee pool.

(aThe NCUA Chairman shall select not less than eight members from among senior staff in NCUA's regional and central offices as a Committee pool from which the Committee Chairman may select Committee members. None of the members appointed by the NCUA Chairman shall also serve as a Regional Director, Associate Regional Director, Executive Director, Deputy Executive Director, General Counsel, Director of the Office of Examination and Insurance, or a senior policy advisor or chief of staff to a Board Member.
