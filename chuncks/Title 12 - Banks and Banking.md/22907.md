
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### PART 324 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF FDIC-SUPERVISED INSTITUTIONS
##### Subpart F - Risk-Weighted Assets - Market Risk
###### ยง 324.202 Definitions.
####### Event risk

means the risk of loss on equity or hybrid equity positions as a result of a financial event, such as the announcement or occurrence of a company merger, acquisition, spin-off, or dissolution.
