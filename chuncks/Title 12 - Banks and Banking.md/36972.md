#### Appendix C to Part 1024 - Instructions for Completing Good Faith Estimate (GFE) Form
##### Homeowner's insurance.

Block 11, ""-The loan originator must estimate in this block the total amount of the premiums for any hazard insurance policy and other similar insurance, such as fire or flood insurance that must be purchased at or before settlement to meet the loan originator's requirements. To the extent a loan originator requires that such insurance be part of an escrow account, the amount of the initial escrow deposit must be included in Block 9.
