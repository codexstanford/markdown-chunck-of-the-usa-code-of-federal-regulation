
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1041-PAYDAY, VEHICLE TITLE, AND CERTAIN HIGH-COST INSTALLMENT LOANS
#### Subpart C-Payments
##### § 1041.9 Disclosure of payment transfer attempts.
###### 7,Explanation of interest-only or negatively amortizing payment.

()If applicable, a statement explaining that the payment will not reduce principal, using the applicable phrase "When you make this payment, your principal balance will stay the same and you will not be closer to paying off your loan" or "When you make this payment, your principal balance will increase and you will not be closer to paying off your loan."
