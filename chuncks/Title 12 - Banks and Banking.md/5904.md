
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 161 - DEFINITIONS FOR REGULATIONS AFFECTING ALL SAVINGS ASSOCIATIONS
#### ยง 161.16 Demand accounts.
##### demand accounts

The termmeans non-interest-bearing demand deposits that are subject to check or to withdrawal or transfer on negotiable or transferable order to the savings association and that are permitted to be issued by statute, regulation, or otherwise and are payable on demand.
