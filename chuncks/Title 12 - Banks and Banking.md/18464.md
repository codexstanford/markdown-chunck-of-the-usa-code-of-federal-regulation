
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart K - Net Stable Funding Ratio

(5)(i) An amount equal to 5 percent of the sum of the gross derivative values of the Board-regulated institution that are liabilities, as calculated under paragraph (b)(5)(ii) of this section, for each of the Board-regulated institution's derivative transactions not subject to a qualifying master netting agreement and each of its QMNA netting sets, multiplied by an RSF factor of 100 percent;
