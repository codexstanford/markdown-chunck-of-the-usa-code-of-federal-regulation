
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1024-REAL ESTATE SETTLEMENT PROCEDURES ACT (REGULATION X)
#### Subpart B-Mortgage Settlement and Escrow Accounts
##### § 1024.15 Affiliated business arrangements.
###### Direct ownership

means the holding of legal title to an interest in a provider of settlement service except where title is being held for the beneficial owner.
