
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Reference to disclosure of total closing costs.

1.Under § 1026.38(e)(2)(i), the amount disclosed is labeled "Total Closing Costs," and such label is accompanied by a reference to the disclosure of "Total Closing Costs" under § 1026.38(h)(1). This reference may take the form, for example, of a cross-reference in parenthesis to the row on the table disclosed under § 1026.38(h) that includes the itemized amount for "Total Closing Costs," as shown on form H-25 of appendix H to this part.
