
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1290 - COMMUNITY SUPPORT REQUIREMENTS
##### § 1290.1 Definitions.
###### Restriction on access to long-term advances

means a member may not borrow long-term advances or renew any maturing advance for a term to maturity greater than one year.
