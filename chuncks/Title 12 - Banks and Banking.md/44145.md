
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1206 - ASSESSMENTS
##### § 1206.2 Definitions.
###### Enterprise

means the Federal National Mortgage Association or the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation; and "Enterprises" means, collectively, the Federal National Mortgage Association and the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation.
