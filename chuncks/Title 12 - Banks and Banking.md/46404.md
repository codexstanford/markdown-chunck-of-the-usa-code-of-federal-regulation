
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1261 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK DIRECTORS
##### Subpart C - Federal Home Loan Bank Directors' Compensation and Expenses
###### § 1261.21 General.
####### Reporting-,Following calendar year.

(b)(1)By December 31 of each calendar year, each Bank shall report to the Director the compensation it anticipates paying to its directors for the following calendar year.
