### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### General rule.

1.Until the rescission period has expired and the creditor is reasonably satisfied that the consumer has not rescinded, the creditor must not, either directly or through a third party:

i. Disburse loan proceeds to the consumer.

ii. Begin performing services for the consumer.

iii. Deliver materials to the consumer.
