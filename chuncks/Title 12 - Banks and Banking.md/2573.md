
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### ยง 9.17 Surrender or revocation of fiduciary powers.
###### Revocation.

(b)If the OCC determines that a national bank has unlawfully or unsoundly exercised, or has failed for a period of five consecutive years to exercise its fiduciary powers, the Comptroller may, in accordance with the provisions of 12 U.S.C. 92a(k), revoke the bank's fiduciary powers.
