
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1271 - MISCELLANEOUS FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK OPERATIONS AND AUTHORITIES
##### Subpart A - Collection, Settlement, and Processing of Payment Instruments
###### § 1271.1 Definitions.
####### Issuance of forms

means the designation and distribution of standardized forms for use in collection, processing, and settlement services.
