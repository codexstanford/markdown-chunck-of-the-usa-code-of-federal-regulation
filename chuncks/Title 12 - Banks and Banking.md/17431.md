
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 242 - DEFINITIONS RELATING TO TITLE I OF THE DODD-FRANK ACT (REGULATION PP)
##### Appendix A to Part 242 - Financial Activities for Purposes of Title I of the Dodd-Frank Act
###### Underwriting and dealing in government obligations and money market instruments.

(i)Underwriting and dealing in obligations of the United States, general obligations of states and their political subdivisions, and other obligations that state member banks of the Federal Reserve System may be authorized to underwrite and deal in under 12 U.S.C. 24 and 335, including banker's acceptances and certificates of deposit.
