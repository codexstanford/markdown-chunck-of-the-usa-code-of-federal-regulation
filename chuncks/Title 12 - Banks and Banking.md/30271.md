
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart B - Alternative Prompt Corrective Action for New Credit Unions
###### ยง 702.207 Consideration of Subordinated Debt and Grandfathered Secondary Capital for new credit unions.
####### Restrictions on payments on Subordinated Debt.

(e)Beginning 60 days after the effective date of a new credit union being classified by the NCUA as "uncapitalized", the new credit union shall not pay principal of or interest on its Subordinated Debt, except that unpaid interest shall continue to accrue under the terms of the related Subordinated Debt Note, to the extent permitted by law.
