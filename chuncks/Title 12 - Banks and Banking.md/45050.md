
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1231 - GOLDEN PARACHUTE AND INDEMNIFICATION PAYMENTS
##### § 1231.2 Definitions.
###### Agreement

means, with respect to a golden parachute payment, any plan, contract, arrangement, or other statement setting forth conditions for any payment by a regulated entity or the OF to an affiliated party.
