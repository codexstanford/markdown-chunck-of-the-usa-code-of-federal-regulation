
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1282 - ENTERPRISE HOUSING GOALS AND MISSION
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1282.1 Definitions.
####### Agricultural worker,

for purposes of subpart C of this part, means any person that meets the definition of an agricultural worker under a federal, state, tribal or local program.
