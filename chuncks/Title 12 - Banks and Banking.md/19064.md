
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 252 - ENHANCED PRUDENTIAL STANDARDS (REGULATION YY)
##### Subpart I - Requirements for Qualified Financial Contracts of Global Systemically Important Banking Organizations
###### ยง 252.86 Foreign bank multi-branch master agreements.
####### Definition of foreign bank multi-branch master agreements.

(b)A foreign bank multi-branch master agreement means a master agreement that permits a U.S. branch or U.S. agency and another place of business of a foreign bank that is outside the United States to enter transactions under the agreement.
