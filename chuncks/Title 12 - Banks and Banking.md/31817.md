
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 715 - SUPERVISORY COMMITTEE AUDITS AND VERIFICATIONS
##### ยง 715.3 General responsibilities of the Supervisory Committee.
###### Basic.

(a)The supervisory committee is responsible for ensuring that the board of directors and management of the credit union-

(1) Meet required financial reporting objectives and

(2) Establish practices and procedures sufficient to safeguard members' assets.
