### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1281 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK HOUSING GOALS
##### Subpart C - Reporting Requirements
###### § 1281.20 Reporting requirements.
####### Reporting for small member participation housing goal.
