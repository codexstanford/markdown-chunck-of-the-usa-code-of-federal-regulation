
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1024-REAL ESTATE SETTLEMENT PROCEDURES ACT (REGULATION X)
#### Subpart C-Mortgage Servicing
##### § 1024.31 Definitions.
###### Service provider

means any party retained by a servicer that interacts with a borrower or provides a service to the servicer for which a borrower may incur a fee.
