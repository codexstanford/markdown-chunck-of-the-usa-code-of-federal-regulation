
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 213 - CONSUMER LEASING (REGULATION M)
##### Supplement I to Part 213-Official Staff Commentary to Regulation M
###### Content of disclosures for month-to-month extensions.

2.The disclosures for a lease extended on a month-to-month basis for more than six months should reflect the month-to-month nature of the transaction.
