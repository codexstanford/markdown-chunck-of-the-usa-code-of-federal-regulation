
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart H-Notice of Addition or Change of Directors and Senior Executive Officers
###### ยง 225.71 Definitions.
####### Senior executive officer,Senior executive officer

(c)means a person who holds the title or, without regard to title, salary, or compensation, performs the function of one or more of the following positions: president, chief executive officer, chief operating officer, chief financial officer, chief lending officer, or chief investment officer.also includes any other person identified by the Board or Reserve Bank, whether or not hired as an employee, with significant influence over, or who participates in, major policymaking decisions of the regulated institution.
