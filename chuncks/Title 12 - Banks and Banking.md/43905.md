
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1200-ORGANIZATION AND FUNCTIONS
##### § 1200.2 Organization of the Federal Housing Finance Agency.
###### Office of the Ombudsman.

(7)The office is responsible for considering complaints and appeals from the regulated entities, the OF and any person that has a business relationship with a regulated entity or the OF concerning any matter relating to FHFA's regulation and supervision of that entity or the OF.
