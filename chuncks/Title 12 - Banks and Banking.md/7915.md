
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System

##### Subpart F-Miscellaneous Requirements
###### ยง 208.62 Suspicious activity reports.
####### Violations aggregating $25,000 or more regardless of a potential suspect.

(3)Whenever the member bank detects any known or suspected Federal criminal violation, or pattern of criminal violations, committed or attempted against the bank or involving a transaction or transactions conducted through the bank and involving or aggregating $25,000 or more in funds or other assets, where the bank believes that it was either an actual or potential victim of a criminal violation, or series of criminal violations, or that the bank was used to facilitate a criminal transaction, even though there is no substantial basis for identifying a possible suspect or group of suspects.
