
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 1 - INVESTMENT SECURITIES
#### ยง 1.4 Calculation of limits.
##### Calculation of Type III and Type V securities holdings,General.

(d)-(1)In calculating the amount of its investment in Type III or Type V securities issued by any one obligor, a bank shall aggregate:

(i) Obligations issued by obligors that are related directly or indirectly through common control; and

(ii) Securities that are credit enhanced by the same entity.
