### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Statement of increase or decrease.

5.Section 1026.38(e)(1)(iii)(A) requires a statement of whether the loan amount increased or decreased. A creditor complies with this requirement by disclosing, "This amount increased" or "This amount decreased" with the words "increase" and "decrease" in boldface font.
