
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1229 - CAPITAL CLASSIFICATIONS AND PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
##### Subpart A - Federal Home Loan Banks
###### § 1229.1 Definitions.
####### Total capital

means the sum of the Bank's permanent capital, the amount paid-in for its Class A stock, the amount of any general allowances for losses, and the amount of any other instruments identified in a Bank's capital plan that the Director has determined to be available to absorb losses incurred by such Bank.
