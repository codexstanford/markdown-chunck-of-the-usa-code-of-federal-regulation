
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

### PART 32 - LENDING LIMITS
#### ยง 32.2 Definitions.
##### Readily marketable collateral

(v)means financial instruments and bullion that are salable under ordinary market conditions with reasonable promptness at a fair market value determined by quotations based upon actual transactions on an auction or similarly available daily bid and ask price market.
