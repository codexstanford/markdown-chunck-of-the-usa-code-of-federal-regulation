
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 703.2 Definitions.
####### Residual interest

means the remainder cash flows from collateralized mortgage obligations/real estate mortgage investment conduits (CMOs/REMICs), or other mortgage-backed security transaction, after payments due bondholders and trust administrative expenses have been satisfied.
