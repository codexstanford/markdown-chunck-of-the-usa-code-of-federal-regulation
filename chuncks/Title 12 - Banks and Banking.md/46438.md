
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1263 - MEMBERS OF THE BANKS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1263.1 Definitions.
####### Gross revenues

means, in the case of a CDFI applicant, total revenues received from all sources, including grants and other donor contributions and earnings from operations.
