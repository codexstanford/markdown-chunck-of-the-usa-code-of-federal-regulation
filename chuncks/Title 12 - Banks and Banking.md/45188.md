###### § 1234.7 Commercial mortgage-backed securities.
####### Number of third-party purchasers.,pari passu

(1)At any time, there are no more than two third-party purchasers of an eligible horizontal residual interest. If there are two third-party purchasers, each third-party purchaser's interest must bewith the other third-party purchaser's interest.
