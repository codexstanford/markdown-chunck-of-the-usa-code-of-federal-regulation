
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XI—Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council
### PART 1102-APPRAISER REGULATION
#### Subpart A-Temporary Waiver Requests

The Order shall be published in thewhich in the case of an Order approving a waiver, shall only be published after FFIEC approval of the waiver (paragraph (b) of this section).
