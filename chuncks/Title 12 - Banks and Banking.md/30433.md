
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 702 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY
##### Subpart D - Subordinated Debt, Grandfathered Secondary Capital, and Regulatory Capital
###### Appendix A to Subpart D of Part 702 - Disclosure and Acknowledgement Form
####### Subordination of claims.

7.In the event of liquidation of [name of credit union], the funds committed to the secondary capital account will be subordinate to all other claims on the assets of the credit union, including claims of member shareholders, creditors and the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund.
