
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1261 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK DIRECTORS
##### Subpart B - Federal Home Loan Bank Boards of Directors: Eligibility and Elections
###### § 1261.5 Director eligibility.
####### Eligibility requirements for independent directors.

(c)Each independent director, and each nominee to an independent directorship, shall be:

(1) A citizen of the United States; and

(2) A bona fide resident of the district in which the Bank is located.
