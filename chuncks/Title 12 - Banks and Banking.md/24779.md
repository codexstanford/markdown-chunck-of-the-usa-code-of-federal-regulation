
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 346 - DISCLOSURE AND REPORTING OF CRA-RELATED AGREEMENTS
##### § 346.9 Compliance provisions.
###### Inadvertent or de minimis errors.

(d)Inadvertent or de minimis errors in annual reports or other documents filed with the FDIC under §§ 346.6 or 346.7 will not subject the reporting party to any penalty.
