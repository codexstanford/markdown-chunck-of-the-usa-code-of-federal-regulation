
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1266 - ADVANCES
##### Subpart A - Advances to Members
###### § 1266.2 Authorization and application for advances; obligation to repay advances.
####### Status of secured lending.

(e)All secured transactions, regardless of the form of the transaction, for money borrowed from a Bank by a member of any Bank shall be considered an advance subject to the requirements of this part.
