
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VI - Farm Credit Administration
### Subchapter B - Farm Credit System
#### PART 628 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF SYSTEM INSTITUTIONS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 628.2 Definitions.
####### Patronage payment

means a cash declaration or equity allocation to member-borrowers that pursuant to Internal Revenue Code section 1381(a) is based on a System institution's net income and allocated to borrowers based on business conducted with the institution. Patronage payments may be paid as cash, allocated equity (stock or surplus), or a combination of cash and allocated equity.
