
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1234 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart B - Credit Risk Retention
###### § 1234.9 Open market CLOs.
####### Secondary market transaction

means a purchase of a senior, secured syndicated loan not in connection with an initial loan syndication transaction but in the secondary market.
