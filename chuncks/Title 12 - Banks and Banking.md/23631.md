
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 329.3 Definitions.
####### Foreign withdrawable reserves

means a FDIC-supervised institution's balances held by or on behalf of the FDIC-supervised institution at a foreign central bank that are not subject to restrictions on the FDIC-supervised institution's ability to use the reserves.
