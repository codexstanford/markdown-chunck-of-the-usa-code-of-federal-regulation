
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Downpayment.,downpayment

1.i. The dollar amount of a downpayment or a statement of the downpayment as a percentage of the price requires further information. By virtue of the definition ofin § 1026.2, this triggering term is limited to credit sale transactions. It includes such statements as:

A. Only 5% down.

B. As low as $100 down.

C. Total move-in costs of $800.
