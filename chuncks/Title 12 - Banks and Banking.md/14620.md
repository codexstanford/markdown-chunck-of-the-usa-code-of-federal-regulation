###### Meaning of mortgage broker.

2.For purposes of ยง 226.36, with respect to a particular transaction, the term "mortgage broker" refers to a loan originator who is not an employee of the creditor. Accordingly, the term "mortgage broker" includes companies that engage in the activities described in ยง 226.36(a) and also includes employees of such companies that engage in these activities. Section 226.36(d) prohibits certain payments to a loan originator.
