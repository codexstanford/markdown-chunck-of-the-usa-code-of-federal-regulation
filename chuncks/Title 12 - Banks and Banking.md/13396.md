
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 226-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
##### Supplement I to Part 226-Official Staff Interpretations
###### Determination of annual percentage rate.

1.If the creditor adjusts its index through the addition of a margin, the disclosure might read, "Your annual percentage rate is based on the index plus a margin." The creditor is not required to disclose a specific value for the margin.
