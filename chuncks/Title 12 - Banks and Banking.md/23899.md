
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 330 - DEPOSIT INSURANCE COVERAGE
##### ยง 330.14 Retirement and other employee benefit plan accounts.
###### Determination of interests,Defined contribution plans.

(c)-(1)The value of an employee's non-contingent interest in a defined contribution plan shall be deemed to be the employee's account balance as of the date of default of the insured depository institution, regardless of whether said amount was derived, in whole or in part, from contributions of the employee and/or the employer to the account.
