
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System

##### Subpart C - High-Quality Liquid Assets
###### ยง 249.22 Requirements for eligible high-quality liquid assets.
####### Maintenance of U.S. eligible HQLA.

(c)A Board-regulated institution is generally expected to maintain as eligible HQLA an amount and type of eligible HQLA in the United States that is sufficient to meet its total net cash outflow amount in the United States under subpart D of this part.
