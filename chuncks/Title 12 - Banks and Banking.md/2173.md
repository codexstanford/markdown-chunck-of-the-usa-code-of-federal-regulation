
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 6 - PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
#### Subpart A - Capital Categories
##### ยง 6.1 Authority, purpose, scope, other supervisory authority, disclosure of capital categories, and transition procedures.
###### Scope.

(c)This subpart implements the provisions of section 38 of the FDI Act as they apply to insured national banks, insured Federal branches, and insured Federal savings associations. Certain of these provisions also apply to officers, directors, and employees of these insured institutions. Other provisions apply to any company that controls an insured national bank, insured Federal branch, or insured Federal savings association and to the affiliates of an insured national bank, insured Federal branch, or insured Federal savings association.
