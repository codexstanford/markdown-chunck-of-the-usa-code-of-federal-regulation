
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy

##### Subpart A - Activities of Insured State Banks
###### ยง 362.2 Definitions.
####### National securities exchange

(l)means a securities exchange that is registered as a national securities exchange by the Securities and Exchange Commission pursuant to section 6 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78f) and the National Market System, i.e., the top tier of the National Association of Securities Dealers Automated Quotation System.
