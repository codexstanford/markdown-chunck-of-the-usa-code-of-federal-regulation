
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1209 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart B - Enforcement Proceedings Under Sections 1371 Through 1379D of the Safety and Soundness Act
###### § 1209.8 Removal and prohibition proceedings.
####### Applicability to business entities.

(3)Under section 1377(f) of the Safety and Soundness Act (12 U.S.C. 4636a(f)), this remedy applies only to a person who is an individual, unless the Director specifically finds that it should apply to a corporation, firm, or other business entity.
