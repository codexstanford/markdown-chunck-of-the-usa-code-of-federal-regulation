
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1261 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK DIRECTORS
##### Subpart C - Federal Home Loan Bank Directors' Compensation and Expenses
###### § 1261.24 Board meetings.
####### Number of meetings.

(a)The board of directors of each Bank shall hold as many meetings each year as necessary and appropriate to carry out its fiduciary responsibilities with respect to the effective oversight of Bank management and such other duties and obligations as may be imposed by applicable laws, provided the board of directors of a Bank must hold a minimum of six in-person meetings in any year.
