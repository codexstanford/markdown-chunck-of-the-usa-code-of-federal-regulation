
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 244 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION (REGULATION RR)
##### Subpart A - Authority, Purpose, Scope and Definitions
###### ยง 244.2 Definitions.
####### Eligible vertical interest

means, with respect to any securitization transaction, a single vertical security or an interest in each class of ABS interests in the issuing entity issued as part of the securitization transaction that constitutes the same proportion of each such class.
