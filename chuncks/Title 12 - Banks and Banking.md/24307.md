
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 337-UNSAFE AND UNSOUND BANKING PRACTICES
##### ยง 337.7 Interest rate restrictions.
###### Definitions,National rate.

(a)-(1)The weighted average of rates paid by all insured depository institutions and credit unions on a given deposit product, for which data are available, where the weights are each institution's market share of domestic deposits.
