#### Appendix B to Part 1024 - Illustrations of Requirements of RESPA
##### Facts:

10.A is a real estate broker who refers business to its affiliate title company B. A makes all required written disclosures to the homebuyer of the arrangement and estimated charges and the homebuyer is not required to use B.
