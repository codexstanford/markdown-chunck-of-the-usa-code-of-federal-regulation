
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1238 - STRESS TESTING OF REGULATED ENTITIES
##### § 1238.7 Publication of results by regulated entities.
###### Public disclosure of results required for stress tests of the Enterprises.

(a)The Enterprises must disclose publicly a summary of the stress test results for the severely adverse scenario not earlier than August 1 and not later than August 15 of each year. The summary may be published on the Enterprise's website or in any other form that is reasonably accessible to the public.
