
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1263 - MEMBERS OF THE BANKS
##### Subpart B - Membership Application Process
###### § 1263.5 Appeals.
####### Record for appeal.,Copy of membership file.

(b)-(1)Upon receiving a copy of an appeal, the Bank whose action has been appealed (appellee Bank) shall provide FHFA with a copy of the applicant's complete membership file. Until FHFA resolves the appeal, the appellee Bank shall supplement the materials provided to FHFA as any new materials are received.
