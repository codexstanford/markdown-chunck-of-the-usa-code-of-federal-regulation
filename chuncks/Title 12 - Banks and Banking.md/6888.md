
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### ยง 204.2 Definitions.
###### United States resident,domestic corporation

(s)means (1) any individual residing (at the time of the transaction) in the United States; (2) any corporation, partnership, association or other entity organized in the United States (); and (3) any branch or office located in the United States of any entity that is not organized in the United States.
