
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1223 - MINORITY AND WOMEN INCLUSION
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1223.1 Definitions.
####### Minority

means any Black (or African) American, Native American (or American Indian), Hispanic (or Latino) American, or Asian American.
