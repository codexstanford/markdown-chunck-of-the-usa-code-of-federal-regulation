### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions

##### ยง 704.12 Permissible services.
###### Payment systems.

(7)Payment systems are any methods used to facilitate the movement of funds for transactional purposes. Payment systems may include Automated Clearing House, wire transfer, item processing and settlement services.
