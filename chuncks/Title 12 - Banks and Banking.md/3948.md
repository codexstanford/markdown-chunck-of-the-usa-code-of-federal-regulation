
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 34 - REAL ESTATE LENDING AND APPRAISALS
#### Subpart C - Appraisals
##### ยง 34.47 Enforcement.
###### et seq.,

Institutions and institution-affiliated parties, including staff appraisers and fee appraisers, may be subject to removal and/or prohibition orders, cease and desist orders, and the imposition of civil money penalties pursuant to the Federal Deposit Insurance Act, 12 U.S.C. 1811as amended, or other applicable law.
