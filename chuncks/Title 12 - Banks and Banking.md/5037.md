#### ยง 47.5 Insolvency proceedings.
##### Definitions relevant to the additional creditor protections for supported QFCs,Stay period. Stay period

(g)-(1)means, with respect to a receivership, insolvency, liquidation, resolution, or similar proceeding, the period of time beginning on the commencement of the proceeding and ending at the later of 5:00 p.m. (eastern time) on the business day following the date of the commencement of the proceeding and 48 hours after the commencement of the proceeding.
