
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### Subpart C - Release of Non-Public OCC Information
##### ยง 4.34 Where to submit a request.
###### Combined requests for non-public and other OCC information.

(b)A person requesting public OCC information and non-public OCC information under this subpart may submit a combined request for both to the address in paragraph (a) of this section. If a requester decides to submit a combined request under this section, the OCC will process the combined request under this subpart and not under subpart B of this part (FOIA).
