
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1010 - LAND REGISTRATION (REGULATION J)
#### Subpart A - General Requirements
##### § 1010.20 Requirements for registering a subdivision - Statement of Record - filing and form.
###### State filings.

(c)A Statement of Record submitted under the provisions of 12 CFR part 1010, subpart C-Certification of Substantially Equivalent State Law, shall consist of the materials designated by the Certification Agreement between the Director and the certified state in which the subdivision is located.
