
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1090-DEFINING LARGER PARTICIPANTS OF CERTAIN CONSUMER FINANCIAL PRODUCT AND SERVICE MARKETS
#### Subpart B-Markets
##### § 1090.107 International Money Transfer Market.
###### International money transfer provider

means any nonbank covered person that provides international money transfers for a consumer, regardless of whether the consumer holds an account with such person.
