
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart D - Risk-Weighted Assets - Standardized Approach
###### § 1240.36 Derivative contracts.
####### Current credit exposure.

(i)The current credit exposure for a single OTC derivative contract is the greater of the fair value of the OTC derivative contract or zero.
