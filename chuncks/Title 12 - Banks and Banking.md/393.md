
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury

#### Subpart C - Definition of Capital
##### ยง 3.22 Regulatory capital adjustments and deductions.
###### Deductions from regulatory capital related to investments in capital instruments or covered debt instruments,Investment in the national bank's or Federal savings association's own capital instruments.

(c)-(1)A national bank or Federal savings association must deduct an investment in the national bank's or Federal savings association's own capital instruments, as follows:

(i) A national bank or Federal savings association must deduct an investment in the national bank's or Federal savings association's own common stock instruments from its common equity tier 1 capital elements to the extent such instruments are not excluded from regulatory capital under ยง 3.20(b)(1);

(ii) A national bank or Federal savings association must deduct an investment in the national bank's or Federal savings association's own additional tier 1 capital instruments from its additional tier 1 capital elements; and

(iii) A national bank or Federal savings association must deduct an investment in the national bank's or Federal savings association's own tier 2 capital instruments from its tier 2 capital elements.
