####### Notices relating to consumer expedited recredit claims,Notice of recredit.

(e)-(1)A bank that recredits a consumer account under paragraph (c) of this section shall send notice to the consumer of the recredit no later than the business day after the banking day on which the bank recredits the consumer account. This notice shall describe-

(i) The amount of the recredit; and

(ii) The date on which the recredited funds will be available for withdrawal.
