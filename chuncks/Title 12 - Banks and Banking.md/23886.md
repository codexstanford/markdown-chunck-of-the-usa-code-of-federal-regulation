##### ยง 330.11 Accounts of a corporation, partnership or unincorporated association.
###### Partnership accounts.

(b)The deposit accounts of a partnership engaged in any "independent activity" (as defined in ยง 330.1(g)) shall be added together and insured up to the SMDIA in the aggregate. Such insurance coverage shall be separate from any insurance provided for individually owned (single ownership) accounts maintained by the individual partners.
