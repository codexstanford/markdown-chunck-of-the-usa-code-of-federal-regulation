#### ยง 161.52 Tax and loan account.
##### tax and loan account

The termmeans an account, the balance of which is subject to the right of immediate withdrawal, established for receipt of payments of Federal taxes and certain United States obligations. Such accounts are not savings accounts or savings deposits.
