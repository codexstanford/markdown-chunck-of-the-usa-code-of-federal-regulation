
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### Safety and soundness.

(6)The business plan or operating plan must demonstrate that the organizing group (and the sponsoring company, if any), is aware of, and understands, applicable depository institution laws and regulations, and safe and sound banking operations and practices.
