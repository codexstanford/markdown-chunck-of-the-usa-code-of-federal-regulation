
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1237 - CONSERVATORSHIP AND RECEIVERSHIP
##### § 1237.2 Definitions.
###### Default

(1)means, with respect to a regulated entity, any official determination by the Director, pursuant to which a conservator or receiver is appointed for a regulated entity.
