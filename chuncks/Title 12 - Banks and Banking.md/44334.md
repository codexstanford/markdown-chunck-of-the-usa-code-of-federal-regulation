### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1209 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart A - Scope and Authority
###### § 1209.3 Definitions.

means any person named as a respondent in any notice of charges, or FHFA, as the context requires in this part.
