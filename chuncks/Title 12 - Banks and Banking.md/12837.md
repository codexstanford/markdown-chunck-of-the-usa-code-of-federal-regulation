
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart G-Special Rules Applicable to Credit Card Accounts and Open-End Credit Offered to College Students
###### ยง 226.56 Requirements for over-the-limit transactions.
####### Definition.

(a)For purposes of this section, the term "over-the-limit transaction" means any extension of credit by a card issuer to complete a transaction that causes a consumer's credit card account balance to exceed the credit limit.
