
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1277 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS, CAPITAL STOCK AND CAPITAL PLANS
##### Subpart B - Bank Capital Requirements
###### § 1277.4 Credit risk capital requirement.
####### Calculation of credit exposures for derivative contracts,Current credit exposure,Single derivative contract.

(i)-(1)-(i)The current credit exposure for derivative contracts that are not subject to an eligible master netting agreement shall be:

(A) If the mark-to-market value of the contract is positive, the mark-to-market value of the contract; or

(B) If the mark-to-market value of the contract is zero or negative, zero.
