####### Initial application.

(c)(1) An FDIC-supervised institution that initially becomes subject to the minimum liquidity standard and other requirements of this part under ยง 329.1(b)(1)(i) must comply with the requirements of this part beginning on the first day of the third calendar quarter after which the FDIC-supervised institution becomes subject to this part, except that an FDIC-supervised institution must:
