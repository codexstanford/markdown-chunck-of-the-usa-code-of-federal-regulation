
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1213 - OFFICE OF THE OMBUDSMAN
##### § 1213.4 Complaints and appeals from a regulated entity or the Office of Finance.
###### Matters subject to appeal.

(2)A regulated entity or the Office of Finance may submit an appeal regarding any final, written regulatory or supervisory conclusion, decision, or examination rating by FHFA. The Ombudsman may further define what matters are subject to appeal.
