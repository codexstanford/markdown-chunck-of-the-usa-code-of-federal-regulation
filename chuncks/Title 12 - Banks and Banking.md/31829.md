
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 715 - SUPERVISORY COMMITTEE AUDITS AND VERIFICATIONS
##### § 715.6 Audit of Federally-insured State-chartered credit unions.
###### Other requirements.

(c)A federally-insured, state-chartered credit union, regardless of which audit it is required to obtain under this section, must meet other applicable requirements of this part except §§ 715.5 and 715.12.
