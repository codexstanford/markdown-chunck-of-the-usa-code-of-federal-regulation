
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 205-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
##### Supplement I to Part 205-Official Staff Interpretations
###### Recurring payments by government agency.

1.The third-party name for recurring payments from federal, state, or local governments need not list the particular agency. For example, "U.S. gov't" or "N.Y. sal" will suffice.
