
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1278 - VOLUNTARY MERGERS OF FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANKS
##### § 1278.1 Definitions.
###### Financial Statements

means statements of condition, income, capital, and cash flows, with explanatory notes, in such form as the Banks are required to include in their filings made under the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934.
