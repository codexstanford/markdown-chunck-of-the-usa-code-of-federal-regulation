
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1223 - MINORITY AND WOMEN INCLUSION
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1223.1 Definitions.
####### Prime contractor (tier 1)

means a supplier that enters into a contract with a regulated entity to provide goods and/or services directly to that regulated entity.
