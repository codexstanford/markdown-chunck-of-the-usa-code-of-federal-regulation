
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1240.2 Definitions.
####### Company

means a corporation, partnership, limited liability company, depository institution, business trust, special purpose entity, association, or similar organization.
