
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 228-COMMUNITY REINVESTMENT (REGULATION BB)
##### Subpart B-Standards for Assessing Performance
###### ยง 228.27 Strategic plan.
####### Election if satisfactory goals not substantially met.

(4)A bank may elect in its plan that, if the bank fails to meet substantially its plan goals for a satisfactory rating, the Board will evaluate the bank's performance under the lending, investment, and service tests, the community development test, or the small bank performance standards, as appropriate.
