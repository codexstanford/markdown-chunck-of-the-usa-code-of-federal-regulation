
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1081-RULES OF PRACTICE FOR ADJUDICATION PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart C-Hearings
##### § 1081.306 Record in proceedings before hearing officer; retention of documents; copies.
###### Substitution of copies.

(c)A true copy of a document may be substituted for any document in the record or any document retained pursuant to paragraph (b) of this section.
