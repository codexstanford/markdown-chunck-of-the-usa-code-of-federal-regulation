
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

####### Limitation on investment in Edge and agreement corporations.

(h)(1)A member bank may invest up to 10 percent of its capital and surplus in the capital stock of Edge and agreement corporations or, with the prior approval of the Board, up to 20 percent of its capital and surplus in such stock.
