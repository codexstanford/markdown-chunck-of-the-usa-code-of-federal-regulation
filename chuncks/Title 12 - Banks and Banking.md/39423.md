
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Expedited.

2.For purposes of § 1026.10(e), the term "expedited" means crediting a payment the same day or, if the payment is received after any cut-off time established by the creditor, the next business day.
