###### ยง 614.4359 Attribution rules.
####### Liability.

(1)(i) The borrower has primary or secondary liability on a loan made to the related borrower. The amount of such loan attributable to the borrower is limited to the amount of the borrower's liability.

(ii) This section does not require attribution of a guarantee taken out of an abundance of caution. To qualify for the abundance of caution exception to the requirements of this subpart, the institution must document in the loan file that the loan, when evaluated under the loan underwriting standards adopted pursuant to ยง 614.4150 of this part without considering the guarantee, would support the credit decision under the same basic terms and conditions.
