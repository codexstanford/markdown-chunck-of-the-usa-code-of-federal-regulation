
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1204 - PRIVACY ACT IMPLEMENTATION
##### § 1204.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### Senior Agency Official for Privacy

means a person designated by the FHFA Director who has the authority and responsibility to oversee and supervise the FHFA privacy program and implementation of the Privacy Act.
