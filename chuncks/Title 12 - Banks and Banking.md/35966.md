
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1015-MORTGAGE ASSISTANCE RELIEF SERVICES (REGULATION O)
#### § 1015.2 Definitions.
##### Telemarketing

means a plan, program, or campaign which is conducted to induce the purchase of any service, by use of one or more telephones and which involves more than one interstate telephone call.
