
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1254 - VALIDATION AND APPROVAL OF CREDIT SCORE MODELS
##### § 1254.2 Definitions.
###### Credit score model

means a statistical tool or algorithm created by a third party used to produce a numerical value or categorization to predict the likelihood of certain credit behaviors.
