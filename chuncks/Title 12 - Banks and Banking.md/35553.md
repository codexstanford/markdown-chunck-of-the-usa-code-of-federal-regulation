
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1010 - LAND REGISTRATION (REGULATION J)
#### Subpart B - Reporting Requirements
##### § 1010.114 Recreational facilities.
###### Facility.,e.g.,

(1)Identify each recreational facility. Identify closely related facilities (swimming pool and bathhouse) separately only if their availability dates differ. If any recreational facility is not owned by the developer, insert a warning below the chart phrased substantially as follows: "We do not own the (name of facility or facilities) so we can not assure its (their) continued availability."
