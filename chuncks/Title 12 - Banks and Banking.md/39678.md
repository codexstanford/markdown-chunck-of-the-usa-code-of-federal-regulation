
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Making disclosures more conspicuous.

2.The terms "finance charge" and (except for private education loan disclosures made in compliance with § 1026.47) "annual percentage rate" may be made more conspicuous in any way that highlights them in relation to the other required disclosures. For example, they may be:

i. Capitalized when other disclosures are printed in capital and lower case.

ii. Printed in larger type, bold print or different type face.

iii. Printed in a contrasting color.

iv. Underlined.

v. Set off with asterisks.
