
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 213 - CONSUMER LEASING (REGULATION M)
##### Supplement I to Part 213-Official Staff Commentary to Regulation M
###### Content.

1.A lessor may delete inapplicable items from the disclosure. For example, if a lease contract does not include a security interest, the reference to a security interest may be omitted.
