Unless counsel for the FDIC requests and is granted an extension of time for filing or files a statement of intent to negotiate under ยง 308.179, failure to file an answer within the 20-day period will be treated as a consent to the award requested.

(2) The answer shall explain in detail any objections to the award requested and identify the facts relied on in support of the FDIC's position.
