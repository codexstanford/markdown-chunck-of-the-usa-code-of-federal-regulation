
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### Subpart A - Conversion of Insured Credit Unions to Mutual Savings Banks
###### ยง 708a.101 Definitions.
####### Independent entity

means a company with experience in conducting corporate elections. No official or senior management official of the credit union, or the immediate family member of any official or senior management official, may have any ownership interest in, or be employed by, the entity.
