
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

####### Advertisement of terms that require additional disclosures,Triggering terms.

(d)-(1)If any of the following terms is set forth in an advertisement, the advertisement shall meet the requirements of paragraph (d)(2) of this section:

(i) The amount or percentage of any downpayment.
