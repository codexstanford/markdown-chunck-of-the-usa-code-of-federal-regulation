
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 51 - RECEIVERSHIPS FOR UNINSURED NATIONAL BANKS
#### ยง 51.6 Administrative expenses of receiver.
##### Priority of administrative expenses.

(a)All administrative expenses of the receiver for an uninsured bank shall be paid out of the assets of the bank in receivership before payment of claims against the receivership.
