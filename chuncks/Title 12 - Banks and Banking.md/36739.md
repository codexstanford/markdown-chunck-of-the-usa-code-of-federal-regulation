
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1024-REAL ESTATE SETTLEMENT PROCEDURES ACT (REGULATION X)
#### Subpart C-Mortgage Servicing
##### § 1024.32 General disclosure requirements.
###### Foreign language disclosures.

(2)Disclosures required under this subpart may be made in a language other than English, provided that the disclosures are made available in English upon a recipient's request.
