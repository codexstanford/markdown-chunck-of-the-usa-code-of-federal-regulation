
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 237 - SWAPS MARGIN AND SWAPS PUSH-OUT (REGULATION KK)
##### Subpart A - Margin and Capital Requirements for Covered Swap Entities (Regulation KK)
###### § 237.9 Cross-border application of margin requirements.
####### Transactions to which this rule does not apply.

(a)The requirements of §§ 237.3 through 237.8 and §§ 237.10 through 237.12 shall not apply to any foreign non-cleared swap or foreign non-cleared security-based swap of a foreign covered swap entity.
