
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### PART 704 - CORPORATE CREDIT UNIONS
##### ยง 704.2 Definitions.
###### Residual interest

means the ownership interest in remainder cash flows from a CMO or ABS transaction after payments due bondholders and trust administrative expenses have been satisfied.
