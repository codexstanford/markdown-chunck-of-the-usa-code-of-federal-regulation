
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 28 - INTERNATIONAL BANKING ACTIVITIES
#### Subpart C - International Lending Supervision
##### ยง 28.53 Accounting for fees on international loans.
###### Restrictions on fees for restructured international loans.

(a)No banking institution shall charge, in connection with the restructuring of an international loan, any fee exceeding the administrative costs of the restructuring unless it amortizes the amount of the fee exceeding the administrative cost over the effective life of the loan.
