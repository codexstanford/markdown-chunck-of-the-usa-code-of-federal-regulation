
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1201-GENERAL DEFINITIONS APPLYING TO ALL FEDERAL HOUSING FINANCE AGENCY REGULATIONS
##### § 1201.1 Definitions.
###### NRSRO

means a credit rating organization registered with the SEC as a nationally recognized statistical rating organization by the Securities and Exchange Commission.
