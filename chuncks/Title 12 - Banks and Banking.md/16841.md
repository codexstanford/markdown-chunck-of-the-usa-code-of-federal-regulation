
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 238 - SAVINGS AND LOAN HOLDING COMPANIES (REGULATION LL)
##### Subpart Q - Single Counterparty Credit Limits for Covered Savings and Loan Holding Companies
###### ยง 238.152 Credit exposure limits.
####### General limit on aggregate net credit exposure.

No covered company may have an aggregate net credit exposure to any counterparty that exceeds 25 percent of the tier 1 capital of the covered company.
