
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1235 - RECORD RETENTION FOR REGULATED ENTITIES AND OFFICE OF FINANCE
##### § 1235.3 Establishment and evaluation of a record retention program.
###### Evaluation.

(b)Management of each regulated entity and the Office of Finance shall evaluate in writing the adequacy and effectiveness of the record retention program at least every two years and provide a copy of the evaluation to the board of directors and the Director.
