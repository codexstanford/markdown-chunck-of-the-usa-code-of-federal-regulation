
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1266 - ADVANCES
##### Subpart A - Advances to Members
###### § 1266.1 Definitions.
####### Credit union

means a credit union as defined in section 101 of the Federal Credit Union Act (12 U.S.C. 1752).
