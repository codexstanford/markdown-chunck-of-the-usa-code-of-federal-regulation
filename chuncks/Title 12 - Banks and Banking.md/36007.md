
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1016-PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION (REGULATION P)
#### Subpart A-Privacy and Opt Out Notices
##### § 1016.5 Annual privacy notice to customers required.
###### Special rule for loans in the case of a financial institution other than a credit union.

(c)If a financial institution other than a credit union does not have a customer relationship with a consumer under the special rule for loans in § 1016.4(c)(2) of this part, then it need not provide an annual notice to that consumer under this section.
