
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1234 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart A - Authority, Purpose, Scope and Definitions
###### § 1234.2 Definitions.
####### United States or U.S.

means the United States of America, including its territories and possessions, any State of the United States, and the District of Columbia.
