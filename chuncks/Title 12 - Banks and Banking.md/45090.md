
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1233 - REPORTING OF FRAUDULENT FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS
##### § 1233.3 Reporting.
###### Retention of records.

(c)A regulated entity or entity-affiliated party shall maintain a copy of any report submitted to the Director and the original or business record equivalent of any supporting documentation for a period of five years from the date of submission.
