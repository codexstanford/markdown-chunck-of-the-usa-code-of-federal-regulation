
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 109 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE IN ADJUDICATORY PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart B - Local Rules
##### ยง 109.102 Discovery.
###### Fees.

(f)Deposition witnesses, including expert witnesses, shall be paid the same expenses in the same manner as are paid witnesses in the district courts of the United States in proceedings in which the United States Government is a party. Expenses in accordance with this paragraph shall be paid by the party seeking to take the deposition.
