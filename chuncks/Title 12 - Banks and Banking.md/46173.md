
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1248 - UNIFORM MORTGAGE-BACKED SECURITIES
##### § 1248.1 Definitions.
###### Fastest paying quartile of a cohort,specified pools,

means the quartile of a cohort that has the fastest prepayment speeds as measured by the three-month CPR. The quartiles shall be determined by ranking outstanding TBA-eligible securities with the same coupon, maturity, and loan-origination year by the three-month CPR, excludingand dividing each cohort into four parts such that the total unpaid principal balance of the pools included in each part is equal.
