
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1269 - STANDBY LETTERS OF CREDIT
##### § 1269.1 Definitions.
###### Residential housing finance

means:

(1) The purchase or funding of "residential housing finance assets," as that term is defined in § 1266.1 of this chapter; or

(2) Other activities that support the development or construction of residential housing.
