
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 704 - CORPORATE CREDIT UNIONS
##### Appendix C to Part 704 - Risk-Based Capital Credit Risk-Weight Categories
###### Risk participation,e.g.,

means a participation in which the originating party remains liable to the beneficiary for the full amount of an obligation (a direct credit substitute), notwithstanding that another party has acquired a participation in that obligation.
