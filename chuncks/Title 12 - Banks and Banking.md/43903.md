
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1200-ORGANIZATION AND FUNCTIONS
##### § 1200.2 Organization of the Federal Housing Finance Agency.
###### Office of Inspector General.

(5)The office is headed by a presidentially appointed and Senate-confirmed Inspector General who serves under the general supervision of the Director. The office carries out activities and responsibilities established in the Inspector General Act of 1978.
