
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1248 - UNIFORM MORTGAGE-BACKED SECURITIES
##### § 1248.1 Definitions.
###### To-Be-Announced Eligible Mortgage-Backed Security (TBA-Eligible MBS)

means Enterprise MBS (including Freddie Mac Participation Certificates, Giants, MBS, UMBS, and Supers; and Fannie Mae MBS, Megas, UMBS, and Supers) that meet criteria such that the market considers them sufficiently fungible to be forward traded in the TBA market.
