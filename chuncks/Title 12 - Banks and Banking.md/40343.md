### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Regular periodic payments.

1.The repayment schedule for a high-cost mortgage must fully amortize the outstanding principal balance through "regular periodic payments." A payment is a "regular periodic payment" if it is not more than two times the amount of other payments. For purposes of open-end credit plans, the term "regular periodic payment" or "periodic payment" means the required minimum periodic payment.
