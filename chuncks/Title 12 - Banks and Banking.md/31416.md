
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 707 - TRUTH IN SAVINGS
##### Appendix C to Part 707 - Official Staff Interpretations
###### Terminology.

3.In disclosing dividends earned for the period, credit unions must use the term "dividends" or terminology such as: "Dividends paid," to describe dividends that have been credited; "Dividends accrued," to indicate that dividends are not yet credited.
