
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.6 Final suspension order.
####### Deadline for decision.

(d)The suspending official shall make a determination on whether to issue a final suspension order with respect to the respondent within thirty (30) calendar days of the deadline given for the respondent's response in the notice of proposed suspension, unless the suspending official notifies the respondent in writing that additional time is needed.
