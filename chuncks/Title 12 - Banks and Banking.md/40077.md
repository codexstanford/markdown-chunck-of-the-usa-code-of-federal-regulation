
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Real property or dwelling.,See

1.For purposes of § 1026.20(e)(1), the term "real property" includes vacant and unimproved land. The term "dwelling" includes vacation and second homes and mobile homes, boats, and trailers used as residences.§ 1026.2(a)(19) and related commentary for additional guidance regarding the term "dwelling."
