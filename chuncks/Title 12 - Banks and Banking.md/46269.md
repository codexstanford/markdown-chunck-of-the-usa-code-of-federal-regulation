
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1254 - VALIDATION AND APPROVAL OF CREDIT SCORE MODELS
##### § 1254.4 Requirements for use of a credit score.
###### Replacement of credit score model.

(b)An Enterprise may replace any credit score model then in use after a new credit score model has been approved in accordance with this part.
