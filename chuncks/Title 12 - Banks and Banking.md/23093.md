
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 325 - STRESS TESTING
##### ยง 325.6 Required reports of stress test results to the FDIC and the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System.
###### Report required for periodic stress test results.

(a)A covered bank must report to the FDIC and to the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, on or before April 5 of the reporting year, the results of the stress test in the manner and form specified by the FDIC.
