
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1234 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart A - Authority, Purpose, Scope and Definitions
###### § 1234.2 Definitions.
####### Sponsor

means a person who organizes and initiates a securitization transaction by selling or transferring assets, either directly or indirectly, including through an affiliate, to the issuing entity.
