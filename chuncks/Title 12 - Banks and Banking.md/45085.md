
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1233 - REPORTING OF FRAUDULENT FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS
##### § 1233.2 Definitions.
###### Fraud

means a misstatement, misrepresentation, or omission that cannot be corrected and that was relied upon by a regulated entity to purchase or sell a loan or financial instrument.
