
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart D - Total Net Cash Outflow
##### ยง 50.32 Outflow amounts.
###### Other contractual outflow amount.

(l)A national bank's or Federal savings association's other contractual outflow amount is 100 percent of funding or amounts, with the exception of operating expenses of the national bank or Federal savings association (such as rents, salaries, utilities, and other similar payments), payable by the national bank or Federal savings association to counterparties under legally binding agreements that are not otherwise specified in this section.
