
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.2 Definitions.
####### Affiliate

means a party that either controls or is controlled by another person, whether directly or indirectly, including one or more persons that are controlled by the same third person.
