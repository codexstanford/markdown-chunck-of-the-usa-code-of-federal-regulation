
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XV - Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter B - Resolution Funding Corporation
#### PART 1511 - BOOK-ENTRY PROCEDURE
##### ยง 1511.1 Definitions of terms.
###### Book-entry Funding Corporation Security

means a Funding Corporation Security in book-entry form that is issued or maintained in the Book-entry System. Solely for the purposes of this Part, it also means the separate interest and principal components of a Book-entry Funding Corporation Security if such security has been divided into such components as authorized by the Securities Documentation and the components are maintained separately on the books of one or more Federal Reserve Banks.
