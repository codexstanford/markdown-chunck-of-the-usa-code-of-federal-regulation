
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.3 Scope of suspension orders.
####### No effect on other actions by FHFA.

(b)Nothing in this part shall limit the authority of FHFA to pursue any other regulatory or supervisory action with respect to any regulated entity or any other person and any affiliates thereof, whether instead of or in addition to any action taken under this part.
