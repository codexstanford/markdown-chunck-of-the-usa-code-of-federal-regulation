#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.20 Requirements for gift cards and gift certificates.

(2)The requirements in paragraph (a)(4)(iii) of this section apply to any card, code, or other device provided to a consumer in connection with a loyalty, award, or promotional program if the period of eligibility for such program began on or after August 22, 2010.
