
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 348 - MANAGEMENT OFFICIAL INTERLOCKS
##### ยง 348.2 Other definitions and rules of construction.
###### Contiguous or adjacent cities, towns, or villages

(d)means cities, towns, or villages whose borders touch each other or whose borders are within 10 road miles of each other at their closest points. The property line of an office located in an unincorporated city, town, or village is the boundary line of that city, town, or village for the purpose of this definition.
