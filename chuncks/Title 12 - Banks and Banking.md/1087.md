##### ยง 4.5 Other OCC supervisory offices.
###### Midsize Bank Supervision (MBS).

(a)Midsize Bank Supervision is responsible for supervising midsize national banks and Federal savings associations that present unique supervisory challenges based on size, complexity, and/or product line. MBS is headquartered in Chicago, IL and located at 425 South Financial Place, Suite 1700, Chicago, IL 60605.
