##### Mutual aid societies.

(4)Notwithstanding the requirements of paragraph (c)(3) of this section, a national bank or Federal savings association may accept a plan issued by a mutual aid society, as defined in ยง 22.2(h), in satisfaction of the flood insurance purchase requirement in paragraph (a) of this section if:
