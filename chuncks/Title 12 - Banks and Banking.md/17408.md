
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 242 - DEFINITIONS RELATING TO TITLE I OF THE DODD-FRANK ACT (REGULATION PP)
##### Appendix A to Part 242 - Financial Activities for Purposes of Title I of the Dodd-Frank Act
###### Extending credit and servicing loans.

(1)Making, acquiring, brokering, or servicing loans or other extensions of credit (including factoring, issuing letters of credit and accepting drafts) for the company's account or for the account of others.
