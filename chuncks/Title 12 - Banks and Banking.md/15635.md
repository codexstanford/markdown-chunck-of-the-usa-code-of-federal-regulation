####### Right to assert claim.

(a)(1) A paying bank or returning bank may be liable to a depositary bank under ยง 229.38 for failing to return a check in an expeditious manner only if the depositary bank has arrangements in place such that the paying bank or returning bank could return a returned check to the depositary bank electronically, directly or indirectly, by commercially reasonable means.
