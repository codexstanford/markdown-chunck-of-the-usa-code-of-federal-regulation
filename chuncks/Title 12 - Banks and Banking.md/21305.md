####### Appearance before the FDIC or an administrative law judge,By attorneys.

(a)-(1)Any member in good standing of the bar of the highest court of any state, commonwealth, possession, territory of the United States, or the District of Columbia may represent others before the FDIC if such attorney is not currently suspended or debarred from practice before the FDIC.
