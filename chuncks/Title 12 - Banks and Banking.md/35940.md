
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1014-MORTGAGE ACTS AND PRACTICES-ADVERTISING (REGULATION N)
#### § 1014.2 Definitions.
##### Dwelling

means a residential structure that contains one to four units, whether or not that structure is attached to real property. The term includes any of the following if used as a residence: an individual condominium unit, cooperative unit, mobile home, manufactured home, or trailer.
