
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart B-Requirements for Remittance Transfers
##### § 1005.31 Disclosures.
###### Estimates.

(d)Estimated disclosures may be provided to the extent permitted by § 1005.32. Estimated disclosures must be described using the term "Estimated" or a substantially similar term in close proximity to the estimated term or terms.
