
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1006-DEBT COLLECTION PRACTICES (REGULATION F)
#### Subpart B-Rules for FDCPA Debt Collectors
##### § 1006.14 Harassing, oppressive, or abusive conduct.
###### Coercive advertisements.

(f)In connection with the collection of a debt, a debt collector must not advertise for sale any debt to coerce payment of the debt.
