
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1011 - PURCHASERS' REVOCATION RIGHTS, SALES PRACTICES AND STANDARDS (REGULATION K)
#### Subpart B - Sales Practices and Standards
##### § 1011.25 Misleading sales practices.
###### Non-profit or institutional name use.

(r)The use of names or trade styles which imply that the developer is a nonprofit research organization, public bureau, group, etc., when such is not the case.
