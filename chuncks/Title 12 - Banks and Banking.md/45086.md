
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1233 - REPORTING OF FRAUDULENT FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS
##### § 1233.2 Definitions.
###### Possible fraud

means that a regulated entity has a reasonable belief, based upon a review of information available to the regulated entity, that fraud may be occurring or has occurred.
