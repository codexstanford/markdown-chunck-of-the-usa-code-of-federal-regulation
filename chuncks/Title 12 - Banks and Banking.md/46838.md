
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1271 - MISCELLANEOUS FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK OPERATIONS AND AUTHORITIES
##### Subpart A - Collection, Settlement, and Processing of Payment Instruments
###### § 1271.1 Definitions.
####### Statement packaging

includes receiving statement information from members or eligible institutions or their service bureaus on respective customer cycle dates; printing statements; matching customer account statements; packaging the statements with appropriate items and informational materials, as authorized by individual members and eligible institutions, for distribution to their customers; sending the packages to the members or eligible institutions or mailing the packages directly to their customers.
