
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Grouped and separate.

1.The disclosures required by § 1026.20(e)(2) must be grouped together on the front side of a separate one-page document that contains no other material.
