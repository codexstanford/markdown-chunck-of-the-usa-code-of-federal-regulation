
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart F - Risk-weighted Assets - Market Risk
###### § 1240.202 Definitions.
####### Reverse mortgage

means a mortgage loan secured by a residential property in which a homeowner relinquishes equity in their home in exchange for regular payments.
