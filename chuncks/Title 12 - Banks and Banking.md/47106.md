
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1281 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK HOUSING GOALS
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1281.1 Definitions.
####### Conventional mortgage

means a mortgage other than a mortgage as to which a Bank has the benefit of any guaranty, insurance or other obligation by the United States or any of its agencies or instrumentalities.
