
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 51 - RECEIVERSHIPS FOR UNINSURED NATIONAL BANKS
#### ยง 51.4 Claims.
##### Right of set-off.

(c)If a person with a claim against an uninsured bank in receivership also has an obligation owed to the bank, the claim and obligation will be set off against each other and only the net balance remaining after set-off shall be considered as a claim, provided such set-off is otherwise legally valid.
