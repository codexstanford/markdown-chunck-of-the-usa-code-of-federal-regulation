
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1010 - LAND REGISTRATION (REGULATION J)
#### Subpart B - Reporting Requirements
##### § 1010.114 Recreational facilities.
###### Who may use the facilities.,e.g.,

(6)Indicate who will be permitted to use the recreational facilities (lot owners, their guests, employees of developer, general public). If the general public will be permitted to use the facilities include the following statement in the narrative under the caption "Who may use the facilities": "The (identify the facility) is open to use by the general public and their use of the facility may limit use of it by lot owners."
