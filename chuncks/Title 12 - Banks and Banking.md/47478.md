
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1290 - COMMUNITY SUPPORT REQUIREMENTS
##### § 1290.3 Community support standards.
###### CRA standard.

(b)A member meets the CRA standard if it is subject to the requirements of the CRA and the rating in the member's most recent CRA evaluation is "Outstanding" or "Satisfactory."
