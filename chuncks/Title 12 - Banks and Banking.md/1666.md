
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury

#### Subpart C - Expansion of Activities
##### ยง 5.33 Business combinations involving a national bank or Federal savings association.
###### Consolidations and mergers under 12 U.S.C. 214a of a national bank with State banks resulting in a State bank as defined in 12 U.S.C. 214(a),In general.

(7)-(i)Prior OCC approval is not required for the merger or consolidation of a national bank with a State bank as defined in 12 U.S.C. 214(a). Termination of a national bank's existence and status as a national banking association is automatic, and its charter cancelled, upon completion of the statutory and regulatory requirements for engaging in the consolidation or merger and consummation of the consolidation or merger.
