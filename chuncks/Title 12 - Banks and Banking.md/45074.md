
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1231 - GOLDEN PARACHUTE AND INDEMNIFICATION PAYMENTS
##### § 1231.6 Filing instructions.
###### Scope.

(a)This section contains procedures for requesting the consent of the Director and for filing any notice, where consent or notice is required by § 1231.3.
