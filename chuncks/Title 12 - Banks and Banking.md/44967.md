
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1229 - CAPITAL CLASSIFICATIONS AND PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
##### Subpart A - Federal Home Loan Banks
###### § 1229.1 Definitions.
####### Class A stock

means capital stock issued by a Bank, including subclasses, that has the characteristics specified in section 6(a)(4)(A)(i) of the Bank Act (12 U.S.C. 1426(a)(4)(A)(i)) and related regulations.
