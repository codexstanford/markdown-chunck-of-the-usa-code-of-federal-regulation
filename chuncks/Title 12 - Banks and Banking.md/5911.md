
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 161 - DEFINITIONS FOR REGULATIONS AFFECTING ALL SAVINGS ASSOCIATIONS
#### ยง 161.37 Parent company; subsidiary.
##### subsidiary

The termmeans any company which is owned or controlled directly or indirectly by a person, and includes any service corporation owned in whole or in part by a savings association, or a subsidiary of such service corporation.
