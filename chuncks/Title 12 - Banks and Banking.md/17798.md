
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 244 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION (REGULATION RR)
##### Subpart D - Exceptions and Exemptions
###### § 244.15 Qualifying commercial loans, commercial real estate loans, and automobile loans.
####### Record maintenance.

(d)A sponsor must retain the disclosures required in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section and the certifications required in §§ 244.16(a)(8), 244.17(a)(10), and 244.18(a)(8), as applicable, in its records until three years after all ABS interests issued in the securitization are no longer outstanding. The sponsor must provide the disclosures and certifications upon request to the Commission and the sponsor's appropriate Federal banking agency, if any.
