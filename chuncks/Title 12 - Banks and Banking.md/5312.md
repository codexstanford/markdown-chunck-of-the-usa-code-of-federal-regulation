
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart D - Total Net Cash Outflow
##### ยง 50.32 Outflow amounts.
###### Debt security buyback outflow amount.

(i)A national bank's or Federal savings association's debt security buyback outflow amount for debt securities issued by the national bank or Federal savings association that mature more than 30 calendar days after the calculation date and for which the national bank or Federal savings association or a consolidated subsidiary of the national bank or Federal savings association is the primary market maker in such debt securities includes:

(1) 3 percent of all such debt securities that are not structured securities; and

(2) 5 percent of all such debt securities that are structured securities.
