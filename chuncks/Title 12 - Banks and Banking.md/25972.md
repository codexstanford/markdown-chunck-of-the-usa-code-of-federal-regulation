
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 364 - STANDARDS FOR SAFETY AND SOUNDNESS
##### Appendix B to Part 364 - Interagency Guidelines Establishing Information Security Standards
###### Consumer Information

b.means any record about an individual, whether in paper, electronic, or other form, that is a consumer report or is derived from a consumer report and that is maintained or otherwise possessed by or on behalf of the institution for a business purpose. Consumer information also means a compilation of such records. The term does not include any record that does not personally identify an individual.
