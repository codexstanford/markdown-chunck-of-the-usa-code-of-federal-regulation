
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1263 - MEMBERS OF THE BANKS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1263.1 Definitions.
####### Community development financial institution,CDFI,et seq.,et seq.

ormeans an institution that is certified as a community development financial institution by the CDFI Fund under the Community Development Banking and Financial Institutions Act of 1994 (12 U.S.C. 4701), other than a bank or savings association insured under the Federal Deposit Insurance Act (12 U.S.C. 1811), a holding company for such a bank or savings association, or a credit union that has Federal share insurance.
