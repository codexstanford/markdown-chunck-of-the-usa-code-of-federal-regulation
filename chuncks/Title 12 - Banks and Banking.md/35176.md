
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1006-DEBT COLLECTION PRACTICES (REGULATION F)
#### Supplement I to Part 1006-Official Interpretations
##### Domain name available for use by the general public.,e.g., john.doe@springsidemortgage.com,e.g., john.doe@agency.gov,,john.doe@university.edu,,john.doe@nonprofit.org

1.Under § 1006.6(d)(4)(ii)(E), the domain name of an email address is available for use by the general public when multiple members of the general public are permitted to use the same domain name, whether for free or through a paid subscription. Such a name does not include one that is reserved for use by specific registrants, such as a domain name branded for use by a particular commercial entity () or reserved for particular types of institutions (or).
