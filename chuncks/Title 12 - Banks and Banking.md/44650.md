
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1214 - AVAILABILITY OF NON-PUBLIC INFORMATION
##### § 1214.4 Exceptions.
###### Privilege.

(d)FHFA retains all privilege claims for non-public information shared under § 1214.4, including, but not limited to attorney-client, attorney-work product, deliberative process, and examination privileges.
