
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 109 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE IN ADJUDICATORY PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart A - Uniform Rules of Practice and Procedure
##### § 109.10 Filing of papers.
###### Filing.

(a)Any papers required to be filed, excluding documents produced in response to a discovery request pursuant to §§ 109.25 and 109.26 of this subpart, shall be filed with the OFIA, except as otherwise provided.
