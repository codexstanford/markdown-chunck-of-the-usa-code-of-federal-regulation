
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 346 - DISCLOSURE AND REPORTING OF CRA-RELATED AGREEMENTS
##### ยง 346.11 Other definitions and rules of construction used in this part.
###### Term of agreement.

(n)An agreement that does not have a fixed termination date is considered to terminate on the last date on which any party to the agreement makes any payment or provides any loan or other resources under the agreement, unless the relevant supervisory agency for the agreement otherwise notifies each party in writing.
