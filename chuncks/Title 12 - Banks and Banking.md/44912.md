
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.2 Definitions.
####### Suspending official

means the Director, or any other FHFA official with delegated authority to sign proposed and final suspension orders and their accompanying notices.
