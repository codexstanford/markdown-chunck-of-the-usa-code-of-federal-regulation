
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 41 - FAIR CREDIT REPORTING
#### Subpart J - Identity Theft Red Flags
##### ยง 41.90 Duties regarding the detection, prevention, and mitigation of identity theft.
###### Scope.

(a)This section applies to a financial institution or creditor that is a national bank; a Federal savings association; a Federal branch or agency of a foreign bank; or an operating subsidiary of any of these institutions that is not a functionally regulated subsidiary within the meaning of section 5(c)(5) of the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956, as amended (12 U.S.C. 1844(c)(5)).
