
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Terminology.

3.For purposes of § 1026.37(g)(2), the term "property taxes" has the same meaning as in § 1026.43(b)(8) and further described in comment 43(b)(8)-2; the term "homeowner's insurance" means the amounts identified in § 1026.4(b)(8); and the term "mortgage insurance" has the same meaning as "mortgage insurance or any functional equivalent" in § 1026.37(c), which means the amounts identified in § 1026.4(b)(5).
