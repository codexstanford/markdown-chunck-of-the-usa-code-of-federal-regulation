
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

####### Equity investments,Prohibited investments.

(a)-(1)No insured state savings association may directly acquire or retain as principal any equity investment of a type, or in an amount, that is not permissible for a Federal savings association unless the exception in paragraph (a)(2) of this section applies.
