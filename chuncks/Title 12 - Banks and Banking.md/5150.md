
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 50.3 Definitions.
###### Affiliated depository institution

means with respect to a national bank or Federal savings association that is a depository institution, another depository institution that is a consolidated subsidiary of a bank holding company or savings and loan holding company of which the national bank or Federal savings association is also a consolidated subsidiary.
