
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1081-RULES OF PRACTICE FOR ADJUDICATION PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart B-Initiation of Proceedings and Prehearing Rules
##### § 1081.201 Answer and disclosure statement and notification of financial interest.
###### Time to file answer.

(a)Within 14 days of service of the notice of charges, respondent must file an answer as designated in the notice of charges.
