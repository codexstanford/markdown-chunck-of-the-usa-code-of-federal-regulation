
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1234 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart A - Authority, Purpose, Scope and Definitions
###### § 1234.2 Definitions.
####### Retaining sponsor

means, with respect to a securitization transaction, the sponsor that has retained or caused to be retained an economic interest in the credit risk of the securitized assets pursuant to subpart B of this part.
