
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### No funds for borrower.,1

2.When the down payment and funds from the borrower is determined in accordance with § 1026.38(i)(4)(ii)(A)(), the amount disclosed under § 1026.38(i)(6)(ii) as "Funds for Borrower" is $0.
