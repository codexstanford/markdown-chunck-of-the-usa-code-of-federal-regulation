
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 370 - RECORDKEEPING FOR TIMELY DEPOSIT INSURANCE DETERMINATION
##### ยง 370.8 Relief.
###### Exemption.

(a)A covered institution may submit a request in the form of a letter to the FDIC for an exemption from this part if it demonstrates that it does not take deposits from any account holder which, when aggregated, would exceed the SMDIA for any owner of the funds on deposit and will not in the future.
