
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1201-GENERAL DEFINITIONS APPLYING TO ALL FEDERAL HOUSING FINANCE AGENCY REGULATIONS
##### § 1201.1 Definitions.
###### Data Reporting Manual or DRM

means a manual issued by FHFA and amended from time to time containing reporting requirements for the Regulated Entities.
