
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection

#### Subpart B-Mortgage Settlement and Escrow Accounts
##### § 1024.20 List of homeownership counseling organizations.
###### Timeshare plans.

(2)A lender is not required to provide an applicant for a mortgage loan secured by a timeshare, as described under 11 U.S.C. 101(53D), the list of homeownership counseling organizations required under this section.
