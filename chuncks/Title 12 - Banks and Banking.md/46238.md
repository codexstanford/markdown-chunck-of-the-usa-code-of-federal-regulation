
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1253 - PRIOR APPROVAL FOR ENTERPRISE PRODUCTS
##### § 1253.2 Definitions.
###### Authorizing statute,et seq.,et seq.

means, in the case of Fannie Mae, the Federal National Mortgage Association Charter Act (12 U.S.C. 1716) and, in the case of Freddie Mac, the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation Act (12 U.S.C. 1451).
