
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1016-PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION (REGULATION P)
#### Subpart A-Privacy and Opt Out Notices
##### § 1016.9 Delivering privacy and opt out notices.
###### Joint notice with other financial institutions.

(f)You may provide a joint notice from you and one or more of your affiliates or other financial institutions, as identified in the notice, as long as the notice is accurate with respect to you and the other institutions.
