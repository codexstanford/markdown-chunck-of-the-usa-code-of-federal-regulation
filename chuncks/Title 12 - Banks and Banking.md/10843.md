
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 223.14 What are the collateral requirements for a credit transaction with an affiliate?
####### Replacement requirement for retired or amortized collateral.

(e)A member bank must ensure that any required collateral that subsequently is retired or amortized is replaced with additional eligible collateral as needed to keep the percentage of the collateral value relative to the amount of the outstanding credit transaction equal to the minimum percentage required at the inception of the transaction.
