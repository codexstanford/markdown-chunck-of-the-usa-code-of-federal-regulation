
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### ยง 7.1030 Permissible derivatives activities for national banks.
###### Safe and sound banking practices.

(f)A national bank must adhere to safe and sound banking practices in conducting the activities described in this section. The bank must have a risk management system (policies, processes, personnel, and control system) that effectively manages (identifies, measures, monitors, and controls) these activities' interest rate, credit, liquidity, price, operational, compliance, and strategic risks.
