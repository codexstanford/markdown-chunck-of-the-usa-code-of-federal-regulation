
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1214 - AVAILABILITY OF NON-PUBLIC INFORMATION
##### § 1214.3 General rule.
###### Persons possessing confidential supervisory information.

(c)All confidential supervisory information, for which the Director authorizes disclosure, remains the property of FHFA and may not be used or disclosed for any purpose other than that authorized under this part without the prior written permission of the Director.
