
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1235 - RECORD RETENTION FOR REGULATED ENTITIES AND OFFICE OF FINANCE
##### § 1235.4 Minimum requirements of a record retention program.
###### Minimum storage requirements for electronic records.

(b)Electronic records, preferably searchable, must be maintained on immutable, non-rewritable storage in a manner that provides for both ready access by any person who is entitled to access the records, including staff of FHFA, and accurate reproduction for later reference by transmission, printing or other means.
