
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 712 - CREDIT UNION SERVICE ORGANIZATIONS (CUSOs)
##### ยง 712.11 What requirements apply to subsidiary CUSOs?
###### FCUs investing in a CUSO with a subsidiary CUSO.

(a)FCUs may only invest in or loan to a CUSO, which has a subsidiary CUSO, if the subsidiary CUSO satisfies all of the requirements of this part. The requirements of this part apply to all tiers or levels of a CUSO's structure.
