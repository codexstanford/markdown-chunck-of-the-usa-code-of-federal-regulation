
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 741-REQUIREMENTS FOR INSURANCE
##### Appendix B to Part 741-Loan Workouts, Nonaccrual Policy, and Regulatory Reporting of Troubled Debt Restructured Loans
###### "Rewrite"

means significantly changing the terms of an existing loan, including payment amounts, interest rates, amortization schedules, or its final maturity.
