### Subchapter C - Enterprises

##### Subpart D - Risk-Weighted Assets - Standardized Approach
###### § 1240.36 Derivative contracts.
####### Exposure amount for derivative contracts.

(a)An Enterprise must use the current exposure methodology (CEM) described in paragraph (b) of this section to calculate the exposure amount for all its OTC derivative contracts.
