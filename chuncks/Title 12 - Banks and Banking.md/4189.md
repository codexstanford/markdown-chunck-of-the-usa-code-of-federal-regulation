
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 35 - DISCLOSURE AND REPORTING OF CRA-RELATED AGREEMENTS
#### § 35.9 Compliance provisions.
##### Inadvertent or de minimis errors.

(d)Inadvertent or de minimis errors in annual reports or other documents filed with the OCC under §§ 35.6 or 35.7 will not subject the reporting party to any penalty.
