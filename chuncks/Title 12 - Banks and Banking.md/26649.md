##### Subpart C - Receivership Administrative Claims Process
###### ยง 380.34 Procedures for filing claim.
####### When claim is deemed filed.

(b)A claim that is mailed to the receiver in accordance with the instructions established under paragraph (a) of this section shall be deemed to be filed as of the date of postmark.
