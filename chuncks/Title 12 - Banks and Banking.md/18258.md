
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 249.3 Definitions.
####### Category IV Board-regulated institution

means:

(1) A covered depository institution holding company that is identified as a Category IV banking organization pursuant to 12 CFR 252.5 or 12 CFR 238.10, as applicable; or

(2) A U.S. intermediate holding company that is identified as a Category IV banking organization pursuant to 12 CFR 252.5.
