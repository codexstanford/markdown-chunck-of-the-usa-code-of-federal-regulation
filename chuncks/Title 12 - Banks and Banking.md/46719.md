
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1267 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK INVESTMENTS
##### § 1267.1 Definitions.
###### Interest-only stripped security

means a class of mortgage-backed or asset-backed security that is allocated only the interest payments made on the underlying mortgages or loans and receives no principal payments.
