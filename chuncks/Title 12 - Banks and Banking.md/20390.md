
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 265 - RULES REGARDING DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY
##### Subpart B - Delegations of Authority
###### ยง 265.10 Functions delegated to the Director of the Division of Monetary Affairs.
####### Term Deposit Facility (TDF) test operations.

(a)With the concurrence of the General Counsel, and in consultation with the Chair if feasible, to adjust the terms and conditions of individual TDF test operations that raise significant technical or operational issues, including but not limited to the authority to:

(1) Delay the open of a TDF operation;

(2) Extend the close of a TDF operation;

(3) Reschedule a TDF operation; and

(4) Delay the announcement of TDF operation results.
