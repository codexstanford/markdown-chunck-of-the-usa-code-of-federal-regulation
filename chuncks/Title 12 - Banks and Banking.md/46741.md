
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1268 - ACQUIRED MEMBER ASSETS
##### § 1268.2 Authorization for acquired member assets.
###### General.

(a)Each Bank is authorized to invest in assets that qualify as AMA, subject to the requirements of this part and part 1272 of this chapter.
