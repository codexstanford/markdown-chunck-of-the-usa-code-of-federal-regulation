
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1212 - POST-EMPLOYMENT RESTRICTION FOR SENIOR EXAMINERS
##### Subpart B - Post-Employment Restriction for Senior Examiners
###### § 1212.2 Definitions.
####### Consultant

means a person who works directly on matters for, or on behalf of, a regulated entity or the Office of Finance.
