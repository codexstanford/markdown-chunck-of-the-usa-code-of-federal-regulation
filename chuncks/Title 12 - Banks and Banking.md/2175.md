
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

### PART 6 - PROMPT CORRECTIVE ACTION
#### Subpart A - Capital Categories
##### ยง 6.1 Authority, purpose, scope, other supervisory authority, disclosure of capital categories, and transition procedures.
###### Disclosure of capital categories.

(e)The assignment of an insured national bank, insured Federal branch, or insured Federal savings association under this subpart within a particular capital category is for purposes of implementing and applying the provisions of section 38. Unless permitted by the OCC or otherwise required by law, no national bank or Federal savings association may state in any advertisement or promotional material its capital category under this subpart or that the OCC or any other Federal banking agency has assigned the national bank or Federal savings association to a particular capital category.
