
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 208.74 What happens if the state member bank or a depository institution affiliate fails to continue to meet certain requirements?
####### Debt rating or alternative requirement.

(b)If a state member bank does not continue to meet any applicable debt rating or alternative requirement of ยง 208.71(b), the bank may not, directly or through a subsidiary, purchase or acquire any additional equity capital of any financial subsidiary until the bank restores its compliance with the requirements of that section.
