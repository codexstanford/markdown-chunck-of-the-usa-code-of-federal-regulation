
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter C - Enterprises
#### PART 1240 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY OF ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart D - Risk-Weighted Assets - Standardized Approach
###### § 1240.42 Risk-weighted assets for CRT and other securitization exposures.
####### Repo-style transactions, eligible margin loans, and derivative contracts.

(3)The exposure amount of a securitization exposure that is a repo-style transaction, eligible margin loan, or derivative contract (other than a credit derivative) is the exposure amount of the transaction as calculated under § 1240.36 or § 1240.39, as applicable.
