
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 50.3 Definitions.
###### Controlled subsidiary

means, with respect to a company or a national bank or Federal savings association, a consolidated subsidiary or a company that otherwise meets the definition of "subsidiary" in section 2(d) of the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956 (12 U.S.C. 1841(d)).
