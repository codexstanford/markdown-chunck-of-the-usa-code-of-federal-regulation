
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### Appendix C to Part 30 - OCC Guidelines Establishing Standards for Residential Mortgage Lending Practices

b.means any national bank, Federal savings association, Federal branch or Federal agency of a foreign bank, and any operating subsidiary thereof that is subject to these Guidelines.
