
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Subpart B-Open-End Credit
##### § 1026.16 Advertising.
###### Promotional period.

(C)A "promotional period" means a period of time, less than the full term of the loan, that the promotional rate or promotional payment may be applicable.
