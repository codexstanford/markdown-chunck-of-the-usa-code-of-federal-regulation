###### Combined deposit account and credit account statements.

1.Some financial institutions provide information about deposit account and open-end credit account activity on one periodic statement. For purposes of providing disclosures on the front of the first page of the periodic statement pursuant to ยง 226.7(b)(13), the first page of such a combined statement shall be the page on which credit transactions first appear.
