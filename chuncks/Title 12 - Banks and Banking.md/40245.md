
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Refinanced obligations.

2.On or after December 9, 1987, when a credit obligation is refinanced, as defined in § 1026.20(a), the new obligation is subject to this section if it is dwelling-secured and allows for increases in the interest rate.
