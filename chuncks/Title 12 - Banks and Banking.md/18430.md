####### Balance-sheet metric.

(a)Unless otherwise provided in this subpart, an NSFR regulatory capital element, NSFR liability, or asset that is not included on a Board-regulated institution's balance sheet is not assigned an RSF factor or ASF factor, as applicable; and an NSFR regulatory capital element, NSFR liability, or asset that is included on a Board-regulated institution's balance sheet is assigned an RSF factor or ASF factor, as applicable.
