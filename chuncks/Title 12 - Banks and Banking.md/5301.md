
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 50 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
#### Subpart D - Total Net Cash Outflow
##### ยง 50.32 Outflow amounts.
###### Mortgage commitment outflow amount.

(d)The mortgage commitment outflow amount as of a calculation date is 10 percent of the amount of funds the national bank or Federal savings association has contractually committed for its own origination of retail mortgages that can be drawn upon 30 calendar days or less from such calculation date.
