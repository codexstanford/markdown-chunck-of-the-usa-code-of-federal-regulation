
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 242 - DEFINITIONS RELATING TO TITLE I OF THE DODD-FRANK ACT (REGULATION PP)
##### Appendix A to Part 242 - Financial Activities for Purposes of Title I of the Dodd-Frank Act
###### Services offered in connection with banking services.

(20)In connection with offering banking services, providing notary public services, selling postage stamps and postage-paid envelopes, providing vehicle registration services, and selling public transportation tickets and tokens.
