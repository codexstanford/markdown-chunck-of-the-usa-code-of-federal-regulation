
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 330 - DEPOSIT INSURANCE COVERAGE
##### ยง 330.15 Accounts held by government depositors.
###### Split of authority or control over public unit funds.

(3)If the exercise of authority or control over the funds of a public unit requires action by, or the consent of, two or more officers, employees, or agents of such public unit, then they will be treated as one "official custodian" for the purposes of this section.
