
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1207 - MINORITY AND WOMEN OUTREACH PROGRAM
##### § 1207.2 FHFA workforce diversity; Equal Employment Opportunity Program.
###### Workforce diversity.

(c)FHFA shall not discriminate in employment against any person because of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, age, genetic information, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or status as a parent.
