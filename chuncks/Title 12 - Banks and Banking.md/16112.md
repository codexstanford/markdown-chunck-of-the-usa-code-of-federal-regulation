### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 235 - DEBIT CARD INTERCHANGE FEES AND ROUTING (REGULATION II)
##### Appendix A to Part 235 - Official Board Commentary on Regulation II
###### Cash withdrawal at the point of sale.

4.The term "electronic debit transaction" includes a transaction in which a cardholder uses the debit card both to make a purchase and to withdraw cash (known as a "cash-back transaction").
