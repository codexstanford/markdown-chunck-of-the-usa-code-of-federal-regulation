
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 34 - REAL ESTATE LENDING AND APPRAISALS
#### Subpart G - Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans
##### Appendix C to Subpart G of Part 34 - OCC Interpretations
###### Price at which the seller acquired the property.

5.The price at which the seller acquired the property refers to the amount paid by the seller to acquire the property. The price at which the seller acquired the property does not include the cost of financing the property.
