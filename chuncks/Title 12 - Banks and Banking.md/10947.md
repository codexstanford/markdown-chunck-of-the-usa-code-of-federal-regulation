
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System

##### Subpart F-General Provisions of Section 23B
###### ยง 223.54 What advertisements and statements are prohibited by section 23B?
####### In general.

(a)A member bank and its affiliates may not publish any advertisement or enter into any agreement stating or suggesting that the member bank will in any way be responsible for the obligations of its affiliates.
