
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Relationship to payment allocation requirements in § 1026.53.

3.Card issuers must comply with the payment allocation requirements in § 1026.53 even if doing so will result in the loss of a grace period.
