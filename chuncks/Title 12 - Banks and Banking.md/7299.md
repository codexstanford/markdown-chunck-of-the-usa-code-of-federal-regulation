
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 205-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
##### Supplement I to Part 205-Official Staff Interpretations
###### Account-holding institution as third party.

4.Transfers to the account-holding institution (by ATM, for example) must show the institution as the recipient, unless other information on the statement (such as, "loan payment from checking") clearly indicates that the payment was to the account-holding institution.
