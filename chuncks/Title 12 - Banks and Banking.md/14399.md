
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 226-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
##### Supplement I to Part 226-Official Staff Interpretations
###### Disclosure of downpayment.

1.The total downpayment as a dollar amount or percentage must be shown, but the word "downpayment" need not be used in making this disclosure. For example, "10% cash required from buyer" or "credit terms require minimum $100 trade-in" would suffice.
