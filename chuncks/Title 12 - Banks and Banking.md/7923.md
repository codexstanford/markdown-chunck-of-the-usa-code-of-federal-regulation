
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System

##### Subpart F-Miscellaneous Requirements
###### ยง 208.62 Suspicious activity reports.
####### Confidentiality of SARs.

(j)SARs are confidential. Any member bank subpoenaed or otherwise requested to disclose a SAR or the information contained in a SAR shall decline to produce the SAR or to provide any information that would disclose that a SAR has been prepared or filed citing this section, applicable law (e.g., 31 U.S.C. 5318(g)), or both, and notify the Board.
