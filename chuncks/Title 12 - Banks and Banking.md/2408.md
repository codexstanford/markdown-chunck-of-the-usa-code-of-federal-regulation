
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 7 - ACTIVITIES AND OPERATIONS
#### Subpart B - Corporate Practices
##### ยง 7.2016 Restricting transfer of national bank stock and record dates; stock certificates.
###### Restricting transfer of stock and record dates,Conditions for stock transfer.

(a)-(1)Under 12 U.S.C. 52, a national bank may impose conditions upon the transfer of its stock reasonably calculated to simplify the work of the bank with respect to stock transfers, voting at shareholders' meetings, and related matters and to protect it against fraudulent transfers.
