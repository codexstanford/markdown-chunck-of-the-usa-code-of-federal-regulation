###### ยง 229.15 General disclosure requirements.
####### Multiple accounts and multiple account holders.

(c)A bank need not give multiple disclosures to a customer that holds multiple accounts if the accounts are subject to the same availability policies. Similarly, a bank need not give separate disclosures to each customer on a jointly held account.
