
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

### Subchapter A - Procedure and Rules of Practice

##### Subpart B - General Rules of Procedure
###### ยง 308.107 Document discovery.

(a) Parties to proceedings set forth at ยง 308.1 of the Uniform Rules and as provided in the Local Rules may obtain discovery only through the production of documents. No other form of discovery shall be allowed.
