
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 373 - CREDIT RISK RETENTION
##### Subpart A - Authority, Purpose, Scope and Definitions
###### ยง 373.2 Definitions.
####### Collateral,collateralize

means, with respect to any issuance of ABS interests, the assets that provide the cash flow and the servicing assets that support such cash flow for the ABS interests irrespective of the legal structure of issuance, including security interests in assets or other property of the issuing entity, fractional undivided property interests in the assets or other property of the issuing entity, or any other property interest in or rights to cash flow from such assets and related servicing assets. Assets or other propertyan issuance of ABS interests if the assets or property serve as collateral for such issuance.
