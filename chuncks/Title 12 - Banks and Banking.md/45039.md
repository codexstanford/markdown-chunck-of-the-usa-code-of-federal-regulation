
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1230 - EXECUTIVE COMPENSATION
##### § 1230.4 Prior approval of termination agreements of Enterprises.
###### Covered agreements or contracts.

(b)An agreement or contract that provides for termination payments to an executive officer of an Enterprise that was entered into before October 28, 1992,is not retroactively subject to approval or disapproval by the Director. However, any renegotiation, amendment, or change to such an agreement or contract shall be considered as entering into an agreement or contract that is subject to approval by the Director.
