
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

##### ยง 28.11 Definitions.
###### International banking facility

(q)means a set of asset and liability accounts segregated on the books and records of a depository institution, a United States branch or agency of a foreign bank, or an Edge corporation or Agreement corporation, that includes only international banking facility time deposits and extensions of credit.
