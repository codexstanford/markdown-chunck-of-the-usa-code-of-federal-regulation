
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1239 - RESPONSIBILITIES OF BOARDS OF DIRECTORS, CORPORATE PRACTICES, AND CORPORATE GOVERNANCE
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1239.2 Definitions.
####### Employee

means an individual, other than an executive officer, who works part-time, full-time, or temporarily for a regulated entity.
