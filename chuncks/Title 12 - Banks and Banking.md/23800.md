
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter III - Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
### Subchapter B - Regulations and Statements of General Policy
#### PART 329 - LIQUIDITY RISK MEASUREMENT STANDARDS
##### Subpart K - Net Stable Funding Ratio
###### ยง 329.107 Calculation of NSFR derivatives amounts.
####### Potential valuation changes.

(5)(i) An amount equal to 5 percent of the sum of the gross derivative values of the FDIC-supervised institution that are liabilities, as calculated under paragraph (b)(5)(ii) of this section, for each of the FDIC-supervised institution's derivative transactions not subject to a qualifying master netting agreement and each of its QMNA netting sets, multiplied by an RSF factor of 100 percent;
