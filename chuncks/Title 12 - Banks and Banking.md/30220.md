
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 702 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY
##### Subpart B - Alternative Prompt Corrective Action for New Credit Unions
###### ยง 702.201 Scope and definition.
####### Effect of spin-offs.

(c)A credit union formed as the result of a "spin-off" of a group from the field of membership of an existing credit union is deemed to be in operation since the effective date of the spin-off. A credit union whose total assets decline below $10 million because a group within its field of membership has been spun-off is deemed "new" if it has been in operation less than 10 years.
