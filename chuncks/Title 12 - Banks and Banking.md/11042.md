
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

####### Reports of bank holding companies.

(b)Each bank holding company shall furnish, in the manner and form prescribed by the Board, an annual report of the company's operations for the fiscal year in which it becomes a bank holding company, and for each fiscal year during which it remains a bank holding company.
