
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1022-FAIR CREDIT REPORTING (REGULATION V)
#### Subpart O-Miscellaneous Duties of Consumer Reporting Agencies
##### § 1022.142 Prohibition on inclusion of adverse information in consumer reporting in cases of human trafficking.
###### Prohibition on inclusion of adverse information of trafficking victims.

(c)A consumer reporting agency may not furnish a consumer report containing any adverse item of information about a consumer that resulted from a severe form of trafficking in persons or sex trafficking if the consumer has provided trafficking documentation as defined under paragraph (b)(6) of this section to the consumer reporting agency.
