
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Subpart B-Open-End Credit
##### § 1026.10 Payments.
###### Specific requirements for payments,General rule.

(b)-(1)A creditor may specify reasonable requirements for payments that enable most consumers to make conforming payments.
