
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1227 - SUSPENDED COUNTERPARTY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1227.3 Scope of suspension orders.
####### No effect on residential mortgage loans secured by respondent's own personal or household residence.

(d)A final suspension order issued pursuant to this part shall have no effect on any transaction involving a residential mortgage loan if the loan is secured by the respondent's own personal or household residence.
