
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 22 - LOANS IN AREAS HAVING SPECIAL FLOOD HAZARDS
#### § 22.2 Definitions.
##### SFIP

(n)means, for purposes of §§ 22.2(k), a standard flood insurance policy issued under the NFIP in effect as of the date private flood insurance is provided to a national bank or Federal savings association.
