
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1002-EQUAL CREDIT OPPORTUNITY ACT (REGULATION B)
#### § 1002.5 Rules concerning requests for information.
##### Permanent residency and immigration status.

(e)A creditor may inquire about the permanent residency and immigration status of an applicant or any other person in connection with a credit transaction.
