
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1006-DEBT COLLECTION PRACTICES (REGULATION F)
#### Subpart B-Rules for FDCPA Debt Collectors
##### § 1006.38 Disputes and requests for original-creditor information.
###### Disputes,Failure to dispute.

(d)-(1)The failure of a consumer to dispute the validity of a debt does not constitute a legal admission of liability by the consumer.
