
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1030-TRUTH IN SAVINGS (REGULATION DD)
#### § 1030.3 General disclosure requirements.
##### Relation to Regulation E (12 CFR Part 1005).,et seq.

(c)Disclosures required by and provided in accordance with the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (15 U.S.C. 1693) and its implementing Regulation E (12 CFR Part 1005) that are also required by this part may be substituted for the disclosures required by this part.
