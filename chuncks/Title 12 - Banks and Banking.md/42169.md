
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1026-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)
#### Supplement I to Part 1026-Official Interpretations
##### Variable-rate multiple-advance loans.

2.The hypothetical disclosure required in variable-rate transactions by § 1026.18(f)(1)(iv) is not required for multiple-advance loans disclosed pursuant to appendix D, part I.
