
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 34 - REAL ESTATE LENDING AND APPRAISALS
#### Subpart G - Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans
##### Appendix C to Subpart G of Part 34 - OCC Interpretations
###### "Provide."

1.For purposes of the requirement to provide a copy of the appraisal within a specified time under ยง 34.203(f)(2), "provide" means "deliver." Delivery occurs three business days after mailing or delivering the copies to the last-known address of the applicant, or when evidence indicates actual receipt by the applicant (which, in the case of electronic receipt, must be based upon consent that complies with the E-Sign Act), whichever is earlier.
