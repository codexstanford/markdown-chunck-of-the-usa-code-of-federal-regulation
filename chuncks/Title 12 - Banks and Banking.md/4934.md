
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 45 - MARGIN AND CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS FOR COVERED SWAP ENTITIES
#### § 45.9 Cross-border application of margin requirements.
##### Transactions to which this rule does not apply.

(a)The requirements of §§ 45.3 through 45.8 and §§ 45.10 through 45.12 shall not apply to any foreign non-cleared swap or foreign non-cleared security-based swap of a foreign covered swap entity.
