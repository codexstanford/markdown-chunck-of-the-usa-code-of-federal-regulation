
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1081-RULES OF PRACTICE FOR ADJUDICATION PROCEEDINGS
#### Subpart C-Hearings
##### § 1081.303 Evidence.
###### Burden of proof.

(a)Enforcement counsel will have the burden of proof of the ultimate issue(s) of the Bureau's claims at the hearing.
