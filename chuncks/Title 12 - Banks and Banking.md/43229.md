
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1080-RULES RELATING TO INVESTIGATIONS
#### § 1080.6 Civil investigative demands.
##### Extensions of time.

(2)The Assistant Director of the Office of Enforcement and the Deputy Assistant Directors of the Office of Enforcement are authorized to rule upon requests for extensions of time within which to file such petitions. Requests for extensions of time are disfavored.
