
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1278 - VOLUNTARY MERGERS OF FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANKS
##### § 1278.1 Definitions.
###### Continuing Bank

means a Bank that will exist as the result of a merger of two or more Constituent Banks, and when used in the singular shall include the plural.
