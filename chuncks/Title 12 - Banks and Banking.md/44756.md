
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1221 - MARGIN AND CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS FOR COVERED SWAP ENTITIES
##### § 1221.2 Definitions.
###### Initial margin

means the collateral as calculated in accordance with § 1221.8 that is posted or collected in connection with a non-cleared swap or non-cleared security-based swap.
