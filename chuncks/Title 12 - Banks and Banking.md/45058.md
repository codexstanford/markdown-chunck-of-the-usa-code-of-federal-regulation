
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1231 - GOLDEN PARACHUTE AND INDEMNIFICATION PAYMENTS
##### § 1231.2 Definitions.
###### Permitted

means, with regard to any agreement, that the agreement either does not require the Director's consent under this part or has received the Director's consent in accordance with this part.
