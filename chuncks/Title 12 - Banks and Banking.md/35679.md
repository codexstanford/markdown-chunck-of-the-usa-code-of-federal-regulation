
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1011 - PURCHASERS' REVOCATION RIGHTS, SALES PRACTICES AND STANDARDS (REGULATION K)
#### Subpart B - Sales Practices and Standards
##### § 1011.27 Fair housing.
###### et seq.,

Title VIII of the Civil Rights Act of 1968, 42 U.S.C. 3601,and its implementing regulations and guidelines apply to land sales transactions to the extent warranted by the facts of the transaction.
