
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1233 - REPORTING OF FRAUDULENT FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS
##### § 1233.3 Reporting.
###### No waiver of privilege.

(e)A regulated entity does not waive any privilege it may possess under any applicable law as a consequence of reporting fraud or possible fraud under this part.
