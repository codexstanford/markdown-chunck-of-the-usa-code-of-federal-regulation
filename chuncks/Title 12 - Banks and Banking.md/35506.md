
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1010 - LAND REGISTRATION (REGULATION J)
#### Subpart B - Reporting Requirements
##### § 1010.109 Title to the property and land use.
###### Oil, gas, and mineral rights.

(4)If oil, gas or mineral rights have been reserved, insert the following statement or one substantially the same in the narrative answer under the caption "oil, gas, and mineral rights": "The (indicate oil, gas, or mineral rights) to (state which lots) in this subdivision will not belong to the purchaser of those lots. The exercise of these rights could affect the use, enjoyment and value of your lot."
