
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter E - Housing Goals and Mission
#### PART 1281 - FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK HOUSING GOALS
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 1281.1 Definitions.
####### Seasoned mortgage

means a mortgage on which the date of the mortgage note is more than one year before the Bank purchased the mortgage.
