
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1214 - AVAILABILITY OF NON-PUBLIC INFORMATION
##### § 1214.3 General rule.
###### In general, FHFA Employees.

(b)Except as authorized in writing by the Director, no FHFA employee in possession or control of non-public information may disclose or permit the use or disclosure of such information in any manner or for any purpose.
