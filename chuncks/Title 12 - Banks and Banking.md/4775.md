
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury

###### Fund-of-funds investments.

(ii)If a banking entity organizes and offers a covered fund pursuant to ยง 44.11 for the purpose of investing in other covered funds (a "fund of funds") and that fund of funds itself invests in another covered fund that the banking entity is permitted to own, then the banking entity's permitted investment in that other fund shall include any investment by the banking entity in that other fund, as well as the banking entity's pro-rata share of any ownership interest in the fund that is held through the fund of funds.
