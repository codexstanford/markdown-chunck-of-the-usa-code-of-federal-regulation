
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System

(g)-(1)Except as provided in paragraph (g)(4) of this section, unless the Reserve Bank, upon notice to the applicant, refers the application to the Board for decision because action under delegated authority is not appropriate, the Reserve Bank shall approve an application under this section:
