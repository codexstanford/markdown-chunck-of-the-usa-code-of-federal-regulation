
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

#### PART 723-MEMBER BUSINESS LOANS; COMMERCIAL LENDING
##### ยง 723.7 Prohibited activities.
###### Equity agreements/joint ventures.

(b)A federally insured credit union may not grant a commercial loan if any additional income received by the federally insured credit union or its senior management employees is tied to the profit or sale of any business or commercial endeavor that benefits from the proceeds of the loan.
