
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1024-REAL ESTATE SETTLEMENT PROCEDURES ACT (REGULATION X)
#### Subpart C-Mortgage Servicing
##### § 1024.31 Definitions.
###### inter vivos

(5) A transfer into antrust in which the borrower is and remains a beneficiary and which does not relate to a transfer of rights of occupancy in the property.
