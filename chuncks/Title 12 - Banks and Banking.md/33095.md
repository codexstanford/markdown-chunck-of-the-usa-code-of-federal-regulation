
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1002-EQUAL CREDIT OPPORTUNITY ACT (REGULATION B)
#### § 1002.5 Rules concerning requests for information.
##### Information about a spouse or former spouse,General rule.

(c)-(1)Except as permitted in this paragraph, a creditor may not request any information concerning the spouse or former spouse of an applicant.
