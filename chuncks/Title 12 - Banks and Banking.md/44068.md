
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1203 - EQUAL ACCESS TO JUSTICE ACT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1203.2 Definitions.
####### Fees and other expenses

means reasonable attorney or agent fees, the reasonable expenses of expert witnesses, and the reasonable cost of any study, analysis, engineering report, or test, which the agency finds necessary for the preparation of the eligible party's case.
