### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 226-TRUTH IN LENDING (REGULATION Z)

###### How notice must be provided.

1.Notice given in a normal business manner-for example, by mail, telephone, or personal visit-is effective even though it is not given to, or does not reach, some particular person within the issuer's organization.
