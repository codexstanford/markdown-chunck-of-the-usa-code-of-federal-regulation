
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1005-ELECTRONIC FUND TRANSFERS (REGULATION E)
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1005.4 General disclosure requirements; jointly offered services.
###### Foreign language disclosures.

(2)Disclosures required under this part may be made in a language other than English, provided that the disclosures are made available in English upon the consumer's request.
