
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 7 - ACTIVITIES AND OPERATIONS
#### Subpart A - National Bank and Federal Savings Association Powers
##### ยง 7.1003 Money lent by a national bank at banking offices or at facilities other than banking offices.
###### In general.

(a)For purposes of what constitutes a branch within the meaning of 12 U.S.C. 36(j) and 12 CFR 5.30, "money" is deemed to be "lent" only at the place, if any, where the borrower in-person receives loan proceeds directly from national bank funds:

(1) From the lending national bank or its operating subsidiary; or

(2) At a facility that is established by the lending national bank or its operating subsidiary.
