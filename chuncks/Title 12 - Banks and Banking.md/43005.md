### PART 1070-DISCLOSURE OF RECORDS AND INFORMATION
#### Subpart E-Privacy Act
##### § 1070.52 Fees.
###### Copies of records.

(a)The CFPB shall provide the requester with copies of records requested pursuant to § 1070.53 at the same cost charged for duplication of records under § 1070.22.
