
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1202-FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT
##### § 1202.2 What do the terms in this part mean?
###### Appeals Officer or FOIA Appeals Officer

means a person designated by FHFA who processes appeals of denied FOIA requests for FHFA records.
