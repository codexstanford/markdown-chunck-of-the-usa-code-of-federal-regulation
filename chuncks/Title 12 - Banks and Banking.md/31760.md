
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 712 - CREDIT UNION SERVICE ORGANIZATIONS (CUSOs)
##### ยง 712.2 How much can an FCU invest in or loan to CUSOs, and what parties may participate?
###### Investments.

(a)An FCU's total investments in CUSOs must not exceed, in the aggregate, 1% of its paid-in and unimpaired capital and surplus as of its last calendar year-end financial report.
