
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1263 - MEMBERS OF THE BANKS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### § 1263.1 Definitions.
####### De novo insured depository institution

means an insured depository institution with a charter approved by its appropriate regulator within the three years prior to the date the institution applies for Bank membership.
