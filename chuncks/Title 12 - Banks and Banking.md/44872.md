
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1223 - MINORITY AND WOMEN INCLUSION
##### Subpart C - Minority and Women Inclusion and Diversity at Regulated Entities
###### § 1223.20 Office of Minority and Women Inclusion.
####### Responsibilities.

(c)Each Office of Minority and Women Inclusion, or the office designated to perform the responsibilities of this part, is responsible for leading the regulated entity's board-approved strategies, for fulfilling the requirements of this part, 12 U.S.C. 1833e(b) and 4520, and such standards and requirements as the Director may issue hereunder.
