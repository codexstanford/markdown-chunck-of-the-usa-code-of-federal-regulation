
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1041-PAYDAY, VEHICLE TITLE, AND CERTAIN HIGH-COST INSTALLMENT LOANS
#### Subpart A-General
##### § 1041.3 Scope of coverage; exclusions; exemptions.
###### Tax year.

(h)For purposes of paragraph (f) of this section, "tax year" has the meaning attributed to it by the IRS as set forth in IRS Publication 538, which provides that a "tax year" is an annual accounting period for keeping records and reporting income and expenses.
