
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter D - Federal Home Loan Banks
#### PART 1271 - MISCELLANEOUS FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK OPERATIONS AND AUTHORITIES
##### Subpart D - Financing Corporation Operations
###### § 1271.37 Non-administrative expenses; assessments.
####### Receivership proceeds,Authority.

(c)-(1)To the extent the amounts collected under paragraph (b) of this section are insufficient to pay the non-administrative expenses of the Financing Corporation approved under § 1271.35, the Financing Corporation shall have authority to require the FDIC to transfer receivership proceeds to the Financing Corporation in accordance with section 21(f)(3) of the Bank Act (12 U.S.C. 1441(f)(3)).
