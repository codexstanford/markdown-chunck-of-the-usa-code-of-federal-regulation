
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter I - Comptroller of the Currency, Department of the Treasury
### PART 3 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY STANDARDS
#### Subpart B - Capital Ratio Requirements and Buffers
##### ยง 3.10 Minimum capital requirements.
###### Federal savings association tangible capital ratio.

(5)A Federal savings association's tangible capital ratio is the ratio of the Federal savings association's core capital (tier 1 capital) to average total assets as calculated under this subpart B. For purposes of this paragraph (b)(5), the term "total assets" means "total assets" as defined in part 6, subpart A of this chapter, subject to subpart G of this part.
