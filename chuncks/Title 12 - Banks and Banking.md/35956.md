
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter X—Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection
### PART 1015-MORTGAGE ASSISTANCE RELIEF SERVICES (REGULATION O)
#### § 1015.2 Definitions.
##### Consumer-Specific Commercial Communication

(2)means a commercial communication that occurs prior to the consumer agreeing to permit the provider to seek offers of mortgage assistance relief on behalf of the consumer, or otherwise agreeing to use the mortgage assistance relief service, and that is directed at a specific consumer.
