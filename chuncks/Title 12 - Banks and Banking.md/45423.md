
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter B - Entity Regulations
#### PART 1238 - STRESS TESTING OF REGULATED ENTITIES
##### § 1238.2 Definitions.
###### Stress test

is a process to assess the potential impact on an Enterprise of economic and financial conditions ("scenarios") on the consolidated earnings, losses, and capital of the Enterprise over a set planning horizon, taking into account the current condition of the Enterprise and the Enterprise's risks, exposures, strategies, and activities.
