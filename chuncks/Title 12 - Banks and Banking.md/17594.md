###### Joint determination regarding deficient resolution plans.

(b)If the Board and Corporation jointly determine that the resolution plan of a covered company submitted under ยง 243.4 is not credible or would not facilitate an orderly resolution of the covered company under the Bankruptcy Code, the Board and Corporation shall jointly notify the covered company in writing of such determination. Any joint notice provided under this paragraph (b) shall be provided pursuant to paragraph (f) of this section and shall identify the deficiencies identified by the Board and Corporation in the resolution plan.
