
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1217 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
##### § 1217.5 Request for approval by the Department of Justice.
###### General.

(a)If the reviewing official determines that the report of investigation supports an action under this part, the reviewing official must submit a written request to the Department of Justice for approval to issue a notice under § 1217.6.
