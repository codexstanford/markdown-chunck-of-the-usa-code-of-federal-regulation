
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

###### ยง 192.515 Information to be filed with Federal banking agency prior to repurchase of shares.
####### Notice requirement.

(a)To repurchase stock in the first year following conversion, other than repurchases under ยง 192.510(a)(3) or (4), a savings association must file a written notice with the appropriate OCC licensing office if Federally chartered, and with the appropriate FDIC region if State-chartered. The savings association must provide the following information:

(1) The proposed repurchase program;

(2) The effect of the repurchases on the savings association's regulatory capital; and
