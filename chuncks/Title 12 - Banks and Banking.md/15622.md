
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 229 - AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS AND COLLECTION OF CHECKS (REGULATION CC)
##### Subpart C - Collection of Checks
###### ยง 229.31 Paying bank's responsibility for return of checks and notices of nonpayment.
####### Exceptions to the expeditious return of checks and notice of nonpayment requirements.

(d)The expeditious return and notice of nonpayment requirements of paragraphs (b) and (c) of this section do not apply if-

(1) The check is deposited in a depositary bank that is not subject to subpart B of this part; or

(2) A paying bank is unable to identify the depositary bank with respect to the check.
