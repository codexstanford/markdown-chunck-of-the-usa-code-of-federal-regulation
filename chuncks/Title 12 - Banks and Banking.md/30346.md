
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter VII - National Credit Union Administration
### Subchapter A - Regulations Affecting Credit Unions
#### PART 702 - CAPITAL ADEQUACY
##### Subpart D - Subordinated Debt, Grandfathered Secondary Capital, and Regulatory Capital
###### ยง 702.402 Definitions.
####### Senior Executive Officer,e.g.,

means a credit union's chief executive officer (for example, president or treasurer/manager), any assistant chief executive officer (any assistant president, any vice president or any assistant treasurer/manager) and the chief financial officer (controller). The term "Senior Executive Officer" also includes employees and contractors of an entity, such as a consulting firm, hired to perform the functions of positions covered by the term Senior Executive Officer.
