
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter XII—Federal Housing Finance Agency
### Subchapter A—Organization and Operations
#### PART 1217 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
##### § 1217.2 Definitions.
###### Benefit

means anything of value, including, but not limited to, any advantage, preference, privilege, license, permit, favorable decision, ruling, or status.
