
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking

####### Calculation of prepaid assessment,Prepaid assessment,Fourth quarter 2009 and all of 2010.

(b)-(1)-(i)An institution's prepaid assessment for the fourth quarter of 2009 and for all of 2010 shall be determined by multiplying its prepaid assessment rate as defined in paragraph (b)(2) of this section times the corresponding prepaid assessment base for each quarter as determined pursuant to paragraph (b)(3) of this section.
