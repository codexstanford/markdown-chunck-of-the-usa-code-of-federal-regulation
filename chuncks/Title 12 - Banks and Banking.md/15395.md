
# Title 12 - Banks and Banking
## Chapter II - Federal Reserve System
### Subchapter A - Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
#### PART 228-COMMUNITY REINVESTMENT (REGULATION BB)
##### Subpart C-Records, Reporting, and Disclosure Requirements
###### ยง 228.41 Assessment area delineation.
####### In general.

(a)A bank shall delineate one or more assessment areas within which the Board evaluates the bank's record of helping to meet the credit needs of its community. The Board does not evaluate the bank's delineation of its assessment area(s) as a separate performance criterion, but the Board reviews the delineation for compliance with the requirements of this section.
