
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1194 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES
#### Appendix C to Part 1194 - Functional Performance Criteria and Technical Requirements
##### Unobstructed Forward Reach.

407.8.3.1Where the operable part is located at the leading edge of the maximum protrusion within the length of the vertical reference plane of the ICT, the operable part shall be 48 inches (1220 mm) high maximum and 15 inches (380 mm) high minimum above the floor.
