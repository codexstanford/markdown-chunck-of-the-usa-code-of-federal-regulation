#### ยง 1008.21 Petitions for amendment: Time limits for processing.
##### Decision on petition.

(b)A petition for amendment shall be processed promptly. A determination whether to accept or reject the petition for amendment shall be made within 30 working days after receipt of the petition by the system manager responsible for the system containing the challenged record.
