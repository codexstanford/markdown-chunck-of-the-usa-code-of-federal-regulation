
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 200 - ORGANIZATION, FUNCTIONS, AND PROCEDURES
#### Subpart A - Organization
##### ยง 200.2 Field organization.
###### Ranger districts.

(2)Each district may include a portion of a national forest, a national grassland or portion thereof, a national recreation area, a wilderness or primitive area, and other lands administered by the Forest Service. Each district has a headquarters office and is supervised by a District Ranger (or Area Ranger in some cases) who is responsible to the Forest Supervisor.
