##### Subpart F - Procedures
###### ยง 1211.620 Hearings.
####### Time and place of hearing.

(b)Hearings shall be held at the offices of the agency in Washington, DC, at a time fixed by the designated agency official unless the official determines that the convenience of the applicant or recipient or of the agency requires that another place be selected. Hearings shall be held before a hearing officer designated in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 556(b).
