
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

##### ยง 1237.12 What record elements must be created and preserved for permanent audiovisual records?
###### Video recordings.

(b)(1) For analog videotapes, the original or earliest generation videotape using industrial-quality or professional videotapes for originals and a copy for reference.
