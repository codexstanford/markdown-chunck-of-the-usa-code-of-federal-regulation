
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XII - National Archives and Records Administration
### Subchapter B - Records Management
#### PART 1220 - FEDERAL RECORDS; GENERAL
##### ยง 1220.18 What definitions apply to the regulations in Subchapter B?
###### Recordkeeping requirements

means all statements in statutes, regulations, and agency directives or other authoritative issuances, that provide general or specific requirements for Federal agency personnel on particular records to be created and maintained by the agency.
