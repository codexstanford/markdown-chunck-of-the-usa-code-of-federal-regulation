
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 223 - SALE AND DISPOSAL OF NATIONAL FOREST SYSTEM TIMBER
#### Subpart C - Suspension and Debarment of Timber Purchasers
##### ยง 223.138 Procedures for debarment.
###### Notice of debarring official's decision.

(7)(i) The purchaser and any affiliates involved shall be given prompt notice of the debarring official's decision by certified mail, return receipt requested. If the debarring official decides to impose debarment, the notice shall:

(A) Refer to the notice of proposed debarment:

(B) Specify the reasons for debarment;

(C) State the period of debarment, including effective dates (see ยง 223.139); and

(D) Specify any limitations on the terms of the debarment.

(ii) The debarring official shall also promptly notify Regional Foresters and Forest Supervisors of the decision.
