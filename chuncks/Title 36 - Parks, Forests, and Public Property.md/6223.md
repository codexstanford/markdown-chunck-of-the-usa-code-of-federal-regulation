
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

##### ยง 1012.8 How will the Presidio Trust process my Touhy Request?

(a) The Executive Director will decide whether to grant or deny yourRequest. The Presidio Trust's General Counsel, or his or her agent, may negotiate with you or your attorney to refine or limit both the timing and content of yourRequest.
