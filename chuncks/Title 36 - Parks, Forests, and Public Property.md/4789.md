
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 297 - WILD AND SCENIC RIVERS
#### Subpart A - Water Resources Projects
##### ยง 297.3 Definitions.
###### Study river

means a river and the adjacent area within one quarter mile of the banks of the river which is designated for study as a potential addition to the National Wild and Scenic Rivers System pursuant to section 5(a) of the Act.
