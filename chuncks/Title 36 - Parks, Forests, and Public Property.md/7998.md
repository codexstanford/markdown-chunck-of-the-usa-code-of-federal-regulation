
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

### Subchapter C - Public Availability and Use

##### Subpart A - General Information About Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Requests
###### ยง 1250.3 Definitions.
####### FOIA request

(i)means a written request, that cites the Freedom of Information Act, for access to NARA operational records, records of the executive branch of the Federal Government held by NARA, or Presidential or Vice Presidential records in NARA's custody that were created after January 19, 1981.
