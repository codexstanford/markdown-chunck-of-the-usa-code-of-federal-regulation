
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XII - National Archives and Records Administration
### Subchapter B - Records Management
#### PART 1220 - FEDERAL RECORDS; GENERAL
##### ยง 1220.18 What definitions apply to the regulations in Subchapter B?
###### Contingent records

are records whose final disposition is dependent on an action or event, such as sale of property or destruction of a facility, which will take place at some unspecified time in the future.
