
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

#### Subpart D - Subsistence Taking of Fish and Wildlife
##### ยง 242.25 Subsistence taking of fish, wildlife, and shellfish: general regulations.
###### Gillnet

means a net primarily designed to catch fish by entanglement in a mesh that consists of a single sheet of webbing which hangs between cork line and lead line, and which is fished from the surface of the water.
