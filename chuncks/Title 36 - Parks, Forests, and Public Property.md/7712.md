
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XII - National Archives and Records Administration
### Subchapter B - Records Management
#### PART 1230 - UNLAWFUL OR ACCIDENTAL REMOVAL, DEFACING, ALTERATION, OR DESTRUCTION OF RECORDS
##### ยง 1230.3 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Deface

means to obliterate, mar, or spoil the appearance or surface of a record that impairs the usefulness or value of the record.
