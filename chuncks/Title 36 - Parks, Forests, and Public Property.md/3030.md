
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 223 - SALE AND DISPOSAL OF NATIONAL FOREST SYSTEM TIMBER
#### Subpart C - Suspension and Debarment of Timber Purchasers
##### ยง 223.131 Applicability.
###### et seq.

These regulations apply to purchasers of National Forest System timber as well as to those persons who violate the Forest Resources Conservation and Shortage Relief Act of 1990 (16 U.S.C. 620,). These regulations do not apply to Forest Service procurement contracts which are governed by regulations at 41 CFR 4-1.6.
