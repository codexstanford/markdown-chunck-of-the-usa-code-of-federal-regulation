
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture

#### Subpart A - Grazing and Livestock Use on the National Forest System
##### ยง 222.1 Authority and definitions.
###### Range betterment

means rehabilitation, protection, and improvement of National Forest System lands to arrest range deterioration and improve forage conditions, fish and wildlife habitat, watershed protection, and livestock production.
