
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 251 - LAND USES
#### Subpart D - Access to Non-Federal Lands
##### ยง 251.111 Definitions.
###### Access

means the ability of landowners to have ingress and egress to their lands. It does not include rights-of-way for power lines or other utilities.
