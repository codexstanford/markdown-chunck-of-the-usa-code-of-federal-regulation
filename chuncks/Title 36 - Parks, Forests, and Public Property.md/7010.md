
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1195 - STANDARDS FOR ACCESSIBLE MEDICAL DIAGNOSTIC EQUIPMENT
#### Appendix to Part 1195 - Standards for Accessible Medical Diagnostic Equipment
##### Exception:

Components shall be permitted to extend into the wheelchair space at a height of 1inches (38 mm) maximum between 17 inches (430 mm) minimum and 25 inches (635 mm) maximum in depth measured from the leading edge of the breast platform. From 25 inches (635 mm) to 28 inches (710 mm) in depth the height of a component above 1inches (38 mm) shall be beveled at a rate of 2.5:3 maximum.
