
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1010 - ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY
#### ยง 1010.3 Definitions.
##### Project applicant

means an individual, firm, partnership, corporation, joint venture, or other public or private entity other than the Trust (including a combination of more than one such entities) which seeks to demolish, construct, reconstruct, develop, preserve, rehabilitate, or restore real property within the Presidio Trust Area.

(b) If not defined in this part or in this chapter, other terms used in this part have the same meanings as those provided in 40 CFR part 1508.
