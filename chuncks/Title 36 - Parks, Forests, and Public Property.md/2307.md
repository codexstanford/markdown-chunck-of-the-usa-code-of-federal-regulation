
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 211 - ADMINISTRATION
#### Subpart A - Cooperation
##### ยง 211.6 Cooperation in forest investigations or the protection, management, and improvement of the National Forest System.
###### Purpose and scope.

(a)Forest Service officers, when engaged in cooperative activities otherwise authorized, may receive monies from cooperators only for cooperative work in forest investigations or for the protection, management, and improvement of the National Forest System and only in accordance with written cooperative agreements. Management of the National Forest System may include such work as planning, analysis, and related studies, as well as resource activities.
