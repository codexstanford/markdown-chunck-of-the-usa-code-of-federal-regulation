
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

#### Subpart D - Glossary of Terms
##### ยง 910.69 Structural bay.
###### Structural bay

means the distance or span from one vertical structural member fronting on a street to the immediately adjacent vertical structural member fronting on the same street.
