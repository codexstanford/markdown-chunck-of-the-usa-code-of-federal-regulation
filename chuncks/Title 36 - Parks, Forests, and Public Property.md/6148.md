##### ยง 1011.6 When will the Presidio Trust allow a debtor to enter into a repayment agreement?
###### Voluntary repayment.

(a)In response to a notice of a debt, the debtor may propose to the Presidio Trust the voluntary repayment of the debt in lieu of the Presidio Trust taking other collection actions under this part.
