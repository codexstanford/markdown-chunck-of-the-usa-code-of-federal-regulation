
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 7 - SPECIAL REGULATIONS, AREAS OF THE NATIONAL PARK SYSTEM
#### ยง 7.74 Virgin Islands National Park.
##### Fishing.,Provided,,Provided further,

(e)(1) Taking of fishes or any other marine life in any way except with rod or line, the rod or line being held in the hand, is prohibited:That fish may be taken by pots or traps of conventional Virgin Islands design and not larger than five feet at the greatest dimension, and bait fish may be taken by nets of no greater overall length than 20 feet and of mesh not larger than 1 inch stretched:That paragraphs (e) (3), (4), and (5) of this section shall apply.
