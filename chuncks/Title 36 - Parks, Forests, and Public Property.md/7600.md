
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XII - National Archives and Records Administration
### Subchapter B - Records Management
#### PART 1222 - CREATION AND MAINTENANCE OF FEDERAL RECORDS
##### Subpart A - Identifying Federal Records
###### ยง 1222.12 What types of documentary materials are Federal records?
####### Record status of copies.

(d)The determination as to whether a particular document is a record does not depend upon whether it contains unique information. Multiple copies of the same document and documents containing duplicative information may each have record status depending on how they are used in conducting agency business.
