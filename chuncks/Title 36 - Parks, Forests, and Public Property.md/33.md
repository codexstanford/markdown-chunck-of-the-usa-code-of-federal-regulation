
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

#### ยง 1.4 What terms do I need to know?
##### Motor vehicle

means every vehicle that is self-propelled and every vehicle that is propelled by electric power, but not operated on rails or water, except an electric bicycle, a snowmobile, and a motorized wheelchair.
