
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter VII - Library of Congress
### PART 703 - DISCLOSURE OR PRODUCTION OF RECORDS OR INFORMATION
#### Subpart A - Availability of Library of Congress Records
##### Appendix A to Subpart A of Part 703 - Fees and Charges for Services Provided to Requesters of Records
###### Duplication of Records.

(b)Fees charged for the duplication of records shall be according to the prevailing rates established by the Library's Photoduplication Service, or in the case of machine media duplication, by the Resources Management Staff, Information Technology Services.
