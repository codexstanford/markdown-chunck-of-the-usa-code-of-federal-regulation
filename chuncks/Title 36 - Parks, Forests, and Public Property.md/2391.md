
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter II - Forest Service, Department of Agriculture
### PART 212 - TRAVEL MANAGEMENT
#### Subpart B - Designation of Roads, Trails, and Areas for Motor Vehicle Use
##### ยง 212.55 Criteria for designation of roads, trails, and areas.
###### General criteria for designation of National Forest System roads, National Forest System trails, and areas on National Forest System lands.

(a)In designating National Forest System roads, National Forest System trails, and areas on National Forest System lands for motor vehicle use, the responsible official shall consider effects on National Forest System natural and cultural resources, public safety, provision of recreational opportunities, access needs, conflicts among uses of National Forest System lands, the need for maintenance and administration of roads, trails, and areas that would arise if the uses under consideration are designated; and the availability of resources for that maintenance and administration.
