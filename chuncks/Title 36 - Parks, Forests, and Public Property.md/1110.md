
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 12 - NATIONAL CEMETERIES
#### ยง 12.3 Definitions.
##### Memorial headstone

means a private or government headstone placed in a memorial section of a national cemetery with the words "In Memory Of" inscribed to honor a deceased eligible person whose remains could not be interred in the national cemetery.
