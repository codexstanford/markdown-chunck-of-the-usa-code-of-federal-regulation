
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1194 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES
#### Appendix C to Part 1194 - Functional Performance Criteria and Technical Requirements
##### Forward Reach.

407.8.3Operable parts of ICT providing a forward reach shall conform to 407.8.3.1 or 407.8.3.2. The vertical reference plane shall be centered, and intersect with, the operable part. Where a forward reach allows a reach over a portion of the ICT, the height of that portion of the ICT shall be 34 inches (865 mm) maximum.
