
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 18 - LEASING OF PROPERTIES IN PARK AREAS
#### ยง 18.2 What definitions do you need to know to understand this part?
##### Non-historic land

(l)means land located within the boundaries of a park area that is not associated property and is not part of a pre-historic or historic district or site included on, or eligible for inclusion on, the National Register of Historic Places.
