
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1011 - DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart C - Procedures for Offset of Presidio Trust Payments To Collect Debts Owed To Other Federal Agencies
##### ยง 1011.21 How do other Federal agencies use the offset process to collect debts from payments issued by the Presidio Trust?
###### Priority of debts owed to the Presidio Trust.

(h)For purposes of this section, debts owed to the Presidio Trust generally take precedence over debts owed to other agencies. The Presidio Trust may determine whether to pay debts owed to other agencies before paying a debt owed to the Presidio Trust. The Presidio Trust will determine the order in which the debts will be collected based on the best interests of the United States.
