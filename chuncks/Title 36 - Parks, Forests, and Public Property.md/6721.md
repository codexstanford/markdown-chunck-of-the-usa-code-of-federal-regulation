
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board

#### Appendix A to Part 1194 - Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act: Application and Scoping Requirements
##### Accessibility Standard.,see

E205.4Electronic content shall conform to Level A and Level AA Success Criteria and Conformance Requirements in WCAG 2.0 (incorporated by reference,702.10.1).
