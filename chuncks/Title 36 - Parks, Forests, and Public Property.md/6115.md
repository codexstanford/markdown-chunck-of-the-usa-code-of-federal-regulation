
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1011 - DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 1011.1 What definitions apply to the regulations in this part?
###### Debt

means any amount of money, funds or property that has been determined by an appropriate agency official to be owed to the United States by a person. As used in this part, the term "debt" does not include debts arising under the Internal Revenue Code.
