
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

#### Subpart D - Subsistence Taking of Fish and Wildlife
##### ยง 242.25 Subsistence taking of fish, wildlife, and shellfish: general regulations.
###### Abalone iron

means a flat device which is used for taking abalone and which is more than 1 inch (24 mm) in width and less than 24 inches (610 mm) in length, with all prying edges rounded and smooth.
