
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 7 - SPECIAL REGULATIONS, AREAS OF THE NATIONAL PARK SYSTEM
#### ยง 7.28 Olympic National Park.
##### General summer season.,Provided however:

(ii)Daily steelhead catch limit shall not exceed two fish,

(A) The Queets River and tributaries shall have a summer season daily limit of one steelhead over 20 inches in length.
