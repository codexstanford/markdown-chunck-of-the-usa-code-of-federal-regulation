
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1194 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES
#### Appendix C to Part 1194 - Functional Performance Criteria and Technical Requirements
##### Speech Delivery Type and Coordination.

402.2.3Speech output shall be delivered through a mechanism that is readily available to all users, including, but not limited to, an industry standard connector or a telephone handset. Speech shall be recorded or digitized human, or synthesized. Speech output shall be coordinated with information displayed on the screen.
