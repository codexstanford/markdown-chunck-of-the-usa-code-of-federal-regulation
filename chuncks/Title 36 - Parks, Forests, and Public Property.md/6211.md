
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1012 - LEGAL PROCESS: TESTIMONY BY EMPLOYEES AND PRODUCTION OF RECORDS
#### General Information
##### § 1012.2 What is the Presidio Trust's policy on granting requests for employee testimony or Presidio Trust records?
###### United States ex rel. Touhy,Ragen,

(b) No Presidio Trust employee may testify or produce records in any proceeding to which this part applies unless authorized by the Presidio Trust under §§ 1012.1 through 1012.11.v.340 U.S. 462 (1951).
