
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1011 - DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart C - Procedures for Offset of Presidio Trust Payments To Collect Debts Owed To Other Federal Agencies
##### ยง 1011.22 What does the Presidio Trust do upon receipt of a request to offset the salary of a Presidio Trust employee to collect a debt owed by the employee to another Federal agency?
###### When the debtor is no longer employed by the Presidio Trust,Offset of final and subsequent payments.

(c)-(1)If the Presidio Trust employee retires or resigns or if his or her employment ends before collection of the debt is complete, the Presidio Trust will continue to offset up to 100% of an employee's subsequent payments until the debt is paid or otherwise resolved. Such payments include a debtor's final salary payment, lump-sum leave payment, and other payments payable to the debtor by the Presidio Trust.
