
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 6 - SOLID WASTE DISPOSAL SITES IN UNITS OF THE NATIONAL PARK SYSTEM
#### ยง 6.3 Definitions.
##### Compostible materials

means organic substances that decay under natural and/or human-assisted conditions within relatively short time intervals, generally not in excess of ninety days.
