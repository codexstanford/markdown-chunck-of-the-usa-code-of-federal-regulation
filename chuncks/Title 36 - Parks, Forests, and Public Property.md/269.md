
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior

#### ยง 7.3 Glacier National Park.
##### Canadian dollars.

(e)To promote the purpose of the Act of May 2, 1932 (47 Stat. 145; 16 U.S.C. 161a), Canadian dollars tendered by Canadian visitors entering the United States section of Glacier National Park will be accepted at the official rate of exchange in payment of the recreation fees prescribed for the park.
