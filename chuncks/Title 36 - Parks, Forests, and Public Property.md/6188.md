
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter X - Presidio Trust
### PART 1011 - DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart B - Procedures To Collect Presidio Trust Debts
##### ยง 1011.18 How does a debtor request a special review based on a change in circumstances such as catastrophic illness, divorce, death or disability?
###### Material change in circumstances.

(a)A debtor who owes a debt may, at any time, request a special review by the Presidio Trust of the amount of any offset, administrative wage garnishment or voluntary payment, based on materially changed circumstances beyond the control of the debtor such as, without limitation, catastrophic illness, divorce, death or disability.
