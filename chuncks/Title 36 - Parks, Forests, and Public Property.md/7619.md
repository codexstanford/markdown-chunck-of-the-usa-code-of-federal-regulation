### Subchapter B - Records Management

##### ยง 1223.2 What definitions apply to this part?
###### National security emergency

means any occurrence, including natural disaster, military attack, technological emergency, or other emergency, that seriously degrades or threatens the national security of the United States, as defined in Executive Order 12656.
