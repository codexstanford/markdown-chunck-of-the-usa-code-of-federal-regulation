
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1194 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES
#### Appendix B to Part 1194 - Section 255 of the Communications Act: Application and Scoping Requirements
##### Word Substitution when Applying WCAG to Non-Web Software.

C205.2.1For non-Web software, wherever the term "Web page" or "page" appears in WCAG 2.0 Level A and AA Success Criteria and Conformance Requirements, the term "software" shall be substituted for the terms "Web page" and "page." In addition, in Success Criterion 1.4.2, the phrase "in software" shall be substituted for the phrase "on a Web page."
