
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter I - National Park Service, Department of the Interior
### PART 34 - EL PORTAL ADMINISTRATIVE SITE REGULATIONS
#### ยง 34.4 Definitions.
##### Leased lands

means all lands within the administrative site in which there is a lawful possessory interest in addition to that of the National Park Service, which have been leased, permitted or otherwise assigned by the Superintendent. All other lands within the administrative site are nonleased lands.
