
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XI - Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board
### PART 1194 - INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES
#### Appendix C to Part 1194 - Functional Performance Criteria and Technical Requirements
##### Accessibility and Compatibility Features.

602.2Documentation shall list and explain how to use the accessibility and compatibility features required by Chapters 4 and 5. Documentation shall include accessibility features that are built-in and accessibility features that provide compatibility with assistive technology.
