
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property
## Chapter XII - National Archives and Records Administration
### Subchapter D - Declassification
#### PART 1260 - DECLASSIFICATION OF NATIONAL SECURITY INFORMATION
##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 1260.2 What definitions apply to the regulations in this part?
####### Integral file block

means a distinct component of a file series, as defined in this section, that should be maintained as a separate unit in order to ensure the integrity of the records. An integral file block may consist of a set of records covering either a specific topic or a range of time such as presidential administration or a 5-year retirement schedule within a specific file series that is retired from active use as a group. For purposes of automatic declassification, integral file blocks shall contain only records dated within 10 years of the oldest record in the file block.
