
# Title 36 - Parks, Forests, and Public Property

### PART 65 - NATIONAL HISTORIC LANDMARKS PROGRAM
#### ยง 65.3 Definitions.
##### Site

(n)means the location of a significant event, a prehistoric or historic occupation or activity, or a building or structure, whether standing, ruined or vanished, where the location itself maintains historical or archeological value regardless of the value of any existing structure.
