####### Tropical disease.

(b)In claims based on peacetime service before January 1, 1947, a veteran of 6 months or more service who contracts a tropical disease listed in ยง 3.309(b) or a resultant disorder or disease originating because of therapy administered in connection with a tropical disease or as a preventative, will be considered to have incurred such disability in service when it is shown to exist to the degree of 10 percent or more within 1 year after separation from active service, or at a time when standard and accepted treatises indicate that the incubation period commenced during active service unless shown by clear and unmistakable evidence not to have been of service origin.
