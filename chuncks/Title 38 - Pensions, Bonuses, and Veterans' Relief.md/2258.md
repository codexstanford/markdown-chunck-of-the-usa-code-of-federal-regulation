###### What does the term "organic loss of speech" mean?,organic loss of speech

(c)The termmeans the loss of the ability to express oneself, both by voice and whisper, through the normal organs of speech if the loss is caused by physical changes in such organs. The fact that some speech can be produced through the use of artificial appliance or other organs of the body will not impact this definition.
