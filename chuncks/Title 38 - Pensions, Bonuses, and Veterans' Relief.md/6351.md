
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

##### Measurement of Courses
###### ยง 21.5870 Measurement of courses.
####### Credit hour measurement: undergraduate, standard term.

(a)An individual who enrolls in a standard quarter or semester for 12 undergraduate credit hours is a full-time student. An individual who enrolls in a standard quarter or semester for less than 12 undergraduate credit hours is a part-time student.
