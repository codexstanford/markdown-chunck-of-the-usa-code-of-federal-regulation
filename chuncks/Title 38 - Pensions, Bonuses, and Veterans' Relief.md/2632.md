##### ยง 14.518 Litigation involving beneficiaries in custody of Department of Veterans Affairs employees acting in official capacity.
###### Service of process generally.

(a)An employee, at a field facility, served with a writ of habeas corpus involving a beneficiary of the Department of Veterans Affairs in the employee's custody will immediately notify the Regional Counsel of the region in addition to taking such steps as in his or her judgment are necessary for self protection.
