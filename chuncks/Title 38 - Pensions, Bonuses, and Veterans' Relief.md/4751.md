
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 20 - BOARD OF VETERANS' APPEALS: RULES OF PRACTICE
#### Subpart O - Revision of Decisions on Grounds of Clear and Unmistakable Error
##### ยง 20.1405 Rule 1405. Disposition.
###### Docketing and assignment; notification of representative,General.

(a)-(1)Motions under this subpart will be docketed in the order received and will be assigned in accordance with ยง 20.106 (relating to assignment of proceedings). Where an appeal is pending on the same underlying issue at the time the motion is received, the motion and the appeal may be consolidated under the same docket number and disposed of as part of the same proceeding. A motion may not be assigned to any Member who participated in the decision that is the subject of the motion. If a motion is assigned to a panel, the decision will be by a majority vote of the panel Members.
