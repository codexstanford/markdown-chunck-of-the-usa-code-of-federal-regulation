
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

### PART 46 - POLICY REGARDING PARTICIPATION IN NATIONAL PRACTITIONER DATA BANK

##### ยง 46.1 Definitions.
###### Claim of medical malpractice

(b)means a written claim or demand for payment based on an act or omission of a physician, dentist, or other health care practitioner in furnishing (or failing to furnish) health care services, and includes the filing of a complaint or administrative tort claim under the Federal Tort Claims Act, 28 U.S.C. 1346(b), 2671-2680.
