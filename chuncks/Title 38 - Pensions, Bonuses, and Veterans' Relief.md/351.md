
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 1 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### Standards for Collection, Compromise, Suspension or Termination of Collection Effort, and Referral of Civil Claims for Money or Property
##### § 1.907 Definitions.
###### VA benefit program

(c) As used in §§ 1.900 through 1.953,means medical care, home loan, and benefits payment programs administered by VA under Title 38 of the United States Code, except as otherwise stated.
