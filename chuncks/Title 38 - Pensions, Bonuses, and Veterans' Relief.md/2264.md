
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 8 - NATIONAL SERVICE LIFE INSURANCE
#### Effective Date
##### ยง 8.1 Effective date for an insurance policy issued under section 1922(a) or 1922B of title 38 U.S.C.
###### What is the effective date of the policy?

(a)The effective date is the date policy coverage begins. Benefits due under a policy issued under section 1922(a) are payable any time after the effective date. Benefits due under a policy issued under section 1922B are payable any time two years after the effective date.
