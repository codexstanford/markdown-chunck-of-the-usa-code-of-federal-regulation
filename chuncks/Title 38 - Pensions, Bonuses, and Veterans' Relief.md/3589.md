
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 17 - MEDICAL
#### Hospital Care and Medical Services for Camp Lejeune Veterans and Families
##### ยง 17.400 Hospital care and medical services for Camp Lejeune veterans.
###### Limitations.

(c)For a Camp Lejeune veteran, VA will assume that a covered illness or condition is attributable to the veteran's active duty service at Camp Lejeune unless it is clinically determined, under VA clinical practice guidelines, that such an illness or condition resulted from a cause other than such service.
