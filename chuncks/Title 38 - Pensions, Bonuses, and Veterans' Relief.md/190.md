
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 1 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### Court Orders Authorizing Disclosures and Use
##### ยง 1.494 Procedures and criteria for orders authorizing disclosure and use of records to criminally investigate or prosecute patients.
###### Review of evidence: Conduct of hearings.

(c)Any oral argument, review of evidence, or hearing on the application shall be held in the judge's chambers or in some other manner which ensures that patient identifying information is not disclosed to anyone other than a party to the proceedings, the patient, or VA. The proceeding may include an examination by the judge of the patient records referred to in the application.
