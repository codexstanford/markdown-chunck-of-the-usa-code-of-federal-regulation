
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 17 - MEDICAL
#### Copayments
##### ยง 17.110 Copayments for medication.
###### Copayments.,Copayment amount.

(b)(1)Unless exempted under paragraph (c) of this section, a veteran is obligated to pay VA a copayment for each 30-day or less supply of medication provided by VA on an outpatient basis (other than medication administered during treatment).

(i) For each 30-day or less supply of Tier 1 medications, the copayment amount is $5.

(ii) For each 30-day or less supply of Tier 2 medications, the copayment amount is $8.

(iii) For each 30-day or less supply of Tier 3 medications, the copayment amount is $11.
