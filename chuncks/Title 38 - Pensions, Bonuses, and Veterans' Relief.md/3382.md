
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 17 - MEDICAL
#### Payment and Reimbursement of the Expenses of Medical Services Not Previously Authorized
##### ยง 17.121 Limitations on payment or reimbursement of the costs of emergency treatment not previously authorized.
###### Refusal of transfer.

(c)If a stabilized veteran who requires continued non-emergency treatment refuses to be transferred to an available VA facility (or other Federal facility that VA has an agreement with to furnish health care services for veterans), VA will make payment or reimbursement only for the expenses related to the initial evaluation and the emergency treatment furnished to the veteran up to the point of refusal of transfer by the veteran.
