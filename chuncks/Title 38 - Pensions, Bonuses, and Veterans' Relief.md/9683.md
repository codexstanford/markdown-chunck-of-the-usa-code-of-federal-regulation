## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs

#### ยง 77.14 Grant agreements.
##### Restrictions on the participation of certain veterans and former servicemembers.

(e)The grant agreement will prohibit grant funds from being used to support or provide services (including adaptive sports activities) to veterans or former servicemembers who are barred from receiving VA benefits based on their service (see 38 U.S.C. 5303-5303A) and to veterans or former servicemembers who, if otherwise eligible, would be barred from receiving VA pension, compensation or dependency and indemnity compensation based on the character of their discharge from military service (see 38 CFR 3.12).
