
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

###### ยง 4.75 General considerations for evaluating visual impairment.
####### Service-connected visual impairment of only one eye.

(c)Subject to the provisions of 38 CFR 3.383(a), if visual impairment of only one eye is service-connected, the visual acuity of the other eye will be considered to be 20/40 for purposes of evaluating the service-connected visual impairment.
