
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 21 - VETERAN READINESS AND EMPLOYMENT AND EDUCATION
#### Subpart A - Veteran Readiness and Employment
##### Veterans Employment Pay for Success Grant Program
###### § 21.441 Definitions.
####### Recipient

is the entity that receives a VEPFS grant under §§ 21.440 through 21.449. The recipient is also the outcomes payor.
