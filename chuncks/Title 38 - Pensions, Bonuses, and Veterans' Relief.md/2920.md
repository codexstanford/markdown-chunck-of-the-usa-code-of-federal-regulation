##### ยง 17.32 Informed consent and advance directives.
###### Mental health (or psychiatric) advance directive.

(iii)A mental health or psychiatric advance directive is executed by patients whose future decision-making capacity is at risk due to mental illness. In this type of directive, the individual indicates future mental health treatment preferences.
