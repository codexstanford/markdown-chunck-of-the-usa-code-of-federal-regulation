#### Disclosures Without Patient Consent
##### § 1.489 Audit and evaluation activities.
###### Congressional oversight.

(c)Records subject to §§ 1.460 through 1.499 of this part upon written request may be released to congressional committees or subcommittees for program oversight and evaluation if such records pertain to any matter within the jurisdiction of such committee or subcommittee.
