
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs

#### Subpart H - Hearings on Appeal
##### ยง 20.709 Rule 709. Subpoenas.
###### Ruling on motion for subpoena,To whom assigned.

(e)-(1)The ruling on the motion will be made by the Member or panel of Members to whom the case is assigned. Where the case has not been assigned, the Chairman, or the Chairman's designee, will assign the case to a Member or panel who will then rule on the motion.
