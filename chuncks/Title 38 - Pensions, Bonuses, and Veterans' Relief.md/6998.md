
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs

###### ยง 21.7635 Discontinuance dates.
####### Forfeiture for fraud.

(s)If a reservist must forfeit his or her educational assistance due to fraud, the date of discontinuance of payment of educational assistance will be the later of-

(1) The effective date of the award, or

(2) The day before the date of the fraudulent act.
