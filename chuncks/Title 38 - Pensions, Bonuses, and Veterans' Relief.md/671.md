
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

#### Subpart A - Pension, Compensation, and Dependency and Indemnity Compensation
##### General

####### Decisions based on service department findings.

(1)The Department of Veterans Affairs shall accept the findings of the appropriate service department that a person was a prisoner of war during a period of war unless a reasonable basis exists for questioning it. Such findings shall be accepted only when detention or internment is by an enemy government or its agents.
