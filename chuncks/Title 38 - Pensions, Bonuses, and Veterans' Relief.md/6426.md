
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 21 - VETERAN READINESS AND EMPLOYMENT AND EDUCATION
#### Subpart I - Temporary Program of Vocational Training for Certain New Pension Recipients
##### Duration of Training
###### ยง 21.6072 Extending the duration of a vocational training program.
####### Extension of the duration of a vocational training program.

(a)An extension of a vocational training program as formulated in the IWRP may only be approved to enable the veteran to achieve a vocational goal identified before the end of the first 24 calendar months of the program.
