###### Suspension of collections by offset.

(f)Notwithstanding the provisions of ยง 1.912a, during the period covered by the work-study debt-liquidation contract with the individual, VA will ordinarily suspend the collection by offset of a debt described in paragraph (a)(1) of this section.
