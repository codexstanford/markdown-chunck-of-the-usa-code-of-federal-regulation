
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 17 - MEDICAL
#### Program for Repayment of Educational Loans for Certain VA Psychiatrists
##### § 17.641 Definitions.
###### PREL

means the program for the repayment of educational loans for certain VA psychiatrists established in §§ 17.640 through 17.647.
