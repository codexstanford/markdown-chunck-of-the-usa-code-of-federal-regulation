
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

#### Disclosures With Patient's Consent
##### ยง 1.479 Disclosures to elements of the criminal justice system which have referred patients.
###### Revocation of consent.

(c)The written consent must state that it is revocable upon the passage of a specified amount of time or the occurrence of a specified, ascertainable event. The time or occurrence upon which consent becomes revocable may be no earlier than the individual's completion of the treatment program and no later than the final disposition of the conditional release or other action in connection with which consent was given.
