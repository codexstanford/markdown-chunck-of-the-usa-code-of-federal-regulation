
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 17 - MEDICAL
#### Veterans Community Care Program
##### ยง 17.4005 Definitions.
###### Full-service VA medical facility

means a VA medical facility that provides hospital care, emergency medical services, and surgical care and having a surgical complexity designation of at least "standard."

Note 1 to the definition of "full-service VA medical facility": VA maintains a website with a list of the facilities that have been designated with at least a surgical complexity of "standard," which can be accessed on VA's website.
