
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

#### Subpart A - Veteran Readiness and Employment
##### Initial and Extended Evaluation
###### ยง 21.52 Determining serious employment handicap.
####### Effects of significant impairment not overcome.

(2)The individual has not overcome the effects of the significant vocational impairment through employment in, or qualifying for employment in, an occupation consistent with his or her abilities, aptitudes, and interests. This includes an individual who qualifies for a suitable job, but who does not obtain or keep the job for reasons beyond his or her control.
