
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief
## Chapter I - Department of Veterans Affairs
### PART 21 - VETERAN READINESS AND EMPLOYMENT AND EDUCATION
#### Subpart A - Veteran Readiness and Employment
##### Periods of Eligibility
###### ยง 21.42 Deferral or extension of the basic period of eligibility.
####### Commencement or continuation of participation prevented by medical condition(s).

(c)(1) The basic period of eligibility does not run during any period when a veteran's participation in a Veteran Readiness and Employment (VR&E) program is determined to be infeasible for 30 days or more because of any medical condition(s) of the veteran, including the disabling effects of chronic alcoholism (see paragraphs (c)(2) through (c)(5) of this section).
