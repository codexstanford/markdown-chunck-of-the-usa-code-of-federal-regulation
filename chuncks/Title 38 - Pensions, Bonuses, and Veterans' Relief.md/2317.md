
# Title 38 - Pensions, Bonuses, and Veterans' Relief

### PART 8 - NATIONAL SERVICE LIFE INSURANCE
#### Appeals
##### ยง 8.33 Cash value for term-capped policies.
###### What is a term-capped policy?

(a)A term-capped policy is a National Service Life Insurance policy prefixed with "V" or Veterans Special Life Insurance policy prefixed with "RS," issued on a 5-year level premium term plan in which premiums have been capped (frozen) at the renewal age 70 rate.
