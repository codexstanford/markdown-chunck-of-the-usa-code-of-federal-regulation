####### Board review of final decisions from the negotiated grievance procedure involving discrimination.

(4)If an employee elects to pursue a matter involving prohibited discrimination (as defined in ยง 28.95) through the negotiated grievance procedure, the employee may ask the Board to review the final decision of the negotiated grievance procedure as it relates to the issue of discrimination.
