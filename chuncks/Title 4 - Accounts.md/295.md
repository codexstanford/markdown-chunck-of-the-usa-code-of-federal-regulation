####### ยง 28.21 Amendments to petitions and motions practice.
######## Amendments to petitions.

(a)The Board, at its discretion, may allow amendments to a petition as long as all persons who are parties to the proceeding have adequate notice to prepare for the new allegations and if to do so would not prejudice the rights of the other parties or unduly delay the proceedings.
