###### Hearings
####### ยง 28.61 Burden and degree of proof.
######## Substantial evidence

means that degree of relevant evidence which a reasonable person, considering the record as a whole, might accept as adequate to support a conclusion, even though other reasonable persons might disagree. This is a lower standard of proof than preponderance of the evidence.
