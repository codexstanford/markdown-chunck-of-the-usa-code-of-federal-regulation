
# Title 4 - Accounts
## Chapter I - Government Accountability Office
### Subchapter F - Records
#### PART 83 - PRIVACY PROCEDURES FOR PERSONNEL RECORDS
##### ยง 83.11 Official Personnel Folder.
###### Maintenance and content of Folder.

(e)GAO shall maintain in the Official Personnel Folder the reports of selection and other personnel actions named in section 2951 of title 5, United States Code. The Folder shall also contain permanent records affecting the employee's status and service as required by U.S. OPM instructions and as designated in FPM Supplement 293-31.
