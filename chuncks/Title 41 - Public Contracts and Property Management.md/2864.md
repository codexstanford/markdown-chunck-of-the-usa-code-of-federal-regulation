
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management

#### Subchapter B - Personal Property
##### PART 102-38 - SALE OF PERSONAL PROPERTY
###### Subpart F - Reporting Requirements
####### ยง 102-38.330 Are there any reports that we must submit to the General Services Administration?
######## Negotiated sales report.

(a)Within 60 calendar days after the close of each fiscal year, you must provide GSA with a listing and description of all negotiated sales with an estimated fair market value in excess of $5,000 (see ยง 102-38.115). For each negotiated sale that meets this criterion, provide the following:

(1) Description of the property (including quantity and condition).

(2) Acquisition cost and date (if not known, estimate and so indicate).

(3) Estimated fair market value (including date of estimate and name of estimator).

(4) Name and address of purchaser.

(5) Date of sale.

(6) Gross and net sales proceeds.

(7) Justification for conducting a negotiated sale.
