###### Subpart B - How Are Advisory Committees Established, Renewed, Reestablished, and Terminated?
####### ยง 102-3.60 What procedures are required to establish, renew, or reestablish a discretionary advisory committee?
######## Fairly balanced membership.

(3)A description of the agency's plan to attain fairly balanced membership. The plan will ensure that, in the selection of members for the advisory committee, the agency will consider a cross-section of those directly affected, interested, and qualified, as appropriate to the nature and functions of the advisory committee. Advisory committees requiring technical expertise should include persons with demonstrated professional or personal qualifications and experience relevant to the functions and tasks to be performed.
