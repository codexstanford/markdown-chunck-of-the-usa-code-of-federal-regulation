
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 102 - Federal Management Regulation
#### Subchapter C - Real Property
##### PART 102-74 - FACILITY MANAGEMENT
###### Appendix to Part 102-74 - Rules and Regulations Governing Conduct on Federal Property
####### Explosives (41 CFR 102-74.435).

No person entering or while on property may carry or possess explosives, or items intended to be used to fabricate an explosive or incendiary device, either openly or concealed, except for official purposes.
