###### ยง 60-741.23 Medical examinations and inquiries.
####### Examination of employees.

(3)The contractor may require a medical examination (and/or inquiry) of an employee that is job-related and consistent with business necessity. The contractor may make inquiries into the ability of an employee to perform job-related functions.
