
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 102 - Federal Management Regulation
#### Subchapter A - General
##### PART 102-3 - FEDERAL ADVISORY COMMITTEE MANAGEMENT
###### Subpart A - What Policies Apply to Advisory Committees Established Within the Executive Branch?
####### ยง 102-3.40 What types of committees or groups are not covered by the Act and this part?
######## Operational committees.

(k)Any committee established to perform primarily operational as opposed to advisory functions. Operational functions are those specifically authorized by statute or Presidential directive, such as making or implementing Government decisions or policy. A committee designated operational may be covered by the Act if it becomes primarily advisory in nature. It is the responsibility of the administering agency to determine whether a committee is primarily operational. If so, it does not fall under the requirements of the Act and this part.
