######## https://www.fedrooms.com

(c) When selecting a commercial lodging facility, first consideration should be given to government lodging agreement programs such as FedRooms&#xAE; (). The advantages of obtaining lodging using the FedRooms&#xAE; program are:

(1) Lodging rates are set at or below per diem rates;

(2) There are no add-on fees;

(3) The room cancellation deadline is 4 p.m. (or later) on the day of arrival;

(4) Most hotels offer last standard room availability rates;

(5) There are no early departure fees; and

(6) Rates are available using all booking channels (e.g., E-Gov Travel Service, Travel Management Service, FedRooms&#xAE; Web site, and hotel reservation call centers). The FedRooms&#xAE; rate code (XVU) must be entered to get the program benefits.
