
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 128 - Department of Justice
#### PART 128-1 - INTRODUCTION
##### Subpart 128-1.1 - Regulation System
###### ยง 128-1.152 Citation.

The JPMR will be cited in accordance with thestandards applicable to the FPMR. Accordingly, when this section is referred to formally in official documents, it should be cited as "41 CFR 128-1.152." When a section of the JPMR is referred to informally, however, it may be identified simply by "JPMR" followed by the complete paragraph reference number, e.g., "JPMR 128-1.152."
