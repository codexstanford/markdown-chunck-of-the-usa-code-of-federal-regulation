
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management

### Chapter 105 - General Services Administration

##### Subpart 105-71.14 - Post-Award Requirements/Reports, Records, Retention, and Enforcement

####### If submitted for negotiation.

(i)If the proposal, plan, or other computation is required to be submitted to the Federal Government (or to the grantee) to form the basis for negotiation of the rate, then the 3-year retention period for its supporting records starts from the date of such submission.
