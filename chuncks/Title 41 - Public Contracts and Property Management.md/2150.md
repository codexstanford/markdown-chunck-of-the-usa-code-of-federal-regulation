means a person assigned to operate or assist in operating an aircraft during flight time. Crewmembers perform duties directly related to the operation of the aircraft (as pilots, co-pilots, flight engineers, navigators) or duties assisting in operation of the aircraft (as flight directors, crew chiefs, electronics technicians, mechanics). See also the terms and definitions for "Qualified non-crewmember" and "Passenger" in this section.
