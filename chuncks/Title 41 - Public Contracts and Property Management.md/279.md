
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle B - Other Provisions Relating to Public Contracts
### Chapter 51 - Committee for Purchase from People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled
#### PART 51-9 - PRIVACY ACT RULES
##### Subpart 51-9.3 - Individual Access to Records
###### ยง 51-9.304-5 Reproduction fee schedule.

(a) The fee for reproducing a copy of a record (by routine electrostatic copying) up to and including material 8x 14 inches shall be $0.10 per page.

(b) The fee for reproducing a copy of a record over 8x 14 inches or whose physical characteristics do not permit reproduction by routine electrostatic copying shall be the direct cost of reproducing the records through Government or commercial sources.
