
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management

###### ยง 60-3.14 Technical standards for validity studies.
####### Content validity of training success.

(7)Where a measure of success in a training program is used as a selection procedure and the content of a training program is justified on the basis of content validity, the use should be justified on the relationship between the content of the training program and the content of the job.
