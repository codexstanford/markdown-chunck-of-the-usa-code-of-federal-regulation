
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 102 - Federal Management Regulation
#### Subchapter C - Real Property
##### PART 102-75 - REAL PROPERTY DISPOSAL
###### Subpart I - Screening of Federal Real Property
####### ยง 102-75.1245 What happens after the landholding agency properly responds to a "Notice of Availability"?

The landholding agency has 60 days (from the expiration date of the "Notice of Availability") to submit a formal transfer request for the property. Absent a formal request for transfer within the prescribed 60 days, GSA may, at its discretion, pursue other disposal options.
