
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 105 - General Services Administration

##### Subpart 105-71.12 - Post-Award Requirements/Financial Administration
###### ยง 105-71.125 Program income.
####### Income after the award period.

(h)There are no Federal requirements governing the disposition of program income earned after the end of the award period (i.e., until the ending date of the final financial report, see paragraph (a) of this section), unless the terms of the agreement or the Federal agency regulations provide otherwise.
