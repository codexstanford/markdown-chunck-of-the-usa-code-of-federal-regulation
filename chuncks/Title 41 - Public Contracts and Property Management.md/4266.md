######## Expedited mail,expedited mail

means mail designated for overnight and 2- or 3-day delivery by service providers. Examples ofinclude Dalsey, Hillblom, Lynn (DHL); Federal Express (FedEx); United Parcel Service (UPS); and United States Postal Service (USPS) express mail.
