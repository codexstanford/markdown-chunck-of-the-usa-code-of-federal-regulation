
# Title 41 - Public Contracts and Property Management
## Subtitle C - Federal Property Management Regulations System
### Chapter 102 - Federal Management Regulation
#### Subchapter G - Administrative Programs
##### PART 102-192 - MAIL MANAGEMENT
###### Subpart A - Introduction to this Part
####### ยง 102-192.35 What definitions apply to this part?
######## Accountable mail,accountable mail

means any piece of mail for which a service provider and the mail center must maintain a record that shows where the mail piece is at any given time, and when and where it was delivered. Examples ofinclude United States Postal Service (USPS) registered mail and all expedited mail.
