
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Nationality Regulations
#### PART 315 - PERSONS INELIGIBLE TO CITIZENSHIP: EXEMPTION FROM MILITARY SERVICE
##### ยง 315.1 Definitions.
###### Treaty national

means an alien who is a national of a country with which the United States has a treaty relating to the reciprocal exemption of aliens from military training or military service.
