###### ยง 1003.107 Reinstatement after disbarment or suspension.
####### Appearance after reinstatement.

(d)A practitioner who has been reinstated to practice by the Board must file a new Notice of Entry of Appearance of Attorney or Representative in each case on the form required by applicable rules and regulations, even if the reinstated practitioner previously filed such a form in a proceeding before the practitioner was disciplined.
