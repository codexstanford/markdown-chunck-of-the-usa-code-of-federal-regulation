## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security

##### ยง 235.1 Scope of examination.
###### Passport card.

(1)A U.S. citizen who possesses a valid unexpired United States passport card, as defined in 22 CFR 53.1, may present the passport card when entering the United States from contiguous territory or adjacent islands at land or sea ports-of-entry.
