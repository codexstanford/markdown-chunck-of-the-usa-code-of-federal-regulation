
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter A - General Provisions
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 1.3 Lawfully present aliens for purposes of applying for Social Security benefits.
###### Non-issuance of a Notice to Appear and non-enforcement of deportation, exclusion, or removal orders.

(b)An alien may not be deemed to be lawfully present solely on the basis of DHS's decision not to, or failure to:

(1) Issue a Notice to Appear; or

(2) Enforce an outstanding order of deportation, exclusion or removal.
