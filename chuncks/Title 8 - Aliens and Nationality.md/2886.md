###### When is an arriving FAS citizen presumed to be a habitual resident?

(c)(1) An arriving FAS citizen will be subject to the rebuttable presumption that he or she is a habitual resident if the Service has reason to believe that the arriving FAS citizen was previously admitted to the territory or possession more than one year ago; and

(2) That the arriving FAS citizen either;

(i) Failed to turn in his or her Form I-94 (see ยง 1.4) when he or she previously departed from the United States; or

(ii) Failed to apply for a replacement Form I-94.
