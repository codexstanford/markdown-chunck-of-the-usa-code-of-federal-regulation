
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 211 - DOCUMENTARY REQUIREMENTS: IMMIGRANTS; WAIVERS
##### ยง 211.1 Visas.
###### Immigrants having occupational status defined in section 101(a)(15) (A), (E), or (G) of the Act.

(c)An immigrant visa, reentry permit, or Form I-551 shall be invalid when presented by an alien who has an occupational status under section 101(a)(15) (A), (E), or (G) of the Act, unless he or she has previously submitted, or submits at the time he or she applies for admission to the United States, the written waiver required by section 247(b) of the Act and 8 CFR part 247.
