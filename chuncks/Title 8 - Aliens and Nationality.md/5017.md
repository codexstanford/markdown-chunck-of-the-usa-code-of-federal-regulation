
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Nationality Regulations
#### PART 336 - HEARINGS ON DENIALS OF APPLICATIONS FOR NATURALIZATION
##### ยง 336.9 Judicial review of denial determinations on applications for naturalization.
###### General.

(a)The provisions in part 310 of this chapter shall provide the sole and exclusive procedures for requesting judicial review of final determinations on applications for naturalization made pursuant to section 336(a) of the Act and the provisions of this chapter by USCIS on or after October 1, 1991.
