
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 252 - LANDING OF ALIEN CREWMEN
##### ยง 252.3 Great Lakes vessels and tugboats arriving in the United States from Canada; special procedures.
###### United States vessels and tugboats.

(a)An immigration examination shall not be required of any crewman aboard a Great Lakes vessel of United States registry or a tugboat of United States registry arriving from Canada at a port of the United States who has been examined and admitted by an immigration officer as a member of the crew of the same vessel or tugboat or of any other vessel or tugboat of the same company during the current calendar year.
