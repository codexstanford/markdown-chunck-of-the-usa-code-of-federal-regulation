
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

###### 9-11 Response and Biometric Entry-Exit Fee for L-1 Visa.

(8)For all petitioners filing an L-1 petition who employ 50 or more employees in the United States, if more than 50 percent of the petitioner's employees in the aggregate are in H-1B, L-1A or L-1B nonimmigrant status, except for petitioners filing an amended petition without an extension of stay request: $4,500.
