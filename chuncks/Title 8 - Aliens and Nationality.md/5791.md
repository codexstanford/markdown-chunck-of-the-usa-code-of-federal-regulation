
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

##### ยง 1212.2 Consent to reapply for admission after deportation, removal or departure at Government expense.
###### Advance approval.

(j)An alien whose departure will execute an order of deportation shall receive a conditional approval depending upon his or her satisfactory departure. However, the grant of permission to reapply does not waive inadmissibility under section 212(a) (16) or (17) of the Act resulting from exclusion, deportation, or removal proceedings which are instituted subsequent to the date permission to reapply is granted.
