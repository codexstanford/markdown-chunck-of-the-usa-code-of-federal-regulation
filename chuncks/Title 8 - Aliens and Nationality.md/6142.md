
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

###### ยง 1240.26 Voluntary departure - authority of the Executive Office for Immigration Review.
####### Periods of time.

(e)If voluntary departure is granted prior to the completion of removal proceedings, the immigration judge may grant a period not to exceed 120 days. If voluntary departure is granted at the conclusion of proceedings, the immigration judge may grant a period not to exceed 60 days.
