
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

####### Form I-94W.

(5)For issuance of Form I-94W or other Nonimmigrant Visa Waiver Arrival/Departure record at a land border port-of-entry under section 217 of the Act: $6.00. The term 'issuance' includes, but is not limited to, the creation of an electronic record of admission or arrival/departure by DHS following an inspection performed by a CBP officer, which may be provided to the nonimmigrant as a printout or other confirmation of the electronic record stored in DHS systems.
