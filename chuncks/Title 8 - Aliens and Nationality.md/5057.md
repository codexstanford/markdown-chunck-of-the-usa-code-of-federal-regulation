
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Nationality Regulations
#### PART 340 - REVOCATION OF NATURALIZATION
##### ยง 340.2 Revocation proceedings pursuant to section 340(a) of the Act.
###### Recommendations for institution of revocation proceedings.

(a)Whenever it appears that any grant of naturalization may have been illegally procured or procured by concealment of a material fact or by willful misrepresentation, and a prima facie case exists for revocation pursuant to section 340(a) of the Act, USCIS will make a recommendation regarding revocation.
