
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

##### ยง 1212.7 Waiver of certain grounds of inadmissibility.
###### Decision on waivers under Pub. L. 103-416 and notification to the alien,Approval.

(ii)-(A)If the Director of USIA submits a favorable waiver recommendation on behalf of a foreign medical graduate pursuant to Pub. L. 103-416, and the Service grants the waiver, the alien shall be notified of the approval on Form I-797 (or I-797A or I-797B, as appropriate).
