
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

##### ยง 212.22 Public charge inadmissibility determination.
###### Disability alone not sufficient.

(4)A finding that an alien has a disability, as defined by Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act, will not alone be a sufficient basis to determine whether the alien is likely at any time to become a public charge.
