### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

###### Unlawful presence,Nonimmigrant admission.

(3)-(i)An alien otherwise eligible for admission as a V nonimmigrant is not subject to the ground of inadmissibility under section 212(a)(9)(B) of the Act.
