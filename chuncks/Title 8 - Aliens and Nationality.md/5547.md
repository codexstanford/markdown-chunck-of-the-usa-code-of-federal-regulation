
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter V - Executive Office for Immigration Review, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 1101 - PRESUMPTION OF LAWFUL ADMISSION
##### ยง 1101.1 Presumption of lawful admission.
###### Entry between May 1, 1934, and July 3, 1946.

(2)An alien who establishes that he entered Hawaii between May 1, 1934, and July 3, 1946, inclusive, under the provisions of the last sentence of section 8(a)(1) of the Act of March 24, 1934, as amended, that he was a citizen of the Philippine Islands when he entered, and that a record of such entry exists.
