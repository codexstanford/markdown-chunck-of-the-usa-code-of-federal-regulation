
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

###### Public Schools.

(i)A petitioning school or school system owned and operated as a public educational institution or system by the United States or a State or a political subdivision thereof must submit a certification to that effect signed by the authorized public official.
