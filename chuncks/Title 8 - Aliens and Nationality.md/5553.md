
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter V - Executive Office for Immigration Review, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 1101 - PRESUMPTION OF LAWFUL ADMISSION
##### ยง 1101.3 Creation of record of lawful permanent resident status for person born under diplomatic status in the United States.
###### Person born to foreign diplomat,Status of person.

(a)-(1)A person born in the United States to a foreign diplomatic officer accredited to the United States, as a matter of international law, is not subject to the jurisdiction of the United States. That person is not a United States citizen under the Fourteenth Amendment to the Constitution. Such a person may be considered a lawful permanent resident at birth.
