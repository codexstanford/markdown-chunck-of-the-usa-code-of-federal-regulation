
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 232 - DETENTION OF ALIENS FOR PHYSICAL AND MENTAL EXAMINATION
##### ยง 232.2 Examination in the United States of alien applicants for benefits under the immigration laws and other aliens.
###### U.S. Public Health Service hospital and outpatient clinic reports.

(d)When an applicant for a benefit under the immigration laws, other than an applicant for status as a permanent resident, is examined by a medical officer of the U.S. Public Health Service, the results of the examination shall be entered on Form I-141, Medical Certificate, in duplicate. The form shall be returned to the Service office by which the alien was referred.
