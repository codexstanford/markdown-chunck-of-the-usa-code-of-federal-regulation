
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

##### ยง 214.2 Special requirements for admission, extension, and maintenance of status.
###### Other P-1, P-2, and P-3 aliens.

(B)An extension of stay may be authorized in increments of 1 year for P-1 athletic teams, entertainment groups, aliens in reciprocal exchange programs, aliens in culturally unique programs, and their essential support personnel to continue or complete the same event or activity for which they were admitted.
