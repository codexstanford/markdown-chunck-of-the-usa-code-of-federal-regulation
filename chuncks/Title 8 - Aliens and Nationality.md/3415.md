
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

###### ยง 236.3 Processing, detention, and release of alien minors.
####### Detention of family units.

(h)DHS's policy is to maintain family unity, including by detaining families together where appropriate and consistent with law and available resources. If DHS determines that detention of a family unit is required by law, or is otherwise appropriate, the family unit may be transferred to an FRC which is a licensed facility and non-secure.
