
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Nationality Regulations
#### PART 316 - GENERAL REQUIREMENTS FOR NATURALIZATION
##### ยง 316.12 Applicant's legal incompetency during statutory period.
###### General.

(a)An applicant who is legally competent at the time of the examination on the naturalization application and of the administration of the oath of allegiance may be admitted to citizenship, provided that the applicant fully understands the purpose and responsibilities of the naturalization procedures.
