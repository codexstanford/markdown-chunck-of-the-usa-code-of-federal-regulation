
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter V - Executive Office for Immigration Review, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 1235 - INSPECTION OF PERSONS APPLYING FOR ADMISSION
##### ยง 1235.10 U.S. Citizen Identification Card.
###### Admission of alienage.

(3)A U.S. Citizen Identification Card is void if the person to whom it was issued admits in a statement signed before an immigration officer that he or she is an alien and consents to the voidance of the card. Upon signing the statement the card must be surrendered to the immigration officer.
