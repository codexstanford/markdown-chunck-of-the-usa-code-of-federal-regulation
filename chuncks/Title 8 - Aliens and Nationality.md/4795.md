
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Nationality Regulations
#### PART 310 - NATURALIZATION AUTHORITY
##### ยง 310.4 Judicial naturalization authority and withdrawal of petitions.
###### Jurisdiction.

(a)No court shall have jurisdiction under section 310(a) of the Act, to naturalize a person unless a petition for naturalization with respect to that person was filed with the naturalization court before October 1, 1991.
