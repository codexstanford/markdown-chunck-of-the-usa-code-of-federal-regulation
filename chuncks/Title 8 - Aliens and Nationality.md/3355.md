
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

##### ยง 235.12 Global Entry program.
###### Application for entry, examination and inspection.

(h)Each successful use of Global Entry constitutes a separate and completed inspection and application for entry by the participant on the date that Global Entry is used. Pursuant to the enforcement provisions of 19 CFR Part 162, Global Entry participants may be subject to further CBP examination and inspection at any time during the arrival process.
