
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations

##### Subpart A - Applying for Benefits, Surety Bonds, Fees
###### ยง 103.6 Immigration bonds.
####### Notice of intention to decline future bonds.,i.e.,

(4)When one or more of the for cause standards provided in paragraph (b)(2) of this section applies to a Treasury-certified surety, ICE may, in its discretion, initiate the process to notify the surety that it will decline future bonds. To initiate this process, ICE will issue written notice to the surety stating ICE's intention to decline bonds underwritten by the surety and the reasons for the proposed non-acceptance of the bonds. This notification will inform the surety of its opportunity to rebut the stated reasons set forth in the notice, and its opportunity to cure the stated reasons,deficient performance.
