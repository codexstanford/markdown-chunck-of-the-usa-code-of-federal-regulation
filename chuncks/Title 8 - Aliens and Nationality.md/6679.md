
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter V - Executive Office for Immigration Review, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 1280 - IMPOSITION AND COLLECTION OF FINES
##### ยง 1280.1 Review of fines and civil monetary penalties imposed by DHS.
###### Adjudication of civil monetary penalty proceedings.

(b)The Board of Immigration Appeals (Board) has appellate authority to review DHS decisions involving fines and civil monetary penalties imposed under 8 CFR part 280, as provided under 8 CFR part 1003. The regulations in 8 CFR part 280 governing the imposition of certain fines and civil monetary penalties are applicable in such proceedings before the Board.
