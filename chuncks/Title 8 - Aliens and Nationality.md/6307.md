
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter V - Executive Office for Immigration Review, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 1244 - TEMPORARY PROTECTED STATUS FOR NATIONALS OF DESIGNATED STATES
##### ยง 1244.1 Definitions.
###### Prima facie

means eligibility established with the filing of a completed application for Temporary Protected Status containing factual information that if unrebutted will establish a claim of eligibility under section 244 of the Act.
