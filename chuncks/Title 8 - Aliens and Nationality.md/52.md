
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 101 - PRESUMPTION OF LAWFUL ADMISSION
##### ยง 101.1 Presumption of lawful admission.
###### Citizens of the Philippine Islands-(,Entry prior to May 1, 1934.

(f)1)An alien who establishes that he entered the United States prior to May 1, 1934, and that he was on the date of his entry a citizen of the Philippine Islands, provided that for the purpose of petitioning for naturalization he shall not be regarded as having been lawfully admitted for permanent residence unless he was a citizen of the Commonwealth of the Philippines on July 2, 1946.
