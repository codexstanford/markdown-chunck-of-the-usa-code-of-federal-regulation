
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 286 - IMMIGRATION USER FEE
##### ยง 286.9 Fee for processing applications and issuing documentation at land border Ports-of-Entry.
###### General.

(a)A fee may be charged and collected by the Commissioner for the processing and issuance of specified Service documents at land border Ports-of-Entry. These fees, as specified in 8 CFR 103.7(d), shall be dedicated to funding the cost of providing application-processing services at land border ports.
