
# Title 8 - Aliens and Nationality

### Subchapter B - Immigration Regulations
#### PART 214 - NONIMMIGRANT CLASSES

###### Multiple beneficiaries.

(F)More than one O-2 accompanying alien may be included on a petition if they are assisting the same O-1 alien for the same events or performances, during the same period, and in the same location. Up to 25 named beneficiaries may be included per petition.
