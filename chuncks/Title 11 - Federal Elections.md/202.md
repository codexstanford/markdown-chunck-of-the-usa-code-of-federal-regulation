
# Title 11 - Federal Elections
## Chapter I - Federal Election Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 100 - SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS (52 U.S.C. 30101)
##### Subpart A - General Definitions
###### ยง 100.26 Public communication (52 U.S.C. 30101(22)).
####### Public communication,general public political advertising

means a communication by means of any broadcast, cable, or satellite communication, newspaper, magazine, outdoor advertising facility, mass mailing, or telephone bank to the general public, or any other form of general public political advertising. The termshall not include communications over the internet, except for communications placed for a fee on another person's website, digital device, application, or advertising platform.
