
# Title 11 - Federal Elections

#### ยง 4.1 Definitions.
##### Non-commercial scientific institution

(m)means an organization that is not operated on a commercial basis, as that term is defined in paragraph (k) of this section, and which is operated solely for the purpose of conducting scientific research the results of which are not intended to promote any particular product or industry.
