
# Title 11 - Federal Elections
## Chapter I - Federal Election Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 113 - PERMITTED AND PROHIBITED USES OF CAMPAIGN ACCOUNTS
##### ยง 113.1 Definitions (52 U.S.C. 30114).
###### Charitable donations.

(2)Donations of campaign funds or assets to an organization described in section 170(c) of Title 26 of the United States Code are not personal use, unless the candidate receives compensation from the organization before the organization has expended the entire amount donated for purposes unrelated to his or her personal benefit.
