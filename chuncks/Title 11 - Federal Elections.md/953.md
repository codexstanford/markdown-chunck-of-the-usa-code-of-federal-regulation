
# Title 11 - Federal Elections
## Chapter I - Federal Election Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 113 - PERMITTED AND PROHIBITED USES OF CAMPAIGN ACCOUNTS
##### ยง 113.1 Definitions (52 U.S.C. 30114).
###### Office account.

(b)Office account means an account established for the purposes of supporting the activities of a Federal or State officeholder which contains campaign funds and funds donated, but does not include an account used exclusively for funds appropriated by Congress, a State legislature, or another similar public appropriating body, or an account of the officeholder which contains only the personal funds of the officeholder.
