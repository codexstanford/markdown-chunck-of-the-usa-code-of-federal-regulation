
# Title 11 - Federal Elections
## Chapter I - Federal Election Commission

#### PART 116 - DEBTS OWED BY CANDIDATES AND POLITICAL COMMITTEES
##### ยง 116.1 Definitions.
###### Disputed debt.,disputed debt

(d)For purposes of this part,means an actual or potential debt or obligation owed by a political committee, including an obligation arising from a written contract, promise or agreement to make an expenditure, where there is a bona fide disagreement between the creditor and the political committee as to the existence or amount of the obligation owed by the political committee.
