
# Title 11 - Federal Elections
## Chapter I - Federal Election Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 100 - SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS (52 U.S.C. 30101)
##### Subpart E - Exceptions to Expenditures
###### ยง 100.142 Bank loans.
####### Overdrafts.

(d)For the purpose of this section, repayment of an overdraft made on a checking or savings account of a political committee shall be considered an expenditure unless:

(1) The overdraft is made on an account that is subject to automatic overdraft protection; and

(2) The overdraft is subject to a definite interest rate and a definite repayment schedule.
