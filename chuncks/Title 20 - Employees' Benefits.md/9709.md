
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 422 - ORGANIZATION AND PROCEDURES
#### Subpart E - Collection of Debts by Administrative Wage Garnishment
##### ยง 422.415 Will we reduce the amount that your employer must withhold from your pay when withholding that amount causes financial hardship?
###### Financial hardship.

(b)We will find financial hardship when you show that withholding a particular amount from your pay would deprive you of income necessary to meet your ordinary and necessary living expenses. You must give us evidence of your financial resources and expenses.
