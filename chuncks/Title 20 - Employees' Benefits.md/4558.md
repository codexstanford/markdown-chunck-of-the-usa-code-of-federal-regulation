
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart N - Wage Credits for Veterans and Members of the Uniformed Services
##### Effect of Other Benefits on Payment of Social Security Benefits and Payments
###### ยง 404.1360 Veterans Administration pension or compensation payable.
####### After we determine and certify payment.

(b)If we are informed by the Veterans Administration that a pension or compensation is payable to you after we determine and certify payment of benefits based on deemed insured status, we-

(1) Stop payment of your benefits or recompute the amount of any further benefits that can be paid to you; and

(2) Determine whether you were erroneously paid and the amount of any erroneous payment.
