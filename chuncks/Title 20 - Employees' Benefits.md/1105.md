
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 219 - EVIDENCE REQUIRED FOR PAYMENT
##### Subpart B - Evidence of Age and Death
###### ยง 219.23 Evidence to prove death.
####### Other evidence of death.

(b)If the preferred evidence of death cannot be obtained, the individual who must furnish evidence of death will be asked to explain the reason therefor and to submit other convincing evidence, such as sworn statements of at least two persons who have personal knowledge of the death. These persons must be able to swear to the date, time, place, and cause of death.
