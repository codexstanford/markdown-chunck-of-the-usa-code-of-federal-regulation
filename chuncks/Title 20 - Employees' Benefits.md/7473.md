
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart B - Eligibility
##### Reasons Why You May Not Get SSI Benefits for Which You Are Otherwise Eligible
###### ยง 416.211 You are a resident of a public institution.
####### Services that a facility must provide in order to be a community residence.

(2)To be a community residence, a facility must provide food and shelter. In addition, it must make available some other services. For example, the other services could be-

(i) Social services;

(ii) Help with personal living activities;

(iii) Training in socialization and life skills; or

(iv) Providing occasional or incidental medical or remedial care.
