###### ยง 216.31 Who is eligible for an age annuity.
####### Change from employee disability to age annuity.

(c)A disability annuity paid to an employee through the end of the month before the month in which the employee attains retirement age is converted to an age annuity beginning with the month in which he or she attains retirement age.
