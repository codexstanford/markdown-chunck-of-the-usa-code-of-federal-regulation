
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 685.110 What definitions apply to this program?
###### Emergency assistance

is a form of "related assistance" and means assistance provided by grantees that addresses immediate needs of eligible MSFWs and their dependents. An applicant's self-certification is accepted as sufficient documentation of eligibility for emergency assistance.
