
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart Q - Referral of Persons Eligible for Supplemental Security Income to Other Agencies
##### Referral for Vocational Rehabilitation Services
###### ยง 416.1710 Whom we refer and when.
####### Whom we refer.

(a)If you are 16 years of age or older and under 65 years old, and receiving supplemental security income (SSI) benefits, we will refer you to the State agency providing vocational rehabilitation services. If you are under age 16, we will refer you to an agency administering services under the Maternal and Child Health Services (Title V) Block Grant Act.
