###### ยง 404.278 Additional cost-of-living increase.
####### Measuring period for the additional increase

(b)-(1) To compute the additional increase for all individuals and for maximum benefits payable to a family, we begin with the year in which the insured individual became eligible for old-age or disability benefits to which he or she is currently entitled, or died before becoming eligible.
