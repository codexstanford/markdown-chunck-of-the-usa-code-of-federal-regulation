
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 336 - DURATION OF NORMAL AND EXTENDED BENEFITS
##### Subpart B - Extended Benefits
###### ยง 336.13 Years of service requirement.
####### Eligibility.

(a)For the purposes of this part, an employee is not eligible for extended unemployment or sickness benefits if he or she does not have at least 10 years of railroad service. An employee who has 120 service months, as defined in part 210 of this chapter, whether or not consecutive, is considered to have 10 years of railroad service.
