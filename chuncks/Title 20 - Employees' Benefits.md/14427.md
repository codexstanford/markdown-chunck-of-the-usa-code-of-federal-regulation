
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 670 - THE JOB CORPS UNDER TITLE I OF THE WORKFORCE INVESTMENT ACT
#### Subpart I - Administrative and Management Provisions
##### ยง 670.900 Are damages caused by the acts or omissions of students eligible for payment under the Federal Tort Claims Act?
###### et seq.

Yes, students are considered Federal employees for purposes of the FTCA (28 U.S.C. 2671). Claims for such damage should be filed pursuant to the procedures found in 29 CFR part 15, subpart D.
