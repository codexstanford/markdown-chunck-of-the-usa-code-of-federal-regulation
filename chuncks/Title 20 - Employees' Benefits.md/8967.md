#### Subpart N - Determinations, Administrative Review Process, and Reopening of Determinations and Decisions
##### Appeals Council Review
###### ยง 416.1472 Effect of dismissal of request for Appeals Council review.

The dismissal of a request for Appeals Council review is binding and not subject to further review.
