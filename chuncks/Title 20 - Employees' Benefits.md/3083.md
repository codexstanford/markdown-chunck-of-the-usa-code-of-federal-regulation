
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart B - Insured Status and Quarters of Coverage
##### Disability Insured Status
###### ยง 404.131 When you must have disability insured status.
####### For a period of disability.

(a)To establish a period of disability, you must have disability insured status in the quarter in which you become disabled or in a later quarter in which you are disabled.
