
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

### PART 655 - TEMPORARY EMPLOYMENT OF FOREIGN WORKERS IN THE UNITED STATES

##### ยง 655.402 Definition of terms.
###### CW-1 worker

means any foreign worker who is lawfully present in the Commonwealth and authorized by DHS to perform temporary labor or services under 48 U.S.C. 1806(d).
