
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

#### Subpart P - Determining Disability and Blindness

###### ยง 404.1567 Physical exertion requirements.
####### Heavy work.

(d)Heavy work involves lifting no more than 100 pounds at a time with frequent lifting or carrying of objects weighing up to 50 pounds. If someone can do heavy work, we determine that he or she can also do medium, light, and sedentary work.
