
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 408 - SPECIAL BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN WORLD WAR II VETERANS
#### Subpart D - Evidence Requirements
##### Residence
###### ยง 408.437 How do you prove that you had good cause for staying in the United States for more than 1 full calendar month?
####### Circumstances prevent you from returning to your home abroad.

(b)To prove that you had good cause for staying in the United States for more than 1 full calendar month, you must give us evidence of your good faith effort to return to your home abroad before the 1-month period had elapsed and of the circumstances/event which prevented your return to your home abroad.
