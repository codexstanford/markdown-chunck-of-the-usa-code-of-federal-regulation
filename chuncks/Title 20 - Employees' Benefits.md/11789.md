
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor

#### Subpart G - Performance Accountability
##### ยง 641.720 How will the Department and grantees initially determine and then adjust expected levels of the core performance measures?
###### Adjustments based on economic conditions and individuals served during the program year.

(d)The Department will, in accordance with the objective statistical model developed pursuant to paragraph (c)(2) of this section, adjust the expected levels of performance for a program year for grantees to reflect the actual economic conditions and characteristics of participants in the corresponding projects during such program year.
