
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

###### Nonpayment of monthly benefits to aliens residing in certain countries,Benefits for months after June 1968.

(c)-(1)Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section, we cannot pay monthly benefits for any month after June 1968 to anyone not a citizen or national of the United States for any month while residing in a country to which payments are being withheld by the Treasury Department pursuant to Vol. II, 31 U.S.C. 3329.
