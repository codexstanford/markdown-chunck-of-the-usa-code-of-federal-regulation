
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 672 - PROVISIONS GOVERNING THE YOUTHBUILD PROGRAM
#### Subpart A - Purpose and Definitions
##### ยง 672.110 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Youth who is an individual with a disability.

The term youth who is an individual with a disability means a youth with any disability (as defined in section 3 of the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (42 U.S.C. 12102)) or a student receiving special education and related services under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA).
