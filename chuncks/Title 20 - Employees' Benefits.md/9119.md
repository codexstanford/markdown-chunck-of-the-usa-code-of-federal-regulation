#### Subpart R - Relationship
##### Who Is Considered Your Spouse
###### ยง 416.1802 Effects of marriage on eligibility and amount of benefits.
####### If you have an ineligible spouse,Counting income.

(a)-(1)If you apply for or receive SSI benefits, and you are married to someone who is not eligible for SSI benefits and are living in the same household as that person, we may count part of that person's income as yours. Counting part of that person's income as yours may reduce the amount of your benefits or even make you ineligible.
