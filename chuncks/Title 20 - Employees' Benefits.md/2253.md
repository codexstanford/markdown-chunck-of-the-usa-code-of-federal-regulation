
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 325 - REGISTRATION FOR RAILROAD UNEMPLOYMENT BENEFITS
##### ยง 325.4 Claim for unemployment benefits.
###### Time for filing.

(c)A claim for unemployment benefits shall be filed at any Board office no later than 15 calendar days after the last day of the claim period, as defined in paragraph (b) of this section, or 15 calendar days after the date on which the claim form was mailed to the employee, whichever is later. In determining whether the time for filing the claim may be extended, the standards set forth in ยง 325.3(d) shall be applied. None of the days included in a claim that is not timely filed shall be considered a day of unemployment.
