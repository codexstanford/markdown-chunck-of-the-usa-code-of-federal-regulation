
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 323 - NONGOVERNMENTAL PLANS FOR UNEMPLOYMENT OR SICKNESS INSURANCE
##### ยง 323.6 Treatment of benefit payments under a nongovernmental plan for purposes of contributions.
###### compensation

Benefit payments under nongovernmental plans approved by the Board under this part are notas defined in section 1(i) of the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act, and therefore they are not subject to contribution under part 345 of this chapter.
