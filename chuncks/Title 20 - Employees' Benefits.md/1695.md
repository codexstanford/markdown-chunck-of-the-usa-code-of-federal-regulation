###### ยง 226.32 Spouse tier II.
####### Initial restored amount.

(1)The restored amount is the amount by which the spouse tier I was reduced by reason of receipt of an employee annuity on the date the restored amount is first payable. The restored amount is only payable if either the employee or spouse had railroad service prior to 1975.
