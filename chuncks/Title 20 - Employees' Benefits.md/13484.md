##### ยง 655.1300 Overview of subpart B and definition of terms.
###### Adverse effect wage rate (AEWR),Application for Temporary Employment Certification

means the minimum wage rate that the Administrator, OFLC has determined must be offered and paid to every H-2A worker employed under the DOL-approvedin a particular occupation and/or area, as well as to U.S. workers hired by employers into corresponding employment during the H-2A recruitment period, to ensure that the wages of similarly employed U.S. workers will not be adversely affected.
