
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 216 - ELIGIBILITY FOR AN ANNUITY
##### Subpart C - Railroad and Last Non-Railroad Employment
###### ยง 216.24 Relinquishment of rights to return to work.
####### What return to work rights must be given up.

(a)Before an individual may receive an annuity based on age, he or she must give up any seniority or other rights to return to work for any railroad employer.
