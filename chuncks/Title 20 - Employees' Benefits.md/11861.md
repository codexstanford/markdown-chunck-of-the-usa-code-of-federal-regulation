
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 651 - GENERAL PROVISIONS GOVERNING THE WAGNER-PEYSER ACT EMPLOYMENT SERVICE
#### § 651.10 Definitions of terms used in this part and parts 652, 653, 654, and 658 of this chapter.
##### Employment Service (ES) office

means a site that provides Wagner-Peyser Act services as a one-stop partner program. A site must be colocated in a one-stop center consistent with the requirements of §§ 678.305 through 678.315 of this chapter.
