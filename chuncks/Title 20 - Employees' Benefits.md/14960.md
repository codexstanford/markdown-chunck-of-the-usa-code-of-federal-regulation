
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 683.510 What is a Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act Pay-for-Performance contract?
###### Validation.

(j)WIOA Pay-for-Performance contracts must include independent validation of the contractor's achievement of the performance benchmarks specified in the contract. This validation must be based on high-quality, reliable, and verified data.
