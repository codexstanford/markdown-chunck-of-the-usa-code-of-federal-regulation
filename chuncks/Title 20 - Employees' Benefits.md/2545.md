
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter E - Administrative Remedies for Fraudulent Claims or Statements
#### PART 355 - REGULATIONS UNDER THE PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT OF 1986
##### ยง 355.2 Definitions.
###### Investigating official

means the Inspector General of the Railroad Retirement Board or an officer or employee of the Office of the Inspector General designated by the Inspector General and serving in a position for which the rate of basic pay is not less than the minimum rate of basic pay for grade GS-16 under the General Schedule.
