
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 422 - ORGANIZATION AND PROCEDURES
#### Subpart E - Collection of Debts by Administrative Wage Garnishment
##### ยง 422.435 What happens when we decide to send an administrative wage garnishment order to your employer?
###### Employer certification.

(c)Along with the garnishment order, we will send your employer a certification form to complete about your employment status and the amount of your disposable pay available for withholding. Your employer must complete the certification and return it to us within 20 days of receipt.
