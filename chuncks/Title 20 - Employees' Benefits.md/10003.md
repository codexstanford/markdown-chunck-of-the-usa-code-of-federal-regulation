
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 429.210 Do I have any appeal rights under this subpart?
###### Claimant.

(b)You may request reconsideration from the SSA Claims Officer by sending a written request for reconsideration to the SSA Claims Officer within 30 days of the date of the original determination. You must clearly state the factual or legal basis upon which you base your request for a more favorable determination. Reconsideration will be granted only for reasons not available or not considered during the original decision.
