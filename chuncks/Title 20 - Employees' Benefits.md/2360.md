
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 340 - RECOVERY OF BENEFITS
##### ยง 340.10 Waiver of recovery of erroneous payments.
###### When recovery is against equity or good conscience.

(d)Recovery is considered to be against equity or good conscience when a person, in reliance on such payments or on notice that such payment would be made, relinquished a valuable right or changed his or her position for the worse.
