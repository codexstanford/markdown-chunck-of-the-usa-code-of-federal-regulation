
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

####### Earnings in the year an employee becomes entitled to an age annuity or becomes disabled.

(2)Earnings in the year an employee becomes entitled to an age annuity or becomes disabled are not used in the initial computation of the PIA. However, the Board does consider those earnings in a recomputation of the PIA and begins paying the higher benefits at the time described in paragraph (b) of this section.
