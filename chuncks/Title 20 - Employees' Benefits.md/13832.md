
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 656.31 Labor certification applications involving fraud, willful misrepresentation, or violations of this part.
###### Denial.

(a)A Certifying Officer may deny any application for permanent labor certification if the officer finds the application contains false statements, is fraudulent, or was otherwise submitted in violation of the Department's permanent labor certification regulations.
