
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 609 - UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION FOR FEDERAL CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 609.1 Purpose and application.
###### Purpose.

(a)Subchapter I of chapter 85, title 5 of the United States Code, as amended by Pub. L. 94-566, 90 Stat. 2667, 5 U.S.C. 8501-8508, provides for a permanent program of unemployment compensation for unemployed Federal civilian employees. The unemployment compensation provided for in subchapter I is hereinafter referred to as unemployment compensation for Federal employees, or UCFE. The regulations in this part are issued to implement the UCFE Program.
