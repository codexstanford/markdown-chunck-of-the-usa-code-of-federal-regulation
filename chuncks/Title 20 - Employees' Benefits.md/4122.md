
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration

#### Subpart K - Employment, Wages, Self-Employment, and Self-Employment Income

####### Legislative Branch Employees.

(2)Service you perform for the legislative branch of the Federal Government for which you are paid remuneration after 1983 is generally covered by Social Security if such service is not covered by the Civil Service Retirement System or by another retirement system established by a law of the United States and in effect on December 31, 1983, for employees of the Federal Government other than a system for members of the uniformed services.
