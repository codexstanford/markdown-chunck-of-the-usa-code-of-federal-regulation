
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart R - Relationship
##### Who Is Considered Your Spouse
###### ยง 416.1802 Effects of marriage on eligibility and amount of benefits.
####### If you are married, we do not consider you a child.

(c)The rules for counting income and resources are different for children than for adults. (Section 416.1851 discusses the effects of being considered a child on eligibility and amount of benefits.) Regardless of your age, if you are married we do not consider you to be a child.
