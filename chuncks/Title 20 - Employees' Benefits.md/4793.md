
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart P - Determining Disability and Blindness
##### Residual Functional Capacity
###### ยง 404.1546 Responsibility for assessing your residual functional capacity.
####### Responsibility for assessing residual functional capacity at the administrative law judge hearing or Appeals Council level.

(c)If your case is at the administrative law judge hearing level or at the Appeals Council review level, the administrative law judge or the administrative appeals judge at the Appeals Council (when the Appeals Council makes a decision) is responsible for assessing your residual functional capacity.
