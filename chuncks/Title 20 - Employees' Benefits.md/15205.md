
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 686 - THE JOB CORPS UNDER TITLE I OF THE WORKFORCE INNOVATION AND OPPORTUNITY ACT
#### Subpart I - Administrative and Management Provisions
##### ยง 686.900 Are damages caused by the acts or omissions of students eligible for payment under the Federal Tort Claims Act?
###### et seq.

Yes, students are considered Federal employees for purposes of the FTCA. (28 U.S.C. 2671) Claims for such damage must be filed pursuant to the procedures found in 29 CFR part 15, subpart D.
