
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 655 - TEMPORARY EMPLOYMENT OF FOREIGN WORKERS IN THE UNITED STATES
#### Subpart E - Labor Certification Process for Temporary Employment in the Commonwealth of the Northern Marianas Islands (CW-1 Workers)
##### ยง 655.402 Definition of terms.
###### Prevailing wage (PW),Application for Prevailing Wage Determination for the CW-1 Program,

means the official wage issued by the NPWC on the Form ETA 9141C,or successor form. At least that amount must be paid to all CW-1 workers and U.S. workers in corresponding employment.
