
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart B - Eligibility
##### Eligibility for Increased Benefits Because of Essential Persons
###### ยง 416.222 Who is an essential person.
####### Absence of a qualified individual from his or her home.

(c)You may be temporarily absent from your home and still have an essential person. For example, you could be hospitalized. We consider an absence to be temporary if-

(1) You intend to return;

(2) The facts support your intention;

(3) It is likely that you will return; and

(4) Your absence does not exceed six months.
