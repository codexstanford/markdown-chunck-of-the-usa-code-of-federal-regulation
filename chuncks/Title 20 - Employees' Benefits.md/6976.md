
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 408 - SPECIAL BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN WORLD WAR II VETERANS
#### Subpart B - SVB Qualification and Entitlement
##### Residence Outside the United States
###### ยง 408.228 When do we consider you to be residing outside the United States?
####### Definition of residing outside the United States.

(b)We consider you to be residing outside the United States if you:

(1) Have established an actual dwelling place outside the United States; and

(2) Intend to continue to live outside the United States.
