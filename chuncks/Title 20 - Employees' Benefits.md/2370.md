
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 341 - STATUTORY LIEN WHERE SICKNESS BENEFITS PAID
##### ยง 341.3 Notice of lien.
###### Notice to alleged tortfeasor.

(a)The Board shall mail a "Notice of Lien" to each person or company identified as liable or potentially liable for causing the employee's infirmity. The "Notice of Lien" will notify the person or company of the Board's right to reimbursement. The notice shall include:

(1) The employee's name, address and social security number;

(2) The date and place of the accident; and

(3) The employee's occupation, if injured on duty.
