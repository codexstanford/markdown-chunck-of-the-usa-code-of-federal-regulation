
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

###### Overall program eligibility.

(a)In addition to all other eligibility criteria contained in this part, an individual must also be authorized to work in the United States to receive benefits under the TAA Program. States are required to verify the status of participants who are not a citizen or national of the United States.
