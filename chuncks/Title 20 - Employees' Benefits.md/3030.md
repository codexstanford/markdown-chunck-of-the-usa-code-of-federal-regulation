
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 403 - TESTIMONY BY EMPLOYEES AND THE PRODUCTION OF RECORDS AND INFORMATION IN LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
#### ยง 403.130 What factors may the Commissioner consider in determining whether SSA will grant your application for testimony?
##### Risk of law violation or compromise of Government privilege.

(a)(1) Would providing the testimony violate a statute (such as 26 U.S.C. 6103 or section 1106 of the Social Security Act, 42 U.S.C. 1306), Executive Order, or regulation (such as 20 CFR part 401)?

(2) Would providing the testimony put confidential, sensitive, or privileged information at risk?
