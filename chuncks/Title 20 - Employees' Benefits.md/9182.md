
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart S - Interim Assistance Provisions
##### Interim Assistance Agreements
###### § 416.1910 Requirements for interim assistance agreement.
####### SSA to repay the State.

(a)We must agree to repay the State for interim assistance it gives you. Repayment to the State takes priority over any underpayments due you (see §§ 416.525 and 416.542).
