
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

### PART 688 - PROVISIONS GOVERNING THE YOUTHBUILD PROGRAM
#### Subpart A - Purpose and Definitions
##### ยง 688.120 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Recognized postsecondary credential

means a credential consisting of an industry-recognized certificate or certification, a certificate of completion of a registered apprenticeship, a license recognized by the State involved or Federal government, or an associate or baccalaureate degree.
