The Secretary and the State shall mutually agree upon a satisfactory audit arrangement to verify that supplementary payments paid by SSA on behalf of the State were made in accordance with the terms of the administration agreement under ยง 416.2005. Resolution of audit findings shall be made in accordance with the provisions of the State's agreement with SSA.
