###### ยง 416.1133 What is a pro rata share of household operating expenses.
####### How we determine a pro rata share.

(b)Your pro rata share of household operating expenses is the average monthly household operating expenses (based on a reasonable estimate if exact figures are not available) divided by the number of people in the household, regardless of age.
