
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 220 - DETERMINING DISABILITY
##### Subpart E - Disability Determinations Governed by the Regulations of the Social Security Administration
###### ยง 220.36 Period of disability.
####### When the Board may establish a period of disability.

(4)The Board has independent authority to decide whether or not to establish a period of disability for any employee who was awarded an annuity under the Railroad Retirement Act, or who-

(i) Has applied for a disability annuity; and

(ii) Has at least 10 years of railroad service.
