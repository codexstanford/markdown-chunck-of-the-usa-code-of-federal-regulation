##### Determining if a State remains "off" as a result of a total unemployment rate indicator after the 13-week mandatory "off" period ends.

(f)(1) The State remains "off" if there is not an IUR "on" indicator the 11th week of the 13-week mandatory "off" period, and there is a TUR "off" indicator for the third week before the last week of the 13-week mandatory "off" period.
