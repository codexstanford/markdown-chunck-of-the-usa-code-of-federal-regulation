
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 416.110 Purpose of program.
###### Protection of personal dignity.

(c)Under the Federal program, payments are made under conditions that are as protective of people's dignity as possible. No restrictions, implied or otherwise, are placed on how recipients spend the Federal payments.
