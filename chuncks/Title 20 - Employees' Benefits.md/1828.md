
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act

##### Subpart F - Computation of the Overall Minimum Rate
###### ยง 229.50 Age reduction in employee or spouse benefit.
####### Spouse age reduction.

(c)The amount of the spouse overall minimum benefit, after any adjustment for the family maximum, is reduced byfor each month the spouse is under retirement age on the date when he or she becomes eligible under the overall minimum. When the spouse benefit increases, the amount of the increase is reduced byfor the same number of months used to compute the initial age reduction.
