
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart I - Records of Earnings
##### Correcting the Earnings Record
###### ยง 404.822 Correction of the record of your earnings after the time limit ends.
####### Written request for correction or application for benefits filed before the time limit ends,Written request for correction.

(c)-(1)We may correct an earnings record if you or your survivor files a request for correction before the time limit for that year ends. The request must state that the earnings record for that year is incorrect. However, we may not correct the record under this paragraph after our determination on the request becomes final.
