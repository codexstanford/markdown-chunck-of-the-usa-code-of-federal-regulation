
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart M - Coverage of Employees of State and Local Governments
##### How Overpayments of Contributions Are Credited or Refunded - for Wages Paid Prior to 1987
###### ยง 404.1284 Offsetting underpayments against overpayments - for wages paid prior to 1987.
####### State fails to make adjustment for allowance of credit.

(a)If SSA notifies a State that a credit is due the State, and the State does not make the adjustment for the allowance of the credit, SSA offsets the credit against any contributions or interest due. Before making the offset, SSA will give the State an opportunity to make the adjustment.
