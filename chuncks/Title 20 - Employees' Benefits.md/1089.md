
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 219 - EVIDENCE REQUIRED FOR PAYMENT
##### Subpart A - General Evidence Requirements
###### ยง 219.5 Where and how to provide evidence.
####### When Board office is not accessible.

(b)A claimant who lives in an area where there is no Board office or who is unable to travel to a Board office may send evidence to the Board office nearest to where the claimant lives. A claimant who lives outside the United States may take evidence to the American embassy or consulate or other Foreign Service Office nearest to where he or she lives or send it to the headquarters of the Board.
