###### ยง 408.912 When are you without fault regarding an overpayment?
####### General-when fault is relevant.

(a)If you request waiver of recovery of an overpayment, we must determine whether you were without fault. You are not relieved of liability and are not without fault solely because we may have been at fault in making the overpayment.
