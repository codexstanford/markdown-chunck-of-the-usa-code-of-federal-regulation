
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 325 - REGISTRATION FOR RAILROAD UNEMPLOYMENT BENEFITS
##### ยง 325.1 General.
###### Day of unemployment.

(a)A "day of unemployment" is a calendar day on which an employee, although ready and willing to work, is unemployed, and on which no remuneration is payable and for which the employee has registered, as required by this part. The amount of compensable days of unemployment shall be computed in accordance with this section.
