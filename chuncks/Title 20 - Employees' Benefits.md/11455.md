
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 620 - DRUG TESTING FOR STATE UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION ELIGIBILITY DETERMINATION PURPOSES
#### ยง 620.5 Conformity and substantial compliance.
##### In general.

(a)A State law implementing the drug testing of applicants for unemployment compensation must conform with-and the law's administration must substantially comply with-the requirements of this part 620 for purposes of certification under 42 U.S.C. 502(a), governing State eligibility to receive Federal grants for the administration of its UC program.
