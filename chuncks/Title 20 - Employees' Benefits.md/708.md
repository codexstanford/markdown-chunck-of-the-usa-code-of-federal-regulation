
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

###### Act.

(a)The term "act," or "1937 act" means the Railroad Retirement Act of 1937 (50 Stat. 307; 45 U.S.C. chapter 9). The term "1935 act," means the Railroad Retirement Act of 1935 (49 Stat. 967; 45 U.S.C. chapter 9).
