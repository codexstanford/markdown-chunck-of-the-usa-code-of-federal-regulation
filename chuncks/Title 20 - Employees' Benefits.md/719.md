
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 201 - DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 201.1 Words and phrases.
###### Local lodges and divisions; local lodge or division.

(l)The term "local lodges and divisions" and the term "local lodge or division" as used in section 1(a) and 1(b), respectively, of the 1937 act, shall be construed to include any subordinate unit of a national railway labor organization defined as an "employer" under the 1937 act, which unit functions in the same manner as, or similar to "local lodges" as that term is ordinarily used, irrespective of the designation of such unit by its national organization.
