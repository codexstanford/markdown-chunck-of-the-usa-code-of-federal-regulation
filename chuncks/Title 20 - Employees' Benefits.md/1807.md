
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 229 - SOCIAL SECURITY OVERALL MINIMUM GUARANTEE
##### Subpart E - When Entitlement Under the Overall Minimum Ends
###### ยง 229.40 When an annuity increase under the overall minimum ends.
####### Employee Age O/M.

(a)An increase in an employee's annuity under the overall minimum based on age ends with the month before the month in which the employee dies. If a disability annuity is increased under the overall minimum based on age rather than disability, and the employee is under retirement age, the increase ends with the second month after the month the disability ends as shown in part 220 of this chapter.
