
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 234 - LUMP-SUM PAYMENTS
##### Subpart D - Residual Lump-Sum Payment
###### ยง 234.47 Election of the RLS by a widow(er) or parent.
####### Filing an election.

(c)An election to have the RLS paid must be made on the certification provided by the Board for that purpose, and must contain an irrevocable election to have the RLS paid in lieu of all benefits based on the employee's railroad service to which the widow(er) or parent might otherwise become entitled. Once the RLS check is negotiated, the election cannot be revoked.
