
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 325 - REGISTRATION FOR RAILROAD UNEMPLOYMENT BENEFITS
##### ยง 325.4 Claim for unemployment benefits.
###### Withdrawal of claim.

(g)An employee may withdraw his or her claim for unemployment benefits by submitting a written statement to that effect and by repaying any benefits paid on the claim, unless the employee's claim was intentionally false or fraudulent.
