(a)If you file an application for old-age benefits, you are presumed to have filed an application for husband's or wife's benefits in the first month of your entitlement to old-age benefits, if-

(1) Your old-age benefits are reduced for age because you choose to receive them before you reach full retirement age (as defined in ยง 404.409); and
