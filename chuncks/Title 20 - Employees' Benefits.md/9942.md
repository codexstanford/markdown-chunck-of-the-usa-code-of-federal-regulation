
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 429 - ADMINISTRATIVE CLAIMS UNDER THE FEDERAL TORT CLAIMS ACT AND RELATED STATUTES
#### Subpart A - Claims Against the Government Under the Federal Tort Claims Act
##### ยง 429.102 How do I file a claim under this subpart?
###### Filing an amendment to your claim.

(b)You may file an amendment to your properly filed claim at any time before the SSA Claims Officer (as defined in ยง 429.201(d)(3)) makes a final decision on your claim or before you bring suit under 28 U.S.C. 2675(a). You must submit an amendment in writing and sign it. If you file a timely amendment, SSA has 6 months in which to finally dispose of the amended claim. Your option to file suit does not begin until 6 months after you file the amendment.
