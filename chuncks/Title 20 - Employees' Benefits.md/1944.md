###### Other than overpaid individual.

(b)The Board may recover an overpayment from a person other than the overpaid individual if such person is receiving benefits based upon the same record of compensation as the overpaid individual under a statute administered by the Board. In such a case, the Board will ordinarily recover the overpayment by setoff against such benefits as are provided for in ยง 255.6 of this part (Example 2 of this section). However, the Board may ask for a cash refund of the overpayment.
