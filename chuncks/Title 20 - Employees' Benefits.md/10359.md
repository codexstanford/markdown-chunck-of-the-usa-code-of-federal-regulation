
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 604 - REGULATIONS FOR ELIGIBILITY FOR UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION
#### ยง 604.5 Application - availability for work.
##### Short-time compensation.

(e)A State must not deny UC to an individual participating in a short-time compensation (also known as worksharing) program under State UC law for failure to be available for work during a week, but such individual will be required to be available for his or her normal workweek.
