
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 688 - PROVISIONS GOVERNING THE YOUTHBUILD PROGRAM
#### Subpart A - Purpose and Definitions
##### ยง 688.120 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Earned income.

(ii)An amount of any earned income of the family, established at the discretion of the Housing Development Agency, which may be based on:

(A) All earned income of the family,

(B) The amount earned by particular members of the family;

(C) The amount earned by families having certain characteristics; or

(D) The amount earned by families or members during certain periods or from certain sources.
