
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 618 - TRADE ADJUSTMENT ASSISTANCE UNDER THE TRADE ACT OF 1974, AS AMENDED
#### Subpart H - Administration by Applicable State Agencies
##### ยง 618.832 Overpayments; penalties for fraud.
###### Fraud detection and prevention.

(f)State procedures for the detection and prevention of fraudulent overpayments of TAA benefits must be, at a minimum, the same as the procedures adopted by the State with respect to State unemployment compensation, and consistent with the Department's "Standard for Fraud and Overpayment Detection," Employment Security Manual, part V, sections 7510 through 7515 (appendix C to this part).
