
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart K - Income
##### Deeming of Income
###### ยง 416.1166a How we deem income to you from your sponsor if you are an alien.
####### If you are not the only alien who is applying for or already eligible for SSI benefits and who has income deemed from your sponsor.

(b)If you and other aliens applying for or already eligible for SSI benefits are sponsored by the same sponsor, we deem the income to each of you as though you were the only alien sponsored by that person. The income deemed to you becomes your unearned income.
