
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 225 - PRIMARY INSURANCE AMOUNT DETERMINATIONS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 225.2 Definitions.
####### Eligibility Year

means the earliest of: the employee's year of attainment of age 62; The year of disability onset; or the year of death. The Eligibility Year determines the formula used to compute a Primary Insurance Amount. Eligibility Year is defined in section 215(a) of the Social Security Act.
