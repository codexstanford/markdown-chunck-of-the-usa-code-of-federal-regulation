
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 640 - STANDARD FOR BENEFIT PAYMENT PROMPTNESS - UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION
#### ยง 640.3 Interpretation of Federal law requirements.
##### Section 303(a)(1).

(a)The Secretary interprets section 303(a)(1) of the Social Security Act to require that a State law include provision for such methods of administration as will reasonable insure the full payment of unemployment benefits to eligible claimants with the greatest promptness that is administratively feasible.
