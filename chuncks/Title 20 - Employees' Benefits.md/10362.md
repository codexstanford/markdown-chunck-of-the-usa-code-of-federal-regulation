
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 604 - REGULATIONS FOR ELIGIBILITY FOR UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION
#### ยง 604.5 Application - availability for work.
##### Work search.

(h)The requirement that an individual be available for work does not require an active work search on the part of the individual. States may, however, require an individual to be actively seeking work to be considered available for work, or States may impose a separate requirement that the individual must actively seek work.
