##### ยง 408.1235 How does the State transfer funds to SSA to administer its recognition payment program?
###### State audit.

(c)Any State entering into an agreement with SSA which provides for Federal administration of the State's recognition payments has the right to an audit (at State expense) of the payments made by SSA on behalf of such State. The Commissioner and the State shall mutually agree upon a satisfactory audit arrangement to verify that recognition payments paid by SSA on behalf of the State were made in accordance with the terms of the administration agreement under ยง 408.1205. Audit findings will be resolved in accordance with the provisions of the State's agreement with SSA.
