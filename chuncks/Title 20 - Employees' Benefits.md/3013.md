
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 403 - TESTIMONY BY EMPLOYEES AND THE PRODUCTION OF RECORDS AND INFORMATION IN LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
#### ยง 403.105 What is the relationship between this part and 20 CFR parts 401 and 402?
##### General.

(a)Disclosure of SSA's records and information contained in those records is governed by the regulations at 20 CFR parts 401 and 402. SSA employees will not disclose records or information in any legal proceeding covered by this part except as permitted by 20 CFR parts 401 and 402.
