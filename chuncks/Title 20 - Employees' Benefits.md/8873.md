
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

###### ยง 416.1425 How to request expedited appeals process.
####### Extension of time to request expedited appeals process.

(c)If you want to use the expedited appeals process but do not request it within the stated time period, you may ask for more time to submit your request. Your request for an extension of time must be in writing and it must give the reasons why the request for the expedited appeals process was not filed within the stated time period. If you show that you had good cause for missing the deadline, the time period will be extended. To determine whether good cause exists, we use the standards explained in ยง 416.1411.
