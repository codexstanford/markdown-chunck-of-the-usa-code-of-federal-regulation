
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 234 - LUMP-SUM PAYMENTS
##### Subpart D - Residual Lump-Sum Payment
###### ยง 234.44 Payment to surviving relatives.
####### Amount surviving relatives are paid.

(b)If more than one relative in an equal degree of relationship survives the employee, each one is paid an equal share of the RLS. If an entitled relative of the employee dies before negotiating the RLS check, that share becomes payable to other surviving relatives of the employee in the same degree of relationship. If no relatives in that degree of relationship survive, relatives in the next degree of relationship are payable.
