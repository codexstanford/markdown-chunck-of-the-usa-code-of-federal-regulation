
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 618 - TRADE ADJUSTMENT ASSISTANCE UNDER THE TRADE ACT OF 1974, AS AMENDED
#### Subpart A-General
##### ยง 618.110 Definitions.
###### Agent State

means a State, other than a liable State, that provides benefits or services to a trade-affected worker. A State can be both an agent State and a liable State.
