
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 226 - COMPUTING EMPLOYEE, SPOUSE, AND DIVORCED SPOUSE ANNUITIES
##### Subpart F - Reduction for Workers' Compensation and Disability Benefits Under a Federal, State, or Local Law or Plan
###### ยง 226.73 Changes in reduction amount.
####### A spouse or divorced spouse becomes entitled to a tier I benefit after the effective date of the reduction.

(a)The reduction amount is recomputed as if the spouse or divorced spouse were entitled to a tier I benefit on the date the reduction first applied. The new reduction amount applies beginning with the date the spouse or divorced spouse tier I benefit begins.
