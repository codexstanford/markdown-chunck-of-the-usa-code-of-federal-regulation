###### ยง 416.1121 Types of unearned income.
####### Annuities, pensions, and other periodic payments.

(a)This unearned income is usually related to prior work or service. It includes, for example, private pensions, social security benefits, disability benefits, veterans benefits, worker's compensation, railroad retirement annuities and unemployment insurance benefits.
