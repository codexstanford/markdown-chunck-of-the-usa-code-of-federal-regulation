
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 656 - LABOR CERTIFICATION PROCESS FOR PERMANENT EMPLOYMENT OF ALIENS IN THE UNITED STATES
#### Subpart C - Labor Certification Process
##### ยง 656.30 Validity of and invalidation of labor certifications.
###### Application for Alien Employment Certification,Application for Permanent Employment Certification

(1) A permanent labor certification for a Schedule A occupation or sheepherders is valid only for the occupation set forth on the(Form ETA 750) or the(Form ETA 9089) and only for the alien named on the original application, unless a substitution was approved prior to July 16, 2007. The certification is valid throughout the United States unless the certification contains a geographic limitation.
