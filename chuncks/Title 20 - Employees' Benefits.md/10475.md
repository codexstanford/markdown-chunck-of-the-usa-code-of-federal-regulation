
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 609 - UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION FOR FEDERAL CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES
#### Subpart B - Administration of UCFE Program
##### ยง 609.8 The applicable State for an individual.
###### The applicable State.

(a)The applicable State for an individual shall be the State to which the individual's Federal civilian service and Federal wages are assigned or transferred under this section. The applicable State law for the individual shall be the State law of such State.
