
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart H - Evidence
##### Evidence for Child's and Parent's Benefits
###### ยง 404.736 Evidence of a child's dependency.
####### When evidence of a child's dependency is needed.

(a)If you apply for child's benefit's we may ask for evidence you were the insured person's dependent at a specific time-usually the time you applied or the time the insured died or became disabled. What evidence we ask for depends upon how you are related to the insured person.
