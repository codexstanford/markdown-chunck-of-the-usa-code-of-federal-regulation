
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart P - Determining Disability and Blindness
##### Continuing or Stopping Disability
###### ยง 404.1588 Your responsibility to tell us of events that may change your disability status.
####### Your responsibility to report changes to us.

(a)If you are entitled to cash benefits or to a period of disability because you are disabled, you should promptly tell us if-

(1) Your condition improves;

(2) You return to work;

(3) You increase the amount of your work; or

(4) Your earnings increase.
