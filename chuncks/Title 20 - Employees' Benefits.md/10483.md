
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 609 - UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION FOR FEDERAL CIVILIAN EMPLOYEES
#### Subpart B - Administration of UCFE Program
##### ยง 609.10 Restrictions on entitlement.
###### Disqualification.

(a)If the week of unemployment for which an individual claims UCFE is a week to which a disqualification for State unemployment compensation applies under the applicable State law, or would apply but for the fact that the individual has no right to such compensation, the individual shall not be entitled to a payment of UCFE for that week.
