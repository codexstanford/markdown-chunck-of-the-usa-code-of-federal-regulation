###### ยง 404.1567 Physical exertion requirements.
####### Medium work.

(c)Medium work involves lifting no more than 50 pounds at a time with frequent lifting or carrying of objects weighing up to 25 pounds. If someone can do medium work, we determine that he or she can also do sedentary and light work.
