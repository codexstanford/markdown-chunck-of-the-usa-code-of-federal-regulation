
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 408 - SPECIAL BENEFITS FOR CERTAIN WORLD WAR II VETERANS
#### Subpart J - Determinations and the Administrative Review Process
##### Reopening and Revising Determinations and Decisions
###### § 408.1070 When will we reopen a final determination?
####### General rules.

(a)For purposes of this part, we use the same rules on reopening and revising determinations and decisions that we use in the title XVI program (see §§ 416.1487-416.1494 of this chapter), except as noted in paragraph (b) of this section.
