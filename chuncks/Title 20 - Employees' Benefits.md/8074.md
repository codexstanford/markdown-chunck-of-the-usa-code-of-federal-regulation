
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 416 - SUPPLEMENTAL SECURITY INCOME FOR THE AGED, BLIND, AND DISABLED
#### Subpart I - Determining Disability and Blindness
##### Vocational Considerations
###### § 416.962 Medical-vocational profiles showing an inability to make an adjustment to other work.
####### If you have done only arduous unskilled physical labor.,see,see

(a)If you have no more than a marginal education (§ 416.964) and work experience of 35 years or more during which you did only arduous unskilled physical labor, and you are not working and are no longer able to do this kind of work because of a severe impairment(s) (§§ 416.920(c), 416.921, and 416.923), we will consider you unable to do lighter work, and therefore, disabled.
