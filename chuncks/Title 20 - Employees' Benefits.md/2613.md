
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter F - Internal Administration, Policy and Procedures
#### PART 361 - RECOVERY OF DEBTS OWED TO THE UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT BY GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES
##### ยง 361.2 Scope.
###### Excluded debts or claims.,et seq.,et seq.,e.g.,

(1)The procedures contained in this part do not apply to debts or claims arising under the Internal Revenue Code of 1954 as amended (26 U.S.C. 1,), the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 301,), or the tariff laws of the United States; or to any case where collection of a debt by salary offset is explicitly provided for or prohibited by another statute (travel advances in 5 U.S.C. 5705 and employee training expenses in 5 U.S.C. 4108).
