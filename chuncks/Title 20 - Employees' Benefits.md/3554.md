
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

#### Subpart E - Deductions; Reductions; and Nonpayments of Benefits
##### ยง 404.446 Definition of "substantial services" and "services."
###### Individual engaged in more than one trade or business.

(b)When an individual, in any month, performs services in more than one trade or business, his services in all trades or businesses are considered together in determining whether he performed substantial services in self-employment in such month.
