
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 670.120 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Job Corps,et seq.

means the agency of the Department established by section 143 of the Workforce Investment Act of 1998 (WIA) (20 U.S.C. 9201) to perform those functions of the Secretary of Labor set forth in subtitle C of WIA Title I.
