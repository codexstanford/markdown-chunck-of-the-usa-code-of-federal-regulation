
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under the Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act
#### PART 327 - AVAILABLE FOR WORK
##### ยง 327.10 Consideration of availability.
###### Failure to work in anticipation of maximum mileage.

(f)(1) An employee in train and engine service who voluntarily lays off work in anticipation of reaching the maximum mileage or earnings permitted under an agreement with his or her employer shall not be considered available for work.
