
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter II - Railroad Retirement Board
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Railroad Retirement Act
#### PART 216 - ELIGIBILITY FOR AN ANNUITY
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 216.2 Definitions.
####### Attainment of age

means that an individual attains a given age on the first moment of the day preceding the anniversary date of his or her birth corresponding to such numerical age.
