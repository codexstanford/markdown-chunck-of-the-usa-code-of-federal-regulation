
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### Subpart C - Railroad and Last Non-Railroad Employment

####### Employee's right to terminate.

(20)The fact that an individual has the right to end his or her relationship with an employer at any time without incurring liability for work to be performed indicates that the individual is an employee. A self-employed individual is legally obligated to satisfactorily complete a specific job.
