
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter VI - Office of Workers' Compensation Programs, Department of Labor

##### Subpart F - Occupational Disease Which Does Not Immediately Result in Death or Disability
###### ยง 702.601 Definitions.
####### Retirement.

(c)For purposes of this subpart, retirement shall mean that the claimant, or decedent in cases involving survivor's benefits, has voluntarily withdrawn from the workforce and that there is no realistic expectation that such person will return to the workforce.
