
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits

##### ยง 416.1231 Burial spaces and certain funds set aside for burial expenses.
###### Burial spaces,General.

(a)-(1)In determining the resources of an individual, the value of burial spaces for the individual, the individual's spouse or any member of the individual's immediate family will be excluded from resources.
