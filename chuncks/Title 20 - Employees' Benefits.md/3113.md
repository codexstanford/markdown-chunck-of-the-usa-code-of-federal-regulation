
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart C - Computing Primary Insurance Amounts
##### General
###### § 404.204 Methods of computing primary insurance amounts - general.
####### Major methods.,average-indexed-monthly-earnings

(b)(1) If after 1978 you reach age 62, or become disabled or die before age 62, we compute your primary insurance amount under what we call themethod, which is described in §§ 404.210 through 404.212. The earliest of the three dates determines the computation method we use.
