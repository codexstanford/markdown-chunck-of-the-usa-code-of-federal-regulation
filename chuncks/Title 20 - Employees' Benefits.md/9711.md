
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 422 - ORGANIZATION AND PROCEDURES
#### Subpart E - Collection of Debts by Administrative Wage Garnishment
##### ยง 422.415 Will we reduce the amount that your employer must withhold from your pay when withholding that amount causes financial hardship?
###### Fraud and willful concealment or failure to furnish information.

(d)(1) We will not reduce the amount that your employer withholds from your disposable pay if your debt was caused by:

(i) Your intentional false statement, or

(ii) Your willful concealment of, or failure to furnish, material information.

(2) "Willful concealment" means an intentional, knowing and purposeful delay in providing, or failure to reveal, material information.
