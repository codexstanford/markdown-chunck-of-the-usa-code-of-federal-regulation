
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter V - Employment and Training Administration, Department of Labor
### PART 640 - STANDARD FOR BENEFIT PAYMENT PROMPTNESS - UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION
#### § 640.6 Review of State compliance.
##### Periodic review.

(b)The administration of any State law may be reviewed at any other time, when there is reason to believe that there may be failure of compliance as set out in §§ 640.2(b) and 640.3(b). Such a review shall be based upon the same elements as may be required for an annual review.
