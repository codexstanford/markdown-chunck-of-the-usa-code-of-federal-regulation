
# Title 20 - Employees' Benefits
## Chapter III - Social Security Administration
### PART 404 - FEDERAL OLD-AGE, SURVIVORS AND DISABILITY INSURANCE (1950- )
#### Subpart E - Deductions; Reductions; and Nonpayments of Benefits
##### ยง 404.410 How does SSA reduce my benefits when my entitlement begins before full retirement age?
###### How does SSA reduce my old-age benefits?

(a)The reduction in your primary insurance amount is based on the number of months of entitlement prior to the month you attain full retirement age. The reduction isof 1 percent for each of the first 36 months andof 1 percent for each month in excess of 36.
