###### ยง 404.1564 Your education as a vocational factor.
####### High school education and above.

(4)High school education and above means abilities in reasoning, arithmetic, and language skills acquired through formal schooling at a 12th grade level or above. We generally consider that someone with these educational abilities can do semi-skilled through skilled work.
