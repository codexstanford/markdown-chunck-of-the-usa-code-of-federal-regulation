## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 76 - STATE-ADMINISTERED PROGRAMS
#### Subpart B - How a State Applies for a Grant
##### Consolidated Grant Applications for Insular Areas
###### § 76.125 What is the purpose of these regulations?
####### Insular Area

(b) For the purpose of §§ 76.125-76.137 of this part the termmeans the Virgin Islands, Guam, American Samoa, the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands, or the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.
