
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 373 - REHABILITATION NATIONAL ACTIVITIES PROGRAM
##### Subpart B - How Does the Secretary Make a Grant?
###### ยง 373.10 What selection criteria does the Secretary use?

The Secretary publishes in theor includes in the application package the selection criteria for each competition under this program. To evaluate the applications for new grants under this program, the Secretary may use the following:

(a) Selection criteria established under 34 CFR 75.209.

(b) Selection criteria in 34 CFR 75.210.

(c) Any combination of selection criteria from paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section.
