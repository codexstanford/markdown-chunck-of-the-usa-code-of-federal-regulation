##### Subpart E - General Provisions
###### Innovative Assessment Demonstration Authority
####### ยง 200.105 Demonstration authority application requirements.

(A) Administering full assessments from both the innovative and statewide assessment systems to all students enrolled in participating schools, such that at least once in any grade span (3-5, 6-8, or 9-12) and subject for which there is an innovative assessment, a statewide assessment in the same subject would also be administered to all such students.
