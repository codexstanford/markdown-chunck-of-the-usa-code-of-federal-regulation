
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter II - Office of Elementary and Secondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 200 - TITLE I - IMPROVING THE ACADEMIC ACHIEVEMENT OF THE DISADVANTAGED
##### Subpart D - Prevention and Intervention Programs for Children and Youth Who are Neglected, Delinquent, or At-Risk of Dropping Out
###### ยง 200.90 Program definitions.
####### Institution for neglected children and youth

means, as determined by the SEA, a public or private residential facility, other than a foster home, that is operated primarily for the care of children and youth who-

(i) Have been committed to the institution or voluntarily placed in the institution under applicable State law due to abandonment, neglect, or death of their parents or guardians; and

(ii) Have had an average length of stay in the institution of at least 30 days.
