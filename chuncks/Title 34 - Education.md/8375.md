
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter VI - Office of Postsecondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 692-LEVERAGING EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM
##### Subpart A-Leveraging Educational Assistance Partnership Program
###### General
####### ยง 692.4 What definitions apply to the LEAP Program?
######## Full-time student

means a student carrying a full-time academic workload-other than by correspondence-as measured by both of the following:

(1) Coursework or other required activities, as determined by the institution that the student attends or by the State.

(2) The tuition and fees normally charged for full-time study by that institution.
