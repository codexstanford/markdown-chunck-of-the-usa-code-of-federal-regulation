#### PART 200 - TITLE I - IMPROVING THE ACADEMIC ACHIEVEMENT OF THE DISADVANTAGED
##### Subpart A - Improving Basic Programs Operated by Local Educational Agencies
###### Participation of Eligible Children in Private Schools
####### ยง 200.65 Determining equitable participation of teachers and families of participating private school children.

(a) From the proportional share reserved for equitable services under ยง 200.77(d), an LEA shall ensure that teachers and families of participating private school children participate on an equitable basis in services and activities under this subpart.
