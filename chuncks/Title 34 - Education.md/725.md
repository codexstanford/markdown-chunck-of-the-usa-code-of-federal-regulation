
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education

##### Participation of Students Enrolled in Private Schools
###### ยง 76.654 Benefits for private school students.
####### Different benefits.

(c)If the needs of students enrolled in private schools are different from the needs of students enrolled in public schools, a subgrantee shall provide program benefits for the private school students that are different from the benefits the subgrantee provides for the public school students.
