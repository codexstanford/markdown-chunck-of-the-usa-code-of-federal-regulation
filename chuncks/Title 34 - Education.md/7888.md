####### Graduated repayment plan for all Direct Loan borrowers who entered repayment before July 1, 2006.

(f)(1) Under this repayment plan, a borrower must repay a loan in full by making payments at two or more levels within a period of time that varies with the total amount of the borrower's loans, as described in paragraph (i) of this section.

(2) The number of payments or the monthly repayment amount may be adjusted to reflect changes in the variable interest rate identified in ยง 685.202(a).
