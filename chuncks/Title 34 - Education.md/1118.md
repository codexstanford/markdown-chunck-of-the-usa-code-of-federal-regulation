
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 97 - PROTECTION OF HUMAN SUBJECTS
#### Subpart A - Federal Policy for the Protection of Human Subjects (Basic ED Policy for Protection of Human Research Subjects)
##### ยง 97.122 Use of Federal funds.

Federal funds administered by a Federal department or agency may not be expended for research involving human subjects unless the requirements of this policy have been satisfied.
