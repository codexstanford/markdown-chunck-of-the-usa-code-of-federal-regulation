
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 363 - THE STATE SUPPORTED EMPLOYMENT SERVICES PROGRAM
##### Subpart C - How Are State Supported Employment Services Programs Financed?
###### ยง 363.20 How does the Secretary allot funds?
####### States.

(a)The Secretary will allot the sums appropriated for each fiscal year to carry out the activities of this part among the States on the basis of relative population of each State, except that-

(1) No State will receive less than $250,000, orof 1 percent of the sums appropriated for the fiscal year for which the allotment is made, whichever amount is greater; and

(2) If the sums appropriated to carry out this part for the fiscal year exceed the sums appropriated to carry out this part (as in effect on September 30, 1992) in fiscal year 1992 by $1,000,000 or more, no State will receive less than $300,000, orof 1 percent of the sums appropriated for the fiscal year for which the allotment is made, whichever amount is greater.
