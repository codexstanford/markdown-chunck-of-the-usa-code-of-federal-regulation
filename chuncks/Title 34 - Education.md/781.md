
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 76 - STATE-ADMINISTERED PROGRAMS
#### Subpart H - How Does a State or Local Educational Agency Allocate Funds to Charter Schools?
##### General
###### ยง 76.787 What definitions apply to this subpart?
####### Covered program

means an elementary or secondary education program administered by the Department under which the Secretary allocates funds to States on a formula basis, except that the term does not include a program or portion of a program under which an SEA awards subgrants on a discretionary, noncompetitive basis.
