
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 370 - CLIENT ASSISTANCE PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 370.6 What definitions apply?
####### Services under the Act,i.e.,

means vocational rehabilitation, independent living, supported employment, and other similar rehabilitation services provided under the Act. For purposes of the CAP, the term "services under the Act" does not include activities carried out under the protection and advocacy program authorized by section 509 of the Act (the Protection and Advocacy of Individual Rights (PAIR) program, 34 CFR part 381).
