
# Title 34 - Education

### Chapter VI - Office of Postsecondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 676 - FEDERAL SUPPLEMENTAL EDUCATIONAL OPPORTUNITY GRANT PROGRAM
##### ยง 676.18 Use of funds.
###### Carry back funds for summer FSEOG awards.

(f)An institution may carry back and expend in the previous award year any portion of its initial and supplemental FSEOG allocations for the current award year to make awards to eligible students for payment periods that begin on or after May 1 of the previous award year but end prior to the beginning of the current award year.
