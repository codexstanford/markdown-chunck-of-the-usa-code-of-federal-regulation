####### Impartiality of mediator.

(c)(1) An individual who serves as a mediator under this part-

(i) May not be an employee of the SEA or the LEA that is involved in the education or care of the child; and

(ii) Must not have a personal or professional interest that conflicts with the person's objectivity.

(2) A person who otherwise qualifies as a mediator is not an employee of an LEA or State agency described under ยง 300.228 solely because he or she is paid by the agency to serve as a mediator.
