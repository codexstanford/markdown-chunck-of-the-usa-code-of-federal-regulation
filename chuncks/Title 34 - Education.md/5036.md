
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter VI - Office of Postsecondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 628 - ENDOWMENT CHALLENGE GRANT PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 628.6 What definitions apply to the Endowment Challenge Grant Program?
####### Quasi-endowment fund

means a fund which the governing board of an institution or foundation establishes to function as an endowment in that the principal is to be retained and invested. However, the entire principal and income may be spent at any time at the discretion of the governing board.
