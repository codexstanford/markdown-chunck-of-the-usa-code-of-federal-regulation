
# Title 34 - Education

###### Federal and State Monitoring and Enforcement
####### § 303.705 Withholding funds.
######## Opportunity for hearing.

(a)Prior to withholding any funds under part C of the Act, the Secretary provides reasonable notice and an opportunity for a hearing to the lead agency involved, pursuant to the procedures in §§ 303.231 through 303.236.
