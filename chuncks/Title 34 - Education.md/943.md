
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 81 - GENERAL EDUCATION PROVISIONS ACT - ENFORCEMENT
#### Appendix to Part 81 - Illustrations of Proportionality
##### Ineligible beneficiaries.

(1)A State uses 15 percent of its grant to meet the special educational needs of children who were migratory, but who have not migrated for more than five years as a Federal program statute requires for eligibility to participate in the program. Result: Recovery of 15 percent of the grant-all program funds spent for the benefit of those children. Although the services were authorized, the children were not eligible to receive them.
