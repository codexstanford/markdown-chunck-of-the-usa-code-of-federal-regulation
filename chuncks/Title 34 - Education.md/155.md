
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 8 - DEMANDS FOR TESTIMONY OR RECORDS IN LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
#### ยง 8.2 What definitions apply?
##### Testimony

means statements made in connection with a legal proceeding, including but not limited to statements in court or other forums, depositions, declarations, affidavits, or responses to interrogatories.
