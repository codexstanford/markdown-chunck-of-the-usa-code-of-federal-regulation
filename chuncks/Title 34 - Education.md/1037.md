
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 86 - DRUG AND ALCOHOL ABUSE PREVENTION
#### Subpart D - Responses and Sanctions Issued or Imposed by the Secretary for Violations by an IHE
##### ยง 86.302 What are the procedures used by the Secretary for providing information or technical assistance?

(a) The Secretary provides information or technical assistance to an IHE in writing, through site visits, or by other means.

(b) The IHE shall inform the Secretary of any corrective action it has taken within a period specified by the Secretary.
