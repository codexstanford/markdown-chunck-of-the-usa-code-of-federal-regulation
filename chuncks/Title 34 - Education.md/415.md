
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 34 - ADMINISTRATIVE WAGE GARNISHMENT
#### ยง 34.3 Definitions.
##### Day

means calendar day. For purposes of computation, the last day of a period will be included unless that day is a Saturday, a Sunday, or a Federal legal holiday; in that case, the last day of the period is the next business day after the end of the period.
