
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 390 - REHABILITATION SHORT-TERM TRAINING
##### Subpart D - How Does the Secretary Make a Grant?
###### ยง 390.30 What additional selection criterion is used under this program?
####### Relevance to State-Federal rehabilitation service program.

(a)(1) The Secretary reviews each application for information that shows that the proposed project appropriately relates to the mission of the State-Federal rehabilitation service programs.

(2) The Secretary looks for information that shows that the proposed project can be expected to improve the skills and competence of-

(i) Personnel engaged in the administration or delivery of rehabilitation services; and

(ii) Others with an interest in the delivery of rehabilitation services.
