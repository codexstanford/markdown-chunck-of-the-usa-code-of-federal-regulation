
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter VI - Office of Postsecondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 664 - FULBRIGHT-HAYS GROUP PROJECTS ABROAD PROGRAM
##### Subpart C - How Does the Secretary Make a Grant?
###### ยง 664.31 What selection criteria does the Secretary use?
####### Adequacy of resources.

(e)(1) The Secretary reviews each application for information that shows that the applicant plans to devote adequate resources to the project.

(2) The Secretary looks for information that shows that the facilities, equipment, and supplies that the applicant plans to use are adequate.
