
# Title 34 - Education

### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 303 - EARLY INTERVENTION PROGRAM FOR INFANTS AND TODDLERS WITH DISABILITIES
##### Subpart F - Use of Funds and Payor of Last Resort
###### Payor of Last Resort & System of Payments Provisions - Use of Insurance, Benefits, Systems of Payments, and Fees
####### ยง 303.521 System of payments and fees.
######## Family fees.

(d)(1) Fees or costs collected from a parent or the child's family to pay for early intervention services under a State's system of payments are program income under 2 CFR 200.307. A State may add this program income to its part C grant funds, rather than deducting the program income from the amount of the State's part C grant. Any fees collected must be used for the purposes of the grant under part C of the Act.

(2) Fees collected under a system of payments are considered neither State nor local funds under ยง 303.225(b).
