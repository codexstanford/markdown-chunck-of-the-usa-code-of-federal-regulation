
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter III - Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, Department of Education
#### PART 363 - THE STATE SUPPORTED EMPLOYMENT SERVICES PROGRAM
##### Subpart C - How Are State Supported Employment Services Programs Financed?
###### ยง 363.24 What is program income and how may it be used?
####### Definition.,Program income

(a)(1)means gross income earned by the State that is directly generated by authorized activities supported under this part or earned as a result of the Federal award during the period of performance.

(2) Program income received through the transfer of Social Security Administration payments from the State Vocational Rehabilitation Services program, in accordance with 34 CFR 361.63(c)(2), will be treated as program income received under this part.
