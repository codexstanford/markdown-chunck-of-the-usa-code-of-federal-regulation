
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter II - Office of Elementary and Secondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 263 - INDIAN EDUCATION DISCRETIONARY GRANT PROGRAMS
##### Subpart A - Professional Development Program
###### ยง 263.11 What are the participant payback reporting requirements?
####### Work-related payback.

(b)(1) Starting within six months after exit from or completion of the program, participants must submit to the Secretary employment information, which includes information explaining how the employment is related to the training received and benefits Indian students in an LEA that serves a high proportion of Indian students.

(2) Participants must submit an employment status report every six months beginning from the date the work-related service is to begin until the payback obligation has been fulfilled.
