
# Title 34 - Education

###### ยง 367.5 What definitions apply?
####### Subrecipient

(18)means a non-Federal entity that receives a subaward from the DSA to carry out part of the program funded under this part; but does not include an individual that is a beneficiary of such program. A subrecipient may also be a recipient of other Federal awards directly from a Federal awarding agency.
