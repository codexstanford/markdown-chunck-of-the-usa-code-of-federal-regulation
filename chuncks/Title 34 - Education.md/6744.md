###### ยง 674.51 Special definitions.
####### Librarian with a master's degree:

(o)A librarian with a master's degree is an information professional trained in library or information science who has obtained a postgraduate academic degree in library science awarded after the completion of an academic program of up to six years in duration, excluding a doctorate or professional degree.
