####### The school or lender or holder is the subject of a lawsuit or Federal administrative proceeding.

(c)In this situation, if the Secretary determines that the proceeding involves allegations that, if proven, would provide the borrower with a full or partial defense on the loan, then the Secretary may suspend collection activity on all or part of a loan until the proceeding ends.
