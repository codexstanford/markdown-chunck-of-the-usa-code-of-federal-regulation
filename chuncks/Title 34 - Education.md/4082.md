
# Title 34 - Education

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 371.6 What definitions apply to this program?
####### Consortium

means two or more eligible governing bodies of Indian tribes that apply for an award under this program by either:

(i) Designating one governing body to apply for the grant; or

(ii) Establishing and designating a tribal organization to apply for a grant.
