
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Offices of the Department of Education
### Chapter VI - Office of Postsecondary Education, Department of Education
#### PART 628 - ENDOWMENT CHALLENGE GRANT PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 628.6 What definitions apply to the Endowment Challenge Grant Program?
####### Endowment fund

means a fund which excludes real estate and which is established by State law, by an institution, or by a foundation that is exempt from taxation and is maintained for the purpose of generating income for the support of the institution. The principal or corpus of the fund may not be spent. "Endowment fund" includes "quasi-endowment fund".
