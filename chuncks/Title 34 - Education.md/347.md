
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 33 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT
#### ยง 33.2 Definitions.
##### Investigating official

means the Inspector General of the Department or an officer or employee of the Office of the Inspector General designated by the Inspector General and serving in a position for which the rate of basic pay is not less than the minimum rate of basic pay for grade GS-16 under the General Schedule.
