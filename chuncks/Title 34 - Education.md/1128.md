
# Title 34 - Education
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Education
### PART 97 - PROTECTION OF HUMAN SUBJECTS
#### Subpart D - Additional ED Protections for Children Who Are Subjects in Research
##### ยง 97.403 IRB duties.

In addition to other responsibilities assigned to IRBs under this part, each IRB shall review research covered by this subpart and approve only research that satisfies the conditions of all applicable sections of this subpart.
