(2) If, prior to the parents' removal of the child from the public school, the public agency informed the parents, through the notice requirements described in ยง 300.503(a)(1), of its intent to evaluate the child (including a statement of the purpose of the evaluation that was appropriate and reasonable), but the parents did not make the child available for the evaluation; or

(3) Upon a judicial finding of unreasonableness with respect to actions taken by the parents.
