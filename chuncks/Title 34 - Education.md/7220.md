
# Title 34 - Education

##### Subpart B - General Provisions
###### ยง 682.215 Income-based repayment plan.
####### Adjusted gross income

(1)(AGI) means the borrower's adjusted gross income as reported to the Internal Revenue Service. For a married borrower filing jointly, AGI includes both the borrower's and spouse's income. For a married borrower filing separately, AGI includes only the borrower's income.
