
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 227 - OCCUPATIONAL NOISE EXPOSURE
##### Appendix B to Part 227 - Methods for Estimating the Adequacy of Hearing Protector Attenuation
###### http://www.ansi.org.,http://www.archives.gov/federal_register/code_of_federal_regulations/ibr_locations.html.

Use Method B, which is found in ANSI S12.6-1997 (Reaffirmed 2002) "Methods for Measuring the Real-Ear Attenuation of Hearing Protectors." The Director of the Federal Register approves the incorporation by reference of this standard in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51.
