######## Viscous liquid

means a liquid material which has a measured viscosity in excess of 2500 centistokes at 25 &#xB0;C. (77 &#xB0;F.) when determined in accordance with the procedures specified in ASTM Method D 445-72 "Kinematic Viscosity of Transparent and Opaque Liquids (and the Calculation of Dynamic Viscosity)" or ASTM Method D 1200-70 "Viscosity of Paints, Varnishes, and Lacquers by Ford Viscosity Cup."
