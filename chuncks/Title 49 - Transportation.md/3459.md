
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter I - Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### Subchapter B - Oil Transportation
##### PART 130 - OIL SPILL PREVENTION AND RESPONSE PLANS
###### Subpart A - Applicability and General Requirements
####### ยง 130.5 Definitions.
######## Maximum extent practicable

means the limits of available technology and the practical and technical limits on an owner or operator of an onshore facility in planning the response resources required to provide the on-water recovery capability and the shoreline protection and cleanup capability to conduct response activities for a worst-case discharge of oil in adverse weather.
