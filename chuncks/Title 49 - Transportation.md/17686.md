######## Headlamps.

(a)Every bus, truck and truck tractor shall be equipped with headlamps as required by ยง 393.11(a). The headlamps shall provide an upper and lower beam distribution of light, selectable at the driver's will and be steady-burning. The headlamps shall be marked in accordance with FMVSS No. 108.
