
# Title 49 - Transportation

###### ยง 571.219 Standard No. 219; Windshield zone intrusion.
####### Definitions. Daylight Opening

S4.(DLO) means the maximum unobstructed opening through the glazing surface, including reveal or garnish moldings adjoining the surface, as measured parallel to the outer surface of the glazing material.
