######## Division 4.3 (Dangerous when wet material).,dangerous when wet material

(c)For the purposes of this chapter,(Division 4.3) means a material that, by contact with water, is liable to become spontaneously flammable or to give off flammable or toxic gas at a rate greater than 1 L per kilogram of the material, per hour, when tested in accordance with UN Manual of Tests and Criteria.
