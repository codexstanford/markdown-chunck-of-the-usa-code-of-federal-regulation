
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VI - Federal Transit Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 673 - PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION AGENCY SAFETY PLANS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 673.5 Definitions.
####### Small public transportation provider

means a recipient or subrecipient of Federal financial assistance under 49 U.S.C. 5307 that has one hundred (100) or fewer vehicles in peak revenue service and does not operate a rail fixed guideway public transportation system.
