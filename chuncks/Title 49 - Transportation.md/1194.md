
# Title 49 - Transportation

#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 26.5 What do the terms used in this part mean?
###### Recipient

is any entity, public or private, to which DOT financial assistance is extended, whether directly or through another recipient, through the programs of the FAA, FHWA, or FTA, or who has applied for such assistance.
