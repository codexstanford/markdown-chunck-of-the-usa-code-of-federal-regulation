
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 571 - FEDERAL MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.116 Standard No. 116; Motor vehicle brake fluids.
####### Kinematic viscosities.

S5.1.3.When brake fluid is tested according to S6.3, the kinematic viscosities in square millimeters per second at stated temperatures shall be neither less than 1.5 mm/s at 100 &#xB0;C. (212 &#xB0;F.) nor more than the following maximum value for the grade indicated:

(a) DOT 3: 1,500 mm/s at minus 40 &#xB0;C. (minus 40 &#xB0;F.).

(b) DOT 4: 1,800 mm/s at minus 40 &#xB0;C. (minus 40 &#xB0;F.).

(c) DOT 5: 900 mm/s at minus 40 &#xB0;C. (minus 40 &#xB0;F.).
