
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 24 - UNIFORM RELOCATION ASSISTANCE AND REAL PROPERTY ACQUISITION FOR FEDERAL AND FEDERALLY-ASSISTED PROGRAMS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 24.2 Definitions and acronyms.
###### initiation of negotiations,initiation of negotiations

(i) Whenever the displacement results from the acquisition of the real property by a Federal Agency or State Agency, themeans the delivery of the initial written offer of just compensation by the Agency to the owner or the owner's representative to purchase the real property for the project. However, if the Federal Agency or State Agency issues a notice of its intent to acquire the real property, and a person moves after that notice, but before delivery of the initial written purchase offer, themeans the actual move of the person from the property.
