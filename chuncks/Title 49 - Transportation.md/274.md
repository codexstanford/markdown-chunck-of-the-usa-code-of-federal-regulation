
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 8 - CLASSIFIED INFORMATION: CLASSIFICATION/DECLASSIFICATION/ACCESS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 8.5 Definitions.
###### Information

means any knowledge that can be communicated, or documentary material, regardless of its physical form or characteristics, that is owned by, produced by or for, or is under the control of the United States Government. "Control" means the authority of the agency that originates information, or its successor in function, to regulate access to the information.
