### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 220 - RAILROAD COMMUNICATIONS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 220.5 Definitions.
####### Railroad operation

means any activity which affects the movement of a train, locomotive, on-track equipment, or track motor car, singly or in combination with other equipment, on the track of a railroad.
