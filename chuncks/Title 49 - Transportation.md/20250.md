
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 563 - EVENT DATA RECORDERS
##### ยง 563.5 Definitions.
###### Time to deploy, side air bag/curtain

means the elapsed time from crash time zero to the deployment command for a side air bag or a side curtain/tube air bag (for both driver and right front passenger).
