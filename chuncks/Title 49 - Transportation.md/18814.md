###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 397.1 Application of the rules in this part.

(a) The rules in this part apply to each motor carrier engaged in the transportation of hazardous materials by a motor vehicle which must be marked or placarded in accordance with ยง 177.823 of this title and to-

(1) Each officer or employee of the motor carrier who performs supervisory duties related to the transportation of hazardous materials; and
