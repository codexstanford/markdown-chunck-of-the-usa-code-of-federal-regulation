
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 578 - CIVIL AND CRIMINAL PENALTIES
##### ยง 578.8 Civil penalty factors under 49 U.S.C. Chapter 301.
###### Whether the respondent has been assessed civil penalties under this section during the most recent 5 years

(8)means whether the respondent has been assessed civil penalties, including a settlement agreement containing a penalty, a consent order or a lawsuit involving a penalty or payment of a civil penalty in the most recent 5 years from the date of the alleged violation, regardless of whether there was any admission of a violation or of liability, under 49 U.S.C. 30165.
