(c)Rigid plastic Large Packagings and Large Packagings with plastic inner receptacles must be conditioned for testing by reducing the temperature of the packaging and its contents to -18 &#xB0;C (0 &#xB0;F) or lower. Test liquids must be kept in the liquid state, if necessary, by the addition of anti-freeze. Water/anti-freeze solutions with a minimum specific gravity of 0.95 for testing at -18 &#xB0;C (0 &#xB0;F) or lower are considered acceptable test liquids, and may be considered equivalent to water for test purposes.
