
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 71 - STANDARD TIME ZONE BOUNDARIES
#### ยง 71.3 Atlantic zone.

The first zone, the Atlantic standard time zone, includes that part of the United States that is between 52&#xB0;30&#x2033; W. longitude and 67&#xB0;30&#x2033; W. longitude and that part of the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico that is west of 67&#xB0;30&#x2033; W. longitude, but does not include any part of the State of Maine.
