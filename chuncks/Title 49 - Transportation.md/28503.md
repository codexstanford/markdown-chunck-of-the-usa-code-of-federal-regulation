###### ยง 1113.7 Intervention; petitions.
####### Broadening issues; filing.

(d)If the petition seeks a broadening of the issues and shows that they would not thereby be unduly broadened, and in respect thereof seeks affirmative relief, the petition should be filed in time to permit service upon and answer by the parties in advance of the hearing.
