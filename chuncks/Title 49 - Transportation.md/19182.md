
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart C - Prehearing Procedures; Motions; Interlocutory Appeals; Summary Judgment; Settlement

####### Rejection.

(h)If the Administrator rejects an offer of settlement, the Administrator shall give written notice of that decision and the reasons therefor to the parties and the Presiding Officer. Promptly thereafter, the Presiding Officer shall issue an order notifying the parties of the resumption of the proceedings, including any modifications to the schedule resulting from the stay of the proceedings.
