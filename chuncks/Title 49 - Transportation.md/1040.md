
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 24 - UNIFORM RELOCATION ASSISTANCE AND REAL PROPERTY ACQUISITION FOR FEDERAL AND FEDERALLY-ASSISTED PROGRAMS
#### Appendix B to Part 24 - Statistical Report Form
##### Line 5.,not

Report the number of households who were permanently displaced during the fiscal year by project or program activities and moved to their replacement dwelling. The term "households" includes all families and individuals. A family shall be reported as "one" household,by the number of people in the family unit.
