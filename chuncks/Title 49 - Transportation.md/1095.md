#### Subpart B - Coverage
##### § 25.220 Admissions.
###### Educational institutions.

(d)Except as provided in paragraph (e) of this section as to recipients that are educational institutions, §§ 25.300 through 25.310 apply only to institutions of vocational education, professional education, graduate higher education, and public institutions of undergraduate higher education.
