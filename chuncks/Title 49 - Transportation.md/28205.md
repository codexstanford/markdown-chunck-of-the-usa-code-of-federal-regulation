
# Title 49 - Transportation

#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1105 - PROCEDURES FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF ENVIRONMENTAL LAWS
###### ยง 1105.7 Environmental reports.
####### Proposed action and alternatives.

(1)Describe the proposed action, including commodities transported, the planned disposition (if any) of any rail line and other structures that may be involved, and any possible changes in current operations or maintenance practices. Also describe any reasonable alternatives to the proposed action. Include a readable, detailed map and drawings clearly delineating the project.
