
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 38 - AMERICANS WITH DISABILITIES ACT (ADA) ACCESSIBILITY SPECIFICATIONS FOR TRANSPORTATION VEHICLES
#### Subpart F - Intercity Rail Cars and Systems
##### ยง 38.113 Doorways.
###### Coordination with boarding platforms,Requirements.

(d)-(1)Cars which provide level-boarding in stations with high platforms shall be coordinated with the boarding platform or mini-high platform design such that the horizontal gap between a car at rest and the platform shall be no greater than 3 inches and the height of the car floor shall be within plus or minusinch of the platform height. Vertical alignment may be accomplished by car air suspension, platform lifts or other devices, or any combination.
