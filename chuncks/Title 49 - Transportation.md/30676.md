
# Title 49 - Transportation

###### Subpart C - Compliance and Enforcement

######## Extension of time.

(h)TSA may grant an extension of time to the limits set forth in this section for good cause shown. A repair station must request an extension of time in writing, and TSA must receive it at least two days before the due date in order to be considered. TSA may grant itself an extension of time for good cause.
