
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.202a Standard No. 202a; Head restraints; Mandatory applicability begins on September 1, 2009.
####### Gaps within the head restraint and between the head restraint and seat using a 165 mm sphere.

S4.2.4.1When measured in accordance with S5.2.4.1 of this section using the head form specified in that paragraph, there must not be any gap greater than 60 mm within or between the anterior surface of the head restraint and anterior surface of the seat, with the head restraint adjusted to its lowest height position and any backset position, except as allowed by S4.4.
