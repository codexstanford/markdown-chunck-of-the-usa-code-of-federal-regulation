
# Title 49 - Transportation

(a)Each passenger car ordered on or after April 1, 2009, or placed in service for the first time on or after April 1, 2011, shall have a minimum of two emergency roof access locations, each with a minimum opening of 26 inches longitudinally (, parallel to the longitudinal axis of the car) by 24 inches laterally.
