####### ยง 173.419 Authorized packages - oxidizing Class 7 (radioactive) materials.

(a) An oxidizing Class 7 (radioactive) material, as referenced in the ยง 172.101 table of this subchapter, is authorized in quantities not exceeding an Aper package, in a DOT Specification 7A package provided that-

(iii) Cushioned with a material that will not react with the contents; and

(2) The outside packaging is made of wood, metal, or plastic.

(c) For shipment by air, the maximum quantity in any package may not exceed 11.3 kg (25 pounds).
