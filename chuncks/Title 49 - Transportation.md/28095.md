
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter X - Surface Transportation Board
#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1101 - DEFINITIONS AND CONSTRUCTION
###### ยง 1101.3 Construction.
####### et seq.

The rules of construction contained in chapter 1 of title 1 of the United States Code (1 U.S.C. 1) apply in this chapter. Among other things, they provide that the singular includes the plural, and vice versa; that the masculine includes the feminine; that the word "person" includes corporations, associations, and the like; that "county" includes parish and similar subdivisions; and that "company" includes successors and assigns.
