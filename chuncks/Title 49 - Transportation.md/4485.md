###### Subpart C - Definitions, Classification and Packaging for Class 1
####### ยง 173.59 Description of terms for explosives.
######## Ignition, means of.

A general term used in connection with the method employed to ignite a deflagrating train of explosive or pyrotechnic substances (for example: a primer for propelling charge, an igniter for a rocket motor or an igniting fuze).
