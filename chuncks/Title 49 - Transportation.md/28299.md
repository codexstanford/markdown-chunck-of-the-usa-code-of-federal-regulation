
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter X - Surface Transportation Board
#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1108 - ARBITRATION OF CERTAIN DISPUTES SUBJECT TO THE STATUTORY JURISDICTION OF THE SURFACE TRANSPORTATION BOARD
###### Subpart A - General Arbitration Procedures
####### ยง 1108.8 Relief.
######## Relief available.

(a)An arbitrator may grant relief in the form of monetary damages or a rate prescription in rate disputes to the extent they are available under this subpart or as agreed to in writing by the parties. A rate prescription shall not exceed 5 years.
