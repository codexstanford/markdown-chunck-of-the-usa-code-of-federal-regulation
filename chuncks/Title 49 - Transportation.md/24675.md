
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.304 Standard No. 304; Compressed natural gas fuel container integrity.
####### Brazing

means a group of welding processes wherein coalescence is produced by heating to a suitable temperature above 800 &#xB0;F and by using a nonferrous filler metal, having a melting point below that to the base metals. The filler metal is distributed between the closely fitted surfaces of the joint by capillary attraction.
