
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 230 - STEAM LOCOMOTIVE INSPECTION AND MAINTENANCE STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Boilers and Appurtenances
###### Injectors, Feedwater Pumps, and Flue Plugs
####### ยง 230.58 Flue plugs.
######## When plugging is permitted.

(a)Flues greater than 2inches in outside diameter (OD) shall not be plugged. Flues 2inches in outside diameter (OD) or smaller may be plugged following failure, provided only one flue is plugged at any one time. Plugs must be removed and proper repairs made no later than 30 days from the time the plug is applied.
