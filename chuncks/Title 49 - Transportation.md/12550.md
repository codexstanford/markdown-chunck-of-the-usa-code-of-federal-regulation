####### Duty of Operating Railroad when operating railroads operate on separate tracks.

The Inventory Form, or its electronic equivalent, which contains this crossing data shall reference the Inventory Number assigned to the crossing by the primary operating railroad and shall be completed and submitted consistent with ยง 234.403 and the Inventory Guide.
