
# Title 49 - Transportation

####### Vehicle speed measurement.

S6.3.2Instruments used to measure vehicle speed during the constant speed pass-by tests in S7 of this standard will be capable of either continuous measurement of speed within +/-0.5 km/h over the entire measurement zone specified in S6.4 or independent measurements of speed within +/-0.2 km/h at the beginning and end of the measurement zone specified in S6.4.
