##### ยง 24.105 Acquisition of tenant-owned improvements.
###### Improvements considered to be real property.

(b)Any building, structure, or other improvement, which would be considered to be real property if owned by the owner of the real property on which it is located, shall be considered to be real property for purposes of this subpart.
