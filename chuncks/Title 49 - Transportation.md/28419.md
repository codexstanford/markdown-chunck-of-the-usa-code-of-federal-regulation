
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter X - Surface Transportation Board
#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1109 - USE OF MEDIATION IN BOARD PROCEEDINGS
###### ยง 1109.4 Mandatory mediation in rate cases to be considered under the stand-alone cost methodology.
####### Mandatory use of mediation.

(a)A shipper seeking rate relief from a railroad or railroads in a case involving the stand-alone cost methodology must engage in non-binding mediation of its dispute with the railroad upon submitting a pre-filing notice under 49 CFR part 1111.
