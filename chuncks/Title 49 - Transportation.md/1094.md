
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 25 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart B - Coverage
##### § 25.220 Admissions.
###### Application of §§ 25.300 through .310.

(c)Except as provided in paragraphs (d) and (e) of this section, §§ 25.300 through 25.310 apply to each recipient. A recipient to which §§ 25.300 through 25.310 apply shall not discriminate on the basis of sex in admission or recruitment in violation of §§ 25.300 through 25.310.
