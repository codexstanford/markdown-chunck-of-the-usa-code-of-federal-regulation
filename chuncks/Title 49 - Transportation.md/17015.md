
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter III - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### Subchapter B - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations
##### PART 389 - RULEMAKING PROCEDURES - FEDERAL MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY REGULATIONS
###### Subpart B - Procedures for Adoption of Rules
####### ยง 389.39 Direct final rulemaking procedures.
######## Withdrawal of a direct final rule.

(d)(1) If FMCSA receives an adverse comment within the comment period, it will either publish a document withdrawing the direct final rule before it becomes effective and may issue an NPRM, or proceed by any other means permitted under the Administrative Procedure Act.

(2) If FMCSA withdraws a direct final rule because of an adverse comment, the Agency may issue a notice of proposed rulemaking if it decides to pursue the rulemaking.
