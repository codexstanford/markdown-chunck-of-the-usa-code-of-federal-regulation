
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

###### ยง 579.25 Reporting requirements for manufacturers of child restraint systems.
####### Production information.

(a)Information that states the manufacturer's name, the quarterly reporting period, the make, the model, the production year, the type, and the production. The production shall be stated as either the cumulative production of the current model year to the end of the reporting period, or the total calendar year production for each calendar year for which production has ceased.
