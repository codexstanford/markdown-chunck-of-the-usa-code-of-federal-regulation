
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 299 - TEXAS CENTRAL RAILROAD HIGH-SPEED RAIL SAFETY STANDARDS
##### Subpart D - Rolling Stock
###### ยง 299.435 Electrical system design.
####### High voltage markings: doors, cover plates, or barriers.

(g)External surfaces of all doors, cover plates, or barriers providing direct access to high voltage equipment shall be conspicuously and legibly marked "DANGER-HIGH VOLTAGE" or with the word "DANGER" and the normal voltage carried by the parts so protected. Labels shall be retro-reflective.
