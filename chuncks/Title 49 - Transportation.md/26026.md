### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 586 - REPLICA MOTOR VEHICLES
##### ยง 586.10 Updating existing registrations.

A registered replica manufacturer shall submit updated registration information prior to commencing manufacture of a new model of replica vehicle or reallocating the number of replica vehicles to be made by two or more replica manufacturers under common ownership. The manufacturer shall submit updated registration information pursuant to ยง 586.6.
