####### Procedure.

S14.9.3.8.2

S14.9.3.8.2.1 The test is conducted in an ambient temperature of 75&#xB0; +/-10 &#xB0;F with the maximum design load connected and the power source for the test circuit adjusted to apply design voltage at the bulbs.

S14.9.3.8.2.2 The voltage drop is measured between the input and load terminals of the flasher during the "on" period after the flashers have completed at least five consecutive cycles.
