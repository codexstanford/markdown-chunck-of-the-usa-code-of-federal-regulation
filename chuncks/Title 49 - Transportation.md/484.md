
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 15 - PROTECTION OF SENSITIVE SECURITY INFORMATION
#### ยง 15.19 Destruction of SSI.
##### DOT.

(a)Subject to the requirements of the Federal Records Act (5 U.S.C. 105), including the duty to preserve records containing documentation of a Federal agency's policies, decisions, and essential transactions, DOT destroys SSI when no longer needed to carry out the agency's function.
