
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 15 - PROTECTION OF SENSITIVE SECURITY INFORMATION
#### ยง 15.15 SSI disclosed by DOT.
##### Disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act and the Privacy Act.

(b)If a record contains both SSI and information that is not SSI, DOT, on a proper Freedom of Information Act or Privacy Act request, may disclose the record with the SSI redacted, provided the record is not otherwise exempt from disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act or Privacy Act.
