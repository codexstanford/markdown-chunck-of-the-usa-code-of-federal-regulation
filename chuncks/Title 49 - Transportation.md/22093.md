
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 571 - FEDERAL MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.116 Standard No. 116; Motor vehicle brake fluids.
####### Summary of the procedure.

S6.12.1Four standard SAE SBR WC cups are measured and their hardnesses determined. The cups, two to a jar, are immersed in the test brake fluid. One jar is heated for 70 hours at 70 &#xB0;C. (158 &#xB0;F), and the other for 70 hours at 120 &#xB0;C (248 &#xB0;F). Afterwards, the cups are washed, examined for disintegration, remeasured and their hardnesses redetermined.
