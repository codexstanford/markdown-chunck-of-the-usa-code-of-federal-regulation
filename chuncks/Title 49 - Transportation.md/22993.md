
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.139 Standard No. 139; New pneumatic radial tires for light vehicles.
####### Pneumatic tire

means a mechanical device made of rubber, chemicals, fabric and steel or other materials, that, when mounted on an automotive wheel, provides the traction and contains the gas or fluid that sustains the load.
