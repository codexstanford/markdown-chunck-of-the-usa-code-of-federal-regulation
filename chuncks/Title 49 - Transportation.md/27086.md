### Chapter VI - Federal Transit Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 674.7 Definitions.
####### Hazard

means any real or potential condition that can cause injury, illness, or death; damage to or loss of the facilities, equipment, rolling stock, or infrastructure of a rail fixed guideway public transportation system; or damage to the environment.
