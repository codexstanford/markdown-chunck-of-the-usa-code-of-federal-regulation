
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VIII - National Transportation Safety Board
#### PART 840 - RULES PERTAINING TO NOTIFICATION OF RAILROAD ACCIDENTS
##### ยง 840.2 Definitions.
###### Railroad

(a)means any system of surface transportation of persons or property over rails. It includes, but is not limited to, line-haul freight and passenger-carrying railroads, and rapid transit, commuter, scenic, subway, and elevated railways.
