
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.105 Standard No. 105; Hydraulic and electric brake systems.
####### First reburnish.

S7.6Repeat S7.4, except make 35 burnish stops or snubs. In the case of vehicles burnished in accordance with S7.4.2.1(a) of this section, reburnish the vehicle by making 35 snubs from 60 to 20 mph, but if the hottest brake temperature reaches 500 &#xB0;F +/-50 &#xB0;F, make the remainder of the brake applications from the highest snub condition listed in Table IV that will maintain the hottest brake temperature at 500 &#xB0;F +/-50 &#xB0;F. If at a snub condition of 40 to 20 mph, the temperature of the hottest brake exceeds 550 &#xB0;F, make the remainder of the 35 brake applications from the snub condition without regard to brake temperature.
