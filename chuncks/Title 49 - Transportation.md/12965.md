
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart I - Positive Train Control Systems
###### ยง 236.1003 Definitions.
####### Interoperability

means the ability of a controlling locomotive to communicate with and respond to the PTC railroad's positive train control system, including uninterrupted movements over property boundaries.
