### Chapter III - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

##### PART 390 - FEDERAL MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY REGULATIONS; GENERAL
###### Subpart A - General Applicability and Definitions
####### ยง 390.4 Delegations and redelegations of authority of FMCSA employees to perform assigned actions or duties.
######## FMCSA Administrator authority to delegate and redelegate.

(b)(1) The FMCSA Administrator is authorized to delegate and redelegate authority and authorize successive redelegations.

(2) The FMCSA Administrator retains concurrent authority to exercise or redelegate any authority that he or she has delegated to an employee in regulation, directive, or memorandum.
