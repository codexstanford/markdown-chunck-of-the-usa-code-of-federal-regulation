
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### PART 376 - LEASE AND INTERCHANGE OF VEHICLES
###### Subpart A - General Applicability and Definitions
####### ยง 376.2 Definitions.
######## Private carrier.

(j)A person, other than a motor carrier, transporting property by motor vehicle in interstate or foreign commerce when (1) the person is the owner, lessee, or bailee of the property being transported; and (2) the property is being transported for sale, lease, rent, or bailment, or to further a commercial enterprise.
