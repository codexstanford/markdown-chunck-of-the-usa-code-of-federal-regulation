
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 587 - DEFORMABLE BARRIERS
##### Subpart C - Offset Deformable Barrier
###### ยง 587.15 Verification of aluminum honeycomb crush strength.
####### Sample size.

(b)Samples of the following size are used for testing. The length is 150 mm (5.9 in) +/-6 mm (0.24 in), the width is 150 mm (5.9 in) +/-6 mm (0.24 in), and the thickness is 25 mm (1 in) +/-2 mm (0.08 in). The walls of incomplete cells around the edge of the sample are trimmed as follows (See Figure 3). In the width ("W") direction, the fringes ("f") are no greater than 1.8 mm (0.07 in); in the length ("L") direction, the fringes ("e") are at least half the length of one bonded cell wall ("d") (in the ribbon direction).
