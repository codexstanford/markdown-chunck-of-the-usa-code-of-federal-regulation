(i)(1) If a carline is assembled in more than one assembly plant, the manufacturer may, at its option, add the following additional information at the end of the explanatory note specified in paragraph (a)(6) of this section, with the specified information inserted in the places indicated:

(2) A manufacturer selecting this option shall divide the carline for purposes of this additional information into portions representing each assembly plant.
