#### Subchapter D - Pipeline Safety

###### Subpart A - General
####### § 192.9 What requirements apply to gathering pipelines?
######## Offshore lines.

(b)An operator of an offshore gathering line must comply with requirements of this part applicable to transmission lines, except the requirements in §§ 192.150, 192.285(e), 192.493, 192.506, 192.607, 192.619(e), 192.624, 192.710, 192.712, and in subpart O of this part.
