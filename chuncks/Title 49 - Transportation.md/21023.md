
# Title 49 - Transportation

###### ยง 571.108 Standard No. 108; Lamps, reflective devices, and associated equipment.
####### Vehicle headlamp aiming device or VHAD

means motor vehicle equipment, installed either on a vehicle or headlamp, which is used for determining the horizontal or vertical aim, or both the vertical and horizontal aim of the headlamp.
