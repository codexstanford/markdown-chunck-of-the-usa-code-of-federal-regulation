
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 571 - FEDERAL MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.111 Standard No. 111; Rear visibility.
####### Temperature exposure test procedure.

S14.3.3The external components are subjected to 4 consecutive 2-hour temperature test cycles. In each temperature test cycle, the external components are first subjected to a temperature of 176&#xB0; +/-5 &#xB0;F (80&#xB0; +/-3 &#xB0;C) for a period of one hour. After a period not to exceed 5 minutes, the external components are subjected to a temperature of 32&#xB0; + 5&#xB0; -0 &#xB0;F (0&#xB0; + 3&#xB0; -0 &#xB0;C) for 1 hour. Allow no more than 5 minutes to elapse between each test cycle.
