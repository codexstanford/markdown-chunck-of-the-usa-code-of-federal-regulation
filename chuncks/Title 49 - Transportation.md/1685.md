
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 30 - DENIAL OF PUBLIC WORKS CONTRACTS TO SUPPLIERS OF GOODS AND SERVICES OF COUNTRIES THAT DENY PROCUREMENT MARKET ACCESS TO U.S. CONTRACTORS
#### ยง 30.3 Applicability.
##### Provided, however,

(e) The restrictions of this part also apply to all products used in the construction, alteration, or repair of public projects and public works;That

(1) The restrictions of this part do not apply to construction equipment or vehicles that do not become part of a delivered structure, product, or project and
