
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VI - Federal Transit Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 673 - PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION AGENCY SAFETY PLANS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 673.5 Definitions.
####### Safety Management System (SMS)

means the formal, top-down, organization-wide approach to managing safety risk and assuring the effectiveness of a transit agency's safety risk mitigation. SMS includes systematic procedures, practices, and policies for managing risks and hazards.
