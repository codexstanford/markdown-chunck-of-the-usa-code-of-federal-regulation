
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 230 - STEAM LOCOMOTIVE INSPECTION AND MAINTENANCE STANDARDS
##### Subpart C - Steam Locomotives and Tenders
###### Ash Pans
####### ยง 230.69 Ash pans.

Ash pans shall be securely supported from mud-rings or frames with no part less than 2inches above the rail. Their operating mechanism shall be so arranged that they may be safely operated and securely closed.
