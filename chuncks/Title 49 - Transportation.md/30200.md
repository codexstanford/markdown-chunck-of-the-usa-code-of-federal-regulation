
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter XII - Transportation Security Administration, Department of Homeland Security
#### Subchapter C - Civil Aviation Security
##### PART 1540 - CIVIL AVIATION SECURITY: GENERAL RULES
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 1540.5 Terms used in this subchapter.
######## Cargo

means property tendered for air transportation accounted for on an air waybill. All accompanied commercial courier consignments, whether or not accounted for on an air waybill, are also classified as cargo. Aircraft operator security programs further define the term "cargo."
