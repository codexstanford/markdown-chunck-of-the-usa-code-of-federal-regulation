###### Subpart I - Protection Against Shifting and Falling Cargo

######## ยง 393.132 What are the rules for securing flattened or crushed vehicles?
######### Prohibition on the use of synthetic webbing.

(b)The use of synthetic webbing to secure flattened or crushed vehicles is prohibited except that such webbing may be used to connect wire rope or chain to anchor points on the commercial motor vehicle. However, the webbing (regardless of whether edge protection is used) must not come into contact with the flattened or crushed cars.
