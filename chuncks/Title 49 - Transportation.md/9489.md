####### Crushed head

(4)means a short length of rail, not at a joint, which has drooped or sagged across the width of the rail head to a depth ofinch or more below the rest of the rail head and 8 inches or more in length. Unlike flattened rail where the depression is visible on the rail head only, the sagging or drooping is also visible in the head/web fillet area.
