
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.304 Standard No. 304; Compressed natural gas fuel container integrity.
####### Service pressure

means the internal settled pressure of a CNG fuel container at a uniform gas temperature of 21 &#xB0;C (70 &#xB0;F) and full gas content. It is the pressure for which the container has been constructed under normal conditions.
