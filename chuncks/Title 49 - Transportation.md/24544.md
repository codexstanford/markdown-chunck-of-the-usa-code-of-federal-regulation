
# Title 49 - Transportation

###### ยง 571.226 Standard No. 226; Ejection Mitigation.
####### Zero displacement plane

means, a vertical plane parallel to the vehicle longitudinal centerline and tangent to the most outboard surface of the ejection headform when the headform is aligned with an impact target location and just touching the inside surface of a window covering the side daylight opening.
