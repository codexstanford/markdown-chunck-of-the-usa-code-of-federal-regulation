
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter III - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration, Department of Transportation

##### PART 398 - TRANSPORTATION OF MIGRANT WORKERS
###### ยง 398.4 Driving of motor vehicles.
####### Doors, tarpaulins, tailgates and other equipment.

(2)No motor vehicle shall be driven unless the tailgate, tailboard, tarpaulins, doors, all equipment and rigging used in the operation of said vehicle, and all means of fastening the load, are securely in place.
