
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 31 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES
#### ยง 31.2 Definitions.
##### Representative

means an attorney who is a member in good standing of the bar of any State, Territory, or possession of the United States or of the District of Columbia or the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico. This definition is not intended to foreclose pro se appearances. An individual may appear for himself or herself, and a corporation or other entity may appear by an owner, officer, or employee of the corporation or entity.
