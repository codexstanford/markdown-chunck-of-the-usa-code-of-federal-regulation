####### General.

(a)(1) The railroad shall develop a written inspection program for the rolling stock, in accordance with and approved under the requirements of ยง 299.713. As further specified in this section, the program shall describe in detail the procedures, equipment, and other means necessary for the safe operation of the passenger equipment, including all inspections set forth in paragraph (e) of this section. This information shall include a detailed description of the methods of ensuring accurate records of required inspections.

(2) The initial inspection, testing, and maintenance program submitted under ยง 299.713 shall, as a minimum, address the specific safety inspections contained in paragraphs (e)(1) through (4) of this section.
