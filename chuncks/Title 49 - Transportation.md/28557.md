
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter X - Surface Transportation Board
#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1114 - EVIDENCE; DISCOVERY
###### Subpart B - Discovery
####### ยง 1114.25 Effect of errors and irregularities in depositions.
######## As to disqualification of officer.

(a)Objection to taking a deposition because of disqualification of the officer before whom it is to be taken is waived unless made before the taking of the deposition begins or as soon thereafter as the disqualification becomes known or could be discovered with reasonable diligence.
