####### Training.

(5)Only a trained individual is permitted to fill the potable water systems. Each individual who fills a potable water system shall be trained in-

(i) The approved method of inspecting, cleaning, and sanitizing hydrants, hoses, and nozzles used for filling potable water systems; and
