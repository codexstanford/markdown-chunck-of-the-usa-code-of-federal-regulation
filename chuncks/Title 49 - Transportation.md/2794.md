
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 40 - PROCEDURES FOR TRANSPORTATION WORKPLACE DRUG AND ALCOHOL TESTING PROGRAMS
#### Subpart O - Substance Abuse Professionals and the Return-to-Duty Process
##### ยง 40.297 Does anyone have the authority to change a SAP's initial evaluation?
###### e.g.,

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, no one (an employer, employee, a managed-care provider, any service agent) may change in any way the SAP's evaluation or recommendations for assistance. For example, a third party is not permitted to make more or less stringent a SAP's recommendation by changing the SAP's evaluation or seeking another SAP's evaluation.
