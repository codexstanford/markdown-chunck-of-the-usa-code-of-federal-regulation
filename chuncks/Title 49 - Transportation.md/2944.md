
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 80 - CREDIT ASSISTANCE FOR SURFACE TRANSPORTATION PROJECTS
#### ยง 80.3 Definitions.
##### Credit agreement

means a contractual agreement between the DOT and the project sponsor (and the lender, if applicable) that formalizes the terms and conditions established in the term sheet (or conditional term sheet) and authorizes the execution of a secured loan, loan guarantee, or line of credit.
