
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 29 - TRIBAL TRANSPORTATION SELF-GOVERNANCE PROGRAM
#### Subpart C - Final Offer Process
##### ยง 29.202 How does a Tribe submit a final offer?
###### ttsgp@dot.gov

(a) A Tribe must submit a written final offer to the Self-Governance Official toor send the final offer using any other method that provides receipt to: Self-Governance Official, U.S. Department of Transportation, Office of the Secretary, Office of the Assistant Secretary for Governmental Affairs (I-10), 1200 New Jersey Avenue SE, Washington, DC 20590.

(b) The final offer should be a separate document from the compact, funding agreement, or amendment and clearly identified as a "Final Offer-Response due within 45 days of receipt."
