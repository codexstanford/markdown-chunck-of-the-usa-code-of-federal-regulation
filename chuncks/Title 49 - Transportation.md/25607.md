
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 579.4 Terminology.
####### Buckle and restraint harness

means the components of a child restraint system that are intended to restrain a child seated in such a system, including the belt webbing, buckles, buckle release mechanism, belt adjusters, belt positioning devices, and shields.
