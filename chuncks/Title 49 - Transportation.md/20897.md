
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards

####### Boiling water conditioning, low temperature impact resistance.

S11.3.11Plastic air brake tubing shall not rupture when subjected to 80 percent of the burst strength pressure in Table VIII for the size of tubing being tested, after the tubing has been conditioned in boiling water for two hours, then conditioned in air at minus 40 degrees Fahrenheit (minus 40 degrees Celsius) for 4 hours, and then impacted with a one pound weight dropped from a height of 12 inches.
