
# Title 49 - Transportation

#### Subpart J - Dispute Resolution and Appeals
##### Termination Appeals
###### ยง 29.931 How can the Department or the Tribe obtain review of the recommended decision of an administrative law judge?
####### Form and content of objections.

(c)The petition for review must set out separately and in detail each objection to the recommended decision, and the basis and reasons supporting such objection. The petition for review must state whether such objections are related to alleged errors of law or fact. The petition for review must also identify the relief requested.
