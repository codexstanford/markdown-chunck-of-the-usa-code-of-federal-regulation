##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 579.4 Terminology.
####### Control

(including the terms controlling, controlled by, and under common control with) means the possession, direct or indirect, of the power to direct or cause the direction of the management and policies of a person, whether through the ownership of voting securities, by contract, or otherwise.
