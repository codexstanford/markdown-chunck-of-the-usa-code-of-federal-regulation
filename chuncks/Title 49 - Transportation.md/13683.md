
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 239 - PASSENGER TRAIN EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 239.7 Definitions.
####### i.e.,

(1) On-board functions connected with the movement of the train (an employee of the railroad, or of a contractor to the railroad, who is assigned to perform service subject to the Federal hours of service requirements during a tour of duty) or

(2) On-board functions in a sleeping car or coach assigned to intercity service, other than food, beverage, or security service.
