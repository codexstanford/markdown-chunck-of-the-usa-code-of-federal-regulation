
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 578 - CIVIL AND CRIMINAL PENALTIES
##### ยง 578.6 Civil penalties for violations of specified provisions of Title 49 of the United States Code.
###### Medium- and heavy-duty vehicle fuel efficiency.

(i)The maximum civil penalty for a violation of the fuel consumption standards of 49 CFR part 535 is not more than $48,779 per vehicle or engine. The maximum civil penalty for a related series of violations shall be determined by multiplying $48,779 times the vehicle or engine production volume for the model year in question within the regulatory averaging set.
