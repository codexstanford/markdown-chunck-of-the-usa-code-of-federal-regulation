
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VI - Federal Transit Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 609 - TRANSPORTATION FOR ELDERLY AND HANDICAPPED PERSONS
##### Appendix A to Part 609 - Elderly and Handicapped
###### Answer:

No. However, FTA encourages consistency among off-peak procedures and the maximizing of availability to eligible individuals, especially among operators within a single urban area. Nevertheless, each operator is permitted to require its own certification of individuals using its service.
