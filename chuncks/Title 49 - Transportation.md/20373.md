
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 568 - VEHICLES MANUFACTURED IN TWO OR MORE STAGES - ALL INCOMPLETE, INTERMEDIATE AND FINAL-STAGE MANUFACTURERS OF VEHICLES MANUFACTURED IN TWO OR MORE STAGES
##### ยง 568.4 Requirements for incomplete vehicle manufacturers.
###### e.g.

(5) Gross axle weight rating (GAWR) for each axle of the completed vehicle for which the incomplete vehicle is intended, listed and identified in order from front to rear (, front, first intermediate, second intermediate, rear).
