
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 25 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart C - Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Admission and Recruitment Prohibited
##### § 25.300 Admission.
###### General.

(a)No person shall, on the basis of sex, be denied admission, or be subjected to discrimination in admission, by any recipient to which §§ 25.300 through §§ 25.310 apply, except as provided in §§ 25.225 and §§ 25.230.
