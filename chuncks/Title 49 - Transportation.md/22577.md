
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.124 Standard No. 124; Accelerator control systems.
####### Driver-operated accelerator control system

S4.1means all vehicle components, except the fuel metering device, that regulate engine speed in direct response to movement of the driver-operated control and that return the throttle to the idle position upon release of the actuating force.
