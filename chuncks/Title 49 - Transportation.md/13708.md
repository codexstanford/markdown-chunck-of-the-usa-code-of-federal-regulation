
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart B - Specific Requirements

(A) Territory familiarization (access points for emergency responders along the railroad's right-of-way; special circumstances (tunnels); parallel operations; and other operating conditions (elevated structures, bridges, and electrified territory) including areas along the railroad's right-of-way that are remote and that would likely present challenges for individuals responding to a passenger train emergency);
