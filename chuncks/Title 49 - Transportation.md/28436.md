### Chapter X - Surface Transportation Board
#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1110 - PROCEDURES GOVERNING INFORMAL RULEMAKING PROCEEDINGS
###### ยง 1110.10 Petitions for reconsideration.

Any person may file a petition for reconsideration of the Board's decision in a rulemaking proceeding. Petitions should be filed within 20 days of the date that the final decision is published in theand should identify the interest of the petitioner, the specific action sought, and the arguments favoring that action.
