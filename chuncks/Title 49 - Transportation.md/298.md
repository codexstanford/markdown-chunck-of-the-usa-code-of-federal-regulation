
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 9 - TESTIMONY OF EMPLOYEES OF THE DEPARTMENT AND PRODUCTION OF RECORDS IN LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
#### ยง 9.3 Definitions.
##### Legal proceeding

means any case or controversy pending before any federal, state, or local court (including grand jury proceedings), any administrative proceeding pending before any federal, state, or local agency, or any legislative proceeding pending before any state or local agency.
