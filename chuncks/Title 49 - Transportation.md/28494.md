
# Title 49 - Transportation

#### Subchapter B - Rules of Practice
##### PART 1113 - ORAL HEARING
###### ยง 1113.4 Prehearing conferences.
####### Facts disclosed privileged.

(b)Facts disclosed in the course of the prehearing conference are privileged and, except by agreement, will not be used against participating parties either before the Board or elsewhere unless fully corroborated by other evidence.
