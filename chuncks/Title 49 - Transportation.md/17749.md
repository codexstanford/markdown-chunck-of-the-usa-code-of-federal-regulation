
# Title 49 - Transportation

###### Subpart C - Brakes
####### ยง 393.50 Reservoirs required.
######## Safeguarding of air and vacuum.

(c)Each service reservoir system on a motor vehicle shall be protected against a loss of air pressure or vacuum due to a failure or leakage in the system between the service reservoir and the source of air pressure or vacuum, by check valves or equivalent devices whose proper functioning can be checked without disconnecting any air or vacuum line, or fitting.
