
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 570 - VEHICLE IN USE INSPECTION STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - Vehicles With GVWR of 10,000 Pounds or Less
###### ยง 570.7 Steering systems.
####### Inspection procedure.

(1)Elevate the front end of the vehicle to load the ball joints. Insure that wheel bearings are correctly adjusted. Grasp the front and rear of a tire and attempt to turn the tire and wheel assembly left and right. If the free movement at the front or rear tread of the tire exceeds one-quarter inch there is excessive steering linkage play.
