
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 273 - METRICS AND MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR INTERCITY PASSENGER TRAIN OPERATIONS
##### ยง 273.3 Definitions.
###### Avoidable operating costs

means costs incurred by Amtrak to operate train service along a route that would no longer be incurred if the route were no longer operated.
