
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 574 - TIRE IDENTIFICATION AND RECORDKEEPING
##### ยง 574.5 Tire identification requirements.
###### Tires for mileage-contract purchasers.

(4)Manufacturers or retreaders of tires exclusively for mileage-contract purchasers may, instead of meeting any other requirements of this section, permanently mold into or onto the tire sidewall in lettering at least 6 mm (0.25 inch) high the phrase "for mileage contract use only".
