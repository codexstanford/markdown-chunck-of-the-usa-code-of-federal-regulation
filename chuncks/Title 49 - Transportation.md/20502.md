
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 570 - VEHICLE IN USE INSPECTION STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Vehicles With GVWR of More Than 10,000 Pounds
###### ยง 570.62 Tires.
####### Tread depth.

(a)The tread shall be not less than four thirty-seconds of an inch deep on each front tire of any vehicle other than a trailer and not less than two thirty-seconds of an inch on all other tires.
