
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 31 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES
#### ยง 31.2 Definitions.
##### Investigating official

means the Inspector General of the Department of Transportation or an officer or employee of the Office of Inspector General designated by the Inspector General and serving in a position for which the rate of basic pay is not less than the minimum rate of basic pay for grade GS-16 under the General Schedule.
