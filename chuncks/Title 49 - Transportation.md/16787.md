
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter III - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### Subchapter B - Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations
##### PART 386 - RULES OF PRACTICE FOR FMCSA PROCEEDINGS
###### Subpart G - Penalties
####### ยง 386.84 Sanction for failure to pay civil penalties or abide by payment plan; suspension or revocation of registration.
######## General rule.,i.e.

(a)(1)The registration of a broker, freight forwarder, for-hire motor carrier, foreign motor carrier or foreign motor private carrier that fails to pay a civil penalty in full within 90 days after the date specified for payment by the FMCSA's final agency order, will be suspended starting on the next (, the 91st) day. The suspension continues until the FMCSA has received full payment of the penalty.
