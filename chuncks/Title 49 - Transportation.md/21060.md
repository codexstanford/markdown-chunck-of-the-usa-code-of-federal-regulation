####### School bus signal lamp aiming.

S6.4.5Each school bus signal lamp must be mounted on the vehicle with its aiming plane vertical and normal to the vehicle longitudinal axis. Aim tolerance must be no more than 5 in vertically and 10 in horizontally at 25 ft from the lamp. If the lamps are aimed or inspected by use of SAE Recommended Practice J602-1963 (incorporated by reference, see ยง 571.5), the graduation settings for aim must be 2&#xB0; D and 0&#xB0; sideways for aiming and the limits must be 3&#xB0; U to 7&#xB0; D and from 10&#xB0; R to 10&#xB0; L for inspection.
