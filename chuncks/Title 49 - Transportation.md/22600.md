
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.125 Standard No. 125; Warning devices.
####### Conditions.

S6.1

S6.1.1 Submit the warning device to the following conditioning sequence, returning the device after each step in the sequence to ambient air at 68 &#xB0;F. for at least 2 hours.

(a) Minus 40 &#xB0;F. for 16 hours in a circulating air chamber using ambient air which would have not less than 30 percent and not more than 70 percent relative humidity at 70 &#xB0;F.;

(b) 150 &#xB0;F. for 16 hours in a circulating air oven using ambient air which would have not less than 30 percent and not more than 70 percent relative humidity at 70 &#xB0;F.;

(c) 100 &#xB0;F. and 90 percent relative humidity for 16 hours;

(d) Salt spray (fog) test in accordance with ASTM B117-64 (incorporated by reference, see ยง 571.5), except that the test shall be for 4 hours rather than 40 hours; and

(e) Immersion for 2 hours in water at a temperature of 100 &#xB0;F.
