
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 238.5 Definitions.
####### Head end power

means power generated on board the locomotive of a passenger train used for purposes other than propelling the train, such as cooking, heating, illumination, ventilation and air conditioning.
