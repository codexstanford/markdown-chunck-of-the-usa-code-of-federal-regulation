
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 578 - CIVIL AND CRIMINAL PENALTIES
##### ยง 578.6 Civil penalties for violations of specified provisions of Title 49 of the United States Code.
###### Section 30166.

(3)A person who violates Section 30166 of Title 49 of the United States Code or a regulation in this chapter prescribed under that section is liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty for failing or refusing to allow or perform an act required under that section or regulation. The maximum penalty under this paragraph (a)(3) is $26,315 per violation per day. The maximum penalty under this paragraph (a)(3) for a related series of daily violations is $131,564,183.
