
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 23 - PARTICIPATION OF DISADVANTAGED BUSINESS ENTERPRISE IN AIRPORT CONCESSIONS
#### Subpart E - Other Provisions
##### ยง 23.79 Does this part permit recipients to use local geographic preferences?
###### e.g.,

No. As a recipient you must not use a local geographic preference. For purposes of this section, a local geographic preference is any requirement that gives an ACDBE located in one place (your local area) an advantage over ACDBEs from other places in obtaining business as, or with, a concession at your airport.
