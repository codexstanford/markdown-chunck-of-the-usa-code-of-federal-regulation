
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VIII - National Transportation Safety Board
#### PART 800 - ADMINISTRATIVE RULES
##### Appendix to Part 800 - Request to the Secretary of the Department of Transportation To Investigate Certain Aircraft Accidents
###### Provided,,Provided further,

(d)That this authority shall not be construed to authorize the Secretary to hold public hearings or to determine the probable cause of the accident; andThat the Secretary will report to the Board in a form acceptable to the Board the facts, conditions, and circumstances surrounding each accident from which the Board may determine the probable cause.
