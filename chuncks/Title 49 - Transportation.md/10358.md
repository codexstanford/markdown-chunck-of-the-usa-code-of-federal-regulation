
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 221 - REAR END MARKING DEVICE - PASSENGER, COMMUTER AND FREIGHT TRAINS
##### Subpart B - Marking Devices
###### ยง 221.15 Marking device inspection.

(a) Each marking device displayed in compliance with this part shall be examined at each crew change point to assure that the device is in proper operating condition.

(b) This examination shall be accomplished either by visually observing that the device is functioning as required or that the device will function when required by either:

(1) Repositioning the activation switch or

(2) Covering the photoelectric cell.
