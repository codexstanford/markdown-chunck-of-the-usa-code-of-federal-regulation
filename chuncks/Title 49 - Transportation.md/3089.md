
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Transportation
### PART 99 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Appendix to Subtitle A - United States Railway Association - Employee Responsibilities and Conduct
##### Misuse of information.

An employee may not, for the purpose of furthering a private interest, directly or indirectly, use or allow the use of official information obtained through or in connection with his Association employment, if that information has not been made available by the Association to the general public.
