
# Title 49 - Transportation

##### Subpart E - Reporting of Remedial Actions
###### ยง 209.405 Reporting of remedial actions.

(a) Except as provided in ยง 209.407, each railroad that has received written notification on Form FRA F 6180.96 from an FRA Safety Inspector both that assessment of a civil penalty will be recommended for the railroad's failure to comply with a provision of the Federal railroad safety laws and that it must submit a remedial actions report, shall report on this form all actions that it takes to remedy that failure.
