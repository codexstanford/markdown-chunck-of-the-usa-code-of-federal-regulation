
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter V - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### PART 570 - VEHICLE IN USE INSPECTION STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - Vehicles With GVWR of More Than 10,000 Pounds
###### ยง 570.54 Definitions.
####### Vacuum brake system

means a system that uses a vacuum and atmospheric pressure for transmitting a force from the driver control to the service brake, but does not include a system that uses vacuum only to assist the driver in applying muscular force to hydraulic or mechanical components.
