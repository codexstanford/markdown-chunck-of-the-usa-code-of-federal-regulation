
# Title 49 - Transportation

### Chapter II - Federal Railroad Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Table 2 to Subpart B of Part 224 - Minimum Quantity Requirement for Retroreflective Sheeting on Freight Rolling Stock
####### New locomotives.

(1)Retroreflective sheeting conforming to this part must be applied to all locomotives constructed after January 26, 2006, before they are placed in service.
