Leakage surveys of a transmission line must be conducted at intervals not exceeding 15 months, but at least once each calendar year. However, in the case of a transmission line which transports gas in conformity with ยง 192.625 without an odor or odorant, leakage surveys using leak detector equipment must be conducted-
