
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation

##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards

####### Final-stage manufacturers and alterers.

S7.7Vehicles that are manufactured in two or more stages or that are altered (within the meaning of 49 CFR 567.7) after having previously been certified in accordance with part 567 of this chapter are not subject to the requirements of S7.1 through S7.4. Instead, vehicles that are manufactured in two or more stages or that are altered must comply with this standard beginning on September 1, 2008.
