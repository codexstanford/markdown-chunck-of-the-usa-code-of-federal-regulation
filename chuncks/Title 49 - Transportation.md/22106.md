##### Subpart B - Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards
###### ยง 571.116 Standard No. 116; Motor vehicle brake fluids.
####### Heated air bath cabinet.

(c)An insulated cabinet or oven having sufficient capacity to house the three mounted brake assemblies or stroking fixture assemblies, master cylinder, and necessary connections. A thermostatically controlled heating system is required to maintain a temperature of 70&#xB0; +/-5 &#xB0;C (158&#xB0; +/-9 &#xB0;F) or 120&#xB0; +/-5 &#xB0;C (248&#xB0; +/-9 &#xB0;F). Heaters shall be shielded to prevent direct radiation to wheel or master cylinder.
