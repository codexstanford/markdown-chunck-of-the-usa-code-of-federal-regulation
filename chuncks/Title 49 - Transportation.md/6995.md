
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter I - Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
#### Subchapter C - Hazardous Materials Regulations
##### PART 178 - SPECIFICATIONS FOR PACKAGINGS
###### Appendix D to Part 178 - Thermal Resistance Test
####### Requirements.

7.

7.1 For a cylinder, the outer package must provide adequate protection such that the outer surface of the cylinder and valve does not exceed a temperature of 93 &#xB0;C (199 &#xB0;F) at any of the three points where the thermocouples are located.

7.2 For an oxygen generator, the generator must not actuate.
