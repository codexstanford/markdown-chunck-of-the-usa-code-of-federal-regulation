####### ยง 173.59 Description of terms for explosives.
######## Cartridges, small arms.,Cartridges for weapons, inert projectile.

Ammunition consisting of a cartridge case fitted with a center or rim fire primer and containing both a propelling charge and solid projectile(s). They are designed to be fired in weapons of caliber not larger than 19.1 mm. Shotgun cartridges of any caliber are included in this description.
