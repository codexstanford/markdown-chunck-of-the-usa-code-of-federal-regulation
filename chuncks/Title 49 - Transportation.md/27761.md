
# Title 49 - Transportation
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to Transportation
### Chapter VIII - National Transportation Safety Board

##### Subpart C - Miscellaneous Provisions
###### ยง 845.32 Petitions for reconsideration or modification of report.
####### Oral presentation.

(d)Oral presentation normally will not form a part of proceedings under this section. However, oral presentation may be permitted where a party or interested person specifically shows the written petition for reconsideration or modification is an insufficient means by which to present the party's or person's position.
