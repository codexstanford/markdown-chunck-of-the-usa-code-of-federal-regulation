### Chapter XX - Occupational Safety and Health Review Commission

##### ยง 2203.3 Public attendance at Commission meetings.
###### Regularly-scheduled meetings.

(c)The Commission will hold regularly-scheduled meetings for the purpose of considering matters that may properly be closed to the public under paragraph (b)(4), (8), (9)(i) or (10) of this section, or any combination thereof. Primarily, these meetings will be held for the purpose of considering or disposing of particular cases of formal Commission adjudication. The Commission therefore expects to close all regularly-scheduled meetings. The procedures established in ยง 2203.4 apply to the public announcement and closing of regularly-scheduled meetings.
