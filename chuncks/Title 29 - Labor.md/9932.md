
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1904 - RECORDING AND REPORTING OCCUPATIONAL INJURIES AND ILLNESSES
##### Subpart C - Recordkeeping Forms and Recording Criteria
###### ยง 1904.5 Determination of work-relatedness.
####### Implementation.

(b)(1) What is the "work environment"? OSHA defines the work environment as "the establishment and other locations where one or more employees are working or are present as a condition of their employment. The work environment includes not only physical locations, but also the equipment or materials used by the employee during the course of his or her work."
