
# Title 29 - Labor

####### Explosive-actuated power devices.

(2)Explosive-actuated power device-any tool or special mechanized device which is actuated by explosives, but not including propellant-actuated power devices. Examples of explosive-actuated power devices are jet tappers and jet perforators.
