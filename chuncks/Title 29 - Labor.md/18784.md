
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart C - Cargo Handling Gear and Equipment
###### ยง 1917.45 Cranes and derricks (See also ยง 1917.50).
####### General,Operating controls.

(f)-(1)(i) Crane and derrick operating controls shall be clearly marked, or a chart indicating their function shall be posted at the operator's position.

(ii) After October 3, 1984, overhead bridge and container gantry crane operating control levers shall be self-centering so that they will automatically move to the "off" position when the operator releases the control.
