
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart L - Scaffolds
###### (Non-mandatory) Appendix A to Subpart L of Part 1926 - Scaffold Specifications
####### Ladder jack scaffolds.

(k)Maximum intended load-25 lb/ft. However, not more than two employees shall occupy any platform at any one time. Maximum span between supports shall be 8 feet.
