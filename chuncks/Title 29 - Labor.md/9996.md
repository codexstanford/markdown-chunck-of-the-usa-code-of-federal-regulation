
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1904 - RECORDING AND REPORTING OCCUPATIONAL INJURIES AND ILLNESSES
##### Subpart C - Recordkeeping Forms and Recording Criteria
###### ยง 1904.11 Recording criteria for work-related tuberculosis cases.
####### Basic requirement.

(a)If any of your employees has been occupationally exposed to anyone with a known case of active tuberculosis (TB), and that employee subsequently develops a tuberculosis infection, as evidenced by a positive skin test or diagnosis by a physician or other licensed health care professional, you must record the case on the OSHA 300 Log by checking the "respiratory condition" column.
