
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart E - Exit Routes and Emergency Planning
###### ยง 1910.36 Design and construction requirements for exit routes.
####### A single exit route.

(3)A single exit route is permitted where the number of employees, the size of the building, its occupancy, or the arrangement of the workplace is such that all employees would be able to evacuate safely during an emergency.
