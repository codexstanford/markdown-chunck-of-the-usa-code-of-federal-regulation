
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 96 - AUDIT REQUIREMENTS FOR GRANTS, CONTRACTS, AND OTHER AGREEMENTS
#### Subpart E - Audit Resolution
##### ยง 96.53 Audit resolution generally.
###### Initial determination.

(a)After the conclusion of any comment period for audits provided the recipient/contractor, the responsible DOL official(s) shall make an initial determination on the allowability of questioned costs or activities, administrative or systemic findings, and the corrective actions outlined by the recipient. Such determination shall be based on applicable statutes, regulations, administrative directives, or terms and conditions of the grant/contract award instrument.
