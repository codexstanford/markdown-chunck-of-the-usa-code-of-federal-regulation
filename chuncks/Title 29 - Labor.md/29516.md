
# Title 29 - Labor

###### ยง 4221.13 Filing and issuance rules.
####### Method and date of filing.

(a)The PBGC applies the rules in subpart A of part 4000 of this chapter to determine permissible methods of filing with the PBGC under this part. The PBGC applies the rules in subpart C of part 4000 of this chapter to determine the date that a submission under this part was filed with the PBGC.
