
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 97 - UNIFORM ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS FOR GRANTS AND COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS TO STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS
#### Subpart C - Post-Award Requirements
##### Financial Administration
###### ยง 97.23 Period of availability of funds.
####### Liquidation of obligations.

(b)A grantee must liquidate all obligations incurred under the award not later than 90 days after the end of the funding period (or as specified in a program regulation) to coincide with the submission of the annual Financial Status Report (SF-269). The Federal agency may extend this deadline at the request of the grantee.
