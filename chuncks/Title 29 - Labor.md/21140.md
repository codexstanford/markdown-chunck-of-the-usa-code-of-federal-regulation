##### Subpart P - Excavations
###### Appendix A to Subpart P of Part 1926 - Soil Classification
####### Layered systems.

(4)In a layered system, the system shall be classified in accordance with its weakest layer. However, each layer may be classified individually where a more stable layer lies under a less stable layer.
