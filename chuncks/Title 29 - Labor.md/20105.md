
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart F - Fire Protection and Prevention
###### ยง 1926.152 Flammable liquids.
####### Scope.

(h)This section applies to the handling, storage, and use of flammable liquids with a flashpoint at or below 199.4 &#xB0;F (93 &#xB0;C). This section does not apply to:

(1) Bulk transportation of flammable liquids; and

(2) Storage, handling, and use of fuel oil tanks and containers connected with oil burning equipment.
