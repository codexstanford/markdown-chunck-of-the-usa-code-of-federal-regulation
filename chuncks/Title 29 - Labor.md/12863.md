####### Halon 1211

(23)means a colorless, faintly sweet smelling, electrically nonconductive liquefied gas (chemical formula CBrC1F) which is a medium for extinguishing fires by inhibiting the chemical chain reaction of fuel and oxygen. It is also known as bromochlorodifluoromethane.
