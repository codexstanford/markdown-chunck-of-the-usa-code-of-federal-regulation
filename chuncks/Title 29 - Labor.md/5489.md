
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter V - Wage and Hour Division, Department of Labor
#### Subchapter A - Regulations
##### PART 530 - EMPLOYMENT OF HOMEWORKERS IN CERTAIN INDUSTRIES
###### Subpart C - Denial/Revocation of Homeworker Employer Certificates
####### ยง 530.205 Mandatory denial or revocation.
######## Failure to pay back wages or civil money penalties judged owing.

(d)Upon the failure of a certificate holder to pay within 60 days back wages or civil money penalties finally judged by a court, administrative law judge or other appropriate authority, as the case may be, to be owed by the certificate holder, or agreed to be paid by the certificate holder, or within such longer period as may be specified in the final order or agreement, a certificate shall be denied or revoked for up to one year or for such period as such obligation shall remain unpaid if longer than one year.
