
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter E - Plan Terminations
##### PART 4050 - MISSING PARTICIPANTS
###### Subpart C - Certain Defined Benefit Plans Not Covered by Title IV
####### ยง 4050.302 Definitions.
######## De minimis

means, with respect to the value of a benefit (or other amount), that the value does not exceed the amount specified under section 203(e)(1) of ERISA and section 411(a)(11)(A) of the Code (without regard to plan provisions).
