
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 22 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT OF 1986
#### ยง 22.2 Definitions.
##### Reviewing official

(r)means the Solicitor of the Department of Labor or his designee who is-

(1) Not subject to supervision by, or required to report to, the investigating official; and

(2) Not employed in the organizational unit of the authority in which the investigating official is employed;

(3) Serving in a Senior Executive Service position.
