
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter E - Plan Terminations
##### PART 4041 - TERMINATION OF SINGLE-EMPLOYER PLANS
###### Subpart C - Distress Termination Process
####### ยง 4041.42 Administration of plan during termination process.
######## General rule.

(a)Except to the extent specifically prohibited by this section, during the pendency of termination proceedings the plan administrator must continue to carry out the normal operations of the plan, such as putting participants into pay status, collecting contributions due the plan, and investing plan assets.
