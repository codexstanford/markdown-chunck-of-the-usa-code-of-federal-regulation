
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 14 - SECURITY REGULATIONS
#### Subpart B - Review of Classified Information
##### ยง 14.10 Mandatory review for declassification.
###### Where requests should be directed.

(b)Requests for mandatory review for declassification should be directed to the Department of Labor, Office of the Assistant Secretary for Administration and Management (OASAM), Washington, DC 20210. Requests should be in writing and should reasonably describe the classified information to allow identification. Whenever a request does not reasonably describe the information sought, the requestor will be notified that unless additional information is provided or the scope of the request is narrowed, no further action will be undertaken.
