
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter E - Plan Terminations
##### PART 4044 - ALLOCATION OF ASSETS IN SINGLE-EMPLOYER PLANS
###### Subpart A - Allocation of Assets
####### Allocation of Assets To Benefit Categories
######## ยง 4044.10 Manner of allocation.
######### General.

(a)The plan administrator shall allocate plan assets available to pay for benefits under the plan using the rules and procedures set forth in paragraphs (b) through (f) of this section, or any other procedure that results in each participant (or beneficiary) receiving the same benefits he or she would receive if the procedures in paragraphs (b) through (f) were followed.
