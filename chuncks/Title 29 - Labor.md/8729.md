
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XIV - Equal Employment Opportunity Commission
#### PART 1605 - GUIDELINES ON DISCRIMINATION BECAUSE OF RELIGION
##### ยง 1605.3 Selection practices.
###### Scheduling of tests or other selection procedures.

(a)When a test or other selection procedure is scheduled at a time when an employee or prospective employee cannot attend because of his or her religious practices, the user of the test should be aware that the principles enunciated in these guidelines apply and that it has an obligation to accommodate such employee or prospective employee unless undue hardship would result.
