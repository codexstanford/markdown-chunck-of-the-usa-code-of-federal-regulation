######## Discrimination.

(a)(1) It is unlawful to intimidate, threaten, restrain, coerce, blacklist, discharge, or in any other manner discriminate against any person because the person has-

(i) Disclosed information to a federal agency or to any person relating to a verification of the producer's compliance with the LVC requirements, or
