
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1917 - MARINE TERMINALS
##### Subpart B - Marine Terminal Operations
###### ยง 1917.30 Emergency action plans.
####### Emergency action plans,Scope and application.

(a)-(1)This paragraph (a) requires all employers to develop and implement an emergency action plan.The emergency action plan shall be in writing (except as provided in paragraph (a)(5)(iv) of this section) and shall cover those designated actions employers and employees must take to ensure employee safety from fire and other emergencies.
