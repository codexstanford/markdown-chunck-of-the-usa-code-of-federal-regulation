
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 14 - SECURITY REGULATIONS
#### Subpart C - Transmission of Classified Information
##### ยง 14.20 Dissemination to individuals and firms outside the executive branch.
###### Access by former presidential appointees.

(f)Individuals who have previously occupied policymaking positions to which they were appointed by the President may be authorized access to classified information which they originated, reviewed, signed, or received while in public office. Upon request, information identified by such individuals will be reviewed for declassification in accordance with the provisions of these regulations.
