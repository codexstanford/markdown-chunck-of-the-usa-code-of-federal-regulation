
# Title 29 - Labor

###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 4043.9 Company low-default-risk safe harbor.
######## Low-default-risk.

(a)An entity (a "company") that is a contributing sponsor of a plan or the highest level U.S. parent of a contributing sponsor is "low-default-risk" on the date of an event if that date falls within a safe harbor period of the company as described in paragraph (b) of this section.
