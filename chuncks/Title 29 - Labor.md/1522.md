
# Title 29 - Labor

### PART 20 - FEDERAL CLAIMS COLLECTION

##### ยง 20.203 Definitions.
###### evidence of service

(i) The termmeans information retained by the Department indicating the nature of the document to which it pertains, the date of mailing of the document, and to whom the document is being sent. Evidence of service may be retained electronically so long as the manner of retention is sufficient for evidentiary purposes.
