
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart F - Powered Platforms, Manlifts, and Vehicle-Mounted Work Platforms

####### Bottom arrangement,Bottom landing.

(10)-(i)At the bottom landing the clear area shall be not smaller than the area enclosed by the guardrails on the floors above, and any wall in front of the down-running side of the belt shall be not less than 48 inches from the face of the belt.
