#### Subpart C - Auditees
##### ยง 99.310 Financial statements.
###### Financial statements.

(a)The auditee shall prepare financial statements that reflect its financial position, results of operations or changes in net assets, and, where appropriate, cash flows for the fiscal year audited. The financial statements shall be for the same organizational unit and fiscal year that is chosen to meet the requirements of this part. However, organization-wide financial statements may also include departments, agencies, and other organizational units that have separate audits in accordance with ยง 99.500(a) and prepare separate financial statements.
