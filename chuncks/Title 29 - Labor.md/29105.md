
# Title 29 - Labor

##### PART 4204 - VARIANCES FOR SALE OF ASSETS
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 4204.2 Definitions.
######## Net income after taxes

means revenue minus expenses after taxes (excluding extraordinary and non-recurring income or expenses), as presented in an audited financial statement or, in the absence of such statement, in an unaudited financial statement, each prepared in conformance with generally accepted accounting principles.
