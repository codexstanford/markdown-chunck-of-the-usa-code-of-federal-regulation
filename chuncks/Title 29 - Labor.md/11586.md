(4)Except as provided in paragraph (e)(5) of this section the withdrawal of flammable liquids and liquids with a flashpoint greater than 199.4 &#xB0;F (93 &#xB0;C) from containers having a capacity of greater than 60 gallons shall be by approved pumps.
