
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart H - Materials Handling, Storage, Use, and Disposal
###### ยง 1926.251 Rigging equipment for material handling.
####### Safe operating temperatures.

(3)Natural and synthetic fiber rope slings, except for wet frozen slings, may be used in a temperature range from minus 20 &#xB0;F (-28.88 &#xB0;C) to plus 180 &#xB0;F (82.2 &#xB0;C) without decreasing the working load limit. For operations outside this temperature range and for wet frozen slings, the sling manufacturer's recommendations shall be followed.
