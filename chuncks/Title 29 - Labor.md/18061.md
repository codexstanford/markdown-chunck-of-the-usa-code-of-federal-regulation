
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart F - General Working Conditions
###### ยง 1915.80 Scope, application, definitions, and effective dates.
####### Definitions applicable to this subpart.,Additional safety measure.

(b)(1)A component of the tags-plus system that provides an impediment (in addition to the energy-isolating device) to the release of energy or the energization or startup of the machinery, equipment, or system being serviced. Examples of additional safety measures include, but are not limited to, removing an isolating circuit element; blocking a controlling switch; blocking, blanking, or bleeding lines; removing a valve handle or wiring it in place; opening an extra disconnecting device.
