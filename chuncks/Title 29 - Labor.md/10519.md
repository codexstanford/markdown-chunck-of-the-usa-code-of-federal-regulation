
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1910 - OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH STANDARDS
##### Subpart E - Exit Routes and Emergency Planning
###### ยง 1910.34 Coverage and definitions.
####### Self-luminous,e.g.,

means a light source that is illuminated by a self-contained power source (tritium) and that operates independently from external power sources. Batteries are not acceptable self-contained power sources. The light source is typically contained inside the device.
