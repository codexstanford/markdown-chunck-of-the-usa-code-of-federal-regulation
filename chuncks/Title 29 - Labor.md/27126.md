
# Title 29 - Labor

####### ยง 2590.717-1 Preventing surprise medical bills for air ambulance services.
######## In general.

(a)If a group health plan or a health insurance issuer offering group health insurance coverage provides or covers any benefits for air ambulance services, the plan or issuer must cover such services from a nonparticipating provider of air ambulance services in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section.
