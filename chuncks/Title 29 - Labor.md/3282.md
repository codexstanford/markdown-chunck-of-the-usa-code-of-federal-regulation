
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 97 - UNIFORM ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS FOR GRANTS AND COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS TO STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 97.3 Definitions.
###### Percentage of completion method

refers to a system under which payments are made for construction work according to the percentage of completion of the work, rather than to the grantee's cost incurred.
