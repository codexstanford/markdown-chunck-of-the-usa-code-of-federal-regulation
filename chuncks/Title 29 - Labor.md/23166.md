
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart B - State Monitoring Reports and Visits to State Agencies
###### ยง 1954.10 Reports from the States.

(a) In addition to any other reports required by the Assistant Secretary under sections 18(c)(8) and 18(f) of the Act and ยง 1902.3(1) of this chapter, the State shall submit quarterly and annual reports as part of the evaluation and monitoring of State programs.
