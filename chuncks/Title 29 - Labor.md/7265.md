###### Requirements for Exemption
####### ยง 793.18 Location of "major studio."
######## Measuring airline miles.

(g)Airline miles for purposes of the section 13(b)(9) exemption are measured, with a straight edge on a map, from the zero milestone, or the city hall, of the "central" city, to the zero milestone, or city or town hall, of the city or town in which the major studio of the radio or television station is located.
