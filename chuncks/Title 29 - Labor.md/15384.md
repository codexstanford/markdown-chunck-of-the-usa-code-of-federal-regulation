
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

######## Over 600 volts, nominal,General.

(h)-(1)Conductors and equipment used on circuits exceeding 600 volts, nominal, shall comply with all applicable provisions of the paragraphs (a) through (g) of this section and with the following provisions, which supplement or modify the preceding requirements. However, paragraphs (h)(2), (h)(3), and (h)(4) of this section do not apply to the equipment on the supply side of the service point.
