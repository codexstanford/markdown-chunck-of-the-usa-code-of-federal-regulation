####### Installation of refrigerated storage containers.

(2)(i) Containers shall be supported on suitable noncombustible foundations designed to accommodate the type of container being used.

(ii) Adequate protection against flotation or other water damage shall be provided wherever high flood water might occur.

(iii) Containers for product storage at less than 32 &#xB0;F. shall be supported in such a way, or heat shall be supplied, to prevent the effects of freezing and consequent frost heaving.
