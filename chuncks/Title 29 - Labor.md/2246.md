
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 35 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF AGE IN PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR
#### Subpart D - Investigation, Conciliation, and Enforcement Procedures
##### ยง 35.32 Mediation.
###### Participation in mediation process.

(b)Both the complainant and the recipient shall participate in the mediation process to the extent necessary to reach an agreement or to make an informed judgment that an agreement is not possible. The recipient and the complainant do not need to meet with the mediator at the same time, and a meeting may be conducted by telephone or other means of effective dialogue if a personal meeting between the party and the mediator is impractical.
