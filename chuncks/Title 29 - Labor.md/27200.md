######## General exception for certain small group health plans.

(b)(1) Subject to paragraph (b)(2) of this section, the requirements of this part do not apply to any group health plan (and group health insurance coverage) for any plan year, if on the first day of the plan year, the plan has fewer than two participants who are current employees.

(2) The following requirements apply without regard to paragraph (b)(1) of this section:

(iv) Section 2590.702-1(b) of this Part.

(v) Section 2590.702-1(c) of this Part.
