
# Title 29 - Labor

######## § 4044.41 General valuation rules.
######### Valuation of benefits,Trusteed plans.

(a)-(1)The plan administrator of a plan that has been or will be placed into trusteeship by the PBGC shall value plan benefits in accordance with §§ 4044.51 through 4044.57.
