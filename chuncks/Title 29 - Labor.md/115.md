#### Subpart A - Service Contract Labor Standards Provisions and Procedures
##### ยง 4.1a Definitions and use of terms.
###### Affiliate,affiliated person

(g)orincludes a spouse, child, parent, or other close relative of the contractor or subcontractor; a partner or officer of the contractor or subcontractor; a corporation closely connected with a contractor or subcontractor as a parent, subsidiary, or otherwise; and an officer or agent of such corporation. An affiliation is also deemed to exist where, directly or indirectly, one business concern or individual controls or has the power to control the other or where a third party controls or has the power to control both.
