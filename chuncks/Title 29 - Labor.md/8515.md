
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XII - Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service
#### PART 1471 - GOVERNMENTWIDE DEBARMENT AND SUSPENSION (NONPROCUREMENT)
##### Subpart I - Definitions
###### ยง 1471.990 Preponderance of the evidence.
####### Preponderance of the evidence

means proof by information that, compared with information opposing it, leads to the conclusion that the fact at issue is more probably true than not.
