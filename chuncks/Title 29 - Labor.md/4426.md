
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter IV - Office of Labor-Management Standards, Department of Labor
#### Subchapter B - Standards of Conduct
##### PART 457 - GENERAL
###### Subpart B - Meaning of Terms as Used in This Chapter
####### ยง 457.17 Administrative Law Judge.
######## Administrative Law Judge

means the Chief Administrative Law Judge or any Administrative Law Judge designated by the Chief Administrative Law Judge to conduct a hearing in cases under 5 U.S.C. 7120 or 22 U.S.C. 4117 as implemented by part 458 of this subchapter and such other matters as may be assigned.
