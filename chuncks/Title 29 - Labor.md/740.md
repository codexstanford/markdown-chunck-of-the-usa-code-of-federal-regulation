
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 14 - SECURITY REGULATIONS
#### Subpart A - Introduction to Security Regulations
##### ยง 14.2 Policy.
###### Exemption from public disclosure.

(b)Official information of a sensitive nature, hereinafter referred to as national security information, is expressly exempted from compulsory public disclosure by Section 552(b)(1) of title 5 U.S.C. Persons wrongfully disclosing such information are subject to prosecution under United States criminal laws.
