
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart G - Cargo Handling Gear and Equipment Other Than Ship's Gear
###### ยง 1918.64 Powered conveyors.
####### Emergency stop.

(a)Readily accessible stop controls shall be provided for use in an emergency. Whenever the operation of any power conveyor requires personnel to work close to the conveyor, the conveyor controls shall not be left unattended while the conveyor is in operation.
