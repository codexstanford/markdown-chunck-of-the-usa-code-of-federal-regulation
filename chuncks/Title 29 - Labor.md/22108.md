
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart Z - Toxic and Hazardous Substances
###### § 1926.1124 Beryllium.
####### Permissible Exposure Limits (PELs),Time-weighted average (TWA) PEL.

(c)-(1)The employer must ensure that no employee is exposed to an airborne concentration of beryllium in excess of 0.2 µg/mcalculated as an 8-hour TWA.
