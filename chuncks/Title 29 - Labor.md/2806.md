
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Complaint Processing Procedures
###### ยง 38.84 Contacting CRC about a complaint.

Both the complainant and the respondent, or their representative, may contact CRC for information about the complaint. The Director will determine what information, if any, about the complaint will be released.
