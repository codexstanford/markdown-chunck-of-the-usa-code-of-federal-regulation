
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 36 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart C - Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Admission and Recruitment Prohibited
##### § 36.310 Recruitment.
###### Nondiscriminatory recruitment.

(a)A recipient to which §§ 36.300 through 36.310 apply shall not discriminate on the basis of sex in the recruitment and admission of students. A recipient may be required to undertake additional recruitment efforts for one sex as remedial action pursuant to § 36.110(a), and may choose to undertake such efforts as affirmative action pursuant to § 36.110(b).
