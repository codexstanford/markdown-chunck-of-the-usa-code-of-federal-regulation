
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart I - Personal Protective Equipment
###### ยง 1910.140 Personal fall protection systems.
####### Scope and application.

(a)This section establishes performance, care, and use criteria for all personal fall protection systems. The employer must ensure that each personal fall protection system used to comply with this part must meet the requirements of this section.
