
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart F - Fire Protection and Prevention
###### ยง 1926.155 Definitions applicable to this subpart.
####### Flammable liquid

(h)means any liquid having a vapor pressure not exceeding 40 pounds per square inch (absolute) at 100 &#xB0;F (37.8 &#xB0;C) and having a flashpoint at or below 199.4 &#xB0;F (93 &#xB0;C). Flammable liquids are divided into four categories as follows:

(1) Category 1 shall include liquids having flashpoints below 73.4 &#xB0;F (23 &#xB0;C) and having a boiling point at or below 95 &#xB0;F (35 &#xB0;C).

(2) Category 2 shall include liquids having flashpoints below 73.4 &#xB0;F (23 &#xB0;C) and having a boiling point above 95 &#xB0;F (35 &#xB0;C).

(3) Category 3 shall include liquids having flashpoints at or above 73.4 &#xB0;F (23 &#xB0;C) and at or below 140 &#xB0;F (60 &#xB0;C).

(4) Category 4 shall include liquids having flashpoints above 140 &#xB0;F (60 &#xB0;C) and at or below 199.4 &#xB0;F (93 &#xB0;C).
