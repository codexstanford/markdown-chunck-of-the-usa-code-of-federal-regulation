
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 26 - ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW BOARD RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
#### ยง 26.3 Filing.
##### Filing by electronic submission (e-filing) via the Department's electronic case management system,Attorneys and lay representatives.

(a)-(1)Except as otherwise provided in this section, beginning on April 12, 2021, attorneys and lay representatives must file all petitions, pleadings, exhibits, and other documents with the ARB via the Department's electronic case management system. Paper copies are not required unless requested by the ARB.
