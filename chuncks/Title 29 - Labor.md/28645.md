
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter E - Plan Terminations
##### PART 4043 - REPORTABLE EVENTS AND CERTAIN OTHER NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
###### Subpart C - Advance Notice of Reportable Events
####### ยง 4043.61 Advance reporting filing obligation.
######## In general.

(a)Unless a waiver or extension applies with respect to the plan, each contributing sponsor of a plan is required to notify PBGC no later than 30 days before the effective date of a reportable event described in this subpart C if the contributing sponsor is subject to advance reporting for the reportable event. If there is a change in contributing sponsor, the responsibility for any failure to file or defective filing lies with the person who is the contributing sponsor of the plan on the notice date.
