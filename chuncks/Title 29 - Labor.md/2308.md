
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 36 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart B - Coverage
##### § 36.225 Educational institutions eligible to submit transition plans.
###### Provision for transition plans.

(b)An educational institution to which this section applies shall not discriminate on the basis of sex in admission or recruitment in violation of §§ 36.300 through 36.310.
