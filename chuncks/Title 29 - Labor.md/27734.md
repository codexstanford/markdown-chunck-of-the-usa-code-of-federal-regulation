
# Title 29 - Labor

##### PART 4001 - TERMINOLOGY
###### ยง 4001.2 Definitions.
####### Nonforfeitable benefit

means a benefit described in section 4001(a)(8) of ERISA. Benefits that become nonforfeitable solely as a result of the termination of a plan are considered forfeitable.
