
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 15 - ADMINISTRATIVE CLAIMS UNDER THE FEDERAL TORT CLAIMS ACT AND RELATED CLAIMS STATUTES
#### Subpart C - Claims Under the Military Personnel and Civilian Employees' Claims Act of 1964
##### ยง 15.207 What are examples of claims allowed under the MPCECA?
###### Transportation or travel losses.

(a)Claims may be allowed for damage to, or loss of, property incident to transportation or storage pursuant to order or in connection with travel under orders, including property in the custody of a carrier, an agent or agency of the Government, or the claimant.
