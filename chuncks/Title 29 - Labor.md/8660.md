
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XIV - Equal Employment Opportunity Commission
#### PART 1602 - RECORDKEEPING AND REPORTING REQUIREMENTS UNDER TITLE VII, THE ADA AND GINA
##### Subpart O - Recordkeeping for Institutions of Higher Education
###### ยง 1602.47 Definition.
####### institution of higher education,college level studies

Under subparts O and P of this part, the termmeans an institutional system, college, university, community college, junior college, and any other educational institution which offers an associate degree, baccalaureate degree or higher degree or which offers a two year program of college level studies without degree. The termmeans a post secondary program which is wholly or principally creditable toward a baccalaureate degree or terminates in an associate degree.
