
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XIV - Equal Employment Opportunity Commission
#### PART 1610 - AVAILABILITY OF RECORDS
##### Subpart A - Production or Disclosure Under 5 U.S.C. 552
###### ยง 1610.15 Schedule of fees and method of payment for services rendered.
####### In general.

(a)(1) The Commission will charge for processing requests under the FOIA in accordance with the provisions of this section and with the OMB Guidelines. For purposes of assessing fees, the FOIA establishes three categories of requesters:

(i) Commercial use requesters;

(ii) Non-commercial scientific or educational institutions or news media requesters; and

(iii) All other requesters.
