
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XXV - Employee Benefits Security Administration, Department of Labor
#### Subchapter J - Fiduciary Responsibility Under the Federal Employees' Retirement System Act of 1986
##### PART 2582 - RULES AND REGULATIONS FOR FIDUCIARY RESPONSIBILITY
###### Subpart A - Temporary Bonding Rules
####### ยง 2582.8478-1 Temporary bonding requirements.
######## General.

(a)Pending the issuance of permanent regulations under section 8478 of the Federal Employees' Retirement System Act of 1986 (FERSA), any fiduciary with respect to the Thrift Savings Fund (Fund) established under FERSA or any person who handles funds or other property of the Fund, shall be deemed to be in compliance with the bonding requirements of section 8478 of FERSA if he or she is bonded in compliance with the temporary bonding regulations under section 412 of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) set forth in part 2580 of title 29 of the Code of Federal Regulations.
