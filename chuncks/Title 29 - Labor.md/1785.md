
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 23 - INCREASING THE MINIMUM WAGE FOR FEDERAL CONTRACTORS
#### Subpart E - Administrative Proceedings
##### § 23.550 Proceedings of the Administrative Law Judge.
###### General.

(a)The Office of Administrative Law Judges has jurisdiction to hear and decide appeals concerning questions of law and fact from the Administrator's investigative findings letters issued under §§ 23.510 and 23.520. Any party may, when requesting an appeal or during the pendency of a proceeding on appeal, timely move an Administrative Law Judge to consolidate a proceeding initiated hereunder with a proceeding initiated under the Service Contract Act or the Davis-Bacon Act.
