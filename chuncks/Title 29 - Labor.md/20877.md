
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart L - Scaffolds
###### (Non-mandatory) Appendix A to Subpart L of Part 1926 - Scaffold Specifications
####### Crawling boards (chicken ladders).

(m)Crawling boards shall be not less than 10 inches wide and 1 inch thick, with cleats having a minimum 1 x 1inch cross-sectional area. The cleats shall be equal in length to the width of the board and spaced at equal intervals not to exceed 24 inches.
