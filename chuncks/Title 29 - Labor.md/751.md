
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 14 - SECURITY REGULATIONS
#### Subpart A - Introduction to Security Regulations
##### ยง 14.4 Definitions.
###### Document

(f)-any recorded information regardless of its physical form or characteristics, including (but not limited to):

(1) Written material-(whether handwritten, printed or typed).

(2) Painted, drawn, or engraved material.

(3) Sound or voice recordings.

(4) Printed photographs and exposed or printed films (either still or motion picture).

(5) Reproductions of the foregoing, by whatever process.
