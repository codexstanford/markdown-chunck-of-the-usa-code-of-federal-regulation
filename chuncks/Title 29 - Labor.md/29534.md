
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation

####### ยง 4231.2 Definitions.
######## Facilitated merger

means a merger of two or more multiemployer plans facilitated by PBGC under section 4231(e) of ERISA, including a merger that is facilitated with financial assistance under section 4231(e)(2) of ERISA.
