
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart O - Machinery and Machine Guarding
###### ยง 1910.211 Definitions.
####### Interlocked press barrier guard

(35)means a barrier attached to the press frame and interlocked so that the press stroke cannot be started normally unless the guard itself, or its hinged or movable sections, enclose the point of operation.
