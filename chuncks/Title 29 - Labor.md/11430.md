
# Title 29 - Labor

###### ยง 1910.106 Flammable liquids.
####### Electrical equipment,Application.

(5)-(i)This paragraph (f)(5)(i) shall apply to areas where Category 1 or 2 flammable liquids, or Category 3 flammable liquids with a flashpoint below 100 &#xB0;F (37.8 &#xB0;C), are stored or handled.
