
# Title 29 - Labor

####### Unfunded scholarship programs.

(k)For purposes of title I of the Act and this chapter, the terms "employe welfare benefit plan" and "welfare plan" shall not include a scholarship program, including a tuition and education expense refund program, under which payments are made solely from the general assets of an employer or employee organization.
