
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XII - Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service
#### PART 1471 - GOVERNMENTWIDE DEBARMENT AND SUSPENSION (NONPROCUREMENT)
##### Subpart I - Definitions
###### ยง 1471.950 Excluded Parties List System
####### Excluded Parties List System (EPLS),EPLS

means the list maintained and disseminated by the General Services Administration (GSA) containing the names and other information about persons who are ineligible. Thesystem includes the printed version entitled, "List of Parties Excluded or Disqualified from Federal Procurement and Nonprocurement Programs," so long as published.
