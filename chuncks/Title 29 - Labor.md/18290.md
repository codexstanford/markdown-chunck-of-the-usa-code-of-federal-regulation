
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart P - Fire Protection in Shipyard Employment
###### ยง 1915.503 Precautions for hot work.
####### Specific requirements,Maintaining fire hazard-free conditions.

(b)-(1)The employer must keep all hot work areas free of new hazards that may cause or contribute to the spread of fire. Unexpected energizing and energy release are covered by 29 CFR 1915.181, Subpart L. Exposure to toxic and hazardous substances is covered in 29 CFR 1915.1000 through 1915.1450, subpart Z.
