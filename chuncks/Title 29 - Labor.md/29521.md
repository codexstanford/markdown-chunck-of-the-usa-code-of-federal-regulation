
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter I - Withdrawal Liability for Multiemployer Plans
##### PART 4221 - ARBITRATION OF DISPUTES IN MULTIEMPLOYER PLANS
###### ยง 4221.14 PBGC-approved arbitration procedures.
####### Procedure for approval of alternative procedures.

(c)The PBGC may approve arbitration procedures on its own initiative by publishing an appropriate notice in theThe sponsor of an arbitration procedure may request PBGC approval of its procedures by submitting an application to the PBGC. The application shall include:

(1) A copy of the procedures for which approval is sought;

(2) A description of the history, structure and membership of the organization that sponsors the procedures; and

(3) A discussion of the reasons why, in the sponsoring organization's opinion, the procedures satisfy the criteria for approval set forth in this section.
