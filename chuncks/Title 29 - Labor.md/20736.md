
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart K - Electrical
###### Definitions
####### ยง 1926.449 Definitions applicable to this subpart.
######## Volatile flammable liquid.

A flammable liquid having a flash point below 38 degrees C (100 degrees F) or whose temperature is above its flash point, or a Class II combustible liquid having a vapor pressure not exceeding 40 psia (276 kPa) at 38 &#xB0;C (100 &#xB0;F) whose temperature is above its flash point.
