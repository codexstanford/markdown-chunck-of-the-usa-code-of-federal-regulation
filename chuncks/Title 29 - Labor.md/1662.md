
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 22 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT OF 1986
#### § 22.21 Discovery.
##### documents

(b) For the purpose of this section and §§ 22.22 and 22.23, the termincludes information, documents, reports, answers, records, accounts, papers, and other data and documentary evidence. Nothing contained herein shall be interpreted to require the creation of a document.

(c) Unless mutually agreed to by the parties, discovery is available only as ordered by the ALJ. The ALJ shall regulate the timing of discovery.
