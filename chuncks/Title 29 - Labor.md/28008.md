
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter C - Certain Reporting and Disclosure Requirements
##### PART 4010 - ANNUAL FINANCIAL AND ACTUARIAL INFORMATION REPORTING
###### ยง 4010.8 Plan actuarial information.
####### Special actuarial assumptions for exempt plan determination.

(3)Solely for purposes of determining whether a plan is an exempt plan for an information year, the value of benefit liabilities may be determined using the same retirement assumptions as used to determine the minimum required contribution under section 303 of ERISA and section 430 of the Code for the plan year ending within that information year without regard to the at-risk assumptions of section 303(i) of ERISA and section 430(i) of the Code.
