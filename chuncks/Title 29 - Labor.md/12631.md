
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart J - General Environmental Controls

####### Vermin control.

(5)Every enclosed workplace shall be so constructed, equipped, and maintained, so far as reasonably practicable, as to prevent the entrance or harborage of rodents, insects, and other vermin. A continuing and effective extermination program shall be instituted where their presence is detected.
