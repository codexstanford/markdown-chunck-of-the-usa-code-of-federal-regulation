
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 26 - ADMINISTRATIVE REVIEW BOARD RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
#### ยง 26.3 Filing.
##### Relief for Technical Failures.

(6)A person who is adversely affected by a technical failure in connection with filing or receipt of an electronic document may seek appropriate relief from the ARB. If a technical malfunction or other issue prevents access to the Department's case management system for a protracted period, the ARB by special order may provide appropriate relief pending restoration of electronic access.
