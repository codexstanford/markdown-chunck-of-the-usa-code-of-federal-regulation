
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 94 - GOVERNMENTWIDE REQUIREMENTS FOR DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE (FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE)
#### Subpart D - Responsibilities of Department of Labor Awarding Officials
##### ยง 94.400 What are my responsibilities as a(n) Department of Labor awarding official?

As a(n) Department of Labor awarding official, you must obtain each recipient's agreement, as a condition of the award, to comply with the requirements in-

(a) Subpart B of this part, if the recipient is not an individual; or

(b) Subpart C of this part, if the recipient is an individual.
