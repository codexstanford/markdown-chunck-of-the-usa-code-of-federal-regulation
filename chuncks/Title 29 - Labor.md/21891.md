
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart X - Stairways and Ladders
###### ยง 1926.1050 Scope, application, and definitions applicable to this subpart.
####### Fixed ladder,side-step fixed ladder,through fixed ladder

means a ladder that cannot be readily moved or carried because it is an integral part of a building or structure. Ais a fixed ladder that requires a person getting off at the top to step to the side of the ladder side rails to reach the landing. Ais a fixed ladder that requires a person getting off at the top to step between the side rails of the ladder to reach the landing.
