
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter I - Withdrawal Liability for Multiemployer Plans
##### PART 4208 - REDUCTION OR WAIVER OF PARTIAL WITHDRAWAL LIABILITY
###### ยง 4208.5 Withdrawal liability payments during pendency of abatement determination.
####### Plan amendments concerning bond/escrow.

(d)A plan may, by amendment, adopt rules decreasing the amount of the bond or escrow specified in paragraph (b) of this section. A plan amendment adopted under this paragraph may be applied only to the extent that it is consistent with the purposes of ERISA. An amendment satisfies this requirement only if it does not create an unreasonable risk of loss to the plan.
