
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter F - Liability
##### PART 4062 - LIABILITY FOR TERMINATION OF SINGLE-EMPLOYER PLANS
###### ยง 4062.5 Net worth record date.
####### Establishment of an earlier net worth record date.

(b)At any time during a termination proceeding, the PBGC, in order to prevent undue loss to or abuse of the plan termination insurance system, may establish as the net worth record date an earlier date during the 120-day period ending with the termination date.
