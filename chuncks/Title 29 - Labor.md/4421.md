
# Title 29 - Labor

#### Subchapter B - Standards of Conduct
##### PART 457 - GENERAL
###### Subpart B - Meaning of Terms as Used in This Chapter
####### ยง 457.12 Authority; Board.
######## Authority,Board,

means the Federal Labor Relations Authority as described in the CSRA, 5 U.S.C. 7104 and 7105.when used in connection with the FSA, means the Foreign Service Labor Relations Board as described in the FSA, 22 U.S.C. 4106(a).
