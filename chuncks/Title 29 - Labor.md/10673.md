
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor

##### Subpart F - Powered Platforms, Manlifts, and Vehicle-Mounted Work Platforms

() The areas between the guardrail and toeboard on the ends and outboard side, and the area between the midrail and toeboard on the inboard side, shall be closed with a material that is capable of withstanding a load of 100 pounds (45.4 KG.) applied horizontally over any area of one square foot (.09 m).
