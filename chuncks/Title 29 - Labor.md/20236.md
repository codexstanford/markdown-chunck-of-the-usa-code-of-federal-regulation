
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1926 - SAFETY AND HEALTH REGULATIONS FOR CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart H - Materials Handling, Storage, Use, and Disposal
###### ยง 1926.251 Rigging equipment for material handling.
####### Safe operating temperatures.

(14)Fiber core wire rope slings of all grades shall be permanently removed from service if they are exposed to temperatures in excess of 200 &#xB0;F (93.33 &#xB0;C). When nonfiber core wire rope slings of any grade are used at temperatures above 400 &#xB0;F (204.44 &#xB0;C) or below minus 60 &#xB0;F (15.55 &#xB0;C), recommendations of the sling manufacturer regarding use at that temperature shall be followed.
