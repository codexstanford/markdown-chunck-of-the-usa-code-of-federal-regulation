
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 97 - UNIFORM ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS FOR GRANTS AND COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS TO STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS
#### Subpart C - Post-Award Requirements
##### Financial Administration
###### ยง 97.24 Matching or cost sharing.
####### Cost or contributions counted towards other Federal costs-sharing requirements.

(3)Neither costs nor the values of third party in-kind contributions may count towards satisfying a cost sharing or matching requirement of a grant agreement if they have been or will be counted towards satisfying a cost sharing or matching requirement of another Federal grant agreement, a Federal procurement contract, or any other award of Federal funds.
