
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter B - Premiums
##### PART 4006 - PREMIUM RATES
###### ยง 4006.5 Exemptions and special rules.
####### New or newly covered plan.

(1)A new plan becomes effective less than one full year before the beginning of its second plan year, or a newly covered plan becomes covered on a date other than the first day of its plan year. (Cessation of coverage before the end of a plan year does not give rise to proration under this section.)
