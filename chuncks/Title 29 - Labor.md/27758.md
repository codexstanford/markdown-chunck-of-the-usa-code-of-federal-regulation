
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter A - General
##### PART 4002 - BYLAWS OF THE PENSION BENEFIT GUARANTY CORPORATION
###### ยง 4002.1 Board of Directors, Chair, and Representatives of Board Members.
####### Composition and responsibilities of the Board of Directors,Board.

(a)-(1)Section 4002(d)(1) of ERISA establishes the Board membership as the Secretaries of Labor (Chair), the Treasury, and Commerce. A person who, at the time of a meeting of the Board of Directors, is serving in an acting capacity as, or performing the duties of, a Member of the Board of Directors will serve as a Member of the Board of Directors with the same authority and effect as the designated Secretary.
