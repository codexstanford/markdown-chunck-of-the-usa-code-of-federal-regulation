Another requirement for exemption under section 7(b)(3) is that the enterprise must be "independently owned and controlled". Since this requirement is in the conjunctive, it must be established that the enterprise which is engaged in the wholesale or bulk distribution of petroleum products is both independently owned and independently controlled.
