
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XX - Occupational Safety and Health Review Commission
#### PART 2203 - REGULATIONS IMPLEMENTING THE GOVERNMENT IN THE SUNSHINE ACT
##### ยง 2203.4 Procedures applicable to regularly-scheduled meetings.
###### Voting.

(d)At the beginning of each regularly-scheduled meeting, the Commission will vote on whether to close the meeting. No proxy vote will be permitted and the vote of each Commissioner will be recorded. This record of each Commissioner's vote will be made available to the public at the Commission's national office immediately after the meeting.
