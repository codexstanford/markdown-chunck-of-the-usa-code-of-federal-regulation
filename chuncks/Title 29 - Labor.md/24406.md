
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XX - Occupational Safety and Health Review Commission
#### PART 2203 - REGULATIONS IMPLEMENTING THE GOVERNMENT IN THE SUNSHINE ACT
##### ยง 2203.2 Definitions.
###### Official Commission business

means matters that are the responsibility of the Commission acting as a collegial body, including the adjudication of litigated cases. The term does not include matters that are the responsibility of the Commission's Chairman. See, e.g., 29 U.S.C. 661(e).
