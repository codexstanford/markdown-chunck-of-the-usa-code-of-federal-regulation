
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 97 - UNIFORM ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS FOR GRANTS AND COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS TO STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 97.3 Definitions.
###### Administrative,programmatic

requirements mean those matters common to grants in general, such as financial management, kinds and frequency of reports, and retention of records. These are distinguished fromrequirements, which concern matters that can be treated only on a program-by-program or grant-by-grant basis, such as kinds of activities that can be supported by grants under a particular program.
