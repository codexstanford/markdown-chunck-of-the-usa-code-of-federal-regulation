
# Title 29 - Labor

##### Subpart R - Special Industries

####### Telecommunication line truck.

(39)A truck used to transport men, tools, and material, and to serve as a traveling workshop for telecommunication installation and maintenance work. It is sometimes equipped with a boom and auxiliary equipment for setting poles, digging holes, and elevating material or men.
