
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter I - Withdrawal Liability for Multiemployer Plans
##### PART 4207 - REDUCTION OR WAIVER OF COMPLETE WITHDRAWAL LIABILITY
###### ยง 4207.6 Partial withdrawals after reentry.
####### Definition of "3-year testing period.

(1)" For purposes of section 4205(b)(1) of ERISA, the term "3-year testing period" means the period consisting of the plan year for which the determination is made and the two immediately preceding plan years, excluding any plan year during the period of withdrawal.
