
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 22 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT OF 1986
#### ยง 22.41 Stay pending appeal.

(a) An initial decision is stayed automatically pending disposition of a motion for reconsideration or of an appeal to the ARB.

(b) No administrative stay is available following a final decision of the authority head.
