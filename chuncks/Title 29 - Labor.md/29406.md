
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter I - Withdrawal Liability for Multiemployer Plans
##### PART 4219 - NOTICE, COLLECTION, AND REDETERMINATION OF WITHDRAWAL LIABILITY
###### Subpart B - Redetermination of Withdrawal Liability Upon Mass Withdrawal
####### ยง 4219.15 Determination of reallocation liability.
######## Contribution base unit.

(3)For purposes of paragraph (c)(1) of this section, a contribution base unit means a unit with respect to which an employer has an obligation to contribute, such as an hour worked or shift worked or a unit of production, under the applicable collective bargaining agreement (or other agreement pursuant to which the employer contributes) or with respect to which the employer would have an obligation to contribute if the contribution requirement with respect to the plan were greater than zero.
