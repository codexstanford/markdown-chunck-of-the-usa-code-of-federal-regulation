
# Title 29 - Labor

#### Subpart C - Post-Award Requirements

###### ยง 97.41 Financial reporting.
####### Frequency.

(3)The Federal agency may prescribe the frequency of the report for each project or program. However, the report will not be required more frequently than quarterly. If the Federal agency does not specify the frequency of the report, it will be submitted annually. A final report will be required upon expiration or termination of grant support.
