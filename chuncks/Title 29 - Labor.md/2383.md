
# Title 29 - Labor

##### ยง 37.4 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Application for assistance

means the process by which required documentation is provided to the Governor, recipient, or Department before and as a condition of receiving WIA Title I financial assistance (including both new and continuing assistance).
