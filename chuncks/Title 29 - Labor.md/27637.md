
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter A - General
##### PART 4000 - FILING, ISSUANCE, COMPUTATION OF TIME, AND RECORD RETENTION
###### Subpart C - Determining Filing and Issuance Dates
####### § 4000.23 When is my submission or issuance treated as filed or issued?
######## Filed or issued when received,In general.

(b)-(1)If you do not meet the requirements for your submission or issuance to be treated as filed or issued when sent (see §§ 4000.24 through 4000.32), we treat it as filed or issued on the date received in a permitted format at the proper address.
