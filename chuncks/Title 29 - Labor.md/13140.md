##### Subpart N - Materials Handling and Storage
###### ยง 1910.179 Overhead and gantry cranes.
####### Storage bridge crane

(13)means a gantry type crane of long span usually used for bulk storage of material; the bridge girders or trusses are rigidly or nonrigidly supported on one or more legs. It may have one or more fixed or hinged cantilever ends.
