
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 2 - GENERAL REGULATIONS
#### Subpart C - Employees Served With Subpoenas
##### ยง 2.20 Purpose, scope and definitions.
###### demand

(a) This subpart sets forth the procedures to be followed whenever a subpoena, order, or other demand (hereinafter referred to as a) of a court or other authority, in connection with a proceeding to which the U.S. Department of Labor is not a party, is issued for the production or disclosure of (1) any material contained in the files of the Department, (2) any information relating to material contained in the files of the Department, or (3) any information or material acquired by any person while such person was an employee of the Department as a part of the performance of his official duties or because of his official status.
