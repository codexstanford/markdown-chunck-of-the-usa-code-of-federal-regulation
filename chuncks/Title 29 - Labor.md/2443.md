
# Title 29 - Labor

##### ยง 37.4 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Title VI,et seq.,

means Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, 42 U.S.C. 2000d,as amended, which forbids recipients of Federal financial assistance from discriminating on the basis of race, color, or national origin.
