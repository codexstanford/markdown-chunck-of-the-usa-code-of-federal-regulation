
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter A - General
##### PART 4001 - TERMINOLOGY
###### ยง 4001.2 Definitions.
####### Mass withdrawal

means:

(1) The withdrawal of every employer from the plan,

(2) The cessation of the obligation of all employers to contribute under the plan, or

(3) The withdrawal of substantially all employers pursuant to an agreement or arrangement to withdraw.
