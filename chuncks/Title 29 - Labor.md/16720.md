
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor

##### Subpart Z - Toxic and Hazardous Substances

####### Respirator program.

(2)(i) The employer must implement a respiratory protection program in accordance with ยง 1910.134(b) through (d) (except (d)(1)(iii), (d)(3)(iii)(b)(1) and (2)), and (f) through (m), which covers each employee required by this section to use a respirator.

(iii) If NIOSH approves an air-purifying element with an end-of-service-life indicator for benzene, such an element may be used until the indicator shows no further useful life.
