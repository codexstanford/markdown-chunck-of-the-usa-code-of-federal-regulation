
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter C - Certain Reporting and Disclosure Requirements
##### PART 4010 - ANNUAL FINANCIAL AND ACTUARIAL INFORMATION REPORTING
###### § 4010.5 Information year.
####### Determinations based on information year.

(a)An information year is used under this part to determine which persons are filers (§ 4010.4), what information a filer must submit (§§ 4010.6-4010.9), whether a plan is an exempt plan (§ 4010.8(c)), and the due date for submitting the information (§ 4010.10(a)).
