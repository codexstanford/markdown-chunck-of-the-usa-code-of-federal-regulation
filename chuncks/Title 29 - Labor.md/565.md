#### Subpart E - Administrative Proceedings

###### Proposed findings of fact, conclusions, and order.

(b)Within 20 calendar days of filing of the transcript of the testimony or such additional time as the Administrative Law Judge may allow, each party may file with the Administrative Law Judge proposed findings of fact, conclusions of law, and a proposed order, together with a supporting brief expressing the reasons for such proposals.
