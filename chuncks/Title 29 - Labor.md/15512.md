
# Title 29 - Labor

######## Warning sign for multiple disconnecting means.

(8)A warning sign shall be mounted on or next to the disconnecting means where multiple disconnecting means are used and parts of the controllers remain energized from a source other than the one disconnected. The sign shall be clearly legible and shall read "WARNING-PARTS OF THE CONTROLLER ARE NOT DEENERGIZED BY THIS SWITCH."
