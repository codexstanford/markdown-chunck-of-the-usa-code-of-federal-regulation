
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XVII - Occupational Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
#### PART 1915 - OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH STANDARDS FOR SHIPYARD EMPLOYMENT
##### Subpart Z - Toxic and Hazardous Substances
###### § 1915.1024 Beryllium.
####### Short-term exposure limit (STEL).

(2)The employer must ensure that no employee is exposed to an airborne concentration of beryllium in excess of 2.0 µg/mas determined over a sampling period of 15 minutes.
