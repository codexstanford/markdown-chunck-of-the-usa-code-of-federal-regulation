
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor

#### Subpart C - Costs for Production of Records
##### ยง 70.40 Charges assessed for the production of records.
###### Authentication of copies,Fees.

(h)-(1)The FOIA does not require certification or attestation under seal of copies of records provided in accordance with its provisions. Pursuant to provisions of the general user-charger statute, 31 U.S.C. 9701 and Subchapter II of title 29 U.S.C., the following charges will be made when, upon request, such services are rendered by the agency in its discretion:

(i) For certification of true copies, $10.00 each certification.

(ii) For attestation under the seal of the Department, $10.00 each attestation under seal.
