
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Labor
### PART 13 - ESTABLISHING PAID SICK LEAVE FOR FEDERAL CONTRACTORS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 13.2 Definitions.
###### Individual related by blood or affinity whose close association with the employee is the equivalent of a family relationship

means any person with whom the employee has a significant personal bond that is or is like a family relationship, regardless of biological or legal relationship.
