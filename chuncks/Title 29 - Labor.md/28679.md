
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter E - Plan Terminations
##### PART 4044 - ALLOCATION OF ASSETS IN SINGLE-EMPLOYER PLANS
###### Subpart A - Allocation of Assets
####### General Provisions
######## § 4044.2 Definitions.
######### Age

means the participant's age at his or her nearest birthday and is determined by rounding the individual's exact age to the nearest whole year. Half years are rounded to the next highest year. This is also known as the "insurance age."
