### Chapter XIV - Equal Employment Opportunity Commission

###### ยง 1627.6 Availability of records for inspection.
####### Place records are to be kept.

(a)The records required to be kept by this part shall be kept safe and accessible at the place of employment or business at which the individual to whom they relate is employed or has applied for employment or membership, or at one or more established central recordkeeping offices.
