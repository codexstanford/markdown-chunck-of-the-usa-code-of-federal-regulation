
# Title 29 - Labor
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Labor
### Chapter V - Wage and Hour Division, Department of Labor
#### Subchapter B - Statements of General Policy or Interpretation Not Directly Related to Regulations
##### PART 778 - OVERTIME COMPENSATION
###### Subpart C - Payments That May Be Excluded From the "Regular Rate"
####### Bonuses
######## ยง 778.214 Benefit plans; including profit-sharing plans or trusts providing similar benefits.
######### Statutory provision.

(a)Section 7(e)(4) of the Act provides that the term "regular rate" shall not be deemed to include: "contributions irrevocably made by an employer to a trustee or third person pursuant to a bona fide plan for providing old age, retirement, life, accident, or health insurance or similar benefits for employees * * *." Such sums may not, however, be credited toward overtime compensation due under the Act.
