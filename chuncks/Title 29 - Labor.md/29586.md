
# Title 29 - Labor

### Chapter XL - Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation
#### Subchapter J - Insolvency, Termination, and Other Rules Applicable to Multiemployer Plans

###### Subpart B - Additional Rules for Facilitated Mergers
####### ยง 4231.18 Jurisdiction over financial assistance merger.
######## General.

(a)PBGC will retain jurisdiction over the merged plan resulting from a financial assistance merger to carry out the purposes, terms, and conditions of the financial assistance merger, the financial assistance agreement, sections 4231 and 4261 of ERISA, and the regulations thereunder.
