####### Expedited treatment.

(ii)A request for expedited processing may be made at any time during the pendency of a FOIA request or appeal. Requests and appeals will be taken out of order and given expedited treatment when warranted. A requester must provide sufficient justification to grant such processing by showing that any one of the following circumstances exists:

(A) The lack of expedited treatment could reasonably be expected to pose an imminent threat to the life or physical safety of an individual; or

(B) There is an urgency to inform the public about an actual or alleged federal government activity, if made by a person primarily engaged in disseminating information; or
