
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 7 - LABELING AND ADVERTISING OF MALT BEVERAGES
##### Subpart I - Classes and Types of Malt Beverages
###### ยง 7.143 Class and type - special rules.
####### Malt beverages aged in barrels,General.

(h)-(1)Label designations for malt beverages aged in barrels or with woodchips, spirals, or staves derived from barrels may, but are not required to, include a description of how the product was aged. Thus, for example, acceptable designations for a standard beer aged in an oak barrel would include "beer," "oak aged beer," and "beer aged in an oak barrel."
