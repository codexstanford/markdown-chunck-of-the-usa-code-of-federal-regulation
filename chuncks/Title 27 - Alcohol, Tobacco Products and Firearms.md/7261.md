
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

##### Subpart D - Collection of Excise and Special (Occupational) Tax

######## Interest on penalties, additional amounts, or additions to the tax,General.

(b)-(1)Interest shall be imposed on any assessable penalty, additional amount, or addition to the tax (other than an addition to tax imposed under section 6651(a)(1) of the Internal Revenue Code) only if such assessable penalty, additional amount, or addition to the tax is not paid within 10 days from the date of notice and demand therefor, and in such case interest shall be imposed only for the period from the date of the notice and demand to the date of payment.
