
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

### Subchapter B - Tobacco
#### PART 46 - MISCELLANEOUS REGULATIONS RELATING TO TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND CIGARETTE PAPERS AND TUBES
##### Subpart I - Floor Stocks Tax on Certain Tobacco Products, Cigarette Papers, and Cigarette Tubes Held for Sale on April 1, 2009
###### Filing Requirements
####### ยง 46.233 Payment of floor stocks tax.
######## Electronic funds transfer.

(a)If the dealer pays any other excise taxes collected by TTB by electronic funds transfer, then the dealer must also send the payment for the floor stocks tax by an electronic funds transfer. Other dealers may voluntarily elect to pay the floor stocks tax by electronic funds transfer. Electronic funds transfers of floor stocks tax must be received on or before July 31, 2009.
