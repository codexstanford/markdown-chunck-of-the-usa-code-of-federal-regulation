
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

###### Taxpaid Removals
####### ยง 24.278 Tax credit for certain small domestic producers.
######## Definitions,Production.

(e)-(1)For purposes of determining if a person's production of wine is within the 250,000 gallon limit, production includes, in addition to wine produced by fermentation, any increase in the volume of wine due to the winery operations of amelioration, wine spirits addition, sweetening, or production of formula wine. Production of champagne and other sparkling wines is included for purposes of determining whether total production of a winery exceeds 250,000 gallons. Production includes all wine produced at qualified bonded wine premises within the United States and wine produced outside the United States by the same person.
