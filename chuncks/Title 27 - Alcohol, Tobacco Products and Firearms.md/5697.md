
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 31 - ALCOHOL BEVERAGE DEALERS
##### Subpart C - Activities Subject to This Part
###### Dealers Classified
####### § 31.33 Retail dealer in beer.
######## Persons not deemed to be retail dealers in beer.

(b)The following persons are not retail dealers in beer within the meaning of this part:

(1) A limited retail dealer as described in § 31.35, or

(2) A person who does not sell or offer for sale distilled spirits or wines and sells beer or offers beer for sale only as provided in § 31.61 through § 31.63 or § 31.65(a).
