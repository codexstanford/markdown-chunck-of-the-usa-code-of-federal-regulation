
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter II - Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Regulations Relating to Alcohol and Tobacco
#### PART 646 - CONTRABAND CIGARETTES
##### Records
###### § 646.150 Retention of records.
####### General.

(a)Each distributor of cigarettes shall retain the records required by §§ 646.146 and 646.147 for three years following the close of the year in which the records are made. The distributor shall keep the required records on his business premises.
