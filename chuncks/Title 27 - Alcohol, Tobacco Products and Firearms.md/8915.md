
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter II - Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Regulations Relating to Alcohol and Tobacco
#### PART 646 - CONTRABAND CIGARETTES
##### General
###### ยง 646.143 Meaning of terms.
####### Appropriate ATF officer.

An officer or employee of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives (ATF) authorized to perform any functions relating to the administration or enforcement of this part by ATF Order 1130.28, Delegation of the Director's Authorities in 27 CFR Parts 45 and 646.
