
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 4 - LABELING AND ADVERTISING OF WINE
##### Subpart D - Labeling Requirements for Wine
###### ยง 4.32a Voluntary disclosure of major food allergens.
####### Voluntary labeling standards.

(b)Major food allergens (defined in paragraph (a)(1) of this section) used in the production of a wine may, on a voluntary basis, be declared on any label affixed to the container. However, if any one major food allergen is voluntarily declared, all major food allergens used in production of the wine, including major food allergens used as fining or processing agents, must be declared, except when covered by a petition for exemption approved by the appropriate TTB officer under ยง 4.32b. The major food allergens declaration must consist of the word "Contains" followed by a colon and the name of the food source from which each major food allergen is derived (for example, "Contains: egg").
