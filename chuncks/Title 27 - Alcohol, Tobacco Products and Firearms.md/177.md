
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 4 - LABELING AND ADVERTISING OF WINE
##### Subpart D - Labeling Requirements for Wine
###### ยง 4.35 Name and address.
####### American wine,Mandatory statement.

(a)-(1)A label on each container of American wine shall state either "bottled by" or "packed by" followed by the name of the bottler or packer and the address (in accordance with paragraph (c)) of the place where the wine was bottled or packed. Other words may also be stated in addition to the required words "bottled by" or "packed by" and the required name and address if the use of such words is in accordance with paragraph (a)(2) of this section.
