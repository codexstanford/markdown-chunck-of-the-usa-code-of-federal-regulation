
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

######## Alcohol fuel plant.

A special type of distilled spirits plant authorized under 26 U.S.C. 5181 and established under this subpart solely for producing, processing, and storing, and using or distributing distilled spirits to be used exclusively for fuel use.
