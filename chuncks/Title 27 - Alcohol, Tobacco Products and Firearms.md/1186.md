(c)The Grand River Valley viticultural area is located in the following Ohio counties: Lake, Geauga, and Ashtabula. The viticultural area consists of all of the land within the Lake Erie viticultural area, described in ยง 9.83, which is also within 2 statute miles, in any direction, of the Grand River.
