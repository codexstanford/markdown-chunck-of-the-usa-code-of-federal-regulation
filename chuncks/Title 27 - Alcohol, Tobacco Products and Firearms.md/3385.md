##### Subpart I - Operations by Dealers and Users of Specially Denatured Spirits

######## Serial number.

(c)A consecutive serial number shall be marked on each package, beginning with the number "1" and continuing in regular sequence. The dealer shall use a separate but similar number series for packages containing specially denatured rum. When any numbering series reaches "1,000,000", the dealer may recommence the series by providing an alphabetical prefix or suffix for each number in the new series.
