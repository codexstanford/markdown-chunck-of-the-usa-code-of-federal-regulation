######## Corporations and controlled subsidiaries.

(c)For purposes of this subpart, the term "corporation" includes a Limited Liability Company (LLC) or Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) in any jurisdiction where the law authorizes such a business organization to operate. Whenever used in this subpart, the term "controlled subsidiary" means a corporation (or LLC or LLP) in which more than 50 percent of the voting power is controlled by a parent corporation.
