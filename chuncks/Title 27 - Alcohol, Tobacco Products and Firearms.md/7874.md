
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter F - Procedures and Practices
#### PART 70 - PROCEDURE AND ADMINISTRATION
##### Subpart G - Losses Resulting From Disaster, Vandalism, or Malicious Mischief
###### Definitions
####### ยง 70.601 Meaning of terms.
######## Tax.

(1) With respect to distilled spirits, "tax" means the internal revenue tax that is paid or determined on spirits.

(2) With respect to wines, "tax" means the internal revenue tax that is paid or determined on the wine.

(3) With respect to beer, "tax" means the internal revenue tax that is paid or determined on the beer.
