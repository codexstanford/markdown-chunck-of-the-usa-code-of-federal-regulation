
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 22 - DISTRIBUTION AND USE OF TAX-FREE ALCOHOL

####### ยง 22.58 Automatic termination of permits.
######## Permit not transferable.

(a)Permits issued under this part are not transferable. In the event of the lease, sale, or other transfer of such a permit, or of the operations authorized by the permit, the permit shall, except as provided for in this section, automatically terminate.
