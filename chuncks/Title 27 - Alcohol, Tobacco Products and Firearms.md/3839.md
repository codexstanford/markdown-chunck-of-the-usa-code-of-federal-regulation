
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 22 - DISTRIBUTION AND USE OF TAX-FREE ALCOHOL
##### Subpart C - Administrative Provisions
###### Authorities
####### ยง 22.26 Detention of containers.
######## Summary detention.

(a)An appropriate TTB officer may detain any container containing, or supposed to contain, alcohol when the appropriate TTB officer believes the alcohol was withdrawn, sold, transported, or used in violation of law of this part. The appropriate TTB officer shall hold the container at a safe place until it is determined if the detained property is liable by law to forfeiture.
