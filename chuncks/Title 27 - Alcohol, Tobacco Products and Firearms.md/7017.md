
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

### Subchapter C - Firearms

##### Subpart K - Exemptions, Registration, Etc.
###### ยง 53.134 Tax-free sale of articles for use by the purchaser as supplies for vessels or aircraft.
####### Evidence required to establish,In general.

(d)-(1)The exemption provided in section 4221(a)(3) of the Code and paragraph (a) of this section for articles sold for use by the purchaser as supplies for vessels or aircraft applies only:
