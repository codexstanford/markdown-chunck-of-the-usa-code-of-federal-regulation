##### Subpart C - Discovery of Liability and Enforcement of Laws

####### ยง 70.42 Returns prepared or executed by appropriate TTB officers.
######## Execution of returns,General.

(b)-(1)If any person, required by provisions of 26 U.S.C. enforced and administered by the Bureau or by the regulations prescribed thereunder to make a return, fails to make a return at the time prescribed therefor, or makes, willfully or otherwise, a false or fraudulent return, the appropriate TTB officer must make such return from such officer's own knowledge and from such information as the officer can obtain through testimony or otherwise.
