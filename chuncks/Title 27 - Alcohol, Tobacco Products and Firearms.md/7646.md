
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

##### Subpart D - Collection of Excise and Special (Occupational) Tax

####### ยง 70.244 Payroll period.
######## Regularly used calendar periods.

(a)In the case of wages, salary or other income paid to the taxpayer on the basis of an established calendar period regularly used by the employer or other person levied upon for payroll or payment purpose (e.g., daily, weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, or monthly), that period is the taxpayer's payroll period.
