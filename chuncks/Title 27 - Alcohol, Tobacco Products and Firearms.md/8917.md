
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter II - Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Regulations Relating to Alcohol and Tobacco
#### PART 646 - CONTRABAND CIGARETTES
##### General
###### ยง 646.143 Meaning of terms.
####### Common or contract carrier.

A carrier holding a certificate of convenience and necessity, a permit for contract carrier by motor vehicle, or other valid operating authority under the Interstate Commerce Act, or under equivalent operating authority from a regulatory agency of the United States or of any State.
