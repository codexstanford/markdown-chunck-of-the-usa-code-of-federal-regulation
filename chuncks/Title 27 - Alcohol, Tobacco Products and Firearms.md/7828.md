
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

##### Subpart E - Procedural Rules Relating to Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives
###### Administrative Remedies
####### ยง 70.481 Agreements for payment of liability in installments.
######## Failure to pay an installment or any other tax liability when due or to provide requested financial information.

(4)The officer who entered into an installment agreement under paragraph (a) of this section may alter, modify, or terminate such agreement in the case of the failure of the taxpayer:

(i) To pay an installment at the time such installment payment is due under such agreement,

(ii) To pay any other tax liability at the time such liability is due, or

(iii) To provide a financial condition update as requested by the appropriate TTB officer.
