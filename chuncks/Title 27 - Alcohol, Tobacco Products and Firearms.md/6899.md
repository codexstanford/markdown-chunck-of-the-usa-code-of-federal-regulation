
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

### Subchapter C - Firearms
#### PART 53 - MANUFACTURERS EXCISE TAXES - FIREARMS AND AMMUNITION
##### Subpart J - Special Provisions Applicable to Manufacturers Taxes
###### Use by Manufacturer or Importer Considered Sale
####### ยง 53.111 Tax on use by manufacturer, producer, or importer.
######## Taxable articles in general,Application of tax.

(b)-(1)If the manufacturer, producer, or importer of an article taxable under chapter 32 of the Code uses the article for any purpose other than that indicated in paragraph (b) (3) of this section, he shall be liable for tax with respect to the use of such article in the same manner as if the article were sold by him.
