
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

##### Subpart D - Collection of Excise and Special (Occupational) Tax

####### ยง 70.149 Refiling of notice of tax lien.
######## Notice of change of taxpayer's residence,In general.

(2)-(i)For purposes of this section, a notice of change of a taxpayer's residence will be effective only if it:

(A) Is received, in writing, from the taxpayer or the taxpayer's representative by the appropriate TTB officer who filed the original notice of lien.

(B) Relates to an unpaid tax liability of the taxpayer, and

(C) States the taxpayer's name and the address of the taxpayer's new residence.
