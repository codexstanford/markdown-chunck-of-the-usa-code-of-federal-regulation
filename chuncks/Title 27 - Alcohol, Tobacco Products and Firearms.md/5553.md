
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 28 - EXPORTATION OF ALCOHOL
##### Subpart N - Proceedings at Ports of Export

####### Distilled spirits and wines as supplies on vessels and aircraft.

(b)When the district director of customs is satisfied that the distilled spirits and wines described on TTB Form 5100.11, 5110.30, or 1582-A (5120.24), as the case may be, have been duly laden for use on vessels and aircraft, and that proper accounting for such spirits or wines has been submitted to him as required by this part, he shall execute his certificate of lading for use on both copies of the form.
