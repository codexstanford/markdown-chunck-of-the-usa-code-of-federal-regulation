
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter II - Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice
### Subchapter E - Explosive License and Permit Proceedings
#### PART 771 - RULES OF PRACTICE IN EXPLOSIVE LICENSE AND PERMIT PROCEEDINGS
##### Subpart F - Hearing Procedure
###### Failure to Appear
####### ยง 771.68 Revocation or denial of renewal.
######## prima facie

If, on the date set for a hearing concerning the revocation or denial of renewal of a license or permit, the licensee or permittee does not appear, no evidence has been offered, and no good cause has been shown for the failure to appear, the Attorney for the Government will proceed ex parte and offer for the record sufficient evidence to make acase. At such hearing, documents, statements, and affidavits may be submitted in lieu of testimony of witnesses.
