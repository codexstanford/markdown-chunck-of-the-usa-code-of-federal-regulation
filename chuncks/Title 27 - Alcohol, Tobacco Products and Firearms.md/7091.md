####### Modification for third calendar quarter.

(4)The safe harbor rule in paragraph (c)(3) of this section does not apply for the third calendar quarter unless-

(i) The deposit of taxes for the semimonthly period July 1-September 15 meets the requirements of paragraph (c)(3) of this section; and
