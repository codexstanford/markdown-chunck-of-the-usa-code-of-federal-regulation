
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

###### ยง 7.147 Statement of composition.
####### Identify added artificial sweeteners.

(4)The statement of composition must disclose any artificial sweetener that is added to a malt beverage product, whether the artificial sweetener is added directly or through flavoring material(s). The artificial sweetener may be identified specifically by either generic name or trademarked brand name, or as a general statement (such as "artificially sweetened") to indicate the presence of any one or combination of artificial sweeteners. However, if aspartame is used, an additional warning statement is required in accordance with ยง 7.63(b)(4).
