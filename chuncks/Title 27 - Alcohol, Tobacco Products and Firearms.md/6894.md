
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms

####### Small manufacturers, producers, and importers,Exemption.

(c)-(1)Section 4182(c) of the Code provides that the tax imposed by section 4181 of the Code shall not attach to any pistol, revolver, or firearm manufactured, produced, or imported by a person who manufactures, produces, and imports less than an aggregate of 50 of those articles during the calendar year, regardless of when the articles are sold.
