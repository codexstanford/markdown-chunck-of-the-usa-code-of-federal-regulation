
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter II - Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Regulations Relating to Alcohol and Tobacco
#### PART 646 - CONTRABAND CIGARETTES
##### General
###### ยง 646.143 Meaning of terms.
####### Business premises.

When used with respect to a distributor, the property on which the cigarettes are kept or stored. The business premises includes the property where the records of a distributor are kept.
