
# Title 27 - Alcohol, Tobacco Products and Firearms
## Chapter I - Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Alcohol
#### PART 25 - BEER
##### Subpart E - Measurement of Beer
###### ยง 25.42 Testing of measuring devices.
####### Requirements for beer meters.

(b)The allowable variation for beer meters as established by testing may not exceed +/-0.5 percent. If a meter test discloses an error in excess of the allowable variation, the brewer shall immediately adjust or repair the meter. Adjustments will reduce the error to as near zero as practicable.
