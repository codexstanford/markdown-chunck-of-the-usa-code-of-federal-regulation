
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3150 - ONSHORE OIL AND GAS GEOPHYSICAL EXPLORATION
###### Subpart 3150 - Onshore Oil and Gas Geophysical Exploration; General
####### ยง 3150.0-5 Definitions.
######## Casual use

(b)means activities that involve practices which do not ordinarily lead to any appreciable disturbance or damage to lands, resources and improvements. For example, activities which do not involve use of heavy equipment or explosives and which do not involve vehicular movement except over established roads and trails are casual use.
