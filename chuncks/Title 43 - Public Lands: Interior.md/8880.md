
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter F - Preservation and Conservation (6000)
##### PART 6300 - MANAGEMENT OF DESIGNATED WILDERNESS AREAS
###### Subpart 6304 - Uses Addressed in Special Provisions of the Wilderness Act
####### Other Uses Specifically Addressed by the Wilderness Act
######## ยง 6304.22 What special provisions apply to control of fire, insects, and diseases?

BLM may prescribe measures to control fire, noxious weeds, non-native invasive plants, insects, and diseases. BLM may require restoration concurrent with or as soon as practicable upon completion of such measures.
