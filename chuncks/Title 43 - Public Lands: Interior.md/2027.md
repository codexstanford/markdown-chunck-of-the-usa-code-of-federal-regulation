
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

### PART 30 - INDIAN PROBATE HEARINGS PROCEDURES
#### Subpart A - Scope of Part; Definitions
##### ยง 30.101 What definitions do I need to know?
###### Trust property

means real or personal property, or an interest therein, the title to which is held in trust by the United States for the benefit of an individual Indian or tribe.
