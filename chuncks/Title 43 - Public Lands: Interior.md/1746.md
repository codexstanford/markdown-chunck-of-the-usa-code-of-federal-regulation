
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 20 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Subpart D - Special Provisions Governing Financial and Other Outside Interests of Certain Employees of the Department
##### ยง 20.401 Interests in Federal lands.
###### Statutory prohibition applicable to employees of the U.S. Geological Survey.

(b)(1) In accordance with 43 U.S.C. 31(a), the Director and members of the U.S. Geological Survey are prohibited from having any personal or private interests in the lands or mineral wealth of the region under survey.
