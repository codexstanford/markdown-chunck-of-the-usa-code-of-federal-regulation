
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter I - Bureau of Reclamation, Department of the Interior
#### PART 429 - USE OF BUREAU OF RECLAMATION LAND, FACILITIES, AND WATERBODIES
##### Subpart A - Purpose, Definitions, and Applicability
###### ยง 429.2 What definitions are used in this part?
####### Reclamation waterbodies

means any body of water situated on Reclamation land and under Reclamation jurisdiction. Examples of Reclamation waterbodies include, but are not limited to, reservoirs, lakes, and impoundments.
