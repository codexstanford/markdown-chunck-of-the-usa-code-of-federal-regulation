
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

#### Subpart B - Paleontological Resources Permitting; Requirements, Modifications, and Appeals
##### ยง 49.130 When and how may a permit be modified, suspended, revoked, or cancelled?
###### Cancellation.

(d)The Federal land manager may cancel a permit when the permittee requests cancellation, or when resource, safety, or other administrative or management reasons make permit cancellation appropriate. Cancellation of a permit does not imply fault on the part of the permittee.
