##### ยง 11.83 Damage determination phase - use value methodologies.
###### Cost- and time-estimating relationships (CERs and TERs).

(vi)CERs and TERs are statistical regression models that mathematically describe the cost of an item or activity as a function of one or more independent variables. The regression models provide statistical relationships between cost or time and physical or performance characteristics of past designs.
