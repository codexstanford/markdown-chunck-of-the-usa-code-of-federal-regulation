
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 2 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT; RECORDS AND TESTIMONY
#### Subpart L - Legal Process: Testimony by Employees and Production of Records
##### General Information
###### § 2.281 What is the Department's policy on granting requests for employee testimony or Department records?
####### United States ex rel. Touhy,Ragen,

(b) No Department employee may testify or produce records in any proceeding to which this subpart applies unless authorized by the Department under §§ 2.80 through 2.90v.340 U.S. 462 (1951).
