##### PART 4100 - GRAZING ADMINISTRATION - EXCLUSIVE OF ALASKA
###### Subpart 4100 - Grazing Administration - Exclusive of Alaska; General
####### ยง 4100.0-5 Definitions.
######## Grazing lease

means a document that authorizes grazing use of the public lands under Section 15 of the Act. A grazing lease specifies grazing preference and the terms and conditions under which lessees make grazing use during the term of the lease.
