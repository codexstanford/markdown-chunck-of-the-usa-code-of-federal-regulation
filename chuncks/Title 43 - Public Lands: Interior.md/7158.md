(s)(1)Federal lands identified by the surface management agency, in consultation with the state in which they are located, as alluvial valley floors according to the definition in ยง 3400.0-5(a) of this title, the standards in 30 CFR Part 822, the final alluvial valley floor guidelines of the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement when published, and approved state programs under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977, where mining would interrupt, discontinue, or preclude farming, shall be considered unsuitable.
