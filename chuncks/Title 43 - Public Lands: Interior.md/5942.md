
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

##### PART 3170 - ONSHORE OIL AND GAS PRODUCTION
###### Subpart 3170 - Onshore Oil and Gas Production: General
####### ยง 3170.3 Definitions and acronyms.
######## Condition of Approval (COA)

means a site-specific requirement included in the approval of an application that may limit or modify the specific actions covered by the application. Conditions of approval may minimize, mitigate, or prevent impacts to public lands or resources.
