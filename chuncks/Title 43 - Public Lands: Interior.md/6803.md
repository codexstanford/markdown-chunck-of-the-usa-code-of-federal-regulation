
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3280 - GEOTHERMAL RESOURCES UNIT AGREEMENTS
###### Subpart 3280 - Geothermal Resources Unit Agreements - General
####### ยง 3280.2 Definitions.
######## Participating area

means that part of the unit area that BLM deems to be productive from a horizon or deposit, and to which production would be allocated in the manner described in the unit agreement, assuming that all lands are committed to the unit agreement.
