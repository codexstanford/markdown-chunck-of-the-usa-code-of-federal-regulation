
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

#### ยง 36.10 Access to inholdings.
##### Adequate and feasible access

(1)means a route and method of access that is shown to be reasonably necessary and economically practicable but not necessarily the least costly alternative for achieving the use and development by the applicant on the applicant's nonfederal land or occupancy interest.
