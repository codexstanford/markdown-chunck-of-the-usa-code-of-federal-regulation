
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 50 - PROCEDURES FOR REESTABLISHING A FORMAL GOVERNMENT-TO-GOVERNMENT RELATIONSHIP WITH THE NATIVE HAWAIIAN COMMUNITY
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 50.4 What definitions apply to terms used in this part?
###### Sponsor

means an individual who makes a sworn statement that another individual is:

(1) A Native Hawaiian or an HHCA Native Hawaiian; and

(2) The sponsor's parent, child, sibling, grandparent, grandchild, aunt, uncle, niece, nephew, or first cousin.
