
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3170 - ONSHORE OIL AND GAS PRODUCTION
###### Subpart 3170 - Onshore Oil and Gas Production: General
####### ยง 3170.3 Definitions and acronyms.
######## Source record,e.g.,,e.g.,

means any unedited and original record, document, or data that is used to determine volume and quality of production, regardless of format or how it was created or stored (paper or electronic). It includes, but is not limited to, raw and unprocessed data (instantaneous and continuous information used by flow computers to calculate volumes); gas charts; measurement tickets; calibration, verification, prover, and configuration reports; pumper and gauger field logs; volume statements; event logs; seal records; and gas analyses.
