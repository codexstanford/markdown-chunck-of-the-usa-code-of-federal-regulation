
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3600 - MINERAL MATERIALS DISPOSAL
###### Subpart 3601 - Mineral Materials Disposal; General Provisions
####### Fundamental Provisions
######## ยง 3601.5 Definitions.
######### Public lands

means any lands and interest in lands owned by the United States and administered by the Secretary of the Interior through BLM without regard to how the United States acquired ownership, except lands held for the benefit of Indians, Aleuts, and Eskimos.
