### PART 49 - PALEONTOLOGICAL RESOURCES PRESERVATION
#### Subpart A - Preserving, Managing, and Protecting Paleontological Resources
##### ยง 49.5 What terms are used in this part?
###### Indian land

means land of federally recognized Indian Tribes or Indian individuals which is either held in trust by the United States or subject to a restriction against alienation imposed by the United States.
