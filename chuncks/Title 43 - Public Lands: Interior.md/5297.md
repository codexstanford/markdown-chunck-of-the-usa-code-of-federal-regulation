##### PART 2920 - LEASES, PERMITS AND EASEMENTS
###### Subpart 2920 - Leases, Permits and Easements: General Provisions
####### ยง 2920.2-1 Discussion of proposals.
######## Suggestions by land use proponent.

(a)Any person who seeks to use public lands may contact the Bureau of Land Management office having jurisdiction over the public lands in question and discuss the land use proposal. This contact should be made as early as possible so that administrative requirements and potential conflicts with other land uses can be identified.
