
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3830 - LOCATING, RECORDING, AND MAINTAINING MINING CLAIMS OR SITES; GENERAL PROVISIONS
###### Subpart A - Introduction
####### ยง 3830.5 Definitions.
######## Mineral Leasing Acts,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.

means the Mineral Leasing Act of [February 25,] 1920, as amended (30 U.S.C. 181); the Geothermal Steam Act of 1970, as amended (30 U.S.C. 1001); the Mineral Leasing Act for Acquired Lands of 1947, as amended, (30 U.S.C. 351); and including all Acts referenced in 30 U.S.C. 505. The definition pertains to all minerals that BLM administers under Groups 3100, 3200, 3400, and 3500 of this chapter.
