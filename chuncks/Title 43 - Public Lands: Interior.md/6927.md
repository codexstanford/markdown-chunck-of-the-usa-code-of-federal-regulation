
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

###### Subpart 3400 - Introduction: General
####### ยง 3400.0-5 Definitions.
######## Sole party in interest

(jj)means a party who is and will be vested with all legal and equitable rights under a lease, bid, or an application for a lease. No one is a sole party in interest with respect to a lease or bid in which any other party has any interest.
