#### Subpart A - Scope of Part; Definitions
##### ยง 30.101 What definitions do I need to know?
###### Order

means any written direction or determination, other than a decision, issued by a judge in a probate case, including a distribution order, an order on rehearing, an order on reopening, or a reconsideration order.
