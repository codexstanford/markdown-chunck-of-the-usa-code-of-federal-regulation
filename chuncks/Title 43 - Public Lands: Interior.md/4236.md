
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

##### PART 2540 - COLOR-OF-TITLE AND OMITTED LANDS
###### Subpart 2540 - Color-of-Title: Authority and Definitions
####### ยง 2540.0-3 Authority.
######## Act of September 21, 1922.

(c)The Act of September 21, 1922 (42 Stat. 992; 43 U.S.C. 992), authorizes the Secretary of the Interior in his judgment and discretion to sell at an appraised price, any of those public lands situated in Arkansas, which were originally erroneously meandered and shown upon the official plats as water-covered areas, and which are not lawfully appropriated by a qualified settler or entryman claiming under the public land laws, to any citizen who in good faith under color of title or claiming as a riparian owner, has prior to September 21, 1922, placed valuable improvements on such land or reduced some part thereof to cultivation.
