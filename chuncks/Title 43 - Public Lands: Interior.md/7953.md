######## ยง 3809.202 Under what conditions will BLM defer to State regulation of operations?
######### State request.

(a)A State may request BLM enter into an agreement for State regulation of operations on public lands in place of BLM administration of some or all of the requirements of this subpart. The State must send the request to the BLM State Director with jurisdiction over public lands in the State.
