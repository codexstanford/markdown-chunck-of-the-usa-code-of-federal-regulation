
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3730 - PUBLIC LAW 359; MINING IN POWERSITE WITHDRAWALS: GENERAL
###### Subpart 3737 - Use
####### ยง 3737.1 Mining claim and millsite use.

(a) The Act in section 6 provides as follows:

(b) Under this section, a mining claim or millsite may not be used for purposes other than for legitimate mining and milling. The claimant, therefore, may not erect on the mining claim any facility or activity such as filling stations, curio shops, cafes, tourist or hunting and fishing lodges, or conduct such businesses thereon.
