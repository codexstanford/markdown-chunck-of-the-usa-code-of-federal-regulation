##### PART 4100 - GRAZING ADMINISTRATION - EXCLUSIVE OF ALASKA
###### Subpart 4100 - Grazing Administration - Exclusive of Alaska; General
####### ยง 4100.0-5 Definitions.
######## Grazing permit

means a document that authorizes grazing use of the public lands under Section 3 of the Act. A grazing permit specifies grazing preference and the terms and conditions under which permittees make grazing use during the term of the permit.
