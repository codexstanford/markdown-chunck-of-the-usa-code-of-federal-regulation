
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

##### PART 2540 - COLOR-OF-TITLE AND OMITTED LANDS
###### Subpart 2540 - Color-of-Title: Authority and Definitions
####### § 2540.0-3 Authority.
######## Act of August 24, 1954.,the Act of 1954,the Act of 1925,

(e)The Act of August 24, 1954 (68 Stat. 789), directs the Secretary of the Interior to issue patents for public lands which lie between the meander line of an inland lake or river in Wisconsin as originally surveyed and the meander line of that lake or river as subsequently resurveyed, under certain terms and conditions. The Act of February 27, 1925 (43 Stat. 1013 43 U.S.C. 994), authorized the Secretary of the Interior to sell such public lands under certain other terms and conditions. These Acts are cited asandrespectively, in §§ 2545.1 to 2545.4.
