
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

##### PART 2540 - COLOR-OF-TITLE AND OMITTED LANDS
###### Subpart 2541 - Color-of-Title Act
####### ยง 2541.2 Procedures.
######## Description of lands applied for.

(b)Application under the act may be made for surveyed or unsurveyed lands. If unsurveyed, the description must be sufficiently complete to identify the location, boundary, and area of the land and, if possible, the approximate description or location of the land by section, township, and range. If unsurveyed land is claimed, final action will be suspended until the plat of survey has been officially filed.
