
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter B - Land Resource Management (2000) <HED1>Group 2000 - Land Resource Management; General</HED1>
##### PART 2610 - CAREY ACT GRANTS
###### Subpart 2613 - Preference Right Upon Restoration
####### ยง 2613.1 Allowance of filing of applications.
######## Status of lands under State laws.

(a)Prior to the restoration of lands segregated under the Carey Act, the Bureau of Land Management shall ascertain from the proper State officials whether any entries have been allowed under the State Carey Act laws on any such lands, and if any such entries have been allowed, the status thereof and action taken by the State with reference thereto.
