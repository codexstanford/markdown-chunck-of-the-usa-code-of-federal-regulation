
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 48 - AMENDMENTS TO THE HAWAIIAN HOMES COMMISSION ACT
#### ยง 48.6 What definitions apply to terms used in this part?
##### Homestead Association

means a beneficiary controlled organization that represents and serves the interests of its homestead community; has as a stated primary purpose the representation of, and provision of services to, its homestead community; and filed with the Secretary a statement, signed by the governing body, of governing procedures and a description of the territory it represents.
