
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3280 - GEOTHERMAL RESOURCES UNIT AGREEMENTS
###### Subpart 3280 - Geothermal Resources Unit Agreements - General
####### ยง 3280.2 Definitions.
######## Unit contraction provision

means a term of a unit agreement providing that the boundaries of the unit area will contract to the size of the participating area, by having those lands outside of the participating area removed. BLM will contract the unit area if additional unit wells are not drilled and completed within the timeframe specified in the unit agreement.
