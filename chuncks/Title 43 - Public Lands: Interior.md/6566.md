
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)

###### Subpart 3206 - Lease Issuance
####### ยง 3206.12 What are the minimum and maximum lease sizes?

Other than for direct use leases (the size for which is addressed in ยง 3205.7), the smallest lease we will issue is 640 acres, or all lands available for leasing in the section, whichever is less.
