
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3600 - MINERAL MATERIALS DISPOSAL
###### Subpart 3602 - Mineral Materials Sales
####### Competitive Sales
######## ยง 3602.47 When and how may I renew my competitive contract and what is the fee?
######### Applying for competitive contract renewal.

(a)When you have paid the United States the full contract price for the mineral materials you purchased under a competitive contract, you may apply for renewal of the contract without further competitive bidding in order to purchase and extract additional material that may be available at the contract site. You must submit your request for renewal of the contract at least 90 days before it expires. You do not need to use a specific form.
