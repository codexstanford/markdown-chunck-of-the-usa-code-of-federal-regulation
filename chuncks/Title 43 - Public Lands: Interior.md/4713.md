###### Subpart 2720 - Conveyance of Federally-Owned Mineral Interests
####### ยง 2720.0-5 Definitions.
######## Known mineral values

(b)means mineral rights in lands containing geologic formations that are valuable in the monetary sense for exploring, developing, or producing natural mineral deposits. The presence of such mineral deposits with potential for mineral development may be known because of previous exploration, or may be inferred based on geologic information.
