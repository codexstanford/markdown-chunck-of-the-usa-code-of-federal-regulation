
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3280 - GEOTHERMAL RESOURCES UNIT AGREEMENTS
###### Subpart 3280 - Geothermal Resources Unit Agreements - General
####### ยง 3280.2 Definitions.
######## Unit agreement

means an agreement for the exploration, development, production, and utilization of separately owned interests in the geothermal resources made subject thereto as a single consolidated unit without regard to separate ownerships, which provides for the allocation of costs and benefits on a basis defined in the agreement or plan.
