
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3710 - PUBLIC LAW 167; ACT OF JULY 23, 1955
###### Subpart 3715 - Use and Occupancy Under the Mining Laws
####### ยง 3715.0-1 What are the purpose and the scope of this subpart?
######## Scope.

(b)This subpart applies to public lands BLM administers. They do not apply to state or private lands in which the mineral estate has been reserved to the United States. They do not apply to Federal lands administered by other Federal agencies, even though those lands may be subject to the operation of the mining laws.

(c) This subpart does not impair the right of any person to engage in recreational activities or any other authorized activity on public lands BLM administers.
