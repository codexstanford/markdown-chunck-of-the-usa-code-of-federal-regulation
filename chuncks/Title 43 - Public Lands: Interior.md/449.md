####### Summary dismissal; waiver of defect in service.

(a)If a complaint when filed does not meet all the requirements of ยง 4.450-4(a) and (c), or if the complaint is not served upon each contestee as required by this section, the complaint will be summarily dismissed by the manager and no answer need be filed.
