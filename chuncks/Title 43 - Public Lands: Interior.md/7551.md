
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter C - Minerals Management (3000)
##### PART 3500 - LEASING OF SOLID MINERALS OTHER THAN COAL AND OIL SHALE
###### Subpart 3515 - Mineral Lease Exchanges
####### Lease Exchange Procedures
######## ยง 3515.25 Is BLM required to publish notice or hold a hearing?

Yes. After you and BLM agree on the lands for exchange, we will publish a notice of the proposed exchange in theand in a newspaper(s) in the county(s) where the lands involved are located. The notice will include:

(a) The time and place of a public hearing(s);

(b) Our preliminary findings that the exchange is in the public interest; and

(c) A request for public comments on the merits of the proposed exchange.
