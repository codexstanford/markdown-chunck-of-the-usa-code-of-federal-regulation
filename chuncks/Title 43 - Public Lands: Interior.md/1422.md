
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 11 - NATURAL RESOURCE DAMAGE ASSESSMENTS
#### Appendix II to Part 11 - Format for Data Inputs and Modifications to the NRDAM/CME
##### Seaward open water

-a body of water that does not contain vegetation (e.g., wetlands, seagrass, or kelp) or invertebrate reef (e.g., coral reef) and is classified as "seaward" in Table 6.2, Volume I of the NRDAM/CME technical document.
