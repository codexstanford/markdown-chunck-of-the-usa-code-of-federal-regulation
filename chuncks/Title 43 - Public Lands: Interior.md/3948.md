####### ยง 2200.0-5 Definitions.
######## Adjustment to relative values

(a)means compensation for exchange-related costs, or other responsibilities or requirements assumed by one party, which ordinarily would be borne by the other party. These adjustments do not alter the agreed upon value of the lands involved in an exchange.
