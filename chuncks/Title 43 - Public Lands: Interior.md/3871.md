
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter A - General Management (1000)
##### PART 1860 - CONVEYANCES, DISCLAIMERS AND CORRECTION DOCUMENTS
###### Subpart 1865 - Correction of Conveyancing Documents
####### ยง 1865.0-5 Definitions.
######## Error

(b)means the inclusion of erroneous descriptions, terms, conditions, covenants, reservations, provisions and names or the omission of requisite descriptions, terms, conditions, covenants, reservations, provisions and names either in their entirety or in part, in a patent or document of conveyance as a result of factual error. This term is limited to mistakes of fact and not of law.
