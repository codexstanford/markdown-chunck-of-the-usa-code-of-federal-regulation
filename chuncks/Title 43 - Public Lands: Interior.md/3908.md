
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter B - Land Resource Management (2000) <HED1>Group 2000 - Land Resource Management; General</HED1>
##### PART 2090 - SPECIAL LAWS AND RULES
###### Subpart 2091 - Segregation and Opening of Lands
####### ยง 2091.0-5 Definitions.
######## Segregation

(b)means the removal for a limited period, subject to valid existing rights, of a specified area of the public lands from the operation of some or all of the public land laws, including the mineral laws, pursuant to the exercise by the Secretary of regulatory authority for the orderly administration of the public lands.
