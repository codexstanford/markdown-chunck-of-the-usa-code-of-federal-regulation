
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 37 - CAVE MANAGEMENT
#### Subpart B - Cave Designation
##### ยง 37.11 Nomination, evaluation, and designation of significant caves.
###### National Park Service policy.,et seq.

(d)The policy of the National Park Service, pursuant to its Organic Act of 1916 (16 U.S.C. 1,) and Management Policies (Chapter 4:20, Dec. 1988), is that all caves are afforded protection and will be managed in compliance with approved resource management plans. Accordingly, all caves on National Park Service-administered lands are deemed to fall within the definition of "significant cave."
