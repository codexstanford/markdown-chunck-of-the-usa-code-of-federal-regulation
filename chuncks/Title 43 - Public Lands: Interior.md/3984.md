
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

##### PART 2200 - EXCHANGES: GENERAL PROCEDURES
###### Subpart 2200 - Exchanges - General
####### ยง 2200.0-6 Policy.
######## Unsurveyed school sections.

(l)For purposes of exchange only, unsurveyed school sections, which would become State lands upon survey by the Secretary, are considered as "non-Federal" lands and may be used by the State in an exchange with the United States. However, minerals shall not be reserved by the State when unsurveyed sections are used in an exchange. As a condition of the exchange, the State shall have waived, in writing, all rights to unsurveyed sections used in the exchange.
