
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

#### Subchapter B - Land Resource Management (2000) <HED1>Group 2000 - Land Resource Management; General</HED1>
##### PART 2610 - CAREY ACT GRANTS
###### Subpart 2610 - Carey Act Grants, General
####### ยง 2610.0-5 Definitions.
######## Ordinary agricultural crops

(g)means any agricultural product to which the land under consideration is generally adapted, and which would return a fair reward for the expense of producing them. Ordinary agricultural crops do not include forest products, but may include orchards and other plants which cannot be grown on the land without irrigation and from which a profitable crop may be harvested.
