##### PART 2930 - PERMITS FOR RECREATION ON PUBLIC LANDS
###### Subpart 2932 - Special Recreation Permits for Commercial Use, Competitive Events, Organized Groups, and Recreation Use in Special Areas
####### ยง 2932.33 When are fees refundable?
######## Overpayments.

(a)For multi-year commercial permits, if your actual fees due are less than the estimated fees you paid in advance, BLM will credit overpayments to the following year or season. For other permits, BLM will give you the option whether to receive refunds or credit overpayments to future permits, less processing costs.
