
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter A - General Management (1000)
##### PART 1820 - APPLICATION PROCEDURES
###### Subpart 1824 - Publication and posting of notices
####### ยง 1824.10 What is publication?

Publication means publishing a notice announcing an event or a proposed action in thea local newspaper of established character and general circulation in the vicinity of the land affected or other appropriate periodical. BLM's purpose in publishing or requiring the publication of such information is to advise you and other interested parties that some action will occur and that the public is invited either to participate or to comment.
