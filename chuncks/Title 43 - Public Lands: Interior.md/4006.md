
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Public Lands
### Chapter II - Bureau of Land Management, Department of the Interior
#### Subchapter B - Land Resource Management (2000) <HED1>Group 2000 - Land Resource Management; General</HED1>
##### PART 2200 - EXCHANGES: GENERAL PROCEDURES
###### Subpart 2201 - Exchanges - Specific Requirements
####### ยง 2201.8 Title standards.
######## Title evidence.

(a)(1) Unless otherwise specified by the Office of the Solicitor of the Department of the Interior, evidence of title for the non-Federal lands being conveyed to the United States shall be in conformance with the Department of Justice regulations and "Standards for the Preparation of Title Evidence in Land Acquisitions by the United States" in effect at the time of conveyance.

(2) The United States is not required to furnish title evidence for the Federal lands being exchanged.
