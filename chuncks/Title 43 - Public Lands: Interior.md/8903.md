##### PART 8340 - OFF-ROAD VEHICLES
###### Subpart 8340 - General
####### ยง 8340.0-1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to establish criteria for designating public lands as open, limited or closed to the use of off-road vehicles and for establishing controls governing the use and operation of off-road vehicles in such areas.
