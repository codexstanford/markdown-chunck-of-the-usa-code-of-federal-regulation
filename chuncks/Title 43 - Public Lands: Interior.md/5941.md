###### Subpart 3170 - Onshore Oil and Gas Production: General
####### ยง 3170.3 Definitions and acronyms.
######## Communitization agreement

(CA) means an agreement to combine a lease or a portion of a lease that cannot otherwise be independently developed and operated in conformity with an established well spacing or well development program, with other tracts for purposes of cooperative development and operations.
