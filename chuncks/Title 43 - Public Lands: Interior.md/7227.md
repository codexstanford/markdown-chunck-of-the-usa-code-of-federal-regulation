###### Subpart 3480 - Coal Exploration and Mining Operations Rules: General
####### ยง 3480.0-5 Definitions.
######## Continued operation

(8)means the production of not less than commercial quantities of recoverable coal reserves in each of the first 2 continued operation years following the achievement of diligent development and an average amount of not less than commercial quantities of recoverable coal reserves per continued operation year thereafter, computed on a 3-year basis consisting of the continued operation year in question and the 2 preceding continued operation years.
