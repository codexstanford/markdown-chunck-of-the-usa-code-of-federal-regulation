
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 23 - SURFACE EXPLORATION, MINING AND RECLAMATION OF LANDS
#### ยง 23.3 Definitions.
##### Reclamation

(i)means measures undertaken to bring about the necessary reconditioning or restoration of land or water that has been affected by exploration or mineral development, mining or onsite processing operations, and waste disposal, in ways which will prevent or control onsite and offsite damage to the environment.
