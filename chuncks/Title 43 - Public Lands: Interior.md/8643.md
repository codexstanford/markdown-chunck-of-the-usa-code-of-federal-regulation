
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

#### Subchapter D - Range Management (4000) <HED1>Group 4100 - Grazing Administration</HED1>
##### PART 4300 - GRAZING ADMINISTRATION; ALASKA; REINDEER; GENERAL
###### Changes That Can Affect Your Permit
####### ยง 4300.51 Will I be notified if another use, disposal, or withdrawal occurs on the land?

Yes. If there is a settlement, location, entry, disposal, or withdrawal on any lands described in your permit, BLM will notify you and will reduce your permit area by the amount of the area involved.
