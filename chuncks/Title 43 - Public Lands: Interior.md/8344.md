
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior

###### Subpart 3900 - Oil Shale Management - Introduction
####### ยง 3900.2 Definitions.
######## Fair market value (FMV)

means the monetary amount for which the oil shale deposit would be leased by a knowledgeable owner willing, but not obligated, to lease to a knowledgeable purchaser who desires, but is not obligated, to lease the oil shale deposit.
