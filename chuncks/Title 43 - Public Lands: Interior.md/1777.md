
# Title 43 - Public Lands: Interior
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Interior
### PART 20 - EMPLOYEE RESPONSIBILITIES AND CONDUCT
#### Subpart F - Disciplinary and Remedial Actions
##### ยง 20.602 Remedial action.
###### Divestiture of the interest.

(3)An employee may be required to divest an interest, including outside employment, that is prohibited by law or regulation. Divestiture of the interest shall be ordered in all situations where it is determined by the appropriate official that there is no other satisfactory remedy. Evidence of divestiture must be provided in the form of broker's sale receipt or other appropriate document.
