Upon completion of an environmental analysis, but prior to the issuance of a notice of decision, the authorized officer shall publish a notice in thesetting a time and place where a public meeting will be held to receive public comment on the public interest factors of the proposed exchange. Such notice shall be distributed in accordance with ยง 2201.7-1 of this title. The public meeting shall:
