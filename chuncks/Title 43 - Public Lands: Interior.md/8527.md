###### Subpart 4100 - Grazing Administration - Exclusive of Alaska; General
####### ยง 4100.0-5 Definitions.
######## Land use plan,et seq.

means a resource management plan, developed under the provisions of 43 CFR part 1600, or a management framework plan. These plans are developed through public participation in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (43 U.S.C 1701) and establish management direction for resource uses of public lands.
