
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter F - Tribal Government
#### PART 81 - SECRETARIAL ELECTION PROCEDURES
##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 81.4 What terms do I need to know?
####### Eligible Voters List

means a list of eligible voters, including their birthdates and their last known mailing addresses. The Eligible Voters List is compiled and certified by the tribe's governing body or the Bureau if the Bureau maintains the current membership roll for the tribe.
