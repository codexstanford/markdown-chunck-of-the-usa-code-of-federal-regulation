
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 179 - LIFE ESTATES AND FUTURE INTERESTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 179.2 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Agency

means the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) agency office, or any other designated office in BIA, having jurisdiction over trust or restricted property. This term also means any office of a tribe that has entered into a contract or compact to fulfill applicable BIA functions.
