
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter G - Financial Activities
#### PART 115 - TRUST FUNDS FOR TRIBES AND INDIVIDUAL INDIANS
##### Subpart E - IIM Accounts: Hearing Process for Restricting an IIM Account
###### ยง 115.612 May you be represented by an attorney during your hearing?

Yes, you may have an attorney or other person represent you during your hearing. However, you are responsible for any and all expenses associated with having an attorney or other person represent you.
