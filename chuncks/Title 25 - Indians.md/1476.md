
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 46 - ADULT EDUCATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 46.2 Definitions.
####### Adult

means an individual who has attained the age of sixteen or is beyond the age of compulsory school attendance under State or tribal law and not currently enrolled in a formal secondary or post-secondary educational program.
