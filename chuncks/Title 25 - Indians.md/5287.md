
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter N - Economic Enterprises
#### PART 292 - GAMING ON TRUST LANDS ACQUIRED AFTER OCTOBER 17, 1988
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 292.2 How are key terms defined in this part?
####### Surrounding community

means local governments and nearby Indian tribes located within a 25-mile radius of the site of the proposed gaming establishment. A local government or nearby Indian tribe located beyond the 25-mile radius may petition for consultation if it can establish that its governmental functions, infrastructure or services will be directly, immediately and significantly impacted by the proposed gaming establishment.
