
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter IV - The Office of Navajo and Hopi Indian Relocation
### PART 700 - COMMISSION OPERATIONS AND RELOCATION PROCEDURES
#### Subpart Q - New Lands Grazing
##### ยง 700.701 Definitions.
###### Animal unit

(i)(AU) means one adult cow with unweaned calf by her side or equivalent thereof based on comparative forage consumption. Accepted conversion factors are: Sheep and Goats-one ewe, doe, buck, or ram equals 0.25 AU. Horses and Mules-one horse, mule, donkey or burro equals 1.25 AU.
