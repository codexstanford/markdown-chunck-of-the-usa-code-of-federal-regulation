
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 150 - RECORD OF TITLE TO INDIAN LAND
##### Subpart A - Purpose and Definitions
###### ยง 150.3 May Tribes administer this part on LTRO's behalf?
####### et seq.

A Tribe may contract or compact under the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act (25 U.S.C. 5301) to administer on LTRO's behalf any portion of this part that is not an inherently Federal function.
