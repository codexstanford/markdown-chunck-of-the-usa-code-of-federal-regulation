
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter K - Housing
#### PART 256 - HOUSING IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM (HIP)
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 256.2 Definitions.
####### Child

means a person under the age of 18 or such other age of majority as is established for purposes of parental support by tribal or state law (if any) applicable to the person at his or her residence, except that no person who has been emancipated by marriage can be deemed a child.
