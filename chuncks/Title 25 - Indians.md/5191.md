
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter N - Economic Enterprises
#### PART 286 - INDIAN BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM
##### ยง 286.1 Definitions.
###### Indian

means a person who is a member of an Indian tribe or a person of Alaska Native descent who is a shareholder in a corporation organized under the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (85 Stat. 688), as amended.
