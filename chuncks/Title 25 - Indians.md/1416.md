
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 41 - GRANTS TO TRIBAL COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES AND DIN&#xC9; COLLEGE
##### Subpart C - Din&#xE9; College
###### ยง 41.69 How must Din&#xE9; College administer its grant?

In administering any grant provided under this subpart, Din&#xE9; College must:

(a) Provide all services or assistance under this subpart in a fair and uniform manner;

(b) Not deny admission to any Indian student because the student is, or is not, a member of a specific Indian Tribe; and

(c) Comply with part 276 of this chapter, unless the BIE expressly waives specific inappropriate provisions of part 276 in response to Din&#xE9; College's request and its justification for a waiver.
