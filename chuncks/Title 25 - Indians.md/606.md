
# Title 25 - Indians

### Subchapter D - Human Services

###### ยง 23.2 Definitions.
####### Indian organization,

solely for purposes of eligibility for grants under subpart D of this part, means any legally established group, association, partnership, corporation, or other legal entity which is owned or controlled by Indians, or a majority (51 percent or more) of whose members are Indians.
