
# Title 25 - Indians

##### Subpart E - IIM Accounts: Hearing Process for Restricting an IIM Account
###### ยง 115.611 Will you be allowed to question opposing witnesses during a hearing?

Yes, you or your guardian, as applicable, may question all opposing witnesses testifying during your hearing. You may also present witnesses to challenge opposing witness testimony.
