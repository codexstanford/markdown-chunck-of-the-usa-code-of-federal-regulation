
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter D - Human Services
#### PART 20 - FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND SOCIAL SERVICES PROGRAMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions, Purpose and Policy
###### ยง 20.100 What definitions clarify the meaning of the provisions of this part?
####### Protective services

means those services necessary to protect an Indian who is the victim of an alleged and/or substantiated incident of abuse, neglect or exploitation or who is under the supervision of the Bureau in regard to the use and disbursement of funds in his or her Individual Indian Money (IIM) account.
