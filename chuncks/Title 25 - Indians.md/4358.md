
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter I - Energy and Minerals
#### PART 215 - LEAD AND ZINC MINING OPERATIONS AND LEASES, QUAPAW AGENCY
##### ยง 215.0 Definitions.
###### Lessee.

(d)The term "lessee," except where otherwise modified or limited in the regulations in this part, shall mean any person, firm, or corporation, their legal representatives, heirs, or assigns, to whom a lead and zinc mining lease has been made by or on behalf of Indians under the provisions of the regulations in this part.
