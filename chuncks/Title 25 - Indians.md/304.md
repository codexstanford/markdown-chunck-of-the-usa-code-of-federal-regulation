
# Title 25 - Indians

##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 15.2 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Individual Indian Money (IIM) account

means an interest bearing account for trust funds held by the Secretary that belong to a person who has an interest in trust assets. These accounts are under the control and management of the Secretary.
