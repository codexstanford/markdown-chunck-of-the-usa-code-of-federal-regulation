
# Title 25 - Indians

### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 37 - GEOGRAPHIC BOUNDARIES
##### ยง 37.101 What definitions apply to the terms in this part?
###### Geographic attendance boundary

means a line of demarcation that clearly delineates and describes the limits of the physical land area that is served by a Bureau-funded school.
