
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 171 - IRRIGATION OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 171.100 What are some of the terms I should know for this part?
####### Water duty

means the amount of water, in acre-feet per acre, necessary for full-service irrigation. This value is established by decree, compact, or other legal document, or by specialized engineering studies.
