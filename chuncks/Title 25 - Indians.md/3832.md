##### Subpart E - Service Delivery for Tribal Transportation Program
###### Miscellaneous Provisions
####### ยง 170.607 Can a Tribe use its allocation of TTP funds for contract support costs?

Yes. Contract support costs are an eligible item out of a Tribe's TTP allocation and must be included in a Tribe's project construction budget.
