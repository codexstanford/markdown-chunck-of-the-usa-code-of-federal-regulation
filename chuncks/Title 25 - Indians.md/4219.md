
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter I - Energy and Minerals
#### PART 212 - LEASING OF ALLOTTED LANDS FOR MINERAL DEVELOPMENT
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 212.3 Definitions.
####### Indian mineral owner

means any individual Indian or Alaska Native who owns mineral interests in oil and gas, geothermal, or solid mineral resources, title to which is held in trust by the United States, or is subject to the restriction against alienation imposed by the United States.
