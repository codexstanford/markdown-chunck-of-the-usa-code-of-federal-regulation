
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter M - Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act Program
#### PART 273 - EDUCATION CONTRACTS UNDER JOHNSON-O'MALLEY ACT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions and Definitions
###### ยง 273.106 What key terms do I need to know?
####### Public school district

means a State-funded school district that:

(1) Serves public elementary schools or public secondary schools; and

(b) Has established or will establish local committees or is using a committee or Indian advisory school board to approve supplementary or operational support programs beneficial to Indian students.
