
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter F - Tribal Government
#### PART 84 - ENCUMBRANCES OF TRIBAL LAND - CONTRACT APPROVALS
##### ยง 84.002 What terms must I know?
###### Tribal lands

means those lands held by the United States in trust for an Indian tribe or those lands owned by an Indian tribe subject to federal restrictions against alienation, as referred to Public Law 106-179 as "Indian lands."
