### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 38 - EDUCATION PERSONNEL
##### ยง 38.12 Leave system for education personnel.
###### Sick leave.

(2)Sick leave is accumulated on the basis of three hours each biweekly pay period in pay status; no precredit or advance of sick leave is authorized. Accumulated sick leave at the time of separation will be recredited to an educator who is reemployed within three years of separation.
