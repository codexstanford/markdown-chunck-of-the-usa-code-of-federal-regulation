
# Title 25 - Indians

### Subchapter I - Energy and Minerals

##### Subpart J - Alternative to TERAs: Tribal Energy Development Organization (TEDO) Certification
###### ยง 224.203 What must the Secretary do upon receipt of an application for certification as a TEDO?

Within 90 days of receiving an application for certification as a TEDO, the Secretary must approve or disapprove the application.
