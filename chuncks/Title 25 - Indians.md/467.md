
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter D - Human Services
#### PART 20 - FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND SOCIAL SERVICES PROGRAMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions, Purpose and Policy
###### ยง 20.100 What definitions clarify the meaning of the provisions of this part?
####### Homemaker services

means non-medical services provided by social services, in the absence of other resources, to assist an eligible Indian in maintaining self-sufficiency, and preventing placement into foster care or residential care. Examples of services included in homemaker services are: cleaning an individual's home, preparing meals for an individual, and maintaining or performing basic household functions.
