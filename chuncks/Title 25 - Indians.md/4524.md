
# Title 25 - Indians

##### Subpart F - Periodic Reviews
###### ยง 224.133 What must the Secretary do after a periodic review and evaluation?

After a periodic review and evaluation, the Secretary must prepare a written report of the results and send the report to the Designated Tribal Official.
