
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 39 - THE INDIAN SCHOOL EQUALIZATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 39.2 What definitions apply to terms in this part?
####### Agency

means an organizational unit of the Bureau which provides direct services to the governing body or bodies and members of one or more specified Indian Tribes. The term includes Bureau Area Offices only with respect to off-reservation boarding schools administered directly by such Offices.
