
# Title 25 - Indians

### Subchapter J - Fish and Wildlife
#### PART 243 - REINDEER IN ALASKA
##### ยง 243.2 What terms do I need to know?
###### Native reindeer organization

means any corporation, association, or other organization, whether incorporated or not, composed solely of Alaska Natives, for the purpose of engaging in or promoting the reindeer industry.
