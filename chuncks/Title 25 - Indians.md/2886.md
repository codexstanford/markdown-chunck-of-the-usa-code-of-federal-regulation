
# Title 25 - Indians

##### Subpart B - Agricultural Leases
###### ยง 162.101 What key terms do I need to know for this subpart?
####### Agricultural land

means Indian land or Government land suited or used for the production of crops, livestock or other agricultural products, or Indian land suited or used for a business that supports the surrounding agricultural community.
