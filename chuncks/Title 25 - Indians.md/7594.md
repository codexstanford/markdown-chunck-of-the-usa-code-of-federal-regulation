
# Title 25 - Indians

##### ยง 1000.316 May the Tribe/Consortium be reimbursed for actual and reasonable "wind up costs" incurred after the effective date of retrocession?

Yes, the Tribe/Consortium may be reimbursed for actual and reasonable "wind up costs" to the extent that funds are available.
