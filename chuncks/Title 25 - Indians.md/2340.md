
# Title 25 - Indians

#### PART 135 - CONSTRUCTION ASSESSMENTS, CROW INDIAN IRRIGATION PROJECT

###### ยง 135.21 Time of payment.

The amount of each annual installment, payable under the private landowner contracts, determined as provided in this part shall be paid by the landowners to the United States, on or before November 15 of each year commencing with the calendar year 1951.
