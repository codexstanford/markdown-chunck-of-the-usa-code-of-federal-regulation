
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter D - Human Services
#### PART 20 - FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND SOCIAL SERVICES PROGRAMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions, Purpose and Policy
###### ยง 20.100 What definitions clarify the meaning of the provisions of this part?
####### Tribal Work Experience Program (TWEP)

means a program operated by tribal contract/grant or self-governance annual funding agreement, which provides eligible participants with work experience and training that promotes and preserves work habits and develops work skills aimed toward self-sufficiency. The Bureau payment standard is established by the Assistant Secretary.
