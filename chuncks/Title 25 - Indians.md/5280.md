
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter N - Economic Enterprises
#### PART 292 - GAMING ON TRUST LANDS ACQUIRED AFTER OCTOBER 17, 1988
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 292.2 How are key terms defined in this part?
####### Nearby Indian tribe

means an Indian tribe with tribal Indian lands located within a 25-mile radius of the location of the proposed gaming establishment, or, if the tribe has no trust lands, within a 25-mile radius of its government headquarters.
