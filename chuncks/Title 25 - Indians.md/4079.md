
# Title 25 - Indians

### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 179 - LIFE ESTATES AND FUTURE INTERESTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 179.2 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Life estate

means an interest in property held for only the duration of a designated person's life. A life estate may be created by a conveyance document or by operation of law.
