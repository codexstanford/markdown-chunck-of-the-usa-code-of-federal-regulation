
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter J - Fish and Wildlife
#### PART 248 - USE OF COLUMBIA RIVER INDIAN IN-LIEU FISHING SITES
##### ยง 248.10 Appeals from administrative actions.

Any decision made by the Regional Director under this part shall be subject to appeal to the Assistant Secretary-Indian Affairs, and any decision of the Assistant Secretary-Indian Affairs on such an appeal may be appealed to the Secretary of the Interior in accordance with part 2 of this chapter.
