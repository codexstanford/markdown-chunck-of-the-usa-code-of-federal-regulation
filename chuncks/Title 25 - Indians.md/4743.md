
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter J - Fish and Wildlife
#### PART 242 - COMMERCIAL FISHING ON RED LAKE INDIAN RESERVATION
##### § 242.8 Penalty.

Any Indian violating the provisions of §§ 242.4 and 242.6 shall forfeit his right to take fish for any purpose for a period of three months.
