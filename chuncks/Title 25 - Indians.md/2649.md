
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 161 - NAVAJO PARTITIONED LANDS GRAZING PERMITS
##### Subpart A - Definitions, Authority, Purpose, and Scope
###### ยง 161.1 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Grazing Permit

means a revocable privilege granted in writing and limited to entering on and utilizing forage by domestic livestock on a specified range unit. The term as used herein shall include authorizations issued to enable the crossing or trailing of domestic livestock within an assigned range unit.
