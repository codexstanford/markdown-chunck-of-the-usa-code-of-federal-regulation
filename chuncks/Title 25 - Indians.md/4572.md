
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter I - Energy and Minerals
#### PART 225 - OIL AND GAS, GEOTHERMAL, AND SOLID MINERALS AGREEMENTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 225.3 Definitions.
####### Indian surface owner

means any individual Indian or Alaska Native, or Indian tribe, band, nation, pueblo, community, rancheria, colony, or other group that owns the surface estate in land the title to which is held in trust by the United States or is subject to a restriction against alienation imposed by the United States.
