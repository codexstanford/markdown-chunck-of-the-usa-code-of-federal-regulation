
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 179 - LIFE ESTATES AND FUTURE INTERESTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 179.2 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Life estate without regard to waste

means that the holder of the life estate interest in land is entitled to the receipt of all income, including bonuses and royalties, from such land to the exclusion of the remaindermen.
