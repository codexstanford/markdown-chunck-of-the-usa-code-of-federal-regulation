
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 168 - GRAZING REGULATIONS FOR THE HOPI PARTITIONED LANDS AREA
##### ยง 168.6 Grazing on range units authorized by permit.
###### Grazing permits to Hopi tribal members on their partitioned lands.

(a)The Area Director shall assign grazing privileges to the Hopi Tribe for lands within Hopi Partitioned Lands. The tribal government will then allocate use to their tribal members for permit periods not to exceed five years. Grazing use by Hopi tribal enterprises may be authorized. The Area Director will issue permits based on the determination of the Hopi tribal government.
