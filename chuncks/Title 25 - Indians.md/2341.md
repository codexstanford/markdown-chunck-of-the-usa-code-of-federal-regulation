
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter G - Financial Activities
#### PART 135 - CONSTRUCTION ASSESSMENTS, CROW INDIAN IRRIGATION PROJECT
##### Subpart B - Charges Assessed Against Non-Indian Lands Not Included in an Irrigation District
###### ยง 135.22 Penalty.

To all assessments not paid on the due date there shall be added a penalty of one-half of one percent per month or fraction thereof, from the due date so long as the delinquency continues.
