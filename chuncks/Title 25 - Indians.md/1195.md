
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter E - Education
#### PART 39 - THE INDIAN SCHOOL EQUALIZATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 39.2 What definitions apply to terms in this part?
####### Tribally operated school,et seq.,

means an elementary school, secondary school, or dormitory that receives financial assistance for its operation under a contract, grant, or agreement with the Bureau under section 102, 103(a), or 208 of 25 U.S.C. 450or under the Tribally Controlled Schools Act of 1988.
