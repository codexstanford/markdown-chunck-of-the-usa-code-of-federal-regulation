
# Title 25 - Indians
## Chapter I - Bureau of Indian Affairs, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter H - Land and Water
#### PART 161 - NAVAJO PARTITIONED LANDS GRAZING PERMITS
##### Subpart A - Definitions, Authority, Purpose, and Scope
###### ยง 161.1 What definitions do I need to know?
####### Navajo Partitioned Lands (NPL)

means that portion of the Former Joint Use Area awarded to the Navajo Nation under the Judgment of Partition issued April 18, 1979, by the United States District Court for the District of Arizona, and now a separate administrative entity within the Navajo Indian Reservation.
