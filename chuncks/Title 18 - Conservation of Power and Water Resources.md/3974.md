###### Notification to Director.

(c)In the event that the consulted agency fails to respond to requests by the project sponsor under paragraph (b) of this section, the project sponsor must notify the Director of the Office of Energy Projects. The notification must include all information, reports, letters, and other correspondence prepared pursuant to this section. The Director will determine whether:

(2) Formal consultation must be initiated under paragraph (d) of this section; or

(3) Construction may proceed.
