
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter B - Regulations Under the Federal Power Act
#### PART 4 - LICENSES, PERMITS, EXEMPTIONS, AND DETERMINATION OF PROJECT COSTS
##### Subpart D - Application for Preliminary Permit, License or Exemption: General Provisions
###### ยง 4.33 Limitations on submitting applications.
####### Limitations on submissions and acceptance of a license application.

(b)The Commission will not accept an application for a license or project works that would develop, conserve, or utilize, in whole or part, the same water resources that would be developed, conserved, and utilized by a project for which there is:

(1) An unexpired preliminary permit, unless the permittee has submitted an application for license; or

(2) An unexpired license, except as provided for in Section 15 of the Federal Power Act.
