
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter VI - Water Resources Council
### PART 740 - STATE WATER MANAGEMENT PLANNING PROGRAM
#### ยง 740.2 Definitions.
##### Milestones

mean key events in the activity implementation schedule. Milestones indicate important dates for design implementation and monitoring tasks. Examples of milestones include but are not limited to hiring of key staff, publication dates, workshop dates, or the completion of specific phases of the implementation schedule.
