
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter R - Approved Forms, Interstate Commerce Act
#### PART 356 - PRESERVATION OF RECORDS FOR OIL PIPELINE COMPANIES
##### ยง 356.2 General instructions.
###### Premature destruction or loss of records.

(f)When records are destroyed or lost before the expiration of the prescribed period of retention, a certified statement listing, as far as may be determined, the records destroyed, and describing the circumstances of accidental or other premature destruction or loss must be filed with the Commission within ninety (90) days from the date of discovery of such destruction.
