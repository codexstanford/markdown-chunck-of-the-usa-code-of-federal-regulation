
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter XIII - Tennessee Valley Authority
### PART 1310 - ADMINISTRATIVE COST RECOVERY
#### ยง 1310.3 Assessment of administrative charge.
##### Assessment of charge when actual administrative costs significantly exceed established range.

(c)When the responsible land manager determines that the actual administrative costs are expected to significantly exceed the range of costs established in paragraph (a) of this section, such manager shall not proceed with the TVA action until agreement is reached on payment of a charge calculated to cover TVA's actual administrative costs.
