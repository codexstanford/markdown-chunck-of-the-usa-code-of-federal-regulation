
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources

##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 367.1 Definitions.
####### Premium

(35), as applied to securities issued or assumed by the service company, means the excess of the cash value of the consideration received from their sale over the sum of their par (stated value of no-par stocks) or face value and interest or dividends accrued at the date of sale.
