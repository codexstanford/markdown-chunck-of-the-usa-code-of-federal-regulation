
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter E - Regulations Under Natural Gas Act
#### PART 154 - RATE SCHEDULES AND TARIFFS
##### Subpart C - Procedures for Changing Tariffs
###### ยง 154.205 Withdrawals and amendments of tariff filings and executed service agreements.
####### Withdrawal of suspended tariffs, executed service agreements, or parts thereof.

(c)A natural gas company may not, within the period of suspension, withdraw a proposed tariff, executed service agreement, or part thereof, that has been suspended by order of the Commission, except by special permission of the Commission granted upon application therefor and for good cause shown.
