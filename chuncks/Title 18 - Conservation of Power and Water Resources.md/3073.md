
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources

##### ยง 301.2 Definitions.
###### Contract System Load.

The total regional retail load included in the most recently filed FERC Form 1 or, for a Consumer-owned Utility, the total retail load from the most recent annual audited financial statement, as adjusted pursuant to the ASC methodology.
