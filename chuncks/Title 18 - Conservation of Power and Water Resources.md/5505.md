
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter XIII - Tennessee Valley Authority
### PART 1306 - RELOCATION ASSISTANCE AND REAL PROPERTY ACQUISITION POLICIES
#### Subpart A - Regulations and Procedures
##### ยง 1306.4 Rent after acquisition.

If TVA rents real property acquired by it to the former owner or former tenant, the amount of rent shall not exceed the fair rental value on a short-term basis.
