
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter XIII - Tennessee Valley Authority
### PART 1306 - RELOCATION ASSISTANCE AND REAL PROPERTY ACQUISITION POLICIES
#### Subpart A - Regulations and Procedures
##### ยง 1306.1 Purpose and applicability.
###### Purpose.

(a)The purpose of the regulations and procedures in this Subpart A is to implement Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property acquisition Policies Act of 1970 (Pub. L. 91-646, 84 Stat. 42 U.S.C. 4601) as amended by the Surface Transportation and Uniform Relocation Assistance Act of 1987 (Title IV of Pub. L. 100-17, Stat. 246-256, 42 U.S.C. 4601 note) (Uniform Act, as amended).
