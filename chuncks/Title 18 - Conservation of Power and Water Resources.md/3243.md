
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter P - Regulations Under the Interstate Commerce Act
#### PART 348 - OIL PIPELINE APPLICATIONS FOR MARKET POWER DETERMINATIONS
##### ยง 348.1 Content of application for a market power determination.
###### Statement A-geographic market.

(1)This statement must describe the geographic markets in which the carrier seeks to establish that it lacks significant market power. The carrier must include the origin market and the destination market related to the service for which it proposes to charge market-based rates. The statement must explain why the carrier's method for selecting the geographic markets is appropriate.
