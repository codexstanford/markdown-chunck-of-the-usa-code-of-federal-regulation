(iv)For purposes of this subparagraph and paragraph (d)(2)(ii) of this section, the amount of any rate increase shall be the difference between the total revenues to be recovered under the rate change and the total revenues recovered or recoverable under the rate to be superseded or supplemented and shall be determined by:

(A) applying the components of the rate to be superseded or supplemented to the billing determinants for the twelve months of Period I;
