
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter R - Approved Forms, Interstate Commerce Act
#### PART 356 - PRESERVATION OF RECORDS FOR OIL PIPELINE COMPANIES
##### ยง 356.2 General instructions.
###### Protection and storage of records.

(c)Each oil pipeline company subject to these regulations must provide reasonable protection for records. The records must have protections from fire, floods, and other hazards. Storage spaces, will also prevent unnecessary exposure to deterioration from excessive humidity, dryness, or lack of proper ventilation.
