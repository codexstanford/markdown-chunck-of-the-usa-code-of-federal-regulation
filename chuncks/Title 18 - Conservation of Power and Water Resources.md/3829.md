
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter W - Revised General Rules
#### PART 375 - THE COMMISSION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 375.104 Transfer of proceedings from other agencies to the Commission.
####### Substitution of Commission for other agencies in court proceedings.

(b)Pursuant to section 705(e) of the DOE Act, the Commission authorizes the Solicitor of the Commission to file the appropriate pleadings to substitute the Commission for the Interstate Commerce Commission or the Federal Power Commission as necessary in any pending court litigation, responsibility for which is transferred to the Commission.
