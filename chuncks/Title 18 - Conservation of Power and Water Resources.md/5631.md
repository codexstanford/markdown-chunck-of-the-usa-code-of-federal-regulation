
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter XIII - Tennessee Valley Authority
### PART 1309 - NONDISCRIMINATION WITH RESPECT TO AGE
#### ยง 1309.5 What are the rules against age discrimination?
##### General rule.

(a)No person in the United States shall, on the basis of age, be excluded from participation in, be denied the benefits of, or be subjected to discrimination under, any program or activity receiving financial assistance from TVA.
