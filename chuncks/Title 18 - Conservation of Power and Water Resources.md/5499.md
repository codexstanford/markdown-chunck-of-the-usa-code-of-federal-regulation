
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter XIII - Tennessee Valley Authority
### PART 1304 - APPROVAL OF CONSTRUCTION IN THE TENNESSEE RIVER SYSTEM AND REGULATION OF STRUCTURES AND OTHER ALTERATIONS
#### Subpart E - Miscellaneous
##### ยง 1304.412 Definitions.
###### Winter drawdown elevation

means the elevation to which a reservoir water level is lowered during fall to provide storage capacity for winter and spring floodwaters.
