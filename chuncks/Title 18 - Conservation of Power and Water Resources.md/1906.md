
# Title 18 - Conservation of Power and Water Resources
## Chapter I - Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Department of Energy
### Subchapter C - Accounts, Federal Power Act
#### PART 125 - PRESERVATION OF RECORDS OF PUBLIC UTILITIES AND LICENSEES
##### ยง 125.2 General instructions.
###### Pending complaint litigation or governmental proceedings.

(l)Notwithstanding the minimum requirements, if a public utility or licensee is involved in pending litigation, complaint procedures, proceedings remanded by the court, or governmental proceedings, it must retain all relevant records.
