
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart D - Limitations on Medicare Payment for Services Covered Under Liability or No-Fault Insurance
###### ยง 411.50 General provisions.
####### Prompt,promptly,

orwhen used in connection with payment by a liability insurer means payment within 120 days after the earlier of the following:

(1) The date a claim is filed with an insurer or a lien is filed against a potential liability settlement.

(2) The date the service was furnished or, in the case of inpatient hospital services, the date of discharge.
