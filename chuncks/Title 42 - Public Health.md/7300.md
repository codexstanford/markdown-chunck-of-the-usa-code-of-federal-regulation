
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 410.2 Definitions.
####### Encounter

means a direct personal contact between a patient and a physician, or other person who is authorized by State licensure law and, if applicable, by hospital or CAH staff bylaws, to order or furnish hospital services for diagnosis or treatment of the patient.
