####### Eligible managed care organization

(D) Any other health plans that provide or arrange for items and services for Medicaid enrollees in accordance with a risk-based contract with a State agency subject to the upper payment limits in ยง 447.361 of this title or an equivalent payment cap approved by the Secretary;

(F) A Federally qualified HMO.
