
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 488.425 Directed inservice training.
####### Required training.

(a)CMS or the State agency may require the staff of a facility to attend an inservice training program if-

(1) The facility has a pattern of deficiencies that indicate noncompliance; and

(2) Education is likely to correct the deficiencies.
