
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart O - Prospective Payment System for Long-Term Care Hospitals

####### Additional interim payments.

(2)A hospital may request additional interim payments at intervals of at least 60 days after the date of the first interim bill submitted under paragraph (d)(1) of this section. Payment for these additional interim bills, as well as the final bill, is determined as if the bill were the final bill with appropriate adjustments made to the payment amount to reflect any previous interim payment made under the provisions of this paragraph.
