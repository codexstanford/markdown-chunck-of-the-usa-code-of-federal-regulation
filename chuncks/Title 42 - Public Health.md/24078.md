
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services

#### PART 484 - HOME HEALTH SERVICES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 484.2 Definitions.
####### Supervised practical training

means training in a practicum laboratory or other setting in which the trainee demonstrates knowledge while providing covered services to an individual under the direct supervision of either a registered nurse or a licensed practical nurse who is under the supervision of a registered nurse.
