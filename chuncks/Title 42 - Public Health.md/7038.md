
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 408 - PREMIUMS FOR SUPPLEMENTARY MEDICAL INSURANCE
##### Subpart C - Deduction From Monthly Benefits
###### § 408.45 Deduction from age 72 special payments.
####### Deduction of premiums.

(a)SMI premiums are deducted from age 72 special payments made under section 228 of the Act or the payments are withheld under procedures that correspond to the rules set forth in §§ 408.40 and 408.43.
