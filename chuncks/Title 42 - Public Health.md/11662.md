
# Title 42 - Public Health

#### PART 414 - PAYMENT FOR PART B MEDICAL AND OTHER HEALTH SERVICES

####### Travel allowance amount,Eligible miles.

(iii)-(A)Eligible miles begin at the laboratory or the starting point of the technician's travel for specimen collection as specified in paragraph (a)(1) of this section, and end at the laboratory or the ending point of the technician's travel for specimen collection as specified in paragraph (a)(1) of this section.
