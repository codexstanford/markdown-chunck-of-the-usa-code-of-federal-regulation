
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 483.5 Definitions.
####### Transfer and discharge

includes movement of a resident to a bed outside of the certified facility whether that bed is in the same physical plant or not. Transfer and discharge does not refer to movement of a resident to a bed within the same certified facility.
