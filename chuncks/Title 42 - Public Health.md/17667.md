
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 424 - CONDITIONS FOR MEDICARE PAYMENT
##### Subpart B - Certification and Plan Requirements
###### ยง 424.16 Timing of certification for individual admitted to a hospital before entitlement to Medicare benefits.
####### Example. (Hospital that is not a psychiatric hospital and is not subject to PPS).

(b)For a patient who is admitted on August 15 and becomes entitled on September 1-

(1) The certification is required no later than September 12;

(2) The first recertification is required no later than September 18; and

(3) Subsequent recertifications are required at least every 30 days after September 18.
