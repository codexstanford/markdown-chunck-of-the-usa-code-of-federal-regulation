
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter G - Standards and Certification
#### PART 493 - LABORATORY REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart R - Enforcement Procedures
###### ยง 493.1834 Civil money penalty.
####### Due date for payment of penalty.

(h)(1) Payment of a civil money penalty is due 15 days from the date of the notice specified in paragraph (g)(2) of this section.

(2) CMS may approve a plan for a laboratory to pay a civil money penalty, plus interest, over a period of up to one year from the original due date.
