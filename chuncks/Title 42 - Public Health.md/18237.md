
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 425.20 Definitions.
####### Newly assigned beneficiary

means a beneficiary that is assigned to the ACO in the current performance year who was neither assigned to nor received a primary care service from any of the ACO participants during the assignment window for the most recent prior benchmark or performance year.
