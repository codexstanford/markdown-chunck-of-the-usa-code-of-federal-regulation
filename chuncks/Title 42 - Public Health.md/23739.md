
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 483.70 Administration.
####### Governing body.

(d)(1) The facility must have a governing body, or designated persons functioning as a governing body, that is legally responsible for establishing and implementing policies regarding the management and operation of the facility; and

(2) The governing body appoints the administrator who is-

(i) Licensed by the State, where licensing is required;

(ii) Responsible for management of the facility; and

(iii) Reports to and is accountable to the governing body.
