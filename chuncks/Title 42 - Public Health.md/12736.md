
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 416 - AMBULATORY SURGICAL SERVICES
##### Subpart H - Requirements Under the Ambulatory Surgical Center Quality Reporting (ASCQR) Program
###### ยง 416.320 Retention and removal of quality measures under the ASCQR Program.
####### General rule for the retention of quality measures.

(a)Quality measures adopted for an ASCQR Program measure set for a previous payment determination year are retained in the ASCQR Program for measure sets for subsequent payment determination years, except when they are removed, suspended, or replaced as set forth in paragraphs (b) and (c) of this section.
