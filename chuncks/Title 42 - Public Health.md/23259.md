
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Quality Improvement Organizations
#### PART 478 - RECONSIDERATIONS AND APPEALS
##### Subpart B - Utilization and Quality Control Quality Improvement Organization (QIO) Reconsiderations and Appeals
###### ยง 478.22 Good cause for late filing of a request for a reconsideration or hearing.
####### General Rule.

(a)In determining whether a party has good cause for not filing a request for reconsideration or hearing timely, the QIO or ALJ, respectively, must consider the following:

(1) What circumstances kept the party from making the request on time.

(2) Whether an action by the QIO misled the party.

(3) Whether the party understood the requirements of the Act as affected by amendments to the Act, other legislation, or court decisions.
