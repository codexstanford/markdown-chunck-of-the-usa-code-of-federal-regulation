
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 414.1305 Definitions.
####### Virtual group

means a combination of two or more TINs assigned to one or more solo practitioners or to one or more groups consisting of 10 or fewer eligible clinicians, or both, that elect to form a virtual group for a performance period for a year.
