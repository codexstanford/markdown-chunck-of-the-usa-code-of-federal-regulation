
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 409 - HOSPITAL INSURANCE BENEFITS
##### Subpart C - Posthospital SNF Care
###### ยง 409.25 Drugs, biologicals, supplies, appliances, and equipment.
####### Supplies, appliances, and equipment.

(c)Except as specified in paragraph (d) of this section, Medicare pays for supplies, appliances, and equipment as posthospital SNF care only if they are-

(1) Ordinarily furnished by the facility to inpatients; and

(2) Furnished to inpatients for use in the facility.
