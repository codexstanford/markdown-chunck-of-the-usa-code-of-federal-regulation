######## Informational notices.

(c)A State must provide an informational notice to each potential enrollee who may enroll in an MCO, PIHP, PAHP, PCCM, or PCCM entity. Such notice must:

(1) Include the MCOs, PIHPs, PAHPs, PCCMs, or PCCM entities available to the potential enrollee;

(2) Explain how to select an MCO, PIHP, PAHP, PCCM, or PCCM entity;

(3) Explain the implications of making or not making an active choice of an MCO, PIHP, PAHP, PCCM or PCCM entity;

(4) Explain the length of the enrollment period as well as the disenrollment policies in ยง 457.1212; and

(5) Comply with the information requirements in ยง 457.1207 and accessibility standards established under ยง 457.340.
