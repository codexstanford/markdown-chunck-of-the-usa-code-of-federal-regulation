
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 408.4 Payment obligations.
####### Premiums not required for certain kidney donors.

(c)(1) No premiums are required for SMI benefits related to the donation of a kidney if the donor is not an enrollee.

(2) A kidney donor who is an enrollee is not relieved of the obligation for premiums.
