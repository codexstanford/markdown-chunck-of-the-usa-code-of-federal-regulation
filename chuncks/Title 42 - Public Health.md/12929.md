
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 417 - HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS, COMPETITIVE MEDICAL PLANS, AND HEALTH CARE PREPAYMENT PLANS
##### Subpart J - Qualifying Conditions for Medicare Contracts
###### ยง 417.401 Definitions.
####### Arrangement

means a written agreement between an HMO or CMP and another entity, under which-

(1) The other entity agrees to furnish specified services to the HMO's or CMP's Medicare enrollees;

(2) The HMO or CMP retains responsibility for the services; and

(3) Medicare payment to the HMO or CMP discharges the beneficiary's obligation to pay for the services.
