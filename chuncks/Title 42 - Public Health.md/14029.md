
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 422.2 Definitions.
####### National coverage determination

(NCD) means a national policy determination regarding the coverage status of a particular service that CMS makes under section 1862(a)(1) of the Act, and publishes as anotice or CMS ruling. (The term does not include coverage changes mandated by statute.)
