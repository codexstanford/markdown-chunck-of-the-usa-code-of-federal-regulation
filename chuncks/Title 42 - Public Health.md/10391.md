###### ยง 413.100 Special treatment of certain accrued costs.
####### Recognition of accrued costs,General.

(c)-(1)Although Medicare recognizes, in the year of accrual, the accrual of costs for which a provider has not actually expended funds during the current cost reporting period, for purposes of payment Medicare does not recognize the accrual of costs unless the related liabilities are liquidated timely.
