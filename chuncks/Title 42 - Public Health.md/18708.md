### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 425 - MEDICARE SHARED SAVINGS PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Shared Savings and Losses
###### § 425.605 Calculation of shared savings and losses under the BASIC track.
####### 2,1,1

() Instead of the revenue-based loss recoupment limit determined under paragraph (d)(1)(v)(D)() of this section, the loss recoupment limit for the ACO is 1 percentage point higher than the percentage, as specified in § 414.1415(c)(3)(i)(B) of this chapter, based on the ACO's updated benchmark as determined under § 425.601 or § 425.652, if the amount determined under paragraph (d)(1)(v)(D)() of this section exceeds this percentage of the ACO's updated benchmark as determined under § 425.601 or § 425.652.
