
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Provisions
#### PART 400 - INTRODUCTION; DEFINITIONS
##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 400.202 Definitions specific to Medicare.
####### National coverage determination (NCD)

means a decision that CMS makes regarding whether to cover a particular service nationally under title XVIII of the Act. An NCD does not include a determination of what code, if any, is assigned to a service or a determination with respect to the amount of payment to be made for the service.
