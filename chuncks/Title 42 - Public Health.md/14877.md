
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 422 - MEDICARE ADVANTAGE PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Payments to Medicare Advantage Organizations
###### ยง 422.314 Special rules for beneficiaries enrolled in MA MSA plans.
####### Establishment and designation of medical savings account (MSA).

(a)A beneficiary who elects coverage under an MA MSA plan-

(1) Must establish an MA MSA with a trustee that meets the requirements of paragraph (b) of this section; and

(2) If he or she has more than one MA MSA, designate the particular account to which payments under the MA MSA plan are to be made.
