
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 414 - PAYMENT FOR PART B MEDICAL AND OTHER HEALTH SERVICES
##### Subpart O - Merit-Based Incentive Payment System and Alternative Payment Model Incentive
###### ยง 414.1430 Qualifying APM participant determination: QP and partial QP thresholds.
####### QP patient count threshold.

(3)(i) The QP patient count thresholds are the following values for the indicated payment years:

(A) 2021 through 2024: 35 percent.

(B) 2025 and later: 50 percent.

(ii) To meet the QP patient count threshold under this option, the eligible clinician must also meet a 20 percent QP patient count threshold under the Medicare Option.
