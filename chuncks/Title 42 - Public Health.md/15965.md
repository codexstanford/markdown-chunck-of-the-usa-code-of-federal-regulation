
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart C - Benefits and Beneficiary Protections
###### ยง 423.100 Definitions.
####### Usual and customary (U&C) price

means the price that an out-of-network pharmacy or a physician's office charges a customer who does not have any form of prescription drug coverage for a covered Part D drug.
