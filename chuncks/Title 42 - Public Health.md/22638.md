(d)Except as otherwise provided below, the enhanced FMAP is available with respect to the following States' expenditures:

(1) Child health assistance under the plan for targeted low-income children in the form of providing health benefits coverage that meets the requirements of section 2103 of the Act; and

(2) Subject to the 10 percent limit provisions under ยง 457.618(a)(2), the following expenditures:
