####### Stage IV chronic kidney disease

means kidney damage with a severe decrease in glomerular filtration rate (GFR) quantitatively defined by a GFR value of 15-29 ml/min/1.73m, using the Modification of Diet in Renal Disease (MDRD) Study formula.
