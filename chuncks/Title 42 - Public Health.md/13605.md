####### Medical appliances and supplies, including drugs and biologicals.

(f)Only drugs as defined in section 1861(t) of the Act and which are used primarily for the relief of pain and symptom control related to the individual's terminal illness are covered. Appliances may include covered durable medical equipment as described in ยง 410.38 of this chapter as well as other self-help and personal comfort items related to the palliation or management of the patient's terminal illness.
