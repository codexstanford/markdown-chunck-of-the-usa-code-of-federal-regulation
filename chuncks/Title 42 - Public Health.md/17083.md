
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart R - Payments to Sponsors of Retiree Prescription Drug Plans
###### ยง 423.890 Appeals.
####### Reopening,Ability to reopen.

(d)-(1)CMS may reopen and revise an initial or reconsidered determination upon its own motion or upon the request of a sponsor:

(i) Within 1 year of the date of the notice of determination for any reason.

(ii) Within 4 years for good cause.

(iii) At any time when the underlying decision was obtained through fraud or similar fault.
