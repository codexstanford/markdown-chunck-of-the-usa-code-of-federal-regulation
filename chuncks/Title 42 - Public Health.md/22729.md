
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - State Children's Health Insurance Programs (SCHIPs)
#### PART 457 - ALLOTMENTS AND GRANTS TO STATES
##### Subpart K - State Plan Requirements: Applicant and Enrollee Protections
###### ยง 457.1130 Program specific review process: Matters subject to review.
####### Health services matter.

(b)A State must ensure that an enrollee has an opportunity for external review of a-

(1) Delay, denial, reduction, suspension, or termination of health services, in whole or in part, including a determination about the type or level of services; and

(2) Failure to approve, furnish, or provide payment for health services in a timely manner.
