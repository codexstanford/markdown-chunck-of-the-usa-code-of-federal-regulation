##### Subpart J - Prospective Payment for Skilled Nursing Facilities
###### ยง 413.338 Skilled nursing facility value-based purchasing program.
####### Value-based incentive payment amount,Total amount available for a fiscal year.

(2)- (i)The total amount available for value-based incentive payments for a fiscal year is at least 60 percent of the total amount of the reduction to the adjusted SNF PPS payments for that fiscal year, as estimated by CMS, and will be increased as appropriate for each fiscal year to account for the assignment of a performance score to low-volume SNFs under paragraph (d)(3) of this section. Beginning with the FY 2023 SNF VBP, the total amount for value-based incentive payments for a fiscal year is 60 percent of the total amount of the reduction to the adjusted SNF PPS payments for that fiscal year, as estimated by CMS.
