###### Notification of enrollment decision.

(d)(1) The WTC Health Program will decide if the applicant meets the current eligibility criteria for WTC responders in ยง 88.4 and is qualified, and notify the applicant of the enrollment decision in writing within 60 calendar days of the date of receipt of the application.
