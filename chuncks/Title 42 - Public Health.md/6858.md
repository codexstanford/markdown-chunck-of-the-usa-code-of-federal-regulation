
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 407 - SUPPLEMENTARY MEDICAL INSURANCE (SMI) ENROLLMENT AND ENTITLEMENT
##### Subpart B - Individual Enrollment and Entitlement for SMI
###### ยง 407.10 Eligibility to enroll.
####### Exception.

(b)An individual is not eligible to enroll for SMI if he or she has been convicted of-

(1) Spying, sabotage, treason, or subversive activities under chapter 37, 105, or 115 of title 18 of the United States Code; or

(2) Conspiracy to establish dictatorship under section 4 of the Internal Security Act of 1950.
