
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Medical Assistance Programs
#### PART 438 - MANAGED CARE
##### Subpart B - State Responsibilities
###### ยง 438.68 Network adequacy standards.
####### Provider-specific network adequacy standards.,Provider types.

(b)-(1)At a minimum, a State must develop a quantitative network adequacy standard for the following provider types, if covered under the contract:

(i) Primary care, adult and pediatric.

(ii) OB/GYN.

(iii) Behavioral health (mental health and substance use disorder), adult and pediatric.

(iv) Specialist (as designated by the State), adult, and pediatric.

(v) Hospital.

(vi) Pharmacy.

(vii) Pediatric dental.
