
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart F - Submission of Bids, Premiums, and Related Information and Plan Approval
###### ยง 422.252 Terminology.
####### Annual MA capitation rate

means a county payment rate for an MA local area (county) for a calendar year. The terms "per capita rate" and "capitation rate" are used interchangeably to refer to the annual MA capitation rate.
