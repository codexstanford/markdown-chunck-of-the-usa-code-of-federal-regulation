###### ยง 431.620 Agreement with State mental health authority or mental institutions.
####### Definition.

(b)For purposes of this section, an "institution for mental diseases" means an institution primarily engaged in providing diagnosis, treatment, or care of persons with mental diseases. This includes medical attention, nursing care, and related services.
