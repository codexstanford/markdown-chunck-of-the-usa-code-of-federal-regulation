
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - State Children's Health Insurance Programs (SCHIPs)
#### PART 457 - ALLOTMENTS AND GRANTS TO STATES
##### Subpart G - Strategic Planning, Reporting, and Evaluation
###### ยง 457.740 State expenditures and statistical reports.
####### Reportable household income categories.

(b)(1) A State that does not impose cost sharing or a State that imposes cost sharing based on a fixed percentage of income must report by two household income categories:

(i) At or below 150 percent of FPL.

(ii) Over 150 percent of FPL.

(2) A State that imposes a different level or percentage of cost sharing at different poverty levels must report by poverty level categories that match the poverty level categories used for purposes of cost sharing.
