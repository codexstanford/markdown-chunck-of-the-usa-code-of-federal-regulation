
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

###### ยง 417.420 Basic rules on enrollment and entitlement.
####### Enrollment.

(a)Eligible individuals who are entitled to benefits under both Part A and Part B of Medicare or only Part B may elect to receive those benefits through an HMO or CMP that has in effect a contract with CMS under subpart L of this part.
