
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart E - Survey and Certification of Long-Term Care Facilities
###### ยง 488.301 Definitions.
####### Substantial compliance

means a level of compliance with the requirements of participation such that any identified deficiencies pose no greater risk to resident health or safety than the potential for causing minimal harm.
