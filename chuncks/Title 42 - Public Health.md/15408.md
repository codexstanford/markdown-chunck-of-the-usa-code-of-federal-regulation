
# Title 42 - Public Health

####### Enrollee's right to an immediate QIO review.

(a)An enrollee has a right to request an immediate review by the QIO when an MA organization or hospital (acting directly or through its utilization committee), with physician concurrence determines that inpatient care is no longer necessary.
