
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter I - Public Health Service, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter M - Indian Health Service, Department of Health and Human Services
#### PART 136 - INDIAN HEALTH
##### Subpart A - Purpose and Definitions
###### ยง 136.3 Administrative instructions.
####### Indian Health Service Manual

The service periodically issues administrative instructions to its officers and employees, which are primarily found in theand the Area Office and program office supplements. These instructions are operating procedures to assist officers and employees in carrying out their responsibilities, and are not regulations establishing program requirements which are binding upon members of the general public.
