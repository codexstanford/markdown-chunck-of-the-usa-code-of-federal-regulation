####### Reconciliation report.

(h)CMS issues each participant hospital a CJR reconciliation report for the performance year or performance year subset. Each CJR reconciliation report contains the following:

(1) Information on the participant hospital's composite quality score described in ยง 510.315.

(2) The total actual episode payments for the participant hospital.
