
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 412.622 Basis of payment.
####### State (or region, as applicable) that is experiencing a surge,https://www.whitehouse.gov/openingamerica/

means a state (or region, as applicable) that is in phase 1 of the President's Guidelines for Opening Up America Again (), specifically, a state (or region, as applicable) that satisfies all of the following, as determined by applicable state and local officials:

(i) All vulnerable individuals continue to shelter in place.

(ii) Individuals continue social distancing.

(iii) Individuals avoid socializing in groups of more than 10.

(iv) Non-essential travel is minimized.

(v) Visits to senior living facilities and hospitals are prohibited.

(vi) Schools and organized youth activities remain closed.
