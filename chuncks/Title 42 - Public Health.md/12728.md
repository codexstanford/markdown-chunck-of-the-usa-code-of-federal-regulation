
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart H - Requirements Under the Ambulatory Surgical Center Quality Reporting (ASCQR) Program
###### ยง 416.310 Data collection and submission requirements under the ASCQR Program.
####### Review and corrections period.

(iii)For measures submitted to CMS via a CMS online tool, ASCs have a review and corrections period, which runs concurrently with the data submission period. During this timeframe, ASCs can enter, review, and correct data submitted. After the submission deadline, this data cannot be changed.
