
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart H - Medicare Prescription Drug Discount Card and Transitional Assistance Program
###### ยง 403.820 Sanctions, penalties, and termination.
####### Reconsideration determination.

(9)A reconsideration determination is a new determination that-

(i) Is based on a review of the contract determination, the evidence and findings upon which it was based, and any other written evidence submitted before notice of the reconsidered determination is mailed, including facts relating to the status of the endorsed sponsor subsequent to the contract determination; and

(ii) Affirms, reverses, or modifies the initial contract determination.
