
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 412.513 Patient classification system.
####### Classification methodology.

(a)CMS classifies specific inpatient hospital discharges from long-term care hospitals by long-term care diagnosis-related groups (LTC-DRGs) to ensure that each hospital discharge is appropriately assigned based on essential data abstracted from the inpatient bill for that discharge.
