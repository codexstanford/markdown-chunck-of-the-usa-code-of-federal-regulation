### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

###### ยง 414.412 Submission of bids under a competitive bidding program.
####### Requiring bid surety bonds for bidding entities,Bidding requirements.

(g)-(1)For competitions beginning on or after January 1, 2017, and no later than January 1, 2019, a bidding entity may not submit a bid(s) for a CBA unless it obtains a bid surety bond for the CBA from an authorized surety on the Department of the Treasury's Listing of Certified Companies and provides proof of having obtained the bond by submitting a copy to CMS by the deadline for bid submission.
