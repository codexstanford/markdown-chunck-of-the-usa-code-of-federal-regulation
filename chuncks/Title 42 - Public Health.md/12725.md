
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 416 - AMBULATORY SURGICAL SERVICES
##### Subpart H - Requirements Under the Ambulatory Surgical Center Quality Reporting (ASCQR) Program
###### ยง 416.310 Data collection and submission requirements under the ASCQR Program.
####### Requirements for claims-based measures not using QDCs.

(b)The data collection period for claims-based quality measures not using QDCs is paid Medicare fee-for-service claims from the calendar year 2 years prior to the payment determination year. Only claims for services furnished in each calendar year paid by the MAC by April 30 of the following year of the ending data collection period will be included in the data used for the payment determination.
