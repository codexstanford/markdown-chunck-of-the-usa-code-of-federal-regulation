
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart E - PACE Administrative Requirements
###### ยง 460.74 Infection control.
####### Infection control plan.

(b)The PACE organization must establish, implement, and maintain a documented infection control plan that meets the following requirements:

(1) Ensures a safe and sanitary environment.

(2) Prevents and controls the transmission of disease and infection.
