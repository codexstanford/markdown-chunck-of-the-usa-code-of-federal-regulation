
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter G - Standards and Certification
#### PART 493 - LABORATORY REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 493.2 Definitions.
####### Approved State laboratory program

means a licensure or other regulatory program for laboratories in a State, the requirements of which are imposed under State law, and the State laboratory program has received CMS approval based on the State's compliance with this part.
