
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General Provisions
#### PART 403 - SPECIAL PROGRAMS AND PROJECTS
##### Subpart K - Access to Identifiable Data for the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Models
###### ยง 403.1110 Evaluation of models.
####### Information.

(b)Any State or other entity participating in the testing of a model under section 1115A of the Act must collect and report such information, including "protected health information" as that term is defined at 45 CFR 160.103, as the Secretary determines is necessary to monitor and evaluate such model. Such data must be produced to the Secretary at the time and in the form and manner specified by the Secretary.
