###### ยง 412.523 Methodology for calculating the Federal prospective payment rates.
####### For long-term care hospital prospective payment system fiscal year beginning October 1, 2015, and ending September 30, 2016.

(xii)The LTCH PPS standard Federal payment rate for the long-term care hospital prospective payment system beginning October 1, 2015, and ending September 30, 2016, is the standard Federal payment rate for the previous long-term care hospital prospective payment system fiscal year updated by 1.7 percent, and further adjusted, as appropriate, as described in paragraph (d) of this section.
