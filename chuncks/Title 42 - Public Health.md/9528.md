###### ยง 412.523 Methodology for calculating the Federal prospective payment rates.
####### For long-term care hospital prospective payment system fiscal year beginning October 1, 2014, and ending September 30, 2015.

(xi)The standard Federal rate for the long-term care hospital prospective payment system beginning October 1, 2014, and ending September 30, 2015, is the standard Federal rate for the previous long-term care hospital prospective payment system fiscal year updated by 2.2 percent, and further adjusted, as appropriate, as described in paragraph (d) of this section.
