
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Medical Assistance Programs
#### PART 456 - UTILIZATION CONTROL
##### Subpart D - Utilization Control: Mental Hospitals
###### UR Plan: Review of Need for Continued Stay
####### § 456.235 Description of methods and criteria: Continued stay review dates; length of stay modification.

The UR plan must describe-

(a) The methods and criteria, including norms if used, that the committee uses to assign initial and subsequent continued stay review dates under §§ 456.233 and 456.234 of this subpart; and

(b) The methods that the committee uses to modify an approved length of stay when the beneficiary's condition or treatment schedule changes.
