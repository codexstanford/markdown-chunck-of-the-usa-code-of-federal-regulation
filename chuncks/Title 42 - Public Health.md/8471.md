
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 412 - PROSPECTIVE PAYMENT SYSTEMS FOR INPATIENT HOSPITAL SERVICES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 412.10 Changes in the DRG classification system.
####### Basis for changes in the DRG classification system.

(b)All changes in the DRG classification system are made using the principles established for the DRG system. This means that cases are classified so each DRG is-

(1) Clinically coherent; and

(2) Embraces an acceptable range of resource consumption.
