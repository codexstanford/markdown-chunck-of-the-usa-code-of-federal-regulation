##### Subpart H - Payment for End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) Services
###### ยง 413.236 Transitional add-on payment adjustment for new and innovative equipment and supplies.
####### Capital-related asset.

Asset that an ESRD facility has an economic interest in through ownership (regardless of the manner in which it was acquired) and is subject to depreciation. Equipment obtained by the ESRD facility through operating leases are not considered capital-related assets.
