
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart C - Permissive Exclusions

####### Coordination and management of care (or coordinating and managing care)

(i)means the deliberate organization of patient care activities and sharing of information between two or more VBE participants, one or more VBE participants and the VBE, or one or more VBE participants and patients, that is designed to achieve safer, more effective, or more efficient care to improve the health outcomes of the target patient population.
