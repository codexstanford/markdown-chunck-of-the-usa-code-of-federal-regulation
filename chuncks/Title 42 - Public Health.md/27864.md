####### Extreme and uncontrollable circumstances adjustment.

(k)(1) The episode spending adjustments specified in paragraph (k)(2) of this section apply for a participant hospital that has a CCN primary address that meets both of the following:

(ii) Is located in a county, parish, or tribal government designated in a major disaster declaration under the Stafford Act.
