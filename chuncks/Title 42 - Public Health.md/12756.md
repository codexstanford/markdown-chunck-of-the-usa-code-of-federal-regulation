
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 416 - AMBULATORY SURGICAL SERVICES
##### Subpart H - Requirements Under the Ambulatory Surgical Center Quality Reporting (ASCQR) Program
###### ยง 416.330 Reconsiderations under the ASCQR Program.
####### Final ASCQR Program payment determination.

(d)For an ASC that submits a timely reconsideration request, the reconsideration determination is the final ASCQR Program payment determination. For an ASC that does not submit a timely reconsideration request, the CMS determination is the final payment determination. There is no appeal of any final ASCQR Program payment determination.
