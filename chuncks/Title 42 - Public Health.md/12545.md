
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

####### Payment.

(b)For services described in paragraph (a) of this section, payment is made under Part B by reducing the reasonable costs of furnishing the services by the beneficiary deductible and paying 80 percent of the remaining amount.
