####### Inadvertent autoinoculation (IA).,vaccinia,vaccinial

(5)IA is the spread ofvirus from an existing vaccination site to a second location usually by scratching the vaccination site and subsequently spreading the virus, which produces a newlesion on the same person who received the vaccination. IA is the most common adverse event associated with the replication-competent smallpox vaccine.
