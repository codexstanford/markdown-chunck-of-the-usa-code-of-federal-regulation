(d)If CMS determines that a qualified HMO has failed to initiate or to carry out corrective action in accordance with paragraph (c)(2) of this section-(1) CMS revokes the HMO's qualification and notifies the HMO of this action.

(2) In the notice, CMS provides the HMO with an opportunity for reconsideration of the revocation, including, at the HMO's election, a fair hearing.
