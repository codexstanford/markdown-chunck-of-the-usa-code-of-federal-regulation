
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 417 - HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS, COMPETITIVE MEDICAL PLANS, AND HEALTH CARE PREPAYMENT PLANS
##### Subpart L - Medicare Contract Requirements
###### ยง 417.484 Requirement applicable to related entities.
####### Definition.,related entity

(a)As used in this section,means any entity that is related to the HMO or CMP by common ownership or control and-

(1) Performs some of the HMO's or CMP's management functions under contract or delegation;

(2) Furnishes services to Medicare enrollees under an oral or written agreement; or

(3) Leases real property or sells materials to the HMO or CMP at a cost of more than $2,500 during a contract period.
