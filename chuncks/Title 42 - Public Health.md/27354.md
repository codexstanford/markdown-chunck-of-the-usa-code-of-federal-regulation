### Subchapter G - Standards and Certification

##### Subpart B - Requirements Specific to the Medicare Program
###### ยง 495.102 Incentive payments to EPs.
####### Payment adjustment effective in CY 2015 and subsequent years for nonqualifying EPs.

(d)(1) Subject to paragraphs (d)(3) and (4) of this section, for CY 2015 through the end of CY 2018, for covered professional services furnished by an EP who is not hospital-based, and who is not a qualifying EP by virtue of not being a meaningful EHR user (for the EHR reporting period applicable to the payment adjustment year), the payment amount for such services is equal to the product of the applicable percent specified in paragraph (d)(2) of this section and the Medicare physician fee schedule amount for such services.
