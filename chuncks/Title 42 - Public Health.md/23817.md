
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 483.90 Physical environment.
####### Dining and resident activities.

(h)The facility must provide one or more rooms designated for resident dining and activities. These rooms must-

(1) Be well lighted;

(2) Be well ventilated;

(3) Be adequately furnished; and

(4) Have sufficient space to accommodate all activities.
