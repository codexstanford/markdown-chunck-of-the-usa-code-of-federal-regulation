####### Standardizing amounts.

(c)(1) CMS determines the portion of costs determined in paragraph (b)(1) of this section that is labor-related. This is known as the "labor-related portion" of hospital outpatient costs.

(2) CMS standardizes the geometric mean costs determined in paragraph (b)(1) of this section by adjusting for variations in hospital labor costs across geographic areas.
