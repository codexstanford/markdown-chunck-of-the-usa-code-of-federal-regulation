
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart O - Prospective Payment System for Long-Term Care Hospitals
###### ยง 412.503 Definitions.
####### MSA-dominant hospital

means a hospital that has discharged more than 25 percent of the total subsection (d) hospital Medicare discharges in the MSA (not including discharges paid by a Medicare Advantage plan) in which the hospital is located.
