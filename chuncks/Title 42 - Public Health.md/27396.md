
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 495.200 Definitions.
####### Under common corporate governance

means that a qualifying MA organization and a qualifying MA-affiliated eligible hospital have a common parent corporation, that one is a subsidiary of the other, or that the organization and the hospital have a common board of directors.
