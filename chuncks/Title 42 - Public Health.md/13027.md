####### Limitation on provision of inpatient hospital services.

(d)If a beneficiary's effective date of coverage, as specified in ยง 417.450, in a risk HMO or CMP occurs during an inpatient stay in a hospital paid for under part 412 of this chapter, the HMO or CMP-
