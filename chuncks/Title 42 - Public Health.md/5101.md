
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 401.607 Claims collection.
####### Collection in installments.

(c)Generally, CMS requires that all claims to be satisfied by installment payments must be liquidated in three years or less. If unusual circumstances exist, such as the possibility of debtor insolvency, an installment agreement that extends beyond three years may be approved.
