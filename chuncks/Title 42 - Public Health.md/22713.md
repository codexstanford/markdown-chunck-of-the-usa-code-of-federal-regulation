
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - State Children's Health Insurance Programs (SCHIPs)
#### PART 457 - ALLOTMENTS AND GRANTS TO STATES
##### Subpart J - Allowable Waivers: General Provisions
###### ยง 457.1005 Cost-effective coverage through a community-based health delivery system.
####### Requirements for obtaining a waiver.

(b)To obtain a waiver for cost-effective coverage through a community-based health delivery system, a State must demonstrate that-

(1) The coverage meets all of the requirements of this part, including subpart D and subpart E.

(2) The cost of such coverage, on an average per child basis, does not exceed the cost of coverage under the State plan.
