
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter G - Standards and Certification

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 489.18 Change of ownership or leasing: Effect on provider agreement.
####### Corporation.

(3)The merger of the provider corporation into another corporation, or the consolidation of two or more corporations, resulting in the creation of a new corporation constitutes change of ownership. Transfer of corporate stock or the merger of another corporation into the provider corporation does not constitute change of ownership.
