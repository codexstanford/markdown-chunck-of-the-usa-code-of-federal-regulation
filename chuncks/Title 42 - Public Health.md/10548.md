##### Subpart G - Capital-Related Costs
###### ยง 413.144 Depreciation: Allowance for depreciation on fully depreciated or partially depreciated assets.
####### Principle.

(a)Depreciation on assets being used by a provider at the time it enters into the Medicare program is allowed. This principle applies even though such assets may be fully or partially depreciated on the provider's books.
