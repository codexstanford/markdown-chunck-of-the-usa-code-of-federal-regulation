
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Medical Assistance Programs
#### PART 440 - SERVICES: GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart C - Benchmark Benefit and Benchmark-Equivalent Coverage
###### ยง 440.305 Scope.
####### General.

(a)This subpart sets out requirements for States that elect to provide medical assistance to certain Medicaid eligible individuals within one or more groups of individuals specified by the State, through enrollment of the individuals in coverage, identified as "benchmark" or "benchmark-equivalent." Groups must be identified by characteristics of individuals rather than the amount or level of FMAP.
