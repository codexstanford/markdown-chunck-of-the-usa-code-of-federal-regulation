
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 417.1 Definitions.
####### Medical group members

means (1) a health professional engaged as a partner, associate, or shareholder in the medical group, or (2) any other health professional employed by the group who may be designated as a medical group member by the medical group.
