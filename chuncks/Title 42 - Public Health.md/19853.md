
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Medical Assistance Programs
#### PART 433 - STATE FISCAL ADMINISTRATION
##### Subpart F - Refunding of Federal Share of Medicaid Overpayments to Providers
###### ยง 433.318 Overpayments involving providers who are bankrupt or out of business.
####### Overpayment debts that the State need not refund.

(b)Overpayments are considered debts that the State is unable to recover within the 1-year period following discovery if the following criteria are met:

(1) The provider has filed for bankruptcy, as specified in paragraph (c) of this section; or

(2) The provider has gone out of business and the State is unable to locate the provider and its assets, as specified in paragraph (d) of this section.
