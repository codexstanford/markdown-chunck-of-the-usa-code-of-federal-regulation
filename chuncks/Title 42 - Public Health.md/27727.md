
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 510.2 Definitions.
####### Downstream distribution arrangement

means a financial arrangement between a collaboration agent that is both a PGP, NPPGP, or TGP and an ACO participant and a downstream collaboration agent for the sole purpose of distributing some or all of a distribution payment received by the PGP, NPPGP, or TGP.
