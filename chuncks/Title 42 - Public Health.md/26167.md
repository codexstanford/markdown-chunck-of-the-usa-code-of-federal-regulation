
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter G - Standards and Certification
#### PART 493 - LABORATORY REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart Q - Inspection
###### ยง 493.1777 Standard: Inspection of laboratories that have requested or have been issued a certificate of compliance.
####### Initial inspection.

(a)(1) A laboratory issued a registration certificate must permit an initial inspection to assess the laboratory's compliance with the requirements of this part before CMS issues a certificate of compliance.

(2) The inspection may occur at any time during the laboratory's hours of operation.
