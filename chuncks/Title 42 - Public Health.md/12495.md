
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 415 - SERVICES FURNISHED BY PHYSICIANS IN PROVIDERS, SUPERVISING PHYSICIANS IN TEACHING SETTINGS, AND RESIDENTS IN CERTAIN SETTINGS
##### Subpart D - Physician Services in Teaching Settings
###### ยง 415.152 Definitions.
####### Nonprovider setting

means a setting other than a hospital, skilled nursing facility, home health agency, or comprehensive outpatient rehabilitation facility in which residents furnish services. These include, but are not limited to, family practice or multispecialty clinics and physician offices.
