
# Title 42 - Public Health

##### Subpart D - Administration
###### ยง 494.180 Condition: Governance.
####### Standard: Relationship with the ESRD network.

(i)The governing body receives and acts upon recommendations from the ESRD network. The dialysis facility must cooperate with the ESRD network designated for its geographic area, in fulfilling the terms of the Network's current statement of work. Each facility must participate in ESRD network activities and pursue network goals.
