
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 417 - HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS, COMPETITIVE MEDICAL PLANS, AND HEALTH CARE PREPAYMENT PLANS
##### Subpart N - Medicare Payment to HMOs and CMPs: General Rules
###### ยง 417.528 Payment when Medicare is not primary payer.
####### Responsibilities of HMO or CMP.

(d)An HMO or CMP must-

(1) Identify payers that are primary to Medicare under section 1862(b) of the Act;

(2) Determine the amounts payable by these payers; and

(3) Coordinate the benefits of its Medicare enrollees with these payers.
