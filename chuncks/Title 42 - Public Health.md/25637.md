####### Computation of 15 percent: An HHA that seeks to become a participating HHA by obtaining assets or ownership interest.

(c)For an HHA that seeks to become a participating HHA by purchasing the assets or the ownership interest of a participating or formerly participating HHA, the 15 percent is computed on the basis of Medicare payments made by CMS to the participating or formerly participating HHA in the most recent fiscal year that a cost report has been accepted.
