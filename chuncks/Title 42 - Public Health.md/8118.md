
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart G - Special Rules: Aged Beneficiaries and Spouses Who Are Also Covered Under Group Health Plans
###### ยง 411.172 Medicare benefits secondary to group health plan benefits.
####### Refusal to accept group health plan coverage.

(c)An employee or spouse may refuse the health plan offered by the employer. If the employee or spouse refuses the plan-

(1) Medicare is primary payer for that individual; and

(2) The plan may not offer that individual coverage complementary to Medicare.
