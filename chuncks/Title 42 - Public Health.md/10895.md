####### Principles for organs intended for research for organ acquisition payment purposes.

(b)(1) An organ is intended for research when the OPO or TH designates it for research

prior to the time the donor enters the hospital's operating room for surgical removal of the organ.

(2) Medicare does not share in the acquisition costs of an organ intended for research and

costs to procure these organs must not be included in organ acquisition costs (except pancreata for islet cell transplants as specified in ยง 413.406(a)).

(3) An organ intended for research is not counted as a Medicare usable organ or as a total usable organ in the ratio used to calculate Medicare's share of organ acquisition costs (except pancreata for islet cell transplants as specified in ยง 413.406(a)).
