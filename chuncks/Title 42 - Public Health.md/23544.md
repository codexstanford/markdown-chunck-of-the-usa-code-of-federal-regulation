
# Title 42 - Public Health

#### PART 482 - CONDITIONS OF PARTICIPATION FOR HOSPITALS

####### ยง 482.104 Condition of participation: Additional requirements for kidney transplant programs.
######## Standard: End stage renal disease (ESRD) services.

(a)Kidney transplant programs must directly furnish transplantation and other medical and surgical specialty services required for the care of ESRD patients. A kidney transplant program must have written policies and procedures for ongoing communications with dialysis patients' local dialysis facilities.
