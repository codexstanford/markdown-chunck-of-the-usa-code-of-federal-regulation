
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Medicare Program
#### PART 409 - HOSPITAL INSURANCE BENEFITS
##### Subpart G - Hospital Insurance Deductibles and Coinsurance
###### ยง 409.87 Blood deductible.
####### Beneficiary's responsibility for the first 3 units of whole blood or packed red cells,Basic rule.

(b)-(1)Except as specified in paragraph (b)(2) of this section, the beneficiary is responsible for the first 3 units of whole blood or packed red cells. He or she has the option of paying the hospital's or CAH's charges for the blood or packed red cells or arranging for it to be replaced.
