
# Title 42 - Public Health

###### ยง 495.100 Definitions.
####### Qualifying eligible professional (qualifying EP)

means an EP who is a meaningful EHR user for the EHR reporting period applicable to a payment or payment adjustment year and who is not a hospital-based EP, as determined for that payment or payment adjustment year.
