
# Title 42 - Public Health
## Chapter IV - Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Medical Assistance Programs
#### PART 447 - PAYMENTS FOR SERVICES
##### Subpart A - Payments: General Provisions
###### ยง 447.25 Direct payments to certain beneficiaries for physicians' or dentists' services.
####### State plan requirements.

(b)Except for groups specified in paragraph (c) of this section, a State may make direct payments to beneficiaries for physicians' or dentists' services. If it does so, the State plan must-

(1) Provide for direct payments; and

(2) Specify the conditions under which payments are made.
