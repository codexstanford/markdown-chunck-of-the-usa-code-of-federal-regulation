
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

##### Subpart D - Direct Remittance: Individual Payment
###### ยง 408.63 Billing procedures when monthly benefits are less than monthly premiums.
####### Notice of amount due.

(a)At the beginning of SMI entitlement, and at the beginning of each succeeding calendar year, SSA-

(1) Notifies the enrollee of the amount of benefits payable for the rest of the year and the total premiums due for those same months; and
