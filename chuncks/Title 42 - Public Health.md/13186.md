
# Title 42 - Public Health

### Subchapter B - Medicare Program

####### Medicare Part D program costs.

(c)To the extent that an HMO or CMP provides qualified prescription drug coverage to enrollees under Part D, no costs related to the offering or provision of Part D benefits are reimbursed under this part. These costs are reimbursed solely under the applicable provisions of part 423 of this chapter.
