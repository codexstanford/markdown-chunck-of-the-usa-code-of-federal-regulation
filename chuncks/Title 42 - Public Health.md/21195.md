###### ยง 441.16 Home health agency requirements for surety bonds; Prohibition on FFP.
####### Change of Surety.

(6)An HHA that obtains a replacement surety bond from a different Surety to cover the remaining term of a previously obtained bond must submit the new surety bond to the Medicaid agency within 60 days (or such earlier date as the Medicaid agency may specify) of obtaining the bond from the new Surety for a term specified by the Medicaid agency.
