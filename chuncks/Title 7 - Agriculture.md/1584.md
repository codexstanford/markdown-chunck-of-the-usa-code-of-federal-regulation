
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 8 - 4-H CLUB NAME AND EMBLEM
#### ยง 8.3 Definitions.
##### 4-H Club Name and Emblem

as used in this part means the emblem consisting of a green four-leaf clover with stem and the letter "H" in white or gold on each leaflet, or any insignia in colorable imitation thereof, or the words, "4-H Club," "4-H Clubs" or any combination of these or other words or characters in colorable imitation thereof.
