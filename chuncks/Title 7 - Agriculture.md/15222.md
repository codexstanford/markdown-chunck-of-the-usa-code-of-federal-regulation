
# Title 7 - Agriculture

####### ยง 301.75-17 Funds for the replacement of certified citrus nursery stock.
######## Eligibility.

(a)A commercial citrus nursery may be eligible to receive funds to replace certified citrus nursery stock removed to control citrus canker if the nursery stock was removed pursuant to a public order after September 30, 2001, and before January 10, 2006.
