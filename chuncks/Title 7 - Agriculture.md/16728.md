
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### PART 331 - POSSESSION, USE, AND TRANSFER OF SELECT AGENTS AND TOXINS
##### ยง 331.19 Notification of theft, loss, or release.
###### e.g.

(i) The name of the select agent or toxin and any identifying information (, strain or other characterization information);
