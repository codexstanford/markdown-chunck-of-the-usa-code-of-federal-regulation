###### Quarantine and Regulations
####### ยง 301.91 Quarantine and regulations; restrictions on interstate movement of regulated articles.

(a)Under the authority of sections 411, 412, 414, and 434 of the Plant Protection Act (7 U.S.C. 7711, 7712, 7714, and 7754),the Secretary of Agriculture establishes a quarantine within the United States to prevent the artificial spread of European larch canker ((Dasycypha)). The regulations in this subpart govern the interstate movement from regulated areas of regulated articles.
