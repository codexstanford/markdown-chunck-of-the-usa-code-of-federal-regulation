
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 330.100 Definitions.
####### Means of conveyance.

Any personal or public property used for or intended for use for the movement of any other property. This specifically includes, but is not limited to, automobiles, trucks, railway cars, aircraft, boats, freight containers, and other means of transportation.
