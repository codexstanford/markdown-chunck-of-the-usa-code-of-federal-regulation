###### ยง 4287.358 Determination of loss and payment.
####### Estimated loss.

(b)In accordance with the requirements of ยง 4287.357(f), the Lender must prepare an estimated loss claim and submit it to the Agency.

(1) Interest accrual eligible for payment under the guarantee on the Defaulted loan will be discontinued when the estimated loss is paid.
