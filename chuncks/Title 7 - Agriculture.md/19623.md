####### ยง 760.1102 Definitions.
######## Non-adult dairy cattle

means a bovine livestock, of a breed used for the purpose of providing milk for human consumption, that weighed 500 pounds or more on the beginning date of the disaster period, but does not meet the definition of an adult dairy cow or bull.
