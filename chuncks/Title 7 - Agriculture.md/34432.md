
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart I - RUS Standard Forms
###### ยง 1726.302 Notice and publication of listed contract forms.
####### Notice.

(a)Upon initially entering into a loan agreement with RUS, borrowers will be provided with all listed contract forms.
