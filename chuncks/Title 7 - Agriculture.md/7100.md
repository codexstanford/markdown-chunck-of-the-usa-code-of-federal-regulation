######## Requirements for Cottage Cheese Bearing USDA Official Identification
######### ยง 58.527 Physical requirements.
########## Flavor.

(a)The cottage cheese shall possess a mild pleasing flavor, similar to fresh whole milk or light cream and may possess the delicate flavor and aroma of a good lactic starter. The product may possess to a slight degree a feed, acid, or salty flavor but shall be free from chalky, bitter, utensil, fruity, yeasty, or other objectionable flavors.
