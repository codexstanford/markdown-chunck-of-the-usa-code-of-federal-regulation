(d)Undermilled milled rice shall be milled rice which is not equal to the milling requirements for "hard milled," "well milled," and "reasonably well milled" rice (see ยง 868.306). Grades U.S. No. 1 and U.S. No. 2 shall contain not more than 2.0 percent, grades U.S. No. 3 and U.S. No. 4 not more than 5.0 percent, grade U.S. No. 5 not more than 10.0 percent, and grade U.S. No. 6 not more than 15.0 percent of well-milled kernels.
