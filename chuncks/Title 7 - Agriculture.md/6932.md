######### ยง 58.240 Cooling dry products.

Prior to packaging and immediately following removal from the drying chamber the dry product shall be cooled to a temperature not exceeding 110 &#xB0;F, however, if the product is to be held in a bulk bin the temperature should be reduced to approximately 90 &#xB0;F but shall be not more than 110 &#xB0;F.
