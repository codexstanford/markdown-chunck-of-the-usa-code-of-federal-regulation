
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter I - Agricultural Marketing Service (Standards, Inspections, Marketing Practices), Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter G - Miscellaneous Marketing Practices Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946
##### PART 170 - USDA FARMERS MARKET
###### ยง 170.12 What are the selection criteria for participation in the USDA Farmers Market?
####### Willingness to Glean.

(c)Participants should commit to supporting the USDA food gleaning/food recovery initiative. This commitment requires farmers and vendors to donate surplus food and food products at the end of each market day to a local nonprofit organization identified by USDA. Questions about tax deductions for gleaning should be referred to the Internal Revenue Service or a tax advisor. Receipts for donated foods may be obtained from the receiving nonprofit organization.
