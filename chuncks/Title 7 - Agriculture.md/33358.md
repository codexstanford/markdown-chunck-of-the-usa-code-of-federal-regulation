
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart A - General Requirements
###### ยง 1709.3 Definitions.
####### High energy cost benchmarks

means the criteria established by RUS for eligibility as an extremely high energy cost community. Extremely high energy cost benchmarks are calculated as 275 percent of the relevant national average household energy benchmarks.
