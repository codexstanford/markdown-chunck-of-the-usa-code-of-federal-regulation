
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture

###### Subpart E - State Agency Provisions
####### ยง 246.12 Food delivery methods.
######## Management.

(1)The State agency is responsible for the fiscal management of, and accountability for, food delivery systems under its jurisdiction. The State agency may permit only authorized vendors, farmers and farmers' markets, home food delivery contractors, and direct distribution sites to accept food instruments and cash-value vouchers.
