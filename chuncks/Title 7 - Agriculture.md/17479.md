
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter IV - Federal Crop Insurance Corporation, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart V - Submission of Policies, Provisions of Policies, Rates of Premium, and Non-Reinsured Supplemental Policies
###### ยง 400.701 Definitions.
####### Viable.

A determination by the Board that the concept proposal, index-based weather plan of insurance, or 508(h) submission is or can be developed into a policy or plan of insurance that can be implemented by the delivery system with actuarially appropriate rates in accordance with Board procedures.
