
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1726 - ELECTRIC SYSTEM CONSTRUCTION POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart C - Substation and Transmission Facilities
###### ยง 1726.76 Substation and transmission line materials and equipment.
####### Contract forms.

(a)(1) The borrower shall use RUS Form 198, Equipment Contract, for purchases of equipment where the total cost of the contract is $1,000,000 or more.

(2) The borrower may, in its discretion, use RUS Form 198, Equipment Contract, or a written purchase order for purchases of equipment of less than $1,000,000 and for all materials.
