
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1720 - GUARANTEES FOR BONDS AND NOTES ISSUED FOR UTILITY INFRASTRUCTURE PURPOSES
##### ยง 1720.3 Definitions.
###### Loan

means any credit instrument that the guaranteed lender extends to a borrower for any utility infrastructure purpose eligible under the RE Act, including loans as set forth in section 4 of the RE Act for electricity transmission lines and distribution systems, loans as set forth in section 201 of the RE Act for telephone lines, facilities, and systems, and loans as set forth in Title VI of the RE Act for broadband systems.
