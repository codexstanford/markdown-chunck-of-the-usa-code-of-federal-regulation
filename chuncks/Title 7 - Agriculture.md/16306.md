###### Rules and Regulations
####### ยง 319.69-5 Types of organic decaying vegetative matter authorized for packing.

The following types of organic decaying vegetative matter are authorized as safe for packing:

(a) Peat;

(b) Peat moss; and

(c) Osmunda fiber.
