
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XV - Foreign Agricultural Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1580 - TRADE ADJUSTMENT ASSISTANCE FOR FARMERS
##### ยง 1580.102 Definitions.
###### Articles like,directly competitive,i.e.,

orgenerally means products falling under the same HTS number used to identify the agricultural commodity in the petition. A "like" product means substantially identical in inherent or intrinsic characteristics, and the term "directly competitive" means articles that are substantially equivalent for commercial purposes (adapted to the same uses and essentially interchangeable therefore). For fishery products, competition could be either from farm-raised or wild-caught products.
