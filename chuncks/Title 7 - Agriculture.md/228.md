
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subpart H - Rules of Practice Governing Formal Adjudicatory Proceedings Instituted by the Secretary Under Various Statutes
##### ยง 1.147 Filing; service; extensions of time; and computation of time.
###### Computation of time.,Provided,

(h)Saturdays, Sundays and Federal holidays shall be included in computing the time allowed for the filing of any document or paper:That, when such time expires on a Saturday, Sunday, or Federal holiday, such period shall be extended to include the next following business day.
