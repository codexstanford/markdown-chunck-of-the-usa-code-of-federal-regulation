
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 9 - PANDEMIC ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS
#### Subpart C - CFAP 2
##### ยง 9.201 Definitions.
###### Producer

means a person or legal entity who shares in the risk of producing a commodity. Except for contract producers, producers who are not in the business of farming at the time of application are not considered eligible producers.
