
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart E - Gypsy Moth
###### ยง 301.45-1 Definitions.
####### Outdoor household articles.

Articles associated with a household that have been kept outside the home such as awnings, barbecue grills, bicycles, boats, dog houses, firewood, garden tools, hauling trailers, outdoor furniture and toys, recreational vehicles and associated equipment, and tents.
