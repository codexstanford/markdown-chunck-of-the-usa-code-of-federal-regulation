
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XXXV - Rural Housing Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 3550 - DIRECT SINGLE FAMILY HOUSING LOANS AND GRANTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 3550.10 Definitions.
####### Program-eligible property.

A property eligible to be financed under this part, as determined by the criteria listed in §§ 3550.56 through 3550.59.
