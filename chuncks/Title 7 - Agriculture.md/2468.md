
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15f - ADJUDICATIONS UNDER SECTION 741
#### Subpart F - How Do I Count Days for Purposes of Deadlines and What Happens if I Miss a Deadline in These Rules?
##### ยง 15f.27 When is something considered "filed" as required by these rules and to whom do I need to give copies of what I file?

A document, or other item, that must be "filed" under these rules is considered filed when postmarked or when it is received and date-stamped by the Docketing Clerk.
