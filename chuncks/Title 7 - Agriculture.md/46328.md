(a)A maximum of 5 points will be awarded for this criterion based on whether the applicant has indicated in the application that the proposed project will have a positive effect on resource conservation (water, soil, forest), public health (potable water, air quality), and the environment (compliance with EPA's renewable fuel standard(s), greenhouse gases, emissions, particulate matter). If the project will have a positive impact on:

(1) Any one of the three impact areas, 1 point will be awarded.

(2) Any two of the three impact areas, 3 points will be awarded.

(3) All three impact areas, 5 points will be awarded.
