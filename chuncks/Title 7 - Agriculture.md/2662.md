
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture

##### Exports for exporter's own account.

(3)(i) Total outstanding balance of exports for exporter's own account that has been shipped from the United States as shown on the previous report by country where located or, if in transit, by country of intermediate destination.

(ii) Quantity of new exports for exporter's own account exported during the week.

(iii) Quantity of previously reported exports for exporter's own account that was applied to outstanding or new export sales during the week.
