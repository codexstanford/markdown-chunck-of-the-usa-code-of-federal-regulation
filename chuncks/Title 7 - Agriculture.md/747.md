
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 1 - ADMINISTRATIVE REGULATIONS
#### Subpart Q - Review and Issuance of Agency Guidance Documents
##### ยง 1.907 Petitions for guidance.
###### OBPA-GuidanceInquiries@usda.gov

Any person may petition a USDA agency to withdraw or modify a particular guidance document. Petitions may be submitted by postal mail to: Guidance Officer, Office of Budget and Program Analysis, USDA, 1400 Independence Avenue SW, Washington, DC 20250-1400. Email petitions may be sent to. The agency shall respond to all requests in a timely manner, but no later than 90 days after receipt of the request.
