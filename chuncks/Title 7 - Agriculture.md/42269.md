###### ยง 3201.117 Intermediates - Personal Care Product Components.
####### Definition.

(a)Intermediates-Personal Care Product Components are ingredients used to formulate finished personal care products. Examples of Intermediates-Personal Care Product Components include surfactants, oils, humectants, emollients, or emulsifiers.
