###### ยง 3560.452 Monetary and non-monetary defaults.
####### Monetary defaults.

(b)A monetary default exists when any amount due to the Agency or a third party (such as real estate taxes and insurance) under a promissory note, loan or grant agreement, workout agreement, or other agreement remains due more than 30 days after the due date.
