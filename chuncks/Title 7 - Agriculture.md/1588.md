
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 8 - 4-H CLUB NAME AND EMBLEM
#### ยง 8.3 Definitions.
##### State Cooperative Extension Service

as used in this part means an organization established at the land-grant college or university under the Smith-Lever Act of May 8, 1914, as amended (7 U.S.C. 341-349); section 209(b) of the Act of October 26, 1974, as amended (D.C. Code, through section 31-1719(b)); or section 1444 of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977, as amended (7 U.S.C. 3221).
