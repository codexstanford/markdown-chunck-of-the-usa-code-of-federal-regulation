
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter G - Miscellaneous Marketing Practices Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946
##### PART 170 - USDA FARMERS MARKET
###### ยง 170.4 Who may participate in the USDA Farmers Market?
####### Nonproduce vendors.

(c)A limited number of non-produce vendors may be selected by market management to sell specialized products that enhance the market atmosphere and historically attract customers to a farmers market. These specialized vendors, such as bakers, may be exempted from the reselling restrictions that apply to the farmers and vendors described in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section.
