
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter I - Agricultural Marketing Service (Standards, Inspections, Marketing Practices), Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter G - Miscellaneous Marketing Practices Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946
##### PART 180 - CATTLE CONTRACTS LIBRARY PILOT PROGRAM
###### ยง 180.2 Definitions.
####### Business day.

The term "business day" means a day on which the packer conducts normal business regarding livestock committed to the packer, or livestock purchased or sold by the packer, and the Department of Agriculture is open to conduct business, typically Monday through Friday and excluding Federal holidays.
