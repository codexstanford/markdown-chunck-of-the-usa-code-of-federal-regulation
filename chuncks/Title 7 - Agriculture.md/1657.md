#### Subpart C - CFAP 2
##### ยง 9.201 Definitions.
###### Price trigger livestock and products

means any of the following livestock and products: Beef cattle, broilers, dairy (cow milk), eggs, lambs, sheep, hogs, and pigs; excluding breeding stock.
