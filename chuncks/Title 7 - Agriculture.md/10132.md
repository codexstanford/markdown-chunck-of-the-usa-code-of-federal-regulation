
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter A - Child Nutrition Programs
##### PART 226 - CHILD AND ADULT CARE FOOD PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 226.2 Definitions.
######## Whole grains

means foods that consist of intact, ground, cracked, or flaked grain seed whose principal anatomical components-the starchy endosperm, germ, and bran-are present in the same relative proportions as they exist in the intact grain seed.
