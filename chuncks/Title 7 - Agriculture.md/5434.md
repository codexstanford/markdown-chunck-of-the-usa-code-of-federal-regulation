###### Subpart X - United States Standards for Shelled English Walnuts (Juglans Regia)
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.2291 Rancidity.
######### Rancidity

means that the portion of kernel is noticeably rancid to the taste. Rancidity should not be confused with a slightly astringent flavor of the pellicle (skin) or with staleness (the state at which the flavor is flat but not objectionable).
