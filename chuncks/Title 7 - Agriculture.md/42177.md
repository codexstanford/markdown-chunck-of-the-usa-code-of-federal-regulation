
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart B - Designated Product Categories and Intermediate Ingredients or Feedstocks
###### ยง 3201.92 Fuel conditioners.
####### Definition.

(a)Products formulated to improve the performance and efficiency of engines by providing benefits such as removing accumulated deposits, increasing lubricity, removing moisture, increasing the cetane number, and/or preventing microbial growths within the fuel system.
