
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1728 - ELECTRIC STANDARDS AND SPECIFICATIONS FOR MATERIALS AND CONSTRUCTION
##### ยง 1728.50 Removal of an item from listing or technical acceptance.
###### Appeal to the Technical Standards Committee "B".

(g)Within ten (10) days of notification of Committee "A's" decision, a sponsor may appeal in writing to Technical Standards Committee "B" to review Committee "A's" decision, specifying the reasons for such a request. Committee "B's" determination, in response to such request, shall be based on the record developed before Committee "A" and such additional information as Committee "B" may request. Formal rules of procedure and evidence shall not apply to proceedings before Committee "B."
