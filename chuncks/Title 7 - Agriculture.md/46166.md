
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 4280 - LOANS AND GRANTS
##### Subpart A - Rural Economic Development Loan and Grant Programs
###### ยง 4280.3 Definitions.
####### Rural area.

This information will be taken from the most recent census data. Any area other than:

(1) A city or town that has a population of greater than 50,000 inhabitants;

(2) The urbanized area contiguous and adjacent to such a city or town; and

(3) Which excludes certain populations pursuant to 7 U.S.C. 1991(a)(13)(H) and (I).
