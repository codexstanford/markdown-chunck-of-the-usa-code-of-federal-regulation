
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 319 - FOREIGN QUARANTINE NOTICES

###### ยง 319.77-4 Conditions for the importation of regulated articles.
####### Stone and quarry products.

(d)Stone and quarry products originating in a Canadian infested area may be imported into the United States only if they are destined for an infested area of the United States and will not be moved through any noninfested areas of the United States, and may be moved through the United States if they are moved only through infested areas.
