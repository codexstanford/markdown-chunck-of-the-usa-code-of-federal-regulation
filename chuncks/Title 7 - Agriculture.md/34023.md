
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1718 - LOAN SECURITY DOCUMENTS FOR ELECTRIC BORROWERS
##### Subpart C - Loan Contracts With Distribution Borrowers
###### ยง 1718.101 Applicability.
####### Other borrowers.

(b)Borrowers other than distribution borrowers may also submit requests for execution of a new loan contract pursuant to this subpart and a new mortgage pursuant to subpart B of this part. RUS may approve such requests if it determines that such approval is in the government's financial interest. If other mortgagees are on the borrower's existing mortgage, their concurrence would be required before a new mortgage could be executed.
