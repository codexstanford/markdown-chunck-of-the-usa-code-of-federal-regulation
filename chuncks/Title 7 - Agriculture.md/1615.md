
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 9 - PANDEMIC ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS
#### Subpart B - CFAP 1
##### ยง 9.101 Definitions.
###### All other cattle

means commercially raised or maintained bovine animals not meeting the definition of another category of cattle in this subpart, excluding beefalo, bison, and animals used for dairy production or intended for dairy production.
