######### ยง 51.1405 Application of tolerances.
########## Provided,

Individual 100-count samples shall have not more than one and one-half times a specified tolerance of 5 percent or more and not more than double a tolerance of less than 5 percent, except that at least one pecan which is seriously damaged by live insects inside the shell is permitted:That the averages for the entire lot are within the tolerances specified for the grade.
