
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart J - Assignment, Conveyance, and Claims

####### Due diligence by lender.

(b)For all loan servicing actions where a market, net recovery or liquidation value determination is required, guaranteed lenders shall perform due diligence in conjunction with the appraisal and submit it to the Agency for review. The Phase I Environmental Site Assessment published by the American Society of Testing and Materials is considered an acceptable format for due diligence.
