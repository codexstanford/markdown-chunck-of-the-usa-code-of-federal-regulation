####### Timing of payments.

(c)The Applicant may request payments not more frequently than once a month by submitting an original, completed, validly signed Standard Form (SF) 271, "Outlay Report and Request for Reimbursement for Construction Programs" including the supporting documentation identified in ยง 4288.23, to reimburse the applicant for the Agency's pro rata share of funds expended on eligible project costs.
