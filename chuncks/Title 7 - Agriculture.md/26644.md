
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter X - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Milk), Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1000 - GENERAL PROVISIONS OF FEDERAL MILK MARKETING ORDERS
##### Subpart E - Rules of Practice and Procedure Governing Handlers
###### ยง 1000.27 Handler responsibility for records and facilities.
####### Availability of records and facilities.

(b)Each handler shall make available all records pertaining to such handler's operations and all facilities the market administrator finds are necessary to verify the information required to be reported by the order and/or to ascertain such handler's reporting, monetary, or other obligation under the order. Each handler shall permit the market administrator to weigh, sample, and test milk and milk products and observe plant operations and equipment and make available to the market administrator such facilities as are necessary to carry out his/her duties.
