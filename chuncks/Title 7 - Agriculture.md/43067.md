
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XXXIV - National Institute of Food and Agriculture
#### PART 3407 - IMPLEMENTATION OF NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY ACT
##### ยง 3407.10 Preparation of environmental assessments.
###### Decision notice.

(c)Upon completion of an EA and any supplement thereto, the responsible NIFA official will evaluate the information it contains, determine whether an EIS is required or whether no significant environmental impact is likely to occur, and will document the decision and the reasons upon which it is based (40 CFR 1508.13). The EA shall be available to the public.
