####### ยง 1924.4 Definitions.
######## Mechanic's and materialmen's liens.

(l)A lien on real property in favor of persons supplying labor and/or materials for the construction for the value of labor and/or materials supplied by them. In some jurisdictions, a mechanic's lien also exists for the value of professional services.
