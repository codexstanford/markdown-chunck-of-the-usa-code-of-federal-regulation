##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 1710.2 Definitions and rules of construction.
####### Integrated Resources Plan (IRP)

means a plan resulting from the planning and selection process for new energy resources that evaluates the benefits and costs of the full range of alternatives, including new generating capacity, power purchases, DSM programs, system operating efficiency, and renewable energy systems.
