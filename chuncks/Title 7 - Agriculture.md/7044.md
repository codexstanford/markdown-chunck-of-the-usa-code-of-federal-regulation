
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter C - Requirements and Standards Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 and the Egg Products Inspection Act
##### PART 58 - GRADING AND INSPECTION, GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS FOR APPROVED PLANTS AND STANDARDS FOR GRADES OF DAIRY PRODUCTS
###### Subpart A - Provisions Governing the Inspection and Grading Services of Manufactured or Processed Dairy Products
####### Miscellaneous
######## Operations and Operating Procedures
######### ยง 58.439 Cheese from unpasteurized milk.

If the cheese is labeled as "heat treated", "unpasteurized," "raw milk", or "for manufacturing" the milk may be raw or heated at temperatures below pasteurization. Cheese made from unpasteurized milk shall be cured for a period of 60 days at a temperature not less than 35 &#xB0;F. If the milk is held more than 2 hours between time of receipt or heat treatment and setting, it shall be cooled to 45 &#xB0;F. or lower until time of setting.
