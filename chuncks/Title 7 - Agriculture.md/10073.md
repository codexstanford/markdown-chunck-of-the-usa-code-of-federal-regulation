
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### PART 226 - CHILD AND ADULT CARE FOOD PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 226.2 Definitions.
######## Independent governing board of directors

means, in the case of a nonprofit organization, or in the case of a for-profit institution required to have a board of directors, a governing board which meets regularly and has the authority to hire and fire the institution's executive director.
