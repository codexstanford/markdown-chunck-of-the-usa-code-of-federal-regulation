###### Subpart GG - United States Standards for Grades of Walnuts in the Shell

"U.S. No. 1" consists of walnuts in shells which are dry, practically clean, bright and free from splits, injury by discoloration, and free from damage caused by broken shells, perforated shells, adhering hulls or other means. The kernels are well dried, free from decay, dark discoloration, rancidity, and free from damage caused by mold, shriveling, insects or other means. (See ยง 51.2954.)
