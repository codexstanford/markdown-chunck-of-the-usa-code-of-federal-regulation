
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XXXIV - National Institute of Food and Agriculture

##### ยง 3419.1 Definitions.
###### Non-Federal sources

means funds made available by the State to the eligible institution either through direct appropriation or under any authority (other than authority to charge tuition and fees paid by students) provided by a State to an eligible institution to raise revenue, such as gift acceptance authority or user fees.
