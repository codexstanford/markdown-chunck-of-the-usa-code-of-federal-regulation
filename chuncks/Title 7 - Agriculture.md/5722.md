######## ยง 52.2 Terms defined.
######### Certificate of sampling.

"Certificate of sampling" means a statement, either written or printed issued pursuant to the regulations in this part, identifying officially drawn samples and may include a description of condition of containers and the condition under which the processed product is stored.
