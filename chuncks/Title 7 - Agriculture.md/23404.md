####### ยง 915.54 Inspection and certification.
######## Provided,

Whenever the handling of any variety of avocados is regulated pursuant to ยง 915.51, each handler who handles avocados shall, prior thereto, cause each lot of avocados handled to be inspected by the Federal-State Inspection Service and certified by it as meeting the applicable requirements of such regulation:That such inspection and certification shall not be required whenever the avocados previously have been so inspected and certified.
