##### PART 273 - CERTIFICATION OF ELIGIBLE HOUSEHOLDS

####### ยง 273.2 Office operations and application processing.
######## Handwritten signatures.

(vi)These provisions apply specifically to handwritten signatures, including handwritten signatures that the household transmits by facsimile or other electronic transmission.

(A) If the signatory cannot sign with a name, an X is a valid signature.

(B) The State agency may require a witness to attest to an X signature.

(C) An employee of the State agency may serve as a witness.
