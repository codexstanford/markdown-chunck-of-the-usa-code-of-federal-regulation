
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15d - NONDISCRIMINATION IN PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES CONDUCTED BY THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE
#### ยง 15d.2 Definitions.
##### Compliance report

means a written review of an agency's compliance with civil rights requirements, to be prepared by OASCR and to identify each finding of non-compliance or other civil rights related issue. The review is conducted at the discretion of OASCR or if there has been a formal finding of non-compliance.
