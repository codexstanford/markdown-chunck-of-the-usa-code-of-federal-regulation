##### ยง 1755.870 RUS specification for terminating cables.
###### Acceptance testing and extent of testing.

(m)(1) The tests described in appendix A of this section are intended for acceptance of cable designs and major modifications of accepted designs. RUS decides what constitutes a major modification. These tests are intended to show the inherent capability of the manufacturer to produce cable products having long life and stability.

(i) An original signature certification that the product fully complies with each section of the specification;
