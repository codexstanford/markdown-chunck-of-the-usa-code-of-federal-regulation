######### Sampling marks.

(e)Processed products which have been sampled for inspection as provided in this part may, at the option of the Department, be identified by an authorized representative of the Department. The products are identified by stamping the container(s) comprising such lot(s), with an official "sampling mark", similar in form and design to the example in figure 15 of this section. The "sampling marks" will identify products officially sampled by a particular field office. Such mark will include a code identifying the field office performing the sampling.
