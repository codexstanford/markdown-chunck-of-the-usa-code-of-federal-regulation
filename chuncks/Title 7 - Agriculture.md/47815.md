
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 4290 - RURAL BUSINESS INVESTMENT COMPANY ("RBIC") PROGRAM
##### Subpart I - Financing of Enterprises by RBICs
###### Determining Eligibility of an Enterprise for RBIC Financing
####### ยง 4290.720 Enterprises that may be ineligible for Financing.
######## Financing RBICs, SBICs, or New Markets Capital Companies (NMVC Companies).

(h)(1) You are not permitted to provide funds, directly or indirectly, that will be used:

(i) To purchase stock in or otherwise provide capital to a RBIC, SBIC or NMVC Company; or

(ii) To repay an indebtedness incurred for the purpose of investing in a RBIC, SBIC, or NMVC Company.

(2) "NMVC Company" is defined in 13 CFR 108.50.
