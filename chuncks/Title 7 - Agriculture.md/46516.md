
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 4280 - LOANS AND GRANTS
##### Subpart B - Rural Energy for America Program General
###### ยง 4280.103 Definitions.
####### Matching funds.

Those project funds required by 7 U.S.C. 8107 to be made available by the applicant in order to be eligible to receive the grant, or combined grant and guaranteed loan. Funds provided by the applicant in excess of matching funds are not matching funds. Unless authorized by statute, other Federal grant funds cannot be used to meet a matching funds requirement.
