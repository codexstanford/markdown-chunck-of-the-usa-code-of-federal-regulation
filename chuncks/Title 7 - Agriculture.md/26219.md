####### Contact information.

(1)(i) For an applicant who is an individual, the application shall include full name of the individual, Employee Identification Number ("EIN") of the business entity, business address, telephone number, and email address (if available).

(ii) For an applicant that is an entity, the application shall include full name of the entity, the principal business location address, and the full name, title, and email address (if available) of each key participant of the entity.
