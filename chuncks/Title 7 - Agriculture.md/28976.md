
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XI - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Miscellaneous Commodities), Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart A - Pecan Promotion, Research, and Information Order
###### Definitions
####### ยง 1223.4 Conflict of interest.
######## Conflict of interest

means a situation in which a member or employee of the Board has a direct or indirect financial interest in a person who performs a service for, or enters into a contract with, the Board for anything of economic value.
