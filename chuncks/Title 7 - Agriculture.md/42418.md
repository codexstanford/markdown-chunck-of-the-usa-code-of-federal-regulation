(a)Manufacturers and vendors seeking USDA approval to use the certification mark for an eligible biobased product must submit a USDA-approved application for each biobased product. A standardized application form and instructions are available on the USDA BioPreferred Program Web site (). The contents of an acceptable application are as specified in paragraphs (a)(1) through (a)(4) of this section.
