
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart D - Intermediary Relending Program (IRP)
###### ยง 4274.310 Eligibility requirements - intermediaries.
####### Legal authority.

(b)The intermediary must have the legal authority necessary for carrying out the proposed loan purposes and for obtaining, giving security for, and repaying the proposed loan. If the intermediary is an affiliate of another entity, the intermediary's governing board must be independent of the affiliated entity.
