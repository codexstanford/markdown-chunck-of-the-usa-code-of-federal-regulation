
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter A - Child Nutrition Programs
##### PART 247 - COMMODITY SUPPLEMENTAL FOOD PROGRAM
###### ยง 247.10 Distribution and use of CSFP commodities.
####### What restrictions apply to State and local agencies in the distribution of CSFP commodities?

(c)State and local agencies must not require, or request, that participants make any payments, or provide any materials or services, in connection with the receipt of CSFP commodities. State and local agencies must not use the distribution of CSFP commodities as a means of furthering the political interests of any person or party.
