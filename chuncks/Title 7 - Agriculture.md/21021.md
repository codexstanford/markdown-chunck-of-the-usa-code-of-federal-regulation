
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter D - Special Programs

####### Security requirements.

(c)The Agency will take a lien on the following security, if available, as necessary to adequately secure the loan:

(1) Real estate;

(2) Chattels;

(3) Crops;

(4) Other assets owned by the applicant; and

(5) Assets owned and pledged by a third party.
