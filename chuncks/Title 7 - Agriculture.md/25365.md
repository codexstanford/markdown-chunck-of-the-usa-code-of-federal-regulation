
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter IX - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Fruits, Vegetables, Nuts), Department of Agriculture
#### PART 983 - PISTACHIOS GROWN IN CALIFORNIA, ARIZONA, AND NEW MEXICO
##### Subpart B - Administrative Requirements
###### ยง 983.150 Aflatoxin regulations.
####### Maximum level.

(a)No handler shall ship for domestic human consumption, pistachios that exceed an aflatoxin level of 15 ppb. All shipments must also be covered by an aflatoxin inspection certificate. Pistachios that fail to meet the aflatoxin requirements shall be disposed in such manner as described in Failed lots/rework procedure of this part.
