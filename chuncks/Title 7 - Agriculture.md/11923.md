
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture

##### PART 247 - COMMODITY SUPPLEMENTAL FOOD PROGRAM
###### ยง 247.31 Audits and investigations.
####### When is a State or local agency audit required?

(d)State and local agency audits must be conducted in accordance with part 3052 of this title, which contains the Department's regulations pertaining to audits of States, local governments, and nonprofit organizations. The value of CSFP commodities distributed by the agency or organization must be considered part of the Federal award.
