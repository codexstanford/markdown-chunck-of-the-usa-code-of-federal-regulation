###### Subpart B - Procedures for Stationary Lot Sampling and Inspection
####### ยง 42.105 Basis for selection of sample.
######## Proportional random sampling.

(1)When the number of codes or other identifying marks within the lot and the approximate number of cases or containers per code are known, select sample units at random within each mark and in a number proportionate to the number of containers represented by such mark.
