
# Title 7 - Agriculture

###### ยง 3201.7 Determining biobased content.
####### Biobased products, intermediate ingredients or feedstocks.

(1)Biobased content will be based on the amount of biobased carbon in the product or material as a percent of the weight (mass) of the total organic carbon in the product or material.
