##### Subpart G - Agriculture and Food Research Initiative
###### ยง 3430.302 Definitions.
####### Minority

means Alaskan Native, American Indian, AsianAmerican, African-American, Hispanic American, Native Hawaiian, or Pacific Islander. The Secretary will determine on a case-by-case basis whether additional groups qualify under this definition, either at the Secretary's initiative, or in response to a written request with supporting explanation.
