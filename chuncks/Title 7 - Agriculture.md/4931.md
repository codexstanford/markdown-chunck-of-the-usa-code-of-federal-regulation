###### Subpart G - United States Standards for Grades of Table Grapes (European or Vinifera Type)
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.899 Waterberry.
######### Waterberry

means a watery, soft, or flabby condition of the berry. Affected berries are low in sugar content, have tender skins, and are easily crushed. This is an advanced or more pronounced stage of the condition referred to as "weak".
