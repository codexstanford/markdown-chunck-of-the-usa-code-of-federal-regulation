###### Subpart B - United States Standards for Grades of Canned Red Tart Pitted Cherries
####### Factors of Quality
######## ยง 52.778 Color.
######### A,classification.

(a) ()Canned red tart pitted cherries that have a good color may be given a score of 18 to 20 points. "Good color" means a practically uniform color that is bright and typical of canned red tart pitted cherries which have been prepared and processed from properly ripened cherries.
