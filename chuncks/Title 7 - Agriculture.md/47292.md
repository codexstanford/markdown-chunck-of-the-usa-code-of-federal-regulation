#### PART 4287 - SERVICING

###### ยง 4287.157 Liquidation.
####### Abandonment of collateral.

(i)When the lender adequately documents that the cost of liquidation would exceed the potential recovery value of certain collateral and receives Agency concurrence, the lender may abandon that collateral. When the lender makes a recommendation for abandonment of collateral, it must comply with 7 CFR part 1970, "Environmental Policies and Procedures."
