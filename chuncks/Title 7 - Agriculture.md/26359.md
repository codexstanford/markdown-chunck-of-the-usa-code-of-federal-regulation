
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter IX - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Fruits, Vegetables, Nuts), Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart B - Administrative Requirements
###### Reserve Control
####### ยง 993.157 Holding and delivery of reserve prunes.
######## Sales and deliveries.

(a)Committee sales and deliveries of reserve prunes from the holdings of any handler shall not exceed the quantity of reserve prunes required to be held by him. The reserve prune holding requirement of the handler shall be reduced by the tonnage so sold or delivered.
