
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15a - EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING OR BENEFITTING FROM FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart E - Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Employment in Education Programs or Activities Prohibited
##### § 15a.510 Recruitment.
###### Recruitment patterns.

(b)A recipient shall not recruit primarily or exclusively at entities that furnish as applicants only or predominantly members of one sex if such actions have the effect of discriminating on the basis of sex in violation of §§ 15a.500 through 15a.550.
