
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### PART 225 - SUMMER FOOD SERVICE PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 225.2 Definitions.
######## Rural

means (a) any area in a county which is not a part of a Metropolitan Statistical Area or (b) any "pocket" within a Metropolitan Statistical Area which, at the option of the State agency and with FNSRO concurrence, is determined to be geographically isolated from urban areas.
