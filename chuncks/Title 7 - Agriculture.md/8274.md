#### Subchapter E - Commodity Laboratory Testing Programs
##### PART 98 - MEALS, READY-TO-EAT (MREs), MEATS, AND MEAT PRODUCTS
###### Subpart B - USDA Certification of Laboratories for the Testing of Trichinae in Horsemeat
####### ยง 98.100 General.
######## Trichinella

A laboratory that has met the requirements for certification specified in this subpart shall receive an AMS Science and Technology certificate to approve its analysis forspiralis in horsemeat. Certification would be granted to a qualified analyst or a laboratory based on having the proper training, facilities, and equipment. This AMS laboratory certification program will enable horsemeat exporters to comply with trichinae testing requirements of the European Community.
