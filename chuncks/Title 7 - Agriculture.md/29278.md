
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### PART 1250 - EGG RESEARCH AND PROMOTION
##### Subpart - Rules and Regulations

####### ยง 1250.500 Terms defined.
######## Nest run eggs.

(bb)"Nest run eggs" means eggs which are packed as they come from the production facilities without having been sized and/or candled with the exception that some checks, dirties, or obvious undergrades may have been removed and provided further that the eggs may have been washed.
