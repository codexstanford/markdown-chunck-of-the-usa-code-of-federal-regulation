(c) The Secretary shall terminate the provisions of this subpart at the end of any fiscal period whenever he finds that such termination is favored by a majority of producers, who during a representative period, as determined by the Secretary have been engaged in the production of potatoes for market:That such majority has, during such representative period, produced for market more than fifty percent of the volume of such potatoes produced for market.
