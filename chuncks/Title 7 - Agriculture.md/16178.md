
# Title 7 - Agriculture

####### Sampling for plant pests at port of first arrival.

(d)Any imported regulated article may be sampled for plant pests at the port of first arrival. If an inspector finds it necessary to order treatment of a regulated article at the port of first arrival, any sampling will be done prior to treatment.
