
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart E - Buildings

####### Procurement procedures.

(b)A borrower may use Multiparty Lump Sum Quotations to award contracts in amounts of up to a cumulative total of $1,500,000 or three percent of NUP (not to exceed $10,000,000), whichever is greater, per calendar year of headquarters construction (including minor modifications or improvements).
