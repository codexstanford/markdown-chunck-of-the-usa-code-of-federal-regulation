### PART 1 - ADMINISTRATIVE REGULATIONS
#### Subpart O - Conditions in FERC Hydropower Licenses
##### Prehearing Conferences and Discovery
###### ยง 1.641 How may parties obtain discovery of information needed for the case?
####### Objections.

(e)(1) A party must file any objections to a discovery motion or to specific portions of a proposed discovery request within 7 days after service of the motion.

(2) An objection must explain how, in the objecting party's view, the discovery sought does not meet the criteria in paragraphs (b)(1) through (6) of this section.
