
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter C - Requirements and Standards Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 and the Egg Products Inspection Act
##### PART 52 - PROCESSED FRUITS AND VEGETABLES, PROCESSED PRODUCTS THEREOF, AND CERTAIN OTHER PROCESSED FOOD PRODUCTS
###### Subpart C - United States Standards for Grades of Frozen Red Tart Pitted Cherries
####### Sample Unit Size
######## ยง 52.803 Sample unit size.

Compliance with requirements for size and the various quality factors is based on the following sample unit sizes for the applicable factor:

(a) Pits, character, and harmless extraneous material-20 ounces of drained cherries.

(b) Size, color, and defects (other than harmless extraneous material)-100 cherries.
