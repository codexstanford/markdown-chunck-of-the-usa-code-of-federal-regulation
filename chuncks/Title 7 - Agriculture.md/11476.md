
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture

###### Subpart E - State Agency Provisions
####### ยง 246.12 Food delivery methods.
######## Sale of store to circumvent WIC sanction.

(6)The State agency may not authorize a vendor applicant if the State agency determines the store has been sold by its previous owner in an attempt to circumvent a WIC sanction. The State agency may consider such factors as whether the store was sold to a relative by blood or marriage of the previous owner(s) or sold to any individual or organization for less than its fair market value.
