
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart B - Definition of Terms Used in Part 4290
###### ยง 4290.50 Definition of terms.
####### Debt Securities

means instruments evidencing a loan with an option or any other right to acquire Equity Securities in an Enterprise or its Affiliates, or a loan which by its terms is convertible into an equity position. Consideration must be paid for all options acquired.
