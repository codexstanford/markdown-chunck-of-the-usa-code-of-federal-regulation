
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 318 - STATE OF HAWAII AND TERRITORIES QUARANTINE NOTICES
##### Subpart A - Regulated Articles From Hawaii and the Territories
###### ยง 318.13-9 Inspection and disinfection of means of conveyance.
####### Inspection of ships upon arrival.

(c)Any person who has moved a ship or other oceangoing craft from Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, or the U.S. Virgin Islands to any other State shall contact an inspector and offer the inspector the opportunity to inspect the ship or other oceangoing craft upon its arrival.
