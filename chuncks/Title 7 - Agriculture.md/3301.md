######## ยง 29.80 Announcing grades.
######### Provided,

The grade of each lot of tobacco as certified by an official inspector on a designated market shall be clearly announced by the warehouseman or his representative at the time the lot is offered in the auction:That the Director may waive the requirement of announcing grades in the auction if he finds it impractical for the warehouseman to render this service.
