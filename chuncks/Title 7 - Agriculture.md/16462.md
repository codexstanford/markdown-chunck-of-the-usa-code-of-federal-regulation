
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 322 - BEES, BEEKEEPING BYPRODUCTS, AND BEEKEEPING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart C - Importation of Restricted Organisms
###### ยง 322.15 APHIS review of permit applications; denial or revocation of permits.
####### Review of permit applications to import restricted organisms,Consultation.

(a)-(1)During our review of your permit application, we may consult with any Federal officials; appropriate officials of any State, Territory, or other jurisdiction in the United States in charge of research or regulatory programs relative to bees; and any other qualified governmental or private research laboratory, institution, or individual. We will conduct these consultations to gain information on the risks associated with the importation of the restricted organisms.
