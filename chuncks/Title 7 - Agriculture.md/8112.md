
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter E - Commodity Laboratory Testing Programs
##### PART 93 - PROCESSED FRUITS AND VEGETABLES
###### Subpart B - Peanuts, Tree Nuts, Corn and Other Oilseeds
####### ยง 93.11 Definitions.
######## Peanut Administrative Committee (PAC).

The committee established under the United States Department of Agriculture Marketing Agreement for Peanuts, 7 CFR part 998, which administers the terms and provisions of this Agreement, including the aflatoxin control program for domestically produced raw peanuts, for peanut shellers. The Peanut Administrative Committee (PAC) headquarters are at 2537 Lafayette Plaza Drive Suite A; Albany, Georgia 31707.
