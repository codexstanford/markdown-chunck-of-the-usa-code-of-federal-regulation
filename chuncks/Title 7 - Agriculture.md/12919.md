###### ยง 271.6 Complaint procedure.
####### Minimum requirements.

(2)The State agency shall follow up on complaints, resolve complaints and take corrective action where warranted, and respond to the complainant on the State agency's disposition of the complaint. The State agency may make the information available through written materials or posters at certification offices or other appropriate means.
