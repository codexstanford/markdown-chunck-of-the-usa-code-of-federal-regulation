
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1789 - USE OF CONSULTANTS FUNDED BY BORROWERS
##### Subpart A - Policy and Procedures With Respect to Consultant Services Funded by Borrowers - General
###### ยง 1789.150 Purpose.
####### et seq.

This part sets forth policies and the procedures for implementing subsection (c) of section 18 of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936, as amended (7 U.S.C. 901)(RE Act) which authorizes the Rural Utilities Service (RUS) to use the services of Consultants funded by the Borrowers to facilitate timely action on Applications by Borrowers for financial assistance and other approvals.
