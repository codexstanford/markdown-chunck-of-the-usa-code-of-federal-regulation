(b)-(1)The applicant will be required to execute Form FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 400-1, "Equal Opportunity Agreement," at the time the loan is closed or before construction is started, whichever occurs first. Municipalities or other public bodies will have to incorporate references to this form in the loan resolution before it is adopted. If the applicant wants to publish for bids, the applicant must obtain Form FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 1924-5, "Invitation for Bid (Construction Contract)" which is in compliance with Executive Order 11246, from the local FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 County Supervisor.
