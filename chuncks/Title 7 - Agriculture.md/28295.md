##### Subpart A - Popcorn Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Order
###### Popcorn Board
####### ยง 1215.21 Establishment and membership.
######## Provided,

(a) There is hereby established a Popcorn Board of five members. The number of members on the board may be changed by rulemaking:that the Board consist of not fewer than four members and not more than nine members. The Board shall be composed of popcorn processors appointed by the Secretary under ยง 1215.24.

(b) For purposes of nominating and appointing processors to the Board, the Secretary shall, to the extent practicable, take into account the geographic distribution of popcorn production.

(c) No more than one officer or employee of a processor may serve as a Board member at the same time.
