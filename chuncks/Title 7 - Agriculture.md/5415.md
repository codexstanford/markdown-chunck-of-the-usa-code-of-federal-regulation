###### Subpart W - United States Standards for Grades of Shelled Almonds
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.2131 Fairly uniform in size.
######### Fairly uniform in size

means that, in a representative sample, the weight of 10 percent, by count, of the largest whole kernels shall not exceed 1.70 times the weight of 10 percent, by count, of the smallest whole kernels.
