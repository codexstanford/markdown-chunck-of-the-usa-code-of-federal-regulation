###### Subpart C - Fire and Rescue and Other Small Community Facilities Projects
####### ยง 1942.126 Planning, bidding, contracting, constructing, procuring.
######## Standard construction contract documents.

(1)When standard construction contract documents available from RD are used, or when the amount of the contract does not exceed $100,000, it will normally not be necessary for the Regional Attorney to perform a detailed legal review. If construction contract documents used are not in the format of guide forms approved by RD, and the contract amount exceeds $100,000, the Regonal Attorney must review the documents before their use.
