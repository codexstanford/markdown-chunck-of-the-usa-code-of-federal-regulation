
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15a - EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING OR BENEFITTING FROM FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart B - Coverage
##### § 15a.220 Admissions.
###### Educational institutions.

(d)Except as provided in paragraph (e) of this section as to recipients that are educational institutions, §§ 15a.300 through 15a.310 apply only to institutions of vocational education, professional education, graduate higher education, and public institutions of undergraduate higher education.
