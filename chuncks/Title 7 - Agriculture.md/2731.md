## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 25.3 Definitions.
###### Funding official

means the state director in the state where the designated rural area is located, or if the designated rural area is located in more than one state, the state where the headquarters office of the lead managing entity is located.
