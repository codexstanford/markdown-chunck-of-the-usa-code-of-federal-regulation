######### ยง 58.641 Hardening and storage.

Immediately after the semifrozen product is placed in its intended container it shall be placed in a hardening tunnel or hardening room to continue the freezing process. Rapid freezing to 0&#xB0; to -15 &#xB0;F is desirable to produce a good textured product.
