
# Title 7 - Agriculture

###### Definitions
####### ยง 201.2 Terms defined.
######## Brand.

(oo)The term "brand" means a name, term, sign, symbol, or design, or a combination of them that identifies the seed of one seller or group of sellers and differentiates that seed from the seed of other sellers.
