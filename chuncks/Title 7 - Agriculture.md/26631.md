
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter X - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Milk), Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1000 - GENERAL PROVISIONS OF FEDERAL MILK MARKETING ORDERS
##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 1000.16 Fluid cream product.
####### Fluid cream product

means cream (other than plastic cream or frozen cream), including sterilized cream, or a mixture of cream and milk or skim milk containing 9 percent or more butterfat, with or without the addition of other ingredients.
