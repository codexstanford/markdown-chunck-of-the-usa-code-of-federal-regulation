
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart G - Managing the Operations of a RBIC
###### Secured Borrowing by RBICs
####### ยง 4290.550 Prior approval of secured third-party debt of RBICs.
######## Conditions for approval.

(c)As a condition of granting its approval under this section, the Agency may impose such restrictions or limitations as it deems appropriate, taking into account your historical performance, current financial position, proposed terms of the secured debt and amount of aggregate debt you will have outstanding (including Leverage). The Agency will not favorably consider any requests for approval which include a blanket lien on all your assets, or a security interest in your investor commitments in excess of 125 percent of the proposed borrowing.
