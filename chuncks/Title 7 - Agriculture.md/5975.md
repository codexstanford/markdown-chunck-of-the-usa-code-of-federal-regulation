(b) ()Whole or pitted dates that are practically uniform in size may be given a score of 9 or 10 points. "Practically uniform in size" means that not more than a total of 10 percent, by weight, of the whole or pitted dates may be conspicuously larger or smaller than the approximate average size of the dates in the container.
