
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter VII - Farm Service Agency, Department of Agriculture

###### Subpart N - Dairy Economic Loss Assistance Payment Program
####### ยง 760.1302 Definitions and acronyms.
######## Fiscal year or FY

means the year beginning October 1 and ending the following September 30. The fiscal year will be designated for this subpart by year reference to the calendar year in which it ends. For example, FY 2009 is from October 1, 2008, through September 30, 2009 (inclusive).
