
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### PART 205 - NATIONAL ORGANIC PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - Definitions
####### ยง 205.2 Terms defined.
######## Organic exporter.

The final certified exporter of the organic agricultural product, who facilitates the trade of, consigns, or arranges for the transport/shipping of the organic agricultural product from a foreign country to the United States.
