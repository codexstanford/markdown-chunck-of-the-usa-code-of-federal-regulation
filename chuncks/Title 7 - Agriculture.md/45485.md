##### Subpart B - Community Facilities Grant Program
###### ยง 3570.53 Definitions.
####### State.

The term "State" means each of the 50 States, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Guam, the Virgin Islands of the United States, American Samoa, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, the Marshall Islands, the Republic of Palau, and the Federated States of Micronesia.
