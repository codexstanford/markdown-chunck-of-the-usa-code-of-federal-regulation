##### Subpart R - Lien Accommodations and Subordinations for 100 Percent Private Financing
###### ยง 1717.851 Definitions.
####### Capital investment.

For the purposes of ยง 1717.860, capital investment means an original investment in an asset that is intended for long-term continued use or possession and, for accounting purposes, is normally depreciated or depleted as it is used. For example, such assets may include land, facilities, equipment, buildings, mineral deposits, patents, trademarks, and franchises. Original investments do not include refinancings or refundings.
