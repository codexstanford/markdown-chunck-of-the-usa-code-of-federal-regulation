
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart D - Mergers and Consolidations of Electric Borrowers
###### ยง 1717.151 Definitions.
####### Active distribution borrower

means an electric distribution borrower that has, on the effective date, an outstanding insured or guaranteed loan from RUS for rural electrification, and whose eligibility for future RUS financing is not restricted pursuant to 7 CFR part 1786.
