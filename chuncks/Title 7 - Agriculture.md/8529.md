
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter I - Agricultural Marketing Service (Standards, Inspections, Marketing Practices), Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter G - Miscellaneous Marketing Practices Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946
##### PART 180 - CATTLE CONTRACTS LIBRARY PILOT PROGRAM
###### ยง 180.2 Definitions.
####### Packer.

The term "packer" means a packer that has slaughtered during the immediately preceding 5 calendar years an average of not less than 5 percent of the number of fed cattle slaughtered nationally during the immediately preceding 5 calendar years.
