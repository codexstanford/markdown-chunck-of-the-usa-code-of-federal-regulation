##### PART 52 - PROCESSED FRUITS AND VEGETABLES, PROCESSED PRODUCTS THEREOF, AND CERTAIN OTHER PROCESSED FOOD PRODUCTS
###### Subpart B - United States Standards for Grades of Canned Red Tart Pitted Cherries
####### Factors of Quality
######## ยง 52.778 Color.
######### B,classification.

(b) ()Canned red tart pitted cherries that have a reasonably good color may be given a score of 16 or 17 points. "Reasonably good color" means a reasonably uniform color, typical of canned red tart pitted cherries which have been properly prepared and processed and which color may range from a slight yellowish-red color to a slightly mottled reddish brown.
