
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XIV - Commodity Credit Corporation, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter A - General Regulations and Policies
##### PART 1400 - PAYMENT LIMITATION AND PAYMENT ELIGIBILITY
###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 1400.3 Definitions.
######## Indian tribe

means any Indian tribe, band, nation, pueblo, or other organized group or community, including any Alaska Native village or regional or village corporation as defined in or established pursuant to the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (43 U.S.C. 1601-1629h), which is recognized as eligible for special programs and services provided by the United States to Indians because of their status as Indians.
