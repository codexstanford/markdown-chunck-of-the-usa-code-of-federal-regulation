### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture

####### ยง 4290.800 Financings in the form of Equity Securities.

You may purchase the Equity Securities of an Enterprise. You may not, inadvertently or otherwise:

(a) Become a general partner in any unincorporated business; or
