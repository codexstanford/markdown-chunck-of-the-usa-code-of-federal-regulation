#### Subpart O - Conditions in FERC Hydropower Licenses

###### ยง 1.602 What terms are used in this subpart?
####### NEPA document,et seq., and the CEQ Regulations Implementing the Procedural Requirements of NEPA (40 CFR parts 21500-1508).

means an environmental document as defined at 40 CFR 1508.10 to include an environmental assessment, environmental impact statement (EIS), finding of no significant impact, and notice of intent to prepare an EIS.
