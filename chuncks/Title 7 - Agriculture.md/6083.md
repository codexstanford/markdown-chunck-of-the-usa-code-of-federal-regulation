##### PART 52 - PROCESSED FRUITS AND VEGETABLES, PROCESSED PRODUCTS THEREOF, AND CERTAIN OTHER PROCESSED FOOD PRODUCTS
###### Subpart F - United States Standards for Grades of Dried Prunes
####### Moisture, Uniformity of Size, Defects
######## ยง 52.3187 Definitions and explanations of defects.
######### Scars.

(f)"Scars" mean:

(1) Tough or thick scab which, singly or in combination on a prune, exceeds the area of a circleinch in diameter such as may result from thrip injury, mildew, leaf chafing, limb rubs, or other means; or

(2) Scab which is not tough or thick and which, singly or in combination on a prune, exceeds the area of a circleinch in diameter and which is inconsequential but unsightly though practically blending in color with the skin on the portion of the prune not affected.
