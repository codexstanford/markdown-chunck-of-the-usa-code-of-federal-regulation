###### Provisions Applicable to Reparation Proceedings
####### ยง 47.11 Examiners.
######## Who may act in absence of examiner.

(d)In case of the absence, illness, resignation, or death of the examiner who has been assigned to a proceeding, or, in case the General Counsel determines that, for other good cause, such examiner should not act, the powers and duties to be performed by the examiner under these rules of practice in connection with such proceeding may, subject to the provisions of paragraph (a) of this section, be assigned to another examiner.
