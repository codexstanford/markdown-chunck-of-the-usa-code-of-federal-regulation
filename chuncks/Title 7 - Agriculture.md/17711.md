
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter IV - Federal Crop Insurance Corporation, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 460 - ADDITIONAL DISASTER PAYMENTS
##### Subpart B - Pandemic Cover Crop Program
###### ยง 460.9 Definitions.
####### Qualifying cover crop

means any of the four types of cover crops:

(1) Cereals and other grasses;

(2) Legumes;

(3) Brassicas; and

(4) Other non-legume broadleaves, and mixtures of two or more cover crop species planted at the same time. An insured crop is not considered a qualifying cover crop.
