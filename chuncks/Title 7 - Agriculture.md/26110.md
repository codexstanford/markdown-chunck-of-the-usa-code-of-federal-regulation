
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 990.1 Meaning of terms.
####### Hemp.,Cannabis sativa

The plant speciesL. and any part of that plant, including the seeds thereof and all derivatives, extracts, cannabinoids, isomers, acids, salts, and salts of isomers, whether growing or not, with a total delta-9 tetrahydrocannabinol concentration of not more than 0.3 percent on a dry weight basis.
