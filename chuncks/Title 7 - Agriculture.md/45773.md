
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### PART 4279 - GUARANTEED LOANMAKING
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 4279.2 Definitions and abbreviations.
####### In-house expenses.

Expenses associated with activities that are routinely the responsibility of a lender's internal staff or its agents. In-house expenses include, but are not limited to, employees' salaries, staff lawyers, travel, and overhead.
