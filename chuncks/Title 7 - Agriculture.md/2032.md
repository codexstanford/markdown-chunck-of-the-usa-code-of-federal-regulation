
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15 - NONDISCRIMINATION
#### Subpart A - Nondiscrimination in Federally-Assisted Programs of the Department of Agriculture - Effectuation of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964
##### ยง 15.12 Effect on other regulations; forms and instructions.
###### Forms and instructions.

(b)Each Agency shall issue and promptly make available forms and such implementing instructions and procedures consistent with the regulations in this part as may be necessary. Each Agency in making available Federal financial assistance to any program or activity may utilize contractual commitments in obtaining compliance with the regulations in this part, including obtaining compliance by recipients other than the contracting recipient.
