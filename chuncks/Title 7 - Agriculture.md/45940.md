######## Assignment Guarantee Agreement.

Form RD 4279-6, "Assignment Guarantee Agreement," is the signed agreement between the Agency, the Lender, and the Holder containing the terms and conditions of an assignment of a guaranteed portion of a loan, using the single Promissory Note system.
