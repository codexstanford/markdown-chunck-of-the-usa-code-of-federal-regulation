
# Title 7 - Agriculture

###### Subpart A - Functional Organization of the Rural Development Mission Area
####### ยง 2003.26 Functional organization of RBS.
######## Alternative Agricultural Research and Commercialization Corporation.,et seq.

(5)Headed by a director, this Corporation is responsible for providing and monitoring financial assistance for the development and commercialization of new nonfood and nonfeed products from agricultural and forestry commodities in accordance with 7 U.S.C. 5901The Corporation acts as a catalyst in forming private and public partnerships and promotes new uses of agricultural materials. It expands market opportunities for U.S. farmers through development of value-added industrial products and promotes environmentally friendly products. For budget and accounting purposes, this office is assigned to RBS. The director of the Corporation is responsible to the Office of the Secretary.
