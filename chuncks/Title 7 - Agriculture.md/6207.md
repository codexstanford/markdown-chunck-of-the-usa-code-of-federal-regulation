
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter C - Requirements and Standards Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 and the Egg Products Inspection Act
##### PART 53 - LIVESTOCK (GRADING, CERTIFICATION, AND STANDARDS)
###### Subpart A - Regulations
####### Service
######## ยง 53.8 How to obtain service.
######### Request by applicant for service,Noncommitment.

(c)-(1)Upon notification of the approval on an application for service, the applicant may, from time to time as desired, make oral or written requests for service under the regulations with respect to specific livestock for which the service is to be furnished under such application. Such requests shall be made at a market news office either directly or through any employee of the Agricultural Marketing Service who may be designated for such purposes.
