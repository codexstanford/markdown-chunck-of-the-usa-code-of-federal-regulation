
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter X - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Milk), Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1000 - GENERAL PROVISIONS OF FEDERAL MILK MARKETING ORDERS
##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 1000.6 Supply plant.
####### Supply plant

means a plant approved by a duly constituted regulatory agency for the handling of Grade A milk that receives milk directly from dairy farmers and transfers or diverts fluid milk products to other plants or manufactures dairy products on its premises.
