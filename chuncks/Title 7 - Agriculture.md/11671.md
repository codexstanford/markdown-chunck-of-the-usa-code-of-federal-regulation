####### ยง 246.16 Distribution of funds.
######## What happens at the end of the fiscal year in which food funds are converted?

(h)At the end of the fiscal year, the Department will determine the amount of food funds which the State agency was entitled to convert to NSA funds under paragraphs (f) and (g) of this section. In the event that the State agency has converted more than the permitted amount of funds, the Department will disallow the amount of excess conversion.
