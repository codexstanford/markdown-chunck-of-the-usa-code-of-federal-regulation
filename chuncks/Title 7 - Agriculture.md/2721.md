
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 25 - RURAL EMPOWERMENT ZONES AND ENTERPRISE COMMUNITIES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 25.3 Definitions.
###### Brownfield

means a "qualified contaminated site" meeting the requirements of section 941 of the Taxpayer Relief Act of 1997, (26 U.S.C. 198(c)), where the site is located in an empowerment zone or enterprise community.
