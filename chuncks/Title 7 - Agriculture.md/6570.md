
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter C - Requirements and Standards Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 and the Egg Products Inspection Act
##### PART 57 - INSPECTION OF EGGS (EGG PRODUCTS INSPECTION ACT)
###### Subpart A - Provisions Governing the Inspection of Eggs
####### General
######## ยง 57.1 Definitions.
######### Nest-run eggs

means eggs that have been packed as they come from the production facilities without having been washed, sized and/or candled for quality, with the exception that some checks, dirties, or other obvious undergrades may have been removed.
