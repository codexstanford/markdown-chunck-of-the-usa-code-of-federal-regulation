
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 18 - EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY IN THE STATE COOPERATIVE EXTENSION SERVICES
#### ยง 18.2 Purpose, applicability and coverage.
##### Purpose.

(a)This part provides a cooperative procedure involving the President and Secretary to assure that the Cooperative Extension Service provides equal opportunity in employment to each individual without regard to race, color, national origin, sex, or religion.
