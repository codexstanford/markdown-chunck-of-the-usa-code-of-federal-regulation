
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 301 - DOMESTIC QUARANTINE NOTICES
##### Subpart X - Phytophthora Ramorum
###### ยง 301.92-1 Definitions.
####### Nursery.,e.g.

Any location where nursery stock is grown, propagated, stored, or sold, or any location from which nursery stock is distributed. Locations that grow trees for sale without roots (, as Christmas trees) are considered to be nurseries for the purposes of this subpart.
