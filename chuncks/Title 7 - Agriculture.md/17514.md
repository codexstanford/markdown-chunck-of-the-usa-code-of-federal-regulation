
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter IV - Federal Crop Insurance Corporation, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart X - Interpretations of Statutory Provisions, Policy Provisions, and Procedures
###### ยง 400.765 Definitions.
####### Proceeding.

The process that starts with the filing of a complaint, notice of appeal, or other such document that commences the appeals process, and ends with the adjudicatory body issuing its decision, and includes all necessary activities, such as discovery, that occur within that time frame.
