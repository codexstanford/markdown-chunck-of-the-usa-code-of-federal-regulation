
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 14 - DETERMINING THE PRIMARY PURPOSE OF CERTAIN PAYMENTS FOR FEDERAL TAX PURPOSES
#### ยง 14.7 Non-Federal programs and payments.
##### Definition of non-Federal programs.

(a)Non-Federal program means any program of a State, a possession of the United States, a political subdivision of any State or possession of the United States, the District of Columbia, or a combination of any of the foregoing.
