
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter VII - Farm Service Agency, Department of Agriculture

###### Subpart G - Supplemental Revenue Assistance Payments Program
####### ยง 760.602 Definitions.
######## County expected yield

means an estimated yield, expressed in a specific unit of measure equal to the average of the most recent five years of official county yields established by FSA, excluding the years with the highest and lowest yields, respectively.
