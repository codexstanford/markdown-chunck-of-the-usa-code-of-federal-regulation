
# Title 7 - Agriculture

####### Architectural barriers.

(d)All facilities financed with Zero-Interest Loans that are open to the public or in which persons may be employed or reside must be designed, constructed, or altered to be readily accessible to and usable by disabled persons. Standards for these facilities must comply with the Architectural Barriers Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4151-4157) and the "Uniform Federal Accessibility Standards", (41 CFR part 101-19.6, Appendix A).
