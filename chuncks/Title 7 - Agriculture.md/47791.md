##### Subpart H - Recordkeeping, Reporting, and Examination Requirements for RBICs
###### Reporting Requirements for RBICs
####### ยง 4290.680 Reporting changes in RBIC not subject to prior approval.
######## Changes to be reported for post-approval.

(a)This section applies to any changes in your Articles, ownership, capitalization, management, operating area, or investment policies that do not require the Agency's prior approval. You must report such changes to the Agency within 30 days after the change, for post approval.
