
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1788 - RUS FIDELITY AND INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS FOR ELECTRIC AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS BORROWERS
##### Subpart A - Borrower Insurance Requirements
###### ยง 1788.3 Flood insurance.
####### et seq.

(a) Borrowers shall purchase and maintain flood insurance for buildings in flood hazard areas to the extent available and required under the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968, as amended (42 U.S.C. 4001,) The insurance should cover, in addition to the building, any machinery, equipment, fixtures, and furnishings contained in the building.
