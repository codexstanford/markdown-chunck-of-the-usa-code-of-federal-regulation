
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter VI - Natural Resources Conservation Service, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter C - Water Resources

###### ยง 625.2 Definitions.
####### 30-year Contract

means a contract that is limited to acreage owned by Indian tribes. The 30-year contract is not eligible for use on tribal lands held in trust or subject to Federal restrictions against alienation.
