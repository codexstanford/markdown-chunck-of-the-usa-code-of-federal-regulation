
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 1719.2 Definitions.
####### Energy efficiency program

(EE Program) means a program set up by an Eligible entity to provide financing to Qualified consumers so that they can implement durable cost-effective Energy efficiency measures.
