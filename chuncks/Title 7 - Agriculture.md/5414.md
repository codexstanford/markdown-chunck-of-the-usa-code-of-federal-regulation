###### Subpart W - United States Standards for Grades of Shelled Almonds
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.2130 Diameter.
######### Diameter

means the greatest dimension of the kernel, or piece of kernel at right angles to the longitudinal axis. Diameter shall be determined by passing the kernel or piece of kernel through a round opening.
