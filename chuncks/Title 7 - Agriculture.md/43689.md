
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XXXIV - National Institute of Food and Agriculture
#### PART 3431 - VETERINARY MEDICINE LOAN REPAYMENT PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - Designation of Veterinarian Shortage Situations
###### ยง 3431.3 Definitions and acronyms.
####### Withdrawal

means a request by a participant for withdrawal from participation in the VMLRP after signing the service agreement, but prior to VMLRP making the first quarterly payment on behalf of the participant. A withdrawal is without penalty to the participant and without obligation to the Program.
