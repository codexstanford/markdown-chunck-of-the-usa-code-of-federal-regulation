
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart B - State Telecommunications Modernization Plan
###### ยง 1751.100 Definitions.
####### Modernization Plan (State Telecommunications Modernization Plan).

A State plan, which has been approved by RUS, for improving the telecommunications network of those Telecommunications Providers covered by the plan. A Modernization Plan must conform to the provisions of this subpart.
