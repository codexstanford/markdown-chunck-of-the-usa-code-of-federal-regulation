###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 246.2 Definitions.
######## Migrant farmworker

means an individual whose principal employment is in agriculture on a seasonal basis, who has been so employed within the last 24 months, and who establishes, for the purposes of such employment, a temporary abode.
