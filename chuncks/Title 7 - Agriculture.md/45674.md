###### ยง 4274.310 Eligibility requirements - intermediaries.
####### Delinquent debt.

(g)An intermediary is ineligible to receive an Agency IRP loan if the intermediary or any principal of the intermediary has any delinquent debt to the Federal government. Agency IRP loan funds cannot be used to satisfy the delinquent debt.
