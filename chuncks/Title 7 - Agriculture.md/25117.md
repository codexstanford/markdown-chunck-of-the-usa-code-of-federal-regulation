(2)For the purpose of classifying receipts by variety to determine a handler's disposition obligation, "variety" shall mean that variety of almonds which constitutes at least 90 percent of the lot:That lots containing a combination of Butte and Padre varieties only, shall be classified as "Butte-Padre", regardless of the percentage of each variety in the lot. If no variety constitutes at least 90 percent of the almonds in a lot, the lot shall be classified as "mixed":That if the variety or varieties of almonds in a lot are not identified, the lot shall be classified as "mixed", regardless of the percentage of each variety in a lot.
