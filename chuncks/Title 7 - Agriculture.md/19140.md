
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### Subchapter D - Special Programs
##### PART 759 - DISASTER DESIGNATIONS AND NOTIFICATIONS
###### ยง 759.3 Abbreviations and definitions.
####### U.S. Drought Monitor

is a system for classifying drought severity according to a range of abnormally dry to exceptional drought. It is a collaborative effort between Federal and academic partners that is produced on a weekly basis to synthesize multiple indices, outlooks, and drought impacts on a map and in narrative form. This synthesis of indices is reported by the National Drought Mitigation Center.
