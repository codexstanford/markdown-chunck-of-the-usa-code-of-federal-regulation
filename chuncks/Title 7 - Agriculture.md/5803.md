######## ยง 52.38a Definitions of terms applicable to statistical sampling.
######### Acceptable Quality Level (AQL).

(1)The maximum percent of defective units of product or the maximum number of defects per hundred units of product which are acceptable as a process average. At the AQL's contained in the statistical sampling plans of this subpart, production has a probability of acceptance ("Pa") of approximately 95 percent.
