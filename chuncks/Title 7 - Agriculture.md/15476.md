
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 301 - DOMESTIC QUARANTINE NOTICES
##### Subpart U - Karnal Bunt
###### ยง 301.89-1 Definitions.
####### Infestation (infected).,i.e.,,Tilletia indica

The presence of Karnal bunt, or any identifiable stage of development (bunted kernels in grain, bunted kernels or teliospores in seed) of the fungus(Mitra) Mundkur, or the existence of circumstances that make it reasonable to believe that Karnal bunt is present.
