##### Subpart E - Procedures for the Conduct of Referendum

####### ยง 1230.616 Public notice.
######## Public notice

The termmeans information regarding a referendum that would be provided by the Secretary, such as press releases, newspapers, electronic media, FSA county newsletters, and the like. Such notice would contain the referendum date and location, registration and voting requirements, rules regarding absentee voting, and other pertinent information.
