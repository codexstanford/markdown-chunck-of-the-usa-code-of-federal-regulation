
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 318 - STATE OF HAWAII AND TERRITORIES QUARANTINE NOTICES
##### Subpart B - Territorial Cotton, Cottonseed, and Cottonseed Products
###### ยง 318.47-1 Definitions.
####### Moved,movement, move

(m)(). Shipped, offered for shipment to a common carrier, received for transportation or transported by a common carrier, or carried, transported, moved, or allowed to be moved, directly or indirectly, from Hawaii, Puerto Rico, or the Virgin Islands of the United States, into or through any other State, Territory, or District of the United States. "Movement" and "move" shall be construed accordingly.
