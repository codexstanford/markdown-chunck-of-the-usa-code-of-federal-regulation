
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter X - Agricultural Marketing Service (Marketing Agreements and Orders; Milk), Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1033 - MILK IN THE MIDEAST MARKETING AREA
##### Subpart - Order Regulating Handling
###### Payments for Milk
####### ยง 1033.73 Payments to producers and to cooperative associations.
######## Partial payment to a cooperative association.

(1)For bulk fluid milk/skimmed milk received during the first 15 days of the month from a cooperative association in any capacity, except as the operator of a pool plant, the partial payment shall be equal to the hundredweight of milk received multiplied by the lowest announced class price for the preceding month.
