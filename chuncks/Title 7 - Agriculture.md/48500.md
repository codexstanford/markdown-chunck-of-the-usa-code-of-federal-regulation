The indemnity bond must:

(1) Be issued by a qualified surety company holding a certificate of authority from the Secretary of the Treasury and listed in Treasury Department Circular 570, except when the outstanding principal balance and accrued Interest due the present holder, in accordance with ยง 5001.450(c), is less than $1 million as verified by the lender via a written letter of certification of balance due;
