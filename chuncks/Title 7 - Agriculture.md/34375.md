
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1726 - ELECTRIC SYSTEM CONSTRUCTION POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart G - Procurement Procedures
###### ยง 1726.201 Formal competitive bidding.
####### Bid openings.

(e)Bid openings are generally conducted by the engineer in the presence of bidders and a representative of the borrower and the borrower's attorney. Each bona fide bid must be opened publicly and reviewed for any irregularities, errors, or exceptions. It must be verified that any addendum or supplement to the specification has been acknowledged by the bidder. The adequacy of bid bonds or certified checks must be verified at this time.
