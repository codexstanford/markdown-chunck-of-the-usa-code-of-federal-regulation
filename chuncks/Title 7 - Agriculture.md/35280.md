
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1752 - SERVICING OF TELECOMMUNICATIONS PROGRAMS
##### ยง 1752.3 Definitions.
###### Settlement.

Compromise, adjustment, cancellation, or charge-off of a debt owed to the Agency. The term "settlement" is used to refer to any of these actions, whether individually or collectively.
