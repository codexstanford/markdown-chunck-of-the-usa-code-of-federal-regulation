###### Subpart D - Processing and Servicing Rural Development Assistance to Employees, Relatives, and Associates
####### ยง 1900.152 Definitions.
######## Immediate working relationship.

A relationship between a subordinate and a supervisor in a direct line, or between co-workers in the same office. For the purposes of this subpart, the relationships among a County Supervisor and members of the local County Committee are immediate working relationships.
