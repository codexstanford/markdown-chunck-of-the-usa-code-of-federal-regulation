
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter III - Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 322 - BEES, BEEKEEPING BYPRODUCTS, AND BEEKEEPING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 322.1 Definitions.
####### Slumgum.

Residue remaining after the beeswax rendering process. It is composed of beeswax mixed with debris or refuse that accumulates when wax cappings or comb are melted. The residue can include wax moth cocoons, dead bees, bee parts, and other detritus from the colony.
