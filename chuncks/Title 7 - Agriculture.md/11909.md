
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter II - Food and Nutrition Service, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter A - Child Nutrition Programs
##### PART 247 - COMMODITY SUPPLEMENTAL FOOD PROGRAM
###### ยง 247.28 Storage and inventory of commodities.
####### What are the requirements for the inventory of commodities?

(b)A physical inventory of all USDA commodities must be conducted annually at each storage and distribution site where these commodities are stored. Results of the physical inventory must be reconciled with inventory records and maintained on file by the State or local agency.
