###### Subpart E - Application of Tolerances
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.696 Firm.
######### Firm

as applied to common oranges, means that the fruit is not soft, or noticeably wilted or flabby; as applied to oranges of the Mandarin group (Satsuma, King, Mandarin), means that the fruit is not extremely puffy, although the skin may be slightly loose.
