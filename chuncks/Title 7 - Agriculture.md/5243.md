##### PART 51 - FRESH FRUITS, VEGETABLES, AND OTHER PRODUCTS (INSPECTION, CERTIFICATION, AND STANDARDS)
###### Subpart R - United States Standards for Grades of Florida Tangerines
####### Definitions
######## ยง 51.1834 Reasonably well colored.
######### Reasonably well colored

means that a good yellow or reddish tangerine color shall predominate over the green color on at least one-half of the fruit surface in the aggregate, and that each fruit shall show practically no lemon color.
