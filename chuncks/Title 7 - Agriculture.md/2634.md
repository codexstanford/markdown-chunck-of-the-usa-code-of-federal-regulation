
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### PART 18 - EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY IN THE STATE COOPERATIVE EXTENSION SERVICES
#### ยง 18.5 Formal complaint procedure.
##### Decision by the President.

(h)The President shall review the entire file on the complaint, including the record of the hearing if a hearing was held, and shall promptly:

(1) Remand to the hearing board or officer for further action; or

(2) Make a decision on the complaint; or

(3) Otherwise dispose of the complaint.
