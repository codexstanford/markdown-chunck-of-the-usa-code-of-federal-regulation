
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 15f - ADJUDICATIONS UNDER SECTION 741
#### Subpart A - What Is the Purpose of These Regulations and to Whom and to What Programs Do They Apply?
##### ยง 15f.4 What do certain words and phrases in these regulations mean?
###### Covered program

means:

(1) A farm ownership, farm operating, or emergency loan funded from the Agricultural Credit Insurance Program Account;

(2) A housing program established under title V of the Housing Act of 1949; or

(3) A commodity program or disaster assistance program.
