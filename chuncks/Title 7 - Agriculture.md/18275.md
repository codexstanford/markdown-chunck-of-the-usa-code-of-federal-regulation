
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter VI - Natural Resources Conservation Service, Department of Agriculture

##### PART 632 - RURAL ABANDONED MINE PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 632.4 Definitions.
######## Financial burden.

The land user's cost of reclamation that cannot be expected to be recovered within the contract period and that would probably prevent participation in the program. The land user must sign a statement to substantiate financial burden.
