
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1726 - ELECTRIC SYSTEM CONSTRUCTION POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart J - Contract Closeout
###### ยง 1726.404 Non-site specific construction contract closeout.
####### Closeout documents.

(c)(1) Upon satisfactory completion of construction of a section of the project (including all changes and corrections by the contractor), the borrower (acting through its engineer, if applicable) will obtain executed copies of the following documents:

(i) RUS Form 792b, Certificate of Contractor and Indemnity Agreement

(ii) RUS Form 213, "Buy American" certificate.

(iii) Certification by the project engineer in accordance with paragraph (a) of this section, if applicable.

(iv) Final design documents, as outlined in part 1724 of this chapter.
