
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XLII - Rural Business-Cooperative Service and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 4280 - LOANS AND GRANTS
##### Subpart B - Rural Energy for America Program General
###### Energy Audit and Renewable Energy Development Assistance Grants
####### ยง 4280.155 Scoring EA and REDA grant applications.

The Agency will score each EA and REDA application using the criteria specified in paragraphs (a) through (f) of this section, with a maximum score of 100 points possible. Unless otherwise altered via anotification, the project must score a minimum of 40 points to be eligible to compete for funding.
