
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter I - Agricultural Marketing Service (Standards, Inspections, Marketing Practices), Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter C - Requirements and Standards Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 and the Egg Products Inspection Act
##### PART 70 - VOLUNTARY GRADING OF POULTRY PRODUCTS AND RABBIT PRODUCTS
###### Subpart A - Grading of Poultry Products and Rabbit Products
####### Application for Grading Service
######## ยง 70.37 Types of Service.
######### Continuous grading service on a scheduled basis.

(b)Service on a scheduled basis has a scheduled tour of duty and is performed when an applicant requests that a USDA licensed grader be stationed in the applicant's plant or warehouse and grade poultry and rabbit products in accordance with U.S. Standards. The applicant agrees to comply with the facility, operating, and sanitary requirements of scheduled service. Minimum fees for service performed under a scheduled agreement shall be based on the hours of the regular tour of duty. Poultry and rabbit products graded under scheduled grading service are eligible to be identified with the official grademarks shown in ยง 70.51 only when processed and graded under the supervision of a grader.
