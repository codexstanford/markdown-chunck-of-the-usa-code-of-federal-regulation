
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter VII - Farm Service Agency, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter D - Special Programs

###### ยง 786.102 Definitions.
####### Base annual production

means the pounds of production determined by multiplying the average annual production per cow calculated from base period information times the average number of cows in the dairy herd during each applicable disaster year.
