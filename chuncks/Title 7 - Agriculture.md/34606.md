
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1734 - DISTANCE LEARNING AND TELEMEDICINE LOAN AND GRANT PROGRAMS
##### Subpart A - Distance Learning and Telemedicine Loan and Grant Program - General
###### § 1734.3 Definitions.
####### Completed application

means an application that includes all those items specified in §§ 1734.125, 1734.134, and in form and substance satisfactory to the Administrator.
