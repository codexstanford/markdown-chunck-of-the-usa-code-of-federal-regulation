
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of Agriculture
### PART 12 - HIGHLY ERODIBLE LAND CONSERVATION AND WETLAND CONSERVATION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 12.2 Definitions.
###### Hydrophytic vegetation

means plants growing in water or in a substrate that is at least periodically deficient in oxygen during a growing season as a result of excessive water content.
