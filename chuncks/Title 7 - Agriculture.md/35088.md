
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1740 - RURAL ECONNECTIVITY PROGRAM
##### Subpart D - Award Terms
###### ยง 1740.42 Interest rates.

Interest rates for the different funding options that will become available will be included in theas part of the funding announcement opening a funding window.

(a) Direct cost-of-money loans shall bear interest at a rate equal to the cost of borrowing to the Department of Treasury for obligations of comparable maturity.

(b) The agency may offer 100 percent loans at a reduced interest rate, and in such cases, the applicable interest rate will be stated in theor applicable funding opportunity notice.
