
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart D - Permits: Allocation, Issuance, Denial, and Revocation
###### ยง 319.7 Definitions.
####### Move.

To carry, enter, import, mail, ship, or transport; to aid, abet, cause, or induce the carrying, entering, importing, mailing, shipping, or transporting; to offer to carry, enter, import, mail, ship, or transport; to receive to carry, enter, import, mail, ship, or transport; to release into the environment; or to allow any of the activities described in this definition.
