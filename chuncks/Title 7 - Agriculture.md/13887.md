######## Definition of a reservation.

(1)For purposes of this section, the term "reservation" shall mean the geographically defined area or areas over which a tribal organization exercises governmental jurisdiction. The term "tribal organization" shall mean the recognized governing body of an Indian tribe (including the tribally recognized intertribal organization of such tribes), as well as any Indian tribe, band, or community holding a treaty with a State government.
