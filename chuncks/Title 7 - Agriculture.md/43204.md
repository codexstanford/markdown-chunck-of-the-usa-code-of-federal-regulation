
# Title 7 - Agriculture

##### Subpart A - General Information
###### ยง 3430.2 Definitions.
####### Grant

means the award by the Authorized Departmental Officer of funds to an eligible grantee to assist in meeting the costs of conducting for the benefit of the public, an identified project which is intended and designed to accomplish the purpose of the program as identified in the program solicitation or RFA.
