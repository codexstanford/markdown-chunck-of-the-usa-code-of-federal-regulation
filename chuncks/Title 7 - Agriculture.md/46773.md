(c)Recipients of grants must comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990, 42 U.S.C. 12101Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, 42 U.S.C. 2000dand section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 29 U.S.C. 794. This may include collection and maintenance of data on the race, sex, and national origin of the recipient's membership/ownership and employees. The data must be available to conduct compliance reviews.

(1) Initial compliance reviews will be conducted by the Agency prior to funds being obligated.

(2) Grants will require one subsequent compliance review following Project completion. This will occur prior to the last disbursement of grant funds.
