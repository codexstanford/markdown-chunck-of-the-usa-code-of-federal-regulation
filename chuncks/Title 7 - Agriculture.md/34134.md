
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1720 - GUARANTEES FOR BONDS AND NOTES ISSUED FOR UTILITY INFRASTRUCTURE PURPOSES
##### ยง 1720.3 Definitions.
###### Guarantee

means the written agreement between the Secretary and a guaranteed lender, pursuant to which the Secretary guarantees full repayment of the principal, interest, and call premium, if any, on a guaranteed bond.
