
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVIII - Rural Housing Service, Rural Business-Cooperative Service, and Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### Subchapter H - Program Regulations
##### PART 1924 - CONSTRUCTION AND REPAIR
###### Subpart A - Planning and Performing Construction and Other Development
####### Exhibit I to Subpart A of Part 1924 - Guidelines for Seasonal Farm Labor Housing
######## Office and Maintenance

302-2.3-An office and maintenance space shall be provided or available, commensurate with the number of living units served, and shall meet the criteria of the Agency Manual of Acceptable Practices. If necessary, the maintenance space shall have sufficient area to accommodate furniture storage.
