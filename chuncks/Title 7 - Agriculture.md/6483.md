######### Nutritional labeling.

(c)Nutrition information must be included on the labeling of each unit container of consumer packaged shell eggs in accordance with the provisions of title 21, chapter I, part 101, Regulations for the Enforcement of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act and the Fair Packaging and Labeling Act. The nutrition information included on labels is subject to review by the Food and Drug Administration prior to approval by the Department.
