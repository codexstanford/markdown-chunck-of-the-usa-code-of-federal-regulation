##### Refund of ineligible amounts.

(d)If a sale has been financed and CCC determines that the sales price exceeds the price permissible under ยง 17.5(b)(4), or that the sale is otherwise ineligible for financing, in whole or in part, the supplier shall refund in dollars such excess price or ineligible amount to CCC promptly on demand.
