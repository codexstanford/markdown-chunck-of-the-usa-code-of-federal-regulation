(d)Each handler shall mark every shipping or storage container (excluding subcontainers) of Export dates with his name or that of the exporting firm, and the lot number. If the dates, including fieldrun dates with cull dates removed, are certified as meeting the grade and size requirements for export to approved countries other than Mexico, the containers shall be marked "Export". Dry dates for processing packed for shipment to approved countries shall be marked "Export Dry". Dates packed for export to Mexico shall be marked "Export Mexico". However, "Export Mexico" shall be in letters not less than three-fourths () inch in height on containers exceeding 5 pounds net weight, and not less than one-eighth () inch in height on smaller containers. DAC dates and FP dates, marked pursuant to paragraphs (b) and (c), respectively, of this section, may be exported without change of marking.
