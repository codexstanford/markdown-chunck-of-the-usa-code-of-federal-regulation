##### PART 29 - TOBACCO INSPECTION
###### Subpart B - Requirements
####### miscellaneous
######## ยง 29.401 Definitions.
######### Port of entry.

(i)Any place designated by Executive order of the President, by order of the Secretary of the Treasury, or by Act of Congress, at which a customs officer is authorized to accept entries of merchandise, to collect duties, and to enforce the various provisions of the Customs and Navigation Laws. The term "port of entry" incorporates the geographical area under the jurisdiction of the port director when such port is one other than a district headquarters port.
