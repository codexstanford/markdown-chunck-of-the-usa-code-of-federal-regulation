###### Subpart C - Emergency Assistance for Livestock, Honeybees, and Farm-Raised Fish Program
####### ยง 760.202 Definitions.
######## Sheep

means a domesticated, ruminant mammal of the genus Ovis. Sheep are further defined by sex (rams and ewes) and age (lambs) for purposes of dividing into categories for loss calculations.
