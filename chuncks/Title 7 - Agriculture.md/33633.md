
# Title 7 - Agriculture

### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture

##### Subpart D - Basic Requirements for Loan Approval
###### ยง 1710.152 Primary support documents.
####### Load forecast.

(a)The load forecast provides the borrower and RUS with an understanding of the borrower's future system loads, the factors influencing those loads, and estimates of future loads. The load forecast provides a basis for projecting annual electricity (kWh) sales and revenues, and for engineering estimates of plant additions required to provide reliable service to meet the forecasted loads. Subpart E of this part contains the information to be included in a load forecast and when an approved load forecast is required.
