(a)If a Portfolio Concern was a Smaller Enterprise at the time of the initial Financing but no longer qualifies as such under the size standard applicable to such entity, you may keep your investment in the Portfolio Concern and:

(1) Subject to the overline limitations of ยง 4290.740, you may provide additional Financing to the Portfolio Concern up to the time it makes a public offering of its securities.
