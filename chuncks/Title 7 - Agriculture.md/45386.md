
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XXXV - Rural Housing Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 3565 - GUARANTEED RURAL RENTAL HOUSING PROGRAM
##### Subpart G - Processing Requirements
###### ยง 3565.303 Issuance of loan guarantee.
####### Conditional commitment to guarantee a loan.,after

(b)The Agency will issue a conditional commitment to guarantee a loan. This commitment will be good for such time frame as the Agency deems appropriate based on project requirements. The commitment to guarantee a loan, will specify any conditions necessary to obtain a determination by the Agency that all program requirements have been met. A conditional commitment can be issued, subject to the availability of funds,:

(1) Completion of environmental review requirements in accordance with 7 CFR part 1970; and

(2) Selection of the proposed project for funding by the Agency in accordance with ranking and selection criteria.
