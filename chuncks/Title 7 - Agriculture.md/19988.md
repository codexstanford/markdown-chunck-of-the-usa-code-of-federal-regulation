
# Title 7 - Agriculture

###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 761.2 Abbreviations and definitions.
######## Agricultural commodity

means livestock, grains, cotton, oilseeds, dry beans, tobacco, peanuts, sugar beets, sugar cane, fruit, vegetable, forage, nursery crops, nuts, aquacultural species, and the products resulting from: livestock, tree farming, and other plant or animal production as determined by the Agency.
