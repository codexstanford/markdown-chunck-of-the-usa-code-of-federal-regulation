####### Financial feasibility

means the Applicant's ability to generate sufficient revenues to cover its expenses, sufficient cash flow to service its debts and obligations as they become due and meet the Net worth and minimum Times Interest Earned Ratio (TIER), CR, or DSCR requirements of ยง 1738.206(b)(2)(i) by the end of the forecast period.
