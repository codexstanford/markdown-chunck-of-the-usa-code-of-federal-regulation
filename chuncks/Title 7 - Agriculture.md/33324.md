
# Title 7 - Agriculture
## Subtitle B - Regulations of the Department of Agriculture
### Chapter XVII - Rural Utilities Service, Department of Agriculture
#### PART 1703 - RURAL DEVELOPMENT
##### Subpart H - Deferments of RUS Loan Payments for Rural Development Projects
###### ยง 1703.302 Definitions and rules of construction.
####### Deferment

means a re-amortization of a payment of principal and/or interest on an RUS direct loan or insured loan for over either a 5- or 10 year period, with the first payment beginning on the date of the deferment.
