###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 761.2 Abbreviations and definitions.
######## Recreation loan

is a loan that was made to eligible applicants to assist in the conversion of all or a portion of the farm they owned or operated to outdoor income producing recreation enterprises to supplement or supplant farm income. RL's are no longer funded, however, such outstanding loans are serviced by the Agency.
