
# Title 7 - Agriculture

#### PART 1735 - GENERAL POLICIES, TYPES OF LOANS, LOAN REQUIREMENTS - TELECOMMUNICATIONS PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 1735.2 Definitions.
####### Modernization Plan (State Telecommunications Modernization Plan)

means a State plan, which has been approved by RUS, for improving the telecommunications network of those telecommunications providers covered by the plan. A Modernization Plan must conform to the provisions of 7 CFR 1751, subpart B.
