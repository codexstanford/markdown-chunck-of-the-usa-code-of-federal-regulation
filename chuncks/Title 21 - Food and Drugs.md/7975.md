####### Eugenia caryophyllata

(a) Cloves are the dried unopened flower buds and calyx tubes, harvested before the flowers have opened, of the clove treeThunberg, native to tropical Asia. Their derivatives include essential oils (cloves, CAS Reg. No. 8000-34-8; buds; leaves, CAS Reg. No. 8015-97-2; stems, CAS Reg. No. 8015-98-3; and eugenol, CAS Reg. No. 97-53-0), oleoresins, and natural extractives obtained from clove buds, leaves, and stems.
