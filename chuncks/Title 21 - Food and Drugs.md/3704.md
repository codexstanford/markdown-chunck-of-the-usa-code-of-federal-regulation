
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 110 - CURRENT GOOD MANUFACTURING PRACTICE IN MANUFACTURING, PACKING, OR HOLDING HUMAN FOOD
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 110.3 Definitions.
####### Safe-moisture level

(n)is a level of moisture low enough to prevent the growth of undesirable microorganisms in the finished product under the intended conditions of manufacturing, storage, and distribution. The maximum safe moisture level for a food is based on its water activity (a). An awill be considered safe for a food if adequate data are available that demonstrate that the food at or below the given awill not support the growth of undesirable microorganisms.
