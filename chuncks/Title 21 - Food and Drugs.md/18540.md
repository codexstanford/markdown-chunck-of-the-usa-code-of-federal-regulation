
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics

###### ยง 640.80 Albumin (Human).
####### Proper name and definition.

(a)The proper name of the product shall be Albumin (Human). The product is defined as a sterile solution of the albumin derived from human plasma.
