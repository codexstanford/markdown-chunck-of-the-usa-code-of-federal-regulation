
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 892 - RADIOLOGY DEVICES
##### Subpart B - Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 892.1840 Radiographic film.
####### Identification.

(a)Radiographic film is a device that consists of a thin sheet of radiotransparent material coated on one or both sides with a photographic emulsion intended to record images during diagnostic radiologic procedures.
