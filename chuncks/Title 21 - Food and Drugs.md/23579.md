##### Subpart B - Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 886.1342 Strabismus detection device.
####### Identification.

(a)A strabismus detection device is a prescription device designed to simultaneously illuminate both eyes with polarized light for automated detection of strabismus by analyzing foveal birefringence properties.
