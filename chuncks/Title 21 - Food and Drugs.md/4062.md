
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 113.3 Definitions.
####### Initial temperature

(l)means the average temperature of the contents of the coldest container to be processed at the time the thermal processing cycle begins, as determined after thorough stirring or shaking of the filled and sealed container.
