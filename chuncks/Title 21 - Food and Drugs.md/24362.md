
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 892 - RADIOLOGY DEVICES
##### Subpart B - Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 892.1980 Radiologic table.
####### Identification.

(a)A radiologic table is a device intended for medical purposes to support a patient during radiologic procedures. The table may be fixed or tilting and may be electrically powered.
