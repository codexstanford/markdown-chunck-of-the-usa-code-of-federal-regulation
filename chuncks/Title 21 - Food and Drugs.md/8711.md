### Subchapter C - Drugs: General

For products with an expected SPF less than 8, administer UV doses that increase by 25 percent with each successive dose (0.64X, 0.80X, 1.00X, 1.25X, and 1.56X). For products with an expected SPF from 8 to 15, administer UV doses that increase by 20 percent with each successive dose (0.69X, 0.83X, 1.00X, 1.20X, and 1.44X). For products with an expected SPF higher than 15, administer UV doses that increase by 15 percent with each successive dose (0.76X, 0.87X, 1.00X, 1.15X, and 1.32X).
