####### Liquid Plasma.

(c)Liquid Plasma shall be separated from the red blood cells and shall be stored at a temperature of 1 to 6 &#xB0;C within 4 hours after filling the final container or within the timeframe specified in the directions for use for the blood collecting, processing, and storage system.
