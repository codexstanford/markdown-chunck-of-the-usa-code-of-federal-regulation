
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

##### Subpart C - General Hospital and Personal Use Monitoring Devices
###### ยง 880.2400 Bed-patient monitor.
####### Identification.

(a)A bed-patient monitor is a battery-powered device placed under a mattress and used to indicate by an alarm or other signal when a patient attempts to leave the bed.
