
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1140 - CIGARETTES, SMOKELESS TOBACCO, AND COVERED TOBACCO PRODUCTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 1140.3 Definitions.
####### Cigarette tobacco

means any product that consists of loose tobacco that is intended for use by consumers in a cigarette. Unless otherwise stated, the requirements applicable to cigarettes under this chapter also apply to cigarette tobacco.
