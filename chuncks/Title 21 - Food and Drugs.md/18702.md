
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics
#### PART 680 - ADDITIONAL STANDARDS FOR MISCELLANEOUS PRODUCTS
##### ยง 680.2 Manufacture of Allergenic Products.
###### Cultures derived from microorganisms.

(b)Culture media into which organisms are inoculated for the manufacture of Allergenic Products shall contain no allergenic substances other than those necessary as a growth requirement. Neither horse protein nor any allergenic derivative of horse protein shall be used in culture media.
