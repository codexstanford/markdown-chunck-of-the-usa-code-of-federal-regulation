
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products

##### Subpart B - Premarket Tobacco Product Applications
###### ยง 1114.7 Required content and format.
####### i.e.,

(i) A description of the specific group(s) to which the labeling, advertising, marketing, promotion, and other consumer-directed activities for the new tobacco product would be targeted (the intended audience(s));
