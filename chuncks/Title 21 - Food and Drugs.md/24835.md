##### ยง 1020.30 Diagnostic x-ray systems and their major components.
###### Fluoroscopy

means a technique for generating x-ray images and presenting them simultaneously and continuously as visible images. This term has the same meaning as the term "radioscopy" in the standards of the International Electrotechnical Commission.
