
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 872 - DENTAL DEVICES
##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 872.5571 Auto titration device for oral appliances.
####### Identification.

(a)An auto-titration device for oral appliances is a prescription home use device that determines a target position to be used for a final oral appliance for the reduction of snoring and mild to moderate obstructive sleep apnea.
