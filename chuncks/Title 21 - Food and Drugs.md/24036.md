
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 888 - ORTHOPEDIC DEVICES
##### Subpart E - Surgical Devices
###### ยง 888.5940 Cast component.
####### Identification.

(a)A cast component is a device intended for medical purposes to protect or support a cast. This generic type of device includes the cast heel, toe cap, cast support, and walking iron.
