
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - Drugs for Human Use
#### PART 347 - SKIN PROTECTANT DRUG PRODUCTS FOR OVER-THE-COUNTER HUMAN USE
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 347.3 Definitions.
####### Lip protectant drug product.

A drug product that temporarily prevents dryness and helps relieve chapping of the exposed surfaces of the lips; traditionally called "lip balm."
