
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart B - Drugs
###### ยง 73.1125 Potassium sodium copper chloropyhllin (chlorophyllin-copper complex).
####### Uses and restrictions.

(c)Potassium sodium copper chlorophyllin may be safely used for coloring dentifrices that are drugs at a level not to exceed 0.1 percent. Authorization for this use shall not be construed as waiving any of the requirements of section 505 of the act with respect to the drug in which it is used.
