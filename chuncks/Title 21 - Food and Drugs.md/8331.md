
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption

##### Subpart B - Prohibited Cattle Materials
###### ยง 189.5 Prohibited cattle materials.
####### Food additive status.

(3)Prohibited cattle materials for use in human food are food additives subject to section 409 of the act, except when used as dietary ingredients in dietary supplements. The use or intended use of any prohibited cattle material in human food causes the material and the food to be adulterated under section 402(a)(2)(C) of the act if the prohibited cattle material is a food additive, unless it is the subject of a food additive regulation or of an investigational exemption for a food additive under ยง 170.17 of this chapter.
