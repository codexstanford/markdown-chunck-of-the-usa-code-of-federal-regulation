
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products

##### ยง 522.1884 Prednisolone sodium succinate.
###### Conditions of use,Amount and indications for use,Horses.

(c)-(1)-(i)Administer 50 to 100 mg as an initial dose by intravenous injection over a period of one-half to 1 minute, or by intramuscular injection, and may be repeated in inflammatory, allergic, or other stress conditions at intervals of 12, 24, or 48 hours, depending upon the size of the animal, the severity of the condition and the response to treatment.
