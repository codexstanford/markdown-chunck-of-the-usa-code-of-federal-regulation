
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 522 - IMPLANTATION OR INJECTABLE DOSAGE FORM NEW ANIMAL DRUGS
##### ยง 522.840 Estradiol.
###### Limitations.

(2)For subcutaneous ear implantation only. Not approved for repeated implantation (reimplantation) with this or any other cattle ear implant within each separate production phase (beef steer calves 2 months of age and older, growing beef steers on pasture (stocker, feeder, and slaughter), growing beef steers and heifers fed in confinement for slaughter). Safety and effectiveness following reimplantation have not been evaluated. Do not use in beef calves less than 2 months of age, dairy calves, and veal calves. A withdrawal period has not been established for this product in pre-ruminating calves. Do not use in dairy cows or in animals intended for subsequent breeding. Use in these cattle may cause drug residues in milk and/or in calves born to these cows.
