
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter L - Regulations Under Certain Other Acts Administered by the Food and Drug Administration
#### PART 1210 - REGULATIONS UNDER THE FEDERAL IMPORT MILK ACT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 1210.3 Definitions.
####### Pasteurization.

(h)Pasteurization is the process of heating every particle of milk or cream to at least 143 &#xB0;F., and holding it at such temperature continuously for at least 30 minutes, or to at least 161 &#xB0;F., and holding it at such temperature continuously for at least 15 seconds.
