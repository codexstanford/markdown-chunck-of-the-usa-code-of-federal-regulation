
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter L - Regulations Under Certain Other Acts Administered by the Food and Drug Administration
#### PART 1210 - REGULATIONS UNDER THE FEDERAL IMPORT MILK ACT
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 1210.3 Definitions.
####### Cream.

(g)Cream is that portion of the milk, rich in milk fat, which rises to the surface of milk on standing or is separated from it by centrifugal force. (See §§ 131.150 through 131.157 of this chapter).
