
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 864 - HEMATOLOGY AND PATHOLOGY DEVICES
##### Subpart I - Hematology Reagents
###### ยง 864.8540 Red cell lysing reagent.
####### Identification.

(a)A red cell lysing reagent is a device used to lyse (destroy) red blood cells for hemoglobin determinations or aid in the counting of white blood cells.
