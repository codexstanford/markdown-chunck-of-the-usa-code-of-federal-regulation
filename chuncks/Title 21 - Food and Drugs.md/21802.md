
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart E - Cardiovascular Surgical Devices
###### ยง 870.4400 Cardiopulmonary bypass blood reservoir.
####### Identification.

(a)A cardiopulmonary bypass blood reservoir is a device used in conjunction with short-term extracorporeal circulation devices to hold a reserve supply of blood in the bypass circulation.
