######## Identification.

(a)A hemoglobin Aassay is a device used to determine the hemoglobin Acontent of human blood. The measurement of hemoglobin Ais used in the diagnosis of the thalassemias (hereditary hemolytic anemias characterized by decreased synthesis of one or more types of hemoglobin polypeptide chains).
