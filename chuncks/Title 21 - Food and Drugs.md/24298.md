
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 892 - RADIOLOGY DEVICES
##### Subpart B - Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 892.1610 Diagnostic x-ray beam-limiting device.
####### Identification.

(a)A diagnostic x-ray beam-limiting device is a device such as a collimator, a cone, or an aperture intended to restrict the dimensions of a diagnostic x-ray field by limiting the size of the primary x-ray beam.
