
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 516.3 Definitions.
####### Small number of animals

means equal to or less than 50,000 horses; 80,000 dogs; 150,000 cats; 310,000 cattle; 1,450,000 pigs; 14,000,000 turkeys; and 72,000,000 chickens.
