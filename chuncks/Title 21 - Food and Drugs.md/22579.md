
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart E - Surgical Devices
###### ยง 878.4350 Cryosurgical unit and accessories.
####### Identification,Cryosurgical unit with a liquid nitrogen cooled cryoprobe and accessories.

(a)-(1)A cryosurgical unit with a liquid nitrogen cooled cryoprobe and accessories is a device intended to destroy tissue during surgical procedures by applying extreme cold.
