###### ยง 862.1780 Urinary calculi (stones) test system.
####### Identification.

(a)A urinary calculi (stones) test system is a device intended for the analysis of urinary calculi. Analysis of urinary calculi is used in the diagnosis and treatment of calculi of the urinary tract.
