
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart F - Immunological Test Systems
###### ยง 866.5600 Low-density lipoprotein immunological test system.
####### Classification.
######## Identification.,beta,beta,beta

(a)A-2-microglobulin immunological test system is a device that consists of the reagents used to measure by immunochemical techniques-2-microglobulin (a protein molecule) in serum, urine, and other body fluids. Measurement of-2-microglobulin aids in the diagnosis of active rheumatoid arthritis and kidney disease.
