###### ยง 184.1472 Menhaden oil.
####### Hydrogenated and partially hydrogenated menhaden oils.

(b)(1) Partially hydrogenated and hydrogenated menhaden oils are prepared by feeding hydrogen gas under pressure to a converter containing crude menhaden oil and a nickel catalyst. The reaction is begun at 150 to 160 &#xB0;C and after 1 hour the temperature is raised to 180 &#xB0;C until the desired degree of hydrogenation is reached. Hydrogenated menhaden oil is fully hydrogenated.
