###### ยง 884.1640 Culdoscope and accessories.
####### Classification.

(b)(1) Class II (performance standards).

(2) Class I for culdoscope accessories that are not part of a specialized instrument or device delivery system; do not have adapters, connectors, channels, or do not have portals for electrosurgical, laser, or other power sources.
