
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Affirmed as GRAS
###### ยง 184.1890 &#x3B1;-Tocopherols.
####### dl

(2)-&#x3B1;-Tocopherol (CAS Reg. No. 10191-41-0) is a mixture of stereoisomers of 2,5,7,8-tetramethyl-2-(4&#x2032;,8&#x2032;,12&#x2032;-trimethyl-tridecyl)-6-chromanol. It is chemically synthesized by condensing racemic isophytol with trimethyl hydroquinone. It is a pale yellow viscous oil at room temperature.
