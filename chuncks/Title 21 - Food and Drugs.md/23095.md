##### Subpart B - Neurological Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 882.1880 Evoked response mechanical stimulator.
####### Identification.

(a)An evoked response mechanical stimulator is a device used to produce a mechanical stimulus or a series of mechanical stimuli for the purpose of measuring a patient's evoked response.
