
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1140 - CIGARETTES, SMOKELESS TOBACCO, AND COVERED TOBACCO PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Prohibition of Sale and Distribution to Persons Younger Than 18 Years of Age
###### ยง 1140.16 Conditions of manufacture, sale, and distribution.
####### Restriction on product names.

(a)A manufacturer shall not use a trade or brand name of a nontobacco product as the trade or brand name for a cigarette or smokeless tobacco product, except for a tobacco product whose trade or brand name was on both a tobacco product and a nontobacco product that were sold in the United States on January 1, 1995.
