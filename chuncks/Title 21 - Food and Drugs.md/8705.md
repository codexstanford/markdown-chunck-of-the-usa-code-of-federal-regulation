
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Drugs: General
#### PART 201 - LABELING
##### Subpart G - Specific Labeling Requirements for Specific Drug Products
###### ยง 201.327 Over-the-counter sunscreen drug products; required labeling based on effectiveness testing.
####### Test subsite.

(ii)Test subsites are the locations to which UV radiation is administered within a test site. At least five test subsites should receive UV doses within each test site. Test subsites should be at least 0.5 square centimeters (cm) in area and should be separated from each other by at least 0.8 cm. Each test subsite should be outlined with indelible ink.
