##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Affirmed as GRAS
###### ยง 184.1201 Calcium glycerophosphate.
####### D-,,L

(a) Calcium glycerophosphate (CHCaOP, CAS Reg. No. 27214-00-2) is a fine, white, odorless, almost tasteless, slightly hygroscopic powder. It is prepared by neutralizing glycerophosphoric acid with calcium hydroxide or calcium carbonate. The commercial product is a mixture of calcium &#x3B2;-, andand-&#x3B1;-glycerophosphate.
