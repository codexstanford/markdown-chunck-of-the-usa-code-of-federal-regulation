
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5955 Intermittent mandatory ventilation attachment.
####### Identification.

(a)An intermittent mandatory ventilation (IMV) attachment is a device attached to a mechanical ventilator that allows spontaneous breathing by a patient while providing mechanical ventilation at a preset rate.
