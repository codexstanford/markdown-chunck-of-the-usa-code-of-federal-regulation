
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 117.3 Definitions.
####### Validation

means obtaining and evaluating scientific and technical evidence that a control measure, combination of control measures, or the food safety plan as a whole, when properly implemented, is capable of effectively controlling the identified hazards.
