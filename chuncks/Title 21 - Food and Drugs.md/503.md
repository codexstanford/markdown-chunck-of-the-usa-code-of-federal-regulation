(a)FDA will withdraw accreditation from a third-party certification body:

(2) Following an evaluation and finding by FDA that the third-party certification body no longer complies with the applicable requirements of this subpart; or

(3) Following its refusal to allow FDA to access records under ยง 1.658 or to conduct an audit, assessment, or investigation necessary to ensure continued compliance with this subpart.
