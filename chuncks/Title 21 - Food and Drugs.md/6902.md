
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption

###### ยง 176.170 Components of paper and paperboard in contact with aqueous and fatty foods.
####### Analytical methods,Selection of extractability conditions.

(d)-(1)First ascertain the type of food product (table 1, paragraph (c) of this section) that is being packed commercially in the paper or paperboard and the normal conditions of thermal treatment used in packaging the type of food involved.
