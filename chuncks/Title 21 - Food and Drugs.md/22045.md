
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 872.3970 Interarticular disc prosthesis (interpositional implant).
####### Identification.

(a)An interarticular disc prosthesis (interpositional implant) is a device that is intended to be an interface between the natural articulating surface of the mandibular condyle and glenoid fossa.
