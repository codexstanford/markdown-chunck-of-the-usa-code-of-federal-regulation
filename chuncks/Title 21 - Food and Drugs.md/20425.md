
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 864.5950 Blood volume measuring device.
####### Identification.

(a)A blood volume measuring device is a manual, semiautomated, or automated system that is used to calculate the red cell mass, plasma volume, and total blood volume.
