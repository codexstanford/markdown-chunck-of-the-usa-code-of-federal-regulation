
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 874 - EAR, NOSE, AND THROAT DEVICES
##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 874.5900 External upper esophageal sphincter compression device.
####### Identification.

(a)An external upper esophageal sphincter compression device is a prescription device used to apply external pressure on the cricoid cartilage for the purpose of reducing the symptoms of laryngopharyngeal reflux disease.
