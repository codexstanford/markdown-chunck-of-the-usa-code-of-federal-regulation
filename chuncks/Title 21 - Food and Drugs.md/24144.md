
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 890 - PHYSICAL MEDICINE DEVICES
##### Subpart D - Physical Medicine Prosthetic Devices
###### ยง 890.3940 Wheelchair platform scale.
####### Identification.

(a)A wheelchair platform scale is a device with a base designed to accommodate a wheelchair. It is intended for medical purposes to weigh a person who is confined to a wheelchair.
