
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 806 - MEDICAL DEVICES; REPORTS OF CORRECTIONS AND REMOVALS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 806.2 Definitions.
####### Stock recovery

(m)means the correction or removal of a device that has not been marketed or that has not left the direct control of the manufacturer, i.e., the device is located on the premises owned, or under the control of, the manufacturer, and no portion of the lot, model, code, or other relevant unit involved in the corrective or removal action has been released for sale or use.
