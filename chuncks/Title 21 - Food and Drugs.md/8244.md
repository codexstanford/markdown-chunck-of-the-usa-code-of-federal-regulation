(a) Sucrose (CHO, CAS Reg. No. 57-50-11-1) sugar, cane sugar, or beet sugar is the chemical &#x3B2;-D-fructofuranosyl-&#x3B1;-D-glucopyranoside. Sucrose is obtained by crystallization from sugar cane or sugar beet juice that has been extracted by pressing or diffusion, then clarified and evaporated.

(b) The ingredient must be of a purity suitable for its intended use.

(c) In accordance with ยง 184.1(b)(1), the ingredient is used in food with no limitation other than current good manufacturing practice.
