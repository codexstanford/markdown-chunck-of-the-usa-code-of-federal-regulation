
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 184 - DIRECT FOOD SUBSTANCES AFFIRMED AS GENERALLY RECOGNIZED AS SAFE
##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Affirmed as GRAS
###### ยง 184.1212 Calcium pantothenate.
####### D-,D-,D-,DL-

(a) Calcium pantothenate ((CHNO)Ca, CAS Reg. No. of theisomer, 137-08-6) is a salt of pantothenic acid, one of the vitamins of the B complex. Only theisomer of pantothenic acid has vitamin activity, although both theisomer and theracemic mixture of calcium pantothenate are used in food. Commercial calcium pantothenate is prepared synthetically from isobutyraldehyde and formaldehyde via 1,1-dimethyl-2-hydroxy-propionaldehyde and pantolactone.
