
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 814.3 Definitions.
####### Serious, adverse health consequences

(l)means any significant adverse experience, including those which may be either life-threatening or involve permanent or long term injuries, but excluding injuries that are nonlife-threatening and that are temporary and reasonably reversible.
