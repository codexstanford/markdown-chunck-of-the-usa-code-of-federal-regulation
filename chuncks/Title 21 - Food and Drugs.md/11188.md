
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - Drugs for Human Use
#### PART 357 - MISCELLANEOUS INTERNAL DRUG PRODUCTS FOR OVER-THE-COUNTER HUMAN USE
##### Subpart C - Cholecystokinetic Drug Products
###### ยง 357.203 Definition.
####### Cholecystokinetic drug product.

A drug product that causes contraction of the gallbladder and is used during the course of diagnostic gallbladder studies (cholecystography).
