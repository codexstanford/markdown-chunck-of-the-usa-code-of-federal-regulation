##### Subpart B - Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 886.1800 Schirmer strip.
####### Identification.

(a)A Schirmer strip is a device made of filter paper or similar material intended to be inserted under a patient's lower eyelid to stimulate and evaluate formation of tears.
