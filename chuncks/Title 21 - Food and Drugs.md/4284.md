
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 115 - SHELL EGGS
##### ยง 115.50 Refrigeration of shell eggs held for retail distribution.
###### Order for diversion or destruction.,et seq.

(i)Any division office of FDA or any State or local agency acting under paragraph (d) of this section, upon finding shell eggs held in violation of this section, may serve upon the person in whose possession such eggs are found a written order that such eggs be diverted, under the supervision of an officer or employee of the issuing entity, for processing in accordance with the EPIA (21 U.S.C. 1031) or destroyed by or under the supervision of said division office, within 10 working days from the date of receipt of the order.
