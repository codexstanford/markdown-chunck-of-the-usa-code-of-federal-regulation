
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter A - General

##### Subpart O - Sanitary Transportation of Human and Animal Food
###### General Provisions
####### ยง 1.904 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Transportation equipment,e.g.,

means equipment used in food transportation operations,bulk and non-bulk containers, bins, totes, pallets, pumps, fittings, hoses, gaskets, loading systems, and unloading systems. Transportation equipment also includes a railcar not attached to a locomotive or a trailer not attached to a tractor.
