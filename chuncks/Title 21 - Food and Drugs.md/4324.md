
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 117.3 Definitions.
####### Known or reasonably foreseeable hazard

means a biological, chemical (including radiological), or physical hazard that is known to be, or has the potential to be, associated with the facility or the food.
