###### ยง 882.5891 Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulator to treat headache.
####### Identification.

(a)A transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulator to treat headache is a device used to apply an electrical current to a patient's cranium through electrodes placed on the skin.
