
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 121.3 Definitions.
####### Contaminant

means, for purposes of this part, any biological, chemical, physical, or radiological agent that may be added to food to intentionally cause illness, injury, or death.
