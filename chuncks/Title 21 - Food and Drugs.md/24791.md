
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter J - Radiological Health
#### PART 1020 - PERFORMANCE STANDARDS FOR IONIZING RADIATION EMITTING PRODUCTS
##### ยง 1020.20 Cold-cathode gas discharge tubes.
###### Cold-cathode gas discharge tube

means an electronic device in which electron flow is produced and sustained by ionization of contained gas atoms and ion bombardment of the cathode.
