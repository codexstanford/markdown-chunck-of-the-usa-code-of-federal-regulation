
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 163 - CACAO PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Requirements for Specific Standardized Cacao Products
###### ยง 163.110 Cacao nibs.
####### L,L

(2) Neutralizing agents. Phosphoric acid, citric acid, and-tartaric acid, added as such, or in aqueous solution. For each 100 parts by weight of cacao nibs, used as such, or before shelling from the cacao beans, the total quantity of phosphoric acid used is not greater than 0.5 part by weight, expressed as PO. The total amount, singly or in combination, of citric acid and-tartaric acid is not greater than 1.0 part by weight.
