
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1107 - EXEMPTION REQUESTS AND SUBSTANTIAL EQUIVALENCE REPORTS
##### Subpart A - Exemptions
###### ยง 1107.1 Exemptions.
####### Exemption determination.

(c)FDA will review the information submitted and determine whether to grant or deny an exemption request based on whether the criteria in section 905(j)(3) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act are met. FDA may request additional information if necessary to make a determination. FDA will consider the exemption request withdrawn if the information is not provided within the requested timeframe.
