
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 114 - ACIDIFIED FOODS
##### Subpart E - Production and Process Controls
###### ยง 114.90 Methodology.
####### Semisolid products.

(iii)Food products of a semisolid consistency, such as puddings, potato salad, etc., may be blended to a paste consistency, and the pH may be determined on the prepared paste. If more fluidity is required, 10 to 20 milliliters of distilled water may be added to 100 grams of product. Adjust the temperature of the prepared paste to 25 &#xB0;C and determine its pH.
