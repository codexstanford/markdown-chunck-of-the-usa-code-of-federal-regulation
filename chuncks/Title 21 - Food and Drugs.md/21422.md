
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5740 Tracheal/bronchial differential ventilation tube.
####### Identification.

(a)A tracheal/bronchial differential ventilation tube is a device used to isolate the left or the right lung of a patient for anesthesia or pulmonary function testing.
