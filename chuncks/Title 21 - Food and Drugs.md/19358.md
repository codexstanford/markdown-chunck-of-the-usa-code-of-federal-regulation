
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart E - Responsibilities of Investigators
###### ยง 812.110 Specific responsibilities of investigators.
####### Supervising device use.

(c)An investigator shall permit an investigational device to be used only with subjects under the investigator's supervision. An investigator shall not supply an investigational device to any person not authorized under this part to receive it.
