
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1100 - General
##### Subpart A - Tobacco Products Subject to FDA Authority
###### ยง 1100.1 Scope.
####### tobacco product

In addition to FDA's authority over cigarettes, cigarette tobacco, roll-your-own tobacco, smokeless tobacco, and any tobacco product containing nicotine not made or derived from tobacco, FDA deems all other products meeting the definition ofunder section 201(rr) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, except accessories of such other tobacco products, to be subject to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
