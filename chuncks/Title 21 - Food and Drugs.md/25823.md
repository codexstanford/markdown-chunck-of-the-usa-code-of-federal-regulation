
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart C - Donor Eligibility
###### § 1271.45 What requirements does this subpart contain?
####### Prohibition on use.

(c)An HCT/P must not be implanted, transplanted, infused, or transferred until the donor has been determined to be eligible, except as provided under §§ 1271.60(d), 1271.65(b), and 1271.90 of this subpart.
