
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 189 - SUBSTANCES PROHIBITED FROM USE IN HUMAN FOOD
##### Subpart B - Prohibited Cattle Materials
###### ยง 189.5 Prohibited cattle materials.
####### Specified risk material

(5)means the brain, skull, eyes, trigeminal ganglia, spinal cord, vertebral column (excluding the vertebrae of the tail, the transverse processes of the thoracic and lumbar vertebrae, and the wings of the sacrum), and dorsal root ganglia of cattle 30 months of age and older and the tonsils and distal ileum of the small intestine of all cattle.
