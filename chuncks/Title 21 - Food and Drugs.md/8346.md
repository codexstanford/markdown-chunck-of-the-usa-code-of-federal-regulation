
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 189 - SUBSTANCES PROHIBITED FROM USE IN HUMAN FOOD
##### Subpart C - Substances Generally Prohibited From Direct Addition or Use as Human Food
###### ยง 189.165 Nordihydroguaiaretic acid (NDGA).

(a) Nordihydroguaiaretic acid is the chemical 4,4&#x2032;-(2,3-dimethyltetramethylene) dipyrocatechol, CHO. It occurs naturally in the resinous exudates of certain plants. The commercial product, which is synthesized, has been used as an antioxidant in foods.

(b) Food containing any added NDGA is deemed to be adulterated in violation of the act based upon an order published in theof April 11, 1968 (33 FR 5619).
