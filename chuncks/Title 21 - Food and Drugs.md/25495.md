
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1140 - CIGARETTES, SMOKELESS TOBACCO, AND COVERED TOBACCO PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Prohibition of Sale and Distribution to Persons Younger Than 18 Years of Age
###### ยง 1140.16 Conditions of manufacture, sale, and distribution.
####### Vending machines, self-service displays, mail-order sales, and other "impersonal" modes of sale.

(c)(1) Except as otherwise provided under this section, a retailer may sell cigarettes and smokeless tobacco only in a direct, face-to-face exchange between the retailer and the consumer. Examples of methods of sale that are not permitted include vending machines and self-service displays.
