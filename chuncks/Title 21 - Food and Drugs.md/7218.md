(iii)Poly-1-butene resins have a density of 0.904 to 0.920 gms/cm, and butene/ethylene copolymers have a density of 0.890 to 0.916 gms/cmas determined by ASTM method D1505-68 (Reapproved 1979), "Standard Test Method for Density of Plastics by the Density-Gradient Technique," which is incorporated by reference.
