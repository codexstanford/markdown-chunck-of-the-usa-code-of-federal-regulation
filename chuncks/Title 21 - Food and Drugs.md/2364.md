
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

####### Uses and restrictions.

(c)The color additive luminescent zinc sulfide may be safely used for coloring externally applied facial makeup preparations and nail polish included under ยง 720.4(c)(7)(ix) and (c)(8)(v) of this chapter, respectively, to the following restrictions:
