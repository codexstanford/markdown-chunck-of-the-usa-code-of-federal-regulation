
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter K - Tobacco Products
#### PART 1141 - CIGARETTE PACKAGE AND ADVERTISING WARNINGS
##### Subpart B - Cigarette Package and Advertising Warnings
###### ยง 1141.10 Required warnings.
####### Irremovable or permanent warnings.

(c)The required warnings shall be indelibly printed on or permanently affixed to the package or advertisement. Such warnings, for example, must not be printed or placed on a label affixed to a clear outer wrapper that is likely to be removed to access the product within the package.
