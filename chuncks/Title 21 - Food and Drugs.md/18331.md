
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics
#### PART 610 - GENERAL BIOLOGICAL PRODUCTS STANDARDS
##### Subpart B - General Provisions
###### ยง 610.15 Constituent materials.
####### Extraneous protein; cell culture produced vaccines.

(b)Extraneous protein known to be capable of producing allergenic effects in human subjects shall not be added to a final virus medium of cell culture produced vaccines intended for injection. If serum is used at any stage, its calculated concentration in the final medium shall not exceed 1:1,000,000.
