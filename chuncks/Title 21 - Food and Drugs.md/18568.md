
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics
#### PART 640 - ADDITIONAL STANDARDS FOR HUMAN BLOOD AND BLOOD PRODUCTS
##### Subpart I - Plasma Protein Fraction (Human)
###### ยง 640.91 Processing.
####### Stabilizer.

(f)Either 0.08+/-0.016 millimole sodium caprylate, or 0.08+/-0.016 millimole sodium acetyltryptophanate and 0.08+/-0.016 millimole sodium caprylate per gram of protein shall be present as a stabilizer(s). Calculations of the stabilizer concentration may employ the labeled value 5 percent for the protein concentration of the product.
