
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart E - Surgical Devices
###### ยง 878.4960 Operating tables and accessories and operating chairs and accessories.
####### Identification.

(a)Operating tables and accessories and operating chairs and accessories are AC-powered or air-powered devices, usually with movable components, intended for use during diagnostic examinations or surgical procedures to support and position a patient.
