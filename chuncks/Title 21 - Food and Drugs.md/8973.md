
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Drugs: General
#### PART 209 - REQUIREMENT FOR AUTHORIZED DISPENSERS AND PHARMACIES TO DISTRIBUTE A SIDE EFFECTS STATEMENT
##### Subpart B - Requirements
###### ยง 209.10 Content and format of the side effects statement.
####### Content.

(a)The side effects statement provided with each prescription drug product approved under section 505 of the act must read: "Call your doctor for medical advice about side effects. You may report side effects to FDA at 1-800-FDA-1088."
