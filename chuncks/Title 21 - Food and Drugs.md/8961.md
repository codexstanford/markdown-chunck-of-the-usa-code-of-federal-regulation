
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Drugs: General
#### PART 208 - MEDICATION GUIDES FOR PRESCRIPTION DRUG PRODUCTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 208.3 Definitions.
####### Serious risk or serious adverse effect

(k)means an adverse drug experience, or the risk of such an experience, as that term is defined in §§ 310.305, 312.32, 314.80, and 600.80 of this chapter.
