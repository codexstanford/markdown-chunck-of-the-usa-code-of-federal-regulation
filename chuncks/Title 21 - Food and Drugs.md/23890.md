###### ยง 888.3170 Elbow joint radial (hemi-elbow) polymer prosthesis.
####### Identification.

(a)An elbow joint radial (hemi-elbow) polymer prosthesis is a device intended to be implanted made of medical grade silicone elastomer used to replace the proximal end of the radius.
