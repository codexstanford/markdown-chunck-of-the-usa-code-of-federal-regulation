##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5330 Breathing gas mixer.
####### Identification.

(a)A breathing gas mixer is a device intended for use in conjunction with a respiratory support apparatus to control the mixing of gases that are to be breathed by a patient.
