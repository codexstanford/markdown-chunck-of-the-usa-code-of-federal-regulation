
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 589 - SUBSTANCES PROHIBITED FROM USE IN ANIMAL FOOD OR FEED
##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Prohibited From Use in Animal Food or Feed
###### ยง 589.2001 Cattle materials prohibited in animal food or feed to prevent the transmission of bovine spongiform encephalopathy.
####### Purpose.

(a)The purpose of this section is to prohibit the use of certain cattle origin materials in the food or feed of all animals to further reduce the risk of the spread of bovine spongiform encephalopathy (BSE) within the United States.
