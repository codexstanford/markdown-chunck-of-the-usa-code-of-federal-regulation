
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 872 - DENTAL DEVICES
##### Subpart D - Prosthetic Devices
###### ยง 872.3530 Mechanical denture cleaner.
####### Identification.

(a)A mechanical denture cleaner is a device, usually AC-powered, that consists of a container for mechanically agitating a denture cleansing solution. The device is intended to clean a denture by submersion in the agitating cleansing solution in the container.
