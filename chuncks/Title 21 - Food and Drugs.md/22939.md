
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 880.6350 Battery-powered medical examination light.
####### Identification.

(a)A battery-powered medical examination light is a battery-powered device intended for medical purposes that is used to illuminate body surfaces and cavities during a medical examination.
