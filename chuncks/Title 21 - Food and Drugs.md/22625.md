
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 878.4550 Autofluorescence detection device for general surgery and dermatological use.
####### Identification.

(a)An autofluorescence detection device for general surgery and dermatological use is an adjunct tool that uses autofluorescence to detect tissues or structures. This device is not intended to provide a diagnosis.
