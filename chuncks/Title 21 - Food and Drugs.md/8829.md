
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Drugs: General
#### PART 203 - PRESCRIPTION DRUG MARKETING
##### Subpart F - Request and Receipt Forms, Reports, and Records
###### ยง 203.60 Request and receipt forms, reports, and records.
####### Retention of request and receipt forms, reports, lists, records, and other documents.

(d)Any person required to create or maintain reports, lists, or other records under PDMA, PDA, or this part, including records relating to the distribution of drug samples, shall retain them for at least 3 years after the date of their creation.
