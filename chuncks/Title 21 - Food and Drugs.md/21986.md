#### PART 872 - DENTAL DEVICES
##### Subpart D - Prosthetic Devices
###### ยง 872.3640 Endosseous dental implant.
####### Classification.

(b)(1) Class II (special controls). The device is classified as class II if it is a root-form endosseous dental implant. The root-form endosseous dental implant is characterized by four geometrically distinct types: Basket, screw, solid cylinder, and hollow cylinder. The guidance document entitled "Class II Special Controls Guidance Document: Root-Form Endosseous Dental Implants and Endosseous Dental Implant Abutments" will serve as the special control.
