
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 522 - IMPLANTATION OR INJECTABLE DOSAGE FORM NEW ANIMAL DRUGS
##### ยง 522.1451 Moxidectin microspheres for injection.
###### Specifications.

(a)The drug product consists of two separate vials. One vial contains 10 percent moxidectin microspheres and the second vial contains a vehicle for constitution of the moxidectin microspheres.

(1) Each milliliter (mL) of constituted suspension contains 3.4 milligrams (mg) moxidectin.

(2) Each mL of constituted suspension contains 10 mg moxidectin.
