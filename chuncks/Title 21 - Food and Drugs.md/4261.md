
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 114 - ACIDIFIED FOODS
##### Subpart E - Production and Process Controls
###### ยง 114.90 Methodology.
####### Temperature.

(ii)To obtain accurate results, a uniform temperature should be maintained for the electrodes, the standard buffer solutions, and the samples. Tests should be made at a temperature between 20&#xB0; and 30 &#xB0;C, the optimum being 25 &#xB0;C. Any temperature determinations made without meter compensation may affect pH values. An automatic temperature compensator may be used.
