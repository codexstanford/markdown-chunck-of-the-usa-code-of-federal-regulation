
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

(1) Software performance and functional requirements including detailed design specifications (algorithms or control characteristics, alarms, device limitations, and safety requirements).
