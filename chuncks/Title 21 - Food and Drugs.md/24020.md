##### Subpart E - Surgical Devices
###### ยง 888.4300 Depth gauge for clinical use.
####### Identification.

(a)A depth gauge for clinical use is a measuring device intended for various medical purposes, such as to determine the proper length of screws for fastening the ends of a fractured bone.
