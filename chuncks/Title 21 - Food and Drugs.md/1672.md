
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

####### short form

(2) Awritten consent document stating that the elements of informed consent required by ยง 50.25 have been presented orally to the subject or the subject's legally authorized representative. When this method is used, there shall be a witness to the oral presentation. Also, the IRB shall approve a written summary of what is to be said to the subject or the representative.
