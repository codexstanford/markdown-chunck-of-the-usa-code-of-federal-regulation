##### Subpart F - Neurological Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 882.5200 Aneurysm clip.
####### Identification.

(a)An aneurysm clip is a device used to occlude an intracranial aneurysm (a balloonlike sac formed on a blood vessel) to prevent it from bleeding or bursting.
