
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1 - GENERAL ENFORCEMENT REGULATIONS
##### Subpart S - Additional Traceability Records for Certain Foods
###### Records Maintenance and Availability
####### ยง 1.1455 How must records required by this subpart be maintained and made available?
######## General requirements for records.

(a)(1) You must keep records as original paper or electronic records or true copies (such as photocopies, pictures, scanned copies, or other accurate reproductions of the original records). Electronic records may include valid, working electronic links to the information required to be maintained under this subpart.

(2) All records must be legible and stored to prevent deterioration or loss.
