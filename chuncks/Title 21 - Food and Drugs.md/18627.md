
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics
#### PART 660 - ADDITIONAL STANDARDS FOR DIAGNOSTIC SUBSTANCES FOR LABORATORY TESTS
##### Subpart C - Blood Grouping Reagent
###### ยง 660.25 Potency tests without reference preparations.
####### Potency requirements.

(a)Blood Grouping Reagents recommended for the test tube methods, including the indirect antiglobulin tests, shall have the following potency titer values, unless other values are approved by the Director, Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research.

(1) For Anti-K, Anti-k, Anti-Jk, Anti-Fy, Anti-C, at least 1 + reaction with a 1:8 dilution of the reagent.

(2) For Anti-S, Anti-s, Anti-P, Anti-M, Anti-I, Anti-e (saline), Anti-c(saline), and Anti-A, at least 1 + reaction with a 1:4 dilution of the reagent.

(3) For Anti-U, Anti-Kp, Anti-Kp, Anti-Js, Anti-Js, Anti-Fy, Anti-N, Anti-Le, Anti-Le, Anti-Lu, Anti-Lu, Anti-Di, Anti-M, Anti-Jk, Anti-Co, Anti-Wr, and Anti-Xg, at least 2 + reaction with undiluted reagent.
