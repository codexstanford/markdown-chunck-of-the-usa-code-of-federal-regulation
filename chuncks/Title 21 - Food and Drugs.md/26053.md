
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter II - Drug Enforcement Administration, Department of Justice
### PART 1300 - DEFINITIONS
#### ยง 1300.02 Definitions relating to listed chemicals.
##### Retail distributor

means a grocery store, general merchandise store, drug store, or other entity or person whose activities as a distributor relating to drug products containing ephedrine, pseudoephedrine, or phenylpropanolamine are limited almost exclusively to sales for personal use, both in number of sales and volume of sales, either directly to walk-in customers or in face-to-face transactions by direct sales. Also for the purposes of this paragraph, a "grocery store" is an entity within Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code 5411, a "general merchandise store" is an entity within SIC codes 5300 through 5399 and 5499, and a "drug store" is an entity within SIC code 5912.
