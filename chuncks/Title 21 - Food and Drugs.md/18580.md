
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

###### ยง 640.100 Immune Globulin (Human).
####### Proper name and definition.

(a)The proper name of this product shall be Immune Globulin (Human). The product is defined as a sterile solution containing antibodies derived from human plasma.
