
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 189 - SUBSTANCES PROHIBITED FROM USE IN HUMAN FOOD
##### Subpart D - Substances Prohibited From Indirect Addition to Human Food Through Food-Contact Surfaces
###### ยง 189.250 Mercaptoimidazoline and 2-mercaptoimidazoline.

(a) Mercaptoimidazoline and 2-mercaptoimidazoline both have the molecular formula CHNS. They are synthetic chemicals not found in natural products and have been used in the production of rubber articles that may come into contact with food.

(b) Food containing any added or delectable levels of these substances is deemed to be adulterated in violation of the act based upon an order published in theof November 30, 1973 (38 FR 33072).
