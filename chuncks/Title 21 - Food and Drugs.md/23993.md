
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

#### PART 888 - ORTHOPEDIC DEVICES
##### Subpart D - Prosthetic Devices
###### ยง 888.3730 Toe joint phalangeal (hemi-toe) polymer prosthesis.
####### Identification.

(a)A toe joint phalangeal (hemi-toe) polymer prosthesis is a device made of silicone elastomer intended to be implanted to replace the base of the proximal phalanx of the toe.
