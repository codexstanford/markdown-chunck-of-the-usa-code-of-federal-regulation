
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter D - Drugs for Human Use

###### ยง 312.300 General.
####### Immediately life-threatening disease or condition

means a stage of disease in which there is reasonable likelihood that death will occur within a matter of months or in which premature death is likely without early treatment.
