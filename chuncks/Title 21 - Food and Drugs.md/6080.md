
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Food for Human Consumption
#### PART 163 - CACAO PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Requirements for Specific Standardized Cacao Products
###### ยง 163.155 Milk chocolate and vegetable fat coating.
####### Nomenclature.

(c)The name of the food is "milk chocolate and vegetable fat coating" or "skim milk chocolate and vegetable fat coating", as appropriate. Alternatively, the common or usual name of the vegetable derived fat ingredient may be used in the name of the food, e.g., "milk chocolate and ______ oil coating", the blank being filled in with the common or usual name of the specific vegetable fat used.
