### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

##### Subpart E - Neurological Surgical Devices
###### ยง 882.4400 Radiofrequency lesion generator.
####### Identification.

(a)A radiofrequency lesion generator is a device used to produce lesions in the nervous system or other tissue by the direct application of radiofrequency currents to selected sites.
