###### ยง 874.3302 Bone-conduction hearing aid.
####### Identification.

(a)A bone-conduction hearing aid is a wearable sound-amplifying device intended to compensate for impaired hearing and that conducts sound to the inner ear through the skull. The non-implantable components of a bone-conduction hearing aid, such as the external sound processor, are subject to the requirements in ยง 801.422 of this chapter.
