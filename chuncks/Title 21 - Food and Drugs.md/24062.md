
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 890 - PHYSICAL MEDICINE DEVICES
##### Subpart B - Physical Medicine Diagnostic Devices
###### ยง 890.1850 Diagnostic muscle stimulator.
####### Identification.

(a)A diagnostic muscle stimulator is a device used mainly with an electromyograph machine to initiate muscle activity. It is intended for medical purposes, such as to diagnose motor nerve or sensory neuromuscular disorders and neuromuscular function.
