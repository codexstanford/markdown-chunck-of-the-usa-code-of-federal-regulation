
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter A - General

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 10.3 Definitions.
####### Administrative action

includes every act, including the refusal or failure to act, involved in the administration of any law by the Commissioner, except that it does not include the referral of apparent violations to U.S. attorneys for the institution of civil or criminal proceedings or an act in preparation of a referral.
