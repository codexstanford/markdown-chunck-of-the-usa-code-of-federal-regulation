###### ยง 882.1855 Electroencephalogram (EEG) telemetry system.
####### Identification.

(a)An electroencephalogram (EEG) telemetry system consists of transmitters, receivers, and other components used for remotely monitoring or measuring EEG signals by means of radio or telephone transmission systems.
