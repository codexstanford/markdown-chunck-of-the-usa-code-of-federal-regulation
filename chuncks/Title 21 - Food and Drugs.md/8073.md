###### ยง 184.1408 Licorice and licorice derivatives.
####### Glycyrrhiza glabra,Glycyrrhiza.

(a)(1) Licorice (glycyrrhiza) root is the dried and ground rhizome and root portions ofor other species ofLicorice extract is that portion of the licorice root that is, after maceration, extracted by boiling water. The extract can be further purified by filtration and by treatment with acids and ethyl alcohol. Licorice extract is sold as a liquid, paste ("block"), or spray-dried powder.

(2) Ammoniated glycyrrhizin is prepared from the water extract of licorice root by acid precipitation followed by neutralization with dilute ammonia. Monoammonium glycyrrhizinate (CHONH5HO, CAS Reg. No. 1407-03-0) is prepared from ammoniated glycyrrhizin by solvent extraction and separation techniques.
