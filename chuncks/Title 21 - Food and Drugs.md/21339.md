
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5270 Breathing system heater.
####### Identification.

(a)A breathing system heater is a device that is intended to warm breathing gases before they enter a patient's airway. The device may include a temperature controller.
