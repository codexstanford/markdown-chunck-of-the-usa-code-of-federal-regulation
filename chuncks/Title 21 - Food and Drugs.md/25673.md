
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter L - Regulations Under Certain Other Acts Administered by the Food and Drug Administration
#### PART 1240 - CONTROL OF COMMUNICABLE DISEASES
##### Subpart D - Specific Administrative Decisions Regarding Interstate Shipments
###### ยง 1240.62 Turtles intrastate and interstate requirements.
####### Definition.

(a)As used in this section the term "turtles" includes all animals commonly known as turtles, tortoises, terrapins, and all other animals of the order Testudinata, class Reptilia, except marine species (families Dermachelidae and Chelonidae).
