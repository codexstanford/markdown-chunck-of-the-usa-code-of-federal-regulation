
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter D - Drugs for Human Use
#### PART 312 - INVESTIGATIONAL NEW DRUG APPLICATION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 312.3 Definitions and interpretations.
####### Independent ethics committee (IEC)

means a review panel that is responsible for ensuring the protection of the rights, safety, and well-being of human subjects involved in a clinical investigation and is adequately constituted to provide assurance of that protection. An institutional review board (IRB), as defined in ยง 56.102(g) of this chapter and subject to the requirements of part 56 of this chapter, is one type of IEC.
