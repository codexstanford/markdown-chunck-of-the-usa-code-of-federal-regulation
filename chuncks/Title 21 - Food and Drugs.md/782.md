
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter A - General

##### Subpart S - Additional Traceability Records for Certain Foods
###### General Provisions
####### ยง 1.1310 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Critical tracking event

means an event in the supply chain of a food involving the harvesting, cooling (before initial packing), initial packing of a raw agricultural commodity other than a food obtained from a fishing vessel, first land-based receiving of a food obtained from a fishing vessel, shipping, receiving, or transformation of the food.
