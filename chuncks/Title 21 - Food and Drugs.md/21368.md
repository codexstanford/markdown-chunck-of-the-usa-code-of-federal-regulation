
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5440 Portable oxygen generator.
####### Identification.

(a)A portable oxygen generator is a device that is intended to release oxygen for respiratory therapy by means of either a chemical reaction or physical means (e.g., a molecular sieve).
