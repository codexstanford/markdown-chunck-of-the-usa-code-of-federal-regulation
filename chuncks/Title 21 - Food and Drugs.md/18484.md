
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter F - Biologics
#### PART 640 - ADDITIONAL STANDARDS FOR HUMAN BLOOD AND BLOOD PRODUCTS
##### Subpart C - Platelets
###### ยง 640.25 General requirements.
####### Storage.

(a)Immediately after resuspension, Platelets shall be placed in storage at the selected temperature range. If stored at 20 to 24 &#xB0;C, a continuous gentle agitation of the platelet concentrate shall be maintained throughout the storage period. Agitation is optional if stored at a temperature between 1 and 6 &#xB0;C.
