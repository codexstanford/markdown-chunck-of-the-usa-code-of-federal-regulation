
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products

##### ยง 520.602 Dichlorvos gel.
###### Conditions of use in horses,Amount.

(c)-(1)Administer 20 mg per kilogram of body weight for the removal of bots and ascarids. Repeat administration every 21 to 28 days for the control of bots and ascarids.
