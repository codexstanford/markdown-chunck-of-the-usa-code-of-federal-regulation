##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 807.3 Definitions.
####### Wholesale distributor

(t)means any person (other than the manufacturer or the initial importer) who distributes a device from the original place of manufacture to the person who makes the final delivery or sale of the device to the ultimate consumer or user.
