
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 589 - SUBSTANCES PROHIBITED FROM USE IN ANIMAL FOOD OR FEED
##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Prohibited From Use in Animal Food or Feed
###### ยง 589.2000 Animal proteins prohibited in ruminant feed.
####### Ruminant

(7)includes any member of the order of animals which has a stomach with four chambers (rumen, reticulum, omasum, and abomasum) through which feed passes in digestion. The order includes, but is not limited to, cattle, buffalo, sheep, goats, deer, elk, and antelopes.
