
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5870 Nonrebreathing valve.
####### Identification.

(a)A nonrebreathing valve is a one-way valve that directs breathing gas flow to the patient and vents exhaled gases into the atmosphere.
