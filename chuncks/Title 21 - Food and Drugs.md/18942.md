
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter H - Medical Devices
#### PART 801 - LABELING
##### Subpart A - General Labeling Provisions
###### ยง 801.3 Definitions.
####### Implantable device,implantable device

means a device that is intended to be placed in a surgically or naturally formed cavity of the human body. A device is regarded as anfor the purpose of this part only if it is intended to remain implanted continuously for a period of 30 days or more, unless the Commissioner of Food and Drugs determines otherwise in order to protect human health.
