
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 117.3 Definitions.
####### Batter

means a semifluid substance, usually composed of flour and other ingredients, into which principal components of food are dipped or with which they are coated, or which may be used directly to form bakery foods.
