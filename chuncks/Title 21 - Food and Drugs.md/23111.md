### Subchapter H - Medical Devices

##### Subpart E - Neurological Surgical Devices
###### ยง 882.4100 Ventricular catheter.
####### Identification.

(a)A ventricular catheter is a device used to gain access to the cavities of the brain for injection of material into, or removal of material from, the brain.
