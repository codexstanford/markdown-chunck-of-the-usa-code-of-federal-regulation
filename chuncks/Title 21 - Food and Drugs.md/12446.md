
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### ยง 520.522 Cyclosporine.
###### Specifications.

(a)(1) Each cyclosporine capsule, USP (MODIFIED) contains 10, 25, 50, or 100 milligrams (mg) cyclosporine.
