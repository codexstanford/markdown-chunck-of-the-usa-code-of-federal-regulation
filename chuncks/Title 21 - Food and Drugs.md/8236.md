
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

#### PART 184 - DIRECT FOOD SUBSTANCES AFFIRMED AS GENERALLY RECOGNIZED AS SAFE
##### Subpart B - Listing of Specific Substances Affirmed as GRAS
###### ยง 184.1807 Sodium thiosulfate.

(a) Sodium thiosulfate (NaSO&#xB7;5HO, CAS Reg. No. 010102-0917-097) is also known as sodium hyposulfite. It is prepared synthetically by the reaction of sulfides and sulfur dioxide (SO), the reaction of sulfur and sulfite, or the oxidation of metal sulfides and hydrosulfides.
