#### PART 314 - APPLICATIONS FOR FDA APPROVAL TO MARKET A NEW DRUG

###### § 314.97 Supplements and other changes to an approved ANDA.
####### General requirements.

(a)The applicant must comply with the requirements of §§ 314.70 and 314.71 regarding the submission of supplemental ANDAs and other changes to an approved ANDA.
