
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 522 - IMPLANTATION OR INJECTABLE DOSAGE FORM NEW ANIMAL DRUGS
##### § 522.558 Dexmedetomidine.
###### Conditions of use,Dogs,Indications for use and amount.

(c)-(1)-(i)(A) For use as a sedative and analgesic to facilitate clinical examinations, clinical procedures, minor surgical procedures, and minor dental procedures, administer 375 micrograms (µg) per square meter (/m) of body surface area by intravenous injection or 500 µg/mof body surface area by intramuscular injection.

(B) For use as a preanesthetic to general anesthesia, administer 125 µg/mof body surface area or 375 µg/mof body surface area by intramuscular injection.
