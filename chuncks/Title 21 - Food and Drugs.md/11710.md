
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter E - Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products
#### PART 510 - NEW ANIMAL DRUGS
##### Subpart E - Requirements for Specific New Animal Drugs
###### ยง 510.455 Requirements for free-choice medicated feeds.
####### When is a medicated feed mill license required for the manufacture of a free-choice medicated feed?

(f)An approved medicated feed mill license is required for the manufacture of the following types of feeds:

(1) All free-choice medicated feeds that contain a Category II drug, and

(2) Free-choice medicated feeds that contain a Category I drug and use a proprietary formula and/or specifications.
