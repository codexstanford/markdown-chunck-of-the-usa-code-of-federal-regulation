
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs
## Chapter I - Food and Drug Administration, Department of Health and Human Services
### Subchapter C - Drugs: General
#### PART 251 - SECTION 804 IMPORTATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 251.2 Definitions.
####### Life-threatening adverse event,i.e.,

means any adverse event that places the patient, in the view of the initial reporter, at immediate risk of death from the adverse event as it occurred,it does not include an adverse event that, had it occurred in a more severe form, might have caused death.
