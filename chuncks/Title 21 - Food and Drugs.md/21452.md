
# Title 21 - Food and Drugs

##### Subpart F - Therapeutic Devices
###### ยง 868.5905 Noncontinuous ventilator (IPPB).
####### Identification.

(a)A noncontinuous ventilator (intermittent positive pressure breathing-IPPB) is a device intended to deliver intermittently an aerosol to a patient's lungs or to assist a patient's breathing.
