
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

#### PART 156 - LABELING REQUIREMENTS FOR PESTICIDES AND DEVICES

####### Gases applied outdoors.

(ii)For products that are formulated or applied as a gas (space and soil fumigants) and that may be used outdoors, the respiratory protection statement shall be: "For handling activities outdoors, use either a respirator with an organic-vapor-removing cartridge with a prefilter approved for pesticides (MSHA/NIOSH approval number prefix TC-23C), or a canister approved for pesticides (MSHA/NIOSH approval number prefix TC-14G)."
