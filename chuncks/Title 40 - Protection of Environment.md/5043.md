
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Subpart H - Prevention of Air Pollution Emergency Episodes
###### § 51.150 Classification of regions for episode plans.
####### Priority II Region

(d)means any area which is not a Priority I region and has ambient concentrations between the following:

(1) Sulfur Dioxides-60-100 µg/m(0.02-0.04 ppm) annual arithmetic mean; 260-445 µg/m(0.10-0.17 ppm) 24-hour maximum; any concentration above 1,300 µg/m(0.50 ppm) three-hour average.

(2) Particulate matter-60-95 µg/mannual geometric mean; 150-325 µg/m24-hour maximum.

(e) In the absence of adequate monitoring data, appropriate models must be used to classify an area under paragraph (b) of this section, consistent with the requirements contained in § 51.112(a).

(f) Areas which do not meet the above criteria are classified Priority III.
