######## Non-selective catalytic reduction (NSCR)

means an add-on catalytic nitrogen oxides (NO) control device for rich burn engines that, in a two-step reaction, promotes the conversion of excess oxygen, NO, CO, and volatile organic compounds (VOC) into CO, nitrogen, and water.
