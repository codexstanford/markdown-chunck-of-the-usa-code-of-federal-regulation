(3) Operation during a demonstration period of at least 15 months, or other period of time as provided in paragraph (f)(1) of this section, that demonstrates the inability of the specific unit to meet the applicable emissions limitation under ยง 76.5, 76.6, or 76.7 and the minimum NOemissions rate that the specific unit can achieve during long-term load dispatch operation; and
