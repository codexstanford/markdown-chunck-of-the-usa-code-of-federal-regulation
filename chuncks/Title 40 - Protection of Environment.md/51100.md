
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### § 97.375 Petitions.
####### § 97.902 Definitions.
######## Compliance account

means an Allowance Management System account, established by the Administrator for a Texas SOTrading Program source under this subpart, in which any Texas SOTrading Program allowance allocations to the Texas SOTrading Program units at the source are recorded and in which are held any Texas SOTrading Program allowances available for use for a control period in a given year in complying with the source's Texas SOTrading Program emissions limitation in accordance with §§ 97.906 and 97.924.
