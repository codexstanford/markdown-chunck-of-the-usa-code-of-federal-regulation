######## Annual monitoring requirement.

(a)(1) The owner or operator of an affected unit subject both to an Acid Rain emission limitation and to a State or federal NOmass reduction program that adopts the provisions of this part must meet the requirements of this part during the entire calendar year.

(2) The owner or operator of an affected unit subject to a State or federal NOmass reduction program that adopts the provisions of this part and that requires monitoring and reporting of hourly emissions on an annual basis must meet the requirements of this part during the entire calendar year.
