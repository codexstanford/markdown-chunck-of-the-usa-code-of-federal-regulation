### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart II - Industrial Wastewater Treatment
###### ยง 98.358 Definitions.
####### Ethanol production

means an operation that produces ethanol from the fermentation of sugar, starch, grain, or cellulosic biomass feedstocks, or the production of ethanol synthetically from petrochemical feedstocks, such as ethylene or other chemicals.
