
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

##### Subpart B - Permit Application and Special NPDES Program Requirements
###### ยง 122.38 Public notification requirements for CSO discharges to the Great Lakes Basin.
####### Extending compliance to avoid undue economic hardship.

(e)The Director may extend the compliance dates in paragraphs (a), (b), and (c) of this section for individual communities if the Director determines the community needs additional time to comply in order to avoid undue economic hardship. Where the Director extends the compliance date of any of these requirements for a community, the Director shall notify the Regional Administrator of the extension and the reason for the extension. The Director shall post on its website a notice that includes the name of the community and the new compliance date(s). The notice shall remain on the Director's website until the new compliance date.
