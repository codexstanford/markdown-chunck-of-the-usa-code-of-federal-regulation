
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 53 - AMBIENT AIR MONITORING REFERENCE AND EQUIVALENT METHODS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 53.1 Definitions.
####### Class III equivalent method

means an equivalent method for PMor PMthat is an analyzer capable of providing PMor PMambient air measurements representative of one-hour or less integrated PMor PMconcentrations as well as 24-hour measurements determined as, or equivalent to, the mean of 24 one-hour consecutive measurements.
