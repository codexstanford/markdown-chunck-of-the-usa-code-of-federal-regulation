
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 702 - GENERAL PRACTICES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart A - Procedures for Prioritization of Chemical Substances for Risk Evaluation
###### ยง 702.3 Definitions.
####### Low-priority substance

means a chemical substance that EPA concludes, based on information sufficient to establish, without consideration of costs or other non-risk factors, does not meet the standard for a High-Priority Substance.
