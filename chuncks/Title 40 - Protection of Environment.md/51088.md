####### ยง 97.902 Definitions.
######## Alternate designated representative

means, for a Texas SOTrading Program source and each Texas SOTrading Program unit at the source, the natural person who is authorized by the owners and operators of the source and all such units at the source, in accordance with this subpart, to act on behalf of the designated representative in matters pertaining to the Texas SOTrading Program. If the Texas SOTrading Program source is also subject to the Acid Rain Program or CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 2 Trading Program, then this natural person shall be the same natural person as the alternate designated representative as defined in the respective program.
