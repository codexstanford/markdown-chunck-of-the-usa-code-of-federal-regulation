
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 279 - STANDARDS FOR THE MANAGEMENT OF USED OIL
##### Subpart D - Standards for Used Oil Collection Centers and Aggregation Points
###### ยง 279.32 Used oil aggregation points owned by the generator.
####### Applicability.

(a)This section applies to owners or operators of all used oil aggregation points. A used oil aggregation point is any site or facility that accepts, aggregates, and/or stores used oil collected only from other used oil generation sites owned or operated by the owner or operator of the aggregation point, from which used oil is transported to the aggregation point in shipments of no more than 55 gallons under the provisions of ยง 279.24(b). Used oil aggregation points may also accept used oil from household do-it-yourselfers.
