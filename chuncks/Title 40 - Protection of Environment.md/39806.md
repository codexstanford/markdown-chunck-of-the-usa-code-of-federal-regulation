###### Definitions
####### ยง 63.12005 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Container

means a portable unit in which a material can be stored, transported, treated, disposed of or otherwise handled. Examples of containers include, but are not limited to, drums, pails, and portable cargo containers known as "portable tanks" or "totes." Container does not include transport vehicles or barges.
