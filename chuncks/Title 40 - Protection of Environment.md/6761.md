
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Appendix S to Part 51 - Emission Offset Interpretative Ruling
###### Condition 1.

The new source is required to meet a more stringent emission limitationand/or the control of existing sources below allowable levels is required so that the source will not cause a violation of any NAAQS.
