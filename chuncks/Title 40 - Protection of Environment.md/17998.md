
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 60.51c Definitions.
####### Wet scrubber

means an add-on air pollution control device that utilizes an alkaline scrubbing liquor to collect particulate matter (including nonvaporous metals and condensed organics) and/or to absorb and neutralize acid gases.
