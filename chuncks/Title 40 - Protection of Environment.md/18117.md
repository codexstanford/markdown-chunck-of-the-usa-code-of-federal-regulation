
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 60.101a Definitions.
####### Flare gas recovery system

means a system of one or more compressors, piping and the associated water seal, rupture disk or similar device used to divert gas from the flare and direct the gas to the fuel gas system or to a fuel gas combustion device.
