######## Common designated representative's share

means, with regard to a specific common designated representative for a control period in a given year and a total amount of NOemissions from all CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 1 units in a State (and Indian country within the borders of such State) during such control period, the total tonnage of NOemissions during such control period from the group of one or more CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 1 units in such State (and such Indian country) having the common designated representative for such control period.
