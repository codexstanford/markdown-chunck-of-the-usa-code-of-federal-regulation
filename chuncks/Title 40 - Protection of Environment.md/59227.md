##### Subpart B - General Rules of Practice Concerning Proceedings (Other Than Expedited Hearings)
###### Administrative Law Judge
####### ยง 164.40 Qualifications and duties of Administrative Law Judge.
######## Absence or change of the Administrative Law Judge.

(e)In the case of the absence or unavailability of the Administrative Law Judge, or his inability to act, or his removal by disqualification or withdrawal, the powers and duties to be performed by him under this part in connection with a hearing assigned to him may, unless otherwise directed by the Administrator, be assigned to another Administrative Law Judge so designated to act by the Chief Administrative Law Judge, the Administrator or the Environmental Appeals Board.
