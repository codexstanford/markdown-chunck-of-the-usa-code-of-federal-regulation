
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

##### Subpart A - Environmental Program Grants
###### General
####### ยง 35.102 Definitions of terms.
######## National program guidance.

Guidance issued by EPA's National Program Managers for establishing and maintaining effective environmental programs. This guidance establishes national goals, objectives, and priorities as well as the core performance measures and other information to be used in monitoring progress. The guidance may also set out specific environmental strategies, criteria for evaluating programs, and other elements of program implementation.
