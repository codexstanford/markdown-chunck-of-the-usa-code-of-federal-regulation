
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart A - Production and Consumption Controls
###### ยง 84.3 Definitions.
####### Etching,e.g.,,e.g.,

means, in the context of semiconductor manufacturing, a process type that uses plasma-generated fluorine atoms and other reactive fluorine-containing fragments that chemically react with exposed thin films (dielectric, metals) or substrate (silicon) to selectively remove portions of material.
