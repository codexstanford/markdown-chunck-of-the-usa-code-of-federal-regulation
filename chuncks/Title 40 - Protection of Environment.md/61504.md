
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs

##### Subpart C - Specific Tolerances
###### ยง 180.552 Sulfosulfuron; tolerances for residues.
####### General.

(a)Tolerances are established for residues of the herbicide sulfosulfuron, 1-(4,6-dimethoxypyrimidin-2-yl)-3-[(2-ethanesulfonyl-imidazo[1,2-a]pyridine-3-yl) sulfonyl]urea and its metabolites converted to 2-(ethylsulfonyl)-imidazo[1,2-a]pyridine and calculated as sulfosulfuron in or on the raw agricultural commodities.
