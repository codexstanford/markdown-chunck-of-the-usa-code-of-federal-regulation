
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart MM - Suppliers of Petroleum Products
###### ยง 98.393 Calculating GHG emissions.
####### Calculation Method 1.

(1)To determine the emission factor (i.e., EFin Equation MM-1) for solid products, multiply the default carbon share factor (i.e., percent carbon by mass) in column B of Table MM-1 to this subpart for the appropriate product by 44/12. For all other products, use the default COemission factor listed in column C of Table MM-1 of this subpart for the appropriate product.
