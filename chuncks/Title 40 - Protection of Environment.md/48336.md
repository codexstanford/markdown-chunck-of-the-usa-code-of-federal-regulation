####### TCMs.
############# ยง 93.117 Criteria and procedures: Compliance with PMand PMcontrol measures.

The FHWA/FTA project must comply with any PMand PMcontrol measures in the applicable implementation plan. This criterion is satisfied if the project-level conformity determination contains a written commitment from the project sponsor to include in the final plans, specifications, and estimates for the project those control measures (for the purpose of limiting PMand PMemissions from the construction activities and/or normal use and operation associated with the project) that are contained in the applicable implementation plan.
