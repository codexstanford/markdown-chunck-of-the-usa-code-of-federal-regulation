
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart G - Criteria for Modifying the Secondary Treatment Requirements Under Section 301(h) of the Clean Water Act
###### ยง 125.58 Definitions.
####### Ocean waters

(n)means those coastal waters landward of the baseline of the territorial seas, the deep waters of the territorial seas, or the waters of the contiguous zone. The term "ocean waters" excludes saline estuarine waters.
