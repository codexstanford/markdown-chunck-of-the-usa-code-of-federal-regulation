
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 63.2 Definitions.
####### Commenced

means, with respect to construction or reconstruction of an affected source, that an owner or operator has undertaken a continuous program of construction or reconstruction or that an owner or operator has entered into a contractual obligation to undertake and complete, within a reasonable time, a continuous program of construction or reconstruction.
