
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 129 - TOXIC POLLUTANT EFFLUENT STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - Toxic Pollutant Effluent Standards and Prohibitions
###### § 129.103 Toxaphene.
####### New sources.

(ii)Discharges from a toxaphene manufacturer shall not contain toxaphene concentrations exceeding an average per working day of 0.1 µg/l calculated over any calendar month; and shall not exceed a monthly average daily loading of 0.000002 kg/kkg of toxaphene produced, and shall not exceed 0.5 µ/l in a sample(s) representing any working day.
