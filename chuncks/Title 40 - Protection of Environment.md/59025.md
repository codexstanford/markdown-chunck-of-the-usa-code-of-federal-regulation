
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 159.153 Definitions.
####### Pesticide

means a pesticide product which is or was registered by EPA, and each active ingredient, inert ingredient, impurity, metabolite, contaminant or degradate contained in, or derived from, such pesticide product.
