##### Subpart KK - National Emission Standards for the Printing and Publishing Industry
###### ยง 63.822 Definitions.
####### Publication rotogravure press

means a rotogravure press used for publication rotogravure printing. A publication rotogravure press may include one or more flexographic imprinters. A publication rotogravure press with one or more flexographic imprinters is not a flexographic press.
