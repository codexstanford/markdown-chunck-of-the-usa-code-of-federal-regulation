
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart J - Tank Systems
###### ยง 265.196 Response to leaks or spills and disposition of leaking or unfit-for-use tank systems.
####### Containment of visible releases to the environment.

(c)The owner or operator must immediately conduct a visual inspection of the release and, based upon that inspection:

(1) Prevent further migration of the leak or spill to soils or surface water; and

(2) Remove, and properly dispose of, any visible contamination of the soil or surface water.
