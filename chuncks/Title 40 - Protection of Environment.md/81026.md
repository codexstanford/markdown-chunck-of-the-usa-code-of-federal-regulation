####### Chemical substance and significant new uses subject to reporting.

(a)(1) The chemical substance 1,2,3,4,7,7-hexachloronorbornadiene, CAS Number 3389-71-7, is subject to reporting under this section for the significant new use described in paragraph (a)(2) of this section.
