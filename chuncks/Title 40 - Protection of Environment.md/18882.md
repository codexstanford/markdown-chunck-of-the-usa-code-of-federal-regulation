
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart RR - Standards of Performance for Pressure Sensitive Tape and Label Surface Coating Operations
###### ยง 60.441 Definitions and symbols.
####### Coating line

means any number or combination of adhesive, release, or precoat coating applicators, flashoff areas, and ovens which coat a continuous web, located between a web unwind station and a web rewind station, to produce pressure sensitive tape and label materials.
