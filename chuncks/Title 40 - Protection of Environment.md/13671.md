(4)To determine compliance with the limits in paragraph (b)(1)(i) of this section, the owner or operator shall calculate hourly SOemissions using all raw material sulfur charged into each affected emission unit and assume 100 percent conversion of total sulfur to SO. The owner or operator shall implement a compliance assurance plan (CAP) for all units except Boilerhouse 2 and any idled units that shall specify the calculation methodology, procedures, and inputs used in these calculations and submit the plan to EPA within 30 days after the effective date of the FIP. The owner or operator must submit a list of idled units to EPA within 30 days of the effective date of the FIP. The owner or operator must submit a CAP for any idled units prior to resuming operations.
