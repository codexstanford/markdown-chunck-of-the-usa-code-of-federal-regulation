
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 721.3 Definitions.
####### Facility

means all buildings, equipment, structures, and other stationary items which are located on a single site or on contiguous or adjacent sites and which are owned or operated by the same person (or by any person which controls, is controlled by, or under common control with such person).
