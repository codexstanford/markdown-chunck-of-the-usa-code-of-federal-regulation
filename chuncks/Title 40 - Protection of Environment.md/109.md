
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 2.201 Definitions.
####### Business information,information

(c)(sometimes referred to simply as) means any information which pertains to the interests of any business, which was developed or acquired by that business, and (except where the context otherwise requires) which is possessed by EPA in recorded form.
