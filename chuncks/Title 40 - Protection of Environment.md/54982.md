
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

####### Dispute Resolution Mechanism

(1)means the EPA mechanism established pursuant to the requirements of Clean Water Act section 518(e) for resolving unreasonable consequences that arise as a result of differing water quality standards that may be set by States and Indian Tribes located on common bodies of water.
