
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart A - Acid Rain Program General Provisions
###### ยง 72.2 Definitions.
####### Ninetieth (90th) percentile

means a value that would divide an ordered set of increasing values so that at least 90 percent are less than or equal to the value and at least 10 percent are greater than or equal to the value.
