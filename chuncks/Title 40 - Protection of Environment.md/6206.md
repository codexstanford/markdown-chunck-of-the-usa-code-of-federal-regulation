######## Best available control measure

(BACM) is any technologically and economically feasible control measure that can be implemented in whole or in part within 4 years after the date of reclassification of a Moderate PMnonattainment area to Serious and that generally can achieve greater permanent and enforceable emissions reductions in direct PMemissions and/or emissions of PMplan precursors from sources in the area than can be achieved through the implementation of RACM on the same source(s). BACM includes best available control technology (BACT).
