This subpart applies to any air agency in whose jurisdiction is located one or more applicable sources of SOemissions that have annual actual SOemissions of 2,000 tons or more; or in whose jurisdiction is located one or more sources of SOemissions that have been identified by the air agency or by the EPA Regional Administrator as requiring further air quality characterization. For the purposes of this subpart, the subject air agency shall identify applicable sources of SObased on the most recently available annual SOemissions data for such sources.
