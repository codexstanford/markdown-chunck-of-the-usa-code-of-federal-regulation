
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

#### PART 35 - STATE AND LOCAL ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart I - Grants for Construction of Treatment Works
###### Appendix A to Subpart I of Part 35 - Determination of Allowable Costs
####### Purpose.
