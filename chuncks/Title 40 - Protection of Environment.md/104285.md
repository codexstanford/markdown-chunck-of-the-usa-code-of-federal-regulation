####### Downstream location,e.g.,

means any point in the fuel distribution system other than a fuel manufacturing facility through which the fuel passes after it leaves the fuel manufacturing facility gate at which it was certified (fuel at facilities of distributors, pipelines, terminals, carriers, retailers, oxygenate blenders, and WPCs).
