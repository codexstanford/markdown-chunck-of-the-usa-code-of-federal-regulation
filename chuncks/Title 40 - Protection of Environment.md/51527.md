
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provision
###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Carbonate-based mineral mass fraction

means the following: For limestone, the mass fraction of calcium carbonate (CaCO) in the limestone; for dolomite, the mass fraction of calcium magnesium carbonate (CaMg(CO)) in the dolomite; for soda ash, the mass fraction of sodium carbonate (NaCO) in the soda ash; for barium carbonate, the mass fraction of barium carbonate (BaCO) in the barium carbonate; for potassium carbonate, the mass fraction of potassium carbonate (KCO) in the potassium carbonate; for lithium carbonate, the mass fraction of lithium carbonate (LiCO); and for strontium carbonate, the mass fraction of strontium carbonate (SrCO).
