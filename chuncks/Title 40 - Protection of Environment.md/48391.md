
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart B - Determining Conformity of General Federal Actions to State or Federal Implementation Plans
###### ยง 93.152 Definitions.
####### Increase the frequency or severity of any existing violation of any standard in any area

means to cause a nonattainment area to exceed a standard more often or to cause a violation at a greater concentration than previously existed and/or would otherwise exist during the future period in question, if the project were not implemented.
