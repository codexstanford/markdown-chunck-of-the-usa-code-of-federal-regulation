
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs

##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1298 Trichoderma hamatum isolate 382; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Trichoderma hamatum

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues ofisolate 382 in or on all food commodities when applied as a fungicide and used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
