
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter V - Council on Environmental Quality
### Subchapter B - Administrative Procedures and Operations
#### PART 1515 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT PROCEDURES
##### Costs
###### ยง 1515.13 Fees for categories of requesters.
####### Educational and non-commercial scientific institutions.

(b)CEQ shall provide records to requesters in this category for the cost of duplication alone, excluding charges for the first 100 pages. To qualify for inclusion in this fee category, a requester must show that the request is authorized by and is made under the auspices of a qualifying institution and that the records are sought to further scholarly research, not an individual goal.
