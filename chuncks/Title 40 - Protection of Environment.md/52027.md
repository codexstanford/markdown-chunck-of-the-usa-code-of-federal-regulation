### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart I - Electronics Manufacturing
###### ยง 98.98 Definitions.
####### Wafer starts

means the number of fresh wafers that are introduced into the fabrication sequence each month. It includes test wafers, which means wafers that are exposed to all of the conditions of process characterization, including but not limited to actual etch conditions or actual film deposition conditions.
