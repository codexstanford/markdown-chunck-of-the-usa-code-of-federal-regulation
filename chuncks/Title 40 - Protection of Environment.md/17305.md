
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 60.2 Definitions.
####### Equivalent method

means any method of sampling and analyzing for an air pollutant which has been demonstrated to the Administrator's satisfaction to have a consistent and quantitatively known relationship to the reference method, under specified conditions.
