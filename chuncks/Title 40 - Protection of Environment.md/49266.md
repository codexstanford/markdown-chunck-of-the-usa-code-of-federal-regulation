### Subchapter C - Air Programs

####### ยง 97.2 Definitions.
######## Maximum potential NO,emission rate

means the emission rate of nitrogen oxides (in lb/mmBtu) calculated in accordance with section 3 of appendix F of part 75 of this chapter, using the maximum potential concentration of NOunder section 2 of appendix A of part 75 of this chapter, and either the maximum oxygen concentration (in percent O2) or the minimum carbon dioxide concentration (in percent CO), under all operating conditions of the unit except for unit start up, shutdown, and upsets.
