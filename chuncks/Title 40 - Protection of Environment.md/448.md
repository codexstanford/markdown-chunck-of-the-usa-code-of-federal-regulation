
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter A - General

###### ยง 6.102 Definitions.
####### Environmental information document

(4)(EID) means a written analysis prepared by an applicant that provides sufficient information for the Responsible Official to undertake an environmental review and prepare either an EA and FONSI or an EIS and record of decision (ROD) for the proposed action.
