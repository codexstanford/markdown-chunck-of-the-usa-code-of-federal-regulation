
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 720 - PREMANUFACTURE NOTIFICATION
##### Subpart F - Commencement of Manufacture or Import
###### ยง 720.102 Notice of commencement of manufacture.
####### When to report.

(b)(1) If manufacture for commercial purposes begins on or after the effective date of this rule, the submitter must submit the notice to EPA on, or no later than 30 calendar days, after the first day of such manufacture.

(2) If manufacture for commercial purposes began or will begin before the effective date of this rule, the submitter must submit the notice by the effective date of this rule.
