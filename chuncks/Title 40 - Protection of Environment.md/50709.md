
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### § 97.375 Petitions.
####### § 97.702 Definitions.
######## Common designated representative

means, with regard to a control period in a given year, a designated representative where, as of April 1 immediately after the allowance transfer deadline for such a control period before 2021, or as of July 1 immediately after such deadline for such a control period in 2021 or thereafter, the same natural person is authorized under §§ 97.713(a) and 97.715(a) as the designated representative for a group of one or more CSAPR SOGroup 2 sources and units in a State (and Indian country within the borders of such State).
