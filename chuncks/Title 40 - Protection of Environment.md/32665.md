
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### General
####### ยง 63.1503 Definitions.
######## Capture and collection system

means the system, including duct systems and fans, and, in some cases, hoods, used to collect a contaminant at or near its source, and for affected sources equipped with an air pollution control device, transport the contaminated air to the air cleaning device.
