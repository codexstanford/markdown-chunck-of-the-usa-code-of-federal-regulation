
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart D - National Volatile Organic Compound Emission Standards for Architectural Coatings
###### ยง 59.401 Definitions.
####### Recycled coating

means an architectural coating that contains some portion of post-consumer coating. Recycled architectural coatings include, but are not limited to, post-consumer coatings that have been repackaged or blended with virgin coating materials.
