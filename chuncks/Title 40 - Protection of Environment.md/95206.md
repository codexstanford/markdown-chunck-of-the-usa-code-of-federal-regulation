
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart E - Exemptions From Test Rules
###### ยง 790.97 Hearing procedures.

(a) Hearing requests must be submitted using the method specified in ยง 790.5(b). Such requests must include the applicant's basis for appealing EPA's decision.

(b) If more than one applicant has requested a hearing on similar grounds, all of those appeals will be considered at the same hearing unless confidentiality claims preclude a joint hearing.
