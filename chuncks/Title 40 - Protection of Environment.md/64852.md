
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 255 - IDENTIFICATION OF REGIONS AND AGENCIES FOR SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT
##### Subpart D - Responsibilities of Identified Agencies and Relationship to Other Programs
###### ยง 255.31 Integration with other acts.
####### et seq.,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.

The Governor shall integrate the provisions of these guidelines for purposes of administration and enforcement, and should avoid duplication to the maximum extent practicable, with the appropriate regional identification provisions of the Clean Air Act (42 U.S.C. 1857), the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1251), the Safe Drinking Water Act (42 U.S.C. 300f), the Toxic Substances Control Act (15 U.S.C. 2601), the Marine Protection, Research and Sanctuaries Act of 1972 (33 U.S.C. 1401) and other appropriate Acts of Congress.
