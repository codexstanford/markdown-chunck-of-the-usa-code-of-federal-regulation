
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter B - Grants and Other Federal Assistance
#### PART 49 - INDIAN COUNTRY: AIR QUALITY PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT
##### Subpart C - General Federal Implementation Plan Provisions
###### Federal Major New Source Review Program for Nonattainment Areas in Indian Country
####### § 49.166 Program overview.
######## What constitutes the Federal major new source review (NSR) program for nonattainment areas in Indian country?

(a)As set forth in this Federal Implementation Plan (FIP), the Federal major NSR program for nonattainment areas in Indian country (or "program") consists of §§ 49.166 through 49.175.
