
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 52 - APPROVAL AND PROMULGATION OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Subpart RR - Tennessee
###### ยง 52.2236 Control strategy; lead.
####### Determination of attaining data.
######## Approval.

EPA is approving the section 182(f) oxides of nitrogen (NO) reasonably available control technology (RACT) and NOconformity exemption request submitted by the Tennessee Department of Environment and Conservation on March 21, 1995, for the five county middle Tennessee (Nashville) ozone moderate nonattainment area. This approval exempts the area from implementing federal NORACT on major sources of NOand exempts Tennessee from NOconformity. This approval does not exempt sources from any State required or State Implementation Plan (SIP) approved NOcontrols. If a violation of the ozone NAAQS occurs in the area, the exemption from the requirement of section 182(f) of the CAA in the applicable area shall not apply.
