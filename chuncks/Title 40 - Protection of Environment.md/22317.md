
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 60 - STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE FOR NEW STATIONARY SOURCES
##### Appendix A-7 to Part 60 - Test Methods 19 through 25E
###### 3.2 Benzo[a]pyrene Toxic Equivalency Quotient (B[a]P-TEQ).,a

One of several schemes that express the toxicity for PAH compounds in terms of the most toxic form of PAH, benzo[]pyrene, as specified in applicable regulations, permits, or other requirements.
