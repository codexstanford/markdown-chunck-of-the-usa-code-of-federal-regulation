###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Municipal wastewater treatment plant

means a series of treatment processes used to remove contaminants and pollutants from domestic, business, and industrial wastewater collected in city sewers and transported to a centralized wastewater treatment system such as a publicly owned treatment works (POTW).
