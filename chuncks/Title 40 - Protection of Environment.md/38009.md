####### ยง 63.9824 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Dry lime scrubber/fabric filter (DLS/FF)

means an add-on air pollution control device that includes continuous injection of humidified hydrated lime or other sorbent into a reaction chamber followed by a fabric filter. These systems may include recirculation of some of the sorbent.
