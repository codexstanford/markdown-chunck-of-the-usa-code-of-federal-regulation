
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 795.232 Inhalation and dermal pharmacokinetics of commercial hexane.
####### Intravenous study.

(A)Group A shall be given a single intravenous dose of the radiolabeled test substance to result in a level of commercial hexane in the blood that approximates the level from the other routes of exposure so that the data can be used to determine absorption and excretion parameters.
