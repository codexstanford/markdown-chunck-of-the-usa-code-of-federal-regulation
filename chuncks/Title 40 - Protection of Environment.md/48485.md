
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 96 - NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM AND CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS FOR STATE IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
####### § 96.2 Definitions.
######## NO,Budget emissions limitation

means, for a NOBudget unit, the tonnage equivalent of the NOallowances available for compliance deduction for the unit and for a control period under § 96.54(a) and (b), adjusted by any deductions of such NOallowances to account for actual utilization under § 96.42(e) for the control period or to account for excess emissions for a prior control period under § 96.54(d) or to account for withdrawal from the NOBudget Program, or for a change in regulatory status, for a NOBudget opt-in source under § 96.86 or § 96.87.
