####### ยง 51.1000 Definitions.
######## Applicable attainment date

means the latest statutory date by which an area is required to attain a particular PMNAAQS, unless the EPA has approved an attainment plan for the area to attain such NAAQS, in which case the applicable attainment date is the date approved under such attainment plan. If the EPA grants an extension of an approved attainment date, then the applicable attainment date for the area shall be the extended date.
