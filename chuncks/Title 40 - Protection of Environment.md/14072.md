####### Continuous emission monitoring system or CEMS

means the equipment required by this section to sample, analyze, measure, and provide, by means of readings recorded at least once every 15 minutes (using an automated data acquisition and handling system (DAHS)), a permanent record of SOor NOemissions, other pollutant emissions, diluent, or stack gas volumetric flow rate.
