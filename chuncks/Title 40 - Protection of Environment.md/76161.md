
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 464 - METAL MOLDING AND CASTING POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### General Provisions
###### ยง 464.02 General definitions.
####### Ferrous casting.

(c)The remelting of ferrous metals to form a cast intermediate or finished product by pouring the molten metal into a mold. Except for grinding scrubber operations which are covered here, processing operations following the cooling of castings are covered under the electroplating and metal finishing point source categories (40 CFR parts 413 and 433).
