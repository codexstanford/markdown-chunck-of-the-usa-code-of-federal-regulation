
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 80 - REGULATION OF FUELS AND FUEL ADDITIVES
##### Subpart M - Renewable Fuel Standard
###### ยง 80.1456 What are the provisions for cellulosic biofuel waiver credits?
####### Purchase of cellulosic biofuel waiver credits.

(c)(1) Only parties with an RVO for cellulosic biofuel may purchase cellulosic biofuel waiver credits.

(2) Cellulosic biofuel waiver credits shall be purchased from EPA at the time that a party submits its annual compliance report to EPA pursuant to ยง 80.1451(a)(1).

(3) Parties may not purchase more cellulosic biofuel waiver credits than their current year cellulosic biofuel RVO minus cellulosic biofuel RINs with a D code of 3 that they own.

(4) Cellulosic biofuel waiver credits may only be used to meet an obligated party's cellulosic biofuel RVO.
