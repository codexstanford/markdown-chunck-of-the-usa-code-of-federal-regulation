
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 63 - NATIONAL EMISSION STANDARDS FOR HAZARDOUS AIR POLLUTANTS FOR SOURCE CATEGORIES
##### Subpart RRR - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Secondary Aluminum Production
###### General
####### ยง 63.1503 Definitions.
######## Bag leak detection system,i.e.,,bag leak detection system

means an instrument that is capable of monitoring particulate matter loadings in the exhaust of a fabric filter (baghouse) in order to detect bag failures. Aincludes, but is not limited to, an instrument that operates on triboelectric, light scattering, light transmittance, or other effect to monitor relative particulate matter loadings.
