### Subchapter C - Air Programs

####### Direct contact evaporator (DCE) recovery furnace

means a kraft or soda recovery furnace equipped with a direct contact evaporator that concentrates strong black liquor by direct contact between the hot recovery furnace exhaust gases and the strong black liquor.
