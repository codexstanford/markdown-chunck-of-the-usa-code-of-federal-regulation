
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 180 - TOLERANCES AND EXEMPTIONS FOR PESTICIDE CHEMICAL RESIDUES IN FOOD
##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1308 Bacillus amyloliquefaciens strain D747; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Bacillus amyloliquefaciens

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of the microbial pesticide,strain D747 in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
