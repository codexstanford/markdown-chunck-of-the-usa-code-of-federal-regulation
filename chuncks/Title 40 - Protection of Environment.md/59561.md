
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 169 - BOOKS AND RECORDS OF PESTICIDE PRODUCTION AND DISTRIBUTION
##### ยง 169.1 Definitions.
###### Amount of pesticide or active ingredient.

(a)The term "amount of pesticide or active ingredient" means the weight or volume of the pesticide or active ingredient used in producing a pesticide expressed as weight for solid or semi-solid products and as weight or volume of liquid products.
