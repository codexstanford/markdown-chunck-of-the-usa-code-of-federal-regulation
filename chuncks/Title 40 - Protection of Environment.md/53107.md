## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 98.463 Calculating GHG emissions.

(a) For each industrial waste landfill subject to the reporting requirements of this subpart, calculate annual modeled CHgeneration according to the applicable requirements in paragraphs (a)(1) through (a)(3) of this section. Apply Equation TT-1 of this section for each waste stream disposed of in the landfill and sum the CHgeneration rates for all waste streams disposed of in the landfill to calculate the total annual modeled CHgeneration rate for the landfill.
