
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

####### i.e.,

(1) For chemical substances manufactured under contract,by a toll manufacturer, the site is the location where the chemical substance is physically manufactured.

(2) The site for an importer who imports a chemical substance described in ยง 710.25 is the U.S. site of the operating unit within the person's organization that is directly responsible for importing the chemical substance.
