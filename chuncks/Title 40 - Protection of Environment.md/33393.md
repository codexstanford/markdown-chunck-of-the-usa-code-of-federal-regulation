######## Dry rotary dryer

means a rotary dryer that dries wood particles or fibers with a maximum inlet moisture content of less than or equal to 30 percent (by weight, dry basis) and operates with a maximum inlet temperature of less than or equal to 600 &#xB0;F. A dry rotary dryer is a process unit.
