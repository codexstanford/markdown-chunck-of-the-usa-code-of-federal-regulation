
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 721 - SIGNIFICANT NEW USES OF CHEMICAL SUBSTANCES
##### Subpart E - Significant New Uses for Specific Chemical Substances
###### ยง 721.4500 Isopropylamine distillation residues and ethylamine distillation residues.
####### Chemical substances and significant new use subject to reporting.

(a)(1) The following chemical substances referred to by their Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) register numbers and chemical names are subject to reporting under this section for the significant new use identified in paragraph (a)(2) of this section: CAS No. 79771-08-7, isopropylamine distillation residues, and CAS No. 79771-09-98, ethylamine distillation residues.

(2) The significant new use is use in metalworking fluids.
