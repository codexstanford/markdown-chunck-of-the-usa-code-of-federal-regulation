
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provision
###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Kerosene-type jet fuel

means a kerosene-based product used in commercial and military turbojet and turboprop aircraft. The product has a maximum distillation temperature of 400 &#xB0;F at the 10 percent recovery point and a final maximum boiling point of 572 &#xB0;F. Included are Jet A, Jet A-1, JP-5, and JP-8.
