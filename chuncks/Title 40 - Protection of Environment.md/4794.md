
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 50 - NATIONAL PRIMARY AND SECONDARY AMBIENT AIR QUALITY STANDARDS
##### Appendix U to Part 50 - Interpretation of the Primary and Secondary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone
###### Minimum data completeness requirements

refer to the amount of data that a site is required to collect in order to make a valid determination that the site is meeting the NAAQS.
