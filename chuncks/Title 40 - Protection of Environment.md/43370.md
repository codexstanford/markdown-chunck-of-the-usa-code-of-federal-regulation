####### Reference substance,i.e.,

means any chemical substance or mixture, analytical standard, or material other than engine/motor vehicle emissions and/or its carrier, that is administered to or used in analyzing the test system in the course of a study. A "reference substance" is used to establish a basis for comparison with the test atmosphere for known chemical or biological measurements,positive or negative control substance.
