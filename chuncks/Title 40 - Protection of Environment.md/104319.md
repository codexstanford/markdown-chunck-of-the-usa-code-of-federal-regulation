####### Liquefied petroleum gas (LPG)

means a liquid hydrocarbon fuel that is stored under pressure and is composed primarily of compounds that are gases at atmospheric conditions (temperature = 25 &#xB0;C and pressure = 1 atm), excluding natural gas.
