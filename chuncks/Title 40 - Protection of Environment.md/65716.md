
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 260.10 Definitions.
####### Underground injection

means the subsurface emplacement of fluids through a bored, drilled or driven well; or through a dug well, where the depth of the dug well is greater than the largest surface dimension. (See also "injection well".)
