
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 63.542 Definitions.
####### Lead bearing material

means material with a lead content equal to or greater than 5 mg/l as measured by EPA Method 1311 (Under Method 1311, only materials with at least 100 ppm lead will be considered to be lead bearing).
