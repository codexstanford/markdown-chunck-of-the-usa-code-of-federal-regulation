
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

####### Test evaluation.,per se

(4)DNA damage tests in bacteria do not measure DNA repairnor do they measure mutations. They measure DNA damage which is expressed as cell killing or growth inhibition. A positive result in a DNA damage test in the absence of a positive result in another system is difficult to evaluate in the absence of a better data base.
