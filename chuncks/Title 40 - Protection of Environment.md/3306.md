
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter B - Grants and Other Federal Assistance

##### Subpart C - General Federal Implementation Plan Provisions
###### Federal Minor New Source Review Program in Indian Country
####### § 49.151 Program overview.
######## What constitutes the Federal minor new source review (NSR) program in Indian country?

(a)As set forth in this Federal Implementation Plan (FIP), the Federal minor NSR program in Indian country (or "program") consists of §§ 49.151 through 49.165.
