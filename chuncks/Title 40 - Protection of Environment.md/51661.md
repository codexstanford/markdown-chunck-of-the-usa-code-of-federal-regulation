### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Methane conversion factor

means the extent to which the CHproducing capacity (B) is realized in each type of treatment and discharge pathway and system. Thus, it is an indication of the degree to which the system is anaerobic.
