####### Unit with wet flue gas pollution control system.

(b)If the owner or operator can demonstrate that condensed water is present in the exhaust flue gas stream and would impede the accuracy of opacity measurements, then the owner or operator of an affected unit equipped with a wet flue gas pollution control system for SOemissions or particulates is exempt from the opacity monitoring requirements of this part.
