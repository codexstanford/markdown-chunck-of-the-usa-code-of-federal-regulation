####### Prime surfacer coat,e.g.,

means a coating used to touch up areas on the surface of automobile or light-duty truck bodies not adequately covered by the prime coat before application of the top coat. The prime surfacer coat is applied between the prime coat and topcoat. An anti-chip coating applied to main body parts (rocker panels, bottom of doors and fenders, and leading edge of roof) is a prime surfacer coat.
