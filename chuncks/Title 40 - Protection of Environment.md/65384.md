
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 257 - CRITERIA FOR CLASSIFICATION OF SOLID WASTE DISPOSAL FACILITIES AND PRACTICES
##### Appendix II to Part 257
###### Composting:

Using the within-vessel composting method, the solid waste is maintained at operating conditions of 55 &#xB0;C or greater for three days. Using the static aerated pile composting method, the solid waste is maintained at operating conditions of 55 &#xB0;C or greater for three days. Using the windrow composting method, the solid waste attains a temperature of 55 &#xB0;C or greater for at least 15 days during the composting period. Also, during the high temperature period, there will be a minimum of five turnings of the windrow.
