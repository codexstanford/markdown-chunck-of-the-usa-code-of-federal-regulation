
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

##### Subpart K - National Emission Standards for Radionuclide Emissions From Elemental Phosphorus Plants
###### ยง 61.125 Test methods and procedures.

(a) Each owner or operator of a source required to test emissions under ยง 61.123, unless an equivalent or alternate method has been approved by the Administrator, shall use the following test methods:

(1) Method 1 of appendix A to 40 CFR part 60 shall be used to determine sample and velocity traverses;

(2) Method 2 of appendix A to 40 CFR part 60 shall be used to determine velocity and volumetric flow rate;
