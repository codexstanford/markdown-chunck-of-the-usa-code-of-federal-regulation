####### Unit-specific daily NO,mass emissions

shall be expressed in lb/day and calculated as the sum of total pounds of NOemitted from the Unit during the Unit Operating Day. Each Unit-specific Daily NOMass Emissions shall include all emissions that occur during all periods within any Operating Day, including emissions from startup, shutdown, and malfunction.
