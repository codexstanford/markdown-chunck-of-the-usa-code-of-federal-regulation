
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 73 - SULFUR DIOXIDE ALLOWANCE SYSTEM
##### Subpart F - Energy Conservation and Renewable Energy Reserve
###### ยง 73.81 Qualified conservation measures and renewable energy generation.
####### i.e.,

(i) Consistent with a least cost plan or a least cost planning process and derived from biomass (combustible energy-producing materials from biological sources which include wood, plant residues, biological wastes, landfill gas, energy crops, and eligible components of municipal solid waste), solar, geothermal, or wind resources;
