
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart E - The Labeling of Products Using Ozone-Depleting Substances
###### ยง 82.104 Definitions.
####### Promotional printed material

(t)means any informational or advertising material (including, but not limited to, written advertisements, brochures, circulars, desk references and fact sheets) that is prepared by the manufacturer for display or promotion concerning a product or container, and that does not accompany the product to the consumer.
