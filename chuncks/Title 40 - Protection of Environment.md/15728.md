
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs

(c)-(1)A sampler associated with a reference method for PM, as specified in appendix O to part 50 of this chapter, shall be subject to the requirements in this paragraph (c)(1).

(i) The PMsampler of the PMsampler pair shall be verified to be either currently designated under this part 53 as a FRM for PM, or shown to meet all requirements for designation as a FRM for PM, in accordance with this part 53.

(ii) The PMsampler of the PMsampler pair shall be verified to be of like manufacturer, design, configuration, and fabrication to the PMsampler of the PMsampler pair, except for replacement of the particle size separator specified in section 7.3.4 of appendix L to part 50 of this chapter with the downtube extension as specified in Figure O-1 of appendix O to part 50 of this chapter.

(iii) For samplers that meet the provisions of paragraphs (c)(1)(i) and (ii) of this section, the candidate PMreference method may be determined to be a FRM without further testing.
