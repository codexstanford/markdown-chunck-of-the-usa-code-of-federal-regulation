
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 129 - TOXIC POLLUTANT EFFLUENT STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - Toxic Pollutant Effluent Standards and Prohibitions
###### ยง 129.2 Definitions.
####### Process wastes

(n)means any designated toxic pollutant, whether in wastewater or otherwise present, which is inherent to or unavoidably resulting from any manufacturing process, including that which comes into direct contact with or results from the production or use of any raw material, intermediate product, finished product, by-product or waste product and is discharged into the navigable waters.
