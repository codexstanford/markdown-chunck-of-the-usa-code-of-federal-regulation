
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 82 - PROTECTION OF STRATOSPHERIC OZONE
##### Subpart F - Recycling and Emissions Reduction
###### ยง 82.152 Definitions.
####### Very high-pressure appliance

means an appliance that uses a refrigerant with a critical temperature below 104 &#xB0;F or with a liquid phase saturation pressure above 355 psia at 104 &#xB0;F. Examples include but are not limited to appliances using R-13, R-23, R-503, R-508A, and R-508B.
