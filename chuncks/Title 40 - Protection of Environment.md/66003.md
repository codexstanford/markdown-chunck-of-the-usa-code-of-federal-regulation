
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 261 - IDENTIFICATION AND LISTING OF HAZARDOUS WASTE
##### Subpart AA - Air Emission Standards for Process Vents
###### ยง 261.1031 Definitions.
####### In light liquid service

means that the piece of equipment contains or contacts a material stream where the vapor pressure of one or more of the organic components in the stream is greater than 0.3 kilopascals (kPa) at 20 &#xB0;C, the total concentration of the pure organic components having a vapor pressure greater than 0.3 kilopascals (kPa) at 20 &#xB0;C is equal to or greater than 20 percent by weight, and the fluid is a liquid at operating conditions.
