## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 8 - ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESSMENT OF NONGOVERNMENTAL ACTIVITIES IN ANTARCTICA
##### ยง 8.3 Definitions.
###### Antarctic environment

means the natural and physical environment of Antarctica and its dependent and associated ecosystems, but excludes social, economic, and other environments.
