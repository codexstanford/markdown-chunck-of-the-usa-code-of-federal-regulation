###### ยง 1068.310 Exclusions for imported engines/equipment.
####### Nonroad engines/equipment used solely for competition.

(a)Nonroad engines/equipment that you demonstrate will be used solely for competition are excluded from the restrictions on imports in ยง 1068.301(b), but only if they are properly labeled. See the standard-setting part for provisions related to this demonstration that may apply. Section 1068.101(b)(4) prohibits anyone from using these excluded engines/equipment for purposes other than competition.
