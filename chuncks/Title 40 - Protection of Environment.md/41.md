
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General

##### Subpart B - Headquarters
###### ยง 1.43 Office of Chemical Safety and Pollution Prevention.
####### Office of Science Coordination and Policy.

(c)The Office of Science Coordination and Policy (OSCP) provides coordination, leadership, peer review, and synthesis of science and science policy within OCSPP. OSCP provides guidance to assure sound scientific decisions are made regarding safe pesticide and chemical management through the leadership of the Scientific Advisory Panel (SAP). OSCP also coordinates emerging exposure and hazard assessment topics such as endocrine disruptors and biotechnology.
