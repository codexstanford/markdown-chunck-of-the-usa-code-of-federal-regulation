
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Subpart H - Prevention of Air Pollution Emergency Episodes
###### § 51.150 Classification of regions for episode plans.
####### Priority I Regions

(b)means any area with greater ambient concentrations than the following:

(1) Sulfur dioxide-100 µg/m(0.04 ppm) annual arithmetic mean; 455 µg/m(0.17 ppm) 24-hour maximum.

(2) Particulate matter-95 µg/mannual geometric mean; 325 µg/m24-hour maximum.

(3) Carbon monoxide-55 mg/m(48 ppm) 1-hour maximum; 14 mg/m(12 ppm) 8-hour maximum.

(4) Nitrogen dioxide-100 µg/m(0.06 ppm) annual arithmetic mean.

(5) Ozone-195 µg/m(0.10 ppm) 1-hour maximum.
