####### Storage tanks.

(6)(i) The owner or operator shall equip tanks storing VOL with a vapor pressure greater than 10 kPa (1.5 psi) at 20 &#xB0;C (68 &#xB0;F) with pressure/vacuum conservation vents set as a minimum at + /-0.2 kPa (0.029 psi). An alternative control system can only be allowed if approved by the Administrator as a SIP or FIP revision.
