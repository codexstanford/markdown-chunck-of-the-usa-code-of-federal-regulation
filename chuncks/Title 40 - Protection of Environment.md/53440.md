
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 116 - DESIGNATION OF HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES
##### ยง 116.3 Definitions.
###### Public vessel

means a vessel owned or bareboat-chartered and operated by the United States, or a State or political subdivision thereof, or by a foreign nation, except when such vessel is engaged in commerce.
