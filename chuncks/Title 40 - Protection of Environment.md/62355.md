
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 180 - TOLERANCES AND EXEMPTIONS FOR PESTICIDE CHEMICAL RESIDUES IN FOOD
##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1316 Pasteuria spp. (Rotylenchulus reniformis nematode) - Pr3; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Pasteuria,Rotylenchulus reniformis

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues ofspp. (nematode)-Pr3 in or on all food commodities when applied as a nematicide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
