
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 766 - DIBENZO-PARA-DIOXINS/DIBENZOFURANS
##### Subpart B - Specific Chemical Testing/Reporting Requirements
###### ยง 766.25 Chemical substances for testing.
####### Grade to be tested.

(b)If the same process is used to manufacture all grades of the same chemical substance, only one grade need be tested. The grade to be tested must be the grade subject to the most intense heat and alkalinity for the longest duration of time, manufactured under each different process. If the heat, alkalinity and duration of reaction do not differ for various grades, the test substance must be the grade of chemical substance with the highest volume of sales.
