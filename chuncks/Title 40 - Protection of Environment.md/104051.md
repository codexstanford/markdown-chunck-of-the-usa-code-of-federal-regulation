####### Examples.

(e)Examples provided in the regulations in subchapter U of this chapter are typically introduced by either "for example" or "such as". Specific examples given in the regulations do not necessarily represent the most common examples. The regulations may specify examples conditionally (that is, specifying that they are applicable only if certain criteria or conditions are met). Lists of examples cannot be presumed to be exhaustive lists.
