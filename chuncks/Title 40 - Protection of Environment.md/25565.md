
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.101 Definitions.
####### Research and development facility,de minimis

means laboratory and pilot plant operations whose primary purpose is to conduct research and development into new processes and products, where the operations are under the close supervision of technically trained personnel, and is not engaged in the manufacture of products for commercial sale, except in amanner.
