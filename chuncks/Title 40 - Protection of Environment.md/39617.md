######## Mercury retort

means a vessel that is operated under a partial vacuum at approximately 1,100&#xB0; to 1,300 &#xB0;F to remove mercury and moisture from the gold bearing sludge material that is recovered from electrowinning, the Merrill-Crowe process, or other processes. Mercury retorts are usually equipped with condensers that recover liquid mercury during the processing.
