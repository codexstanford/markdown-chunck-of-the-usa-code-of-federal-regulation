
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 60.51a Definitions.
####### Modular excess air MWC

means a combustor that combusts MSW and that is not field-erected and has multiple combustion chambers, all of which are designed to operate at conditions with combustion air amounts in excess of theoretical air requirements.
