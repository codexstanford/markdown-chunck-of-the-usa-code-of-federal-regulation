
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 180 - TOLERANCES AND EXEMPTIONS FOR PESTICIDE CHEMICAL RESIDUES IN FOOD
##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1310 Trichoderma virens strain G-41; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Trichoderma virens

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues ofstrain G-41, in or on all food commodities, when applied as a fungicide and used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
