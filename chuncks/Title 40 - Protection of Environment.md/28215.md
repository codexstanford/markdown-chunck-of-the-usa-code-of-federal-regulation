##### Subpart JJ - National Emission Standards for Wood Furniture Manufacturing Operations
###### ยง 63.801 Definitions.
####### Sealer

means a finishing material used to seal the pores of a wood substrate before additional coats of finishing material are applied. Special purpose finishing materials that are used in some finishing systems to optimize aesthetics are not sealers.
