
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 63.801 Definitions.
####### Capture device

means a hood, enclosed room, floor sweep, or other means of collecting solvent emissions or other pollutants into a duct so that the pollutant can be directed to a pollution control device such as an incinerator or carbon adsorber.
