############### ยง 96.60 Submission of NOallowance transfers.

The NOauthorized account representatives seeking recordation of a NOallowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator. To be considered correctly submitted, the NOallowance transfer shall include the following elements in a format specified by the Administrator:

(a) The numbers identifying both the transferor and transferee accounts;

(b) A specification by serial number of each NOallowance to be transferred; and

(c) The printed name and signature of the NOauthorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed.
