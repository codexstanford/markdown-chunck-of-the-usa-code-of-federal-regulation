
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.341 Definitions and nomenclature.
####### Enclosed hard chromium electroplating tank

means a chromium electroplating tank that is equipped with an enclosing hood and ventilated at half the rate or less that of an open surface tank of the same surface area.
