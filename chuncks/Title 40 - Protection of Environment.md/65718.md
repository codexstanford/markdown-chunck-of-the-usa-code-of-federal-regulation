
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 260.10 Definitions.
####### Unfit-for use tank system

means a tank system that has been determined through an integrity assessment or other inspection to be no longer capable of storing or treating hazardous waste without posing a threat of release of hazardous waste to the environment.
