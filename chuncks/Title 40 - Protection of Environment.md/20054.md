### Subchapter C - Air Programs

####### ยง 60.1465 What definitions must I know?
######## Modular starved-air municipal waste combustion unit

means a municipal waste combustion unit that combusts municipal solid waste, is not field-erected, and has multiple combustion chambers in which the primary combustion chamber is designed to operate at substoichiometric conditions.
