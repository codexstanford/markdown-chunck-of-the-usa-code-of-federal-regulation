
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 86 - CONTROL OF EMISSIONS FROM NEW AND IN-USE HIGHWAY VEHICLES AND ENGINES
##### Subpart S - General Compliance Provisions for Control of Air Pollution From New and In-Use Light-Duty Vehicles, Light-Duty Trucks, and Heavy-Duty Vehicles
###### § 86.1862-04 Maintenance of records and submittal of information relevant to compliance with fleet-average standards.
####### Overview.

(a)This section describes reporting and recordkeeping requirements for vehicles subject to the following standards:

(1) Tier 2 NOemission standard for LDV and LDT in § 86.1811-04.

(2) Tier 3 FTP emission standard for NMOG + NOfor LDV and LDT in § 86.1811.

(3) Tier 3 SFTP emission standard for NMOG + NOfor LDV and LDT (including MDPV) in § 86.1811.

(4) Tier 3 evaporative emission standards in § 86.1813.

(5) Tier 3 FTP emission standard for NMOG + NOfor HDV (other than MDPV) in § 86.1816.

(6) Cold temperature NMHC standards in § 86.1811.
