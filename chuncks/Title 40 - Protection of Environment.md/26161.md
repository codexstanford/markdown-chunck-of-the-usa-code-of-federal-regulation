### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.341 Definitions and nomenclature.
####### Operating parameter value

means a minimum or maximum value established for a control device or process parameter which, if achieved by itself or in combination with one or more other operating parameter values, determines that an owner or operator is in continual compliance with the applicable emission limitation or standard.
