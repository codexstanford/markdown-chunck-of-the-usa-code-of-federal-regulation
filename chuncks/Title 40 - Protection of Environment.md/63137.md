(a) Motorcycles must be grouped into classes determined by separate combinations of the following parameters:

(i) Gasoline-two stroke.

(ii) Gasoline-four stroke.

(iii) Gasoline-rotary.

(2) Engine displacement.

(3) Engine configuration:
