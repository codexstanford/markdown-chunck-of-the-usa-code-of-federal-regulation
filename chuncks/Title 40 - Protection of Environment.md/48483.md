
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 96 - NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM AND CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS FOR STATE IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
####### ยง 96.2 Definitions.
######## NO,allowance transfer deadline

means midnight of November 30 or, if November 30 is not a business day, midnight of the first business day thereafter and is the deadline by which NOallowances may be submitted for recordation in a NOBudget unit's compliance account, or the overdraft account of the source where the unit is located, in order to meet the unit's NOBudget emissions limitation for the control period immediately preceding such deadline.
