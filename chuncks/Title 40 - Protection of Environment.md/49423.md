
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart I - Individual Unit Opt-ins
###### ยง 97.84 Opt-in process.
####### Withdrawal of application for NO,Budget opt-in permit.
######## Notification.

(d)(1) After the requirements for withdrawal under paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section are met (including deduction of the full amount of NOallowances required), the Administrator will issue a notification to the permitting authority and the NOauthorized account representative of the NOBudget opt-in unit of the acceptance of the withdrawal of the NOBudget opt-in unit as of a specified effective date that is after such requirements have been met and that is prior to May 1 or after September 30.

(2) If the requirements for withdrawal under paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section are not met, the Administrator will issue a notification to the permitting authority and the NOauthorized account representative of the NOBudget opt-in unit that the request to withdraw is denied. If the NOBudget opt-in unit's request to withdraw is denied, the NOBudget opt-in unit shall remain subject to the requirements for a NOBudget opt-in unit.
