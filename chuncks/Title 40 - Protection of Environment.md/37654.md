####### ยง 63.8698 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Sealant applicator

means the equipment that uses open pan-type application (e.g., a roller partially submerged in an open pan of sealant) to apply a sealant strip to a roofing product. The sealant strip is used to seal overlapping pieces of roofing product after they have been applied.
