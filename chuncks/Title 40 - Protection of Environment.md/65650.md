
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 260.10 Definitions.
####### Landfill cell

means a discrete volume of a hazardous waste landfill which uses a liner to provide isolation of wastes from adjacent cells or wastes. Examples of landfill cells are trenches and pits.
