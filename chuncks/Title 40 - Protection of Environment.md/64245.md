##### Subpart J - Compensatory Mitigation for Losses of Aquatic Resources
###### ยง 230.92 Definitions.
####### Interagency Review Team

(IRT) means an interagency group of federal, tribal, state, and/or local regulatory and resource agency representatives that reviews documentation for, and advises the district engineer on, the establishment and management of a mitigation bank or an in-lieu fee program.
