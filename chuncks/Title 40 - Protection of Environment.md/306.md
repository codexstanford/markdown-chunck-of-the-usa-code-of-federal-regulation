
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - CROSS-MEDIA ELECTRONIC REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 3.3 What definitions are applicable to this part?
####### Existing electronic document receiving system

means an electronic document receiving system that is being used to receive electronic documents in lieu of paper to satisfy requirements under an authorized program on October 13, 2005 or the system, if not in use, has been substantially developed on or before that date as evidenced by the establishment of system services or specifications by contract or other binding agreement.
