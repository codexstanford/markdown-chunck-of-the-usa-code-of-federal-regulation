
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 133 - SECONDARY TREATMENT REGULATION
##### ยง 133.102 Secondary treatment.
###### BOD

(a).

(1) The 30-day average shall not exceed 30 mg/l.

(2) The 7-day average shall not exceed 45 mg/l.

(3) The 30-day average percent removal shall not be less than 85 percent.

(4) At the option of the NPDES permitting authority, in lieu of the parameter BODand the levels of the effluent quality specified in paragraphs (a)(1), (a)(2) and (a)(3), the parameter CBODmay be substituted with the following levels of the CBODeffluent quality provided:

(i) The 30-day average shall not exceed 25 mg/l.

(ii) The 7-day average shall not exceed 40 mg/l.

(iii) The 30-day average percent removal shall not be less than 85 percent.
