
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 72 - PERMITS REGULATION
##### Subpart A - Acid Rain Program General Provisions
###### ยง 72.2 Definitions.
####### Diluent cap

value means a default value of percent COor Owhich may be used to calculate the hourly NOemission rate, when the measured hourly average percent COis below the default value or when the measured hourly average percent Ois above the default value. The diluent cap values for boilers are 5.0 percent COand 14.0 percent O. For combustion turbines, the diluent cap values are 1.0 percent COand 19.0 percent O.
