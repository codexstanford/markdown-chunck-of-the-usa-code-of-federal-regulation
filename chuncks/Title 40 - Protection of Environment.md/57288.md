
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart E - Indian Tribes
###### ยง 145.52 Requirements for Tribal eligibility.

(b) The Indian Tribe has a Tribal governing body which is currently "carrying out substantial governmental duties and powers" over a defined area, (is currently performing governmental functions to promote the health, safety, and welfare of the affected population within a defined geographic area).

(c) The Indian Tribe demonstrates that the functions to be performed in regulating the underground injection wells that the applicant intends to regulate are within the area of the Indian Tribal government's jurisdiction.
