######## Reasonably available control measure

(RACM) is any technologically and economically feasible measure that can be implemented in whole or in part within 4 years after the effective date of designation of a PMnonattainment area and that achieves permanent and enforceable reductions in direct PMemissions and/or PMplan precursor emissions from sources in the area. RACM includes reasonably available control technology (RACT).
