
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 129 - TOXIC POLLUTANT EFFLUENT STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - Toxic Pollutant Effluent Standards and Prohibitions
###### § 129.104 Benzidine.
####### Effluent standards,Existing sources.

(3)-(i)Discharges from benzidine-based dye applicators shall not contain benzidine concentrations exceeding an average per working day of 10 µg/l calculated over any calendar month; and shall not exceed 25 µg/l in a sample(s) or calculation(s) representing any working day.
