
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act

###### ยง 797.1600 Fish early life stage toxicity test.
####### Test parameters,Dissolved oxygen concentration.

(3)-(i)It is recommended that the dissolved oxygen concentration be maintained between 90 and 100 percent saturation; but it shall be no less than 75 percent saturation at all times for both minnow species and between 90 and 100 percent saturation for the trout species in all test chambers.
