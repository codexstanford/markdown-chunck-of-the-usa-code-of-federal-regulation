
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 158 - DATA REQUIREMENTS FOR PESTICIDES
##### Subpart R - Product Performance for Products Claiming Effectiveness Against Invertebrate Pests
###### ยง 158.1701 Definitions.
####### Pest-specific labeling claim

means a claim or statement on the labeling of the pesticide product that the product is effective against a particular arthropod species, such as German cockroach or house fly.
