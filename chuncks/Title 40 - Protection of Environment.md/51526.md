
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provision
###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Carbonate-based mineral

means any of the following minerals used in the manufacture of glass: Calcium carbonate (CaCO), calcium magnesium carbonate (CaMg(CO)), sodium carbonate (NaCO), barium carbonate (BaCO), potassium carbonate (KCO), lithium carbonate (LiCO), and strontium carbonate (SrCO).
