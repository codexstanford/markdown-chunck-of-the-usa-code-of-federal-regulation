
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 116 - DESIGNATION OF HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES
##### ยง 116.3 Definitions.
###### Mixture

means any combination of two or more elements and/or compounds in solid, liquid, or gaseous form except where such substances have undergone a chemical reaction so as to become inseparable by physical means.
