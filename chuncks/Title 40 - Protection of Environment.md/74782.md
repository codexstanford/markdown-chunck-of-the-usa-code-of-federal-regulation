
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 432 - MEAT AND POULTRY PRODUCTS POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### Subpart D - High-Processing Packinghouse
###### ยง 432.40 Applicability.

This part applies to discharges of process wastewater resulting from the production of meat carcasses, in whole or in part, by high-processing packinghouses. Process wastewater includes water from animal holding areas at these facilities.
