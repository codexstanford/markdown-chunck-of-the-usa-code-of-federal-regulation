
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 792 - GOOD LABORATORY PRACTICE STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 792.3 Definitions.
####### Testing facility

means a person who actually conducts a study, i.e., actually uses the test substance in a test system. "Testing facility" encompasses only those operational units that are being or have been used to conduct studies.
