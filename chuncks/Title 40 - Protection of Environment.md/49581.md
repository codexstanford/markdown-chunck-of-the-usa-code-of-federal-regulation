########### Subpart GG - CAIR NOAllowance Transfers
############### ยง 97.160 Submission of CAIR NOallowance transfers.

A CAIR authorized account representative seeking recordation of a CAIR NOallowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator. To be considered correctly submitted, the CAIR NOallowance transfer shall include the following elements, in a format specified by the Administrator:

(a) The account numbers for both the transferor and transferee accounts;

(b) The serial number of each CAIR NOallowance that is in the transferor account and is to be transferred; and

(c) The name and signature of the CAIR authorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed.
