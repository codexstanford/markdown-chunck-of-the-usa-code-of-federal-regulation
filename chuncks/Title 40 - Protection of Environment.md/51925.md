
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart F - Aluminum Production
###### ยง 98.62 GHGs to report.

You must report:

(a) Perfluoromethane (CF), and perfluoroethane (CF) emissions from anode effects in all prebake and S&#xF8;derberg electrolysis cells.

(b) COemissions from anode consumption during electrolysis in all prebake and S&#xF8;derberg electrolysis cells.

(c) COemissions from on-site anode baking.
