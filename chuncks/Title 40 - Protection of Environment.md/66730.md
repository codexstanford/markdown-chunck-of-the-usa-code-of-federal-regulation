
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart AA - Air Emission Standards for Process Vents
###### ยง 264.1031 Definitions.
####### Closed-vent system

means a system that is not open to the atmosphere and that is composed of piping, connections, and, if necessary, flow-inducing devices that transport gas or vapor from a piece or pieces of equipment to a control device.
