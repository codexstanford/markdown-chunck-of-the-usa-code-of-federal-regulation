
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 799.9346 TSCA 90-day inhalation toxicity.
####### Concentration

in a subchronic inhalation study is the amount of test substance administered via inhalation for a period of 90-days. Concentration is expressed as weight of the test substance per unit volume of air (milligrams per liter or parts per million).
