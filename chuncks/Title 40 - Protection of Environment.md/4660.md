
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 50 - NATIONAL PRIMARY AND SECONDARY AMBIENT AIR QUALITY STANDARDS
##### Appendix M to Part 50 [Reserved]
####### Daily values

refer to the 24-hour average concentrations of PMmass measured (or averaged from hourly measurements in AQS) from midnight to midnight (local standard time) from suitable monitors.
