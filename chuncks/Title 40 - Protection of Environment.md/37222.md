
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 63 - NATIONAL EMISSION STANDARDS FOR HAZARDOUS AIR POLLUTANTS FOR SOURCE CATEGORIES
##### Subpart GGGGG - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Site Remediation
###### Other Requirements and Information
####### ยง 63.7957 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Treatment process,e.g.,,e.g.,

means a process in which remediation material is physically, chemically, thermally, or biologically treated to destroy, degrade, or remove hazardous air pollutants contained in the material. A treatment process can be composed of a single unit (a steam stripper) or a series of units (a wastewater treatment system). A treatment process can be used to treat one or more remediation material streams at the same time.
