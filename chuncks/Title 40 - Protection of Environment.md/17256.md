####### ยง 59.680 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Adjustable parameter

means any device, system, or element of design that someone can adjust and that, if adjusted, may affect emissions. You may ask us to exclude a parameter if you show us that it will not be adjusted in use in a way that affects emissions.
