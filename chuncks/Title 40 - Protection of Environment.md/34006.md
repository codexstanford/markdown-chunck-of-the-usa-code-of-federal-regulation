####### ยง 63.3004 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Loss-on-ignition

means the percentage decrease in weight of fiberglass mat measured before and after it has been ignited to burn off the applied binder. The loss-on-ignition is used to monitor the weight percent of binder in fiberglass mat.
