### Subchapter Q - Energy Policy

###### ยง 600.002 Definitions.
####### City fuel economy

means the city fuel economy determined by operating a vehicle (or vehicles) over the driving schedule in the Federal emission test procedure, or determined according to the vehicle-specific 5-cycle or derived 5-cycle procedures.
