
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 170 - WORKER PROTECTION STANDARD
##### Subpart E - Requirements for Protection of Agricultural Workers
###### § 170.409 Oral and posted notification of worker entry restrictions.
####### General Requirement.

(a)The agricultural employer must notify workers of all entry restrictions required by §§ 170.405 and 170.407 in accordance with this section.
