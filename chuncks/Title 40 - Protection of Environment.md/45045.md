
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 82 - PROTECTION OF STRATOSPHERIC OZONE
##### Subpart F - Recycling and Emissions Reduction
###### Appendix A to Subpart F of Part 82 - Specifications for Refrigerants
####### Cylinder Preparation.

5.2.2Place a clean, empty sample cylinder with the valve open in an oven at 110 &#xB0;C (230 &#xB0;F) for one hour. Remove it from the oven while hot, immediately connect it to an evacuation system and evacuate to less than 56 kPa. Close the valve and allow it to cool. Weigh the empty cylinder.
