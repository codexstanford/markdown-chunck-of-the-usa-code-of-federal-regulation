
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Appendix M to Part 51 - Recommended Test Methods for State Implementation Plans
###### Sampling Head.

8.6.6You must preheat the combined sampling head to the stack temperature of the gas stream at the test location (+/-28 &#xB0;C, +/-50 &#xB0;F). This will heat the sampling head and prevent moisture from condensing from the sample gas stream.
