
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart AA - Air Emission Standards for Process Vents
###### ยง 264.1031 Definitions.
####### Solvent extraction operation

means an operation or method of separation in which a solid or solution is contacted with a liquid solvent (the two being mutually insoluble) to preferentially dissolve and transfer one or more components into the solvent.
