
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Appendix M to Part 51 - Recommended Test Methods for State Implementation Plans
###### 6.5 Sling Psychrometer (optional).

In case of the formation of a steam plume, a wet- and dry-bulb thermometer, accurate to 0.5 &#xB0;C, are mounted on a sturdy assembly and swung rapidly in the air in order to determine the relative humidity.
