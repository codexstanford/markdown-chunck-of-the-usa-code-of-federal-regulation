
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 798 - HEALTH EFFECTS TESTING GUIDELINES
##### Subpart E - Specific Organ/Tissue Toxicity
###### ยง 798.4700 Reproduction and fertility effects.
####### Principle of the test method.

(b)The test substance is administered to parental (P) animals prior to their mating, during the resultant pregnancies, and through the weaning of their Foffspring. The substance is then administered to selected Foffspring during their growth into adulthood, mating, and production of an Fgeneration, up until the Fgeneration is weaned.
