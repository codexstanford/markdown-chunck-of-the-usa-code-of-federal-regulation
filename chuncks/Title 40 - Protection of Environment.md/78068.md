
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

###### ยง 702.31 General provisions.
####### Purpose.

(a)This subpart establishes the EPA process for conducting a risk evaluation to determine whether a chemical substance presents an unreasonable risk of injury to health or the environment as required under TSCA section 6(b)(4)(B) (15 U.S.C. 2605(b)(4)(B)).
