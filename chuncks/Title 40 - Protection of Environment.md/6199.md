######## Additional reasonable measure

is any control measure that otherwise meets the definition of "reasonably available control measure" (RACM) but can only be implemented in whole or in part during the period beginning 4 years after the effective date of designation of a nonattainment area and no later than the end of the sixth calendar year following the effective date of designation of the area.
