
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 63 - NATIONAL EMISSION STANDARDS FOR HAZARDOUS AIR POLLUTANTS FOR SOURCE CATEGORIES

###### General
####### ยง 63.1201 Definitions and acronyms used in this subpart.
######## Hazardous waste burning cement kiln

means a rotary kiln and any associated preheater or precalciner devices that produce clinker by heating limestone and other materials for subsequent production of cement for use in commerce, and that burns hazardous waste at any time.
