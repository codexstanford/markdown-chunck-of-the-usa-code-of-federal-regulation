
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart YYYYY - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Area Sources: Electric Arc Furnace Steelmaking Facilities
###### Other Information and Requirements
####### ยง 63.10692 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Electric arc furnace (EAF) steelmaking facility

means a steel plant that produces carbon, alloy, or specialty steels using an EAF. This definition excludes EAF steelmaking facilities at steel foundries and EAF facilities used to produce nonferrous metals.
