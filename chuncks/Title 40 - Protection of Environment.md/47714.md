###### ยง 86.1811-04 Emission standards for light-duty vehicles, light-duty trucks and medium-duty passenger vehicles.
####### Highway NO,exhaust emission standard.

(j)The NOemissions measured on the federal Highway Fuel Economy Test in 40 CFR 1066.840 must not be greater than 1.33 times the applicable FTP NOstandard to which the manufacturer certifies the test group. Both the measured emissions and the product of the NOstandard and 1.33 must be rounded to the nearest 0.01 g/mi before being compared.
