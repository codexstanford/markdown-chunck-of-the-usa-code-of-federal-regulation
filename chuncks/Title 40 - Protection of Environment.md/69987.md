
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 300.5 Definitions.
####### National Priorities List

(NPL) means the list, compiled by EPA pursuant to CERCLA section 105, of uncontrolled hazardous substance releases in the United States that are priorities for long-term remedial evaluation and response.
