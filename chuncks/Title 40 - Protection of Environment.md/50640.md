######### Availability for deduction for compliance.

(a)CSAPR SOGroup 1 allowances are available to be deducted for compliance with a source's CSAPR SOGroup 1 emissions limitation for a control period in a given year only if the CSAPR SOGroup 1 allowances:

(1) Were allocated or auctioned for such control period or a control period in a prior year; and

(2) Are held in the source's compliance account as of the allowance transfer deadline for such control period.
