
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 176 - TIME-LIMITED TOLERANCES FOR EMERGENCY EXEMPTIONS
##### ยง 176.3 Definitions.
###### Tolerance exemption

means a formal determination by the Agency pursuant to FFDCA section 408(c), 21 U.S.C 346a(c), that no tolerance is needed for a given pesticide chemical residue in or on a particular food commodity. For purposes of this part, the term "tolerance" shall include an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
