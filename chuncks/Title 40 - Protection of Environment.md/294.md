
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - CROSS-MEDIA ELECTRONIC REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 3.3 What definitions are applicable to this part?
####### Agreement collection certification

means a signed statement by which a local registration authority certifies that a subscriber agreement has been received from a registrant; the agreement has been stored in a manner that prevents unauthorized access to these agreements by anyone other than the local registration authority; and the local registration authority has no basis to believe that any of the collected agreements have been tampered with or prematurely destroyed.
