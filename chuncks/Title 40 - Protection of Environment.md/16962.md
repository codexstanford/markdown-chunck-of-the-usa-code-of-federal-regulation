
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart C - National Volatile Organic Compound Emission Standards for Consumer Products
###### ยง 59.202 Definitions.
####### Pump spray

means a packaging system in which the product ingredients are expelled only while a pumping action is applied to a button, trigger, or other actuator. Pump spray product ingredients are not under pressure.
