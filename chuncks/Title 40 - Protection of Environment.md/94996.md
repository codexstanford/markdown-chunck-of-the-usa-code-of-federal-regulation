##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 770.3 Definitions.
####### Veneer

means a sheet of wood or woody grass with a maximum thickness of 6.4 millimeters (inch) that is rotary cut, sliced, or sawed from a log, bolt, flitch, block, or culm; including engineered veneer.
