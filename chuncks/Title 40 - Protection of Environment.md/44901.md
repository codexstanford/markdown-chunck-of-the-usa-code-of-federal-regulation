
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 82 - PROTECTION OF STRATOSPHERIC OZONE
##### Subpart F - Recycling and Emissions Reduction
###### ยง 82.152 Definitions.
####### High-pressure appliance

means an appliance that uses a refrigerant with a liquid phase saturation pressure between 170 psia and 355 psia at 104 &#xB0;F. Examples include but are not limited to appliances using R-22, R-407A, R-407C, R-410A, and R-502.
