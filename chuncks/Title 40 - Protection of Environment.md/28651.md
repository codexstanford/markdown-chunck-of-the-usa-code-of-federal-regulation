
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.981 Definitions.
####### Run

means one of a series of emission or other measurements needed to determine emissions for a representative operating period or cycle as specified in this subpart. Unless otherwise specified, a run may be either intermittent or continuous within the limits of good engineering practice.
