
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provision
###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Carbonate

means compounds containing the radical CO. Upon calcination, the carbonate radical decomposes to evolve carbon dioxide (CO). Common carbonates consumed in the mineral industry include calcium carbonate (CaCO) or calcite; magnesium carbonate (MgCO) or magnesite; and calcium-magnesium carbonate (CaMg(CO)) or dolomite.
