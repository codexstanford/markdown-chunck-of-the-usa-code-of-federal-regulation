
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 241 - SOLID WASTES USED AS FUELS OR INGREDIENTS IN COMBUSTION UNITS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 241.2 Definitions.
####### Established tire collection program

means a comprehensive collection system or contractual arrangement that ensures scrap tires are not discarded and are handled as valuable commodities through arrival at the combustion facility. This can include tires that were not abandoned and were received from the general public at collection program events.
