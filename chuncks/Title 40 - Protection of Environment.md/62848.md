
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter G - Noise Abatement Programs
#### PART 202 - MOTOR CARRIERS ENGAGED IN INTERSTATE COMMERCE
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 202.10 Definitions.
####### Private carrier of property by motor vehicle

(p)means any person not included in terms "common carrier by motor vehicle" or "contract carrier by motor vehicle", who or which transports in interstate or foreign commerce by motor vehicle property of which such person is the owner, lessee, or bailee, when such transportation is for sale, lease, rent or bailment, or in furtherance of any commercial enterprise.
