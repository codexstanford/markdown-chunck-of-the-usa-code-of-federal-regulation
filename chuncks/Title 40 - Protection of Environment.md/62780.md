
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter G - Noise Abatement Programs

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 201.1 Definitions.
####### Energy Summation of Levels

(j)means a quantity calculated by taking ten times the common logarithm of the sum of the antilogs of one-tenth of each of the levels being summed. The levels may be of any consistent type, e.g., day-night sound level or equivalent sound level.
