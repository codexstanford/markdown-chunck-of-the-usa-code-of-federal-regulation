
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 52 - APPROVAL AND PROMULGATION OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Subpart ZZ - Wyoming
###### ยง 52.2636 Implementation plan for regional haze.
####### Applicability.

(a)(1) This section applies to each owner and operator of the following emissions units in the State of Wyoming for which EPA approved the State's BART determination:

(i) FMC Westvaco Trona Plant Units NS-1A and NS-1B (PM and NO);

(ii) TATA Chemicals Partners (previously General Chemical) Boilers C and D (PM and NO);

(iii) Basin Electric Power Cooperative Laramie River Station Units 1, 2, and 3 (PM);

(iv) PacifiCorp Dave Johnston Power Plant Unit 3 (PM);

(v) PacifiCorp Dave Johnston Power Plant Unit 4 (PM and NO);

(vi) PacifiCorp Jim Bridger Power Plant Units 1, 2, 3, and 4 (PM and NO);

(vii) PacifiCorp Naughton Power Plant Units 1 and 2 (PM and NO); and

(viii) PacifiCorp Wyodak Power Plant Unit 1 (PM).

(2) This section also applies to each owner and operator of the following emissions units in the State of Wyoming for which the EPA disapproved the State's BART determination and issued a SOand/or NOBART Federal Implementation Plan:

(i) Basin Electric Power Cooperative Laramie River Station Units 1, 2, and 3;

(ii) PacifiCorp Dave Johnston Unit 3; and

(iii) PacifiCorp Wyodak Power Plant Unit 1.
