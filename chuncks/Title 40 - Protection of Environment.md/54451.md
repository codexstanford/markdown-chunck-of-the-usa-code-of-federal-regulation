
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter D - Water Programs

###### ยง 125.58 Definitions.
####### Application

(d)means a final application previously submitted in accordance with the June 15, 1979, section 301(h) regulations (44 FR 34784); an application submitted between December 29, 1981, and December 29, 1982; or a section 301(h) renewal application submitted in accordance with these regulations.
