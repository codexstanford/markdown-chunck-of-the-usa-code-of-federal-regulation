(a) Approval-On May 18, 2006, New Jersey submitted an early PMimplementation plan to set motor vehicle emissions budgets for the New Jersey portion of the New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island, NY-NJ-CT, PMnonattainment area. The budgets were allocated by metropolitan planning organization as follows: North Jersey Transportation Planning Authority: 1,207 tons per year of direct PMand 61,676 tons per year of NO; Delaware Valley Regional Planning Commission: 89 tons per year of direct PMand 4,328 tons per year of NO.

(b) Approval-On February 25, 2008, New Jersey submitted a revision to its early PMimplementation plan to revise the motor vehicle emissions budgets for the Mercer County, New Jersey portion of the New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island, NY-NJ-CT, PMnonattainment area. The revised budgets, applicable to the Delaware Valley Regional Planning Commission, are as follows: 108 tons per year of direct PMand 5,056 tons per year of NO.
