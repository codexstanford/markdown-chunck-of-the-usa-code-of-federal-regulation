
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.1271 Definitions.
####### Boiler

means an enclosed device using controlled flame combustion and having the primary purpose of recovering and exporting thermal energy in the form of steam or hot water. Boiler also means any industrial furnace as defined in 40 CFR 260.10.
