
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 20 - CERTIFICATION OF FACILITIES
##### Appendix A to Part 20 - Guidelines for Certification
###### Collection devices used to collect products or process material.

(4)In some manufacturing operations, devices are used to collect product or process material, as in the case of the manufacture of carbon black. The baghouse would be eligible for certification, but the certification should notify the Treasury Department of the profitable waste recovery involved. (See paragraph 8 below.)
