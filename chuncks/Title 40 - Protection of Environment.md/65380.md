
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 257 - CRITERIA FOR CLASSIFICATION OF SOLID WASTE DISPOSAL FACILITIES AND PRACTICES
##### Appendix II to Part 257
###### Anaerobic digestion:

The process is conducted in the absence of air at residence times ranging from 60 days at 20 &#xB0;C to 15 days at 35 to 55 &#xB0;C, with a volatile solids reduction of at least 38 percent.
