
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 60 - STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE FOR NEW STATIONARY SOURCES
##### Subpart EE - Standards of Performance for Surface Coating of Metal Furniture
###### ยง 60.311 Definitions and symbols.
####### VOC emissions

means the mass of volatile organic compounds (VOC's), expressed as kilograms of VOC's per liter of applied coating solids, emitted from a metal furniture surface coating operation.
