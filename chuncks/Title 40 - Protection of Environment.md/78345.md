
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act

###### ยง 710.3 Definitions.
####### Small quantities solely for research and development (or,small quantities solely for purposes of scientific experimentation or analysis or chemical research on, or analysis of, such substance or another substance, including such research or analysis for the development of a product")

"means quantities of a chemical substance manufactured, imported, or processed or proposed to be manufactured, imported, or processed solely for research and development that are not greater than reasonably necessary for such purposes.
