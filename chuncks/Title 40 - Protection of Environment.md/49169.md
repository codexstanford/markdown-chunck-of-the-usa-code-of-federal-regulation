########### Subpart GGGG - CAIR NOOzone Season Allowance Transfers
############### ยง 96.360 Submission of CAIR NOOzone Season allowance transfers.

A CAIR authorized account representative seeking recordation of a CAIR NOOzone Season allowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator. To be considered correctly submitted, the CAIR NOOzone Season allowance transfer shall include the following elements, in a format specified by the Administrator:

(a) The account numbers for both the transferor and transferee accounts;

(b) The serial number of each CAIR NOOzone Season allowance that is in the transferor account and is to be transferred; and

(c) The name and signature of the CAIR authorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed.
