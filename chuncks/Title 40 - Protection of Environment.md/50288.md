
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### § 97.375 Petitions.
####### § 97.502 Definitions.
######## Assurance account

means an Allowance Management System account, established by the Administrator under § 97.525(b)(3) for certain owners and operators of a group of one or more CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 1 sources and units in a given State (and Indian country within the borders of such State), in which are held CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 1 allowances available for use for a control period in a given year in complying with the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 1 assurance provisions in accordance with §§ 97.506 and 97.525.
