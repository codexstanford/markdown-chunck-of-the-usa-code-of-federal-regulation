##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 174.3 Definitions.
####### Conventional breeding,i.e.

of plants means the creation of progeny through either: The union of gametes,, syngamy, brought together through processes such as pollination, including bridging crosses between plants and wide crosses, or vegetative reproduction.
