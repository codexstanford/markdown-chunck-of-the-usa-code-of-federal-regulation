
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 246.101 Definitions.
####### Compartmentalized vehicle

(i)means a collection vehicle which has two or more compartments for placement of solid wastes or recyclable materials. The compartments may be within the main truck body or on the outside of that body as in the form of metal racks.
