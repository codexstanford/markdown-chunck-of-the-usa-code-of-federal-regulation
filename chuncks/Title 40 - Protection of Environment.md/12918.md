(202) On June 10, 2014, Illinois submitted revised regulations that are consistent with 40 CFR 51.100(s)(1). The compounds (difluoromethoxy) (difluoro)methane (CHFOCHFor HFE-134), bis(difluoromethoxy) (difluoro)methane (CHFOCFOCHFor HFE-236cal2), 1-(difluoromethoxy)-2-[(difluoromethoxy) ((difluoro)methoxy]-1,1,2,2-tetrafluoroethane (CHFOCFOCFCFOCHFor HFE-43-10pccc), 1,2-bis(difluoromethoxy)-1,1,2,2-tetrafluoroethane (CHFOCFCFOCHFor HFE-338pcc13), and1-chloro-3,3,3-trifluoroprop-1-ene (CFCHCHCl) were added to the list of negligibly reactive compounds excluded from the definition of "Volatile Organic Material (VOM)" or "Volatile Organic Compound (VOC)" at 35 IAC 211.7150(a).
