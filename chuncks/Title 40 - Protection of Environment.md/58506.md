
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart K - Worker Protection Statements

####### Gaseous formulations and applications.

(5)For products formulated or applied as gases, any existing glove statement established before the effective date of this subpart, including any glove prohibition statement, will continue to apply. If no glove statement or glove prohibition now exists, the glove statement shall specify "chemical-resistant (such as nitrile or butyl) gloves."
