
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 62 - APPROVAL AND PROMULGATION OF STATE PLANS FOR DESIGNATED FACILITIES AND POLLUTANTS
##### Subpart Y - Minnesota
###### Existing Large Municipal Waste Combustors
####### ยง 62.5871 Identification of sources.

The plan applies to all existing municipal waste combustor units with the design capacity of 93.75*10Btu/hr or more. This is the same as having an applicability threshold of the capacity to process 250 tons per day or more of municipal solid waste.
