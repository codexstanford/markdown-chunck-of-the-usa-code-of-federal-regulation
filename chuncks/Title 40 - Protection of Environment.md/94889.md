
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 766 - DIBENZO-PARA-DIOXINS/DIBENZOFURANS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 766.3 Definitions.
####### Homolog

means a group of isomers that have the same degree of halogenation. For example, the homologous class of tetrachlorodibenzo-p-dioxins consists of all dibenzo-p-dioxins containing four chlorine atoms.
