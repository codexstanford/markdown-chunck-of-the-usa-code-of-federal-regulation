
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 63.801 Definitions.
####### Alternative method

means any method of sampling and analyzing for an air pollutant that is not a reference or equivalent method but has been demonstrated to the Administrator's satisfaction to, in specific cases, produce results adequate for a determination of compliance.
