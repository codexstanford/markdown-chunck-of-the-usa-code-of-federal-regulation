
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### ยง 97.375 Petitions.
####### ยง 97.902 Definitions.
######## Owner

means, for a Texas SOTrading Program source or a Texas SOTrading Program unit at a source, any of the following persons:

(1) Any holder of any portion of the legal or equitable title in a Texas SOTrading Program unit at the source or the Texas SOTrading Program unit;

(2) Any holder of a leasehold interest in a Texas SOTrading Program unit at the source or the Texas SOTrading Program unit, provided that, unless expressly provided for in a leasehold agreement, "owner" shall not include a passive lessor, or a person who has an equitable interest through such lessor, whose rental payments are not based (either directly or indirectly) on the revenues or income from such Texas SOTrading Program unit; and

(3) Any purchaser of power from a Texas SOTrading Program unit at the source or the Texas SOTrading Program unit under a life-of-the-unit, firm power contractual arrangement.
