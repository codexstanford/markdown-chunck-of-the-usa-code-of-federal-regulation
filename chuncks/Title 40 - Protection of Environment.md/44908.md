##### Subpart F - Recycling and Emissions Reduction

####### Low-pressure appliance

means an appliance that uses a refrigerant with a liquid phase saturation pressure below 45 psia at 104 &#xB0;F. Examples include but are not limited to appliances using R-11, R-123, R-113, and R-245fa.
