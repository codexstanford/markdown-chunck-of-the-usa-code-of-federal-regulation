
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 176 - TIME-LIMITED TOLERANCES FOR EMERGENCY EXEMPTIONS
##### § 176.3 Definitions.
###### Emergency exemption

means a specific, quarantine, or public health exemption authorized under FIFRA section 18 and the regulations at §§ 166.20 through 166.35 of this chapter.
