###### ยง 63.341 Definitions and nomenclature.
####### Large, hard chromium electroplating facility

means a facility that performs hard chromium electroplating and has a maximum cumulative potential rectifier capacity greater than or equal to 60 million ampere-hours per year (amp-hr/yr).
