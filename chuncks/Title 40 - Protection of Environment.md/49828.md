
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### § 97.275 Petitions.
####### § 97.281 General.

(a) Except as otherwise provided in §§ 97.201 through 97.204, §§ 97.206 through 97.208, and subparts BBB and CCC and subparts FFF through HHH of this part, a CAIR SOopt-in unit shall be treated as a CAIR SOunit for purposes of applying such sections and subparts of this part.

(b) Solely for purposes of applying, as provided in this subpart, the requirements of subpart HHH of this part to a unit for which a CAIR opt-in permit application is submitted and not withdrawn and a CAIR opt-in permit is not yet issued or denied under this subpart, such unit shall be treated as a CAIR SOunit before issuance of a CAIR opt-in permit for such unit.
