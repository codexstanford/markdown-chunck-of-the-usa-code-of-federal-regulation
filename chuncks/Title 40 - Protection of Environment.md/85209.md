###### ยง 721.10281 Hexabromocyclododecane and 1,2,5,6,9,10-hexabromocyclododecane.
####### Chemical substances and significant new uses subject to reporting.

(a)(1) The chemical substances identified as hexabromocyclododecane (CASRN 25637-99-4) and 1,2,5,6,9,10-hexabromocyclododecane (CASRN 3194-55-6) are subject to reporting under this section for the significant new use described in paragraph (a)(2) of this section.
