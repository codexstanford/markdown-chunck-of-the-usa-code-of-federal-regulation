##### Subpart D - Storage and Disposal
###### ยง 761.75 Chemical waste landfills.
####### Suction lysimeters.

(iii)This system consists of a network of porous ceramic cups connected by hoses/tubing to a vacuum pump. The porous ceramic cups or suction lysimeters are installed along the sides and under the bottom of the waste disposal unit liner. This type of system works best when installed in a relatively permeable unsaturated soil immediately adjacent to the bottom and/or sides of the disposal facility.
