####### Sampling.

(D)Unless a given test rule specifies otherwise, cross-sections of the following areas shall be examined: The forebrain, the center of the cerebrum, the midbrain, the cerebellum and pons, and the medulla oblongata; the spinal cord at cervical and lumbar swelling (C-Cand L-L); Gasserian ganglia, dorsal root ganglia (C-C, L-L), dorsal and ventral root fibers (C-C, L-L), proximal sciatic nerve (mid-thigh and sciatic notch), sural nerve (at knee), and tibial nerve (at knee).
