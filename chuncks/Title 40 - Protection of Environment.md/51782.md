
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart A - General Provision
###### ยง 98.6 Definitions.
####### Used oil,e.g.,,etc.,e.g.,,etc

means a petroleum-derived or synthetically-derived oil whose physical properties have changed as a result of handling or use, such that the oil cannot be used for its original purpose. Used oil consists primarily of automotive oils (used motor oil, transmission oil, hydraulic fluids, brake fluid,) and industrial oils (industrial engine oils, metalworking oils, process oils, industrial grease,).
