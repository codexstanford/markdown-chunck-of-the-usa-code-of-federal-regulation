### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs

##### Subpart C - Standard for Pesticide Handlers

####### Open cockpit.

(ii)Persons occupying an open cockpit shall use the personal protective equipment specified in the product labeling for use during application, except that chemical-resistant footwear need not be worn.
