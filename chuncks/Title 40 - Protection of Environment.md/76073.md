
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 464 - METAL MOLDING AND CASTING POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### Subpart C - Ferrous Casting Subcategory
###### ยง 464.31 Specialized definitions.
####### Ductile Iron.

(c)A cast iron that has been treated while molten with a master alloy containing an element such as magnesium or cerium to induce the formation of free graphite as nodules or spherules, which imparts a measurable degree of ductility to the cast metal.
