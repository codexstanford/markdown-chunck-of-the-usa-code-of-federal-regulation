
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart D - Lead-Based Paint Hazards
###### ยง 745.63 Definitions.
####### Arithmetic mean

means the algebraic sum of data values divided by the number of data values (e.g., the sum of the concentration of lead in several soil samples divided by the number of samples).
