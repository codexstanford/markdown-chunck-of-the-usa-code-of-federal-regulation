
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

####### Local distribution company (LDC) custody transfer station

means a metering station where the LDC receives a natural gas supply from an upstream supplier, which may be an interstate transmission pipeline or a local natural gas producer, for delivery to customers through the LDC's intrastate transmission or distribution lines.
