######## Cupola startup,Cupola startup

means the commencement of activities needed to take a banked cupola or a cupola that has had the bottom dropped back into melt production.includes the following steps: refractory curing, if needed; cupola bed preparation (during which the sand bed is preheated), if needed; coke bed preparation (during which coke is added to the cupola and lit); and initial metallics charging.
