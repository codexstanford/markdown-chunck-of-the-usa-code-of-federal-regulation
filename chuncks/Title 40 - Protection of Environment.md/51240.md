####### ยง 97.1002 Definitions.
######## Alternate designated representative

means, for a CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 source and each CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 unit at the source, the natural person who is authorized by the owners and operators of the source and all such units at the source, in accordance with this subpart, to act on behalf of the designated representative in matters pertaining to the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 Trading Program. If the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 source is also subject to the Acid Rain Program, CSAPR NOAnnual Trading Program, or CSAPR SOGroup 1 Trading Program, then this natural person shall be the same natural person as the alternate designated representative as defined in the respective program.
