
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards

###### ยง 430.02 Monitoring requirements.
####### Certification in Lieu of Monitoring for Chloroform,Under what circumstances may a discharger be exempt from the minimum monitoring requirements of this section for chloroform?

(f)-(1)A discharger subject to limitations or standards for chloroform under subpart B of this part is not subject to the minimum monitoring requirements specified in this section for chloroform at a fiber line to which the limitations or standards apply if the discharger meets the requirements of this section.
