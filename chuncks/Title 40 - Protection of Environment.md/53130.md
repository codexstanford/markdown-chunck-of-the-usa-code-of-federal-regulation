
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart UU - Injection of Carbon Dioxide
###### ยง 98.470 Definition of the source category.

(a) The injection of carbon dioxide (CO) source category comprises any well or group of wells that inject a COstream into the subsurface.

(b) If you report under subpart RR of this part for a well or group of wells, you are not required to report under this subpart for that well or group of wells.

(c) A facility that is subject to this part only because it is subject to subpart UU of this part is not required to report emissions under subpart C of this part or any other subpart listed in ยง 98.2(a)(1) or (a)(2).
