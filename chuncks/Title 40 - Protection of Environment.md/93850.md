
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 747 - METALWORKING FLUIDS
##### Subpart B - Specific Use Requirements for Certain Chemical Substances
###### ยง 747.200 Triethanolamine salt of tricarboxylic acid.
####### Use limitations.

(c)(1) Any person producing a metalworking fluid, or a product which could be used in or as a metalworking fluid, which includes as one of its components P-83-1005 is prohibited from adding any nitrosating agent to the metalworking fluid or product.

(2) Any person using as metalworking fluid a product containing P-83-1005 is prohibited from adding any nitrosating agent to the product.
