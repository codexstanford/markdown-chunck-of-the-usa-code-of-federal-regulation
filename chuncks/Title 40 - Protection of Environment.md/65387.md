
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 257 - CRITERIA FOR CLASSIFICATION OF SOLID WASTE DISPOSAL FACILITIES AND PRACTICES
##### Appendix II to Part 257
###### Thermophilic Aerobic Digestion:

Liquid sludge is agitated with air or oxygen to maintain aerobic conditions at residence times of 10 days at 55-60 &#xB0;C, with a volatile solids reduction of at least 38 percent.
