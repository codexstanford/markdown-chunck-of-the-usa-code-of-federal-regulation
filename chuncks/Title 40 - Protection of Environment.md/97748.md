
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 799.9120 TSCA acute dermal toxicity.
####### Preparation of animal skin.

(v)Fur must be clipped from the dorsal area of the trunk of the test animals. Shaving may be employed, but it should be carried out at least 24 hours before dosing. Care must be taken to avoid abrading the skin, which would alter its permeability.
