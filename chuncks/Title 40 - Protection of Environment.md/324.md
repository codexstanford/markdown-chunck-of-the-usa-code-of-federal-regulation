
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3 - CROSS-MEDIA ELECTRONIC REPORTING
##### Subpart D - Electronic Reporting Under EPA-Authorized State, Tribe, and Local Programs
###### ยง 3.1000 How does a state, tribe, or local government revise or modify its authorized program to allow electronic reporting?
####### General procedures for program modification or revision:

(1)To revise or modify an authorized program to meet the requirements of this part, a state, tribe, or local government must submit an application that complies with paragraph (b)(1) of this section and must follow either the applicable procedures for program revision or modification in other parts of Title 40, or, at the applicant's option, the procedures provided in paragraphs (b) through (e) of this section.
