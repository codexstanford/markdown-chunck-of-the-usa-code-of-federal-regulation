
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter U - Air Pollution Controls

##### Subpart D - Testing Production-Line Vehicles and Engines
###### ยง 1051.315 How do I know when my engine family fails the production-line testing requirements?
####### Initial and final test results.

(1)Calculate and round the test results for each vehicle or engine. If you do several tests on a vehicle or engine, calculate the initial results for each test, then add all the test results together and divide by the number of tests. Round this final calculated value for the final test results on that vehicle or engine.
