######## Escherichia coli

A temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of lytic bacteriophages that are specific toO157:H7, sequence negative for shiga toxins I and II, and grown on atoxigenic host bacteria when used/applied on food contact surfaces in food processing plants in accordance with the terms of Experimental Use Permit (EUP) No. 74234-EUP-2.
