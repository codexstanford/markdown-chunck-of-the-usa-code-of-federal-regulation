
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 131 - WATER QUALITY STANDARDS
##### Subpart C - Procedures for Review and Revision of Water Quality Standards
###### ยง 131.21 EPA review and approval of water quality standards.
####### For how long does an applicable water quality standard for purposes of the Act remain the applicable water quality standard for purposes of the Act?

(e)A State or authorized Tribe's applicable water quality standard for purposes of the Act remains the applicable standard until EPA approves a change, deletion, or addition to that water quality standard, or until EPA promulgates a more stringent water quality standard.
