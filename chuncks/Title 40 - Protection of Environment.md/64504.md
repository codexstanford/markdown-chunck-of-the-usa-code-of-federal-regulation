
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter H - Ocean Dumping
#### PART 238 - DEGRADABLE PLASTIC RING CARRIERS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 238.20 Definitions.
####### Percent elongation at break

means the percent increase in length of the plastic material caused by a tensile load. Percent elongation at break shall be calculated by dividing the extension at the moment of rupture of the specimen by the initial gage length of the specimen and multiplying by 100.
