
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 70 - STATE OPERATING PERMIT PROGRAMS
##### § 70.2 Definitions.
###### Part 70 source

means any source subject to the permitting requirements of this part, as provided in §§ 70.3(a) and 70.3(b) of this part.
