
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 260.10 Definitions.
####### Thermostat

means a temperature control device that contains metallic mercury in an ampule attached to a bimetal sensing element, and mercury-containing ampules that have been removed from these temperature control devices in compliance with the requirements of 40 CFR 273.13(c)(2) or 273.33(c)(2).
