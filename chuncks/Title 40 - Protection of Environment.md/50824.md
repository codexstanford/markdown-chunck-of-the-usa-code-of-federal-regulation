############### ยง 97.722 Submission of CSAPR SOGroup 2 allowance transfers.

(a) An authorized account representative seeking recordation of a CSAPR SOGroup 2 allowance transfer shall submit the transfer to the Administrator.

(b) A CSAPR SOGroup 2 allowance transfer shall be correctly submitted if:

(1) The transfer includes the following elements, in a format prescribed by the Administrator:

(i) The account numbers established by the Administrator for both the transferor and transferee accounts;

(ii) The serial number of each CSAPR SOGroup 2 allowance that is in the transferor account and is to be transferred; and

(iii) The name and signature of the authorized account representative of the transferor account and the date signed; and

(2) When the Administrator attempts to record the transfer, the transferor account includes each CSAPR SOGroup 2 allowance identified by serial number in the transfer.
