
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 77 - EXCESS EMISSIONS
##### ยง 77.3 Offset plans for excess emissions of sulfur dioxide.
###### Applicability.

(a)The owners and operators of any affected source that has excess emissions of sulfur dioxide in any calendar year shall be liable to offset the amount of such excess emissions by an equal amount of allowances from the source's compliance account.
