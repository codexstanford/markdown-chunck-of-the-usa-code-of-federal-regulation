####### Baseline year for various NAAQS.

(e)The baseline year is defined as follows:

(1) 1990, in areas designated nonattainment for the 1990 CO NAAQS or the 1990 NONAAQS.

(2) 1990, in areas designated nonattainment for the 1990 PMNAAQS, unless the conformity implementation plan revision required by ยง 51.390 of this chapter defines the baseline emissions for a PMarea to be those occurring in a different calendar year for which a baseline emissions inventory was developed for the purpose of developing a control strategy implementation plan.

(3) 2002, in areas designated nonattainment for the 1997 ozone NAAQS or 1997 PMNAAQS.

(4) The most recent year for which EPA's Air Emission Reporting Rule (40 CFR Part 51, Subpart A) requires submission of on-road mobile source emissions inventories as of the effective date of designations, in areas designated nonattainment for a NAAQS that is promulgated after 1997.
