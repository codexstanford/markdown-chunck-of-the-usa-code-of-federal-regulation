### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.482 Definitions.
####### Affirmative defense

means, in the context of an enforcement proceeding, a response or a defense put forward by a defendant, regarding which the defendant has the burden of proof, and the merits of which are independently and objectively evaluated in a judicial or administrative proceeding.
