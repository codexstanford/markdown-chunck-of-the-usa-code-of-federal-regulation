
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 98 - MANDATORY GREENHOUSE GAS REPORTING
##### Subpart RR - Geologic Sequestration of Carbon Dioxide
###### ยง 98.449 Definitions.
####### Maximum monitoring area

means the area that must be monitored under this regulation and is defined as equal to or greater than the area expected to contain the free phase COplume until the COplume has stabilized plus an all-around buffer zone of at least one-half mile.
