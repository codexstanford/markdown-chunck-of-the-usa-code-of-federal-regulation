
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart O - Ethylene Oxide Emissions Standards for Sterilization Facilities
###### ยง 63.361 Definitions.
####### Source(s) using 1 to 10 tons

means source(s) using 907 kg (1 ton) or more of ethylene oxide in any consecutive 12-month period but less than 9,070 kg (10 tons) of ethylene oxide in all consecutive 12-month periods after December 6, 1996.
