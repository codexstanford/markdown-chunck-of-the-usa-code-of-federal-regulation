##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 195.2 Definitions.
####### Radon Measurement Proficiency (RMP) program

refers to EPA's program to evaluate organizations and individuals offering measurement services to consumers. It provides a means for organizations to demonstrate their proficiency in measuring radon and its decay products in indoor air.
