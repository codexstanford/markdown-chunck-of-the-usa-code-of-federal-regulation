
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 60 - STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE FOR NEW STATIONARY SOURCES
##### Subpart CC - Standards of Performance for Glass Manufacturing Plants
###### ยง 60.291 Definitions.
####### Soda-lime recipe

means glass product composition of the following ranges of weight proportions: 60 to 75 percent silicon dioxide, 10 to 17 percent total RO (e.g., NaO and KO), 8 to 20 percent total RO but not to include any PbO (e.g., CaO, and MgO), 0 to 8 percent total RO(e.g., AlO), and 1 to 5 percent other oxides.
