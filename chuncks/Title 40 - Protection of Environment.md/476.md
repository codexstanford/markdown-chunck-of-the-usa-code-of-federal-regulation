
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 6 - PROCEDURES FOR IMPLEMENTING THE NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY ACT AND ASSESSING THE ENVIRONMENTAL EFFECTS ABROAD OF EPA ACTIONS
##### Subpart D - Assessing the Environmental Effects Abroad of EPA Actions
###### ยง 6.402 Definitions.
####### environment,global commons,responsible official,significantly,significant

As used in this subpart,means the natural and physical environment and excludes social, economic and other environments;is that area (land, air, water) outside the jurisdiction of any nation; andis either the EPA Assistant Administrator or Regional Administrator as appropriate for the particular EPA program. Also, an actionaffects the environment if it doesharm to the environment even though on balance the action may be beneficial to the environment. To the extent applicable, the responsible official shall address the considerations set forth in the CEQ regulations under 40 CFR 1508.27 in determining significant effect.
