######## Excess emissions

means any ton, or portion of a ton, of sulfur dioxide emitted by the CAIR SOunits at a CAIR SOsource during a control period that exceeds the CAIR SOemissions limitation for the source, provided that any portion of a ton of excess emissions shall be treated as one ton of excess emissions.
