####### § 63.1213 How can the compliance date be extended to install pollution prevention or waste minimization controls?
######## Approval of request for extension of compliance date.

(c)Based on the information provided in any request made under paragraph (a) of this section, the Administrator or State with an approved title V program may grant an extension of the compliance date of this subpart. The extension will be in writing in accordance with §§ 63.6(i)(10)(i) through 63.6(i)(10)(v)(A).
