######### Availability for deduction.

(a)CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 allowances are available to be deducted for compliance with the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 assurance provisions for a control period in a given year by the owners and operators of a group of one or more base CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 sources and units in a State (and Indian country within the borders of such State) only if the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 allowances:

(1) Were allocated or auctioned for a control period in a prior year or the control period in the given year or in the immediately following year; and

(2) Are held in the assurance account, established by the Administrator for such owners and operators of such group of base CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 sources and units in such State (and Indian country within the borders of such State) under paragraph (b)(3) of this section, as of the deadline established in paragraph (b)(4) of this section.
