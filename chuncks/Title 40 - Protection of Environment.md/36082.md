###### Other Requirements and Information
####### ยง 63.5935 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Centrifugal casting

means a process for fabricating cylindrical composites, such as pipes, in which composite materials are positioned inside a rotating hollow mandrel and held in place by centrifugal forces until the part is sufficiently cured to maintain its physical shape.
