### Subchapter C - Air Programs

###### ยง 63.742 Definitions.
####### Topcoat

means a coating that is applied over a primer on an aerospace vehicle or component for appearance, identification, camouflage, or protection. Coatings that are defined as specialty coatings are not included under this definition.
