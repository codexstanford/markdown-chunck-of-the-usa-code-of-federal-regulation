####### ยง 63.8050 How do I comply with emissions averaging for stationary process vessels at existing sources?
######## General requirements.

(b)(1) A State may prohibit averaging of HAP emissions and require the owner or operator of an existing affected source to comply with the emission limits and work practice standards in Table 1 to this subpart.

(2) All stationary process vessels in an emissions averaging group must be equipped with a tightly-fitting vented cover.
