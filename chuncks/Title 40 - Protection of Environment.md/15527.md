
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 53 - AMBIENT AIR MONITORING REFERENCE AND EQUIVALENT METHODS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 53.1 Definitions.
####### PM,sampler

means a sampler, or a collocated pair of samplers, associated with a manual method for measuring PMand designed to collect either PMdirectly or PMand PMseparately and simultaneously from concurrent ambient air samples, but lacking the ability to automatically analyze or measure the collected sample(s) to determine the mass concentrations of PMin the sampled air.
