
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 442 - TRANSPORTATION EQUIPMENT CLEANING POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### Subpart C - Tank Barges and Ocean/Sea Tankers Transporting Chemical and Petroleum Cargos
###### ยง 442.30 Applicability.

This subpart applies to discharges resulting from the cleaning of tank barges or ocean/sea tankers which have been used to transport chemical or petroleum cargos.
