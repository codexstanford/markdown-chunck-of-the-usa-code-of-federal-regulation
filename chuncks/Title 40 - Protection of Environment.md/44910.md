
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 82 - PROTECTION OF STRATOSPHERIC OZONE
##### Subpart F - Recycling and Emissions Reduction
###### ยง 82.152 Definitions.
####### Medium-pressure appliance

means an appliance that uses a refrigerant with a liquid phase saturation pressure between 45 psia and 170 psia at 104 &#xB0;F. Examples include but are not limited to appliances using R-114, R-124, R-12, R-134a, and R-500.
