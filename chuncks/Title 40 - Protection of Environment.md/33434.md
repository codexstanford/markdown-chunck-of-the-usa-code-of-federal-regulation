
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 63 - NATIONAL EMISSION STANDARDS FOR HAZARDOUS AIR POLLUTANTS FOR SOURCE CATEGORIES
##### Subpart DDDD - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Plywood and Composite Wood Products
###### Other Requirements and Information
####### ยง 63.2292 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Representative operating conditions,e.g.,

means operation of a process unit during performance testing under the conditions that the process unit will typically be operating in the future, including use of a representative range of materials (wood material of a typical species mix and moisture content or typical resin formulation) and representative operating temperature range. Representative operating conditions exclude periods of startup and shutdown.
