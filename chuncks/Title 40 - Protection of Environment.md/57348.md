
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 146 - UNDERGROUND INJECTION CONTROL PROGRAM: CRITERIA AND STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 146.3 Definitions.
####### State Director

means the chief administrative officer of any State, interstate, or Tribal agency operating an "approved program," or the delegated representative of the State Director. If the responsibility is divided among two or more State, interstate, or Tribal agencies, "State Director" means the chief administrative officer of the State, interstate, or Tribal agency authorized to perform the particular procedure or function to which reference is made.
