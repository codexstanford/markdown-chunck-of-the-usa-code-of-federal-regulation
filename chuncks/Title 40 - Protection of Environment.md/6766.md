
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 51 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PREPARATION, ADOPTION, AND SUBMITTAL OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Appendix S to Part 51 - Emission Offset Interpretative Ruling
###### Condition 3.,offsets

Emission reductions () from existing sourcesin the area of the proposed source (whether or not under the same ownership) are required such that there will be reasonable progress toward attainment of the applicable NAAQS.Except as provided in paragraph IV.G.5 of this Ruling (addressing PMand its precursors), only intrapollutant emission offsets will be acceptable (e.g., hydrocarbon increases may not be offset against SOreductions).

Subject to the provisions of paragraph IV.C of this Ruling.

The discussion in this paragraph is a proposal, but represents EPA's interim policy until final rulemaking is completed.
