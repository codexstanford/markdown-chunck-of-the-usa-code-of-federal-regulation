### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes

##### Subpart K - UST Systems with Field-Constructed Tanks and Airport Hydrant Fuel Distribution Systems
###### ยง 280.252 Additions, exceptions, and alternatives for UST systems with field-constructed tanks and airport hydrant systems.
####### Applicability of closure requirements to previously closed UST systems.

(e)When directed by the implementing agency, the owner and operator of an UST system with field-constructed tanks or airport hydrant system permanently closed before October 13, 2015 must assess the excavation zone and close the UST system in accordance with subpart G of this part if releases from the UST may, in the judgment of the implementing agency, pose a current or potential threat to human health and the environment.
