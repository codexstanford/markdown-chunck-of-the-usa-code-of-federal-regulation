##### Subpart UUUU - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Cellulose Products Manufacturing
###### Other Requirements and Information
####### ยง 63.5610 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Solvent coating process

means a manufacturing process in which cellophane film is coated (e.g., with Saran &#xAE; or nitrocellulose) to impart moisture impermeability to the film and to make it printable. Both Saran and nitrocellulose use the same solvents-tetrahydrofuran and toluene.
