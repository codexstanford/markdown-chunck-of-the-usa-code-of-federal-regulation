####### ยง 63.9824 What definitions apply to this subpart?
######## Period of natural gas curtailment or supply interruption

means a period of time during which the supply of natural gas to an affected facility is halted for reasons beyond the control of the facility. An increase in the cost or unit price of natural gas does not constitute a period of natural gas curtailment or supply interruption.
