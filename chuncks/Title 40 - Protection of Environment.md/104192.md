We may review your records at any time so it is important that you keep required information readily available. Keep records associated with your recall campaign for five years after you send the last report we require under ยง 1068.525(b). Organize and maintain your records as described in this section.

(a) Keep a paper copy of the written reports described in ยง 1068.525.

(b) Keep a record of the names and addresses of owners you notified. For each engine or piece of equipment, state whether you did any of the following:

(c) You may keep the records in paragraph (b) of this section in any form we can inspect, including computer databases.
