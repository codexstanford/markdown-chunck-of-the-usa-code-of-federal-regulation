
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter I - Solid Wastes
#### PART 257 - CRITERIA FOR CLASSIFICATION OF SOLID WASTE DISPOSAL FACILITIES AND PRACTICES
##### Appendix II to Part 257
###### Gamma ray irradiation:

Sludge is irradiated with gamma rays from certain isotopes, such asCobalt andCesium, at dosages of at least 1.0 megarad at room temperature (ca. 20 &#xB0;C).
