
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 796 - CHEMICAL FATE TESTING GUIDELINES
##### Subpart B - Physical and Chemical Properties
###### ยง 796.1050 Absorption in aqueous solution: Ultraviolet/visible spectra.
####### Blank solutions.

(B)A blank must be prepared which contains the solvent and all chemical species other than the test chemical. The absorption spectrum of this solution should be recorded in a manner identical to that of the test solution and preferably on the same chart. This "baseline" spectrum should never record an absorbance reading varying more than +/-0.05 from the nominal zero value.
