
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 75 - CONTINUOUS EMISSION MONITORING
##### Subpart G - Reporting Requirements
###### § 75.68 Temporary modifications to otherwise applicable quality-assurance requirements during the COVID-19 national emergency.
####### § 75.73 Recordkeeping and reporting.
######## Specific NO,record provisions for gas-fired or oil-fired units using the optional low mass emissions excepted methodology in § 75.19.

(7)In lieu of recording the information in §§ 75.57(b), (c)(2), (d), and (g), the owner or operator shall record, for each hour when the unit is operating for any portion of the hour, the following information for each affected low mass emissions unit for which the owner or operator is using the low mass emissions excepted methodology in § 75.19(c):

(i) Date and hour;

(ii) If one type of fuel is combusted in the hour, fuel type (pipeline natural gas, natural gas, residual oil, or diesel fuel) or, if more than one type of fuel is combusted in the hour, the fuel type which results in the highest emission factors for NO;

(iii) Average hourly NOemission rate (in lb/mmBtu, rounded to the nearest thousandth); and

(iv) Hourly NOmass emissions (in lbs, rounded to the nearest tenth).

(8) Formulas from monitoring plan for total NOmass.
