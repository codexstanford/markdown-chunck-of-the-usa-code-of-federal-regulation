
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 66 - ASSESSMENT AND COLLECTION OF NONCOMPLIANCE PENALTIES BY EPA
##### Subpart D - Exemption Requests; Revocation of Exemptions
###### ยง 66.33 De Minimis exemptions: malfunctions.
####### de minimis

(a) The Administrator may, upon notice and opportunity for a public hearing, exempt the owner or operator of a source if he finds with respect to a particular instance of noncompliance, that such noncompliance wasin nature and duration, and was caused solely by a sudden and unavoidable breakdown of process or pollution control equipment.
