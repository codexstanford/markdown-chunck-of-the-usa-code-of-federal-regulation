
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter J - Superfund, Emergency Planning, and Community Right-to-Know Programs
#### PART 300 - NATIONAL OIL AND HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES POLLUTION CONTINGENCY PLAN
##### Appendix A to Part 300 - The Hazard Ranking System
###### Octanol-water partition coefficient (K,or P

[]): Measure of the extent of partitioning of a substance between water and octanol at equilibrium. The Kis determined by the ratio between the concentration in octanol divided by the concentration in water at equilibrium. [unitless].
