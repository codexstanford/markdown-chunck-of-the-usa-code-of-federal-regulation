######### Availability for deduction.

(a)Texas SOTrading Program allowances are available to be deducted for compliance with the Texas SOTrading Program assurance provisions for a control period in a given year by the owners and operators of a group of one or more Texas SOTrading Program sources and units only if the Texas SOTrading Program allowances:

(1) Were allocated for a control period in a prior year or the control period in the given year or in the immediately following year; and

(2) Are held in the assurance account, established by the Administrator for such owners and operators of such group of Texas SOTrading Program sources and units under paragraph (b)(3) of this section, as of the deadline established in paragraph (b)(4) of this section.
