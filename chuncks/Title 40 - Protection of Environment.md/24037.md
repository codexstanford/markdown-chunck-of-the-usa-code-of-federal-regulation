####### ยง 62.7870 Identification of plan - negative declarations.

Letters from the New Mexico Environment Department and the City of Albuquerque Environmental Health Department dated February 11, 2014, and February 4, 2014, respectively, certifying that there are no existing hospital/medical/infectious waste incinerator (HMIWI) units subject to 40 CFR part 60, subpart Ce, within their respective jurisdictions in the State of New Mexico.
