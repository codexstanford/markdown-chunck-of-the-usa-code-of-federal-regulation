######## Lead oxide manufacturing facility

means a facility that produces lead oxide from lead for use in lead acid batteries, including lead oxide production and product recovery operations. Local exhaust ventilation or building ventilation exhausts serving lead oxide production areas are not part of the lead oxide manufacturing facility.
