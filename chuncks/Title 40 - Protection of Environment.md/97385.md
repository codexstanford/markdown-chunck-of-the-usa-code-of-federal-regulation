
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act

##### Subpart D - Multichemical Test Rules
###### ยง 799.5089 Chemical testing requirements for third group of high production volume chemicals (HPV3).
####### How are EPA's data reimbursement procedures affected for purposes of this section?

(f)If persons subject to this section are unable to agree on the amount or method of reimbursement for test data development for one or more chemical substances included in this section, any person may request a hearing as described in 40 CFR part 791. In the determination of fair reimbursement shares under this section, if the hearing officer chooses to use a formula based on production volume, the total production volume amount will include amounts of a chemical substance produced as an impurity.
