
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs

##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1328 Beauveria bassiana strain ANT-03; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Beauveria bassiana

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues ofstrain ANT-03 in or on all food commodities, when applied as a microbial insecticide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
