##### Subpart D - Reference Information
###### ยง 1031.205 Definitions.
####### Rated pressure ratio (rPR)

means the ratio between the combustor inlet pressure and the engine inlet pressure achieved by an engine operating at rated output, expressed to at least three significant figures.
