
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 170 - WORKER PROTECTION STANDARD
##### Subpart F - Requirements for Protection of Agricultural Pesticide Handlers
###### ยง 170.505 Requirements during applications to protect handlers, workers, and other persons.
####### Prohibition from contacting workers and other persons with pesticides during application.

(a)The handler employer and the handler must ensure that no pesticide is applied so as to contact, directly or through drift, any worker or other person, other than an appropriately trained and equipped handler involved in the application.
