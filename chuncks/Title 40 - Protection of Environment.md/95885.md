
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 797 - ENVIRONMENTAL EFFECTS TESTING GUIDELINES
##### Subpart B - Aquatic Guidelines
###### § 797.1600 Fish early life stage toxicity test.
####### Saltwater.,1

(C)() Marine dilution water is considered to be of constant quality if the minimum salinity is greater than 15/and the weekly range of the salinity is less than 15/. The monthly range of pH shall be less than 0.8 pH units. Saltwater shall be filtered to remove larval predators. A pore size of &#x2264;20 micrometers (µm) is recommended. For silversides, the recommended salinity is 20 ppt and shall be maintained between 15 and 25 ppt throughout testing.
