
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart FFF - Standards of Performance for Flexible Vinyl and Urethane Coating and Printing
###### ยง 60.581 Definitions and symbols.
####### Flexible vinyl and urethane products

mean those products, except for resilient floor coverings (1977 Standard Industry Code 3996) and flexible packaging, that are more than 50 micrometers (0.002 inches) thick, and that consist of or contain a vinyl or urethane sheet or a vinyl or urethane coated web.
