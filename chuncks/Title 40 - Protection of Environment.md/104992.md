
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter IV - Environmental Protection Agency and Department of Justice
### Subchapter A - Accidental Release Prevention Requirements; Risk Management Programs Under the Clean Air Act Section 112(r)(7); Distribution of Off-Site Consequence Analysis Information
#### PART 1400 - DISTRIBUTION OF OFF-SITE CONSEQUENCE ANALYSIS INFORMATION
##### Subpart B - Public Access
###### ยง 1400.4 Vulnerable zone indicator system.
####### In general.

(a)The Administrator shall provide access to a computer-based indicator that shall inform any person located in any state whether an address specified by that person might be within the vulnerable zone of one or more stationary sources, according to the data reported in RMPs. The indicator also shall provide information about how to obtain further information.
