######## Most stringent measure

(MSM) is any permanent and enforceable control measure that achieves the most stringent emissions reductions in direct PMemissions and/or emissions of PMplan precursors from among those control measures which are either included in the SIP for any other NAAQS, or have been achieved in practice in any state, and that can feasibly be implemented in the relevant PMNAAQS nonattainment area.
