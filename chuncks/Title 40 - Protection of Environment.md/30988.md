
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 63 - NATIONAL EMISSION STANDARDS FOR HAZARDOUS AIR POLLUTANTS FOR SOURCE CATEGORIES
##### Subpart HHH - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants From Natural Gas Transmission and Storage Facilities
###### ยง 63.1271 Definitions.
####### Temperature monitoring device

means an instrument used to monitor temperature and having a minimum accuracy of +/-2 percent of the temperature being monitored expressed in &#xB0;C, or +/-2.5 &#xB0;C, whichever is greater. The temperature monitoring device may measure temperature in degrees Fahrenheit or degrees Celsius, or both.
