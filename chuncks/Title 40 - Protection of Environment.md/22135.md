
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 60 - STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE FOR NEW STATIONARY SOURCES
##### Appendix A-4 to Part 60 - Test Methods 6 through 10B
###### 7.1 Calibration Gas. What calibration gases do I need?

Refer to section 7.1 of Method 7E for the calibration gas requirements. Example calibration gas mixtures are listed below.

(a) SOin nitrogen (N).

(b) SOin air.

(c) SOand COin N.

(d) SOandOin N.

(e) SO/CO/Ogas mixture in N.

(f) CO/NOgas mixture in N.

(g) CO/SO/NOgas mixture in N.
