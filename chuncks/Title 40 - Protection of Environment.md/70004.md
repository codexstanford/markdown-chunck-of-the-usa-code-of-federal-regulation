
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency

##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 300.5 Definitions.
####### Preliminary assessment

(PA) under CERCLA means review of existing information and an off-site reconnaissance, if appropriate, to determine if a release may require additional investigation or action. A PA may include an on-site reconnaissance, if appropriate.
