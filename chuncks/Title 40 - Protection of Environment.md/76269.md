
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards

##### General Provisions
###### ยง 467.02 General definitions.
####### Continuous casting

(d)is the production of sheet, rod, or other long shapes by solidifying the metal while it is being poured through an open-ended mold using little or no contact cooling water. Continuous casting of rod and sheet generates spent lubricants and rod casting also generates contact cooling water.
