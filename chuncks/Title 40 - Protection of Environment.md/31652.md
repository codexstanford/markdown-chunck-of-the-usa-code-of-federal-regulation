####### Container,

as used in the wastewater provisions, means any portable waste management unit that has a capacity greater than or equal to 0.1 min which a material is stored, transported, treated, or otherwise handled. Examples of containers are drums, barrels, tank trucks, barges, dumpsters, tank cars, dump trucks, and ships.
