
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart E - Satisfaction of Data Requirements and Protection of Data Submitters' Rights
###### ยง 152.96 Claim of data gap.
####### When a data gap may be claimed.

(a)Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, an applicant may defer his obligation to satisfy an applicable data requirement until the Agency requires the data if no other person has previously submitted to the Agency a valid study that would satisfy the data requirement in question.
