
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter B - Grants and Other Federal Assistance

######## Employment.

(B)The contractor shall not accept employment from any party other than the recipient or Federal agencies for work directly related to the site(s) covered under the contract for five years after the contract has terminated. The recipient agency may exempt the contractor from this requirement through a written release. This release must include EPA concurrence.
