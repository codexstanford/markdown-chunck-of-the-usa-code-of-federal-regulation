
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 160 - GOOD LABORATORY PRACTICE STANDARDS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 160.3 Definitions.
####### Control substance

means any chemical substance or mixture, or any other material other than a test substance, feed, or water, that is administered to the test system in the course of a study for the purpose of establishing a basis for comparison with the test substance for known chemical or biological measurements.
