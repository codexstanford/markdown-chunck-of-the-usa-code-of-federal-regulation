##### Subpart J - Definitions and Other Reference Information
###### ยง 1042.901 Definitions.
####### Recreational vessel

means a vessel that is intended by the vessel manufacturer to be operated primarily for pleasure or leased, rented or chartered to another for the latter's pleasure. However, this does not include the following vessels:

(1) Vessels below 100 gross tons that carry more than 6 passengers.

(2) Vessels at or above 100 gross tons that carry one or more passengers.

(3) Vessels used solely for competition (see ยง 1042.620).
