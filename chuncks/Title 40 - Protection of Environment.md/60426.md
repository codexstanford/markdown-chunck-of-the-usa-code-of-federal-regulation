
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

(ii)The commodities included in Crop Group 17 are: Forage, fodder, stover, and hay of any grass,family (either green or cured) except sugarcane and those included in the cereal grains group, that will be fed to or grazed by livestock, all pasture and range grasses and grasses grown for hay or silage.
