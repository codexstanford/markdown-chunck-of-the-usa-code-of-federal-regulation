(c)-(1)The P of a substance between water and a lipophilic solvent (-octanol) is one model variable which may be used to describe the transfer of a substance from the aquatic environment into an organism and the potential bioaccumulation of the substance. Studies show a highly significant relationship between the P of different substances in the system water/-octanol and their bioaccumulation in fish described in paragraph (f)(1) of this section.
