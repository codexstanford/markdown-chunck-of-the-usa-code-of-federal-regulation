(164) On June 13, 2003, and as supplemented on October 3, 2003, Indiana submitted a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision for the control of emissions of particulate matter (PM) in the state of Indiana. Revisions to 326 IAC 6-1-10.1 and 326 IAC 6-1-10.2 amend the PMemission limits at U.S. Steel-Gary Works and U.S. Steel-Gary Coke Operations, located in Lake County, Indiana, and should result in decreased PMemissions of approximately 350 tons per year.
