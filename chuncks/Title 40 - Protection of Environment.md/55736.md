
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 141.2 Definitions.
####### Trihalomethane

(THM) means one of the family of organic compounds, named as derivatives of methane, wherein three of the four hydrogen atoms in methane are each substituted by a halogen atom in the molecular structure.
