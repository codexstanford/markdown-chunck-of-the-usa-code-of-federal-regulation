
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 97 - FEDERAL NOX BUDGET TRADING PROGRAM, CAIR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, CSAPR NOX AND SO2 TRADING PROGRAMS, AND TEXAS SO2 TRADING PROGRAM
##### Subpart HHHH - Monitoring and Reporting
###### § 97.375 Petitions.
####### § 97.1002 Definitions.
######## Compliance account

means an Allowance Management System account, established by the Administrator for a CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 source under this subpart, in which any CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 allowance allocations to the CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 units at the source are recorded and in which are held any CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 allowances available for use for a control period in a given year in complying with the source's CSAPR NOOzone Season Group 3 emissions limitation in accordance with §§ 97.1006 and 97.1024.
