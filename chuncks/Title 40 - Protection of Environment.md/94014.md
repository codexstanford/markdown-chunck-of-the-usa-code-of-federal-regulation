
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter R - Toxic Substances Control Act
#### PART 761 - POLYCHLORINATED BIPHENYLS (PCBs) MANUFACTURING, PROCESSING, DISTRIBUTION IN COMMERCE, AND USE PROHIBITIONS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 761.3 Definitions.
####### Non-liquid PCBs

means materials containing PCBs that by visual inspection do not flow at room temperature (25 &#xB0;C or 77 &#xB0;F) or from which no liquid passes when a 100 g or 100 ml representative sample is placed in a mesh number 60 +/-5 percent paint filter and allowed to drain at room temperature for 5 minutes.
