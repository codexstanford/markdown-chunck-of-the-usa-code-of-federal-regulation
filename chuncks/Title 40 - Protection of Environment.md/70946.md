
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter J - Superfund, Emergency Planning, and Community Right-to-Know Programs
#### PART 302 - DESIGNATION, REPORTABLE QUANTITIES, AND NOTIFICATION
##### ยง 302.8 Continuous releases.
###### Continuous.

A continuous release is a release that occurs without interruption or abatement or that is routine, anticipated, and intermittent and incidental to normal operations or treatment processes.
