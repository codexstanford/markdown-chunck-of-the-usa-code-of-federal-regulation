
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 439 - PHARMACEUTICAL MANUFACTURING POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### Subpart A - Fermentation Products
###### ยง 439.12 Effluent limitations attainable by the application of the best practicable control technology currently available (BPT).

Except as provided in 40 CFR 125.30 through 125.32, any existing point source subject to this subpart must achieve the following effluent limitations representing the application of BPT:

(a) The maximum monthly average limitation for BOD, expressed as mass loading (lbs., kg) per day, must reflect not less than 90 percent reduction in the long-term average daily BODload of the raw (untreated) process wastewater, multiplied by a variability factor of 3.0.
