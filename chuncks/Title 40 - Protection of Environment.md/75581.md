
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 442 - TRANSPORTATION EQUIPMENT CLEANING POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### ยง 442.2 General definitions.
###### Heel

means any material remaining in a tank following unloading, delivery, or discharge of the transported cargo. Heels may also be referred to as container residue, residual materials or residuals.
