
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs

##### Subpart M - National Perchloroethylene Air Emission Standards for Dry Cleaning Facilities
###### ยง 63.321 Definitions.
####### Colorimetric detector tube

means a glass tube (sealed prior to use), containing material impregnated with a chemical that is sensitive to perchloroethylene and is designed to measure the concentration of perchloroethylene in air.
