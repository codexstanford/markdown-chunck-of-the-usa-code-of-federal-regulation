
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter D - Water Programs
#### PART 105 - RECOGNITION AWARDS UNDER THE CLEAN WATER ACT
##### General
###### ยง 105.2 Definitions.
####### Industrial organization

means any company, corporation, association, partnership, firm, university, not-for-profit organization, or wastewater treatment facility, as well as a Federal, State or Tribal government wastewater treatment facility, or U.S. military command to the extent such government and other organizations operate in an "industrial" capacity in the treatment of wastes or abatement of pollution.
