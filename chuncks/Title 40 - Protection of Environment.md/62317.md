
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 180 - TOLERANCES AND EXEMPTIONS FOR PESTICIDE CHEMICAL RESIDUES IN FOOD
##### Subpart D - Exemptions From Tolerances
###### ยง 180.1276 Tobacco mild green mosaic tobamovirus strain U2; exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
####### Tobacco mild green mosaic tobamovirus

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues ofstrain U2 in or on all commodities of crop groups 17 and 18 when applied as a post-emergent herbicide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
