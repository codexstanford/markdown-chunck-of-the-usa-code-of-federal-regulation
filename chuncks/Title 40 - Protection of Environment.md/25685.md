####### Temperature monitoring device

means a unit of equipment used to monitor temperature and having a minimum accuracy of (a) +/-1 percent of the temperature being monitored expressed in degrees Celsius ((&#xB0;C) or (b) +/-0.5 degrees (&#xB0;C), whichever is greater.
