
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 50 - NATIONAL PRIMARY AND SECONDARY AMBIENT AIR QUALITY STANDARDS
##### Appendix T to Part 50 - Interpretation of the Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Oxides of Sulfur (Sulfur Dioxide)
###### Pollutant Occurrence Code (POC),etc.

refers to a numerical code (1, 2, 3,) used to distinguish the data from two or more monitors for the same parameter at a single monitoring site.
