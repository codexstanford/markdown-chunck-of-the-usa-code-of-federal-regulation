##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 171.3 Definitions.
####### Calibration

means measurement of dispersal or output of application equipment and adjustment of such equipment to establish a specific rate of dispersal and, if applicable, droplet or particle size of a pesticide, and/or equalized dispersal pattern.
