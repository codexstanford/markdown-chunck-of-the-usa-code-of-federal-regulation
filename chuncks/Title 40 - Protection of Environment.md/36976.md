##### Subpart EEEEE - National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Iron and Steel Foundries

######## Electric induction furnace

means a vessel in which forms of iron and steel such as scrap and foundry returns are melted though resistance heating by an electric current that is induced in the metal by passing an alternating current through a coil surrounding the metal charge or surrounding a pool of molten metal at the bottom of the vessel.
