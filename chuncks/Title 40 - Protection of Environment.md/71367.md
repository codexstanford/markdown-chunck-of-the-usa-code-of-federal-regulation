
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter J - Superfund, Emergency Planning, and Community Right-to-Know Programs
#### PART 370 - HAZARDOUS CHEMICAL REPORTING: COMMUNITY RIGHT-TO-KNOW
##### Subpart D - Community Access to Information
###### ยง 370.60 How does a person obtain MSDS (or SDS) information about a specific facility?

Any person may obtain an MSDS (or SDS) for a specific facility by writing to the LEPC and asking for it.

(a) If the LEPC has the MSDS, (or SDS) it must provide it to the person making the request.

(b) If the LEPC does not have the MSDS (or SDS), (or (SDS) it must request the MSDS (or SDS) from the facility's owner or operator.
