
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 52 - APPROVAL AND PROMULGATION OF IMPLEMENTATION PLANS
##### Subpart S - Kentucky
###### ยง 52.937 Review of new sources and modifications.

(a) Approval-EPA is approving the section 182(f) oxides of nitrogen (NO) reasonably available control technology (RACT) exemption request submitted by the Kentucky Department for Environmental Protection on August 16, 1994, for the Kentucky portion of the Huntington-Ashland ozone (O) moderate nonattainment area. This approval exempts this area from implementing NORACT on major sources of NO. If a violation of the ONAAQS occurs in the area, the exemption from the requirement of section 182(f) of the CAA in the applicable area shall not apply.

(b) Approval-EPA is approving the section 182(f) oxides of nitrogen (NO) reasonably available control technology (RACT) exemption for the Kentucky portion of the Cincinnati-Hamilton ozone (O) moderate nonattainment area. This approval exempts this area from implementing NORACT on major sources of NO.
