
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 75 - CONTINUOUS EMISSION MONITORING
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 75.1 Purpose and scope.
####### Purpose.

(a)The purpose of this part is to establish requirements for the monitoring, recordkeeping, and reporting of sulfur dioxide (SO), nitrogen oxides (NO), and carbon dioxide (CO) emissions, volumetric flow, and opacity data from affected units under the Acid Rain Program pursuant to sections 412 and 821 of the CAA, 42 U.S.C. 7401-7671q as amended by Public Law 101-549 (November 15, 1990) [the Act]. In addition, this part sets forth provisions for the monitoring, recordkeeping, and reporting of NOmass emissions with which EPA, individual States, or groups of States may require sources to comply in order to demonstrate compliance with a NOmass emission reduction program, to the extent these provisions are adopted as requirements under such a program.
