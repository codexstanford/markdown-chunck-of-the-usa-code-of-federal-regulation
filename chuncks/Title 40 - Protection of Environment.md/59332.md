
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - Pesticide Programs
#### PART 165 - PESTICIDE MANAGEMENT AND DISPOSAL
##### Subpart B - Nonrefillable Container Standards: Container Design and Residue Removal
###### § 165.23 Scope of pesticide products included.
####### What does "pesticide product" or "pesticide" mean in the rest of this subpart?

(f)In §§ 165.25 through 165.27, the term "pesticide product" or "pesticide" refers only to a pesticide product or a pesticide that is subject to the regulations in this subpart as described in paragraphs (a) through (e) of this section.
