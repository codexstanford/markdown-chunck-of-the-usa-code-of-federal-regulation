######## CAIR permit

means the legally binding and federally enforceable written document, or portion of such document, issued by the permitting authority under subpart CCC of this part, including any permit revisions, specifying the CAIR SOTrading Program requirements applicable to a CAIR SOsource, to each CAIR SOunit at the source, and to the owners and operators and the CAIR designated representative of the source and each such unit.
