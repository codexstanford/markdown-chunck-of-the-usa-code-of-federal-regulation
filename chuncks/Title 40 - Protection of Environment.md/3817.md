
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

### Subchapter B - Grants and Other Federal Assistance
#### PART 49 - INDIAN COUNTRY: AIR QUALITY PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT
##### Subpart L - Implementation Plans for Tribes - Region IX
###### Implementation Plan for the Navajo Nation
####### ยง 49.5512 Federal Implementation Plan Provisions for Four Corners Power Plant, Navajo Nation.
######## Emissions Standards and Control Measures,Sulfur Dioxide.

(d)-(1)No owner or operator shall discharge or cause the discharge of sulfur dioxide (SO) into the atmosphere in excess of:

(i) 12.0 percent of the potential combustion concentration assuming all of the sulfur in the coal is converted to SO. This percent emitted is determined by a daily calculation of the plantwide heatinput weighted annual average.

(ii) 17,900 pounds of total SOemissions per hour averaged over any consecutive three (3) hour period, determined on a plant-wide basis.
