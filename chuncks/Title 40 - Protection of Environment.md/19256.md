
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 60.561 Definitions.
####### High density polyethylene (HDPE)

means a thermoplastic polymer or copolymer comprised of at least 50 percent ethylene by weight and having a density of greater than 0.940 gm/cm3 (58.7 lb/ft).
