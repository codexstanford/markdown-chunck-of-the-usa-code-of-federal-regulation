
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter N - Effluent Guidelines and Standards
#### PART 441 - DENTAL OFFICE POINT SOURCE CATEGORY
##### ยง 441.50 Reporting and recordkeeping requirements.
###### One-Time Compliance Report deadlines.

(1)For existing sources, a One-Time Compliance Report must be submitted to the Control Authority no later than October 12, 2020, or 90 days after a transfer of ownership. For new sources, a One-Time Compliance Report must be submitted to the Control Authority no later than 90 days following the introduction of wastewater into a POTW.
