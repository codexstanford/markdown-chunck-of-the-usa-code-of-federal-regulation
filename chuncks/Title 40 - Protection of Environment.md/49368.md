(2)The Administrator will deduct NOallowances for a control period from the compliance account, in the absence of an identification or in the case of a partial identification of NOallowances by serial number under paragraph (c)(1) of this section, or the overdraft account on a first-in, first-out (FIFO) accounting basis in the following order:

(iii) Those NOallowances that were allocated for a prior control period to the unit under subpart E or I of this part; and

(iv) Those NOallowances that were allocated for a prior control period to any unit and transferred and recorded in the account pursuant to subpart G of this part, in order of their date of recordation.
