
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment

###### ยง 1090.80 Definitions.
####### Distributor

means any person who transports, stores, or causes the transportation or storage of fuel, fuel additive, or regulated blendstock at any point between any fuel manufacturing facility, fuel additive manufacturing facility, or regulated blendstock production facility and any retail outlet or WPC facility.
