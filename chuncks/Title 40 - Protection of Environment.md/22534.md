
# Title 40 - Protection of Environment
## Chapter I - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter C - Air Programs
#### PART 60 - STANDARDS OF PERFORMANCE FOR NEW STATIONARY SOURCES
##### Appendix A-8 to Part 60 - Test Methods 26 through 30B
###### System Calibration Error

3.16means the difference between the measured concentration of a low-, mid-, or high-level Hgcalibration gas and the certified concentration of the gas when it is introduced in system calibration mode.
