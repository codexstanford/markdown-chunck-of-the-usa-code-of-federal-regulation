
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### PART 86 - BOATING INFRASTRUCTURE GRANT PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 86.3 What terms do I need to know?
####### Construction

means the act of building or significantly altering, renovating, or repairing a structure. Clearing and reshaping land and demolishing structures are types or phases of construction. Examples of structures are buildings, docks, piers, breakwaters, and slips.
