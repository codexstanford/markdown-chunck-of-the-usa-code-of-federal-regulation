###### § 32.32 Illinois.
####### Chautauqua National Wildlife Refuge,Migratory game bird hunting.

(a)-(1)We allow hunting of waterfowl on designated areas of the refuge subject to the following condition: Hunters must remove boats, decoys, blinds, and blind materials at the end of each day's hunt (see §§ 27.93 and 27.94 of this chapter).
