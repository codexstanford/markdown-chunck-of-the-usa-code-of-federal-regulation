
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### Subpart R - Spiny Lobster Fishery of the Gulf of Mexico and South Atlantic
##### ยง 622.403 Seasons.
###### Special recreational fishing seasons,EEZ off Florida.

(2)-(i)There is a 2-day special recreational fishing season in the EEZ off Florida on the last Wednesday and successive Thursday of July each year during which fishing for spiny lobster is limited to diving or use of a bully net or hoop net. (See ยง 622.404 for general prohibitions on gear and methods.) In the EEZ off Monroe County, Florida, no person may harvest spiny lobster by diving at night, that is, from 1 hour after official sunset to 1 hour before official sunrise, during this 2-day special recreational fishing season.
