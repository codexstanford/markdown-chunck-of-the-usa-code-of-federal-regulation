
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

### Subchapter C - Marine Mammals

##### Subpart O - Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; U.S. Navy's Northwest Training and Testing (NWTT)
###### ยง 218.144 Mitigation requirements.
####### After activity.,e.g.,

(F)After each detonation and completion of an activity, the Navy must observe for marine mammals for 30 min in the vicinity of where detonations occurred and immediately downstream of the detonation location; if any injured or dead marine mammals are observed, Navy personnel must follow established incident reporting procedures. If additional platforms are supporting this activity (providing range clearance), Navy personnel on these assets must assist in the visual observation of the area where detonations occurred.
