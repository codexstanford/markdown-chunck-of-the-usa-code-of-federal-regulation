##### Subpart C - Take Reduction Plan Regulations and Emergency Regulations
###### § 229.36 Atlantic Pelagic Longline Take Reduction Plan (PLTRP).
####### Geographic scope.

(2)The geographic scope of the PLTRP is the Atlantic Federal EEZ off the U.S. East Coast. The regulations specified in paragraphs (b) through (d) of this section apply throughout the Atlantic Federal EEZ off the U.S. East Coast. The regulation specified in paragraph (e) of this section applies to all U.S. Atlantic pelagic longline vessels operating in the EEZ portion of the Mid-Atlantic Bight.
