###### ยง 229.32 Atlantic large whale take reduction plan regulations.
####### Exemption for vessels in transit with gillnet aboard.

(iii)Possession of gillnet gear aboard a vessel in transit is allowed if:

(A) All nets are covered with canvas other similar material and securely fastened to the deck, rail, or drum; and

(B) All buoys, high flyers, and anchors are disconnected from all gillnets.
