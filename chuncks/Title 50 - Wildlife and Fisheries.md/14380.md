###### Restrictions on satellite buoy reactivations.

(e)A vessel owner or operator that reactivates a satellite buoy must comply with the reporting requirements for satellite buoy reactivations in ยง 300.22(c)(4). A U.S. vessel owner or operator shall only remotely reactivate a satellite buoy at sea that was activated in the IATTC Convention Area in the following circumstances:
