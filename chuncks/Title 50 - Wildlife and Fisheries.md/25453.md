
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

###### Swordfish limits.

(j)When fishing north of the Equator (0&#xB0; lat.), owners and operators of vessels registered for use under a Hawaii longline limited access permit, on a trip for which the permit holder notified NMFS under ยง 665.803(a) that the vessel would deep-set, may possess or land no more than the following number of swordfish for such trip:
