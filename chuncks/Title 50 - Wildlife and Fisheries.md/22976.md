###### Stonewall Bank Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area.

(g)The Stonewall Bank Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area (YRCA) is an area off central Oregon, near Stonewall Bank, intended to protect yelloweye rockfish.The Stonewall Bank YRCA is defined by straight lines connecting the following specific latitude and longitude coordinates in the order listed:

(1) 44&#xB0;37.46&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;24.92&#x2032; W. long.;

(2) 44&#xB0;37.46&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;23.63&#x2032; W. long.;

(3) 44&#xB0;28.71&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;21.80&#x2032; W. long.;

(4) 44&#xB0;28.71&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;24.10&#x2032; W. long.;

(5) 44&#xB0;31.42&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;25.47&#x2032; W. long.;

and connecting back to 44&#xB0;37.46&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;24.92&#x2032; W. long.
