
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### Subpart A - Introduction and General Provisions
###### ยง 222.102 Definitions.
####### Conservation plan

means the plan required by section 10(a)(2)(A) of the Act that an applicant must submit when applying for an incidental take permit. Conservation plans also are known as "habitat conservation plans" or "HCPs."
