
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

###### Use of Amendment 80 halibut PSC in the Amendment 80 sector,Amendment 80 halibut PSC assigned to a Amendment 80 cooperative.

(4)-(i)An amount of Amendment 80 halibut PSC is assigned to the CQ permit issued to an Amendment 80 cooperative for use while fishing for all groundfish species in the BSAI or adjacent waters open by the State of Alaska for which it adopts a Federal fishing season. Any halibut PSC used by an Amendment 80 cooperative must be deducted from the amount of halibut PSC CQ on its CQ permit. Amendment 80 halibut PSC on a CQ permit may only be used by the members of the Amendment 80 cooperative to which it is assigned.
