#### Subpart I - Snapper-Grouper Fishery of the South Atlantic Region
##### ยง 622.170 Permits and endorsements.
###### Procedure for appealing black sea bass pot endorsement eligibility and/or landings information.

(3)The only items subject to appeal are initial eligibility for a black sea bass pot endorsement based on ownership of a qualifying snapper-grouper permit, the accuracy of the amount of landings, and correct assignment of landings to the permittee. Appeals based on hardship factors will not be considered. Appeals must be submitted to the RA postmarked no later than October 1, 2012, and must contain documentation supporting the basis for the appeal. The RA will review all appeals, render final decisions on the appeals, and advise the appellant of the final NMFS decision.
