(y)-(1)The commercial ACL for red snapper is 124,815 lb (56,615 kg), round weight. See ยง 622.183(b)(5) for details on the commercial fishing season. On and after the effective date of the closure notification, all sale or purchase of red snapper is prohibited and harvest or possession of red snapper is limited to the recreational bag and possession limits and only during such time as harvest by the recreational sector is allowed as described in ยง 622.183(b)(5).
