#### Subpart F - Offshore Marine Aquaculture in the Gulf of Mexico
##### ยง 622.102 Recordkeeping and reporting.
###### Landing information.

(E)Report the intended time, date, and port of landing for any vessel landing fish harvested from an aquaculture facility at least 72 hours prior to landing.
