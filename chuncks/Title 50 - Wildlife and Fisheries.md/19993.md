
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 648.5 Operator permits.
###### Sanctions.

(n)Vessel operators with suspended or revoked permits may not be aboard a federally permitted fishing vessel in any capacity while the vessel is at sea or engaged in offloading. Procedures governing enforcement related permit sanctions and denials are found at subpart D of 15 CFR part 904.
