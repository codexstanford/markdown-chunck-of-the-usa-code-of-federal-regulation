###### Calculating per-tag fee.

(ii)To facilitate fee collection, NMFS will convert the annual fee percentages into per-tag fees for both the surfclam and ocean quahog ITQ fisheries. NMFS will use the following equation to determine each per-tag fee: Per-Tag Fee = (Fee Percentage x V)/T, where:

(A) "T" is the number of cage tags used, pursuant to ยง 648.77, to land shellfish in the ITQ fishery in the previous calendar year.

(B) "Fee percentage" and "V" are defined in paragraph (c)(3)(i) of this section.

(C) The per-tag fee is rounded down so that it is expressed in whole cents.
