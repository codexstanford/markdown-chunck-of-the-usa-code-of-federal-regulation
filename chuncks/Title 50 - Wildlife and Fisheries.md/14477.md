
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter III - International Fishing and Related Activities
### PART 300 - INTERNATIONAL FISHERIES REGULATIONS
#### Subpart E - Pacific Halibut Fisheries
##### ยง 300.61 Definitions.
###### Chiniak Bay

means all waters bounded by the shoreline and straight lines connecting the coordinates in the order listed:

(1) North from Cape Chiniak (57&#xB0;37.22&#x2032; N. lat., 152&#xB0;9.36&#x2032; W. long.);

(2) To Buoy #1 at Williams Reef (57&#xB0;50.36&#x2032; N. lat., 152&#xB0;8.82&#x2032; W. long.);

(3) To East Cape on Spruce Island (57&#xB0;54.89&#x2032; N. lat., 152&#xB0;19.45&#x2032; W. long.);

(4) To Termination Point on Kodiak Island (57&#xB0;51.31&#x2032; N. lat., 152&#xB0;24.01&#x2032; W. long.); and

(5) Connecting to a line running counterclockwise along the shoreline of Kodiak Island to Cape Chiniak (57&#xB0;37.22&#x2032; N. lat., 152&#xB0;9.36&#x2032; W. long.).
