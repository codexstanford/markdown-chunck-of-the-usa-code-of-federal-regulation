##### ยง 622.480 Annual catch limits (ACLs), annual catch targets (ACTs), and accountability measures (AMs).
###### Queen conch.

(e)(1) ACL-50,000 lb (22,679.6 kg), round weight.

(2) If NMFS estimates landings reach or are projected to reach the ACL specified in paragraph (e)(1) of this section, the AA will close the area east of 64&#xB0;34&#x2032; W longitude in the EEZ around St. Croix to the harvest and possession of queen conch by filing a notification of the closure with the Office of the Federal Register.
