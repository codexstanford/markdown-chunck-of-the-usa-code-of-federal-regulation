
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter V - Marine Mammal Commission
### PART 560 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE GOVERNMENT IN THE SUNSHINE ACT
#### ยง 560.2 Definitions.
##### Commission

means the Marine Mammal Commission, a collegial body established under 16 U.S.C. 1401 that functions as a unit and is composed of three individual members, each of whom is appointed by the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate.
