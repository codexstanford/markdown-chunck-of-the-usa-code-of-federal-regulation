
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### Subpart D - National Standards
##### ยง 600.310 National Standard 1 - Optimum Yield.
###### Management uncertainty,i.e.,

(v)refers to uncertainty in the ability of managers to constrain catch so that the ACL is not exceeded, and the uncertainty in quantifying the true catch amounts (estimation errors). The sources of management uncertainty could include: Late catch reporting; misreporting; underreporting of catches; lack of sufficient inseason management, including inseason closure authority; or other factors.
