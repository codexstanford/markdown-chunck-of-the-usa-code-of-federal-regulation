
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter I - United States Fish and Wildlife Service, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter A - General Provisions
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 1.5 Officer in Charge.
###### Officer in Charge

means any person in charge of a national fish hatchery, national wildlife refuge, research center, or other U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service installation, or the authorized representative of such official.
