Kauai 10--a, Kauai 11--b, and Kauai 20--c, identified in the legal descriptions in paragraph (a)(1) of this section, constitute critical habitat foron Kauai. Within these units, the primary constituent elements of critical habitat are:

(i) Elevation: Less than 3,000 ft (914 m).

(ii) Annual precipitation: Greater than 75 inches (190 centimeters).

(iii) Substrate: Clays, ashbeds, deep well-drained soils, lowland bogs.
