##### Subpart H - Lists of Species
###### ยง 23.90 What are the criteria for listing species in Appendix III?
####### U.S. procedure.

(e)The procedure to list a species native to the United States in Appendix III is as follows:

(1) We will consult with and solicit comments from all States and Tribes where the species occurs and all other range countries.

(2) We will publish a proposed rule in theto solicit comments from the public.

(3) If after evaluating the comments received and available information we determine the species should be listed in Appendix III, we will publish a final rule in theand notify the Secretariat of the listing.
