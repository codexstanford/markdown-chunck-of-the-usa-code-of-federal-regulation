###### Limited access scallop vessels fishing in Sea Scallop Access Areas.

(ii)A vessel issued a valid monkfish incidental catch (Category E) permit or a valid limited access Category C, D, F, G, or H permit, and also possessing a limited access sea scallop permit while fishing exclusively with scallop dredge gear as specified in ยง 648.51(b), and fishing in one of the established Sea Scallop Access Areas specified under ยง 648.59, may possess, retain, and land up to 300 lb (136 kg) tail weight or 873 lb (396 kg) whole weight of monkfish per day or partial day fished within the boundaries of the Sea Scallop Access Area.
