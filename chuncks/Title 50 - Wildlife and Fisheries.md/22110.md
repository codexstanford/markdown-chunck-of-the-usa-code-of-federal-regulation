
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

###### Mutilation restrictions.

(3)(i) A vessel may not retain, possess, or land red crab claws and legs separate from crab bodies.

(ii) A vessel may not retain, possess, or land more than two claws and eight legs per crab.
