##### ยง 679.21 Prohibited species bycatch management.
###### Red King Crab Savings Subarea,1

(B)(RKCSS). () The RKCSS is the portion of the RKCSA between 56&#xB0;00&#x2032; and 56&#xB0;10&#x2032; N. lat. Notwithstanding other provisions of this part, vessels using non-pelagic trawl gear in the RKCSS may engage in directed fishing for groundfish in a given year, if the ADF&G had established a guideline harvest level the previous year for the red king crab fishery in the Bristol Bay area.
