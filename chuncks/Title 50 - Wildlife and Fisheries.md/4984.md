###### ยง 20.108 Nontoxic shot zones.

Beginning September 1, 1991, the contiguous 48 United States, and the States of Alaska and Hawaii, the Territories of Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands, and the territorial waters of the United States, are designated for the purpose of ยง 20.21(j) as nontoxic shot zones for hunting waterfowl, coots and certain other species.
