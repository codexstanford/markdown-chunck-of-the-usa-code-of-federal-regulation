
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter I - United States Fish and Wildlife Service, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter B - Taking, Possession, Transportation, Sale, Purchase, Barter, Exportation, and Importation of Wildlife and Plants
#### PART 17 - ENDANGERED AND THREATENED WILDLIFE AND PLANTS
##### Subpart B - Lists
###### ยง 17.12 Endangered and threatened plants.
####### "Where listed" column.

(d)The "Where listed" column sets forth the geographic area where the species is listed for purposes of the Act. Except when providing a geographic description of an experimental population designation, "Wherever found" will be used to indicate the Act's protections apply to all individuals of the species, wherever found.
