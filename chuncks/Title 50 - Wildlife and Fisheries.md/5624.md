##### Subpart B - Prohibitions, Exemptions, and Requirements
###### ยง 23.22 What are the requirements for in-transit shipments?
####### Purpose.

(a)Article VII(1) of the Treaty allows for a shipment to transit an intermediary country that is a Party before reaching its final destination without the need for the intermediary Party to issue CITES documents. To control any illegal trade, Parties are to inspect, to the extent possible under their national legislation, specimens in transit through their territory to verify the presence of valid documentation.
