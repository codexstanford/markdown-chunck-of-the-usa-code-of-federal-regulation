
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter II - National Marine Fisheries Service, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter K - Continental Shelf
#### PART 296 - FISHERMEN'S CONTINGENCY FUND
##### ยง 296.2 Definitions.
###### Commercial fishing vessel

means any marine craft which is documented under the laws of the United States or, if under five net tons, registered under the laws of any State, and used for commercial fishing or activities directly related to commercial fishing.
