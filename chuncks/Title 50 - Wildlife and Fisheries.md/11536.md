
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### Subpart A - Introduction and General Provisions
###### ยง 222.102 Definitions.
####### Approved turtle excluder device (TED)

means a device designed to be installed in a trawl net forward of the cod end for the purpose of excluding sea turtles from the net, as described in 50 CFR 223.207.
