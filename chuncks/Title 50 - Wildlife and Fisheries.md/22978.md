###### Stonewall Bank Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area, Expansion 2.

(i)The Stonewall Bank Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area (YRCA) Expansion 2 is an area off central Oregon, near Stonewall Bank, intended to protect yelloweye rockfish. The Stonewall Bank YRCA Expansion 2 is defined by straight lines connecting the following specific latitude and longitude coordinates in the order listed:

(1) 44&#xB0;38.54&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;27.41&#x2032; W. long.;

(2) 44&#xB0;38.54&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;23.86&#x2032; W. long.;

(3) 44&#xB0;27.13&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;21.50&#x2032; W. long.;

(4) 44&#xB0;27.13&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;26.89&#x2032; W. long.;

(5) 44&#xB0;31.30&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;28.35&#x2032; W. long.; and connecting back to 44&#xB0;38.54&#x2032; N. lat.; 124&#xB0;27.41&#x2032; W. long.
