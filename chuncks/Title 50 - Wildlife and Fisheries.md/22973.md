
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### ยง 660.70 Groundfish conservation areas.
###### Salmon Troll Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area.

(d)The Salmon Troll Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area (YRCA) is an area off the northern Washington coast, overlapping the southern part of North Coast Recreational YRCA, intended to protect yelloweye rockfish.The Salmon Troll YRCA is defined by straight lines connecting the following specific latitude and longitude coordinates in the order listed:

(1) 48&#xB0;00.00&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;14.00&#x2032; W. long.;

(2) 48&#xB0;02.00&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;14.00&#x2032; W. long.;

(3) 48&#xB0;02.00&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;16.50&#x2032; W. long.;

(4) 48&#xB0;00.00&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;16.50&#x2032; W. long.; and connecting back to 48&#xB0;00.00&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;14.00&#x2032; W. long.
