
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter III - International Fishing and Related Activities
### PART 300 - INTERNATIONAL FISHERIES REGULATIONS
#### Subpart M - International Trade Documentation and Tracking Programs for Highly Migratory Species
##### ยง 300.181 Definitions.
###### Separate customs territory of a U.S. insular possession

means the customs territory of a U.S. insular possession when that possession's customs territory is not a part of the Customs territory of the United States.
