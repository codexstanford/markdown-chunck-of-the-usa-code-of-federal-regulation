####### Upper Yuba River Central Valley spring-run Chinook salmon nonessential experimental population.

(2)All Central Valley spring-run Chinook salmon within the NEP area in the upper Yuba River watershed upstream of Englebright Dam, as defined in this paragraph (d)(2), are considered part of the Upper Yuba River Central Valley spring-run Chinook salmon nonessential experimental population.
