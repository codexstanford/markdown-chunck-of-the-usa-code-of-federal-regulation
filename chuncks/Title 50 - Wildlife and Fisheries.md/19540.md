
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

###### Monkfish-only DAS

means monkfish DAS allocated to a limited access monkfish Category C, D, F, G, or H permit that are in excess of that permit's initial allocation of Northeast multispecies Category A DAS at the beginning of a fishing year.
