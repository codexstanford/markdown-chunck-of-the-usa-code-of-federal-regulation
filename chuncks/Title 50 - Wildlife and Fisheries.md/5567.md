##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 23.5 How are the terms used in these regulations defined?
####### Party

means a country that has given its consent to be bound by the provisions of CITES by depositing an instrument of ratification, acceptance, approval, or accession with the Depositary Government (Switzerland), and for which such consent is in effect.
