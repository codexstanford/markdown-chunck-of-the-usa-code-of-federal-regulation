
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

####### Hart Mountain National Antelope Refuge,Migratory game bird hunting.

(f)-(1)We allow hunting of duck, goose, and coot on designated areas of the refuge subject to the following conditions:

(i) We allow only portable blinds and temporary blinds constructed of synthetic or nonliving natural materials.
