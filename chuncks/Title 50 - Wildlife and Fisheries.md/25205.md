
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter VI - Fishery Conservation and Management, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Department of Commerce
### PART 665 - FISHERIES IN THE WESTERN PACIFIC
#### Subpart C - Hawaii Fisheries
##### ยง 665.241 Definitions.
###### Lobster grounds

refers, singularly or collectively, to the following four areas in Crustacean Permit Area 1 that shall be used to manage the lobster fishery:

(1) Necker Island Lobster Grounds-waters bounded by straight lines connecting the following coordinates in the order presented: 24&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 165&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.; 24&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 164&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.; 23&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 164&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.; and 23&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 165&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.

(2) Gardner Pinnacles Lobster Grounds-waters bounded by straight lines connecting the following coordinates in the order presented: 25&#xB0;20&#x2032; N. lat., 168&#xB0;20&#x2032; W. long.; 25&#xB0;20&#x2032; N. lat., 167&#xB0;40&#x2032; W. long.; 24&#xB0;20&#x2032; N. lat., 167&#xB0;40&#x2032; W. long.; and 24&#xB0;20&#x2032; N. lat., 168&#xB0;20&#x2032; W. long.

(3) Maro Reef Lobster Grounds-waters bounded by straight lines connecting the following coordinates in the order presented: 25&#xB0;40&#x2032; N. lat., 171&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.; 25&#xB0;40&#x2032; N. lat., 170&#xB0;20&#x2032; W. long.; 25&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 170&#xB0;20&#x2032; W. long.; and 25&#xB0;00&#x2032; N. lat., 171&#xB0;00&#x2032; W. long.

(4) General NWHI Lobster Grounds-all waters within Crustacean Permit Area 1 except for the Necker Island, Gardner Pinnacles, and Maro Reef Lobster Grounds.
