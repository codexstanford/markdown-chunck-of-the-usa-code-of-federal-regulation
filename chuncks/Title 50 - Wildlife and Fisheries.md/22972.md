
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### ยง 660.70 Groundfish conservation areas.
###### North Coast Commercial Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area.

(c)The North Coast Commercial Yelloweye Rockfish Conservation Area (YRCA) is an area off the northern Washington coast, overlapping the northern part of North Coast Recreational YRCA, intended to protect yelloweye rockfish. The North Coast Commercial YRCA is defined by straight lines connecting the following specific latitude and longitude coordinates in the order listed:

(1) 48&#xB0;11.77&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;13.03&#x2032; W. long.;

(2) 48&#xB0;16.43&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;07.55&#x2032; W. long.;

(3) 48&#xB0;14.72&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;01.84&#x2032; W. long.;

(4) 48&#xB0;13.36&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;03.20&#x2032; W. long.;

(5) 48&#xB0;12.74&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;05.83&#x2032; W. long.;

(6) 48&#xB0;11.55&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;04.99&#x2032; W. long.;

(7) 48&#xB0;09.96&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;06.63&#x2032; W. long.;

(8) 48&#xB0;09.68&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;08.75&#x2032; W. long.;

and connecting back to 48&#xB0;11.77&#x2032; N. lat., 125&#xB0;13.03&#x2032; W. long.
