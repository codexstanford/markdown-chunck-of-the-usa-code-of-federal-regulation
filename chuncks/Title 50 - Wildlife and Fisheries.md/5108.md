
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

### Subchapter B - Taking, Possession, Transportation, Sale, Purchase, Barter, Exportation, and Importation of Wildlife and Plants
#### PART 21 - MIGRATORY BIRD PERMITS
##### Subpart B - Exceptions to Permit Requirements
###### ยง 21.48 Permit exceptions for captive-bred migratory waterfowl other than mallard ducks.
####### Anas platyrhynchos,Cairina moschata

You may acquire captive-bred and properly marked migratory waterfowl (family Anatidae) of all species other than mallard ducks (), alive or dead, or their eggs, and possess and transport such birds or eggs and any progeny or eggs for your use without a permit, subject to the following conditions and restrictions. Additional restrictions on the acquisition and transfer of muscovy ducks () are in paragraph (g) of this section.
