
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter VI - Fishery Conservation and Management, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Department of Commerce

##### ยง 635.2 Definitions.
###### Mid-Atlantic shark closed area

means the Atlantic Ocean area seaward of the inner boundary of the U.S. EEZ from a point intersecting the inner boundary of the U.S. EEZ at 35&#xB0;41&#x2032; N. lat. just south of Oregon Inlet, North Carolina, and connecting by straight lines the following coordinates in the order stated: 35&#xB0;41&#x2032; N. lat., 75&#xB0;25&#x2032; W. long. proceeding due east to 35&#xB0;41&#x2032; N. lat., 74&#xB0;51&#x2032; W. long.; then proceeding southeast to 35&#xB0;30&#x2032; N. lat., 74&#xB0;46&#x2032; W. long.; then proceeding southwest, roughly following the 55 fathom mark, to 33&#xB0;51&#x2032; N. lat., 76&#xB0;24&#x2032; W. long.; then proceeding due west to intersect the inner boundary of the U.S. EEZ at 33&#xB0;51&#x2032; N. lat., 77&#xB0;53&#x2032; W. long. near Cape Fear, North Carolina.
