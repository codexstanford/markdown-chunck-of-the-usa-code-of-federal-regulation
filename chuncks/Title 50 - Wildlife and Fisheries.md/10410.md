
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter II - National Marine Fisheries Service, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Department of Commerce
### Subchapter C - Marine Mammals
#### PART 218 - REGULATIONS GOVERNING THE TAKING AND IMPORTING OF MARINE MAMMALS
##### Subpart B - Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; U.S. Navy's Point Mugu Sea Range (PMSR) Training and Testing Study Area
###### ยง 218.14 Mitigation requirements.
####### Procedural mitigation.,i.e.,,i.e.,,i.e.,,e.g.,

(a)Procedural mitigation is mitigation that the Navy will implement whenever and wherever an applicable training or testing activity takes place within the PMSR Study Area for each applicable activity category or stressor category and includes acoustic stressors (weapons firing noise), explosive stressors (medium-caliber and large-caliber projectiles, missiles and rockets, bombs), and physical disturbance and strike stressors (vessel movement; towed in-water devices (surface targets); small-, medium-, and large-caliber non-explosive practice munitions; non-explosive missiles and rockets; and non-explosive bombs).
