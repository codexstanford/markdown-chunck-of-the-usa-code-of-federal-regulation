####### Nonstaffed, nondesignated port base inspection fees.

(e)You must pay a nondesignated port base inspection fee, as defined in ยง 14.94(h)(3), for each wildlife shipment imported or exported at a nonstaffed, nondesignated port using a designated port exception permit issued under subpart C of this part. All applicable base and premium fees apply to each shipment.
