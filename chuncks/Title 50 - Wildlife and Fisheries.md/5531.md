####### Appropriate and acceptable destination

, when used in an Appendix-II listing annotation for the export of, or international trade in, live animals, means that the Management Authority of the importing country has certified, based on advice from the Scientific Authority of that country, that the proposed recipient is suitably equipped to house and care for the animal (see criteria in ยง 23.65). Such certification must be provided before a CITES document is issued by the Management Authority of the exporting or re-exporting country.
