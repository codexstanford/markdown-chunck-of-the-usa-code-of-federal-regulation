
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### ยง 660.111 Trawl fishery - definitions.
###### Quota share (QS)

means the amount of fishing quota for an individual species/species group and area expressed as a percentage of the annual allocation of fish to the Shorebased IFQ Program. The QS is used as the basis for the annual calculation and allocation of a QS permit owner's QP in the Shorebased IFQ Program. Both QS and IBQ may be listed on a QS permit and in the associated QS account. Species for which QS will be issued for the Shorebased IFQ Program are listed at ยง 660.140, subpart D.
