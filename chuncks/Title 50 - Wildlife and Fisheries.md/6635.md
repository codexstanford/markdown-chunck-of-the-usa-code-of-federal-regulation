
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

### Subchapter C - The National Wildlife Refuge System

####### Upland game hunting.

(2)We allow hunting of cottontail rabbit, and black-tailed and white-tailed jackrabbit, on designated areas of the refuge subject to the following condition: The only acceptable methods of take are shotgun, rifle firing rimfire cartridges less than .23 caliber, hand-held bow, pellet gun, slingshot, and hawking/falconry.
