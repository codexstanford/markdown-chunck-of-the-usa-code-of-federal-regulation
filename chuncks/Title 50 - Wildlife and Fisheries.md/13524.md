####### Mid/South Atlantic Gillnet Waters,Area.

(8)-(i)The Mid/South Atlantic Gillnet Waters consists of all U.S. waters bounded on the north from Long Island, NY, at 72&#xB0;30&#x2032; W long. south to 36&#xB0;33.03&#x2032; N lat. and east to the eastern edge of the EEZ, and bounded on the south by 32&#xB0;00&#x2032; N lat., and east to the eastern edge of the EEZ. When the Mid/South Atlantic Gillnet Waters Area overlaps the Southeast U.S. Restricted Area and its restricted period as specified in paragraphs (f)(1) and (2) of this section, then the closure and exemption for the Southeast U.S. Restricted Area as specified in paragraph (f)(2) of this section applies.
