
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### ยง 622.2 Definitions and acronyms.
###### Coral area

means marine habitat in the Gulf or South Atlantic EEZ where coral growth abounds, including patch reefs, outer bank reefs, deep water banks, and hard bottoms.
