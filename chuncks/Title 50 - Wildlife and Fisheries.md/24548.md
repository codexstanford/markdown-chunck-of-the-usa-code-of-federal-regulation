
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

### PART 660 - FISHERIES OFF WEST COAST STATES
#### Subpart H - West Coast Salmon Fisheries
##### ยง 660.402 Definitions.
###### Fishery management area

means the EEZ off the coasts of Washington, Oregon, and California, bounded on the north by the Provisional International Boundary between the United States and Canada, and bounded on the south by the International Boundary between the United States and Mexico. The northeastern, northern, and northwestern boundaries of the fishery management area are as follows:

(1) Northeastern boundary-that part of a line connecting the light on Tatoosh Island, WA, with the light on Bonilla Point on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, southerly of the International Boundary between the United States and Canada (at 48&#xB0;29&#x2032;37&#x2033; N. lat., 124&#xB0;43&#x2032;33&#x2033; W. long.), and northerly of the point where that line intersects with the boundary of the U.S. territorial sea.
