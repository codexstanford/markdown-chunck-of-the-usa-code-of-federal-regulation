
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### Subpart T - FMP for the EEZ Around St. Croix

###### Presumption of ownership of spiny lobster traps.

(2)A spiny lobster trap in the EEZ around St. Croix will be presumed to be the property of the most recently documented owner. This presumption will not apply with respect to such traps that are lost or sold if the owner reports the loss or sale within 15 days to the RA.
