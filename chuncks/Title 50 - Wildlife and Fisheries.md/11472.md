####### ยง 221.42 When must a party supplement or amend information it has previously provided?
######## Discovery.

(a)A party must promptly supplement or amend any prior response to a discovery request if it learns that the response:

(1) Was incomplete or incorrect when made; or

(2) Though complete and correct when made, is now incomplete or incorrect in any material respect.
