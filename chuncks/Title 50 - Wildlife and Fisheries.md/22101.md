Upon notification of the closure, a vessel issued a limited access red crab permit may not fish for, catch, possess, transport, land, sell, trade, or barter, in excess of 500 lb (226.8 kg) of red crab, or its equivalent in weight as specified at ยง 648.263(a)(2)(i) and (ii), per fishing trip in or from the Red Crab Management Unit.
