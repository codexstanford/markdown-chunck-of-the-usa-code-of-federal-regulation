
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

###### ยง 222.102 Definitions.
####### Commercial activity

means all activities of industry and trade, including, but not limited to, the buying or selling of commodities and activities conducted for the purpose of facilitating such buying and selling: Provided, however, that it does not include the exhibition of commodities by museums or similar cultural or historical organizations.
