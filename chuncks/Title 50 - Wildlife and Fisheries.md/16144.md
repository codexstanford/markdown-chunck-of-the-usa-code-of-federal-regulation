
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

#### Subpart D - National Standards
##### ยง 600.310 National Standard 1 - Optimum Yield.
###### Overview of Magnuson-Stevens Act concepts and provisions related to NS1,MSY.

(2)-(i)The Magnuson-Stevens Act establishes MSY as the basis for fishery management and requires that: The fishing mortality rate must not jeopardize the capacity of a stock or stock complex to produce MSY; the abundance of an overfished stock or stock complex must be rebuilt to a level that is capable of producing MSY; and OY must not exceed MSY.
