
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter I - United States Fish and Wildlife Service, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter B - Taking, Possession, Transportation, Sale, Purchase, Barter, Exportation, and Importation of Wildlife and Plants
#### PART 17 - ENDANGERED AND THREATENED WILDLIFE AND PLANTS
##### Subpart J - Manatee Protection Areas
###### ยง 17.108 List of designated manatee protection areas.
####### The Shell Island Manatee Refuge.

(8)(i) The Shell Island Manatee Refuge is described as all waters within the marked Intracoastal Waterway channel between Green Marker "99" (approximate latitude 26 degrees 31&#x2032;00&#x2033; N, approximate longitude 82 degrees 00&#x2032;52&#x2033; W) and Green Marker "93" (approximate latitude 26 degrees 31&#x2032;37&#x2033; N, approximate longitude 81 degrees 59&#x2032;46&#x2033; W), Lee County, Florida; containing approximately 32.6 ha (80.5 acres).

(ii) Watercraft are required to proceed at slow speed (channel included) year-round. Watercraft are prohibited from traveling in excess of slow speed in this area, year-round.
