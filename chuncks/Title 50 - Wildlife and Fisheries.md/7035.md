####### Silvio O. Conte National Fish and Wildlife Refuge,Migratory game bird hunting.

(h)-(1)We allow hunting of migratory game birds on designated areas subject to the following conditions:

(i) Hunters may access the refuge 1hours before legal sunrise until 1hours after legal sunset.

(ii) We prohibit access to Third Island between January 1 and June 30.

(iii) We allow the use of dogs consistent with State regulations.

(iv) Migratory waterfowl hunting hours arehour before legal sunrise to legal sunset.
