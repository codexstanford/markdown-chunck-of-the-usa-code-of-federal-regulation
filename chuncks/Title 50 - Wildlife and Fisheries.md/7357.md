
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

####### Silvio O. Conte National Fish and Wildlife Refuge,Migratory game bird hunting.

(b)-(1)We allow hunting of duck, goose, coot, common snipe, and American woodcock on designated areas of the refuge subject to the following condition: We allow the use of dogs consistent with State regulations, except hunters using more than two dogs must possess a Special Use Permit (FWS Form 3-1383-G) issued by the refuge manager.
