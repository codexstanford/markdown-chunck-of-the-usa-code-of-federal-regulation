
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries
## Chapter I - United States Fish and Wildlife Service, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter C - The National Wildlife Refuge System
#### PART 32 - HUNTING AND FISHING
##### Subpart B - Refuge-Specific Regulations for Hunting and Fishing
###### ยง 32.26 Connecticut.
####### Silvio O. Conte National Fish and Wildlife Refuge,Migratory game bird hunting.

(a)-(1)We allow hunting of migratory game birds on designated areas of the refuge subject to the following conditions:

(i) We allow refuge access 1hours prior to legal sunrise until 1hours after legal sunset.

(ii) We allow the use of dogs consistent with State regulations.
