###### LAGC permit restrictions,1,Change of permit category,i,IFQ scallop permit.

(G)-()-()A vessel issued an IFQ scallop permit may not change its general category scallop permit category at any time without voluntarily relinquishing its IFQ scallop permit eligibility as specified in paragraph (a)(2)(ii)(M) of this section.
