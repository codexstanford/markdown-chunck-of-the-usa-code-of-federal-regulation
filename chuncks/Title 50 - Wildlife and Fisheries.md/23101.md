
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

##### ยง 660.111 Trawl fishery - definitions.
###### Catcher/processor coop or C/P coop

means a harvester group that includes all eligible catcher/processor at-sea Pacific whiting endorsed permit owners who voluntarily form a coop and who manage the catcher/processor-specified allocations through private agreements and contracts.
