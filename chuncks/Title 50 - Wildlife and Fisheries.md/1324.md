####### Herbicide application for removal of invasive and/or nonnative plant species by hand wiping, wicking, and spot-spray applications.

(D)Hand wiping, wicking, and spot-spray applications of herbicides for either the removal of nonnative, invasive plant species or to prevent resprouting of woody species subsequent to cutting are authorized year-round.
