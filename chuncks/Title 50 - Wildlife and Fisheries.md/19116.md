
# Title 50 - Wildlife and Fisheries

### PART 635 - ATLANTIC HIGHLY MIGRATORY SPECIES
#### Subpart B - Individual Vessel Measures
##### ยง 635.15 Individual bluefin tuna quotas (IBQs).
###### Temporary prohibition on leasing IBQ allocation.

(iv)No leasing of IBQ allocation is permitted between 6 p.m. eastern time on December 31 of one year and 2 p.m. eastern time on January 1 of the next year. This period is necessary to provide NMFS time to reconcile IBQ accounts, and update IBQ shares and allocations for the upcoming fishing year.
