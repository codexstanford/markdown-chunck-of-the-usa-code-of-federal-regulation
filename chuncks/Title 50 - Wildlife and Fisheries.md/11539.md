####### Atlantic Shrimp Fishery-Sea Turtle Conservation Area (Atlantic SFSTCA)

means the inshore and offshore waters extending to 10 nautical miles (18.5 km) offshore along the coast of the States of Georgia and South Carolina from the Georgia-Florida border (defined as the line along 30&#xB0;42&#x2032;45.6&#x2033; N. lat.) to the North Carolina-South Carolina border (defined as the line extending in a direction of 135&#xB0;34&#x2032;55&#x2033; from true north from the North Carolina-South Carolina land boundary, as marked by the border station on Bird Island at 33&#xB0;51&#x2032;07.9&#x2033; N. lat., 078&#xB0;32&#x2032;32.6&#x2033; W. long.).
