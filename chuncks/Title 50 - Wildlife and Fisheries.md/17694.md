###### Private angling component ACT.

(C)The private angling component ACT applies to vessels that fish under the bag limit and have not been issued a Federal charter vessel/headboat permit for Gulf reef fish any time during the fishing year. The component ACT is 3,483,234 lb (1,579,968 kg), round weight.
