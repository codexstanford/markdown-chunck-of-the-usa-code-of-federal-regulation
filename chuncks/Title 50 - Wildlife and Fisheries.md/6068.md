####### Bogue Chitto National Wildlife Refuge.

(5)(i) We allow primitive camping within 100 feet (30 meters) of designated streams. Campers must mark their campsite with the owner's State license/identification number, or boat identification number, and dates of occupancy placed in a conspicuous location in the center of camp.
