
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 270 - RULES AND REGULATIONS, INVESTMENT COMPANY ACT OF 1940
#### ยง 270.12d1-4 Exemptions for investments in certain investment companies.
##### Group of investment companies

means any two or more registered investment companies or business development companies that hold themselves out to investors as related companies for purposes of investment and investor services.
