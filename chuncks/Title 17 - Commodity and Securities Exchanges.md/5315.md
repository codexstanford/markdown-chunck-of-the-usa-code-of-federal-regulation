
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 156 - BROKER ASSOCIATIONS
#### ยง 156.2 Registration of broker association.
##### Registration required.

(a)It shall be unlawful for any member of a broker association to receive or to execute an order unless the broker association is registered with the appropriate contract market in accordance with part (b) of this section.
