
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 190 - BANKRUPTCY RULES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 190.01 Definitions.
###### Open commodity contract,i.e.,

means a commodity contract which has been established in fact and which has not expired, been redeemed, been fulfilled by delivery or exercise, or been offset (liquidated) by another commodity contract.
