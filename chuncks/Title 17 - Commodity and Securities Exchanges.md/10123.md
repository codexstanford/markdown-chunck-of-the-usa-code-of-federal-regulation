
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission

#### Subpart A - Rules and Regulations Under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
##### Manipulative and Deceptive Devices and Contrivances
###### ยง 240.10b-10 Confirmation of transactions.
####### Asset-backed security

(9)means a security that is primarily serviced by the cashflows of a discrete pool of receivables or other financial assets, either fixed or revolving, that by their terms convert into cash within a finite time period plus any rights or other assets designed to assure the servicing or timely distribution of proceeds to the security holders.
