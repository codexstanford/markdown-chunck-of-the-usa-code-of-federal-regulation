
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 270 - RULES AND REGULATIONS, INVESTMENT COMPANY ACT OF 1940
#### ยง 270.12d2-1 Definition of insurance company for purposes of sections 12(d)(2) and 12(g) of the Act.
##### insurance company

For purposes of sections 12(d)(2) and 12(g) of the Act [15 U.S.C. 80a-12(d)(2) and 80a-12(g)],shall include a foreign insurance company as that term is used in rule 3a-6 under the Act (17 CFR 270.3a-6).
