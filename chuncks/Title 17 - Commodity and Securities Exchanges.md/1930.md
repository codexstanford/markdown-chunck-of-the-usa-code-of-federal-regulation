
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 15 - REPORTS - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### ยง 15.00 Definitions of terms used in parts 15 to 19, and 21 of this chapter.
##### Option, options, option contract, or options contract,

(o)unless specifically provided otherwise, means any contract for the purchase or sale of a commodity option that is executed on or subject to the rules of a reporting market, including all agreements, contracts and transactions that are treated by a clearing organization as fungible with such contracts.
