
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 37 - SWAP EXECUTION FACILITIES
#### Subpart P - Designation of Chief Compliance Officer
##### ยง 37.1501 Chief compliance officer.
###### Submission of annual compliance report and related matters,Furnishing the annual compliance report prior to submission to the Commission.

(e)-(1)Prior to submission to the Commission, the chief compliance officer shall provide the annual compliance report for review to the board of directors of the swap execution facility or, in the absence of a board of directors, to the senior officer of the swap execution facility. Members of the board of directors and the senior officer shall not require the chief compliance officer to make any changes to the report.
