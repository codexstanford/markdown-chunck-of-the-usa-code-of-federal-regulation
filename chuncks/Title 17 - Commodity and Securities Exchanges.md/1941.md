
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 15 - REPORTS - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### ยง 15.00 Definitions of terms used in parts 15 to 19, and 21 of this chapter.
##### Omnibus reportable sub-account

(z)means any trading sub-account of an omnibus volume threshold account, which sub-account executes reportable trading volume on an omnibus basis. Omnibus reportable sub-account also means any trading account that is itself an omnibus account, executes reportable trading volume, and is a sub-account of another omnibus reportable sub-account.
