
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 165 - WHISTLEBLOWER RULES
#### Appendix A to Part 165 - Guidance With Respect to the Protection of Whistleblowers Against Retaliation
##### Commission authority to bring action.

(2)The Commission may bring an enforcement action against an employer that retaliates against a whistleblower by discharge, demotion, suspension, direct or indirect threats or harassment, or any other manner of discrimination.
