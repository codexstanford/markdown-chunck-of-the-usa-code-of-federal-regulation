
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 12 - RULES RELATING TO REPARATIONS
#### Subpart E - Rules Applicable to Formal Decisional Proceedings
##### ยง 12.313 Subpoenas for attendance at an oral hearing.
###### Attendance and mileage fees.

(e)Persons summoned to testify at a hearing under requirement of subpoenas are entitled to the same fees and mileage as are paid to witnesses in the courts of the United States. Fees and mileage shall be paid by the party at whose instance the persons are subpoenaed or called.
