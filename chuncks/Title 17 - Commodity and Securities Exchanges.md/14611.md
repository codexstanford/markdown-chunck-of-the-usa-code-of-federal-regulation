
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 260 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, TRUST INDENTURE ACT OF 1939
#### Terms Used in the Rules and Regulations
##### ยง 260.0-2 Definitions of terms used in the rules and regulations.
###### Affiliate.

(b)The term "affiliate" means a person controlling, controlled by, or under common control with, another person. The terms "affiliated" and "affiliation" have meanings correlative to the foregoing.
