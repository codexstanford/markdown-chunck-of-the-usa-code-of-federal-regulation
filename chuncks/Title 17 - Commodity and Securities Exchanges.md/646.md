(2) A futures commission merchant or derivatives clearing organization may hold customer funds outside the United States, in a jurisdiction that is not a money center country, or the country of origin of the currency only to the extent authorized by the customer,that the futures commission merchant or derivatives clearing organization must make and maintain a written record of such authorization.
