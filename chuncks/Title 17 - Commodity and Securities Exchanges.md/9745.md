
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### Subpart A - Rules and Regulations Under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
##### Miscellaneous Exemptions
###### ยง 240.3a12-10 Exemption of certain securities issued by the Resolution Funding Corporation.
####### et seq.

Securities that are issued by the Resolution Funding Corporation pursuant to section 21B(f) of the Federal Home Loan Bank Act (12 U.S.C. 1421) are exempt from the operation of all provisions of the Act that by their terms do not apply to any "exempted security" or to "exempted securities."
