
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

###### Related cash or forward transaction

(i)means a purchase or sale for immediate or deferred physical shipment or delivery of an asset related to a swap where the swap and the related cash or forward transaction are used to hedge, mitigate the risk of, or offset one another.
