
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 260 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, TRUST INDENTURE ACT OF 1939
#### Rules Under Section 305
##### § 260.5b-2 General requirements as to form and content of applications.

Rule 5a-2 (§ 260.5a-2 of this chapter) and rules 7a-15 through 7a-37 [§§ 260.7a-15 through 260.7a-37 of this chapter] shall be applicable to applications pursuant to rule 5b-1 (§ 260.56b-1 of this chapter).
