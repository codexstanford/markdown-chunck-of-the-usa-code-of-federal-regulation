#### ยง 270.3a-7 Issuers of asset-backed securities.
##### Eligible assets

(1)means financial assets, either fixed or revolving, that by their terms convert into cash within a finite time period plus any rights or other assets designed to assure the servicing or timely distribution of proceeds to security holders.
