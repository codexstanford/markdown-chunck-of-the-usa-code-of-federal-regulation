
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 201 - RULES OF PRACTICE
#### Subpart D - Rules of Practice
##### General Rules
###### ยง 201.140 Commission orders and decisions: Signature and availability.
####### Signature required.

(a)All orders and decisions of the Commission shall be signed by the Secretary or any other person duly authorized by the Commission. The signature may be an electronic signature that consists of an "/s/" notation or any other digital signature.
