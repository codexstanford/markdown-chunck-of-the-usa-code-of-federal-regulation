
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 302 - ORDERLY LIQUIDATION OF COVERED BROKERS OR DEALERS
#### ยง 302.104 Claims of customers and other creditors of a covered broker or dealer.
##### Notice requirements.

(1)The notice of the appointment of the Corporation as receiver for a covered broker or dealer shall also include notice of the appointment of SIPC as trustee. The Corporation as receiver shall coordinate with SIPC as trustee to post the notice on SIPC's public website in addition to the publication procedures set forth in 12 CFR 380.33.
