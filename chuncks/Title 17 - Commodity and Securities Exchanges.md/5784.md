###### Petitions for stay pending review or for an emergency effective date,Stay pending review.

(b)-(1)Within ten days of service of the notice described in ยง 171.21, any aggrieved party may seek from the Commission a stay pending consideration of the merits of an appeal by filing and serving an appropriate petition.
