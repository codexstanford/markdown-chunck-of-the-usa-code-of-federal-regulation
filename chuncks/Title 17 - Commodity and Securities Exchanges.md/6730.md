
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

### PART 201 - RULES OF PRACTICE
#### Subpart D - Rules of Practice
##### Initiation of Proceedings and Prehearing Rules
###### ยง 201.231 Enforcement and disciplinary proceedings: Production of witness statements.
####### Failure to produce-harmless error.

(b)In the event that a statement required to be made available for inspection and copying by a respondent is not turned over by the Division of Enforcement, no rehearing or redecision of a proceeding already heard or decided shall be required unless the respondent establishes that the failure to turn over the statement was not harmless error.
