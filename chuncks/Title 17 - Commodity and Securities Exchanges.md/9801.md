
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### Subpart A - Rules and Regulations Under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
##### Security-Based Swap Dealer and Participant Definitions
###### ยง 240.3a67-3 Definition of "substantial position."
####### Aggregate uncollateralized outward exposure,General. Aggregate uncollateralized outward exposure

(b)-(1)in general means the sum of the current exposure, obtained by marking-to-market using industry standard practices, of each of the person's security-based swap positions with negative value in a major security-based swap category, less the value of the collateral the person has posted in connection with those positions.
