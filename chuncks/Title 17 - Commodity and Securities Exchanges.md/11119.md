
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### National and Affiliated Securities Associations
##### ยง 240.15Ba1-1 Definitions.
###### Independent registered municipal advisor.,independent registered municipal advisor,o,o

(A)An independent registered municipal advisor is providing advice with respect to the same aspects of the municipal financial product or issuance of municipal securities. For purposes of this paragraph (d)(3)(vi), the termmeans a municipal advisor registered pursuant to section 15B of the Act (15 U.S.C. 78-4) and the rules and regulations thereunder and that is not, and within at least the past two years was not, associated (as defined in section 15B(e)(7) (15 U.S.C. 78-4(e)(7)) of the Act) with the person seeking to rely on this paragraph (d)(3)(vi).
