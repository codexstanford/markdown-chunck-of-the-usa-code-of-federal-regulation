###### ยง 240.15g-4 Disclosure of compensation to brokers or dealers.
####### Disclosure requirement.

(a)It shall be unlawful for any broker or dealer to effect a transaction in any penny stock for or with the account of a customer unless such broker or dealer discloses to such customer, within the time periods and in the manner required by paragraph (b) of this section, the aggregate amount of any compensation received by such broker or dealer in connection with such transaction.
