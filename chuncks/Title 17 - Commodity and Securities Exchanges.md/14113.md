
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

###### Scope of opt out,In general.

(a)-(1)Except as otherwise provided in this section, the consumer's election to opt out prohibits any affiliate covered by the opt out notice from using eligibility information received from another affiliate as described in the notice to make marketing solicitations to the consumer.
