
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 260 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, TRUST INDENTURE ACT OF 1939
#### General Requirements as to Contents
##### ยง 260.7a-24 Words relating to periods of time in the past.

Unless the context clearly shows otherwise, wherever any fixed period of time in the past is indicated, such period shall be computed from the date of filing with the Commission.
