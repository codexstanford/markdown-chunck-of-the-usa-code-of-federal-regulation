#### ยง 270.18f-4 Exemption from the requirements of section 18 and section 61 for certain senior securities transactions.
##### Designated reference portfolio,designated index

means a designated index or the fund's securities portfolio. Notwithstanding the first sentence of the definition ofof this section, if the fund's investment objective is to track the performance (including a leverage multiple or inverse multiple) of an unleveraged index, the fund must use that index as its designated reference portfolio.
