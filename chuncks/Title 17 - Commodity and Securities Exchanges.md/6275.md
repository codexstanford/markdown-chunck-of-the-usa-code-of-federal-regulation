###### Requirements for making requests for records,How made and addressed.,foiapa@sec.gov,http://www.sec.gov

(b)-(1)The Commission has a centralized system for responding to FOIA requests, with all requests processed by the Office of FOIA Services. Requests for agency records must be in writing and include the requester's full name and a legible return address. Requesters may also include other contact information, such as an email address and a telephone number. Requests may be submitted by U.S. mail or delivery service and addressed to the Freedom of Information Act Officer, SEC, 100 F Street NE, Washington, DC 20549. Requests may also be made by facsimile (202-772-9337), email (), or online at the Commission's website (). The request (and envelope, if the request is mailed or hand-delivered) should be marked "Freedom of Information Act Request."
