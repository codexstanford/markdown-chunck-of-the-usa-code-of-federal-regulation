(b) All money amounts required to be shown in financial statements may be expressed in whole dollars or multiples thereof, as appropriate:That, when stated in other than whole dollars, an indication to that effect is inserted immediately beneath the caption of the statement or schedule, at the top of the money columns, or at an appropriate point in narrative material.
