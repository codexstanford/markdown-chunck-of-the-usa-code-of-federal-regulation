
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### General
##### ยง 240.12b-2 Definitions.
###### Parent.

A "parent" of a specified person is an affiliate controlling such person directly, or indirectly through one or more intermediaries.
