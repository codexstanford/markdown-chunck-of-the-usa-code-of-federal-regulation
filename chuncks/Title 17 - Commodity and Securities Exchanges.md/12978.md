(B) Effected during regular trading hours;

(ii) Such person did not effect a short sale, that is reported to an effective transaction reporting plan, within the 30 minutes prior to the close of regular trading hours (as defined in ยง 242.600(b)(77)) on the business day prior to the day of pricing.
