## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission

##### Exchange-set limits or accountability outside of the spot month,Non-spot month speculative position limit or accountability levels.

(2)-(i)For any commodity derivative contract subject to paragraph (b) of this section, a designated contract market or swap execution facility that is a trading facility shall adopt either speculative position limits or position accountability outside of the spot month at a level that is necessary and appropriate to reduce the potential threat of market manipulation or price distortion of the contract's or the underlying commodity's price or index.
