
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 239 - FORMS PRESCRIBED UNDER THE SECURITIES ACT OF 1933
#### Subpart B - Forms Pertaining to Exemptions
##### § 239.201 Form 2-E, report of sales pursuant to Rule 609 of Regulation E.

This form shall be used for report of sales of securities under Regulation E (§§ 230.601-230.610a of this chapter) by a small business investment company described in Rule 602 (§ 230.602 of this chapter) as required by Rule 609 of Regulation E (§ 230.609 of this chapter).
