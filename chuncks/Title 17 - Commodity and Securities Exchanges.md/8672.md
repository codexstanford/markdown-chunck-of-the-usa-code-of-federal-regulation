
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

##### ยง 230.405 Definitions of terms.
###### Majority-owned subsidiary.,majority-owned subsidiary

The termmeans a subsidiary more than 50 percent of whose outstanding securities representing the right, other than as affected by events of default, to vote for the election of directors, is owned by the subsidiary's parent and/or one or more of the parent's other majority-owned subsidiaries.
