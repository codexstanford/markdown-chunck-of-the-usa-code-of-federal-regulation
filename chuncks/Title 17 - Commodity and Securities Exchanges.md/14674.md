
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 260 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, TRUST INDENTURE ACT OF 1939
#### Formal Requirements
##### ยง 260.7a-17 Quality, color and size of paper.

The application, statement or report, including all amendments and, where practicable, all papers and documents filed as a part thereof, shall be on good quality, unglazed, white paper, no larger than 8x 11 inches in size. To the extent that the reduction of larger documents would render them illegible, such documents may be filed on paper larger than 8x 11 inches in size.
