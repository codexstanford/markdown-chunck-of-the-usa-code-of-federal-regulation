
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

#### Regulation SCI - Systems Compliance and Integrity
##### ยง 242.1000 Definitions.
###### Systems compliance issue

means an event at an SCI entity that has caused any SCI system of such entity to operate in a manner that does not comply with the Act and the rules and regulations thereunder or the entity's rules or governing documents, as applicable.
