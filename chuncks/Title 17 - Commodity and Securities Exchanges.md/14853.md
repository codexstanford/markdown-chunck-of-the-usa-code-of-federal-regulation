
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 270 - RULES AND REGULATIONS, INVESTMENT COMPANY ACT OF 1940
#### ยง 270.2a-7 Money market funds.
##### 3,Ten percent obligors.,(1),i

()For purposes of determining ten percent obligors pursuant to paragraph (d)(3)(ii)(D)() of this section, the money market fund must treat as a single person two or more persons whose obligations in the aggregate constitute ten percent or more of the principal amount of the qualifying assets of the primary ABS if one person controls the other, is controlled by the other person, or is under common control with the person, provided that "control" for this purpose means ownership of more than 50 percent of the person's voting securities.
