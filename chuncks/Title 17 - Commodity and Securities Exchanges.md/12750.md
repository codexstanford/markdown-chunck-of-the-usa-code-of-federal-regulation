
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### ยง 240.14d-4 Dissemination of tender offers to security holders.
##### Adequate publication.,Provided, however,

(c)Depending on the facts and circumstances involved, adequate publication of a tender offer pursuant to this section may require publication in a newspaper with a national circulation or may only require publication in a newspaper with metropolitan or regional circulation or may require publication in a combination thereof:That publication in all editions of a daily newspaper with a national circulation shall be deemed to constitute adequate publication.
