
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission

###### 1,Executive summary.

()Briefly summarize the most significant information in the technical report summary, including property description (including mineral rights) and ownership, geology and mineralization, the status of exploration, development and operations, mineral resource and mineral reserve estimates, summary capital and operating cost estimates, permitting requirements, and the qualified person's conclusions and recommendations.
