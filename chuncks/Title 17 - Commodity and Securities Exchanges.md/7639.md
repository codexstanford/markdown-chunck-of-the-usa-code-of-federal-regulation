
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

#### ยง 210.3-17 Financial statements of natural persons.
##### and,Provided,

(c) Separate financial statements may be omitted, however, for each corporation, business trust, unincorporated association, or similar business organization if the person's total investment in such entity does not exceed 5 percent of his total assetsthe person's total income from such entity does not exceed 5 percent of his gross income;that the person's aggregate investment in and income from all such omitted entities shall not exceed 15 percent of his total assets and gross income, respectively.
