
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 23 - SWAP DEALERS AND MAJOR SWAP PARTICIPANTS
#### Subpart J - Duties of Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants
##### ยง 23.600 Risk Management Program for swap dealers and major swap participants.
###### Monitoring of compliance with Risk Management Program.

(7)Each swap dealer and major swap participant shall establish policies and procedures to detect violations of the Risk Management Program; to encourage employees to report such violations to senior management, without fear of retaliation; and to take specified disciplinary action against employees who violate the Risk Management Program.
