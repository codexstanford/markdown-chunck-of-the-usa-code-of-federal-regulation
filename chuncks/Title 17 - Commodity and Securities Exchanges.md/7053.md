
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 203 - RULES RELATING TO INVESTIGATIONS
#### Subpart B - Formal Investigative Proceedings
##### § 203.4 Applicability of §§ 203.4 through 203.8.
###### officer conducting the investigation

(b) Formal investigative proceedings may be held before the Commission, before one or more of its members, or before any officer designated by it for the purpose of taking testimony of witnesses and received other evidence. The termshall mean any of the foregoing.
