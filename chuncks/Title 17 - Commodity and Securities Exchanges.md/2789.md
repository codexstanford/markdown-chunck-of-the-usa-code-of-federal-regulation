
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 34 - REGULATION OF HYBRID INSTRUMENTS
#### ยง 34.2 Definitions.
##### Commodity-dependent component.

(d)A commodity-dependent component means a component of a hybrid instrument, the payment of which results from indexing to, or calculation by reference to, the price of a commodity.
