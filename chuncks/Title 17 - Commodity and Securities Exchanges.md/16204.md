
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter IV - Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Regulations Under Section 15C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
#### PART 420 - LARGE POSITION REPORTING
##### ยง 420.2 Definitions.
###### Aggregating entity,e.g.,

means a single entity (a parent company, affiliate, or organizational component) that is combined with other entities, as specified in the definition of "reporting entity" of this section, to form a reporting entity. In those cases where an entity has no affiliates, the aggregating entity is the same as the reporting entity.
