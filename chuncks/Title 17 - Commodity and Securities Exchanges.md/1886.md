###### Commission review of a final decision in a voluntary decisional proceeding.

(b)If such action is necessary to prevent manifest injustice, the Commission may, upon its own motion, review a final decision issued pursuant to ยง 12.106 of these rules by appropriate order filed with the Proceedings Clerk within 30 days after service upon the parties of the final decision.
