
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 240 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES EXCHANGE ACT OF 1934
#### General
##### ยง 240.12b-2 Definitions.
###### Registrant.

The term "registrant" means an issuer of securities with respect to which a registration statement or report is to be filed.
