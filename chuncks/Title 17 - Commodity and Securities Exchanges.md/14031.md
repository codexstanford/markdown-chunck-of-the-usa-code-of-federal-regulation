
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 248 - REGULATIONS S-P, S-AM, AND S-ID
#### Subpart A - Regulation S-P: Privacy of Consumer Financial Information and Safeguarding Personal Information
##### Appendix A to Subpart A of Part 248 - Forms
###### Last revised date.

(a)The financial institution must insert in the upper right-hand corner the date on which the notice was last revised. The information shall appear in minimum 8-point font as "rev. [month/year]" using either the name or number of the month, such as "rev. July 2009" or "rev. 7/09".
