##### ยง 12.313 Subpoenas for attendance at an oral hearing.
###### Service of subpoenas,How effected.

(c)-(1)Service of a subpoena upon a party shall be made in accordance with ยง 12.10. Service of a subpoena upon any other person shall be made by delivering a copy of the subpoena to them as provided in paragraph (c)(2) or (3) of this section, and by tendering to them the fees for one day's attendance and the mileage as specified in paragraph (e) of this section.
