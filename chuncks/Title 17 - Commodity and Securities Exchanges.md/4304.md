
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

#### ยง 49.2 Definitions.
##### SDR data

means the specific data elements and information required to be reported to a swap data repository or disseminated by a swap data repository pursuant to two or more of parts 43, 45, 46, and/or 49 of this chapter, as applicable in the context.
