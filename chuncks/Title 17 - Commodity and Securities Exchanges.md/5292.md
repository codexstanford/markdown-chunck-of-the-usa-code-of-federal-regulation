
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 150 - LIMITS ON POSITIONS
#### Appendix C to Part 150 - Guidance Regarding the Definition of Referenced Contract
##### Outright price reporting agency index contract,provided, however,

(5)means any outright commodity derivative contract whose settlement price is based solely on an index published by a price reporting agency that surveys cash-market transaction prices,that this term does not include any commodity derivative contract that settles at a basis, or differential, between a referenced contract and a price reporting agency index.
