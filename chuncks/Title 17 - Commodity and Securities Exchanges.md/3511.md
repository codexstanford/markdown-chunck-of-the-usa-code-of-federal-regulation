
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

#### Subpart B - Compliance with Core Principles
##### ยง 39.18 System safeguards.
###### Vulnerability testing

means testing of a derivatives clearing organization's automated systems to determine what information may be discoverable through a reconnaissance analysis of those systems and what vulnerabilities may be present on those systems.
