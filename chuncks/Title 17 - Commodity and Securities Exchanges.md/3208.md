
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 38 - DESIGNATED CONTRACT MARKETS
#### Subpart V - Financial Resources
##### ยง 38.1101 General requirements.
###### Types of financial resources.

(b)Financial resources available to satisfy the requirements of paragraph (a) of this section may include:

(1) The designated contract market's own capital, calculated in accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles; and

(2) Any other financial resource deemed acceptable by the Commission.
