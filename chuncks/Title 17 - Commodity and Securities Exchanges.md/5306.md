## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 150 - LIMITS ON POSITIONS
#### Appendix G to Part 150 - Guidance on Spread Transaction Exemptions Granted for Contracts that are Subject to Federal Speculative Position Limits
##### Intra-commodity spread

(iii)means a long (short) position in one or more commodity derivatives contracts in a particular commodity, or its product or by-products, and a short (long) position in one or more commodity derivative contracts in the same, or similar, commodity, or its products or by-products.
