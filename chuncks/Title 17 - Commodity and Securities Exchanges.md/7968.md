
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

###### Promoters and certain control persons.

(c)(1) Registrants that are filing a registration statement on Form S-1 under the Securities Act (ยง 239.11 of this chapter) or on Form 10 under the Exchange Act (ยง 249.210 of this chapter) and that had a promoter at any time during the past five fiscal years shall:
