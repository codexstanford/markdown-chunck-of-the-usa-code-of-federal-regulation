
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission
### PART 270 - RULES AND REGULATIONS, INVESTMENT COMPANY ACT OF 1940
#### ยง 270.18f-4 Exemption from the requirements of section 18 and section 61 for certain senior securities transactions.
##### Securities portfolio,i.e.,

means the fund's portfolio of securities and other investments, excluding any derivatives transactions, that is approved by the derivatives risk manager for purposes of the relative VaR test, provided that the fund's securities portfolio reflects the markets or asset classes in which the fund invests (the markets or asset classes in which the fund invests directly through securities and other investments and indirectly through derivatives transactions).
