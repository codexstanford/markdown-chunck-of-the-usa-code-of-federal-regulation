
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 15 - REPORTS - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### ยง 15.00 Definitions of terms used in parts 15 to 19, and 21 of this chapter.
##### Guided account program

(l)means any customer trading program which limits trading to the purchase or sale of a particular contract for future delivery of a commodity or a particular commodity option that is advised or recommended to the participant in the program.
