
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

### PART 230 - GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS, SECURITIES ACT OF 1933
#### Regulation A - Conditional Small Issues Exemption

###### Tier 2.

(2)Offerings pursuant to §§ 230.251 through 230.263 (Regulation A) in which the sum of the aggregate offering price and aggregate sales does not exceed $75,000,000, including not more than $22,500,000 offered by all selling securityholders that are affiliates of the issuer ("Tier 2 offerings").
