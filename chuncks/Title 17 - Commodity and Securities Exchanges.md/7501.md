#### Registered Investment Companies and Business Development Companies
##### § 210.6-10 What schedules are to be filed.
###### Schedule VIII-Investments-other than those presented in §§ 210.12-12, 12-12A, 12-12B, 12-13, 12-13A, 12-13B and 12-13C.

The schedule prescribed by § 210.12-13D shall be filed in support of captions 3(d) and 9(f) of each balance sheet.
