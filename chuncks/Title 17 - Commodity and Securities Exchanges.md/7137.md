### PART 204 - RULES RELATING TO DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart D - Administrative Wage Garnishment
##### ยง 204.64 Hearing.
###### Record.

(h)The hearing official will maintain a record of any hearing provided under this section. A hearing is not required to be a formal evidentiary-type hearing, however, witnesses who testify in oral hearings will do so under oath or affirmation.
