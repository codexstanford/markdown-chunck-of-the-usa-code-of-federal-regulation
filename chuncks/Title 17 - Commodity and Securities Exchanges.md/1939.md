
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 15 - REPORTS - GENERAL PROVISIONS
#### ยง 15.00 Definitions of terms used in parts 15 to 19, and 21 of this chapter.
##### Volume threshold account

(x)means any trading account that carries reportable trading volume on or subject to the rules of a reporting market that is a board of trade designated as a contract market under section 5 of the Act or a swap execution facility registered under section 5h of the Act.
