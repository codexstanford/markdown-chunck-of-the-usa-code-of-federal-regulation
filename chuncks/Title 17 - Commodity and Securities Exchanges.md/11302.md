
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

###### Audit of security-based swap trading relationship documentation.

(c)Each security-based swap dealer and major security-based swap participant shall have an independent auditor conduct periodic audits sufficient to identify any material weakness in its documentation policies and procedures required by this section. A record of the results of each audit shall be retained.
