
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter II - Securities and Exchange Commission

###### Exemption.

(a)Offers and sales of securities by an issuer that satisfy the conditions in paragraph (b) or (c) of this section shall be deemed to be transactions not involving any public offering within the meaning of section 4(a)(2) of the Act.
