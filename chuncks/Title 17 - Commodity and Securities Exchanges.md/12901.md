
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

### PART 242 - REGULATIONS M, SHO, ATS, AC, NMS, AND SBSR AND CUSTOMER MARGIN REQUIREMENTS FOR SECURITY FUTURES
#### Regulation M
##### ยง 242.100 Preliminary note; definitions.
###### Syndicate covering transaction

means the placing of any bid or the effecting of any purchase on behalf of the sole distributor or the underwriting syndicate or group to reduce a short position created in connection with the offering.
