
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

### PART 39 - DERIVATIVES CLEARING ORGANIZATIONS

##### ยง 39.15 Treatment of funds.
###### Customer funds,Segregation.

(b)- (1)A derivatives clearing organization shall comply with the applicable segregation requirements of section 4d of the Act and Commission regulations in this part, or any other applicable Commission regulation in this chapter or order requiring that customer funds and assets, including money, securities, and property, be segregated, set aside, or held in a separate account.
