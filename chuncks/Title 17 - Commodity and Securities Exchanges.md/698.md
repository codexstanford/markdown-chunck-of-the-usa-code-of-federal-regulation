
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 1 - GENERAL REGULATIONS UNDER THE COMMODITY EXCHANGE ACT
#### Miscellaneous
##### ยง 1.59 Activities of self-regulatory organization employees, governing board members, committee members, and consultants.
###### Governing board members, committee members, and consultants; Registered futures association rules.

(c)Each registered futures association must maintain in effect rules which have been submitted to the Commission pursuant to section 17(j) of the Act which provide that no governing board member, committee member, or consultant shall use or disclose-for any purpose other than the performance of official duties as a governing board member, committee member, or consultant-material, non-public information obtained as a result of the performance of such person's official duties.
