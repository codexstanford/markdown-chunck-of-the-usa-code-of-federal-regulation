###### Commodity contract

(2) A futures or option on futures contract executed on or subject to the rules of a foreign board of trade;

(5) Notwithstanding paragraphs (1) through (4) of this definition, a security futures product as defined in section 1a(45) of the Act is not a commodity contract for purposes of this part when such contract is held in a securities account.
