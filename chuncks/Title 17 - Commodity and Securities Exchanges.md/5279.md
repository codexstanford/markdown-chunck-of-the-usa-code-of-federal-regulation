
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges
## Chapter I - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
### PART 150 - LIMITS ON POSITIONS
#### Appendix A to Part 150 - List of Enumerated Bona Fide Hedges
##### Hedges of anticipated mineral royalties.,provided that

(8)Short positions in a person's commodity derivative contracts offset by the anticipated change in value of mineral royalty rights that are owned by that person,the royalty rights arise out of the production of the commodity underlying the commodity derivative contracts.
