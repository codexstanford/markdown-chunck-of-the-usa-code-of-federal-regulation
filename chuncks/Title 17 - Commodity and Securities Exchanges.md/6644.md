
# Title 17 - Commodity and Securities Exchanges

####### Time limit.

(c)(1)Postponements, adjournments or extensions of time for filing papers shall not exceed 21 days unless the Commission or the hearing officer states on the record or sets forth in a written order the reasons why a longer period of time is necessary.
