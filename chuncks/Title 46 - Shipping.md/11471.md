
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 177-CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart D-Fire Protection
###### § 177.405 General arrangement and outfitting.
####### Fire hazards to be minimized.

(a)The general construction of the vessel must be such as to minimize fire hazards insofar as it is reasonable and practicable.
