####### Assistance-needed alarm.

(e)The engineer's assistance-needed alarm (see subpart 113.27 of this chapter) must annunciate if-

(1) An alarm at the ECC is not acknowledged in the period of time necessary for an engineer to respond at the ECC from the machinery spaces or engineers' accommodations; or
