
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 189-INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION
##### Subpart 189.60-Certificates Under International Convention for Safety of Life at Sea, 1974
###### § 189.60-35 Availability of Certificates.

The Convention certificates must be on board the vessel and readily available for examination at all times.
