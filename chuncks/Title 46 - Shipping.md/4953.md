##### Subpart B - Construction and Arrangement
###### Accommodation Spaces
####### ยง 108.210 Hospital space not required.

(a) The hospital space required under ยง 108.209 is not required on a unit if one single or double occupancy sleeping space, designated and equipped as a treatment or isolation room or both is available for immediate medical use, and has-
