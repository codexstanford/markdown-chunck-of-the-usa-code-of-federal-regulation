
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter M - Towing Vessels
#### PART 138 - TOWING SAFETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (TSMS)
##### Subpart B - Towing Safety Management System (TSMS)
###### ยง 138.220 TSMS elements.
####### Safety, environment, and security.

(2)Procedures must be in place to ensure safety of property, the environment, and personnel. This must include procedures to ensure the selection of the appropriate vessel, including adequate maneuverability and horsepower, appropriate rigging and towing gear, proper management of the navigational watch, and compliance with applicable security measures.
