
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter G - Documentation and Measurement of Vessels
#### PART 69 - MEASUREMENT OF VESSELS
##### Subpart C - Standard Regulatory Measurement System
###### ยง 69.108 Uppermost complete deck.
####### Defined.

(a)"Uppermost complete deck" means the uppermost deck which extends from stem to stern and from side to side at all points of its length and is bound by the vessel's hull.
