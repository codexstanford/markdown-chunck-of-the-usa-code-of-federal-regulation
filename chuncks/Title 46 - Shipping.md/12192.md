
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter V—Marine Occupational Safety and Health Standards
#### PART 197-GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart B-Commercial Diving Operations
###### general
####### § 197.204 Definitions.
######## Surface-supplied mixed-gas diving

means a diving mode in which the diver is supplied from the dive location or bell with a compressed breathing mixture other than air.
