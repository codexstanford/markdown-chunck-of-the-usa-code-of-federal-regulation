
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter V—Marine Occupational Safety and Health Standards
#### PART 197-GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart B-Commercial Diving Operations
###### general
####### § 197.204 Definitions.
######## Working pressure

means the pressure to which a pressure containment device is exposed at any particular instant during normal operating conditions.
