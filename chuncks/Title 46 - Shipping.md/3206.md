####### Hydrostatic tests under pressure.

(d)Each pressure vessel, other than one exempted by this section, must be subjected to a hydrostatic test at a pressure of 1times the maximum allowable working pressure twice within any five-year period, except that no more than three years may elapse between any test and its immediate predecessor.
