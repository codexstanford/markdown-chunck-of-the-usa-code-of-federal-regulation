
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter J - Miscellaneous
#### PART 388 - ADMINISTRATIVE WAIVERS OF THE COASTWISE TRADE LAWS
##### ยง 388.4 Criteria for grant of a waiver.
###### Advance notice and approval needed for changes.

(d)When we approve a waiver application, we will notify the applicant that no substantial change in the employment of the vessel in the coastwise trade may be made without prior notice to MARAD. In general, a substantial change in operating area will require a new waiver application.
