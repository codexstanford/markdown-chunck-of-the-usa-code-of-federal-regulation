
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.111-Draperies, Curtains, and Other Suspended Textiles
###### § 164.111-3 Testing, marking, and inspection requirements.

(a) Draperies, curtains, and other suspended textiles submitted for type approval must be tested for qualities of resistance to the propagation of flame not inferior to those of wool of mass 0.8 kg/munder Annex 1, Part 7, of the FTP Code (incorporated by reference, see § 164.111-2).
