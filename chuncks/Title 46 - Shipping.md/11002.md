
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S—Subdivision and Stability
#### PART 171-SPECIAL RULES PERTAINING TO VESSELS CARRYING PASSENGERS
##### Subpart A-General
###### § 171.010 Definitions.
####### Passenger space

(k)means a space which is provided for the accommodation and use of passengers, other than a baggage, store, provision or mail room.
