
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter O - Certain Bulk Dangerous Cargoes

##### Subpart E-Operations
###### ยง 154.1860 Integral tanks: Cargo colder than -10 &#xB0;C (14 &#xB0;F).

The master shall ensure that an integral tank does not carry a cargo colder than -10 &#xB0;C (14 &#xB0;F) unless that carriage is specially approved by the Commandant (CG-OES).
