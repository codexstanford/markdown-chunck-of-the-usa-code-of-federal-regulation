
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter I-A - Mobile Offshore Drilling Units
#### PART 108 - DESIGN AND EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart D - Fire Extinguishing Systems
###### Fixed Carbon Dioxide Fire Extinguishing Systems
####### ยง 108.457 Pressure release.

Each air tight or vapor tight space, such as a paint locker, that is protected by a COsystem must have a means for releasing pressure that accumulates within the space if COis discharged into the space.
