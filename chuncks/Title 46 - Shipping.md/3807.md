
# Title 46 - Shipping

###### ยง 69.109 Under-deck tonnage.
####### Spaces open to the sea.

(n)In calculating the tonnage of spaces below the uppermost complete deck, subtract from each breadth measurement the portion of that measurement that spans a space, or a portion thereof, that is open to the sea.
