
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 64 - MARINE PORTABLE TANKS AND CARGO HANDLING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart B - Standards for an MPT
###### ยง 64.35 Bottom filling or discharge connection.

If an MPT is designed with a filling or discharge connection in the bottom, the connection must be fitted with a bolted blank flange, threaded cap, or similar device to protect against leakage of the product, and a manually operated valve that is located-

(a) Inside the tank and operated outside the tank; or

(b) Outside the tank but as close to it as practicable.
