
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.156-Rescue Boats and Fast Rescue Boats (SOLAS)
###### § 160.156-13 Approval inspections and tests for prototype rescue boats and fast rescue boats.
####### Rescue boat operational test, operation of engine

(iv)(Paragraph 1/7.1.5). For the 4-hour rescue boat maneuvering period, the rescue boat must not (except for a short period to measure towing force and to demonstrate towing fixture durability) be secured, and must be run through its full range of speeds and full range of all controls throughout the period.
