
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 190-CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart 190.00-General Provisions
###### § 190.00-1 Preemptive effect.

The regulations in this part have preemptive effect over State or local regulations in the same field.
