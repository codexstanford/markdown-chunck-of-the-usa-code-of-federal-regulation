
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Regulations Affecting Subsidized Vessels and Operators
#### PART 249 - APPROVAL OF UNDERWRITERS FOR MARINE HULL INSURANCE
##### § 249.5 Eligibility criteria.
###### Other Foreign Underwriters.

(c)Foreign underwriters, other than those specified in paragraphs (b) (1) and (2) of this section, may also be eligible to participate in the writing of marine hull insurance on MARAD program vessels, if approved to do so in accordance with the procedures contained in §§ 249.6 and 249.7.
