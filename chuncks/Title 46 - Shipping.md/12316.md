#### PART 197-GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart C-Benzene
###### Appendix B to Subpart C of Part 197-Substance Technical Guidelines, Benzene
####### Steps to be taken if the material is released or spilled.

(a)As much benzene as possible should be absorbed with suitable materials, such as dry sand or earth. That remaining must be flushed with large amounts of water. Do not flush benzene into a confined space, such as a sewer, because of explosion danger. Remove all ignition sources. Ventilate enclosed places.
