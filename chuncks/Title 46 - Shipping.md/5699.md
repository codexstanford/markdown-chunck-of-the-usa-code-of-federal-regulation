
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security

##### ยง 114.400 Definitions of terms used in this subchapter.
###### Freeing port

means any direct opening through the vessel's bulwark or hull to quickly drain overboard water that has been shipped on exposed decks.
