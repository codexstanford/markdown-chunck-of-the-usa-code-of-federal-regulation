##### Subpart C-Design, Construction and Equipment
###### Firefighting

(a) The discharge density of each water spray system must be at least:

(1) 10000 cm/m/min. (0.25 gpm/ft.) over each horizontal surface; and

(2) 4000 cm/m/min. (0.10 gpm/ft.) against vertical surface, including the water rundown.
