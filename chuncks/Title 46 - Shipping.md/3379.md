
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 64 - MARINE PORTABLE TANKS AND CARGO HANDLING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 64.5 Definitions.
####### Maximum allowable working pressure

(d)means the maximum gauge pressure at the top of the tank in the operating position at 122 &#xB0;F, equal to or greater than the total containment pressure as defined in paragraph (c) of this section. The maximum allowable working pressure is used in the calculation of the minimum thickness of each element of the tank, excluding the allowance for corrosion and the thickness for loadings other than pressure, as provided for in the ASME Code.
