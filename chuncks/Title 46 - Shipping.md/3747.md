
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter G - Documentation and Measurement of Vessels
#### PART 69 - MEASUREMENT OF VESSELS
##### Subpart B - Convention Measurement System
###### ยง 69.57 Gross tonnage ITC.

Gross tonnage ITC (GT ITC) is determined by the following formula GT ITC = KV, in which V = total volume of all enclosed spaces in cubic meters and K= 0.2 + 0.02 logV.
