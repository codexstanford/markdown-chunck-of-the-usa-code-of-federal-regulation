
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Tank Vessels
#### PART 32 - SPECIAL EQUIPMENT, MACHINERY, AND HULL REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart 32.60 - Hull Requirements for Tank Vessels Constructed On or After July 1, 1951
###### ยง 32.60-10 Segregation of cargo; Grade A, B, C, or D - TB/ALL.
####### General.

(a)The galleys, living quarters, navigation spaces, general cargo spaces, boiler rooms, and enclosed spaces where sources of vapor ignition are normally present, shall be segregated from cargo tanks by cofferdams or pump rooms or tanks, either empty or used to carry liquid having a flashpoint of 150 &#xB0;F. or above, or deck spaces enclosed or open.
