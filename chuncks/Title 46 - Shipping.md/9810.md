
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 161-ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 161.006-Searchlights, Motor Lifeboat, for Merchant Vessels
###### § 161.006-4 Requirements.
####### Lamp and socket.

(e)The motor lifeboat searchlight shall be provided with a lamp of not less than 80 watts nor more than 100 watts, and a suitable lamp socket. Means shall be provided for adjusting and securing the lamp socket at any position between the focal point and a point not less thaninch away from the focal point in either direction in the axis of the beam.
