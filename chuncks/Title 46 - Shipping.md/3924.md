
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter H - Passenger Vessels
#### PART 70 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 70.10 - Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### ยง 70.10-1 Definitions.
####### Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection

means any person from the civilian or military branch of the Coast Guard designated as such by the Commandant and who, under the direction of the Coast Guard District Commander, is in charge of an inspection zone for the performance of duties related to the inspection, enforcement, and administration of Subtitle II, Title 46 U.S. Code; Title 33 U.S. Code; and regulations issued under these statutes.
