### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval

##### Subpart 160.005-Life Preservers, Fibrous Glass, Adult and Child (Jacket Type), Models 52 and 56

####### Reinforcing tape.

(g)Binding tape shall be stitched approximately 15 inches for adult life preservers and 12 inches for child life preservers around the back of the neck, and also around the openings of the drawstring tunnel and around the bottom of the arm holes as indicated by the drawings.
