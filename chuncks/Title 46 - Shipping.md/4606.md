
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter I - Cargo and Miscellaneous Vessels

##### Subpart 97.01 - Application
###### ยง 97.01-1 General; preemptive effect.

(a) The provisions of this part shall apply to all vessels except as specifically noted in this part.
