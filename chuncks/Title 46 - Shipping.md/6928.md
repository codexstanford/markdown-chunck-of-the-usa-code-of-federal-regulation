
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter M - Towing Vessels
#### PART 144 - CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart H - Rails and Guards
###### ยง 144.830 Protection against hot piping.

Each exhaust pipe from an internal combustion engine which is within reach of personnel must be insulated or otherwise guarded to prevent burns. On a new vessel, each pipe that contains vapor, gas, or liquid that has a temperature exceeding 150 &#xB0;F (65.5 &#xB0;C) which is within reach of personnel must be insulated where necessary or otherwise guarded to prevent injury.
