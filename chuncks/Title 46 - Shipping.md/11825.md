
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 189-INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION
##### Subpart 189.01-General Provisions; Certificate of Inspection
###### § 189.01-1 Preemptive effect.

The regulations in this part have preemptive effect over State or local regulations in the same field.
