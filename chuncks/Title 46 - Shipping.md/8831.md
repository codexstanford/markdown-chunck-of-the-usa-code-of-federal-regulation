
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.064-Marine Buoyant Devices
###### § 160.064-4 Marking.
####### Durability of marking.

(b)Marking must be of a type which will be durable and legible for the expected life of the device.
