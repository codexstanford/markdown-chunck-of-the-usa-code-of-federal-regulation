###### ยง 42.05-27 Credential.
####### credential

As used in this subchapter,means any or all of the following:

(a) Merchant mariner's document.

(b) Merchant mariner's license.

(c) STCW endorsement.

(d) Certificate of registry.

(e) Merchant mariner credential.
