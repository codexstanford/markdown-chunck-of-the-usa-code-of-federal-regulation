
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 167-PUBLIC NAUTICAL SCHOOL SHIPS
##### Subpart 167.45-Special Firefighting and Fire Prevention Requirements
###### § 167.45-15 Capacity of pipes and hose.
####### Provided, however,

The capacity of the pipes and hose leading from the pumps shall in no case be less than that of the discharge opening of the pump:That the pipe and hose shall in no instance be less than 1inches in internal diameter.
