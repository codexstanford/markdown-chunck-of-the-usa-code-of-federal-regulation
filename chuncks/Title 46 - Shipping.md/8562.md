
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.051-Inflatable Liferafts for Domestic Service
###### § 160.051-7 Design and performance of Coastal Service inflatable liferafts.
####### Cold-inflation test.

(l)The cold-inflation test required under paragraph 1/5.17.3.3.2 of IMO Revised recommendation on testing and § 160.151-27(a) must be conducted at a test temperature of -18 &#xB0;C (0 &#xB0;F).
