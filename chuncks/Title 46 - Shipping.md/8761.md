
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.060-Specification for a Buoyant Vest, Unicellular Polyethylene Foam, Adult and Child
###### § 160.060-6 Construction-nonstandard vests.
####### General.

(a)The construction methods used for a nonstandard buoyant vest must be equivalent to the requirements in § 160.060-5 for standard vests and also meet the requirements specified in this section.
