
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.700-Vessel Control, Miscellaneous Systems, and Equipment
###### Markings
####### § 169.731 General alarm bells.

On vessels of 100 gross tons and over each general alarm bell must be identified by red lettering at leastinch high: "GENERAL ALARM-WHEN BELL RINGS GO TO YOUR STATION."
