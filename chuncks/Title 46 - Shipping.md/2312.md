
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter E - Load Lines
#### PART 44 - SPECIAL SERVICE LIMITED DOMESTIC VOYAGES
##### Subpart B - Rules for Assigning Special Service Load Lines
###### § 44.05-25 Freeboards.
####### Towed cargo barges with cargo only on deck.

(d)The freeboard for barges of this type is to be computed in accordance with the requirements of §§ 45.20-1 to 45.20-70 of this subchapter. The fresh water and seasonal markings where applicable are to be the same as determined under part 42 of this subchapter.
