
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.057-Floating Orange Smoke Distress Signals (15 Minutes)
###### § 160.057-4 Approval and production tests.
####### Corrosion resistance.

(7)Expose the complete specimen with cover secured hand-tight to a finely divided spray of 20 percent by weight sodium chloride solution at a temperature between 32 &#xB0;C and 38 &#xB0;C (90 &#xB0;F and 100 &#xB0;F) for 100 hours. The container and cap must not be corroded in any fashion that would impair their proper functioning.
