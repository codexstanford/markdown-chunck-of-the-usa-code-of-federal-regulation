
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter G - Documentation and Measurement of Vessels

###### ยง 69.103 Definitions.
####### Ceiling

means the permanent planking or plating fitted directly on the inboard side of frames, floors, or double bottom and includes cargo battens and refrigeration insulation but does not include false ceiling which stands off from the framing.
