
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter I-B - Control and Utilization of Ports
#### PART 345 - RESTRICTIONS UPON THE TRANSFER OR CHANGE IN USE OR IN TERMS GOVERNING UTILIZATION OF PORT FACILITIES
##### Section 1 Definitions.
###### Transfer

(g)means to sell, lease, trade, lend, give, relinquish title or possession to, or to physically transfer in any other way.
