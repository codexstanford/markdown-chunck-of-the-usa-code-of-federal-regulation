
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 179-SUBDIVISION, DAMAGE STABILITY, AND WATERTIGHT INTEGRITY
##### Subpart C-Watertight Integrity Requirements
###### § 179.340 Trunks.
####### i.e.

Where a trunk (, an enclosed passageway through a deck or bulkhead) is installed, it must comply with the requirements of § 179.360(a)(1) and with the requirements of § 171.113 in subchapter S of this chapter.
