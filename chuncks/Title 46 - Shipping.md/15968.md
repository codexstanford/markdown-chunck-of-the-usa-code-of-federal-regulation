
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 535-OCEAN COMMON CARRIER AND MARINE TERMINAL OPERATOR AGREEMENTS SUBJECT TO THE SHIPPING ACT OF 1984
##### Subpart A-General Provisions
###### § 535.104 Definitions.
####### Person

(w)means individuals, corporations, partnerships and associations existing under or authorized by the laws of the United States or of a foreign country.
