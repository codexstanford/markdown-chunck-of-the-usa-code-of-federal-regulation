
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 163-CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart 163.002-Pilot Hoist
###### § 163.002-13 Construction.
####### Nonfunctional sharp edges and projections of excessive length.

(f)A pilot hoist must not have nonfunctional sharp edges and must not have fastening devices or other projections of excessive length.
