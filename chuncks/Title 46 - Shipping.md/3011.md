
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 56 - PIPING SYSTEMS AND APPURTENANCES
##### Subpart 56.97 - Pressure Tests
###### ยง 56.97-25 Preparation for testing (reproduces 137.2).
####### Precautions against test medium expansion.

(f)If a pressure test is to be maintained for a period of time and the test medium in the system is subject to thermal expansion, precautions must be taken to avoid excessive pressure. A small relief valve set to 1times the test pressure is recommended during the pressure test.
