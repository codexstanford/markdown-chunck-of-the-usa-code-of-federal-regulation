
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S—Subdivision and Stability
#### PART 172-SPECIAL RULES PERTAINING TO BULK CARGOES
##### Subpart D-Special Rules Pertaining to a Vessel That Carries a Cargo Regulated Under 33 CFR Part 157
###### § 172.070 Intact stability.

All tank vessels of 5,000 deadweight tons (DWT) and above, contracted after December 3, 2001, must comply with the intact stability requirements of IMO Res. MEPC.117(52) (incorporated by reference, see § 172.020).
