
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 162-ENGINEERING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 162.050-Pollution Prevention Equipment
###### § 162.050-3 Definitions.
####### Response time

means the time elapsed between an alteration in the sample being supplied to the bilge alarm and the ppm display showing the correct response.
