
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.100-General Provisions
###### § 169.107 Definitions.
####### Length,Length on deck,Length between perpendiculars

means the mean length. It is the mean or average between length on deck (LOD) and length between perpendiculars (LBP).(LOD) means the length between the forward-most and after-most points on the weather deck, excluding sheer.(LBP) means the horizontal distance between the perpendiculars taken at the forward-most and after-most points on a vessel's waterline corresponding to the deepest operating draft.
