
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 167-PUBLIC NAUTICAL SCHOOL SHIPS
##### Subpart 167.05-Definitions
###### § 167.05-40 Underwater survey.
####### Underwater survey

means the examination of the vessel's underwater hull including all through-hull fittings and appurtenances, while the vessel is afloat.
