
# Title 46 - Shipping

##### Subpart 4.03 - Definitions
###### ยง 4.03-15 Commandant.

The Commandant, U.S. Coast Guard, is that officer who acts as chief of the Coast Guard and is charged with the administration of the Coast Guard.
