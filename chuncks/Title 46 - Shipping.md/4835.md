
# Title 46 - Shipping

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 107.111 Definitions.
####### Mobile offshore drilling unit,unit

ormeans a vessel, except a public vessel of the United States, capable of engaging in drilling operations for the exploration or exploitation of subsea resources that is-

(1) Seagoing and 300 or more gross tons and self-propelled by motor;

(2) Seagoing and 100 or more gross tons and non-self-propelled; or
