
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 163-CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart 163.003-Pilot Ladder
###### § 163.003-11 Materials.
####### Wooden parts.

(b)Each wooden part of a pilot ladder must be hardwood that is free from knots and any other defects affecting its strength or durability.
