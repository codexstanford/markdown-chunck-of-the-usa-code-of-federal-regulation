##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 45.3 Definitions.
####### Superstructure

(i)means a deck structure on the freeboard deck, extending from side to side of the ship or with the side plating not being inboard of the shell plating more than 4 percent of the breadth (B). A raised quarterdeck is a superstructure.
