
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.174-Thermal Protective Aids
###### § 160.174-11 Performance.
####### Storage Temperature.

(c)A thermal protective aid must not be damaged by storage in its storage case at any temperature between -30 &#xB0;C (-22 &#xB0;F) and + 65 C (149 &#xB0;F).
