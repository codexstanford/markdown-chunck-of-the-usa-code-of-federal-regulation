
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 196-OPERATIONS
##### Subpart 196.37-Markings for Fire and Emergency Equipment, etc.
###### § 196.37-7 General alarm bells.

(a) All general alarm bells shall be identified by red lettering at leastinch high: "GENERAL ALARM-WHEN BELL RINGS GO TO YOUR STATION."
