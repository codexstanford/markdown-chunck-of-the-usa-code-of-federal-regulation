
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter M - Towing Vessels
#### PART 138 - TOWING SAFETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (TSMS)
##### Subpart B - Towing Safety Management System (TSMS)
###### ยง 138.220 TSMS elements.
####### Employment procedures.

(1)The TSMS must contain procedures related to the employment of individuals. Procedures must be in place to ensure adequate qualifications of personnel, to include background checks, compliance with drug and alcohol standards, and that personnel are able to perform required tasks.
