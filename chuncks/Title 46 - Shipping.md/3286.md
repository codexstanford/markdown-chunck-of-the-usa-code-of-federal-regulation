
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering

###### ยง 62.25-20 Instrumentation, alarms, and centralized stations.
####### Summarized and grouped alarms.

(f)Visual alarms at a control location that are summarized or grouped by function, system, or item of equipment must-

(1) Be sufficiently specific to allow any necessary action to be taken; and

(2) Have a display at the equipment or an appropriate control location to identify the specific alarm condition or location.
