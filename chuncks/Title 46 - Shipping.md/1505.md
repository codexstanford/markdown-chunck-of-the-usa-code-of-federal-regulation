
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Tank Vessels
#### PART 30 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 30.10 - Definitions
###### ยง 30.10-47 Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection - TB/ALL.
####### Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection,

The termmeans any person from the civilian or military branch of the Coast Guard designated as such by the Commandant and who under the superintendence and direction of the Coast Guard District Commander is in charge of an inspection zone for the performance of duties with respect to the enforcement and administration of Subtitle II, Title 46, U.S. Code, Title 46 and Title 33, U.S. Code, and regulations issued under these statutes.
