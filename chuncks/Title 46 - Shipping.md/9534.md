
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.171-Immersion Suits
###### § 160.171-11 Performance.
####### Vision.

(e)Each suit must be designed to allow unrestricted vision throughout an arc of 60&#xB0; to either side of the wearer's straight-ahead line of sight when the wearer's head is turned to any angle between 30&#xB0; to the right and 30&#xB0; to the left. Each suit must be designed to allow a standing wearer to move head and eyes up and down far enough to see both feet and a spot directly overhead.
