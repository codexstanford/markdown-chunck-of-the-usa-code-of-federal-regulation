
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 64 - MARINE PORTABLE TANKS AND CARGO HANDLING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart B - Standards for an MPT
###### ยง 64.19 External pressure.

(a) A tank without a vacuum breaker must be designed to withstand an external pressure of 7psig or more.

(b) A tank with a vacuum breaker must be designed to withstand an external pressure of 3 psig or more.
