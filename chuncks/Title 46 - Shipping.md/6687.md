
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter M - Towing Vessels
#### PART 138 - TOWING SAFETY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (TSMS)
##### Subpart B - Towing Safety Management System (TSMS)
###### ยง 138.220 TSMS elements.
####### Master authority.

(iii)Each owner or managing operator must define the scope of the master's authority. The master's authority must provide for the ability to make final determinations on safe operations of the towing vessel. Specifically, it must provide the authority for the master to cease operation if an unsafe condition exists.
