
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.171-Immersion Suits
###### § 160.171-11 Performance.
####### Water penetration.

(f)An immersion suit must be designed to prevent undue ingress of water into the suit following a period of flotation in calm water of one hour.
