
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Uninspected Vessels
#### PART 24 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 24.10 - Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### ยง 24.10-1 Definitions.
####### Coast Guard District Commander

means an officer of the Coast Guard designated as such by the Commandant to command all Coast Guard activities within his or her district, which includes the inspection, enforcement, and administration of Subtitle II, Title 46 U.S. Code; Title 33 U.S. Code; and regulations issued under these statutes.
