
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Regulations Affecting Subsidized Vessels and Operators
#### PART 296 - MARITIME SECURITY PROGRAM (MSP)
##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 296.2 Definitions.
####### Noncontiguous Domestic Trade

means transportation of cargo between a point in the contiguous 48 states and a point in Alaska, Hawaii, or Puerto Rico, other than a point in Alaska north of the Arctic Circle.
