##### Subpart 160.017-Chain Ladder
###### § 160.017-11 Materials.
####### Lashing rings.

(e)The inside diameter of each lashing ring must be at least 75 mm (3 in.). Each lashing ring must have a minimum breaking strength of at least 16 kN (3,560 lbs.).
