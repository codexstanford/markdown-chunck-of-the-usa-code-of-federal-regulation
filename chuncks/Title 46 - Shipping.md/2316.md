### Subchapter E - Load Lines
#### PART 44 - SPECIAL SERVICE LIMITED DOMESTIC VOYAGES
##### Subpart B - Rules for Assigning Special Service Load Lines
###### § 44.05-25 Freeboards.
####### Self-propelled tank barges.

(h)The freeboard is to be computed in accordance with §§ 45.20-1 to 45.20-70 of this subchapter. The fresh water and seasonal markings where applicable are to be determined under part 42 of this subchapter.
