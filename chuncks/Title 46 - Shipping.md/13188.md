
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Regulations Affecting Subsidized Vessels and Operators
#### PART 295 - MARITIME SECURITY PROGRAM (MSP)
##### Subpart B - Establishment of MSP Fleet and Eligibility
###### ยง 295.11 Applications.
####### Action by MARAD,Time Deadlines.

(a)-(1)Not later than 30 days after the enactment of the Maritime Security Act of 1996, Pub. L. 104-239, MARAD shall accept applications for Enrollment of vessels in the MSP Fleet. Within 90 days after receipt of a completed application, MARAD shall enter into a MSP Operating Agreement with the applicant or provide in writing the reason for denial of that application.
