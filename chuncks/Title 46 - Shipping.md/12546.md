
# Title 46 - Shipping

##### Subpart C - Additional Requirements for Passenger Vessels
###### ยง 199.250 Drills.

(a) An abandon-ship drill and a fire drill, as described in ยง 199.180, must be conducted on each passenger vessel at least weekly.
