
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Certain Bulk Dangerous Cargoes
#### PART 154-SAFETY STANDARDS FOR SELF-PROPELLED VESSELS CARRYING BULK LIQUEFIED GASES
##### Appendix B to Part 154-Stress Analyses Definitions
###### Primary general membrane stress

means the primary membrane stress that is so distributed in the structure that no redistribution of load occurs as a result of yielding.
