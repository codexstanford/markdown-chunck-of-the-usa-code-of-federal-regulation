
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.100-General Provisions
###### § 169.107 Definitions.
####### Anniversary date

means the day and the month of each year, which corresponds to the date of expiration of the Certificate of Inspection.
