
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 56 - PIPING SYSTEMS AND APPURTENANCES
##### Subpart 56.97 - Pressure Tests
###### ยง 56.97-30 Hydrostatic tests (modifies 137.4).
####### Check of test equipment before applying pressure.

(c)The test equipment must be examined before pressure is applied to ensure that it is tight and that all low-pressure filling lines and other items that should not be subjected to the test pressure have been disconnected or isolated by valves or other suitable means.
