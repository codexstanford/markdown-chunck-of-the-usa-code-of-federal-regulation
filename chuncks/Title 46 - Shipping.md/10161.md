
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.007-Structural Insulations
###### § 164.007-9 Procedure for approval.
####### Samples to be submitted.

(c)If the material is indicated as being suitable for testing, the manufacturer shall submit a 100 cm. x 150 cm. (40&#x2033; x 60&#x2033;) sample, a 30 cm. x 30 cm. (12&#x2033; x 12&#x2033;) sample and a 60 cm. x 60cm. (24&#x2033; x 24&#x2033;) sample for each thickness and density proposed to the Fire Research section of the National Bureau of Standards, Washington, DC 20234, and shall advise the Coast Guard of the shipment. A separate test will be made for each density of the material for which approval is desired.
