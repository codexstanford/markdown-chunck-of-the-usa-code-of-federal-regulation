
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S—Subdivision and Stability
#### PART 172-SPECIAL RULES PERTAINING TO BULK CARGOES
##### Subpart C-Special Rules Pertaining to a Barge That Carries a Cargo Regulated Under Subchapter D of This Chapter
###### § 172.048 Definitions.
####### MARPOL 73/38

means the International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution from Ships, 1973, as modified by the Protocol of 1978 relating to that Convention.
