
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.047-Specification for a Buoyant Vest, Kapok or Fibrous Glass, Adult and Child
###### § 160.047-3 Materials.
####### Tie tapes and body strap loops.

(f)The tie tapes and body strap loops for an adult or child size buoyant vest specified by this subpart must be-inch cotton webbing meeting the requirements in military specification MIL-T-43566 (Class I) for Type I webbing.
