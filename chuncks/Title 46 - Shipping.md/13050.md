##### ยง 287.19 Requirements as to new vessels.
###### Time of construction.

(b)A vessel will be deemed to be constructed after December 31, 1939, only if construction was commenced after that date. Subject to the provisions of this section, a new vessel may be newly built for the taxpayer, or may be acquired after it is built.
