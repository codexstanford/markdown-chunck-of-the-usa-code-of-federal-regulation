
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.174-Thermal Protective Aids
###### § 160.174-11 Performance.
####### In water performance.

(d)The thermal protective aid must be designed to permit the wearer to remove it in the water within two minutes, if it impairs ability to swim.
