
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 531-NVOCC SERVICE ARRANGEMENTS
##### Subpart E-Recordkeeping and Audit
###### § 531.12 Recordkeeping and audit.
####### Production for audit within 30 days of request.

(b)Every NVOCC shall, upon written request of the FMC's Director, Bureau of Enforcement, any Area Representative or the Director, Bureau of Trade Analysis, submit copies of requested original NSAs or their associated records within thirty (30) days of the date of the request.
