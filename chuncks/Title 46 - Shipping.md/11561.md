
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 180-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT AND ARRANGEMENTS
##### Subpart D-Survival Craft Arrangements and Equipment
###### § 180.175 Survival craft equipment.
####### Lifeline and pendants.

(1)The lifeline and pendants must be as furnished by the manufacturer with the approved life float or buoyant apparatus. Replacement lifelines and pendants must meet the requirements in Subpart 160.010 of this chapter.
