
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.055-Life Preservers, Unicellular Plastic Foam, Adult and Child, for Merchant Vessels
###### § 160.055-5 Construction-standard life preservers.
####### Construction-standard, cloth covered life preserver.

(c)This device is constructed from three sections of unicellular plastic foam contained in a cloth envelope and has a neck hole, the body slit in the front, and a fitted and adjustable body strap.
