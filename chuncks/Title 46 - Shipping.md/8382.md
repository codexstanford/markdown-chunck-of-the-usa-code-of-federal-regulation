
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.040-Line-Throwing Appliance, Impulse-Projected Rocket Type (and Equipment)
###### § 160.040-3 Materials, construction, workmanship, and performance requirements.
####### Workmanship.

(c)Impulse-projected rocket type line-throwing appliances shall be of first class workmanship and shall be free from imperfections of manufacture affecting their appearance or that may affect their serviceability.
