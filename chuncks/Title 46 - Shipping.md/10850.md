
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.700-Vessel Control, Miscellaneous Systems, and Equipment
###### Markings
####### § 169.737 Hand portable fire extinguishers.

Each hand portable fire extinguisher must be marked with a number, and the location where it is stowed must be marked with a corresponding number. The marks must be at leastinch high. Where only one type and size of hand portable fire extinguisher is carried, the numbering may be omitted.
