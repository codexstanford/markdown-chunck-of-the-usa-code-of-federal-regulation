
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter E - Load Lines
#### PART 44 - SPECIAL SERVICE LIMITED DOMESTIC VOYAGES
##### Subpart B - Rules for Assigning Special Service Load Lines
###### § 44.05-25 Freeboards.
####### Towed cargo barges with cargo under deck.

(c)The freeboard is to be computed under §§ 45.15-1 to 45.15-97 of this subchapter. The fresh water and seasonal markings where applicable are to be determined under part 42 of this subchapter.
