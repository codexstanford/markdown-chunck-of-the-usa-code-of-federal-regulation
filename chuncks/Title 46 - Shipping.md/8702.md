
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.057-Floating Orange Smoke Distress Signals (15 Minutes)
###### § 160.057-3 Materials, workmanship, construction, and performance requirements.
####### Workmanship.

(b)Floating orange smoke distress signals shall be of first class workmanship and shall be free from imperfections of manufacture affecting their appearance or that may affect their serviceability.
