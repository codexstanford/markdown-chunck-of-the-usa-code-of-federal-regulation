
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Regulations Affecting Subsidized Vessels and Operators
#### PART 296 - MARITIME SECURITY PROGRAM (MSP)
##### Subpart D - Maritime Security Program Operating Agreements
###### ยง 296.31 MSP assistance conditions.
####### Term of MSP Operating Agreement.

(a)MSP Operating Agreements are authorized for 20 years, starting on October 1, 2005, and ending on September 30, 2025, but payments to Contractors are subject to annual appropriations each fiscal year. MARAD may enter into MSP Operating Agreements for a period less than the full term authorized under the MSA 2003, as amended.
