
# Title 46 - Shipping

##### Subpart B - Inspection and Certification
###### ยง 107.275 Other inspections.

When the Coast Guard receives the report required in ยง 109.411 or ยง 109.425, the Coast Guard conducts the following inspections of a unit to determine if the unit meets the requirements under which it was issued its original Certificate of Inspection:

(a) An inspection after an accident.

(b) An inspection after a defect is found that affects-

(1) The seaworthiness of the unit; or

(c) An inspection of repairs made because of an accident or a defect.
