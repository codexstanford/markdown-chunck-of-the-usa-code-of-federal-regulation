
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.017-Chain Ladder
###### § 160.017-11 Materials.
####### Wooden parts.

(c)Each wooden part of a ladder must be made of hardwood that is free of defects affecting its strength or durability.
