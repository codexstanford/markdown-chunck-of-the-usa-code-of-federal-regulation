
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 161-ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 161.006-Searchlights, Motor Lifeboat, for Merchant Vessels
###### § 161.006-5 Sampling, inspections and tests.
####### Corrosion resistance.

(3)The searchlight shall be subjected to a 200-hour salt spray test in accordance with ASTM B 117 (incorporated by reference, see § 161.006-1). There shall be no evidence of corrosion that will be detrimental to the operation of the searchlight.
