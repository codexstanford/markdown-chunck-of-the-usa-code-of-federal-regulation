
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter E - Load Lines
#### PART 45 - GREAT LAKES LOAD LINES
##### Subpart E - Unmanned River Barges on Lake Michigan Routes
###### ยง 45.195 Additional equipment requirements for the Muskegon route.
####### Cutting gear.

(b)Equipment that can quickly cut the towline at the towing vessel. The cutting gear must be in operable condition and appropriate for the type of towline being used, such as wire, polypropylene, or nylon. At least two persons aboard the vessel must be capable of using the cutting gear.
