
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter I - Cargo and Miscellaneous Vessels
#### PART 105 - COMMERCIAL FISHING VESSELS DISPENSING PETROLEUM PRODUCTS
##### ยง 105.5 Definitions.
###### Dispensing tank

means any tank from which a quantity of a flammable or combustible liquid is filled or emptied onboard the vessel by means of pumping, gravitation, or displacement.
