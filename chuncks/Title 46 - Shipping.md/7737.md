
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Certain Bulk Dangerous Cargoes
#### PART 154-SAFETY STANDARDS FOR SELF-PROPELLED VESSELS CARRYING BULK LIQUEFIED GASES
##### Subpart C-Design, Construction and Equipment
###### Cargo Containment Systems
####### § 154.401 Definitions.

As used in §§ 154.440 and 154.447:

"&#x3C3;" means the minimum yield strength of the tank material, including weld metal, at room temperature.

"&#x3C3;" means minimum tensile strength of the tank material, including weld metals, at room temperature.
