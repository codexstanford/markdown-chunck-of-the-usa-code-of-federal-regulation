
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.017-Chain Ladder
###### § 160.017-13 Construction.
####### Workmanship.

(i)A ladder must not have splinters, burrs, sharp edges, corners, projections, or other defects that could injure a person using the ladder.
