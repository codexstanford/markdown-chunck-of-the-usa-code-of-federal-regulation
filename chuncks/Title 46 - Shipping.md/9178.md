
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.133-Release Mechanisms for Lifeboats and Rescue Boats
###### § 160.133-15 Production inspections, tests, quality control, and conformance of release mechanisms.
####### Production inspections and tests.

(e)Each finished release mechanism must be visually inspected. The manufacturer must develop and maintain a visual inspection checklist designed to ensure that all applicable requirements have been met.
