
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.171-Immersion Suits
###### § 160.171-17 Approval testing for adult size immersion suit.
####### Test equipment.

(1)The following equipment is required for this test:

(i) A metal pan that is at least 300 mm (12 in.) wide, 450 mm (18 in.) long, and 60 mm (2in.) deep. The pan must have at least 12 mm (in.) of water on the bottom with approximately 40 mm (1in.) of gasoline floating on top of the water.

(ii) An arrangement to hold the suit over the gasoline.
