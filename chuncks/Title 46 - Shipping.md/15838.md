
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 530 - SERVICE CONTRACTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 530.5 Duty to file.
####### Registration,Application.

(c)-(1)Authority to file or delegate the authority to file must be requested by a responsible official of the service contract carrier in writing by submitting to BTA the Registration Form (FMC-83) in Exhibit 1 to this part.
