
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.051-Inflatable Liferafts for Domestic Service
###### § 160.051-7 Design and performance of Coastal Service inflatable liferafts.
####### Markings (IMO LSA Code, chapter IV/4.2.3.6).

(i)The words "COASTAL SERVICE" must appear on the container, and the type of equipment pack must be identified as "Coastal Service". No "SOLAS" markings may appear on the container.
