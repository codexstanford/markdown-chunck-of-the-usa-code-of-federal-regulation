
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Tank Vessels
#### PART 30 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 30.10 - Definitions
###### ยง 30.10-5a Cargo area - TB/ALL.
####### cargo area

The termmeans that part of a vessel that includes the cargo tanks and other tanks into which cargo or cargo vapors are intentionally introduced, holds containing these tanks, all intervening space within, between, below, or outboard of these tanks or holds, and the deck area over the length and beam of the vessel above these tanks, holds, or spaces.
