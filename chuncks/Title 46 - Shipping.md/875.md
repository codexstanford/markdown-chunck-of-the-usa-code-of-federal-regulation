
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Merchant Marine Officers and Seamen
#### PART 12 - REQUIREMENTS FOR RATING ENDORSEMENTS
##### Subpart D - Requirements for National Deck Rating Endorsements
###### ยง 12.409 General requirements for lifeboatman-limited endorsements.
####### General.

(a)Every person fulfilling the manning requirements for lifeboatman on any United States vessel fitted with liferafts, but not fitted with lifeboats, must hold an endorsement as lifeboatman or as lifeboatman-limited. No endorsement as lifeboatman or lifeboatman-limited is required of any person employed on any unrigged vessel, except on a seagoing barge and on a tank barge navigating waters other than rivers and/or canals.
