## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 189-INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION
##### Subpart 189.05-Permit To Proceed to Another Port for Repair

(a) Such permit will only be issued upon the written application of the master, owner, or agent of the vessel.
