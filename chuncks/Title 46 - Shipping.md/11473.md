
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 177-CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart D-Fire Protection
###### § 177.405 General arrangement and outfitting.
####### Separation of machinery and fuel tank spaces from accommodation spaces.

(c)Machinery and fuel tank spaces must be separated from accommodation spaces by boundaries that prevent the passage of vapors.
