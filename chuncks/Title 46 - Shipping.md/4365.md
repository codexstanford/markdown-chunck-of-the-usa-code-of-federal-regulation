
# Title 46 - Shipping

#### PART 90 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 90.10 - Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### ยง 90.10-36 Seagoing barge.
####### nonself-propelled vessel

A seagoing barge is a nonself-propelled vessel of at least 100 gross tons making voyages beyond the Boundary Line (as defined in 46 CFR part 7). The phrasemeans a vessel without sufficient means for self-propulsion and is required to be towed.
