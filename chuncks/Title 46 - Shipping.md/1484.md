
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Tank Vessels
#### PART 30 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 30.10 - Definitions
###### ยง 30.10-19a Control space - TB/ALL.
####### control space

The termmeans an enclosed space in which is located a ship's radio, main navigating equipment, or emergency source of power or in which is located centralized fire recording or fire control equipment, but not including firefighting apparatus that must be located in the cargo area or individual pieces of firefighting equipment.
