
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 520 - CARRIER AUTOMATED TARIFFS
##### § 520.2 Definitions.
###### Scope

means the location group(s) (geographic groupings(s)) listing the ports or ranges of ports to and from which the tariff's rates apply.
