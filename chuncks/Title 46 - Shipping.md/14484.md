##### ยง 390.11 Sale or other disposition of agreement vessels.
###### Deposit requirement.

(4)The Maritime Administrator will not normally require the deposit of the net proceeds from the sale of a qualified agreement vessel but shall require the deposit of the net proceeds from the mortgage of a qualified agreement vessel for which qualified withdrawals from the fund have been made.
