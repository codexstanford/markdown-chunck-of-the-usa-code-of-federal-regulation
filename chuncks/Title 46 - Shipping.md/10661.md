
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.100-General Provisions
###### § 169.107 Definitions.
####### Ship's Company

means the officers and crew of a sailing school vessel, sailing school students, and sailing school instructors.
