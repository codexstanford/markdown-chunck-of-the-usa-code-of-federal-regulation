
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Certain Bulk Dangerous Cargoes
#### PART 151-BARGES CARRYING BULK LIQUID HAZARDOUS MATERIAL CARGOES
##### Subpart 151.50-Special Requirements
###### ยง 151.50-86 Alkyl (C7-C9) nitrates.

(a) The carriage temperature of octyl nitrates must be maintained below 100 &#xB0;C (212 &#xB0;F) in order to prevent the occurrence of a self-sustaining exothermic decomposition reaction.

(b) Octyl nitrates may not be carried in a deck tank unless the tank has a combination of insulation and a water deluge system sufficient to maintain the tank's cargo temperature below 100 &#xB0;C (212 &#xB0;F) and the cargo temperature rise at or below 1.5 &#xB0;C(2.7 &#xB0;F)/hour, for a fire of 650 &#xB0;C (1200 &#xB0;F).
