
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter J - Electrical Engineering
#### PART 111 - ELECTRIC SYSTEMS - GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart 111.60 - Wiring Materials and Methods
###### ยง 111.60-4 Minimum cable conductor size.

Each cable conductor must be #18 AWG (0.82 mm) or larger except-

(a) Each power and lighting cable conductor must be #14 AWG (2.10 mm) or larger; and

(b) Each thermocouple, pyrometer, or instrumentation cable conductor must be #22 AWG (0.33 mm) or larger.
