
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter O - Certain Bulk Dangerous Cargoes
#### PART 153-SHIPS CARRYING BULK LIQUID, LIQUEFIED GAS, OR COMPRESSED GAS HAZARDOUS MATERIALS
##### Subpart B-Design and Equipment
###### Design and Equipment for Pollution Control
####### § 153.490 Cargo Record Book and Approved Procedures and Arrangements Manual: Categories A, B, C, and D.
######## Standards for Procedures and Arrangements for the Discharge of Noxious Liquid Substances,

(1) The standard format and content prescribed in Chapter 2 and Appendix D of the IMOResolution MEPC 18(22), 1985, or, for ships for which the only NLS carried is a Category D NLS and ships having a waiver under § 153.483 or § 153.491, the format and content prescribed by the Commandant (CG-ENG).

(2) If the ship has a tank that carries a cargo under a waiver issued under § 153.483, procedures ensuring that-

(i) Category B and C NLSs are discharged from the tank only in the ports or terminals listed in accordance with § 153.483(b); and

(ii) The tank is prewashed after discharging each Category B or C NLS unless § 153.1114 allows the prewash to be omitted.
