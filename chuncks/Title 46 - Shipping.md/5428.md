
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter J - Electrical Engineering
#### PART 111 - ELECTRIC SYSTEMS - GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart 111.75 - Lighting Circuits and Protection
###### ยง 111.75-5 Lighting branch circuits.
####### 25 or 30 ampere lighting branch circuits.

(e)Lighting branch circuits rated at 25 and 30 amperes supplying only fixed nonswitched lighting fixtures for cargo hold or deck lighting having only lampholders of the mogul type, or other lampholding devices required for lamps of more than 300 watts, may be supplied by a 30 ampere branch circuit wired with at least No. 10 AWG (5.3 mm) conductors if each fixture wire used in wiring each lighting fixture is No. 12 AWG (3.3 mm) or larger.
