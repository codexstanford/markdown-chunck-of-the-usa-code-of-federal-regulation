
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Regulations Affecting Subsidized Vessels and Operators
#### PART 296 - MARITIME SECURITY PROGRAM (MSP)
##### Subpart A - Introduction
###### ยง 296.2 Definitions.
####### Person

includes corporations, limited liability companies, partnerships, and associations existing under or authorized by the laws of the United States, or any State, Territory, District, or possession thereof, or of any foreign country. For purposes of holding an MSP Operating Agreement, the term "person" excludes a trust.
