
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 177-CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart D-Fire Protection
###### § 177.410 Structural fire protection.
####### Limitations on the use of general purpose resin,Overnight accommodations.

(d)-(1)Vessels with overnight passenger accommodations for more than 12 persons must not be constructed with general purpose resin.
