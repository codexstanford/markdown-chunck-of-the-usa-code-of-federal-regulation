
# Title 46 - Shipping

#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.077-Hybrid Inflatable Personal Flotation Devices
###### § 160.077-2 Definitions.
####### Hybrid PFD

(d)means a personal flotation device that has at least one inflation chamber in combination with inherently buoyant material.
