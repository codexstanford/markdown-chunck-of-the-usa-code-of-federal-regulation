
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Merchant Marine Officers and Seamen

##### Subpart E - Requirements for National Engineer Rating Endorsements
###### ยง 12.501 General requirements for a qualified member of the engine department (QMED).
####### General.

(a)A qualified member of the engine department is any person below officer and above the rating of coal passer or wiper, who holds an MMC or MMD endorsed as QMED by the Coast Guard.
