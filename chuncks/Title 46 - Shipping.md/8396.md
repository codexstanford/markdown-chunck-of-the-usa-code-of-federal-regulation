
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.042-Skids, Liferaft, for Merchant Vessels
###### § 160.042-3 Construction.

(a) The trackways of the skids shall be constructed of 6&#x2033; x 3&#x2033; x&#x2033; structural angles, or of material of approved shape and equivalent strength, inclined approximately 60 degrees from the horizontal. The trackways shall be spaced 8&#x2032;-4&#x2033; from the inside of the 3&#x2033; vertical leg of one trackway angle to the inside of the 3&#x2033; vertical leg of the other trackway angle. The inside of the 6&#x2033; leg of the trackway angles shall form the skid surface for the life raft. The trackways shall be supported by a substantial structure suitable for stowing a Type A life raft at a 60-degree angle without having the raft project over the side of the vessel.

(b) The lower end of the life raft shall be supported by a base plate so arranged as to permit launching of the raft by a quick release assembly.

(c) All bearing surfaces of the quick release mechanism shall be constructed of non-corrosive metal. Alemite fittings shall be provided to insure positive lubrication of all bearing surfaces.
