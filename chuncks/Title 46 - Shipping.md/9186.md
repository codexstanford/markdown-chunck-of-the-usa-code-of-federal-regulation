
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.135-Lifeboats
###### § 160.135-3 Definitions.
####### Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic (FRP)

means a composite structural material formed by electrical-grade glass fibers in Coast Guard accepted catalyst activated resin.
