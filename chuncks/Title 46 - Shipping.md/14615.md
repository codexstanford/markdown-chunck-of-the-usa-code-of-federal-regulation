
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter K - Regulations Under Public Law 91-469
#### PART 391 - FEDERAL INCOME TAX ASPECTS OF THE CAPITAL CONSTRUCTION FUND
##### ยง 391.3 Nontaxability of deposits.
###### Deposits of dividends.

(g)The deduction provided by section 243 of the Code (relating to the deductions for dividends from a domestic corporation received by a corporation) shall not apply in respect of dividends (earned on assets held in the fund) which are deposited into a fund, and which, by virtue of such deposits and the provisions of section 607(d) of the Act and this section, are not included in the gross income of the party.
