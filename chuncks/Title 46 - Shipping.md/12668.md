
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - Policy, Practice and Procedure
#### PART 201 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart M - Hearing Procedures (Rule 13)
###### ยง 201.132 Conduct of the hearing.
####### Cross-examination.

(e)(1)Cross-examination shall be limited to the scope of the direct examination and, except for Public Counsel and counsel for public bodies which have intervened as their interests may appear, to witnesses whose testimony is adverse to the party desiring to cross-examine-this being intended specifically to prohibit so-called "friendly cross-examination". Cross-examination, which is not necessary to test the truth and completeness of the direct testimony and exhibits, will not be permitted.
