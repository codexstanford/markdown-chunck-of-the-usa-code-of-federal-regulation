
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.037-Hand Orange Smoke Distress Signals
###### § 160.037-4 Approval and production tests.
####### Volume and density of smoke.

(8)The test specimen shall show less than 70 percent transmission for not less than 30 seconds when measured with apparatus having a light path of 19 cm (7in.), an optical system aperture of + 3.7 degrees, and an entrance air flow of 18.4mper minute (650 cu. ft. per minute), such apparatus to be as described in National Bureau of Standards Report No. 4792.
