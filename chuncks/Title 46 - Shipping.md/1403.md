##### Subpart E - Stability
###### ยง 28.510 Definition of stability terms.
####### Open boat

means a vessel not protected from entry of water by means of a complete deck, or by a combination of partial weather deck and superstructure which is seaworthy for the waters upon which the vessel operates.
