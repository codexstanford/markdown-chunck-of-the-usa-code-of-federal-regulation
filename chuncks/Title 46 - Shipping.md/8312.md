
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.036-Hand-Held Rocket-Propelled Parachute Red Flare Distress Signals
###### § 160.036-3 Materials, workmanship, construction and performance requirements.
####### Workmanship.

(b)Handheld rocket-propelled parachute red flare distress signals shall be of first class workmanship and shall be free from imperfections of manufacture affecting their appearance or that may affect their serviceability.
