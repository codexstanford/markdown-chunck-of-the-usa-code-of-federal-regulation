
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 189-INSPECTION AND CERTIFICATION
##### Subpart 189.20-Initial Inspection
###### § 189.20-1 Prerequisite of certificate of inspection.

(a) The initial inspection is a prerequisite of the issuance of the original certificate of inspection.
