
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Uninspected Vessels
#### PART 24 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 24.10 - Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### ยง 24.10-1 Definitions.
####### Passenger-for-hire

means a passenger for whom consideration is contributed as a condition of carriage on the vessel, whether directly or indirectly flowing to the owner, charterer, operator, agent, or any other person having an interest in the vessel.
