
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter I-A - National Shipping Authority
#### PART 327 - SEAMEN'S CLAIMS; ADMINISTRATIVE ACTION AND LITIGATION
##### Subpart A - Clarification Act Claims: Seamen's Claims; Administrative Action and Litigation.
###### § 327.3 Required claims submission.

All claims specified in 50 U.S.C. App. 1291(a) shall be submitted for administrative consideration, as provided in §§ 327.4 and 327.5, prior to institution of court action thereon.
