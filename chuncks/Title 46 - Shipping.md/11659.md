
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 183-ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION
##### Subpart C-Power Sources and Distribution Systems
###### § 183.354 Battery installations.
####### Small batteries.

(b)Each small battery installation must be located in a well ventilated space and protected from falling objects. A small battery installation must not be in a closet, storeroom or similar space.
