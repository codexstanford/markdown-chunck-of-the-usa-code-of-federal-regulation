
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.077-Hybrid Inflatable Personal Flotation Devices
###### § 160.077-11 Materials-Recreational Hybrid PFD's.
####### Temperature range.

(4)Unless otherwise specified in standards incorporated by reference in this section, all materials must be designed for use in all weather conditions throughout a temperature range of -30 &#xB0;C to + 65 &#xB0;C (-22 &#xB0;F to + 150 &#xB0;F).
