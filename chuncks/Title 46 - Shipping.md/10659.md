
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.100-General Provisions
###### § 169.107 Definitions.
####### Sailing School Student

means any person who is aboard a sailing school vessel for the purpose of receiving sailing instruction.
