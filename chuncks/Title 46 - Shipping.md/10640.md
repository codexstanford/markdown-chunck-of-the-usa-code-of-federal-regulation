
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 169-SAILING SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 169.100-General Provisions
###### § 169.107 Definitions.
####### Coast Guard District Commander

means an officer of the Coast Guard designated by the Commandant to command all Coast Guard activities within a district.
