
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S—Subdivision and Stability
#### PART 172-SPECIAL RULES PERTAINING TO BULK CARGOES
##### Subpart C-Special Rules Pertaining to a Barge That Carries a Cargo Regulated Under Subchapter D of This Chapter
###### § 172.050 Damage stability.
####### Type I barge hull not in an integrated tow.

(1)If a Type I hull is required and the barge is not a box barge designed for use in an integrated tow, design calculations must show that the barge hull can survive damage at any location including on the intersection of a transverse and longitudinal watertight bulkhead.
