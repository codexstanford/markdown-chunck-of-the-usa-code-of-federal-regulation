
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 64 - MARINE PORTABLE TANKS AND CARGO HANDLING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart B - Standards for an MPT
###### ยง 64.29 Tank saddles.

If a tank is not completely supported by a framework, it must be supported by two or more external saddles, each of which extends to 120 degrees or more of the shell circumference.
