#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.051-Inflatable Liferafts for Domestic Service
###### § 160.051-9 Design and performance of A and B inflatable liferafts.
####### Markings (IMO LSA Code, chapter IV/4.2.6.3).

(b)The type of equipment pack must be identified as "A" or "B", respectively, instead of "SOLAS A" or "SOLAS B". No "SOLAS" markings may appear on the container.
