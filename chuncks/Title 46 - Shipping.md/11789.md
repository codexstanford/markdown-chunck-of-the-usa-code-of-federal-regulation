
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 188-GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 188.10-Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### § 188.10-21 Compressed gas.

This term includes any material or mixture having in the container an absolute pressure exceeding 40 p.s.i. at 70 &#xB0;F.; or regardless of the pressure at 70 &#xB0;F., having an absolute pressure exceeding 104 p.s.i. at 130 &#xB0;F.; or any liquid flammable material having a vapor pressure exceeding 40 p.s.i. absolute at 100 &#xB0;F. as determined by the Reid method covered by the American Society for Testing Materials Method of Test for Vapor Pressure of Petroleum Products (D-323). Compressed gases are discussed in more detail in 49 CFR parts 171-179.
