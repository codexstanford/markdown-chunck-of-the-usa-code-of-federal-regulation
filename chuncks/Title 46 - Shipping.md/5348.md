
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter J - Electrical Engineering
#### PART 111 - ELECTRIC SYSTEMS - GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart 111.30 - Switchboards
###### ยง 111.30-19 Buses and wiring.
####### Wiring.

(b)Instrumentation and control wiring must be-

(1) Suitable for installation within in a switchboard enclosure and be rated at 90 &#xB0;C or higher;

(2) Stranded copper;

(3) No. 14 AWG (2.10 mm) or larger or must be ribbon cable or similar conductor size cable recommended for use in low-power instrumentation, monitoring, or control circuits by the equipment manufacturer;

(4) Flame-retardant meeting test VW-1 of UL 1581 or IEC 60332-1 (both incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1); and

(5) Extra flexible, if used on a hinged panel.
