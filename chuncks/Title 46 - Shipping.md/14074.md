
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter II - Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter I-B - Control and Utilization of Ports
#### PART 346 - FEDERAL PORT CONTROLLERS
##### Sec. 2 Definitions.
###### Federal Port Controller

(b)means a person designated as such under a standard form of service agreement to exercise delegated authorities of the Director, NSA, in the use of port facilities of a designated port or group of ports in connection with the deployment of the Armed Forces of the United States, or other requirements of the nation's defense.
