### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.060-Specification for a Buoyant Vest, Unicellular Polyethylene Foam, Adult and Child
###### § 160.060-6 Construction-nonstandard vests.
####### Adjustment, fit, and donning.

(2)The buoyant inserts from adult size buoyant vests shall provide not less than 15pounds of buoyancy in fresh water; the inserts from the child medium size buoyant vests shall provide not less than 11 pounds buoyancy; and the inserts from the child small size buoyant vests shall provide not less than 7 pounds buoyancy.
