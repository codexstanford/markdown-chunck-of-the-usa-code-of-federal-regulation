
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 64 - MARINE PORTABLE TANKS AND CARGO HANDLING SYSTEMS
##### Subpart B - Standards for an MPT
###### ยง 64.15 Allowable stress; framework.

The calculated stress for the framework must be 80 percent or less of the minimum yield stress of the framework material under the dynamic loading conditions that are applied simultaneously.
