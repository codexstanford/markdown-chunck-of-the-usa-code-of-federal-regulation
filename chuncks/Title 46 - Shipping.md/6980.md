
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter N - Dangerous Cargoes
#### PART 147A - INTERIM REGULATIONS FOR SHIPBOARD FUMIGATION
##### General
###### ยง 147A.7 Definitions.
####### Qualified person

(a)means a person who has experience with the particular fumigant or knowledge of its properties and is familiar with fumigant detection equipment and procedures, or an applicator who is certified by the Environmental Protection Agency if his certification covers the fumigant that is used.
