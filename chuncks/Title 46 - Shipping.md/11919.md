
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter U—Oceanographic Research Vessels
#### PART 190-CONSTRUCTION AND ARRANGEMENT
##### Subpart 190.07-Structural Fire Protection
###### § 190.07-5 Definitions.
####### A,A

(b)Class divisions. Bulkheads or decks of theClass shall be composed of steel or equivalent metal construction, suitably stiffened and made intact with the main structure of the vessel; such as shell, structural bulkheads, and decks. They shall be so constructed, that if subjected to the standard fire test, they would be capable of preventing the passage of flame and smoke for 1 hour.
