
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.017-Chain Ladder
###### § 160.017-13 Construction.
####### General.

(a)Each chain ladder must have two suspension members. Each step in the ladder must be supported at each end by a suspension member.
