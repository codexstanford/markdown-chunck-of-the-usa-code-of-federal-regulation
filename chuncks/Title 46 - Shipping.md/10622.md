
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 168-CIVILIAN NAUTICAL SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 168.10-Definitions of Terms Used in This Part
###### § 168.10-5 Civilian nautical school.
####### civilian nautical school

The termmeans any school or branch thereof operated and conducted in the United States, except State nautical schools and schools operated by the United States or any agency thereof, which offers instruction for the primary purpose of training for service in the merchant marine.
