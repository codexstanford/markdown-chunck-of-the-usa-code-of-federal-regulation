
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.015-Plastic Foam, Unicellular, Buoyant, Sheet and Molded Shape
###### § 164.015-4 Inspections and tests.
####### Flexibility

(j)-(1) The size of the specimen shall be approximately 1&#x2032; x 8&#x2032; with a thickness of&#x2032; +/-&#x2033;. The test specimens and equipment shall be conditioned for at least 4 hours at 0 &#xB0;F. +/-2 &#xB0;F., and bent 180&#xB0; around a&#x2033; diameter steel mandrel within 5 seconds at the test temperature. Care shall be taken to avoid warming the test specimens, particularly at or near the bend point, in performing the test.
