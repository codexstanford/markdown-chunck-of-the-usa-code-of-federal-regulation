
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 160-LIFESAVING EQUIPMENT
##### Subpart 160.062-Releases. Lifesaving Equipment, Hydraulic and Manual
###### § 160.062-4 Inspections and tests.
####### Visual and dimensional examination.

(1)The marine inspector shall examine the 4 hydraulic release samples of the preapproval sample for their visual and dimensional characteristics. If all 4 of the devices are in agreement with the manufacturer's plans previously reviewed by the Commandant, the 4 devices will be accepted and are to be assembled for further testing under the "Physical and operational tests" of paragraph (c)(2) of this section.
