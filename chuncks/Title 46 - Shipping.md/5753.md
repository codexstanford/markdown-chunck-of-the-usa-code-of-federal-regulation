
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security

##### ยง 114.400 Definitions of terms used in this subchapter.
###### Rivers

means a route on any of the following waters:

(1) A river;

(2) A canal; or

(3) Such other similar waters as may be designated by a Coast Guard District Commander.
