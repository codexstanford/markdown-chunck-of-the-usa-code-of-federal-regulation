### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval

##### Subpart 164.019-Personal Flotation Device Components
###### § 164.019-13 Production quality control requirements.
####### Inspection responsibility.

(2)The manufacturer shall ensure that the inspection required by paragraph (h)(1) of this section is performed by a manufacturer's representative familiar with the performance requirements for the component, and all of the production quality control requirements. The manufacturer's representative must not be responsible for meeting production schedules, or be subject to supervision by someone responsible for meeting production schedules.
