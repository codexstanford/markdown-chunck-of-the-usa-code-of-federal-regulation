
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter T—Small Passenger Vessels (Under 100 Gross Tons)
#### PART 185-OPERATIONS
##### Subpart C-Miscellaneous Operating Requirements
###### § 185.350 Fueling of vessels using fuel having a flash point of 43.3 &#xB0;C (110 &#xB0;F) or lower (such as gasoline).

A vessel must not take on fuel having a flash point of 43.3 &#xB0;C (110 &#xB0;F) or lower when passengers are on board.
