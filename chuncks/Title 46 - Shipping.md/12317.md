#### PART 197-GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart C-Benzene
###### Appendix B to Subpart C of Part 197-Substance Technical Guidelines, Benzene
####### Waste disposal method.

(b)Disposal methods must conform to state and local regulations. If allowed, benzene may be disposed of (a) by absorbing it in dry sand or earth and disposing in a sanitary landfill, (b), if in small quantities, by removing it to a safe location away from buildings or other combustible sources or by pouring onto dry sand or earth and cautiously igniting it, and (c), if in large quantities, by atomizing it in a suitable combustion chamber.
