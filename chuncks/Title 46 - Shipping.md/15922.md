
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 531-NVOCC SERVICE ARRANGEMENTS
##### Subpart D-Exceptions and Implementation
###### § 531.10 Excepted and exempted commodities.
####### Department of Defense cargo.

(2)Transportation of U.S. Department of Defense cargo moving in foreign commerce under terms and conditions approved by the Military Transportation Management Command and published in a universal service contract. An exact copy of the universal service contract, including any amendments thereto, shall be filed with the Commission as soon as it becomes available.
