
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.009-Noncombustible Materials for Merchant Vessels
###### § 164.009-15 Test procedure.
####### Conditioning of specimen.

(c)Each specimen is conditioned for at least 20 hours in a ventilated oven maintained at 60 +/-5 &#xB0;C. and is then cooled to room temperature in a desiccator.
