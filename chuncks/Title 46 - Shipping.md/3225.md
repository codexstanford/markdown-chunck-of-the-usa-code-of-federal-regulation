
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 61 - PERIODIC TESTS AND INSPECTIONS
##### Subpart 61.30 - Tests and Inspections of Fired Thermal Fluid Heaters
###### ยง 61.30-1 Scope.
####### thermal fluid heater

The termas used in this part includes any fired automatic auxiliary heating unit which uses a natural or synthetic fluid in the liquid phase as the heat exchange medium and whose operating temperature and pressure do not exceed 204 &#xB0;C (400 &#xB0;F) and 225 psig, respectively. Thermal fluid heaters having operating temperatures and pressures higher than 204 &#xB0;C (400 &#xB0;F) and 225 psig, respectively, are inspected under subpart 61.05-Tests and Inspections of Boilers.
