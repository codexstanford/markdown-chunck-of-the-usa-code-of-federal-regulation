
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter F - Marine Engineering
#### PART 52 - POWER BOILERS
##### Subpart 52.01 - General Requirements
###### ยง 52.01-3 Definitions of terms used in this part.
####### Fusible plugs.

(11)Fusible plugs are plugs made with a bronze casing and a tin filling which melts at a temperature of 445&#xB0; to 450 &#xB0;F. They are intended to melt in the event of low water and thus warn the engineer on watch.
