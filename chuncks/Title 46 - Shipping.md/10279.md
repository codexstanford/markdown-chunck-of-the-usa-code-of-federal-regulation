
# Title 46 - Shipping

### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.019-Personal Flotation Device Components
###### § 164.019-3 Definitions.
####### Inspector

means a Coast Guard marine inspector, authorized representative of the Coast Guard, or a recognized laboratory representative.
