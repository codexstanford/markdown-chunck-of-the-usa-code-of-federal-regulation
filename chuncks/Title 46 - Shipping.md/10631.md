
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter R—Nautical Schools
#### PART 168-CIVILIAN NAUTICAL SCHOOL VESSELS
##### Subpart 168.15-Accommodations
###### § 168.15-40 Lighting.

All quarters, including washrooms, toilet rooms, and hospital spaces, must be adequately lighted.
