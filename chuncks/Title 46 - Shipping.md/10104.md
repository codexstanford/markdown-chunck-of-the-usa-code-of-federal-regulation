
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 163-CONSTRUCTION
##### Subpart 163.003-Pilot Ladder
###### § 163.003-13 Construction.
####### General.

(a)Each pilot ladder must have two suspension members on each side. Each step in the ladder must be supported by each suspension member.
