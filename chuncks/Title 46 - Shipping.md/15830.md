
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 530 - SERVICE CONTRACTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### § 530.3 Definitions.
####### Motor vehicle

(m)means a wheeled vehicle whose primary purpose is ordinarily the non-commercial transportation of passengers, including an automobile, pickup truck, minivan or sport utility vehicle.
