
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter S—Subdivision and Stability
#### PART 170-STABILITY REQUIREMENTS FOR ALL INSPECTED VESSELS
##### Subpart B-Definitions
###### § 170.055 Definitions concerning a vessel.
####### Length on deck (LOD)

(4)means the length between the forward-most and after-most points on a specified deck measured along the deck, excluding sheer.
