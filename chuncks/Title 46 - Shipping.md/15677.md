
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter IV - Federal Maritime Commission
### Subchapter B—Regulations Affecting Ocean Shipping in Foreign Commerce
#### PART 520 - CARRIER AUTOMATED TARIFFS
##### § 520.2 Definitions.
###### Trade name

means a name used for conducting business, but which is not necessarily its legal name. This is also known as a "d/b/a" (doing business as) name.
