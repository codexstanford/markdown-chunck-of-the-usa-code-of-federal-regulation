
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter H - Passenger Vessels
#### PART 70 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart 70.10 - Definition of Terms Used in This Subchapter
###### ยง 70.10-1 Definitions.
####### Carrying freight for hire

means the carriage of any goods, wares, or merchandise, or any other freight for a consideration, whether directly or indirectly flowing to the owner, charterer, operator, agent, or any other person interested in the vessel.
