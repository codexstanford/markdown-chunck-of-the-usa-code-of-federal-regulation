
# Title 46 - Shipping
## Chapter I - Coast Guard, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter Q—Equipment, Construction, and Materials: Specifications and Approval
#### PART 164-MATERIALS
##### Subpart 164.019-Personal Flotation Device Components
###### § 164.019-3 Definitions.
####### Acceptance

means certification by the Coast Guard that a component is suitable for use in the manufacture of Coast Guard-approved PFDs.
