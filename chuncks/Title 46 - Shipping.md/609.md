####### No-fee MMC for certain applicants.

(h)For the purpose of this section, a no-fee MMC applicant is a person who is a volunteer or a part- or full-time employee of an organization that is-

(1) Charitable in nature;

(2) Not for profit; and

(3) Youth oriented.
