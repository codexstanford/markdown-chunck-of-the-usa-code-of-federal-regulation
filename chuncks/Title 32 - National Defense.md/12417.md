
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### ยง 623.3 Submission of requests for loan of Army materiel.
####### Loans/Leases under the provisions of Title 10 U.S.C. 2667.

(3)Requests for loans from other civil activities and organizations may come into the DOD through various channels; e.g., telephone call to local installation commander, letter to Congressmen, or directly to the Secretary of Defense or Army. Each request will be forwarded to the authority having the item and having the authority to approve the request. (See appendix B and table 2-1.) In cases where approval is questionable, the request may be submitted through channels to HQDA (DALO-SMD) WASH DC 20310 (para 2, app B) recommending approval/disapproval action.
