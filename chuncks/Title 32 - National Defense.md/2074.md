
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian
##### PART 79 - CHILD DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS (CDPs)
###### ยง 79.3 Definitions.
####### Individual with a disability.

A handicapped person as defined in 32 CFR part 56, in accordance with 29 U.S.C. 705, also known as "Section 7 of The Rehabilitation Act of 1973," as amended, and consistent with 42 U.S.C. 12102, also known as "The Americans with Disabilities Act, as amended". Synonymous with the phrase "person with a disability."
