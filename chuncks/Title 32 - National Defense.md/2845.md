
# Title 32 - National Defense

### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian

###### ยง 117.3 Acronyms and Definitions.
####### United States and its territorial areas

means the 50 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, Guam, American Samoa, the Virgin Islands, Wake Island, Johnston Atoll, Kingman Reef, Palmyra Atoll, Baker Island, Howland Island, Jarvis Island, Midway Islands, Navassa Island, and Northern Mariana Islands.
