
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter N - Freedom of Information Act Program
##### PART 286h - RELEASE OF ACQUISITION-RELATED INFORMATION
###### ยง 286h.3 Policy.
####### Classified information.

(2)(i) Any information or material, regardless of its physical form or characteristics, that is owned by, produced by or for, or under the control of the United States Government, and which, for national security purposes, must be protected against unauthorized disclosure and is so designated or marked with the appropriate classification.

(ii) Release, access, and dissemination of classified information shall be made through existing security channels in accordance with DoD 5220.22-R;DoD 5220.22-M;and DoD 5200.1-R,which are implementing publications for safeguarding classified information release, access, and dissemination to United States and foreign concerns.
