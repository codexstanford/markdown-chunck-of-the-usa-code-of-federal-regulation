
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter E - Regulations Pertaining to Military Justice
##### PART 152 - REVIEW OF THE MANUAL FOR COURTS-MARTIAL
###### Appendix A to Part 152 - Guidance to the Joint Service Committee (JSCA)
####### Internal Rules and Record-Keeping.

(E)(1) In furthering DoD policy, studying issues, or performing other duties relating to the administration of military justice, the JSC may establish internal rules governing its operation.

(2) The JSC shall create a file system and maintain appropriate JSC records.
