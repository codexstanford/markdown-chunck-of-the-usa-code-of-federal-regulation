
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### ยง 199.4 Basic program benefits.
####### Biofeedback Therapy.

(17)Biofeedback therapy is a technique by which a person is taught to exercise control over a physiologic process occurring within the body. By using modern biomedical instruments the patient learns how a specific physiologic system within his body operates and how to modify the performance of this particular system.
