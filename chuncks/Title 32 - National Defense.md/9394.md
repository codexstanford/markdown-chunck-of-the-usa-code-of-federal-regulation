
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### Subpart B - Reutilization, Transfer, and Sale of Property
####### ยง 273.12 Definitions.
######## Zone of interior (ZI).

The United States and its territories and possessions, applicable to areas covered by GSA and where excess property is considered domestic excess. Includes the 50 States, District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, American Samoa, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands, and the U.S. Virgin Islands.
