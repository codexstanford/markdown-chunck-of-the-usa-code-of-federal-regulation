
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter E - Regulations Pertaining to Military Justice
##### PART 150 - COURTS OF CRIMINAL APPEALS RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
###### ยง 150.2 Jurisdiction.
####### Review under Article 66.

(1)All cases of trial by court-martial in which the sentence as approved extends to:

(i) Death; or

(ii) Dismissal of a commissioned officer, cadet or midshipman, dishonorable or bad-conduct discharge, or confinement for 1 year or longer; and in which the accused has not waived or withdrawn appellate review.
