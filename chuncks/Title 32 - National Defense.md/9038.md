
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 269 - CIVIL MONETARY PENALTY INFLATION ADJUSTMENT
###### ยง 269.5 Application of increase to violations.
####### i.e.,

Any increase in a civil monetary penalty under this part must apply only to civil monetary penalties, including those whose associated violation predated such increase, which are assessed after the date the increase takes effect (July 1, 2016).
