###### ยง 103.3 Definitions.
####### Personal Identifiable Information.,e.g.,,e.g.,

Includes the person's name, other particularly identifying descriptions (physical characteristics or identity by position, rank, or organization), or other information about the person or the facts and circumstances involved that could reasonably be understood to identify the person (a female in a particular squadron or barracks when there is only one female assigned).
