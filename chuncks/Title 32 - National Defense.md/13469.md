
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter VI - Department of the Navy
#### Subchapter E - Claims
##### PART 751 - PERSONNEL CLAIMS REGULATIONS
###### Subpart A - Claims Against the United States
####### ยง 751.10 Form of claim.

The claim should be submitted on DD Form 1842 (Claim for Personal Property) accompanied by DD Form 1844 (List of Property). If DD Forms 1842 and 1844are not available, any writing will be accepted and considered if it asserts a demand for a specific sum and substantially describes the facts necessary to support a claim cognizable under these regulations. The claim must be signed by a proper claimant (see ยง 751.5) or by a person with a power of attorney for a proper claimant. A copy of the power of attorney must be included with the claim.
