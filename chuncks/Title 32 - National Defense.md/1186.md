
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian
##### PART 57 - PROVISION OF EARLY INTERVENTION AND SPECIAL EDUCATION SERVICES TO ELIGIBLE DOD DEPENDENTS
###### ยง 57.6 Procedures.
####### Responses and actions required following receipt of a petition or request for expedited hearing.

(6)(i) Immediately upon receipt of the petition, the Director, DOHA, shall appoint a hearing officer to take charge of the case.

(A) The hearing officer shall immediately notify the parties of his or her appointment.

(B) Upon receipt of notice that a hearing officer is appointed, the parties shall communicate all motions, pleadings, or amendments in writing to the hearing officer, with a copy to the opposing party, unless the hearing officer directs otherwise.
