
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 236 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DoD) - DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL BASE (DIB) CYBER SECURITY (CS) ACTIVITIES
###### ยง 236.2 Definitions.
####### Threat

means any circumstance or event with the potential to adversely impact organization operations (including mission, functions, image, or reputation), organization assets, individuals, other organizations, or the Nation through an information system via unauthorized access, destruction, disclosure, modification of information and/or denial of service.
