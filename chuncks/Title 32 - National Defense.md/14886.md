
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to National Defense
### Chapter XVI - Selective Service System
#### PART 1645 - CLASSIFICATION OF MINISTERS OF RELIGION
##### ยง 1645.1 Purpose; definitions.
###### regular minister of religion

(2) The termmeans one who as his customary vocation preaches and teaches the principles of religion of a church, a religious sect, or organization of which he is a member, without having been formally ordained as a minister of religion, and who is recognized by such church, sect, or organization as a regular minister.
