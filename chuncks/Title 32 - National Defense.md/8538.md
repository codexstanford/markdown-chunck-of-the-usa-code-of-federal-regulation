
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 236 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DoD) - DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL BASE (DIB) CYBER SECURITY (CS) ACTIVITIES
###### ยง 236.4 Mandatory cyber incident reporting procedures.
####### Media preservation and protection.

(i)When a contractor discovers a cyber incident has occurred, the contractor shall preserve and protect images of known affected information systems identified in paragraph (b) of this section and all relevant monitoring/packet capture data for at least 90 days from submission of the cyber incident report to allow DoD to request the media or decline interest.
