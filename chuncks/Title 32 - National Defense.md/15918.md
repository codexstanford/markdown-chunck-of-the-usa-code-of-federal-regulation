
# Title 32 - National Defense

### Chapter XIX - Central Intelligence Agency

##### ยง 1909.2 Definitions.
###### Former Presidential or Vice Presidential appointee or designee

means any person who has previously occupied a senior policy-making position in the Executive branch of the United States Government to which they were appointed or designated by the current or a former President or Vice President.
