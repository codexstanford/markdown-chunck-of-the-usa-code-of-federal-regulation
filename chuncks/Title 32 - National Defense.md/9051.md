
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### Subpart A - Disposal Guidance and Procedures
####### ยง 273.3 Definitions.
######## Commercial off the shelf (COTS) software.

Software that is available through lease or purchase in the commercial market. Included in COTS are the operating system software that runs on the information technology equipment and other significant software purchased with a license that supports system or customer requirements.
