
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 220 - COLLECTION FROM THIRD PARTY PAYERS OF REASONABLE CHARGES FOR HEALTHCARE SERVICES
###### ยง 220.11 Special rules for automobile liability insurance and no-fault automobile insurance.
####### Effect of concurrent applicability of the Federal Medical Care Recovery Act,In general.,et seq.

(b)-(1)In many cases covered by this section, the United States has a right to collect under both 10 U.S.C. 1095 and the Federal Medical Care Recovery Act (FMCRA), Pub. L. 87-693 (42 U.S.C. 2651). In such cases, the authority is concurrent and the United States may pursue collection under both statutory authorities.
