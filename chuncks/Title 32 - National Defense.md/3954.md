###### Subpart A - Identification (ID) Cards for Members of the Uniformed Services, Their Dependents, and Other Eligible Individuals
####### ยง 161.3 Definitions.
######## Intergovernmental Personnel Act personnel.

Employees covered by Public Law 91-648, "Intergovernmental Personnel Act of 1970." The Intergovernmental Personnel Act mobility program provides temporary assignment of personnel between the Federal Government and State and local governments, colleges and universities, tribal governments, federally funded research and development centers, and other eligible organizations.
