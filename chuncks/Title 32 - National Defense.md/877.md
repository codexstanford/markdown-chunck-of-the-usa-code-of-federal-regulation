
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### ยง 57.3 Definitions.
####### FAPE.

Special education and related services that are provided under the general supervision and direction of DoDEA at no cost to parents of a child with a disability, in conformity with an IEP, in accordance with the requirements of the IDEA and DoD guidance.
