
# Title 32 - National Defense

### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense

####### Commingling.

(9)Commingling of RD, FRD, and TFNI with NSI in the same document should be avoided to the greatest degree possible. When mixing this information cannot be avoided, the marking requirements in 10 CFR part 1045, section 140(f) and declassification requirements of 10 CFR part 1045, section 155 apply.
