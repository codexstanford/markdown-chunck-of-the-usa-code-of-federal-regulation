
# Title 32 - National Defense

#### Subchapter O - Privacy Program
##### PART 339 - DOD GUIDANCE DOCUMENTS
###### ยง 339.10 Petitions for guidance.
####### osd.mc-alex.ocmo.mbx.guidance-documents@mail.mil

(a) Any person may petition the Department to withdraw or modify a particular guidance document by sending a written request to the DoD Regulatory Program staff at email address. Please use the words "GUIDANCE: [Insert the title of the guidance document]" in the subject line of the email message. The DoD Regulatory Program staff will provide the request to the issuing component of the guidance document for response.
