means an award of financial assistance that, consistent with 31 U.S.C. 6305, is used to enter into the same kind of relationship as a grant (see definition of grant in ยง 26.650), except that substantial involvement is expected between the Federal agency and the recipient when carrying out the activity contemplated by the award.
