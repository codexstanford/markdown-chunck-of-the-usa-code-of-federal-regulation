
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter V - Department of the Army
#### Subchapter H - Supplies and Equipment
##### PART 623 - LOAN OF ARMY MATERIEL
###### Appendix A to Part 623 - Explanation of Terms
####### a.

Minimize the effects upon the civilian population caused, or which would be caused, by an enemy attack upon the United States.
