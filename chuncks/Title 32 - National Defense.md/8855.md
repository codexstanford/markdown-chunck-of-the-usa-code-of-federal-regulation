###### Appendix A to Part 247 - Funded Newspapers and Magazines
####### Name.

B.The name of the publication may include the name of the command or installation, or, the name of the command or installation may appear separately in the nameplate (flag). The emblem of the command or installation may be included in the nameplate, also.
