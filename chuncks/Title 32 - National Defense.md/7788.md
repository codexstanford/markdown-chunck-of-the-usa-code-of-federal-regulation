
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 211 - MISSION COMPATIBILITY EVALUATION PROCESS
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 211.3 Definitions.
######## Adverse impact on military operations and readiness.

Any adverse impact upon military operations and readiness, including flight operations research, development, testing, and evaluation and training, that is demonstrable and is likely to impair or degrade the ability of the armed forces to perform their warfighting missions.
