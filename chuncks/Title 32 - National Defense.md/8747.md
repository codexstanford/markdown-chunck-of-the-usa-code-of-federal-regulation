###### Subpart B - Explanation of Terms, Acronyms and Abbreviations
####### ยง 245.5 Terms.
######## National emergency.

A condition declared by the President or the Congress by virtue of powers previously vested in them that authorize certain emergency actions to be undertaken in the national interest. Actions to be taken may include partial, full, or total mobilization of national resources.
