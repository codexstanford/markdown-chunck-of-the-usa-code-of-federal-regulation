
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 236 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DoD) - DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL BASE (DIB) CYBER SECURITY (CS) ACTIVITIES
###### ยง 236.2 Definitions.
####### Cyber incident damage assessment

means a managed, coordinated process to determine the effect on defense programs, defense scientific and research projects, or defense warfighting capabilities resulting from compromise of a contractor's unclassified computer system or network.
