
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian
##### PART 117 - NATIONAL INDUSTRIAL SECURITY PROGRAM OPERATING MANUAL (NISPOM)
###### ยง 117.14 Marking requirements.
####### Marking U.S. documents containing FGI.,e.g.,

(n)(1) U.S. documents containing FGI must be marked on the front, "THIS DOCUMENT CONTAINS (indicate country of origin) INFORMATION." In addition, the portions must be marked to identify both the country and classification level, ((UK-C), (GE-C)). The "Derived From" line will identify U.S. as well as foreign classification sources.
