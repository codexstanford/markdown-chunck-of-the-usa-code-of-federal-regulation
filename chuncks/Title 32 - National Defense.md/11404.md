
# Title 32 - National Defense

####### ยง 536.16 Release of information policies.
######## Conflict of interest.

(a)Except as part of their official duties, government personnel are forbidden from advising or representing claimants or from receiving any payment or gratuity for services rendered. They may not accept any share or interest in a claim or assist in its presentation, under penalty of federal criminal law (18 U.S.C. 203 and 205).
