
# Title 32 - National Defense

### Chapter V - Department of the Army
#### Subchapter B - Claims and Accounts

###### Subpart I - Claims Cognizable Under Article 139, Uniform Code of Military Justice
####### ยง 536.126 Purpose of UCMJ claims.

This subpart sets forth the standards to apply and the procedures to follow in processing claims for the wrongful taking or willful damage or destruction of property by military members of the Department of the Army.
