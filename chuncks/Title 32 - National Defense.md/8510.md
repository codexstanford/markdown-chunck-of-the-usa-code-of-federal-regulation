
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 236 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DoD) - DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL BASE (DIB) CYBER SECURITY (CS) ACTIVITIES
###### ยง 236.2 Definitions.
####### Controlled Technical Information,http://www.dtic.mil/whs/directives/corres/pdf/523024p.pdf

means technical information with military or space application that is subject to controls on the access, use, reproduction, modification, performance, display, release, disclosure, or dissemination. Controlled technical information would meet the criteria, if disseminated, for distribution statements B through F using the criteria set forth in DoD Instruction 5230.24, "Distribution Statements of Technical Documents," available at. The term does not include information that is lawfully publicly available without restrictions.
