###### ยง 3.4 Definitions.
####### Qualified Independent Public Accountant.

An accountant that is licensed or works for a firm that is licensed in the state or other political jurisdiction where they operate their professional practice and comply with the applicable provisions of the public accountancy law and rules of the jurisdiction where the audit is being conducted.
