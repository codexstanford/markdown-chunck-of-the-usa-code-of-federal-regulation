
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 200 - CIVIL MONEY PENALTY AUTHORITIES FOR THE TRICARE PROGRAM
###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 200.110 Definitions.
######## Preventive care,

for purposes of the definition of the term "remuneration" as set forth in this section and the preventive care exception to section 231(h) of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA), means any service that-

(1) Is a prenatal service or a post-natal well-baby visit or is a specific clinical service covered by TRICARE; and

(2) Is reimbursable in whole or in part by TRICARE as a preventive care service.
