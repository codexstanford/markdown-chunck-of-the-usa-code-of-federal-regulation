
# Title 32 - National Defense

### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter E - Regulations Pertaining to Military Justice
##### PART 150 - COURTS OF CRIMINAL APPEALS RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
###### ยง 150.2 Jurisdiction.
####### Review upon direction of the Judge Advocate General under Article 69.

(2)All cases of trial by court-martial in which there has been a finding of guilty and a sentence:

(i) For which Article 66 does not otherwise provide appellate review, and
