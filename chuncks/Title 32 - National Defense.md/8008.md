
# Title 32 - National Defense

#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 221 - DOD IDENTITY MANAGEMENT
###### ยง 221.3 Definitions.
####### Former member.

An individual who is eligible for, or entitled to, retired pay for non-regular service in accordance with 31 U.S.C. chapter 1223, but who has been discharged from the Service and who maintains no military affiliation.
