
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter V - Department of the Army
#### Subchapter A - Aid of Civil Authorities and Public Relations
##### PART 516 - LITIGATION
###### Subpart E - Legal Proceedings Initiated by the United States Medical Care and Property Claims
####### Assertion of Other Claims
######## ยง 516.37 Proceedings to repossess government real property or quarters or to collect delinquent rent.
######### General.

(a)U.S. Attorneys are authorized to accept a Federal agency's request for the following purposes: to initiate an action to recover possession of real property from tenants, trespassers, and others; to enjoin trespasses on Federal property; and, to collect delinquent rentals or damages for use and occupancy of real property for amounts less than $200,000.
