
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### ยง 57.3 Definitions.
####### Qualified.

A person who meets the DoD-approved or recognized certification, licensing, or registration requirements or other comparable requirements in the area in which the person provides evaluation or assessment, EIS, special education or related services to an infant, toddler, or child with a disability.
