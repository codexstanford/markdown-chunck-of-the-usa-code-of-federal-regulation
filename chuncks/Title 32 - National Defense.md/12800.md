### Chapter VI - Department of the Navy

###### ยง 705.23 Guest cruises.
####### Secretary of the Navy Guest Cruise and Guest of the Navy Cruise Programs.

(c)(1) The objective of these two programs is: To expose top-level and middle-level opinion leaders in the fields of business, industry, science, education, and labor to the operation of the U.S. Navy, in order that they may gain a better understanding of its capabilities and problems, the complicated nature of modern sea-based equipment, and the high levels of responsibility and training required of Navy men and women.
