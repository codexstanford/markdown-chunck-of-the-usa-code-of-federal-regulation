
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter V - Department of the Army
#### Subchapter D - Military Reservations and National Cemeteries
##### PART 553 - ARMY CEMETERIES
###### Subpart B - Army Post Cemeteries
####### § 553.36 Definitions.
######## Primarily eligible person.

Any person who is entitled to interment or inurnment based on his or her service as specified in §§ 553.39 through 553.41.
