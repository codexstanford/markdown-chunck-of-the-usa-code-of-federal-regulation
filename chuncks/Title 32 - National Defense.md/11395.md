
# Title 32 - National Defense

####### ยง 536.15 Claims policies.
######## Notice to claimants of technical errors in claim.

(5)When technical errors are found in a claim's filing or contents, claimants should be advised of such errors and the need to correct the claim. If the errors concern a jurisdictional matter, a record should be maintained and the claimant should be immediately warned that the error must be corrected before the statute of limitations (SOL) expires.
