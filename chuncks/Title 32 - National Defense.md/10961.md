
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter O - Privacy Program
##### PART 339 - DOD GUIDANCE DOCUMENTS
###### ยง 339.13 Reports to Congress and GAO.

Upon the issuance of a final guidance document, the appropriate Federal Register Liaison Officer will submit a report to Congress and GAO in accordance with the procedures described in 5 U.S.C. 801 (the "Congressional Review Act"). If the CRA procedures are not followed, the guidance document can be nullified.
