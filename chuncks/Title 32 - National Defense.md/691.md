
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian
##### PART 47 - ACTIVE DUTY SERVICE FOR CIVILIAN OR CONTRACTUAL GROUPS
###### ยง 47.3 Definitions.
####### Recognized group.

A group whose service the Secretary of the Air Force administratively has determined to have been "active duty for the purposes of all laws administered by the Department of Veterans Affairs"; i.e., VA benefits under 38 U.S.C. 101.
