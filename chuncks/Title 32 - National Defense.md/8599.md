
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 239 - HOMEOWNERS ASSISTANCE PROGRAM - APPLICATION PROCESSING
###### ยง 239.6 Eligibility.
####### Eligibility based on economic impact, timing, price, orders, and submission of application.,Minimum economic impact.

(b)(1)(i) BRAC 2005 Members and Civilian Employees as well as permanently reassigned members of the Armed Forces whose primary residence have suffered at least a 10 percent personal home value loss from the date of purchase to date of sale. Market value of the home will be verified by the USACE.

(ii) Applicants qualifying as Wounded, Injured, or Ill or as surviving spouse do not need to show minimum economic impact.
