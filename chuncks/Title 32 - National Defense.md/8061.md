
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 222 - DOD MANDATORY DECLASSIFICATION REVIEW (MDR) PROGRAM
###### ยง 222.3 Definitions.
####### Formal Control System.

A system designed to ensure DoD Component accountability and compliance. For each MDR request, the system shall contain, at a minimum, a unique tracking number, requester's name and organizational affiliation, information requested, date of receipt, and date of closure.
