
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter M - Miscellaneous
##### PART 236 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE (DoD) - DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL BASE (DIB) CYBER SECURITY (CS) ACTIVITIES
###### ยง 236.2 Definitions.
####### Operationally critical support

means supplies or services designated by the Government as critical for airlift, sealift, intermodal transportation services, or logistical support that is essential to the mobilization, deployment, or sustainment of the Armed Forces in a contingency operation.
