
# Title 32 - National Defense

##### PART 243 - DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE RATEMAKING PROCEDURES FOR CIVIL RESERVE AIR FLEET CONTRACTS
###### ยง 243.3 Definitions.
####### Historical Costs.

Those allowable costs for airlift services for a 12 month period, gathered from Department of Transportation (DOT) Uniform System of Accounts and Reports (USAR) (hereinafter referred to as "Form 41") reporting (required by 14 CFR parts 217 and 241).
