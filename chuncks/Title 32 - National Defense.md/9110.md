
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### Subpart A - Disposal Guidance and Procedures
####### ยง 273.3 Definitions.
######## Nonsalable materiel.

Materiel that has no reutilization, transfer, donation, or sale value as determined by the DLA Disposition Services site, but is not otherwise restricted from disposal by U.S. law or Federal or military regulations.
