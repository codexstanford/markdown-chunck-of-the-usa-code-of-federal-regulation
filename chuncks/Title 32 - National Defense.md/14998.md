
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle B - Other Regulations Relating to National Defense
### Chapter XVI - Selective Service System
#### PART 1662 - FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT (FOIA) PROCEDURES
##### ยง 1662.6 Fee schedule; waiver of fees.
###### Fees for nonproductive search.

(5)Fees for record searches and review may be charged even if not responsive documents are located or if the request is denied, particularly if the requester insists upon a search after being informed that it is likely to be nonproductive or that any records found are likely to be exempt from disclosure. The Records Manager shall apply the standards set out in paragraph (c)(4) of this section in determining whether to waive or reduce fees.
