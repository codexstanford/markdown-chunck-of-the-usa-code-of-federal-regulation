
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter VI - Department of the Navy
#### Subchapter G - Miscellaneous Rules
##### PART 765 - RULES APPLICABLE TO THE PUBLIC
###### ยง 765.14 Unofficial use of the seal, emblem, names, or initials of the Marine Corps.
####### Standards,No unofficial use or imitation of the Marine Corps seal.

(c)-(1)Reproduction and use of the Marine Corps seal, as designated in Executive Order No. 10538 of June 22, 1954, is restricted to materials emanating from Headquarters Marine Corps. Except for manufacture of official letterhead stationery and related items of official Marine Corps use, reproduction and use of the Marine Corps seal is prohibited.
