####### Accommodation of Discounts Under Provider Reimbursement Methods,General rule.

(m)-(1)The Director. OCHAMPUS (or designee) has authority to reimburse a provider at an amount below the amount usually paid pursuant to this section when, under a program approved by the Director, the provider has agreed to the lower amount.
