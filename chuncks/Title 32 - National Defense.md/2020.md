
# Title 32 - National Defense
## Subtitle A - Department of Defense
### Chapter I - Office of the Secretary of Defense
#### Subchapter D - Personnel, Military and Civilian
##### PART 75 - EXCEPTIONAL FAMILY MEMBER PROGRAM (EFMP)
###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 75.3 Definitions.
######## Early Intervention Services (EIS).

Developmental services for infants and toddlers with disabilities, as defined in 32 CFR part 57, that are provided under the supervision of a Military Department, including evaluation, IFSP development and revision, and service coordination provided at no cost to the child's parents.
