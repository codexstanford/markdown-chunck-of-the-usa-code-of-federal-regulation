###### Appendix A to Part 247 - Funded Newspapers and Magazines

Normally, printing is accomplished by a commercial printer under contract or in government printing facilities in accordance with DoD Directive 5330.3.The editorial content of these publications and distribution are accomplished by the contracting command.
