
# Title 32 - National Defense

###### ยง 199.6 TRICARE - authorized providers.
####### Extramedical individual providers.

(iv)Extramedical individual providers are those who do counseling or nonmedical therapy and whose training and therapeutic concepts are outside the medical field. The services of extramedical individual professionals are coverable following the CHAMPUS determined allowable charge methodology provided such services are otherwise authorized in this or other sections of the regulation.
