
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

####### Moving and storage expenses.

(5)Necessary expenses and serious needs related to moving and storing personal property to avoid additional disaster damage generally include storage of personal property while disaster-related repairs are being made to the primary residence, and return of the personal property to the individual or household's primary residence.
