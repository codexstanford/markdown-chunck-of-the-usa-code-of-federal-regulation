
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 5 - PRODUCTION OR DISCLOSURE OF INFORMATION
##### Subpart F - Subpoenas or Other Legal Demands for Testimony or the Production or Disclosure of Records or Other Information
###### ยง 5.82 Definitions.
####### Demand

(a)refers to a subpoena, order, or other demand of a court of competent jurisdiction, or other specific authority (e.g., an administrative or State legislative body), signed by the presiding officer, for the production, disclosure, or release of FEMA records or information or for the appearance and testimony of FEMA personnel as witnesses in their official capacities.
