
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Disaster Assistance
#### PART 208 - NATIONAL URBAN SEARCH AND RESCUE RESPONSE SYSTEM
##### Subpart C - Response Cooperative Agreements
###### ยง 208.43 Rehabilitation.
####### Costs for Equipment Cache List items,Non-consumable items.

(a)-(1)DHS will reimburse costs incurred to repair or replace any non-consumable item on the Equipment Cache List that was lost, damaged, destroyed, or donated at DHS direction to another entity, during Activation. For each such item, the Sponsoring Agency must document, in writing, the circumstances of the loss, damage, destruction, or donation.
