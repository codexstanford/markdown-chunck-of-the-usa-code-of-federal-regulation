
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

### Subchapter E - Fire Assistance

###### ยง 296.4 Definitions.
####### Subsistence Resources

means food and other items obtained through hunting, fishing, firewood gathering, timbering, grazing or agricultural activities undertaken by the claimant without financial remuneration, on land damaged by the Hermit's Peak/Calf Canyon Fire.
