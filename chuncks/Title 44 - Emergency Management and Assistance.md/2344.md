
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Disaster Assistance
#### PART 208 - NATIONAL URBAN SEARCH AND RESCUE RESPONSE SYSTEM
##### Subpart D - Reimbursement Claims and Appeals
###### ยง 208.52 Reimbursement procedures.
####### Time for submission.

(b)(1) Claims for reimbursement must be submitted within 90 days after the end of the Personnel Rehabilitation Period specified in the Demobilization Order.

(2) The Assistant Administrator may extend and specify the time limitation in paragraph (b)(1) of this section when the Sponsoring Agency justifies and requests the extension in writing.
