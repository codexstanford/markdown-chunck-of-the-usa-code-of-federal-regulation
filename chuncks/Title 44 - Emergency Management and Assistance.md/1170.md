
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter C - Fire Prevention and Control
#### PART 150 - PUBLIC SAFETY AWARDS TO PUBLIC SAFETY OFFICERS
##### ยง 150.2 Definitions.
###### Law enforcement officer

means a person involved in the control or reduction of crime and juvenile delinquency or enforcement of the criminal laws. This includes, but is not limited to, police, corrections, probation, parole, and court officers, and Federal civilian officers in such capacities.
