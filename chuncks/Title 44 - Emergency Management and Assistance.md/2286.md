
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Disaster Assistance
#### PART 208 - NATIONAL URBAN SEARCH AND RESCUE RESPONSE SYSTEM
##### Subpart B - Preparedness Cooperative Agreements
###### ยง 208.22 Preparedness Cooperative Agreement process.
####### Amendment,Procedure.

(c)-(1)Absent special circumstances, DHS will fund and amend Preparedness Cooperative Agreements on an annual basis. Before amendment, the Assistance Officer will issue a call for Cooperative Agreement amendment applications. The Assistance Officer will specify required application forms and supporting documentation to be submitted with the application.
