
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

### Subchapter F - Preparedness

###### Discontinuation of charges.

(e)When we receive a copy from the NRC of their approved exemption to 10 CFR 50.54(q) requirements stating that offsite radiological emergency planning and preparedness are no longer required at a particular commercial nuclear power plant site, we will discontinue REP Program services at that site. We will no longer assess a user fee for that site from the beginning of the next fiscal year.
