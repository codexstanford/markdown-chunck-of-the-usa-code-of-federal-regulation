
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

### Subchapter E - Fire Assistance

###### ยง 295.50 Definitions.
####### Public Assistance Program,et seq.,

means the FEMA program establish under Subchapter IV of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act, as amended, 42 U.S.C. 5121,which provides grants to States, local governments, Indian tribes and private nonprofit organizations for emergency measures and repair, restoration and replacement of damaged facilities.
