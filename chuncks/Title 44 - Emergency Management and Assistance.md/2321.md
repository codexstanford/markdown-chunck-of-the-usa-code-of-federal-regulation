
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

### Subchapter D - Disaster Assistance

##### Subpart C - Response Cooperative Agreements
###### ยง 208.39 Reimbursement for personnel costs incurred during Activation.
####### Tour of duty.

(c)The tour of duty for all Activated System Members will be 24 hours. DHS will reimburse the Sponsoring Agency for salary and overtime costs incurred in compensating System Members for meal periods and regularly scheduled sleep periods during Activation. Activated System Members are considered "on-duty" and must be available for immediate response at all times during Activation.
