
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter B - Insurance and Hazard Mitigation
#### PART 80 - PROPERTY ACQUISITION AND RELOCATION FOR OPEN SPACE
##### Subpart C - Post-Award Requirements
###### ยง 80.17 Project implementation.
####### Removal of existing buildings.

(d)Existing incompatible facilities must be removed by demolition or by relocation outside of the hazard area within 90 days of settlement of the property transaction. The FEMA Regional Administrator may grant an exception to this deadline only for a particular property based upon written justification if extenuating circumstances exist, but will specify a final date for removal.
