
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance

### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 19 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
##### Subpart B - Coverage
###### § 19.220 Admissions.
####### Public institutions of undergraduate higher education.

(e)§§ 19.300 through 19.310 do not apply to any public institution of undergraduate higher education that traditionally and continually from its establishment has had a policy of admitting students of only one sex.
