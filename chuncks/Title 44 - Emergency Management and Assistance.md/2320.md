
# Title 44 - Emergency Management and Assistance
## Chapter I - Federal Emergency Management Agency, Department of Homeland Security
### Subchapter D - Disaster Assistance
#### PART 208 - NATIONAL URBAN SEARCH AND RESCUE RESPONSE SYSTEM
##### Subpart C - Response Cooperative Agreements
###### ยง 208.39 Reimbursement for personnel costs incurred during Activation.
####### Public Safety Exemption not applicable.

(b)DHS will reimburse Sponsoring Agencies for costs incurred by Non-Exempt System Members in accordance with 29 U.S.C. 207(a) of the Fair Labor Standards Act, without regard to the public safety exemption contained in 29 U.S.C. 207(k). In other words, DHS will reimburse Sponsoring Agencies on an overtime basis for any hours worked by Non-Exempt System Members greater than 40 hours during a regular workweek.
