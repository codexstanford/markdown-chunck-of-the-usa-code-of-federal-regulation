##### Subpart B - The Declaration Process
###### ยง 206.40 Designation of affected areas and eligible assistance.
####### Requests for additional designations after a declaration.

(c)After a declaration by the President, the Governor, or the GAR, may request that additional areas or types of supplementary Federal assistance be authorized by the Assistant Administrator for the Disaster Assistance Directorate. Such requests shall be accompanied by appropriate verified assessments and commitments by State and local governments to demonstrate that the requested designations are justified and that the unmet needs are beyond State and local capabilities without supplementary Federal assistance.
