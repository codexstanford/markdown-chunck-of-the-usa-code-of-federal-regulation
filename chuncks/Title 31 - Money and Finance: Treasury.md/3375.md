####### ยง 240.2 Definitions.
######## Forged or unauthorized drawer's signature

(q)means a drawer's signature that has been placed on the front of a Treasury check by a person other than:

(1) A disbursing official; or

(2) A person authorized to sign on behalf of a disbursing official.
