
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 548 - BELARUS SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 548.309 Government of Belarus.
####### Government of Belarus

The termmeans the Government of Belarus, any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof, including the National Bank of the Republic of Belarus, and any person owned, controlled, or directed by, or acting for or on behalf of, the Government of Belarus.
