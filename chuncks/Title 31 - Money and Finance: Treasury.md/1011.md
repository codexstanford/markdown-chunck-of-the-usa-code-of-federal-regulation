
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 15 - POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
#### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
##### ยง 15.737-20 Hearings.
###### In general.

(a)The Administrative Law Judge shall preside at the hearing on a complaint for the suspension of a former officer or employee from practice before the Department. Hearings shall be stenographically recorded and transcribed and the testimony of witnesses shall be taken under oath or affirmation. Hearings will be conducted pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 556.
