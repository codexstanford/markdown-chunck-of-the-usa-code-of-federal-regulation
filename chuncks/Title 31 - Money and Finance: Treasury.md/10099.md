
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 592.310 Rough diamond.
####### rough diamond

The termmeans any diamond that is unworked or simply sawn, cleaved, or bruted and classifiable under subheading 7102.10, 7102.21, or 7102.31 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States.
