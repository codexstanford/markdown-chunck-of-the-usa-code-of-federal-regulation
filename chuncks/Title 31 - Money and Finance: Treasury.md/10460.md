
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 599 - ILLICIT DRUG TRADE SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 599.314 Proliferation of illicit drugs.
####### proliferation of illicit drugs

The termmeans any illicit activity to produce, manufacture, distribute, sell, or knowingly finance or transport: narcotic drugs, controlled substances, listed chemicals, or controlled substance analogues, as defined in section 102 of the Controlled Substances Act (21 U.S.C. 802).
