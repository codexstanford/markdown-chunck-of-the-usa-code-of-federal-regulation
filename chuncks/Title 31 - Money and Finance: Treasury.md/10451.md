
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 599 - ILLICIT DRUG TRADE SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 599.306 Foreign person.
####### foreign person

The termmeans any citizen or national of a foreign state (including any such individual who is also a citizen or national of the United States, provided such individual does not reside in the United States) or any entity not organized under the laws of the United States.
