
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 548 - BELARUS SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart B - Prohibitions
###### ยง 548.202 Prohibited transactions with respect to the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Belarus and the Development Bank of the Republic of Belarus (Directive 1).

The following activities by a U.S. person or within the United States are prohibited: all transactions in, provision of financing for, and other dealings in new debt with a maturity of longer than 90 days issued on or after December 2, 2021 by the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Belarus or the Development Bank of the Republic of Belarus.
