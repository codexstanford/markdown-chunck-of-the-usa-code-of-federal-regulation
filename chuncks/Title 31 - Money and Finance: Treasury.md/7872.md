####### potassium chloride (potash) sector of the Belarus economy

The termincludes the extraction, mining, harvesting, production, procurement, refinement, manufacturing, testing, financing, distribution, or transport to, from, or involving Belarus, of potassium chloride (potash) or potash products; and any related activities, including the provision or receipt of goods, services, or technology to, from, or involving the potash sector of the Belarus economy.
