
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 50.4 Definitions.
###### Direct written premium

(i)means the premium information for property and casualty insurance that is included by an insurer in column 1 of the Exhibit of Premiums and Losses of the NAIC Annual Statement or in an equivalent reporting requirement. The Federal terrorism policy surcharge is not included in amounts reported as direct written premium.
