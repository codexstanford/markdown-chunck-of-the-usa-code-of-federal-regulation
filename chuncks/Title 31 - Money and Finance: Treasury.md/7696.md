
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 546 - DARFUR SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 546.305 Interest.
####### interest

Except as otherwise provided in this part, the term, when used with respect to property (e.g., "an interest in property"), means an interest of any nature whatsoever, direct or indirect.
