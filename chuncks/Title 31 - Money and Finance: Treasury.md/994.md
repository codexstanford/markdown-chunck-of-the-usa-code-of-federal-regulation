
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 15 - POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
#### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
##### ยง 15.737-10 Conferences.
###### In general.

(a)The Director may confer with a former officer or employee concerning allegations of misconduct irrespective of whether an administrative disciplinary proceeding has been instituted against him/her. If such conference results in a stipulation in connection with a proceeding in which such person is the respondent, the stipulation may be entered in the record at the instance of either party to the proceeding.
