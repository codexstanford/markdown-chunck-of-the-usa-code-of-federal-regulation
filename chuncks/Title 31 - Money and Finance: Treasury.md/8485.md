
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 560 - IRANIAN TRANSACTIONS AND SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart B - Prohibitions
###### ยง 560.215 Prohibitions on foreign entities owned or controlled by U.S. persons.
####### Definitions:

(b)(1) For purposes of paragraph (a) of this section, an entity is "owned or controlled" by a United States person if the United States person:

(i) Holds a 50 percent or greater equity interest by vote or value in the entity;

(ii) Holds a majority of seats on the board of directors of the entity; or

(iii) Otherwise controls the actions, policies, or personnel decisions of the entity.
