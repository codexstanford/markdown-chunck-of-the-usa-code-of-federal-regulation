
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter VI - Bureau of Engraving and Printing, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 601 - DISTINCTIVE PAPER FOR UNITED STATES CURRENCY AND OTHER SECURITIES
##### ยง 601.4 Use of paper; interest-bearing securities of the United States.

The existing distinctive papers shall be used for the printing of interest-bearing securities of the United States, and for any other printing where the use of distinctive paper is indicated.
