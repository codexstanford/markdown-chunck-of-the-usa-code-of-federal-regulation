
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 597 - FOREIGN TERRORIST ORGANIZATIONS SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart G - Penalties
###### ยง 597.703 Response to prepenalty notice.
####### Form and contents of written response.

(b)The written response need not be in any particular form, but shall contain information sufficient to indicate that it is in response to the prepenalty notice. It should respond to the allegations in the prepenalty notice and set forth the reasons why the respondent believes the penalty should not be imposed or, if imposed, why it should be in a lesser amount than proposed.
