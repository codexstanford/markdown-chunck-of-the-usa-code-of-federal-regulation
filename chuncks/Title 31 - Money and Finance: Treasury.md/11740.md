
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1023 - RULES FOR BROKERS OR DEALERS IN SECURITIES
##### Subpart C - Reports Required To Be Made By Brokers or Dealers in Securities
###### ยง 1023.320 Reports by brokers or dealers in securities of suspicious transactions.
####### Prohibition on disclosures by Self-Regulatory Organizations.

(3)Any self-regulatory organization registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, or any director, officer, employee, or agent of any of the foregoing, shall not disclose a SAR, or any information that would reveal the existence of a SAR except as necessary to fulfill self-regulatory duties with the consent of the Securities Exchange Commission, in a manner consistent with Title II of the Bank Secrecy Act. For purposes of this section, "self-regulatory duties" shall not include the disclosure of a SAR, or any information that would reveal the existence of a SAR, in response to a request for disclosure of non-public information or a request for use in a private legal proceeding.
