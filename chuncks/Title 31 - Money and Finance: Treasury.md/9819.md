
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 589 - UKRAINE-/RUSSIA-RELATED SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 589.309 Defense and related materiel sector of the Russian Federation economy.
####### defense and related materiel sector of the Russian Federation economy

The termincludes: The military, armed forces, or security forces of the Russian Federation; the military, armed forces, or security forces of the Russian Federation's use of arms or related materiel; and any person manufacturing, supplying, providing financing for, procuring, or distributing goods, services, or technology to the military, armed forces, or security forces of the Russian Federation; or financing, developing, testing, producing, or sustaining such goods or services or technology, or arms or related materiel, within or transiting the Russian Federation.
