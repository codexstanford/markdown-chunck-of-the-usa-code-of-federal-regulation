
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 550 - ETHIOPIA SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 550.306 Government of Eritrea.
####### Government of Eritrea

The termmeans the Government of Eritrea, any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof, including the Bank of Eritrea, and any person owned, controlled, or directed by, or acting for or on behalf of, the Government of Eritrea.
