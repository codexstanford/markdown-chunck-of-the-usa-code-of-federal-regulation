
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1028 - RULES FOR OPERATORS OF CREDIT CARD SYSTEMS
##### Subpart B - Programs
###### ยง 1028.210 Anti-money laundering programs for operators of credit card systems.
####### Anti-money laundering program requirement.

(a)Effective July 24, 2002, each operator of a credit card system shall develop and implement a written anti-money laundering program reasonably designed to prevent the operator of a credit card system from being used to facilitate money laundering and the financing of terrorist activities. The program must be approved by senior management. Operators of credit card systems must make their anti-money laundering programs available to the Department of the Treasury or the appropriate Federal regulator for review.
