
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1024 - RULES FOR MUTUAL FUNDS
##### Subpart B - Programs
###### ยง 1024.220 Customer identification programs for mutual funds.
####### Customer identification program: minimum requirements,In general.

(a)-(1)A mutual fund must implement a written Customer Identification Program ("CIP") appropriate for its size and type of business that, at a minimum, includes each of the requirements of paragraphs (a)(1) through (5) of this section. The CIP must be a part of the mutual fund's anti-money laundering program required under the regulations implementing 31 U.S.C. 5318(h).
