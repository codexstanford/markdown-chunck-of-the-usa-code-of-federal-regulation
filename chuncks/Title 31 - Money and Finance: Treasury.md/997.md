
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 15 - POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
#### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
##### ยง 15.737-12 Contents of complaint.
###### Charges.

(a)A complaint shall give a plain and concise description of the allegations which constitute the basis for the proceeding. A complaint shall be deemed sufficient if it fairly informs the respondent of the charges against him/her so that the respondent is able to prepare a defense.
