
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury

##### Applicant,application

means a person who submits an application, request, or plan required to be approved by an official of the Department of the Treasury, or designee thereof, or by a primary recipient, as a condition to eligibility for federal financial assistance, andmeans such an application, request, or plan.
