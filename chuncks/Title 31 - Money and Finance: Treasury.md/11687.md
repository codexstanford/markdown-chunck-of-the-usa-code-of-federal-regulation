
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1023 - RULES FOR BROKERS OR DEALERS IN SECURITIES
##### Subpart B - Programs
###### ยง 1023.220 Customer identification programs for broker-dealers.
####### Customer identification program: minimum requirements,In general.

(a)-(1)A broker-dealer must establish, document, and maintain a written Customer Identification Program ("CIP") appropriate for its size and business that, at a minimum, includes each of the requirements of paragraphs (a)(1) through (a)(5) of this section. The CIP must be a part of the broker-dealer's anti-money laundering compliance program required under 31 U.S.C. 5318(h).
