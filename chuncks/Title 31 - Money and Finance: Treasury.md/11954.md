
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1027 - RULES FOR DEALERS IN PRECIOUS METALS, PRECIOUS STONES, OR JEWELS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 1027.100 Definitions.
####### Precious metal

(d)means:

(1) Gold, iridium, osmium, palladium, platinum, rhodium, ruthenium, or silver, having a level of purity of 500 or more parts per thousand; and

(2) An alloy containing 500 or more parts per thousand, in the aggregate, of two or more of the metals listed in paragraph (d)(1) of this section.
