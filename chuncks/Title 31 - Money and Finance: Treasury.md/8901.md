
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

####### Determination,Determination that a Finding of Violation is warranted.

(c)-(1)If, after considering the response, OFAC determines that a final Finding of Violation should be issued, OFAC will issue a final Finding of Violation that will inform the violator of its decision.
