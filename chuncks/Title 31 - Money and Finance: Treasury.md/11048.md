
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

#### PART 1010 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
##### Subpart A - General Definitions
###### ยง 1010.100 General definitions.
####### Bank Secrecy Act.

(e)The Currency and Foreign Transactions Reporting Act, its amendments, and the other statutes relating to the subject matter of that Act, have come to be referred to as the Bank Secrecy Act. These statutes are codified at 12 U.S.C. 1829b, 12 U.S.C. 1951-1959, 18 U.S.C. 1956, 18 U.S.C. 1957, 18 U.S.C. 1960, and 31 U.S.C. 5311-5314 and 5316-5332 and notes thereto.
