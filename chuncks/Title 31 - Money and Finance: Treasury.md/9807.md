
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 589 - UKRAINE-/RUSSIA-RELATED SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart B - Prohibitions
###### § 589.214 Exempt transactions.
####### Official business.

(e)The prohibitions contained in §§ 589.201(a)(5), 589.206, 589.207, and 589.208 do not apply to transactions for the conduct of the official business of the United States Government by employees, grantees, or contractors thereof.
