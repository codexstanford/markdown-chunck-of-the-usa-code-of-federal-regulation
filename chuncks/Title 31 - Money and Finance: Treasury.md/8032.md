
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 550 - ETHIOPIA SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 550.307 Government of Ethiopia.
####### Government of Ethiopia

The termmeans the Government of Ethiopia, any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof, including the National Bank of Ethiopia, and any person owned, controlled, or directed by, or acting for or on behalf of, the Government of Ethiopia.
