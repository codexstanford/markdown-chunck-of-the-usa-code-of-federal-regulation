
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury

##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 598.313 Significant foreign narcotics trafficker.
####### significant foreign narcotics trafficker

The termmeans any foreign person that plays a significant role in international narcotics trafficking that the President has determined to be appropriate for sanctions and has publicly identified under section 804(b) or section 804(h)(1) of the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act.
