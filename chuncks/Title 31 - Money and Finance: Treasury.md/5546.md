####### ยง 357.12 A Participant's Security Entitlement.
######## Must the United States and the Federal Reserve Banks agree to act on behalf of any Person or to recognize the interest of any transferee of a security interest or other limited interest in favor of any Person?

(e)(1)No, they need not agree to act or recognize any party's interest, except:
