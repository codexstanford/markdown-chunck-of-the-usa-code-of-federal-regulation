
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 561 - IRANIAN FINANCIAL SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 561.307 Payable-through account.
####### payable-through account

The termmeans a correspondent account maintained by a U.S. financial institution for a foreign financial institution by means of which the foreign financial institution permits its customers to engage, either directly or through a subaccount, in banking activities usual in connection with the business of banking in the United States.
