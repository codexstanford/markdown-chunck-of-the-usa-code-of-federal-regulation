
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 525 - BURMA SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart B - Prohibitions
###### ยง 525.201 Prohibited transactions.

All transactions prohibited pursuant to Executive Order (E.O.) 14014 of February 10, 2021, or any further Executive orders issued pursuant to the national emergency declared in E.O. 14014, are prohibited pursuant to this part.
