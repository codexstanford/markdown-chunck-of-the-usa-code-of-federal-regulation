
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 19 - GOVERNMENTWIDE DEBARMENT AND SUSPENSION (NONPROCUREMENT)
#### Subpart F - General Principles Relating to Suspension and Debarment Actions
##### ยง 19.635 May the Department of the Treasury settle a debarment or suspension action?

Yes, we may settle a debarment or suspension action at any time if it is in the best interest of the Federal Government.
