####### ยง 356.2 What definitions do I need to know to understand this part?
######## Yield

means the annualized rate of return to maturity on a non-indexed security. Yield is expressed as a percentage. For an inflation-protected security, yield means the real yield. Yield is also referred to as "yield to maturity." (See appendix B.)
