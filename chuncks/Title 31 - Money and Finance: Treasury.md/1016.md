
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 15 - POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
#### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
##### ยง 15.737-21 Evidence.
###### Proof of documents.

(c)Official documents, records and papers of the Department shall be admissible in evidence without the production of an officer or employee to authenticate them. Any such documents, records, and papers may be evidenced by a copy attested or identified by an officer or employee of the Department.
