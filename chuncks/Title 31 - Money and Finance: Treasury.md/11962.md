
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1027 - RULES FOR DEALERS IN PRECIOUS METALS, PRECIOUS STONES, OR JEWELS
##### Subpart C - Reports Required To Be Made by Dealers in Precious Metals, Precious Stones, or Jewels
###### ยง 1027.300 General.

Dealers in precious metals, precious stones, or jewels are subject to the reporting requirements set forth and cross referenced in this subpart. Dealers in precious metals, precious stones, or jewels should also refer to subpart C of part 1010 of this Chapter for reporting requirements contained in that subpart which apply to dealers in precious metals, precious stones, or jewels.
