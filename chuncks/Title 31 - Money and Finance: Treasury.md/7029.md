####### General license for amateur and semi-professional international sports federation competitions.

(a)The travel-related transactions set forth in ยง 515.560(c) and such other transactions as are directly incident to participation in athletic competitions in Cuba by amateur or semi-professional athletes or athletic teams, or organization of such competitions, are authorized, provided that:

(1) The athletic competition in Cuba is held under the auspices of the international sports federation for the relevant sport;

(3) The competition is open for attendance, and in relevant situations, participation, by the Cuban public.
