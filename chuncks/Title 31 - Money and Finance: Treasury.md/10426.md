
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 598 - FOREIGN NARCOTICS KINGPIN SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart G - Penalties
###### ยง 598.703 Response to prepenalty notice; informal settlement.
####### Form and contents of response.

(b)The written response need not be in any particular form, but must contain information sufficient to indicate that it is in response to the prepenalty notice. It should contain responses to the allegations in the prepenalty notice and set forth the reasons why the respondent believes the penalty should not be imposed or why, if imposed, it should be in a lesser amount than proposed.
