
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 587 - RUSSIAN HARMFUL FOREIGN ACTIVITIES SANCTIONS REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 587.305 Government of the Russian Federation.
####### Government of the Russian Federation

The termmeans the Government of the Russian Federation, any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof, including the Central Bank of the Russian Federation, and any person owned, controlled, or directed by, or acting for or on behalf of, the Government of the Russian Federation.
