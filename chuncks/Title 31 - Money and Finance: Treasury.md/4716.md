
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter II - Fiscal Service, Department of the Treasury
#### Subchapter A - Bureau of the Fiscal Service
##### PART 344 - U.S. TREASURY SECURITIES - STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT SERIES
###### Subpart C - Demand Deposit Securities
####### ยง 344.9 How do I redeem a Demand Deposit security?
######## When must I notify Fiscal Service to redeem a security?

(a)A Demand Deposit security can be redeemed at the owner's option, if Fiscal Service receives a request for redemption not less than:

(1) One business day before the requested redemption date for redemptions of $10 million or less; and

(2) Three business days before the requested redemption date for redemptions of more than $10 million.
