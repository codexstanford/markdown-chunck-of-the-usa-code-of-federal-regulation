
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter V - Office of Foreign Assets Control, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 592 - ROUGH DIAMONDS CONTROL REGULATIONS
##### Subpart C - General Definitions
###### ยง 592.304 Exporting authority.
####### exporting authority

(a) The termmeans one or more entities designated by a Participant from whose territory a shipment of rough diamonds is being exported as having the authority to validate the Kimberley Process Certificate.

(b) The exporting authority for the United States is the U.S. Bureau of the Census.
