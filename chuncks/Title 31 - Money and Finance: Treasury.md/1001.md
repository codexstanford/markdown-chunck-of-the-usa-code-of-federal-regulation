
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary of the Treasury
### PART 15 - POST EMPLOYMENT CONFLICT OF INTEREST
#### Subpart C - Administrative Enforcement Proceedings
##### ยง 15.737-13 Service of complaint and other papers.
###### Filing of papers.

(c)Whenever the filing of a paper is required or permitted in connection with a proceeding, and the place of filing is not specified by this subpart or by rule or order of the Administrative Law Judge, the paper shall be filed with the Director of Practice, Department of the Treasury, Washington, DC 20220. All papers shall be filed in duplicate.
