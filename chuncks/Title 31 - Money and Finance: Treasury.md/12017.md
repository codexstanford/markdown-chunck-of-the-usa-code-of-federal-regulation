
# Title 31 - Money and Finance: Treasury
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Money and Finance
### Chapter X - Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, Department of the Treasury
#### PART 1030 - RULES FOR HOUSING GOVERNMENT SPONSORED ENTERPRISES
##### Subpart B - Programs
###### ยง 1030.210 Anti-money laundering programs for housing government sponsored enterprises.
####### Anti-money laundering program requirements for housing government sponsored enterprises.

(a)Each housing government sponsored enterprise shall develop and implement a written anti-money laundering program that is reasonably designed to prevent the housing government sponsored enterprise from being used to facilitate money laundering or the financing of terrorist activities. The program must be approved by senior management. A housing government sponsored enterprise shall make a copy of its anti-money laundering program available to the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network or its designee upon request.
