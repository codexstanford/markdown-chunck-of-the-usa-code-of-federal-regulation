
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter VI - Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board
### PART 1690 - THRIFT SAVINGS PLAN
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 1690.1 Definitions.
###### PEBD

means the pay entry base date (or pay entry basic date for some services), which is determined by each uniformed service and is used to calculate how much time in service a member has for the purpose of determining longevity pay rates.
