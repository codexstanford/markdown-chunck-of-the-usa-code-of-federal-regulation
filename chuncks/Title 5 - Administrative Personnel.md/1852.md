
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

###### ยง 430.203 Definitions.
####### Appraisal system

means a framework of policies and parameters established by an agency as defined at 5 U.S.C. 4301(1) for the administration of performance appraisal programs under subchapter I of chapter 43 of title 5, United States Code, and this subpart.
