
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations

##### Subpart D - Separate Maintenance Allowance for Duty at Johnston Island
###### ยง 591.401 Purpose and applicability.
####### Applicability.

(b)This subpart applies to an employee (as defined in 5 U.S.C. 2105) in an executive department (as defined in section 101 of title 5, United States Code) or an independent establishment (as defined in section 104 of title 5, United States Code) who is assigned to a post of duty at Johnston Island.
