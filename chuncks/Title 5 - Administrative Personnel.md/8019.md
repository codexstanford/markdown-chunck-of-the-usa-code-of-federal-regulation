
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter II - Merit Systems Protection Board
### Subchapter A - Organization and Procedures
#### PART 1204 - AVAILABILITY OF OFFICIAL INFORMATION
##### Subpart B - Procedures for Obtaining Records Under the Freedom of Information Act
###### ยง 1204.14 Requests for access to confidential commercial information.
####### Notice to business submitters.

(c)The Board will provide a business submitter with prompt written notice of a request for its confidential commercial information whenever such written notice is required under paragraph (d) of this section. Exceptions to such written notice are at paragraph (h) of this section. This written notice will either describe the exact nature of the confidential information requested or provide copies of the records or parts of records containing the commercial information.
