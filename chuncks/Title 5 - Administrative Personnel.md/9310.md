
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter VI - Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board
### PART 1690 - THRIFT SAVINGS PLAN
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 1690.1 Definitions.
###### Catch-up contributions

means TSP contributions from basic pay that are made by participants age 50 and over, which exceed the elective deferral limit of 26 U.S.C. 402(g) and meet the requirements of 5 CFR 1600.23.
