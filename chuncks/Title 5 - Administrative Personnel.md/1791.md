
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 410 - TRAINING
##### Subpart C - Establishing and Implementing Training Programs
###### ยง 410.302 Responsibilities of the head of an agency.
####### Training of Presidential appointees.

(b)(1)The Office of Personnel Management delegates to the head of each agency authority to authorize training for officials appointed by the President. In exercising this authority, the head of an agency must ensure that the training is in compliance with chapter 41 of title 5, United States Code, and with this part. This authority may not be delegated to a subordinate.
