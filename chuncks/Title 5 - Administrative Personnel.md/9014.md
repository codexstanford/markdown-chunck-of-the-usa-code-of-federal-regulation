
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter VI - Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board
### PART 1639 - CLAIMS COLLECTION
#### Subpart A - Administrative Collection, Compromise, Termination, and Referral of Claims
##### ยง 1639.4 Definitions.
###### Hearing official

means an individual responsible for conducting any hearing with respect to the existence or amount of a debt claimed, and rendering a decision on the basis of the hearing.
