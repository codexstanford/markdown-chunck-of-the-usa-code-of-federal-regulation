
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 339 - MEDICAL QUALIFICATION DETERMINATIONS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 339.104 Definitions.
####### Practitioner

means a person providing health services who is not a medical doctor, but who is certified by a national organization, licensed by a State, and/or registered as a health professional to provide the health service in question.
