
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 536 - GRADE AND PAY RETENTION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 536.103 Definitions.
####### Covered pay system,i.e.

means a covered pay schedule as defined in 5 U.S.C. 5361(5)-, the General Schedule pay system established under 5 U.S.C. chapter 53, subchapter III; a prevailing rate system established under 5 U.S.C. chapter 53, subchapter IV; or a special occupational pay system established under 5 U.S.C. chapter 53, subchapter IX. The various prevailing rate systems under 5 U.S.C. chapter 53, subchapter IV, are considered separate systems if they have separate job grading structures.
