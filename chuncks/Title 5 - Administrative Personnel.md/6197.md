
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 850 - ELECTRONIC RETIREMENT PROCESSING
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 850.103 Definitions.
####### Personal identification number (PIN) or password

means a non-cryptographic method of authenticating the identity of a user of an electronic application, involving the use of an identifier known only to the user and to the electronic system, which checks the identifier against data in a database to authenticate the user's identity.
