
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 337 - EXAMINING SYSTEM
##### Subpart C - Alternative Rating and Selection Procedures
###### ยง 337.302 Definitions.
####### Category rating

(a)is synonymous with alternative rating as described at 5 U.S.C. 3319, and is a process of evaluating qualified eligibles by quality categories rather than by assigning individual numeric scores. The agency assesses candidates against job-related criteria and then places them into two or more pre-defined categories.
