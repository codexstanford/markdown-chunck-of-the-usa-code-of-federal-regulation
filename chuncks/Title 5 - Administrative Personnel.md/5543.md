
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

###### ยง 842.305 Deposits for civilian service.
####### Government contributions.

(8)(i) The department or agency where service described in ยง 842.304(e) was performed must pay Government contributions for each period of service covered by a deposit under this section.
