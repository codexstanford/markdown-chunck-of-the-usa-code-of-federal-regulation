
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart E - Employee Medical File System Records
###### ยง 293.502 Definitions.
####### Employee Assistance and Counseling Record

means the record created when an employee participates in an agency assistance/counseling program (e.g., drug or alcohol abuse or personal counseling programs under Pub. L. 91-616, 92-255, and 79-658, respectively).
