
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter XCIX - Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization Commission
### PART 9901 - DISCLOSURE OF RECORDS AND INFORMATION
#### Freedom of Information Act
##### ยง 9901.8 Fees.
###### Direct costs

(3)means those costs incurred searching for and duplicating (and, in the case of commercial use requests, reviewing) documents in response to a FOIA request. Direct costs include, for example, salaries of employees who perform the work and costs of conducting large-scale computer searches. Direct costs do not include, for example, overhead expenses such as the costs of space, and of heating or lighting a facility.
