
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter XVI - Office of Government Ethics

###### ยง 2640.201 Exemptions for interests in mutual funds, unit investment trusts, and employee benefit plans.
####### Matters affecting mutual funds and unit investment trusts.

(d)In addition to participation in the particular matters affecting the holdings of mutual funds and unit investment trusts as permitted under paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section, an employee may participate in any particular matter of general applicability affecting a mutual fund or unit investment trust where the disqualifying financial interest arises because of the ownership of an interest in the mutual fund or unit investment trust.
