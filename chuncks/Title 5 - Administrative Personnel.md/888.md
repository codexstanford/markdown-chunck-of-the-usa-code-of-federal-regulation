
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart F - Career or Career-Conditional Appointment Under Special Authorities
###### ยง 315.611 Appointment of certain veterans who have competed under agency merit promotion announcements.
####### Definitions.

(b)"Agency" in this context means an executive agency as defined in 5 U.S.C. 105. The agency determines in individual cases whether a candidate was released "shortly before" completing the required 3 years and should therefore be eligible for appointment.
