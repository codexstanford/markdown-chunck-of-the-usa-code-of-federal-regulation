
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 849 - REPRESENTATIVE PAYEES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 849.102 Definitions.
####### Misuse of benefits

means the embezzlement or conversion of all or any part of the amount received by the representative payee for a use other than for the use and benefit of the minor or individual on whose behalf such payments were received.
