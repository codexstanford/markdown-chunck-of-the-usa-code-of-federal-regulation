
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 180 - EMPLOYEES' PERSONAL PROPERTY CLAIMS
##### ยง 180.104 Allowable claims.
###### Property used for benefit of the Government.

(7)Claims may be allowed for damage to or loss of property (except motor vehicles) used for the benefit of the Government at the request of, or with the knowledge and consent of, superior authority or by reason of necessity.
