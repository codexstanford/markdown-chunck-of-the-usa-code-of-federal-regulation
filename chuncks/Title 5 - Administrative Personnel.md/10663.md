
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart D - Qualified Trusts
###### ยง 2634.404 Summary of procedures for creation of a qualified trust.
####### Confidentiality agreement.

(e)If any person other than the independent trustee or designated fiduciary has access to information that may not be shared with an interested party or that party's representative, that person must file a Confidentiality Agreement with the Office of Government Ethics. Persons filing a Confidentiality Agreement must certify that they will not make prohibited contacts with an interested party or that party's representative.
