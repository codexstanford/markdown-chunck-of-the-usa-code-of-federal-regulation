
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 845 - FEDERAL EMPLOYEES RETIREMENT SYSTEM - DEBT COLLECTION
##### Subpart D - Agency Requests to OPM for Recovery of a Debt From the Civil Service Retirement Fund
###### ยง 845.403 Definitions.
####### Fraud claim

means any debt designated by the Attorney General (or designee) as involving an indication of fraud, the presentation of a false claim, or misrepresentation on the part of the debtor or any other party having an interest in the claim.
