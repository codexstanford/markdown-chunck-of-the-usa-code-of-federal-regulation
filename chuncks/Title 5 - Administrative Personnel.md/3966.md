
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 630 - ABSENCE AND LEAVE
##### Subpart Q - Paid Parental Leave
###### ยง 630.1701 Purpose, applicability, and agency responsibilities.
####### Purpose.

(a)This subpart provides regulations to govern the granting of paid parental leave to covered employees. Since paid parental leave may only be substituted for unpaid leave granted following a birth or placement under specific provisions of the Family and Medical Leave Act in title 5, United States Code-specifically, section 6382(a)(1)(A) and (B) in 5 U.S.C. chapter 63, subchapter V-this subpart links to subpart L (Family and Medical Leave) of this part.
