
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 302 - EMPLOYMENT IN THE EXCEPTED SERVICE
##### Subpart C - Accepting, Rating, and Arranging Applications
###### ยง 302.303 Maintenance of employment lists.
####### Reemployment list.

(c)A reemployment list may be established at the agency's discretion to include the names of current employees of the agency and of former employees of the agency who are to be considered for future employment and who are not eligible for inclusion on the priority reemployment list. Employees may be entered on the reemployment list only for positions in which tenure and/or work schedule is no greater than that of the position previously held.
