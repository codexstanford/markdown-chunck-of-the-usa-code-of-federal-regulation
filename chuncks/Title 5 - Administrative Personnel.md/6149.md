
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 849 - REPRESENTATIVE PAYEES
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 849.102 Definitions.
####### Representative payee

means a person, who is at least 18 years of age, or an organization designated to receive annuity payments on behalf of a minor or an individual mentally incompetent or under other legal disability.
