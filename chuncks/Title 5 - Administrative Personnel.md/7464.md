##### Subpart A - Motor Vehicle Operators
###### ยง 930.102 Definitions.
####### Incidental operator

means an employee, other than one occupying a position officially classified as a motor vehicle operator, who is required to operate a Government-owned or -leased motor vehicle to properly carry out his or her assigned duties.
