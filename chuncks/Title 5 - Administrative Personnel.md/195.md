
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 179 - CLAIMS COLLECTION STANDARDS
##### Subpart C - Administrative Offset
###### ยง 179.302 Definitions.
####### Administrative offset,

as defined in 31 U.S.C. 3701(a)(1), means withholding money payable by the United States Government to, or held by the Government for, a person to satisfy a debt the person owes the Government.
