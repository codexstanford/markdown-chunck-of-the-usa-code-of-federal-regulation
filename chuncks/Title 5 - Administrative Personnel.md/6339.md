
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations

##### Subpart A - Administration and General Provisions
###### ยง 875.101 Definitions.
####### Carrier

means a "qualified carrier" as defined in section 9001 of title 5, United States Code, with which OPM has contracted to provide long term care insurance coverage under this section. A Carrier may designate one or more administrators to perform some of its obligations.
