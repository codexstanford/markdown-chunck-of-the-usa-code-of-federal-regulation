
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 535 - CRITICAL POSITION PAY AUTHORITY
##### ยง 535.102 Definitions.
###### Critical position

means a position for which OPM has granted authority to the head of an agency to exercise the pay-setting authority provided in 5 U.S.C. 5377.
