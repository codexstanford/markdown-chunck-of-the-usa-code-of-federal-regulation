####### ยง 550.1603 Definitions.
######## Level 2 regular tour of duty

means an officially established weekly regular tour of duty generally consisting of five 9-hour workdays (including 1 overtime hour each workday) that provides entitlement to a 12.5 percent overtime supplement.
