####### ยง 890.1012 Time limits for OPM to initiate permissive debarments.
######## Licensure cases.

(a)If the basis for the proposed debarment is a licensure action, OPM shall send the provider a notice of proposed debarment within 6 years of the effective date of the State licensing authority's revocation, suspension, restriction, or nonrenewal action, or the date on which the provider surrendered his license to the State authority.
