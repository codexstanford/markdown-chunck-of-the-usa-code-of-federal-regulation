###### ยง 2634.301 Interests in property.
####### In general.

(a)Except reports required under ยง 2634.201(f), each financial disclosure report filed pursuant to this subpart must include a brief description of any interest in property held by the filer at the end of the reporting period in a trade or business, or for investment or the production of income, having a fair market value in excess of $1,000.
