
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 370 - INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY EXCHANGE PROGRAM
##### ยง 370.102 Definitions.
###### Small business concern,http://www.sba.gov,

means a business concern that satisfies the definitions and standards specified by the Administrator of the Small Business Administration (SBA), under section 3(a)(2) of the Small Business Act, codified at 13 CFR 121. Federal agencies can find more information through the "Frequently Asked Questions" page on the SBA's Web site atwhich addresses small business size standards.
