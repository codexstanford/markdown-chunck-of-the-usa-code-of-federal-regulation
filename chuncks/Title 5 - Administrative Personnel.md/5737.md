##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 843.102 Definitions.
####### Retiree,Retiree,,Retiree

means a former employee or Member who is receiving recurring payments under FERS based on service by the employee or Member.as used in this subpart, does not include a current spouse, former spouse, child, or person with an insurable interest receiving a survivor annuity.for purposes of determining a person's status at the time of death means that the person had been separated from the service and had met all the requirements to receive an annuity including having filed an application for the annuity prior to his or her death.
