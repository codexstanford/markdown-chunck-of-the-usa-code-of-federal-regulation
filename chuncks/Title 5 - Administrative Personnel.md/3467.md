
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 582 - COMMERCIAL GARNISHMENT OF FEDERAL EMPLOYEES' PAY
##### Subpart C - Compliance With Legal Process
###### § 582.301 Suspension of payment.
####### i.e.,

Upon proper service of legal process as specified in §§ 582.202 and 582.203, the agency shall suspend,withhold, payment of such moneys for the amount necessary to permit compliance with the legal process in accordance with this part.
