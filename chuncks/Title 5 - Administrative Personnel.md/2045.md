
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

#### PART 511 - CLASSIFICATION UNDER THE GENERAL SCHEDULE
##### Subpart F - Classification Appeals
###### ยง 511.616 Availability of information.
####### the parties concerned

(b) The Office will disclose to the parties concerned the information contained in an appeal file in proceedings under this part. For the purposes of this section,means the Government employee or former Government employee involved in the proceedings, his or her representative designated in writing, and the representative of the agency or the Office involved in the proceeding.
