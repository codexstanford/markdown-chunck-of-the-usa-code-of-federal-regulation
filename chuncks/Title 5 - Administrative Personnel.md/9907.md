
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### ยง 2422.16 May parties enter into election agreements, and if they do not will the Regional Director direct an election?
###### Regional Director directed election.

(b)If the parties are unable to agree on procedural matters, specifically, the eligibility period, method of election, dates, hours, or locations of the election, the Regional Director will decide election procedures and issue a Direction of Election, without prejudice to the rights of a party to file objections to the procedural conduct of the election.
