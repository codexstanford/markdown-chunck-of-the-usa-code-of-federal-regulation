
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter II - Merit Systems Protection Board
### Subchapter A - Organization and Procedures
#### PART 1204 - AVAILABILITY OF OFFICIAL INFORMATION
##### Subpart B - Procedures for Obtaining Records Under the Freedom of Information Act
###### ยง 1204.12 Fees.
####### duplication

(3) The termmeans the process of copying a document or electronically maintained information in response to a FOIA request. Copies can take the form of paper, microfilm, audio-visual materials, or machine-readable documentation (e.g., magnetic tape or disk), among others. The copy provided will be in a form or format requested if the record is readily reproducible by the Board in that form or format. The Board will make a reasonable effort to maintain its records in forms or formats that are reproducible.
