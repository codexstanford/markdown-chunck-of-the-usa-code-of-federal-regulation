
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 732 - NATIONAL SECURITY POSITIONS
##### Subpart B - Designation and Investigative Requirements
###### ยง 732.202 Waivers and exceptions to investigative requirements.
####### Waivers,General.

(a)-(1)A waiver of the preappointment investigative requirement contained in section 3(b) of Executive Order 10450 for employment in a sensitive national security position may be made only for a limited period: (i) In case of emergency if the head of the department or agency concerned finds that such action is necessary in the national interest; and (ii) when such finding is made a part of the records of the department or agency.
