
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations

####### Debt claims previously processed under 5 U.S.C. 5514.

(2)If the creditor agency previously processed the debt claim under section 5514, it should-

(i) Notify the debtor that the claim is being sent to OPM to complete collection from the Fund; and
