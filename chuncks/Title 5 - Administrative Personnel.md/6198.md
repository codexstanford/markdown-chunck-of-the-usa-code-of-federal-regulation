
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 850 - ELECTRONIC RETIREMENT PROCESSING
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 850.103 Definitions.
####### Public/private key (asymmetric) cryptography

is a method of creating a unique mark, known as a digital signature, on an electronic document or file. This method involves the use of two computer-generated, mathematically-linked keys: A private signing key that is kept private and a public validation key that is available to the public.
