
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 214 - SENIOR EXECUTIVE SERVICE
##### Subpart B - General Provisions
###### ยง 214.201 Definitions.
####### Equivalent position

as used in section 3132(a)(2) of title 5, United States Code, means a position under any pay system where the level of the duties and responsibilities of the position and the rate of pay are comparable to that of a position above GS-15 or at Executive Level IV or V.
