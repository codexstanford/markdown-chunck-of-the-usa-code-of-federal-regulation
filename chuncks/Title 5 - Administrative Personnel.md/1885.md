##### Subpart C - Managing Senior Executive Performance
###### ยง 430.303 Definitions.
####### Critical element

means a key component of an executive's work that contributes to organizational goals and results and is so important that unsatisfactory performance of the element would make the executive's overall job performance unsatisfactory.
