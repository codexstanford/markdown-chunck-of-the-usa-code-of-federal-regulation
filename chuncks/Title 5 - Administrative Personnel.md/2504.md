
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 534 - PAY UNDER OTHER SYSTEMS
##### Subpart F - Pay for Administrative Appeals Judge Positions
###### ยง 534.602 Definitions.
####### Administrative appeals judge position

means a position not classified above GS-15 under 5 U.S.C. 5108 and for which the duties primarily involve reviewing decisions of administrative law judges appointed under 5 U.S.C. 3105 and rendering final administrative decisions.
