
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 250 - PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT IN AGENCIES
##### Subpart B - Strategic Human Capital Management
###### ยง 250.202 Definitions.
####### Evaluation system

is an agency's overarching system for evaluating the results of all human capital planning and implementation of human capital strategies to inform the agency's continuous process improvement efforts. This system is also used for ensuring compliance with all applicable statutes, rules, regulations, and agency policies.
