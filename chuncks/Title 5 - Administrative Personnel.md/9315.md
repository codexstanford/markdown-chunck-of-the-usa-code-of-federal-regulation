
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter VI - Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board
### PART 1690 - THRIFT SAVINGS PLAN
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 1690.1 Definitions.
###### Court of competent jurisdiction

means the court of any state, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Guam, the Northern Mariana Islands, or the Virgin Islands, and any Indian court as defined by 25 U.S.C. 1301(3).
