
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 511 - CLASSIFICATION UNDER THE GENERAL SCHEDULE
##### Subpart F - Classification Appeals
###### ยง 511.605 Time limits.
####### Reconsideration.

(c)An employee or agency may request reconsideration of an Office appellate decision. The request must be in writing, and filed not later than 45 calendar days after the decision is issued. This time limit may be waived under exceptional circumstances by either the Director or the Director's designee.
