
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 733 - POLITICAL ACTIVITY - FEDERAL EMPLOYEES RESIDING IN DESIGNATED LOCALITIES
##### ยง 733.101 Definitions.
###### Person

means an individual; a State, local, or foreign government; or a corporation and the subsidiaries it controls, company, association, firm, partnership, society, joint stock company, or any other organization or institution, including any officer, employee, or agent of such person or entity.
