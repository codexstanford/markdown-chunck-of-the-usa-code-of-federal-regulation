
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

#### PART 2422 - REPRESENTATION PROCEEDINGS
##### ยง 2422.19 When is it appropriate for a party to file a motion at a representation hearing?
###### Posthearing motions.

(d)Parties must file motions made after the hearing closes in writing with the Regional Director. Any response to a posthearing motion must be filed with the Regional Director within five (5) days after service of the motion.
