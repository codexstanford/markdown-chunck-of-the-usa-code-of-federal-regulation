
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart A - Administration and General Provisions
###### ยง 870.101 Definitions.
####### Annuitant

means a former employee entitled to an annuity under a retirement system established for employees. This includes the retirement system of a nonappropriated fund instrumentality of the Department of Defense or the Coast Guard.
