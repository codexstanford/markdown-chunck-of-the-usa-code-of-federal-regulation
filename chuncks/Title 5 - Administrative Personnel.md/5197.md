
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 839.102 Definitions.
####### Lost earnings

means earnings that you would have received had your make-up contributions to the Thrift Savings Fund been made during the period of the error when they should have otherwise been made.
