
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 850 - ELECTRONIC RETIREMENT PROCESSING
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 850.103 Definitions.
####### Shared symmetric key cryptography

means a method of authentication in which a single key is used to sign and verify an electronic document. The single key (also known as a "private key") is known only by the user and the recipient or recipients of the electronic document.
