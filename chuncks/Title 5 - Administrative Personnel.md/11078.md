
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

###### ยง 2638.208 Written guidance on the executive branch ethics program.
####### Informal advisory opinions.

(c)Upon request or upon its own initiative, the Office of Government Ethics issues informal advisory opinions. Informal advisory opinions address subjects that in the opinion of the Director do not meet the criteria for issuance of formal advisory opinions. They are intended primarily to provide guidance to individuals and illustrate the application of government ethics laws and regulations to specific circumstances.
