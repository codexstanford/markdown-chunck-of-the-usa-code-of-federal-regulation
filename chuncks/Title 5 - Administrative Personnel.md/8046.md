
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter II - Merit Systems Protection Board
### Subchapter A - Organization and Procedures
#### PART 1205 - PRIVACY ACT REGULATIONS
##### Subpart B - Procedures for Obtaining Records
###### ยง 1205.13 Identification.
####### By mail.

(b)The identification of a requester making a request by mail must be certified by a notary public or equivalent official or contain other information to identify the requester. Information could be the date of birth of the requester and some item of information in the record that only the requester would be likely to know.
