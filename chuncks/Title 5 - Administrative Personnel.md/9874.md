##### ยง 2422.8 What is required to file an intervention or cross-petition?
###### Cross-petitions.

(a)A cross-petition is a petition that involves any employees in a unit covered by a pending representation petition. If you file a cross-petition, it must be filed under the requirements of this subpart.
