
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 410 - TRAINING
##### Subpart D - Paying for Training Expenses
###### ยง 410.402 Paying premium pay.
####### Continuation of premium pay.

(1)An employee given training during a period of duty for which he or she is already receiving premium pay for overtime, night, holiday, or Sunday work shall continue to receive that premium pay. This exception does not apply to an employee assigned to full-time training at institutions of higher learning.
