
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 178 - PROCEDURES FOR SETTLING CLAIMS
##### Subpart B - Settlement of Accounts for Deceased Civilian Officers and Employees
###### ยง 178.203 Designation of beneficiary.
####### Executing and filing a designation of beneficiary form.

(d)The Standard Form 1152 must be executed in duplicate by the employee and filed with the employing agency where the proper officer will sign it and insert the date of receipt in the space provided on each part, file the original, and return the duplicate to the employee. When a designation of beneficiary is changed or revoked, the employing agency should return the earlier designation to the employee, keeping a copy of only the current designation on file.
