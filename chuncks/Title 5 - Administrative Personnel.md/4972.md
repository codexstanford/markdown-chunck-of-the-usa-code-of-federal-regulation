
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 837 - REEMPLOYMENT OF ANNUITANTS
##### Subpart D - Reemployment of Disability Annuitants
###### ยง 837.403 Termination of annuity during reemployment.
####### Agency action.

(a)When a reemployed disability annuitant is found recovered from disability or restored to earning capacity by OPM, OPM shall terminate the annuity as of the date of the finding, and the employing agency shall cease reducing pay by the amount of annuity allocable to the period of reemployment effective that same date. If the appointment is subject to retirement deductions, retirement deductions will begin or continue, as the case may be.
