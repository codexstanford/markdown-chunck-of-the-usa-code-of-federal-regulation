
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 553 - REEMPLOYMENT OF CIVILIAN RETIREES TO MEET EXCEPTIONAL EMPLOYMENT NEEDS
##### Subpart B - Special Provisions for Reemployment Without Penalty To Meet Exceptional Recruiting or Retention Needs
###### ยง 553.201 Requesting OPM approval for reemployment without reduction or termination of annuity in individual cases.
####### Request by agency head.

(a)The head of an agency may request OPM to approve individual exceptions on a case-by-case basis to meet temporary hiring needs based on an emergency or other unusual circumstances or when the agency has encountered exceptional difficulty in recruiting or retaining a qualified candidate for a particular position. Authority to submit such a request may not be redelegated to an official below the agency's headquarters level.
