
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 332 - RECRUITMENT AND SELECTION THROUGH COMPETITIVE EXAMINATION
##### Subpart D - Consideration for Appointment
###### ยง 332.406 Objections to eligibles.
####### Agency's obligation while request for objection is pending.

(d)Subject to the exception set forth in paragraph (e) of this section, if an agency makes an objection against an applicant for a position (including seeking to pass over the applicant), and the individual that the agency wishes to select would be within reach of selection only if the objection is sustained, or the pass over granted, that agency may not make a selection for the position until a final ruling is made.
