#### PART 837 - REEMPLOYMENT OF ANNUITANTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 837.102 Definitions.
####### CSRS-Offset service

means service by a reemployed CSRS annuitant that is subject to the OASDI tax by operation of section 101 of Public Law 98-21. It does not include any service performed before January 1, 1984.
