##### ยง 2422.12 What circumstances does the Region consider to determine whether your petition is timely filed?
###### Contract bar where the contract is for three (3) years or less.

(d)Where a collective bargaining agreement is in effect covering the claimed unit and has a term of three (3) years or less from the date it became effective, a petition seeking an election will be considered timely if filed not more than one hundred and five (105) and not less than sixty (60) days before the expiration of the agreement.
