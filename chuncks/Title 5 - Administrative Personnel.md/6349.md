
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 875 - FEDERAL LONG TERM CARE INSURANCE PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - Administration and General Provisions
###### ยง 875.101 Definitions.
####### Stepparent

means any person, other than your mother or father, who is currently married to one of your parents, or, if one of your parents is dead, a person who was married to that parent at the time of that parent's death.
