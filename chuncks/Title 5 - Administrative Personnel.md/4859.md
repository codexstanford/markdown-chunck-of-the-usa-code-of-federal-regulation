
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

###### ยง 831.2001 Definitions.
####### Court order or decree

means the order or decree of any court of any State, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, Guam, the Northern Mariana Islands, the Virgin Islands or any Indian court, as defined section 8331(24) of title 5, United States Code.
