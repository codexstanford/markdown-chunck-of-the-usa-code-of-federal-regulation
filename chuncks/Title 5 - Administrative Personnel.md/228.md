
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 180 - EMPLOYEES' PERSONAL PROPERTY CLAIMS
##### ยง 180.105 Claims not allowed.
###### Articles acquired for other persons.

(8)Claims are not payable for articles intended directly or indirectly for persons other than the claimant or members of the claimant's immediate household. This prohibition includes articles acquired at the request of others and articles for sale.
