
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 890 - FEDERAL EMPLOYEES HEALTH BENEFITS PROGRAM
##### Subpart J - Administrative Sanctions Imposed Against Health Care Providers
###### Civil Monetary Penalties and Financial Assessments
####### § 890.1067 Provider contests of proposed penalties and assessments.
######## Contesting debarments and financial sanctions concurrently.

(b)If OPM proposes debarment and penalties and assessments in the same notice, the provider may contest both the debarment and the financial sanctions in the same proceeding. If the provider pursues a combined contest, the requirements set forth in §§ 890.1022 through 890.1024, as well as this section, apply.
