
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 733 - POLITICAL ACTIVITY - FEDERAL EMPLOYEES RESIDING IN DESIGNATED LOCALITIES
##### ยง 733.101 Definitions.
###### Political activity

means an activity directed toward the success or failure of a political party, candidate for partisan political office, or partisan political group.
