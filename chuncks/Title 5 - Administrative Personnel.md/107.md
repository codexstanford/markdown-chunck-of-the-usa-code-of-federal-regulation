
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter I - Office of Personnel Management
### Subchapter B - Civil Service Regulations
#### PART 178 - PROCEDURES FOR SETTLING CLAIMS
##### Subpart B - Settlement of Accounts for Deceased Civilian Officers and Employees
###### ยง 178.201 Scope of subpart.
####### Accounts covered.

(a)This subpart prescribes forms and procedures for the prompt settlement of accounts of deceased civilian officers and employees of the Federal Government and of the government of the District of Columbia (including wholly owned and mixed-ownership Government corporations), as stated in 5 U.S.C. 5581, 5582, 5583.
