##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 837.102 Definitions.
####### CSRS-Offset wages

means basic pay, as defined under 5 U.S.C. 8331(3), of an employee or Member performing CSRS-Offset service, but not to exceed the contribution and benefit base for the calendar year involved.
