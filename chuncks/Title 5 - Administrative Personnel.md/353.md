
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel

##### Subpart A - Applicability of Regulations; Definitions
###### ยง 210.102 Definitions.
####### Status quo employee

(16)means an employee who failed to acquire a competitive status when the position in which he was serving was placed in the competitive service by a statute, Executive order, or Civil Service rule, which permitted his retention without the acquisition of status.
