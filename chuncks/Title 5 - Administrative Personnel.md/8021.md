
# Title 5 - Administrative Personnel
## Chapter II - Merit Systems Protection Board
### Subchapter A - Organization and Procedures
#### PART 1204 - AVAILABILITY OF OFFICIAL INFORMATION
##### Subpart B - Procedures for Obtaining Records Under the Freedom of Information Act
###### ยง 1204.14 Requests for access to confidential commercial information.
####### Opportunity to object to disclosure.

(e)Through the notice described in paragraph (c) of this section, the Board will give a business submitter a reasonable period to provide a detailed statement of any objection to disclosure. The statement must specify all grounds for withholding any of the information under any exemption of the Freedom of Information Act. In addition, in the case of Exemption 4, the statement must state why the information is considered to be a trade secret, or to be commercial or financial information that is privileged or confidential. Information a business submitter provides under this paragraph may itself be subject to disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act.
