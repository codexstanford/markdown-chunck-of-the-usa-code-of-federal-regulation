
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 8 - FORFEITURE AUTHORITY FOR CERTAIN STATUTES
#### Subpart A - Seizure and Forfeiture of Property
##### ยง 8.2 Definitions.
###### Property subject to forfeiture

refers to all property that federal law authorizes to be forfeited to the United States of America in any administrative forfeiture proceeding, in any civil judicial forfeiture proceeding, or in any criminal forfeiture proceeding.
