
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter V - Bureau of Prisons, Department of Justice
### Subchapter B - Inmate Admission, Classification, and Transfer
#### PART 527 - TRANSFERS
##### Subpart D - Transfer of Inmates to State Agents for Production on State Writs
###### ยง 527.30 Purpose and scope.
####### ad prosequendum,ad testificandum.

The Bureau of Prisons will consider a request made on behalf of a state or local court that an inmate be transferred to the physical custody of state or local agents pursuant to state writ of habeas corpusorThe Warden at the institution in which the inmate is confined is authorized to approve this transfer in accordance with the provisions of this rule.
