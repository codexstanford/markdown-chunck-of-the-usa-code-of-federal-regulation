###### Limitation to circumstances in which a defendant is under a sentence of imprisonment for the offense.

(b)The requirement of section 3600A to preserve biological evidence secured in the investigation or prosecution of a Federal offense begins to apply when a defendant is convicted and sentenced to imprisonment for the offense, and ceases to apply when the defendant or defendants are released following such imprisonment.
