
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 73 - NOTIFICATIONS TO THE ATTORNEY GENERAL BY AGENTS OF FOREIGN GOVERNMENTS
#### ยง 73.1 Definition of terms.
##### agent

(a) The termmeans all individuals acting as representatives of, or on behalf of, a foreign government or official, who are subject to the direction or control of that foreign government or official, and who are not specifically excluded by the terms of the Act or the regulations thereunder.
