
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 21 - WITNESS FEES
#### ยง 21.2 Employees of the United States serving as witnesses.
##### Leave and attendance fee,Leave.

(e)-(1)An employee is considered to be in official duty status when appearing as a witness in his or her official capacity or on behalf of the United States in an unofficial capacity. An employee is entitled to court leave when he or she appears as a witness in an unofficial capacity not on behalf of the United States, and the United States, the District of Columbia, or a State or local government is a party to the case. An employee must use annual leave or leave without pay to appear as a witness when the United States, the District of Columbia, or a State or local government is not a party.
