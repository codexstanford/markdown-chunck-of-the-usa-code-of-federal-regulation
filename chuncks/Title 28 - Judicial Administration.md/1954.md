
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 32 - PUBLIC SAFETY OFFICERS' DEATH, DISABILITY, AND EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE BENEFIT CLAIMS
#### Subpart B - Death Benefit Claims
##### ยง 32.13 Definitions.
###### Participation in a training exercise

-A public safety officer participates (as a trainer or trainee) in a training exercise only when actually taking formal part in a structured activity that itself is-

(1) Within an official training (or -fitness) program of his public safety agency; and

(2) Mandatory, rated (i.e., officially tested, -graded, -judged, -timed, etc.), or directly supervised, -proctored, or -monitored.
