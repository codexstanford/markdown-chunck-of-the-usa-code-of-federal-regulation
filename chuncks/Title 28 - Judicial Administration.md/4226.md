
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration

#### Subpart C - Determining the Exact Language
##### ยง 55.13 Language used for oral assistance and publicity.
###### Language minority groups having more than one language.

(b)In some jurisdictions members of an applicable language minority group speak more than one language other than English. Where a jurisdiction is obligated to provide oral assistance in the language of such a group, the jurisdiction's obligation is to ascertain the languages that are commonly used by members of that group in the jurisdiction and to provide oral assistance in such languages.
