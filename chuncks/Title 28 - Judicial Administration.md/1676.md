
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 28 - DNA IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM
#### Subpart C - Preservation of Biological Evidence
##### ยง 28.22 The requirement to preserve biological evidence.
###### Applicability in general.

(a)The requirement of section 3600A to preserve biological evidence applies to evidence that has been retained in cases in which the offense or conviction occurred prior to the enactment of section 3600A or the adoption of this subpart, as well as to evidence secured in pending and future cases.
