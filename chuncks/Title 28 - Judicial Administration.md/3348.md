
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 38 - PARTNERSHIPS WITH FAITH-BASED AND OTHER NEIGHBORHOOD ORGANIZATIONS
#### Appendix A to Part 38 - Notice or Announcement of Award Opportunities
##### et seq.

(a) Faith-based organizations may apply for this award on the same basis as any other organization, as set forth at, and subject to the protections and requirements of this part and 42 U.S.C. 2000bbThe Department of Justice will not, in the selection of recipients, discriminate against an organization on the basis of the organization's religious character, exercise or affiliation.
