
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 38 - PARTNERSHIPS WITH FAITH-BASED AND OTHER NEIGHBORHOOD ORGANIZATIONS
#### ยง 38.4 Policy.
##### Political or religious affiliation.

(b)Decisions about awards of Federal financial assistance must be free from political interference or even the appearance of such interference and must be made on the basis of merit, not on the basis of religion, religious belief, or lack thereof.
