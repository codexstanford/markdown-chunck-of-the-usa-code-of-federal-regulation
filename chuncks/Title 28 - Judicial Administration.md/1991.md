
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 32 - PUBLIC SAFETY OFFICERS' DEATH, DISABILITY, AND EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE BENEFIT CLAIMS
#### Subpart D - Educational Assistance Benefit Claims
##### ยง 32.33 Definitions.
###### Financial need

-An individual is in financial need for a particular grading period to the extent that the amount of his eligible educational expenses for that period exceed the sum of-

(1) The amount of his educational assistance benefits as described in the Act, at 34 U.S.C. 10302(a)(3); and

(2) His expected family contribution calculated pursuant to 20 U.S.C. 1087nn (higher education assistance).
