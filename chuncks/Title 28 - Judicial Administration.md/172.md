
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 0 - ORGANIZATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE
#### Subpart S - Immigration and Naturalization Service
##### ยง 0.106 Certificates for expenses of unforeseen emergencies.
###### Provided,

The Commissioner of Immigration and Naturalization is authorized to exercise the power and authority vested in the Attorney General by section 6 of the act of July 28, 1950, 64 Stat. 380 (8 U.S.C. 1555), to make certificates with respect to expenses of unforeseen emergencies of a confidential character:That each such certificate made by the Commissioner of Immigration and Naturalization shall be approved by the Attorney General.
