
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration

### PART 55 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PROVISIONS OF THE VOTING RIGHTS ACT REGARDING LANGUAGE MINORITY GROUPS

##### ยง 55.6 Coverage under section 203(c).
###### Definitions.,limited-English proficient,Indian reservation,illiteracy

(b)For the purpose of determinations of coverage under section 203(c),means unable to speak or understand English adequately enough to participate in the electoral process;means any area that is an American Indian or Alaska Native area, as defined by the Census Bureau for the purposes of the 1990 decennial census; andmeans the failure to complete the fifth primary grade.
