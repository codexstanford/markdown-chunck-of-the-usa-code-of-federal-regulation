
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 40 - STANDARDS FOR INMATE GRIEVANCE PROCEDURES
#### Subpart A - Minimum Standards for Inmate Grievance Procedures
##### ยง 40.7 Operation and decision.
###### Review.

(f)The grievant shall be entitled to review by a person or other entity, not under the institution's supervision or control, of the disposition of all grievances, including alleged reprisals by an employee against an inmate. A request for review shall be allowed automatically without interference by administrators or employees of the institution and such review shall be conducted without influence or interference by administrators or employees of the institution.
