
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 5 - ADMINISTRATION AND ENFORCEMENT OF FOREIGN AGENTS REGISTRATION ACT OF 1938, AS AMENDED
#### ยง 5.2 Inquiries concerning application of the Act.
##### Anonymous, hypothetical, non-party and ex post facto review requests excluded.

(b)The entire transaction which is the subject of the review request must be an actual, as opposed to hypothetical, transaction and involve disclosed, as opposed to anonymous, agents and principals. Review requests must be submitted by a party to the transaction or the party's attorney, and have no application to a party that does not join in the request. A review request may not involve only past conduct.
