
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 8 - FORFEITURE AUTHORITY FOR CERTAIN STATUTES
#### Subpart A - Seizure and Forfeiture of Property
##### ยง 8.2 Definitions.
###### Appraised value

means the estimated market value of property at the time and place of seizure if such or similar property were freely offered for sale by a willing seller to a willing buyer.
