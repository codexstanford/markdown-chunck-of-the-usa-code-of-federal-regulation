##### ยง 2.15 Petition for consideration of parole prior to date set at hearing.

When a prisoner has served the minimum term of imprisonment required by law, the Bureau of Prisons may petition the responsible Regional Commissioner for reopening the case under ยง 2.28(a) and consideration for parole prior to the date set by the Commission at the initial or review hearing.
