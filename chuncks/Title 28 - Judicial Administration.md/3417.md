
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 40 - STANDARDS FOR INMATE GRIEVANCE PROCEDURES
#### Subpart A - Minimum Standards for Inmate Grievance Procedures
##### ยง 40.10 Records - nature; confidentiality.
###### Nature.

(a)Records regarding the filing and disposition of grievances shall be collected and maintained systematically by the institution. Such records shall be preserved for at least three years following final disposition of the grievance. At a minimum, such records shall include aggregate information regarding the numbers, types and dispositions of grievances, as well as individual records of the date of and the reasons for each disposition at each stage of the procedure.
