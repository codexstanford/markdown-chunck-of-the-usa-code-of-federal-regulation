
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 22 - CONFIDENTIALITY OF IDENTIFIABLE RESEARCH AND STATISTICAL INFORMATION
#### ยง 22.2 Definitions.
##### Research or statistical information

(d)means any information which is collected during the conduct of a research or statistical project and which is intended to be utilized for research or statistical purposes. The term includes information which is collected directly from the individual or obtained from any agency or individual having possession, knowledge, or control thereof.
