
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 48 - NEWSPAPER PRESERVATION ACT
#### ยง 48.2 Definitions.
##### joint newspaper operating arrangement,Provided,

(e) The termmeans any contract, agreement, joint venture (whether or not incorporated), or other arrangement entered into between two or more newspaper owners for the publication of two or more newspaper publications, pursuant to which joint or common production facilities are established or operated and joint or unified action is taken or agreed to be taken with respect to any of the following: Printing; time, method, and field of publication; allocation of production facilities; distribution; advertising solicitation; circulation solicitation; business department; establishment of advertising rates; establishment of circulation rates and revenue distribution:That there is no merger, combination, or amalgamation of editorial or reportorial staffs, and that editorial policies be independently determined.
