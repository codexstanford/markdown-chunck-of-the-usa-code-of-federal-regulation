
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 40 - STANDARDS FOR INMATE GRIEVANCE PROCEDURES
#### Subpart A - Minimum Standards for Inmate Grievance Procedures
##### ยง 40.7 Operation and decision.
###### Reasoned, written responses.

(d)Each grievance shall be answered in writing at each level of decision and review. The response shall state the reasons for the decision reached and shall include a statement that the inmate is entitled to further review, if such is available, and shall contain simple directions for obtaining such review.
