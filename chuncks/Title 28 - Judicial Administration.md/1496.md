
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 21 - WITNESS FEES
#### ยง 21.2 Employees of the United States serving as witnesses.
##### Payable by the Department of Justice.

(2)If an employee appears on behalf of the United States in an unofficial capacity in a judicial proceeding involving the Department of Justice, the employee's travel expenses are payable by the Department of Justice. The employing agency may advance or pay the travel expenses of the employee and later obtain reimbursement from the Department of Justice by submitting an appropriate bill together with a copy of the approved advance or travel voucher.
