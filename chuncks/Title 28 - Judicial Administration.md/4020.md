
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 51 - PROCEDURES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION OF SECTION 5 OF THE VOTING RIGHTS ACT OF 1965, AS AMENDED
#### Subpart D - Communications From Individuals and Groups
##### ยง 51.32 Establishment and maintenance of registry of interested individuals and groups.
###### et seq.,

The Attorney General shall establish and maintain a Registry of Interested Individuals and Groups, which shall contain the name and address of any individual or group that wishes to receive notice of section 5 submissions. Information relating to this registry and to the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552ais contained in JUSTICE/CRT-004. 48 FR 5334 (Feb. 4, 1983).
