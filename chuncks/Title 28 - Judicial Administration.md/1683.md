
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 28 - DNA IDENTIFICATION SYSTEM
#### Subpart C - Preservation of Biological Evidence
##### ยง 28.23 Evidence subject to the preservation requirement.
###### Biological evidence generally.

(a)The evidence preservation requirement of section 3600A applies to "biological evidence," which is defined in section 3600A(b). The covered evidence is sexual assault forensic examination kits under section 3600A(b)(1) and semen, blood, saliva, hair, skin tissue, or other identified biological material under section 3600A(b)(2).
