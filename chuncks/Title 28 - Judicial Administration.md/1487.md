
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 21 - WITNESS FEES
#### ยง 21.1 Definitions.
##### Pre-trial conference.

(d)A conference between the Government Attorney and a witness to discuss the witness' testimony. The conference must take place after a trial, hearing or grand jury proceeding has been scheduled but prior to the witness' actual appearance at the proceeding.
