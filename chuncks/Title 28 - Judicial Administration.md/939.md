## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 11 - DEBT COLLECTION

##### ยง 11.8 Salary offset.
###### Lump-sum deductions from final check.

(iii)A lump-sum deduction exceeding 15 percent of disposable pay may be made pursuant to 31 U.S.C. 3716 from any final salary payment due a former employee in order to liquidate a debt, whether the former employee was separated voluntarily or involuntarily.
