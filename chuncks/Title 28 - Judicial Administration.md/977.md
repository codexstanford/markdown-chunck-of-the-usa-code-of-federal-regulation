
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 11 - DEBT COLLECTION
#### Subpart D - Administrative Wage Garnishment
##### ยง 11.21 Administrative wage garnishment.
###### Withholding order

means any order for withholding or garnishment of pay issued by the agency, or judicial or administrative body. For purposes of this section, the terms "wage garnishment order" and "garnishment order" have the same meaning as "withholding order."
