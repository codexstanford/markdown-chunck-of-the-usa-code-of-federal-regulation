
# Title 28 - Judicial Administration
## Chapter I - Department of Justice
### PART 21 - WITNESS FEES
#### ยง 21.3 Aliens.
##### Doubtful cases.

(d)If the Immigration and Naturalization Service advises that the alien has admitted deportability, or that he or she was paroled into the United States for prosecution, or that deportation proceedings have been completed against the alien with a result favorable to the Government, no payment under 28 U.S.C. 1821 may be made.
