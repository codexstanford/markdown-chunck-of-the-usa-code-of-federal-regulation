
# Title 6 - Domestic Security
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security, Office of the Secretary
### PART 11 - CLAIMS
#### Subpart A - Debt Collection
##### ยง 11.4 Collection by administrative offset.
###### Salary offset process,Determination of disposable pay.

(g)-(1)The Chief Financial Officer consults with the appropriate DHS payroll office to determine the amount of a DHS employee's disposable pay and will implement salary offset when requested to do so by a DHS component or another federal agency. If the debtor is not employed by DHS, the agency employing the debtor will determine the amount of the employee's disposable pay and implement salary offset upon request.
