#### Subpart A - Debt Collection
##### ยง 11.10 Interest, penalty charges, and administrative costs.
###### Assessment and notice.

(a)DHS shall assess interest, penalties and administrative costs on DHS debts in accordance with 31 U.S.C. 3717 and 31 CFR 901.9. Administrative costs of processing and handling a delinquent debt shall be determined by DHS.
