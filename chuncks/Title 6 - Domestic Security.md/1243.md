
# Title 6 - Domestic Security
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security, Office of the Secretary

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 158.104 Definitions.
###### Cybersecurity talent market

means the availability, in terms of supply and demand, of talent relating to cybersecurity and employment relating to cybersecurity, including at other Federal agencies such as the Department of Defense.
