
# Title 6 - Domestic Security
## Chapter I - Department of Homeland Security, Office of the Secretary
### PART 11 - CLAIMS
#### Subpart A - Debt Collection
##### ยง 11.15 Applying the debt against DHS payments.
###### Transfer of employee debtor to another Federal agency.

(c)If an employee debtor transfers to another Federal agency before the debt is paid in full, DHS notifies the creditor agency and provides it a certification of the total amount of its collection on the debt. The creditor agency is responsible for submitting a certified claim to the debtor's new employing agency before collection may begin.
