(1) You may appeal any decision by the Board to deny, in whole or in part, your request under ยง 1002.3 no later than 60 days after the decision is rendered.

(2) Your appeal must be in writing, sent to the General Counsel at the address specified in ยง 1002.3(b) and contain the following information:
