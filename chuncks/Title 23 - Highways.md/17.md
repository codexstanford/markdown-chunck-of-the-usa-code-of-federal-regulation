##### ยง 1.11 Engineering services.
###### Governmental engineering organizations.

(b)The State highway department may utilize, under its supervision, the services of well-qualified and suitably equipped engineering organizations of other governmental instrumentalities for making surveys, preparing plans, specifications and estimates, and for supervising the construction of any project.
