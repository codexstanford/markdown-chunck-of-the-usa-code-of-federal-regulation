
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations
#### PART 655 - TRAFFIC OPERATIONS
##### Subpart F - Traffic Control Devices on Federal-Aid and Other Streets and Highways
###### ยง 655.603 Standards.
####### Compliance,Existing highways

(d)-(1). Each State, in cooperation with its political subdivisions, and Federal agency shall have a program as required by 23 U.S.C. 402(a), which shall include provisions for the systematic upgrading of substandard traffic control devices and for the installation of needed devices to achieve conformity with the MUTCD. The FHWA may establish target dates of achieving compliance with changes to specific devices in the MUTCD.
