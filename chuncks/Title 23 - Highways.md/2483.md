
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter H - Right-of-Way and Environment
#### PART 752 - LANDSCAPE AND ROADSIDE DEVELOPMENT
##### ยง 752.3 Definitions.
###### Safety rest area.

(a)A roadside facility safely removed from the traveled way with parking and such facilities for the motorist deemed necessary for his rest, relaxation, comfort and information needs. The term is synonymous with "rest and recreation areas."
