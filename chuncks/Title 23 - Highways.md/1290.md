
# Title 23 - Highways

##### Subpart B - Quality Assurance Procedures for Construction
###### ยง 637.203 Definitions.
####### Qualified laboratories.

Laboratories that are capable as defined by appropriate programs established by each STD. As a minimum, the qualification program shall include provisions for checking test equipment and the laboratory shall keep records of calibration checks.
