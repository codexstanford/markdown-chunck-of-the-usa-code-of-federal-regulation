##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 636.103 What are the definitions of terms used in this part?
####### Clarifications

means a written or oral exchange of information which takes place after the receipt of proposals when award without discussions is contemplated. The purpose of clarifications is to address minor or clerical revisions in a proposal.
