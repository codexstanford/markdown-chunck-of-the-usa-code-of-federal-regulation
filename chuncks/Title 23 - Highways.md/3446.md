
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter III - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, Department of Transportation
### PART 1300 - UNIFORM PROCEDURES FOR STATE HIGHWAY SAFETY GRANT PROGRAMS
#### Subpart C - National Priority Safety Program and Racial Profiling Data Collection Grants
##### ยง 1300.23 Impaired Driving Countermeasures Grants.
###### Standard offense for driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs

means the offense described in a State's statute that makes it a criminal offense to operate a motor vehicle while under the influence of alcohol or drugs, but does not require a measurement of alcohol or drug content.
