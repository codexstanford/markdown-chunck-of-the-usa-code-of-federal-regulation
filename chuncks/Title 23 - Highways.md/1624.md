
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations

##### Subpart E - National Tunnel Inspection Standards
###### ยง 650.505 Definitions.
####### Procedures.

The term "procedures" means the written documentation of policies, methods, considerations, criteria, and other conditions that direct the actions of personnel so that a desired end result is achieved consistently.
