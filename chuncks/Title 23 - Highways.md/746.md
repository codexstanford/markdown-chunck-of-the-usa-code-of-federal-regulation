
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter E - Planning and Research
#### PART 490 - NATIONAL PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT MEASURES
##### Subpart C - National Performance Management Measures for the Assessing Pavement Condition
###### ยง 490.305 Definitions.
####### Rutting

means longitudinal surface depressions in the pavement derived from measurements of a profile transverse to the path of travel on a highway lane. It may have associated transverse displacement.
