
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations
#### PART 668 - EMERGENCY RELIEF PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - Procedures for Federal-Aid Highways
###### ยง 668.103 Definitions.
####### Emergency repairs.

Those repairs including temporary traffic operations undertaken during or immediately following the disaster occurrence for the purpose of:

(1) Minimizing the extent of the damage,

(2) Protecting remaining facilities, or

(3) Restoring essential traffic.
