
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General Management and Administration
#### PART 1 - GENERAL
##### ยง 1.2 Definitions.
###### Project.

An undertaking by a State highway department for highway construction, including preliminary engineering, acquisition of rights-of-way and actual construction, or for highway planning and research, or for any other work or activity to carry out the provisions of the Federal laws for the administration of Federal aid for highways.
