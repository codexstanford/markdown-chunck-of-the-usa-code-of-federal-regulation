
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter L - Federal Lands Highways
#### PART 971 - FOREST SERVICE MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 971.104 Definitions.
####### Federal lands pavement management system (PMS)

means a systematic process used by the FS, FWS, and NPS that provides information for use in implementing cost-effective pavement reconstruction, rehabilitation, and preventive maintenance programs and policies, and that results in pavement designed to accommodate current and forecasted traffic in a safe, durable, and cost-effective manner.
