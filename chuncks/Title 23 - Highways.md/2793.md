
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter L - Federal Lands Highways
#### PART 970 - NATIONAL PARK SERVICE MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 970.104 Definitions.
####### Park Road Program transportation improvement program (PRPTIP)

means a staged, multi-year, multimodal program of NPS transportation projects in a State area. The PRPTIP is consistent with the NPS transportation plan and developed through the NPS planning processes pursuant to 23 U.S.C. 204.
