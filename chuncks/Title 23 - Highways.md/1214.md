
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations
#### PART 636 - DESIGN-BUILD CONTRACTING
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 636.103 What are the definitions of terms used in this part?
####### Prequalification

means the contracting agency's process for determining whether a firm is fundamentally qualified to compete for a certain project or class of projects. The prequalification process may be based on financial, management and other types of qualitative data. Prequalification should be distinguished from short listing.
