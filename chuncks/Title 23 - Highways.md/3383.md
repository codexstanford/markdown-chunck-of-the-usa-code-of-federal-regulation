
# Title 23 - Highways

###### Use of grant funds for unattended passengers program.

(d)The State must use a portion of grant funds received under Section 402 to carry out a program to educate the public regarding the risks of leaving a child or unattended passenger in a vehicle after the vehicle motor is deactivated by the operator.
