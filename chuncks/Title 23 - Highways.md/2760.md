
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter K - Intelligent Transportation Systems
#### PART 940 - INTELLIGENT TRANSPORTATION SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE AND STANDARDS
##### ยง 940.3 Definitions.
###### Region

is the geographical area that identifies the boundaries of the regional ITS architecture and is defined by and based on the needs of the participating agencies and other stakeholders. In metropolitan areas, a region should be no less than the boundaries of the metropolitan planning area.
