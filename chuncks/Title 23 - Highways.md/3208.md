
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter II - National Highway Traffic Safety Administration and Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter B - Guidelines
#### PART 1225 - OPERATION OF MOTOR VEHICLES BY INTOXICATED PERSONS
##### ยง 1225.7 Certification requirements for sanction program.
###### Certifications submitted in FY 2003.

(e)(1) Any State that submits a certification of compliance under ยง 1225.5 of this part, in conformance with the requirements of 23 U.S.C. 163, on or before July 15, 2003, will qualify for an incentive grant in FY 2003 and will avoid the withholding of funds in FY 2004.
