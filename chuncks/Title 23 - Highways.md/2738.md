
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter J - Highway Safety
#### PART 924 - HIGHWAY SAFETY IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM
##### ยง 924.3 Definitions.
###### Public road

means any highway, road, or street under the jurisdiction of and maintained by a public authority and open to public travel, including non-State-owned public roads and roads on tribal land.
