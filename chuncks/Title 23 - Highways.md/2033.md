
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations

###### ยง 660.103 Definitions.
####### Statewide transportation plan

means the official transportation plan that is: (1) Intermodal in scope, including bicycle and pedestrian features, (2) addresses at least a 20-year planning horizon, and (3) covers the entire State pursuant to the provisions of part 450 of this title.
