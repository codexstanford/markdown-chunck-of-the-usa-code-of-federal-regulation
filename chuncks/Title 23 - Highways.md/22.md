
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General Management and Administration
#### PART 1 - GENERAL
##### ยง 1.23 Rights-of-way.
###### Other use or occupancy.

(c)Subject to 23 U.S.C. 111, the temporary or permanent occupancy or use of right-of-way, including air space, for nonhighway purposes and the reservation of subsurface mineral rights within the boundaries of the rights-of-way of Federal-aid highways, may be approved by the Administrator, if he determines that such occupancy, use or reservation is in the public interest and will not impair the highway or interfere with the free and safe flow of traffic thereon.
