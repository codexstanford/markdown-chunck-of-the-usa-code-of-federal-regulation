
# Title 23 - Highways

##### Subpart E - Construction Manager/General Contractor (CM/GC) Contracting
###### ยง 635.502 Definitions.
####### Contracting agency

means the State Transportation Agency (STA), and any State or local government agency, public-private partnership, or Indian tribe (as defined in 2 CFR 200.54) that is the acting under the supervision of the STA and is awarding and administering a CM/GC contract.
