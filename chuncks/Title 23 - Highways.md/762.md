
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter E - Planning and Research
#### PART 490 - NATIONAL PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT MEASURES
##### Subpart D - National Performance Management Measures for Assessing Bridge Condition
###### ยง 490.409 Calculation of National performance management measures for assessing bridge condition.
####### Good:

(1)When the lowest rating of the 3 NBI items for a bridge (Items 58-Deck, 59-Superstructure, 60-Substructure) is 7, 8, or 9, the bridge will be classified as Good. When the rating of NBI item for a culvert (Item 62-Culverts) is 7, 8, or 9, the culvert will be classified as Good.
