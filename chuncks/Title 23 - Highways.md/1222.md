
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations
#### PART 636 - DESIGN-BUILD CONTRACTING
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 636.103 What are the definitions of terms used in this part?
####### Request for Qualification (RFQ)

means the document issued by the owner in Phase I of the two-phased selection process. It typically describes the project in enough detail to let potential offerors determine if they wish to compete and forms the basis for requesting qualifications submissions from which the most highly qualified offerors can be identified.
