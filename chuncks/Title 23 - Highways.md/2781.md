
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter L - Federal Lands Highways
#### PART 970 - NATIONAL PARK SERVICE MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS
##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 970.104 Definitions.
####### Federal lands congestion management system (CMS)

means a systematic process used by the NPS, the FWS and the FS for managing congestion that provides information on transportation system performance, and alternative strategies for alleviating congestion and enhancing the mobility of persons and goods to levels that meet Federal, State and local needs.
