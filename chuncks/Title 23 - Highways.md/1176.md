
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations

###### ยง 635.602 Definitions.
####### JOC, or Job order contracting,

means a form of ID/IQ contracting that uses a unit price book in the solicitation and the bidder's adjustment factors or multipliers to establish contract prices.
