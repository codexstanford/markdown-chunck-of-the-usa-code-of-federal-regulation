
# Title 23 - Highways

##### Subpart E - National Tunnel Inspection Standards
###### ยง 650.505 Definitions.
####### Tunnel inspection experience.

The term "tunnel inspection experience" means active participation in the performance of tunnel inspections in accordance with the National Tunnel Inspection Standards, in either a field inspection, supervisory, or management role.
