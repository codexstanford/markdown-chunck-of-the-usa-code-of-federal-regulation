
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General Management and Administration
#### PART 1 - GENERAL
##### ยง 1.2 Definitions.
###### Urban area.

An area including and adjacent to a municipality or other urban place having a population of five thousand or more, as determined by the latest available published official Federal census, decennial or special, within boundaries to be fixed by a State highway department, subject to the approval of the Administrator.
