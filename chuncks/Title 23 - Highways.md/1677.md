
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Engineering and Traffic Operations
#### PART 655 - TRAFFIC OPERATIONS
##### Subpart F - Traffic Control Devices on Federal-Aid and Other Streets and Highways
###### ยง 655.604 Achieving basic uniformity.
####### Programs.

(a)Programs for the orderly and systematic upgrading of existing traffic control devices or the installation of needed traffic control devices on or off the Federal-aid system should be based on inventories made in accordance with the Highway Safety Program Guideline 21, "Roadway Safety." These inventories provide the information necessary for programming traffic control device upgrading projects.
