
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter E - Planning and Research
#### PART 490 - NATIONAL PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT MEASURES
##### Subpart D - National Performance Management Measures for Assessing Bridge Condition
###### § 490.405 Definitions.
####### Structurally deficient,structurally deficient

as used in §§ 490.411 and 490.413 is a classification given to a bridge which has any component in Poor or worse condition or the adequacy of the waterway opening provided by the bridge is determined to be insufficient to the point of causing overtopping with intolerable traffic interruptions. Beginning with calendar year 2018 and thereafter,as used in §§ 490.411 and 490.413 is a classification given to a bridge which has any component in Poor or worse condition.
