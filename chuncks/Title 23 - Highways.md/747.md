
# Title 23 - Highways

### Subchapter E - Planning and Research

##### Subpart C - National Performance Management Measures for the Assessing Pavement Condition
###### ยง 490.305 Definitions.
####### Sampling

as applied to pavements, means measuring pavement conditions on a short section of pavement as a statistical representation for the entire section. Sampling is not to be used to measure or rate NHS pavement conditions.
