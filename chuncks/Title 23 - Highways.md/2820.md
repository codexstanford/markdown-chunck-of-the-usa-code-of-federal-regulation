
# Title 23 - Highways

##### Subpart A - Definitions
###### ยง 971.104 Definitions.
####### Forest Highway Program transportation improvement program (FHTIP)

means a staged, multiyear, multimodal program of transportation projects in a State area consistent with the FH transportation plan and developed through the tri-party FH planning processes pursuant to 23 U.S.C. 204, and 23 CFR 660 subpart A.
