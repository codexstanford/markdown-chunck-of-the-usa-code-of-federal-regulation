
# Title 23 - Highways
## Chapter I - Federal Highway Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter J - Highway Safety
#### PART 924 - HIGHWAY SAFETY IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM
##### ยง 924.3 Definitions.
###### Public railway-highway crossing

means a railway-highway crossing where the roadway (including associated sidewalks, pathways, and shared use paths) is under the jurisdiction of and maintained by a public authority and open to public travel, including non-motorized users. All roadway approaches must be under the jurisdiction of a public roadway authority, and no roadway approach may be on private property.
