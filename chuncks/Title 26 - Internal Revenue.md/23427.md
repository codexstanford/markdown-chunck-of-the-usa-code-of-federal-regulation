####### Procedures for obtaining approval to use substitute mortality tables,Written request for approval to use substitute mortality tables,General requirements.

(b)-(1)-(i)To use substitute mortality tables, a plan sponsor must first submit a written request to the Commissioner demonstrating that those substitute mortality tables meet the requirements of section 430(h)(3)(C) and this section. This request must specify the first plan year, and the term of years (not more than 10), for which the tables are to apply.
