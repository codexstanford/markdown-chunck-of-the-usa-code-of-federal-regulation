####### Example 26. Items attributable to a combined separate unit,Facts.

(26)-(i)P owns DE1. DE1owns a 50 percent interest in PRS, a Country Z entity that is classified as a partnership both for Country Z tax purposes and for U.S. tax purposes. FS, which is unrelated to P, owns the remaining 50 percent interest in PRS. PRScarries on operations in Country X that, if carried on by a U.S. person, would constitute a foreign branch as defined in ยง 1.367(a)-6T(g)(1). Therefore, P's share of the Country X operations carried on by PRSconstitutes a foreign branch separate unit. PRSalso owns assets that do not constitute a part of its Country X branch, including all of the interests in TE, a disregarded entity. TEis an entity incorporated under the laws of Country T, a country that does not have an income tax. Under the laws of Country X, an interest holder of TEdoes not take into account on a current basis the interest holder's share of items of income, gain, deduction, and loss of TE.
