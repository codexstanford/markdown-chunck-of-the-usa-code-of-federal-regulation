####### Term of loan.,1,1

(C)The term of a split-dollar loan described in paragraph (e)(5)(iii)(A)() of this section is based on the period from the date the loan is made until the loan's stated maturity date. However, if a split-dollar loan described in paragraph (e)(5)(iii)(A)() of this section does not have a stated maturity date, the term of the loan is presumed to be seven years.
