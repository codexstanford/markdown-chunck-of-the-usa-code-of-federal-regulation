
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Other Insurance Companies
###### ยง 1.848-2 Determination of net premiums.
####### Policy enhancement or update program defined.

(B)For purposes of paragraph (c)(4)(iii)(A) of this section, a policy enhancement or update program means any offer or commitment by the insurance company to all of the policyholders holding a particular policy form to change the interest, mortality, morbidity, or expense guarantees used to determine the contract's nonforfeiture benefits.
