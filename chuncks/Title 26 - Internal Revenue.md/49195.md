
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.1411-3 Application to estates and trusts.
####### Accumulated net investment income.

(iii)The accumulated net investment income of a CRT is the total amount of net investment income received by a CRT for all taxable years that begin after December 31, 2012, less the total amount of net investment income distributed for all prior taxable years of the trust that begin after December 31, 2012.
