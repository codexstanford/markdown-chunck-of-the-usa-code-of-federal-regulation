
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 53.4960-3 Determination of whether there is a parachute payment.
####### Present value.

(6)For purposes of this paragraph (f), the present value of a payment is determined based on the payment date absent the acceleration and the date on which the accelerated payment is scheduled to be made. The amount that is treated as contingent on the separation from employment is the amount by which the present value of the accelerated payment exceeds the present value of the payment absent the acceleration.
