
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Empowerment Zone Employment Credit
###### ยง 1.1396-1 Qualified zone employees.
####### Calendar year method,Relevant period.

(2)-(i)Under the calendar year method, the relevant period for an employee is the entire calendar year with respect to which the credit is being claimed. However, for any employee who is employed by the employer for less than the entire calendar year, the relevant period is the portion of that calendar year during which the employee is employed by the employer.
