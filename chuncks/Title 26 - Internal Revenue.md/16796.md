####### Certain section 351 exchanges treated as purchases,In general,Treatment of stock received by transferor.

(3)-(i)-(A)Under section 355(d)(5)(B), a purchase includes any acquisition of property in an exchange to which section 351 applies to the extent the property is acquired in exchange for any cash or cash item, any marketable stock, or any debt of the transferor. The property treated as acquired by purchase is the property received by the transferor in the exchange.
