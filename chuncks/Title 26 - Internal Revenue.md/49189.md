
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.1411-3 Application to estates and trusts.
####### Example.

(3)(i) In Year 1 (a year that section 1411 is in effect), the non-S portion of Trust, an ESBT, has dividend income of $15,000, interest income of $10,000, and capital loss of $5,000.
