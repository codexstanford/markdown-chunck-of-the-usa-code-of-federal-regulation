
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.5000A-1 Maintenance of minimum essential coverage and liability for the shared responsibility payment.
####### Liability for shared responsibility payment,In general.

(c)-(1)A taxpayer is liable for the shared responsibility payment for a month for which-

(i) The taxpayer is a nonexempt individual without minimum essential coverage; or

(ii) A nonexempt individual for whom the taxpayer is liable under paragraph (c)(2) or (c)(3) of this section does not have minimum essential coverage.
