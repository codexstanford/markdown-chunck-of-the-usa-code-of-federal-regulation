
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### inventories
###### ยง 1.475(b)-2 Exemptions - identification requirements.
####### Identification of the basis for exemption.

(a)An identification of a security as exempt from mark to market does not satisfy section 475(b)(2) if it fails to state whether the security is described in-

(1) Either of the first two subparagraphs of section 475(b)(1) (identifying a security as held for investment or not held for sale); or

(2) The third subparagraph thereof (identifying a security as a hedge).
