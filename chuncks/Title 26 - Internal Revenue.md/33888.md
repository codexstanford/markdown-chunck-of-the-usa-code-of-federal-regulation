
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### REGULATED INVESTMENT COMPANIES AND REAL ESTATE INVESTMENT TRUSTS
###### ยง 1.852-11 Treatment of certain losses attributable to periods after October 31 of a taxable year.
####### October 31 treated as last day of taxable year for purpose of determining taxable income under certain circumstances,In general.

(3)-(i)If a regulated investment company has a post-October capital loss for a taxable year, any item that must be marked to market for purposes of section 4982 on October 31 as if it were the last day of the regulated investment company's taxable year must also be marked to market on October 31 and again on the last day of the regulated investment company's taxable year for purposes of determining its taxable income. If the regulated investment company does not have a post-October capital loss for a taxable year, the regulated investment company must treat items that must be marked to market for purposes of section 4982 on October 31 as if it were the last day of the regulated investment company's taxable year as marked to market only on the last day of its taxable year for purposes of determining its taxable income.
