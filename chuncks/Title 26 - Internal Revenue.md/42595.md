
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.987-2 Attribution of items to eligible QBUs; definition of a transfer and related rules.
####### Transfers of liabilities to and from section 987 QBUs owned through section 987 aggregate partnerships,Assumptions of partner liabilities.

(4)-(i)Solely for purposes of section 987, a liability of the owner of a section 987 aggregate partnership shall be treated as transferred to a section 987 QBU of a partner if, and to the extent, the section 987 aggregate partnership assumes such liability, provided that, immediately prior to the transfer, the liability is not reflected on the books and records of the section 987 QBU within the meaning of paragraph (b) of this section, and the liability is reflected on the books and records of the section 987 QBU immediately following the transfer.
