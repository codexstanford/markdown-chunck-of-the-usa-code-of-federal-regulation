
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.197-2 Amortization of goodwill and certain other intangibles.
####### Special rules for persons not otherwise subject to Federal income tax.

(xi)If the person making the election under this paragraph (h)(9) with respect to a disposition is not otherwise subject to Federal income tax, the election statement satisfying the requirements of paragraph (h)(9)(viii) of this section must be filed with the Philadelphia Service Center. For purposes of this paragraph (h)(9) and subtitle F, the statement is treated as an income tax return for the calendar year in which the disposition occurs and as a return due on or before March 15 of the following year.
