####### Foreign government-related individual,In general.

(2)-(i)A foreign government-related individual is an individual (and that individual's immediate family) who is temporarily present in the United States-

(A) As a full-time employee of an international organization;

(B) By reason of diplomatic status; or

(C) By reason of a visa that the Secretary of the Treasury or his or her delegate (after consultation with the Secretary of State when appropriate) determines represents full-time diplomatic or consular status. An individual described in this paragraph shall be considered to be temporarily present in the United States if the individual is not a lawful permanent resident as described in ยง 301.7701(b)-1(b)(1), regardless of the actual amount of time that the individual is present in the United States.
