
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 143 - TEMPORARY EXCISE TAX REGULATIONS UNDER THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1969
##### ยง 143.2 Taxes on self-dealing; scholarship and fellowship grants by private foundations.
###### In general.

(a)Section 4941(d)(1)(D) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954 as added by section 101(b) of the Tax Reform Act of 1969 (83 Stat. 500) provides that the term "self-dealing" includes any direct or indirect payment of compensation (or payment or reimbursement of expenses) by a private foundation to a disqualified person. Section 4941(d)(1)(E) provides that the term "self-dealing" includes any direct or indirect transfer to, or use by, or for the benefit of, a disqualified person of the income or assets of a private foundation.
