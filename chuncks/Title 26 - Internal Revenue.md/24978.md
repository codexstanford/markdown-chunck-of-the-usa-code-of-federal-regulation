
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.451-8 Advance payments for goods, services, and certain other items.
####### Taxpayers eligible to use the non-AFS deferral method.

(2)A taxpayer is eligible to use the non-AFS deferral method if the taxpayer does not have an applicable financial statement and can determine the extent to which advance payments are earned in the taxable year of receipt and, if applicable, a short taxable year described in paragraph (d)(6) of this section. The determination whether the advance payment is earned in the taxable year of receipt, or a short taxable year described in paragraph (d)(6) of this section, if applicable, is determined on an item by item basis.
