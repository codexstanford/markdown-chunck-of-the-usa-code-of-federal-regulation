###### ยง 1.45Q-1 Credit for Carbon Oxide Sequestration.
####### Equipment placed in service before February 9, 2018.

(i)In the case of qualified carbon oxide captured using carbon capture equipment that is originally placed in service at a qualified facility before February 9, 2018, the section 45Q credit is attributable to the person that captures and physically or contractually ensures the disposal, injection, or utilization of such qualified carbon oxide.
