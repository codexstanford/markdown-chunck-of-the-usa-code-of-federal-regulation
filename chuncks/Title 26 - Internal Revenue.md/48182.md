###### ยง 1.1394-1 Enterprise zone facility bonds.
####### Original use requirement for purposes of qualified zone property.,original use

(h)In general, for purposes of section 1397C(a)(1)(B), the termmeans the first use to which the property is put within the zone. For purposes of section 1394, if property is vacant for at least a one-year period including the date of zone designation, use prior to that period is disregarded for purposes of determining original use. For this purpose, de minimis incidental uses of property, such as renting the side of a building for a billboard, are disregarded.
