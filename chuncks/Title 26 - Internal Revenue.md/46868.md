
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Special Rules for Determining Capital Gains and Losses
###### ยง 1.1295-3 Retroactive elections.
####### 2,Special rules,i,Addition of partner to non-TEFRA partnership.,1

()-()In the case of any individual who becomes a partner in a domestic partnership other than a TEFRA partnership (non-TEFRA partnership) in a taxable year subsequent to the year in which the partnership filed a Protective Statement, the partner and the partnership must comply with the rules applicable to non-TEFRA partnerships, as provided in paragraph (c)(4)(i)(B)() of this section, by the due date, as extended, for the Federal income tax return of the partnership for the taxable year during which the individual became a partner. Failure to so comply will render the Protective Statement invalid with respect to the partnership and partners.
