
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Determination of Sources of Income
###### ยง 1.861-12 Characterization rules and adjustments for certain assets.
####### Example 1: Capitalized interest under section 263A,Facts.

(i)-(A)X is a domestic corporation that uses the tax book value method of apportionment. X has $1,000x of indebtedness and incurs $100x of interest expense. Using $800x of the $1,000x debt proceeds to produce tangible property, X capitalizes $80x of interest expense under the rules of section 263A. X deducts the remaining $20x of interest expense.
