###### ยง 1.7704-4 Qualifying income - mineral and natural resources.
####### Transportation to retail customers or to a place that sells to retail customers.

(ii)Transportation includes the movement of minerals or natural resources, and products under paragraph (c)(4), (5), or (6) of this section, via pipeline to a place that sells to retail customers. Transportation also includes the movement of liquefied petroleum gas via trucks, rail cars, or pipeline to a place that sells to retail customers or directly to retail customers.
