
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

####### Distribution of net assets.

(6)A private foundation will meet the requirement to "distribute all of its net assets" within the meaning of section 507(b)(1)(A) only if it transfers all of its right, title, and interest in and to all of its net assets to one or more organizations referred to in section 507(b)(1)(A).
