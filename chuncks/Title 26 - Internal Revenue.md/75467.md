
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### ยง 301.7433-1 Civil cause of action for certain unauthorized collection actions.
######## Actual, direct economic damages,Definition.

(b)-(1)Actual, direct economic damages are actual pecuniary damages sustained by the taxpayer as the proximate result of the reckless or intentional, or negligent, actions of an officer or an employee of the Internal Revenue Service. Injuries such as inconvenience, emotional distress and loss of reputation are compensable only to the extent that they result in actual pecuniary damages.
