###### ยง 1.465-8 General rules; interest other than that of a creditor.
####### Nonrecourse loans secured by assets with a readily ascertainable fair market value,General rule.

(c)-(1)This paragraph shall apply in the case of a nonrecourse loan for use in an activity where the loan is secured by property which has a readily ascertainable fair market value. In the case of such a loan a person shall be considered a person with an interest in the activity other than that of a creditor only if the person has either a capital interest in the activity or an interest in the net profits of the activity.
