####### Pre-realization recapture events.

(B)The foreign tax is imposed upon the occurrence of an event before a realization event (a "pre-realization event") that results in the recapture (in whole or part) of a tax deduction, tax credit, or other tax allowance previously accorded to the taxpayer (for example, the recapture of an incentive tax credit if required investments are not completed within a specified period).
