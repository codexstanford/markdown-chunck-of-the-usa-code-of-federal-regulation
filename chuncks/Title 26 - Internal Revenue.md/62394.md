
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 20.2056(d)-2 Marital deduction; effect of disclaimers of post-December 31, 1976 transfers.
####### Disclaimer by a person other than a surviving spouse.

(b)If an interest in property passes from a decedent to a person other than the surviving spouse, and the interest is created in a transfer made after December 31, 1976, and-

(1) The person other than the surviving spouse makes a qualified disclaimer with respect to such interest; and

(2) The surviving spouse is entitled to such interest in property as a result of such disclaimer, the disclaimed interest is treated as passing directly from the decedent to the surviving spouse.
