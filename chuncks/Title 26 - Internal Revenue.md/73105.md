####### ยง 301.6225-3 Treatment of partnership adjustments that do not result in an imputed underpayment.
######## Treatment of adjustments by the partnership,In general.

(b)-(1)Except as described in paragraphs (b)(2) through (7) of this section, a partnership adjustment that does not result in an imputed underpayment is taken into account as part of non-separately stated income or loss for the adjustment year depending on whether the adjustment is to a partnership-related item that is an item of income or loss.
