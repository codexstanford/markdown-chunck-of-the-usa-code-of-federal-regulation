
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### export trade corporations
###### § 1.971-1 Definitions with respect to export trade corporations.
####### Unrelated person.

(f)For purposes of sections 970 through 972 and §§ 1.970-1 to 1.972-1, inclusive, the term "unrelated person" means a person other than a related person as defined in section 954(d)(3) and paragraph (e) of § 1.954-1.
