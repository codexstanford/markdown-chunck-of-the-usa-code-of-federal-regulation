####### ยง 301.6103(k)(6)-1 Disclosure of return information by certain officers and employees for investigative purposes.
######## Disclosure of return information to the extent necessary,necessary,necessary

(1)means a disclosure of return information which an internal revenue or TIGTA employee, based on the facts and circumstances, at the time of the disclosure, reasonably believes is necessary to obtain information to perform properly the official duties described by this section, or to accomplish properly the activities connected with carrying out those official duties. The termin this context does not mean essential or indispensable, but rather appropriate and helpful in obtaining the information sought. Nor doesin this context refer to the necessity of conducting an investigation or the appropriateness of the means or methods chosen to conduct the investigation. Section 6103(k)(6) does not limit or restrict internal revenue or TIGTA employees with respect to the decision to initiate or the conduct of an investigation. Disclosures under this paragraph (c)(1), however, may not be made indiscriminately or solely for the benefit of the recipient or as part of a negotiated quid pro quo arrangement.
