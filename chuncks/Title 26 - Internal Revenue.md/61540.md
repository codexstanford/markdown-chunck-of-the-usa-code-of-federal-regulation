
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 7 - TEMPORARY INCOME TAX REGULATIONS UNDER THE TAX REFORM ACT OF 1976
##### ยง 7.999-1 Computation of the international boycott factor.
###### Payroll paid or accrued for services performed in a country.

(8)The terms "payroll paid or accrued for services performed in a boycotting country" and "payroll paid or accrued for services performed in any country other than the United States" mean, in respect of a particular country, the total amount paid or accrued as compensation to officers and employees, including wages, salaries, commissions, and bonuses, for services performed in that country.
