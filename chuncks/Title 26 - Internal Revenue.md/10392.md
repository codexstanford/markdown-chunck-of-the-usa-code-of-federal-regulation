
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.170A-9 Definition of section 170(b)(1)(A) organization.
####### Hospitals and medical research organizations,Hospitals.

(d)-(1)An organization (other than one described in paragraph (d)(2) of this section) is described in section 170(b)(1)(A)(iii) if-

(i) It is a hospital; and

(ii) Its principal purpose or function is the providing of medical or hospital care or medical education or medical research.
