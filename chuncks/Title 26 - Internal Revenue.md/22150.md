
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Pension, Profit-Sharing, Stock Bonus Plans, etc.
###### § 1.414(c)-6 Effective date.
####### Existing plans.

(b)In the case of a plan in existence on January 1, 1974, unless paragraph (c) of this section applies, the provisions of "§ 1.414 (b)-1 and §§ 1.414(c)-1 through 1.414(c)-4 shall apply for plan years beginning after December 31, 1975. For definition of the term "existing plan", see § 1.410(a)-2(c).
