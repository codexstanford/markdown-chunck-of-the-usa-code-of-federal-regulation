####### Example 6: Minimum Tax,Facts.

(vi)-(A)Country X imposes a net income tax ("Income Tax") and a minimum tax ("Minimum Tax") on its residents. Under Country X tax law, alternative minimum taxable income for purposes of the Minimum Tax equals the taxable income under the Income Tax increased by certain disallowed deductions. The Minimum Tax equals the excess, if any, of the alternative minimum taxable income times the Minimum Tax rate over the amount of the Income Tax.
