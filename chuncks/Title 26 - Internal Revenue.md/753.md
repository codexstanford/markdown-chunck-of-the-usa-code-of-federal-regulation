
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.41-4A Qualified research for taxable years beginning before January 1, 1986.
####### Funding determinable only in subsequent taxable year.

(5)If at the time the taxpayer files its return for a taxable year, it is impossible to determine to what extent particular research performed by the taxpayer during that year may be funded, then the taxpayer shall treat the research as completely funded for purposes of completing that return. When the amount of funding is finally determined, the taxpayer should amend the return and any interim returns to reflect the proper amount of funding.
