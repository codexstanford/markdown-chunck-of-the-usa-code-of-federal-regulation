
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Information and Returns

####### ยง 301.6109-1 Identifying numbers.
######## In general,Taxpayer identifying numbers,Principal types.

(a)-(1)-(i)There are several types of taxpayer identifying numbers that include the following: social security numbers, Internal Revenue Service (IRS) individual taxpayer identification numbers, IRS adoption taxpayer identification numbers, and employer identification numbers. Social security numbers take the form 000-00-0000. IRS individual taxpayer identification numbers and IRS adoption taxpayer identification numbers also take the form 000-00-0000 but include a specific number or numbers designated by the IRS. Employer identification numbers take the form 00-0000000.
