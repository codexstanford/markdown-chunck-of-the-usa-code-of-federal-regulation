###### ยง 1.460-4 Methods of accounting for long-term contracts.
####### Estimating total contract price.

(ii)A taxpayer must estimate the total contract price based upon all the facts and circumstances known as of the last day of the taxable year. For this purpose, an event that occurs after the end of the taxable year must be taken into account if its occurrence was reasonably predictable and its income was subject to reasonable estimation as of the last day of that taxable year.
