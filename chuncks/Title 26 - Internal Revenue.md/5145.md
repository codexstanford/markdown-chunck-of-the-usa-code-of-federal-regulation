
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Items Specifically Excluded From Gross Income
###### ยง 1.127-2 Qualified educational assistance program.
####### Educational assistance,In general.

(c)-(1)The benefits provided under the program must consist solely of educational assistance. The term "educational assistance" means-

(i) The employer's payment of expenses incurred by or on behalf of an employee for education, or

(ii) The employer's provision of education to an employee.
