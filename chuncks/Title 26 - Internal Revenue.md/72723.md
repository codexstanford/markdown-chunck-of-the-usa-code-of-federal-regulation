##### Time and Place for Paying Tax

####### ยง 301.6159-1 Agreements for payment of tax liabilities in installments.
######## Biennial review of partial payment installment agreements.

(i)The Commissioner shall perform a review of the taxpayer's financial condition in the case of a partial payment installment agreement at least once every two years. The purpose of this review is to determine whether the taxpayer's financial condition has significantly changed so as to warrant an increase in the value of the payments being made or termination of the agreement.
