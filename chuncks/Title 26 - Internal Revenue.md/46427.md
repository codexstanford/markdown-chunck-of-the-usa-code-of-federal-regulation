
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Special Rules for Determining Capital Gains and Losses
###### ยง 1.1275-2 Special rules relating to debt instruments.
####### Remote contingencies.

(2)A contingency is remote if there is a remote likelihood either that the contingency will occur or that the contingency will not occur. If there is a remote likelihood that the contingency will occur, it is assumed that the contingency will not occur. If there is a remote likelihood that the contingency will not occur, it is assumed that the contingency will occur.
