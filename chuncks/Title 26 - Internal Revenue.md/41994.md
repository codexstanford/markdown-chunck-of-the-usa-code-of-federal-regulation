###### ยง 1.965-7 Elections, payment, and other special rules.
####### 6,Leverage ratio.,ix,leverage ratio

()For purposes of paragraph (b)(3)(iii)(B)(4)() of this section, and except as otherwise provided in publications, forms, instructions, or other guidance, the termmeans the ratio that the total indebtedness of the eligible section 965(h) transferee bears to the sum of its money and all other assets reduced (but not below zero) by such total indebtedness. For this purpose, the amount taken into account with respect to any asset is the adjusted basis thereof for purposes of determining gain, and the amount taken into account with respect to any indebtedness with original issue discount is its issue price plus the portion of the original issue discount previously accrued as determined under the rules of section 1272 (determined without regard to subsection (a)(7) or (b)(4) thereof).
