
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.6012-2 Corporations required to make returns of income.
####### Charitable and other organizations with unrelated business income.

(e)Every organization described in section 511(a)(2) which is subject to the tax imposed by section 511(a)(1) on its unrelated business taxable income shall make a return on Form 990-T for each taxable year if it has gross income, included in computing unrelated business taxable income for such taxable year, of $1,000 or more. The filing of a return of unrelated business income does not relieve the organization of the duty of filing other required returns.
