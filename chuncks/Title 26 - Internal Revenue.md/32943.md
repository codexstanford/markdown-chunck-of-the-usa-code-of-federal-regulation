###### ยง 1.752-2 Partner's share of recourse liabilities.
####### Partner guarantee with right of subrogation.,i.e.,

(4)G, a limited partner in the GH partnership, guarantees a portion of a partnership liability. The liability is a general obligation of the partnership,no partner has been relieved from personal liability. If under state law G is subrogated to the rights of the lender, G would have the right to recover the amount G paid to the recourse lender from the general partner. Therefore, G does not bear the economic risk of loss for the partnership liability.
