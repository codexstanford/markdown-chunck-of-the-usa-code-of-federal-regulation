###### ยง 1.921-1T Temporary regulations providing transition rules for DISCs and FSCs.
####### A-5:

The dollar limitations are to be prorated on a daily basis. Thus, for example, if for its 1985 taxable year a small FSC has a short taxable year of 73 days, then in determining exempt foreign trade income, any foreign trading gross receipts that exceed $1 million (73/365 x $5 million) will not be taken into account.
