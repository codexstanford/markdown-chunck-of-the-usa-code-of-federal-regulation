
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### § 1.45Q-2 Definitions for Purposes of §§ 1.45Q-1 through 1.45Q-5.
####### Direct air capture facility.,direct air capture facility

(f)Ameans any facility that uses carbon capture equipment to capture carbon oxide directly from the ambient air. It does not include any facility that captures carbon dioxide (1) that is deliberately released from naturally occurring subsurface springs or (2) using natural photosynthesis.
