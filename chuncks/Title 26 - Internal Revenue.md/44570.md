
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Special Rules
###### ยง 1.1061-2 Applicable partnership interests and applicable trades or businesses.
####### Example 2: Combining Specified Actions in multiple entities.

(ii)GP, a partnership, conducts Raising or Returning Capital Actions. Management Company, a partnership that is a Related Person to GP, conducts Investing or Developing Actions. When GP's and Management Company's activities are combined, the ATB Activity Test is satisfied. Accordingly, both GP and Management Company are engaged in an ATB, and services performed by either GP or Management Company are performed in an ATB under paragraph (b)(1) of this section.
