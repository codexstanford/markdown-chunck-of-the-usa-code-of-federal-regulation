
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Appeal of rejection of an offer to compromise,In general.

(5)-(i)The taxpayer may administratively appeal a rejection of an offer to compromise to the IRS Office of Appeals (Appeals) if, within the 30-day period commencing the day after the date on the letter of rejection, the taxpayer requests such an administrative review in the manner provided by the Secretary.
