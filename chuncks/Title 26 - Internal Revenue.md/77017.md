
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter F - Procedure and Administration
#### PART 403 - DISPOSITION OF SEIZED PERSONAL PROPERTY
##### Subpart D - Remission or Mitigation of Forfeitures
###### ยง 403.44 Acquisition for official use and sale for account of petitioner in the case of an allowed petition.
####### Acquisition for official use.

(a)The seized property may be purchased by the United States pursuant to agreement and retained for official use. Where the petitioner is the owner, the purchase price is the appraised value of the property less all costs. Where the petitioner is a creditor, the purchase price is the smaller of:

(1) The petitioner's equity, or (2) the appraised value of the property less the amount of all costs.
