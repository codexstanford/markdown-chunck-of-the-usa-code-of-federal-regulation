
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.1502-75 Filing of consolidated returns.
####### Method of filing return and forms,Consolidated return made by common parent corporation.

(h)-(1)The consolidated return shall be made on Form 1120 for the group by the common parent corporation. The consolidated return, with Form 851 (affiliations schedule) attached, shall be filed with the district director with whom the common parent would have filed a separate return.
