
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Exempt Organizations
###### ยง 1.501(c)(4)-1 Civic organizations and local associations of employees.
####### Civic organizations,In general.

(a)-(1)A civic league or organization may be exempt as an organization described in section 501(c)(4) if-

(i) It is not organized or operated for profit; and

(ii) It is operated exclusively for the promotion of social welfare.
