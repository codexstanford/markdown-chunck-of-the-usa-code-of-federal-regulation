###### ยง 1.336-1 General principles, nomenclature, and definitions for a section 336(e) election.
####### 80-percent purchaser.

(16)An 80-percent purchaser is any purchaser that, after application of the attribution rules of section 318(a), other than section 318(a)(4), owns 80 percent or more of the voting power or value of target stock.
