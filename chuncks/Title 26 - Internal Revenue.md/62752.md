
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter B - Estate and Gift Taxes

####### Death resulting from common accidents.

(iii)The mortality component prescribed under section 7520 may not be used to determine the present value of an annuity, income interest, remainder interest, or reversionary interest if the decedent, and the individual who is the measuring life, die as a result of a common accident or other occurrence.
