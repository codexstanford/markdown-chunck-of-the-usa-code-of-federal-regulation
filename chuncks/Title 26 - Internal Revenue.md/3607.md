####### Exception for entertainment flights provided to specified individuals after October 22, 2004.

(iii)Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph (g)(14)(i) of this section, an employer may use the general valuation rules of paragraph (b) of this section to value the entertainment use of an aircraft provided after October 22, 2004, to a specified individual.
