The interest imposed on an imputed underpayment under paragraph (b) of this section begins on the day after the due date of the partnership return (without regard to extension) for the reviewed year and ends on the earlier of-

(i) The date prescribed for payment (as described in ยง 301.6232-1(b));

(ii) The due date of the partnership return (without regard to extension) for the adjustment year (as defined in ยง 301.6241-1(a)(1)); or

(iii) The date the imputed underpayment is fully paid.
