####### Reportable policy sale payment recipient,reportable policy sale payment recipient

(16)-(i) Except as provided in paragraph (a)(16)(ii) of this section, the termmeans any person that receives a reportable policy sale payment in a reportable policy sale. A broker or other intermediary that retains a portion of the cash or other consideration transferred in a reportable policy sale is also a reportable policy sale payment recipient.

(ii) A person other than the seller is not a reportable policy sale payment recipient with respect to a reportable policy sale if that person receives aggregate payments of less than $600 with respect to that reportable policy sale.
