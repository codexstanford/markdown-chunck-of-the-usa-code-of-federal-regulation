
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.61-21 Taxation of fringe benefits.
####### Replacement automobiles.

(v)Notwithstanding anything in this paragraph (d)(7) to the contrary, if the automobile lease valuation rule is used by an employer, or by an employer and an employee, with respect to a particular automobile, and a replacement automobile is provided to the employee for the primary purpose of reducing Federal taxes, then the employer, or the employer and the employee, using the rule must continue to use the rule with respect to the replacement automobile.
