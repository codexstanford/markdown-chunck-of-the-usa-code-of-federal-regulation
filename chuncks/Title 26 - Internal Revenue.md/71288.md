##### ยง 56.4911-3 Expenditures for direct and/or grass roots lobbying communications.
###### Transfers treated as grass roots expenditures.

(A)The transfer is treated as a grass roots expenditure to the extent of the lesser of two amounts: The amount of the transfer and the amount of the transferee's grass roots expenditures.
