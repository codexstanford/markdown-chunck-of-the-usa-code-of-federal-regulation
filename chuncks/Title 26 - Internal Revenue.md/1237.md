###### ยง 1.43-3 Certification
####### Petroleum engineer's certification of a project,In general.

(a)-(1)A petroleum engineer must certify, under penalties of perjury, that an enhanced oil recovery project meets the requirements of section 43(c)(2)(A). A petroleum engineer's certification must be submitted for each project. The petroleum engineer certifying a project must be duly registered or certified in any State.
