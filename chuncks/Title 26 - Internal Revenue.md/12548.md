####### Limitation based on modified adjusted gross income,In general.

(d)-(1)The deduction allowed under section 221 is phased out ratably for taxpayers with modified adjusted gross income between $40,000 and $55,000 ($60,000 and $75,000 for married individuals who file a joint return).
