
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.45Q-4 Utilization of Qualified Carbon Oxide.
####### LCA verification.

(2)The taxpayer verifies the amount of qualified carbon oxide utilized through an LCA. The LCA must demonstrate that the proposed process results in a net reduction of carbon dioxide equivalents when compared to a comparison system. The results of the LCA must be documented in a written LCA report.
