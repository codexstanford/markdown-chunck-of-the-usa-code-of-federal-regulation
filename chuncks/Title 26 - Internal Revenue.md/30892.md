
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Election to Treat Trust as Part of an Estate

####### 2,If there is no executor.

()If there is no executor and a valid section 645 election is made for a QRT after a Form 1041 has been filed for the QRT as a trust (see paragraph (d)(2)(ii)(A) of this section) for the short taxable year beginning with the decedent's death and ending December 31 of that year, the trustee must file an amended return for the QRT.
