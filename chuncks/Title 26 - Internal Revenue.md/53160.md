
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Special Taxes and Taxpayers
###### ยง 1.1502-42 Mutual savings banks, etc.
####### Allocation of tentative taxable loss of other thrifts.

(2)For purposes of paragraph (f)(1)(i) of this section, a thrift's allocable share of another thrift's tentative taxable loss is the loss multiplied by a fraction. The numerator of the fraction is the tentative taxable income (if greater than zero) of the thrift, and the denominator is the aggregate of such tentative taxable income of each thrift.
