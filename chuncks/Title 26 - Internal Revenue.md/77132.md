
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter G - Regulations Under Tax Conventions
#### PART 514 - FRANCE
##### Subpart - Withholding of Tax
###### § 514.1 Introductory.
####### France.

(2)As used in §§ 514.1 to 514.10, the term "France", when used in a geographical sense, means continental France, exclusive of Algeria and the Colonies.
