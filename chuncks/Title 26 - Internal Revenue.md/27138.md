
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.475(b)-1 Scope of exemptions from mark-to-market requirement.
####### Securities held for investment or not held for sale.

(a)Except as otherwise provided by this section and subject to the identification requirements of section 475(b)(2), a security is held for investment (within the meaning of section 475(b)(1)(A)) or not held for sale (within the meaning of section 475(b)(1)(B)) if it is not held by the taxpayer primarily for sale to customers in the ordinary course of the taxpayer's trade or business.
