###### ยง 1.50B-3 Estates and trusts.
####### General rule,In general.

(a)-(1)In the case of an estate or trust, WIN expenses (as defined in paragraph (a) of ยง 1.50B-1) shall be apportioned among the estate or trust and its beneficiaries on the basis of the income of such estate or trust allocable to each. There shall be apportioned to the estate or trust for its taxable year, and to each beneficiary of such estate or trust for his taxable year in which or with which the taxable year of such estate or trust ends, his share (as determined under paragraph (b) of this section) of the total WIN expenses.
