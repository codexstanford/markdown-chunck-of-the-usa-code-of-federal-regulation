
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes

##### Subpart F - Special Fuels
###### ยง 48.4041-8 Definitions.
####### Diesel fuel.

(e)The term "diesel fuel" means any liquid (other than a product taxable as gasoline under the provisions of section 4081) which is sold for use or used as a fuel in a diesel-powered highway vehicle.
