
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.367(d)-1T Transfers of intangible property to foreign corporations (temporary).
####### Unpaid receivable treated as contribution to capital.

(ii)If any portion of an account receivable established under this paragraph (g)(1) remains unpaid as of the last day of the third taxable year following the taxable year to which the account relates, then-

(A) Such portion shall be deemed to have been paid on that date; and

(B) The U.S. person shall be deemed to have contributed an equivalent amount to the capital of the foreign corporation, and the U.S. person's basis in the stock of the foreign corporation shall, therefore, be increased by that amount.
