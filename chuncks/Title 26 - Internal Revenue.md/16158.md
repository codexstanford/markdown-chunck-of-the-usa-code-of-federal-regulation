
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.337(d)-2 Loss limitation rules.
####### Basis reduction on deconsolidation,General rule.

(b)-(1)If the basis of a member of a consolidated group in a share of stock of a subsidiary exceeds its value immediately before a deconsolidation of the share, the basis of the share is reduced at that time to an amount equal to its value. If both a disposition and a deconsolidation occur with respect to a share in the same transaction, paragraph (a) of this section applies and, to the extent necessary to effectuate the purposes of this section, this paragraph (b) applies following the application of paragraph (a) of this section.
