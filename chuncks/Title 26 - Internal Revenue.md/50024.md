
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Guarantees and other non-conforming security,Guarantee.

(v)-(A)The Service may in its discretion accept as security with respect to a transferor's tax liability the applicant's guarantee that it will pay such liability. The Service will in general accept such a guarantee only from a corporation, foreign or domestic, any class of stock of which is regularly traded on an established securities market on the date of the transfer.
