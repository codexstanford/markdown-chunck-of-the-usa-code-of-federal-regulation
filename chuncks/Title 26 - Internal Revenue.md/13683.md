
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

####### Unavailability of replacement parts.

(iii)If a taxpayer replaces a part of a unit of property that cannot reasonably be replaced with the same type of part (for example, because of technological advancements or product enhancements), the replacement of the part with an improved, but comparable, part does not, by itself, result in a betterment to the unit of property.
