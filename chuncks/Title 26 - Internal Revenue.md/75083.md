####### ยง 301.6867-1 Presumptions where owner of large amount of cash is not identified.
######## Rights of possessor of cash,Action permitted.

(d)-(1)Section 6867 provides that the possessor of cash is treated as the taxpayer for purposes of chapter 63 (relating to assessment) and chapter 64 (relating to collection) of the Internal Revenue Code. Accordingly, the possessor of cash may file a petition with the United States Tax Court, within the applicable time limits, challenging the notice of deficiency issued to the possessor solely in that person's capacity as possessor of cash.
