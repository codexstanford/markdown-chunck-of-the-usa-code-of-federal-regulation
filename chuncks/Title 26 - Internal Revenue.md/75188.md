
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Crimes, Other Offenses, and Forfeitures

####### ยง 301.7216-1 Penalty for disclosure or use of tax return information.
######## Exclusions.

(v)A person is not a tax return preparer merely because he leases office space to a tax return preparer, furnishes credit to a taxpayer whose tax return is prepared by a tax return preparer, furnishes information to a tax return preparer at the taxpayer's request, furnishes access (free or otherwise) to a separate person's tax return preparation Web site through a hyperlink on his own Web site, or otherwise performs some service that only incidentally relates to the preparation of tax returns.
