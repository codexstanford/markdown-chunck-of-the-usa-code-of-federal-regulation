####### Time and manner of making election.

(b)The election shall be made on or before November 15, 1960, by filing a statement with the district director with whom the taxpayer's income tax return for the taxable year in which the election is made is required to be filed. The statement shall include the following:

(2) The taxable years to which the election applies.
