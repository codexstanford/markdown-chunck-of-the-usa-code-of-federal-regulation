
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.958-1 Direct and indirect ownership of stock.
####### In general.

(a)Section 958(a) provides that, for purposes of sections 951 to 964 (other than sections 955(b)(1)(A) and (B) and 955(c)(2)(A)(ii) (as in effect before the enactment of the Tax Reduction Act of 1975), and 960(a)(1)), stock owned means-

(1) Stock owned directly; and

(2) Stock owned with the application of paragraph (b) of this section.
