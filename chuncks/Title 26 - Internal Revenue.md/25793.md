###### ยง 1.467-1 Treatment of lessors and lessees generally.
####### Counting conventions.

(1)Any reasonable counting convention may be used (for example, 30 days per month/360 days per year) to determine the length of a rental period or to perform any computation. Rental periods of the same descriptive length, for example annual, semiannual, quarterly, or monthly, may be treated as being of equal length.
