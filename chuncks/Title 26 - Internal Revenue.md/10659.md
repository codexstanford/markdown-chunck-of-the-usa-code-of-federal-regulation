###### ยง 1.170A-13 Recordkeeping and return requirements for deductions for charitable contributions.
####### Substantiation of matched payments,In general.

(17)-(i)For purposes of section 170, if a taxpayer's payment to a donee organization is matched, in whole or in part, by another payor, and the taxpayer receives goods or services in consideration for its payment and some or all of the matching payment, those goods or services will be treated as provided in consideration for the taxpayer's payment and not in consideration for the matching payment.
