
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### export trade corporations
###### § 1.988-1 Certain definitions and special rules.
####### Hyperinflationary currency,Definition,General rule.

(f)-(1)-(i)For purposes of section 988, a hyperinflationary currency means a currency described in § 1.985-1(b)(2)(ii)(D). Unless otherwise provided, the currency in any example used in §§ 1.988-1 through 1.988-5 is not a hyperinflationary currency.
