
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### information returns
###### ยง 1.6045B-1 Returns relating to actions affecting basis of securities.
####### Special rule for S corporations.

(c)An S corporation (as defined in section 1361(a)) is deemed to satisfy the requirements of paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section for any organizational action affecting the basis of its stock if the corporation reports the effect of the organizational action on a timely filed Schedule K-1 (Form 1120S), "Shareholder's Share of Income, Deductions, Credits, etc.," for each shareholder and timely furnishes copies of these schedules to all proper parties.
