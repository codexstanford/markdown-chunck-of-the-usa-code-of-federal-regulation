####### Function test,In general.

(4)-(i)A corporation meets the function test if substantially all the corporation's activities for a taxable year involve the performance of services in one or more of the following fields-

(A) Health,

(B) Law,

(C) Engineering (including surveying and mapping),

(D) Architecture,

(E) Accounting,

(F) Actuarial science,

(G) Performing arts, or

(H) Consulting.
