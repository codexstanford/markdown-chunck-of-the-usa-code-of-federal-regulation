
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 25.2702-3 Qualified interests.
####### Fixed percentage.

(ii)A fixed percentage is a fraction or percentage of the net fair market value of the trust assets, determined annually, payable periodically but not less frequently than annually, but only to the extent the fraction or percentage does not exceed 120 percent of the fixed fraction or percentage payable in the preceding year.
