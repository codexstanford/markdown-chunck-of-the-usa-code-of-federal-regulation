###### ยง 1.179-1 Election to expense certain depreciable assets.
####### Disallowance of the section 38 credit.

(g)If a taxpayer elects to expense under section 179, no section 38 credit is allowable for the portion of the cost expensed. In addition, no section 38 credit shall be allowed under section 48(d) to a lessee of property for the portion of the cost of the property that the lessor expensed under section 179.
