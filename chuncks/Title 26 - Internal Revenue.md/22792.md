####### Exclusions from annual additions for certain employee stock ownership plans that allocate to a broad range of participants,General rule.

(3)-(i)Pursuant to section 415(c)(6), in the case of an employee stock ownership plan (as described in section 4975(e)(7)) that meets the requirements of paragraph (f)(3)(ii) of this section for a limitation year, the limitations imposed by this section do not apply to-
