###### ยง 1.954-6 Foreign base company shipping income.
####### Aircraft or vessel income,In general.

(c)-(1)The term "income derived from, or in connection with, the use (or hiring or leasing for use) of any aircraft or vessel in foreign commerce" as used in paragraph (b)(1)(i) of this section means-

(i) Income derived from transporting passengers or property by aircraft or vessel in foreign commerce and

(ii) Income derived from hiring or leasing an aircraft or vessel to another for use in foreign commerce.
