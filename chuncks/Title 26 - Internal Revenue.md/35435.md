
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Determination of Sources of Income
###### ยง 1.863-3 Allocation and apportionment of income from certain sales of inventory.
####### Allocation or apportionment for Possession Purchase Sales,Determination of source of gross income from Possession Purchase Sales.

(3)-(i)Gross income from Possession Purchase Sales is allocated in its entirety to the taxpayer's business activity, and is then apportioned between sources within the United States and sources within a possession of the United States under paragraph (e)(3)(ii) of this section.
