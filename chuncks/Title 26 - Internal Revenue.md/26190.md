###### ยง 1.468B-9 Disputed ownership funds.
####### Statement to the disputed ownership fund and the Internal Revenue Service with respect to transfers of property other than cash,In general.

(g)-(1)By February 15 of the year following each calendar year in which a transferor (or other person acting on behalf of a transferor) makes a transfer of property other than cash to a disputed ownership fund, the transferor must provide a statement to the administrator of the fund setting forth the information described in paragraph (g)(3) of this section. The transferor must attach a copy of this statement to its return for the taxable year of transfer.
