###### ยง 1.358-6 Stock basis in certain triangular reorganizations.
####### Triangular G reorganization.

(v)A triangular G reorganization is an acquisition by S (other than by statutory merger) of substantially all of T's assets in a title 11 or similar case in exchange for P stock in a transaction that qualifies as a reorganization under section 368(a)(1)(G) by reason of the application of section 368(a)(2)(D).
