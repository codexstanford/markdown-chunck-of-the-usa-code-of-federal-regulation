###### ยง 1.451-8 Advance payments for goods, services, and certain other items.
####### Example 1: Services.

(i)On November 1, 2021, A, in the business of giving dancing lessons, receives an advance payment of $480 for a 1-year contract commencing on that date and providing for up to 48 individual, 1-hour lessons. A provides eight lessons in 2021 and another 35 lessons in 2022. A takes into account($80) of the payment as AFS revenue for 2021, and($400) of the payment as AFS revenue for 2022. For Federal income tax purposes, under the deferral method in paragraph (c) of this section, A must include($80) of the payment in gross income for 2021, and the remaining($400) of the payment in gross income for 2022.
