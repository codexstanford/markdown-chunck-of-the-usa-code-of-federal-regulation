
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter F - Procedure and Administration
#### PART 303 - TAXES UNDER THE TRADING WITH THE ENEMY ACT
##### ยง 303.1-1 Definitions.
###### Property vested by or in the Attorney General.

(h)The terms "property vested by the Attorney General" and "property vested in the Attorney General" include property conveyed, transferred, assigned, delivered, or paid to or held or controlled by or vested in the Attorney General, under the Trading With the Enemy Act.
