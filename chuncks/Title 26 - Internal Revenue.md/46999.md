##### Special Rules for Determining Capital Gains and Losses
###### ยง 1.1297-1 Definition of passive foreign investment company.
####### Facts.,1

(A)() USP is a domestic corporation that owns 30% of TFC, a foreign corporation. The remaining 70% of TFC is owned by FP, a foreign corporation that is unrelated to USP.
