
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 156 - EXCISE TAX ON GREENMAIL
##### Subpart B - Procedure and Administration
###### ยง 156.6161-1 Extension of time for paying tax or deficiency.
####### In general,Tax shown or required to be shown on return.

(a)-(1)A reasonable extension of the time for payment of the amount of any tax imposed by chapter 54 (Greenmail) of the Code and shown or required to be shown on any return may be granted by the appropriate district director at the request of the taxpayer. The period of such extension shall not exceed 6 months from the date for payment of such tax.
