###### ยง 1.468B-9 Disputed ownership funds.
####### Property distributed by the disputed ownership fund,Computing gain or loss.

(4)-(i)Except in the case of a distribution or deemed distribution described in paragraph (e)(3) of this section, a disputed ownership fund must treat a distribution of disputed property as a sale or exchange of that property for purposes of section 1001(a). In computing gain or loss, the amount realized by the disputed ownership fund is the fair market value of that property on the date of distribution.
