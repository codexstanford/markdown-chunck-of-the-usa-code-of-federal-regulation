####### Cash settlement options, phantom stock, stock appreciation rights, or similar interests.

(2)A cash settlement option, phantom stock, stock appreciation right, or similar interest is treated as reasonably certain to be exercised if it is reasonably certain that the option will have value at some time during the period in which the option may be exercised.
