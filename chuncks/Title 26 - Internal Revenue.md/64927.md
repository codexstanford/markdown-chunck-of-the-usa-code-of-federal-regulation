(2)-(i)A spouse who files a joint return may obtain a determination that withholding should stop or not start with respect to payments made to his or her individual accounts, if the spouse shows that-

(A) He or she did not underreport income because he or she is a spouse described in section 6013(e), i.e., innocent spouse; or
