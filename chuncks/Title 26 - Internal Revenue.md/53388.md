####### Calculation of net CFC tested income.

(C)Under paragraph (e)(12) of this section, a member's net CFC tested income is the excess (if any) of the member's aggregate tested income over the member's allocable share of the consolidated tested loss. As a result, the net CFC tested income of USS1, USS2, and USS3 are $0 ($0-$0), $125x ($200x-$75x), and $375x ($600x-$225x), respectively.
