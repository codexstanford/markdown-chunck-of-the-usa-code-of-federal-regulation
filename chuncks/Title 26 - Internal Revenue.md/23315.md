###### ยง 1.430(f)-1 Effect of prefunding balance and funding standard carryover balance.
####### Elections,Method of making elections,In general.

(f)-(1)-(i)Any election under this section by the plan sponsor must be made by providing written notification of the election to the plan's enrolled actuary and the plan administrator. The written notification must set forth the relevant details of the election, including the specific dollar amount involved in the election (except as provided in this paragraph (f)(1). Thus, except as provided in this paragraph (f)(1), a conditional or formula-based election generally does not satisfy the requirements of this paragraph (f).
