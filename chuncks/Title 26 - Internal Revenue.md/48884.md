####### Holding period and use within a qualified opportunity zone of owned or leased tangible property,In general.

(d)-(1)In the case of tangible property that is owned or leased by an eligible entity, during substantially all of the eligible entity's holding period for the tangible property, substantially all of the use of the tangible property must be in a qualified opportunity zone.
