####### Meaning of the provision of services in investing and investment management.,performance of services that consist of investing and investment management

(xi)For purposes of section 199A(d)(2) and paragraph (b)(1)(x) of this section only, therefers to a trade or business involving the receipt of fees for providing investing, asset management, or investment management services, including providing advice with respect to buying and selling investments. The performance of services of investing and investment management does not include directly managing real property.
