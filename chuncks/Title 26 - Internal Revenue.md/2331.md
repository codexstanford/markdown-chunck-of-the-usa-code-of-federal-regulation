###### ยง 1.48-9 Definition of energy property.
####### Alternative energy property,In general.

(c)-(1)Alternative energy property means property described in paragraphs (c)(3) through (10) of this section. In general alternative energy property includes certain property that uses an alternate substance as a fuel or feedstock or converts an alternate substance to a synthetic fuel and certain associated equipment.
