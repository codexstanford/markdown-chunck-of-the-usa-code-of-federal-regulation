###### ยง 1.148-8 Small issuer exception to rebate requirement.
####### Scope.

(a)Under section 148(f)(4)(D), bonds issued to finance governmental activities of certain small issuers are treated as meeting the arbitrage rebate requirement of section 148(f)(2) (the "small issuer exception"). This section provides guidance on the small issuer exception.
