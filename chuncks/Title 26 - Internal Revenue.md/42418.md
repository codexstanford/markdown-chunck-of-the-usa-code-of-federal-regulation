
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### export trade corporations
###### § 1.985-5 Adjustments required upon change in functional currency.
####### Step 3A-Additional adjustments that are necessary when a QBU changes functional currency,QBU changing to a functional currency other than the owner's functional currency,Rule.

(d)-(1)-(i)If a QBU changes its functional currency, and after the change the QBU is a section 987 QBU that is subject to §§ 1.987-1 through 1.987-11 pursuant to § 1.987-1(b)(1), then the adjustments described in either paragraph (d)(1)(ii) or (d)(1)(iii) of this section shall be taken into account for purposes of section 987.
