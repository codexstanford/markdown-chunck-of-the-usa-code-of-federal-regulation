###### ยง 1.467-1 Treatment of lessors and lessees generally.
####### Section 467 rent,In general.

(d)-(1)The section 467 rent for a taxable year is the sum of-

(i) The fixed rent for any rental period (determined under paragraph (d)(2) of this section) that begins and ends in the taxable year;

(ii) A ratable portion of the fixed rent for any other rental period beginning or ending in the taxable year; and

(iii) In the case of a section 467 rental agreement that provides for contingent rent, the contingent rent that accrues during the taxable year.
