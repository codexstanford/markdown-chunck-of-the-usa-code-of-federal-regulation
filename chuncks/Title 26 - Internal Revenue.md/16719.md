###### ยง 1.355-6 Recognition of gain on certain distributions of stock or securities in controlled corporation.
####### Distributing securities.

(3)For purposes of determining if stock of any controlled corporation received in the distribution is disqualified stock described in section 355(d)(3)(B)(ii)(II) (relating to a distribution of controlled corporation stock on any securities in the distributing corporation acquired by purchase during the five-year period), references in this section to stock of a corporation that is or becomes a distributing corporation includes securities of the corporation.
