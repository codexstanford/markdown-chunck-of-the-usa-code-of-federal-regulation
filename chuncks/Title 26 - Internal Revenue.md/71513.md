
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury

##### ยง 145.4052-1 Special rules and definitions.
###### Tax treatment of leases,Long-term lease.

(b)-(1)For purposes of this section and ยง 145.4051-1, the leasing of an article on a long-term basis (as defined in paragraph (d)(6) of this section) will be deemed to be a sale of the article and will be deemed to be a taxable sale unless one of the exceptions contained in paragraph (a)(2) of this section applies.
