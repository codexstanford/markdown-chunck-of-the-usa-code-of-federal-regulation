###### ยง 1.684-1 Recognition of gain on transfers to certain foreign trusts and estates.
####### Immediate recognition of gain,In general.

(a)-(1)Any U.S. person who transfers property to a foreign trust or foreign estate shall be required to recognize gain at the time of the transfer equal to the excess of the fair market value of the property transferred over the adjusted basis (for purposes of determining gain) of such property in the hands of the U.S. transferor unless an exception applies under the provisions of ยง 1.684-3. The amount of gain recognized is determined on an asset-by-asset basis.
