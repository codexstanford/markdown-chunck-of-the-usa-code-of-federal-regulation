###### ยง 1.471-8 Inventories of retail merchants.
####### Ending inventory retail selling prices.

(4)A taxpayer must include all permanent markups and markdowns but may not include temporary markups or markdowns in determining the retail selling prices of goods on hand at the end of the taxable year. A taxpayer may not include a markdown that is not an actual reduction of retail selling price.
