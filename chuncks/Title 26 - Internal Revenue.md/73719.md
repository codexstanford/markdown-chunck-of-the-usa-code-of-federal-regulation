####### ยง 301.6323(c)-1 Protection for commercial transactions financing agreements.
######## Commercial financing security,In general.

(c)-(1)The term "commercial financing security" means-

(i) Paper of a kind ordinarily arising in commercial transactions.

(ii) Accounts receivable (as defined in subparagraph (2) of this paragraph (c)),

(iii) Mortgages on real property, and

(iv) Inventory.
