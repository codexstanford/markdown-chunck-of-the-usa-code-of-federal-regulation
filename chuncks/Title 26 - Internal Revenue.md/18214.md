
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.381(c)(1)-1 Net operating loss carryovers in certain corporate acquisitions.
####### Computation of carryovers and carrybacks when date of distribution or transfer is not on last day of acquiring corporation's taxable year,General rule.

(f)-(1)Pursuant to the provisions of section 381(c)(1)(C), the taxable income of the acquiring corporation for its taxable year which is a prior taxable year for purposes of section 172(b)(2) and paragraph (e) of this section shall be determined in the manner prescribed in this paragraph, if the date of distribution or transfer occurs within, but not on the last day of, such taxable year.
