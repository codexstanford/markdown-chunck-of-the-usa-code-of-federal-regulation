
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Changes in Rates During a Taxable Year
###### § 1.25-3T Qualified mortgage credit certificate (Temporary).
####### Definition of qualified mortgage credit certificate.

(a)For purposes of §§ 1.25-1T through 1.25-8T, the term "qualified mortgage credit certificate" means a certificate that meets all of the requirements of this section.
