###### ยง 1.338-9 International aspects of section 338.
####### Character of FT stock as carryover FT stock eliminated upon disposition.

(vi)A share of FT stock is not considered carryover FT stock after it is disposed of provided that all gain realized on the transfer is recognized at the time of the transfer, or that, if less than all of the realized gain is recognized, the recognized amount equals or exceeds the remaining carryover amount for that share.
