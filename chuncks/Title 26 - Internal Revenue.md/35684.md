
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.864-5 Foreign source income effectively connected with U.S. business.
####### Dividends, interest, or royalties paid by a related foreign corporation.

(1)Dividends, interest, or royalties paid by a foreign corporation in which the nonresident alien individual or the foreign corporation described in paragraph (a) of this section owns, within the meaning of section 958(a), or is considered as owning, by applying the ownership rules of section 958(b), at the time such items are paid more than 50 percent of the total combined voting power of all classes of stock entitled to vote.
