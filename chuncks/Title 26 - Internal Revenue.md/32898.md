###### ยง 1.752-1 Treatment of partnership liabilities.
####### Decrease in partner's share of liabilities.

(c)Any decrease in a partner's share of partnership liabilities, or any decrease in a partner's individual liabilities by reason of the partnership's assumption of the individual liabilities of the partner, is treated as a distribution of money by the partnership to that partner.
