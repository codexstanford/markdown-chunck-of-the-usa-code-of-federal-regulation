###### ยง 1.338-3 Qualification for the section 338 election.
####### Post-acquisition elimination of the purchasing corporation.

(2)An election under section 338 may be made for target after the acquisition of assets of the purchasing corporation by another corporation in a transaction described in section 381(a), provided that the purchasing corporation is considered for tax purposes as the purchaser of the target stock. The acquiring corporation in the section 381(a) transaction may make an election under section 338 for target.
