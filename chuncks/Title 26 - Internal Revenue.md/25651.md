###### ยง 1.465-8 General rules; interest other than that of a creditor.
####### Loans for which the borrower is personally liable for repayment,General rule.

(b)-(1)If a borrower is personally liable for the repayment of a loan for use in an activity, a person shall be considered a person with an interest in the activity other than that of a creditor only if the person has either a capital interest in the activity or an interest in the net profits of the activity.
