#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Farmers' Cooperatives
###### ยง 1.527-3 Exempt function income.
####### General rule.

(a)(1) For purposes of section 527, exempt function income consists solely of amounts received as:

(i) Contributions of money or other property,

(ii) Membership dues, fees, or assessments from a member of a political organization, or

(iii) Proceeds from a political fund raising or entertainment event, or proceeds from the sale of political campaign materials, which are not received in the ordinary course of any trade or business,

(2) Income will be considered segregated for use only for an exempt function only if it is received into and disbursed from a segregated fund as defined in ยง 1.527-2(b).
