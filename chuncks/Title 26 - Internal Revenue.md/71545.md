
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 156 - EXCISE TAX ON GREENMAIL
##### Subpart A - Tax on Greenmail
###### ยง 156.5881-1 Imposition of excise tax on greenmail.
####### In general.

(a)Section 5881 of the Code imposes a tax equal to 50 percent of the gain or other income realized by any person on the receipt of greenmail, whether or not the gain or other income is recognized.
