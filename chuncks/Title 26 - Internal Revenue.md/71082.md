
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 54 - PENSION EXCISE TAXES
##### § 54.9825-2T Applicability (temporary).
###### In general.

(a)The requirements in §§ 54.9825-4T through 54.9825-6T apply to group health plans (including grandfathered health plans as defined in § 54.9815-1251), except as specified in paragraph (b) of this section.
