However, if advance credit payments are made for coverage of an individual who is not included in any taxpayer's family, as defined in ยง 1.36B-1(d), the taxpayer who attested to the Exchange to the intention to include such individual in the taxpayer's family as part of the advance credit payment eligibility determination for coverage of the individual must file a tax return and reconcile the advance credit payments.
