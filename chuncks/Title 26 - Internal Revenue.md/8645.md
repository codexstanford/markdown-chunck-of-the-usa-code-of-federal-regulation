
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Itemized Deductions for Individuals and Corporations

####### Example 7: Dispositions with a principal purpose of shifting basis,Facts.

(7)-(i)U and V are members of a consolidated group of which P is the common parent. U conducts an electing farming business (Business F), and V conducts a farm equipment leasing business (Business L) that is a non-excepted trade or business. After the end of a farming season, the P group, with a principal purpose of shifting basis from Business L to Business F, has V sell to U all off-lease farming equipment that previously was leased out as part of Business L. Immediately before the start of the next season, U sells the farming equipment back to V for use in Business L.
