
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.48-9 Definition of energy property.
####### Turbulators.

(11)Turbulators increase the rate of transfer of heat from combustion gases to heat exchange surfaces by increasing the turbulence in the gases. A turbulator is a baffle placed in a boiler firetube or in a heat exchange tube in industrial process equipment to deflect gases to the heat transfer surface.
