
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.199A-12 Expanded affiliated groups.
####### Definition of an expanded affiliated group.

(1)An EAG is an affiliated group as defined in section 1504(a), determined by substituting "more than 50 percent" for "at least 80 percent" in each place it appears and without regard to section 1504(b)(2) and (4).
