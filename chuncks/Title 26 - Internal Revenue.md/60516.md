#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### ยง 1.408A-8 Definitions.
###### Taxable conversion amount.

(8)The term taxable conversion amount means the portion of a conversion amount includible in income on account of a conversion, determined under the rules of section 408(d)(1) and (2).
