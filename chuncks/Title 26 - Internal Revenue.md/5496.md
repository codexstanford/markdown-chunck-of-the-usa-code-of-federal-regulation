
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Tax Exemption Requirements for State and Local Bonds
###### ยง 1.141-4 Private security or payment test.
####### Aggregation of private payments and security.

(2)For purposes of the private security or payment test, payments taken into account as private payments and payments or property taken into account as private security are aggregated. However, the same payments are not taken into account as both private security and private payments.
