####### Example 1: Tax on gross interest income of certain residents; no deductions allowed,1,Facts.

(A)-()Country X imposes a net income tax on corporations resident in Country X. Country X imposes a second tax (the "bank tax") of 1 percent on the gross amount of interest income derived by banks resident in Country X; no deductions are allowed in determining the base of the bank tax. Banks resident in Country X incur substantial costs and expenses, including interest expense, attributable to their interest income.
