####### Example 5. Foreign use-general rule and de minimis reduction exception,Facts.

(5)-(i)P owns DE1. DE1owns FS. In year 1, there is a $100x loss attributable to P's interest in DE1that is a dual consolidated loss. Also in year 1, FSearns $200x of income. DE1and FSfile a Country X consolidated tax return. For Country X tax purposes, the year 1 $100x loss of DE1is used to offset $100x of year 1 income generated by FS. Under Country X tax law, unused losses are carried forward and available to offset income in subsequent taxable years.
