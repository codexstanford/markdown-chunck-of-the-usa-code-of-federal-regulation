
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.382-6 Allocation of income and loss to periods before and after the change date for purposes of section 382.
####### pre-change section 163(j) limit,post-change section 163(j) limit

(C) The loss corporation deducts its pre-change BIE to the extent of its pre-change section 163(j) limit, and the loss corporation deducts its post-change BIE to the extent of its post-change section 163(j) limit. For purposes of this paragraph (b)(4)(ii), the termmeans the sum of the pre-change ATI and the amount of business interest income and floor plan financing interest expense allocated to the pre-change period; the termmeans the sum of the post-change ATI limit and the amount of business interest income and floor plan financing interest expense allocated to the post-change period.
