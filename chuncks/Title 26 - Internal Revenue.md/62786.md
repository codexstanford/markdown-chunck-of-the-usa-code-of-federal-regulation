
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Determination of Tax Liability
###### ยง 25.2501-1 Imposition of tax.
####### Certain residents of possessions considered citizens of the United States.

(c)As used in this part, the term "citizen of the United States" includes a person who makes a gift after September 2, 1958 and who, at the time of making the gift, was domiciled in a possession of the United States and was a United States citizen, and who did not acquire his United States citizenship solely by reason of his being a citizen of such possession or by reason of his birth or residence within such possession. The gift of such a person is, therefore, subject to the tax imposed by section 2501 in the same manner in which a gift made by a resident of the United States is subject to the tax.
