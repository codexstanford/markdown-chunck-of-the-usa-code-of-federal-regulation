
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter F - Procedure and Administration
#### PART 301 - PROCEDURE AND ADMINISTRATION
##### Assessment
###### In General
####### ยง 301.6231(a)(7)-1 Designation or selection of tax matters partner.
######## Person who may be designated tax matters partner,General requirement.

(b)-(1)A person may be designated as the tax matters partner of a partnership for a taxable year only if that person-

(i) Was a general partner in the partnership at some time during the taxable year for which the designation is made; or

(ii) Is a general partner in the partnership as of the time the designation is made.
