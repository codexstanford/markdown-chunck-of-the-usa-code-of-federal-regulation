###### ยง 1.6043-4 Information returns relating to certain acquisitions of control and changes in capital structure.
####### Information about acquisition of control or substantial change in capital structure.

(iv)(A) A description of the transaction or transactions that gave rise to the acquisition of control or the substantial change in capital structure of the corporation;

(B) The date or dates of the transaction or transactions that gave rise to the acquisition of control or the substantial change in capital structure; and
