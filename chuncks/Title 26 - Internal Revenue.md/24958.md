####### Example 13: Computer software subscription with one performance obligation,Facts.

(xiii)-(A)On July 1, 2021, O, in the business of licensing off the shelf, fully customized, and semi-customized computer software and providing customer support, receives a payment of $100 for a 2-year "software subscription contract" under which O will provide software updates if it develops an update within the contract period, as well as online and telephone customer support. O determines that its obligations under the software subscription contract are one performance obligation for financial accounting purposes, which yields one item of gross income. O takes into account($25) of the payment as AFS revenue for 2021,($50) as AFS revenue for 2022, and the remaining($25) as AFS revenue for 2023, regardless of when O provides updates or customer support.
