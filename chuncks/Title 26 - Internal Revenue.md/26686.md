###### ยง 1.469-4T Definition of activity (temporary).
####### Multiple undertakings treated as a single activity by passthrough entity.

(3)A person (including a passthrough entity) must treat interests in two or more undertakings as part of the same activity for a taxable year if any passthrough entity through which the person holds such undertakings treats such undertakings as part of the same activity on the applicable return of the passthrough entity for the taxable year of such person.
