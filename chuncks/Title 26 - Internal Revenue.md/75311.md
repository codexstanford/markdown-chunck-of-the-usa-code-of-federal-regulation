
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Judicial Proceedings
###### Civil Actions by the United States
####### ยง 301.7426-1 Civil actions by persons other than taxpayers.
######## Substitution of value.

(4)A person who obtains a certificate of discharge under section 6325(b)(4) with respect to any property may, within 120 days after the day on which the certificate is issued, bring a civil action against the United States in a district court of the United States for a determination of whether the value of the interest of the United States (if any) in such property is less than the value determined by the appropriate official. A civil action under this provision shall be the exclusive judicial remedy for a person other than the taxpayer who obtains a certificate of discharge for a filed notice of Federal tax lien.
