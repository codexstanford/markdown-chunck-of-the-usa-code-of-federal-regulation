
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Special rules relating to first effective plan year,Determination of minimum required contribution for pre-effective plan year.

(5)-(i)In the case of the plan's first effective plan year, the minimum required contribution for the preceding plan year for purposes of paragraph (c)(5)(ii)(B) of this section is equal to the minimum required contribution under section 412 for the pre-effective plan year (determined without regard to any funding waiver under section 412), determined as of the last day of the pre-effective plan year and without regard to the plan's credit balance.
