
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### taxable year for which deductions taken
###### § 1.468A-5 Nuclear decommissioning fund - miscellaneous provisions.
####### Trust provisions.

(4)Each qualified nuclear decommissioning fund trust agreement must provide that assets in the fund must be used as authorized by section 468A and §§ 1.468A-1 through 1.468A-9 and that the agreement may not be amended so as to violate section 468A or §§ 1.468A-1 through 1.468A-9.
