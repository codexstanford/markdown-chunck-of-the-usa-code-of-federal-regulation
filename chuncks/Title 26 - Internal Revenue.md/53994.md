####### Special rule for use of dual consolidated loss to offset tainted income,In general.

(e)-(1)The dual consolidated loss of any dual resident corporation that ceases to be a dual resident corporation shall not be used to offset income of such corporation to the extent that such income is tainted income, as defined in paragraph (e)(2) of this section.
