###### ยง 1.48-11 Qualified rehabilitated building; expenditures incurred before January 1, 1982.
####### In general.

(a)Under section 48(a)(1)(E), that portion of the basis of a qualified rehabilitated building which is attributable to qualified rehabilitation expenditures qualifies as section 38 property. In general, property which is treated as section 38 property by reason of section 48(a)(1)(E) is treated as new section 38 property and therefore is not subject to the used property limitation.
