
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 49 - FACILITIES AND SERVICES EXCISE TAXES
##### Subpart D - Transportation of Persons
###### ยง 49.4263-6 Transportation outside the northern portion of the Western Hemisphere.
####### Northern portion of the Western Hemisphere.

(c)For purposes of the regulations in this subpart, the term "northern portion of the Western Hemisphere" means the area lying west of the 30th meridian west of Greenwich, east of the International Date Line, and north of the equator, but not including any country of South America.
