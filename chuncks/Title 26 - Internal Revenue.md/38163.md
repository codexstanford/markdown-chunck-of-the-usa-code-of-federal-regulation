
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.904-4 Separate application of section 904 with respect to certain categories of income.
####### 3,Foreign branch owner group income.

()The income of a foreign branch owner group is attributed to the foreign branch owner that owns the group. The income of a foreign branch owner group income is the aggregate of the U.S. gross income that is attributed, under the rules of this paragraph (f)(2), to each member of the foreign branch owner group, determined after accounting for all disregarded payments made and received by each member of the foreign branch owner group.
