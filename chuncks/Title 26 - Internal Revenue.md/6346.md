##### Tax Exemption Requirements for State and Local Bonds
###### ยง 1.148-7 Spending exceptions to the rebate requirement.
####### Real property

(i)means land and improvements to land, such as buildings or other inherently permanent structures, including interests in real property. For example, real property includes wiring in a building, plumbing systems, central heating or air-conditioning systems, pipes or ducts, elevators, escalators installed in a building, paved parking areas, roads, wharves and docks, bridges, and sewage lines.
