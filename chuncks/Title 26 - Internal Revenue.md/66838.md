
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 48 - MANUFACTURERS AND RETAILERS EXCISE TAXES
##### Subpart K - Sporting Goods
###### ยง 48.4161(b)-1 Imposition and rates of tax; bows and arrows.
####### Rate of tax.

(b)The tax is imposed on the sale of articles enumerated in section 4161(b) and paragraph (a) of this section at the rate of 11 percent of the price for which such articles are sold. For the definition of the term "price", see section 4216 and the regulations thereunder.
