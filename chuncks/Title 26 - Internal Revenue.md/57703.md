
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Extension of Time for Filing Returns
###### ยง 1.6081-3 Automatic extension of time for filing corporation income tax returns.
####### Termination of automatic extension.

(c)The Commissioner may terminate an automatic extension at any time by mailing a notice of termination to the corporation (parent corporation in the case of an affiliated group of corporations filing a consolidated return). The notice shall be mailed at least 10 days prior to the termination date designated in such notice. The notice of termination shall be sufficient for all purposes when mailed to the corporation at the address shown on Form 7004 or to the corporation's last known address. For further guidance regarding the definition of last known address, see ยง 301.6212-2 of this chapter.
