
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### foreign tax credit
###### ยง 1.901-3 Reduction in amount of foreign taxes on foreign mineral income allowed as a credit.
####### Foreign mineral income defined,In general.

(b)-(1)The term "foreign mineral income" means income (determined under chapter 1 of the Code) from sources within a foreign country or possession of the United States derived from-

(i) The extraction of minerals from mines, wells, or other natural deposits,

(ii) The processing of minerals into their primary products, or

(iii) The transportation, distribution, or sale of minerals or of the primary products derived from minerals.
