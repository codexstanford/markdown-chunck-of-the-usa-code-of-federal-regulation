####### Computation of taxable income of terminal railroad corporations,Income not considered received or accrued.

(a)-(1)A terminal railroad corporation (as defined in paragraph (a) of ยง 1.281-3) shall not be considered to have received or accrued the "reduced amount" described in paragraph (c) of this section in the computation of its taxable income.
