###### ยง 1.6050M-1 Information returns relating to persons receiving contracts from certain Federal executive agencies.
####### Special rule for certain contracts of the Small Business Administration.

(iii)Any subcontract entered into by the Small Business Administration (SBA) under a prime contract between the SBA and a procuring Federal executive agency pursuant to section 8(a) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 637(a)) shall not be treated as a contract of the SBA but shall be treated as a contract of the procuring agency for purposes of this section.
