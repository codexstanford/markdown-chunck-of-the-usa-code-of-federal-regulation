
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Itemized Deductions for Individuals and Corporations (Continued)
###### ยง 1.179C-1 Election to expense certain refineries.
####### Election,In general.

(d)-(1)A taxpayer may make an election to deduct as an expense 50 percent of the cost of any qualified refinery property. A taxpayer making this election takes the 50 percent deduction for the taxable year in which the qualified refinery property is placed in service.
