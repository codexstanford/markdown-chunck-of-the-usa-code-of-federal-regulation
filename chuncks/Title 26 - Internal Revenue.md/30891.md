###### ยง 1.645-1 Election by certain revocable trusts to be treated as part of estate.
####### Requirement to amend Form 1041 if a section 645 election is made,1,If there is an executor.

(B)-()If there is an executor and a valid section 645 election is made for a QRT after a Form 1041 has been filed for the QRT as a trust (see paragraph (d)(2)(ii)(A) of this section), the trustee must amend the Form 1041.
