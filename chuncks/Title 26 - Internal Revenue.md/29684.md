
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Long-term capital gains.

(e)(1) There is allowed as a deduction the excess of the net long-term capital gain for the taxable year over the net short-term capital loss for such year, minus the taxes attributable to such excess, as provided in section 545(b)(5).
