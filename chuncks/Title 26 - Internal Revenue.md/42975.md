
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.988-3 Character of exchange gain or loss.
####### Special rule for contracts that become part of a straddle after an election is made.

(2)If a contract which is the subject of an election under paragraph (b)(1) of this section becomes part of a straddle within the meaning of section 1092(c) (without regard to subsections (c)(4) or (e)) after the date of the election, the election shall be invalid with respect to gains from such contract and the Commissioner, in his sole discretion, may invalidate the election with respect to losses.
