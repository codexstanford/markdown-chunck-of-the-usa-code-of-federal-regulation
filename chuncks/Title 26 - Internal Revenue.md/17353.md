
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.367(a)-3 Treatment of transfers of stock or securities to foreign corporations.
####### Gain recognition transaction.,1

(D)() A transaction described in this paragraph (d)(2)(vi)(D) is one where a principal purpose of the transfer by the domestic acquired corporation is the avoidance of U.S. tax that would have been imposed on the domestic acquired corporation on the disposition of the re-transferred assets. A transfer may have a principal purpose of tax avoidance even though the tax avoidance purpose is outweighed by other purposes when taken together.
