###### ยง 1.355-6 Recognition of gain on certain distributions of stock or securities in controlled corporation.
####### Acquisition of stock in a distribution to which section 305(a) applies.

(ii)An acquisition of stock in a distribution qualifying under section 305(a) is not a purchase to the extent section 307(a) applies to determine the recipient's basis. However, to the extent the distribution is of rights to acquire stock, see paragraph (c)(3) of this section for rules regarding options, warrants, convertible obligations, and other similar interests.
