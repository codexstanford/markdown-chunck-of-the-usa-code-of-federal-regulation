####### Debt used to pay borrowing costs,Borrowing costs with respect to different debt.

(iii)-(A)To the extent the proceeds of a debt (the "ancillary debt") are used to pay borrowing costs (other than interest) with respect to another debt (the "primary debt"), the ancillary debt is allocated in the same manner as the primary debt is allocated from time to time. To the extent the primary debt is repaid, the ancillary debt will continue to be allocated in the same manner as the primary debt was allocated immediately before its repayment.
