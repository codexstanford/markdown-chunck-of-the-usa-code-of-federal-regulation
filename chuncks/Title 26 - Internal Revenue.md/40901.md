####### Services directly related,In general.

(d)-(1)The term "income derived from, or in connection with, the performance of services directly related to the use of an aircraft or vessel in foreign commerce", as used in paragraph (b)(1)(ii) of this section, means-

(i) Income derived from, or in connection with, the performance of services described in subparagraph (2) or (3) of this paragraph, and
