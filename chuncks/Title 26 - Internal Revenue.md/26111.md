###### ยง 1.468B-3 Rules applicable to the transferor.
####### Distributions to transferors,In general.

(f)-(1)A transferor must include in gross income any distribution (including a deemed distribution described in paragraph (f)(2) of this section) it receives from a qualified settlement fund. If property is distributed, the amount includible in gross income and the basis in that property, is the fair market value of the property on the date of the distribution.
