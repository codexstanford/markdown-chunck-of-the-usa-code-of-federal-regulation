
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.871-15 Treatment of dividend equivalents.
####### Rule for the first year of an index.

(ii)In the case of an index that was not in existence on the first business day of the calendar year containing the calculation time for the transaction, paragraph (l)(2) of this section is applied by testing the index on the first business day it is created, and the dividend yield calculation required by paragraph (l)(3)(vi) of this section is determined by using the dividend yield that the index would have had in the immediately preceding year if it had the same components throughout that year that it has on the day it is created.
