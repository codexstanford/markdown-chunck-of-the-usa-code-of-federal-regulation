
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 40 - EXCISE TAX PROCEDURAL REGULATIONS
##### ยง 40.0-1 Introduction.
###### Definition of semimonthly period.

(c)The term "semimonthly period" means the first 15 days of a calendar month (the "first semimonthly period") or the portion of a calendar month following the 15th day of the month (the "second semimonthly period").
