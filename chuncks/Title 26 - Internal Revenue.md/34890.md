
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

####### Qualified nonrecourse indebtedness,In general.

(b)-(1)In the case of qualified nonrecourse indebtedness (as defined in paragraph (b)(2) of this section), the deduction for interest shall be considered directly allocable solely to the gross income which the property acquired, constructed, or improved with the proceeds of the indebtedness generates, has generated, or could reasonably be expected to generate.
