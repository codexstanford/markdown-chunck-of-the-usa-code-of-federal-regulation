####### Definition of motor carrier-operating authority.

(d)For purposes of ยง 1.9200-2 and this section, the term "motor carrier operating authority" means a certificate or permit held by a motor common carrier or motor contract carrier of property and issued pursuant to the Revised Interstate Commerce Act, 49 U.S.C. 10921-10933 (Supp. III 1979).
