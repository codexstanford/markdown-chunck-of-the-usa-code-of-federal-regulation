###### ยง 1.451-8 Advance payments for goods, services, and certain other items.
####### Inventory costs for the advance payment cost offset method,Inventory costs not affected by cost of goods in progress offset.

(8)-(i)The cost of goods comprising the cost of goods in progress offset does not reduce the costs that are capitalized to the items of inventory produced or items of inventory acquired for resale by the taxpayer. While the cost of goods in progress offset reduces the amount of the advance payment inventory inclusion amount, the cost of goods in progress offset does not affect how and when costs are capitalized to inventory under sections 471 and 263A or any other applicable provision of the Internal Revenue Code or when those capitalized costs will be recovered.
