
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### regulations applicable to taxable years prior to december 30, 1996
###### § 1.864(c)(8)-1 Gain or loss by foreign persons on the disposition of certain partnership interests.
####### Determination of outside gain and loss,In general.,e.g.,,outside capital gain,outside capital loss,,outside ordinary gain,outside ordinary loss,

(2)-(i)The amount of gain or loss recognized by the foreign transferor in connection with the transfer of its partnership interest is determined under all relevant provisions of the Internal Revenue Code and the regulations thereunder. See,§§ 1.741-1(a) and 1.751-1(a)(2). For purposes of this section, the amount of gain or loss that is treated as capital gain or capital loss under sections 741 and 751 is referred to asorrespectively. The amount of gain or loss that is treated as ordinary gain or ordinary loss under sections 741 and 751 is referred to asorrespectively.
