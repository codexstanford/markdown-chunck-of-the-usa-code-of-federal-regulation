
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Real Estate Investment Trusts
###### ยง 1.860C-1 Taxation of holders of residual interests.
####### Pass-thru of income or loss.

(a)Any holder of a residual interest in a REMIC must take into account the holder's daily portion of the taxable income or net loss of the REMIC for each day during the taxable year on which the holder owned the residual interest.
