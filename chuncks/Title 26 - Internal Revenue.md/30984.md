
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.663(c)-4 Applicability of separate share rule to estates and qualified revocable trusts.
####### Shares with multiple beneficiaries and beneficiaries of multiple shares.

(c)A share may be considered as separate even though more than one beneficiary has an interest in it. For example, two beneficiaries may have equal, disproportionate, or indeterminate interests in one share which is economically separate and independent from another share in which one or more beneficiaries have an interest. Moreover, the same person may be a beneficiary of more than one separate share.
