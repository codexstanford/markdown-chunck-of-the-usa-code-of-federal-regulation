
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### ยง 13.11 Revocation of election to report income on the installment basis.
###### In general.

(a)Under section 453(c)(4) taxpayers who are dealers in personal property and who elected installment-basis income reporting, subject to the provisions of section 453(c)(1) (relating to change from accrual to installment basis), may revoke their previously made election.
