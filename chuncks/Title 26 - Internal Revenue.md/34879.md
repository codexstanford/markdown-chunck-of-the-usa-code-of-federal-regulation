
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.861-10 Special allocations of interest expense.
####### Foreign and U.S. base period ratios.

(B)In computing the foreign and U.S. base period ratios, the foreign and U.S. base periods of the U.S. shareholder shall be considered to be only the period prior to the current year that the U.S. shareholder was in existence if this prior period is less than five taxable years.
