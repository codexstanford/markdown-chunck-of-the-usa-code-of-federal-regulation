
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 57 - HEALTH INSURANCE PROVIDERS FEE
##### ยง 57.2 Explanation of terms.
###### Consistency rule.

(ii)For purposes of paragraph (b)(3)(i) of this section, an entity must use the same test year as it used in its first fee year beginning after December 31, 2014, and in each subsequent fee year. Thus, for example, if an entity used the 2014 data year as its test year for the 2015 fee year, that entity must use the data year as its test year for each subsequent fee year.
