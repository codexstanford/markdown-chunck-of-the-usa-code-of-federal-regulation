##### Small Business Corporations and Their Shareholders
###### § 1.1377-3 Applicability dates.
####### In general.

(a)Except as otherwise provided in this section, §§ 1.1377-1 and 1.1377-2 apply to taxable years of an S corporation beginning after December 31, 1996.
