####### Certain activities of limited partners and limited entrepreneurs,In general.

(3)-(i)Except as provided in this paragraph, a taxpayer that owns an interest, as a limited partner or a limited entrepreneur (as defined in section 464(e)(2)), in an activity described in section 465(c)(1), may not group that activity with any other activity.
