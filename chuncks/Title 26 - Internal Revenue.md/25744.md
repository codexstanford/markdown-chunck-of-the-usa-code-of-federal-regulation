###### ยง 1.467-1 Treatment of lessors and lessees generally.
####### Section 467 interest,In general.

(e)-(1)The section 467 interest for a taxable year is the sum of-

(i) The interest on fixed rent for any rental period that begins and ends in the taxable year;

(ii) A ratable portion of the interest on fixed rent for any other rental period beginning or ending in the taxable year; and

(iii) In the case of a section 467 rental agreement that provides for contingent rent, any interest that accrues on the contingent rent during the taxable year.
