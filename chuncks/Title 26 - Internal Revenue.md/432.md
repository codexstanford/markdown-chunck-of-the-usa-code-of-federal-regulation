
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury

###### ยง 1.36B-2 Eligibility for premium tax credit.
####### Eligibility for months during a plan year,Failure to enroll in plan.

(iii)-(A)An employee or related individual may be eligible for minimum essential coverage under an eligible employer-sponsored plan for a month during a plan year if the employee or related individual could have enrolled in the plan for that month during an open or special enrollment period for the plan year. If an enrollment period relates to coverage for not only the upcoming plan year (or the current plan year in the case of an enrollment period other than an open enrollment period), but also coverage in one or more succeeding plan years, this paragraph (c)(3)(iii)(A) applies only to eligibility for the coverage in the upcoming plan year (or the current plan year in the case of an enrollment period other than an open enrollment period).
