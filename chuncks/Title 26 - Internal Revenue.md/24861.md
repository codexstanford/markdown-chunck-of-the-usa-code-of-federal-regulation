###### ยง 1.451-7 Election relating to livestock sold on account of drought.
####### Special rules,Connection with drought area.

(c)-(1)To qualify under section 451(e) and this section, the livestock need not be raised, and the sale or exchange need not take place, in a drought area. However, the sale or exchange of the livestock must occur solely on account of drought conditions, the existence of which affected the water, grazing, or other requirements of the livestock so as to necessitate their sale or exchange.
