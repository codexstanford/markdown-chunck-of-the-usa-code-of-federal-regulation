
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury

##### Special Rules for Determining Capital Gains and Losses
###### ยง 1.1234-2 Special rule for grantors of straddles applicable to certain options granted on or before September 1, 1976.
####### Allocation of premium.

(d)The allocation of a premium received for a straddle or a multiple option between or among the component options thereof shall be made on the basis of the relative market value of such component options at the time of their issuance or on any other reasonable and consistently applied basis which is acceptable to the Commissioner.
