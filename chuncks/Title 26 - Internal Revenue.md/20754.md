
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Primary beneficiary under the plan.

(C)For purposes of paragraph (d)(3)(ii)(B) of this section, a "primary beneficiary under the plan" is an individual who is named as a beneficiary under the plan and has an unconditional right, upon the death of the employee, to all or a portion of the employee's account balance under the plan.
