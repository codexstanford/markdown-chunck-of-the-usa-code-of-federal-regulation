###### ยง 1.482-7 Methods to determine taxable income in connection with a cost sharing arrangement.
####### Cost sharing arrangement.

(b)A cost sharing arrangement is an arrangement by which controlled participants share the costs and risks of developing cost shared intangibles in proportion to their RAB shares. An arrangement is a CSA if and only if the requirements of paragraphs (b)(1) through (4) of this section are met.
