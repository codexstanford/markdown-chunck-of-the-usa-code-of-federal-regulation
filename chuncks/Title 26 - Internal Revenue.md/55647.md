###### ยง 1.6038A-1 General requirements and definitions.
####### 25-percent foreign shareholder,In general.

(3)-(i)A foreign person is a 25-percent foreign shareholder of a corporation if the person owns at least 25 percent of-

(A) The total voting power of all classes of stock of the corporation entitled to vote, or

(B) The total value of all classes of stock of the corporation.
