####### Valuation of fuel where cost reimbursed by or charged to an employer.

(C)The fair market value of fuel, the cost of which is reimbursed by or charged to an employer, is generally the amount of the actual reimbursement or the amount charged, provided the purchase of the fuel is at arm's-length.
