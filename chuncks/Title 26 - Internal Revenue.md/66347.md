
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Subpart G - Fuel Used on Inland Waterways
###### ยง 48.4042-3 Certain types of commercial waterway transportation excluded.
####### Meaning of "high seas."

(3)For purposes of this section, "high seas" shall mean waters other than the territorial waters of the United States or any other country. Thus, the high seas shall not include the internal waters of any country, the Great Lakes, harbors, or narrow coastal indentations.
