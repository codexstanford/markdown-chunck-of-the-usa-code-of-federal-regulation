
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.6072-2 Time for filing returns of corporations.
####### Exception.

(2)For taxable years beginning before January 1, 2026, the income tax return of a C corporation described in paragraph (a)(1)(i) of this section that has a taxable year that ends on June 30 shall be filed on or before the fifteenth day of the third month following the close of the taxable year.
