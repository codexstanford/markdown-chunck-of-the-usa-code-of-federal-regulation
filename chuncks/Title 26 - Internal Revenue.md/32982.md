
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.752-4 Special rules.
####### Tiered partnerships.

(a)An upper-tier partnership's share of the liabilities of a lower-tier partnership (other than any liability of the lower-tier partnership that is owed to the upper-tier partnership) is treated as a liability of the upper-tier partnership for purposes of applying section 752 and the regulations thereunder to the partners of the upper-tier partnership.
