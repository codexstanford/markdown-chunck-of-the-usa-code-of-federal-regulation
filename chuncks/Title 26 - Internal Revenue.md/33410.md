
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.815-4 Policyholders surplus account.
####### In general.

(a)Every stock life insurance company subject to the tax imposed by section 802 shall establish and maintain a policyholders surplus account. This account shall be established as of January 1, 1959, and the beginning or opening balance of the policyholders surplus account on that date shall be zero.
