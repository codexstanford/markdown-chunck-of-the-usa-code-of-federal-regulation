(2) For purposes of section 631(a) and this section, the termincludes evergreen trees which are more than six years old at the time severed from their roots and are sold for ornamental purposes, such as Christmas decorations. Section 631(a) is not applicable to evergreen trees which are sold in a live state, whether or not for ornamental purposes. Tops and other parts of standing timber are not considered as evergreen trees within the meaning of section 631(a). The termis used in its commonly accepted sense and includes pine, spruce, fir, hemlock, cedar, and other coniferous trees.
