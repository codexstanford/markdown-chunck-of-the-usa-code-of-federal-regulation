######## Real property tax and special assessment liens,In general.

(f)-(1)Even though a notice of a lien imposed by section 6321 is filed in accordance with ยง 301.6323(f)-1, the lien is not valid against the holder of another lien upon the real property (regardless of when such other lien arises), if such other lien is entitled under local law to priority over security interests in real property which are prior in time and if such other lien on real property secures payment of-

(i) A tax of general application levied by any taxing authority based upon the value of the property;

(iii) Charges for utilities or public services furnished to the property by the United States, a State or political subdivision thereof, or an instrumentality of any one or more of the foregoing.
