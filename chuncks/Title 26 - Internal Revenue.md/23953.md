####### Presumed underfunding for purposes of benefit limitations,Presumption of continued underfunding,In general.

(h)-(1)-(i)This paragraph (h)(1) applies to a plan for a plan year if a limitation under paragraph (b), (c), (d), or (e) of this section applied to the plan on the last day of the preceding plan year.
