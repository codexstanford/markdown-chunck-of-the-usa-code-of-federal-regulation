
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter G - Regulations Under Tax Conventions
#### PART 509 - SWITZERLAND
##### Subpart - General Income Tax
###### ยง 509.104 Definitions.
####### Swiss corporation or other entity.

(8)The term "Swiss corporation or other entity" means a corporation or institution or foundation having juridical personality, or a partnership (association "en nom collectif" or "en commandite"), or other association without juridical personality, created or organized under Swiss laws.
