
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.170A-9 Definition of section 170(b)(1)(A) organization.
####### Definition of normally; general rule,[object Object]

(4)-(i)An organization "normally" receives the requisite amount of public support and meets the 33percent support test for a taxable year and the taxable year immediately succeeding that year, if, for the taxable year being tested and the four taxable years immediately preceding that taxable year, the organization meets the 33percent support test on an aggregate basis.
