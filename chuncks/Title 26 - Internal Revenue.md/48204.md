
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### Empowerment Zone Employment Credit
###### ยง 1.1396-1 Qualified zone employees.
####### In general.

(a)A qualified zone employee of an employer is an employee who satisfies the location-of-services requirement and the abode requirement with respect to the same empowerment zone and is not otherwise excluded by section 1396(d).
