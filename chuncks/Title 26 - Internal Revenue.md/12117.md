###### ยง 1.199A-7 Section 199A(a) Rules for Cooperatives and their patrons.
####### Example. Patron allocating expenses between qualified trade or business and SSTB income from a Cooperative.,1

(B)() Cooperative provides to its patrons a service that is an SSTB under section 199A(d)(2). P, a patron, runs a qualified trade or business under section 199A(d)(1) and incurs expenses for the service from the Cooperative in P's qualified trade or business. P pays the Cooperative $1,000 for the service. Cooperative later pays P a patronage dividend of $50 related to the service.
