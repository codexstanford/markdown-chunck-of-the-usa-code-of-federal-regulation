
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Domestic International Sales Corporations
###### ยง 1.994-1 Inter-company pricing rules for DISC's.
####### DISC must incur export promotion expenses,In general.,a,b,c,d,e

(7)-(i)In order for an expense to be an export promotion expense it must be incurred or treated as incurred under this subparagraph by the DISC. For example, an expense is incurred by a DISC if the expense results from () the DISC incurring an obligation to pay compensation to its employees, () depreciation of property owned by the DISC and used by its employees, () the DISC incurring an obligation to pay for office supplies used by its employees, () the DISC incurring an obligation to pay space costs for use by its employees, or () the DISC incurring an obligation to pay other costs supporting efforts by its employees.
