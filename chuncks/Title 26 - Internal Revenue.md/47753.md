
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Small Business Corporations and Their Shareholders

####### Aggregation of deductions or exclusions for purposes of limitations,In general.

(5)-(i)A shareholder aggregates the shareholder's separate deductions or exclusions with the shareholder's pro rata share of the S corporation's separately stated deductions or exclusions in determining the amount of any deduction or exclusion allowable to the shareholder under subtitle A of the Internal Revenue Code as to which a limitation is imposed.
