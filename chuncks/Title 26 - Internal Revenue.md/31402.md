###### ยง 1.671-5 Reporting for widely held fixed investment trusts.
####### item

(9) Anrefers to an item of income, expense, or credit as well as any trust event (for example, the sale of an asset) or any characteristic or attribute of the trust that affects the income, deductions, and credits reported by a beneficial owner in any taxable year that the beneficial owner holds an interest in the trust.
