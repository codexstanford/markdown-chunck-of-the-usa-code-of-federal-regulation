###### ยง 53.4960-4 Liability for tax on excess remuneration and excess parachute payments.
####### Example 2 (Multiple parachute payments),1,Facts.

(B)-()Employee B is a covered employee of ATEO 3 with a base amount of $200,000 who is entitled to receive two parachute payments: One of $200,000 and the other of $900,000. The $200,000 payment is made upon separation from employment, and the $900,000 payment is to be made on a date in a future taxable year. The present value of the $900,000 payment is $800,000 as of the date of the separation from employment.
