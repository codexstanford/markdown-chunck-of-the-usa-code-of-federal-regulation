
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### nonresident alien individuals
###### ยง 1.871-10 Election to treat real property income as effectively connected with U.S. business.
####### Statement to be filed with return.,a,b,c,d,e

(ii)An election made under this section without the consent of the Commissioner shall be made for a taxable year by filing with the income tax return required under section 6012 and the regulations thereunder for such taxable year a statement to the effect that the election is being made. This statement shall include () a complete schedule of all real property, or any interest in real property, of which the taxpayer is titular or beneficial owner, which is located in the United States, () an indication of the extent to which the taxpayer has direct or beneficial ownership in each such item of real property, or interest in real property, () the location of the real property or interest therein, () a description of any substantial improvements on any such property, and () an identification of any taxable year or years in respect of which a revocation or new election under this section has previously occurred. This statement may not be filed with any return under section 6851 and the regulations thereunder.
