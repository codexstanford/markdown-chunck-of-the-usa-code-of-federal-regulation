
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 48 - MANUFACTURERS AND RETAILERS EXCISE TAXES
##### Subpart L - Taxable Medical Devices
###### § 48.4191-1 Imposition and rate of tax.
####### Rate of tax.,price,

(b)Tax is imposed on the sale of a taxable medical device at the rate of 2.3 percent of the price for which the device is sold. For the definition of the termsee section 4216 and §§ 48.4216(a)-1 through 48.4216(e)-3.
