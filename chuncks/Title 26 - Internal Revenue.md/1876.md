###### ยง 1.46-5 Qualified progress expenditures.
####### New section 38 property with a 7-year useful life,In general.

(f)-(1)The taxpayer must determine if property will be new section 38 property with a useful life of 7 years or more when placed in service. The determination must be made at the close of the taxable year in which construction begins or, if later, at the close of the first taxable year to which an election under section 46(d) applies for the taxpayer.
