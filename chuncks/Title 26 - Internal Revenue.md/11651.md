###### ยง 1.197-2 Amortization of goodwill and certain other intangibles.
####### Anti-churning rules,Scope and purpose,Scope.

(h)-(1)-(i)This paragraph (h) applies to section 197(f)(9) intangibles. For this purpose, section 197(f)(9) intangibles are goodwill and going concern value that was held or used at any time during the transition period and any other section 197 intangible that was held or used at any time during the transition period and was not depreciable or amortizable under prior law.
