###### ยง 1.985-8 Special rules applicable to the European Monetary Union (conversion to euro).
####### Special rule for legacy nonfunctional currency.

(iii)The QBU shall realize or otherwise take into account for all purposes of the Internal Revenue Code the amount of any unrealized exchange gain or loss attributable to nonfunctional currency (as described in section 988(c)(1)(C)(ii)) that is denominated in a legacy currency as if the currency were disposed of on the last day of the taxable year immediately prior to the year of change. The character and source of the gain or loss are determined under section 988.
