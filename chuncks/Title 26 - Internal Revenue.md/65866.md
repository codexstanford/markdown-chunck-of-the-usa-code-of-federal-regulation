
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 40 - EXCISE TAX PROCEDURAL REGULATIONS
##### ยง 40.6302(c)-3 Deposits under chapter 33.
###### Period during which tax is considered as collected.

(3)For purposes of this section, the tax included in amounts billed or tickets sold during a semimonthly period (as defined in ยง 40.0-1(c)) is considered as collected during the first seven days of the second following semimonthly period. Thus, the tax included in amounts billed or tickets sold during the first semimonthly period of a calendar month is considered as collected during the period of the 1st day through the 7th day of the following month; the tax included in amounts billed or tickets sold during the second semimonthly period of a calendar month is considered as collected during the period of the 16th day through the 22nd day of the following month.
