###### ยง 1.1245-1 General rule for treatment of gain from dispositions of certain depreciable property.
####### Sale, exchange, or involuntary conversion.

(b)(1) In the case of a sale, exchange, or involuntary conversion of section 1245 property, the gain to which section 1245(a)(1) applies is the amount by which (i) the lower of the amount realized upon the disposition of the property or the recomputed basis of the property, exceeds (ii) the adjusted basis of the property.
