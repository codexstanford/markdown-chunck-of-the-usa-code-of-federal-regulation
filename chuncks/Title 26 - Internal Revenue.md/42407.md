###### ยง 1.985-3 United States dollar approximate separate transactions method.
####### Step 8-adjust dollar income and expense by DASTM gain or loss from non-interest bearing liabilities,Short-term, non-interest-bearing trade payables.

(viii)-(A)The taxpayer shall allocate DASTM gain or loss on short-term non-interest-bearing trade payables for purposes of subtitle A (including sections 904 and 952) to the same category or type of gross income as the cost or expense to which the trade payable relates. For this purpose, a short-term, non-interest-bearing trade payable is a non-interest-bearing liability with a term of 183 days or less that is incurred to purchase property or services to be used by the obligor in an active trade or business.
