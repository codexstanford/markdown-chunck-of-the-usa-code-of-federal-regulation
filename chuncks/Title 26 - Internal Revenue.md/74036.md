###### ยง 301.6331-2 Procedures and restrictions on levies.
####### Restriction on levy on date of appearance.

(c)Except for continuing levies on salaries or wages described in ยง 301.6331-1(b)(1), no levy may be made on any property of a person on the day that person, or an officer or employee of that person, is required to appear in response to a summons served for the purpose of collecting any underpayment of tax from that person.
