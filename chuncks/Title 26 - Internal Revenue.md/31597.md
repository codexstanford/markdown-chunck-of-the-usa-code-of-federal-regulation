
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### grantors and others treated as substantial owners
###### ยง 1.671-5 Reporting for widely held fixed investment trusts.
####### Reporting OID information under the safe harbor

(vii)-The trustee or middleman must aggregate the amounts of OID that are allocable to each trust interest held by a TIH for each calculation period. The amount of OID that is allocable to a trust interest, with respect to each calculation period, is determined by multiplying-

(A) The product of the OID factor and the original principal balance of the trust interest, divided by 1,000; by

(B) The number of days during the OID calculation period in that calendar year that the TIH held the trust interest.
