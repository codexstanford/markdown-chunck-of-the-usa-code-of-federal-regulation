
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.334-1 Basis of property received in liquidations.
####### Effect of basis determination under this paragraph (b)(3),1,Determination by reference to transferor's basis.

(C)-()A determination of basis under section 334(b)(1)(B) and this paragraph (b)(3) is a determination by reference to the transferor's basis, including for purposes of sections 1223(2) and 7701(a)(43).
