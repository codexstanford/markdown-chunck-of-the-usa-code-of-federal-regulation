###### ยง 1.468A-4 Treatment of nuclear decommissioning fund.
####### Gain or loss upon distribution of property by a fund.

(2)A distribution of property by a nuclear decommissioning fund (whether an actual distribution or a deemed distribution) shall be considered a disposition of property by the nuclear decommissioning fund for purposes of section 1001. In determining the amount of gain or loss from such disposition, the amount realized by the nuclear decommissioning fund shall be the fair market value of the property on the date of disposition.
