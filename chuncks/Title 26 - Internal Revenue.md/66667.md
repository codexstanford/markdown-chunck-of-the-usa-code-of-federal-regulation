
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

######## Rate of tax.

(iii)The rate of tax imposed on gasoline described in paragraph (f)(2)(i)(A) of this section is the difference between the rate of tax applicable to gasoline not described in this section and the rate of tax previously imposed on the gasoline.
