###### ยง 1.45R-4 Uniform percentage of premium paid.
####### Employers offering one QHP, employee-only coverage, composite billing.

(1)For an eligible small employer offering employee-only coverage and using composite billing, the employer satisfies the requirements of this paragraph if it pays the same amount toward the premium for each employee receiving employee-only coverage under the QHP, and that amount is equal to at least 50 percent of the premium for employee-only coverage.
