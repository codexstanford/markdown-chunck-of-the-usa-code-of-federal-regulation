
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 40 - EXCISE TAX PROCEDURAL REGULATIONS
##### ยง 40.6011(a)-2 Final returns.
###### Special rule for one-time filings,In general.

(b)-(1)A first return is also a final return if it is a one-time filing. A return is a one-time filing if the person reporting tax does not engage in any activity with respect to which tax is reportable on the return in the course of a trade or business.
