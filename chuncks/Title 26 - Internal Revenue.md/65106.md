####### In general.,wages,compensation

(a)A person (as defined in section 7701(a)(1)) that pays wages or compensation ("payor") to the individual(s) performing services for any client pursuant to a service agreement, except as provided in paragraph (d) of this section, is designated to perform the acts required of an employer with respect to the wages or compensation paid. For purposes of this section the termhas the same meaning as the term wages has for purposes of chapters 21, 23, and 24, and the termhas the same meaning as the term compensation has for purposes of chapter 22.
