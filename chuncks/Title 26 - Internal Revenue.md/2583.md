####### Apportionment of $25,000 amount among component members of a controlled group,In general.

(f)-(1)In determining the limitation based on amount of tax under section 50A(a)(2) in the case of corporations which are component members of a controlled group of corporations on a December 31, only one $25,000 amount is available to such component members for their taxable years that include such December 31.
