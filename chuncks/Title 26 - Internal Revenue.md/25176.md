####### Rollovers to individual retirement arrangements and other eligible retirement plans.

(2)A trustee-to-trustee transfer in accordance with section 401(a)(31) (generally referred to as a direct rollover) from an eligible government plan is not includible in gross income of a participant or beneficiary in the year transferred. For this purpose, the rules of section 402(c)(2) through (7) and (9) apply.
