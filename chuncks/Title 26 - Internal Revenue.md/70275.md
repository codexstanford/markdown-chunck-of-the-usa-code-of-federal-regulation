
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 54 - PENSION EXCISE TAXES
##### ยง 54.9811-1 Standards relating to benefits for mothers and newborns.
###### Hospital length of stay,General rule.

(a)-(1)Except as provided in paragraph (a)(5) of this section, a group health plan that provides benefits for a hospital length of stay in connection with childbirth for a mother or her newborn may not restrict benefits for the stay to less than-

(i) 48 hours following a vaginal delivery; or

(ii) 96 hours following a delivery by cesarean section.
