
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

##### controlled foreign corporations
###### ยง 1.951A-6 Adjustments related to tested losses.
####### Scope.

(a)This section provides rules relating to adjustments related to tested losses. Paragraph (b) of this section provides rules that increase the earnings and profits of a tested loss CFC for purposes of section 952(c)(1)(A). Paragraph (c) of this section is reserved for a rule for tested loss adjustments.
