###### ยง 1.904-5 Look-through rules as applied to controlled foreign corporations and other entities.
####### Allocating and apportioning expenses of a noncontrolled 10-percent owned foreign corporation.

(iii)Expenses of a noncontrolled 10-percent owned foreign corporation shall be allocated and apportioned in the same manner as expenses of a controlled foreign corporation under paragraph (c)(2)(ii) of this section, except that the related person interest rule of paragraphs (c)(2)(ii)(C) and (D) of this section shall not apply.
