###### ยง 1.951A-2 Tested income and tested loss.
####### Gross income attributable to a tested unit,1,Items properly reflected on separate set of books and records.,2,2,5

(B)-()Items of gross income of a controlled foreign corporation are attributable to a tested unit of the controlled foreign corporation to the extent they are properly reflected on the separate set of books and records of the tested unit, as modified under paragraph (c)(7)(ii)(B)() of this section. Each item of gross income of a controlled foreign corporation is attributable to a tested unit (and not to more than one tested unit) of the controlled foreign corporation. See paragraphs (c)(8)(iii)(D)() and (c)(8)(iii)(D)() (Example 4) of this section for illustrations of the application of the rule set forth in this paragraph (c)(7)(ii)(B).
