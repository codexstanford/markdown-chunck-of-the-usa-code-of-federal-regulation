
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury

####### Interest in a partnership,In general.

(3)-(i)A foreign corporation that is a partner in a partnership must take into account its interest in the partnership (and not the partnership assets) in determining its U.S. assets. For purposes of determining the proportion of the partnership interest that is a U.S. asset, a foreign corporation may elect to use either the asset method described in paragraph (d)(3)(ii) of this section or the income method described in paragraph (d)(3)(iii) of this section.
