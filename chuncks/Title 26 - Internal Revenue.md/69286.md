
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes

##### Subpart L - Procedure and Administration
###### ยง 53.6081-1 Automatic extension of time for filing the return to report taxes due under section 4951 for self-dealing with a nuclear decommissioning fund.
####### In general.

(a)A "disqualified person" for purposes of section 4951(e)(4) who engaged in self-dealing with a Nuclear Decommissioning Fund, and must report tax due under section 4951 on Form 1120-ND, "Return for Nuclear Decommissioning Funds and Certain Related Persons," will be allowed an automatic 6-month extension of time to file the return after the date prescribed for filing the return if the disqualified person files an application under this section in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section. For guidance on requesting an extension of time to file Form 1120-ND for purposes of reporting contributions received, income earned, administrative expenses of operating the fund, and the tax on modified gross income, see ยง 1.6081-3 of this chapter.
