
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter C - Employment Taxes and Collection of Income Tax at Source

##### Subpart E - Collection of Income Tax at Source
###### ยง 31.3406(d)-5 Backup withholding when the Service or a broker notifies the payor to withhold because the payee's taxpayer identification number is incorrect.
####### Date of receipt.

(5)For purposes of this section, the date set forth on the notice from the Internal Revenue Service or broker under paragraph (c) (1) or (2) of this section is considered to be the date of receipt of the notice by the payor. However, if the payor demonstrates to the satisfaction of the Internal Revenue Service that the date of actual receipt of the notice is later than the date on the notice, the actual date of receipt is controlling.
