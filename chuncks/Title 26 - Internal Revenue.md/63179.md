
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter B - Estate and Gift Taxes
#### PART 25 - GIFT TAX; GIFTS MADE AFTER DECEMBER 31, 1954
##### Special Valuation Rules
###### ยง 25.2701-3 Determination of amount of gift.
####### Family interest percentage.

(ii)The family interest percentage is the highest ownership percentage (determined on the basis of relative fair market values) of family-held interests in-

(A) Any class of subordinate equity interest; or

(B) All subordinate equity interests, valued in the aggregate.
