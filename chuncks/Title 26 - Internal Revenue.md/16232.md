####### Election of section 1374 treatment,In general,Property owned by a C corporation that becomes property of a RIC or REIT.

(c)-(1)-(i)Paragraph (b) of this section does not apply if the RIC or REIT that was formerly a C corporation or that acquired property from a C corporation makes the election described in paragraph (c)(4) of this section.
