
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.197-2 Amortization of goodwill and certain other intangibles.
####### Termination by sale or exchange of interest.

(B)In applying paragraph (g)(2)(ii) of this section to a partnership that is terminated pursuant to section 708(b)(1)(B) (relating to deemed terminations from the sale or exchange of an interest), the terminated partnership is treated as the transferor and the new partnership is treated as the transferee with respect to any section 197 intangible held by the terminated partnership immediately preceding the termination. (See paragraph (g)(3) of this section for the treatment of increases in the bases of property of the terminated partnership under section 743(b).)
