
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Regulations Applying Section 382 With Respect to Testing Dates (and Corporations Joining or Leaving Consolidated Groups) Before June 25, 1999
###### ยง 1.1502-91A Application of section 382 with respect to a consolidated group generally applicable for testing dates before June 25, 1999.
####### Pre-change consolidated attribute,Defined.

(e)-(1)A pre-change consolidated attribute of a loss group is-

(i) Any loss described in paragraph (c)(1) (i) or (ii) of this section (relating to the definition of loss group) that is allocable to the period ending on or before the change date; and

(ii) Any recognized built-in loss of the loss group.
