###### ยง 1.6063-1 Signing of returns, statements, and other documents made by partnerships.
####### Evidence of authority to sign.

(b)A partner's signature on a return, statement, or other document made by or for a partnership of which he is a member shall be prima facie evidence that such partner is authorized to sign such return, statement, or other document.
