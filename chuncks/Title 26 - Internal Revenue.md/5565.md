##### Tax Exemption Requirements for State and Local Bonds
###### § 1.141-6 Allocation and accounting rules.
####### Definition of qualified equity.,qualified equity,See

(3)For purposes of this section,means proceeds of bonds that are not tax-advantaged bonds and funds that are not derived from proceeds of a borrowing that are spent on the same eligible mixed-use project as the proceeds of the applicable bonds. Qualified equity does not include equity interests in real property or tangible personal property.
