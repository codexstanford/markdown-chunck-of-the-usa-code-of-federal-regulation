####### Mixed straddle account defined,In general.,mixed straddle account

(b)-(1)The termmeans an account for determining gains and losses from all positions held as capital assets in a designated class of activities by the taxpayer at the time the taxpayer elects to establish a mixed straddle account. A separate mixed straddle account must be established for each separate designated class of activities.
