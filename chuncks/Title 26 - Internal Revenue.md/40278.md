####### Anti-avoidance rules related to certain transfers of property,Disregard of adjusted basis in specified tangible property held temporarily,In general.,tested quarter close,acquiring CFC,applicable U.S. shareholder

(h)-(1)-(i)For purposes of determining a controlled foreign corporation's aggregate adjusted bases in specified tangible property as of the close of a quarter (), the adjusted basis in specified tangible property is disregarded as of the tested quarter close if the controlled foreign corporation () acquires the property temporarily before the tested quarter close with a principal purpose of increasing the deemed tangible income return of a U.S. shareholder () for a U.S. shareholder year, and the holding of the property by the acquiring CFC as of the tested quarter close would, without regard to this paragraph (h)(1)(i), increase the deemed tangible income return of the applicable U.S. shareholder for the U.S. shareholder inclusion year.
