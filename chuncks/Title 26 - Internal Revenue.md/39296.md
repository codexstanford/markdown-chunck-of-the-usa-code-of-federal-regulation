###### ยง 1.921-2 Foreign Sales Corporation - general rules.
####### A-8.

Foreign trade income, defined in section 923(b), is gross income of an FSC attributable to foreign trading gross receipts. It includes both the profits earned by the FSC itself from exports and commissions earned by the FSC from products and services exported by others.
