####### Section 1374 treatment,In general,Property owned by a C corporation that becomes property of a RIC or REIT.

(b)-(1)-(i)If property owned by a C corporation becomes the property of a RIC or REIT in a conversion transaction, then the RIC or REIT will be subject to tax on the net built-in gain in the converted property under the rules of section 1374 and the regulations thereunder, as modified by this paragraph (b), as if the RIC or REIT were an S corporation.
