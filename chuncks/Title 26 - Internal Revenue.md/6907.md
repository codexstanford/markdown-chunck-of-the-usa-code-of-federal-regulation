####### Effect of election on otherwise deductible expenses for travel away from home,Legislative days,Living expenses.

(f)-(1)-(i)For any legislative day for which an election under section 162(h) and this section is in effect, the amount of an electing taxpayer's living expenses while away from home is the greater of the amount of the living expenses-
