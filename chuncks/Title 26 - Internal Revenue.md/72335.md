(h)Before disclosing any return or return information received under section 6104(c) in a proceeding described in paragraph (g)(2) of this section, the ASO must notify the IRS of the intention to make such a disclosure. No State officer or employee may make such a disclosure except in accordance with any conditions the IRS might impose in response to the ASO's notice of intent. No such disclosure may be made if the IRS determines that the disclosure would seriously impair Federal tax administration.
