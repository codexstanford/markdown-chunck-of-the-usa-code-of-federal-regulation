###### ยง 1.367(d)-1T Transfers of intangible property to foreign corporations (temporary).
####### Subsequent transfer of intangible property to related person.

(3)The requirement that a U.S. person recognize gain under paragraph (c) or (e) of this section shall not be affected by the transferee foreign corporation's subsequent disposition of the transferred intangible property to a related person.
