###### ยง 1.148-6 General allocation and accounting rules.
####### Bona fide deviations from accounting method.

(2)An accounting method does not fail to be reasonable and consistently applied solely because a different accounting method is used for a bona fide governmental purpose to consistently account for a particular item. Bona fide governmental purposes may include special State law restrictions imposed on specific funds or actions to avoid grant forfeitures.
