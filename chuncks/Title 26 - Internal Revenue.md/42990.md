####### Residence,In general.

(d)-(1)Except as otherwise provided in this paragraph (d), for purposes of sections 985 through 989, the residence of any person shall be-

(i) In the case of an individual, the country in which such individual's tax home (as defined in section 911(d)(3)) is located;

(ii) In the case of a corporation, partnership, trust or estate which is a United States person (as defined in section 7701(a)(30)), the United States; and

(iii) In the case of a corporation, partnership, trust or estate which is not a United States person, a country other than the United States.
