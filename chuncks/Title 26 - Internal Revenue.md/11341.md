
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.186-1 Recoveries of damages for antitrust violations, etc.
####### Unrecovered losses,In general.,unrecovered losses sustained as a result of such compensable injury

(d)-(1)For purposes of this section, the termmeans the sum of the amounts of the net operating losses for each taxable year in whole or in part within the injury period, to the extent that such net operating losses are attributable to such compensable injury, reduced by (i) the sum of any amounts of such net operating losses which were allowed as a net operating loss carryback or carryover for any prior taxable year under the provisions of section 172, and (ii) the sum of any amounts allowed as deductions under section 186 (a) and this section for all prior taxable years with respect to the same compensable injury. Accordingly, a deduction is permitted under section 186(a) and this section with respect to net operating losses whether or not the period for carryover under section 172 has expired.
