##### Earned Income of Citizens or Residents of United States
###### ยง 1.909-2 Splitter arrangements.
####### Split taxes from a partnership inter-branch payment splitter arrangement.

(ii)The split taxes from a partnership inter-branch splitter arrangement equal the excess of the amount of the inter-branch payment tax allocated to a partner under the partnership agreement over the amount of the inter-branch payment tax that would have been allocated to the partner if the inter-branch payment tax had been allocated to the partners in the same proportion as the distributive shares of income in the CFTE category referred to in paragraph (b)(4)(i) of this section.
