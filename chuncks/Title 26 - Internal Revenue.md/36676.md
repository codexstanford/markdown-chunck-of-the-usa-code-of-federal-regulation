####### Definition of the amount of a U.S. asset,In general.

(2)-(i)For purposes of this section, the term "amount of a U.S. asset" means the U.S. asset's adjusted basis for purposes of computing earnings and profits ("E&P basis") multiplied by the proportion of the asset that is treated as a U.S. asset under paragraphs (d) (1) through (4) of this section.
