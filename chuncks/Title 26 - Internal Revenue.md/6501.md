####### Redemption at first call date,General rule.,first call requirement,savings test

(f)-(1)Under sections 149(d)(3)(A) (ii) and (iii) (the), bonds refunded by an advance refunding must be redeemed on their first call date if the savings test under section 149(d)(3)(B)(i) (the) is satisfied. The savings test is satisfied if the issuer may realize present value debt service savings (determined without regard to administrative expenses) in connection with the issue of which the refunding bond is a part.
