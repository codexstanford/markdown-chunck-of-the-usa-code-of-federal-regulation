
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.965-7 Elections, payment, and other special rules.
####### Annualized earnings and profits amount.,annualized earnings and profits amount

(ii)The termmeans, with respect to a specified foreign corporation, an E&P measurement date, and a notional measurement date, the amount equal to the product of the number of days between the notional measurement date and the E&P measurement date (not including the former, but including the latter) multiplied by the daily earnings amount of the specified foreign corporation. The annualized earnings and profits amount is expressed as a negative number if the E&P measurement date precedes the notional measurement date.
