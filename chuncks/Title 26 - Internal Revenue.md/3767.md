###### ยง 1.66-2 Treatment of community income where spouses live apart.
####### Transferred income.,de minimis

(c)For purposes of this section, transferred income does not include aamount of earned income that is transferred between the spouses. In addition, any amount of earned income transferred for the benefit of the spouses' child will not be treated as an indirect transfer to one spouse. Additionally, income transferred between spouses is presumed to be a transfer of earned income. This presumption is rebuttable.
