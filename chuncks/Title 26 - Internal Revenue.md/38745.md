(ii)-(A)U.S.C., an accrual method taxpayer, is a domestic corporation that operates in branch form in Country X. U.S.C. uses the calendar year for Country X tax purposes. For Federal income tax purposes, U.S.C. elects pursuant to ยง 1.441-2(a) to use a 52-53 week taxable year that ends on the last Friday of December.
