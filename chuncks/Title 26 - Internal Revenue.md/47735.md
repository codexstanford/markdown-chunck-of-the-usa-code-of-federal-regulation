
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Small Business Corporations and Their Shareholders

####### Definitions and special rules,Recapture date.

(c)-(1)In the case of a transaction described in paragraph (a)(1) or (b)(1) of this section, the recapture date is the day before the effective date of the S corporation election.
