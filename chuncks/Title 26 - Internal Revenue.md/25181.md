###### ยง 1.457-7 Taxation of Distributions Under Eligible Plans.
####### Initial election to defer commencement of distributions,In general.

(ii)-(A)An eligible plan of a tax-exempt entity may provide a period for making an initial election during which the participant or beneficiary may elect, in accordance with the terms of the plan, to defer the payment of some or all of the amounts deferred to a fixed or determinable future time. The period for making this initial election must expire prior to the first time that any such amounts would be considered made available under the plan under paragraph (c)(2)(i) of this section.
