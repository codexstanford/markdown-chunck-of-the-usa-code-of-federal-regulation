
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter C - Employment Taxes and Collection of Income Tax at Source

##### Subpart E - Collection of Income Tax at Source
###### ยง 31.3401(a)(6)-1 Remuneration for services of nonresident alien individuals.
####### Remuneration for services performed outside the United States.

(b)Remuneration paid to a nonresident alien individual (other than a resident of Puerto Rico) for services performed outside the United States is excepted from wages and hence is not subject to withholding.
