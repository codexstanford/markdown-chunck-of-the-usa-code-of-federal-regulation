####### Securities never held in connection with the taxpayer's activities as a dealer in securities.

(a)If a security is never held in connection with the taxpayer's activities as a dealer in securities, section 475(d)(3)(A) does not affect the character of gain or loss from the security, even if the taxpayer fails to identify the security under section 475(b)(2).
