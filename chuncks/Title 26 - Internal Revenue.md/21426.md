(ii)The relief of section 411(a)(13) does not permit the accumulated benefit under a lump sum-based benefit formula to be reduced in a manner that would be prohibited if that reduction were applied to the accrued benefit. (A) Benefit payments;

(B) Qualified domestic relations orders under section 414(p);

(D) Amendments that would reduce the accrued benefit but that are permitted under section 411(d)(6);
