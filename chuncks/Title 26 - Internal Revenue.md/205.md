####### Aggregate amount of mortgage credit certificates,In general.

(b)-(1)The aggregate amount of qualified mortgage credit certificates issued under a qualified mortgage credit certificate program is the sum of the products determined by multiplying-

(i) The certified indebtedness amount of each qualified mortgage credit certificate issued under that program, by
