(f)-(1)-(i)Except as provided in paragraph (f)(1)(ii), (iii), or (iv) of this section, the termmeans income of a United States person, other than a pass-through entity, that is-

(A) Income attributable to foreign branches of the United States person held directly or indirectly through disregarded entities;
