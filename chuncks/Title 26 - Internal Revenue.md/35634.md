
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.864-4 U.S. source income effectively connected with U.S. business.
####### Direct relationship between holding of asset and trade or business,a,In general.,1,2,3,4

(iv)-()In determining whether an asset is held in a direct relationship to the trade or business conducted in the United States, principal consideration shall be given to whether the asset is needed in that trade or business. An asset shall be considered needed in a trade or business, for this purpose, only if the asset is held to meet the present needs of that trade or business and not its anticipated future needs. An asset shall be considered as needed in the trade or business conducted in the United States if, for example, the asset is held to meet the operating expenses of that trade or business. Conversely, an asset shall be considered as not needed in the trade or business conducted in the United States if, for example, the asset is held for the purpose of providing for () future diversification into a new trade or business, () expansion of trade or business activities conducted outside of the United States, () future plant replacement, or () future business contingencies.
