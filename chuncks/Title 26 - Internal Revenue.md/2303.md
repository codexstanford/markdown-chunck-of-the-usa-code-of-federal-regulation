###### ยง 1.48-4 Election of lessor of new section 38 property to treat lessee as purchaser.
####### General election,In general.

(g)-(1)In lieu of making elections on a property-by-property basis in the manner and time prescribed in paragraph (f) of this section, a lessor may, with respect to a particular taxable year of a particular lessee, make a general election to treat such lessee as having purchased all properties possession of which is transferred under lease by the lessor to the lessee during such taxable year of the lessee.
