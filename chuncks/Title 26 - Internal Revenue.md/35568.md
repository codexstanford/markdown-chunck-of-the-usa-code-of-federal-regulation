####### Income derived from space/ocean communications activity. Income derived by a taxpayer from space/ocean communications activity

(v)(space/ocean communications income) is income derived from communications activity, as defined in paragraph (h)(2) of this section, when the taxpayer is paid to transmit between a point in space or international water and another point in space or international water.
