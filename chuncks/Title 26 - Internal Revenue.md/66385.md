####### ยง 48.4061(a)-2 Bonding of importers.
######## Application for determination whether bond required,Requirement of application,In general.

(b)-(1)-(i)Except as otherwise provided in subparagraph (2) of this paragraph, every importer of articles taxable under section 4061(a) shall make application for a determination whether the importer is required to give bond in accordance with the provisions of paragraph (c) of this section.
