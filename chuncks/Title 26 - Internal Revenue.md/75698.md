
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### ยง 301.7602-1 Examination of books and witnesses.
######## Hiring of certain non-government attorneys.

(C)The IRS may not hire an attorney as a contractor to assist in an examination under section 7602 unless the attorney is hired by the IRS as a specialist in foreign, state, or local law (including foreign, state, or local tax law), or in non-tax substantive law that is relevant to an issue in the examination, such as patent law, property law, or environmental law, or is hired for knowledge, skills, or abilities other than providing legal services as an attorney.
