
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Items Not Deductible
###### ยง 1.274-4 Disallowance of certain foreign travel expenses.
####### Lack of control over travel.

(i)Expenses of travel otherwise deductible under section 162 or 212 shall be considered fully allocable to business activity if, considering all the facts and circumstances, the individual incurring such expenses did not have substantial control over the arranging of the business trip. A person who is required to travel to a business destination will not be considered to have substantial control over the arranging of the business trip merely because he has control over the timing of the trip.
