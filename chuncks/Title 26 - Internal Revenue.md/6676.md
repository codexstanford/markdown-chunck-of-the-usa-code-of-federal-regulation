####### Custodial parent,In general.,custodial parent,noncustodial parent

(d)-(1)Theis the parent with whom the child resides for the greater number of nights during the calendar year, and theis the parent who is not the custodial parent. A child is treated as residing with neither parent if the child is emancipated under state law. For purposes of this section, a child resides with a parent for a night if the child sleeps-

(i) At the residence of that parent (whether or not the parent is present); or

(ii) In the company of the parent, when the child does not sleep at a parent's residence (for example, the parent and child are on vacation together).
