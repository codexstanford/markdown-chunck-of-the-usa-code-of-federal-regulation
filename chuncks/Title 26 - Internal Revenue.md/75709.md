######## Elements of third-party contact explained,Initiation by an IRS employee,Explanation,Initiation.

(c)-(1)-(i)-(A)An IRS employee initiates a communication whenever it is the employee who first tries to communicate with a person other than the taxpayer. Returning unsolicited telephone calls or speaking with persons other than the taxpayer as part of an attempt to speak to the taxpayer are not initiations of third-party contacts.
