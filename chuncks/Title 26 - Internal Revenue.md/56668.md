
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.6046-1 Returns as to organization or reorganization of foreign corporations and as to acquisitions of their stock.
####### Actual ownership of stock.

(h)If any shareholder, referred to in this section, is not the actual owner of the stock of the foreign corporation, the information required under this section shall be furnished in the name of and by such actual owner. For example, in the case of stock held by a nominee, the information required under this section shall be furnished by the actual owner of such stock.
