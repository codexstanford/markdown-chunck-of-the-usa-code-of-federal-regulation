
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Rules Relating to Individuals' Title 11 Cases
###### ยง 1.1398-2 Treatment of section 465 losses in individuals' title 11 cases.
####### Chapter 11 cases.

(B)In a case under chapter 11 of title 11 of the United States Code, the election is made by incorporating the election into a bankruptcy plan that is confirmed by the bankruptcy court or into an order of such court and filing the pertinent portion of the plan or order with the returns (or amended returns) of the debtor and the estate for their first taxable years ending after November 9, 1992.
