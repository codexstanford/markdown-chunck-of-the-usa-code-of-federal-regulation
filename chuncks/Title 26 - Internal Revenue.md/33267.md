
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.806-3 Certain changes in reserves and assets.
####### Manner in which adjustments shall be made,Daily basis.

(b)-(1)The means of the life insurance reserves, and the mean of the assets, shall be appropriately adjusted, on a daily basis, to reflect the amounts involved in a transfer described in section 806(a) and paragraph (a) of this section. The transferor and the transferee shall be treated as having held such life insurance reserves and assets for a fraction of the year in which the transfer occurs.
