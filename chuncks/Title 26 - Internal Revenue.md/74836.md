####### ยง 301.6688-1 Assessable penalties with respect to information required to be furnished with respect to possessions.
######## In general.

(a)Each individual described in section 7654(a) who is subject to an information reporting requirement promulgated under the authority of section 937(c) or 7654 and who fails to fully satisfy such requirement within the time prescribed for reporting such information must, in addition to any criminal penalty provided by law, pay a penalty of $1000 for each such failure. Information reporting requirements promulgated under the authority of sections 937(c) and 7654(e) include the requirement for an individual to file Form 8898, "Statement for Individuals who Begin or End Bona Fide Residence in a U.S. Possession," under ยง 1.937-1(h) of this chapter, to report that he or she became or ceased to be a bona fide resident of a possession.
