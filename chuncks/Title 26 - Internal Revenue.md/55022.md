
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.6011-4 Requirement of statement disclosing participation in certain transactions by taxpayers.
####### Reportable transactions,In general.

(b)-(1)A reportable transaction is a transaction described in any of the paragraphs (b)(2) through (7) of this section. The term transaction includes all of the factual elements relevant to the expected tax treatment of any investment, entity, plan, or arrangement, and includes any series of steps carried out as part of a plan.
