###### ยง 1.732-1 Basis of distributed property other than money.
####### Distribution in liquidation.

(b)Where a partnership distributes property (other than money) in liquidation of a partner's entire interest in the partnership, the basis of such property to the partner shall be an amount equal to the adjusted basis of his interest in the partnership reduced by the amount of any money distributed to him in the same transaction.
