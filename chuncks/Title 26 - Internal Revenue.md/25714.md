####### Method of accounting for section 467 rental agreements.

(b)If a rental agreement is a section 467 rental agreement, as described in paragraph (c) of this section, the lessor and lessee must each take into account for any taxable year the sum of-

(1) The section 467 rent for the taxable year (as defined in paragraph (d) of this section); and
