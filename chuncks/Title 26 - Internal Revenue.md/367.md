####### Recapture percentage.

(5)For purposes of this section, the recapture percentage is-

(i) 100, if the recapture date is within the first full year after the date the vehicle is placed in service;

(ii) 66, if the recapture date is within the second full year after the date the vehicle is placed in service; or

(iii) 33, if the recapture date is within the third full year after the date the vehicle is placed in service.
