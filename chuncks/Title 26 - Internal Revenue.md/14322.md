
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Items Not Deductible
###### ยง 1.263A-3 Rules relating to property acquired for resale.
####### LIFO increment.

(B)If the taxpayer determines there has been an inventory increment, the taxpayer must state the amount of the increment in current-year dollars (stated in terms of section 471 costs). The taxpayer then multiplies this amount by the combined absorption ratio. The resulting product is the additional section 263A costs that must be added to the taxpayer's increment for the year stated in terms of section 471 costs.
