
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### ยง 26.2612-1 Definitions.
###### Certain interests disregarded.

(ii)An interest which is used primarily to postpone or avoid the GST tax is disregarded for purposes of chapter 13. An interest is considered as used primarily to postpone or avoid the GST tax if a significant purpose for the creation of the interest is to postpone or avoid the tax.
