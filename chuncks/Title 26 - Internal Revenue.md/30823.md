
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Estates, Trusts, and Beneficiaries
###### ยง 1.643(d)-1 Definition of "foreign trust created by a United States person".
####### Determination of a foreign trust created by a U.S. person,Transfers of money or property only by a U.S. person.

(b)-(1)If all the items of money or property constituting the corpus of a foreign trust are transferred to the trust by a U.S. person, the entire foreign trust is a foreign trust created by a U.S. person.
