####### End of dormancy.

(iii)An account that is a dormant account under paragraph (d)(6)(ii) of this section ceases to be a dormant account when-

(A) The account holder initiates a transaction with regard to the account or any other account held by the account holder with the FFI;

(B) The account holder communicates with the FFI that maintains such account regarding the account or any other account held by the account holder with the FFI; or

(C) The account ceases to be a dormant account under applicable laws or regulations or the participating FFI's normal operating procedures.
