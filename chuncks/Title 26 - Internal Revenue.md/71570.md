
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 156 - EXCISE TAX ON GREENMAIL
##### Subpart B - Procedure and Administration

####### Returns filed with service centers or by hand carrying.

(e)Notwithstanding paragraph (a), (b), (c), or (d) of this section, unless a return is filed by hand carrying, whenever instructions applicable to tax returns under chapter 54 of the Code provide that the returns be filed with a service center, the returns must be so filed in accordance with the instructions.
