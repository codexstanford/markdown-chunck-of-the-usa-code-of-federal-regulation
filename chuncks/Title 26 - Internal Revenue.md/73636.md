####### ยง 301.6314-1 Receipt for taxes.
######## Duplicate receipt for payment of estate taxes.

(b)Upon request, the district director or the director of a service center will issue duplicate receipts to the person paying the estate tax, either of which will be sufficient evidence of such payment and entitle the executor to be credited with the amount by any court having jurisdiction to audit or settle his accounts. For definition of the term "executor", see section 2203.
