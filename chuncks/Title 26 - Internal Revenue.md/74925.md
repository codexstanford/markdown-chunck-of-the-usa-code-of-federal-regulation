
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### Additions to the Tax and Additional Amounts
####### ยง 301.6708-1 Failure to maintain lists of advisees with respect to reportable transactions.
######## Failure to make list available.

(d)A failure to make the list available includes any failure to furnish the requested list to the IRS in a timely manner and in the form required under section 6112 and its corresponding regulations. Examples of failures to make a list available include instances in which a person fails to furnish any list; furnishes an incomplete list; or furnishes a list, whether or not complete, after the time required by this section.
