####### Notice of intent to levy,In general.

(a)-(1)Levy may be made upon the salary, wages, or other property of a taxpayer for any unpaid tax no less than 30 days after the district director, the service center director, or the compliance center director (director) has notified the taxpayer in writing of the intent to levy. The notice must be given in person, be left at the dwelling or usual place of business of the taxpayer, or be sent by registered or certified mail to the taxpayer's last known address. For further guidance regarding the definition of last known address, see ยง 301.6212-2. The notice of intent to levy is separate from, but may be given at the same time as, the notice and demand described in ยง 301.6331-1.
