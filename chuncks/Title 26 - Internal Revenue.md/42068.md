####### Applicable amount for section 965(n) election.

(ii)If a person makes a section 965(n) election, the amount referred to in paragraph (e)(1)(i) of this section is the sum of-

(A) The person's section 965(a) inclusions for the taxable year reduced by the person's section 965(c) deductions for the taxable year, and
