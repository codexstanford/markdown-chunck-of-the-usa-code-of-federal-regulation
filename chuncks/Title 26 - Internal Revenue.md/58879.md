####### Retirement and reissuance of loan.,1

(D)If a split-dollar loan described in paragraph (e)(5)(iii)(A)() of this section remains outstanding longer than the term determined under paragraph (e)(5)(iii)(C) of this section because of the continued performance of substantial services, the split-dollar loan is treated for purposes of this section as retired and reissued as a split-dollar demand loan at that time for an amount of cash equal to the loan's adjusted issue price on that date.
