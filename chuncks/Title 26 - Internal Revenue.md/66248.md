
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 48 - MANUFACTURERS AND RETAILERS EXCISE TAXES
##### Subpart F - Special Fuels
###### ยง 48.4041-8 Definitions.
####### Kerosene, gas oil, or fuel oil.

(g)(1) The term "kerosene, gas, oil or fuel oil" means any product (i) 10 percent of which has not been recovered when the thermometer reads 347 &#xB0;F. (175 &#xB0;C.), and (ii) 95 percent of which has not been recovered when the thermometer reads 464 &#xB0;F. (240 &#xB0;C.), when subjected to distillation in accordance with the "Standard Method of Test for Distillation of Gasoline, Naptha, Kerosene, and Similar Petroleum Products" (A.S.T.M. designation: D86) of the American Society for Testing Materials.

(2) Products designated as kerosene, gas, oil, or fuel oil which do not fall within the specifications of both paragraphs (g)(1) (i) and (ii) of this section are taxable as special motor fuel if sold or used as a fuel in a motor vehicle or motorboat.
