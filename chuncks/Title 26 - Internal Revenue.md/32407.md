##### PARTNERS AND PARTNERSHIPS

####### Permissible conventions for each variation,Rules applicable to all partnerships.

(3)-(i)A partnership generally shall use the calendar day convention for each variation; however, for all variations during a taxable year for which the partnership uses the interim closing method, the partnership may instead use the semi-monthly or monthly convention by agreement of the partners (within the meaning of paragraph (f) of this section). The partnership must use the same convention for all variations for which the partnership uses the interim closing method.
