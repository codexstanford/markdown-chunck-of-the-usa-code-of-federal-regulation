
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.6012-2 Corporations required to make returns of income.
####### Subchapter T cooperatives,In general.

(f)-(1)For taxable years ending on or after December 31, 2007, a cooperative organization described in section 1381 (including a farmers' cooperative exempt from tax under section 521) is required to make a return, whether or not it has taxable income and regardless of the amount of its gross income, on Form 1120-C, "U.S. Income Tax Return for Cooperative Associations," or such other form as may be designated by the Commissioner.
