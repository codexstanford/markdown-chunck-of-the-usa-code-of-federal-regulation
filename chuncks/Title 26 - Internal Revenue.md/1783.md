
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.46-1 Determination of amount.
####### Nonrefundable energy credit tax liability limitation.

(3)(i) For nonrefundable energy credit carrybacks to a taxable year ending before October 1, 1978, the tax liability limitation is the portion of tax liability that does not exceed $25,000 plus a percentage of the excess, as determined under section 46(a)(3)(B).

(ii) For a taxable year ending after September 30, 1978, the tax liability limitation for available nonrefundable energy credit is 100 percent of the year's tax liability.
