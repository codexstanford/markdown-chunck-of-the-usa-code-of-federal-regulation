###### ยง 1.45R-3 Calculating the credit.
####### Average premium limitation,In general.

(b)-(1)The amount of an eligible small employer's premium payments that is taken into account in calculating the credit is limited to the premium payments the employer would have made under the same arrangement if the average premium for the small group market in the rating area in which the employee enrolls for coverage were substituted for the actual premium.
