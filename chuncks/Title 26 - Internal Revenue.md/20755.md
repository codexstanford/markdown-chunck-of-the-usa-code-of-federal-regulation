
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.401(k)-1 Certain cash or deferred arrangements.
####### Distribution necessary to satisfy financial need,Distribution may not exceed amount of need.

(iii)-(A)A distribution is treated as necessary to satisfy an immediate and heavy financial need of an employee only to the extent the amount of the distribution is not in excess of the amount required to satisfy the financial need (including any amounts necessary to pay any federal, state, or local income taxes or penalties reasonably anticipated to result from the distribution).
