##### Deductions for Personal Exemptions
###### ยง 1.152-4 Special rule for a child of divorced or separated parents or parents who live apart.
####### Attachment to return.

(iii)The parent revoking the written declaration must attach a copy of the revocation to the parent's return for each taxable year for which the parent claims a child as a dependent as a result of the revocation. The parent revoking the written declaration must keep a copy of the revocation and evidence of delivery of the notice to the other parent, or of the reasonable efforts to provide actual notice.
