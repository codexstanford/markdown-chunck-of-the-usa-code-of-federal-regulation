
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### methods of accounting in general
###### ยง 1.446-7 Net asset value method for certain money market fund shares.
####### Adjustments,Dispositions in which gain or loss is not recognized.

(iii)-(A)If, during the computation period, any shares in an MMF are disposed of in transactions in which gain or loss would not be recognized if the taxpayer did not apply the NAV method to the shares, the net investment in the MMF for the computation period is decreased by the fair market value of each such share at the time of its disposition.
