
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 52 - ENVIRONMENTAL TAXES
##### ยง 52.4681-1 Taxes imposed with respect to ozone-depleting chemicals.
###### Floor stocks tax,Imposition of tax.

(3)-(i)Section 4682(h) imposes a floor stocks tax on ODCs that-

(A) Are held by any person other than the manufacturer or importer of the ODC on a date specified in paragraph (a)(3)(ii) of this section; and

(B) Are held on such date for sale or for use in further manufacture.

(ii) Dates on which tax imposed. The floor stocks tax is imposed on January 1 of each calendar year after 1989.
