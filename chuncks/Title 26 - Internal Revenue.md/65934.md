
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes

##### Subpart B - Tax on Use of Certain Highway Motor Vehicles
###### ยง 41.4483-3 Exemption for trucks used for 5,000 or fewer miles and agricultural vehicles used for 7,500 or fewer miles on public highways.
####### Cessation of suspension from tax.

(b)If a highway motor vehicle on which the tax under section 4481(a) is suspended for a particular taxable period under paragraph (a)(1) of this section is used for more than 5,000 miles on public highways during such taxable period, the owner of the vehicle is liable for the tax for the entire taxable period in accordance with section 4481(a).
