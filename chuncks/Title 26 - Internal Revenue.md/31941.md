
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES

###### ยง 1.702-3T 4-Year spread (temporary).
####### Year unamortized items taken into account,In general.

(2)-(i)If, at the end of a partner's taxable year, the fraction determined under paragraph (g)(2)(ii) of this section is-

(A) Greater than, the partner must continue to take the unamortized income and expense items into account ratably over the 4-year spread period;

(B) Greater thanbut less than or equal to, the partner must, in addition to its ratable amortization, take into account in such year 50 percent of the income and expense items that would otherwise be unamortized at the end of such year (however, this paragraph (g)(2)(i)(B) is only applied once with respect to a partner's interest in a particular partnership); or

(C) Less than or equal to, the partner must take into account the entire balance of unamortized income and expense items in such year.
