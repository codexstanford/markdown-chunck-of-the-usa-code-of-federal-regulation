
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.936-6 Intangible property income when an election out is made: Cost sharing and profit split options; covered intangibles.
####### A. 8:

The term "total sales" means aggregate sales or other dispositions of products in the same product area as the possession product, less returns and allowances and less indirect taxes imposed on the production of the product, for the taxable year to persons who are not members of the affiliated group. The sales price to be used is the sales price received by the affiliated group from persons who are not members of the affiliated group.
