
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Special Deductions for Corporations
###### ยง 1.249-1 Limitation on deduction of bond premium on repurchase.
####### Limitation,General rule.

(a)-(1)No deduction is allowed to the issuing corporation for any "repurchase premium" paid or incurred to repurchase a convertible obligation to the extent the repurchase premium exceeds a "normal call premium."
