
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.901-2 Income, war profits, or excess profits tax paid or accrued.
####### Subsidy.

(ii)The term "subsidy" includes any benefit conferred, directly or indirectly, by a foreign country to one of the parties enumerated in paragraph (e)(3)(i)(A) of this section. Substance and not form shall govern in determining whether a subsidy exists. The fact that the U.S. taxpayer may derive no demonstrable benefit from the subsidy is irrelevant in determining whether a subsidy exists.
