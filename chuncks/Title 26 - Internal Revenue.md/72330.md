######## Disclosure limitations.

(e)Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, the IRS will not disclose or make available for inspection under section 6104(c) any information, the disclosure of which it determines would seriously impair Federal tax administration, including, but not limited to-

(1) Identification of a confidential informant or interference with a civil or criminal tax investigation; and

(2) Information obtained pursuant to a tax convention, as defined in section 6105(c)(2), between the United States and a foreign government.
