###### ยง 1.904-5 Look-through rules as applied to controlled foreign corporations and other entities.
####### Interest payments from noncontrolled 10-percent owned foreign corporations.

(ii)If interest is received or accrued by a shareholder from a noncontrolled 10-percent owned foreign corporation (where the shareholder is a domestic corporation that is a United States shareholder of such noncontrolled 10-percent owned foreign corporation), the rules of paragraph (m)(2)(i) of this section apply in determining the portion of the interest payment that is from sources within the United States, except that the related party interest rules of paragraph (c)(2)(ii)(C) of this section do not apply.
