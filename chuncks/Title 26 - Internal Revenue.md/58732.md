
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.7704-4 Qualifying income - mineral and natural resources.
####### In general.

(a)For purposes of section 7704(d)(1)(E), qualifying income is income and gains from qualifying activities with respect to minerals or natural resources as defined in paragraph (b) of this section. Qualifying activities are section 7704(d)(1)(E) activities (as described in paragraph (c) of this section) and intrinsic activities (as described in paragraph (d) of this section).
