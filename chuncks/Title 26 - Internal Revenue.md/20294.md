
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

###### ยง 1.401(a)(5)-1 Special rules relating to nondiscrimination requirements.
####### Defined benefit plans integrated with social security,In general.

(e)-(1)Under section 401(a)(5)(D), a defined benefit plan does not discriminate in favor of highly compensated employees (as defined in section 414(q)) with respect to the amount of employer-provided contributions or benefits solely because the plan provides that, with respect to each employee, the employer-provided accrued retirement benefit under the plan is limited to the excess (if any) of-

(i) The employee's final pay from the employer, over

(ii) The employer-provided retirement benefit created under the Social Security Act and attributable to service by the employee for the employer.
