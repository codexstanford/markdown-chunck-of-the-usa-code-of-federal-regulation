
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

####### Determination of depreciation allowance,In general.

(d)-(1)Depreciation allowances are determined for each general asset account. The depreciation allowances must be recorded in a depreciation reserve account for each general asset account. The allowance for depreciation under this section constitutes the amount of depreciation allowable under section 167(a).
