####### Premium assistance amount,Premium assistance amount.

(d)-(1)The premium assistance amount for a coverage month is the lesser of-

(i) The premiums for the month, reduced by any amounts that were refunded in the same taxable year as the premium liability is incurred, for one or more qualified health plans in which a taxpayer or a member of the taxpayer's family enrolls (enrollment premiums); or

(ii) The excess of the adjusted monthly premium for the applicable benchmark plan (benchmark plan premium) overof the product of a taxpayer's household income and the applicable percentage for the taxable year (the taxpayer's contribution amount).
