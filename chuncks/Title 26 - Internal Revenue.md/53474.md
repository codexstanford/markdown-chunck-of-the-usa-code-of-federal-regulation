
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Special Taxes and Taxpayers
###### ยง 1.1502-59A Application of section 59A to consolidated groups.
####### Application of the allocation rule in paragraph (c)(4) of this section.

(C)After the application of the classification rule in paragraph (c)(3) of this section, the group has $150x each of domestic related current year BIE deduction and foreign related current year BIE deduction from the group's aggregate current year BIE in Year 1.
