####### Safe harbors for C corporations and specified passthrough entities making payments in exchange for State or local tax credits,Safe harbor for C corporations.

(3)-(i)If a C corporation makes a payment to or for the use of an entity described in section 170(c) and receives or expects to receive in return a State or local tax credit that reduces a State or local tax imposed on the C corporation, the C corporation may treat such payment as meeting the requirements of an ordinary and necessary business expense for purposes of section 162(a) to the extent of the amount of the credit received or expected to be received.
