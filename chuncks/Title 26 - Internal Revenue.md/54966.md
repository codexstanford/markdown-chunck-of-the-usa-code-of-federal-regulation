
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Information and Returns
###### ยง 1.5000C-2 Withholding on specified Federal procurement payments.
####### Determine whether the payment is for personal services under the simplified acquisition threshold.

(5)The acquiring agency determines whether payments for services under contracts with a single individual do not exceed the simplified acquisition threshold as described in 48 CFR 2.101 on an annual basis for all years of the contract. If that is the case, the acquiring agency does not have an obligation to withhold under section 5000C on the payment.
