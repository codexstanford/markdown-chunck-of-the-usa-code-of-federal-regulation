
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 52 - ENVIRONMENTAL TAXES
##### ยง 52.4682-1 Ozone-depleting chemicals.
###### ODCs used in the manufacture of rigid foam insulation,Phase-in of tax,In general.

(d)-(1)-(i)The amount of tax imposed on an ODC is determined under section 4682(g) if the manufacturer or importer of the ODC-

(A) Uses the ODC during 1990, 1991, 1992, or 1993 in the manufacture of rigid foam insulation; or
