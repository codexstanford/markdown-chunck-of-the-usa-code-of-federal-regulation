####### Special rules to determine whether a debt instrument is a short-term obligation,Counting of either the issue date or maturity date.

(f)-(1)For purposes of determining whether a debt instrument is a short-term obligation (i.e., a debt instrument with a fixed maturity date that is not more than 1 year from the date of issue), the term of the debt instrument includes either the issue date or the maturity date, but not both dates.
