
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Changes in Rates During a Taxable Year
###### ยง 1.25A-5 Special rules relating to characterization and timing of payments.
####### Educational expenses paid by claimed dependent.

(a)For any taxable year for which the student is a claimed dependent of another taxpayer, qualified tuition and related expenses paid by the student are treated as paid by the taxpayer to whom the deduction under section 151 is allowed.
