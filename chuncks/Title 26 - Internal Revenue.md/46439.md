
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

#### PART 1 - INCOME TAXES
##### Special Rules for Determining Capital Gains and Losses
###### ยง 1.1275-2 Special rules relating to debt instruments.
####### Additional debt instruments.

(ii)Additional debt instruments are debt instruments that, without the application of this paragraph (k)-

(A) Are part of a single issue of debt instruments;

(B) Are not part of the same issue as the original debt instruments; and

(C) Have terms that are in all respects identical to the terms of the original debt instruments as of the reopening date.
