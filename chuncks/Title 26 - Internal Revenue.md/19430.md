
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Pension, Profit-Sharing, Stock Bonus Plans, etc.

####### Exception with respect to certain new plans.

(2)Plans that are new plans as defined in paragraph (b)(1) of this Q&A-6, under which the availability of an optional form of benefit is discriminatory or may reasonably be expected to be discriminatory, and that receive a favorable determination letter that covered such plan provisions with respect to an application submitted prior to July 11, 1988, will be treated as existing plans with respect to such optional form of benefit for purposes of the transitional rules of this section.
