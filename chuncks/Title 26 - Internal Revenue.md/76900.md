
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter F - Procedure and Administration
#### PART 303 - TAXES UNDER THE TRADING WITH THE ENEMY ACT
##### ยง 303.1-3 Protection of internal revenue prior to tax determination.
###### Vested property subject to debt claims,Notice to Commissioner.

(b)-(1)With respect to vested property available for the payment of debt claims under section 34 of the Act, and with respect to which debt claims have been filed, prior to the allowance of any such claims the Attorney General shall, in writing, notify the Commissioner of the property involved, the citizenship, residence, business organization, and other necessary information concerning the debtor and the aggregate of debt claims filed in respect thereof.
