
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### ยง 1.901-2 Income, war profits, or excess profits tax paid or accrued.
####### Example 2,1,Facts.

(B)-()A U.S. bank lends money to a development bank in Country X. The development bank relends the money to companies resident in Country X. A withholding tax is imposed by Country X on the U.S. bank with respect to the interest that the development bank pays to the U.S. bank, and appropriate receipts are provided. On the date that the tax is withheld, fifty percent of the tax is credited by Country X to an account of the development bank. Country X requires the development bank to transfer the amount credited to the borrowing companies.
