(3)-(i)For purposes of this paragraph (r), an individual is considered a "bona fide volunteer" if the individual does not have a profit motive for purposes of section 162. For example, an individual is considered a "bona fide volunteer" if the total value of the benefits provided with respect to the volunteer services is substantially less than the total value of the volunteer services the individual provides to an exempt organization or government employer.
