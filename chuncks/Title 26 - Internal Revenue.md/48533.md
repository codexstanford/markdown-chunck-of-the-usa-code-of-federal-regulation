####### Treatment of mixed-funds investments,Investments to which no election under section 1400Z-2(a) applies.

(f)-(1)If a taxpayer invests in a QOF and makes a deferral election with respect to less than all of that investment, the portion of the investment to which the election does not apply is a non-qualifying investment. Similarly, an investment in a QOF with respect to which no deferral election is made is a non-qualifying investment.
