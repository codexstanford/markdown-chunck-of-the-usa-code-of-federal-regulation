
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter A - Income Tax
#### PART 5 - TEMPORARY INCOME TAX REGULATIONS UNDER THE REVENUE ACT OF 1978
##### ยง 5.1502-45 Limitation on losses to amount at risk.
###### Amount parent is at risk in subsidiary's activity.

(3)The amount the parent is at risk in an activity of a subsidiary is the lesser of (i) the amount the parent is at risk in the subsidiary or (ii) the amount the subsidiary is at risk in the activity. These amounts are determined under paragraph (b) of this section and the principles of section 465. See section 465 and the regulations thereunder and the examples in paragraph (e) of this section.
