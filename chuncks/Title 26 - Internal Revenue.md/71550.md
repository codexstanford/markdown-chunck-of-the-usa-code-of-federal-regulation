
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue
## Chapter I - Internal Revenue Service, Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Miscellaneous Excise Taxes
#### PART 156 - EXCISE TAX ON GREENMAIL
##### Subpart B - Procedure and Administration
###### ยง 156.6001-1 Notice or regulations requiring records, statements, and special returns.
####### Notice by district director requiring returns, statements, or the keeping of records.

(b)The district director may require any person, by notice served upon him, to make such returns, render such statements, or keep such specific records as will enable the district director to determine whether or not the person is liable for tax under chapter 54 of the Code.
