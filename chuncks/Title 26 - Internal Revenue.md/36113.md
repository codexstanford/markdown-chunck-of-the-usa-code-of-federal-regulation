###### ยง 1.871-15 Treatment of dividend equivalents.
####### Securities lending or sale-repurchase transactions.

(i)For a securities lending or sale-repurchase transaction, the amount of the dividend equivalent for each dividend on an underlying security equals the amount of the actual per-share dividend paid on the underlying security multiplied by the number of shares of the underlying security.
