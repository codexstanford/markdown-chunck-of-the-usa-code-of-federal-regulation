(4)Unless the Commissioner determines that collection of the tax is in jeopardy, the Commissioner will notify the taxpayer in writing at least 30 days prior to modifying or terminating an installment agreement pursuant to paragraph (e)(1) or (2) of this section. Upon receiving notice, the taxpayer may provide information showing that the reason for the proposed modification or termination is incorrect.
