###### ยง 1.338-8 Asset and stock consistency.
####### Introduction,Overview.

(a)-(1)This section implements the consistency rules of sections 338(e) and (f). Under this section, no election under section 338 is deemed made or required with respect to target or any target affiliate. Instead, the person acquiring an asset may have a carryover basis in the asset.
