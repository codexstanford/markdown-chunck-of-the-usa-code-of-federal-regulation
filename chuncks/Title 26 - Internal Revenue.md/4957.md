
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

### Subchapter A - Income Tax

####### Adjusted basis,Exclusion from basis.

(d)-(1)Except for a repayment described in paragraph (d)(2) of this section, to the extent that a water or sewerage disposal facility is acquired or constructed with an amount received as a contribution to the capital of the taxpayer under paragraph (a) of this section, the basis of the facility is reduced by the amount of the contribution.
