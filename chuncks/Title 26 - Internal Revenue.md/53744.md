
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### Administrative Provisions and Other Rules
###### ยง 1.1502-79 Separate return years.
####### Portion of consolidated unused investment credit attributable to a member,Investment credit carryback.

(2)-(i)In the case of a consolidated unused credit which is an investment credit carryback, the portion of such consolidated unused credit attributable to a member of the group is an amount equal to such consolidated unused credit multiplied by a fraction, the numerator of which is the credit earned of such member for the consolidated unused credit year, and the denominator of which is the consolidated credit earned for such unused credit year.
