
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

###### Representatives constituting less than 15% of governing board.

(4)Notwithstanding paragraph (b) (2) or (3) of this section, if the number of representatives of one organization is less than 15 percent of the incumbents on the governing board of a second organization, the two organizations are not affiliated by reason of interlocking governing boards.
