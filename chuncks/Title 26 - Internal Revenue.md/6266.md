####### Allocation of gross proceeds to expenditures,Expenditures in general,General rule.

(d)-(1)-(i)Reasonable accounting methods for allocating funds from different sources to expenditures for the same governmental purpose include any of the following methods if consistently applied: a specific tracing method; a gross proceeds spent first method; a first-in, first-out method; or a ratable allocation method.
