
# Title 26 - Internal Revenue

##### ยง 4.954-2 Foreign personal holding company income; taxable years beginning after December 31, 1986.
###### Election out of tracing.

(iii)A controlled foreign corporation may elect a method of accounting under which all foreign currency gains or losses attributable to section 988 transactions are included in foreign personal holding company income. The scope and requirements for this election are provided in paragraph (g)(5) of this section. This election does not apply to foreign currency gains or losses of a qualified business unit included in the computation of gain or loss under paragraph (g)(2)(i) of this section.
