####### ESBT terminations,Ceasing to meet ESBT requirements.

(5)-(i)A trust ceases to be an ESBT on the first day the trust fails to meet the definition of an ESBT under section 1361(e). The last day the trust is treated as an ESBT is the day before the date on which the trust fails to meet the definition of an ESBT.
