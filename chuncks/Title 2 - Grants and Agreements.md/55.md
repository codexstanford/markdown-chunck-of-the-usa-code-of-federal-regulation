
# Title 2 - Grants and Agreements
## Subtitle A - Office of Management and Budget Guidance for Grants and Agreements
### Chapter I - Office of Management and Budget Governmentwide Guidance for Grants and Agreements
#### PART 170 - REPORTING SUBAWARD AND EXECUTIVE COMPENSATION INFORMATION
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 170.110 Exceptions to which this part applies.
####### General.

(a)Through a Federal awarding agency's implementation of the guidance in this part, this part applies to recipients, other than those exempted by law or excepted in accordance with paragraphs (b) and (c) of this section, that-

(1) Apply for or receive Federal awards; or

(2) Receive subawards under Federal awards.
