
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.3 - Transportation in Supply Contracts
###### 47.303-8 F.a.s. vessel, port of shipment.
####### Explanation of delivery term. F.a.s. vessel, port of shipment

(a)means free of expense to the Government delivered alongside the ocean vessel and within reach of its loading tackle at the specified port of shipment.
