
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 833 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 833.1 - Protests
###### 833.103-70 Protests to VA.
####### Agency protests.

(a)Pursuant to FAR 33.103(d)(4), an interested party may protest to the contracting officer or, as an alternative, may request an independent review at a level above the contracting officer as provided in this section. An interested party may also appeal to VA a contracting officer's decision on a protest.
