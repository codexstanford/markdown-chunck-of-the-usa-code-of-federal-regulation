
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 53 - FORMS
##### Subpart 53.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 53.212 Acquisition of commercial products and commercial services.
####### SF 1449 (Rev. NOV 2021), Solicitation/Contract/Order for Commercial Products and Commercial Services.

SF 1449 is prescribed for use in solicitations and contracts for commercial products and commercial services. Agencies may prescribe additional detailed instructions for use of the form.
