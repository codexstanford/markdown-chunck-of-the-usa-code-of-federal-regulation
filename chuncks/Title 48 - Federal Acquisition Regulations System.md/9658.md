
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 541 - ACQUISITION OF UTILITY SERVICES
##### Subpart 541.5 - Solicitation Provisions and Contract Clauses
###### 541.501 Contract clauses.
####### FAR deviation.

(a)GSA has a FAR deviation that allows use of the clause at 552.241-70 in lieu of the FAR clause at 52.232-19. Insert the clause at 552.241-70, Availability of Funds for the Next Fiscal Year or Quarter, in lieu of the FAR clause at 52.232-19, in all utility acquisitions.
