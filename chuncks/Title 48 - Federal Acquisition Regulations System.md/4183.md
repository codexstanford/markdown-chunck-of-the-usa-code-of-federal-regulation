
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 36 - CONSTRUCTION AND ARCHITECT-ENGINEER CONTRACTS
##### 36.001 Definitions.
###### Construction and demolition materials and debris

means materials and debris generated during construction, renovation, demolition, or dismantling of all structures and buildings and associated infrastructure.
