
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 217 - SPECIAL CONTRACTING METHODS
##### Subpart 217.74 - Undefinitized Contract Actions
###### 217.7401 Definitions.
####### Qualifying proposal

means a proposal that contains sufficient information to enable DoD to conduct meaningful analyses and audits of the information contained in the proposal.
