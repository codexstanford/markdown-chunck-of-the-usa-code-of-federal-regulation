
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 43 - CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 43.1 - General
###### 43.103 Types of contract modifications.
####### Unilateral.

(b)A unilateral modification is a contract modification that is signed only by the contracting officer. Unilateral modifications are used, for example, to-

(1) Make administrative changes;

(2) Issue change orders;
