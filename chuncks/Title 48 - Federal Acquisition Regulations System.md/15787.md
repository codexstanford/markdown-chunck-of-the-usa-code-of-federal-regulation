
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 61 - Civilian Board of Contract Appeals, General Services Administration
### PART 6101 - RULES OF PROCEDURE OF THE CIVILIAN BOARD OF CONTRACT APPEALS
#### 6101.9 Record; content and access[Rule 9].
##### Review and copying.,in camera,

(e)The Clerk makes records for decision, except evidence submitted under a protective order oravailable for review on reasonable notice during business hours, and provides copies of such available documents for a reasonable fee. The Clerk will not relinquish possession of material in the Board's files.
