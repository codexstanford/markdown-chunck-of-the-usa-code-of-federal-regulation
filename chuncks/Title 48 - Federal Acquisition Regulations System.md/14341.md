
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 20 - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2002 - DEFINITIONS
##### Subpart 2002.1 - Definitions
###### 2002.100 Definitions.
####### Competition Advocate

means the individual appointed as such by the Agency Head as required by Public Law 98-369. The Director, Division of Contracts and Property Management, has been appointed the Competition Advocate for the NRC.
