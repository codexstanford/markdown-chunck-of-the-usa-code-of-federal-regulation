
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 7 - Agency for International Development
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 731 - CONTRACT COST PRINCIPLES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 731.2 - Contracts With Commercial Organizations
###### 731.205-46 Travel costs.

It is USAID policy to require prior written approval of international travel by the contracting officer, or the contracting officer's representative (COR) if delegated in the Contracting Officer's Representative Designation Letter. See (48 CFR) AIDAR 752.7032 for specific requirements and procedures.
