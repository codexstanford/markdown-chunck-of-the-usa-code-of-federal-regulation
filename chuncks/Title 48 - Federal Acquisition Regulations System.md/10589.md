
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 7 - Agency for International Development
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 731 - CONTRACT COST PRINCIPLES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 731.3 - Contracts With Educational Institutions
###### 731.372 Fringe benefits.

USAID's policies on certain fringe benefits related to overseas service, including but not limited to leave, holidays, differentials and allowances, etc. are set forth in the appropriate contract clauses in (48 CFR) AIDAR 752.70.
