
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.2 - Policies
###### 619.202-70 The Department of State Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Eligibility of mentor firms.

(g)A mentor firm:

(1) May be either a large or small business;

(2) Must be eligible for award of U.S. Government contracts;

(3) Must be able to provide developmental assistance that will enhance the ability of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; to perform as subcontractors; and,

(4) Will be encouraged to enter into arrangements with prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; and firms with whom they have established business relationships.
