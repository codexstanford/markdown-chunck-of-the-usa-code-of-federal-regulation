### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types

##### Subpart 16.1 - Selecting Contract Types
###### 16.104 Factors in selecting contract types.
####### Acquisition history.

(l)Contractor risk usually decreases as the requirement is repetitively acquired. Also, product descriptions or descriptions of services to be performed can be defined more clearly.
