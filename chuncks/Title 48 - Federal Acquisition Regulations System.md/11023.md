
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 832 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### 832.001 Definitions.
###### Designated agency office

(a)means the office designated by the purchase order, agreement, or contract to first receive and review invoices. This office can be contractually designated as the receiving entity. This office may be different from the office issuing the payment.
