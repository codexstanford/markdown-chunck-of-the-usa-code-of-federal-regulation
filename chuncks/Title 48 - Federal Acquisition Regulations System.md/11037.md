###### 833.103-70 Protests to VA.
####### Affirmative determination of responsibility by the contracting officer.

(5)An affirmative determination of responsibility will not be reviewed unless there is a showing that such determination was made fraudulently or in bad faith or that definitive responsibility criteria in the solicitation were not met.
