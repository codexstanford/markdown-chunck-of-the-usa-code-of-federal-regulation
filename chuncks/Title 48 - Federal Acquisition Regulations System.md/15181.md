
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 29 - Department of Labor
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 2917 - SPECIAL CONTRACTING METHODS
##### Subpart 2917.5 - Interagency Acquisitions Under The Economy Act
###### 2917.501 Definitions.
####### Military Interdepartmental Procurement Request (MIPR)

means a type of interagency agreement used to place orders for supplies and non-personal services with a military department.
