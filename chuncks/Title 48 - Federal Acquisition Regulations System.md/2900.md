
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 24 - PROTECTION OF PRIVACY AND FREEDOM OF INFORMATION
##### Subpart 24.1 - Protection of Individual Privacy
###### 24.101 Definitions.
####### Operation of a system of records

means performance of any of the activities associated with maintaining the system of records, including the collection, use, and dissemination of records.
