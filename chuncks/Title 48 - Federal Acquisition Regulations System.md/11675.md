
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 945 - GOVERNMENT PROPERTY
##### Subpart 945.1 - General
###### 945.101 Definitions.
####### Capital equipment,

as used in this part, means personal property items having anticipated service life in excess of two years, regardless of type of funding, and having the potential for maintaining their integrity as capital items, i.e., not expendable due to use; dollar threshold for capital equipment is as established by the DOE Financial Management Handbook.
