
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 4 - Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter I - Food Assistance Programs
#### PART 470 - COMMODITY ACQUISITIONS
##### 470.102 Policy.
###### Freight.

(c)With respect to the acquisition of freight for the shipment of agricultural commodities and products of agricultural commodities, the provisions of the FAR, including part 47, shall be utilized and various types of services to be obtained may include multi-trip voyage charters.
