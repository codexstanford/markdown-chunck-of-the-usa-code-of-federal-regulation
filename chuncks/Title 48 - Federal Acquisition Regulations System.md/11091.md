
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 839 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### 839.000 Scope of part.
###### see

This part prescribes acquisition policies and procedures for use in acquiring VA information technology and information technology-related contracts (802.101) and applies to both VA-procured information technology systems as well as interagency acquisitions defined in FAR part 17 and part 817.
