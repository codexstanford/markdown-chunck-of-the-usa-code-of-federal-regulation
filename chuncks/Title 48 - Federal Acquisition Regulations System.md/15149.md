
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 29 - Department of Labor
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 2909 - CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 2909.4 - Debarment, Suspension, and Ineligibility
###### 2909.407-1 General.
####### Investigation and referral.

(a)Whenever a DOL employee knows of a cause for suspension, as listed in FAR 9.407-2, the appropriate HCA affected must be notified. The HCA must consult with the Office of the Solicitor and the Office of the Inspector General, as appropriate, and submit a formal recommendation documenting the cause for suspension, to the Senior Procurement Executive.
