
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2404 - ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS
##### Subpart 2404.8 - Government Contract Files
###### 2404.805 Storage, handling and disposal of contract files.
####### By the program office.

(b)Unsuccessful proposals shall be retained on file in the program office which conducted the technical evaluation for a period of two months following the contract award. Upon expiration of the two month period, the program office shall return one copy of each unsuccessful bid or proposal not required for the conduct of debriefings to the contracting activity for proper disposition. The remaining copies will be destroyed.
