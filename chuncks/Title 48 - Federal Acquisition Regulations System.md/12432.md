
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 1242 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND AUDIT SERVICES
##### Subpart 1242.1 - Contract Audit Services
###### 1242.170 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 1252.242-74, Contract Audit Support, in solicitation and contracts when other than firm-fixed-price contracts are contemplated.
