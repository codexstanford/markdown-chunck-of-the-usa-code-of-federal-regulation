
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 852 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 852.1 - Instructions for Using Provisions and Clauses
###### 852.101-70 Using part 852.

Part 852 prescribes supplemental provisions and clauses to the FAR. Provision and clause numbering are as prescribed in FAR 52.101 (e.g., supplementary construction clauses under part 836 are numbered 852.236-71, 852.236-72, etc.).
