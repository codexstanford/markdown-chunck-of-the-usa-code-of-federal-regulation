
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.10 - Service Contract Labor Standards
###### 22.1008-1 Obtaining wage determinations.
####### SAM.gov,SAM.gov,SAM.gov

(a) Contracting officers may obtain most prevailing wage determinations using the Wage Determinations atwebsite. Contracting officers may also use the Department of Labor's e98 electronic process, located on the Wage Determinations atwebsite, to request a wage determination directly from the Department of Labor. If the Wage Determinations atdatabase does not contain the applicable prevailing wage determination for a contract action, the contracting officer must use the e98 process to request a wage determination from the Department of Labor.

(b) In using the e98 process to obtain prevailing wage determinations, contracting officers shall provide as complete and accurate information on the e98 as possible. Contracting officers shall ensure that the email address submitted on an e98 request is accurate.
