
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 3 - Health and Human Services
### Subchapter B - Competition and Acquisition Planning
#### PART 305 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS
##### Subpart 305.70 - Publicizing Requirements Funded From the Affordable Care Act Prevention and Public Health Fund
###### 305.7003 Publicizing preaward.
####### http://www.hhs.gov/open/prevention/index.html

Notices of all proposed contract actions, funded in whole or in part by the PPHF, shall be identified on HHS' Prevention and Public Health Fund Web site atno later than 1-day after issuance of the solicitation or other request for proposal or quotation document. When applicable, the notice shall provide a link to the full text; for example, a link to the FedBizOpps notice required by FAR 5.201.
