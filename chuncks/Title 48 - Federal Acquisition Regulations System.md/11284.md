
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter I - Department Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 873 - SIMPLIFIED PROCEDURES FOR HEALTH-CARE RESOURCES
##### 873.109 General requirements for acquisition of health-care resources.
###### Cancellation of procurements.

(e)Any acquisition may be canceled by the contracting officer at any time during the acquisition process if cancellation is determined to be in the best interest of the Government and a memorandum for the record in included in the solicitation file explaining the reasons for the cancellation. (38 U.S.C. 8153)
