
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 44 - SUBCONTRACTING POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 44.1 - General
###### 44.101 Definitions.
####### Contractor purchasing system review (CPSR)

means the complete evaluation of a contractor's purchasing of material and services, subcontracting, and subcontract management from development of the requirement through completion of subcontract performance.
