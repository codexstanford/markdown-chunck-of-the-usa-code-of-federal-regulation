
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.2 - Contracts for Transportation or for Transportation-Related Services
###### 47.201 Definitions.
####### Office furniture

means furniture, equipment, fixtures, records, and other equipment and materials used in Government offices, hospitals, and similar establishments.
