
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter I - Department Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 873 - SIMPLIFIED PROCEDURES FOR HEALTH-CARE RESOURCES
##### 873.102 Definitions.
###### Commercial service

means a service that is offered and sold competitively in the commercial marketplace, is performed under standard commercial terms and conditions, and is procured using firm-fixed price contracts. (38 U.S.C. 8153)
