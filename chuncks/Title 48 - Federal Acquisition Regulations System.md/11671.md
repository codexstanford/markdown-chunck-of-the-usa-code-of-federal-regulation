
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 942 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND AUDIT SERVICES
##### Subpart 942.7 - Indirect Cost Rates
###### 942.705-4 State and local governments.

A list of cognizant agencies for State/local government organizations is periodically published in theby the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The responsible agencies are notified of such assignments. The current negotiated rates for State/local government activities is distributed to each CDO by the Office of Procurement and Assistance Policy, within the Headquarters procurement organization.
