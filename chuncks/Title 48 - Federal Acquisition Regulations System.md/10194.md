
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.4 - Cooperation with the Small Business Administration
###### 619.402-70 DOS designee.

Where the FAR requires action by a Small Business Administration procurement center representative, but one has not been assigned to the DOS contracting activity, the OSDBU Operations Director shall perform the action so required.
