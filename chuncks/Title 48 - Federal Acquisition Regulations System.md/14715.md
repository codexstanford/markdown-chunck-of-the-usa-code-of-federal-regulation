
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contracting Types
#### PART 2416 - TYPES OF CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 2416.5 - Indefinite-Delivery Contracts
###### 2416.506-70 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.
####### Labor categories, requirements, and estimated level of effort.

(f)The Contracting Officer shall insert provision 2452.216-82, Labor Categories, Requirements, and Estimated Level of Effort, in all level-of-effort solicitations. Contracting Officer's Representatives will provide the labor descriptions and estimated number of hours. Contracting Officers will obtain wage rate determinations for any classifications covered by the Service Contract Act.
