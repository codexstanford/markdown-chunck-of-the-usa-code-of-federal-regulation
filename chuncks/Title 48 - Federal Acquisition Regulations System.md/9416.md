
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 502 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 502.1 - Definitions
###### 502.101 Definitions.
####### External information system

(2)means information systems that reside in contractor facilities and typically do not connect to the GSA network. External information systems may be government-owned and contractor-operated or contractor-owned and -operated on behalf of Federal Government (when GSA is the managing agency).
