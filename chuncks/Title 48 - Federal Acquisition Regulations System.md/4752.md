
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.3 - Transportation in Supply Contracts
###### 47.303-12 Ex dock, pier, or warehouse, port of importation.
####### Explanation of delivery term. Ex dock, pier, or warehouse, port of importation

(a)means free of expense to the Government delivered on the designated dock or pier or in the warehouse at the specified port of importation.
