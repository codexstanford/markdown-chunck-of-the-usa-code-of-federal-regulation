
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 1246 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 1246.1 - General
###### 1246.101-70 Additional definitions.
####### At no additional cost to the Government,see

means at no increase in price for firm-fixed-price contracts, at no increase in target or ceiling price for fixed price incentive contracts (FAR 46.707), or at no increase in estimated cost or fee for cost-reimbursement contracts.
