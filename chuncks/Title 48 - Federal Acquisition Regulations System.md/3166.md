
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 26 - OTHER SOCIOECONOMIC PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 26.4 - Food Donations to Nonprofit Organizations
###### 26.403 Procedures.
####### Encourage donations.

(1)In the applicable contracts stated at section 26.404, encourage contractors, to the maximum extent practicable and safe, to donate apparently wholesome excess food to nonprofit organizations that provide assistance to food-insecure people in the United States.
