
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 632 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### Subpart 632.9 - Prompt Payment
###### 632.906 Making payments.
####### General.

(a)The authority to make the determination prescribed in FAR 32.906(a) is delegated, without power of redelegation, to the head of the contracting activity. Before making this determination, the head of the contracting activity shall consult with the appropriate financial office.
