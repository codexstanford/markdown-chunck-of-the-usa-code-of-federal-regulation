
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.2 - Policies
###### 619.202-70 The Department of State Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Developmental assistance.

(l)The forms of developmental assistance a mentor can provide to a prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; include:

(1) Management guidance relating to:

(i) Financial management;

(ii) Organizational management;

(iii) Overall business management/planning;

(iv) Business development; and,

(v) Technical assistance.

(2) Loans;

(3) Rent-free use of facilities and/or equipment;

(4) Property;

(5) Temporary assignment of personnel to prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; for purpose of training; and,

(6) Any other types of permissible, mutually beneficial assistance.
