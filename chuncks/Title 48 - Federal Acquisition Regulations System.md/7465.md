
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 226 - OTHER SOCIOECONOMIC PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 226.72 - Demonstration Project for Contractors Employing Persons With Disabilities
###### 226.7201 Definitions.
####### Severely disabled individual

means an individual with a disability (as defined in 42 U.S.C. 12102) who has a severe physical or mental impairment that seriously limits one or more functional capacities.
