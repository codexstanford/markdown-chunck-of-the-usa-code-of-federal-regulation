
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 208 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES
##### Subpart 208.74 - Enterprise Software Agreements
###### 208.7401 Definitions.
####### Enterprise software agreement

means an agreement or a contract that is used to acquire designated commercial software or related commercial software services such as software maintenance.
