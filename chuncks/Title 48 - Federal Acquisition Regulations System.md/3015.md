
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 25 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 25.6 - American Recovery and Reinvestment Act - Buy American Statute - Construction Materials
###### 25.601 Definitions.
####### Recovery Act designated country

means a World Trade Organization Government Procurement Agreement country, a Free Trade Agreement country, or a least developed country.
