
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 219 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 219.71 - Pilot Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program
###### 219.7100 Scope.

This subpart implements the Pilot Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program (referred to as the Program) established under section 831 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1991 (Pub. L. 101-510; 10 U.S.C. 4901 note prec.). The purpose of the Program is to provide incentives for DoD contractors to assist prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms in enhancing their capabilities and to increase participation of such firms in Government and commercial contracts.
