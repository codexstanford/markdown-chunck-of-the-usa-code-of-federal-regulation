
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.5 - Use of Project Labor Agreements for Federal Construction Projects
###### 22.502 Definitions.
####### Large-scale construction project

means a construction project where the total cost to the Federal Government is $25 million or more.
