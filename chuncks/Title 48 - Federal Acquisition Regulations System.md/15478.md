
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 30 - Department of Homeland Security, Homeland Security Acquisition Regulation (HSAR)
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 3046 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 3046.7 - Warranties
###### 3046.790-2 Definitions (USCG).
####### Defect

means any condition or characteristic in any supplies or services furnished by the contractor under the contract that is not in compliance with the requirements of the contract.
