
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 29 - Department of Labor
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 2933 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 2933.1 - Protests
###### 2933.104 Protests to GAO.
####### General procedures.

(a)The HCA has the responsibility to prepare and provide to the General Accounting Office (GAO) the agency report with the information required by FAR 33.104(a). The agency report must be coordinated with the Office of the Solicitor before the report is signed and sent to the GAO.
