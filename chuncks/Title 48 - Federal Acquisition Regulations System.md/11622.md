
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 931 - CONTRACT COST PRINCIPLES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 931.2 - Contracts With Commercial Organizations
###### 931.205-47 Costs related to legal and other proceedings.
####### Retaliatory act

means a discharge, demotion, reduction in pay, coercion, restraint, threat, intimidation or other similar negative action taken against an employee by a contractor as a result of an employee's activity protected as a whistleblower activity by a Federal or state statute or regulation.
