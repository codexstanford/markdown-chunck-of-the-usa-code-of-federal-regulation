
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 15 - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1501 - GENERAL
##### Subpart 1501.1 - Purpose, Authority, Issuance
###### 1501.105-3 Copies.

Copies of the EPAAR inand CFR form may be purchased from the Superintendent of Documents, Government Printing Office (GPO), Washington, DC 20402. Copies of loose-leaf EPAAR are distributed within EPA and may be obtained from the EPA Facilities and Support Services Division.
