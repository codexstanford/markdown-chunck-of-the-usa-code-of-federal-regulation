
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 919 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 919.8 - Contracting With the Small Business Administration (The 8(a) Program)
###### 919.805-2 Procedures.

Acquisitions involving section 8(a) competition must comply with source selection procedures set forth in 48 CFR chapter 1 in accordance with 13 CFR part 124.
