
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 246 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 246.4 - Government Contract Quality Assurance
###### 246.406 Foreign governments.
####### Reciprocal quality assurance agreements.

(3)A Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with a foreign country may contain an annex that provides for the reciprocal performance of quality assurance services. MOUs should be checked to determine whether such an annex exists for the country where a defense contract will be performed. (See subpart 225.8 for more information about MOUs.)
