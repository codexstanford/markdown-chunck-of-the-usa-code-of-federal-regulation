
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 34 - Department of Education Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3402 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 3402.1 - Definitions
###### 3402.101 Definitions.
####### Senior Procurement Executive,SPE

ormeans the single agency official appointed as such by the head of the agency and delegated broad responsibility for acquisition functions, including issuing agency acquisition policy and reporting on acquisitions agency-wide. The SPE also acts as the official one level above the contracting officer when the HCA is acting as a contracting officer.
