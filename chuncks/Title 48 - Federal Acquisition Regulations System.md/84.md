
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System

### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2.1 - Definitions

(2) Studies, analyses and evaluations,contracted services that provide organized, analytical assessments/evaluations in support of policy development, decision-making, management, or administration.
