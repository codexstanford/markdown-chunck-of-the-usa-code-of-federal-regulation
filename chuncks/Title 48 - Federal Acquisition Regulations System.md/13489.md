
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 15 - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 1532 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### 1532.003 Simplified acquisition procedures financing.
###### Scope.

(a)This subpart provides for authorization of advance and interim payments on commercial item orders not exceeding the simplified acquisition threshold. Advance payments are payments that are made prior to performance. Interim payments are payments that are made during the order period according to a payment schedule.
