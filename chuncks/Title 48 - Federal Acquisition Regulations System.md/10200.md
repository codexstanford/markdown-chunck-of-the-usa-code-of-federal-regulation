
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.7 - The Small Business Subcontracting Program
###### 619.705-3 Preparing the solicitation.

To further promote the use of small, disadvantaged, women-owned small, HUBZone small, veteran-owned small, and service-disabled veteran-owned small business firms by large prime contractors, contracting officers are encouraged to consider the adequacy of the subcontracting plans, and/or past performance in achieving negotiated subcontract goals, as part of the overall evaluation of the technical proposals.
