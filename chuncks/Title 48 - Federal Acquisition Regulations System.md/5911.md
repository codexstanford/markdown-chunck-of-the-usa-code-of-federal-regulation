
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 52 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 52.2 - Text of Provisions and Clauses
###### 52.248-3 Value Engineering - Construction.
####### Alternate I

(APR 1984). When the head of the contracting activity determines that the cost of calculating and tracking collateral savings will exceed the benefits to be derived in a construction contract, delete paragraph (g) from the basic clause and redesignate the remaining paragraphs accordingly.
