
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 16 - TYPES OF CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 16.6 - Time-and-Materials, Labor-Hour, and Letter Contracts
###### 16.601 Time-and-materials contracts.
####### Direct materials

means those materials that enter directly into the end product, or that are used or consumed directly in connection with the furnishing of the end product or service.
