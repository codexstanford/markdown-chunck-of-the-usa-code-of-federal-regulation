
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 53 - FORMS
##### Subpart 53.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 53.251 Contractor use of Government supply sources (OF 347).
####### OF 347, Order for Supplies or Services.

OF 347, prescribed in 53.213(f), may be used by contractors when requisitioning from the VA, as specified in 51.102(e)(3)(ii).
