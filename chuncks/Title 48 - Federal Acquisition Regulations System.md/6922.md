
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 218 - EMERGENCY ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 218.2 - Emergency Acquisition Flexibilities
###### 218.203 Emergency declaration or major disaster declaration.
####### Electronic submission and processing of payment requests.

(2)Contractors do not have to submit payment requests in electronic form for contracts awarded by contracting officers in the conduct of emergency operations, such as responses to natural disasters or national or civil emergencies. See 232.7002(a)(4).
