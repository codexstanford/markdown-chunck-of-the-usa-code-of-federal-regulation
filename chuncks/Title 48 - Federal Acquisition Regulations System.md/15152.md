
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 29 - Department of Labor
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 2909 - CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 2909.4 - Debarment, Suspension, and Ineligibility
###### 2909.407-1 General.
####### Suspension decisions.

(d)The Senior Procurement Executive must make a final decision on suspension as prescribed in FAR 9.407-3(d). Notice of the decision must be provided to the contractor and any affiliates involved, in accordance with the provisions in FAR 9.407-3(d)(4).
