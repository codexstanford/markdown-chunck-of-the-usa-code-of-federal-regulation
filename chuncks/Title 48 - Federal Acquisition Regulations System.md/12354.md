
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1239 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 1239.1 - General
###### 1239.101-72 Definitions.
####### System software

means a platform comprised of Operating System (OS) programs and services, including settings and preferences, file libraries and functions used for system applications. System software also includes device drivers that run basic computer hardware and peripherals.
