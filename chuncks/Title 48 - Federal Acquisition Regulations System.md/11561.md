
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 925 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 925.70 - Acquisition of Nuclear Hot Cell Services
###### 925.7001 Definitions.
####### Costs related to the decommissioning of nuclear facilities,

as used in this subpart, means any cost associated with the compliance with regulatory requirements governing the decommissioning of nuclear facilities licensed by the Nuclear Regulatory Commission. Such costs for foreign facilities and for Department of Energy facilities are costs of decommissioning associated with the compliance with foreign regulatory requirements or the Department's own requirements.
