
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.3 - Transportation in Supply Contracts
###### 47.303-16 F.o.b. designated air carrier's terminal, point of importation.
####### Explanation of delivery term. F.o.b. designated air carrier's terminal, point of importation

(a)means free of expense to the Government delivered to the air carrier's terminal at the point of importation specified in the contract.
