### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 423 - ENVIRONMENT, ENERGY AND WATER EFFICIENCY, RENEWABLE ENERGY TECHNOLOGIES, OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY, AND DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE
##### Subpart 423.4 - Use of Recovered Materials
###### 423.404 Agency affirmative procurement programs.
####### Annual review and monitoring of effectiveness of the program.

(d)USDA agencies will provide an annual assessment of the effectiveness of their affirmative procurement program actions in increasing the purchase and use of EPA designated products.
