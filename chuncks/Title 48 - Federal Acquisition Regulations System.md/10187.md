
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.2 - Policies
###### 619.202-70 The Department of State Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Selection of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms.

(i)(1) Mentor firms are solely responsible for selecting prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms. The mentor is encouraged to identify and select a broad base of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms whose core competencies support DOS's mission.

(2) Mentors may have multiple prot&#xE9;g&#xE9;.

(3) The selection of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms by mentor firms may not be protested, except that any protest regarding the size or eligibility status of an entity selected by a mentor shall be handled in accordance with FAR and SBA regulations.
