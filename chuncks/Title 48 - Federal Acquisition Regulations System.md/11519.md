
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 919 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 919.70 - The Department of Energy Mentor-Protege Program
###### 919.7002 Definitions.
####### Small disadvantaged business

means a small business concern owned and controlled by socially and economically disadvantaged individuals that meets the requirements of 13 CFR part 124, subpart B.
