
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1239 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 1239.72 - Cloud Computing
###### 1239.7201 Definitions.
####### Authorizing official

means the senior Federal official or executive with the authority to formally assume responsibility for operating an information system at an acceptable level of risk to organizational operations (including mission, functions, image, or reputation), organizational assets, individuals, other organizations, and the Nation.
