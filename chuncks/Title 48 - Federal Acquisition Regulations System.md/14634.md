
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2401 - FEDERAL ACQUISITION REGULATION SYSTEM
##### Subpart 2401.1 - Purpose, Authority, Issuance
###### 2401.105-2 Arrangement of regulations.
####### Citation.

(c)The HUDAR will be cited in accordance withstandards approved for the FAR. Thus, this section when referred to in the HUDAR is cited as 2401.105-2(c). When this section is referred to formally in official documents, such as legal briefs, it should be cited as "48 CFR 2401.105-2(c)." Any section of the HUDAR may be formally identified by the section number, e.g., "HUDAR 2401.105-2." In the HUDAR, any reference to the FAR will be indicated by "FAR" followed by the section number, for example FAR 37.108.
