
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 245 - GOVERNMENT PROPERTY
##### Subpart 245.6 - Reporting, Reutilization, and Disposal
###### 245.604-1 Sales procedures.
####### Informal bid procedures.

(2)The plant clearance officer may direct the contractor to issue informal invitations for bid (orally, telephonically, or by other informal media), provided-

(i) Maximum practical competition is obtained;

(ii) Sources solicited are recorded; and

(iii) Informal bids are confirmed in writing.
