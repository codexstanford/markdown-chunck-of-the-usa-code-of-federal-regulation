
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 2446 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 2446.5 - Acceptance
###### 2446.502-70 Contract clause.

The Contracting Officer shall insert the clause at 2452.246-70, Inspection and Acceptance, in all solicitations and contracts.
