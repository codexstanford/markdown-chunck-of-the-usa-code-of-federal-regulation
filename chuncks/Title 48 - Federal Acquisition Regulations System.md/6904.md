
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System

### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 218 - EMERGENCY ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 218.1 - Available Acquisition Flexibilities
###### 218.170 Additional acquisition flexibilities.
####### Mortuary services.

(k)In an epidemic or other emergency, the contracting activity may obtain services beyond the capacity of the contractor's facilities from other sources. See 237.7003(a) and 252.237-7003.
