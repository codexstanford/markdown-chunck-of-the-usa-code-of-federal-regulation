
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 919 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 919.70 - The Department of Energy Mentor-Protege Program
###### 919.7002 Definitions.
####### Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs)

means an institution determined by the Secretary of Education to meet the requirements of 34 CFR 608.2.
