
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 14 - Department of the Interior
### Subchapter B - Competition and Acquisition Planning
#### PART 1409 - CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 1409.4 - Debarment, Suspension, and Ineligibility
###### 1409.403 Definitions.
####### Debarring Official

refers to the Director, PAM. The Debarring Official is the official authorized to impose debarment or suspension. The Debarring Official also may settle a debarment or suspension action at any time if it is in the best interest of the Government.
