
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 225 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 225.72 - Reporting Contract Performance Outside the United States
###### 225.7201 Policy.

10 U.S.C. 4603 requires offerors and contractors to notify DoD of any intention to perform any part of a DoD contract outside the United States and Canada that-

(a) Exceeds $750,000 in value; and

(b) Could be performed inside the United States or Canada.
