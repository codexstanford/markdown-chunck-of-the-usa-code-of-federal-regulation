
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 18 - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1837 - SERVICE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 1837.1 - Service Contracts - General
###### 1837.101 Definitions.
####### Pension portability

means the recognition and continuation in a successor service contract of the predecessor service contract employees' pension rights and benefits.
