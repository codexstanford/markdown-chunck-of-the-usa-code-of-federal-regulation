
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 18 - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 1825 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 1825.11 - Solicitation Provisions and Contract Clauses
###### 1825.1103-70 Export control.
####### Contract clause.

(b)Insert the clause at 1852.225-70, Export Licenses, in all solicitations and contracts, except in contracts with foreign entities. Insert the clause with its Alternate I when the NASA project office indicates that technical data (including software) is to be exchanged by the contractor with a NASA foreign partner pursuant to an international agreement.
