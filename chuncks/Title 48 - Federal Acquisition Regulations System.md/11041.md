
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 833 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 833.1 - Protests
###### 833.103-70 Protests to VA.
####### Alternative dispute resolution.

(c)Bidders/offerors and VA contracting officers are encouraged to use alternative dispute resolution (ADR) procedures to resolve protests at any stage in the protest process. If ADR is used, VA will not furnish any documentation in an ADR proceeding beyond what is allowed by the FAR.
