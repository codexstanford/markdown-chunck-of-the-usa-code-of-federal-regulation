
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 2453 - FORMS
##### Subpart 2453.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 2453.217-70 Form HUD-730, Award/Modification of Interagency Agreement.

As prescribed in 2417.504(b), form HUD-730 shall be used by Contracting Officers when placing or modifying an order for supplies or services from another Government agency.
