
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 833 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 833.1 - Protests
###### 833.103-70 Protests to VA.
####### Protests to the contracting officer.

(1)Protests to the contracting officer shall be in writing and shall be addressed where the offer/bid is to be submitted or as indicated in the solicitation.
