
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 945 - GOVERNMENT PROPERTY
##### Subpart 945.1 - General
###### 945.101 Definitions.
####### Personal property,

as used in this part, means property of any kind or interest therein, except real property, records of the Federal Government, and nuclear and special source materials, atomic weapons, and by-product materials.
