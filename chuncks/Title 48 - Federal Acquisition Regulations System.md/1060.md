#### PART 8 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES

(2)(i) For proposed orders or BPAs with an estimated value exceeding the micro-purchase threshold, but not exceeding the simplified acquisition threshold, the ordering activity contracting officer shall document the basis for restricting consideration to an item peculiar to one manufacturer.
