
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 43 - CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 43.2 - Change Orders
###### 43.201 General.
####### provided

(c) The contracting officer may issue a change order by electronic means without a SF 30 under unusual or urgent circumstances,that the message contains substantially the information required by the SF 30 and immediate action is taken to issue the SF 30.
