
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter B - Competition Acquisition Planning
#### PART 908 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES
##### Subpart 908.71 - Acquisition of Special Items
###### 908.7103 Office machines.

Acquisitions of office machines by DOE offices and its authorized contractors shall be in accordance with FPMR 41 CFR 101-25.104, 101-25.302, and DOE-PMR 41 CFR 109-25.302, and 109-25.4.
