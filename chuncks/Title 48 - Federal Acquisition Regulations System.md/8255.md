
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 246 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 246.7 - Warranties
###### 246.702-70 Definitions.
####### Enterprise,e.g.,

means the entity (a manufacturer or vendor) responsible for granting the warranty and/or assigning unique item identifiers to serialized warranty items.
