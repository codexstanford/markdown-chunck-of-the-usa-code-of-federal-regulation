
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1239 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 1239.74 - Records Management
###### 1239.7402 Policy.
####### Applicability.

(2)In accordance with 36 CFR 1222.32, all data created for Government use and delivered to, or falling under the legal control of, the Government are Federal records subject to the provisions of 44 U.S.C. chapters 21, 29, 31, and 33, the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) (5 U.S.C. 552), as amended, and the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), as amended, and must be managed and scheduled for disposition only as permitted by relevant records management laws and regulations and DOT Order 1351.28, Departmental Records Management Policy.
