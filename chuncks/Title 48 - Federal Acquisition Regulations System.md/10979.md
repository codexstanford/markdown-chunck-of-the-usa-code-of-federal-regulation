
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 824 - PROTECTION OF PRIVACY AND FREEDOM OF INFORMATION
##### Subpart 824.1 - Protection of Individual Privacy
###### 824.103-71 Liquidated damages - protection of information.
####### Purpose.

(a)As required by 38 U.S.C. 5725 any contracts where sensitive personal information such as PHI must be disclosed to the contractor for the contractor to perform certain functions or services on behalf of VHA shall include a liquidated damages clause as prescribed at 811.503-70.
