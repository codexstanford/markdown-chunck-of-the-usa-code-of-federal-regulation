
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 253 - FORMS
##### Subpart 253.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 253.209-1 Responsible prospective contractors.
####### Technical capability.

(A)An assessment of the prospective contractor's key management personnel to determine if they have the basic technical knowledge, experience, and understanding of the requirements necessary to produce the required product or provide the required service.
