
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 619 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 619.2 - Policies
###### 619.202-70 The Department of State Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Internal controls.

(n)(1) OSDBU shall oversee the program and shall work with the cognizant contracting officer to achieve program objectives.

(2) DOS may rescind approval of an existing Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; agreement if it determines that such an action is in the Department's best interest. The rescission shall be in writing and sent to the mentor and prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms after approval by the OSDBU Operations Director. Rescission of an agreement does not change the terms of the subcontract between the mentor and the prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; or the prime contractor's obligations under its subcontracting plan.
