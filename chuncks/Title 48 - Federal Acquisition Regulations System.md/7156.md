
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 225 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 225.7 - Prohibited Sources
###### 225.772-1 Definitions.
####### Government of a covered foreign country

includes the state and the government of a covered foreign country, as well as any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof.
