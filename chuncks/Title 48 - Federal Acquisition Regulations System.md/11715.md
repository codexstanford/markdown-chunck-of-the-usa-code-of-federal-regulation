
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 951 - USE OF GOVERNMENT SOURCES BY CONTRACTORS
##### Subpart 951.70 - Contractor Employee Travel Discounts
###### 951.7002 Responsibilities.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 952.251-70, Contractor employee travel discounts, in all cost-reimbursable solicitations and contracts when significant costs for rail travel, car rental, or lodging will be required to perform the contract. The contracting officer may furnish the contractor with appropriate identification letters.
