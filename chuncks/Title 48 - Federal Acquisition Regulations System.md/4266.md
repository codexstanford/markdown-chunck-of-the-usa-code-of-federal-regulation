
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 39 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 39.2 - Information and Communication Technology
###### 39.205 Exemptions.
####### Fundamental alteration.

(2)When an agency determines that acquisition of ICT that conforms with all applicable ICT accessibility standards would result in a fundamental alteration in the nature of the ICT, such acquisition is required to conform only to the extent that conformance will not result in a fundamental alteration in the nature of the ICT.
