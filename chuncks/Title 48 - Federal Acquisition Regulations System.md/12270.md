
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 1219 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 1219.2 - Policies
###### 1219.202-70 Procurement Forecast.
####### https://www.transportation.gov/osdbu/procurement-forecast/summary.

The OSDBU shall prepare and maintain DOT's Procurement Forecast in coordination with DOT Operating Administrations. The forecast will be published every year on or before October 1st and can be found atContracting officers and small business specialists will work with the OSDBU to maintain accurate procurement forecast information.
