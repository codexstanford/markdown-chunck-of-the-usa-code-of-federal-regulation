
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 53 - FORMS
##### Subpart 53.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 53.235 Research and Development Contracting (SF 298).
####### SF 298 (2/89), Report Documentation Page.

SF 298 is prescribed for use in submitting scientific and technical reports to contracting officers and to technical information libraries, as specified in 35.010.
