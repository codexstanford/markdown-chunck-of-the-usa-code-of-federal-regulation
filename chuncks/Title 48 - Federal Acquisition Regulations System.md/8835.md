
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 3 - Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 319 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 319.2 - Policies
###### 319.270-1 Mentor Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program Solicitation provision and contract clause.

(a) The contacting officer shall insert the provision at 352.219-70, Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program, in solicitations that include the clause at FAR 52.219-9, Small Business Subcontracting Plan. The provision requires offerors to provide the contracting officer a copy of their HHS Office of OSDBU-approved mentor-prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; agreement in response to a solicitation.

(b) The contacting officer shall insert the clause at 352.219-71, Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program Reporting Requirements, in contracts that include the clause at FAR 52.219-9, Small Business Subcontracting Plan, and which are awarded to a contractor with an HHS OSDBU-approved mentor-prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; agreement.
