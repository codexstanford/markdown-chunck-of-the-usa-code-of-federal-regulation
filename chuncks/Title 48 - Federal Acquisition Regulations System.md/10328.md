
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 637 - SERVICE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 637.1 - Service Contracts - General
###### 637.104-71 Personal services agreements.
####### Definition.

(b)"Personal Services Agreement (PSA)" is a method of employment using the statutory authority under 22 U.S.C. 2669(c). The Procurement Executive has delegated program management responsibility for PSAs awarded under the Department of State basic authority at 22 U.S.C. 2669(c). When applied to U.S. citizens hired under this authority, the term "PSA Plus" is normally used.
