
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.8 - Equal Employment Opportunity
###### 22.801 Definitions.
####### Subcontractor,first-tier subcontractor

means any person who holds, or has held, a subcontract subject to E.O. 11246. The termmeans a subcontractor holding a subcontract with a prime contractor.
