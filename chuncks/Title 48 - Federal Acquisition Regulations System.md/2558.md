
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.10 - Service Contract Labor Standards
###### 22.1001 Definitions.
####### Multiple year contracts

means contracts having a term of more than 1 year regardless of fiscal year funding. The term includes multi-year contracts (see 17.103).
