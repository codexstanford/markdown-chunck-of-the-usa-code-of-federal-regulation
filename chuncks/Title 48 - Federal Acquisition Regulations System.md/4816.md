
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.4 - Air Transportation by U.S.-Flag Carriers
###### 47.401 Definitions.
####### Gateway airport abroad

means the airport from which the traveler last embarks en route to the United States or at which the traveler first debarks incident to travel from the United States.
