
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 842 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION AND AUDIT SERVICES
##### 842.070 Definitions.
###### Administrative Contracting Officer Letter of Delegation

means a delegation of functions as set forth in FAR 42.202, 42.302 and 842.271, Administrative Contracting Officer's role in contract administration and delegated functions, that is issued by a contracting officer to delegate certain contract administration or specialized support services.
