
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 243 - CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 243.1 - General
###### 243.172 Application of modifications.

Follow the procedures in 204.1671 for determining the sequence for application of modifications to a contract or order.
