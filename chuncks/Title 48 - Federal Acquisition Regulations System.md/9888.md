
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter I - Special Contracting Programs
#### PART 570 - ACQUIRING LEASEHOLD INTERESTS IN REAL PROPERTY
##### Subpart 570.1 - General
###### 570.102 Definitions.
####### Lease renewal (option)

means the right, but not the obligation of the Government to continue a lease upon specified terms and conditions, including lease term and rent.
