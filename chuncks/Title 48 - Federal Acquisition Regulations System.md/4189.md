
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 37 - SERVICE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 37.1 - Service Contracts - General
###### 37.101 Definitions.
####### Performance-based contracting

means structuring all aspects of an acquisition around the purpose of the work to be performed as opposed to either the manner by which the work is to be performed or broad and imprecise statements of work.
