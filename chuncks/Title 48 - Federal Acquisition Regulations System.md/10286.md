
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 633 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 633.1 - Protests
###### 633.102 General.

All communications relative to protests filed with the Government Accountability Office (GAO) shall be coordinated with the Office of the Legal Adviser.
