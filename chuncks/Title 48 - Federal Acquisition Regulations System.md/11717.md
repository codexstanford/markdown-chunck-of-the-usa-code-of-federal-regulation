
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 952 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 952.0 - General
###### 952.001 General policy.

It is DOE policy to use the prescribed FAR and DOE contract clauses wherever practicable. Uniformity in the use of contract clauses helps to ensure impartial treatment of all contractors, expedites negotiation and contract review, and facilitates contract administration.
