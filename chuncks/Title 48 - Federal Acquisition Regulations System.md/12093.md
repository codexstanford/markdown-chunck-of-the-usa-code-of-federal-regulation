
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 10 - Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 1019 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 1019.2 - Policies
###### 1019.202-70 The Treasury Mentor Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### General policy.

(d)(1) Eligible contractors, not included on the "System for Award Management Exclusions" that are approved as mentors will enter into agreements with eligible prot&#xE9;g&#xE9;s. Mentors provide appropriate developmental assistance to enhance the capabilities of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9;s to perform as contractors or subcontractors.

(2) A firm's status as a prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; under a Treasury contract shall not have an effect on the firm's eligibility to seek other contracts or subcontracts.
