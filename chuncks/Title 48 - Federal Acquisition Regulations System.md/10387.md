
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 652 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 652.2 - Text of Provisions and Clauses
###### 652.236-70 Additional Safety Measures.
####### Mishap reporting.,immediately

(c)The contractor is required to reportall mishaps to the COR and the contracting officer. A "mishap" is any event causing injury, disease or illness, death, material loss or property damage, or incident causing environmental contamination. The mishap reporting requirement shall include fires, explosions, hazardous materials contamination, and other similar incidents that may threaten people, property, and equipment.
