(f) In addition to price (8.404(d) and 8.405-4), when determining best value, the ordering activity may consider, among other factors, the following:

(3) Trade-in considerations.

(4) Probable life of the item selected as compared with that of a comparable item.

(5) Warranty considerations.

(6) Maintenance availability.

(7) Environmental and energy efficiency considerations.
