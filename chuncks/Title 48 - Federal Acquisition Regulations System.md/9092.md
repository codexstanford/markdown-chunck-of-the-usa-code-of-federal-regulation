
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 4 - Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter B - Competition and Acquisition Planning
#### PART 409 - CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 409.4 - Debarment, Suspension and Ineligibility
###### 409.406-3 Procedures.
####### Decision-making process.

(b)If, after reviewing the recommendations and consulting with the Office of Inspector General and Office of the General Counsel, as appropriate, the debarring official determines debarment is justified, the debarring official shall initiate the proposed debarment in accordance with FAR 9.406-3(c) and notify the HCA of the action taken.
