
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 835 - RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT CONTRACTING
##### 835.001-70 Veterans Affairs (VA) definitions.
###### Research

means a systematic investigation, including research development, testing and evaluation, designed to develop or contribute to generalized knowledge.
