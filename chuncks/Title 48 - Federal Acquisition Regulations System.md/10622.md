
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 7 - Agency for International Development
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 736 - CONSTRUCTION AND ARCHITECT-ENGINEER CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 736.5 - Contract Clauses
###### 736.570 Disability standards in construction contracts.

Contracting officers must insert the clause at 752.236-70, Standards for Accessibility for the Disabled in USAID Construction Contracts, in all solicitations and contracts for construction or renovations made using program funds.
