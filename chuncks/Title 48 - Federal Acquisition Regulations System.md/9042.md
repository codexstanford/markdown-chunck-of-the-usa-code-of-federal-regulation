
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 4 - Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 402 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 402.1 - Definitions
###### 402.101 Definitions.
####### Senior Procurement Executive

(SPE) means the agency official appointed as such by the head of the agency pursuant to Executive Order 12931. The Director, Office of Procurement and Property Management, has been designated as the USDA SPE.
