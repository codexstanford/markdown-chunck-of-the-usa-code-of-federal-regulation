
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 52 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 52.2 - Text of Provisions and Clauses
###### 52.211-8 Time of Delivery.
####### Alternate III,within days after the date of receipt of a written notice of award

(APR 1984). If the delivery schedule is to be based on the actual date the contractor receives a written notice of award, the contracting officer may delete paragraph (b) of the basic clause. The time may be expressed by substitutingas the heading for the third column of paragraph (a) of the basic clause.
