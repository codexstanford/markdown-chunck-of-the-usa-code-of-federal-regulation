Acquisition personnel shall document the acquisition plan with findings that detail the particular facts and circumstances, (complexity of the requirements, uncertain duration of the work, contractor's technical capability and financial responsibility, or adequacy of the contractor's accounting system), and associated reasoning essential to support the contract type selection.
