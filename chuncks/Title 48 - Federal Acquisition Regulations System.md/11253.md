
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 853 - FORMS
##### Subpart 853.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 853.236-72 VA Form 10101, Contractor Production Report.

VA Form 10101 is prescribed for use by contractors to submit required information to the resident engineer, as specified in 836.7001(c).
