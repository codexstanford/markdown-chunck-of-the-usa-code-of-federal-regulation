
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 15 - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 1532 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### 1532.003 Simplified acquisition procedures financing.
###### Administration.

(e)Regardless of dollar value, the contracting officer is responsible for ensuring that supplies or services have been delivered. The contracting officer shall document the file with evidence of receipt of supplies or services throughout the order period as appropriate to the acquisition.
