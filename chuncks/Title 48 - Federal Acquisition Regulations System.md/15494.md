
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 30 - Department of Homeland Security, Homeland Security Acquisition Regulation (HSAR)
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 3049 - TERMINATION OF CONTRACTS
##### 3049.9002 Contract clause (USCG).

USCG contracting officers shall insert the clause at 3052.249-90, Contract Termination (USCG), in all solicitations and contracts, including contracts for commercial items, with a total value of more than $1,000,000.
