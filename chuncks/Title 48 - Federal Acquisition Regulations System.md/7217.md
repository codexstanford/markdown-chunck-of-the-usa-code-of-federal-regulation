
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 225 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 225.70 - Authorization Acts, Appropriations Acts, and Other Statutory Restrictions on Foreign Acquisition
###### 225.7001 Definitions.
####### Component

means any item supplied to the Government as part of an end item or of another component except that for use in 225.7007, the term means an article, material, or supply incorporated directly into an end product.
