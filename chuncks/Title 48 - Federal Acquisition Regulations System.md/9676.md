
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 552 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 552.1 - Instructions for Using Provisions and Clauses
###### 552.103 Identification of provisions and clauses.
####### General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation deviations.

(2)When a GSAR provision or clause is used with an authorized deviation in a solicitation or contract, it shall be identified by number, title, date, and the text "(DEVIATION)" inserted after the date (e.g., 552.232-1, Payments (NOV 2009) (DEVIATION)).
