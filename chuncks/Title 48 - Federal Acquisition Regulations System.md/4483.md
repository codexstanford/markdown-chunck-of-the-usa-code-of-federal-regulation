
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 45 - GOVERNMENT PROPERTY
##### Subpart 45.1 - General
###### 45.101 Definitions.
####### Discrepancies incident to shipment,e.g.

means any differences (, count or condition) between the items documented to have been shipped and items actually received.
