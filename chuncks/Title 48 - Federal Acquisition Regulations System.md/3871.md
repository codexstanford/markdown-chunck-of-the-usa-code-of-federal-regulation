
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 32 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### Subpart 32.9 - Prompt Payment
###### 32.902 Definitions.
####### Settlement date,

as it applies to electronic funds transfer, means the date on which an electronic funds transfer payment is credited to the contractor's financial institution.
