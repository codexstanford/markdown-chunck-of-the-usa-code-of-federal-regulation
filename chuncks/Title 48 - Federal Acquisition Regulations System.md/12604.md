
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 13 - Department of Commerce
### Subchapter B - Competition and Acquisition Planning
#### PART 1306 - COMPETITION REQUIREMENTS
##### Subpart 1306.3 - Other Than Full and Open Competition
###### 1306.303-70 Documentation and legal review of justifications.
####### Justification for Other than Full and Open Competition.

The justification for providing for other than full and open competition in accordance with FAR 6.303-2 shall be provided on Form CD-492,If the estimated value of the procurement is over legal review thresholds, concurrence by the Procurement Counsel is required.
