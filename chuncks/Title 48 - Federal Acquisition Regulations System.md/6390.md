
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 208 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES
##### Subpart 208.74 - Enterprise Software Agreements
###### 208.7401 Definitions.
####### Software maintenance,e.g.,

means services normally provided by a software company as standard services at established catalog or market prices,the right to receive and use upgraded versions of software, updates, and revisions.
