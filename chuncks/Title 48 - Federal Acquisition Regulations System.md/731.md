
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 4 - ADMINISTRATIVE AND INFORMATION MATTERS
##### Subpart 4.21 - Prohibition on Contracting for Certain Telecommunications and Video Surveillance Services or Equipment
###### 4.2104 Waivers.
####### Executive agencies.,e.g.,

(a)The head of an executive agency may, on a one-time basis, waive the prohibition at 4.2102(a) with respect to a Government entity (requirements office, contracting office) that requests such a waiver.
