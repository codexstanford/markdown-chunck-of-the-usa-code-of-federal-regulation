
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2.1 - Definitions
###### 2.101 Definitions.
####### Signature,signed

ormeans the discrete, verifiable symbol of an individual that, when affixed to a writing with the knowledge and consent of the individual, indicates a present intention to authenticate the writing. This includes electronic symbols.
