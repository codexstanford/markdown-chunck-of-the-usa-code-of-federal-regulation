
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 45 - GOVERNMENT PROPERTY
##### Subpart 45.1 - General
###### 45.101 Definitions.
####### Property Administrator

means an authorized representative of the contracting officer appointed in accordance with agency procedures, responsible for administering the contract requirements and obligations relating to Government property in the possession of a contractor.
