
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 4 - Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 402 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 402.1 - Definitions
###### 402.101 Definitions.
####### Head of the contracting activity,i.e.,

(HCA) means the official who has overall responsibility for managing the contracting activity (Chief, Forest Service; Administrator, Agricultural Research Service; etc.), or the individual designated by such an official to carry out the functions of the HCA.
