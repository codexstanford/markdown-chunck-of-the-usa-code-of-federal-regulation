
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 15 - CONTRACTING BY NEGOTIATION
##### Subpart 15.5 - Preaward, Award, and Postaward Notifications, Protests, and Mistakes
###### 15.503 Notifications to unsuccessful offerors.
####### Preaward notices,Preaward notices of exclusion from competitive range.

(a)-(1)The contracting officer shall notify offerors promptly in writing when their proposals are excluded from the competitive range or otherwise eliminated from the competition. The notice shall state the basis for the determination and that a proposal revision will not be considered.
