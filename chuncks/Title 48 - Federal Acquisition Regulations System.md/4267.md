
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 39 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 39.2 - Information and Communication Technology
###### 39.205 Exemptions.
####### Nonavailability of conforming commercial products and commercial services.

(3)Where there are no commercial products and commercial services that fully conform to the ICT accessibility standards, the agency shall procure the supplies or service available in the commercial marketplace that best meets the ICT accessibility standards consistent with the agency's needs.
