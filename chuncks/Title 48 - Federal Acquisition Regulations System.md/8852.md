
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 3 - Health and Human Services
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 326 - OTHER SOCIOECONOMIC PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 326.5 - Indian Preference in Employment, Training, and Subcontracting Opportunities
###### 326.502 Definitions.
####### Indian tribe

(b)means an Indian tribe, pueblo, band, nation, or other organized group or community, including any Alaska Native Village or regional or village corporation as defined in or established pursuant to the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (85 Stat. 688, 43 U.S.C. 1601), which the United States recognizes as eligible for special programs and services because of its status as Indian.
