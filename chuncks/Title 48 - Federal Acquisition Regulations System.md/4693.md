
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.2 - Contracts for Transportation or for Transportation-Related Services
###### 47.207-6 Rates and charges.
####### Combination of pricing bases.

(1)If various types of services with different bases for assessing charges are required under the same contract, show each service separately and the applicable basis for that service.
