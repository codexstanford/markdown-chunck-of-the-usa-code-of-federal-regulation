
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter I - Department Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 871 - VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION AND EMPLOYMENT PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 871.2 - Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Service
###### 871.201-1 Requirements for the use of contracts.
####### Courses of instruction by correspondence

(a)means a course of education or training conducted by mail consisting of regular lessons or reading assignments, the preparation of required written work that involves the application of principles studied in each lesson, the correction of assigned work with such suggestions or recommendation as may be necessary to instruct the student, the keeping of student achievement records, and issuance of a diploma, certificate, or other evidence to the student upon satisfactorily completing the requirements of the course.
