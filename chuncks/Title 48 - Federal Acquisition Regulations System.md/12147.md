
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1201 - FEDERAL ACQUISITION REGULATIONS SYSTEM
##### Subpart 1201.1 - Purpose, Authority, Issuance
###### 1201.105-2 Arrangement of regulations.
####### General.,see

(a)The TAR, which encompasses both Department and Operating Administration (OA)/Office of the Assistant Secretary for Research and Technology (OST-R)-specific guidance (subpart 1201.3), conforms with the arrangement and numbering system prescribed by FAR 1.104. Guidance that is OA-specific contains the OA's acronym directly after the heading.
