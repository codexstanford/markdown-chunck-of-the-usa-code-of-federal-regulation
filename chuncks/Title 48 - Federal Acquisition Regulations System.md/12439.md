
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 1246 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 1246.1 - General
###### 1246.101-70 Additional definitions.
####### Defect

means any condition or characteristic in any supplies or services furnished by the contractor under the contract that is not in compliance with the requirements of the contract.
