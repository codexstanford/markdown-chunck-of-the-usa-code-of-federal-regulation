#### PART 733 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 733.1 - Protests
###### 733.103-73 Protests excluded from Agency consideration.
####### Judicial proceedings.

(g)Protests will not be considered when the matter involved is the subject of litigation before a court of competent jurisdiction or when the matter involved has been decided on the merits by a court of competent jurisdiction.
