
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System

###### 252.227-7005 License term.

(AUG 1984). The license hereby granted shall remain in full force and effect for the full term of each of the patents referred to in the "License Grant" clause of this contract and any and all patents hereafter issued on applications for patent referred to in such "License Grant" clause.
