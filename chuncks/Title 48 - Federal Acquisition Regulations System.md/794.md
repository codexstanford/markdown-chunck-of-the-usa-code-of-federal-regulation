
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 5 - PUBLICIZING CONTRACT ACTIONS
##### Subpart 5.5 - Paid Advertisements
###### 5.504 Use of advertising agencies.
####### Use of commission-paying media.

(b)The services of advertising agencies in placing advertising with media often can be obtained at no cost to the Government, over and above the space cost, as many media give advertising agencies a commission or discount on the space cost that is not given to the Government.
