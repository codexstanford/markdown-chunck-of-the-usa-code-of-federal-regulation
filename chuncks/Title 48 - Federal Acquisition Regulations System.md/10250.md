
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 625 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 625.70 - Arab League Boycott and Related Provisions
###### 625.7002 Solicitation provision and contract clause.

Contracting officers shall include the following provision and clause in all solicitations and contracts exceeding the simplified acquisition threshold, unless a waiver has been granted in accordance with 625.7001(c):

(a) 652.225-70, Arab League Boycott of Israel; and.

(b) 652.225-71, Section 8(a) of the Export Administration Act, as amended.
