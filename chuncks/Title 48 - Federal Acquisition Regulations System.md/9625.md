
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 537 - SERVICE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 537.2 - Advisory and Assistance Services
###### 537.201 Definitions.
####### Evaluation or analysis of a proposal

means proposal evaluation as described in FAR 15.305. It includes: Cost or price evaluation using cost or price analysis, as defined in FAR 15.404.
