
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 537 - SERVICE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 537.1 - Service Contracts - General
###### 537.110 Contract clauses.
####### Contracts for building services.

(a)Except for solicitations and contracts for building services placed under FAR subpart 8.7, insert the clause at 552.237-71, Qualifications of Employees, in solicitations and contracts for building services that are anticipated to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold.
