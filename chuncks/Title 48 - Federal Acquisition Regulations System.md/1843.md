### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 16 - TYPES OF CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 16.2 - Fixed-Price Contracts
###### 16.203-1 Description.
####### Adjustments based on actual costs of labor or material.

(2)These price adjustments are based on increases or decreases in specified costs of labor or material that the contractor actually experiences during contract performance.
