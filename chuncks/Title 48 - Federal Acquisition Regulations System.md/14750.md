
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 2432 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### Subpart 2432.9 - Prompt Payment
###### 2432.906 Making payments.
####### General.

(a)The authority to make the determination prescribed in FAR 32.906(a) is delegated to the HCA. Before making this determination, the HCA shall consult with the appropriate payment office to ensure that procedures are in place to permit timely payment.
