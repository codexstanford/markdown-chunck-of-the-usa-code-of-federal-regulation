
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.8 - Equal Employment Opportunity
###### 22.801 Definitions.
####### Recruiting and training agency

means any person who refers workers to any contractor or provides or supervises apprenticeship or training for employment by any contractor.
