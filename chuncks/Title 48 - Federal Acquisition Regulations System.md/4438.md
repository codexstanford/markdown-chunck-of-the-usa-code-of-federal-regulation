
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 43 - CONTRACT MODIFICATIONS
##### Subpart 43.2 - Change Orders
###### 43.205 Contract clauses.

(a)(1) The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 52.243-1, Changes-Fixed-Price, in solicitations and contracts when a fixed-price contract for supplies is contemplated.
