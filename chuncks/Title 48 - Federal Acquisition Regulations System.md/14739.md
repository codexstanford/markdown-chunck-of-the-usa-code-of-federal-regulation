
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 2428 - BONDS AND INSURANCE
##### Subpart 2428.1 - Bonds
###### 2428.106-6 Furnishing information.

(c) The Contracting Officer shall furnish the certified copy of the bond and the contract for which it was given to any person who requests them in accordance with FAR 28.106-6.
