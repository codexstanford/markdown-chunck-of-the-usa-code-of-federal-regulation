
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 61 - Civilian Board of Contract Appeals, General Services Administration
### PART 6104 - TRAVEL AND RELOCATION EXPENSES CASES
#### 6104.405 Proceedings [Rule 405].
##### Conferences.,ex parte

(b)The judge will not engage incommunications involving the underlying facts or merits of the claim. The judge may hold a conference with the claimant and the agency contact, at any time, for any purpose. The judge may provide the participants a memorandum reflecting the results of a conference.
