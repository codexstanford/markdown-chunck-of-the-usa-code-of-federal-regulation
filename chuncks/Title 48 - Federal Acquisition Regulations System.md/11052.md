
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 835 - RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT CONTRACTING
##### 835.001-70 Veterans Affairs (VA) definitions.
###### VA facility

means a component of the VA national health care system, such as a VA Medical Center, VA Health Care System, or VA Medical and Regional Office Center.
