
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 253 - FORMS
##### Subpart 253.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 253.209-1 Responsible prospective contractors.
####### Quality assurance capability.

(C)An assessment of the prospective contractor's capability to meet the quality assurance requirements of the proposed contract. It may involve an evaluation of the prospective contractor's quality assurance system, personnel, facilities, and equipment.
