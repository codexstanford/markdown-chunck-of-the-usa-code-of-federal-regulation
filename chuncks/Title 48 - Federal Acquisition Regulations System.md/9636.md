
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 538 - FEDERAL SUPPLY SCHEDULE CONTRACTING
##### Subpart 538.70 - Purchasing by Non-Federal Entities
###### 538.7001 Definitions.
####### Ordering activity

(also called "ordering agency" and "ordering office") means an eligible ordering activity (see 552.238-113) authorized to place orders under Federal supply schedule contracts.
