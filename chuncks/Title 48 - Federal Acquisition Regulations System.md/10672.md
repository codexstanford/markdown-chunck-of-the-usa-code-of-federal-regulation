
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 7 - Agency for International Development
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 752 - SOLICITATION PROVISIONS AND CONTRACT CLAUSES
##### Subpart 752.2 - Texts of Provisions and Clauses
###### 752.200 Scope of subpart.

None of the clauses specified in this subpart are for use in USAID personal services contracts. For personal services contract clauses, see (48 CFR) AIDAR Appendix D-Direct USAID Contracts with U.S. Citizens or U.S. Residents for Personal Services Abroad and (48 CFR) AIDAR Appendix J-Direct USAID Contracts with Cooperating Country Nationals and with Third Country Nationals for Personal Services Abroad.
