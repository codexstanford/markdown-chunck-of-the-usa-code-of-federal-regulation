
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 47 - TRANSPORTATION
##### Subpart 47.3 - Transportation in Supply Contracts
###### 47.303-9 F.o.b. vessel, port of shipment.
####### Explanation of delivery term. F.o.b. vessel, port shipment

(a)means free of expense to the Government loaded, stowed, and trimmed on board the ocean vessel at the specified port of shipment.
