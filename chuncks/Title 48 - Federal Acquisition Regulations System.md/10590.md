
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 7 - Agency for International Development
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 731 - CONTRACT COST PRINCIPLES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 731.3 - Contracts With Educational Institutions
###### 731.373 Overseas recruitment incentive.

USAID's policies regarding overseas recruitment incentives are set forth in (48 CFR) AIDAR 731.205-70. These policies are also applicable to contracts with an educational institution.
