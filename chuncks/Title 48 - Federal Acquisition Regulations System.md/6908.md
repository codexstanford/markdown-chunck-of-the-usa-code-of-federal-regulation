
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 218 - EMERGENCY ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 218.2 - Emergency Acquisition Flexibilities
###### 218.201 Contingency operation.
####### Governmentwide commercial purchase card.

(4)A contracting office supporting a contingency operation or a humanitarian or peacekeeping operation may use the Governmentwide commercial purchase card to make a purchase that exceeds the micro-purchase threshold but does not exceed the simplified acquisition threshold if certain conditions are met. See 213.301(3).
