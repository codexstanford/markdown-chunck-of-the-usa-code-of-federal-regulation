
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter H - Clauses and Forms
#### PART 53 - FORMS
##### Subpart 53.2 - Prescription of Forms
###### 53.215-1 Solicitation and receipt of proposals.
####### SF 33 (Rev. 9/97), Solicitation, Offer and Award.

(c)SF 33, prescribed in 53.214(c), may be used in connection with the solicitation and award of negotiated contracts. Award of such contracts may be made by either OF 307, SF 33, or SF 26, as specified in 53.214(c) and 15.509.
