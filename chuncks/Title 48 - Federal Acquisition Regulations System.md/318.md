
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2.1 - Definitions
###### 2.101 Definitions.
####### Supporting a diplomatic or consular mission

means performing outside the United States under a contract administered by Federal agency personnel who are subject to the direction of a Chief of Mission.
