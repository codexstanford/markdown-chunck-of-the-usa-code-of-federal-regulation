
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 917 - SPECIAL CONTRACTING METHODS
##### Subpart 917.6 - Management and Operating Contracts
###### 917.601 Definitions.
####### Performance-based management contract

means a management and operating contract that employs, to the maximum extent practicable, performance-based contracting concepts and methodologies through the application of results-oriented statements of work; clear objective performance standards and measurement tools; and incentives to encourage superior contractor performance.
