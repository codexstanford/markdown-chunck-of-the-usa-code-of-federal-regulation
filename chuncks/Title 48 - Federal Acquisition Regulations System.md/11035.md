
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 833 - PROTESTS, DISPUTES, AND APPEALS
##### Subpart 833.1 - Protests
###### 833.103-70 Protests to VA.
####### Small business certificate of competency program.

(3)A protest made under section 8(b)(7) of the Small Business Act, or in regard to any issuance of a certificate of competency or refusal to issue a certificate under that section, is not reviewed in accordance with bid protest procedures unless there is a showing of possible fraud or bad faith on the part of Government officials.
