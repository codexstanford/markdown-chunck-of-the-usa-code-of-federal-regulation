
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 4 - ADMINISTRATIVE AND INFORMATION MATTERS
##### Subpart 4.10 - Uniform Use of Line Items
###### 4.1004 Establishing subline items.
####### Informational subline items.,e.g.,

(b)(1) Informational subline items may be used by agencies for administrative purposes. This type of subline item identifies information that relates directly to the line item and is an integral part of it (parts of an assembly or parts of a kit).

(2) Position informational subline items within the line item description, not in the quantity or price fields.
