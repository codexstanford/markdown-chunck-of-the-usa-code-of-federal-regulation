
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 31 - CONTRACT COST PRINCIPLES AND PROCEDURES
##### Subpart 31.2 - Contracts With Commercial Organizations
###### 31.205-17 Idle facilities and idle capacity costs.
####### Costs of idle facilities or idle capacity

means costs such as maintenance, repair, housing, rent, and other related costs; e.g., property taxes, insurance, and depreciation.
