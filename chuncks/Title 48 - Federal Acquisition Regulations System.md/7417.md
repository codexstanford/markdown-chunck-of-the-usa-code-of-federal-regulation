
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 225 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 225.79 - EXPORT CONTROL
###### 225.7901-2 General.
####### et seq.,et seq.

Certain types of items are subject to export controls in accordance with the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2751,), the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (22 CFR parts 120-130), the Export Administration Act of 1979, as amended (50 U.S.C. App. 2401), and the Export Administration Regulations (15 CFR parts 730-774). See PGI 225.7901-2 for additional information.
