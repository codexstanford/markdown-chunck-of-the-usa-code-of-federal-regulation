
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 4 - Department of Agriculture
### Subchapter I - Food Assistance Programs
#### PART 470 - COMMODITY ACQUISITIONS
##### 470.101 Definitions.
###### Lowest landed cost

means, as authorized by 46 U.S.C. 55314(c), with respect to an agricultural product acquired under this part the lowest aggregate cost for the acquisition of such product and the shipment of such product to a foreign destination.
