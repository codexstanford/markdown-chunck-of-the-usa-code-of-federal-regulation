
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 18 - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 1827 - PATENTS, DATA, AND COPYRIGHTS
##### Subpart 1827.3 - Patent Rights Under Government Contracts
###### 1827.301 Definitions.
####### Subject invention,,et seq.

in lieu of the definition in FAR 27.301, means any reportable item that is or may be patentable or otherwise protectable under Title 35 of the United States Code, or any novel variety of plant that is or may be protectable under the Plant Variety Protection Act (7 U.S.C. 2321,).
