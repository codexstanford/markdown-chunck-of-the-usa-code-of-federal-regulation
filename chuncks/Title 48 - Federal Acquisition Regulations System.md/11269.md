
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter I - Department Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 873 - SIMPLIFIED PROCEDURES FOR HEALTH-CARE RESOURCES
##### 873.102 Definitions.
###### Health-care providers

include health-care plans and insurers and any organizations, institutions, or other entities or individuals who furnish health-care resources. (38 U.S.C. 8153)
