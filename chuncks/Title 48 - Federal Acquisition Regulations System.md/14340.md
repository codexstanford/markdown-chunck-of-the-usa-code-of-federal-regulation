
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 20 - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2002 - DEFINITIONS
##### Subpart 2002.1 - Definitions
###### 2002.100 Definitions.
####### Commission

means the NRC Commission of five members, or a quorum thereof, sitting as a body, as provided by Section 201 of the Energy Reorganization Act of 1974 (42 U.S.C. 5841).
