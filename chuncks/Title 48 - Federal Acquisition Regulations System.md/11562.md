
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 925 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 925.70 - Acquisition of Nuclear Hot Cell Services
###### 925.7001 Definitions.
####### Costs related to the storage and disposal of nuclear waste,

as used in this subpart, means any costs, whether required by regulation or incurred as a matter of prudent business practice, associated with the storage or disposal of nuclear waste.
