
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 15 - Environmental Protection Agency
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 1542 - CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION
##### Subpart 1542.12 - Novation and Change of Name Agreements
###### 1542.1200 Scope of subpart.

This subpart implements FAR subpart 42.12 and provides policies and procedures for executing and processing novation and change-of-name agreements.
