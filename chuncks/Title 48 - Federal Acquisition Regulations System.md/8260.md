
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 246 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 246.7 - Warranties
###### 246.702-70 Definitions.
####### Warranty tracking

means the ability to trace a warranted item from delivery through completion of the effectivity of the warranty.
