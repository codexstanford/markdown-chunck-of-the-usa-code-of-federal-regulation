
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2.1 - Definitions
###### 2.101 Definitions.
####### Principal,e.g.

means an officer, director, owner, partner, or a person having primary management or supervisory responsibilities within a business entity (, general manager; plant manager; head of a division or business segment; and similar positions).
