
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 10 - Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 1019 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 1019.2 - Policies
###### 1019.202-70 The Treasury Mentor Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Selection of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms.

(i)(1) Mentor firms will be solely responsible for selecting prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms. The mentor is encouraged to identify and select the types of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms listed in 1019.202-70(h). Mentor firms may have multiple prot&#xE9;g&#xE9;s.

(2) The selection of prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; firms by mentor firms may not be protested. Any question regarding the size or eligibility status of an entity selected by a mentor to be a prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; must be referred solely to Treasury's OSDBU for resolution. Treasury, at its discretion, may seek an advisory opinion from the Small Business Administration (SBA).
