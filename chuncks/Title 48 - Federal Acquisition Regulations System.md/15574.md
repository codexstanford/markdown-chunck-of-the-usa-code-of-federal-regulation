
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 34 - Department of Education Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 3401 - ED ACQUISITION REGULATION SYSTEM
##### Subpart 3401.6 - Career Development, Contracting Authority, and Responsibilities
###### 3401.602-3 Ratification of unauthorized commitments.
####### Definitions.,commitment

(a)As used in this subpart,includes issuance of letters of intent and arrangements for free vendor services or use of equipment with the promise or the appearance of commitment that a contract, modification, or order will, or may, be awarded.
