
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 46 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 46.1 - General
###### 46.101 Definitions.
####### Suspect counterfeit item

means an item for which credible evidence (including but not limited to, visual inspection or testing) provides reasonable doubt that the item is authentic.
