### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 210 - MARKET RESEARCH
##### 210.002 Procedures.

(e)(i) When contracting for services, see PGI 210.070 for the "Market Research Report Guide for Improving the Tradecraft in Services Acquisition".
