
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 932 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### Subpart 932.9 - Prompt Payment
###### 932.970 Implementing DOE policies and procedures.
####### Invoice payments,Contract settlement date.

(a)-(1)For purposes of determining any interest penalties under cost-type contracts, the effective date of contract settlement shall be the effective date of the final contract modification issued to acknowledge contract settlement and to close out the contract.
