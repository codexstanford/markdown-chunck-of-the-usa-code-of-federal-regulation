### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs

##### Subpart 22.8 - Equal Employment Opportunity
###### 22.801 Definitions.
####### Deputy Assistant Secretary

means the Deputy Assistant Secretary for Federal Contract Compliance, U.S. Department of Labor, or a designee.
