
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 2451 - USE OF GOVERNMENT SOURCES BY CONTRACTORS
##### Subpart 2451.70 - Contractor Use of Government Discount Travel Rates
###### 2451.7001 Contract clause.

The Contracting Officer shall insert the clause at 48 CFR 2452.251-70, Contractor Employee Travel, in cost-reimbursement solicitations and contracts involving contractor travel.
