
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 19 - Broadcasting Board of Governors
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1902 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 1902.1 - Definitions
###### 1902.101 Definitions.
####### Head of the Board,Board head,authorized representative

(also called) means the Board Director or Deputy Director; and the termmeans any person, persons or board (other than the contracting officer) authorized to act for the Head of the Board.
