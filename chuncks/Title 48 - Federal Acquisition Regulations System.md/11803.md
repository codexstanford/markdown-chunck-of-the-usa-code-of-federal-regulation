
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter I - Agency Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 970 - DOE MANAGEMENT AND OPERATING CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 970.08 - Required sources of supplies and services
###### 970.0801-2 Policy.

The provisions of 48 CFR subpart 8.1, 41 CFR 101-43 (Federal Property Management Regulation), and 41 CFR 109-43, (Department of Energy (DOE) Property Management Regulation) apply to DOE's management and operating contracts.
