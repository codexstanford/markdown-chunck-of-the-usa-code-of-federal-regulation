
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 6 - Department of State
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 625 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 625.1 - Buy American - Supplies
###### 625.105 Determining reasonableness of cost.

(a)(1) The authority to make the determinations prescribed in FAR 25.105(a)(1) is delegated, without power of redelegation, to the head of the contracting activity.
