
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 24 - Department of Housing and Urban Development
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2402 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2402.1 - Definitions
###### 2402.101 Definitions.
####### Chief Procurement Officer

means the HUD official having authority for all of the Department's procurement activities.
