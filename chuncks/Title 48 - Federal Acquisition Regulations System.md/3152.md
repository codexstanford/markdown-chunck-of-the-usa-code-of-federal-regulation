
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 26 - OTHER SOCIOECONOMIC PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 26.2 - Major Disaster or Emergency Assistance Activities
###### 26.205 Disaster Response Registry.
####### https://www.sam.gov,

(b) A list of prospective vendors voluntarily participating in the Disaster Response Registry can be retrieved using the System for Award Management (SAM) search tool, which can be accessed viaSearch Records, Advanced Search, Disaster Response Registry Search These vendors may be identified by selecting the criteria for "Disaster Response Contractors". Contractors are required to register in SAM in order to gain access to the Disaster Response Registry.
