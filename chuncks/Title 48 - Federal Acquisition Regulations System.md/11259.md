
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter I - Department Supplementary Regulations
#### PART 871 - VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION AND EMPLOYMENT PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 871.2 - Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Service
###### 871.201-1 Requirements for the use of contracts.
####### Special services or special courses

(b)means those services or courses that VA requests that are supplementary to those the institution customarily provides for similarly circumstanced non-Veteran students and that the contracting officer considers to be necessary for the rehabilitation of the trainee.
