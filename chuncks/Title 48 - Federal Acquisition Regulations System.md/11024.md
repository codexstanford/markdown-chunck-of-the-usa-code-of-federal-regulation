
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 8 - Department of Veterans Affairs
### Subchapter E - General Contracting Requirements
#### PART 832 - CONTRACT FINANCING
##### 832.001 Definitions.
###### Electronic form

(b)means an automated system transmitting information electronically according to the accepted electronic data transmission methods identified in 832.7002-1. Facsimile, email, and scanned documents are not acceptable electronic forms for submission of payment requests.
