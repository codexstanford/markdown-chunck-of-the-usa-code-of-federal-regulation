
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 10 - Department of the Treasury
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 1019 - SMALL BUSINESS PROGRAMS
##### Subpart 1019.2 - Policies
###### 1019.202-70 The Treasury Mentor Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
####### Solicitation provisions and contract clauses

(p)(1) Insert the provision at 1052.219-73, Department of the Treasury Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program, in all unrestricted solicitations exceeding $650,000 ($1,500,000 for construction) that offer subcontracting possibilities.

(2) Insert the clause at 1052.219-75, Mentor Requirements and Evaluation, in solicitations and contracts where the contractor is a participant in the Treasury Mentor-Prot&#xE9;g&#xE9; Program.
