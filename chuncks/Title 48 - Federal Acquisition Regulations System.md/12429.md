
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1239 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### 1239.002 Definitions.
###### Media

means physical devices or writing surfaces including, but not limited to, magnetic tapes, optical disks, magnetic disks, large-scale integration memory chips, and printouts onto which information is recorded, stored, or printed within an information system.
