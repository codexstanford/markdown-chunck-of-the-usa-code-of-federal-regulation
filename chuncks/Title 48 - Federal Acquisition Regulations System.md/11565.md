
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 925 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 925.70 - Acquisition of Nuclear Hot Cell Services
###### 925.7001 Definitions.
####### Nuclear waste,

as used in this subpart, means any radioactive waste material subject to regulation by the Nuclear Regulatory Commission or the Department of Energy, or in the case of foreign offers, by comparable foreign organizations.
