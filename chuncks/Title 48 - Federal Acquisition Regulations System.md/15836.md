
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 61 - Civilian Board of Contract Appeals, General Services Administration
### PART 6101 - RULES OF PROCEDURE OF THE CIVILIAN BOARD OF CONTRACT APPEALS
#### 6101.21 Hearing procedures [Rule 21].
##### Generally.,in camera

(a)The Board generally holds hearings in public hearing rooms. Except as necessary under a protective order orprocedures, hearings are open to the public. The Board entrusts the conduct of hearings to the discretion of the presiding judge.
