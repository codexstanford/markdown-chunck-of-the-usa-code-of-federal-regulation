
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 12 - Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 1239 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 1239.74 - Records Management
###### 1239.7402 Policy.
####### Non-public information.

(b)Contractors shall not create or maintain any records containing any non-public DOT information that are not specifically tied to or authorized by the contract.
