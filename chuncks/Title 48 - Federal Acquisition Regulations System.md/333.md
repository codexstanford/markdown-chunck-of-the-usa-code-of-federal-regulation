### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 2 - DEFINITIONS OF WORDS AND TERMS
##### Subpart 2.1 - Definitions
###### 2.101 Definitions.
####### Unique entity identifier,www.sam.gov

means a number or other identifier used to identify a specific commercial, nonprofit, or Government entity. Seefor the designated entity for establishing unique entity identifiers.
