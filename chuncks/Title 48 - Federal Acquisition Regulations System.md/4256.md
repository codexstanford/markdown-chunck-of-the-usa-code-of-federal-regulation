
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 39 - ACQUISITION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
##### Subpart 39.2 - Information and Communication Technology
###### 39.203 Applicability.
####### Commercial products and commercial services.

(d)When acquiring commercial products and commercial services, an agency must comply with those ICT accessibility standards that can be met with supplies or services that are available in the commercial marketplace and that best address the agency's needs, but see 39.205(a)(3).
