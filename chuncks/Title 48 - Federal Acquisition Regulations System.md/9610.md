
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 5 - General Services Administration
### Subchapter F - Special Categories of Contracting
#### PART 536 - CONSTRUCTION AND ARCHITECT-ENGINEER CONTRACTS
##### Subpart 536.71 - Construction-Manager-as-Constructor Contracting
###### 536.7105-3 Accounting and auditing requirements.
####### Payments and Reconciliation.

(2)All payments shall be reconciled with the open book accounting records and the schedule of values adjusted, as appropriate. Reconciliation shall occur each month and should be coordinated with monthly progress payments. The reconciliation shall be documented in the contract file.
