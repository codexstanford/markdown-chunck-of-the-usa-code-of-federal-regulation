
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 30 - Department of Homeland Security, Homeland Security Acquisition Regulation (HSAR)
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 3046 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 3046.7 - Warranties
###### 3046.790-2 Definitions (USCG).
####### Performance requirements

means the operating capabilities, maintenance, and reliability characteristics of a system that are determined to be necessary for it to fulfill the requirement for which the system is designed.
