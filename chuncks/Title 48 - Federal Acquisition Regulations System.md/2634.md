
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 1 - Federal Acquisition Regulation
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 22 - APPLICATION OF LABOR LAWS TO GOVERNMENT ACQUISITIONS
##### Subpart 22.13 - Equal Opportunity for Veterans
###### 22.1301 Definitions.
####### Qualified disabled veteran

means a disabled veteran who has the ability to perform the essential functions of the employment positions with or without reasonable accommodation.
