
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 29 - Department of Labor
### Subchapter C - Contracting Methods and Contract Types
#### PART 2917 - SPECIAL CONTRACTING METHODS
##### Subpart 2917.5 - Interagency Acquisitions Under The Economy Act
###### 2917.503 Determinations and findings requirements.
####### Applicability.

Before the execution of an interagency agreement under the Economy Act, the contracting officer, or other authorized official, must sign the determination required in FAR 17.503 and 31 U.S.C. 1535.
