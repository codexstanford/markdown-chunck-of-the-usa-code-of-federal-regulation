
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 9 - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Socioeconomic Programs
#### PART 925 - FOREIGN ACQUISITION
##### Subpart 925.70 - Acquisition of Nuclear Hot Cell Services
###### 925.7001 Definitions.
####### United States company,

as used in this subpart, means a company which offers to perform nuclear hot cell services at a facility subject to the laws and regulations of the United States, its agencies, and its political subdivisions.
