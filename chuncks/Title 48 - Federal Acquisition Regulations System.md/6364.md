
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 208 - REQUIRED SOURCES OF SUPPLIES AND SERVICES
##### Subpart 208.70 - Coordinated Acquisition
###### 208.7001 Definitions.
####### Acquiring department

means the department, agency, or General Services Administration which has contracting responsibility under the Coordinated Acquisition Program.
