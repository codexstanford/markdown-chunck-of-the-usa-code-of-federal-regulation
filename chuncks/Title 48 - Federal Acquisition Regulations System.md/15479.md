
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 30 - Department of Homeland Security, Homeland Security Acquisition Regulation (HSAR)
### Subchapter G - Contract Management
#### PART 3046 - QUALITY ASSURANCE
##### Subpart 3046.7 - Warranties
###### 3046.790-2 Definitions (USCG).
####### Design and manufacturing requirement

means structural and engineering plans and manufacturing particulars, including precise measurements, tolerances, materials and finished product tests for the major system being produced.
