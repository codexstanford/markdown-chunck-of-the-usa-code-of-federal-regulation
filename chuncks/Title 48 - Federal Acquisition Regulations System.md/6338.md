
# Title 48 - Federal Acquisition Regulations System
## Chapter 2 - Defense Acquisition Regulations System, Department of Defense
### Subchapter B - Acquisition Planning
#### PART 207 - ACQUISITION PLANNING
##### Subpart 207.1 - Acquisition Plans
###### 207.171-2 Definition.
####### Component,

as used in this section, includes subsystems, assemblies, subassemblies, and other major elements of an end item; it does not include elements of relatively small annual acquisition value.
