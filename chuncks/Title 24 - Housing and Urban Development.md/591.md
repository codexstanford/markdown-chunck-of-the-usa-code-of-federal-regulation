##### Section 8 Project-Based Assistance: Occupancy Requirements
###### ยง 5.653 Section 8 project-based assistance programs: Admission - Income-eligibility and income-targeting.
####### Limitation on admission of non-very low income families,Admission to units available before October 1, 1981.

(d)-(1)Not more than 25 percent of the Section 8 project-based dwelling units that were available for occupancy under Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments Contracts effective before October 1, 1981 and that are leased on or after that date shall be available for leasing by low income families other than very low income families. HUD reserves the right to limit the admission of low income families other than very low income families to these units.
