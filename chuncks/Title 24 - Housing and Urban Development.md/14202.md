###### ยง 983.301 Determining the rent to owner.
####### Reasonable rent.

(e)The PHA shall determine the reasonable rent in accordance with ยง 983.303. The rent to the owner for each contract unit may at no time exceed the reasonable rent, except in cases where, the PHA has elected within the HAP contract not to reduce rents below the initial rent to owner and, upon redetermination of the rent to owner, the reasonable rent would result in a rent below the initial rent.
