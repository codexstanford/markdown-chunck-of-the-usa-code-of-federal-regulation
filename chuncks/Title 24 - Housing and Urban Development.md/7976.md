
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter III - Government National Mortgage Association, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 300 - GENERAL
##### ยง 300.13 Power of attorney.

In order to efficiently carry out the purposes of the Association, the Association may appoint any person its true and lawful attorney-in-fact by publication in theor by appointment from the President of the Association in writing. Any such attorney-in-fact shall have the power outlined in the publication or appointment.
