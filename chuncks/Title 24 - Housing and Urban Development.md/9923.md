
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

###### Subpart B - Assistance Provided
####### ยง 582.100 Program component descriptions.
######## Sponsor-based rental assistance (SRA).

(c)Sponsor-based rental assistance provides grants for rental assistance through contracts between the grant recipient and sponsor organizations. A sponsor may be a private, nonprofit organization or a community mental health agency established as a public nonprofit organization. Participants reside in housing owned or leased by the sponsor. The term of the grant between HUD and the grant recipient for SRA is five years.
