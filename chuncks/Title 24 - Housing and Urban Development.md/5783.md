(b)-(1)(i) During the First 12-Month Disbursement Period, no payment plan change shall cause disbursements to exceed the Initial Disbursement Limit.

A mortgage will continue to bear interest at an adjustable interest rate as agreed between the mortgagee and the borrower at loan origination.
