####### Evaporative coolers.

(3)(i) A roof-mounted cooler must be listed or certified by a nationally recognized testing agency for the application for which the unit is intended and installed in accordance with the terms of its listing (see ยง 3280.714 of this chapter).

(A) Any discharge grill must not be closer than three feet from a smoke alarm.
