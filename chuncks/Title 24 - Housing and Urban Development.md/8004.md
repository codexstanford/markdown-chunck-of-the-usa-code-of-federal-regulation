
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter III - Government National Mortgage Association, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 320 - GUARANTY OF MORTGAGE-BACKED SECURITIES
##### Subpart A - Pass-Through Type Securities
###### ยง 320.10 Financial reporting.
####### Government Auditing Standards,

Issuers shall submit to the Association audited annual financial statements within 90 days of their fiscal year end. All financial statements shall include a balance sheet and a statement of operations and cash flows. The audit shall be conducted in accordance with the standards for financial audits of the U.S. Government Accountability Office'sissued by the Comptroller General of the United States.
