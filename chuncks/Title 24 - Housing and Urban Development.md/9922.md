
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

#### Subchapter C - Community Facilities
##### PART 582 - SHELTER PLUS CARE
###### Subpart B - Assistance Provided
####### ยง 582.100 Program component descriptions.
######## Project-based rental assistance (PRA).

(b)Project-based rental assistance provides grants for rental assistance to the owner of an existing structure, where the owner agrees to lease the subsidized units to participants. Participants do not retain rental assistance if they move. Rental subsidies are provided to the owner for a period of either five or ten years. To qualify for ten years of rental subsidies, the owner must complete at least $3,000 of eligible rehabilitation for each unit (including the unit's prorated share of work to be accomplished on common areas or systems), to make the structure decent, safe and sanitary. This rehabilitation must be completed with in 12 months of the grant award.
