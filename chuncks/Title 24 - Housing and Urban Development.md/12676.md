
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter IX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Public and Indian Housing, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 906 - PUBLIC HOUSING HOMEOWNERSHIP PROGRAMS
##### Subpart D - Program Administration
###### ยง 906.31 Requirements applicable to net proceeds resulting from sale.
####### PRE use of resale net proceeds.

(b)The PHA may require the PRE to return the net proceeds from the resale of the units to the PHA. If the PHA permits the PRE to retain the net proceeds, the PRE must use these proceeds for low-income housing purposes.
