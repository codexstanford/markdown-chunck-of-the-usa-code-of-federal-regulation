#### Subchapter I - HUD-Owned Properties
##### PART 290 - DISPOSITION OF MULTIFAMILY PROJECTS AND SALE OF HUD-HELD MULTIFAMILY MORTGAGES
###### Subpart A - Disposition of Multifamily Projects
####### ยง 290.3 Definitions.
######## Nonprofit organization

means a corporation or association organized for purposes other than making a profit or gain for itself. Stockholders or trustees do not share in profits or losses. Profits are used to accomplish the charitable, humanitarian, or educational purposes of the corporation.
