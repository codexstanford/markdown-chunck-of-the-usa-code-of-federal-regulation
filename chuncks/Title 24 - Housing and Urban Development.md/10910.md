##### Subpart C - Definitions and Other Requirements

####### Contract-(Housing Assistance Payments Contract).

The Contract entered into by the owner and the State Agency upon satisfactory completion of a new construction or substantial rehabilitation project which sets forth the rights and duties of the parties with respect to the project and the payments under the Contract.
