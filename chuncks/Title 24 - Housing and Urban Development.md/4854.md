
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

### Chapter II - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development

###### Subpart A - General
####### ยง 201.2 Definitions.
######## Borrower

means one who applies for and receives a loan insured under this part. The term may also include any co-maker or co-signer or any assumptor who is obligated for the repayment of a loan obligation insured under this part.
