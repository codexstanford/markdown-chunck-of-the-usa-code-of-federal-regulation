
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 3288 - MANUFACTURED HOME DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROGRAM
##### Subpart B - HUD Manufactured Home Dispute Resolution Program in HUD-Administered States
###### ยง 3288.20 Reporting a defect.
####### Making a report.

(a)To preserve the right to request dispute resolution through HUD, alleged defects must be reported to the manufacturer, retailer, installer, HUD, or a State Administrative Agency. An alleged defect may be reported by a homeowner, manufacturer, retailer, or installer.
