
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 3280 - MANUFACTURED HOME CONSTRUCTION AND SAFETY STANDARDS
##### Subpart C - Fire Safety
###### ยง 3280.208 Requirements for foam plastic thermal insulating materials.
####### Wall assemblies.

(i)The foam plastic system shall demonstrate equivalent or superior performance to the control module as determined by:

(A) Time to reach flashover (600 &#xB0;C in the upper part of the room);

(B) Time to reach an oxygen (O) level of 14% (rate of Odepletion), a carbon monoxide (CO) level of 1%, a carbon dioxide (CO) level of 6%, and a smoke level of 0.26 optical density/meter measured at 5 feet high in the doorway; and

(C) Rate of change concentration for O, CO, COand smoke measured 3 inches below the top of the doorway.
