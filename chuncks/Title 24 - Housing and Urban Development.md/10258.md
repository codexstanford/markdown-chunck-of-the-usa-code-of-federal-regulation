
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter V - Office of Assistant Secretary for Community Planning and Development, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### Subchapter C - Community Facilities
##### PART 599 - RENEWAL COMMUNITIES
###### Subpart E - Selection of Nominated Areas To Be Renewal Communities
####### ยง 599.405 Selection of Renewal Communities.
######## Selection of Category 1 applications,Six or less rural nominations.

(a)-(1)If there are six or fewer Category 1 rural area nominations, HUD will select all of the nominated rural areas in Category 1 for designation as Renewal Communities. HUD will then select the highest ranking Category 1 urban area nominations, but will not exceed a total of 20 Category 1 designations.
