
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development

#### PART 983 - PROJECT-BASED VOUCHER (PBV) PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 983.3 PBV definitions.
####### Rehabilitated housing.

Housing units that exist on the proposal selection date, but do not substantially comply with the HQS on that date, and are developed, pursuant to an Agreement between the PHA and owner, for use under the PBV program.
