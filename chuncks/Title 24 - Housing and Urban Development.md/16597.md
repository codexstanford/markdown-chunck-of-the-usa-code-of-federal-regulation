
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development

##### Subpart A - Generally Applicable Provisions and Requirements
###### ยง 3286.3 Definitions.
####### Installation standards

means the standards established by HUD in 24 CFR part 3285, or any set of state standards that the Secretary has determined provide protection to the residents of manufactured homes that equals or exceeds the protection provided by the standards in 24 CFR part 3285.
