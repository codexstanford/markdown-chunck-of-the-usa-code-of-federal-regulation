
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter IX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Public and Indian Housing, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 963 - PUBLIC HOUSING - CONTRACTING WITH RESIDENT-OWNED BUSINESSES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 963.5 Definitions.
####### Resident-owned business.

Any business concern which is owned and controlled by public housing residents. (The term "resident-owned business" includes sole proprietorships.) For purposes of this part, "owned and controlled" means a business:

(1) Which is at least 51 percent owned by one or more public housing residents; and

(2) Whose management and daily business operations are controlled by one or more such individuals.
