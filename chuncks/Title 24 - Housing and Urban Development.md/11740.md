###### Section 202 Projects for the Nonelderly Handicapped Families and Individuals - Section 162 Assistance
####### ยง 891.780 Adjustment of rents.
######## Contract rents.

(a)HUD will calculate contract rent adjustments based on the sum of the project's operating costs and debt service (as calculated by HUD), with adjustments for vacancies, the project's nonrental income, and other factors that HUD deems appropriate. The calculation will be made on the basis of information provided by the Borrower on a form prescribed by HUD.
