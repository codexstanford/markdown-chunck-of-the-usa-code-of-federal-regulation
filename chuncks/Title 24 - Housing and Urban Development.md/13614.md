##### Subpart G - Leasing a Unit
###### ยง 982.308 Lease and tenancy.
####### Tenant's legal capacity.

(a)The tenant must have legal capacity to enter a lease under State and local law. "Legal capacity" means that the tenant is bound by the terms of the lease and may enforce the terms of the lease against the owner.
