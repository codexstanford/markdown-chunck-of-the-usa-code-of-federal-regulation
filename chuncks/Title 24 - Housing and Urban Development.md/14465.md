
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development

#### PART 990 - THE PUBLIC HOUSING OPERATING FUND PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - Purpose, Applicability, Formula, and Definitions
###### ยง 990.115 Definitions.
####### Annual contributions contract (ACC)

is a contract prescribed by HUD for loans and contributions, which may be in the form of operating subsidy, whereby HUD agrees to provide financial assistance and the PHA agrees to comply with HUD requirements for the development and operation of its public housing projects.
