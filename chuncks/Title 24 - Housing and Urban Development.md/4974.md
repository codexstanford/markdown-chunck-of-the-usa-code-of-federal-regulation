####### ยง 201.40 Post-disbursement loan requirements.
######## Discovery of misstatements of fact.

(a)If, after a loan has been made, the lender discovers any material misstatement of fact or that the loan proceeds have been misused by the borrower, dealer or any other party, it shall promptly report this to the Secretary. In such case, the insurance of the loan shall not be affected unless such material misstatement of fact or misuse of loan proceeds was caused by or was knowingly sanctioned by the lender or its employees (see ยง 201.31(e)(3)), provided that the validity of any lien on the property has not been impaired.
