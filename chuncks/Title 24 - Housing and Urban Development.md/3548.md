
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

### PART 93 - HOUSING TRUST FUND
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 93.2 Definitions.
###### Very low-income renter households

means a household whose income is in excess of 30 percent but not greater than 50 percent of the area median income, with adjustments for smaller and larger families, as determined by HUD.
