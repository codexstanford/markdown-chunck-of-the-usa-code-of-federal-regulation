
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

##### PART 583 - SUPPORTIVE HOUSING PROGRAM
###### Subpart D - Program Requirements
####### ยง 583.310 Displacement, relocation, and acquisition.
######## Definition of project.

(h)For purposes of this section, the term "project" means an undertaking paid for in whole or in part with assistance under this part. Two or more activities that are integrally related, each essential to the others, are considered a single project, whether or not all component activities receive assistance under this part.
