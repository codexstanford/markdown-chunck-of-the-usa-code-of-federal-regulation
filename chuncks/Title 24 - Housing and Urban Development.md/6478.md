
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

###### Subpart A - Eligibility Requirements - Individually Owned Units
####### ยง 234.3 Definitions.
######## Bona fide tenants' organization

means an association of tenants formed by the tenants to promote their interests in a particular project, with membership in the association open to each tenant, and all requirements of the association applying equally to every tenant.
