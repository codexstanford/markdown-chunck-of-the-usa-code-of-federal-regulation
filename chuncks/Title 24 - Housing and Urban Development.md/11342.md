####### Appeals.

(e)A person who disagrees with the owner's determination concerning whether the person qualifies as a "displaced person," or the amount of relocation assistance for which the person is found to be eligible, may file a written appeal of that determination with the owner. A low-income person who is dissatisfied with the owner's determination on such appeal may submit a written request for review of that determination to the HUD Field Office.
