
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 3285.5 Definitions.
####### Design Approval Primary Inspection Agency (DAPIA).

A state or private organization that has been accepted by the Secretary in accordance with the requirements of Part 3282, Subpart H of this chapter, which evaluates and approves or disapproves manufactured home designs and quality control procedures.
