
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Housing and Urban Development
### PART 30 - CIVIL MONEY PENALTIES: CERTAIN PROHIBITED CONDUCT
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 30.10 Definitions.
###### Dealer.

A seller, contractor or supplier of goods or services having a direct or indirect financial interest in the transaction between the borrower and the lender, and who assists the borrower in preparing the credit application or otherwise assists the borrower in obtaining the loan from the lender.
