
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 3286 - MANUFACTURED HOME INSTALLATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart C - Installer Licensing in HUD-Administered States
###### ยง 3286.207 Process for obtaining installation license.
####### Proof of experience.

(b)Every applicant for an initial installation license must submit verification of the experience required in ยง 3286.205(a). This verification may be in the form of statements by past or present employers or a self-certification that the applicant meets those experience requirements, but HUD may contact the applicant for additional verification at any time. The applicant must also provide to HUD employment information relevant to the applicant's experience as an installer, including the dates and type of such employment. An installer who is certified or licensed to perform manufactured home installations in a state with a qualifying installation program may seek an exemption from the experience requirement by submitting proof of such certification or license.
