##### Subpart B - HUD Manufactured Home Dispute Resolution Program in HUD-Administered States

####### Effect on nonparticipating parties.

(g)If a party chooses not to participate in the arbitration, the process will continue without further input from that party. In such a case, the arbitrator may rely on the record developed through the arbitration to find a nonparticipating party responsible for correction or repair of a defect.
