
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter IX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Public and Indian Housing, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 906 - PUBLIC HOUSING HOMEOWNERSHIP PROGRAMS
##### Subpart C - Purchaser Requirements
###### ยง 906.19 Requirements applicable to a purchase and resale entity (PRE).
####### In general.

(a)In the case of a purchase of units for resale to low-income families by a PRE, the PHA must have an approved homeownership program that describes the use of a PRE to sell the units to low-income families within 5 years from the date of the PRE's acquisition of the units.
