
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

####### ยง 576.2 Definitions.
######## Metropolitan city

means a city that was classified as a metropolitan city under 42 U.S.C. 5302(a) for the fiscal year immediately preceding the fiscal year for which ESG funds are made available. This term includes the District of Columbia.
