
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

##### Subpart C - Financial Condition Indicator
###### ยง 902.35 Financial condition scoring and thresholds.
####### Months Expendable Net Assets Ratio (MENAR).

(2)The MENAR measures a project's ability to operate using its net available, unrestricted resources without relying on additional funding. This ratio compares the adjusted net available unrestricted resources to the average monthly operating expenses. The result of this calculation shows how many months of operating expenses can be covered with currently available, unrestricted resources.
