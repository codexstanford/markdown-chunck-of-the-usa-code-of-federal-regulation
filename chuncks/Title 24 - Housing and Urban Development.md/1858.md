
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

#### Subpart J - Rehabilitation
##### ยง 35.900 Purpose and applicability.
###### Purpose and applicability.

(a)(1) The purpose of this subpart J is to establish procedures to eliminate as far as practicable lead-based paint hazards in a residential property that receives Federal rehabilitation assistance under a program administered by HUD. Rehabilitation assistance does not include project-based rental assistance, rehabilitation mortgage insurance or assistance to public housing.
