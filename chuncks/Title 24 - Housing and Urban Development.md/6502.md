###### Subpart B - Contract Rights and Obligations - Individually Owned Units

(b)For the purposes of this subpart, all references in §§ 203.251 through 203.436 of this chapter (part 203, subpart B) to section 203 of the Act, one- to four-family, and the Mutual Mortgage Insurance Fund, shall be construed to refer to section 234 of the act, one-family unit, and the General Insurance Fund. The termoras used in §§ 203.251 through 203.436 of this chapter (part 203, subpart B) shall be construed to include "the one-family unit and the undivided interest in the common areas and facilities as may be designated".
