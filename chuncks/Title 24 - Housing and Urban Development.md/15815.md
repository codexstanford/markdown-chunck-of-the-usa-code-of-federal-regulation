
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development

##### Subpart G - Plumbing Systems
###### ยง 3280.610 Drainage systems.
####### Preassembly of drain lines.

(5)Section(s) of the drain system, designed to be located underneath the manufactured home or between stories of the manufactured home, are not required to be factory installed when the manufacturer designs the system for site assembly and also provides all materials and components, including piping, fittings, cement, supports, and instructions necessary for proper site installation.
