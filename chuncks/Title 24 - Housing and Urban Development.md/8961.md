
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

####### ยง 570.606 Displacement, relocation, acquisition, and replacement of housing.
######## General policy for minimizing displacement.

(a)Consistent with the other goals and objectives of this part, grantees (or States or state recipients, as applicable) shall assure that they have taken all reasonable steps to minimize the displacement of persons (families, individuals, businesses, nonprofit organizations, and farms) as a result of activities assisted under this part.
