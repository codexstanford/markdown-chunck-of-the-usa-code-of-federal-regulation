
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Housing and Urban Development
### PART 5 - GENERAL HUD PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS; WAIVERS
#### Subpart A - Generally Applicable Definitions and Requirements; Waivers
##### Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing
###### ยง 5.151 Affirmatively Further Fair Housing: Definitions.
####### Meaningful actions

means significant actions that are designed and can be reasonably expected to achieve a material positive change that affirmatively furthers fair housing by, for example, increasing fair housing choice or decreasing disparities in access to opportunity.
