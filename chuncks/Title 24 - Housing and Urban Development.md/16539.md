
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 3285 - MODEL MANUFACTURED HOME INSTALLATION STANDARDS
##### Subpart G - Ductwork and Plumbing and Fuel Supply Systems
###### ยง 3285.605 Fuel supply system.
####### Proper supply pressure.

(a)The gas piping system in the home is designed for a pressure that is at least 7 inches of water column [4oz./in.or 0.25 psi] and not more than 14 inches of water column [8 oz./in.or 0.5 psi]. If gas from any supply source exceeds, or could exceed this pressure, a regulator must be installed if required by the LAHJ.
