
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

### Chapter II - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development

###### Subpart B - Contract Rights and Obligations
####### Termination of Insurance Contract
######## ยง 203.318 Notice of termination by mortgagee.

No contract of insurance shall be terminated until the mortgagee has given written notice thereof to the Commissioner within 15 calendar days from the occurrence of one of the approved methods of termination set forth in this subpart.
