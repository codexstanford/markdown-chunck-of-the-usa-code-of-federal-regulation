
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

###### ยง 960.205 Drug use by applicants: Obtaining information from drug treatment facility.
####### Policy A-Request for all families.

(i)Under Policy A, the PHA must submit a request for information to a drug abuse treatment facility in accordance with paragraph (d) of this section before admitting any family to the PHA's public housing program.
