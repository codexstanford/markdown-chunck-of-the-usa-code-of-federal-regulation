######## ยง 266.634 Reinstatement of the contract of insurance.
######### Conditions for reinstatement.

(a)After the initial claim payment, HUD may reinstate the contract of insurance on the following conditions:

(1) The HFA has not acquired the project;

(2) The mortgagor has cured the default; and

(3) The HFA requests that HUD reinstate the contract of insurance.
