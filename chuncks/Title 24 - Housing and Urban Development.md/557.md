###### ยง 5.615 Public housing program and Section 8 tenant-based assistance program: How welfare benefit reduction affects family income.
####### Covered families.

Families who receive welfare assistance or other public assistance benefits ("welfare benefits") from a State or other public agency ("welfare agency") under a program for which Federal, State, or local law requires that a member of the family must participate in an economic self-sufficiency program as a condition for such assistance.
