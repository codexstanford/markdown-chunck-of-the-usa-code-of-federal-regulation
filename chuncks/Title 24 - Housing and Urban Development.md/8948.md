
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

###### Subpart J - Grant Administration
####### ยง 570.513 Lump sum drawdown for financing of property rehabilitation activities.
######## Rehabilitation loans made with non-CDBG funds.

(8)If the deposited funds or program income derived from deposited funds are used to subsidize or guarantee repayment of rehabilitation loans made with non-CDBG funds, or to provide a supplemental loan or grant to the borrower of the non-CDBG funds, the rehabilitation activities are considered to be CDBG-assisted activities subject to the requirements applicable to such activities, except that repayment of non-CDBG funds shall not be treated as program income.
