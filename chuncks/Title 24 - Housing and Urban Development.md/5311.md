######## ยง 203.49 Eligibility of adjustable rate mortgages.
######### Types of mortgages insurable.

(a)The types of adjustable rate mortgages that are insurable are those for which the interest rate may be adjusted annually by the mortgagee, beginning after one, three, five, seven, or ten years from the date of the mortgagor's first debt service payment.
