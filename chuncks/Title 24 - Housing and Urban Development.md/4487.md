##### PART 200 - INTRODUCTION TO FHA PROGRAMS
###### Subpart G - Appraiser Roster
####### ยง 200.204 What actions may HUD take against unsatisfactory appraisers on the Appraiser Roster?
######## Reinstatement.

(b)If an appraiser who has been removed from the Roster wants to be reinstated on the Roster, the appraiser must follow the procedures and requirements contained in this subpart for placement on the Roster. Before an appraiser is eligible to reapply for placement on the Roster, the appraiser shall comply with the terms of any applicable remedial training education requirements, and the time period for the appraiser's removal from the Roster shall have expired.
