###### Subpart A - Eligibility Requirements - Individually Owned Units
####### ยง 234.17 Mortgagor and mortgagee requirements for maintaining flood insurance coverage.

The maintenance of flood insurance coverage on the project by the condominium association will satisfy the requirements of ยง 203.16a of this chapter if such coverage protects the interest of the mortgagor in the family unit.
