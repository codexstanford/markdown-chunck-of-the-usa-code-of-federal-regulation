
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Housing and Urban Development
### PART 15 - PUBLIC ACCESS TO HUD RECORDS UNDER THE FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT AND TESTIMONY AND PRODUCTION OF INFORMATION BY HUD EMPLOYEES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 15.2 Definitions.
###### Agency record

means any documentary material that is either created or obtained by an agency in the transaction of agency business and is under agency control. "Agency record" does not include records that are not already in existence and which would have to be created specifically to meet a request.
