##### Subpart H - Section 8 Moderate Rehabilitation Single Room Occupancy Program for Homeless Individuals
###### ยง 882.802 Definitions.
####### Covered housing provider.

For the Section 8 Moderate Rehabilitation Single Room Occupancy Program for Homeless Individuals, "covered housing provider," as such term is used in HUD's regulations in 24 CFR part 5, subpart L (Protection for Victims of Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Sexual Assault, or Stalking), refers to the owner.
