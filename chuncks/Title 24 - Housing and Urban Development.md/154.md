
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle A - Office of the Secretary, Department of Housing and Urban Development
### PART 4 - HUD REFORM ACT
#### Subpart A - Accountability in the Provision of HUD Assistance
##### ยง 4.3 Definitions.
###### Interested party

means any person involved in the application for assistance, or in the planning, development or implementation of the project or activity for which assistance is sought and any other person who has a pecuniary interest exceeding the lower of $50,000 or 10 percent in the project or activity for which assistance is sought.
