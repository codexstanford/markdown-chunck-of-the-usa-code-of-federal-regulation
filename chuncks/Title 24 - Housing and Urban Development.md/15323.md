
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XII - Office of Inspector General, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 2003 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE PRIVACY ACT OF 1974
##### § 2003.2 Definitions.
###### Department

means the OIG, except that in the context of §§ 16.1(d); 16.11(b) (1), (3), and (4); and 16.12(e), when those sections are incorporated by reference, the term means the Department of Housing and Urban Development.
