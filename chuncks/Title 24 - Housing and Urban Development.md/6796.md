###### Subpart A - General Eligibility Requirements
####### ยง 242.6 Property requirements.

The mortgage, to be eligible for insurance, shall be on property located in a state, as defined in ยง 242.1. The mortgage shall cover real estate in which the mortgagor has one of the following interests:
