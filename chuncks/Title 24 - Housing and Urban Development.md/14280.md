
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 984.103 Definitions.
####### Other services.

(ix)Any other services and resources, including case management, optional services, and specialized services for individuals with disabilities, that are determined to be appropriate in assisting FSS families to achieve economic independence and self-sufficiency. Reasonable accommodations and modifications must be made for individuals with disabilities consistent with applicable Federal civil rights and nondiscrimination laws.
