
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development

##### PART 570 - COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT BLOCK GRANTS
###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 570.3 Definitions.
######## Low-income person

means a member of a family that has an income equal to or less than the Section 8 very low-income limit established by HUD. Unrelated individuals shall be considered as one-person families for this purpose.
