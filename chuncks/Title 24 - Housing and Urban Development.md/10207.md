###### Subpart B - Eligibility Requirements for Nomination of Renewal Communities
####### ยง 599.103 Geographic and population requirements for a nominated area.
######## Population requirements,In general.

(b)-(1)Except as provided in paragraph (b)(2) of this section, a nominated area must have a population of not more than 200,000 and at least:

(i) 4,000 if any portion of the area (other than a nominated rural area) is located within an MA which has a population of 50,000 or greater; or
