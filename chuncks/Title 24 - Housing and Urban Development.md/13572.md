##### Subpart E - Admission to Tenant-Based Program
###### ยง 982.202 How applicants are selected: General requirements.
####### Admission policy.

(d)The PHA must admit applicants for participation in accordance with HUD regulations and other requirements, including, but not limited to, 24 CFR part 5, subpart L (Protection for Victims of Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Sexual Assault, or Stalking), and with PHA policies stated in the PHA administrative plan and the PHA plan.
