###### Subpart A - General Provisions
####### ยง 578.3 Definitions.
######## Victim service provider

means a private nonprofit organization whose primary mission is to provide services to victims of domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, or stalking. This term includes rape crisis centers, battered women's shelters, domestic violence transitional housing programs, and other programs.
