####### ยง 291.307 Servicing requirements.
######## Use of HUD-approved servicing mortgagees.

(a)All mortgage loans must be serviced by HUD-approved servicing mortgagees for the remaining life of the mortgage loans. A purchaser that is not a HUD-approved servicing mortgagee must retain a HUD-approved servicing mortgagee to service the mortgage loans.
