###### ยง 5.356 Housing programs: Pet rule violation procedures.
####### Initiation of procedures to remove a pet or terminate the pet owner's tenancy.

(c)(1) The project owner may not initiate procedures to terminate a pet owner's tenancy based on a pet rule violation, unless:

(i) The pet owner has failed to remove the pet or correct a pet rule violation within the applicable time period specified in this section (including any additional time permitted by the owner); and

(ii) The pet rule violation is sufficient to begin procedures to terminate the pet owner's tenancy under the terms of the lease and applicable regulations.

(2) The project owner may initiate procedures to remove a pet under ยง 5.327 at any time, in accordance with the provisions of applicable State or local law.
