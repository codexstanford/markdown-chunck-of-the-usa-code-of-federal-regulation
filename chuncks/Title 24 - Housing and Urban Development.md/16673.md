
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter XX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Housing - Federal Housing Commissioner, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 3286 - MANUFACTURED HOME INSTALLATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart D - Training of Installers in HUD-Administered States
###### ยง 3286.303 Responsibilities of qualified trainers.
####### Attendance records.

(b)Qualified trainers must maintain records of the times, locations, names of attendees at each session, and content of all courses offered. When an attendee misses a significant portion of any training session, the trainer must assure that the attendee makes up the missed portion of the instruction.
