
# Title 24 - Housing and Urban Development
## Subtitle B - Regulations Relating to Housing and Urban Development
### Chapter IX - Office of Assistant Secretary for Public and Indian Housing, Department of Housing and Urban Development
#### PART 906 - PUBLIC HOUSING HOMEOWNERSHIP PROGRAMS
##### Subpart C - Purchaser Requirements
###### ยง 906.19 Requirements applicable to a purchase and resale entity (PRE).
####### Resale within five years.

(d)A PRE must agree that, with respect to any units it acquires under a homeownership program under this part, it will transfer ownership to the PHA if the PRE fails to resell the unit to a low-income family within 5 years of the PRE's acquisition of the unit.
