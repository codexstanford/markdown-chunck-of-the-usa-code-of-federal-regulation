
# Title 1 - General Provisions
## Chapter VI - National Capital Planning Commission
### PART 601 - IMPLEMENTATION OF THE NATIONAL ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY ACT
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 601.3 Definitions.
###### National Capital Planning Act,et seq.

means the July 1952 legislative enactment, codified at 40 U.S.C. 8701that created the present day National Capital Planning Commission and conferred authority upon it to serve as the planning authority for the Federal government in the National Capital Region.
