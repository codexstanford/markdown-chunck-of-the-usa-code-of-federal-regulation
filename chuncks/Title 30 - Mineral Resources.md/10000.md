###### ยง 1218.103 Payments to States.

(a) Any amount that is payable by ONRR to a State but is not paid on the due date, as specified in ยง 1219.100 of this chapter, or that is held in a suspense account pending resolution of a dispute as specified in ยง 1219.101 of this chapter, shall accrue interest payable to the State.
