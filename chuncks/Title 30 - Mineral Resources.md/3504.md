
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter O - Coal Mine Safety and Health
#### PART 75 - MANDATORY SAFETY STANDARDS - UNDERGROUND COAL MINES
##### Subpart T - Diesel-Powered Equipment
###### ยง 75.1900 Definitions.
####### Permanent underground diesel fuel storage facility.

A facility designed and constructed to remain at one location for the storage or dispensing of diesel fuel, which does not move as mining progresses.
