######## What are the requirements for nearby vessels?

(9)If vessels are stationed overnight alongside facilities in areas of HS present or HS unknown, you must equip vessels with an HS-detection system that activates audible and visual alarms when the concentration of HS in the atmosphere reaches 20 ppm. This requirement does not apply to vessels positioned upwind and at a safe distance from the facility in accordance with the positioning procedure described in the approved HS Contingency Plan.
