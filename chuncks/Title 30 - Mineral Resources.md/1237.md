
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter K - Metal and Nonmetal Mine Safety and Health
#### PART 56 - SAFETY AND HEALTH STANDARDS - SURFACE METAL AND NONMETAL MINES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 56.2 Definitions.
####### Combustible liquids

means liquids having a flash point at or above 100 &#xB0;F (37.8 &#xB0;C). They are divided into the following classes:

(1) Class II liquids-those having flash points at or above 100 &#xB0;F (37.8 &#xB0;C) and below 140 &#xB0;F (60 &#xB0;C).

(2) Class IIIA liquids-those having flash points at or above 140 &#xB0;F (60 &#xB0;C) and below 200 &#xB0;F (93.4 &#xB0;C).

(3) Class IIIB liquids-those having flash points at or above 200 &#xB0;F (93.4 &#xB0;C).
