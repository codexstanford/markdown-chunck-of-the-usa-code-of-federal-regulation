###### ยง 50.20-3 Criteria - Differences between medical treatment and first aid.
####### Cuts and Lacerations.

(4)(i) First aid treatment is the same as for abrasions except the application of butterfly closures for cosmetic purposes only can be considered first aid.

(ii) Medical treatment includes the application of butterfly closures for non-cosmetic purposes, sutures, (stitches), surgical debridement, treatment of infection, or other professional treatment.
