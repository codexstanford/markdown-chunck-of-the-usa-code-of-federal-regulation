
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter B - Testing, Evaluation, and Approval of Mining Products
#### PART 14 - REQUIREMENTS FOR THE APPROVAL OF FLAME-RESISTANT CONVEYOR BELTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 14.2 Definitions.
####### Extension of approval.

A document issued by MSHA, which states that a change to a product previously approved by MSHA meets the requirements of this Part and which authorizes the continued use of the approval marking after the appropriate extension number has been added.
