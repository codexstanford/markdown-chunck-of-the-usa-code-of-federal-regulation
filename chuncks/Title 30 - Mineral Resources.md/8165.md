##### ยง 817.67 Use of explosives: Control of adverse effects.
###### Monitoring.

(2)(i) The operator shall conduct periodic monitoring to ensure compliance with the airblast standards. The regulatory authority may require airblast measurement of any or all blasts and may specify the locations at which such measurements are taken.
