###### Procedure that BOEM follows to disclose acquired data and information to a contractor for reproduction, processing, and interpretation.

(c)(1) When practical, the Regional Director will advise the person who submitted data and information under ยง 551.11 or ยง 551.12 of the intent to disclose the data or information to an independent contractor or agent.
