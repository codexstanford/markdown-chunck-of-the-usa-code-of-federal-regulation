
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter T - Programs for the Conduct of Surface Mining Operations Within Each State
#### PART 910 - GEORGIA
##### ยง 910.780 Surface mining permit applications - minimum requirements for reclamation and operation plan.
###### Surface Mining Permit Applications-Minimum Requirement for Reclamation and Operation Plan,

(a) Part 780 of this chapter,shall apply to any person who makes application to conduct surface coal mining and reclamation operations.

(b) The application for a permit shall also demonstrate compliance with the air quality control standards in Chapter 391-3-1 of the Rules and Regulations for Air Quality Control of the Georgia Department of Natural Resources.
