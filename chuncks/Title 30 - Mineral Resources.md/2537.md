
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter K - Metal and Nonmetal Mine Safety and Health
#### PART 58 - HEALTH STANDARDS FOR METAL AND NONMETAL MINES
##### Subpart E - Miscellaneous
###### ยง 58.610 Abrasive blasting.
####### Surface and underground mines.

(a)When an abrasive blasting operation is performed, all exposed miners shall use in accordance with 30 CFR 56.5005 or 57.5005 respirators approved for abrasive blasting by NIOSH under 42 CFR part 84, or the operation shall be performed in a totally enclosed device with the miner outside the device.
