
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter M - Uniform Mine Health Regulations
#### PART 62 - OCCUPATIONAL NOISE EXPOSURE
##### ยง 62.101 Definitions.
###### Action level.

An 8-hour time-weighted average sound level (TWA) of 85 dBA, or equivalently a dose of 50%, integrating all sound levels from 80 dBA to at least 130 dBA.
