###### Lignite coal

means consolidated lignite coal having less than 8,300 British thermal units per pound, moist and mineral-matter-free. Moist, mineral-matter free British thermal units per pound are determined by Parr's formula, equation 3, on page 222 of "Standard Specification for Classification of Coals by Rank," in American Society for Testing and Materials ASTM D 388-77 (Philadelphia, 1977).
