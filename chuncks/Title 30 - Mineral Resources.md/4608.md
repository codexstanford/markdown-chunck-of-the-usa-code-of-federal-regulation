-the amount of heat needed to raise the temperature of one pound of water from 59.5 degrees Fahrenheit (59.5 &#xB0;F) to 60.5 degrees Fahrenheit (60.5 &#xB0;F) at standard pressure base (14.73 pounds per square inch absolute (psia)).
