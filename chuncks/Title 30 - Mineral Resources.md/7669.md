
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter G - Surface Coal Mining and Reclamation Operations Permits and Coal Exploration Systems Under Regulatory Programs
#### PART 773 - REQUIREMENTS FOR PERMITS AND PERMIT PROCESSING
##### ยง 773.5 Regulatory coordination with requirements under other laws.
###### et seq.,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.,et seq.

Each regulatory program shall, to avoid duplication, provide for the coordination of review and issuance of permits for surface coal mining and reclamation operations with applicable requirements of the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (16 U.S.C. 1531); the Fish and Wildlife Coordination Act, as amended (16 U.S.C. 661); the Migratory Bird Treaty Act of 1918, as amended (16 U.S.C. 703); The National Historic Preservation Act of 1966, as amended (16 U.S.C. 470); the Bald Eagle Protection Act, as amended (16 U.S.C. 668a); for Federal programs only, the Archeological and Historic Preservation Act of 1974 (16 U.S.C. 469); and the Archaeological Resources Protection Act of 1979 (16 U.S.C. 470aa) where Federal and Indian lands covered by that Act are involved.
