
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter B - Testing, Evaluation, and Approval of Mining Products
#### PART 19 - ELECTRIC CAP LAMPS
##### ยง 19.6 Specific requirements for approval.
###### Light distribution, photometric.

(d)(1) Excepting special headpieces for inspection purposes, the maximum candlepower of the light beam shall not be greater than 25 times the average or mean candlepower of the beam.

(2) The minimum candlepower of the beam based upon readings at the design voltage of the bulb shall not be less than 1.
