
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 706 - RESTRICTION ON FINANCIAL INTERESTS OF FEDERAL EMPLOYEES
##### ยง 706.3 Definitions.
###### Employee.

Means any person employed by the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement within the U.S. Department of the Interior and any other person employed by the Federal Government who performs functions or duties under the Act without regard to the duration or nature of his or her appointment.
