
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter R - Abandoned Mine Land Reclamation
#### PART 884 - STATE RECLAMATION PLANS
##### § 884.13 Content of proposed State reclamation plan.
###### Additional requirement applicable to certified States and Indian tribes.

(b)If you are a certified State or Indian tribe, the plan must include a commitment to address eligible coal problems found or occurring after certification as required in §§ 875.13(a)(3) and 875.14(b) of this chapter.
