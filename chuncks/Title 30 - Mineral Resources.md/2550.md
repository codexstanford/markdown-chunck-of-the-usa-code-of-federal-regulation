
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter M - Uniform Mine Health Regulations
#### PART 62 - OCCUPATIONAL NOISE EXPOSURE
##### ยง 62.101 Definitions.
###### Hearing protector.

Any device or material, capable of being worn on the head or in the ear canal, sold wholly or in part on the basis of its ability to reduce the level of sound entering the ear, and which has a scientifically accepted indicator of noise reduction value.
