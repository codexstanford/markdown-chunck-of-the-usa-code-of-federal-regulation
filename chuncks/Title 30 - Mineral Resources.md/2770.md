
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter O - Coal Mine Safety and Health
#### PART 74 - COAL MINE DUST SAMPLING DEVICES
##### Subpart B - Approval Requirements for Coal Mine Dust Personal Sampler Unit
###### ยง 74.4 Specifications of sampler unit.
####### Hose.

(5)A 3-foot (91 centimeter) long,-inch (0.64 centimeters) (inside diameter) clear plastic hose shall be provided to form an airtight connection between the inlet of the sampler pump and the outlet of the filter assembly. A device, capable of sliding along the hose and attaching to the miner's outer garment, shall be provided.
