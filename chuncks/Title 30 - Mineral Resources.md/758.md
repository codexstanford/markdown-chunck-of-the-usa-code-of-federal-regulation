###### ยง 35.20 Autogenous-ignition temperature test.
####### Purpose.

(a)The purpose of this test, referred to hereinafter as the ignition-temperature test, is to determine the lowest autogenous-ignition temperature of a hydraulic fluid at atmospheric pressure when using the syringe-injection method.
