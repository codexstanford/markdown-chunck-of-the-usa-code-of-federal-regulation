
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources

### Subchapter K - Metal and Nonmetal Mine Safety and Health

##### Subpart C - Fire Prevention and Control
###### ยง 56.4000 Definitions.
####### Safety can.

A container of not over five gallons capacity that is designed to safely relieve internal pressure when exposed to heat and has a spring-closing lid and spout cover.
