
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter XII - Office of Natural Resources Revenue, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter B - Appeals
#### PART 1290 - APPEAL PROCEDURES
##### ยง 1290.102 What definitions apply to this part?
###### Indian lessor

means an Indian tribe or individual Indian mineral owner with a beneficial or restricted interest in a property that is subject to a lease issued or administered by the Secretary on behalf of the tribe or individual Indian mineral owner.
