
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources

### Subchapter A - Natural Resources Revenue
#### PART 1206 - PRODUCT VALUATION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions and Definitions
###### ยง 1206.20 What definitions apply to this part?
####### Indian Tribe

means any Indian Tribe, band, nation, pueblo, community, rancheria, colony, or other group of Indians for which any minerals or interest in minerals is held in trust by the United States or is subject to Federal restriction against alienation.
