
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter T - Programs for the Conduct of Surface Mining Operations Within Each State

###### Exemption for Coal Extraction Incidental to the Extraction of Other Minerals,

Part 702 of this chapter,shall apply to any person who conducts coal extraction incidental to the extraction of other minerals for purposes of commercial use or sale.
