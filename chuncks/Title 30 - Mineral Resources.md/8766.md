
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter VII - Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter T - Programs for the Conduct of Surface Mining Operations Within Each State

##### ยง 910.784 Underground mining permit applications - minimum requirements for reclamation and operation plan.
###### Underground Mining Permit Applications-Minimum Requirements for Reclamation and Operation Plan,

(a) Part 784 of this chapter,shall apply to any person who makes application to conduct underground mining.

(b) The applicant for a permit to conduct underground mining operations shall demonstrate compliance with Chapter 391-3-1 of the Rules and Regulations of the Georgia Department of Natural Resources.
