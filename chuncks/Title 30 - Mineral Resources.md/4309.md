
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter II - Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement, Department of the Interior
### Subchapter B - Offshore
#### PART 250 - OIL AND GAS AND SULPHUR OPERATIONS IN THE OUTER CONTINENTAL SHELF
##### Subpart D - Oil and Gas Drilling Operations
###### Hydrogen Sulfide
####### ยง 250.490 Hydrogen sulfide.
######## When do I need to display operational danger signs, display flags, or activate visual or audible alarms?

(2)

(i) You must display warning signs at all times on facilities with wells capable of producing HS and on facilities that process gas containing HS in concentrations of 20 ppm or more.

(ii) In addition to the signs, you must activate audible alarms and display flags or activate flashing red lights when atmospheric concentration of HS reaches 20 ppm.
