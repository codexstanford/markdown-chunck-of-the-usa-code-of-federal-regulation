(a) Each piece of nonpermissible heavy-duty diesel-powered equipment (as defined by ยง 75.1908(a) of this part), generator or compressor introduced into an underground area of an underground coal mine after May 21, 2001 must emit no more than 5.0 grams per hour of diesel particulate matter.
