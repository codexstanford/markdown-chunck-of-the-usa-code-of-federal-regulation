### Subchapter B - Initial Program Regulations
#### PART 710 - INITIAL REGULATORY PROGRAM
##### ยง 710.5 Definitions.
###### Flood irrigation

means irrigation through natural overflow or the temporary diversion of high flows in which the entire surface of the soil is covered by a sheet of water.
