
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources

### Subchapter K - Metal and Nonmetal Mine Safety and Health

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 56.2 Definitions.
####### Secondary safety connection

means a second connection between a conveyance and rope, intended to prevent the conveyance from running away or falling in the event the primary connection fails.
