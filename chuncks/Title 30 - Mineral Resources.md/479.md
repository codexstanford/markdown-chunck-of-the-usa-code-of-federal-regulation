
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter B - Testing, Evaluation, and Approval of Mining Products
#### PART 18 - ELECTRIC MOTOR-DRIVEN MINE EQUIPMENT AND ACCESSORIES
##### Subpart C - Inspections and Tests
###### ยง 18.66 Tests of windows and lenses.
####### Thermal-shock tests.

(b)Four samples of the window or lens will be heated in an oven for 15 minutes to a temperature of 150 &#xB0;C. (302 &#xB0;F.) and immediately upon withdrawal of the samples from the oven they will be immersed in water having a temperature between 15 &#xB0;C. (59 &#xB0;F) and 20 &#xB0;C. (68 &#xB0;F.). Three of the four samples shall show no defect or breakage from this thermal-shock test.
