
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources

###### Permanent Program Performance Standards-Coal Preparation Plants Not Located Within the Permit Area of a Mine,

Part 827 of this chapter,shall apply to any person who conducts surface coal mining and reclamation operations which include the operation of a coal preparation plant not located within the permit area of a mine.
