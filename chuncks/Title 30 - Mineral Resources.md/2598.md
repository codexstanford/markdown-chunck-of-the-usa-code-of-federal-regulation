
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources
## Chapter I - Mine Safety and Health Administration, Department of Labor
### Subchapter O - Coal Mine Safety and Health
#### PART 70 - MANDATORY HEALTH STANDARDS - UNDERGROUND COAL MINES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 70.2 Definitions.
####### Production shift.

With regard to an MMU, a shift during which material is produced; with regard to a DA of a mine, a shift during which material is produced and routine day-to-day activities are occurring in the DA.
