######## What are the requirements for nearby facilities?

(10)The District Manager may require you to equip nearby facilities with portable or fixed HS detector(s) and to test and calibrate those detectors. To invoke this requirement, the District Manager will consider dispersion modeling results from a possible release to determine if 20 ppm HS concentration levels could be exceeded at nearby facilities.
