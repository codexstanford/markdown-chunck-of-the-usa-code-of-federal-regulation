
# Title 30 - Mineral Resources

#### PART 872 - MONEYS AVAILABLE TO ELIGIBLE STATES AND INDIAN TRIBES
##### ยง 872.5 Definitions.
###### Allocate

means to identify moneys in our records at the time they are received by the Fund. The allocation process identifies moneys in the Fund by the type of funds collected, including the specific State or Indian tribal share.
