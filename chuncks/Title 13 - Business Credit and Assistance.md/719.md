
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 107 - SMALL BUSINESS INVESTMENT COMPANIES
#### Subpart J - Licensee's Noncompliance With Terms of Leverage
##### Computation of Licensee's Capital Impairment
###### ยง 107.1830 Licensee's Capital Impairment - definition and general requirements.
####### Applicability of this section.

(a)This section applies to Leverage issued on or after April 25, 1994. For Leverage issued before April 25, 1994, you must comply with paragraphs (e) and (f) of this section and the Capital Impairment regulations in this part in effect when you issued your Leverage. For all Leverage issued, you must also comply with any contractual provisions to which you have agreed.
