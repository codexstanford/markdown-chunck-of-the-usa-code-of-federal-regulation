
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 142 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES ACT REGULATIONS
#### Hearing Provisions
##### ยง 142.28 Where is the hearing held?

The ALJ will hold the hearing in any judicial district of the United States:

(a) In which the defendant resides or transacts business; or

(b) In which the claim or statement on which liability is based was made, presented or submitted to SBA; or

(c) As agreed upon by the defendant and the ALJ.
