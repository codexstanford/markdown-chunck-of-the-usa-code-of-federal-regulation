
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance

####### 7(a) Loans,et seq.,et seq.

(a)-All references to 7(a) loans under this subpart include loans made under section 7(a) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 631) and loans made under section 502 of the Small Business Investment Act (15 U.S.C. 661), both of which may be securitized under this subpart.
