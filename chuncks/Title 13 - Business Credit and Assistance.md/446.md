
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance

####### Reporting of economic impact information on Form 468.

(d)Your annual filing of SBA Form 468 must include an assessment of the economic impact of each Financing, specifying the full-time equivalent jobs created or retained, and the impact of the Financing on the revenues and profits of the business and on taxes paid by the business and its employees.
