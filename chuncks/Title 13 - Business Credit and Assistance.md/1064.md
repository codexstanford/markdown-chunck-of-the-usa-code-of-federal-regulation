
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 108 - NEW MARKETS VENTURE CAPITAL ("NMVC") PROGRAM
#### Subpart J - SBA Financial Assistance for NMVC Companies (Leverage)
##### Funding Leverage by use of SBA Guaranteed Trust Certificates ("TCs")
###### ยง 108.1600 SBA authority to issue and guarantee Trust Certificates.
####### Authorization.

(a)Section 356 of the Act authorizes SBA to issue TCs and to guarantee the timely payment of the principal and interest thereon. Any guarantee by SBA of such TC is limited to the principal and interest due on the Debentures in any Trust or Pool backing such TC. The full faith and credit of the United States is pledged to the payment of all amounts due under the guarantee of any TC.
