
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 107 - SMALL BUSINESS INVESTMENT COMPANIES
#### Subpart F - Recordkeeping, Reporting, and Examination Requirements for Licensees
##### Recordkeeping Requirements for Licensees
###### ยง 107.620 Requirements to obtain information from Portfolio Concerns.
####### Information for initial Financing decision.

(a)Before extending any Financing, you must require the applicant to submit such financial statements, plans of operation (including intended use of financing proceeds), cash flow analyses and projections as are necessary to support your investment decision. The information submitted must be consistent with the size and type of the business and the amount of the proposed Financing.
