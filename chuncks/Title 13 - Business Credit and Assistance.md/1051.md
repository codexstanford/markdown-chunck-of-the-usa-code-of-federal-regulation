
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 108 - NEW MARKETS VENTURE CAPITAL ("NMVC") PROGRAM
#### Subpart J - SBA Financial Assistance for NMVC Companies (Leverage)
##### Conditional Commitments by SBA To Reserve Leverage for a NMVC Company
###### § 108.1200 SBA's Leverage commitment to a NMVC Company - application procedure, amount, and term.
####### General.

(a)Under the provisions in §§ 108.1200 through 108.1240, you may apply for SBA's conditional commitment to reserve a specific amount and type of Leverage for your future use. You may then apply to draw down Leverage against the commitment.
