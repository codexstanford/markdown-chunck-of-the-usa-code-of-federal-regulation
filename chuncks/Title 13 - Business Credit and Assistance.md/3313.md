### PART 126 - HUBZONE PROGRAM
#### Subpart G - Contract Performance Requirements
##### ยง 126.702 How can the subcontracting percentage requirements be changed?

SBA may change the required subcontracting percentage for a specific industry if the Administrator determines that such action is necessary to reflect conventional industry practices among SBCs that are below the numerical size standard for businesses in that industry group.
