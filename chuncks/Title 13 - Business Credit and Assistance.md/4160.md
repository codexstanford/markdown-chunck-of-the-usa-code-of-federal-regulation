
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 134 - RULES OF PROCEDURE GOVERNING CASES BEFORE THE OFFICE OF HEARINGS AND APPEALS
#### Subpart L - Borrower Appeals of Final SBA Loan Review Decisions
##### ยง 134.1203 Standing.

Only the borrower on a loan, or its legal successor in interest, for which SBA has issued a final SBA loan review decision that makes a finding in ยง 134.1201(b)(1) through (4) has standing to appeal the final SBA loan review decision to OHA.
