###### General.

(a)To be certified by SBA as a HUBZone small business concern, a concern must submit a completed application and all documents requested by SBA. The concern must also represent to SBA that it meets the requirements set forth in ยง 126.200 and that all of the information provided as of the date of the application (and any subsequent information provided) is complete, true and accurate.
