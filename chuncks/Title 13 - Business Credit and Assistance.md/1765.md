
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 120 - BUSINESS LOANS
#### Subpart C - Special Purpose Loans
##### America's Recovery Capital (Business Stabilization) Loan Program - ARC Loan Program
###### ยง 120.398 America's Recovery Capital (ARC) Loan Program.
####### Repayment period.

(iv)The borrower will be required to pay the loan principal over five years beginning in the 13th month following the final loan disbursement. The ARC Loan balance will be fully amortized over the five year repayment period. Balloon payments may not be required by lenders. The borrower may prepay all or a portion of the principal during the life of the loan without penalty.
