
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration

#### Subpart J - SBA Financial Assistance for NMVC Companies (Leverage)

###### ยง 108.1150 Maximum amount of Leverage for a NMVC Company.

The face amount of a NMVC Company's outstanding Debentures may not exceed 150 percent of its Leverageable Capital.
