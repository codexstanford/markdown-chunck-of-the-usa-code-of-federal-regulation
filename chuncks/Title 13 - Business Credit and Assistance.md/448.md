
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 107 - SMALL BUSINESS INVESTMENT COMPANIES
#### Subpart F - Recordkeeping, Reporting, and Examination Requirements for Licensees
##### Reporting Requirements for Licensees
###### ยง 107.650 Requirement to report portfolio valuations to SBA.

You must determine the value of your Loans and Investments in accordance with ยง 107.503. You must report such valuations to SBA within 90 days of the end of the fiscal year in the case of annual valuations, and within 30 days following the close of other reporting periods. You must report material adverse changes in valuations at least quarterly, within thirty days following the close of the quarter.
