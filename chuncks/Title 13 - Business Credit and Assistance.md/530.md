
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 107 - SMALL BUSINESS INVESTMENT COMPANIES
#### Subpart G - Financing of Small Businesses by Licensees
##### Structuring Licensee's Financing of an Eligible Small Business: Terms and Conditions of Financing
###### ยง 107.855 Interest rate ceiling and limitations on fees charged to Small Businesses ("Cost of Money").
####### When to determine the Cost of Money ceiling for a Financing.

(b)You may determine your Cost of Money ceiling for a particular Financing as of the date you issue a Commitment or as of the date of the first closing of the Financing. Once determined, the Cost of Money ceiling remains fixed for the duration of the Financing.
