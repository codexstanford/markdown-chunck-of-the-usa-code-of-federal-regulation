
# Title 13 - Business Credit and Assistance
## Chapter I - Small Business Administration
### PART 108 - NEW MARKETS VENTURE CAPITAL ("NMVC") PROGRAM
#### Subpart H - Recordkeeping, Reporting, and Examination Requirements for NMVC Companies
##### Reporting Requirements for NMVC Companies
###### ยง 108.680 Reporting changes in NMVC Company not subject to prior SBA approval.
####### Changes to be reported for post-approval.

(a)This section applies to any changes in your Articles, ownership, capitalization, management, operating area, or investment policies that do not require SBA's prior approval. You must report such changes to SBA within 30 days for post approval.
