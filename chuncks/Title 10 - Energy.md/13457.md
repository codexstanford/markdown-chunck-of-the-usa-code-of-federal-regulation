
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter H - Assistance Regulations
#### PART 600 - FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE RULES
##### Subpart C - Uniform Administrative Requirements for Grants and Cooperative Agreements to State and Local Governments
###### General
####### ยง 600.202 Definitions.
######## Grantee

means the government to which a grant is awarded and which is accountable for the use of the funds provided. The grantee is the entire legal entity even if only a particular component of the entity is designated in the grant award document.
