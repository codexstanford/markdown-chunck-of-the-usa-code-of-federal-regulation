#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 835.2 Definitions.
###### General employee

means an individual who is either a DOE or DOE contractor employee; an employee of a subcontractor to a DOE contractor; or an individual who performs work for or in conjunction with DOE or utilizes DOE facilities.
