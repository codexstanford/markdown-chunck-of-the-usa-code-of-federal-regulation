
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 452 - PRODUCTION INCENTIVES FOR CELLULOSIC BIOFUELS
##### ยง 452.3 Solicitations.
###### www.eere.energy.gov

The reverse auction process commences with the issuance of a solicitation by DOE. DOE will publish a solicitation in theand shall post the solicitation on its website atno later than 60 days before the bidding in a reverse auction under this part commences. The solicitation shall:

(a) Invite interested persons and businesses to submit pre-qualification statements;

(b) Set forth the terms on which bids will be accepted;

(c) Specify the open window for bidding; and

(d) Specify the date by which successful bidders will be required to file pre-auction eligibility submissions.
