
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 420 - STATE ENERGY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions for State Energy Program Financial Assistance
###### ยง 420.2 Definitions.
####### State or local government building

means any building owned and primarily occupied by offices or agencies of a State; and any building of a unit of local government or a public care institution which could be covered by part H, title III, of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, 42 U.S.C. 6372-6372i.
