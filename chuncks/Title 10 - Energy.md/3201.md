###### Preservice Inspection of Steam Generator Tubes: First provision.

(A)When applying the provisions of NB-5283 in the 2019 Edition of Section III, a full-length preservice examination of 100 percent of the steam generator tubing in each newly installed steam generator must be performed prior to plant startup.
