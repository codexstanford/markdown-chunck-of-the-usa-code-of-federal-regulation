
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 420 - STATE ENERGY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions for State Energy Program Financial Assistance
###### ยง 420.2 Definitions.
####### Preferential traffic control

means any one of a variety of traffic control techniques used to give carpools, vanpools and public transportation vehicles priority treatment over single occupant vehicles other than bicycles and other two-wheeled motorized vehicles.
