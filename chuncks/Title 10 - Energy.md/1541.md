
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 21 - REPORTING OF DEFECTS AND NONCOMPLIANCE
#### General Provisions
##### ยง 21.3 Definitions.
###### Substantial safety hazard

means a loss of safety function to the extent that there is a major reduction in the degree of protection provided to public health and safety for any facility or activity licensed or otherwise approved or regulated by the NRC, other than for export, under parts 30, 40, 50, 52, 60, 61, 63, 70, 71, or 72 of this chapter.
