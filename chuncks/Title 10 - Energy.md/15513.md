#### Subpart B - Administrative Requirements
##### ยง 850.10 Development and approval of the CBDPP.
###### Update.

(c)The responsible employer must submit an update of the CBDPP to the appropriate Head of DOE Field Element for review and approval whenever a significant change or significant addition to the CBDPP is made or a change in contractors occurs.
