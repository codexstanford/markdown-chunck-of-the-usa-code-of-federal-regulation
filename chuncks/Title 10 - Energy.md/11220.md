
# Title 10 - Energy

##### Subpart K - Distribution Transformers
###### ยง 431.192 Definitions.
####### Low-voltage dry-type distribution transformer

means a distribution transformer that has an input voltage of 600 volts or less and has the core and coil assembly immersed in a gaseous or dry-compound insulating medium.
