
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 460 - ENERGY CONSERVATION STANDARDS FOR MANUFACTURED HOMES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 460.2 Definitions.
####### Solar heat gain coefficient (SHGC)

means the ratio of the solar heat gain entering a space through a fenestration assembly to the incident solar radiation. Solar heat gain includes directly transmitted solar heat and absorbed solar radiation that is then reradiated, conducted, or convected into the space.
