
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Appendix X1 to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Dehumidifiers
###### Pressure.

3.1.2.2.3The pressure instruments used to measure the external static pressure and velocity pressures must have an accuracy within +/-0.01 in. w.c. and a resolution of no more than 0.01 in. w.c.
