(i) For each non-SDHV basic model, at a minimum, a manufacturer must test the model of outdoor unit with a "tested combination" composed entirely of non-ducted indoor units. The ducted "tested combination" must comprise the highest static variety of ducted indoor unit distributed in commerce (conventional, mid-static, or low-static).
