
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 460 - ENERGY CONSERVATION STANDARDS FOR MANUFACTURED HOMES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 460.2 Definitions.
####### Heated water circulation system

means a water distribution system in which one or more pumps are operated in the service hot water piping to circulate heated water from the water heating equipment to fixtures and back to the water heating equipment.
