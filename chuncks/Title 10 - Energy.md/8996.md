
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix I to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Microwave Ovens
####### Room temperature indicating system.

2.6.2.1For the test of microwave ovens, the room temperature indicating system must have an error no greater than +/-1 &#xB0;F (+/-0.6 &#xB0;C) over the range 65&#xB0; to 90 &#xB0;F (18 &#xB0;C to 32 &#xB0;C).
