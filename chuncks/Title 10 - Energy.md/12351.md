
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 440 - WEATHERIZATION ASSISTANCE FOR LOW-INCOME PERSONS
##### ยง 440.3 Definitions.
###### Community Action Agency

means a private corporation or public agency established pursuant to the Economic Opportunity Act of 1964, Pub. L. 88-452, which is authorized to administer funds received from Federal, State, local, or private funding entities to assess, design, operate, finance, and oversee antipoverty programs.
