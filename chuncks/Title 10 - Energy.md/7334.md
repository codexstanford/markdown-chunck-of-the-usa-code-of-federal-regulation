
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 420 - STATE ENERGY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions for State Energy Program Financial Assistance
###### ยง 420.2 Definitions.
####### Energy efficiency measure

means any capital investment that reduces energy costs in an amount sufficient to recover the total cost of purchasing and installing such measure over an appropriate period of time and maintains or reduces non-renewable energy consumption.
