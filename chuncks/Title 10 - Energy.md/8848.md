
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix F to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Room Air Conditioners
####### Power measurement accuracy.

3.1.2All instruments used for measuring electrical inputs to the test unit, reconditioning equipment, and any other equipment that operates within the calorimeter walls must be accurate to +/-0.5 percent of the quantity measured.
