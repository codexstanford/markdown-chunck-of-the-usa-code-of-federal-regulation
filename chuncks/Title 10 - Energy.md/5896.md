
# Title 10 - Energy

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 74.4 Definitions.
###### Standard Error of the Inventory Difference

(SEID) means the standard deviation of an inventory difference that takes into account all measurement error contributions to the components of the ID.
