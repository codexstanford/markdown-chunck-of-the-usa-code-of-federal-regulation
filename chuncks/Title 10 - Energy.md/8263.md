####### Conventional room air cleaner

means an air cleaner that-

(1) Is a portable or wall mounted (fixed) unit, excluding ceiling mounted unit, that plugs into an electrical outlet;

(2) Operates with a fan for air circulation; and

(3) Contains means to remove, destroy, and/or deactivate particulates. The term portable is as defined in section 2.1.3.1 of AHAM AC-7-2022 (incorporated by reference; see ยง 430.3) and fixed is as defined in section 2.1.3.2 of AHAM AC-7-2022.
