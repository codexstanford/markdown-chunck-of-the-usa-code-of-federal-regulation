
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 19 - NOTICES, INSTRUCTIONS AND REPORTS TO WORKERS: INSPECTION AND INVESTIGATIONS
#### ยง 19.3 Definitions.
##### Regulated activities

means any activity carried on which is under the jurisdiction of the NRC under the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended, or any title of the Energy Reorganization Act of 1972, as amended.
