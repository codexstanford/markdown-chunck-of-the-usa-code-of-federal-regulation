#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 960.2 Definitions.
###### Tectonics

means the branch of geology dealing with the broad architecture of the outer part of the Earth, that is, the regional assembling of structural or deformational features and the study of their mutual relations, origin, and historical evolution.
