
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 810 - ASSISTANCE TO FOREIGN ATOMIC ENERGY ACTIVITIES
#### ยง 810.3 Definitions.
##### Publicly available technology

means technology that is already published or has been prepared for publication; arises during, or results from, fundamental research; or is included in an application filed with the U.S. Patent Office and eligible for foreign filing under 35 U.S.C. 184.
