####### Scope.

A.This section VI applies only to pumps sold with electric motors, including single-phase induction motors, and continuous or non-continuous controls. For the purposes of this section VI, all references to "driver input power" in this section VI or HI 40.6-2014 (incorporated by reference, see ยง 431.463) refer to the input power to the continuous or non-continuous controls.
