
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 434 - ENERGY CODE FOR NEW FEDERAL COMMERCIAL AND MULTI-FAMILY HIGH RISE RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS
##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 434.201 Definitions.
####### Heated space:

an enclosed space within a building that is heated by a heating system whose output capacity

(1) Exceeds 10 Btu/(h&#xB7;ft), or

(2) Is capable of maintaining a space dry-bulb temperature of 50 &#xB0;F or more at design heating conditions.
