
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix E to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Water Heaters
####### 5.3.1 General.,Maximum GPM Rating Test for Flow-Activated Water Heaters,

For flow-activated water heaters, conduct the maximum GPM test, as described in section 5.3.2,of this appendix. For all other water heaters, conduct the first-hour rating test as described in section 5.3.3 of this appendix.
