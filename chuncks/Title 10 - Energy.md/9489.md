
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix M1 to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps
####### Minimum-speed-limiting variable-speed heat pump

means a heat pump for which the compressor minimum speed (represented by revolutions per minute or motor power input frequency) is higher than its minimum value for operation in a 47 &#xB0;F ambient temperature for any bin temperature Tfor which the calculated heating load is less than the calculated intermediate-speed capacity.
