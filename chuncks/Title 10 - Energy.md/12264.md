
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 436 - FEDERAL ENERGY MANAGEMENT AND PLANNING PROGRAMS
##### Subpart C - Agency Procurement of Energy Efficient Products
###### ยง 436.41 Definitions.
####### Agency

means each authority of the Government of the United States, whether or not it is within or subject to review by another agency, but does not include-

(1) The Congress, and agencies thereof;

(2) The courts of the United States;

(3) The governments of the territories or possessions of the United States; or

(4) The government of the District of Columbia.
