##### Subpart B - Definitions
###### ยง 434.201 Definitions.
####### Thermal conductance (C):

the constant time rate of heat flow through the unit area of a body induced by a unit temperature difference between the surfaces, expressed in Btu/(h&#xB7;ft&#xB7;&#xB0;F). It is the reciprocal of thermal resistance. (See "thermal resistance")
