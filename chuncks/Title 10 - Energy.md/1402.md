#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 20.1004 Units of radiation dose.
###### Rad

is the special unit of absorbed dose. One rad is equal to an absorbed dose of 100 ergs/gram or 0.01 joule/kilogram (0.01 gray).
