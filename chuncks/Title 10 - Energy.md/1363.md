
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 20 - STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 20.1003 Definitions.
###### Powered air-purifying respirator (PAPR)

means an air-purifying respirator that uses a blower to force the ambient air through air-purifying elements to the inlet covering.
