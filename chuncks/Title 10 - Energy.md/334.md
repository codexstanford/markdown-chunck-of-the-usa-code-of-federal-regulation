
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 2 - AGENCY RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
#### Subpart I - Special Procedures Applicable to Adjudicatory Proceedings Involving Restricted Data and/or National Security Information
##### ยง 2.902 Definitions.
###### Interested party

(b)means a party having an interest in the issue or issues to which particular Restricted Data or National Security Information is relevant. Normally the interest of a party in an issue may be determined by examination of the notice of hearing, the answers and replies.
