
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1022 - COMPLIANCE WITH FLOODPLAIN AND WETLAND ENVIRONMENTAL REVIEW REQUIREMENTS
#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 1022.4 Definitions.
###### Critical action floodplain

means, at a minimum, the 500-year floodplain, that is, a floodplain with a 0.2 percent chance of flooding in any given year. When another requirement directing evaluation of a less frequent flood event also is applicable to the proposed action, a flood less frequent than the 500-year flood may be appropriate for determining the floodplain for purposes of this part.
