
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Appendix CC to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Portable Air Conditioners
###### Single-duct evaporator inlet test conditions.

3.1.1.2When testing single-duct portable air conditioners, maintain the evaporator inlet dry-bulb temperature within a range of 1.0 &#xB0;F with an average difference within 0.3 &#xB0;F.
