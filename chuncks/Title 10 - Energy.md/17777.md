
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1049 - LIMITED ARREST AUTHORITY AND USE OF FORCE BY PROTECTIVE FORCE OFFICERS OF THE STRATEGIC PETROLEUM RESERVE
#### ยง 1049.5 Exercise of arrest authority - General guidelines.
##### Miranda

(c) After the arrest is effected, the person arrested shall be advised of his constitutional right against self-incrimination ("warnings"). If the circumstances are such that immediately advising the person arrested of this right would result in imminent danger to the Officer or other persons, the Protective Force Officer may postpone this requirement. The person arrested shall be advised of this right as soon as practicable after the imminent danger has passed.
