###### ยง 431.62 Definitions concerning commercial refrigerators, freezers and refrigerator-freezers.
####### Ice-cream freezer

means a commercial freezer that is designed to operate at or below -5 &#xB0;F (+/-2 &#xB0;F) (-21 &#xB0;C +/-1.1 &#xB0;C) and that the manufacturer designs, markets, or intends for the storing, displaying, or dispensing of ice cream.
