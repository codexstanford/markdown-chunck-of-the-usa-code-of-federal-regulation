
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 50 - DOMESTIC LICENSING OF PRODUCTION AND UTILIZATION FACILITIES
#### Appendix J to Part 50 - Primary Reactor Containment Leakage Testing for Water-Cooled Power Reactors
##### Overall integrated leakage rate

means the total leakage rate through all tested leakage paths, including containment welds, valves, fittings, and components that penetrate the containment system.
