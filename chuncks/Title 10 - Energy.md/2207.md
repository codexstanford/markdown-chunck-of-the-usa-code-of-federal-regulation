
# Title 10 - Energy

### PART 35 - MEDICAL USE OF BYPRODUCT MATERIAL
#### Subpart A - General Information
##### ยง 35.2 Definitions.
###### Patient intervention

means actions by the patient or human research subject, whether intentional or unintentional, such as dislodging or removing treatment devices or prematurely terminating the administration.
