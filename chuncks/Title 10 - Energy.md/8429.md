### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 430.2 Definitions.
####### Sign service lamp

means a vacuum type or gas-filled lamp that has sufficiently low bulb temperature to permit exposed outdoor use on high-speed flashing circuits, is designed and marketed as a sign service lamp, and has a maximum rated wattage of 15 watts.
