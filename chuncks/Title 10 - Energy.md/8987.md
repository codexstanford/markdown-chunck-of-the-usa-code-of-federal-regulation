
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix I to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Microwave Ovens
####### Voltage.

2.2.1.1For microwave oven testing, maintain the electrical supply to the unit at 240/120 volts +/-1 percent. Maintain the electrical supply frequency for all products at 60 hertz +/-1 percent.
