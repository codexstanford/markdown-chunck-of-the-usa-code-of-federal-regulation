
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 21 - REPORTING OF DEFECTS AND NONCOMPLIANCE
#### General Provisions
##### ยง 21.3 Definitions.
###### Critical characteristics.

When applied to nuclear power plants licensed pursuant to 10 CFR part 50, critical characteristics are those important design, material, and performance characteristics of a commercial grade item that, once verified, will provide reasonable assurance that the item will perform its intended safety function.
