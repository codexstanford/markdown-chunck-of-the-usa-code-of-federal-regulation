
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1017 - IDENTIFICATION AND PROTECTION OF UNCLASSIFIED CONTROLLED NUCLEAR INFORMATION
#### Subpart A - General Overview
##### ยง 1017.4 Definitions.
###### Exploitable security-related information

means information whose unauthorized dissemination could significantly increase the likelihood of the theft, diversion, or sabotage of nuclear material, equipment, or facilities.
