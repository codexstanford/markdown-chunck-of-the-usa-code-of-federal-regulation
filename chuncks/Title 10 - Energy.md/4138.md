
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 52 - LICENSES, CERTIFICATIONS, AND APPROVALS FOR NUCLEAR POWER PLANTS
#### General Provisions
##### ยง 52.1 Definitions.
###### Decommission

means to remove a facility or site safely from service and reduce residual radioactivity to a level that permits-

(i) Release of the property for unrestricted use and termination of the license; or

(ii) Release of the property under restricted conditions and termination of the license.
