
# Title 10 - Energy

### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 430.2 Definitions.
####### Ceiling electric heater

means an electric heater which is intended to be recessed in, surface mounted on, or hung from a ceiling, and which transfers heat by radiation and/or convection (either natural or forced).
