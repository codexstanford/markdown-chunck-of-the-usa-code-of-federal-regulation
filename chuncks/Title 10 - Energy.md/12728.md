
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 490 - ALTERNATIVE FUEL TRANSPORTATION PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 490.2 Definitions.
####### After-Market Converted Vehicle

means an Original Equipment Manufacturer vehicle that is reconfigured by a conversion company, which is not under contract to the Original Equipment Manufacturer, to operate on an alternative fuel and whose conversion kit components are under warranty of the conversion company.
