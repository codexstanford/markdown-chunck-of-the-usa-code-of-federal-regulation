####### ยง 603.535 Value of proposed real property or equipment.
######## i.e.

(a) The value of the property as shown in the recipient's accounting records (, purchase price less accumulated depreciation); and

(b) The current fair market value. The contracting officer may accept the use of any reasonable basis for determining the fair market value of the property.
