
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission

#### General Provisions
##### ยง 95.5 Definitions.
###### Facility (Security) Clearance (FCL)

means an administrative determination that, from a security viewpoint, a facility is eligible for access to classified information of a certain category (and all lower categories).
