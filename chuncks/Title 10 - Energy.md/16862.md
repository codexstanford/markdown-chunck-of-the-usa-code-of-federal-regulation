
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1016 - SAFEGUARDING OF RESTRICTED DATA BY ACCESS PERMITTEES
#### Control of Information
##### ยง 1016.19 Review, classification and marking of classified information.
###### Challenges.

(b)If a person receives a document or other classified matter which, in his opinion, is not properly classified, or omits the appropriate classification markings, he is encouraged to challenge the classification and there shall be no retribution for submitting a challenge. Challenges shall be submitted in accordance with 10 CFR part 1045.
