
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)

#### Subpart A - General
##### ยง 1046.3 Definitions.
###### Officially Designated Federal Security Authority (ODFSA)

means the Departmental Federal authority at the Field or Headquarters (HQ) Element with the primary and delegated responsibility for oversight of a site PF. Also may be referred to as the Department or Federal cognizant security authority.
