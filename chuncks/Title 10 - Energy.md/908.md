
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 9 - PUBLIC RECORDS
#### Subpart B - Privacy Act Regulations
##### nrc procedures for processing requests
###### ยง 9.61 Procedures for processing requests for records exempt in whole or in part.
####### Testing or examination material exempted pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552a(k)(6).

(5)Testing or examination material used solely to determine individual qualifications for appointment or promotion in the Federal service which has been exempted pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552a(k)(6) shall not be made available to an individual if disclosure would compromise the objectivity or fairness of the testing or examination process but may be made available if no possibility of such compromise exists.
