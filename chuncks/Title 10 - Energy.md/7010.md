
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter A - Oil
#### PART 209 - INTERNATIONAL VOLUNTARY AGREEMENTS
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 209.3 Definitions.
####### International Energy Program

(d)(IEP) means the program established pursuant to the Agreement on an International Energy Program signed at Paris on November 18, 1974, including (1) the Annex entitled "Emergency Reserves", (2) any amendment to such Agreement which includes another nation as a Party to such Agreement, and (3) any technical or clerical amendment to such Agreement.
