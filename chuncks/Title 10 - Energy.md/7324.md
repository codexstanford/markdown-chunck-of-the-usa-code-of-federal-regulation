
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 420 - STATE ENERGY PROGRAM
##### Subpart A - General Provisions for State Energy Program Financial Assistance
###### ยง 420.2 Definitions.
####### British thermal unit (Btu)

means the quantity of heat necessary to raise the temperature of one pound of water one degree Fahrenheit at 39.2 degrees Fahrenheit and at one atmosphere of pressure.
