
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter A - Oil
#### PART 218 - STANDBY MANDATORY INTERNATIONAL OIL ALLOCATION
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 218.3 Definitions.
####### International energy supply emergency

means any period (a) beginning on any date that the President determines allocation of petroleum products to nations participating in the IEP is required by chapters III and IV of the IEP and (b) ending on a date on which he determines such allocation is no longer required.
