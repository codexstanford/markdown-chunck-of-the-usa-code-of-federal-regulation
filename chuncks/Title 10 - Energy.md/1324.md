
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 20 - STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### § 20.1003 Definitions.
###### Effective dose equivalent

(H) is the sum of the products of the dose equivalent to the organ or tissue (H) and the weighting factors (w) applicable to each of the body organs or tissues that are irradiated (H= Σ wH).
