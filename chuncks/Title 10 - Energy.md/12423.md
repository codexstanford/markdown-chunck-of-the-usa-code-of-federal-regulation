
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 451 - RENEWABLE ENERGY PRODUCTION INCENTIVES
##### ยง 451.2 Definitions.
###### Not-for-profit electrical cooperative

means a cooperative association that is legally obligated to operate on a not-for-profit basis and is organized under the laws of any State for the purpose of providing electric service to its members.
