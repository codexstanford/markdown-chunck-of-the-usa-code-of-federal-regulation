
# Title 10 - Energy

#### Subpart B - Enforcement Process
##### ยง 820.20 Purpose and scope.
###### Not-for-profit.

(f)For purposes of this part, a "not-for-profit" contractor, subcontractor, or supplier is one for which no part of the net earnings of the contractor, subcontractor, or supplier inures to the benefit of any natural person or for-profit artificial person.
