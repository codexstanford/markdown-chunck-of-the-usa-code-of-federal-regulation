
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix C2 to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Dishwashers
####### Preconditioning requirements.

2.5

The preconditioning requirements described in section 2.9 of AHAM DW-1-2020 are applicable to all dishwashers except the measurement of the prewash fill water volume, V, if any, and measurement of the main wash fill water volume, V, are not required.
