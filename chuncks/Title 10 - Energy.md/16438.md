
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1003 - OFFICE OF HEARINGS AND APPEALS PROCEDURAL REGULATIONS
#### ยง 1003.7 General filing requirements.
##### Obligation to supply information.

(d)A person who files a petition is under a continuing obligation during the proceeding to provide the OHA with any new or newly discovered information that is relevant to that proceeding. Such information includes, but is not limited to, information regarding any other submission that is subsequently filed by that person with any DOE office.
