
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 830 - NUCLEAR SAFETY MANAGEMENT
#### ยง 830.3 Definitions.
##### Surveillance requirements

means requirements relating to test, calibration, or inspection to ensure that the necessary operability and quality of safety structures, systems, and components and their support systems required for safe operations are maintained, that facility operation is within safety limits, and that limiting control settings and limiting conditions for operation are met.
