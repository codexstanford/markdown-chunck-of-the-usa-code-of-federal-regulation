
# Title 10 - Energy

##### Subpart T - Compressors
###### ยง 431.342 Definitions concerning compressors.
####### Liquid-cooled compressor

means a compressor that utilizes liquid coolant provided by an external system to cool both the compressed air and, if present, any auxiliary substance used to facilitate compression.
