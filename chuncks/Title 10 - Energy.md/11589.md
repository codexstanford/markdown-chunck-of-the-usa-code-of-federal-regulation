##### Subpart T - Compressors
###### Appendix A to Subpart T of Part 431 - Uniform Test Method for Certain Air Compressors

Set up the unit under test so that backpressure on the unit can be adjusted (by valves) incrementally, causing the measured discharge pressure to change, until the compressor is in an unloaded condition.
