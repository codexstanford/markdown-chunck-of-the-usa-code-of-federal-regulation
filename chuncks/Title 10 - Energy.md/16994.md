
# Title 10 - Energy

##### ยง 1021.104 Definitions.
###### Mitigation Action Plan

means a document that describes the plan for implementing commitments made in a DOE EIS and its associated ROD, or, when appropriate, an EA or FONSI, to mitigate adverse environmental impacts associated with an action.
