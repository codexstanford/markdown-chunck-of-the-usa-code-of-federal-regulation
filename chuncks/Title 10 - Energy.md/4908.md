
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission

#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 70.4 Definitions.
###### Transient shipment

means a shipment of nuclear material, originating and terminating in foreign countries, on a vessel or aircraft which stops at a United States port.
