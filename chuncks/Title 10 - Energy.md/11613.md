####### Duration.

(h)(1) Within one year of issuance of an interim waiver, DOE will either:

(i) Publish in thea final determination on the petition for waiver; or

(ii) Publish in thea new or amended test procedure that addresses the issues presented in the waiver.

(2) When DOE publishes a decision and order on a petition for waiver in thepursuant to paragraph (f) of this section, the interim waiver will terminate upon the date specified in the decision and order, in accordance with paragraph (i) of this section.

(3) When DOE amends the test procedure to address the issues presented in a waiver, the waiver or interim waiver will automatically terminate on the date on which use of that test procedure is required to demonstrate compliance.

(4) When DOE publishes a decision and order in theto modify a waiver pursuant to paragraph (k) of this section, the existing waiver will terminate upon the date specified in the decision and order, in accordance with paragraph (i) of this section.
