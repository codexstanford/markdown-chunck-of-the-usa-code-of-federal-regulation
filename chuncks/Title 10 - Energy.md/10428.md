
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Appendix CC to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Portable Air Conditioners
###### Duct temperature measurements.

3.1.1.6Install any insulation and sealing provided by the manufacturer. Then adhere four equally spaced thermocouples per duct to the outer surface of the entire length of the duct. Measure the surface temperatures of each duct. Temperature measurements must have an error no greater than +/-0.5 &#xB0;F over the range being measured.
