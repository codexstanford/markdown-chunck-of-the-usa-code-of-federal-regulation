
# Title 10 - Energy

### PART 1017 - IDENTIFICATION AND PROTECTION OF UNCLASSIFIED CONTROLLED NUCLEAR INFORMATION
#### Subpart A - General Overview
##### ยง 1017.4 Definitions.
###### Essential technology-related information

means technical information whose unauthorized dissemination could significantly increase the likelihood of the illegal production of a nuclear weapon.
