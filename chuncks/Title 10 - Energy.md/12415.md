
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 451 - RENEWABLE ENERGY PRODUCTION INCENTIVES
##### ยง 451.2 Definitions.
###### Date of first use

means, at the option of the facility owner, the date of the first kilowatt-hour sale, the date of completion of facility equipment testing, or the date when all approved permits required for facility construction are received.
