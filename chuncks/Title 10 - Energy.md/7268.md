(d)Direct greenhouse gas emissions that must be reported are the emissions resulting from stationary or mobile sources within the organizational boundaries of an entity, including but not limited to emissions resulting from combustion of fossil fuels, process emissions, and fugitive emissions. Process emissions (, PFC emissions from aluminum production) must be reported along with fugitive emissions (, leakage of greenhouse gases from equipment).
