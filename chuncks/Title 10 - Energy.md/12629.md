
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 460 - ENERGY CONSERVATION STANDARDS FOR MANUFACTURED HOMES
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 460.2 Definitions.
####### Whole-house mechanical ventilation system

means an exhaust system, supply system, or combination thereof that is designed to mechanically exchange indoor air with outdoor air when operating continuously or through a programmed intermittent schedule to satisfy the whole house ventilation rates.
