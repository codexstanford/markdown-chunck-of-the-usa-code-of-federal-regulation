
# Title 10 - Energy

### PART 851 - WORKER SAFETY AND HEALTH PROGRAM
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 851.3 Definitions.
###### Compliance order

means an order issued by the Secretary to a contractor that mandates a remedy, work stoppage, or other action to address a situation that violates, potentially violates, or otherwise is inconsistent with a requirement of this part.
