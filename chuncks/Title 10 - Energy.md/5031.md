
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 71 - PACKAGING AND TRANSPORTATION OF RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### § 71.4 Definitions.
###### Contamination

means the presence of a radioactive substance on a surface in quantities in excess of 0.4 Bq/cm(1 x 10µCi/cm) for beta and gamma emitters and low toxicity alpha emitters, or 0.04 Bq/cm(1 x 10µCi/cm) for all other alpha emitters.
