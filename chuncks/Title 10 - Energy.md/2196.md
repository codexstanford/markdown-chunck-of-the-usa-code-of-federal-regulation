#### Subpart A - General Information
##### ยง 35.2 Definitions.
###### High dose-rate remote afterloader,

as used in this part, means a brachytherapy device that remotely delivers a dose rate in excess of 12 gray (1200 rads) per hour at the point or surface where the dose is prescribed.
