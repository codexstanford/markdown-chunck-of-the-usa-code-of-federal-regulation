
# Title 10 - Energy

##### ยง 150.32 Funds for reclamation or maintenance of byproduct material.
###### i.e.

(a) The total amount of funds an Agreement State collects, pursuant to a license for byproduct material as defined in ยง 150.3(c)(2) of this part or for any activity that results in the production of such material, for reclamation or long term maintenance and monitoring of such material, shall after November 8, 1981, be transferred to the United States if title and custody of such material and its disposal site is transferred to the United States upon termination of such license.
