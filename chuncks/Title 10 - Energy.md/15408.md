
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 835 - OCCUPATIONAL RADIATION PROTECTION
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### § 835.2 Definitions.
###### Effective dose

(E) means the summation of the products of the equivalent dose received by specified tissues or organs of the body (H) and the appropriate tissue weighting factor (w)-that is, E = ΣwH. It includes the dose from radiation sources internal and/or external to the body. For purposes of compliance with this part, equivalent dose to the whole body may be used as effective dose for external exposures. The effective dose is expressed in units of rem (or Sv).
