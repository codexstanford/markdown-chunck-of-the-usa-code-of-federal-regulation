
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 16 - SALARY OFFSET PROCEDURES FOR COLLECTING DEBTS OWED BY FEDERAL EMPLOYEES TO THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT
#### ยง 16.3 Definitions.
##### Centralized salary offset computer matching

describes the computerized process used to match delinquent debt records with Federal salary payment records when the purpose of the match is to identify Federal employees who owe debt to the Federal Government.
