
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter A - Oil
#### PART 215 - COLLECTION OF FOREIGN OIL SUPPLY AGREEMENT INFORMATION
##### ยง 215.2 Definitions.
###### Person

means any natural person, corporation, partnership, association, consortium, or any other entity doing business or domiciled in the U.S. and includes (a) any entity controlled directly or indirectly by such a person and (b) the interest of such a person in any joint venture, consortium or other entity to the extent of entitlement to crude oil by reason of such interest.
