
# Title 10 - Energy

###### ยง 429.42 Commercial refrigerators, freezers, and refrigerator-freezers.
####### Determination of represented value.

(a)Manufacturers must determine the represented value, which includes the certified rating, for each basic model of commercial refrigerator, freezer, or refrigerator-freezer either by testing, in conjunction with the applicable sampling provisions, or by applying an AEDM.
