
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 960 - GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR THE PRELIMINARY SCREENING OF POTENTIAL SITES FOR A NUCLEAR WASTE REPOSITORY
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 960.2 Definitions.
###### Hydraulic gradient

means a change in the static pressure of ground water, expressed in terms of the height of water above a datum, per unit of distance in a given direction.
