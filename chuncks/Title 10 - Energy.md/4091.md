
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission

#### Subpart C - Combined Licenses
##### ยง 52.99 Inspection during construction; ITAAC schedules and notifications; NRC notices.
###### Licensee and applicant conduct of activities subject to ITAAC.

(b)With respect to activities subject to an ITAAC, an applicant for a combined license may proceed at its own risk with design and procurement activities, and a licensee may proceed at its own risk with design, procurement, construction, and preoperational activities, even though the NRC may not have found that any one of the prescribed acceptance criteria are met.
