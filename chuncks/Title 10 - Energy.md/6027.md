
# Title 10 - Energy

###### U.S.-IAEA Safeguards Agreement

means the Agreement between the United States of America and the International Atomic Energy Agency for the Application of Safeguards in the United States (INFCIRC/288), and all protocols and subsidiary arrangements thereto.
