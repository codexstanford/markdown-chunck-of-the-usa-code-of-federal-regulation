
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 140 - FINANCIAL PROTECTION REQUIREMENTS AND INDEMNITY AGREEMENTS
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 140.9 Modification of indemnity agreements.

The Commission will publish in thea notice of its intent to enter into an indemnity agreement, or agreement amending an indemnity agreement, which contains provisions different from the form of the applicable indemnity agreement set forth in the appendices to this part, as such appendices may be amended from time to time.
