
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 440 - WEATHERIZATION ASSISTANCE FOR LOW-INCOME PERSONS
##### ยง 440.3 Definitions.
###### Capital-Intensive furnace or cooling efficiency modifications

means those major heating and cooling modifications which require a substantial amount of funds, including replacement and major repairs, but excluding such items as tune-ups, minor repairs, and filters.
