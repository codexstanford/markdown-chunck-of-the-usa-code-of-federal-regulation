
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 903 - POWER AND TRANSMISSION RATES
#### Subpart A - Procedures for Public Participation in Power and Transmission Rate Adjustments and Extensions for the Alaska, Southeastern, Southwestern, and Western Area Power Administrations
##### ยง 903.2 Definitions.
###### Minor rate adjustment

(e)means a rate adjustment which (1) will produce less than 1 percent change in the annual revenues of the power system or (2) is for a power system which has either annual sales normally less than 100 million kilowatt hours or an installed capacity of less than 20,000 kilowatts.
