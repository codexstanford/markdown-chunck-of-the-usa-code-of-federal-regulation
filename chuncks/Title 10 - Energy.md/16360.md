
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 963 - YUCCA MOUNTAIN SITE SUITABILITY GUIDELINES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 963.2 Definitions.
###### Preclosure safety evaluation

means a preliminary assessment of the adequacy of repository support facilities to prevent or mitigate the effects of postulated initiating events and event sequences and their consequences (including fire, radiation, criticality, and chemical hazards), and the site, structures, systems, components, equipment, and operator actions that would be relied on for safety.
