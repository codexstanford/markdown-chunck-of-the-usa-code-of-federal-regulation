
# Title 10 - Energy

##### Review and Certification of Grants, Cooperative Agreements, Contracts, and Projects
###### ยง 473.2 Definitions.
####### Research and development

means activities constituting a project to create an advanced automobile propulsion system and does not mean activities involving technology transfer to mass production, evaluative testing, preliminary planning for a DOE or an agency project, or program administration and management.
