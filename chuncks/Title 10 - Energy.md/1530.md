
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 21 - REPORTING OF DEFECTS AND NONCOMPLIANCE
#### General Provisions
##### ยง 21.3 Definitions.
###### Dedicating entity.

When applied to nuclear power plants licensed pursuant to 10 CFR part 50, dedicating entity means the organization that performs the dedication process. Dedication may be performed by the manufacturer of the item, a third-party dedicating entity, or the licensee itself. The dedicating entity, pursuant to ยง 21.21(c) of this part, is responsible for identifying and evaluating deviations, reporting defects and failures to comply for the dedicated item, and maintaining auditable records of the dedication process.
