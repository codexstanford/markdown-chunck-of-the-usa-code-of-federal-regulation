
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix C1 to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Dishwashers
####### Preconditioning requirements.

2.4

The preconditioning requirements described in section 2.9 of AHAM DW-1-2020 are applicable to all dishwashers. For dishwashers that do not have a direct water line, measurement of the prewash fill water volume, V, if any, and measurement of the main wash fill water volume, V, are not taken.
