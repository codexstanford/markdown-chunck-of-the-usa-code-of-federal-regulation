####### Limited work authorization submitted as part of an early site permit application.

(c)Each applicant for an early site permit under subpart A of part 52 of this chapter requesting a limited work authorization shall submit with its application the environmental report required by ยง 51.50(b). Each environmental report must contain the following information:
