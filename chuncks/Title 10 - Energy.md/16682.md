
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter X - Department of Energy (General Provisions)
### PART 1013 - PROGRAM FRAUD CIVIL REMEDIES AND PROCEDURES
#### ยง 1013.2 Definitions.
##### Investigating official

means the Inspector General of the Department of Energy or an officer or employee of the Inspector General designated by the Inspector General and serving in a position for which the rate of basic pay is not less than the minimum rate of basic pay for grade GS-16 under the General Schedule.
