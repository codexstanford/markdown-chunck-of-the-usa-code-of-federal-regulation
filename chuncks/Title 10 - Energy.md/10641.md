##### Subpart B - Electric Motors
###### ยง 431.12 Definitions.
####### Rated voltage

means the input voltage of a motor or inverter used when making representations of the performance characteristics of a given electric motor and selected by the motor's manufacturer to be used for testing the motor's efficiency.
