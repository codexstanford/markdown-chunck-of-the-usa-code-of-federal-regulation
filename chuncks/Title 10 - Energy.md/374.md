
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission

###### Presiding Officer

means one or more members of the Commission, or an atomic safety and licensing board, or a named officer who has been delegated final authority in the matter, designated in the notice of hearing to preside.
