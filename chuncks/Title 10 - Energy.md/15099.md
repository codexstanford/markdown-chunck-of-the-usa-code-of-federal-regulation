
# Title 10 - Energy

### PART 820 - PROCEDURAL RULES FOR DOE NUCLEAR ACTIVITIES

###### Exclusion.

(c)Activities and facilities covered under E.O. 12344, 42 U.S.C. 7158 note, pertaining to Naval nuclear propulsion are excluded from the requirements of subparts D and E of this part regarding interpretations and exemptions related to this part.
