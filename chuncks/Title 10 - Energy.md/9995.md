### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation

##### Appendix P to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Pool Heaters
###### Pool heaters with a seasonal off switch.

4.3.1For pool heaters with a seasonal off switch, the average electric power consumption during the off mode, P= 0, and the fossil fuel energy consumed during the off mode, Q= 0.
