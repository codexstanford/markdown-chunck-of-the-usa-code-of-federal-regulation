
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter III - Department of Energy
### PART 963 - YUCCA MOUNTAIN SITE SUITABILITY GUIDELINES
#### Subpart A - General Provisions
##### ยง 963.2 Definitions.
###### Cladding

is the metallic outer sheath of a fuel rod element; it is generally made of a corrosion resistant zirconium alloy or stainless steel, and is intended to isolate the fuel from the external environment.
