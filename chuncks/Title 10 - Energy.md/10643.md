##### Subpart B - Electric Motors
###### ยง 431.12 Definitions.
####### Special purpose electric motor

means any electric motor, other than a general purpose motor or definite electric purpose motor, which has special operating characteristics or special mechanical construction, or both, designed for a particular application.
