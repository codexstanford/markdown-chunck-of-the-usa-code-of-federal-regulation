
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix G to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Unvented Home Heating Equipment
####### i.e.,,e.g.,

2.3.1 The measurement of Qis not required for unvented heaters where the pilot light is designed to be turned off by the user when the heater is not in use (for units where turning the control to the OFF position will shut off the gas supply to the burner(s) and the pilot light). This provision applies only if an instruction to turn off the unit is provided on the heater near the gas control value (by label) by the manufacturer.
