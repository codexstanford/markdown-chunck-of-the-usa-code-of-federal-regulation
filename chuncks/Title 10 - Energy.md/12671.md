
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 470 - APPROPRIATE TECHNOLOGY SMALL GRANTS PROGRAM
##### ยง 470.2 Definitions.
###### Small business

means a concern, including its affiliates, which is organized for profit, is independently owned and operated, is not dominant in the field of operation in which it is submitting a proposal to DOE, and has 100 employees or less.
