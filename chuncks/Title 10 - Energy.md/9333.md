
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix M to Subpart B of Part 430 - Uniform Test Method for Measuring the Energy Consumption of Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps
####### Airflow-control settings,e.g.,,i.e.,

are programmed or wired control system configurations that control a fan to achieve discrete, differing ranges of airflow-often designated for performing a specific function (cooling, heating, or constant circulation)-without manual adjustment other than interaction with a user-operable control (a thermostat) that meets the manufacturer specifications for installed-use. For the purposes of this appendix, manufacturer specifications for installed-use are those found in the product literature shipped with the unit.
