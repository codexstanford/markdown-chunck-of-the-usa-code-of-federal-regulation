## Chapter I - Nuclear Regulatory Commission
### PART 16 - SALARY OFFSET PROCEDURES FOR COLLECTING DEBTS OWED BY FEDERAL EMPLOYEES TO THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT

##### Priorities.

(d)(1) A levy pursuant to the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall take precedence over other deductions under this section.
