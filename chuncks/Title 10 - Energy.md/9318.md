
# Title 10 - Energy
## Chapter II - Department of Energy
### Subchapter D - Energy Conservation
#### PART 430 - ENERGY CONSERVATION PROGRAM FOR CONSUMER PRODUCTS
##### Subpart B - Test Procedures
###### Appendix J3 to Subpart B of Part 430 - Energy Test Cloth Specifications and Procedures for Determining Correction Coefficients of New Energy Test Cloth Lots
####### Energy test cloth.

3.7.1The energy test cloth must be made from energy test cloth material, as specified in section 3.1 of this appendix, that is 24 +/-inches by 36 +/-inches (61.0 +/- 1.3 cm by 91.4 +/- 1.3 cm) and has been hemmed to 22 +/-inches by 34 +/-inches (55.9 +/- 1.3 cm by 86.4 +/- 1.3 cm) before washing.
