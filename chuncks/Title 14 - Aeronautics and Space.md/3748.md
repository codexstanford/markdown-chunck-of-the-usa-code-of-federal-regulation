####### Propellers and propeller blades and hubs.

(c)Each person who produces a propeller, propeller blade, or propeller hub under a type certificate or production certificate must mark each product or part. Except for a fixed-pitch wooden propeller, the marking must be accomplished using an approved fireproof method. The marking must-

(1) Be placed on a non-critical surface;

(2) Contain the information specified in ยง 45.13;

(3) Not likely be defaced or removed during normal service; and

(4) Not likely be lost or destroyed in an accident.
