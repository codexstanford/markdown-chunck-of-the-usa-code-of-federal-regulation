
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - Definitions and General Requirements
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS AND ABBREVIATIONS
##### ยง 1.1 General definitions.
###### Extended Operations (ETOPS)

means an airplane flight operation, other than an all-cargo operation in an airplane with more than two engines, during which a portion of the flight is conducted beyond a time threshold identified in part 121 or part 135 of this chapter that is determined using an approved one-engine-inoperative cruise speed under standard atmospheric conditions in still air.
