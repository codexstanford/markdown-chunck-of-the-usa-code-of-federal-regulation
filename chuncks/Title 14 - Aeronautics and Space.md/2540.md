
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space

##### Subpart F - Equipment
###### General
####### ยง 27.1303 Flight and navigation instruments.

The following are the required flight and navigation instruments:

(a) An airspeed indicator.

(b) An altimeter.

(c) A magnetic direction indicator.
