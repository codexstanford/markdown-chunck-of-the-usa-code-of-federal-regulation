##### Appendix C to Part 121 - C-46 Nontransport Category Airplanes
###### Engine compartment.

The shutoff means must be located aft of the engine firewall. The operator's manual must include, in the emergency portion, adequate instructions for proper operation of the additional shutoff means to assure correct sequential positioning of engine cowl flaps under emergency conditions.
