### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix C to Part 152 - Procurement Procedures and Requirements
###### Construction work.

4.All construction work under a project must be performed under contract, except in a case where the Administrator determines that the project, or a part of it, can be more effectively and economically accomplished on a force account basis by the sponsor or by another public agency acting for or as agent of the sponsor.
