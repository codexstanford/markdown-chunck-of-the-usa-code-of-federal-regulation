### Subchapter K - Administrative Regulations
#### PART 187 - FEES
##### ยง 187.3 Definitions.
###### U.S.-controlled airspace

means all airspace over the territory of the United States, extending 12 nautical miles from the coastline of U.S. territory; any airspace delegated to the United States for U.S. control by other countries or under a regional air navigation agreement; or any international airspace, or airspace of undetermined sovereignty, for which the United States has accepted responsibility for providing air traffic control services.
