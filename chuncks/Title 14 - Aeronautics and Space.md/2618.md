
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 27 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: NORMAL CATEGORY ROTORCRAFT
##### Subpart G - Operating Limitations and Information
###### Operating Limitations
####### ยง 27.1521 Powerplant limitations.
######## Two and one-half-minute OEI power operation.

(g)Unless otherwise authorized, the use of 2-minute OEI power must be limited to engine failure operation of multiengine, turbine-powered rotorcraft for not longer than 2minutes after failure of an engine. The use of 2-minute OEI power must also be limited by-

(1) The maximum rotational speed, which may not be greater than-

(i) The maximum value determined by the rotor design; or

(ii) The maximum demonstrated during the type tests;

(2) The maximum allowable gas temperature; and

(3) The maximum allowable torque.
