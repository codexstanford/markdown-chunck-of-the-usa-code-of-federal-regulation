### Subchapter K - Administrative Regulations
#### PART 193 - PROTECTION OF VOLUNTARILY SUBMITTED INFORMATION
##### ยง 193.3 Definitions.
###### Disclose

means to release information to a person other than another agency. Examples are disclosures under the Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. 552), in rulemaking proceedings, in a press release, or to a party to a legal action.
