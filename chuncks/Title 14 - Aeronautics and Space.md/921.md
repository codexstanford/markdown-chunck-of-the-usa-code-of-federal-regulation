
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter B - Procedural Rules
#### PART 17 - PROCEDURES FOR PROTESTS AND CONTRACT DISPUTES
##### Appendix A to Part 17 - Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)
###### Neutral Evaluation.

(2)At any stage during the ADR process, as the parties may agree, the neutral or compensated neutral will provide a candid assessment and opinion of the strengths and weaknesses of the parties" positions as to the facts and law, so as to facilitate further discussion and resolution.
