## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter B - Procedural Regulations
#### PART 302 - RULES OF PRACTICE IN PROCEEDINGS
##### Subpart D - Rules Applicable to Enforcement Proceedings
###### ยง 302.413 Evidence of previous violations.

Evidence of previous violations by any person or of any provision of 49 U.S.C. Subtitle VII or any requirement thereunder found by the Department or a court in any other proceeding or criminal or civil action may, if relevant and material, be admitted in any enforcement proceeding involving such person.
