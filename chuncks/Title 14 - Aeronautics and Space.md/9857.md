## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 258 - DISCLOSURE OF CHANGE-OF-GAUGE SERVICES
##### ยง 258.5 Notice requirement.
###### Notice in schedules.

(a)Carriers holding out or operating change-of-gauge services to, from, or within the United States shall ensure that in the written and electronic schedule information they provide to the public, to the Official Airline Guide and comparable publications, and to computer reservations systems, these services are shown as requiring a change of aircraft.
