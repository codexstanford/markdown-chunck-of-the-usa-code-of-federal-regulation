####### Flight instructor certificates.

(a)The holder of an expired flight instructor certificate who has not complied with the flight instructor renewal requirements of ยง 61.197 may reinstate that flight instructor certificate and ratings by filing a completed and signed application with the FAA and satisfactorily completing one of the following reinstatement requirements:
