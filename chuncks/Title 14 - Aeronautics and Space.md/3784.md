
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 47 - AIRCRAFT REGISTRATION
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 47.7 United States citizens and resident aliens.
####### Partnerships.

(d)A partnership may apply for a Certificate of Aircraft Registration, AC Form 8050-3, under 49 U.S.C. 44102 only if each partner, whether a general or limited partner, is an individual who is a citizen of the United States. Nothing in this section makes ineligible for registration an aircraft which is not owned as a partnership asset but is co-owned by-

(1) Resident aliens; or

(2) One or more resident aliens and one or more U.S. citizens.
