
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 29 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: TRANSPORT CATEGORY ROTORCRAFT
##### Appendix B to Part 29 - Airworthiness Criteria for Helicopter Instrument Flight
###### Climb.

(b)Stability must be shown in climb thoughout the speed range 20 knots either side of trim with-

(1) The helicopter trimmed at V;

(2) Landing gear retracted (if retractable); and

(3) Power required for limit climb rate (at least 1,000 fpm) at Vor maximum continuous power, whichever is less.
