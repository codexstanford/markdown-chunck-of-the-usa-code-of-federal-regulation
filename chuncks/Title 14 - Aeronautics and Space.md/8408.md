### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix A to Part 152 - Contract and Labor Provisions
###### I. Working conditions.

No contractor may require any laborer or mechanic employed in the performance of any contract to work in surroundings or under working conditions that are unsanitary, hazardous, or dangerous to his health or safety as determined under construction safety and health standards (29 CFR part 1926) and other occupational and health standards (29 CFR part 1910) issued by the Department of Labor.
