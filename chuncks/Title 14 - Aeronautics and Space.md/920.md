
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter B - Procedural Rules
#### PART 17 - PROCEDURES FOR PROTESTS AND CONTRACT DISPUTES
##### Appendix A to Part 17 - Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)
###### Mediation.

(1)The neutral or compensated neutral ascertains the needs and interests of both parties and facilitates discussions between or among the parties and an amicable resolution of their differences, seeking approaches to bridge the gaps between the parties" respective positions. The neutral or compensated neutral can meet with the parties separately, conduct joint meetings with the parties" representatives, or employ both methods in appropriate cases.
