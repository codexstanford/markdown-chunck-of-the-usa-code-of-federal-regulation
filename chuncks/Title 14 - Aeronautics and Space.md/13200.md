## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1203a - NASA SECURITY AREAS
#### ยง 1203a.104 Violation of security areas.
##### Criminal penalties for violation.

(b)Whoever willfully violates, attempts to violate, or conspires to violate any regulation or order establishing requirements or procedures for authorized entry into an area designated controlled, limited, or exclusion pursuant to the provisions of this part may be subject to prosecution under 18 U.S.C. 799 which provides penalties for a fine of not more than $5,000 or imprisonment for not more than 1 year, or both.
