
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space

###### ยง 91.1007 Flights conducted under part 121 or part 135 of this chapter.

(a) Except as provided in ยง 91.501(b), when a nonprogram aircraft is used to substitute for a program flight, the flight must be operated in compliance with part 121 or part 135 of this chapter, as applicable.
