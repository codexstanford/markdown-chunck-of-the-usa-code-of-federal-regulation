## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 243 - PASSENGER MANIFEST INFORMATION
##### ยง 243.3 Definitions.
###### Covered flight segment,i.e.

means a passenger-carrying flight segment operating to or from the United States (, the flight segment where the last point of departure or the first point of arrival is in the United States). A covered flight segment does not include a flight segment in which both the point of departure and point of arrival are in the United States.
