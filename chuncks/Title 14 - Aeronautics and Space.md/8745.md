### Subchapter J - Navigational Facilities

###### ยง 170.3 Definitions.
####### Instrument landing system

(ILS) means an instrument landing system whereby the pilot guides his approach to a runway solely by reference to instruments in the cockpit. In some instances, the signals received from the ground can be fed into the automatic pilot for automatically controlled approaches.
