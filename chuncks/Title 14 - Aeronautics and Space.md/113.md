
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - Definitions and General Requirements
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS AND ABBREVIATIONS
##### ยง 1.1 General definitions.
###### Maximum speed for stability characteristics, V

/Mmeans a speed that may not be less than a speed midway between maximum operating limit speed (V/M) and demonstrated flight diving speed (V/M), except that, for altitudes where the Mach number is the limiting factor, Mneed not exceed the Mach number at which effective speed warning occurs.
