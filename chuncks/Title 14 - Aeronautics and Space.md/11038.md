## Chapter III - Commercial Space Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 401 - ORGANIZATION AND DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 401.5 Definitions.
###### Contingency abort

means cessation of vehicle flight during ascent or descent in a manner that does not jeopardize public health and safety and the safety of property, in accordance with mission rules and procedures. Contingency abort includes landing at an alternative location that has been designated as a contingency abort location in advance of vehicle flight.
