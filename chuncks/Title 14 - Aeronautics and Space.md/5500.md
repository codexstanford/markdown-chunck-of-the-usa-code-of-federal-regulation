
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Air Traffic and General Operating Rules
#### PART 93 - SPECIAL AIR TRAFFIC RULES
##### Subpart V - Washington, DC Metropolitan Area Special Flight Rules Area
###### ยง 93.335 Definitions.
####### Washington, DC Metropolitan Area Flight Restricted Zone (DC FRZ)

is an area bounded by a line beginning at the Washington VOR/DME (DCA) 311&#xB0; radial at 15 nautical miles (NM) (Lat. 38&#xB0;59&#x2032;31&#x2033; N., Long. 077&#xB0;18&#x2032;30&#x2033; W.); then clockwise along the DCA 15 nautical mile arc to the DCA 002&#xB0; radial at 15 NM (Lat. 39&#xB0;06&#x2032;28&#x2033; N., Long 077&#xB0;04&#x2032;32&#x2033; W.); then southeast via a line drawn to the DCA 049&#xB0; radial at 14 NM (Lat. 39&#xB0;02&#x2032;18&#x2033; N., Long. 076&#xB0;50&#x2032;38&#x2033; W.); thence south via a line drawn to the DCA 064&#xB0; radial at 13 NM (Lat. 38&#xB0;59&#x2032;01&#x2033; N., Long. 076&#xB0;48&#x2032;32&#x2033; W.); thence clockwise along the 13 NM arc to the DCA 276&#xB0; radial at 13 NM (Lat.38&#xB0;50&#x2032;53&#x2033; N., Long 077&#xB0;18&#x2032;48&#x2033; W.); thence north to the point of beginning, excluding the airspace within a one nautical mile radius of the Freeway Airport, W00, Mitchellville, MD from the surface up to but not including flight level (FL) 180. The DC FRZ is within and part of the Washington, DC Metropolitan Area SFRA.
