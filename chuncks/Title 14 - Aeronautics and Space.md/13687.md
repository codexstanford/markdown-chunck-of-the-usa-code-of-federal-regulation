#### Subpart 1 - Awards for Scientific and Technical Contributions
##### ยง 1240.109 Hearing procedure.

(a) An Oral hearing held by the Board will be in accordance with the following procedures:

(1) If the applicant requests a hearing within the time set in accordance with ยง 1240.108(d) or (e), the Board will set a place and date for such hearing and notify the applicant.
