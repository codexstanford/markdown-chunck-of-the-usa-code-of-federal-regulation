## Chapter III - Commercial Space Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 401 - ORGANIZATION AND DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 401.5 Definitions.
###### Emergency abort

means cessation of vehicle flight during ascent or descent in a manner that minimizes risk to public health and safety and the safety of property. Emergency abort involves failure of a vehicle, safety-critical system, or flight safety system such that contingency abort is not possible.
