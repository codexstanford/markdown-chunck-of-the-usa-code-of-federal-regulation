## Chapter III - Commercial Space Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation

##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 420.5 Definitions.
####### Debris dispersion radius

(D) means the estimated maximum distance from a launch point that debris travels given a worst-case launch vehicle failure and flight termination early in flight. For an expendable launch vehicle, flight termination is assumed to occur at 10 seconds into flight.
