### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Subpart E - Nondiscrimination in Airport Aid Program
###### ยง 152.423 Investigation and enforcement.
####### Complaints.

(a)Any person who believes that he or she has been subjected to discrimination prohibited by this subpart may personally, or through a representative, file a complaint with the Director of the Departmental Office of Civil Rights. A complaint must be in writing and filed not later than 180 days after the date of the alleged discrimination, unless the time for filing is extended by the Director.
