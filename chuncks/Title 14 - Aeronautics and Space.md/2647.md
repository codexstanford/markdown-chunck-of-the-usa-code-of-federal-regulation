### Subchapter C - Aircraft

##### Subpart G - Operating Limitations and Information
###### Rotorcraft Flight Manual and Approved Manual Material
####### § 27.1583 Operating limitations.
######## Weight and loading distribution.

(c)The weight and center of gravity limits required by §§ 27.25 and 27.27, respectively, must be furnished. If the variety of possible loading conditions warrants, instructions must be included to allow ready observance of the limitations.
