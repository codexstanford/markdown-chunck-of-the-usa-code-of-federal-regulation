### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix C to Part 152 - Procurement Procedures and Requirements
###### Out-of-state labor.

2.No procedure or requirement shall be imposed by any grantee which will operate to discriminate against the employment of labor from any other State, possession, or territory of the United States in the construction of a project.
