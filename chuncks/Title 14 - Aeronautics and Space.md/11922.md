## Chapter III - Commercial Space Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Licensing
#### PART 417 - LAUNCH SAFETY
##### Appendix B to Part 417 - Flight Hazard Area Analysis for Aircraft and Ship Protection
###### General.

(a)A flight hazard area analysis must establish ship hazard areas bound by the 1 x 10ship impact contour in the vicinity of the launch site and the vehicle's three-sigma dispersion limit plus a 5 nm buffer in the vicinity of a planned, downrange impact location.
