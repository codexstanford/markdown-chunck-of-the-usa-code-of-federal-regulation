## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1203a - NASA SECURITY AREAS
#### ยง 1203a.101 Definitions.
##### Exclusion area.

(3)An area that is a permanent facility dedicated solely to the safeguarding and use of Classified National Security Information. It is used when vaults are unsuitable or impractical and where entry to the area alone provides visible or audible access to classified material. To prevent unauthorized access to an exclusion area, visitors will be escorted or other internal restrictions implemented, as determined by the Center Security Office.
