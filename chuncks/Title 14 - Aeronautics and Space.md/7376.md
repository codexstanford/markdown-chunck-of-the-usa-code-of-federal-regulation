####### Persons who work for more than one certificate holder.

(c)A certificate holder that uses or assigns a person to perform or directly supervise a job function specified in ยง 135.501(a), when that person also performs or directly supervises the same job function for another certificate holder, need only train that person in its own policies and procedures regarding those job functions, if all of the following are met:
