## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 258 - DISCLOSURE OF CHANGE-OF-GAUGE SERVICES
##### ยง 258.5 Notice requirement.
###### Oral notice to prospective consumers.

(b)In any direct oral communication with a consumer in the United States concerning a change-of-gauge service, any carrier or ticket agent doing business in the United States shall tell the consumer before booking scheduled passenger air transportation to, from, or within the United States that the service requires a change of aircraft en route.
