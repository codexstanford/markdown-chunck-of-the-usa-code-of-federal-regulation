### PART 1245 - PATENTS AND OTHER INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

##### ยง 1245.205 Processing of administrative claims.
###### Filing and forwarding of claims.

(a)All communications regarding claims should be addressed to: Agency Counsel for Intellectual Property, Office of the General Counsel, National Aeronautics and Space Administration, Washington, DC 20546-0001. If any communication relating to a claim or possible claim of patent or copyright infringement is received by an agency, organization, office, or field installation within NASA, it shall be forwarded to the Agency Counsel for Intellectual Property.
