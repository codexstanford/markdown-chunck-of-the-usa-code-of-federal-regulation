
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft

##### Subpart B - Design and Construction
###### ยง 35.16 Propeller critical parts.

The integrity of each propeller critical part identified by the safety analysis required by ยง 35.15 must be established by:

(a) A defined engineering process for ensuring the integrity of the propeller critical part throughout its service life,
