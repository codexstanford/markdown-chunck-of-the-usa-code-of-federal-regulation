## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 221 - TARIFFS
##### Subpart I - Suspension of Tariff Provisions by Department
###### ยง 221.84 Cancellation of suspended matter subsequent to date to which suspended.
####### Endeavor to cancel prior to expiration of suspension period.

(a)When an order of the Department requires the cancellation of tariff provisions which were suspended by the Department and such cancellation is required to be made effective on or before a date which is after the date to which such tariff provisions were suspended, the issuing carrier or agent shall, if possible, make the cancellation effective prior to the date to which such tariff provisions were suspended.
