
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - Definitions and General Requirements
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS AND ABBREVIATIONS
##### ยง 1.2 Abbreviations and symbols.
###### V

means the maximum speed in the takeoff at which the pilot must take the first action (e.g., apply brakes, reduce thrust, deploy speed brakes) to stop the airplane within the accelerate-stop distance. Valso means the minimum speed in the takeoff, following a failure of the critical engine at V, at which the pilot can continue the takeoff and achieve the required height above the takeoff surface within the takeoff distance.
