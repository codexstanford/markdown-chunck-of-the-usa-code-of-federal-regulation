
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space

##### Subpart B - Flight

####### ยง 29.71 Helicopter angle of glide: Category B.

For each category B helicopter, except multiengine helicopters meeting the requirements of ยง 29.67(b) and the powerplant installation requirements of category A, the steady angle of glide must be determined in autorotation-
