## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 259 - ENHANCED PROTECTIONS FOR AIRLINE PASSENGERS
##### ยง 259.7 Response to consumer problems.
###### Designated advocates for passengers' interests.

(a)Each covered carrier shall designate for its scheduled flights an employee who shall be responsible for monitoring the effects of flight delays, flight cancellations, and lengthy tarmac delays on passengers. This employee shall have input into decisions on which flights to cancel and which will be delayed the longest.
