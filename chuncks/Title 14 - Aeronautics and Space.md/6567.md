
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space

### Subchapter G - Air Carriers and Operators for Compensation or Hire: Certification and Operations

##### Subpart U - Dispatching and Flight Release Rules
###### ยง 121.657 Flight altitude rules.
####### Day VFR operations.

(b)No certificate holder conducting domestic operations may operate a passenger-carrying aircraft and no certificate holder conducting flag or supplemental operations may operate any aircraft under VFR during the day at an altitude less than 1,000 feet above the surface or less than 1,000 feet from any mountain, hill, or other obstruction to flight.
