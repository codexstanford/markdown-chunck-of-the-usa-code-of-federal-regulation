### Subchapter K - Administrative Regulations
#### PART 193 - PROTECTION OF VOLUNTARILY SUBMITTED INFORMATION
##### ยง 193.3 Definitions.
###### Voluntary

means that the information was not required to be submitted as part of a mandatory program, and was not submitted as a condition of doing business with the government. "Voluntarily-provided information" does not include information submitted as part of complying with statutory, regulatory, or contractual requirements, except that information submitted as part of complying with a voluntary program under this part is considered to be voluntarily provided.
