### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix C to Part 152 - Procurement Procedures and Requirements
###### Advertising general.

9.Unless the Administrator approves another method for use on a particular airport development project, each contract and supplemental agreement for construction work on a project in the amount of more than $10,000 must be awarded on the basis of public advertising and open competitive bidding under the local law applicable to the letting of public contracts.
