
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space

### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 29 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: TRANSPORT CATEGORY ROTORCRAFT
##### Subpart B - Flight
###### Flight Characteristics
####### ยง 29.175 Demonstration of static longitudinal stability.
######## Climb.

(a)Static longitudinal stability must be shown in the climb condition at speeds from Vy - 10 kt to Vy + 10 kt with-

(1) Critical weight;

(2) Critical center of gravity;

(3) Maximum continuous power;

(4) The landing gear retracted; and

(5) The rotorcraft trimmed at Vy.
