## Chapter III - Commercial Space Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 401 - ORGANIZATION AND DEFINITIONS
##### ยง 401.7 Definitions.
###### Operating environment

means an environment that a launch or reentry vehicle component will experience during its lifecycle. Operating environments include shock, vibration, thermal cycle, acceleration, humidity, thermal vacuum, or other environments relevant to system or material degradation.
