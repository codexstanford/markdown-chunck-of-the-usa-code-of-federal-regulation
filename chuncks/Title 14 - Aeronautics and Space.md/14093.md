## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1253 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF SEX IN EDUCATION PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart E - Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Employment in Education Programs or Activities Prohibited
##### § 1253.535 Effect of state or local law or other requirements.
###### Prohibitory requirements.

(a)The obligation to comply with §§ 1253.500 through 1253.550 is not obviated or alleviated by the existence of any State or local law or other requirement that imposes prohibitions or limits upon employment of members of one sex that are not imposed upon members of the other sex.
