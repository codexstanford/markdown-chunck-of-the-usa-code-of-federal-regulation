### Subchapter J - Navigational Facilities

##### Subpart J - Microwave Landing System (MLS)
###### ยง 171.313 Azimuth performance requirements.
####### Dynamic sidelobe levels.

(i)With the antenna scanning normally, the dynamic sidelobe level that is detected by a receiver at any point within the proportional coverage sector must be down at least 10 dB from the peak of the main beam. Outside the coverage sector, the radiation from the scanning beam antenna must be of such a nature that receiver warning will not be removed or suitable OCI signals must be provided.
