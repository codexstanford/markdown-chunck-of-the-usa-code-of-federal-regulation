#### PART 139 - CERTIFICATION OF AIRPORTS

###### ยง 139.5 Definitions.
####### Wildlife hazard

means a potential for a damaging aircraft collision with wildlife on or near an airport. As used in this part, "wildlife" includes feral animals and domestic animals out of the control of their owners.
