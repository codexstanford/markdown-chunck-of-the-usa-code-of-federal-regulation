#### PART 153 - AIRPORT OPERATIONS
##### Subpart A - Aviation Safety Inspector Access
###### ยง 153.3 Definitions.
####### Security Identification Display Area (SIDA)

means a portion of an airport, specified in the airport security program, in which security measures specified in Title 49 of the Code of Federal Regulations are carried out. This area includes the secured area and may include other areas of the airport.
