### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 151 - FEDERAL AID TO AIRPORTS
##### Appendix H to Part 151
###### I. Subcontracts.

The contractor will insert in each of his subcontracts the clauses contained in paragraphs A through H and J of this provision, and also a clause requiring the subcontractors to include these provisions in any lower tier subcontracts which they may enter into, together with a clause requiring this insertion in any further subcontracts that may in turn be made (29 CFR 5.5(a)(6), 5.5(c)(4)).
