## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration

###### Subrecipient

(l)means any of the entities in the definition of "recipient" to which a recipient extents or passes on Federal financial assistance. A subrecipient is generally regarded as a recipient of Federal financial assistance and has all the duties of a recipient in these regulations.
