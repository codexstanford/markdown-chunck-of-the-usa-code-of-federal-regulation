## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter E - Organization
#### PART 385 - STAFF ASSIGNMENTS AND REVIEW OF ACTION UNDER ASSIGNMENTS
##### Subpart C - Procedure on Review of Staff Action
###### ยง 385.30 Persons who may petition for review.
####### Provided,

Petitions for review may be filed by the applicant; by persons who have availed themselves of the opportunity, if any, to participate in the matter at the staff action level; and by persons who have not had opportunity to so participate or show good and sufficient cause for not having participated:That such persons, other than the applicant, disclose a substantial interest which would be adversely affected by the respective staff action.
