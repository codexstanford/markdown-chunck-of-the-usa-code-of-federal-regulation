### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix A to Part 152 - Contract and Labor Provisions
###### H. Withholding for unpaid wages and liquidated damages.

The FAA may withhold or cause to be withheld, from any monies payable on account of work performed by the contractor or subcontractor, such sums as may administratively be determined to be necessary to satisfy any liabilities of such contractor or subcontractor for unpaid wages and liquidated damages as provided in paragraph G of this provision (29 CFR 5.5(c)(3)).
