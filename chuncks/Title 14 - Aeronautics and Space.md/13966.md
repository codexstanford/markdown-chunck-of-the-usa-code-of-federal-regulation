## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1252 - NONDISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF AGE IN PROGRAMS OR ACTIVITIES RECEIVING FEDERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE
#### Subpart 1252.2 - Standards for Determining Age Discrimination
##### ยง 1252.200 Rules against age discrimination.
###### General rule:

(a)No person in the United States shall, on the basis of age be excluded from participation in, be denied the benefits of, or be subjected to discrimination under any program or activity receiving Federal financial assistance.
