### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 158 - PASSENGER FACILITY CHARGES (PFC'S)
##### Subpart A - General
###### § 158.3 Definitions.
####### Notice of intent (to impose or use PFC revenue)

means a notice under § 158.30 from a public agency controlling a non-hub airport that it intends to impose a PFC and/or use PFC revenue. Except for §§ 158.25 through 30, "notice of intent" can be used interchangeably with "application."
