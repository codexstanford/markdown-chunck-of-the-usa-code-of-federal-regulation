### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 151 - FEDERAL AID TO AIRPORTS
##### Appendix H to Part 151
###### J. Contract termination; debarment.

A breach of paragraphs A through I of this provision may be grounds for termination of the contract. A breach of paragraphs A through E and I may also be grounds for debarment as provided in 29 CFR 5.6 of the regulations of the Secretary of Labor (29 CFR 5.5(a)(8)).
