### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix D to Part 152 - Assurances
###### Interfering right.

31.If at any time it is determined by the FAA that there is any outstanding right or claim of right in or to the Airport property, other than those set forth in Part II of the Application for Federal Assistance, the existence of which creates an undue risk of interference with the operation of the Airport or the performance of the covenants of this part, the sponsor will acquire, extinguish, or modify such right or claim of right in a manner acceptable to the FAA.
