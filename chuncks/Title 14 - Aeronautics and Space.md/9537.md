## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter A - Economic Regulations
#### PART 234 - AIRLINE SERVICE QUALITY PERFORMANCE REPORTS
##### ยง 234.2 Definitions.
###### Extra-section flight

means a flight conducted as an integral part of scheduled passenger service, that has not been provided for in published schedules and is required for transportation of traffic that cannot be accommodated on the regularly scheduled flight.
