## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1214 - SPACE FLIGHT
#### Subpart 1214.6 - Mementos Aboard NASA Missions
##### ยง 1214.603 Official Flight Kit.
###### Purpose.

(a)The Official Flight Kit (OFK) on a particular mission allows NASA, and other domestic and friendly foreign countries' organizations with NASA approval, to utilize mementos as awards and commendations or preserve them in museums or archives. No personal items will be carried in the OFK.
