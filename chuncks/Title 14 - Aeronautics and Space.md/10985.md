## Chapter II - Office of the Secretary, Department of Transportation (Aviation Proceedings)
### Subchapter F - Policy Statements
#### PART 399 - STATEMENTS OF GENERAL POLICY
##### Subpart E - Policies Relating to Hearing Matters
###### ยง 399.62 Target dates in hearing cases.
####### Issuance of target dates.

(b)In cases to which this section applies, the Department or the administrative law judge, as the situation calls for, shall issue a notice of the target date for the completion of the initial or recommended decision, final decision, or decision on a petition for review or reconsideration. The Department or the administrative law judge shall endeavor to render the pending decision not later than the target date.
