#### PART 139 - CERTIFICATION OF AIRPORTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 139.5 Definitions.
####### Class II airport

means an airport certificated to serve scheduled operations of small air carrier aircraft and the unscheduled passenger operations of large air carrier aircraft. A Class II airport cannot serve scheduled large air carrier aircraft.
