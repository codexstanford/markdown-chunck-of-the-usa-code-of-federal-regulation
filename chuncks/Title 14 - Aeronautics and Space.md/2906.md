## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft

##### Subpart D - Design and Construction

####### ยง 29.861 Fire protection of structure, controls, and other parts.

Each part of the structure, controls, and the rotor mechanism, and other parts essential to controlled landing and (for category A) flight that would be affected by powerplant fires must be isolated under ยง 29.1191, or must be-
