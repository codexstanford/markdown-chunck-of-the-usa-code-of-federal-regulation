
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 29 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: TRANSPORT CATEGORY ROTORCRAFT
##### Subpart G - Operating Limitations and Information
###### Operating Limitations
####### ยง 29.1521 Powerplant limitations.
######## Two and one-half minute OEI power operation.

(f)Unless otherwise authorized, the use of 2-minute OEI power must be limited to engine failure operation of multiengine, turbine-powered rotorcraft for not longer than 2minutes for any period in which that power is used. The use of 2-minute OEI power must also be limited by-

(1) The maximum rotational speed, which may not be greater than-

(i) The maximum value determined by the rotor design; or

(ii) The maximum value shown during the type tests;

(2) The maximum allowable gas temperature;

(3) The maximum allowable torque; and

(4) The maximum allowable oil temperature.
