
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter C - Aircraft
#### PART 25 - AIRWORTHINESS STANDARDS: TRANSPORT CATEGORY AIRPLANES
##### Subpart G - Operating Limitations and Information
###### Operating Limitations
####### § 25.1511 Flap extended speed.
######## V,V

The established flap extended speedmust be established so that it does not exceed the design flap speedchosen under §§ 25.335(e) and 25.345, for the corresponding flap positions and engine powers.
