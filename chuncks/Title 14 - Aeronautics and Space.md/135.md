
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter A - Definitions and General Requirements
#### PART 1 - DEFINITIONS AND ABBREVIATIONS
##### ยง 1.1 General definitions.
###### Powered parachute

means a powered aircraft comprised of a flexible or semi-rigid wing connected to a fuselage so that the wing is not in position for flight until the aircraft is in motion. The fuselage of a powered parachute contains the aircraft engine, a seat for each occupant and is attached to the aircraft's landing gear.
