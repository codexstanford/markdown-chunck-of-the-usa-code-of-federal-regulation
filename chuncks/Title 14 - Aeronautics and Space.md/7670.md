#### PART 139 - CERTIFICATION OF AIRPORTS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 139.5 Definitions.
####### Safety area

means a defined area comprised of either a runway or taxiway and the surrounding surfaces that is prepared or suitable for reducing the risk of damage to aircraft in the event of an undershoot, overshoot, or excursion from a runway or the unintentional departure from a taxiway.
