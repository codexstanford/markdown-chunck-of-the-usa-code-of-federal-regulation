#### PART 141 - PILOT SCHOOLS
##### Appendix C to Part 141 - Instrument Rating Course

1.This appendix prescribes the minimum curriculum for an instrument rating course and an additional instrument rating course, required under this part, for the following ratings:

(a) Instrument-airplane.

(b) Instrument-helicopter.
