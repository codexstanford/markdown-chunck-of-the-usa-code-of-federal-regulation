
# Title 14 - Aeronautics and Space
## Chapter I - Federal Aviation Administration, Department of Transportation
### Subchapter F - Air Traffic and General Operating Rules
#### PART 99 - SECURITY CONTROL OF AIR TRAFFIC
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 99.3 Definitions.
####### Defense visual flight rules

(DVFR) means, for the purposes of this subpart, a flight within an ADIZ conducted by any aircraft (except for Department of Defense and law enforcement aircraft) in accordance with visual flight rules in part 91 of this title.
