### Subchapter I - Airports
#### PART 152 - AIRPORT AID PROGRAM
##### Appendix D to Part 152 - Assurances
###### Fee and rental structure.

28.The airport operator or owner will maintain a fee and rental structure for the facilities and services being provided the airport users which will make the Airport as self-sustaining as possible under the circumstances existing at the Airport, taking into account such factors as the volume of traffic and economy of collection.
