## Chapter V - National Aeronautics and Space Administration
### PART 1241 - TO RESEARCH, EVALUATE, ASSESS, AND TREAT (TREAT) ASTRONAUTS
#### ยง 1241.15 Definitions.
##### Secondary Payer

means the entity that pays after all primary payers have paid, up to the limits of their coverage. Secondary payments, as described in the TREAT Astronauts Act, are payments or reimbursement for the medical monitoring, diagnosis, or treatment secondary to any obligation of the U.S. Government or any third party under any other provision of law or contractual agreement to pay for or provide such medical monitoring, diagnosis, or treatment.
