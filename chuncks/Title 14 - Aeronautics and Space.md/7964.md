### Subchapter H - Schools and Other Certificated Agencies
#### PART 142 - TRAINING CENTERS
##### Subpart A - General
###### ยง 142.3 Definitions.
####### Specialty Curriculum

means a set of courses that is designed to satisfy a requirement of the Federal Aviation Regulations and that is approved by the Administrator for use by a particular training center or satellite training center. The specialty curriculum includes training requirements unique to one or more training center clients.
