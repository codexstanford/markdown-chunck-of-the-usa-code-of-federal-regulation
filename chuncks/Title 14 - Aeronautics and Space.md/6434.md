######## ยง 121.467 Flight attendant duty period limitations and rest requirements: Domestic, flag, and supplemental operations.
######### Flight attendant

means an individual, other than a flight crewmember, who is assigned by a certificate holder conducting domestic, flag, or supplemental operations, in accordance with the required minimum crew complement under the certificate holder's operations specifications or in addition to that minimum complement, to duty in an aircraft during flight time and whose duties include but are not necessarily limited to cabin-safety-related responsibilities.
