### Subchapter I - Airports

##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 150.7 Definitions.
####### Day-night average sound level

(DNL) means the 24-hour average sound level, in decibels, for the period from midnight to midnight, obtained after the addition of ten decibels to sound levels for the periods between midnight and 7 a.m., and between 10 p.m., and midnight, local time. The symbol for DNL is L.
