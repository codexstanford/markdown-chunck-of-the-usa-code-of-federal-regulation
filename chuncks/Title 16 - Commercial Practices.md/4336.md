##### Subpart A - The Standard
###### ยง 1204.1 Scope of the standard.
####### Recommended materials.

(2)(i) Since a substantial portion of the accidents addressed by this standard occur when the antenna is being taken down after it has been installed in an outdoor environment for a number of years, the materials selected to provide protection from shock should be weather resistant.

(ii) Although other materials may also be suitable, materials meeting the following criteria should be reasonably weather resistant:

(A) Material composition includes an ultraviolet stabilizer or screen.

(B) Heat resistance of 212 &#xB0;F (100 &#xB0;C) without loss of elasticity (ANSI/ASTM D 746-79).

(C) Moisture absorption of not more than 0.2 percent (ANSI/ASTM D 570-77).

(D) For heat shrinkable sleeving, temperature flexibility to -40 &#xB0;F (-40 &#xB0;C) with no cracks (Mil Spec. MIL-I-23053C, 20 May 1976).
