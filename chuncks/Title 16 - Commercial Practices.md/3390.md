####### Net worth exhibit; confidential treatment.

(b)(1) Each applicant except a qualified tax-exempt organization or cooperative association must provide with its application a detailed exhibit showing the net worth of the applicant and any affiliates (as defined in ยง 1025.70(d)(6) of this subpart) when the proceeding was initiated.
