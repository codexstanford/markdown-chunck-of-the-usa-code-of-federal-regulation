##### ยง 303.7 Generic names and definitions for manufactured fibers.
###### Rayon.,lyocell

(d)A manufactured fiber composed of regenerated cellulose, as well as manufactured fibers composed of regenerated cellulose in which substituents have replaced not more than 15% of the hydrogens of the hydroxyl groups. Where the fiber is composed of cellulose precipitated from an organic solution in which no substitution of the hydroxyl groups takes place and no chemical intermediates are formed, the termmay be used as a generic description of the fiber.
