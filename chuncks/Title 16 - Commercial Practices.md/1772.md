
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under Specific Acts of Congress
#### PART 313 - PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION
##### § 313.2 Model privacy form and examples.
###### Model privacy form.

(a)Use of the model privacy form in appendix A of this part, consistent with the instructions in appendix A, constitutes compliance with the notice content requirements of §§ 313.6 and 313.7 of this part, although use of the model privacy form is not required.
