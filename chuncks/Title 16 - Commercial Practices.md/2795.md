
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter H - Rules, Regulations, Statements and Interpretations Under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976
#### PART 801 - COVERAGE RULES
##### ยง 801.31 Acquisitions of voting securities by offerees in tender offers.
###### Provided however,

(d) The voting securities to be acquired by the offeree may be placed into escrow, for the benefit of the offeree, pending expiration or termination of the waiting period with respect to the acquisition of such securities;That no person may vote any voting securities placed into escrow pursuant to this paragraph.
