
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1306 - BAN OF HAZARDOUS LAWN DARTS
##### ยง 1306.4 Findings.
###### The need of the public for lawn darts, and the effects of the rule on their utility, cost, and availability.

(d)The need of the public for lawn darts is for recreational enjoyment. Substitute recreational enjoyment can be obtained from other products. Lawn darts will not be available through commercial channels after the effective date of the ban.
