
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter F - Fair Credit Reporting Act
#### PART 682 - DISPOSAL OF CONSUMER REPORT INFORMATION AND RECORDS
##### ยง 682.3 Proper disposal of consumer information.
###### Standard.

(a)Any person who maintains or otherwise possesses consumer information for a business purpose must properly dispose of such information by taking reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access to or use of the information in connection with its disposal.
