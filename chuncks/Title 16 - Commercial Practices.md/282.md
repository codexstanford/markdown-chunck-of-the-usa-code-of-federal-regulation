
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

### Subchapter A - Organization, Procedures and Rules of Practice

####### Modification of requests.

(3)The authorized representative shall modify the request for additional information or documentary material, or recommend such modification to the responsible Assistant Director of the Bureau of Competition, if he or she determines that a less burdensome request would be consistent with the needs of the investigation. A request for additional information or documentary material may be modified only in writing signed by the authorized representative.
