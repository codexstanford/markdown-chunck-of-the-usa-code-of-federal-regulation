
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter F - Refrigerator Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1750 - STANDARD FOR DEVICES TO PERMIT THE OPENING OF HOUSEHOLD REFRIGERATOR DOORS FROM THE INSIDE
##### ยง 1750.5 Detailed requirements.
###### Description and location of knob(s).

(b)The knob(s) shall resemble a conventional doorknob in shape and size and shall be mounted near the latch side of the door extending into the cabinet at least 6.3 millimeters (inch) beyond any inner door surface within a 15.2-centimeter (6-inch) radius of the knob center. The knob(s) shall be mounted in such a manner that there is a minimum of 19.0-millimeter (-inch) clearance between the inner periphery of the knob(s) and adjacent inner door surfaces.
