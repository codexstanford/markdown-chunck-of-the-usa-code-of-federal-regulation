
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

#### PART 1205 - SAFETY STANDARD FOR WALK-BEHIND POWER LAWN MOWERS
##### Subpart B - Certification
###### ยง 1205.32 Definitions.
####### Manufactured

(b)means the earliest point at which the mower is in the form in which it will be sold or offered for sale to the consumer or is in the form in which it will be shipped to a distributor or retailer. In these forms, a "manufactured" mower may still require partial assembly by the consumer or the lawn mower dealer.
