
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1220 - SAFETY STANDARD FOR NON-FULL-SIZE BABY CRIBS
##### ยง 1220.2 Requirements for non-full-size baby cribs.
###### Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Non-Full-Size Baby Cribs/Play Yards,,www.astm.org.,fedreg.legal@nara.gov,,www.archives.gov/federal-register/cfr/ibr-locations.html.

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, each non-full-size baby crib shall comply with all applicable provisions of ASTM F406-19,approved March 15, 2019. The Director of the Federal Register approves the incorporation by reference listed in this section in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 552(a) and 1 CFR part 51. (b) Comply with the ASTM F406-19 standard with the following exclusions:

(1) Do not comply with sections 5.6.2 through 5.6.2.4 of ASTM F406-19.

(2) Do not comply with section 5.16.2 of ASTM F406-19.
