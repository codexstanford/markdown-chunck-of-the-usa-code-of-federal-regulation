
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter D - Trade Regulation Rules
#### PART 453 - FUNERAL INDUSTRY PRACTICES
##### ยง 453.3 Misrepresentations.
###### General provisions on legal and cemetery requirements,Deceptive acts or practices.

(d)-(1)In selling or offering to sell funeral goods or funeral services to the public, it is a deceptive act or practice for funeral providers to represent that federal, state, or local laws, or particular cemeteries or crematories, require the purchase of any funeral goods or funeral services when such is not the case.
