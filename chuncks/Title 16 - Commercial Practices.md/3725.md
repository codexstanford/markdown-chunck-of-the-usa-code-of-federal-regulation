
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1102 - PUBLICLY AVAILABLE CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY INFORMATION DATABASE
##### Subpart C - Procedural Requirements
###### ยง 1102.24 Designation of confidential information.
####### Assistance with defense.

(e)No request to redact confidential information from a report of harm pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552(b)(4) should be made by any person who does not intend in good faith, and so certifies in writing, to assist the Commission in the defense of any judicial proceeding that thereafter might be brought to compel the disclosure of information that the Commission has determined to be a trade secret or privileged or confidential commercial or financial information.
