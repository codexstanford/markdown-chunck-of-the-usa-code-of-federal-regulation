
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

###### ยง 1203.4 Definitions.
####### Basic plane

(a)means an anatomical plane that includes the auditory meatuses (the external ear openings) and the inferior orbital rims (the bottom edges of the eye sockets). The ISO headforms are marked with a plane corresponding to this basic plane (see Figures 1 and 2 of this part).
