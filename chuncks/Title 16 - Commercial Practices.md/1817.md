
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter C - Regulations Under Specific Acts of Congress
#### PART 313 - PRIVACY OF CONSUMER FINANCIAL INFORMATION
##### Appendix A to Part 313 - Model Privacy Form
###### Last revised date.

(a)The financial institution must insert in the upper right-hand corner the date on which the notice was last revised. The information shall appear in minimum 8-point font as "rev. [month/year]" using either the name or number of the month, such as "rev. July 2009" or "rev. 7/09".
