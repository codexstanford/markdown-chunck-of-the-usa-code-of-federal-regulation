
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

##### ยง 1500.135 Summary of guidelines for determining chronic toxicity.
###### Modeling.

(B)Predictions of exposure to a chemical using mathematical models can be based on physical and chemical principles, such as mass balance principles. Mass balance models should consider the source strength of the product of interest, housing characteristics, and ambient conditions likely to be encountered by the studied population.
