
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter F - Fair Credit Reporting Act
#### PART 609 - FREE ELECTRONIC CREDIT MONITORING FOR ACTIVE DUTY MILITARY
##### ยง 609.3 Requirement to provide free electronic credit monitoring service.
###### Determining whether a consumer must receive electronic credit monitoring service.

(b)Nationwide consumer reporting agencies may condition provision of the service required under paragraph (a) of this section upon the consumer providing:

(1) Appropriate proof of identity;

(2) Contact information; and

(3) Appropriate proof that the consumer is an active duty military consumer.
