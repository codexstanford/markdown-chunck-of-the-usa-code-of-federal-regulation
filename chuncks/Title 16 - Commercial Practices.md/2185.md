
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter D - Trade Regulation Rules
#### PART 453 - FUNERAL INDUSTRY PRACTICES
##### ยง 453.1 Definitions.
###### Outer burial container.

(n)An "outer burial container" is any container which is designed for placement in the grave around the casket including, but not limited to, containers commonly known as burial vaults, grave boxes, and grave liners.
