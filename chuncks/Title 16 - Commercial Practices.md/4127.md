
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1130 - REQUIREMENTS FOR CONSUMER REGISTRATION OF DURABLE INFANT OR TODDLER PRODUCTS
##### ยง 1130.6 Requirements for format and text of registration forms.
###### Size of form.

(a)The form shall be at least the size of two standard post cards, connected with perforation for later separation, so that each of the two portions is at least 3inches high x 5 inches wide x 0.007 inches thick.
