
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter B - Consumer Product Safety Act Regulations
#### PART 1102 - PUBLICLY AVAILABLE CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY INFORMATION DATABASE
##### Subpart C - Procedural Requirements
###### ยง 1102.20 Transmission of reports of harm to the identified manufacturer or private labeler.
####### Method of transmission.

(d)The Commission will use the method of transmission and contact information provided by the manufacturer or private labeler. The Commission will transmit reports of harm to a manufacturer or private labeler who has registered with the Commission as described in paragraph (f) of this section. If a manufacturer or private labeler has not registered with the Commission, the Commission will send reports of harm through the United States mail to the firm's principal place of business, unless the Commission selects another equally effective method of transmission.
