
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

###### ยง 1203.30 Purpose, basis, and scope.
####### Purpose.

(a)The purpose of this subpart is to establish requirements that manufacturers and importers of bicycle helmets subject to the Safety Standard for Bicycle Helmets (subpart A of this part 1203) shall issue certificates of compliance in the form specified.
