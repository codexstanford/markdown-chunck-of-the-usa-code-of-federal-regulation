
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter II - Consumer Product Safety Commission
### Subchapter A - General
#### PART 1019 - EXPORT OF NONCOMPLYING, MISBRANDED, OR BANNED PRODUCTS
##### Subpart A - Procedures for Export of Noncomplying, Misbranded, or Banned Products
###### ยง 1019.5 Time notification must be made to Commission; reductions of time.
####### Time of notification.

(a)The notification of intent to export must be received by the Commission's Assistant Executive Director for Compliance at least 30 days before the noncomplying goods are to leave the customs territory of the United States. If the notification of intent to export includes more than one shipment of noncomplying goods to a foreign country, the Assistant Executive Director for Compliance must receive the notification at least 30 days before the first shipment of noncomplying goods is to leave the customs territory of the United States.
