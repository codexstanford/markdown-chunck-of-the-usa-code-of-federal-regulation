
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter A - Organization, Procedures and Rules of Practice
#### PART 1 - GENERAL PROCEDURES
##### Subpart T - Procedures for Review of Final Civil Sanctions Imposed under the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Act
###### ยง 1.146 Review of civil sanction by an Administrative Law Judge.
####### Decision by the Administrative Law Judge,When filed.

(d)-(1)The Administrative Law Judge must file a decision within 30 days of closing statements or, if no closing statements are ordered, within 30 days of the date on which reply findings of fact, conclusions of law, and briefs are due. The Administrative Law Judge may extend this time period for up to 30 days for good cause. The decision must be filed within 60 days of the conclusion of the administrative hearing.
