##### Subpart A - The Standard
###### § 1616.4 Sampling and acceptance procedures.
####### Disposition of rejected units.,Garment production testing

(4)Rejected units shall not be retested, used, or promoted for use in children's sleepwear as defined in §§ 1616.2(a) and 1615.1(a) of the Standard for the Flammability of Children's Sleepwear: Sizes 0 through 6X (FF 3-71) (subpart A of part 1615 of this chapter) except after reworking to improve the flammability characteristics and subsequent retesting in accordance with the procedures set forth in[Paragraph (c)(3) of this section].
