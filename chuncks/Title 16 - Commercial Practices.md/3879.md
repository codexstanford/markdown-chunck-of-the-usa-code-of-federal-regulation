###### ยง 1112.13 How does a third party conformity assessment body apply for CPSC acceptance?
####### Retraction of application.

(h)A third party conformity assessment body may retract a submitted CPSC Form 223 any time before the CPSC has acted on the submission. A retraction will not end or nullify any enforcement action that the CPSC is otherwise authorized by law to pursue.
