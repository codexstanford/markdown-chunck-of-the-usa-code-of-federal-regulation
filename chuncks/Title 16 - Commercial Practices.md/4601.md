####### Applicability.

(b)(1) Cellulose insulation which is subject to the standard includes all cellulose insulation, manufactured after the effective date (as described in ยง 1209.41), produced or distributed for sale to, or for the personal use, consumption, or enjoyment of, consumers in or around a permanent or temporary household or residence, a school, in recreation or otherwise.
