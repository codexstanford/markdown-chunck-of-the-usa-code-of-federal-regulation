
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter A - Organization, Procedures and Rules of Practice
#### PART 1 - GENERAL PROCEDURES
##### Subpart U - Procedures for Oversight of the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority's Annual Budget
###### ยง 1.151 Commission's decision on Authority's proposed budget.
####### Decisional criteria.

(c)The Commission will approve the proposed budget if the Commission determines that, on balance, the proposed budget serves the goals of the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Act in a prudent and cost-effective manner, utilizing commercially reasonable terms with all outside vendors, and that its anticipated revenues are sufficient to meet its anticipated expenditures.
