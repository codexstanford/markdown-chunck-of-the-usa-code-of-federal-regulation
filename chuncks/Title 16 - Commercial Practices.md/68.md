
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter A - Organization, Procedures and Rules of Practice
#### PART 1 - GENERAL PROCEDURES
##### Subpart C - Rules Promulgated Under Authority Other Than Section 18(a)(1)(B) of the FTC Act
###### ยง 1.22 Rulemaking.
####### Use of rules in adjudicative proceedings.

(c)When a rule is relevant to any issue involved in an ajudicative proceeding thereafter instituted, the Commission may rely upon the rule to resolve such issue, provided that the respondent shall have been given a fair hearing on the applicability of the rule to the particular case.
