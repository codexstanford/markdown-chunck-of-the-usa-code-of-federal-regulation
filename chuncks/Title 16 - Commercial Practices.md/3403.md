(e)The applicant and complaint counsel may agree on a proposed settlement of the award before final action on the application, either in connection with a settlement of the underlying proceeding, or after the underlying proceeding has been concluded, in accordance with the Commission's standard settlement procedure (16 CFR 1115.20(b), 1118.20, 1025.26, and 1605.3).
