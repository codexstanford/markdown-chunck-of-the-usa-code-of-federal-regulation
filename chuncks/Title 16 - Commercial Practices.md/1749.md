
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices

####### Information you receive outside of an exception.

(b)(1)If you receive nonpublic personal information from a nonaffiliated financial institution other than under an exception in ยง 313.14 or ยง 313.15 of this part, you may disclose the information only:
