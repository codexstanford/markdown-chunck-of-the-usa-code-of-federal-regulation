
# Title 16 - Commercial Practices
## Chapter I - Federal Trade Commission
### Subchapter A - Organization, Procedures and Rules of Practice
#### PART 1 - GENERAL PROCEDURES
##### Subpart B - Rules and Rulemaking Under Section 18(a)(1)(B) of the FTC Act
###### ยง 1.11 Commencement of a rulemaking proceeding.
####### Notice of proposed rulemaking.

(a)A trade regulation rule proceeding will commence with a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM). An NPRM will be published in thenot sooner than 30 days after it has been submitted to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate and to the Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives.
