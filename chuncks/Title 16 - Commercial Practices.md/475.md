(e)The applicant and complaint counsel may agree on a proposed settlement of the award before final action on the application. A proposed award settlement entered into in connection with a consent agreement covering the underlying proceeding will be considered in accordance with ยง 3.25. The Commission may request findings of fact or recommendations on the award settlement from the Administrative Law Judge.
