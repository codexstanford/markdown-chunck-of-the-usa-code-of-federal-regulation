
# Title 39 - Postal Service
## Chapter III - Postal Regulatory Commission
### Subchapter B - Seeking Information from the Commission
#### PART 3006 - PUBLIC RECORDS AND FREEDOM OF INFORMATION ACT
##### ยง 3006.50 Fees - definitions as used in this part.
###### Direct costs

means the expenditures the Commission incurs in searching for, duplicating, and, where applicable, reviewing documents to respond to a request. They include (without limitation) the salary of the employee(s) performing work (the basic pay rate of such employee(s) plus 16 percent to cover benefits).
