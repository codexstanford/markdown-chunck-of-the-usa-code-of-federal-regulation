
# Title 39 - Postal Service
## Chapter III - Postal Regulatory Commission
### Subchapter C - General Rules of Practice for Proceedings Before the Commission
#### PART 3010 - RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE
##### Subpart A - General Provisions
###### ยง 3010.105 Consideration of matters before the Commission.
####### en banc,en banc,

(a) Unless it orders otherwise, the Commission shall sitin all matters that come before it. In those proceedings in which a presiding officer is appointed, the Commission will continue to situnless modified by Commission notice or order, with the presiding officer responsible for those matters within the scope of the presiding officer's authority.

(b) A decision to establish a docket (other than certain negotiated service agreement dockets), close an active docket, or reach a final decision in any docket shall be by majority vote of the Commissioners then in office.
