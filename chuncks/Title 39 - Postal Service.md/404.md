
# Title 39 - Postal Service
## Chapter I - United States Postal Service
### Subchapter D - Organization and Administration
#### PART 233 - INSPECTION SERVICE AUTHORITY
##### ยง 233.12 Civil penalties.
###### Misleading references to the United States Government; Sweepstakes and deceptive mailings.

(c)Persons sending certain deceptive mail matter described in 39 U.S.C. 3001(h)-(k), including:

(1) Solicitations making false claims of Federal Government connection or approval;

(2) Certain solicitations for the purchase of a product or service that may be obtained without cost from the Federal Government;

(3) Solicitations containing improperly prepared "facsimile checks"; and

(4) Solicitations for "skill contests" and "sweepstakes" sent to individuals who, in accordance with 39 U.S.C. 3017(d), have requested that such materials not be mailed to them; may be liable to the United States for a civil penalty under 39 U.S.C. 3012(d). As adjusted under Public Law 114-74, this penalty is not to exceed $17,128 for each mailing.
