###### Statement of Uncontested Facts,

(1) Motions for summary judgment shall include a separate document titledwhich shall contain in separately numbered paragraphs all of the material facts upon which the moving party bases its motion and as to which it contends there is no genuine issue. This statement shall include references to affidavits, declarations and/or documents relied upon to support such statement.
